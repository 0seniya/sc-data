17. Water

|| de dag khyim la mi dga’ bar || dran daṅ ldan pa ’bad pa yis ||
ṅaṅ pas mtsho ṅan spoṅ ba ltar || de dag khyim spaṅs chu bo rgal ||

brtan pa ’jig rten ges byuṅ nas || bdud kyi sde rnams rab bcom ste ||
ñi ma’i lam nas ṅaṅ pa ltar || rdzu ’phrul gyis ni mkha’ la ’gro ||

tshaṅs par spyod pa ma spyad ciṅ || gźon nur nor ni ma rñed pa ||
mtsho ṅan ña ñuṅ ṅogs dag na || chu skyar rgan sems bźin du ’gyur ||

tshaṅs par spyod pa ma spyad ciṅ || gźon nur nor ni ma rñed pa ||
sṅan chad byas pa rjes dran źiṅ || lus bskums nas ni ñal bar ’gyur ||

sdig pa chuṅ byas bdag ñid kyi || phyi bźin mi ’oṅ ma sems śig ||
chu yi thigs pa lhuṅ ba yis || bum chen gaṅ ba ji bźin du ||
chuṅ zad chuṅ zad bsags pa yi || sdig pas byis pa gaṅ bar ’gyur ||

dge ba chuṅ byas bdag ñid kyi || phyi bźin mi ’od ma sems śig ||
chu yi thigs pa lhuṅ ba yis || bum chen gaṅ ba ji bźin du ||
chuṅ zad chuṅ zad bsags pa yi || dge bas brtan pa gaṅ bar ’gyur ||

gaṅ źig rags brtsigs mtsho ṅan sṅaṅs || rgya mtsho’i chu bo rgas ’dod pa ||
skye bos gziṅs ni bcas nas kyaṅ || skye bo mkhas pa rgal bar byed ||

rgal nas saṅs rgyas bcom ldan ’das || bram ze sgam sar gnas par mdzad ||
de la dge sloṅ khrus byed ciṅ || ñan thos rnams kyis gziṅs kyaṅ bcas ||
ji ltar mtsho chen zab mo dag || rab tu mnam daṅ rñog med ltar ||
de bźin mkhas pa raṅ daṅ bas || ’di ni chos ni ñan par byed ||

’di ltar kun na chu yod pas || su źig tshol źiṅ rgyu byed de ||
khron pa’i chu lta ci źig dgos || sred pa rtsa nas gcad par gyis ||

btso blag mkhan dag chus ’khru źiṅ || mda’ mkhan mes ni sroṅ ba daṅ ||
śiṅ mkhan śiṅ la ’jog sbyor ltar || mkhas pas bdag ñid gdul bar bya ||

nam mkha’ lta bur chags med ciṅ || sgo them lta bur g-yo ba med ||
rñog pa can gyi mtsho lta bur || mkhas pa ’khor ba la mi dga’ ||

chu’i tshoms te bcu bdun pa’o ||

Translation by Vidyākaraprabhā and Rin chen mchog.

9–10th century A.D.
