Udānavarga

1. Anityavarga

siddham

stīnamiddham vinodyeha
saṃpraharṣya ca mānasam |
śṛṇutemaṃ pravakṣyāmi
udānaṃ jinabhāṣitam ||

evam uktaṃ bhagavatā
sarvābhijñena tāyinā |
anukampakenarṣiṇā
śarīrāntimadhāriṇā ||

anityā bata samṣkārā
utpādavyayadharmiṇaḥ |
utpadya hi nirudhyante
teṣāṃ vyupaśamaḥ sukham ||

ko nu harṣaḥ ka ānanda
evaṃ prajvalite sati |
andhakāraṃ praviṣṭāḥ stha
pradīpaṃ na gaveṣatha ||

yānīmāny apaviddhāni
vikṣiptāni diśo diśam |
kapotavarṇāny asthīni
tāni drṣṭveha kā ratiḥ ||

yām eva prathamāṃ rātriṃ
garbhe vasati mānavaḥ |
aviṣṭhitaḥ sa vrajati
gataś ca na nivartate ||

sāyam eke na dṛśyante
kālyaṃ dṛṣṭā mahājanāḥ |
kālyaṃ caike na dṛṣyante
sāyaṃ dṛṣṭā mahājanāḥ ||

tatra ko viśvasen martyo
daharo ’smīti jīvite |
daharāpi mriyante hi
narā nāryaś ca-n ekaśaḥ ||

garbha eke vinaśyante
tathaike sūtikākule |
parisṛptās tathā hy eke
tathaike paridhāvinaḥ ||

ye ca vṛddhā ye ca dahrā
ye ca madhyamapuruṣāḥ |
anupūrvaṃ prav­raja­nti
phalaṃ pakvaṃ va bandhanāt ||

yathā phalānāṃ pakvānāṃ
nityaṃ patanato bhayaṃ |
evaṃ jātasya martyasya
nityaṃ maraṇato bhayam ||

yathāpi kumbhakāreṇa
mṛttikābhājanaṃ kṛtaṃ |
sarvaṃ bhedanaparyantaṃ
evaṃ martyasya jīvitam ||

yathāpi tantre vitate
yad yad utaṃ samupyate |
alpaṃ bhavati vātavyam
evaṃ martyasya jīvitam ||

yathā nadī pārvatīyā1
gacchate na nivartate |
evam āyur manuṣyāṇāṃ
gacchate na nivartate ||

kisaraṃ ca parittaṃ ca
tac ca duḥkhena saṃyutam |
udake daṇḍarājīva
kṣipram eva vinaśyati ||

yathā daṇḍena gopālo
gāḥ prāpayati gocaram |
evaṃ rogair jarāmṛtyuḥ
āyuḥ prāpayate nṛṇām ||

atiyānti hy ahorātrā
jīvitaṃ coparudhyate |
āyuḥ kṣīyati martyānāṃ
kunadīṣu yathaudakam ||

putro me ’sti dhanaṃ me ’stīty
evaṃ bālo vihanyate |
ātmaiva hy ātmano nāsti
kasya putraḥ kuto dhanam ||

anekāni sahasrāṇi
naranārīśatāni ca |
bhogāṃ vai samudānīya
vaśaṃ gacchanti mṛtyunaḥ ||

sarve kṣayāntā nicayāḥ
patanāntāḥ samucchrayāḥ |
saṃyogā viprayogāntā
maraṇāntaṃ hi jīvitam ||

sarve satvā mariṣyanti
maraṇāntaṃ hi jīvitaṃ |
yathākarma gamiṣyanti
puṇyapāpaphalopagāḥ ||

narakaṃ pāpakarmāṇaḥ
kṛtapuṇyās tu sadgatiṃ |
anye tu mārgaṃ bhāvyeha
nirvāsyanti nirāsravāḥ ||

naivāntarīkṣe na samudramadhye
na parvatānāṃ vivaraṃ praviśya |
na vidyate ’sau pṛthivīpradeśo
yatra sthitaṃ na prasaheta mṛtyuḥ ||

ye ceha bhutā bhaviṣyanti vā punaḥ
sarve gamiṣyanti prahāya deham |
tāṃ sarvahāniṃ kuśalo viditvā
dharme sthito brahmacaryaṃ careta ||

jirṇaṃ ca dṛṣṭveha tathaiva rogiṇaṃ
mṛtaṃ ca dṛṣṭvā vyapayātacetasaṃ |
jahau sa dhīro gṛhabandhanāni
kāmā hi lokasya na supraheyāḥ ||

jīryanti vai rājarathāḥ sucitrā
hy atho śarīram api jarām upaiti
satāṃ tu dharmo na jarām upaiti
santo hi taṃ satsu nivedayanti ||

dhik tvām astu jare grāmye
virupakaraṇī hy asi |
tathā manoramaṃ bimbaṃ
jarayā hy abhimarditam ||

yo ’pi varṣaśataṃ jīvet
so ’pi mṛtyuparāyaṇaḥ |
anu hy enaṃ jarā hanti
vyādhir va yadi vāntakaḥ ||

sadā vrajanti hy anivartamānā
divā ca rātrau ca vilujyamānāḥ |
matsya ivātīva hi tapyamānā
duḥkhena jātimaraṇena yuktāḥ ||

āyur divā ca rātrau ca
caratas tiṣṭhatas tathā |
nadīnāṃ vā yathā sroto
gacchate na nivartate ||

yeṣāṃ rātridivāpāye
hy āyur alpataraṃ bhavet |
alpodake va matsyānāṃ
kā nu teṣāṃ ratir bhavet ||

pari­jīrṇam idaṃ rupaṃ
roganīdaṃ prabhaṅguram
bhetsyate pūty asaṃdehaṃ
maraṇāntaṃ hi jīvitam ||

aciraṃ bata kāyo ’yaṃ
pṛthivīm adhiśeṣyate |
śunyo vyapetavijñāno
nirastaṃ vā kaḍaṅgaram ||

kim anena śarīreṇa
sravatā pūtinā sadā |
nityaṃ rogābhibhutena
jarāmaraṇabhīruṇā ||

anena pūtikāyena
hy ātureṇa prabhaṅguṇā |
nigacchatha parāṃ śāntiṃ
yogakṣemam anuttaram ||

iha varṣaṃ kariṣyāmi
hemantaṃ grīṣmam eva ca |
bālo vicintayaty evaṃ
antarāyaṃ na paśyati ||

taṃ putrapaśusaṃmattaṃ
vyāsaktamanasaṃ naram |
suptaṃ grāmaṃ mahaughaiva
mṛtyur ādāya gacchati ||

na santi putrās trāṇāya
na pitā nāpi bāndhavāḥ |
antakonābhibhūtasya
na hi trāṇā bhavanti te ||

idaṃ kṛtaṃ me kartavyaṃ
idaṃ kṛtvā bhaviṣyati |
ity evaṃ spandato martyāṃ
jarā mṛtyuś ca mardati ||

tasmāt sadā dhyānaratāh samāhitā
hy ātāpino jātijarāntadarśinaḥ |
māraṃ sasainyaṃ hy abhibhūya bhikṣavo
bhaveta jātīmaraṇasya pāragāḥ ||

|| anityavargaḥ || ||
