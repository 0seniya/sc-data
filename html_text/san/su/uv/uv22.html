Udānavarga

Srutavarga

sādhu śrutaṃ sucaritaṃ
sādhu cāpy aniketatā |
pradakṣiṇaṃ pravrajyā ca
śrāmaṇyasyānulomikam ||

bālā ihāvijānantaś
caranti hy amarā iva |
vijānatāṃ tu saddharmam
āturasyaiva śarvarī ||

yathā hy agāraṃ succhannaṃ
praviśya tamasā sphuṭam |
vidyamānāni rūpāṇi
cakṣuṣmāṃ hi na paśyati ||

tathaiveha naro nityaṃ
jñānavān api yo bhavet |
aśrutvā na vijānāti
dharmāṃ kalyāṇapāpakān ||

pradīpena tu rūpāṇi
cakṣuṣmāṃ paśyate yathā |
evaṃ srutvā vijānāti
dharmām kalyāṇapāpakān ||

srutvā dharmāṃ vijānāti
śrutvā pāpaṃ na sevate |
śrutvā hy anarthaṃ varjayate
śrutvā prāpnoti nirvṛtim ||

bahuśruto ’pi ced bhavati
śīloṣu tv asamāhitaḥ |
śīlatas taṃ vigarhanti
nāsya saṃ­padyato śrutam ||

alpaśruto ’pi ced bhavati
śīleṣu susamāhitaḥ |
śīlatas taṃ praśaṃsanti
tasya saṃpadyate śrutam ||

alpaśruto ’pi ced bhavati
śīlesu tv asamāhitaḥ |
ubhayatas taṃ vigarhanti
nāsya saṃpadyate vratam ||

bahu­śruto ’pi ced bhavati
śīleṣu susamāhitaḥ |
ubhayatas taṃ praśaṃsanti
tasya saṃpadyate vratam ||

bahu­śrutaṃ dharmadharaṃ
prājñaṃ nityaṃ samāhitam |
niṣkaṃ jāmbunadasyaiva
kas taṃ ninditum arhati ||

ye me rūpeṇa minvanti
ye rūpeṇa pramiṇvanti ye me ghoṣeṇā cānvagāḥ |
māṃ ghoṣeṇānuyānti ca | chandarāgavaśopetā
chandarāgavaśopetā na māṃ jānanti te janāḥ ||
na māṃ jānanti te janāḥ ||

ādhyātmaṃ ca na jānāti
bahirdhā tu vipaśyati |
bahirdhāphaladarśī tu
sa vai ghoṣena nīyate ||

ādhyātmaṃ tu prajānāti
bahirdhā ca na paśyati |
ādhyātmaphaladarśī tu
sa vai ghoṣeṇa nīyate ||

ādhyātmaṃ ca na jānāti
bahirdhā ca na paśyati |
ubhaye ’phaladarśī tu
sa vai ghoseṇa nīyate ||

ādhyatmaṃ ca prajānāti
bahirdhā ca vipaśyati |
sa tu niḥsaraṇaprajño
na vai ghoṣeṇa nīyate ||

bahu śṛṇoti śrotreṇa
śrotreṇa śruyato bahu bahu paśyati cakṣuṣā |
dṛśyate bahu cakṣuṣā | na tad dṛṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ dhīraḥ
na tad drṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ dhiraḥ sarvaṃ śraddhātum arhati ||
sarvaṃ sraddhātum arhati ||

vij­ñāna­sārāni subhāṣitāni
jñātaṃ śrutaṃ caiva samādhisāram |
na tasya vijñānaśrutaṃ mahārthaṃ
yaḥ sāhaso bhavati naraḥ pramattaḥ ||

dharme tu ye hy āryanivedite ratās
tadā carante vacasā yadāhuḥ |
te kṣānti­sauratyasamādhigocarāḥ
śrutasya buddher api sāram adhyaguḥ ||

|| śrutavargaḥ || ||
