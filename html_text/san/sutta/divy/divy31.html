31. toyikāmahāvadānam

Vai 301.001 tatra bhagavbānāyuṣmantamāmantrayate—sma āgamaya ānanda yena śrāavastīti |
Vai 301.002 evaṃ bhadantetyāyucmānānando bhagavataḥ pratyaśrauṣīt |

Vai 301.015 sa cārayitumārabdhaḥ |
bhagavatā tathā adhiṣṭhitā yathā sarvasaṃghena pītā |
Vai 301.016 sa ca jīrṇakūpo vāpyāyamānastathaiva peyāpūrṇo ’vasthitaḥ |
Vai 301.017 tato ’sau brāhmaṇo bhūyasyā mātrayā abhiprasanno bhagavataḥ pādābhivandanaṃ kṛtvā purastānniṣaṇṇo dharmaśravaṇāya |

Vai 301.020 atikrānto ’haṃ bhadanta, atikrāntaḥ |
eṣo ’haṃ bhagavantaṃ buddhaṃ śaraṇaṃ gacchāmi dharmaṃ ca bhikṣusaṃghaṃ ca |
Vai 301.021 upāsakaṃ ca māṃ dhāraya adyāgreṇa yāvajjīvaṃ prāṇopetaṃ śaraṇaṃ gatamabhiprasannam |
Vai 301.022 athāsau brāhmaṇo vaṇigiva labdhalābhaḥ śasyasampanna iva kṛṣīvalaḥ śūra iva vijitasaṃgrāmaḥ sarvaroganirmukta ivāturo bhagavato bhāṣitamabhyānandyānumodya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā bhagavato ’ntikāt prakrānto yāvatkṣetraṃ gataḥ |

tairyujyamānaiḥ pūrvavadabhivādyāśca saṃvṛttāḥ |
te ’pi balīvardā yoktrāṇi varatrāṇi ca cchittvā yena bhagavāṃstenopasaṃkrāntāḥ |
Vai 302.018 upasaṃkramya bhagavantaṃ sāmantakena anuparivāryāvasthitāḥ |
Vai 302.019 teṣāṃ bhagavatā tribhiḥ padārthairdharmo deśitaḥ pūrvavadyāvadyathā gaṅgāvatāre haṃsamatsyakūrmāṇām yāvad dṛṣṭasatyāḥ svarbhavanaṃ gatāḥ |
|
Vai 302.021 bhikṣavaḥ saṃśayajātāḥ sarvasaṃśayacchettāraṃ buddhaṃ bhagavantaṃ papracchuh—kiṃ nu taiḥ kārṣakapūrvakairbhikṣubhiḥ karma kṛtam yena kārṣakāḥ saṃvṛttāḥ, bhagavataśca śāsane pravrajya sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkṛtam? tairbalīvardapūrvakairdevaputraiḥ kiṃ karma kṛtam, yana balīvardeṣūpapannāḥ, satyadarśanaṃ ca kṛtamiti? bhagavānāha—ebhireva bhikṣavaḥ karmāṇi kṛtānyupacitāni labdhasambhārāṇi pūrvavadyāvatphalanti khalu dehinām |
|

yadebhirvihārasvāmisantakaṃ śraddhādeyaṃ paribhujya na paṭhitaṃ na svādhyāyitaṃ nāpi manasikāro vihitaḥ, kiṃ tu saṃgaṇikābhirataiḥ kausīdyenābhināmitam, tena karmaṇā pañca janmaśatāni tasya vihārasvāminaḥ kārṣakāḥ saṃvṛttāḥ |
