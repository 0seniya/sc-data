5. stutibrāhmaṇāvadānam

Vai 045.001 atha bhagavān hastināpuramanuprāptaḥ |
Vai 045.002 anyatamo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ dūrādeva dvātriṃśatā mahāpuruṣalakṣaṇaiḥ samalaṃkṛtamaśītyānuvyañjanairvirājitagātraṃ vyāmaprabhālaṃkṛtaṃ sūryasahasrātirekaprabhaṃ jaṅgamamiva parvataṃ samantato bhadrakaṃ dṛṣṭvā ca punarbhagavantamabhigamya gāthābhiḥ stotumārabdhah—Vai 045.004 suvarṇavarṇo nayanābhirāmaḥ prītyākaraḥ sarvaguṇairupetaḥ |
Vai 045.006 devātidevo naradamyasārathis tīrṇo ’si pāraṃ bhavasāgarasya (1)|
iti |
|
Vai 045.009 tato bhagavatā smitamupadarśitam |
dharmatā khalu yasmin samaye buddhā bhagavantaḥ smitaṃ prāviṣkurvanti, pūrvavad yāvad bhagavata ūrṇāyāmantarhitāḥ |
Vai 045.010 athāyuṣmānānandaḥ kṛtakarapuṭo bhagavantaṃ papraccha—Vai 045.012 nānāvidho raṅgasahasracitro vaktrāntarānnṣkramitaḥ kalāpaḥ |
Vai 045.014 avabhāsitā yena diśaḥ samantāddivākareṇodayatā yathaiva (2)|
Vai 045.016 gāthāṃ ca bhāṣate—Vai 045.017 vigatodbhavā dainyamadaprahīṇā buddhā jagatyuttamahetubhūtāḥ |
Vai 045.019 nākāraṇaṃ śaṅkhamṛṇālagauraṃ smitamupadarśayanti jinā jitārayaḥ (3)|
Vai 045.021 tatkālaṃ svayamadhigamya dhīra buddhyā śrotṛrṇāṃ śramaṇa jinendra kāṅkṣitānām |
Vai 045.023 dhīrābhirmunivṛṣa vāgbhiruttamābhirutpannaṃ vyapanaya saṃśayaṃ śubhābhiḥ (4)|
Vai 045.025 nākasmāllavaṇajalādrirājadhairyāḥ saṃbuddhāḥ smitamupadarśayanti nāthāḥ |
Vai 045.027 yasyārthe smitamupadarśayanti dhīrāstaṃ śrotuṃ samabhilaṣanti te janaughāḥ (5)|
iti |
|
Vai 045.029 bhagavānāha—evametadānanda, evametat |
nāhetupratyayamānanda tathāgatā arhantaḥ samyaksambuddhāḥ smitaṃ prāviṣkurvanti |
Vai 045.030 dṛṣṭaste ānanda brāhmaṇo yena tathāgato gāthayā abhiṣṭutah?Vai 46.000 dṛṣṭo bhadanta | Vai 046.001

asau anena kuśalamūlena viṃśatikalpaṃ vinipātaṃ na gamiṣyati |
Vai 046.002 kiṃ tu devāṃśca manuṣyāṃśca gatvā saṃsṛtya paścime nikete paścime samucchraye paścime ātmabhāvapratilambhe stavārho nāma pratyekabuddho bhaviṣyati |
Vai 046.003 bhikṣavaḥ saṃśayajātāḥ sarvasaṃśayacchettāraṃ buddhaṃ bhagavantaṃ pṛcchanti—paśya bhadanta anena brāhmaṇena bhagavānekayā gāthayā stuto bhagavatā ca pratyekāyāṃ bodhau vyākṛta iti |
Vai 046.005 bhagavānāha—na bhikṣava etarhi, yathā atīte ’dhvani anenāhamekayā gāthayā stutaḥ, mayā ca pañcasu grāmavareṣu pratiṣṭhāpitaḥ |
Vai 046.006 tacchṛṇuta, sādhu ca suṣṭhu ca manasi kuruta, bhāṣiṣye |
|
Vai 046.008 bhūtapūrvaṃ bhikṣavo ’tīte ’dhvani vārāṇasyāṃ nagaryāṃ brahmadatto nāma rājā rājyaṃ kārayati ṛddhaṃ ca sphītaṃ ca subhikṣaṃ ca ākīrṇabahujanamanuṣyaṃ ca |
Vai 046.009 sa cātīva kavipriyaḥ |
vārāṇasyāmanyatamo brāhmaṇaḥ kaviḥ |
Vai 046.010 sa brāhmaṇyocyate—brāhmaṇa śītakālo vartate |
gaccha, asya rājñaḥ kaccidanukūlaṃ bhāṣitaṃ kṛtvā kadācit kiṃcit śītatrāṇaṃ saṃpadyata iti |
Vai 046.011 sa saṃprasthitaḥ |
Vai 046.012 yāvadrājā hastiskandhārūḍho nirgacchati |
sa brāhmaṇaḥ saṃlakṣayati—kiṃ tāvadrājānaṃ stunomi āhosvid hastināgamiti |
Vai 046.013 tasyaitadabhavat—ayaṃ hastināgaḥ sarvalokasya priyo manāpaśca |
Vai 046.014 tiṣṭhatu tāvadrājā, hastināgaṃ tāvadabhiṣṭaumīti |
gāthāṃ ca bhāṣate—Vai 046.015 erāvaṇasyākṛtitulyadeho rūpopapanno varalakṣaṇaiśca |
Vai 046.017 lakṣe praśasto ’si mahāgajendra varṇapramāṇena surūparūpa (6)|
iti |
|
Vai 046.019 tato rājā abhiprasanno gāthāṃ bhāṣate—Vai 046.020 yo me gajendro dayito manāpaḥ prītiprado dṛṣṭiharo narāṇām |
Vai 046.022 taṃ bhāṣase varṇapadāni tasya dadāmi te grāmavarāṇi pañca (7)|
iti |
|
Vai 046.024 kiṃ manyadhve bhikṣavo yo ’sau hastināgaḥ, ahameva tena kālena tena samayena |
Vai 046.025 tadāpyahamanenaikayā gāthayā stutaḥ, mayā cāyaṃ pañcagrāmavareṣu pratiṣṭhāpitaḥ |
etarhi anenaikagāthayā stutaḥ, mayāpi cāyaṃ pratyekabodhau vyākṛta iti |
|
Vai 046.027 idamavocadbhagavān |
āttamanasas te bhikṣavo bhagavato bhāṣitamabhyanandan |
|
Vai 046.028 iti śrīdivyāvadāne stutibrāhmaṇāvadānaṃ pañcamam |
|
