24. nāgakumārāvadānam

Vai 213.001 bhikṣuvaḥ saṃśayajātāḥ sarvasaṃśayacchettāraṃ buddhaṃ bhagavantaṃ pṛcchanti—kuto bhadanta tena nāgakumāreṇa tatprathamataḥ śraddhā pratilabdhā? bhagavānāha—bhūtapūrvaṃ bhikṣuvo ’sminneva bhadrakalpe viṃśativarṣasahasrāyuṣi prajāyāṃ kāśyapo nāma śāstā lokam utpannastathāgato ’rhan samyaksambuddho vidyācaraṇasampannaḥ sugato lokavidanuttaraḥ puruṣadamyasārathiḥ śāstā devamanuṣyāṇāṃ buddho bhagavān |
Vai 213.005 sa evaṃ śrāvakāṇāṃ dharmaṃ deśayati—etāni bhikṣavo ’raṇyāni śūnyāgārāṇi parvatakandaragiriguhāpalālapuñjābhyavakāśaśmaśānavanaprasthāni prāntāni śayanāsanāni dhyāyata |
Vai 213.006 bhikṣavo mā pramādata |
Vai 213.007 mā paścādvipratisāriṇo bhūta |
idamasmākamanuśāsanam |
tatra kecidbhikṣavaḥ sumerupariṣaṇḍāyāṃ gatvā dhyāyanti, kecinmandākinyāḥ puṣkariṇyāstīre, kecidanavatapte mahāsarasi, kecit saptasu kāñcanamayeṣu parvateṣu, kecit tāsu tāsu grāmanigamarājarāṣṭradhānīṣu gatvā dhyāyanti |

Vai 213.011 anyatamaśca cirajātako nāgakumāraḥ suparṇinā pakṣirājena sumerupariṣaṇḍāyāmupariṣṭādapahriyate |
Vai 213.012 yāvat tena bhikṣavo dhyānādhyayanayogamanasikārayuktā viharanto dṛṣṭāḥ |
dṛṣṭvā cāsya cittamabhiprasannam |
Vai 213.013 prasādajātaḥ saṃlakṣayati—muktā hyete āryakā evaṃvidhād duḥkhāt |
Vai 213.014 cyutaḥ kālagato vārāṇasyāṃ ṣaṭkarmanirate brāhmaṇakule jātaḥ |
unnīto vardhito mahān saṃvṛttaḥ |
Vai 213.015 so ’pareṇa samayena kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasya śāsane pravrajitaḥ |
tenodyatā ghaṭatā vyāyacchatā sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkṛtam |
Vai 213.016 arhan saṃvṛttaḥ pūrvavadyāvanmānyaśca pūjyaścābhivandyaśca saṃvṛttaḥ |
Vai 213.017 sa saṃlakṣayati—kuto ’haṃ cyutah? tiryakṣu |
kutropapannaH? manuṣyeṣu |
kutra mama mātāpitarau? yāvat paśyati nāgabhavane rudantau tiṣṭhataḥ |
Vai 213.018 sa tatra gataḥ |
gatvā pṛcchitumārabdhah—amba tāta kasyārthe ruditah? tau kathayatah—ārya, sucirajātako ’smākaṃ nāgakumāraḥ suparṇinā pakṣirājenāpahṛtaḥ |
Vai 213.020 sa kathayati—ahamevāsau |
ārya, tādṛśaḥ sa duṣṭānāgo yadvayaṃ sugatigamanamapi na saṃbhāvayāmaḥ, prāgevedṛśānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ lābhī bhaviṣyati |
Vai 213.021 tena tau smāritau |
pādayor nipatya kathayatah—ārya, evaṃvidhāstvayā guṇagaṇā adhigatāḥ |
Vai 213.022 ārya tvaṃ piṇḍakenārthī, vayaṃ puṇyenārthikāḥ |
Vai 213.023 ihaiva tvamāgamya divase divase bhaktakṛtyaṃ kṛtvā gaccha |
sa nāgabhavane divyāṃ sudhāṃ paribhuktvā āgacchati |
Vai 213.024 tasya śrāmaṇerakaḥ sārdhavihārī |
sa bhikṣubhiruktah—śrāmaṇeraka, ayaṃ te upādhyāyaḥ kutra bhuktvā āgacchati? sa kathayati—nāhaṃ jāne |
Vai 213.025 te kathayanti—nāgabhavane divyāṃ sudhāṃ paribhujya pāribhujyāgacchati |
Vai 213.026 tvaṃ kasyārthe na gacchati? sa kathayati—ayaṃ maharddhiko mahānubhāvo yena gacchati |
Vai 213.027 kathamahaṃ gacchāmi? te kathayanti—yadā ayam ṛddhyā gacchati, tadā tvamasya cīvarakarṇikaṃ gṛhāṇa |
Vai 213.028 sa kathayati—mā pateyam |
te kathayanti—bhadramukha, yadi sumeruḥ parvatarājā cīvarakarṇikamavalambate, nāsau patet, prāgeva tvaṃ patiṣyasīti |
Vai 213.030 yo yasmin sthāne ’ntardhāsyati, tena tatra nimittamudgṛhītam |
sa tatpradeśaṃ pūrvameva gatvā avasthitaḥ |
Vai 213.031 sa cāntardhāsyatīti tena cīvarakarṇikaṃ gṛhītam |
tau upari vihāyasā Vai 214.000prakrāntau yāvat tau nāgairdṛṣṭau | Vai 214.001

tayordve te āsanaprajñaptikṛtau |
dvau maṇḍalakau āmārjitau |
sa saṃlakṣayati—kasyārthe ’yamapara āsanaḥ prajñaptah? sa pratinivartya paśyati yāvat śrāmaṇerakam |
Vai 214.003 sa kathayati—bhadramukha, tvamapyāgatah? upādyāya, āgato ’ham |
śobhanam |
nāgāḥ saṃlakṣayanti—ayamāryo maharddhiko mahānubhāvaḥ |
Vai 214.004 śakyate divyāṃ sudhāṃ kārayitum |
ayamanyo na śakyate |
Vai 214.005 taistasya divyā sudhā dattā, tasyāpi prākṛta āhāraḥ |
sa tasya pātragrāhakaḥ |
tena tasya pātraṃ gṛhītam yāvat tatraikā odanamijya(?) vatiṣṭhate |
Vai 214.006 sā tenāsye prakṣiptā yāvaddivyamāsvādanam |
Vai 214.007 sa saṃlakṣayati—īdṛśā api matsariṇo nāgāḥ |
ekadhye niṣaṇṇayorasya divyā sudhā dattā, mamāpi prākṛta āhāraḥ |
Vai 214.008 sa praṇidhānaṃ kartumārabdhah—yanmayā bhagavati kāśyape samyaksambuddhe ’nuttare mahādakṣiṇīye brahmacaryaṃ cīrṇam, anenāhaṃ kuśalamūlenaitaṃ nāgamasmādbhavanāccyāvayitvā atraivopapadyeyamiti |
Vai 214.010 tasya dṛṣṭa eva dharme ubhābhyāṃ pāṇibhyāṃ jalaṃ syanditumārabdham |
Vai 214.011 nāgasyāpi śirortirbādhitumārabdhā |
sa kathayati—ārya, anena śrāmaṇerakenāśobhanacittamutpāditam |
Vai 214.012 pratinivartāpayatu enam |
sa kathayati—bhadramukha, apāyā hyete, nivartaya cittam |
Vai 214.013 sa gāthāṃ bhāṣate—Vai 214.014 pravaṇībhūutamidaṃ cittaṃ na śaknomi nivārayitum |
Vai 214.015 ihasthasyaiva me bhadanta pāṇibhyāṃ syandate jalam (1)|
Vai 214.016 sa taṃ nāgaṃ tasmādbhavanāccyāvayitvā tatraivopapannaḥ |
tatra bhikṣavastena nāgakumāreṇa tatprathamataḥ śraddhā pratilabdhā |
|
Vai 214.018 iti śrīdivyāvadāne nāgakumārāvadānam |
