• Mahākarmavibhaṅga

69

SC 1katame daśānuśaṃsā bhojanapradānasya. ucyate. balavān bhavati. varṇavān bhavati. sukhito bhavati. pratibhānavān bhavati. dīrghāyur bhavati. mahājanābhigamyo bhavati. priyadarśano bhavati. mahābhogaś ca bhavati. svargeṣūpapadyate. kṣipraṃ ca parinirvāti.

SC 2a) yathoktaṃ bhagavatā vaiśālyāṃ siṃhasya rājaputrasya. dadan siṃha priyo bhavati. santo janā bhajanti. kalyāṇaśabdo bhavati. viśāradaḥ parṣadam avagāhati. kālagato deveṣūpapadyate.

SC 3gāthā coktā.

SC 4Mkv 93te tatra nandanti ramanti ca modayanti
samarpitāḥ kāmaguṇeṣu pañcasu
kṛtvā hi vākyam amitasya tāyino
modanti te sugatavarasya śrāvakāḥ.

SC 5etad dānasya phalaṃ bhagavatoktam.

SC 6svargeṣūpapadyate kṣipraṃ ca parinirvāti. āha ca.

SC 7nānārasavyañjanasupraṇītaṃ
kāle śubhaṃ gandharasopapannam
dadāti yaḥ pātram avekṣya dānaṃ
sadāryasaṃghasya guṇān vicintya
āyuś ca varṇaṃ ca yaśo balaṃ ca
saṃprāpya bhogān pratibhāṃ sukhaṃ ca
divīha caiva prathitottamaśrīḥ
sukhāt sukhaṃ yāti vimokṣam ante.

SC 8ime daśa guṇā bhojanapradānasya.
