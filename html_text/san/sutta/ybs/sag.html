Śarīrārthagāthā

Sag 1.1pāpaṃ na kuryān manasā na vācā kāyena vā kiṃcana sarvaloke
riktaḥ kāmaiḥ smṛtimān saṃprajānaṃ duḥkhaṃ na seveta anarthasaṃhitam

Sag 2.1ākhyeyasaṃjñinaḥ satvā ākhyeye ’smin pratiṣṭhitāḥ
ākhyeyam aparijñāya yogam āyānti mṛtyunaḥ

Sag 2.2ākhyeyaṃ tu parijñāya ākhyātāraṃ na manyate
tad vai na vidyate tasya vadeyur yena taṃ pare

Sag 2.3samo viśeṣa uta vāpi hīno yo manyate sa vivadeta tena
vidhātraye ’smin na vikampate yaḥ samo viśiṣṭaś ca na tasya bhavati

Sag 2.4ācchidya tṛṣṇām iha nāmarūpe prahāya mānaṃ ca na saṃgam eti
taṃ śāntadhūmam anighaṃ nirāśaṃ nādrākṣus te devamanuṣyaloke
iha bāhirataś ca

Sag 3.1kāmarāgābhibhūtatvāc cittaṃ me paridahyate
aṃga me gautama brūhi śāntiṃ tvam anukaṃpayā

Sag 3.2viparyāsena saṃjñānāṃ cittaṃ te paridahyate
nimittaṃ varjyatāṃ tasmāc chubhaṃ rāgopasaṃhitaṃ

Sag 3.3aśubhāṃ bhāvaya sadā tvam ekāgraḥ susamāhitaḥ
nirvāpayāśu rāgāgniṃ dahyase mā punaḥ punaḥ

Sag 3.4saṃskārāvaratāṃ paśya duḥkhato ’nātmatas tathā
smṛtiṃ kāyagatāṃ kṛtvā nirvedabahulo bhava

Sag 3.5bhāvyatām animittaṃ ca mānānuśayanāśanaṃ
tato mānābhisamayād duḥkhasyāntaṃ kariṣyasi

kumārikāpraśnagāthā

Sag 4.1kathaṃvihārabahulo bhikṣuḥ pañcaughatīrṇas taratīha ṣaṣṭhaṃ
kathaṃdhyāyī vipulāṃ kāmatṛṣṇāṃ tīrṇo bhavaty apratilabdhayoktraḥ

Sag 4.2praśrabdhakāyaḥ suvimuktacitto hy asaṃskurvan smṛtimān akopyaḥ
ājñāya dharmam avitarkadhyāyī kopaspṛhāstyānadoṣaiḥ viyuktaḥ

Sag 4.3evaṃvihārabahulo bhikṣuḥ pañcaughatīrṇas taratīha ṣaṣṭhaṃ
evaṃdhyāyī vipulāṃ kāmatṛṣṇāṃ tīrṇo bhavaty apratilabdhayoktraḥ

Sag 5.1nityotrasto hy ayaṃ loko nityodvignā iyaṃ prajā
anutpanneṣu duḥkheṣu samutpanneṣu vā punaḥ
yadi kiṃcid anutrastaṃ pṛṣṭa ācakṣva tan mama

Sag 5.2nānyatra jñānatapaso nānyatrendriyanigrahāt
nānyatra sarvasaṃtyāgān mokṣaṃ paśyāmi devate

Sag 5.3cirasya bata paśyāmi brāhmaṇaṃ parinirvṛtaṃ
sarvavairabhayātītaṃ tīrṇaṃ loke viṣaktikām

Sag 6.1kenābhivarṇā janatā praṇītā mārgaś ca nairyāṇikataḥ prayuktaḥ
kutra sthitaḥ kutra ca śikṣamāṇo nāyaṃ martyaḥ paralokād bibheti

Sag 6.2yaḥ śīlavāñ jñānavān bhāvitātmā samāhitaḥ smṛtimān ṛjugataś ca
sarve ’sya śokajvarathāḥ prahīṇāḥ samyaksmṛto yasya cittaṃ vimuktaṃ

Sag 6.3tenābhivarṇā janatā praṇītā mārgaś ca nairyāṇikataḥ prayuktaḥ
atra sthitaḥ atra ca śikṣamāṇo nāyaṃ martyaḥ paralokād bibheti

Sag 7.1kathaṃ yaśasvī bhavati kathaṃ bhavati bhogavān
kathaṃ kīrtim avāpnoti kathaṃ mitrāṇi vindati

Sag 7.2śīlād yaśasvī bhavati dānād bhavati bhogavān
satyena kīrtim āpnoti dadan mitrāṇi vindati

Sag 8.1kutaḥ sarā nivartante kutra vartma na vartate
kutra duḥkhasukhaṃ loke niḥśeṣam uparudhyate

Sag 8.2cakṣuḥ śrotraṃ tathā ghrāṇaṃ jihvā kāyo manas tathā
yatra nāma ca rūpaṃ ca niḥśeṣam uparuhyate

Sag 8.3tataḥ sarā nivartante tatra vartma na vartate
tatra duḥkhasukhaṃ loke niḥśeṣam uparudhyate

Sag 9.1kena svid oghaṃ tarati kenottarati cārṇavaṃ
duḥkhaṃ tyajati kena svit kena svit pariśudhyati

Sag 9.2śraddhayā tarati hy ogham apramādena cārṇavaṃ
vīryeṇa duḥkhaṃ tyajati prajñayā pariśudhyati

Sag 10.1ka etam oghaṃ tarati rātriṃdivam atandritaḥ
anālambe ’pratiṣṭhe ca ko gaṃbhīre na sīdati

Sag 10.2sarvataḥ śīlasaṃpannaḥ prajñāvān susamāhitaḥ
adhyātmacintī smṛtimāṃs taratīmaṃ sudustaraṃ

Sag 10.3viraktaḥ kāmasaṃjñābhyo rūpasaṃyojanātigaḥ
anālambe ’pratiṣṭhe ca sa gaṃbhīre na sīdati

Sag 11.1rāgadveṣau bhagavan kinnidānāv aratiratī romaharṣaḥ kuto ’yam
kutaḥsamutthāś ca mano vitarkāḥ kumārakā dhātrīm ivāśrayante

Sag 11.2snehajā ātmasaṃbhūtā nyagrodhaskandhakā yathā
pṛthagviṣaktāḥ kāmeṣu mālutā vā latā vane

Sag 11.3rāgaś ca dveṣaś ca itonidānāv aratiratī romaharṣaḥ ito ’yaṃ
itaḥsamutthāś ca mano vitarkāḥ kumārakā dhātrīm ivāśrayante

Sag 11.4ye tān prajānanti yatonidānāṃs te tāṃ janā yakṣa vinodayanti
ta arṇavaṃ saṃpratarantīhaugham atīrṇapūrvam apunarbhavāya

Sag 12.1kāryam etad brāhmaṇena prahāṇam akilāsinā
kāmānāṃ viprahāṇārthaṃ na hi kāṃkṣaty asau bhavaṃ

Sag 12.2sa kāryaṃ brāhmaṇasyāsti kṛtārtho brāhmaṇaḥ smṛtaḥ

Sag 12.3yāvan na gādhaṃ labhate na tīram āyūhate sarvagātraiḥ sa tāvat
tīraṃ tu labdhveha saṃtiṣṭhati sthale nāyūhate pāragato nirucyate

Sag 12.4eṣopamā dāmale brāhmaṇasya kṣīṇāsravo yo nipako dhyānalābhī
sarve ’sya śokajvarathāḥ prahīṇāḥ samyaksmṛto yasya cittaṃ vimuktam

geya

Sag 13bhikṣo bhikṣo ogham atārṣīḥ

āma devate

anālambe ’pratiṣṭhe ogham atārṣīḥ

āma devate

yathā kathaṃ tvaṃ bhikṣo anālambe apratiṣṭhe ogham atārṣīḥ

yathā yathāhaṃ devate āyūhāmi tathā tathā saṃsīdāmi yathā yathā saṃsīdāmi tathā tathā saṃtiṣṭhe yathā yathā saṃtiṣṭhe tathā tathā uhye yathā yathāhaṃ devate nāyūhāmi tathā tathā na saṃsīdāmīti vistareṇa śuklapakṣo veditavyaḥ

kumārikāpraśnagāthā

Sag 14.1eko ’raṇye prasṛto dhyāyase tvaṃ vittād vihīna uta vā prārthayānaḥ
grāmasya vā kiṃcanāgo nv akārṣīḥ kasmāj janena na karoṣi sakhyaṃ
sakhyaṃ na saṃvidyate kena cit tava

Sag 14.2arthāprāptyā hṛdayasyeha śāntir jitveha senāṃ priyaśātarūpaṃ
eko dhyāyī sukham asmy anvabhotsaṃ tasmād janena na karomi sakhyaṃ
sakhyaṃ na saṃvidyate kena cid mama

Sag 15anityā bata saṃskārā utpādavyayadharmiṇaḥ
utpadya hi nirudhyante teṣāṃ vyupaśamaḥ sukham

Sag 16apramādo ’mṛtapadaṃ pramādo mṛtyunaḥ padaṃ
apramattā na mriyante pramattās tu sadā mṛtāḥ

Sag 17vitarkapramathitasya dehinas tīvrarāgasya śubhānudarśinaḥ
bhūyas tṛṣṇā vivardhate sā gāḍhīkurute ’sya bandhanaṃ

Sag 18dharmasthaṃ śīlasaṃpannaṃ hrīmantaṃ satyavādinaṃ
ātmanaḥ priyakartāraṃ taṃ janaḥ kurute priyaṃ

Sag 19yat pareṣūpanidhyāyet karma dṛṣṭveha pāpakam
ātmanā tan na kurvīta karmabaddho hi pāpakaḥ

Sag 20subhāṣitaṃ hy uttamam āhur āryāḥ priyaṃ vaden nāpriyaṃ tad dvitīyaṃ
satyaṃ vaden nānṛtaṃ tat tṛtīyaṃ dharmaṃ vaden nādharmaṃ tat caturtham

Sag 21śraddhātha hrīḥ śīlam athāpi dānaṃ dharmā ime satpuruṣapraśastāḥ
etaṃ hi mārgaṃ divigaṃ vadanti etena vai gacchati devalokam

Sag 22śrutvā dharmaṃ vijānāti śrutvā pāpān nivartate
śrutvā anarthaṃ tyajati śrutvā prāpnoti nirvṛtim

Sag 23ākāśasamo na lipyate indrakīlapratimo na kaṃpate
hrada iva samupetakardame saṃsāre ramate na paṇḍitaḥ

Sag 24.1ye rūpeṇa pramiṇvanti māṃ ghoṣeṇānuyānti ca
cchandarāgavaśopetā na mā jānanti te janāḥ

Sag 24.2adhyātmaṃ ca vijānāti bahirdhā ca na paśyati
adhyātmaphaladarśī yaḥ sa vai ghoṣeṇa nīyate

Sag 24.3adhyātmaṃ ca na jānāti bahirdhā ca vipaśyati
bahirdhāphaladarśī yaḥ so ’pi ghoṣeṇa nīyate

Sag 24.4adhyātmaṃ ca na jānāti bahirdhā ca na paśyati
samantāvaraṇo bālaḥ so ’pi ghoṣeṇa nīyate

Sag 24.5adhyātmaṃ ca vijānāti bahirdhā ca vipaśyati
dhīro niḥsaraṇaprajño na sa ghoṣeṇa nīyate

Sag 25ṣaṣṭhe adhipatau rājñi rajyamāne rajasvalaḥ
arajasy arajā bhavati rakto bālo nirucyate

Sag 26nagaram asthiprākāraṃ snāyumāṃsānulepanaṃ
yatra rāgaś ca dveṣaś ca māno mrakṣaś ca gāhate

Sag 27kūrmaḥ svake ’ṅgāni yathā kapāle bhikṣur nidadhyān manaso vitarkāni
aniśrito ’nyān aviheṭhamānaḥ parinirvṛto nāpavadeta kaṃ cit

Sag 28tulyam atulyaṃ ca saṃbhavaṃ bhavasaṃskāram avāsṛjan muniḥ
adhyātmarataḥ samāhita abhinat kośam ivāṇḍasaṃbhavaḥ

Sag 29nāsti kāmasamaḥ paṃko nāsti dveṣasamo grahaḥ
nāsti mohasamaṃ jālaṃ nāsti tṛṣṇāsamā nadī

Sag 30ākāśe vai padaṃ nāsti śramaṇo nāsti bāhyakaḥ
prapañcābhiratā bālā niṣprapañcās tathāgatāḥ

Sag 31sthitiḥ prapaṃcāś ca na santi yasya yaḥ saṃdānaṃ parighaṃ cātivṛttaḥ
taṃ nistṛṣṇaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ na vijānāti sadevako ’pi lokaḥ

Sag 32yasya vitarkā vidhūpitā adhyātmam avikalpitā aśeṣaṃ
saṃgaṃ so ’tītya rūpasaṃjñāṃ caturyogāpagato na jātim eti

Sag 33dadataḥ puṇyaṃ pravardhate vairaṃ saṃyamato na cīyate
kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ kleśānāṃ kṣayatas tu nirvṛtaḥ

Sag 34sarvapāpasyākaraṇaṃ kuśalasyopasaṃpadā
svacittaparyavadamanam etaṃ buddhānuśāsanaṃ

Sag 35durnigrahasya laghuno yatrakāmanipātinaḥ
cittasya dāmanaṃ sādhu cittaṃ dāntaṃ sukhāvaham

Sag 36cittanimittasya kovidaḥ pravivekasya ca vindate rasaṃ
dhyāyī nipakaḥ pratismṛto bhuṃkte prītisukhaṃ nirāmiṣaṃ

Sag 37aśilpajīvī laghur ātmakāmo jitendriyaḥ sarvato vipramuktaḥ
anokasārī hy amamo nirāśaḥ kāmān prahāyaikacaro yas sa bhikṣuḥ

Sag 38dūraṃgamam ekacaram aśarīraṃ guhāśayaṃ
damayati durdamaṃ cittaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ taṃ bravīmy ahaṃ

pārāyaṇeṣv ajitapraśnaḥ

Sag 39.1kenāyaṃ nivṛto lokaḥ kenāyaṃ na prakāśate
kiṃ cābhilepanaṃ brūṣe kiṃ ca tasya mahad bhayam

Sag 39.2avidyānivṛto lokaḥ pramādān na prakāśate
jalpābhilepanaṃ brūmi duḥkhaṃ tasya mahad bhayaṃ

Sag 39.3sravanti sarvataḥ srotāḥ srotasāṃ kiṃ nivāraṇaṃ
srotasāṃ saṃvaraṃ brūhi kena srotaḥ pidhīyate

Sag 39.4yāni srotāṃsi lokasya smṛtiḥ teṣāṃ nivāraṇaṃ
srotasāṃ saṃvaraṃ brūmi prajñayā hi pidhīyate

Sag 39.5prajñāyāś ca smṛteś caiva nāmarūpasya sarvaśaḥ
ācakṣva pṛṣṭa etan me kutraitad uparudhyate

Sag 39.6prajñā caiva smṛtiś caiva nāmarūpaṃ ca sarvaśaḥ
vijñānasya nirodhād dhi atraitad uparudhyate

Sag 39.7kathaṃ smṛtasya carato vijñānam uparudhyate
ācakṣva pṛṣṭa etan me yathātatham asaṃśayaḥ

Sag 39.8adhyātaṃ ca bahirdhā ca vedanāṃ nābhinandataḥ
evaṃ smṛtasya carato vijñānam uparudhyate

Sag 39.9ye ca saṃkhyātadharmāṇo ye ca śaikṣāḥ pṛthagvidhāḥ
teṣāṃ me nipakasyeryāṃ pṛṣṭaḥ prabrūhi mārṣa

Sag 39.10kāmeṣu nābhigṛdhyeta manasānāvilo bhavet
kuśalaḥ sarvadharmeṣu smṛto bhikṣuḥ parivrajet

arthavargīyeṣu kāmān ārabhya gāthā

Sag 40.1kāmān kāmāyamānasya tasya cet tat samṛdhyati
addhā prītamanā bhavati labdhvā martyo yad īpsitaṃ

Sag 40.2tasya cet kāmāyamānasya chandajātasya jaṃtunaḥ
te kāmāḥ parihīyaṃte śalyaviddha iva rūpyate

Sag 40.3yaḥ kāmāṃ parivarjayati sarpasyeva śirāt padaṃ
sa imāṃ viṣaktikāṃ loke smṛtaḥ samativartate

Sag 40.4kṣetravastuhiraṇyaṃ ca gavāśvamaṇikuṇḍalaṃ
striyo dāsān pṛthakkāmān yo naro hy abhigṛdhyati

Sag 40.5abalaṃ vā balīyāṃso mṛdnaṃty enaṃ parisravāḥ
tata enaṃ duḥkham anveti bhinnāṃ nāvam ivodakaṃ

Sag 40.6yasya tv etat samucchinnaṃ tālamastakavad dhataṃ
śokās tasya nivartante udabindur iva puṣkarāt

bhadraikarāgāthā (bhadragarātrīya, bhadrakarātriya)

Sag 41.1atītaṃ nānvāgamayen na pratikāṃkṣed anāgataṃ
pratyutpannāś ca ye dharmās tatra tatra vipaśyakaḥ
asaṃhāryam asaṃkṣobhyaṃ tad vidvān anubṛṃhayet
