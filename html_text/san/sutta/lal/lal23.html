23: saṃstavaparivartastrayoviṃśaḥ

SC 1Vai 259atha khalu śuddhāvāsakāyikā devaputrā bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṃ tathāgataṃ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya divyaiścandanacūrṇavarṣairabhyavakīrya ābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhituṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 2utpanno lokapradyoto lokanāthaḥ prabhaṃkaraḥ |
andhabhūtasya lokasya cakṣurdātā raṇaṃjahaḥ || Verse 23.1

SC 3bhavān vijitasaṃgrāmaḥ puṇyaiḥ pūrṇamanorathaḥ |
saṃpūrṇaḥ śukladharmaiśca jagattvaṃ tarpayiṣyasi || Verse 23.2

SC 4uttīrṇapaṅko hyanighaḥ sthale tiṣṭhati gautamaḥ |
anyāṃ sattvāṃ mahoghena prodgatastārayiṣyasi || Verse 23.3

SC 5udgatastvaṃ mahāprājño lokeṣvapratipudgalaḥ |
lokadharmairaliptastvaṃ jalasthamiva paṅkajaḥ || Verse 23.4

SC 6ciraprasuptamimaṃ lokaṃ tamaskandhāvaguṇṭhitam |
bhavān prajñāpradīpena samarthaḥ pratibodhitum || Verse 23.5

SC 7cirāture jīvaloke kleśavyādhiprapīḍite |
vaidyarāṭ tvaṃ samutpannaḥ sarvavyādhipramocakaḥ || Verse 23.6

SC 8bhaviṣyantyakṣaṇā śūnyā tvayi nāthe samudgate |
manuṣyāścaiva devāśca bhaviṣyanti sukhānvitāḥ || Verse 23.7

SC 9yeṣāṃ tvaddarśanaṃ saumya eṣyase puruṣarṣabha |
na te kalpasahasrāṇi jātu yāsyanti durgatim || Verse 23.8

SC 10paṇḍitāścāpyarogāśca dharmaṃ śroṣyanti ye ’pi te |
gambhīrāścopadhīkṣīṇā bhaviṣyanti viśāradāḥ || Verse 23.9

SC 11mokṣyante ca laghuṃ sarve chittvā vai kleśabandhanam |
yāsyanti nirupādānāḥ phalaprāptivaraṃ śubham || Verse 23.10

SC 12dakṣiṇīyāśca te loke āhutīnāṃ pratigrahāḥ |
na teṣu dakṣiṇā nyūnā sattvānirvāṇahetukī || Verse 23.11

SC 13evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ śuddhāvāsakāyikā devaputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte prāñjalayastasthuḥ, prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 14atha khalu cābhāsvarā devaputrāstathāgataṃ bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṃ divyairnānāprakāraiḥ puṣpadhūpagandhamālyavilepanachatradhvajapatākābhiḥ saṃpūjya tripradakṣiṇīkṛtya cābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ— Vai 260

SC 15gambhīrabuddhe madhurasvarā mune brahmasvarā munivaragīta susvaram |
varāgrabodhi paramārthaprāptā sarvasvare pāragate namaste || Verse 23.12

SC 16trātāsi dīpo ’si parāyaṇo ’si nātho ’si loke kṛpamaitracittaḥ |
vaidyottamastvaṃ khalu śalyahartā cikitsakastvaṃ paramaṃ hitaṃkaraḥ || Verse 23.13

SC 17dīpaṃkarasya sahadarśanaṃ tvayā samudānitaṃ maitrakṛpābhrajālam |
pramuñca nāthā amṛtasya dhārāṃ śamehi tāpaṃ suramānuṣāṇām || Verse 23.14

SC 18tvaṃ padmabhūtaṃ tribhaveṣvaliptaṃ tvaṃ merukalpo vicalo hyakampyaḥ |
tvaṃ vajrakalpo hyacalapratijña tvaṃ candramā sarvaguṇāgradhārī || Verse 23.15

SC 19evaṃ khalu bhikṣava ābhāsvarā devāstathāgatamabhisaṃstutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 20atha khalu subrahmadevaputrapramukhā brahmakāyikā devāstathāgataṃ bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇamanekamaṇiratnakoṭīnayutaśatasahasrapratyuptena ratnajālenābhichādya tripradakṣiṇīkṛtya cābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 21śubhavimalaprajña prabhatejadharā dbātriṃśallakṣaṇavarāgradharā |
smṛtimaṃ matimaṃ guṇajñānadharā akilāntakā śirasi vandami te || Verse 23.16

SC 22amalā vimalā trimalairvimalā trailokyaviśruta trividyagatā |
trividhāvimokṣavaracakṣudadā vandāmi tvāṃ trinayanaṃ vimalam || Verse 23.17

SC 23Vai 261kalikaluṣa uddhṛta sudāntamanā kṛpakaruṇa udgata jagārthakarā |
muni mudita udgata praśāntamanā dvayamativimocaka upekṣaratā || Verse 23.18

SC 24vrata tapasa udgata jagārthakarā | svacarīviśuddhacaripāragatā |
catusatyadarśaka vimokṣaratā mukto vimocayasi cānyajagat || Verse 23.19

SC 25balavīrya āgatu ihā namuci prajñāya vīrya tava maitrya jito |
prāptaṃ ca te padavaraṃ amṛtaṃ vandāma te śaṭhacamūmathanā || Verse 23.20

SC 26evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ subrahmadevaputrapramukhā brahmakāyikā devāstathāgatamābhirgāthābhirabhiṣṭutya ekānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 27atha khalu te śuklapākṣikā māraputrā yena tathāgatastenopasaṃkrāman | upasaṃkramya mahāratnachatravitānaistathāgatamabhicchādya prāñjalayastathāgatamābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 28pratyakṣe ’smi bale tavātivipule mārasya ghorā camū
yatsā māracamū mahāpratibhayā ekakṣaṇe te jitā |
na ca te utthitu naiva kāyu trasito no vā girā vyāhṛtā
tvāṃ vandāmahi sarvalokamahitaṃ sarvārthasiddhaṃ munim || Verse 23.21

SC 29mārā koṭisahasranekanayutā gaṅgāṇubhiḥ saṃmitāḥ
te tubhyaṃ na samartha bodhisuvaṭā saṃcālituṃ kampitum |
yajñā koṭisahasranekanayutā gaṅgā yathā vālikā
yaṣṭā bodhivaṭāsitena bhavatā tenādya vibhrājase || Verse 23.22

SC 30bhāryā ceṣṭatamā sutāśca dayitā dāsyaśca
dāsāstathā udyānā nagarāṇi rāṣṭranigamā rājyāni sāntaḥpurāḥ |
hastā pādaśirottamāṅgamapi vā cakṣūṃṣi jihvā tathā
tyaktā te varabodhicarya caratā tenādya vibhrājase || Verse 23.23

SC 31Vai 262uktaṃ yadvacanaṃ tvayā subahuśo buddho bhaviṣyāmyahaṃ
tāriṣye bahusattvakoṭinayutā duḥkhārṇavenohyatā |
dhyānādhīndriyabuddhibhiḥ kavacitaḥ saddharmanāvā svayaṃ
sā caiṣā pratipūrṇa tubhya praṇidhistāriṣyase prāṇinaḥ || Verse 23.24

SC 32yatpuṇyaṃ ca stavitva vādivṛṣabhaṃ lokasya cakṣurdadaṃ
sarve bhūtva udagrahṛṣṭamanasaḥ prārthema sarvajñatām |
samudānītva varāgrabodhimatulāṃ buddhaiḥ susaṃvarṇitāṃ
evaṃ tadvinihatya mārapariṣāṃ buddhema sarvajñatām || Verse 23.25

SC 33evaṃ khalu bhikṣavo māraputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 34atha khalu paranirmitavaśavartī devaputro ’nekairdevaputraśatasahasraiḥ parivṛtaḥ puraskṛto jāmbūnadasuvarṇavarṇaiḥ padmaistathāgatamabhyavakīrya saṃmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 35apīḍita aluḍita avitathavacanā apagatatamaraja amṛtagatigatā |
arahasi divi bhuvi śriyakriyamatulā atidyutismṛtimati praṇipati śirasā || Verse 23.26

SC 36ratikara raṇajaha rajamalamathanā ramayasi suranara suviśadavacanaiḥ |
vikasita suvipula varatanu kiraṇaiḥ suranarapatiriva jayasi jagadidam || Verse 23.27

SC 37paragaṇipramathana paracarikuśalā priyu bhava naramaru paramati dhunatā |
paracari vibhajasi sunipuṇamatimān pathi iha vicaratu daśabalagamane || Verse 23.28

SC 38tyaji pṛthu bhavagrahi vitathadukha mahā vinayasi suranara yathamati vinaye |
vicarasi catudiśa śaśiriva gagane cakṣu bhava parāyaṇa iha bhuvi tribhave || Verse 23.29

SC 39priyu bhava naramaru na ca khali viṣaye ramayasi śubharati kāmarativirato |Vai 263
dinadarśi pariṣadi na ti samu tribhave nāthu gati parāyaṇu tvamiha hi jagataḥ || Verse 23.30

SC 40evaṃ khalu bhikṣavo vaśavartidevapramukhāḥ parinirmitavaśavartino devaputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 41atha khalu sunirmito devaputro devasaṃghaparivṛtaḥ puraskṛto nānāratnapaṭṭadāmaistathāgatamabhichādya saṃmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 42dharmāloka bhavān samudgata trividhamalanucchido
mohādṛṣṭiavidyaghātako hiriśiribharitaḥ |
mithyāmārgaratāmimāṃ prajāmamṛte thapayito
utpanno iha loki cetiyo divi bhuvi mahitaḥ || Verse 23.31

SC 43tvaṃ vaidyo kuśalacikitsako hyamṛtasukhadado
dṛṣṭikleśamavidyasaṃcayaṃ purimamanuśayam |
sarvavyādhyapanesi dehināṃ purimajinapathe
tasmādvaidyatamo ’si nāyakā vicarasi dharaṇīm || Verse 23.32

SC 44candrāsūryaprabhāśca jyotiṣā maṇi tatha jvalanā
śakrabrahmaprabhā na bhāsate puratu śirighane |
prajñālokakarā prabhaṃkarā prabhasiribharitā
pratyakṣāstava jñāti adbhute praṇipati śirasā || Verse 23.33

SC 45satyāsatyakathī vināyakā sumadhuravacanā
dāntā śāntamanā jitendriya praśamitamanasā |
śāstā śāsaniyāṃ praśāsase naramarupariṣāṃ
vande śākyamuniṃ nararṣabhaṃ suranaramahitam || Verse 23.34

SC 46jñāniṃ jñānakathāgradhārakā jñapayasi tribhave
traividyatrivimokṣadeśakā trimalamalanudā |
bhavyābhavya mune prajānase yathamati vinaye
vande tvāṃ trisahasri adbhutaṃ divi bhuvi mahitam || Verse 23.35

SC 47evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ sunirmito devaputraḥ saparivārastathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṃ namaskurvan ||

SC 48Vai 264atha khalu saṃtuṣito devaputraḥ sārdhaṃ tuṣitakāyikairdevairyena tathāgatastenopasaṃkrāmat | upasaṃkramya mahatā divyavastrajālena bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṃ tathāgatamabhisaṃchādya saṃmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastauṣīt—

SC 49tuṣitālayi yadvasitastvaṃ tatra ti deśitu dharma udāro |
na ca chidyati sā anuśāsti adyapi dharmacarī suraputrā || Verse 23.36

SC 50na ca darśana tṛpti labhāmo dharma śṛṇotu na vindati tṛptim |
guṇasāgara lokapradīpā vandima te śirasā manasā ca || Verse 23.37

SC 51tuṣitālaya yaccalitastvaṃ śoṣita akṣaṇa sarvi tadā te |
yada bodhivaṭe upaviṣṭaḥ sarvajagasya kileśa praśāntāḥ || Verse 23.38

SC 52yasya kṛtena ca bodhi udārā eṣa ti prāpti jinitvana māram |
tvā praṇidhī tapasā paripūrṇā kṣipra pravartaya cakramudāram || Verse 23.39

SC 53bahu dikṣiṣu prāṇisahasrā dharmaratā kṣuṇiyāmatha dharmam |
kṣipra pravartaya cakramudāraṃ mocaya prāṇisahasra bhaveṣu || Verse 23.40

SC 54evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ saṃtuṣito devaputraḥ saparivārastathāgatamabhiṣṭatyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṃ namasyamānaḥ ||

SC 55atha khalu suyāmadevaputrapramukhāḥ suyāmā devā yena tathāgatastenopasaṃkrāmat |
upasaṃkramya nānāpuṣpadhūpagandhamālyavilepanairbodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṃ tathāgataṃ saṃpūjya saṃmukhamābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhistuṣṭavuḥ—

SC 56sadṛśo ’sti na te kutontare śīla samādhi tathaiva prajñayā |
adhimuktivimuktikovidā śirasā vandima te tathāgatam || Verse 23.41

SC 57Vai 265dṛṣṭā sa viyūha śobhanā bodhimaṇḍasmi marubhi yā kṛtā |
na tamarhati anya kaścanā yatha tvaṃ devamanuṣyapūjitaḥ || Verse 23.42

SC 58na mudhāya bhavān samudgato yasya arthe bahu cīrṇa duṣkarā |
vijito hi śaṭhaḥ sasainyakaḥ prāptā bodhi anuttarā tvayā || Verse 23.43

SC 59āloka kṛto daśādiśe prajñādīpena triloka jvālitaḥ |
timiraṃ apanāyayiṣyase dāsyasi cakṣuranuttaraṃ jage || Verse 23.44

SC 60bahukalpa stuvanti bhāṣato romarūpasya na cāntu asti te |
guṇasāgara lokaviśrutā śirasā vandima te tathāgatam || Verse 23.45

SC 61evaṃ khalu te suyāmadevaputrapramukhā devāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 62atha khalu śakro devānāmindraḥ sārdhaṃ trāyatriṃśakāyikairdevairnānāpuṣpadhūpadīpagandhamālyavilepanacūrṇacīvarachatradhvajapatākāvyūhaistathāgataṃ saṃpūjya ābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 63askhalitā anavadyā sadā susthitā merukalpā mune
daśadiśi suvighuṣṭa jñānaprabhā puṇyatejānvitā |
buddhaśatasahasra saṃpūjitā pūrvi tubhyaṃ mune
tasya viśeṣu yena bodhidrume mārasenā jitā || Verse 23.46

SC 64śīlaśrutasamādhiprajñākarā jñānaketudhvajā
jaramaraṇanighāti vaidyottamā lokacakṣurdadā |
trimalakhilaprahīṇa śāntendriyā śāntacittā mune
śaraṇu tavamupema śākyarṣabhā dharmarājā jage || Verse 23.47

SC 65bodhicarī anantatulyā abhūdvīryasthāmodgatā
prajñābala upāya maitrābalaṃ brāhmapuṇyaṃ balam |Vai 266
eti balamanantatulyā bhavaṃ bodhi saṃprasthite
daśabalabaladhārī adyā punarbodhimaṇḍe bhuto || Verse 23.48

SC 66dṛṣṭva camu anantasattve surā bhītatrastābhavan
mā khu śramaṇarāju bādhiṣyate bodhimaṇḍe sthitaḥ |
na ca bhavatu babhūva tebhyo bhayaṃ no ca kāyeñjanā
karahata gurubhāra saṃkampanā mārasenā jitā || Verse 23.49

SC 67yatha ca purimakebhi siṃhāsane prāpta bodhi varā
tatha tvayā anubuddha tulyā samā anyathā tvaṃ na hi |
samamanasa samacitta sarvajñatā sthāma prāptaṃ tvayā
tena bhava svayaṃbhu lokottamo puṇyakṣetraṃ jage || Verse 23.50

SC 68evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ śakro devānāmindraḥ sārdhaṃ devaputraistrāyatriṃśaistathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṃ namaskurvan ||

SC 69atha khalu catvāro mahārājānaḥ sārdhaṃ caturmahārājakāyikairdevaputrairyena tathāgatastenopasaṃkrāmat | upasaṃkramyābhimuktakacampakasumanāvārṣikadhānuskārimālyadāmaparigṛhītā apsaraḥśatasahasraparivṛtā divyasaṃgītisaṃpravāditena tathāgatasya pūjāṃ kṛtvā ābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhistuṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 70sumadhuravacanā manojñaghoṣā śaśi va praśāntikarā prasannacittā |
prahasitavadanā prabhūtajihvā paramasuprītikarā mune namaste || Verse 23.51

SC 71rutaravita ya asti sarvaloke sumadhura premaṇiyā narāmarūṇām |
bhavata svaru pramukta mañjughoṣo abhibhavate ruta sarvi bhāṣamāṇāṃ || Verse 23.52

SC 72rāgu samayi doṣamohakleśā prīti janeti amānuṣāṃ viśuddhām |
akaluṣahṛdayā niśāmya dharmaṃ ārya vimukti labhanti te hi sarve || Verse 23.53

SC 73na ca bhava atimanyase avidvāṃ na ca puna vidvamadena jātu mattaḥ |Vai 267
unnatu na ca naiva conatastvaṃ giririva susthitu sāgarasya madhye || Verse 23.54

SC 74lābha iha sulabdha mānuṣāṇāṃ yatra hi tādṛśu jātu sattva loke |
śrīriva padumo dhanasya dātrī tatha tava dāsyati dharmu sarvaloke || Verse 23.55

SC 75evaṃ khalu caturmahārājapramukhā mahārājakāyikā devā bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṃ tathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 76atha khalvantarikṣā devāstathāgatasyāntikamupasaṃkramyābhisaṃbodheḥ pūjākarmaṇe sarvamantarīkṣaṃ ratnajālena kiṅkiṇījālena ratnachatrai ratnapatākābhī ratnapaṭṭadāmai ratnāvataṃsakairvividhamuktāhārapuṣpadāmārdhakāyikadevatāparigṛhītairardhacandrakaiśca samalaṃkṛtya tathāgatāya niryātayanti sma | niryātya ca saṃmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 77asmāku vāsaṃ gagane dhruvaṃ mune paśyāma sattvā cariyā yathā jage |
bhavataścariṃ prekṣiya śuddhasattva skhalitaṃ na paśyāma tavaikacitte || Verse 23.56

SC 78ye āgatā pūjana bodhisattvā gaganaṃ sphuṭaṃ tairnaranāyakebhiḥ |
hānirvimānāna na cābhavanta tathā hi te vai gaganātmabhāvāḥ || Verse 23.57

SC 79ye antarīkṣātu pravarṣi puṣpāṃ syāccūḍabandhā hi mahāsahasrā |
te tubhya kāye patitā aśeṣā nadyo yathā sāgari saṃpraviṣṭāḥ || Verse 23.58

SC 80paśyāma chatrāṇyavataṃsakā ca mālāguṇāṃ campakapuṣpadāmāṃ |
hārāṃśca candrāṃśca tathārdhacandrāṃ kṣipanti devā na ca saṃskaroti || Verse 23.59

SC 81vālasya nābhūdavakāśamasmin devaiḥ sphuṭaṃ sarvata antarīkṣam |
kurvanti pūjāṃ dvipadottamasya na ca te mado jāyati vismayo vā || Verse 23.60

SC 82Vai 268evaṃ khalvantarīkṣadevā bodhimaṇḍe niṣaṇṇaṃ tathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’vatasthivantaḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṃ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 83atha khalu bhaumā devāstathāgatasya pūjākarmaṇe sarvāvantaṃ dharaṇītalaṃ suśodhitopaliptaṃ gandhodakapariṣiktaṃ puṣpāvakīrṇaṃ ca kṛtvā nānādūṣyavitānavitataṃ ca tathāgatāya niryātayanti sma | ābhirgāthābhirabhituṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 84vajramiva abhedyā saṃsthitā triḥsahasrā vajramayapadenāyaṃ sthito bodhimaṇḍe |
iha mama tvacamāṃsaṃ śuṣyatāmasthimajjā na ca ahu aspṛśitvā bodhi uttheṣya asmāt || Verse 23.61

SC 85savibhava narasiṃhā sarviyaṃ triḥsahasrā na kariṣu adhisthānaṃ syādvidīrṇaśeṣā |
tādṛśa mahavegā āgatā bodhisattvā yeṣa kramatalebhiḥ kampitā kṣetrakoṭyaḥ || Verse 23.62

SC 86lābha iha sulabdhā bhūmidevairudārā yatra paramasattvaścaṃkramī medinīye |
yatra ku raju loke sarva obhāsitāste cetibhu trisahasraḥ kiṃ punastubhya kāyaḥ || Verse 23.63

SC 87hesti śatasahasraṃ yāvataścāpaskandho dharaṇitalu jagasyā yāvataścopajīvyaḥ |
sarva vayu dharemo medinī triḥsahasrāṃ sarva tava dadāmo bhuṅkṣvimāṃ tvaṃ yatheṣṭam || Verse 23.64

SC 88yatra bhava sthihedvā caṃkramedvā śayedvā ye ’pi sugataputrāḥ śrāvakā gautamasya |
dharmakatha kathentī ye ’pi vā tāṃ śṛṇonti sarvakuśalamūlaṃ bodhiye nāmayāmaḥ || Verse 23.65
