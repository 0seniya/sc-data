Candrasūtra

Wal 70a evaṃ mayā śrutam | ekasamayaṃ bhagavāṃ caṃpāyāṃ viharati gargarapuṣkariṇyās tīre |

Wal 70a tena khalu samayena rāhuṇā asurendreṇa sarvaṃ candramaṇḍalam āvṛtaṃ |

Wal 70a atha yā devatā tasmiṃ candramaṇḍale sthitā bhītā trastā saṃvignā hṛṣṭaromakūpā yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagāma | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt | ekāntasthitā bhagavaṃtaṃ gāthayā babhāṣe |

Wal 70a namas te buddha vīrāstu vipramuktāya savataḥ |
saṃbādhapratipanno ’smi tasya me śaraṇaṃ bhava ||

Wal 70a loke sugatam arhantaṃ candramāḥ śaraṇaṃ gataḥ |
rāhoś candramasaṃ muñca buddhā lokānukaṃpakāḥ ||

Wal 70a bhagavāṃ gāthayā pratyābhāṣīt |

yo hy andhakāre nabhasi
prabhākaro vairocano … |

rāho śaśāṅkaṃ grasa māntarīkṣe
prajāpradīpaṃ tvaritaṃ vimuñca ||

Wal 70a … | saṃsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṃbādhito yathā naraḥ | saṃtvaramāṇo ’sau rāhuś candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭaḥ ||

Wal 70a tena samayena baḍir virocanapautra āsīd asuraḥ | adrākṣīd baḍir virocanapautro rāhuṇā asurendreṇa tvaritatvaritaṃ candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭam dṛṣṭvā ca tasyāṃ velāyāṃ gāthāṃ babhāṣe ||

Wal 70a kiṃ nu saṃtvaramāṇas tvaṃ rāhuś candraṃ vimuñcasi | saṃsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṃbādhito yathā naraḥ ||

Wal 70a rāhur avocat || saptadhā me sphalen mūrdhā jīvan na sukham āpnuyāṃ tatra buddhābhigītena muñceyaṃ śaśinaṃ na cet ||

Wal 70a baḍir virocanapautra gāthayā babhāṣe ||

… madarśinaḥ saṃbuddhāḥ … |
teṣāṃ gāthābhigītena rāhuś candraṃ vimuñcati ||

Wal 70a candrasūtraṃ samāptam ||

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1970a. Buddha frees the disc of the moon (Candrasūtra). Bulletin of the School of Oriental and African Studies 33: 179–183. Reprinted in AKS, 296–301.
