Śūnyatā

Tri 15.1 kuruṣu nidānam |

Tri 15.2 tatra bhagavān bhikṣūn āmantrayati |

Tri 15.3 dharmaṃ vo deśayiṣye ādau kalyāṇaṃ madhye kalyāṇaṃ paryavasāne kalyāṇaṃ yāvat prakāśayiṣye | yad uta mahāśūnyatānāma dharmaparyāyas tac chṛṇuta sādhu ca suṣṭhu ca manasi kuruta bhāṣiṣye |

Tri 15.4 mahāśūnyatādharmaparyāyāḥ katamāḥ | yad utāsmin satīdaṃ bhavaty asyotpādād idam utpadyate | yad utāvidyāpratyayāḥ saṃskārā yāvat samudayo bhavati |

Tri 15.5a jātipratyayaṃ jarāmaraṇam iti | tatra ko jarāmaraṇaṃ kasya vā jarāmaraṇam iti hi syuḥ praṣṭāra iti ya evaṃ vaded ayaṃ jarāmaraṇam asya vā jarāmaraṇam | yaś caivaṃ vadet taj jīvaṃ tac charīram anyaj jīvam anyac charīram | ubhayam etad ekam | vyañjanam atra nānā |

Tri 15.5b taj jīvaṃ tac charīram iti dṛṣṭau satyāṃ brahmacaryavāso na bhavati | anyaj jīvam anyac charīram iti bhikṣavo dṛṣṭau satyāṃ brahmacaryavāso na bhavati | ity etāv ubhāv antāv anupagamyāsti madhyamā pratipad āryā lokottarā yathābhūtā aviparītā samyagdṛṣṭiḥ | yad uta jātipratyayaṃ jarāmaraṇam |

Tri 15.6 evaṃ jātir bhava upādānaṃ tṛṣṇā vedanā sparśaḥ ṣaḍāyatanaṃ nāmarūpaṃ vijñānam |

Tri 15.7a avidyāpratyayāḥ saṃskārā iti | tatra kaḥ saṃskārāḥ kasya vā saṃskārā iti hi syuḥ praṣṭāra iti ya evaṃ vaded ayaṃ saṃskārā asya vā saṃskārāḥ | yaś caivaṃ vadet taj jīvaṃ tac charīram anyaj jīvam anyac charīram | ubhayam etad ekam | vyañjanam atra nānā |

Tri 15.7b taj jīvaṃ tac charīram iti bhikṣavo dṛṣṭau satyāṃ brahmacaryavāso na bhavati | anyaj jīvam anyac charīram iti dṛṣṭau satyāṃ brahmacaryavāso na bhavati | ity etāv ubhāv antāv anupagamyāsti madhyamā pratipad āryā lokottarā yathābhūtā aviparītā samyagdṛṣṭiḥ | yad utāvidyāpratyayāḥ saṃskārāḥ |

Tri 15.8a yataś ca bhikṣor avidyā viraktā bhavati vidyotpannā

Tri 15.8b tasya ko jarāmaraṇaṃ kasya vā jarāmaraṇam |

Tri 15.8c avidyāvirāgād vidyotpādād evam asya taj jarāmaraṇaṃ prahīṇaṃ bhavati parijñātam ucchinnamūlaṃ tālamastakavad anābhavagatikam āyatyām anutpādadharma |

Tri 15.9a yataś ca bhikṣor avidyā viraktā bhavati vidyotpannā

Tri 15.9b tasya ko jātir bhava upādānaṃ tṛṣṇā vedanā sparśaḥ ṣaḍāyatanaṃ nāmarūpaṃ vijñānam |

Tri 15.10b tasya kaḥ saṃskārāḥ kasya vā saṃskārāḥ |

Tri 15.10c avidyāvirāgād vidyotpādād evam asya te saṃskārāḥ prahiṇā bhavanti parijñātā ucchinnamūlās tālamastakavad anābhavagatikam āyatyām anutpādadharmāṇaḥ |

Tri 15.11a yataś ca bhikṣor avidyā viraktā bhavati vidyotpannā

Tri 15.11b tasyāvidyānirodhāt saṃskāranirodho yāvan mahato duḥkhaskandhasya nirodho bhavati |

Tri 15.12 ayam ucyate mahāśūnyatānāma dharmaparyāyaḥ |

Tri 15.13 dharmaṃ vo deśayiṣye ādau kalyāṇaṃ madhye pūrvavad iti me yad uktam idaṃ tat pratyuktam ||
