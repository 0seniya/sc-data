Svāgatasūtra

Wal 58 śrāvastyāṃ |

Wal 58 svāgataṃ vo bhikṣavaḥ | supravrajyā vo bhiṣavaḥ | lābhā sulabdhā | kadācit karhicid āryāyatane pratyājāti indriyair avikalatā ajaḍatā aneḍamūkatā ahastasaṃvācakatā pratibalatā subhāṣitadurbhāṣitānāṃ dharmāṇām artham ājñātum |

Wal 58 ahaṃ caitarhi śāstā loke utpannas tathāgato ’rhāṃ samyaksaṃbuddho vidyācaraṇasaṃpannaḥ sugato lokavid anuttara puruṣadamyasārathiḥ śāstā devamanuṣyāṇāṃ buddho bhagavāṃ |

Wal 58 dharmaś ca deśyate aupaśamikaḥ pārinirvāṇikaḥ saṃbodhagāmī sugatapraveditaḥ | yad utāsmiṃ satīdaṃ bhavaty asyotpādād idam utpadyate |

yad utāvidyāpratyayāḥ saṃskārā yāvat samudayo nirodhaś ca bhavati |

Wal 58 tad idaṃ bhikṣavaḥ sudurlabhadurlabhaṃ yat kadācit karhicid āryāyatane pratyājāti indriyair avikalatā yāvat samudayo nirodhaś ca bhavati |

Wal 58 tasmāt tarhi bhikṣava ātmārthaṃ samanupaśyadbhiḥ parāthañ cobhayārthaṃ ca yāvad evaṃ vo bhikṣavaḥ śikṣitavyam ||

Wal 58 svāgatasūtraṃ | samāptaḥ || samā ||
