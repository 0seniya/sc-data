13. The Sixth Bhūmi

SC 1Sen 1.121 tataḥ sthaviraḥ kāśyapaḥ kātyāyanam athābravīt* |
kiṃ cittaṃ bodhisatvānāṃ paṃcamāyāṃ vipaścitāṃ ||

SC 2tataḥ kātyāyanasthaviraḥ munikāśyapaṃ dhutadharmadharaṃ gāthayādhyabhāṣi ||

SC 3alpāsvādanibaddho ’yaṃ lokāvarto ’tidāruṇaḥ |
+ + + + + + + + + + + + + + + + + + jāyate ||

SC 4evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapaḥ āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || kṣetram iti ||

SC 5tataḥ sthaviraḥ kātyāyanaḥ mahākāśyapam athābravīt* |
śrūyatāṃ lokanāthānāṃ kṣetraṃ tatvārthaniśritaṃ ||

SC 6upakṣetraṃ ca vakṣyāmi teṣāṃ paramavādināṃ |
tāni niśamya vākyāni śāsanaṃ ca naravara ||

SC 7ekaṣaṣṭiṃ trisahasrāṇi buddhakṣetraṃ parīkṣitaṃ |
ato caturguṇaṃ jñeyam upakṣetraṃ tathāvidhaṃ ||

SC 8evam ukte āyuṣmāṃ mahākāśyapaḥ āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || kiṃ punar bho jinaputra sarveṣu buddhakṣetreṣu utpadyanti samyaksaṃbuddhā utāho keṣucid eva utpadyanti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyana āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapaṃ gāthābhiḥ adhyabhāṣe ||

SC 9kiṃcit eva bhavati apariśūnyaṃ
Sen 1.122 kṣetram apratimarūpadharehi |
kṣetrakoṭinayutāni bahūni
śūnyakāni puruṣapravarehi ||

SC 10durlabho hi varalakṣaṇadhārī
dīrghakālasamudāgatabuddhī |
sarvadharmakuśalo atitejaḥ
sarvasatvasukhatādharasatvo iti ||

SC 11evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapa āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || khalu bho jinaputra ko hetuḥ kaḥ pratyayaḥ yaṃ ekasmiṃ kṣetre dvau samyaksaṃbuddhau nopapadyanti iti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyana āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapaṃ gāthābhiḥ adhyabhāṣate ||

SC 12yat kāryaṃ naranāgena buddhakarma suduḥkaraṃ |
tat sarvaṃ paripūreti eṣā buddhāna dharmatā ||

SC 13asamartho yadi siyād buddhadharmeṣu cakṣumāṃ |
tato duve mahātmānau utpadyete tathāgatau ||

SC 14taṃ cāsamarthasadbhāvaṃ varjayanti maharṣiṇāṃ |
tasmāt duve na jāyante ekakṣetre nararṣabhau ||

SC 15na jātu sāvaśeṣeṣu buddhadharmeṣu śruyyate |
nirvṛtāḥ puruṣaśreṣṭhā atītādhve jinātmajā ||

SC 16Sen 1.123 anāgatā atikrāntā saṃbuddhā ye ca sāṃprataṃ |
kṛtena buddhadharmeṇa nirvāyanti narottamā iti ||

SC 17evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapaḥ āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || katamāni bho jinaputra samprati anyāni buddhakṣetrāṇi yatraitarhi samyaksaṃbuddhā dharmaṃ deśayantīti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyana āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapaṃ gāthābhir adhyabhāṣe ||

SC 18purastime diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ sunirmitaṃ |
tatra mṛgapatiskandho nāmena jinapuṅgavaḥ ||

SC 19purastime diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ kṛtāgadaṃ |
tatra siṃhahanur nāma jino dvātriṃśalakṣaṇaḥ ||

SC 20purastime diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ vibhūṣitaṃ |
tatra lokagurur nāma sarvadarśī mahāmuniḥ ||

SC 21purastime diśo bhāge buddhakṣetram akaṇṭhakaṃ |
tatra jñānadhvajo nāma śāstā śāsati prāṇināṃ ||

SC 22purastime diśo bhāge buddhakṣetram avekṣitaṃ |
tatra kanakabimbābho jino nāmena sundaraḥ ||

SC 23dakṣiṇasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ drumadhvajaṃ |
tatra anihato nāma saṃbuddho devananditaḥ ||

SC 24dakṣiṇasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ manoramaṃ |
tatra nāmena saṃbuddho cārunetro mahāmuniḥ ||

SC 25Sen 1.124 dakṣiṇasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetram akardamaṃ |
tatra nāmena saṃbuddho mālādhārī vināyakaḥ ||

SC 26paścimasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetram avigrahaṃ |
tatra nāmena saṃbuddho ambaro bhavasūdanaḥ ||

SC 27uttarasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ manoramaṃ |
tatra nāmena saṃbuddho pūrṇacandro vidhiśrutaḥ ||

SC 28heṣṭimasmiṃ diśo bhāge buddhakṣetraṃ suniṣṭhitaṃ |
tatra nāmena saṃbuddho dṛḍhabāhus tathāgataḥ ||

SC 29upariṣṭā diśo bhāge buddhakṣetram anuddhṛtaṃ |
tatra buddho mahābhāgo nāmena arisūdanaḥ ||

SC 30buddhakṣetrasahasrāṇi anekāni ataḥ paraṃ |
buddhakṣetrasahasrāṇāṃ koṭī na prajñāyate ’parā ||

SC 31buddhakṣetrāṇāṃ śūnyānāṃ koṭī na prajñāyate ’ntarā |
lokadhātūsahasrāṇāṃ koṭī na prajñāyate ’ntarā ||

SC 32yathā saṃsāracakrasya pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
tathāiva lokadhātūnāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 33atikrāntānāṃ buddhānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
praṇidhentāna bodhāya pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 34avaivartikadharmāṇāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
abhiṣekabhūmiprāptānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 35Sen 1.125 tuṣiteṣu vasantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
tuṣitebhyaś cyavantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 36mātu kukṣau śayantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
sthitānāṃ mātuḥ kukṣau tu pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 37jāyamānānāṃ vīrāṇāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
jātānāṃ lokanāthānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 38aṅkeṣu gṛhyamāṇānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
pādāni vikramantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 39mahāhāsaṃ hasantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
diśāṃ vilokayantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 40aṅkena dhāriyantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
upanīyamānānāṃ gandharvaiḥ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 41pūrebhyo niṣkramantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
bodhimūlam upentānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 42prāpnuvantānāṃ tathāgatajñānaṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
dharmacakraṃ pravartentānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 43satvakoṭī vinentānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
siṃhanādaṃ nadantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 44āyuḥsaṃskāraṃ utsṛjantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
nirvāyantānāṃ vīrāṇāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 45Sen 1.126 nirvṛtānāṃ śayantānāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate |
dhyāpiyantānāṃ vīrāṇāṃ pūrvā koṭī na prajñāyate ||

SC 46evam etaṃ yathābhūtaṃ śāstupūgaṃ vijānatha |
kvacit kvacit ca saṃbuddho buddhakṣetreṣu dṛśyate ||

SC 47evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapo āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || yadi bho jinaputrā ettakā samyaksaṃbuddhā eko ca samyaksaṃbuddho aparimitān satvā parinirvāpayati nanu acireṇa kālena sarvasatvān parinirvāpayiṣyanti | evam ayaṃ lokaḥ sarveṇa sarvaśūnyaṃ bhaviṣyati sarvasatvavirahita iti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyana āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapa gāthābhiḥ adhyabhāṣe ||

SC 48samanantarasaṃpūraṃ śūnyaṃ bhavatu sarvadā |
apratiṣṭham anālambanaṃ niviṣṭaṃ bhavatu sarvadā ||

SC 49yattikā pṛthivīdhātu satvā bahutarakā ato |
pṛthagjanā khu nirdiṣṭā tena paramadarśinā ||

SC 50śṛṇvatāṃ puruṣavarasya śāsanaṃ bahunāṃ kutaḥ |
paryanto bheṣyati satvānām iti uktaṃ maharṣiṇeti ||

SC 51evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapo āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || ye ime bho Sen 1.127 jinaputra satvā samyaksaṃbodhāye praṇidhenti te te katibhir ākāraiḥ ṣaṣṭhyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānāḥ saptamāyāṃ bhūmau vivartanta iti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyana āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapam uvāca || dubhi khalu bho dhutadharmadhara ākārair bodhisatvā bodhāya praṇidhento ṣaṣṭhyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānāḥ saptamāyāṃ bhūmau vivartanti || katamehi dubhi || saṃjñāvedayitanirodhasamāpattiyo ca spṛhayanti yasmiṃś ca kāle samyaksaṃbuddhā satvaparijñayā ahaṃ mahātmā śamīkaro tti devatāṃ satkṛtya avahitaśrotā śṛṇvanti || ye hi kecid bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ ṣaṣṭhyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānāḥ saptamāyāṃ bhūmau vivartensu vivartanti vivartiṣyanti vā sarve imehi dubhi ākārehi vivartanti vivartensu vivartiṣyanti iti ||

SC 52evaṃ eṣā ṣaṣṭhī bhūmir bhavati teṣāṃ guṇavarāṇāṃ |
hariṇapatīnāṃ hitāna maharṣiṇāṃ bodhisatvānām iti ||

iti śrīmahāvastu-avadāne ṣaṣṭhī bhūmiḥ samāptā ||
