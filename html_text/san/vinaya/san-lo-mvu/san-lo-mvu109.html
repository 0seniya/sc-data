109. The Chapter of the Thousand

SC 1bhagavāṃ uruvilvākāśyapasya āśramāto mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārdhaṃ ardhatrayodaśabhir bhikṣuśatair maharṣiṇām āśramapadaṃ dharmāraṇyaṃ gato || tahiṃ dāni sapta jaṭilaśatāni prativasanti sarvāṇi caturdhyānalābhīni paṃcābhijñāni saviṃśaśatavarṣikāni jātīye sarvāṇi ca paripakvakuśalamūlāni caramabhavikāni | bhagavān teṣāṃ anugrahārthaṃ tahiṃ āśramapadaṃ gato | teṣāṃ bhagavāṃ jaṭilānāṃ dharmapadeṣu sahasravargaṃ bhāṣati ||

SC 2sahasram api vācānāṃ anarthapadasaṃhitā |
ekā arthavatī śreyā yāṃ śrutvā upaśāmyati ||

SC 3sahasram api gāthānām anarthapadasaṃhitā |
ekā arthavatī śreyā yāṃ śrutvā upaśāmyati ||

SC 4yo śatāni sahasrāṇāṃ saṃgrāme manujā jaye |
yo caikaṃ jaye ātmānaṃ sa vai saṃgrāmajit* varaḥ ||

SC 5yo jayeta sahasrāṇāṃ māse māse śataṃ śataṃ |
na so buddhe prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 6SC 6Sen 3.435 yo jayeta sahasrāṇāṃ māse māse śataṃ śataṃ |
na so dharme prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 7yo jayeta sahasrāṇāṃ māse māse śataṃ śataṃ |
na so saṃghe prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 8yo jayeta sahasrāṇāṃ māse māse śataṃ śataṃ |
so va saṃpannaśīlānāṃ kalāṃ nārghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 9yo jayeta sahasrāṇāṃ māse māse śataṃ śataṃ |
na so svākhyātadharmāṇāṃ kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 10māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhuṃjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so buddhe prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 11māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhuṃjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so dharme prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 12māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhuṃjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so saṃghe prasādasya kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 13māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhūmjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so dhyānaprasādānāṃ kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 14māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhuṃjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so sampannaśīlānāṃ kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 15māse māse kuśāgreṇa bālo bhuṃjeya bhojanaṃ |
na so svākhyātadharmāṇāṃ kalām arghati ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

SC 16yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve agniparicaraṃ caret* |
patrāhāro chavāvāsī karonto vividhaṃ tapaṃ ||

SC 17yo caikaṃ bhāvitātmānaṃ muhūrtam api pūjayet |
sā ekapūjanā śreyo na ca varṣaśataṃ hutaṃ ||

SC 18yat kiṃcid iṣṭaṃ ca hutaṃ ca loke
Sen 3.436 saṃvatsaraṃ yajati puṇyaprekṣī |
sarvaṃ pi taṃ na caturbhāgam eti
abhivādanaṃ ujjugateṣu śreyaṃ ||

SC 19yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve duḥśīlo asamāhitaḥ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyaṃ śīlavantasya dhyāyato ||

SC 20yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve kuśido hīnavīryavān* |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyo vīryam āraṃbhato dṛḍhaṃ ||

SC 21yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve apaśyaṃ buddhaśāsanaṃ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyo paśyato buddhaśāsanaṃ ||

SC 22yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve apaśyaṃ dharmam uttamaṃ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyo paśyato dharmam uttamaṃ ||

SC 23yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve apaśyaṃ udayavyayaṃ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyo paśyato udayavyayaṃ ||

SC 24yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve apaśyaṃ acyutaṃ padaṃ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyo paśyato acyutaṃ padaṃ ||

SC 25yo ca varṣaśataṃ jīve apaśyaṃ amṛtaṃ padaṃ |
ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ śreyaṃ paśyato amṛtaṃ padaṃ ||

SC 26te ca dāni bhagavatā balavaśībhāve vinītā sarve ca te parinirvṛtā | teṣāṃ bhagavāṃ śarīrapūjāṃ ca kṛtvā stūpāṃ ca dharmāraṇyāto ajapālasya nyagrodhamāgato ||
