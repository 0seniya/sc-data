SC 132svakaṃ ca darśanaṃ rājā samākhyāti maharṣiṇaḥ |
tac chrutvā naraśārdūlo rājānam idam abravīt* ||

SC 133na te lābhā bhūmipate yas tvaṃ durgatigāminaṃ |
Sen 1.192 pāpaṃ śraddadhase mārgantāṃ dṛṣṭiṃ pratiniḥsṛja ||

SC 134tāṃ dṛṣṭiṃ pratiniḥsṛjya rājā vacanam abravīt* |
dharmam ākhyāhi māṃ dhīra yatra duḥkhaṃ nirudhyate ||

SC 135tasya ca dharmasaṃyuktaṃ bhāvaye puruṣottamaḥ |
kuśalaṃ sarvasatvānāṃ buddhadharmaviśāradaḥ ||

SC 136so taṃ dharmaṃ vijānitvā rājā parijanaiḥ saha |
trīṇi saṃyojanāṃ tyaktvā prāptavāṃ prathamaṃ phalaṃ ||

SC 137asaṃkhyeyā ca janatā prāptavān prathamaṃ phalaṃ |
paśya satpuruṣā rājaṃ maitriyā balam uttamaṃ ||

SC 138ye tatra nirmitā bhikṣūḥ na caite bhikṣuṇo matā |
upahāraṃ vadanty etaṃ jinā śāstraviśāradāḥ ||

SC 139asthānam eva jinaputra yadā sthūlāhi bhūmihi |
tat pure adhigaccheyuḥ sarvajñatvaṃ tathāgatāḥ ||

SC 140kālaṃ va nātināmenti paripūrṇāhi bhūmihi |
darśensu vādiśārdūlā ity evaṃ puruṣottamā ||

SC 141vārāṇasīṃ vanaṃ gatvā buddhadharmapuraskṛto |
vistareṇa prakāśayati nāyako bhūmayo daśa ||

SC 142nayānayajñāḥ saṃbuddhāḥ sarvaparamataṃ viduḥ |
adhyāśayaṃ parīkṣanti jātakā sarvaprāṇināṃ ||

SC 143madhureṇa sugītena nayena guṇadarśinaḥ |
Sen 1.193 suvinītā bahu janatā saṃbuddhena prajānatā ||

SC 144na jāyanti na jīryanti na mṛyanti kathaṃcana |
paramaṃ mitram āsādya vinītā varabuddhinā ||

SC 145gambhīracaritaṃ dhīrā prajānanti parasparaṃ |
anantapratibhānaṃ ca sarve sarvāṅgaśobhanā iti ||

iti śrīmahāvastu-avadāne abhiṣekavatī nāma daśamā bhūmiḥ samāptā ||

SC 146śikhare gṛddhakūṭasmiṃ pañcānāṃ vaśībhūtaśatānāṃ samavāye daśabhūmikaṃ nāma upadeśamukhaṃ bhāṣitaṃ ||

samāptaṃ daśabhūmikaṃ ||

SC 147ye satvā buddhatvāya praṇidhenti tehi uddeśitavyaṃ daśabhūmikaṃ bodhisatvānāṃ ca dṛṣṭasatyānāṃ śraddadhānānāṃ dātavyaṃ nānyeṣāṃ ete hy atra śraddadhānā anye vicikitseyuḥ ||

samāptā ādibhūmi yāvad daśamā daśabhūmayaḥ mahāvastuparisaraṃ ||
