3. Talaghātakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_2587Msdiv 802Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena dve bhikkhuniyo anabhiratiyā pīḷitā ovarakaṃ pavisitvā talaghātakaṃ karonti. Bhikkhuniyo tena saddena upadhāvitvā tā bhikkhuniyo etadavocuṃ—“kissa tumhe, ayye, purisena saddhiṃ sampadussathā”ti? “Na mayaṃ, ayye, purisena saddhiṃ sampadussāmā”ti bhikkhunīnaṃ etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṃ karissantī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṃ karontī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṃ karissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 2Ms 2V_2588Msdiv 803“Talaghātake pācittiyan”ti. (3:58)

SC 3PTS vp Pali 4.261 Ms 2V_2589Msdiv 804Talaghātakaṃ nāma samphassaṃ sādiyantī antamaso uppalapattenapi muttakaraṇe pahāraṃ deti, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_2590Msdiv 805Anāpatti—ābādhapaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_2591Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
