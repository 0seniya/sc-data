37. Antoraṭṭhasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_2952SC 1Ms 2V_2952Msdiv 961Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ caranti. Dhuttā dūsenti.

SC 2SC 2Ms 2V_2953Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissantī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_2954Msdiv 962SC 3Ms 2V_2954Msdiv 962“Yā pana bhikkhunī antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ careyya, pācittiyan”ti. (37:92)

SC 4Ms 2V_2955SC 4Ms 2V_2955Msdiv 963Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_2956Antoraṭṭheti yassa vijite viharati, tassa raṭṭhe.

SC 6SC 6Ms 2V_2957Sāsaṅkaṃ nāma tasmiṃ magge corānaṃ niviṭṭhokāso dissati, bhuttokāso dissati, ṭhitokāso dissati, nisinnokāso dissati, nipannokāso dissati.

SC 7SC 7Ms 2V_2958Sappaṭibhayaṃ nāma tasmiṃ magge corehi manussā hatā dissanti, viluttā dissanti, ākoṭitā dissanti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 2V_2959Asatthikā nāma vinā satthena.

SC 9SC 9Ms 2V_2960Cārikaṃ careyyāti kukkuṭasampāte gāme gāmantare gāmantare āpatti pācittiyassa. Agāmake araññe addhayojane addhayojane āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 10Ms 2V_2961SC 10Ms 2V_2961Msdiv 964Anāpatti—satthena saha gacchati, kheme appaṭibhaye gacchati, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 11SC 11Ms 2V_2962Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
