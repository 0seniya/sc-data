77. Sikkhamāna-navuṭṭhāpanasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.332 Ms 2V_3361Msdiv 1150Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarā sikkhamānā thullanandaṃ bhikkhuniṃ upasaṅkamitvā upasampadaṃ yāci. Thullanandā bhikkhunī taṃ sikkhamānaṃ—“sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī”ti vatvā, neva vuṭṭhāpeti na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karoti.

SC 2Ms 2V_3362Atha kho sā sikkhamānā bhikkhunīnaṃ etamatthaṃ ārocesi. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā sikkhamānaṃ—‘sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī’ti vatvā, neva vuṭṭhāpessati na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissatī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī sikkhamānaṃ— ‘sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī’ti vatvā, neva vuṭṭhāpeti na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karotī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī sikkhamānaṃ—‘sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī’ti vatvā, neva vuṭṭhāpessati na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_3363Msdiv 1151“Yā pana bhikkhunī sikkhamānaṃ—‘sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi, evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmī’ti vatvā, sā pacchā anantarāyikinī neva vuṭṭhāpeyya na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ kareyya, pācittiyan”ti. (77:132)

SC 4Ms 2V_3364Msdiv 1152Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5Ms 2V_3365Sikkhamānā nāma dve vassāni chasu dhammesu sikkhitasikkhā.

SC 6Ms 2V_3366Sace me tvaṃ, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassasi evāhaṃ taṃ vuṭṭhāpessāmīti evāhaṃ taṃ upasampādessāmi.

SC 7Ms 2V_3367Sā pacchā anantarāyikinīti asati antarāye.

SC 8Ms 2V_3368Neva vuṭṭhāpeyyāti na sayaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya.

SC 9Ms 2V_3369Na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ kareyyāti na aññaṃ āṇāpeyya. “Neva vuṭṭhāpessāmi na vuṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissāmī”ti dhuraṃ nikkhittamatte āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 10Ms 2V_3370Msdiv 1153Anāpatti—sati antarāye, pariyesitvā na labhati, gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 11Ms 2V_3371Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
