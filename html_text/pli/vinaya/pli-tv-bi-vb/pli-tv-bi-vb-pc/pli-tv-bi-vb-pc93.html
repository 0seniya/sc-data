Pācittiyā 93: Theravāda Vinaya

2. Bhikkhunivibhaṅga

2.4. Pācittiyakaṇḍa

2.4.9 Chattupāhanavagga

2.4.9.‍10 Gihiniummaddāpanasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.343 Ms 2V_3490Msdiv 1210Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo gihiniyā ummaddāpentipi parimaddāpentipi. Manussā vihāracārikaṃ āhiṇḍantā passitvā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gihiniyā ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipi, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.

SC 2Ms 2V_3491Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gihiniyā ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiniyā ummaddāpentipi parimaddāpentipī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiniyā ummaddāpessantipi parimaddāpessantipi. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_3492Msdiv 1211“Yā pana bhikkhunī gihiniyā ummaddāpeyya vā parimaddāpeyya vā, pācittiyan”ti. (91--93:146–‍148)

SC 4Ms 2V_3493Msdiv 1212Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5Ms 2V_3496Gihinī nāma agārinī vuccati.

SC 6Ms 2V_3497Ummaddāpeyya vāti ummaddāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 7Ms 2V_3498Parimaddāpeyya vāti sambāhāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 8Ms 2V_3499Msdiv 1213Anāpatti—gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 11Ms 2V_3500Aṭṭhama-navama-dasamasikkhāpadāni niṭṭhitāni.
