55. Kulamaccharinīsikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.312 Ms 2V_3137Msdiv 1042Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarā bhikkhunī sāvatthiyaṃ aññatarissā visikhāya piṇḍāya caramānā yena aññataraṃ kulaṃ tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā paññatte āsane nisīdi. Atha kho te manussā taṃ bhikkhuniṃ bhojetvā etadavocuṃ— “aññāpi, ayye, bhikkhuniyo āgacchantū”ti. Atha kho te manussā taṃ bhikkhuniṃ bhojetvā etadavocuṃ—“kissa, ayye, bhikkhuniyo na āgacchantī”ti?

Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_3141Kulaṃ nāma cattāri kulāni—khattiyakulaṃ, brāhmaṇakulaṃ, vessakulaṃ, suddakulaṃ.

SC 6SC 6Ms 2V_3142Maccharinī assāti “kathaṃ bhikkhuniyo nāgaccheyyun”ti bhikkhunīnaṃ santike kulassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Kulassa vā santike bhikkhunīnaṃ avaṇṇaṃ bhāsati, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 7Ms 2V_3143SC 7Ms 2V_3143Msdiv 1045Anāpatti—kulaṃ na maccharāyantī santaṃyeva ādīnavaṃ ācikkhati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
