96. Chattupāhanavagga

13. Asaṅkaccikasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.345 Ms 2V_3517Msdiv 1224Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarā bhikkhunī asaṅkaccikā gāmaṃ piṇḍāya pāvisi. Tassā rathikāya vātamaṇḍalikā saṅghāṭiyo ukkhipiṃsu. Manussā ukkuṭṭhiṃ akaṃsu—“sundarā ayyāya thanudarā”ti. Sā bhikkhunī tehi manussehi uppaṇḍiyamānā maṅku ahosi.

SC 2Ms 2V_3518Atha kho sā bhikkhunī upassayaṃ gantvā bhikkhunīnaṃ etamatthaṃ ārocesi. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma bhikkhunī asaṅkaccikā gāmaṃ pavisissatī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī asaṅkaccikā gāmaṃ pāvisī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī asaṅkaccikā gāmaṃ pavisissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_3519Msdiv 1225“Yā pana bhikkhunī asaṅkaccikā gāmaṃ paviseyya, pācittiyan”ti. (196:151)

SC 4Ms 2V_3520Msdiv 1226Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5Ms 2V_3521Asaṅkaccikāti vinā saṅkaccikaṃ.

SC 6Ms 2V_3522Saṅkaccikaṃ nāma adhakkhakaṃ ubbhanābhi, tassa paṭicchādanatthāya.

SC 7Ms 2V_3523Gāmaṃ paviseyyāti parikkhittassa gāmassa parikkhepaṃ atikkāmentiyā āpatti pācittiyassa. Aparikkhittassa gāmassa upacāraṃ okkamantiyā āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 8Ms 2V_3524Msdiv 1227Anāpatti—acchinnacīvarikāya, naṭṭhacīvarikāya, gilānāya, assatiyā, ajānantiyā, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 9Ms 2V_3525Terasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
Chattupāhanavaggo navamo.

Dhammikavagga

10 Nandasikkhāpada

SC 10Ms 2V_3526Uddiṭṭhā kho, ayyāyo, chasaṭṭhisatā pācittiyā dhammā. Tatthāyyāyo pucchāmi— “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Dutiyampi pucchāmi—“kaccittha parisuddhā”? Tatiyampi pucchāmi—“kaccittha parisuddhā”? Parisuddhetthāyyāyo, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṃ dhārayāmīti.

SC 11Ms 2V_3527Khuddakaṃ samattaṃ.
Bhikkhunivibhaṅge pācittiyakaṇḍaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ. ()
