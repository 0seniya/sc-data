88. Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.341 Ms 2V_3469Msdiv 1198Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.

SC 2Ms 2V_3470Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissantī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_3471Msdiv 1199“Yā pana bhikkhunī gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyeyya, pācittiyan”ti. (88:143)

SC 4Ms 2V_3472Msdiv 1200Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5Ms 2V_3473Gandho nāma yo koci gandho. Vaṇṇakaṃ nāma yaṃ kiñci vaṇṇakaṃ. Nahāyeyyāti nahāyati. Payoge dukkaṭaṃ, nahānapariyosāne āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 6Ms 2V_3474Msdiv 1201Anāpatti—ābādhappaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 7Ms 2V_3475Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
