26. Gaṇacīvarasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_2833Msdiv 907Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandāya bhikkhuniyā upaṭṭhākakulaṃ thullanandaṃ bhikkhuniṃ etadavoca—“bhikkhunisaṃghassa, ayye, cīvaraṃ dassāmā”ti. Thullanandā bhikkhunī—“tumhe bahukiccā bahukaraṇīyā”ti antarāyaṃ akāsi. Tena kho pana samayena tassa kulassa gharaṃ ḍayhati. Te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā amhākaṃ deyyadhammaṃ antarāyaṃ karissati. Ubhayenāmha paribāhirā bhogehi ca puññena cā”ti.

SC 2Ms 2V_2834Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā … pe … tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā gaṇassa cīvaralābhaṃ antarāyaṃ karissatī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī gaṇassa cīvaralābhaṃ antarāyaṃ akāsī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī gaṇassa cīvaralābhaṃ antarāyaṃ karissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

SC 3Ms 2V_2835Msdiv 908“Yā pana bhikkhunī gaṇassa cīvaralābhaṃ antarāyaṃ kareyya, pācittiyan”ti. (26:81)

SC 4Ms 2V_2836Msdiv 909Yā panāti yā yādisā … pe … bhikkhunīti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

SC 5Ms 2V_2837Gaṇo nāma bhikkhunisaṃgho vuccati.

SC 6Ms 2V_2838Cīvaraṃ nāma channaṃ cīvarānaṃ aññataraṃ cīvaraṃ vikappanupagaṃ pacchimaṃ.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 4.284 Ms 2V_2839Antarāyaṃ kareyyāti “kathaṃ ime cīvaraṃ na dadeyyun”ti antarāyaṃ karoti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Aññaṃ parikkhāraṃ antarāyaṃ karoti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Sambahulānaṃ bhikkhunīnaṃ vā ekabhikkhuniyā vā anupasampannāya vā cīvaraṃ vā aññaṃ vā parikkhāraṃ antarāyaṃ karoti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 8Ms 2V_2840Msdiv 910Anāpatti—ānisaṃsaṃ dassetvā nivāreti, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

SC 9Ms 2V_2841Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
