25. Ukkuṭikasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1805Msdiv 600Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1806“Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (25:170)

SC 3Ms 2V_1807Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1808Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1809Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
