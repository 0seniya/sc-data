28. Pattasaññīpaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1820Msdiv 603Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1821“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggahessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (28:173)

SC 3Ms 2V_1822Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto paṭiggahetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1823Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1824Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
