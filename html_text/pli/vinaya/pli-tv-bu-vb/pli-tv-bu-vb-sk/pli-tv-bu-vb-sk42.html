42. Dutiyaanāhaṭasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1894Msdiv 618Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhuñjamānā sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1895“Na bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (42:187)

SC 3Ms 2V_1896Na bhuñjamānena sabbo hattho mukhe pakkhipitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca bhuñjamāno sabbaṃ hatthaṃ mukhe pakkhipati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1897Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1898Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
