50. Capucapukārakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1934Msdiv 626Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjanti … pe … .

SC 2Ms 2V_1935“Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (50:195)

SC 3Ms 2V_1936Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1937Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1938Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
Kabaḷavaggo pañcamo.
