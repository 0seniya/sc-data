37. Sūpodanaviññattisikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.193 Ms 2V_1865SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.193 Ms 2V_1865Msdiv 612Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma samaṇā sakyaputtiyā sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjissanti. Kassa sampannaṃ na manāpaṃ. Kassa sāduṃ na ruccatī”ti.

SC 2SC 2Ms 2V_1866Assosuṃ kho bhikkhū tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā … pe … te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjissantī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjathā”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjissatha. Netaṃ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha—

SC 3Ms 2V_1867SC 3Ms 2V_1867“Na sūpaṃ vā odanaṃ vā attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1868Evañcidaṃ bhagavatā bhikkhūnaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ paññattaṃ hoti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1869SC 5Ms 2V_1869Msdiv 613Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū gilānā honti. Gilānapucchakā bhikkhū gilāne bhikkhū etadavocuṃ— “kaccāvuso, khamanīyaṃ, kacci yāpanīyan”ti? “Pubbe mayaṃ, āvuso, sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjāma, tena no phāsu hoti. Idāni pana— ‘bhagavatā paṭikkhittan’ti kukkuccāyantā na viññāpema, tena no na phāsu hotī”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ … pe … anujānāmi, bhikkhave, gilānena bhikkhunā sūpampi odanampi attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjituṃ. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha—

SC 6Ms 2V_1870SC 6Ms 2V_1870“Na sūpaṃ vā odanaṃ vā agilāno attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (37:182)

SC 7SC 7Ms 2V_1871Na sūpaṃ vā odanaṃ vā agilānena attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sūpaṃ vā odanaṃ vā agilāno attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 8SC 8Ms 2V_1872Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ñātakānaṃ pavāritānaṃ, aññassatthāya, attano dhanena, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 2V_1873Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
