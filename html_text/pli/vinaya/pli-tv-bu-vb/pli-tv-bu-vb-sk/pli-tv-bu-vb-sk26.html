26. Pallatthikasikkhāpada

Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū SC 1Ms 2V_1810Msdiv 601Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1811SC 2Ms 2V_1811“Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (26:171)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1812Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthapallatthikāya vā dussapallatthikāya vā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1813Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1814Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
