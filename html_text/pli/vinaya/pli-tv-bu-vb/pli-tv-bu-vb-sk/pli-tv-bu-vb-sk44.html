44. Piṇḍukkhepakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1904Msdiv 620Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū piṇḍukkhepakaṃ bhuñjanti … pe … .

SC 2Ms 2V_1905“Na piṇḍukkhepakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (44:189)

SC 3Ms 2V_1906Na piṇḍukkhepakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca piṇḍukkhepakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1907Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, khajjake, phalāphale, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1908Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
