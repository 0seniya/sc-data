8. Dutiyaokkhittacakkhusikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1720Msdiv 583Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā antaraghare nisīdanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1721“Okkhittacakkhu antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (8:153)

SC 3Ms 2V_1722Okkhittacakkhunā antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ yugamattaṃ pekkhantena. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1723Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1724Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
