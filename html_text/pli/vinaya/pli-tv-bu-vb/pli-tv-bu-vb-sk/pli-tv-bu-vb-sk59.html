59. Satthapāṇisikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1990Msdiv 636Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1991“Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (59:204)

SC 3PTS vp Pali 4.201 Ms 2V_1992Satthaṃ nāma ekatodhāraṃ ubhatodhāraṃ paharaṇaṃ.

SC 4Ms 2V_1993Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 5Ms 2V_1994Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 6Ms 2V_1995Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
