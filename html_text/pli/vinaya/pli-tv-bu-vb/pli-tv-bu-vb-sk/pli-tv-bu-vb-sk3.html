3. Suppaṭicchannasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1695SC 1Ms 2V_1695Msdiv 578Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kāyaṃ vivaritvā antaraghare gacchanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1696“SC 2Ms 2V_1696“Suppaṭicchanno antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (3:148)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1697Suppaṭicchannena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca kāyaṃ vivaritvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1698Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1699Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
