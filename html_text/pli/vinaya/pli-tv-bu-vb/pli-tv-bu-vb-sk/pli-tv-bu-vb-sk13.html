13. Uccasaddasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1745Msdiv 588Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū uccāsaddaṃ mahāsaddaṃ karontā antaraghare gacchanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1746“Appasaddo antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (13:158)

SC 3Ms 2V_1747Appasaddena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca uccāsaddaṃ mahāsaddaṃ karonto antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1748Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1749Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
