32. Pattasaññīsikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1840SC 1Ms 2V_1840Msdiv 607Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1841SC 2Ms 2V_1841“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (32:177)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1842Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto bhuñjitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1843Anāpatti— asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1844Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
