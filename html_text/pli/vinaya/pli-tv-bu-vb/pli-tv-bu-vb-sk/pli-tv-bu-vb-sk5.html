5. Susaṃvutasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1705SC 1Ms 2V_1705Msdiv 580Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare gacchanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1706SC 2Ms 2V_1706“Susaṃvuto antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (5:150)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1707Susaṃvutena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthaṃ vā pādaṃ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1708Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1709Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
