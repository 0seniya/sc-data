48. Sitthāvakārakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1924Msdiv 624Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sitthāvakārakaṃ bhuñjanti … pe … .

SC 2Ms 2V_1925“Na sitthāvakārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (48:193)

SC 3Ms 2V_1926Na sitthāvakārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sitthāvakārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 4.197 Ms 2V_1927Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, kacavaraṃ chaḍḍento sitthaṃ chaḍḍayati, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1928Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
