53. Pattanillehakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1950SC 1Ms 2V_1950Msdiv 629Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjanti … pe … .

SC 2Ms 2V_1951SC 2Ms 2V_1951“Na pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (53:198)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1952Na pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pattanillehakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1953Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, parittake sese ekato saṅkaḍḍhitvā nillehitvā bhuñjati, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1954Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
