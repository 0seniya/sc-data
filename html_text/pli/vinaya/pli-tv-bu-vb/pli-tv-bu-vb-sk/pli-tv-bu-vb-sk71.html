71. Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.205 Ms 2V_2062Msdiv 649Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pacchato gacchantā purato gacchantassa dhammaṃ desenti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_2063“Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (71:216)

SC 3Ms 2V_2064Na pacchato gacchantena purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_2065Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_2066Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
