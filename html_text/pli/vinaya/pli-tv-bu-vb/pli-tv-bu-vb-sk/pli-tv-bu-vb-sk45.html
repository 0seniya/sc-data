45. Kabaḷāvacchedakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1909Msdiv 621Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjanti … pe ….

SC 2PTS vp Pali 4.196 Ms 2V_1910“Na kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (45:190)

SC 3Ms 2V_1911Na kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca kabaḷāvacchedakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1912Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, khajjake phalāphale, uttaribhaṅge, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1913Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
