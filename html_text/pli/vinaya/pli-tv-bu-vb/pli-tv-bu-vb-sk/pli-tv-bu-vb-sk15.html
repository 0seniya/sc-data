15. Kāyappacālakasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.188 Ms 2V_1755SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.188 Ms 2V_1755Msdiv 590Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti kāyaṃ olambentā … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1756SC 2Ms 2V_1756“Na kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (15:160)

SC 3SC 3Ms 2V_1757Na kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Kāyaṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati kāyaṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4SC 4Ms 2V_1758Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 2V_1759Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
