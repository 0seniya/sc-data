19. Sīsappacālakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1775Msdiv 594Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti sīsaṃ olambentā … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1776“Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (19:164)

SC 3Ms 2V_1777Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Sīsaṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati sīsaṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1778Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1779Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
