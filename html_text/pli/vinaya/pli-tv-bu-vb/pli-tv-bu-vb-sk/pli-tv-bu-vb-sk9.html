9. Ukkhittakasikkhāpada

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.187 Ms 2V_1725Msdiv 584Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkhittakāya antaraghare gacchanti … pe ….

SC 2Ms 2V_1726“Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (9:154)

SC 3Ms 2V_1727Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā ukkhipitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 4Ms 2V_1728Anāpatti—asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1729Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
