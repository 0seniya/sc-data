76. Amūlakasikkhāpada

SC 1Ms 2V_1367Msdiv 459Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsenti.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 4.148 Ms 2V_1368Ye te bhikkhū appicchā … pe … te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—“kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsessantī”ti … pe … “saccaṃ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsethā”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā … pe … kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃsessatha. Netaṃ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya … pe … evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha—

SC 3Ms 2V_1369Msdiv 460“Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuṃ amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseyya, pācittiyan”ti. (76:125)

SC 4Ms 2V_1370Msdiv 461Yo panāti yo yādiso … pe … bhikkhūti … pe … ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.

SC 5Ms 2V_1371Bhikkhunti aññaṃ bhikkhuṃ.

SC 6Ms 2V_1372Amūlakaṃ nāma adiṭṭhaṃ assutaṃ aparisaṅkitaṃ.

SC 7Ms 2V_1373Saṃghādisesenāti terasannaṃ aññatarena.

SC 8Ms 2V_1374Anuddhaṃseyyāti codeti vā codāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 9Ms 2V_1375Msdiv 462Upasampanne upasampannasaññī amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne vematiko amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne anupasampannasaññī amūlakena saṃghādisesena anuddhaṃseti, āpatti pācittiyassa.

SC 10Ms 2V_1376Ācāravipattiyā vā diṭṭhivipattiyā vā anuddhaṃseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampannaṃ anuddhaṃseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne upasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne anupasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

SC 11Ms 2V_1377Msdiv 463Anāpatti—tathāsaññī codeti, vā codāpeti vā, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

SC 12Ms 2V_1378Amūlakasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
