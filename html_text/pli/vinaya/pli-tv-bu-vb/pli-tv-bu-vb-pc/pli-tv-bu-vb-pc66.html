Te magge pariyuṭṭhiṃsu. Atha kho te kammiyā taṃ satthaṃ gahetvā acchinditvā taṃ bhikkhuṃ etadavocuṃ—“kissa tvaṃ, bhante, jānaṃ theyyasatthena saddhiṃ gacchasī”ti? Palibundhetvā muñciṃsu.
