SC 66SC 66Ms 5V_1764Tattha katamaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ no kiccaṃ? Vivādādhikaraṇaṃ anuvādādhikaraṇaṃ āpattādhikaraṇaṃ—idaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ no kiccaṃ.

SC 67SC 67Ms 5V_1765Tattha katamaṃ adhikaraṇañceva kiccañca? Kiccādhikaraṇaṃ adhikaraṇañceva kiccaṃ cāti.
