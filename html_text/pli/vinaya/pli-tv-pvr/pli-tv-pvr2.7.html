7. Samathavāra

SC 1Ms 5V_1003Msdiv 245Avassutāya bhikkhuniyā avassutassa purisapuggalassa kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ katihi samathehi sammanti? Avassutāya bhikkhuniyā avassutassa purisapuggalassa kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ tīhi samathehi sammanti—siyā sammukhāvinayena ca paṭiññātakaraṇena ca, siyā sammukhāvinayena ca tiṇavatthārakena ca … pe …. (1)

SC 2PTS vp Pali 5.81 Ms 5V_1004Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ katihi samathehi sammanti? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ tīhi samathehi sammanti—siyā sammukhāvinayena ca paṭiññātakaraṇena ca, siyā sammukhāvinayena ca tiṇavatthārakena ca. (2)

SC 3Ms 5V_1005Samathavāro niṭṭhito sattamo.
