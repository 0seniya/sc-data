Milindapañha

Arū­pa­dham­ma­va­vatthā­na­vagga

14. Sukhumapañha

Rājā āha—“bhante nāgasena, sakkā sabbaṃ sukhumaṃ chinditun”ti? “Āma, mahārāja, sakkā sabbaṃ sukhumaṃ chinditun”ti. “Kiṃ pana, bhante, sabbaṃ sukhuman”ti? “Dhammo kho, mahārāja, sabbasukhumo, na kho, mahārāja, dhammā sabbe sukhumā, ‘sukhuman’ti vā ‘thūlan’ti vā ­dhammāna­meta­madhi­vacanaṃ. Yaṃ kiñci chinditabbaṃ, sabbaṃ taṃ paññāya chindati, natthi dutiyaṃ paññāya chedanan”ti.

“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

Sukhumapañho cuddasamo.
