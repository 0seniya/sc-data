Dhammataṇhānadukkhasamudayotikathā

SC 1Ms 32Kv_2194Msdiv 681SC 1Ms 32Kv_2194Msdiv 681☑ Dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Rūpataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe … dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Saddataṇhā … pe … gandhataṇhā … pe … rasataṇhā … pe … phoṭṭhabbataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe ….

SC 2Ms 32Kv_2195SC 2Ms 32Kv_2195☑ Rūpataṇhā dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Dhammataṇhā dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe … saddataṇhā … pe … gandhataṇhā … pe … rasataṇhā … pe … phoṭṭhabbataṇhā dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Dhammataṇhā dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe ….

SC 3Ms 32Kv_2196Msdiv 682SC 3Ms 32Kv_2196Msdiv 682☑ Dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Nanu taṇhā dukkhasamudayo vutto bhagavatāti? Āmantā. Hañci taṇhā dukkhasamudayo vutto bhagavatā, no ca vata re vattabbe—“dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayo”ti. Dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Nanu lobho dukkhasamudayo vutto bhagavatā, dhammataṇhā lobhoti? Āmantā. Hañci lobho dukkhasamudayo vutto bhagavatā, dhammataṇhā lobho, no ca vata re vattabbe—“dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayo”ti.

SC 4Ms 32Kv_2197Msdiv 683SC 4Ms 32Kv_2197Msdiv 683☑ Dhammataṇhā lobho, na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Rūpataṇhā lobho, na dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe … dhammataṇhā lobho, na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Saddataṇhā … pe … gandhataṇhā … pe … rasataṇhā … pe … phoṭṭhabbataṇhā lobho, na dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe ….

SC 5Ms 32Kv_2198SC 5Ms 32Kv_2198☑ Rūpataṇhā lobho dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Dhammataṇhā lobho dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe … saddataṇhā … pe … phoṭṭhabbataṇhā lobho dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Dhammataṇhā lobho dukkhasamudayoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe … pe ….

SC 6Ms 32Kv_2199Msdiv 684SC 6Ms 32Kv_2199Msdiv 684☑ Dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti? Āmantā. Nanu vuttaṃ bhagavatā—“ yāyaṃ taṇhā ponobbhavikā nandīrāgasahagatā tatratatrābhinandinī, seyyathidaṃ—kāmataṇhā, bhavataṇhā, vibhavataṇhā”ti. Attheva suttantoti? Āmantā. Tena hi na vattabbaṃ— “dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayo”ti.

SC 7Ms 32Kv_2200Msdiv 685SC 7Ms 32Kv_2200Msdiv 685☒ Na vattabbaṃ—“dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayo”ti? Āmantā. Nanu sā dhammataṇhāti? Āmantā. Hañci sā dhammataṇhā, tena vata re vattabbe— “dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayo”ti.

Ms 32Kv_2201Dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayotikathā niṭṭhitā.
Terasamo vaggo.

Ms 32Kv_2202Tassuddānaṃ

SC 8SC 8Ms 32Kv_2203Kappaṭṭho kappaṃ tiṭṭheyya, kappaṭṭho kusalaṃ cittaṃ na paṭilabheyya, anantarāpayutto puggalo sammattaniyāmaṃ okkameyya, niyato niyāmaṃ okkamati, nivuto nīvaraṇaṃ jahati, sammukhībhūto saṃyojanaṃ jahati, jhānanikanti, asātarāgo, dhammataṇhā abyākatā, dhammataṇhā na dukkhasamudayoti.
