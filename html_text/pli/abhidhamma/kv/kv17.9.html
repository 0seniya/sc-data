Navattabbaṃsaṃghassadinnaṃmahapphalantikathā

SC 1Ms 32Kv_2484Msdiv 797☑ Na vattabbaṃ—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti? Āmantā. Nanu saṃgho āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjalikaraṇīyo anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassāti? Āmantā. Hañci saṃgho āhuneyyo … pe … anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa, tena vata re vattabbe—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti.

SC 2Ms 32Kv_2485☑ Na vattabbaṃ—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti? Āmantā. Nanu cattāro purisayugā aṭṭha purisapuggalā dakkhiṇeyyā vuttā bhagavatāti? Āmantā. Hañci cattāro purisayugā aṭṭha purisapuggalā dakkhiṇeyyā vuttā bhagavatā, tena vata re vattabbe—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti.

SC 3Ms 32Kv_2486Msdiv 798☑ Na vattabbaṃ—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti? Āmantā. Nanu vuttaṃ bhagavatā—“ saṃghe, gotami, dehi, saṃghe te dinne ahañceva pūjito bhavissāmi saṃgho cā”ti. Attheva suttantoti? Āmantā. Tena hi saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalanti.

SC 4Ms 32Kv_2487☑ Na vattabbaṃ—“saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti? Āmantā. Nanu sakko devānamindo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—

SC 5 Ms 32Kv_2488“Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
kattha dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti.

SC 6Ms 32Kv_2489“Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṃgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito.

SC 7Ms 32Kv_2490Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalanti.

SC 8 Ms 32Kv_2491Eso hi saṃgho vipulo mahaggato,
Esappameyyo udadhīva sāgaro;
Ete hi seṭṭhā naravīrasāvakā,
Pabhaṅkarā dhammamudīrayanti.

SC 9Ms 32Kv_2492Tesaṃ sudinnaṃ suhutaṃ suyiṭṭhaṃ,
Ye saṃghamuddissa dadanti dānaṃ;
Sā dakkhiṇā saṃghagatā patiṭṭhitā,
Mahapphalā lokavidūna vaṇṇitā.

SC 10Ms 32Kv_2493Etādisaṃ yaññamanussarantā,
Ye vedajātā vicaranti loke;
Vineyya maccheramalaṃ samūlaṃ,
Aninditā saggamupenti ṭhānan”ti.

SC 11Ms 32Kv_2494Attheva suttantoti? Āmantā. Tena hi saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalanti.

Ms 32Kv_2495Na vattabbaṃ saṃghassa dinnaṃ mahapphalantikathā niṭṭhitā.
