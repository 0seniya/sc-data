1.4. Catukkauddesa

SC 1Ms 31Pu_111Msdiv 10Cattāro puggalā—

SC 2Ms 31Pu_112Asappuriso, asappurisena asappurisataro, sappuriso, sappurisena sappurisataro. (1)

SC 3Ms 31Pu_113Pāpo, pāpena pāpataro, kalyāṇo, kalyāṇena kalyāṇataro. (2)

SC 4Ms 31Pu_114Pāpadhammo, pāpadhammena pāpadhammataro, kalyāṇadhammo, kalyāṇadhammena kalyāṇadhammataro. (3)

SC 5Ms 31Pu_115Sāvajjo, vajjabahulo, appavajjo, anavajjo. (4)

SC 6Ms 31Pu_116Ugghaṭitaññū, vipañcitaññū, neyyo, padaparamo. (5)

SC 7Ms 31Pu_117Yuttappaṭibhāno, no muttappaṭibhāno, muttappaṭibhāno, no yuttappaṭibhāno, yuttappaṭibhāno ca muttappaṭibhāno ca, neva yuttappaṭibhāno no muttappaṭibhāno. (6)

SC 8Ms 31Pu_118Cattāro dhammakathikā puggalā. (7)

SC 9Ms 31Pu_119Cattāro valāhakūpamā puggalā. (8)

SC 10Ms 31Pu_120Cattāro mūsikūpamā puggalā. (9)

SC 11Ms 31Pu_121Cattāro ambūpamā puggalā. (10)

SC 12Ms 31Pu_122Cattāro kumbhūpamā puggalā. (11)

SC 13Ms 31Pu_123Cattāro udakarahadūpamā puggalā. (12)

SC 14Ms 31Pu_124Cattāro balībaddūpamā puggalā. (13)

SC 15Ms 31Pu_125Cattāro āsīvisūpamā puggalā. (14)

SC 16Ms 31Pu_126Atthekacco puggalo ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā avaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā vaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā appasādanīye ṭhāne pasādaṃ upadaṃsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā pasādanīye ṭhāne appasādaṃ upadaṃsitā hoti. (15)

SC 17Ms 31Pu_127Atthekacco puggalo anuvicca pariyogāhetvā avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo anuvicca pariyogāhetvā vaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo anuvicca pariyogāhetvā appasādanīye ṭhāne appasādaṃ upadaṃsitā hoti, atthekacco puggalo anuvicca pariyogāhetvā pasādanīye ṭhāne pasādaṃ upadaṃsitā hoti. (16)

SC 18Ms 31Pu_128Atthekacco puggalo avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena, no ca kho vaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena; atthekacco puggalo vaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena, no ca kho avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena; atthekacco puggalo avaṇṇārahassa ca avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena; vaṇṇārahassa ca vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena, atthekacco puggalo neva avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena, no ca vaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṃ bhāsitā hoti bhūtaṃ tacchaṃ kālena. (17)

SC 19Ms 31Pu_129Uṭṭhānaphalūpajīvī no puññaphalūpajīvī, puññaphalūpajīvī no uṭṭhānaphalūpajīvī, uṭṭhānaphalūpajīvī ca puññaphalūpajīvī ca, neva uṭṭhānaphalūpajīvī no puññaphalūpajīvī. (18)

SC 20Ms 31Pu_130Tamo tamaparāyano, tamo jotiparāyano, joti tamaparāyano, joti jotiparāyano. (19)

SC 21Ms 31Pu_131Oṇatoṇato, oṇatuṇṇato, uṇṇatoṇato, uṇṇatuṇṇato. (20)

SC 22Ms 31Pu_132Cattāro rukkhūpamā puggalā. (21)

SC 23Ms 31Pu_133Rūpappamāṇo, rūpappasanno, ghosappamāṇo, ghosappasanno. (22)

SC 24Ms 31Pu_134Lūkhappamāṇo, lūkhappasanno, dhammappamāṇo, dhammappasanno. (23)

SC 25Ms 31Pu_135Atthekacco puggalo attahitāya paṭipanno hoti, no parahitāya; atthekacco puggalo parahitāya paṭipanno hoti, no attahitāya; atthekacco puggalo attahitāya ceva paṭipanno hoti parahitāya ca; atthekacco puggalo neva attahitāya paṭipanno hoti no parahitāya. (24)

SC 26Ms 31Pu_136Atthekacco puggalo attantapo hoti attaparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto; atthekacco puggalo parantapo hoti paraparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto; atthekacco puggalo attantapo ca hoti attaparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto, parantapo ca paraparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto; atthekacco puggalo neva attantapo hoti na attaparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto, na parantapo na paraparitāpanānuyogamanuyutto. So anattantapo aparantapo diṭṭheva dhamme nicchāto nibbuto sītībhūto sukhappaṭisaṃvedī brahmabhūtena attanā viharati. (25)

SC 27Ms 31Pu_137Sarāgo, sadoso, samoho, samāno. (26)

SC 28Ms 31Pu_138Atthekacco puggalo lābhī hoti ajjhattaṃ cetosamathassa, na lābhī adhipaññādhammavipassanāya; atthekacco puggalo lābhī hoti adhipaññādhammavipassanāya, na lābhī ajjhattaṃ cetosamathassa; atthekacco puggalo lābhī ceva hoti ajjhattaṃ cetosamathassa, lābhī ca adhipaññādhammavipassanāya; atthekacco puggalo neva lābhī hoti ajjhattaṃ cetosamathassa, na lābhī adhipaññādhammavipassanāya. (27)

SC 29Ms 31Pu_139Anusotagāmī puggalo, paṭisotagāmī puggalo, ṭhitatto puggalo, tiṇṇo pāraṅgato thale tiṭṭhati brāhmaṇo. (28)

SC 30Ms 31Pu_140Appassuto sutena anupapanno, appassuto sutena upapanno, bahussuto sutena anupapanno, bahussuto sutena upapanno. (29)

SC 31Ms 31Pu_141Samaṇamacalo, samaṇapadumo, samaṇapuṇḍarīko, samaṇesu samaṇasukhumālo. (30)

SC 32Ms 31Pu_142Catukkaṃ.
