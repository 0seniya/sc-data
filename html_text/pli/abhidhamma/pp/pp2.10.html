2.10. Dasakapuggalapaññatti

SC 1Ms 31Pu_546Msdiv 209Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā? Sattakkhattuparamassa, kolaṃkolassa, ekabījissa, sakadāgāmissa, yo ca diṭṭheva dhamme arahā—imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā. (1–‍5)

SC 2Ms 31Pu_547Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhā? Antarāparinibbāyissa, upahaccaparinibbāyissa, asaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, sasaṅkhāraparinibbāyissa, uddhaṃsotassa akaniṭṭhagāmino—imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhāti. (6–‍10)

SC 3Ms 31Pu_548Ettāvatā puggalānaṃ puggalapaññattīti.

SC 4Ms 31Pu_549Dasakaniddeso.

SC 5Ms 31Pu_550Puggalapaññattipakaraṇaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
