2.6. Chakkapuggalapaññatti

SC 1Ms 31Pu_512SC 1Ms 31Pu_512Msdiv 202Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisambujjhati, tattha ca sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti balesu ca vasībhāvaṃ, sammāsambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (1)

SC 2SC 2Ms 31Pu_513Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisambujjhati, na ca tattha sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti na ca balesu vasībhāvaṃ, paccekasambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (2)

SC 3Ms 31Pu_514Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme SC 3Ms 31Pu_514Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, sāvakapāramiñca pāpuṇāti, sāriputtamoggallānā tena daṭṭhabbā. (3)

SC 4SC 4Ms 31Pu_515Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, na ca sāvakapāramiṃ pāpuṇāti, avasesā arahantā tena daṭṭhabbā. (4)

SC 5SC 5Ms 31Pu_516Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṃ, anāgāmī tena daṭṭhabbo. (5)

SC 6SC 6Ms 31Pu_517Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, āgantā itthattaṃ, sotāpannasakadāgāmino tena daṭṭhabbā. (6)

SC 7SC 7Ms 31Pu_518Chakkaniddeso.
