24.3. Hetuduka, Cūḷantaraduka

SC 1Ms 40P24_20Msdiv 8Nahetu nasappaccayo dhammo hetussa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P24_21Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye tīṇi. (Saṅkhataṃ sappaccayasadisaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P24_22Msdiv 9Nahetuṃ nasanidassanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P24_23Nahetu naanidassano dhammo hetussa anidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P24_24Msdiv 10Nahetuṃ nasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 6Ms 40P24_25Nahetuṃ naappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu appaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 7Ms 40P24_26Msdiv 11Nahetuṃ narūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu rūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 8Ms 40P24_27Nahetuṃ naarūpiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu arūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 9Ms 40P24_28Msdiv 12Nahetuṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nanahetuṃ nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Gaṇitakena tīṇi. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 10Ms 40P24_29Nahetuṃ nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 11Ms 40P24_30Msdiv 13Nahetuṃ nakenaci viññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.

SC 12Ms 40P24_31Nahetuṃ nakenaci naviññeyyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu kenaci naviññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
