14.17. Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_68SC 1Ms 40P14_68Msdiv 20Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P14_69Āsavasampayuttañceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P14_70Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañceva no ca āsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P14_71Hetuyā nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
