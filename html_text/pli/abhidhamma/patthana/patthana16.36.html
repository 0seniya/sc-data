16.36. Parittattika, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P16_205Msdiv 78Parittaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namahaggato nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava … pe … aññamaññe ekādasa … pe … avigate terasa.

SC 2Ms 40P16_206Parittaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naparitto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cuddasa, ārammaṇe cuddasa, adhipatiyā nava … pe … avigate cuddasa.
