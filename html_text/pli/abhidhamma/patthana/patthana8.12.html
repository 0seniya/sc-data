8.12. Lokiyaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_66Msdiv 26Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nalokiyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokiyo ca nalokuttaro ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P8_67Nalokuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

SC 3Ms 40P8_68Nalokiyañca nalokuttarañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nalokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_69Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
