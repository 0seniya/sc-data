14.21. Sārammaṇaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_81Msdiv 24Sārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P14_82Anārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P14_83Sārammaṇañca anārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P14_84Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … aññamaññe cha … pe … purejāte āsevane ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
