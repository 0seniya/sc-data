8.31. Ganthavippayuttaganthaniyaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_145Msdiv 45Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naganthaniyo ca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P8_146Ganthavippayuttaṃ naaganthaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 3Ms 40P8_147Ganthavippayuttaṃ naganthaniyañca ganthavippayuttaṃ naaganthaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ganthavippayutto naaganthaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
