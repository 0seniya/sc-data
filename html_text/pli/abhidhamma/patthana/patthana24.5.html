24.5. Hetuduka, Saññojanagocchakādi

SC 1Ms 40P24_48Msdiv 20Nahetuṃ nosaññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu saññojano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P24_49Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P24_50Msdiv 21Nahetuṃ noganthaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu gantho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P24_51Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.

SC 5Ms 40P24_52Msdiv 22Nahetuṃ nooghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu ogho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P24_53Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 7Ms 40P24_54Msdiv 23Nahetuṃ noyogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu yogo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 8Ms 40P24_55Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 9Ms 40P24_56Msdiv 24Nahetuṃ nonīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu nīvaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 10Ms 40P24_57Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 11Ms 40P24_58Msdiv 25Nahetuṃ noparāmāsaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu parāmāso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 40P24_59Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
