8.71. Niyyānikaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_298Msdiv 90Naniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P8_299Naaniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P8_300Naniyyānikañca naaniyyānikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_301Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.
