24.2. Hetuduka, Hetusampayuttadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P24_12Msdiv 4Nahetuṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri … pe … purejāte āsevane tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 2Ms 40P24_13Nahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte pañca, aññamaññe cattāri … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 3Ms 40P24_14Msdiv 5Nahetuṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 4Ms 40P24_15Nanahetuṃ naahetukañceva nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 5Ms 40P24_16Msdiv 6Nahetuṃ nahetuñceva nahetuvippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu hetu ceva hetusampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 6Ms 40P24_17Nanahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttañceva nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu hetusampayutto ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 7Ms 40P24_18Msdiv 7Nahetuṃ nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 8Ms 40P24_19Nahetuṃ nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
