11.5. Kusalattika, Vitakkattika

SC 1Ms 40P11_48Msdiv 18Nakusalaṃ nasavitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P11_49Hetuyā catuvīsa, ārammaṇe sattarasa … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate catuvīsa.

SC 5Ms 40P11_52Msdiv 20Nakusalaṃ naavitakkaavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
