23.16. Kusalattika, Uppannattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_69Msdiv 20Nakusalo nauppanno dhammo kusalassa uppannassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P23_70Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā nava, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa.
