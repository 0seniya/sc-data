18.1. Hetuduka, Sahetukaduka

SC 1Ms 40P18_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2Ms 40P18_2Msdiv 1Hetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

SC 3Ms 40P18_3Hetuṃ sahetukañca nahetuṃ sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P18_4Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe pañca … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 5Ms 40P18_5Msdiv 2Nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā … (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P18_6Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā pañca, napurejāte cattāri … pe … navippayutte ekaṃ … pe … novigate tīṇi.

SC 7Ms 40P18_7(Sahajātavārampi … pe … sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

Hetu-ārammaṇa

SC 8Ms 40P18_8Msdiv 3Hetu sahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

SC 9Ms 40P18_9Hetu sahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Hetu sahetuko dhammo nanahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Hetu sahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa ca nanahetussa nasahetukassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (3)

SC 10Ms 40P18_10Nahetu sahetuko dhammo nanahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nahetu sahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nahetu sahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa ca nanahetussa nasahetukassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 11Ms 40P18_11Hetu sahetuko ca nahetu sahetuko ca dhammā nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Hetu sahetuko ca nahetusahetuko ca dhammā nanahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 40P18_12Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve, anantare cattāri, sahajāte pañca, aññamaññe cattāri, nissaye pañca, upanissaye cha, pacchājāte tīṇi, āsevane ekaṃ, kamme … pe … āhāre tīṇi, indriye pañca, jhāne tīṇi, magge pañca, sampayutte ekaṃ, vippayutte tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca. (Pañhāvāraṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
