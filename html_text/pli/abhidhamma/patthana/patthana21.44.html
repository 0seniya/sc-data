21.44. Lokiyaduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_235Msdiv 92Nalokiyaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P21_236Nalokuttaraṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P21_237(Gaṇitakena gaṇetabbā tīṇi pañhā. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P21_238Nalokiyaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanasappaṭigho …. (Tīṇiyeva pañhā kātabbā. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P21_239Nalokuttaraṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
