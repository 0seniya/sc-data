17.11. Kusalattika, Parittattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_60SC 1Ms 40P17_60Msdiv 23Abyākataṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naparitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P17_61Kusalaṃ mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo namahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P17_62Kusalaṃ appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naappamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
