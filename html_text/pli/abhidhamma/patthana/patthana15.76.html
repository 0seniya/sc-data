15.76. Saraṇaduka, Pītittika

SC 1Ms 40P15_237Msdiv 88Saraṇaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P15_238Araṇaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P15_239Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 4Ms 40P15_240Saraṇaṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P15_241Araṇaṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 6Ms 40P15_242Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 7Ms 40P15_243Saraṇaṃ upekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naupekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.
