13.8. Dassanattika

Hetu

SC 1Ms 40P13_84SC 1Ms 40P13_84Msdiv 36Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nadassanena pahātabbo ca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (5)
