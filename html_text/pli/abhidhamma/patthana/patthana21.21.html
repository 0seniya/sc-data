21.21. Hetuduka, Vitakkattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_157Msdiv 66Nahetuṃ nasavitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu savitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P21_158Nahetuṃ naavitakkavicāramattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu avitakkavicāramatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_159Nahetuṃ naavitakkaavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu avitakkaavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
