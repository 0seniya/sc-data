12.1. Hetuduka, Sahetukadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P12_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2Ms 40P12_2Msdiv 1Nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P12_3Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P12_4Msdiv 2Nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (1)

SC 5Ms 40P12_5Nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P12_6Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … nakamme ekaṃ … pe … naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge ekaṃ … pe … novigate tīṇi.

Hetu-ārammaṇa

SC 7Ms 40P12_7Msdiv 3Nanahetu nasahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

SC 8Ms 40P12_8Nahetu nasahetuko dhammo nahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nahetu nasahetuko dhammo nanahetussa nasahetukassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 9Ms 40P12_9Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe cattāri, anantare cattāri … pe … sahajāte tīṇi, aññamaññe ekaṃ, nissaye upanissaye cattāri, purejāte pacchājāte āsevane dve, kamme … pe … jhāne ekaṃ, sampayutte ekaṃ, vippayutte tīṇi … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 10Ms 40P12_10Msdiv 4Nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko ca nanahetu naahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 11Ms 40P12_11Nanahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nanahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nanahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko ca nanahetu naahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 12Ms 40P12_12Nahetuṃ naahetukañca nanahetuṃ naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ naahetukañca nanahetuṃ naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ naahetukañca nanahetuṃ naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naahetuko ca nanahetu naahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 13Ms 40P12_13Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha nava.)

SC 14Ms 40P12_14Msdiv 5Nahetuṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 15Ms 40P12_15Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 16Ms 40P12_16Msdiv 6Nahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 17Ms 40P12_17Nanahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 18Ms 40P12_18Nahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttañca nanahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 19Ms 40P12_19Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

SC 20Ms 40P12_20Msdiv 7Nahetuṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Yāva pañhāvārepi ekaṃ.)

SC 21Ms 40P12_21Nanahetuṃ naahetukañceva nana ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ.)

SC 23Ms 40P12_23Nanahetuṃ nahetuvippayuttañceva nana ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nahetuvippayutto ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ.)
