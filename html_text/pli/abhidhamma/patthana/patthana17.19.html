17.19. Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya

SC 1Ms 40P17_82Msdiv 31Kusalo ajjhatto dhammo naajjhattassa nakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā nava, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte cha, āhāre tīṇi, atthiyā avigate cha.

SC 2Ms 40P17_83Kusalo bahiddhā dhammo nabahiddhā nakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā pannarasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte cha, āhāre tīṇi, atthiyā avigate cha.

SC 3Ms 40P17_84Msdiv 32Kusalaṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 4Ms 40P17_85Kusalaṃ bahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nabahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.
