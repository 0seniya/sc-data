17.23. Upādinnattika, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_108Msdiv 40Anupādinnupādāniyaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinnupādāniyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P17_109Anupādinnaanupādāniyaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanupādinnaanupādāniyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 3Ms 40P17_110Anupādinnupādāniyaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinnupādāniyo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P17_111Upādinnupādāniyaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naupādinnupādāniyo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
