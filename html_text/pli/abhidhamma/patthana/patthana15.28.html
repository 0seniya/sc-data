15.28. Sanidassanadukādi, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_104Msdiv 37Anidassanaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassano nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi. (Akusalaṃ kusalasadisaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P15_105Anidassanaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasanidassano naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P15_106Appaṭighaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naappaṭigho nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P15_107Appaṭighaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naappaṭigho naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P15_108Abyākate paccayavasena ekaṃ. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
