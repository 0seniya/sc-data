8.25. Saññojanavippayuttasaññojaniyaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_120Msdiv 39Saññojanavippayuttaṃ nasaññojaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojanavippayutto nasaññojaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Saññojanavippayuttaṃ nasaññojaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojanavippayutto naasaññojaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Saññojanavippayuttaṃ nasaññojaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojanavippayutto nasaññojaniyo ca saññojanavippayutto naasaññojaniyo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P8_121Saññojanavippayuttaṃ naasaññojaniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojanavippayutto naasaññojaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 3Ms 40P8_122Saññojanavippayuttaṃ nasaññojaniyañca saññojanavippayuttaṃ naasaññojaniyañca dhammaṃ paṭicca saññojanavippayutto naasaññojaniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_123Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)

SC 5Ms 40P8_124Saññojanagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
