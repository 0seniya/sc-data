8.60. Bhāvanāyapahātabbahetukaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_254Msdiv 79Nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P8_255Nanabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P8_256Nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukañca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_257Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
