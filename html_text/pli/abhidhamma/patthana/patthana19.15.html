19.15. Ajjhattattikadvaya

SC 1Ms 40P19_97Msdiv 28Naajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P19_98Nabahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P19_99Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

SC 4Ms 40P19_100Msdiv 29Naajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 5Ms 40P19_101Nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 6Ms 40P19_102Naajjhattārammaṇañca nabahiddhārammaṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 7Ms 40P19_103Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve … pe … purejāte āsevane dve … pe … avigate cha.
