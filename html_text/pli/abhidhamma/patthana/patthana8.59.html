8.59. Dassanenapahātabbahetukaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_250Msdiv 78Nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P8_251Nanadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P8_252Nadassanena pahātabbahetukañca nanadassanena pahātabbahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_253Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
