18.26. Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

SC 1Ms 40P18_212SC 1Ms 40P18_212Msdiv 90Araṇo appaccayo dhammo nasaraṇassa naappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P18_213Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P18_214SC 3Ms 40P18_214Msdiv 91Araṇo asaṅkhato dhammo nasaraṇassa naasaṅkhatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P18_215Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṃ.

SC 5Ms 40P18_216SC 5Ms 40P18_216Msdiv 92Araṇo sanidassano dhammo naaraṇassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Araṇo sanidassano dhammo nasaraṇassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6SC 6Ms 40P18_217Ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā ekaṃ, upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate dve.
