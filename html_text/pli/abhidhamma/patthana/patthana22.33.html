22.33. Sanidassanattika, Upādānadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P22_213Msdiv 95Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ noupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho upādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
