10.8. Kusalattika, Upādānadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P10_88Msdiv 40Nakusalaṃ noupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate ekūnatiṃsa.

SC 2Ms 40P10_89Nakusalaṃ nanoupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)

SC 3Ms 40P10_90Msdiv 41Nakusalaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekūnatiṃsa, ārammaṇe catuvīsa … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate ekūnatiṃsa.

SC 4Ms 40P10_91Nakusalaṃ nanokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
