16.58. Sanidassanattika, Āsavaduka

SC 1Ms 40P16_249SC 1Ms 40P16_249Msdiv 101Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho noāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pe … anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
