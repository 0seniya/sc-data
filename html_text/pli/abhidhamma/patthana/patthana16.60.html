16.60. Sanidassanattika, Āsavasampayuttaduka

SC 1Ms 40P16_253Msdiv 103Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pe … anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

SC 2Ms 40P16_254Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
