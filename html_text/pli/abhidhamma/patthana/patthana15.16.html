15.16. Hetuduka, Maggārammaṇattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_68Msdiv 24Hetuṃ maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu namaggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Sabbattha tīṇi.)

SC 2Ms 40P15_69Hetuṃ maggahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu namaggahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Sabbattha tīṇi.)

SC 3Ms 40P15_70Hetuṃ maggādhipatiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu namaggādhipati dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
