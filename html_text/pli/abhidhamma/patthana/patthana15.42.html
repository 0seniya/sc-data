15.42. Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaduka, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_150Msdiv 51Cittasaṃsaṭṭhaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasaṃsaṭṭho nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
