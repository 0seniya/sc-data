8.40. Cittasahabhūduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_186SC 1Ms 40P8_186Msdiv 59Nacittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P8_187Hetuyā nava … pe … purejāte āsevane pañca … pe … avigate nava.
