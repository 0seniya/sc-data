22.31. Sanidassanattika, Saññojanagocchakādi

SC 1Ms 40P22_175Msdiv 75Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nosaññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho saññojano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
