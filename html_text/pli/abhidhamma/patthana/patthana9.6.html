9.6. Sahetukadukādi, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P9_91Msdiv 33Nasahetukaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasahetuko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā … pe … dve mahābhūte … pe … nasahetukaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naahetuko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—vicikicchāsahagataṃ uddhaccasahagataṃ mohaṃ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṃ paṭicca sahetukā khandhā. Nasahetukaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasahetuko nakusalo ca naahetuko nakusalo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
