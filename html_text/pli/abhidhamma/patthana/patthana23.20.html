23.20. Vedanāttika, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_89SC 1Ms 40P23_89Msdiv 27Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P23_90Nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P23_91Naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve. (Cattāri gaṇitakena dve dve pañhā kātabbā. Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P23_92Hetuyā cuddasa … pe … avigate cuddasa. (Vipākaṃ natthi.)

SC 5Ms 40P23_93SC 5Ms 40P23_93Msdiv 28Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6SC 6Ms 40P23_94Hetuyā ekavīsa … pe … avigate ekavīsa. (Vipākaṃ natthi.)

SC 7Ms 40P23_95SC 7Ms 40P23_95Msdiv 29Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto naabyākato dhammo sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo.

SC 8SC 8Ms 40P23_96Ārammaṇe cuddasa, adhipatiyā dvādasa, anantare terasa, upanissaye ekavīsa, kamme sattarasa, natthiyā vigate terasa.
