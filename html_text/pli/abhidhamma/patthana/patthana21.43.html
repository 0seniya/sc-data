21.43. Rūpīduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_232Msdiv 91Narūpiṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P21_233Narūpiṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_234Naarūpiṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
