15.54. Bhāvanāyapahātabbaduka, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_177Msdiv 63Nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P15_178Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

SC 3Ms 40P15_179Nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.
