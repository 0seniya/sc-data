22.6. Kusalattika, Āsavagocchaka

SC 1Ms 40P22_42Msdiv 16Nakusalaṃ noāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P22_43Nakusalaṃ nanoāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo noāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 3Ms 40P22_44Msdiv 17Nakusalaṃ nasāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato sāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 4Ms 40P22_45Nakusalaṃ naanāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo anāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

SC 5Ms 40P22_46Msdiv 18Nakusalaṃ naāsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 6Ms 40P22_47Nakusalaṃ naāsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 7Ms 40P22_48Msdiv 19Nakusalaṃ naāsavañceva naanāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavo ceva sāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 8Ms 40P22_49Nakusalaṃ naanāsavañceva nano ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 9Ms 40P22_50Msdiv 20Nakusalaṃ naāsavañceva naāsavavippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 10Ms 40P22_51Nakusalaṃ naāsavavippayuttañceva nano ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo āsavasampayutto ceva no ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 11Ms 40P22_52Msdiv 21Nakusalaṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ nasāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato āsavavippayutto sāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.
