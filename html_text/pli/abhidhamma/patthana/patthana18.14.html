18.14. Cūḷantaraduka, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P18_106Msdiv 47Sappaccayaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naappaccayo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
