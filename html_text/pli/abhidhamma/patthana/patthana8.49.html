8.49. Kilesaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_210SC 1Ms 40P8_210Msdiv 68Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso ca nanokileso ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P8_211Nanokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P8_212Nokilesañca nanokilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P8_213Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
