8.37. Cittasampayuttaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_179Msdiv 56Nacittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—cittaṃ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Nacittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacittavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—cittaṃ paṭicca cittasampayuttakā khandhā.

SC 2Ms 40P8_180(Paccanīyadukamattāni na vattabbaṃ dhammaṃ pūretuṃ nava nava pañhāni karotu.)

SC 3Ms 40P8_181Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … aññamaññe cha … pe … purejāte āsevane ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
