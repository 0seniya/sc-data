23.2. Kusalattika, Vipākattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_20Msdiv 4Nakusalaṃ navipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato vipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P23_21Naakusalaṃ navipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato vipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P23_22Nakusalaṃ navipākañca naakusalaṃ navipākañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato vipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P23_23Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P23_24Msdiv 5Nakusalaṃ navipākadhammadhammaṃ paccayā kusalo vipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṃ navipākadhammadhammaṃ paccayā akusalo vipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 6Ms 40P23_25Naakusalaṃ navipākadhammadhammaṃ paccayā akusalo vipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 7Ms 40P23_26Nakusalaṃ navipākadhammadhammañca naakusalaṃ navipākadhammadhammañca dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo vipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 8Ms 40P23_27Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 9Ms 40P23_28Msdiv 6Nakusalaṃ nanevavipākanavipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 10Ms 40P23_29Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
