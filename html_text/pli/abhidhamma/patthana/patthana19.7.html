19.7. Pītittika

SC 1Ms 40P19_65Msdiv 15Napītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri.

SC 2Ms 40P19_66Nasukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri.

SC 3Ms 40P19_67Naupekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca upekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naupekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P19_68Hetuyā aṭṭhavīsa … pe … avigate aṭṭhavīsa.
