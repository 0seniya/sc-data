18.6. Hetuduka, Saññojanagocchakādi

SC 1Ms 40P18_52Msdiv 21Hetuṃ saññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nosaññojano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P18_53Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P18_54Msdiv 22Hetuṃ ganthaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nogantho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P18_55Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.

SC 5Ms 40P18_56Msdiv 23Hetuṃ oghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu noogho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P18_57Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 7Ms 40P18_58Msdiv 24Hetuṃ yogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu noyogo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 8Ms 40P18_59Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 9Ms 40P18_60Msdiv 25Hetuṃ nīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nonīvaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 10Ms 40P18_61Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 11Ms 40P18_62Msdiv 26Nahetuṃ parāmāsaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu noparāmāso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 40P18_63Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
