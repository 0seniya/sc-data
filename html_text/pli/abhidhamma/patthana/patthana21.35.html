21.35. Hetuduka, Ajjhattattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_199Msdiv 82Nahetu naajjhatto dhammo hetussa ajjhattassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P21_200Nahetu nabahiddhā dhammo hetussa bahiddhā dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_201(Ajjhattattiko na labbhati paṭiccavārādīsu.)
