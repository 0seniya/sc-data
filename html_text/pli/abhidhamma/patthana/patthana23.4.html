23.4. Kusalattika, Saṃkiliṭṭhattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_33Msdiv 8Nakusalaṃ nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Akusalāneva tīṇi. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P23_34Nakusalaṃ naasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 3Ms 40P23_35Nakusalaṃ naasaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha … pe … āsevane tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
