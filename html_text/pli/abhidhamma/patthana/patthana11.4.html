11.4. Kusalattika, Saṃkiliṭṭhattika

SC 1Ms 40P11_42SC 1Ms 40P11_42Msdiv 15Nakusalaṃ nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P11_43Hetuyā ekavīsa, ārammaṇe sattarasa … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate ekavīsa.

SC 3Ms 40P11_44SC 3Ms 40P11_44Msdiv 16Nakusalaṃ naasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P11_45Hetuyā catuvīsa … pe … āsevane sattarasa … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate catuvīsa.
