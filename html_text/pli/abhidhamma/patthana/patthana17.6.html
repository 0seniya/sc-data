17.6. Kusalattika, Pītittika

SC 1Ms 40P17_44Msdiv 18Kusalaṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate terasa.

SC 2Ms 40P17_45Kusalaṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate terasa.

SC 3Ms 40P17_46Kusalaṃ upekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā terasa, ārammaṇe nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate terasa.
