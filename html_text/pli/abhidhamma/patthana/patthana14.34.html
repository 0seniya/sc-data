14.34. Niyyānikaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_157Msdiv 49Niyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P14_158Aniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 3Ms 40P14_159Niyyānikañca aniyyānikañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P14_160Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.
