7.21. Ajjhattārammaṇattika

SC 1Ms 40P7_77Msdiv 35Naajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (2)

SC 2Ms 40P7_78Nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P7_79Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
