21.2. Sahetukadukādi, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_6SC 1Ms 40P21_6Msdiv 3Nasahetukaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sahetuko kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P21_7Nasahetukaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sahetuko akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_8SC 3Ms 40P21_8Msdiv 4Nasahetukaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naahetukaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasahetukaṃ naabyākatañca naahetukaṃ naabyākatañca dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P21_9Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P21_10SC 5Ms 40P21_10Msdiv 5Nahetusampayuttaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā … pe … hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P21_11SC 6Ms 40P21_11Msdiv 6Nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo dhammo sahetukassa ceva na ca hetussa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa kusalassa ca sahetukassa ceva na ca hetussa kusalassa ca dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 7SC 7Ms 40P21_12Naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu nakusalo dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 8SC 8Ms 40P21_13Nahetu ceva naahetuko nakusalo ca naahetuko ceva nanahetu nakusalo ca dhammā hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava pañhā.)

SC 9SC 9Ms 40P21_14Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye nava.

SC 10Ms 40P21_15SC 10Ms 40P21_15Msdiv 7Nahetu ceva naahetuko ca naakusalo dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Nava pañhā.) Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye nava.

SC 11SC 11Ms 40P21_16Nahetu ceva naahetuko ca naabyākato dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Nava pañhā.) Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā anantare samanantare upanissaye nava, kamme tīṇi, natthiyā vigate nava.
