24.19. Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

SC 1Ms 40P24_150Msdiv 67Nasaraṇo nasappaccayo dhammo araṇassa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P24_151Msdiv 68Nasaraṇo nasaṅkhato dhammo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
