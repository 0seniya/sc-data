SC 1Ms 40P7_34Msdiv 16Nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pe ….
