18.17. Āsavasampayuttaduka, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P18_130Msdiv 55Āsavasampayuttaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P18_131Āsavavippayuttaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P18_132Āsavasampayuttaṃ hetuñca āsavavippayuttaṃ hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.

SC 4Ms 40P18_133Āsavasampayuttaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P18_134Āsavavippayuttaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.
