15.80. Saraṇaduka, Sekkhattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_253SC 1Ms 40P15_253Msdiv 92Araṇaṃ sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P15_254Araṇaṃ asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P15_255Araṇaṃ nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasaraṇo nanevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
