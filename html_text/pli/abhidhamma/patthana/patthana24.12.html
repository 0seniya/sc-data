24.12. Āsavagocchaka, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P24_127Msdiv 58Noāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P24_128Nanoāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P24_129Noāsavaṃ nahetuñca nanoāsavaṃ nahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 4Ms 40P24_130Noāsavaṃ nanahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 5Ms 40P24_131Nanoāsavaṃ nanahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 6Ms 40P24_132Noāsavaṃ nanahetuñca nanoāsavaṃ nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
