23.8. Kusalattika, Dassanahetuttika

SC 1Ms 40P23_45Msdiv 12Nakusalaṃ nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P23_46Nakusalaṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P23_47Nakusalaṃ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … sahajāte nava … pe … nissaye nava … pe … kamme … pe … magge nava … pe … avigate nava.
