23.17. Kusalattika, Atītattikadvaya

SC 1Ms 40P23_71Msdiv 21Nakusalo napaccuppanno dhammo kusalassa paccuppannassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P23_72Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā anantare samanantare soḷasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, natthiyā vigate soḷasa.

SC 3Ms 40P23_73Msdiv 22Nakusalaṃ naatītārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato atītārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P23_74Nakusalaṃ naanāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 5Ms 40P23_75Nakusalaṃ napaccuppannārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato paccuppannārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
