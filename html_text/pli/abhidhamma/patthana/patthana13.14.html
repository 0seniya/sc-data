13.14. Hīnattika

SC 1Ms 40P13_113Msdiv 42Hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahīno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikatikasadisaṃ.)
