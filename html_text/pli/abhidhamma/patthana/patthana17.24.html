17.24. Saṃkiliṭṭhattika, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_112Msdiv 41Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P17_113Asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 3Ms 40P17_114Saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P17_115Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naasaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
