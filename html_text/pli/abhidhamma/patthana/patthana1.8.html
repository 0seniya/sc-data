1.8.7.3. Paccayānulomapaccanīya

Duka

SC 144Ms 37P1_783Msdiv 51Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe dasa, naadhipatiyā dasa, naanantare nasamanantare naupanissaye napurejāte napacchājāte naāsevane nakamme navipāke naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge navippayutte nonatthiyā novigate sabbattha dasa.

SC 145Ms 37P1_784Anulomapaccanīyaṃ anumajjantena gaṇetabbaṃ.

1.8.7.4. Paccayapaccanīyānuloma

Duka

SC 147Ms 37P1_786Msdiv 52Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe soḷasa, adhipatiyā anantare samanantare soḷasa, sahajāte dasa, aññamaññe dasa, nissaye dasa, upanissaye soḷasa, āsevane dasa, kamme soḷasa, vipāke dasa, āhāre dasa, indriye dasa, jhāne dasa, magge dasa, sampayutte dasa, atthiyā dasa, natthiyā soḷasa, vigate soḷasa, avigate dasa.

SC 148Ms 37P1_787Paccanīyānulomaṃ anumajjantena gaṇetabbaṃ.
