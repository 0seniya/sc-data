22.23. Hīnattikādi, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P22_117Msdiv 49Nahīnaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca majjhimo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P22_118Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke cha … pe … avigate nava.

SC 3Ms 40P22_119Msdiv 50Namicchattaniyataṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sammattaniyato hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P22_120Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 5Ms 40P22_121Msdiv 51Namaggārammaṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca maggārammaṇo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P22_122Hetuyā chabbīsa … pe … āsevane pannarasa … pe … avigate chabbīsa.

SC 7Ms 40P22_123Msdiv 52Naanuppanno nanahetu dhammo uppannassa nahetussa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. Nauppādī nanahetu dhammo uppannassa nahetussa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. Naanuppanno nanahetu ca nauppādī nanahetu ca dhammā uppannassa nahetussa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 8Ms 40P22_124Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā tīṇi, sahajāte aññamaññe nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye cha, pacchājāte vipāke indriye magge sampayutte vippayutte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
