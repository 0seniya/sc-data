21.14. Upādānadukādi, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_101Msdiv 42Naupādānaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā upādāno akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
