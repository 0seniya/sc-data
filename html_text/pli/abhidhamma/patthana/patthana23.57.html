23.57. Sanidassanattika, Atītārammaṇattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_205Msdiv 66Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naatītārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho atītārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P23_206Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naanāgatārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho anāgatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P23_207Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ napaccuppannārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho paccuppannārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
