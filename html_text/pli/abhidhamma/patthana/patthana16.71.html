16.71. Sanidassanattika, Cittasahabhūduka

SC 1Ms 40P16_283SC 1Ms 40P16_283Msdiv 117Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ cittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
