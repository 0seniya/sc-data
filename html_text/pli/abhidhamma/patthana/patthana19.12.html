19.12. Parittattikādi

SC 1Ms 40P19_79SC 1Ms 40P19_79Msdiv 20Naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
