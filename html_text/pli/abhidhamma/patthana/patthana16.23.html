16.23. Kusalattika, Ajjhattikadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P16_121SC 1Ms 40P16_121Msdiv 43Kusalaṃ ajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ, cittadukasadisaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P16_122Kusalaṃ bāhiraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nabāhiro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P16_123Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 4Ms 40P16_124SC 4Ms 40P16_124Msdiv 44Abyākataṃ upādā dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5SC 5Ms 40P16_125Kusalaṃ noupādā dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nanoupādā dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pannarasa, adhipatiyā pannarasa … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pannarasa.

SC 6Ms 40P16_126SC 6Ms 40P16_126Msdiv 45Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo naupādinno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Abyākataṃ upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naupādinno ca naakusalo naupādinno ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 7SC 7Ms 40P16_127Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
