24.16. Saraṇaduka, Hetusampayuttaduka

SC 1Ms 40P24_140SC 1Ms 40P24_140Msdiv 63Nasaraṇaṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo hetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P24_141Hetuyā dve … pe … purejāte āsevane ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 3Ms 40P24_142SC 3Ms 40P24_142Msdiv 64Nasaraṇaṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nahetuvippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo hetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P24_143Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte cattāri … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.
