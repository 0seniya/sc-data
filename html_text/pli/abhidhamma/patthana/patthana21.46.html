21.46. Āsavaduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_245Msdiv 94Naāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P21_246Nanoāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_247Noāsavaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Purimanayena tīṇi pañhā.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P21_248Noāsavaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
