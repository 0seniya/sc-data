21.31. Hetuduka, Maggārammaṇattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_189SC 1Ms 40P21_189Msdiv 78Nahetuṃ namaggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu maggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
