14.5. Hetuhetusampayuttaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_23Msdiv 7Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P14_24Hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 3Ms 40P14_25Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu dhammo ca uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
