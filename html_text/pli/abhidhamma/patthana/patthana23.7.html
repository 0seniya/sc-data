23.7. Kusalattika, Dassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_42Msdiv 11Nakusalaṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P23_43Nakusalaṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P23_44Nakusalaṃ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
