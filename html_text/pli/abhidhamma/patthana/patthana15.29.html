15.29. Rūpīduka, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_109SC 1Ms 40P15_109Msdiv 38Arūpiṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naarūpī nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
