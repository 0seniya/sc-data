9.16. Saraṇaduka, Vedanāttikādi

Hetu

SC 1Ms 40P9_209Msdiv 86Nasaraṇaṃ nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … nasaraṇaṃ nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … nasaraṇaṃ naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P9_210Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.
