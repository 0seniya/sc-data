17.10. Kusalattika, Sekkhattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_57SC 1Ms 40P17_57Msdiv 22Kusalaṃ sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Dve mūlāni.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P17_58Abyākataṃ asekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P17_59Abyākataṃ nevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … āsevane tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate pañca.
