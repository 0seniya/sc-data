17.29. Sanidassanattika, Vipākattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_129Msdiv 46Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

SC 2Ms 40P17_130Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ vipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho navipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 3Ms 40P17_131Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ nevavipākanavipākadhammadhammaṃ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nanevavipākanavipākadhammadhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
