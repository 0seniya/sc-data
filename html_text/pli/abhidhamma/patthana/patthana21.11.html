21.11. Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_57SC 1Ms 40P21_57Msdiv 21Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā āsavavippayutto anāsavo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā āsavavippayutto sāsavo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P21_58Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā āsavavippayutto sāsavo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P21_59Āsavavippayuttaṃ nasāsavaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto sāsavo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto sāsavo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, adhipatiyā dve … pe … avigate dve.
