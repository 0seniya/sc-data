14.20. Parāmāsaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_79Msdiv 23Parāmāsaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noparāmāso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

SC 2Ms 40P14_80Hetuyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca. (Āsavagocchakasadisaṃ.)
