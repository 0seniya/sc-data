18.25. Saraṇaduka, Hetudukādi

SC 1Ms 40P18_194Msdiv 84Saraṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P18_195Araṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 3Ms 40P18_196Saraṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 4Ms 40P18_197Araṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 5Ms 40P18_198Msdiv 85Saraṇaṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 6Ms 40P18_199Araṇaṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā dve … pe … sahajāte cattāri, aññamaññe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 7Ms 40P18_200Saraṇaṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 8Ms 40P18_201Araṇaṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … purejāte āsevane ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 9Ms 40P18_202Msdiv 86Saraṇaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. Araṇaṃ hetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 10Ms 40P18_203Hetuyā dve, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā dve … pe … sahajāte cattāri, aññamaññe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri. (Sahetukadukasadisaṃ.)

SC 11Ms 40P18_204Msdiv 87Saraṇaṃ hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 12Ms 40P18_205Araṇaṃ hetuñceva sahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu ceva naahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 13Ms 40P18_206Saraṇaṃ sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 14Ms 40P18_207Araṇaṃ sahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 15Ms 40P18_208Msdiv 88Saraṇaṃ hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Hetusahetukadukasadisaṃ.)

SC 16Ms 40P18_209Msdiv 89Saraṇaṃ nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 17Ms 40P18_210Araṇaṃ nahetuṃ sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu nasahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, adhipatiyā dve … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 18Ms 40P18_211Araṇaṃ nahetuṃ ahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nahetu naahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
