15.7. Hetuduka, Pītittika

SC 1Ms 40P15_41SC 1Ms 40P15_41Msdiv 15Hetuṃ pītisahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu napītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P15_42Hetuṃ sukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P15_43Hetuṃ upekkhāsahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naupekkhāsahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
