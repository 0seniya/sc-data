21.38. Sahetukadukādi, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_211SC 1Ms 40P21_211Msdiv 86Nasahetukaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P21_212Naahetukaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Gaṇitakena tīṇi pañhā. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P21_213(Nasahetukanaanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi tīṇiyeva.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P21_214Nasahetukaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ahetuko anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 5SC 5Ms 40P21_215(Nahetusampayuttaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ sahetukadukasadisaṃ. Tīṇi pañhā. Hetusahetukaduke ca hetuhetusampayuttaduke ca pañhā no labbhati.)
