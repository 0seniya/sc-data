24.21. Saraṇaduka, Mahantaradukādi

SC 1Ms 40P24_175Msdiv 81Nasaraṇaṃ nasārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P24_176Nasaraṇaṃ naanārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ naanārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve, adhipatiyā dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.

SC 3Ms 40P24_177Msdiv 82Nasaraṇaṃ nacittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo citto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naaraṇaṃ nacittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca saraṇo citto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate dve.
