23.42. Sanidassanattika, Upādinnattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_162SC 1Ms 40P23_162Msdiv 51Nasanidassanasappaṭigho naupādinnupādāniyo dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa upādinnupādāniyassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe cha, anantare samanantare tīṇi, upanissaye purejāte cha, kamme āhāre indriye tīṇi, atthiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P23_163Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naanupādinnupādāniyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho anupādinnupādāniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … avigate ekavīsa.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P23_164Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naanupādinnaanupādāniyaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho anupādinnaanupādāniyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
