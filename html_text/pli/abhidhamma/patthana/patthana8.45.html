8.45. Ajjhattikaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_196SC 1Ms 40P8_196Msdiv 64Naajjhattikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P8_197Nabāhiraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabāhiro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ paṭicca ajjhattikaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P8_198Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe pañca … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
