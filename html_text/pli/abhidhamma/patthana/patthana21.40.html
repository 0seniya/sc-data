21.40. Sappaccayadukādi, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_220Msdiv 88Naappaccayaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaccayo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P21_221Naappaccayaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaccayo anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P21_222(Naappaccayanaanidassanaappaṭighamūlepi ekameva.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 4Ms 40P21_223(Nasaṅkhataṃ nasappaccayasadisaṃ.)
