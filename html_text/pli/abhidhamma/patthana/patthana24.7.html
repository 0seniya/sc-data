24.7. Hetuduka, Upādānagocchaka

SC 1Ms 40P24_88Msdiv 40Nahetuṃ naupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu upādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P24_89Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
