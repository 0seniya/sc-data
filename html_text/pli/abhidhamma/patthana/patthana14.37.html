14.37. Saraṇaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_169Msdiv 52Saraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Saraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Saraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo ca naaraṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P14_170Araṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 3Ms 40P14_171Saraṇañca araṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 4Ms 40P14_172Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.

Paccanīya

Nahetu

SC 5Ms 40P14_173Msdiv 53Saraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (1)

SC 6Ms 40P14_174Araṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanaaraṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 7Ms 40P14_175Nahetuyā dve, na ārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā pañca … pe … napurejāte cattāri … pe … nakamme dve … pe … naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge ekaṃ … pe … navippayutte dve … pe … novigate tīṇi.

SC 8Ms 40P14_176(Sahajātavārampi paccayavārampi nissayavārampi saṃsaṭṭhavārampi sampayuttavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

SC 9Ms 40P14_177Msdiv 54Saraṇo dhammo nasaraṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo naaraṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo nasaraṇassa ca naaraṇassa ca dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (3)

SC 10Ms 40P14_178Araṇo dhammo nasaraṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

SC 11Ms 40P14_179Msdiv 55Saraṇo dhammo nasaraṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo naaraṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 40P14_180Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe cattāri, adhipatiyā pañca, anantare cattāri … pe … sahajāte pañca, aññamaññe dve, nissaye satta, upanissaye cattāri, purejāte pacchājāte āsevane dve, kamme cattāri, vipāke ekaṃ, āhāre … pe … magge cattāri, sampayutte dve, vippayutte tīṇi … pe … avigate satta.

SC 13Ms 40P14_181Msdiv 56Saraṇo dhammo nasaraṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo, sahajātapaccayena paccayo, upanissayapaccayena paccayo, pacchājātapaccayena paccayo, kammapaccayena paccayo.

SC 14Ms 40P14_182Saraṇo dhammo naaraṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo, sahajātapaccayena paccayo, upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 15Ms 40P14_183Msdiv 57Nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta, naadhipatiyā satta, naanantare satta … pe … nasahajāte pañca, naaññamaññe nanissaye pañca … pe … napurejāte cha … pe … nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte cattāri … pe … noavigate cattāri.

SC 16Ms 40P14_184Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe cattāri. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 17Ms 40P14_185Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe cattāri. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 18Ms 40P14_186(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṃ evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
