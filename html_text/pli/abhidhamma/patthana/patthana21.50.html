21.50. Āsavavippayuttasāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_262SC 1Ms 40P21_262Msdiv 98Āsavavippayuttaṃ nasāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P21_263Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Gaṇitakena tīṇi. Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P21_264(Āsavavippayuttanasāsavanaanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi purimanayeneva tīṇi pañhā.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P21_265Āsavavippayuttaṃ naanāsavaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsavavippayutto sāsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
