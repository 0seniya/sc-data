24.18. Saraṇaduka, Nahetusahetukaduka

SC 1Ms 40P24_147SC 1Ms 40P24_147Msdiv 66Nasaraṇaṃ nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca araṇo nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
