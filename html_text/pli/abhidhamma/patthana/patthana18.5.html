18.5. Hetuduka, Āsavagocchaka

SC 1Ms 40P18_39Msdiv 15Hetuṃ āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 2Ms 40P18_40Hetuṃ noāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nanoāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 3Ms 40P18_41Msdiv 16Nahetuṃ sāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanahetu nasāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P18_42Hetuṃ anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P18_43Msdiv 17Hetuṃ āsavasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 6Ms 40P18_44Hetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naāsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 7Ms 40P18_45Msdiv 18Hetuṃ āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 8Ms 40P18_46Hetuṃ sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naanāsavo ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 9Ms 40P18_47Msdiv 19Hetuṃ āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 10Ms 40P18_48Nahetuṃ āsavasampayuttañceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naāsavavippayutto ceva nanoāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 11Ms 40P18_49Msdiv 20Nahetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ sāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanahetu āsavavippayutto nasāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Lokiyasadisaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 40P18_50Hetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavañca nahetuṃ āsavavippayuttaṃ anāsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu āsavavippayutto naanāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 13Ms 40P18_51Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
