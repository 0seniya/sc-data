17.26. Pītittika, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P17_119SC 1Ms 40P17_119Msdiv 43Pītisahagataṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca napītisahagato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P17_120Sukhasahagataṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhasahagato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P17_121Upekkhāsahagataṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupekkhāsahagato nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P17_122Hetuyā ekavīsati, adhipatiyā ekavīsati … pe … avigate ekavīsati. (Vedanāttikasadisaṃ. Saṃkhittaṃ.)
