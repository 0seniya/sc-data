8.32. Oghadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P8_150Msdiv 46Nooghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … noyogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe …. (Oghagocchakayogagocchakesu āmasanaṃ āsavagocchake āmasanasadisaṃ.)

SC 2Ms 40P8_151Msdiv 47Nonīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe …. (Nīvaraṇagocchake āmasanaṃ saññojanagocchake āmasanasadisaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P8_152Nīvaraṇagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
