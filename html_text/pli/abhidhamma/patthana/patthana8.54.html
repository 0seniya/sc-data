8.54. Kilesasaṃkiliṭṭhaduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_230Msdiv 73Nakilesañceva naasaṃkiliṭṭhañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nakileso ceva naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P8_231Naasaṃkiliṭṭhañceva nano ca kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkiliṭṭho ceva nano ca kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P8_232Nakilesañceva naasaṃkiliṭṭhañca naasaṃkiliṭṭhañceva nano ca kilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nakileso ceva naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_233Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
