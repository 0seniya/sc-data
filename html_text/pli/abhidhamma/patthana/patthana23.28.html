23.28. Ācayagāmittika, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P23_118Msdiv 37Naācayagāmiṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ācayagāmī kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

SC 2Ms 40P23_119Naācayagāmiṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā ācayagāmī akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P23_120Naācayagāmiṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nevācayagāmināpacayagāmī abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
