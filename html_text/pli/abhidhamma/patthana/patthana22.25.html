22.25. Ajjhattattikadvaya, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P22_129Msdiv 55Naajjhattaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhā hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

SC 2Ms 40P22_130Nabahiddhā nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P22_131Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

SC 4Ms 40P22_132Msdiv 56Naajjhattārammaṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 5Ms 40P22_133Nabahiddhārammaṇaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhārammaṇo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P22_134Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā dve … pe … purejāte āsevane dve … pe … avigate cha.
