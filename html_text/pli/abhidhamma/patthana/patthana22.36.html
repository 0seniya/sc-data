22.36. Sanidassanattika, Saraṇaduka

SC 1Ms 40P22_251Msdiv 114Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho saraṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P22_252Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 3Ms 40P22_253Naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 4Ms 40P22_254Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naaraṇañca naanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naaraṇañca dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho araṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 5Ms 40P22_255Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa … pe … avigate ekavīsa.

SC 6Ms 40P22_256(Sahajātavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

22.36.1. Hetu-ārammaṇādi

SC 7Ms 40P22_257Msdiv 115Nasanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo … satta.

SC 8Ms 40P22_258Naanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo … satta.

SC 9Ms 40P22_259Nasanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo … satta.

SC 10Ms 40P22_260Msdiv 116Nasanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Naanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Nasanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naaraṇo ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa araṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 11Ms 40P22_261Msdiv 117Hetuyā ekavīsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā ekavīsa, anantare tīṇi … pe … sahajāte nissaye ekavīsa, upanissaye tīṇi, pacchājāte kamme āhāre indriye jhāne magge vippayutte ekavīsa … pe … avigate ekavīsa.

SC 12Ms 40P22_262(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
