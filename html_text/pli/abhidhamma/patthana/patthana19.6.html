19.6. Vitakkattika

SC 1Ms 40P19_61SC 1Ms 40P19_61Msdiv 14Nasavitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca savitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasavitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca avitakkavicāramatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasavitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca avitakkaavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P19_62Naavitakkavicāramattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca avitakkavicāramatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P19_63Naavitakkaavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca avitakkaavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P19_64Hetuyā ekūnapaññāsa, ārammaṇe tiṃsa, adhipatiyā ekūnatiṃsa … pe … purejāte āsevane ekūnatiṃsa … pe … avigate ekūnapaññāsa.
