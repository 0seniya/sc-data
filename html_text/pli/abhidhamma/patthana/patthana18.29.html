18.29. Saraṇaduka, Dassanenapahātabbadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P18_224SC 1Ms 40P18_224Msdiv 96Saraṇaṃ dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P18_225Araṇaṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naaraṇo nanadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P18_226SC 3Ms 40P18_226Msdiv 97Araṇaṃ sauttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasaraṇo nasauttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P18_227Araṇaṃ anuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naanuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 5SC 5Ms 40P18_228(Sahajātavārampi … pe … pañhāvārampi vitthāretabbaṃ.)

SC 6SC 6Ms 40P18_229Dhammānulomapaccanīye dukadukapaṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 7SC 7Ms 40P18_230Anulomapaccanīyapaṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
