10.3. Kusalattika, Hetusampayuttadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P10_29SC 1Ms 40P10_29Msdiv 10Nakusalaṃ nahetusampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P10_30Hetuyā pannarasa, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … vipāke nava … pe … avigate pannarasa.
