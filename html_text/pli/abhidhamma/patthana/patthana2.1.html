SC 540Ms 38P2_540Msdiv 200Ārammaṇapaccayā nahetuyā cattāri, naadhipatiyā cattāri, naanantare cattāri (sabbattha cattāri), nonatthiyā cattāri, novigate cattāri, noavigate cattāri. (Evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)

SC 541Ms 38P2_541Anulomapaccanīyaṃ.
