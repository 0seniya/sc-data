21.37. Hetuduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_205Msdiv 84Nahetuṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P21_206Nanahetuṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 3Ms 40P21_207Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P21_208(Nahetunaanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi tīṇiyeva.)

SC 5Ms 40P21_209Msdiv 85Nahetuṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P21_210Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
