15.1. Hetuduka, Kusalattika

15.1.1. Kusalapada

SC 1Ms 40P15_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2Ms 40P15_2Msdiv 1Hetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuṃ kusalañca nahetuṃ kusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi (cittasamuṭṭhānameva, ārammaṇaṃ natthi.)

SC 3Ms 40P15_3Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P15_4(Sahajātavārampi … pe … nissayavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

SC 5Ms 40P15_5Msdiv 2Hetu kusalo dhammo nahetussa nakusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

SC 6Ms 40P15_6Hetu kusalo dhammo nahetussa nakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 7Ms 40P15_7Nahetu kusalo dhammo nanahetussa nakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 8Ms 40P15_8Hetu kusalo ca nahetu kusalo ca dhammā nahetussa nakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 9Ms 40P15_9Msdiv 3Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare samanantare nava, sahajāte nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme ekaṃ, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye tīṇi, jhāne ekaṃ, magge tīṇi, vippayutte tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi. (Pañhāvārampi vitthāretabbaṃ.)

15.1.2. Akusalapada

SC 10Ms 40P15_10Msdiv 4Hetuṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 11Ms 40P15_11Nahetuṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 12Ms 40P15_12Hetuṃ akusalañca nahetuṃ akusalañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 13Ms 40P15_13(Sahajātavārampi … pe … nissayavārampi paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.)

SC 14Ms 40P15_14Msdiv 5Hetu akusalo dhammo nahetussa naakusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

SC 15Ms 40P15_15Hetu akusalo dhammo nahetussa naakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 16Ms 40P15_16Nahetu akusalo dhammo nanahetussa naakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi.

SC 17Ms 40P15_17Hetu akusalo ca nahetu akusalo ca dhammā nahetussa naakusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 18Ms 40P15_18Msdiv 6Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā ekaṃ, anantare samanantare nava, sahajāte nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme ekaṃ, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye tīṇi, jhāne ekaṃ, magge tīṇi, vippayutte tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

15.1.3. Abyākatapada

SC 19Ms 40P15_19Msdiv 7Nahetuṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi. (Nissayavārampi … pe … pañhāvārampi vitthāretabbaṃ.)
