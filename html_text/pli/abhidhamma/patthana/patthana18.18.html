18.18. Āsavasāsavaduka, Hetuduka

SC 1Ms 40P18_135Msdiv 56Āsavañceva sāsavañca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P18_136Sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P18_137Āsavañceva sāsavaṃ hetuñca sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṃ hetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naanāsavo ca nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 4Ms 40P18_138Āsavañceva sāsavañca nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanāsavo ceva nanoāsavo ca nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
