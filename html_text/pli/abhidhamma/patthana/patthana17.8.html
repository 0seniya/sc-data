17.8. Kusalattika, Dassanahetuttika

SC 1Ms 40P17_50Msdiv 20Akusalaṃ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P17_51Akusalaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P17_52Akusalaṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P17_53Abyākataṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
