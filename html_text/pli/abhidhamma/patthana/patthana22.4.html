22.4. Kusalattika, Hetusahetukadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P22_20Msdiv 6Nakusalaṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṃ nahetuñceva naahetukañca dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … dve.

SC 2Ms 40P22_21(Naakusale dve, naabyākate dve. Paṭhamaṃ gaṇitakena ekaṃ, dutiyaṃ gaṇitakena ekaṃ, tatiyaṃ gaṇitakena ekaṃ, sabbattha nava pañhā.)

SC 3Ms 40P22_22Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 4Ms 40P22_23Nakusalaṃ naahetukañceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 5Ms 40P22_24Msdiv 7Nakusalaṃ nahetuñceva nahetuvippayuttañca dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo hetu ceva hetusampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava. (Hetusahetukasadisaṃ.)

SC 6Ms 40P22_25Nakusalaṃ nahetuvippayuttañceva nanahetuñca dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo hetusampayutto ceva na ca hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

SC 7Ms 40P22_26Msdiv 8Nakusalaṃ nahetuṃ nasahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 8Ms 40P22_27Msdiv 9Nakusalaṃ nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca akusalo nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 9Ms 40P22_28Naakusalaṃ nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 10Ms 40P22_29Naabyākataṃ nahetuṃ naahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Gaṇitakena tīṇi pañhā.)

SC 11Ms 40P22_30Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
