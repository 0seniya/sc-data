18.8. Hetuduka, Dassanenapahātabbadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P18_86Msdiv 38Hetuṃ dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P18_87Nahetuṃ nadassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanahetu nanadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P18_88Msdiv 39Hetuṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P18_89Nahetuṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanahetu nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 5Ms 40P18_90Msdiv 40Hetuṃ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 6Ms 40P18_91Nahetuṃ nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nanadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 7Ms 40P18_92Msdiv 41Hetuṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte pañca … pe … avigate pañca.

SC 8Ms 40P18_93Nahetuṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanahetu nanabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
