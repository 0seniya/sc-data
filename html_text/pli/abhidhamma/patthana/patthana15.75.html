15.75. Saraṇaduka, Vitakkattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_232Msdiv 86Saraṇaṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nasavitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P15_233Araṇaṃ savitakkasavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo nasavitakkasavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P15_234Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 4Ms 40P15_235Msdiv 87Saraṇaṃ avitakkavicāramattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naavitakkavicāramatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate cattāri.

SC 5Ms 40P15_236Araṇaṃ avitakkaavicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naavitakkaavicāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
