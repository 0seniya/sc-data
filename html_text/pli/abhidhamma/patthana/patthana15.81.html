15.81. Saraṇaduka, Parittattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_256Msdiv 93Araṇaṃ parittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P15_257Araṇaṃ mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo namahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P15_258Araṇaṃ appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naappamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
