15.38. Sārammaṇaduka, Kusalattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_139Msdiv 47Sārammaṇaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P15_140Sārammaṇaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasārammaṇo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

SC 3Ms 40P15_141Anārammaṇaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naanārammaṇo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
