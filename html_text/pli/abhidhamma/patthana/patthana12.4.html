12.4. Hetuduka, Saññojanadukādi

SC 1Ms 40P12_48SC 1Ms 40P12_48Msdiv 10Nahetuṃ nosaññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … nahetuṃ noganthaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava.)

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P12_49Nahetuṃ nooghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … nahetuṃ noyogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P12_50Nahetuṃ nonīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava.)

SC 4SC 4Ms 40P12_51Nahetuṃ noparāmāsaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Sabbattha nava. Vipākaṃ natthi.)
