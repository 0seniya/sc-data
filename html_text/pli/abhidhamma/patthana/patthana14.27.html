14.27. Bhāvanāyapahātabbaduka

SC 1Ms 40P14_117Msdiv 36Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

SC 2Ms 40P14_118Nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 3Ms 40P14_119Bhāvanāya pahātabbañca nabhāvanāya pahātabbañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

SC 4Ms 40P14_120Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca.
