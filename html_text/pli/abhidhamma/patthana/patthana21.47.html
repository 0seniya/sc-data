21.47. Sāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P21_249Msdiv 95Nasāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

SC 2Ms 40P21_250Naanāsavaṃ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P21_251(Nasāsavaṃ naanidassanasappaṭighamūlassa purimanayena tīṇi pañhā.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 4Ms 40P21_252Naanāsavaṃ naanidassanaappaṭighaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sāsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
