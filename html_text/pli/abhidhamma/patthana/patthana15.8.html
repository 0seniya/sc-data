15.8. Hetuduka, Dassanattika

SC 1Ms 40P15_44SC 1Ms 40P15_44Msdiv 16Hetuṃ dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 2SC 2Ms 40P15_45Hetuṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

SC 3SC 3Ms 40P15_46Nahetuṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanahetu nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
