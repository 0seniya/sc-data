8.65. Sukhasahagataduka

SC 1Ms 40P8_274Msdiv 84Nasukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 2Ms 40P8_275Nanasukhasahagataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

SC 3Ms 40P8_276Nasukhasahagatañca nanasukhasahagatañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

SC 4Ms 40P8_277Hetuyā nava … pe … purejāte āsevane cha … pe … avigate nava.
