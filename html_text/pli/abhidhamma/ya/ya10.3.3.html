10.3.3. Anāgatavāra

10.3.3.1. Anuloma

SC 1Ms 35Y10_4157Msdiv 451» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissati so domanassindriyaṃ pajahissatīti?

SC 2Ms 35Y10_4158Dve puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissanti, no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahissanti. Pañca puggalā cakkhundriyañca parijānissanti domanassindriyañca pajahissanti.

SC 3Ms 35Y10_4159« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ pajahissati so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 4Ms 35Y10_4160» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti?

SC 5Ms 35Y10_4161Cha puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessanti. Ye puthujjanā maggaṃ paṭilabhissanti te cakkhundriyañca parijānissanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvessanti.

SC 6Ms 35Y10_4162« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessati so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 7Ms 35Y10_4163» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissati so aññindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 8Ms 35Y10_4164« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvessati so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 9Ms 35Y10_4165» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissati so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 10Ms 35Y10_4166« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissatīti?

SC 11Ms 35Y10_4167Aggamaggasamaṅgī aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ parijānissati. Satta puggalā aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarissanti cakkhundriyañca parijānissanti. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 35Y10_4168Msdiv 452» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti?

SC 13PTS vp Pali 2.204Ms 35Y10_4169Cattāro puggalā domanassindriyaṃ pajahissanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessanti. Ye puthujjanā maggaṃ paṭilabhissanti te domanassindriyañca pajahissanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvessanti.

SC 14Ms 35Y10_4170« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessati so domanassindriyaṃ pajahissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 15Ms 35Y10_4171» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahissati so aññindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 16Ms 35Y10_4172« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvessati so domanassindriyaṃ pajahissatīti?

SC 17Ms 35Y10_4173Dve puggalā aññindriyaṃ bhāvessanti no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahissanti, puggalā aññindriyaṃ bhāvessanti no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahissanti, pañca puggalā aññindriyañca bhavissanti domanassindriyañca pajahissanti.

SC 18Ms 35Y10_4174» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahissati so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 19Ms 35Y10_4175« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati so domanassindriyaṃ pajahissatīti?

SC 20Ms 35Y10_4176Tayo puggalā aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissanti, no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahissanti. Pañca puggalā aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarissanti domanassindriyañca pajahissanti. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 21Ms 35Y10_4177Msdiv 453» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessati so aññindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 22Ms 35Y10_4178« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvessati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti?

SC 23Ms 35Y10_4179Cha puggalā aññindriyaṃ bhāvessanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessanti. Ye puthujjanā maggaṃ paṭilabhissanti te aññindriyañca bhāvessanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvessanti.

SC 24Ms 35Y10_4180» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessati so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 25Ms 35Y10_4181« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti?

SC 26Ms 35Y10_4182Satta puggalā aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvessanti. Ye puthujjanā maggaṃ paṭilabhissanti te aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarissanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvessanti. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 27Ms 35Y10_4183Msdiv 454» Yo aññindriyaṃ bhāvessati so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 28Ms 35Y10_4184« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati so aññindriyaṃ bhāvessatīti?

SC 29Ms 35Y10_4185Aggamaggasamaṅgī aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati, no ca aññindriyaṃ Aggamaggasamaṅgī aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarissati, no ca aññindriyaṃ bhāvessati. Satta puggalā aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarissanti aññindriyañca bhāvessanti. (Aññindriyamūlakaṃ.)

10.3.3.2. Paccanīka

SC 30Ms 35Y10_4186Msdiv 455» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissati so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 31Ms 35Y10_4187« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissatīti?

SC 32Ms 35Y10_4188Dve puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissanti. Tayo puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti cakkhundriyañca na parijānissanti.

SC 33Ms 35Y10_4189» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 34Ms 35Y10_4190« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissatīti.

SC 35PTS vp Pali 2.205Ms 35Y10_4191Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissanti. Tayo puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti cakkhundriyañca na parijānissanti.

SC 36Ms 35Y10_4192» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissati so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 37Ms 35Y10_4193« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 38Ms 35Y10_4194» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissati so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissatīti?

SC 39Ms 35Y10_4195Aggamaggasamaṅgī cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissati, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissati. Dve puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānissanti aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarissanti.

SC 40Ms 35Y10_4196« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānissatīti? Āmantā. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 41Ms 35Y10_4197Msdiv 456» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti? Āmantā.

SC 42Ms 35Y10_4198« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissatīti?

SC 43Ms 35Y10_4199Cattāro puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti. Pañca puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti.

SC 44Ms 35Y10_4200» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti?

SC 45Ms 35Y10_4201Dve puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti. puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti. Tayo puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti aññindriyañca na bhāvessanti.

SC 46Ms 35Y10_4202« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissatīti? Āmantā.

SC 47Ms 35Y10_4203» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissatīti?

SC 48Ms 35Y10_4204Tayo puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissanti. Dve puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarissanti.

SC 49Ms 35Y10_4205« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissati so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissatīti? Āmantā. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 50Ms 35Y10_4206Msdiv 457» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti?

SC 51Ms 35Y10_4207Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti. Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti. Tayo puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti aññindriyañca na bhāvessanti.

SC 54Ms 35Y10_4210Satta puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissanti. Dve puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarissanti.

SC 55Ms 35Y10_4211« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarissati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti? Āmantā. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)
