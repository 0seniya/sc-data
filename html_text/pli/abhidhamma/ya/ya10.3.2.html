10.3.2. Atītavāra

10.3.2.1. Anuloma

SC 1Ms 35Y10_4099Msdiv 443» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 2Ms 35Y10_4100« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānitthāti?

SC 3Ms 35Y10_4101Dve puggalā domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha. Arahā domanassindriyañca pajahittha cakkhundriyañca parijānittha.

SC 4Ms 35Y10_4102» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 5Ms 35Y10_4103« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānitthāti?

SC 6Ms 35Y10_4104Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha. Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha cakkhundriyañca parijānittha.

SC 7Ms 35Y10_4105» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 8Ms 35Y10_4106« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 9Ms 35Y10_4107» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti?

SC 10Ms 35Y10_4108Yo aggaphalaṃ sacchikaroti so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānittha, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha. Yo aggaphalaṃ sacchākāsi so cakkhundriyañca parijānittha aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarittha.

SC 11Ms 35Y10_4109« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānitthāti? Āmantā. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 12Ms 35Y10_4110Msdiv 444» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 13Ms 35Y10_4111« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahitthāti?

SC 14Ms 35Y10_4112Cattāro puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha. Tayo puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha domanassindriyañca pajahittha.

SC 15Ms 35Y10_4113» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti?

SC 16Ms 35Y10_4114Dve puggalā domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha, no ca aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha. Arahā domanassindriyañca pajahittha aññindriyañca bhāvittha.

SC 17Ms 35Y10_4115« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 18Ms 35Y10_4116» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti?

SC 19Ms 35Y10_4117Tayo puggalā domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha. Arahā domanassindriyañca pajahittha aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarittha.

SC 20Ms 35Y10_4118« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahitthāti? Āmantā. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 21Ms 35Y10_4119Msdiv 445» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti?

SC 22Ms 35Y10_4120Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha. Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha aññindriyañca bhāvittha.

SC 23Ms 35Y10_4121« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 24Ms 35Y10_4122» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti?

SC 25Ms 35Y10_4123Satta puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha. Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarittha.

SC 26Ms 35Y10_4124« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 27Ms 35Y10_4125Msdiv 446» Yo aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti?

SC 28Ms 35Y10_4126Yo aggaphalaṃ sacchikaroti so aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha. Yo aggaphalaṃ sacchākāsi so aññindriyañca bhāvittha aññātāvindriyañca sacchikarittha.

SC 29Ms 35Y10_4127« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā. (Aññindriyamūlakaṃ.)

10.3.2.2. Paccanīka

SC 30Ms 35Y10_4128Msdiv 447» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahitthāti?

SC 31Ms 35Y10_4129Dve puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha. Cha puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha domanassindriyañca nappajahittha.

SC 32Ms 35Y10_4130« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 33Ms 35Y10_4131» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 34Ms 35Y10_4132Cha puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 35Ms 35Y10_4133« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 36Ms 35Y10_4134» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 37Ms 35Y10_4135« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 38Ms 35Y10_4136» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikaritthāti? Āmantā.

SC 39Ms 35Y10_4137« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti?

SC 40Ms 35Y10_4138Yo aggaphalaṃ sacchikaroti so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha. Aṭṭha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 41Ms 35Y10_4139Msdiv 448» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 42Ms 35Y10_4140Cattāro puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 43Ms 35Y10_4141« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 44Ms 35Y10_4142» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 45Ms 35Y10_4143« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahitthāti?

SC 46Ms 35Y10_4144Dve puggalā aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha. Cha puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāvittha domanassindriyañca nappajahittha.

SC 47Ms 35Y10_4145» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikaritthāti? Āmantā.

SC 48Ms 35Y10_4146« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahitthāti?

SC 49Ms 35Y10_4147Tayo puggalā aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha. Cha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha domanassindriyañca nappajahittha. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 50Ms 35Y10_4148Msdiv 449» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 51Ms 35Y10_4149« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 52Ms 35Y10_4150Cha puggalā aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāvittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 55Ms 35Y10_4153Satta puggalā aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)
