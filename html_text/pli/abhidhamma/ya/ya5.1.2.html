5.1.2.1. Padasodhanavāra

5.1.2.1.1. Anuloma

SC 1Ms 33Y5_116Msdiv 10» Dukkhaṃ dukkhasaccanti? Āmantā.

SC 2Ms 33Y5_117« Dukkhasaccaṃ dukkhanti?

SC 3Ms 33Y5_118Kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ ṭhapetvā avasesaṃ dukkhasaccaṃ, na dukkhaṃ. Kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ dukkhañceva dukkhasaccañca.

SC 4Ms 33Y5_119» Samudayo samudayasaccanti?

SC 5Ms 33Y5_120Samudayasaccaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso samudayo, na samudayasaccaṃ. Samudayasaccaṃ samudayo ceva samudayasaccañca.

SC 7Ms 33Y5_122» Nirodho nirodhasaccanti?

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.175Ms 33Y5_123Nirodhasaccaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso nirodho, na nirodhasaccaṃ. Nirodhasaccaṃ nirodho ceva nirodhasaccañca.

SC 9Ms 33Y5_124« Nirodhasaccaṃ nirodhoti? Āmantā.

SC 10Ms 33Y5_125» Maggo maggasaccanti?

SC 11Ms 33Y5_126Maggasaccaṃ ṭhapetvā avaseso maggo, na maggasaccaṃ. Maggasaccaṃ maggo ceva maggasaccañca.

SC 12Ms 33Y5_127« Maggasaccaṃ maggoti? Āmantā.
