10.3.4. Paccuppannātītavāra

10.3.4.1. Anuloma

SC 1Ms 35Y10_4215Msdiv 459» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānāti so domanassindriyaṃ pajahitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 2Ms 35Y10_4216« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānātīti?

SC 3Ms 35Y10_4217Dve puggalā domanassindriyaṃ pajahittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ parijānanti. Aggamaggasamaṅgī domanassindriyañca pajahittha cakkhundriyañca parijānāti.

SC 4Ms 35Y10_4218» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānāti so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 5Ms 35Y10_4219« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānātīti?

SC 6Ms 35Y10_4220Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ parijānanti. Aggamaggasamaṅgī anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha cakkhundriyañca parijānāti.

SC 7Ms 35Y10_4221» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānāti so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? No.

SC 8Ms 35Y10_4222« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānātīti? No.

SC 9Ms 35Y10_4223» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ parijānāti so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti? No.

SC 10Ms 35Y10_4224« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so cakkhundriyaṃ parijānātīti? No. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 11Ms 35Y10_4225Msdiv 460» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? Āmantā.

SC 12Ms 35Y10_4226« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahatīti?

SC 13Ms 35Y10_4227Cha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ pajahanti. Anāgāmimaggasamaṅgī anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca bhāvittha domanassindriyañca pajahati.

SC 14Ms 35Y10_4228» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahati so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? No.

SC 15Ms 35Y10_4229« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahatīti? No.

SC 16Ms 35Y10_4230» Yo domanassindriyaṃ pajahati so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti? No.

SC 17Ms 35Y10_4231« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so domanassindriyaṃ pajahatīti? No. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 18Ms 35Y10_4232Msdiv 461» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāveti so aññindriyaṃ bhāvitthāti? No.

SC 19Ms 35Y10_4233« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ bhāvittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvetīti? No.

SC 20Ms 35Y10_4234» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāveti so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti? No.

SC 21Ms 35Y10_4235« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ bhāvetīti? No. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 22Ms 35Y10_4236Msdiv 462» Yo aññindriyaṃ bhāveti so aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikaritthāti? No.

SC 23Ms 35Y10_4237« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ sacchikarittha so aññindriyaṃ bhāvetīti? No. (Aññindriyamūlakaṃ.)

10.3.4.2. Paccanīka

SC 24Ms 35Y10_4238Msdiv 463» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahitthāti?

SC 25Ms 35Y10_4239Dve puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānanti, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha. puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānanti, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha. Cha puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti domanassindriyañca nappajahittha.

SC 26Ms 35Y10_4240« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ nappajahittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānātīti? Āmantā.

SC 27Ms 35Y10_4241» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 28Ms 35Y10_4242Cha puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 29Ms 35Y10_4243« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānātīti? Āmantā.

SC 30Ms 35Y10_4244» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 31Ms 35Y10_4245Arahā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Satta puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti aññindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 32Ms 35Y10_4246« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānātīti?

SC 33Ms 35Y10_4247Aggamaggasamaṅgī aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti. Satta puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāvittha cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti.

SC 34Ms 35Y10_4248» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikaritthāti?

SC 35Ms 35Y10_4249Arahā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha. Aṭṭha puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha.

SC 36Ms 35Y10_4250« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānātīti?

SC 37Ms 35Y10_4251Aggamaggasamaṅgī aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānāti. Aṭṭha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha cakkhundriyañca na parijānanti. (Cakkhundriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 38Ms 35Y10_4252Msdiv 464» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 39Ms 35Y10_4253Cha puggalā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahanti, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Dve puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahanti anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 40Ms 35Y10_4254« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahatīti? Āmantā.

SC 41Ms 35Y10_4255» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 42Ms 35Y10_4256Arahā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Satta puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahanti aññindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 43Ms 35Y10_4257« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahatīti?

SC 44Ms 35Y10_4258Anāgāmimaggasamaṅgī aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati. Satta puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāvittha domanassindriyañca nappajahanti.

SC 45Ms 35Y10_4259» Yo domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikaritthāti?

SC 46Ms 35Y10_4260Arahā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha. Aṭṭha puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahanti aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha.

SC 47Ms 35Y10_4261« Yo vā pana aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahatīti?

SC 48Ms 35Y10_4262Anāgāmimaggasamaṅgī aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahati. Aṭṭha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha domanassindriyañca nappajahanti. (Domanassindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 49Ms 35Y10_4263Msdiv 465» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti so aññindriyaṃ na bhāvitthāti?

SC 50Ms 35Y10_4264Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha. Satta puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāventi aññindriyañca na bhāvittha.

SC 51Ms 35Y10_4265« Yo vā pana aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvetīti?

SC 52Ms 35Y10_4266Aṭṭhamako aññindriyaṃ na bhāvittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti. Satta puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāvittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāventi.

SC 53Ms 35Y10_4267» Yo anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti so aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikaritthāti?

SC 54Ms 35Y10_4268Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha. Aṭṭha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāventi aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha.

SC 56Ms 35Y10_4270Aṭṭhamako aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāveti. Aṭṭha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāventi. (Anaññātaññassāmītindriyamūlakaṃ.)

SC 58Ms 35Y10_4272Arahā aññindriyaṃ na bhāveti, no ca aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha. Cha puggalā aññindriyañca na bhāventi aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha.

SC 60Ms 35Y10_4274Tayo maggasamaṅgino aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāventi. Tayo maggasamaṅgino aññātāvindriyaṃ na sacchikarittha, no ca aññindriyaṃ na bhāventi. Cha puggalā aññātāvindriyañca na sacchikarittha aññindriyañca na bhāventi. (Aññindriyamūlakaṃ.)
