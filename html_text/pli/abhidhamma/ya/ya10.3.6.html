10.3.6.2. Paccanīka

SC 29Ms 35Y10_4363Msdiv 479» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissatīti?

SC 30Ms 35Y10_4364Pañca puggalā cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha, no ca domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissanti. Cattāro puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti.

SC 31Ms 35Y10_4365« Yo vā pana domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti?

SC 32Ms 35Y10_4366Arahā domanassindriyaṃ nappajahissati, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha. Cattāro puggalā domanassindriyañca nappajahissanti cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha.

SC 33Ms 35Y10_4367» Yo cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha so anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessatīti?

SC 34Ms 35Y10_4368Ye puthujjanā maggaṃ paṭilabhissanti te cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha, no ca anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessanti. Aṭṭha puggalā cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti.

SC 35Ms 35Y10_4369« Yo vā pana anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessati so cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānitthāti?

SC 36Ms 35Y10_4370Arahā anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ na bhāvessati, no ca cakkhundriyaṃ na parijānittha. Aṭṭha puggalā anaññātaññassāmītindriyañca na bhāvessanti cakkhundriyañca na parijānittha.
