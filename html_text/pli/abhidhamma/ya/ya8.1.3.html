8.1.3. Abhidhammacittamissakavisesa

SC 1Ms 34Y8_255SC 1Ms 34Y8_255Msdiv 62» Yassa kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati … pe … yassa akusalaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati … yassa abyākataṃ cittaṃ uppajjati … yassa sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati … ?

SC 2SC 2Ms 34Y8_256(Etena upāyena yāva saraṇaaraṇā uddharitabbā.)

SC 3SC 3Ms 34Y8_257» Yassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati na nirujjhati tassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ nirujjhissati na uppajjissati?

SC 4SC 4Ms 34Y8_258« Yassa vā pana araṇaṃ cittaṃ nirujjhissati na uppajjissati tassa araṇaṃ cittaṃ uppajjati na nirujjhati?

SC 5SC 5Ms 34Y8_259Uddesavāro.
