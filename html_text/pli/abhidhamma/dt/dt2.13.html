2.13. Terasamanaya Asaṅgahitenasampayuttavippayuttapadaniddesa

SC 1Ms 31Dht_548Msdiv 448Rūpakkhandhena ye dhammā khandhasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā āyatanasaṅgahena asaṅgahitā dhātusaṅgahena asaṅgahitā, te dhammā katihi khandhehi katihāyatanehi katihi dhātūhi sampayuttā? Te dhammā tīhi khandhehi sampayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici sampayuttā. Katihi vippayuttā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi dasahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.

Katihi vippayuttā? Ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi dasahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.
