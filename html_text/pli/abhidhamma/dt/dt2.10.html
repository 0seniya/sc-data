Dukkhasaccañca jīvitaṃ;
Saḷāyatanaṃ nāmarūpaṃ,
Cattāro ca mahābhavā.

SC 58Ms 31Dht_500Jāti jarā ca maraṇaṃ,
tikesvekūnavīsati;
Gocchakesu ca paññāsa,
aṭṭha cūḷantare padā.

SC 59Ms 31Dht_501Mahantare pannarasa,
aṭṭhārasa tato pare;
Tevīsa padasataṃ etaṃ,
sampayoge na labbhatīti. [123]

SC 60Ms 31Dht_502Vippayuttena vippayuttapadaniddeso dasamo.
