Dukanipāta

Tatiyavagga

2.23. Sobhitattheragāthā

“Satimā paññavā bhikkhu,
āraddhabalavīriyo;
Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,
ekarattiṃ anussariṃ.

Cattāro satipaṭṭhāne,
satta aṭṭha ca bhāvayaṃ;
Pañca kappasatānāhaṃ,
ekarattiṃ anussarin”ti.

… Sobhito thero ….
