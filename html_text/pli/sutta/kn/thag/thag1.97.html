Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

1.97. Tissattheragāthā

“Hitvā satapalaṃ kaṃsaṃ,
Sovaṇṇaṃ satarājikaṃ;
Aggahiṃ mattikāpattaṃ,
Idaṃ dutiyābhisecanan”ti.

… Tisso thero ….
