Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.28 Jambugāmikaputtattheragāthā

“Kacci no vatthapasuto,
Kacci no bhūsanārato;
Kacci sīlamayaṃ gandhaṃ,
Kiṃ tvaṃ vāyasi netarā pajā”ti.

… Jambugāmikaputto thero ….
