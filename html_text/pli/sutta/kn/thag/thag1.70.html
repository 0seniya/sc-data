Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

1.70. Puṇṇattheragāthā

“Sīlameva idha aggaṃ,
paññavā pana uttamo;
Manussesu ca devesu,
sīlapaññāṇato jayan”ti.

… Puṇṇo thero ….
Vaggo sattamo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Vappo ca vajjiputto ca,
pakkho vimalakoṇḍañño;
Ukkhepakatavaccho ca,
meghiyo ekadhammiko;
Ekudāniyachannā ca,
puṇṇatthero mahabbaloti.
