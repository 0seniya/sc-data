Ekakanipāta

Dutiyavagga

1.11. Cūḷavacchattheragāthā

“Pāmojjabahulo bhikkhu,
dhamme buddhappavedite;
Adhigacche padaṃ santaṃ,
saṅkhārūpasamaṃ sukhan”ti.

… Cūḷavaccho thero ….
