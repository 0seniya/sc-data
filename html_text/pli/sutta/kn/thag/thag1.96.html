Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

1.96. Khaṇ­ḍa­sumanat­thera­gāthā

“Ekapupphaṃ cajitvāna,
asīti vassakoṭiyo;
Saggesu paricāretvā,
sesakenamhi nibbuto”ti.

… Khaṇḍasumano thero ….
