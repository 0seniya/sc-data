Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.29. Hāritattheragāthā

“Samunnamayamattānaṃ,
usukārova tejanaṃ;
Cittaṃ ujuṃ karitvāna,
avijjaṃ bhinda hāritā”ti.

… Hārito thero ….
