Ekakanipāta

Chaṭṭhavagga

1.59. Kosala­vihārit­thera­gāthā

“Saddhāyāhaṃ pabbajito,
araññe me kuṭikā katā;
Appamatto ca ātāpī,
sampajāno patissato”ti.

… Kosala­vihārit­thero ….
