Ayogharacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 94 PTS 2nd ed. 26 Ms 21Cp_294Msdiv 24“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
kāsirājassa atrajo;
Ayogharamhi saṃvaḍḍho,
nāmenāsi ayogharo.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_295Msdiv 25‘Dukkhena jīvito laddho,
saṃpīḷe patiposito;
Ajjeva putta paṭipajja,
kevalaṃ vasudhaṃ imaṃ’.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_296Msdiv 26Saraṭṭhakaṃ sanigamaṃ,
sajanaṃ vanditva khattiyaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_297Msdiv 27‘Ye keci mahiyā sattā,
hīnamukkaṭṭhamajjhimā;
Nirārakkhā sake gehe,
vaḍḍhanti sakañātibhi.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_298Msdiv 28Idaṃ loke uttariyaṃ,
saṃpīḷe mama posanaṃ;
Ayogharamhi saṃvaḍḍho,
appabhe candasūriye.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_299Msdiv 29Pūtikuṇapasampuṇṇā,
muccitvā mātukucchito;
Tato ghoratare dukkhe,
puna pakkhittayoghare.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_300Msdiv 30Yadihaṃ tādisaṃ patvā,
dukkhaṃ paramadāruṇaṃ;
Rajjesu yadi rajjāmi,
pāpānaṃ uttamo siyaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_301Msdiv 31Ukkaṇṭhitomhi kāyena,
rajjenamhi anatthiko;
Nibbutiṃ pariyesissaṃ,
yattha maṃ maccu na maddiye’.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_302Msdiv 32Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
viravante mahājane;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
pāvisiṃ kānanaṃ vanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_303Msdiv 33Mātāpitā na me dessā,
napi me dessaṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā rajjaṃ pariccajin”ti.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_304Ayogharacariyaṃ tatiyaṃ.
