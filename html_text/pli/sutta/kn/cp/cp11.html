Mātuposakacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 84 PTS 2nd ed. 14 Ms 21Cp_162Msdiv 1“Yadā ahosiṃ pavane,
kuñjaro mātuposako;
Na tadā atthi mahiyā,
guṇena mama sādiso.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_163Msdiv 2Pavane disvā vanacaro,
rañño maṃ paṭivedayi;
‘Tavānucchavo mahārāja,
gajo vasati kānane.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_164Msdiv 3Na tassa parikkhāyattho,
napi āḷakakāsuyā;
Saha gahite soṇḍāya,
sayameva idhehi’ti.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_165Msdiv 4Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
rājāpi tuṭṭhamānaso;
Pesesi hatthidamakaṃ,
chekācariyaṃ susikkhitaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_166Msdiv 5Gantvā so hatthidamako,
addasa padumassare;
Bhisamuḷālaṃ uddharantaṃ,
yāpanatthāya mātuyā.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_167Msdiv 6Viññāya me sīlaguṇaṃ,
lakkhaṇaṃ upadhārayi;
‘Ehi puttā’ti vatvāna,
mama soṇḍāya aggahi.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_168Msdiv 7Yaṃ me tadā pākatikaṃ,
sarīrānugataṃ balaṃ;
Ajja nāgasahassānaṃ,
balena samasādisaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_169Msdiv 8Yadihaṃ tesaṃ pakuppeyyaṃ,
upetānaṃ gahaṇāya maṃ;
Paṭibalo bhave tesaṃ,
yāva rajjampi mānusaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_170Msdiv 9Api cāhaṃ sīlarakkhāya,
Sīlapāramipūriyā;
Na karomi citte aññathattaṃ,
Pakkhipantaṃ mamāḷake.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_171Msdiv 10Yadi te maṃ tattha koṭṭeyyuṃ,
pharasūhi tomarehi ca;
Neva tesaṃ pakuppeyyaṃ,
sīlakhaṇḍabhayā mamā”ti.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_172Mātuposakacariyaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
