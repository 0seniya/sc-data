Soṇapaṇḍitacariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 28 Ms 21Cp_314Msdiv 42“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
nagare brahmavaḍḍhane;
Tattha kulavare seṭṭhe,
mahāsāle ajāyahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_315Msdiv 43Tadāpi lokaṃ disvāna,
Andhībhūtaṃ tamotthaṭaṃ;
Cittaṃ bhavato patikuṭati,
Tuttavegahataṃ viya.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_316Msdiv 44Disvāna vividhaṃ pāpaṃ,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
‘Kadāhaṃ gehā nikkhamma,
pavisissāmi kānanaṃ’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_317Msdiv 45Tadāpi maṃ nimantesuṃ,
kāmabhogehi ñātayo;
Tesampi chandamācikkhiṃ,
‘mā nimantetha tehi maṃ’.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_318Msdiv 46Yo me kaniṭṭhako bhātā,
nando nāmāsi paṇḍito;
Sopi maṃ anusikkhanto,
pabbajjaṃ samarocayi.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_319Msdiv 47Ahaṃ soṇo ca nando ca,
ubho mātāpitā mama;
Tadāpi bhoge chaḍḍetvā,
pāvisimhā mahāvanan”ti.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_320Soṇapaṇḍitacariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
