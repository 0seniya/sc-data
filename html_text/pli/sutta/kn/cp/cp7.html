Candakumāracariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 77 Ms 21Cp_52Msdiv 45“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
ekarājassa atrajo;
Nagare pupphavatiyā,
kumāro candasavhayo.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_53Msdiv 46Tadāhaṃ yajanā mutto,
nikkhanto yaññavāṭato;
Saṃvegaṃ janayitvāna,
mahādānaṃ pavattayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_54Msdiv 47Nāhaṃ pivāmi khādāmi,
napi bhuñjāmi bhojanaṃ;
Dakkhiṇeyye adatvāna,
api chappañcarattiyo.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_55Msdiv 48Yathāpi vāṇijo nāma,
Katvāna bhaṇḍasañcayaṃ;
Yattha lābho mahā hoti,
Tattha taṃ harati bhaṇḍakaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_56Msdiv 49Tatheva sakabhuttāpi,
pare dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ;
Tasmā parassa dātabbaṃ,
satabhāgo bhavissati.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_57Msdiv 50Etamatthavasaṃ ñatvā,
demi dānaṃ bhavābhave;
Na paṭikkamāmi dānato,
sambodhimanupattiyā”ti.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_58Candakumāracariyaṃ sattamaṃ.
