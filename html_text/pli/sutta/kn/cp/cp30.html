Maccharājacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 99 PTS 2nd ed. 32 Ms 21Cp_360Msdiv 83“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
maccharājā mahāsare;
Uṇhe sūriyasantāpe,
sare udaka khīyatha.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_361Msdiv 84Tato kākā ca gijjhā ca,
kaṅkā kulalasenakā;
Bhakkhayanti divārattiṃ,
macche upanisīdiya.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_362Msdiv 85Evaṃ cintesahaṃ tattha,
saha ñātīhi pīḷito;
‘Kena nu kho upāyena,
ñātī dukkhā pamocaye’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_363Msdiv 86Vicintayitvā dhammatthaṃ,
saccaṃ addasa passayaṃ;
Sacce ṭhatvā pamocesiṃ,
ñātīnaṃ taṃ atikkhayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_364Msdiv 87Anussaritvā sataṃ dhammaṃ,
paramatthaṃ vicintayaṃ;
Akāsiṃ saccakiriyaṃ,
yaṃ loke dhuvasassataṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_365Msdiv 88‘Yato sarāmi attānaṃ,
yato pattosmi viññutaṃ;
Nābhijānāmi sañcicca,
ekapāṇampi hiṃsitaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_366Msdiv 89Etena saccavajjena,
pajjunno abhivassatu;
Abhitthanaya pajjunna,
nidhiṃ kākassa nāsaya;
Kākaṃ sokāya randhehi,
macche sokā pamocaya’.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_367Msdiv 90Saha kate saccavare,
pajjunno abhigajjiya;
Thalaṃ ninnañca pūrento,
khaṇena abhivassatha.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_368Msdiv 91Evarūpaṃ saccavaraṃ,
katvā vīriyamuttamaṃ;
Vassāpesiṃ mahāmeghaṃ,
saccatejabalassito;
Saccena me samo natthi,
esā me saccapāramī”ti.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_369Maccharājacariyaṃ dasamaṃ.
