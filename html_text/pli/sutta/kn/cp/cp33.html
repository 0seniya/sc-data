Suvaṇṇasāmacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_391SC 1Ms 21Cp_391Msdiv 111“Sāmo yadā vane āsiṃ,
sakkena abhinimmito;
Pavane sīhabyagghe ca,
mettāya mupanāmayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_392SC 2Ms 21Cp_392Msdiv 112Sīhabyagghehi dīpīhi,
acchehi mahisehi ca;
Pasadamigavarāhehi,
parivāretvā vane vasiṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_393SC 3Ms 21Cp_393Msdiv 113Na maṃ koci uttasati,
napi bhāyāmi kassaci;
Mettābalenupatthaddho,
ramāmi pavane tadā”ti.

SC 4SC 4Ms 21Cp_394Suvaṇṇasāmacariyaṃ terasamaṃ.
