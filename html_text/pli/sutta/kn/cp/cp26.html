  • Adiṭṭhānapāramī 1

Temiyacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 96 Ms 21Cp_321Msdiv 48“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
kāsirājassa atrajo;
Mūgapakkhoti nāmena,
temiyoti vadanti maṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_322Msdiv 49Soḷasitthisahassānaṃ,
na vijjati pumo tadā;
Ahorattānaṃ accayena,
nibbatto ahamekako.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_323Msdiv 50Kicchā laddhaṃ piyaṃ puttaṃ,
abhijātaṃ jutindharaṃ;
Setacchattaṃ dhārayitvāna,
sayane poseti maṃ pitā.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_324Msdiv 51Niddāyamāno sayanavare,
Pabujjhitvānahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ paṇḍaraṃ chattaṃ,
Yenāhaṃ nirayaṃ gato.

SC 5PTS 2nd ed. 29 Ms 21Cp_325Msdiv 52Saha diṭṭhassa me chattaṃ,
tāso uppajji bheravo;
Vinicchayaṃ samāpanno,
‘kathāhaṃ imaṃ muñcissaṃ’.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_326Msdiv 53Pubbasālohitā mayhaṃ,
devatā atthakāminī;
Sā maṃ disvāna dukkhitaṃ,
tīsu ṭhānesu yojayi.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_327Msdiv 54‘Mā paṇḍiccayaṃ vibhāvaya,
Bālamato bhava sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Sabbo taṃ jano ocināyatu,
Evaṃ tava attho bhavissati’.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_328Msdiv 55Evaṃ vuttāyahaṃ tassā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Karomi te taṃ vacanaṃ,
yaṃ tvaṃ bhaṇasi devate;
Atthakāmāsi me amma,
hitakāmāsi devate’.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_329Msdiv 56Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
sāgareva thalaṃ labhiṃ;
Haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso,
tayo aṅge adhiṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_330Msdiv 57Mūgo ahosiṃ badhiro,
pakkho gativivajjito;
Ete aṅge adhiṭṭhāya,
vassāni soḷasaṃ vasiṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_331Msdiv 58Tato me hatthapāde ca,
jivhaṃ sotañca maddiya;
Anūnataṃ me passitvā,
‘kāḷakaṇṇī’ti nindisuṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_332Msdiv 59Tato jānapadā sabbe,
senāpatipurohitā;
Sabbe ekamanā hutvā,
chaḍḍanaṃ anumodisuṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Cp_333Msdiv 60Sohaṃ tesaṃ matiṃ sutvā,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
‘Yassatthāya tapociṇṇo,
so me attho samijjhatha’.

SC 14Ms 21Cp_334Msdiv 61Nhāpetvā anulimpitvā,
veṭhetvā rājaveṭhanaṃ;
Chattena abhisiñcitvā,
kāresuṃ puraṃ padakkhiṇaṃ.

SC 15Ms 21Cp_335Msdiv 62Sattāhaṃ dhārayitvāna,
uggate ravimaṇḍale;
Rathena maṃ nīharitvā,
sārathī vanamupāgami.

SC 16PTS 1st ed. 97 Ms 21Cp_336Msdiv 63Ekokāse rathaṃ katvā,
sajjassaṃ hatthamuccito;
Sārathī khaṇatī kāsuṃ,
nikhātuṃ pathaviyā mamaṃ.

SC 17Ms 21Cp_337Msdiv 64Adhiṭṭhitamadhiṭṭhānaṃ,
tajjento vividhakāraṇā;
Na bhindiṃ tamadhiṭṭhānaṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 18Ms 21Cp_338Msdiv 65Mātāpitā na me dessā,
attā me na ca dessiyo;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā vatamadhiṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 19Ms 21Cp_339Msdiv 66Ete aṅge adhiṭṭhāya,
Vassāni soḷasaṃ vasiṃ;
Adhiṭṭhānena me samo natthi,
Esā me adhiṭṭhānapāramī”ti.

SC 20Ms 21Cp_340Temiyacariyaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
