Mahiṃsarājacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 87 Ms 21Cp_202Msdiv 37“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
mahiṃso pavanacārako;
Pavaḍḍhakāyo balavā,
mahanto bhīmadassano.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_203Msdiv 38Pabbhāre giridugge ca,
rukkhamūle dakāsaye;
Hotettha ṭhānaṃ mahiṃsānaṃ,
koci koci tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_204Msdiv 39Vicaranto brahāraññe,
ṭhānaṃ addasa bhaddakaṃ;
Taṃ ṭhānaṃ upagantvāna,
tiṭṭhāmi ca sayāmi ca.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_205Msdiv 40Athettha kapimāgantvā,
pāpo anariyo lahu;
Khandhe nalāṭe bhamuke,
mutteti ohadeti taṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_206Msdiv 41Sakimpi divasaṃ dutiyaṃ,
tatiyaṃ catutthampi ca;
Dūseti maṃ sabbakālaṃ,
tena homi upadduto.

SC 6PTS 2nd ed. 18 Ms 21Cp_207Msdiv 42Mamaṃ upaddutaṃ disvā,
yakkho maṃ idamabravi;
‘Nāsehetaṃ chavaṃ pāpaṃ,
siṅgehi ca khurehi ca’.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_208Msdiv 43Evaṃ vutte, tadā yakkhe,
ahaṃ taṃ idamabraviṃ;
‘Kiṃ tvaṃ makkhesi kuṇapena,
pāpena anariyena maṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_209Msdiv 44Yadihaṃ tassa pakuppeyyaṃ,
tato hīnataro bhave;
Sīlañca me pabhijjeyya,
viññū ca garaheyyu maṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_210Msdiv 45Hīḷitā jīvitā vāpi,
parisuddhena mataṃ varaṃ;
Kyāhaṃ jīvitahetūpi,
kāhāmi paraheṭhanaṃ’.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_211Msdiv 46Mamevāyaṃ maññamāno,
aññepevaṃ karissati;
Teva tassa vadhissanti,
sā me mutti bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_212Msdiv 47Hīnamajjhimaukkaṭṭhe,
sahanto avamānitaṃ;
Evaṃ labhati sappañño,
manasā yathāpatthitan”ti.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_213Mahiṃsarājacariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
