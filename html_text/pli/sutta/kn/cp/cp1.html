Akitticariya

SC 1SC 1Ms 21Cp_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2PTS 1st ed. 73 PTS 2nd ed. 1 Ms 21Cp_2SC 2PTS 1st ed. 73 PTS 2nd ed. 1 Ms 21Cp_2Msdiv 1“Kappe ca satasahasse,
caturo ca asaṅkhiye;
Etthantare yaṃ caritaṃ,
sabbaṃ taṃ bodhipācanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_3SC 3Ms 21Cp_3Msdiv 2Atītakappe caritaṃ,
ṭhapayitvā bhavābhave;
Imamhi kappe caritaṃ,
pavakkhissaṃ suṇohi me.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_4SC 4Ms 21Cp_4Msdiv 3Yadā ahaṃ brahāraññe,
suññe vipinakānane;
Ajjhogāhetvā viharāmi,
akitti nāma tāpaso.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_5SC 5Ms 21Cp_5Msdiv 4Tadā maṃ tapatejena,
santatto tidivābhibhū;
Dhārento brāhmaṇavaṇṇaṃ,
bhikkhāya maṃ upāgami.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_6SC 6Ms 21Cp_6Msdiv 5Pavanā ābhataṃ paṇṇaṃ,
atelañca aloṇikaṃ;
Mama dvāre ṭhitaṃ disvā,
sakaṭāhena ākiriṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_7SC 7Ms 21Cp_7Msdiv 6Tassa datvānahaṃ paṇṇaṃ,
nikkujjitvāna bhājanaṃ;
Punesanaṃ jahitvāna,
pāvisiṃ paṇṇasālakaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_8SC 8Ms 21Cp_8Msdiv 7Dutiyampi tatiyampi,
upagañchi mamantikaṃ;
Akampito anolaggo,
evamevamadāsahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_9SC 9Ms 21Cp_9Msdiv 8Na me tappaccayā atthi,
sarīrasmiṃ vivaṇṇiyaṃ;
Pītisukhena ratiyā,
vītināmemi taṃ divaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_10SC 10Ms 21Cp_10Msdiv 9Yadi māsampi dvemāsaṃ,
dakkhiṇeyyaṃ varaṃ labhe;
Akampito anolīno,
dadeyyaṃ dānamuttamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_11SC 11Ms 21Cp_11Msdiv 10Na tassa dānaṃ dadamāno,
yasaṃ lābhañca patthayiṃ;
Sabbaññutaṃ patthayāno,
tāni kammāni ācarin”ti.

SC 12SC 12Ms 21Cp_12Akitticariyaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
