Yudhañjayacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 92 PTS 2nd ed. 24 Ms 21Cp_269SC 1PTS 1st ed. 92 PTS 2nd ed. 24 Ms 21Cp_269Msdiv 1“Yadāhaṃ amitayaso,
Rājaputto yudhañjayo;
Ussāvabinduṃ sūriyātape,
Patitaṃ disvāna saṃvijiṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_270SC 2Ms 21Cp_270Msdiv 2Taññevādhipatiṃ katvā,
saṃvegamanubrūhayiṃ;
Mātāpitū ca vanditvā,
pabbajjamanuyācahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_271SC 3Ms 21Cp_271Msdiv 3Yācanti maṃ pañjalikā,
sanegamā saraṭṭhakā;
‘Ajjeva putta paṭipajja,
iddhaṃ phītaṃ mahāmahiṃ’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_272SC 4Ms 21Cp_272Msdiv 4Sarājake sahorodhe,
sanegame saraṭṭhake;
Karuṇaṃ paridevante,
anapekkhova pariccajiṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_273SC 5Ms 21Cp_273Msdiv 5Kevalaṃ pathaviṃ rajjaṃ,
ñātiparijanaṃ yasaṃ;
Cajamāno na cintesiṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_274SC 6Ms 21Cp_274Msdiv 6Mātāpitā na me dessā,
napi me dessaṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā rajjaṃ pariccajin”ti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 21Cp_275Yudhañjayacariyaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
