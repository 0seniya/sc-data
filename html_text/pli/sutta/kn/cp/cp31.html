Kaṇhadīpāyanacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_370Msdiv 92“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
kaṇhadīpāyano isi;
Paropaññāsavassāni,
anabhiratocariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_371Msdiv 93Na koci etaṃ jānāti,
Anabhiratimanaṃ mama;
Ahañhi kassaci nācikkhiṃ,
Arati me carati mānase.

SC 3PTS 1st ed. 100 PTS 2nd ed. 33 Ms 21Cp_372Msdiv 94Sabrahmacārī maṇḍabyo,
sahāyo me mahāisi;
Pubbakammasamāyutto,
sūlamāropanaṃ labhi.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_373Msdiv 95Tamahaṃ upaṭṭhahitvāna,
ārogyamanupāpayiṃ;
Āpucchitvāna āgañchiṃ,
yaṃ mayhaṃ sakamassamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_374Msdiv 96Sahāyo brāhmaṇo mayhaṃ,
bhariyaṃ ādāya puttakaṃ;
Tayo janā samāgantvā,
āgañchuṃ pāhunāgataṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_375Msdiv 97Sammodamāno tehi saha,
nisinno sakamassame;
Dārako vaṭṭamanukkhipaṃ,
āsīvisamakopayi.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_376Msdiv 98Tato so vaṭṭagataṃ maggaṃ,
anvesanto kumārako;
Āsīvisassa hatthena,
uttamaṅgaṃ parāmasi.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_377Msdiv 99Tassa āmasane kuddho,
sappo visabalassito;
Kupito paramakopena,
aḍaṃsi dārakaṃ khaṇe.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_378Msdiv 100Sahadaṭṭho āsīvisena,
dārako papati bhūmiyaṃ;
Tenāhaṃ dukkhito āsiṃ,
mama vāhasi taṃ dukkhaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_379Msdiv 101Tyāhaṃ assāsayitvāna,
dukkhite sokasallite;
Paṭhamaṃ akāsiṃ kiriyaṃ,
aggaṃ saccaṃ varuttamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_380Msdiv 102‘Sattāhamevāhaṃ pasannacitto,
Puññatthiko acariṃ brahmacariyaṃ;
Athāparaṃ yaṃ caritaṃ mamedaṃ,
Vassāni paññāsasamādhikāni.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_381Msdiv 103Akāmako vāhi ahaṃ carāmi,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu;
Hataṃ visaṃ jīvatu yaññadatto’.

SC 13Ms 21Cp_382Msdiv 104Saha sacce kate mayhaṃ,
visavegena vedhito;
Abujjhitvāna vuṭṭhāsi,
arogo cāsi māṇavo;
Saccena me samo natthi,
esā me saccapāramī”ti.

SC 14Ms 21Cp_383Kaṇhadīpāyanacariyaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
