Mahāgovindacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 76 Ms 21Cp_42Msdiv 37“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
sattarājapurohito;
Pūjito naradevehi,
mahāgovindabrāhmaṇo.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_43Msdiv 38Tadāhaṃ sattarajjesu,
Yaṃ me āsi upāyanaṃ;
Tena demi mahādānaṃ,
Akkhobbhaṃ sāgarūpamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_44Msdiv 39Na me dessaṃ dhanaṃ dhaññaṃ,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
Tasmā demi varaṃ dhanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_45Mahāgovindacariyaṃ pañcamaṃ.
