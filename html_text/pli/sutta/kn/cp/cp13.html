Campeyyanāgacariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 16 Ms 21Cp_183SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 16 Ms 21Cp_183Msdiv 20“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
campeyyako mahiddhiko;
Tadāpi dhammiko āsiṃ,
sīlabbatasamappito.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_184SC 2Ms 21Cp_184Msdiv 21Tadāpi maṃ dhammacāriṃ,
upavutthaṃ uposathaṃ;
Ahituṇḍiko gahetvāna,
rājadvāramhi kīḷati.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_185SC 3Ms 21Cp_185Msdiv 22Yaṃ yaṃ so vaṇṇaṃ cintayi,
nīlaṃva pītalohitaṃ;
Tassa cittānuvattanto,
homi cintitasannibho.

SC 4PTS 1st ed. 86 Ms 21Cp_186SC 4PTS 1st ed. 86 Ms 21Cp_186Msdiv 23Thalaṃ kareyyamudakaṃ,
udakampi thalaṃ kare;
Yadihaṃ tassa pakuppeyyaṃ,
khaṇena chārikaṃ kare.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_187SC 5Ms 21Cp_187Msdiv 24Yadi cittavasī hessaṃ,
parihāyissāmi sīlato;
Sīlena parihīnassa,
uttamattho na sijjhati.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_188SC 6Ms 21Cp_188Msdiv 25Kāmaṃ bhijjatuyaṃ kāyo,
idheva vikirīyatu;
Neva sīlaṃ pabhindeyyaṃ,
vikirante bhusaṃ viyā”ti.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_189SC 7Ms 21Cp_189Campeyyanāgacariyaṃ tatiyaṃ.
