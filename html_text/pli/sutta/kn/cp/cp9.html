  • Dānapāramī 9

Vessantaracariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 7 Ms 21Cp_76Msdiv 67“Yā me ahosi janikā,
phussatī nāma khattiyā;
Sā atītāsu jātīsu,
sakkassa mahesī piyā.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_77Msdiv 68Tassā āyukkhayaṃ ñatvā,
devindo etadabravi;
‘Dadāmi te dasa vare,
varabhadde yadicchasi’.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_78Msdiv 69Evaṃ vuttā ca sā devī,
sakkaṃ punidamabravi;
‘Kiṃ nu me aparādhatthi,
kiṃ nu dessā ahaṃ tava;
Rammā cāvesi maṃ ṭhānā,
vātova dharaṇīruhaṃ’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_79Msdiv 70Evaṃ vutto ca so sakko,
puna tassidamabravi;
‘Na ceva te kataṃ pāpaṃ,
na ca me tvaṃsi appiyā.

SC 5PTS 1st ed. 79 Ms 21Cp_80Msdiv 71Ettakaṃyeva te āyu,
cavanakālo bhavissati;
Paṭiggaṇha mayā dinne,
vare dasa varuttame’.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_81Msdiv 72Sakkena sā dinnavarā,
tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā;
Mamaṃ abbhantaraṃ katvā,
phussatī dasa vare varī.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_82Msdiv 73Tato cutā sā phussatī,
khattiye upapajjatha;
Jetuttaramhi nagare,
sañjayena samāgami.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_83Msdiv 74Yadāhaṃ phussatiyā kucchiṃ,
okkanto piyamātuyā;
Mama tejena me mātā,
sadā dānaratā ahu.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_84Msdiv 75Adhane āture jiṇṇe,
yācake addhike jane;
Samaṇe brāhmaṇe khīṇe,
deti dānaṃ akiñcane.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_85Msdiv 76Dasa māse dhārayitvāna,
karonte puraṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Vessānaṃ vīthiyā majjhe,
janesi phussatī mamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_86Msdiv 77Na mayhaṃ mattikaṃ nāmaṃ,
napi pettikasambhavaṃ;
Jātettha vessavīthiyā,
tasmā vessantaro ahu.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_87Msdiv 78Yadāhaṃ dārako homi,
jātiyā aṭṭhavassiko;
Tadā nisajja pāsāde,
dānaṃ dātuṃ vicintayiṃ.

SC 13PTS 2nd ed. 8 Ms 21Cp_88Msdiv 79‘Hadayaṃ dadeyyaṃ cakkhuṃ,
maṃsampi rudhirampi ca;
Dadeyyaṃ kāyaṃ sāvetvā,
yadi koci yācaye mamaṃ’.

SC 14Ms 21Cp_89Msdiv 80Sabhāvaṃ cintayantassa,
akampitamasaṇṭhitaṃ;
Akampi tattha pathavī,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 15Ms 21Cp_90Msdiv 81Anvaddhamāse pannarase,
puṇṇamāse uposathe;
Paccayaṃ nāgamāruyha,
dānaṃ dātuṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 16Ms 21Cp_91Msdiv 82Kaliṅgaraṭṭhavisayā,
brāhmaṇā upagañchu maṃ;
Ayācuṃ maṃ hatthināgaṃ,
dhaññaṃ maṅgalasammataṃ.

SC 17Ms 21Cp_92Msdiv 83‘Avuṭṭhiko janapado,
dubbhikkho chātako mahā;
Dadāhi pavaraṃ nāgaṃ,
sabbasetaṃ gajuttamaṃ’.

SC 18Ms 21Cp_93Msdiv 84Dadāmi na vikampāmi,
yaṃ maṃ yācanti brāhmaṇā;
Santaṃ nappatigūhāmi,
dāne me ramate mano.

SC 19Ms 21Cp_94Msdiv 85Na me yācakamanuppatte,
paṭikkhepo anucchavo;
Mā me bhijji samādānaṃ,
dassāmi vipulaṃ gajaṃ.

SC 20Ms 21Cp_95Msdiv 86Nāgaṃ gahetvā soṇḍāya,
bhiṅgāre ratanāmaye;
Jalaṃ hatthe ākiritvā,
brāhmaṇānaṃ adaṃ gajaṃ.

SC 21Ms 21Cp_96Msdiv 87Punāparaṃ dadantassa,
sabbasetaṃ gajuttamaṃ;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 22Ms 21Cp_97Msdiv 88Tassa nāgassa dānena,
sivayo kuddhā samāgatā;
Pabbājesuṃ sakā raṭṭhā,
‘vaṅkaṃ gacchatu pabbataṃ’.

SC 23Ms 21Cp_98Msdiv 89Tesaṃ nicchubhamānānaṃ,
akampitamasaṇṭhitaṃ;
Mahādānaṃ pavattetuṃ,
ekaṃ varamayācisaṃ.

SC 24PTS 1st ed. 80 Ms 21Cp_99Msdiv 90Yācitā sivayo sabbe,
ekaṃ varamadaṃsu me;
Sāvayitvā kaṇṇabheriṃ,
mahādānaṃ dadāmahaṃ.

SC 25Ms 21Cp_100Msdiv 91Athettha vattatī saddo,
tumulo bheravo mahā;
Dānenimaṃ nīharanti,
puna dānaṃ dadātayaṃ.

SC 26Ms 21Cp_101Msdiv 92Hatthiṃ asse rathe datvā,
dāsiṃ dāsaṃ gavaṃ dhanaṃ;
Mahādānaṃ daditvāna,
nagarā nikkhamiṃ tadā.

SC 27Ms 21Cp_102Msdiv 93Nikkhamitvāna nagarā,
nivattitvā vilokite;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 28Ms 21Cp_103Msdiv 94Catuvāhiṃ rathaṃ datvā,
ṭhatvā cātummahāpathe;
Ekākiyo adutiyo,
maddideviṃ idamabraviṃ.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 9 Ms 21Cp_104Msdiv 95‘Tvaṃ maddi kaṇhaṃ gaṇhāhi,
lahukā esā kaniṭṭhikā;
Ahaṃ jāliṃ gahessāmi,
garuko bhātiko hi so’.

SC 30Ms 21Cp_105Msdiv 96Padumaṃ puṇḍarīkaṃva,
maddī kaṇhājinaggahī;
Ahaṃ suvaṇṇabimbaṃva,
jāliṃ khattiyamaggahiṃ.

SC 31Ms 21Cp_106Msdiv 97Abhijātā sukhumālā,
khattiyā caturo janā;
Visamaṃ samaṃ akkamantā,
vaṅkaṃ gacchāma pabbataṃ.

SC 32Ms 21Cp_107Msdiv 98Ye keci manujā enti,
anumagge paṭippathe;
Maggante paṭipucchāma,
‘kuhiṃ vaṅkantapabbato’.

SC 33Ms 21Cp_108Msdiv 99Te tattha amhe passitvā,
karuṇaṃ giramudīrayuṃ;
Dukkhante paṭivedenti,
dūre vaṅkantapabbato.

SC 34Ms 21Cp_109Msdiv 100Yadi passanti pavane,
dārakā phaline dume;
Tesaṃ phalānaṃ hetumhi,
uparodanti dārakā.

SC 35Ms 21Cp_110Msdiv 101Rodante dārake disvā,
ubbiddhā vipulā dumā;
Sayamevoṇamitvāna,
upagacchanti dārake.

SC 36Ms 21Cp_111Msdiv 102Idaṃ acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Sāhukāraṃ pavattesi,
maddī sabbaṅgasobhanā.

SC 37Ms 21Cp_112Msdiv 103Accheraṃ vata lokasmiṃ,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Vessantarassa tejena,
sayamevoṇatā dumā.

SC 38Ms 21Cp_113Msdiv 104Saṅkhipiṃsu pathaṃ yakkhā,
anukampāya dārake;
Nikkhantadivaseneva,
cetaraṭṭhamupāgamuṃ.

SC 39Ms 21Cp_114Msdiv 105Saṭṭhirājasahassāni,
tadā vasanti mātule;
Sabbe pañjalikā hutvā,
rodamānā upāgamuṃ.

SC 40Ms 21Cp_115Msdiv 106Tattha vattetvā sallāpaṃ,
cetehi cetaputtehi;
Te tato nikkhamitvāna,
vaṅkaṃ agamu pabbataṃ.

SC 41Ms 21Cp_116Msdiv 107Āmantayitvā devindo,
vissakammaṃ mahiddhikaṃ;
Assamaṃ sukataṃ rammaṃ,
paṇṇasālaṃ sumāpaya.

SC 42PTS 1st ed. 81 Ms 21Cp_117Msdiv 108Sakkassa vacanaṃ sutvā,
vissakammo mahiddhiko;
Assamaṃ sukataṃ rammaṃ,
paṇṇasālaṃ sumāpayi.

SC 43Ms 21Cp_118Msdiv 109Ajjhogāhetvā pavanaṃ,
appasaddaṃ nirākulaṃ;
Caturo janā mayaṃ tattha,
vasāma pabbatantare.

SC 44Ms 21Cp_119Msdiv 110Ahañca maddidevī ca,
jālī kaṇhājinā cubho;
Aññamaññaṃ sokanudā,
vasāma assame tadā.

SC 45PTS 2nd ed. 10 Ms 21Cp_120Msdiv 111Dārake anurakkhanto,
asuñño homi assame;
Maddī phalaṃ āharitvā,
poseti sā tayo jane.

SC 46Ms 21Cp_121Msdiv 112Pavane vasamānassa,
addhiko maṃ upāgami;
Āyāci puttake mayhaṃ,
jāliṃ kaṇhājinaṃ cubho.

SC 47Ms 21Cp_122Msdiv 113Yācakaṃ upagataṃ disvā,
hāso me upapajjatha;
Ubho putte gahetvāna,
adāsiṃ brāhmaṇe tadā.

SC 48Ms 21Cp_123Msdiv 114Sake putte cajantassa,
jūjake brāhmaṇe yadā;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 49Ms 21Cp_124Msdiv 115Punadeva sakko oruyha,
hutvā brāhmaṇasannibho;
Āyāci maṃ maddideviṃ,
sīlavantiṃ patibbataṃ.

SC 50Ms 21Cp_125Msdiv 116Maddiṃ hatthe gahetvāna,
udakañjali pūriya;
Pasannamanasaṅkappo,
tassa maddiṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 51Ms 21Cp_126Msdiv 117Maddiyā dīyamānāya,
gagane devā pamoditā;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 52Ms 21Cp_127Msdiv 118Jāliṃ kaṇhājinaṃ dhītaṃ,
maddideviṃ patibbataṃ;
Cajamāno na cintesiṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 53Ms 21Cp_128Msdiv 119Na me dessā ubho puttā,
maddidevī na dessiyā;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā piye adāsahaṃ.

SC 54Ms 21Cp_129Msdiv 120Punāparaṃ brahāraññe,
mātāpitusamāgame;
Karuṇaṃ paridevante,
sallapante sukhaṃ dukhaṃ.

SC 55Ms 21Cp_130Msdiv 121Hirottappena garunā,
ubhinnaṃ upasaṅkami;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 56Ms 21Cp_131Msdiv 122Punāparaṃ brahāraññā,
nikkhamitvā sañātibhi;
Pavisāmi puraṃ rammaṃ,
jetuttaraṃ puruttamaṃ.

SC 57Ms 21Cp_132Msdiv 123Ratanāni satta vassiṃsu,
mahāmegho pavassatha;
Tadāpi pathavī kampi,
sineruvanavaṭaṃsakā.

SC 58Ms 21Cp_133Msdiv 124Acetanāyaṃ pathavī,
aviññāya sukhaṃ dukhaṃ;
Sāpi dānabalā mayhaṃ,
sattakkhattuṃ pakampathā”ti.

SC 59Ms 21Cp_134Vessantaracariyaṃ navamaṃ.
