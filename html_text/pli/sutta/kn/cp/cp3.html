Kururājacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_23SC 1Ms 21Cp_23Msdiv 20“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
indapatthe puruttame;
Rājā dhanañcayo nāma,
kusale dasahupāgato.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_24SC 2Ms 21Cp_24Msdiv 21Kaliṅgaraṭṭhavisayā,
brāhmaṇā upagañchu maṃ;
Āyācuṃ maṃ hatthināgaṃ,
dhaññaṃ maṅgalasammataṃ.

SC 3PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 21Cp_25SC 3PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 21Cp_25Msdiv 22‘Avuṭṭhiko janapado,
dubbhikkho chātako mahā;
Dadāhi pavaraṃ nāgaṃ,
nīlaṃ añjanasavhayaṃ’.

SC 4PTS 1st ed. 75 Ms 21Cp_26SC 4PTS 1st ed. 75 Ms 21Cp_26Msdiv 23‘Na me yācakamanuppatte,
paṭikkhepo anucchavo;
Mā me bhijji samādānaṃ,
dassāmi vipulaṃ gajaṃ’.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_27SC 5Ms 21Cp_27Msdiv 24Nāgaṃ gahetvā soṇḍāya,
bhiṅgāre ratanāmaye;
Jalaṃ hatthe ākiritvā,
brāhmaṇānaṃ adaṃ gajaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_28SC 6Ms 21Cp_28Msdiv 25Tassa nāge padinnamhi,
amaccā etadabravuṃ;
‘Kiṃ nu tuyhaṃ varaṃ nāgaṃ,
yācakānaṃ padassasi.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_29SC 7Ms 21Cp_29Msdiv 26Dhaññaṃ maṅgalasampannaṃ,
saṅgāmavijayuttamaṃ;
Tasmiṃ nāge padinnamhi,
kiṃ te rajjaṃ karissati’.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_30SC 8Ms 21Cp_30Msdiv 27‘Rajjampi me dade sabbaṃ,
sarīraṃ dajjamattano;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā nāgaṃ adāsahan’”ti.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_31SC 9Ms 21Cp_31Kururājacariyaṃ tatiyaṃ.
