Mahāsudassanacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_32Msdiv 28“Kusāvatimhi nagare,
yadā āsiṃ mahīpati;
Mahāsudassano nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_33Msdiv 29Tatthāhaṃ divase tikkhattuṃ,
ghosāpemi tahiṃ tahiṃ;
‘Ko kiṃ icchati pattheti,
kassa kiṃ dīyatū dhanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_34Msdiv 30Ko chātako ko tasito,
ko mālaṃ ko vilepanaṃ;
Nānārattāni vatthāni,
ko naggo paridahissati.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_35Msdiv 31Ko pathe chattamādeti,
kopāhanā mudū subhā’;
Iti sāyañca pāto ca,
ghosāpemi tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_36Msdiv 32Na taṃ dasasu ṭhānesu,
napi ṭhānasatesu vā;
Anekasataṭhānesu,
paṭiyattaṃ yācake dhanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_37Msdiv 33Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
yadi eti vanibbako;
Laddhā yadicchakaṃ bhogaṃ,
pūrahatthova gacchati.

SC 7PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 21Cp_38Msdiv 34Evarūpaṃ mahādānaṃ,
Adāsiṃ yāvajīvikaṃ;
Napāhaṃ dessaṃ dhanaṃ dammi,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_39Msdiv 35Yathāpi āturo nāma,
rogato parimuttiyā;
Dhanena vejjaṃ tappetvā,
rogato parimuccati.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_40Msdiv 36Tathevāhaṃ jānamāno,
paripūretumasesato;
Ūnamanaṃ pūrayituṃ,
demi dānaṃ vanibbake;
Nirālayo apaccāso,
sambodhimanupattiyā”ti.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_41Mahāsudassanacariyaṃ catutthaṃ.
