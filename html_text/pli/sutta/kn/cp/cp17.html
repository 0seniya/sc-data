Mātaṅgacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_227Msdiv 60“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Mātaṅgo nāma nāmena,
sīlavā susamāhito.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_228Msdiv 61Ahañca brāhmaṇo eko,
gaṅgākūle vasāmubho;
Ahaṃ vasāmi upari,
heṭṭhā vasati brāhmaṇo.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_229Msdiv 62Vicaranto anukūlamhi,
uddhaṃ me assamaddasa;
Tattha maṃ paribhāsetvā,
abhisapi muddhaphālanaṃ.

SC 4PTS 2nd ed. 20 Ms 21Cp_230Msdiv 63Yadihaṃ tassa pakuppeyyaṃ,
yadi sīlaṃ na gopaye;
Oloketvānahaṃ tassa,
kareyyaṃ chārikaṃ viya.

SC 5PTS 1st ed. 89 Ms 21Cp_231Msdiv 64Yaṃ so tadā maṃ abhisapi,
kupito duṭṭhamānaso;
Tasseva matthake nipati,
yogena taṃ pamocayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_232Msdiv 65Anurakkhiṃ mama sīlaṃ,
nārakkhiṃ mama jīvitaṃ;
Sīlavā hi tadā āsiṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā”ti.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_233Mātaṅgacariyaṃ sattamaṃ.
