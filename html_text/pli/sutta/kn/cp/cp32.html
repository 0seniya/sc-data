Sutasomacariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 34 Ms 21Cp_384Msdiv 105“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
sutasomo mahīpati;
Gahito porisādena,
brāhmaṇe saṅgaraṃ sariṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_385Msdiv 106Khattiyānaṃ ekasataṃ,
āvuṇitvā karattale;
Etesaṃ pamilāpetvā,
yaññatthe upanayī mamaṃ.

SC 3PTS 1st ed. 101 Ms 21Cp_386Msdiv 107Apucchi maṃ porisādo,
‘kiṃ tvaṃ icchasi nissajaṃ;
Yathāmati te kāhāmi,
yadi me tvaṃ punehisi’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_387Msdiv 108Tassa paṭissuṇitvāna,
paṇhe āgamanaṃ mama;
Upagantvā puraṃ rammaṃ,
rajjaṃ niyyādayiṃ tadā.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_388Msdiv 109Anussaritvā sataṃ dhammaṃ,
pubbakaṃ jinasevitaṃ;
Brāhmaṇassa dhanaṃ datvā,
porisādaṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_389Msdiv 110Natthi me saṃsayo tattha,
Ghātayissati vā na vā;
Saccavācānurakkhanto,
Jīvitaṃ cajitumupāgamiṃ;
Saccena me samo natthi,
Esā me saccapāramī”ti.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_390Sutasomacariyaṃ dvādasamaṃ.
