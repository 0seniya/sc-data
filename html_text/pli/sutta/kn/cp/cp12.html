Bhūridattacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 85 PTS 2nd ed. 15 Ms 21Cp_173Msdiv 11“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
bhūridatto mahiddhiko;
Virūpakkhena mahāraññā,
devalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_174Msdiv 12Tattha passitvāhaṃ deve,
ekantaṃ sukhasamappite;
Taṃ saggagamanatthāya,
sīlabbataṃ samādiyiṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_175Msdiv 13Sarīrakiccaṃ katvāna,
bhutvā yāpanamattakaṃ;
Caturo aṅge adhiṭṭhāya,
semi vammikamuddhani.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_176Msdiv 14‘Chaviyā cammena maṃsena,
Nahāruaṭṭhikehi vā;
Yassa etena karaṇīyaṃ,
Dinnaṃyeva harātu so’.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_177Msdiv 15Saṃsito akataññunā,
ālampāyano mamaggahi;
Peḷāya pakkhipitvāna,
kīḷeti maṃ tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_178Msdiv 16Peḷāya pakkhipantepi,
sammaddantepi pāṇinā;
Ālampāyane na kuppāmi,
sīlakhaṇḍabhayā mama.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_179Msdiv 17Sakajīvitapariccāgo,
tiṇato lahuko mama;
Sīlavītikkamo mayhaṃ,
pathavīuppatanaṃ viya.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_180Msdiv 18Nirantaraṃ jātisataṃ,
cajeyyaṃ mama jīvitaṃ;
Neva sīlaṃ pabhindeyyaṃ,
catuddīpāna hetupi.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_181Msdiv 19Api cāhaṃ sīlarakkhāya,
Sīlapāramipūriyā;
Na karomi citte aññathattaṃ,
Pakkhipantampi peḷake”ti.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_182Bhūridattacariyaṃ dutiyaṃ.
