  • Sīlapāramī 9

Alīnasattucariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 90 PTS 2nd ed. 21 Ms 21Cp_243Msdiv 74“Pañcālaraṭṭhe nagaravare,
Kapilāyaṃ puruttame;
Rājā jayaddiso nāma,
Sīlaguṇamupāgato.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_244Msdiv 75Tassa rañño ahaṃ putto,
Sutadhammo susīlavā;
Alīnasatto guṇavā,
Anurakkhaparijano sadā.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_245Msdiv 76Pitā me migavaṃ gantvā,
porisādaṃ upāgami;
So me pitumaggahesi,
‘bhakkhosi mama mā cali’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_246Msdiv 77Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhīto tasitavedhito;
Ūrukkhambho ahu tassa,
disvāna porisādakaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_247Msdiv 78Migavaṃ gahetvā muñcassu,
katvā āgamanaṃ puna;
Brāhmaṇassa dhanaṃ datvā,
pitā āmantayī mamaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_248Msdiv 79‘Rajjaṃ putta paṭipajja,
mā pamajji puraṃ idaṃ;
Kataṃ me porisādena,
mama āgamanaṃ puna’.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_249Msdiv 80Mātāpitū ca vanditvā,
nimminitvāna attanā;
Nikkhipitvā dhanuṃ khaggaṃ,
porisādaṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_250Msdiv 81Sasatthahatthūpagataṃ,
kadāci so tasissati;
Tena bhijjissati sīlaṃ,
parittāsaṃ kate mayi.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_251Msdiv 82Sīlakhaṇḍabhayā mayhaṃ,
tassa dessaṃ na byāhariṃ;
Mettacitto hitavādī,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_252Msdiv 83‘Ujjālehi mahāaggiṃ,
papatissāmi rukkhato;
Tvaṃ pakkakālamaññāya,
bhakkhaya maṃ pitāmaha’.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_253Msdiv 84Iti sīlavataṃ hetu,
nārakkhiṃ mama jīvitaṃ;
Pabbājesiṃ cahaṃ tassa,
sadā pāṇātipātikan”ti.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_254Alīnasattucariyaṃ navamaṃ.
