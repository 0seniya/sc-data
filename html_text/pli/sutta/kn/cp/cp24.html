  • Nekkhammapāramī 4

Bhisacariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 27 Ms 21Cp_305Msdiv 34“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
kāsīnaṃ puravaruttame;
Bhaginī ca bhātaro satta,
nibbattā sotthiye kule.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_306Msdiv 35Etesaṃ pubbajo āsiṃ,
hirīsukkamupāgato;
Bhavaṃ disvāna bhayato,
nekkhammābhirato ahaṃ.

SC 3PTS 1st ed. 95 Ms 21Cp_307Msdiv 36Mātāpitūhi pahitā,
sahāyā ekamānasā;
Kāmehi maṃ nimantenti,
‘kulavaṃsaṃ dharehi’ti.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_308Msdiv 37Yaṃ tesaṃ vacanaṃ vuttaṃ,
gihīdhamme sukhāvahaṃ;
Taṃ me ahosi kaṭhinaṃ,
tattaphālasamaṃ viya.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_309Msdiv 38Te maṃ tadā ukkhipantaṃ,
pucchiṃsu patthitaṃ mama;
‘Kiṃ tvaṃ patthayase samma,
yadi kāme na bhuñjasi’.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_310Msdiv 39Tesāhaṃ evamavacaṃ,
atthakāmo hitesinaṃ;
‘Nāhaṃ patthemi gihībhāvaṃ,
nekkhammābhirato ahaṃ’.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_311Msdiv 40Te mayhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
pitu mātu ca sāvayuṃ;
Mātāpitā evamāhu,
‘sabbeva pabbajāma bho’.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_312Msdiv 41Ubho mātāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhaginī ca satta bhātaro;
Amitadhanaṃ chaḍḍayitvā,
pāvisimhā mahāvanan”ti.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_313Bhisacariyaṃ catutthaṃ.
