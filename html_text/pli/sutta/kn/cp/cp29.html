Vaṭṭapotakacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 98 PTS 2nd ed. 31 Ms 21Cp_348Msdiv 72“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
magadhe vaṭṭapotako;
Ajātapakkho taruṇo,
maṃsapesi kulāvake.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_349Msdiv 73Mukhatuṇḍakenāharitvā,
mātā posayatī mamaṃ;
Tassā phassena jīvāmi,
natthi me kāyikaṃ balaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_350Msdiv 74Saṃvacchare gimhasamaye,
davaḍāho padippati;
Upagacchati amhākaṃ,
pāvako kaṇhavattanī.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_351Msdiv 75Dhamadhamāiti evaṃ,
saddāyanto mahāsikhī;
Anupubbena jhāpento,
aggi mamamupāgami.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_352Msdiv 76Aggivegabhayātītā,
tasitā mātāpitā mama;
Kulāvake maṃ chaḍḍetvā,
attānaṃ parimocayuṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_353Msdiv 77Pāde pakkhe pajahāmi,
natthi me kāyikaṃ balaṃ;
Sohaṃ agatiko tattha,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_354Msdiv 78‘Yesāhaṃ upadhāveyyaṃ,
bhīto tasitavedhito;
Te maṃ ohāya pakkantā,
kathaṃ me ajja kātave.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_355Msdiv 79Atthi loke sīlaguṇo,
saccaṃ soceyyanuddayā;
Tena saccena kāhāmi,
saccakiriyamuttamaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_356Msdiv 80Āvejjetvā dhammabalaṃ,
saritvā pubbake jine;
Saccabalamavassāya,
saccakiriyamakāsahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_357Msdiv 81Santi pakkhā apatanā,
santi pādā avañcanā;
Mātāpitā ca nikkhantā,
jātaveda paṭikkama’.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_358Msdiv 82Sahasacce kate mayhaṃ,
Mahāpajjalito sikhī;
Vajjesi soḷasakarīsāni,
Udakaṃ patvā yathā sikhī;
Saccena me samo natthi,
Esā me saccapāramī”ti.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_359Vaṭṭapotakacariyaṃ navamaṃ.
