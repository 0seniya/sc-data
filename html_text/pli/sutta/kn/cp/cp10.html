Sasako pavanacārako;
Tiṇapaṇṇasākaphalabhakkho,
Paraheṭhanavivajjito.

suttapoto cahaṃ tadā;

sāyaṃ pāto ca dissare.

kiriye kalyāṇapāpake;
‘Pāpāni parivajjetha,
kalyāṇe abhinivissatha’.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_138SC 4Ms 21Cp_138Msdiv 128Uposathamhi divase,
candaṃ disvāna pūritaṃ;
Etesaṃ tattha ācikkhiṃ,
‘divaso ajjuposatho.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_139SC 5Ms 21Cp_139Msdiv 129Dānāni paṭiyādetha,
dakkhiṇeyyassa dātave;
Datvā dānaṃ dakkhiṇeyye,
upavassathuposathaṃ’.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_140SC 6Ms 21Cp_140Msdiv 130Te me sādhūti vatvāna,
yathāsatti yathābalaṃ;
Dānāni paṭiyādetvā,
dakkhiṇeyyaṃ gavesisuṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_141SC 7Ms 21Cp_141Msdiv 131Ahaṃ nisajja cintesiṃ,
dānaṃ dakkhiṇanucchavaṃ;
‘Yadihaṃ labhe dakkhiṇeyyaṃ,
kiṃ me dānaṃ bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_142SC 8Ms 21Cp_142Msdiv 132Na me atthi tilā muggā,
māsā vā taṇḍulā ghataṃ;
Ahaṃ tiṇena yāpemi,
na sakkā tiṇa dātave.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_143SC 9Ms 21Cp_143Msdiv 133Yadi koci eti dakkhiṇeyyo,
Bhikkhāya mama santike;
Dajjāhaṃ sakamattānaṃ,
Na so tuccho gamissati’.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_144SC 10Ms 21Cp_144Msdiv 134Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
sakko brāhmaṇavaṇṇinā;
Āsayaṃ me upāgacchi,
dānavīmaṃsanāya me.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_145SC 11Ms 21Cp_145Msdiv 135Tamahaṃ disvāna santuṭṭho,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Sādhu khosi anuppatto,
ghāsahetu mamantike.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_146SC 12Ms 21Cp_146Msdiv 136Adinnapubbaṃ dānavaraṃ,
ajja dassāmi te ahaṃ;
Tuvaṃ sīlaguṇūpeto,
ayuttaṃ te paraheṭhanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Cp_147SC 13Ms 21Cp_147Msdiv 137Ehi aggiṃ padīpehi,
nānākaṭṭhe samānaya;
Ahaṃ pacissamattānaṃ,
pakkaṃ tvaṃ bhakkhayissasi’.

SC 14Ms 21Cp_148SC 14Ms 21Cp_148Msdiv 138‘Sādhū’ti so haṭṭhamano,
nānākaṭṭhe samānayi;
Mahantaṃ akāsi citakaṃ,
katvā aṅgāragabbhakaṃ.

SC 15Ms 21Cp_149SC 15Ms 21Cp_149Msdiv 139Aggiṃ tattha padīpesi,
yathā so khippaṃ mahā bhave;
Phoṭetvā rajagate gatte,
ekamantaṃ upāvisiṃ.

SC 16Ms 21Cp_150SC 16Ms 21Cp_150Msdiv 140Yadā mahākaṭṭhapuñjo,
āditto dhamadhamāyati;
Taduppatitvā papatiṃ,
majjhe jālasikhantare.

Paviṭṭhaṃ yassa kassaci;
Sameti darathapariḷāhaṃ,
Assādaṃ deti pīti ca.

SC 18Ms 21Cp_152SC 18Ms 21Cp_152Msdiv 142Tatheva jalitaṃ aggiṃ,
paviṭṭhassa mamaṃ tadā;
Sabbaṃ sameti darathaṃ,
yathā sītodakaṃ viya.

SC 19Ms 21Cp_153SC 19Ms 21Cp_153Msdiv 143Chaviṃ cammaṃ maṃsaṃ nhāruṃ,
Aṭṭhiṃ hadayabandhanaṃ;
Kevalaṃ sakalaṃ kāyaṃ,
Brāhmaṇassa adāsahan”ti. (143)

imaṃ pārami pūrayiṃ.
