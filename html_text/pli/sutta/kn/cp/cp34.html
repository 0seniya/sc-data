Ekarājacariya

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 35 Ms 21Cp_395Msdiv 114“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
ekarājāti vissuto;
Paramaṃ sīlaṃ adhiṭṭhāya,
pasāsāmi mahāmahiṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_396Msdiv 115Dasa kusalakammapathe,
vattāmi anavasesato;
Catūhi saṅgahavatthūhi,
saṅgaṇhāmi mahājanaṃ.

SC 3PTS 1st ed. 102 Ms 21Cp_397Msdiv 116Evaṃ me appamattassa,
idha loke parattha ca;
Dabbaseno upagantvā,
acchindanto puraṃ mama.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_398Msdiv 117Rājūpajīve nigame,
sabalaṭṭhe saraṭṭhake;
Sabbaṃ hatthagataṃ katvā,
kāsuyā nikhaṇī mamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_399Msdiv 118Amaccamaṇḍalaṃ rajjaṃ,
phītaṃ antepuraṃ mama;
Acchinditvāna gahitaṃ,
piyaṃ puttaṃva passahaṃ;
Mettāya me samo natthi,
esā me mettāpāramī”ti.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_400Ekarājacariyaṃ cuddasamaṃ.
