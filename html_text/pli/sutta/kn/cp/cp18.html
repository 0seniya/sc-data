Dhammadevaputtacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_234SC 1Ms 21Cp_234Msdiv 66 “Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
mahāpakkho mahiddhiko;
Dhammo nāma mahāyakkho,
sabbalokānukampako.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_235SC 2Ms 21Cp_235Msdiv 67Dasakusalakammapathe,
samādapento mahājanaṃ;
Carāmi gāmanigamaṃ,
samitto saparijjano.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_236SC 3Ms 21Cp_236Msdiv 68Pāpo kadariyo yakkho,
dīpento dasa pāpake;
Sopettha mahiyā carati,
samitto saparijjano.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_237SC 4Ms 21Cp_237Msdiv 69Dhammavādī adhammo ca,
ubho paccanikā mayaṃ;
Dhure dhuraṃ ghaṭṭayantā,
samimhā paṭipathe ubho.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_238SC 5Ms 21Cp_238Msdiv 70Kalaho vattatī bhesmā,
kalyāṇapāpakassa ca;
Maggā okkamanatthāya,
mahāyuddho upaṭṭhito.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_239SC 6Ms 21Cp_239Msdiv 71Yadihaṃ tassa kuppeyyaṃ,
yadi bhinde tapoguṇaṃ;
Sahaparijanaṃ tassa,
rajabhūtaṃ kareyyahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_240SC 7Ms 21Cp_240Msdiv 72Api cāhaṃ sīlarakkhāya,
nibbāpetvāna mānasaṃ;
Saha janenokkamitvā,
pathaṃ pāpassa dāsahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_241SC 8Ms 21Cp_241Msdiv 73Saha pathato okkante,
katvā cittassa nibbutiṃ;
Vivaraṃ adāsi pathavī,
pāpayakkhassa tāvade”ti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 21Cp_242Dhammadevaputtacariyaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
