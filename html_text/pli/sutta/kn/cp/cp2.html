Saṅkhacariya

SC 1PTS 1st ed. 74 PTS 2nd ed. 2 Ms 21Cp_13SC 1PTS 1st ed. 74 PTS 2nd ed. 2 Ms 21Cp_13Msdiv 11“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
Brāhmaṇo saṅkhasavhayo;
Mahāsamuddaṃ taritukāmo,
Upagacchāmi paṭṭanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_14SC 2Ms 21Cp_14Msdiv 12Tatthaddasaṃ paṭipathe,
Sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Kantāraddhānaṃ paṭipannaṃ,
Tattāya kaṭhinabhūmiyā.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_15SC 3Ms 21Cp_15Msdiv 13Tamahaṃ paṭipathe disvā,
imamatthaṃ vicintayiṃ;
‘Idaṃ khettaṃ anuppattaṃ,
puññakāmassa jantuno.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_16SC 4Ms 21Cp_16Msdiv 14Yathā kassako puriso,
khettaṃ disvā mahāgamaṃ;
Tattha bījaṃ na ropeti,
na so dhaññena atthiko.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_17SC 5Ms 21Cp_17Msdiv 15Evamevāhaṃ puññakāmo,
Disvā khettavaruttamaṃ;
Yadi tattha kāraṃ na karomi,
Nāhaṃ puññena atthiko.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_18SC 6Ms 21Cp_18Msdiv 16Yathā amacco muddikāmo,
rañño antepure jane;
Na deti tesaṃ dhanadhaññaṃ,
muddito parihāyati.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_19SC 7Ms 21Cp_19Msdiv 17Evamevāhaṃ puññakāmo,
Vipulaṃ disvāna dakkhiṇaṃ;
Yadi tassa dānaṃ na dadāmi,
Parihāyissāmi puññato’.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_20SC 8Ms 21Cp_20Msdiv 18Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
orohitvā upāhanā;
Tassa pādāni vanditvā,
adāsiṃ chattupāhanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_21SC 9Ms 21Cp_21Msdiv 19Tenevāhaṃ sataguṇato,
Sukhumālo sukhedhito;
Api ca dānaṃ paripūrento,
Evaṃ tassa adāsahan”ti.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_22SC 10Ms 21Cp_22Saṅkhacariyaṃ dutiyaṃ.
