  • Dānapāramī 8

Sivirājacariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_59Msdiv 51“Ariṭṭhasavhaye nagare,
sivināmāsi khattiyo;
Nisajja pāsādavare,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 2PTS 2nd ed. 6 Ms 21Cp_60Msdiv 52‘Yaṃ kiñci mānusaṃ dānaṃ,
adinnaṃ me na vijjati;
Yopi yāceyya maṃ cakkhuṃ,
dadeyyaṃ avikampito’.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_61Msdiv 53Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
sakko devānamissaro;
Nisinno devaparisāya,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_62Msdiv 54‘Nisajja pāsādavare,
sivirājā mahiddhiko;
Cintento vividhaṃ dānaṃ,
adeyyaṃ so na passati.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_63Msdiv 55Tathaṃ nu vitathaṃ netaṃ,
handa vīmaṃsayāmi taṃ;
Muhuttaṃ āgameyyātha,
yāva jānāmi taṃ manaṃ’.

SC 6Ms 21Cp_64Msdiv 56Pavedhamāno palitasiro,
Valigatto jarāturo;
Andhavaṇṇova hutvāna,
Rājānaṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 7PTS 1st ed. 78 Ms 21Cp_65Msdiv 57So tadā paggahetvāna,
vāmaṃ dakkhiṇabāhu ca;
Sirasmiṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_66Msdiv 58‘Yācāmi taṃ mahārāja,
dhammika raṭṭhavaḍḍhana;
Tava dānaratā kitti,
uggatā devamānuse.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_67Msdiv 59Ubhopi nettā nayanā,
andhā upahatā mama;
Ekaṃ me nayanaṃ dehi,
tvampi ekena yāpaya’.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_68Msdiv 60Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
Katañjalī vedajāto,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_69Msdiv 61‘Idānāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
pāsādato idhāgato;
Tvaṃ mama cittamaññāya,
nettaṃ yācitumāgato.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_70Msdiv 62Aho me mānasaṃ siddhaṃ,
saṅkappo paripūrito;
Adinnapubbaṃ dānavaraṃ,
ajja dassāmi yācake.

SC 13Ms 21Cp_71Msdiv 63Ehi sivaka uṭṭhehi,
mā dandhayi mā pavedhayi;
Ubhopi nayanaṃ dehi,
uppāṭetvā vaṇibbake’.

SC 14Ms 21Cp_72Msdiv 64Tato so codito mayhaṃ,
sivako vacanaṃ karo;
Uddharitvāna pādāsi,
tālamiñjaṃva yācake.

SC 15Ms 21Cp_73Msdiv 65Dadamānassa dentassa,
dinnadānassa me sato;
Cittassa aññathā natthi,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 16Ms 21Cp_74Msdiv 66Na me dessā ubho cakkhū,
attā na me na dessiyo;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā cakkhuṃ adāsahan”ti.

SC 17Ms 21Cp_75Sivirājacariyaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
