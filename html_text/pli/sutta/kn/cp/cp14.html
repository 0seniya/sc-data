Cūḷabodhicariya

SC 1Ms 21Cp_190Msdiv 26“Punāparaṃ yadā homi,
cūḷabodhi susīlavā;
Bhavaṃ disvāna bhayato,
nekkhammaṃ abhinikkhamiṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Cp_191Msdiv 27Yā me dutiyikā āsi,
brāhmaṇī kanakasannibhā;
Sāpi vaṭṭe anapekkhā,
nekkhammaṃ abhinikkhami.

SC 3Ms 21Cp_192Msdiv 28Nirālayā chinnabandhū,
anapekkhā kule gaṇe;
Carantā gāmanigamaṃ,
bārāṇasimupāgamuṃ.

SC 4Ms 21Cp_193Msdiv 29Tattha vasāma nipakā,
asaṃsaṭṭhā kule gaṇe;
Nirākule appasadde,
rājuyyāne vasāmubho.

SC 5Ms 21Cp_194Msdiv 30Uyyānadassanaṃ gantvā,
rājā addasa brāhmaṇiṃ;
Upagamma mamaṃ pucchi,
‘tuyhesā kā kassa bhariyā’.

SC 6PTS 2nd ed. 17 Ms 21Cp_195Msdiv 31Evaṃ vutte, ahaṃ tassa,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Na mayhaṃ bhariyā esā,
sahadhammā ekasāsanī’.

SC 7Ms 21Cp_196Msdiv 32Tissā sārattagadhito,
gāhāpetvāna ceṭake;
Nippīḷayanto balasā,
antepuraṃ pavesayi.

SC 8Ms 21Cp_197Msdiv 33Odapattakiyā mayhaṃ,
sahajā ekasāsanī;
Ākaḍḍhitvā nayantiyā,
kopo me upapajjatha.

SC 9Ms 21Cp_198Msdiv 34Saha kope samuppanne,
sīlabbatamanussariṃ;
Tattheva kopaṃ niggaṇhiṃ,
nādāsiṃ vaḍḍhitūpari.

SC 10Ms 21Cp_199Msdiv 35Yadi naṃ brāhmaṇiṃ koci,
koṭṭeyya tiṇhasattiyā;
Neva sīlaṃ pabhindeyyaṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 11Ms 21Cp_200Msdiv 36Na mesā brāhmaṇī dessā,
napi me balaṃ na vijjati;
Sabbaññutaṃ piyaṃ mayhaṃ,
tasmā sīlānurakkhisan”ti.

SC 12Ms 21Cp_201Cūḷabodhicariyaṃ catutthaṃ.
