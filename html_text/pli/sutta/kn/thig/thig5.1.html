Therīgāthā

Pañcakanipāta

1. Aññata­ra­therī­gāthā

“Paṇ­ṇa­vīsa­ti­vassāni,
yato pabbajitā ahaṃ;
­Nāccha­rā­saṅghā­ta­mattampi,
cittas­sū­pasa­maj­jhagaṃ.

Aladdhā cetaso santiṃ,
­kāmarā­ge­navas­sutā;
Bāhā paggayha kandantī,
vihāraṃ pāvisiṃ ahaṃ.

Sā bhikkhuniṃ upāgacchiṃ,
yā me saddhāyikā ahu;
Sā me dhammamadesesi,
khan­dhā­yatana­dhātuyo.

Tassā dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
ekamante upāvisiṃ;
Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ.

Ceto­paric­ca­ñāṇañca,
sotadhātu visodhitā;
Iddhīpi me sacchikatā,
patto me āsavakkhayo;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Aññatarā therī ….
