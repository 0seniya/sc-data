7. Tiṇakuṭidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3800Msdiv 74“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
ahosiṃ parakammiko;
Parakammāyane yutto,
parabhattaṃ apassito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3801Msdiv 75Rahogato nisīditvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
Buddho loke samuppanno,
adhikāro ca natthi me.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3802Msdiv 76Kālo me gatiṃ sodhetuṃ,
khaṇo me paṭipādito;
Dukkho nirayasamphasso,
apuññānañhi pāṇinaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3803Msdiv 77Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
kammasāmiṃ upāgamiṃ;
Ekāhaṃ kammaṃ yācitvā,
vipinaṃ pāvisiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3804Msdiv 78Tiṇakaṭṭhañca valliñca,
āharitvānahaṃ tadā;
Tidaṇḍake ṭhapetvāna,
akaṃ tiṇakuṭiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3805Msdiv 79Saṃghassatthāya kuṭikaṃ,
niyyādetvāna taṃ ahaṃ;
Tadaheyeva āgantvā,
kammasāmiṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3806Msdiv 80Tena kammena sukatena,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ;
Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
kuṭikāya sunimmitaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3807Msdiv 81Sahassakaṇḍaṃ satabheṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayaṃ;
Satasahassaniyyūhā,
byamhe pātubhaviṃsu me.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3808Msdiv 82Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
pāsādo upatiṭṭhati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3809Msdiv 83Bhayaṃ vā chambhitattaṃ vā,
lomahaṃso na vijjati;
Tāsaṃ mama na jānāmi,
tiṇakuṭikāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3810Msdiv 84Sīhabyagghā ca dīpī ca,
acchakokataracchakā;
Sabbe maṃ parivajjenti,
tiṇakuṭikāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3811Msdiv 85Sarīsapā ca bhūtā ca,
ahī kumbhaṇḍarakkhasā;
Tepi maṃ parivajjenti,
tiṇakuṭikāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3812Msdiv 86Na pāpasupinassāpi,
sarāmi dassanaṃ mama;
Upaṭṭhitā sati mayhaṃ,
tiṇakuṭikāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3813Msdiv 87Tāyeva tiṇakuṭikāya,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
dhammaṃ sacchikariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3814Msdiv 88Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇakuṭikāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3815Msdiv 89Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3816Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇakuṭidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3817Tiṇakuṭidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
