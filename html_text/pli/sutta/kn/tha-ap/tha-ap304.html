2. Sabbagandhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3438Msdiv 5“Gandhamālaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vipassissa mahesino;
Adāsiṃ ujubhūtassa,
koseyyavatthamuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3439Msdiv 6Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ vatthamadadiṃ pure;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
gandhadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3440Msdiv 7Ito pannarase kappe,
suceḷo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3441Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3442Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sabbagandhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3443Sabbagandhiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
