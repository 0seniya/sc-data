8 Saḷalamaṇḍapiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4761SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4761Msdiv 449“Nibbute kakusandhamhi,
brāhmaṇamhi vusīmati;
Gahetvā saḷalaṃ mālaṃ,
maṇḍapaṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4762SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4762Msdiv 450Tāvatiṃsagato santo,
labhāmi byamhamuttamaṃ;
Aññe devetirocāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4763SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4763Msdiv 451Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
caṅkamanto ṭhito cahaṃ;
Channo saḷalapupphehi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4764SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4764Msdiv 452Imasmiṃyeva kappamhi,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4765SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4765Msdiv 453Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4766SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4766Msdiv 454Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4767SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4767Msdiv 455Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4768Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saḷalamaṇḍapiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4769Saḷalamaṇḍapiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
