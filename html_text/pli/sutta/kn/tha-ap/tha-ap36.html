4. Kāḷudāyittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1126Msdiv 48“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Addhānaṃ paṭipannassa,
carato cārikaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1127Msdiv 49Suphullaṃ padumaṃ gayha,
uppalaṃ mallikañcahaṃ;
Paramannaṃ gahetvāna,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1128Msdiv 50Paribhuñji mahāvīro,
paramannaṃ subhojanaṃ;
Tañca pupphaṃ gahetvāna,
janassa sampadassayi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1129Msdiv 51Iṭṭhaṃ kantaṃ piyaṃ loke,
jalajaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Sudukkaraṃ kataṃ tena,
yo me pupphaṃ adāsidaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1130Msdiv 52Yo pupphamabhiropesi,
paramannañcadāsi me;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1131Msdiv 53‘Dasa aṭṭha cakkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Uppalaṃ padumañcāpi,
mallikañca taduttari.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1132Msdiv 54Assa puññavipākena,
dibbagandhasamāyutaṃ;
Ākāse chadanaṃ katvā,
dhārayissati tāvade.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1133Msdiv 55Pañcavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ pañcasataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1134Msdiv 56Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1135Msdiv 57Sakakammābhiraddho so,
sukkamūlena codito;
Sakyānaṃ nandijanano,
ñātibandhu bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1136Msdiv 58So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1137Msdiv 59Paṭisambhidamanuppattaṃ,
katakiccamanāsavaṃ;
Gotamo lokabandhu taṃ,
etadagge ṭhapessati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1138Msdiv 60Padhānapahitatto so,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Udāyī nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1139Msdiv 61Rāgo doso ca moho ca,
māno makkho ca dhaṃsito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1140Msdiv 62Tosayiñcāpi sambuddhaṃ,
ātāpī nipako ahaṃ;
Pasādito ca sambuddho,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1141Msdiv 63Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1142Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kāḷudāyī thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1143Kāḷudāyittherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
