3 Sīhāsanabījiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5899Msdiv 17“Tissassāhaṃ bhagavato,
bodhirukkhamavandiyaṃ;
Paggayha bījaniṃ tattha,
sīhāsanamabījahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5900Msdiv 18Dvenavute ito kappe,
sīhāsanamabījahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bījanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5901Msdiv 19Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5902Msdiv 20Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5903Msdiv 21Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5904Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sīhāsanabījiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5905Sīhāsanabījiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
