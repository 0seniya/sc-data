10. Piyālaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6552Msdiv 104“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
vipine vicaraṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6553Msdiv 105Piyālaphalamādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Puññakkhettassa vīrassa,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6554Msdiv 106Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6555Msdiv 107Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6556Msdiv 108Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6557Msdiv 109Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5267)

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6558Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā piyālaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6559Piyālaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Kiṅkaṇipupphavaggo paññāsamo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6560Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6561Kiṅkaṇī paṃsukūlañca,
koraṇḍamatha kiṃsukaṃ;
Upaḍḍhadussī ghatado,
udakaṃ thūpakārako.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6562Naḷakārī ca navamo,
piyālaphaladāyako;
Satamekañca gāthānaṃ,
navakañca taduttari.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6563Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6564Metteyyavaggo bhaddāli,
sakiṃsammajjakopi ca;
Ekavihārī vibhītakī,
jagatī sālapupphiyo.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6565Naḷāgāraṃ paṃsukūlaṃ,
kiṅkaṇipupphiyo tathā;
Asīti dve ca gāthāyo,
catuddasasatāni ca.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6566Metteyyavaggadasakaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6567Pañcamasatakaṃ samattaṃ.
