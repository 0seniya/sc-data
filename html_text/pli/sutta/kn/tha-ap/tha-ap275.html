3 Caṅkoṭakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3236Msdiv 10“Mahāsamuddaṃ nissāya,
vasatī pabbatantare;
Paccuggantvāna katvāna,
caṅkoṭaka madāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3237Msdiv 11Siddhatthassa mahesino,
sabbasattānukampino;
Pupphacaṅkoṭakaṃ datvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3238Msdiv 12Catunnavutito kappe,
caṅkoṭakamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
caṅkoṭakassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3239Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3240Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā caṅkoṭakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3241Caṅkoṭakiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
