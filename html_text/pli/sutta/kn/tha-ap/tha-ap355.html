3. Kiṃsukapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3984Msdiv 11“Kiṃsukaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Buddhaṃ saritvā siddhatthaṃ,
ākāse abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3985Msdiv 12Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3986Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3987Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃsukapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3988Kiṃsukapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
