5. Gandhamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1796Msdiv 24“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
gandhathūpaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Sumanehi paṭicchannaṃ,
buddhānucchavikaṃ kataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1797Msdiv 25Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Indīvaraṃva jalitaṃ,
ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1798Msdiv 26Byagghūsabhaṃva pavaraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ;
Nisinnaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1799Msdiv 27Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho;
Catunnavutito kappe,
gandhamālaṃ yato adaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1800Msdiv 28Buddhe katassa kārassa,
phalenāhaṃ visesato;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1801Msdiv 29Cattārīsamhi ekūne,
kappe āsiṃsu soḷasa;
Devagandhasanāmā te,
rājāno cakkavattino.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1802Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1803Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1804Gandhamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
