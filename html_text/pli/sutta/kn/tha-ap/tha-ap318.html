6 Āsanasanthavikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3541SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3541Msdiv 26“Cetiyaṃ uttamaṃ nāma,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Araññe irīṇe vane,
andhāhiṇḍāmahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3542SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3542Msdiv 27Pavanā nikkhamantena,
diṭṭhaṃ sīhāsanaṃ mayā;
Ekaṃsaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3543SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3543Msdiv 28Divasabhāgaṃ thavitvāna,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
imaṃ vācaṃ udīrayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3544SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3544Msdiv 29‘Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Sabbaññūsi mahāvīra,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3545SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3545Msdiv 30Abhitthavitvā sikhinaṃ,
nimittakaraṇenahaṃ;
Āsanaṃ abhivādetvā,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3546SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3546Msdiv 31Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ thaviṃ vadataṃ varaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
thomanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3547SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3547Msdiv 32Sattavīse ito kappe,
atulā satta āsu te;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3548SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3548Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3549Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3549Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āsanasanthaviko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3550Āsanasanthavikattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
