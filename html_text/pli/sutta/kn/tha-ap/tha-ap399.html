7. Girimānandattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4729Msdiv 419“Bhariyā me kālaṅkatā,
putto sīvathikaṃ gato;
Mātā pitā matā bhātā,
ekacitamhi ḍayhare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4730Msdiv 420Tena sokena santatto,
kiso paṇḍu ahosahaṃ;
Cittakkhepo ca me āsi,
tena sokena aṭṭito.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4731Msdiv 421Sokasallaparetohaṃ,
vanantamupasaṅkamiṃ;
Pavattaphalaṃ bhuñjitvā,
rukkhamūle vasāmahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4732Msdiv 422Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
dukkhassantakaro jino;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgañchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4733Msdiv 423Padasaddaṃ suṇitvāna,
sumedhassa mahesino;
Paggahetvānahaṃ sīsaṃ,
ullokesiṃ mahāmuniṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4734Msdiv 424Upāgate mahāvīre,
pīti me udapajjatha;
Tadāsimekaggamano,
disvā taṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4735Msdiv 425Satiṃ paṭilabhitvāna,
paṇṇamuṭṭhimadāsahaṃ;
Nisīdi bhagavā tattha,
anukampāya cakkhumā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4736Msdiv 426Nisajja tattha bhagavā,
sumedho lokanāyako;
Dhammaṃ me kathayī buddho,
sokasallavinodanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4737Msdiv 427‘Anavhitā tato āguṃ,
ananuññātā ito gatā;
Yathāgatā tathā gatā,
tattha kā paridevanā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4738Msdiv 428Yathāpi pathikā sattā,
vassamānāya vuṭṭhiyā;
Sabhaṇḍā upagacchanti,
vassassāpatanāya te.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4739Msdiv 429Vasse ca te oramite,
sampayanti yadicchakaṃ;
Evaṃ mātā pitā tuyhaṃ,
tattha kā paridevanā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4740Msdiv 430Āgantukā pāhunakā,
caliteritakampitā;
Evaṃ mātā pitā tuyhaṃ,
tattha kā paridevanā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4741Msdiv 431Yathāpi urago jiṇṇaṃ,
hitvā gacchati saṃ tacaṃ;
Evaṃ mātā pitā tuyhaṃ,
saṃ tanuṃ idha hīyare’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4742Msdiv 432Buddhassa giramaññāya,
sokasallaṃ vivajjayiṃ;
Pāmojjaṃ janayitvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ avandahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4743Msdiv 433Vanditvāna mahānāgaṃ,
Pūjayiṃ girimañjariṃ;
Dibbagandhaṃ sampavantaṃ,
Sumedhaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4744Msdiv 434Pūjayitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Anussaraṃ guṇaggāni,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4745Msdiv 435‘Nittiṇṇosi mahāvīra,
sabbaññu lokanāyaka;
Sabbe satte uddharasi,
ñāṇena tvaṃ mahāmune.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4746Msdiv 436Vimatiṃ dveḷhakaṃ vāpi,
sañchindasi mahāmune;
Paṭipādesi me maggaṃ,
tava ñāṇena cakkhuma.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4747Msdiv 437Arahā vasipattā ca,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Antalikkhacarā dhīrā,
parivārenti tāvade.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4748Msdiv 438Paṭipannā ca sekhā ca,
phalaṭṭhā santi sāvakā;
Sūrodayeva padumā,
pupphanti tava sāvakā.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4749Msdiv 439Mahāsamuddovakkhobho,
atulopi duruttaro;
Evaṃ ñāṇena sampanno,
appameyyosi cakkhuma’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4750Msdiv 440Vanditvāhaṃ lokajinaṃ,
cakkhumantaṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Puthu disā namassanto,
paṭikuṭiko agañchahaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4751Msdiv 441Devalokā cavitvāna,
sampajāno patissato;
Okkamiṃ mātuyā kucchiṃ,
sandhāvanto bhavābhave.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4752Msdiv 442Agārā abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Ātāpī nipako jhāyī,
paṭisallānagocaro.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4753Msdiv 443Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
tosayitvā mahāmuniṃ;
Candovabbhaghanā mutto,
vicarāmi ahaṃ sadā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4754Msdiv 444Vivekamanuyuttomhi,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_4755Msdiv 445Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4756Msdiv 446Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4757Msdiv 447Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_4758Msdiv 448Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_4759Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā girimānando thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_4760Girimānandattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
