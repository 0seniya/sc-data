7. Lasuṇadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1173Msdiv 89“Himavantassāvidūre,
tāpaso āsahaṃ tadā;
Lasuṇaṃ upajīvāmi,
lasuṇaṃ mayhabhojanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1174Msdiv 90Khāriyo pūrayitvāna,
saṃghārāmamagacchahaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
saṃghassa lasuṇaṃ adaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1175Msdiv 91Vipassissa naraggassa,
sāsane niratassahaṃ;
Saṃghassa lasuṇaṃ datvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1176Msdiv 92Ekanavutito kappe,
lasuṇaṃ yamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
lasuṇassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1177Msdiv 93Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1178Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā lasuṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1179Lasuṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
