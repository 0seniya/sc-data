6 Tiṇasantharadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1620Msdiv 22“Himavantassāvidūre,
mahājātassaro ahu;
Satapattehi sañchanno,
nānāsakuṇamālayo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1621Msdiv 23Tamhi nhatvā ca pitvā ca,
avidūre vasāmahaṃ;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1622Msdiv 24Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Abbhato oruhitvāna,
bhūmiyaṃṭhāsi tāvade.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1623Msdiv 25Visāṇena tiṇaṃ gayha,
nisīdanamadāsahaṃ;
Nisīdi bhagavā tattha,
tisso lokagganāyako.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1624Msdiv 26Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
avandi lokanāyakaṃ;
Paṭikuṭiko apasakkiṃ,
nijjhāyanto mahāmuniṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1625Msdiv 27Tena cittappasādena,
nimmānaṃ upapajjahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.122Ms 20Ap1_1626Msdiv 28Ito dutiyake kappe,
miga sammatakhattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1627Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1628Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇasantharadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1629Tiṇasantharadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
