4. Taraṇiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2770Msdiv 15“Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
gaṅgātīramupāgami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2771Msdiv 16Samatitti kākapeyyā,
gaṅgā āsi duruttarā;
Uttārayiṃ bhikkhusaṃghaṃ,
buddhañca dvipaduttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2772Msdiv 17Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
taraṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2773Msdiv 18Teraseto kappasate,
pañca sabbobhavā ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2774Msdiv 19Pacchime ca bhave asmiṃ,
jātohaṃ brāhmaṇe kule;
Saddhiṃ tīhi sahāyehi,
pabbajiṃ satthu sāsane.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2775Msdiv 20Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2776Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā taraṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2777Taraṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
