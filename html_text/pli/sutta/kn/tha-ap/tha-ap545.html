3. Sīvalittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7391Msdiv 54“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7392Msdiv 55Sīlaṃ tassa asaṅkheyyaṃ,
samādhi vajirūpamo;
Asaṅkheyyaṃ ñāṇavaraṃ,
vimutti ca anopamā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7393Msdiv 56Manujāmaranāgānaṃ,
brahmānañca samāgame;
Samaṇabrāhmaṇākiṇṇe,
dhammaṃ desesi nāyako.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7394Msdiv 57Sasāvakaṃ mahālābhiṃ,
puññavantaṃ jutindharaṃ;
Ṭhapesi etadaggamhi,
parisāsu visārado.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7395Msdiv 58Tadāhaṃ khattiyo āsiṃ,
nagare haṃsasavhaye;
Sutvā jinassa taṃ vākyaṃ,
sāvakassa guṇaṃ bahuṃ.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.493Ms 20Ap1_7396Msdiv 59Nimantayitvā sattāhaṃ,
bhojayitvā sasāvakaṃ;
Mahādānaṃ daditvāna,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7397Msdiv 60Tadā maṃ vinataṃ pāde,
disvāna purisāsabho;
Sarena mahatā vīro,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7398Msdiv 61‘Tato jinassa vacanaṃ,
sotukāmā mahājanā;
Devadānavagandhabbā,
brahmāno ca mahiddhikā’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7399Msdiv 62Samaṇabrāhmaṇā ceva,
namassiṃsu katañjalī;
‘Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7400Msdiv 63Khattiyena mahādānaṃ,
dinnaṃ sattāhikampi vo;
Sotukāmā phalaṃ tassa,
byākarohi mahāmune’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7401Msdiv 64Tato avoca bhagavā,
‘suṇātha mama bhāsitaṃ;
Appameyyamhi buddhamhi,
sasaṃghamhi patiṭṭhitā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7402Msdiv 65Dakkhiṇā tāya ko vattā,
appameyyaphalā hi sā;
Api ce sa mahābhogo,
ṭhānaṃ pattheti uttamaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7403Msdiv 66Lābhī vipulalābhānaṃ,
yathā bhikkhu sudassano;
Tathāhampi bhaveyyanti,
lacchase taṃ anāgate.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7404Msdiv 67Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7405Msdiv 68Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sīvali nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7406Msdiv 69Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsūpago ahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7407Msdiv 70Ekanavutito kappe,
vipassī lokanāyako;
Uppajji cārudassano,
sabbadhammavipassako.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7408Msdiv 71Tadāhaṃ bandhumatiyaṃ,
kulassaññatarassa ca;
Dayito passito ceva,
āsiṃ kammantavāvaṭo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7409Msdiv 72Tadā aññataro pūgo,
vipassissa mahesino;
Parivesaṃ akārayi,
mahantamativissutaṃ.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 2.494Ms 20Ap1_7410Msdiv 73Niṭṭhite ca mahādāne,
daduṃ khajjakasañhitaṃ;
Navaṃ dadhiṃ madhuñceva,
vicinaṃ neva addasuṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7411Msdiv 74Tadāhaṃ taṃ gahetvāna,
navaṃ dadhiṃ madhumpi ca;
Kammassāmigharaṃ gacchiṃ,
tamesantā mamaddasuṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7412Msdiv 75Sahassamapi datvāna,
nālabhiṃsu ca taṃ dvayaṃ;
Tatohaṃ evaṃ cintesiṃ,
‘netaṃ hessati orakaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7413Msdiv 76Yathā ime janā sabbe,
sakkaronti tathāgataṃ;
Ahampi kāraṃ kassāmi,
sasaṃghe lokanāyake’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7414Msdiv 77Tadāhamevaṃ cintetvā,
dadhiṃ madhuñca ekato;
Madditvā lokanāthassa,
sasaṃghassa adāsahaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7415Msdiv 78Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7416Msdiv 79Punāhaṃ bārāṇasiyaṃ,
rājā hutvā mahāyaso;
Sattukassa tadā duṭṭho,
dvārarodhamakārayiṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7417Msdiv 80Tadā tapassino ruddhā,
ekāhaṃ rakkhitā ahuṃ;
Tato tassa vipākena,
pāpatiṃ nirayaṃ bhusaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7418Msdiv 81Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jātohaṃ koliye pure;
Suppavāsā ca me mātā,
mahāli licchavī pitā.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7419Msdiv 82Khattiye puññakammena,
dvārarodhassa vāhasā;
Satta vassāni nivasiṃ,
mātukucchimhi dukkhito.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7420Msdiv 83Sattāhaṃ dvāramūḷhohaṃ,
mahādukkhasamappito;
Mātā me chandadānena,
evaṃ āsi sudukkhitā.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7421Msdiv 84Suvatthitohaṃ nikkhanto,
buddhena anukampito;
Nikkhantadivaseyeva,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7422Msdiv 85Upajjhā sāriputto me,
moggallāno mahiddhiko;
Kese oropayanto me,
anusāsi mahāmati.

SC 33PTS vp Pali 2.495Ms 20Ap1_7423Msdiv 86Kesesu chijjamānesu,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Devā nāgā manussā ca,
paccaye upanenti me.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7424Msdiv 87Padumuttaranāthañca,
vipassiñca vināyakaṃ;
Yaṃ pūjayiṃ pamudito,
paccayehi visesato.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7425Msdiv 88Tato tesaṃ visesena,
kammānaṃ vipuluttamaṃ;
Lābhaṃ labhāmi sabbattha,
vane gāme jale thale.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7426Msdiv 89Revataṃ dassanatthāya,
yadā yāti vināyako;
Tiṃsabhikkhusahassehi,
saha lokagganāyako.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7427Msdiv 90Tadā devopaṇītehi,
mamatthāya mahāmati;
Paccayehi mahāvīro,
sasaṃgho lokanāyako.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7428Msdiv 91Upaṭṭhito mayā buddho,
gantvā revatamaddasa;
Tato jetavanaṃ gantvā,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7429Msdiv 92‘Lābhīnaṃ sīvali aggo,
mama sissesu bhikkhavo’;
Sabbalokahito satthā,
kittayī parisāsu maṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7430Msdiv 93Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_7431Msdiv 94Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_7432Msdiv 95Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_7433Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sīvalithero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_7434Sīvalittherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
