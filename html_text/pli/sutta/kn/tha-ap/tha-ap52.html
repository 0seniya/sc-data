10. Cundattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1352Msdiv 125“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Agghiyaṃ kārayitvāna,
jātipupphehi chādayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1353Msdiv 126Niṭṭhāpetvāna taṃ pupphaṃ,
buddhassa upanāmayiṃ;
Pupphāvasesaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1354Msdiv 127Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pupphagghiyamupānayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1355Msdiv 128Vitiṇṇakaṅkho sambuddho,
tiṇṇoghehi purakkhato;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1356Msdiv 129‘Dibbagandhaṃ pavāyantaṃ,
yo me pupphagghiyaṃ adā;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1357Msdiv 130Ito cuto ayaṃ poso,
devasaṅghapurakkhato;
Jātipupphehi parikiṇṇo,
devalokaṃ gamissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1358Msdiv 131Ubbiddhaṃ bhavanaṃ tassa,
Sovaṇṇañca maṇīmayaṃ;
Byamhaṃ pātubhavissati,
Puññakammappabhāvitaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1359Msdiv 132Catusattatikkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Anubhossati sampattiṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1360Msdiv 133Pathabyā rajjaṃ tisataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati;
Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1361Msdiv 134Dujjayo nāma nāmena,
hessati manujādhipo;
Anubhotvāna taṃ puññaṃ,
sakakammaṃ apassito.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1362Msdiv 135Vinipātaṃ agantvāna,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Hiraññaṃ tassa nicitaṃ,
koṭisatamanappakaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1363Msdiv 136Nibbattissati yonimhi,
brāhmaṇe so bhavissati;
Vaṅgantassa suto dhīmā,
sāriyā oraso piyo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1364Msdiv 137So ca pacchā pabbajitvā,
aṅgīrasassa sāsane;
Cūḷacundoti nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1365Msdiv 138Sāmaṇerova so santo,
khīṇāsavo bhavissati;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1366Msdiv 139Upaṭṭhahiṃ mahāvīraṃ,
aññe ca pesale bahū;
Bhātaraṃ me cupaṭṭhāsiṃ,
uttamatthassa pattiyā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1367Msdiv 140Bhātaraṃ me upaṭṭhitvā,
dhātuṃ pattamhi ohiya;
Sambuddhaṃ upanāmesiṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1368Msdiv 141Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
buddho loke sadevake;
Sandassayanto taṃ dhātuṃ,
kittayi aggasāvakaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1369Msdiv 142Cittañca suvimuttaṃ me,
saddhā mayhaṃ patiṭṭhitā;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1370Msdiv 143Paṭisambhidānuppattā,
Vimokkhāpi ca phassitā;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
Kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1246)

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1371Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā cundo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_1372Cundattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1373Upālivaggo pañcamo.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1374Tassuddānaṃ

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_1375Upāli soṇo bhaddiyo,
sanniṭṭhāpakahatthiyo;
Chadanaṃ seyyacaṅkamaṃ,
subhaddo cundasavhayo;
Gāthāsataṃ satālīsaṃ,
catasso ca taduttari.
