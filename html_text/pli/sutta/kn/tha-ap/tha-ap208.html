6. Udakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2784Msdiv 25“Bhuñjantaṃ samaṇaṃ disvā,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ;
Ghaṭenodakamādāya,
siddhatthassa adāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2785Msdiv 26Nimmalo homahaṃ ajja,
vimalo khīṇasaṃsayo;
Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
phalaṃ nibbattate mama.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2786Msdiv 27Catunnavutito kappe,
udakaṃ yamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dakadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2787Msdiv 28Ekasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
ekova vimalo ahu;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2788Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2789Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2790Udakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
