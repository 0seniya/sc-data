2. Paṃsukūlapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6439Msdiv 8“Himavantassāvidūre,
udaṅgaṇo nāma pabbato;
Tatthaddasaṃ paṃsukūlaṃ,
dumaggamhi vilambitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6440Msdiv 9Tīṇi kiṅkaṇipupphāni,
ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
paṃsukūlamapūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6441Msdiv 10Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6442Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pūjitvā arahaddhajaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6443Msdiv 12Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6444Msdiv 13Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6445Msdiv 14Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6446Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṃsukūlapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6447Paṃsukūlapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
