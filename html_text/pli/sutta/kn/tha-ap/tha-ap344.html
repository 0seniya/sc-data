2 Tīṇuppalamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3893SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3893Msdiv 11 “Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ vānaro tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3894SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3894Msdiv 12Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ;
Lakkhaṇabyañjanūpetaṃ,
disvā attamano ahuṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3895SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3895Msdiv 13Udaggacitto sumano,
pītiyā haṭṭhamānaso;
Tīṇi uppalapupphāni,
matthake abhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3896SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3896Msdiv 14Pupphāni abhiropetvā,
vipassissa mahesino;
Sagāravo bhavitvāna,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3897SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3897Msdiv 15Gacchanto paṭikuṭiko,
vippasannena cetasā;
Selantare patitvāna,
pāpuṇiṃ jīvitakkhayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3898SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3898Msdiv 16Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3899SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3899Msdiv 17Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3900SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3900Msdiv 18Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3901SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3901Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
