4 Pupphacchadaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2213Msdiv 26“Sunando nāma nāmena,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Ajjhāyako yācayogo,
vājapeyyaṃ ayājayi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2214Msdiv 27Padumuttaro lokavidū,
aggo kāruṇiko isi;
Janataṃ anukampanto,
ambare caṅkamī tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2215Msdiv 28Caṅkamitvāna sambuddho,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Mettāya aphari satte,
appamāṇe nirūpadhi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2216Msdiv 29Vaṇṭe chetvāna pupphāni,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Sabbe sisse samānetvā,
ākāse ukkhipāpayi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2217Msdiv 30Yāvatā nagaraṃ āsi,
pupphānaṃ chadanaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
sattāhaṃ na vigacchatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2218Msdiv 31Teneva sukkamūlena,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
tiṇṇo loke visattikaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2219Msdiv 32Ekārase kappasate,
pañcatiṃsāsu khattiyā;
Ambaraṃsasanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2220Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2221Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphacchadaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2222Pupphacchadaniyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
