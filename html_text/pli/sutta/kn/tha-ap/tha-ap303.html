1. Padumakesariyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3432Msdiv 1“Isisaṃghe ahaṃ pubbe,
āsiṃ mātaṅgavāraṇo;
Mahesīnaṃ pasādena,
padmakesaramokiriṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3433Msdiv 2Paccekajinaseṭṭhesu,
dhutarāgesu tādisu;
Tesu cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3434Msdiv 3Ekanavutito kappe,
kesaraṃ okiriṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3435Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3436Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumakesariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3437Padumakesariyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
