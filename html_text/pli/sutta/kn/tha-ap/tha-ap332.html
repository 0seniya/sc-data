10. Saparivārachattadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3686Msdiv 82“Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Ākāse jalavuṭṭhīva,
vassate dhammavuṭṭhiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3687Msdiv 83Tamaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
desentaṃ amataṃ padaṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
agamāsiṃ sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3688Msdiv 84Chattaṃ alaṅkataṃ gayha,
upagacchiṃ naruttamaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3689Msdiv 85Susaṅgahitayānaṃva,
dantova sāvakuttamo;
Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
matthake sampatiṭṭhahi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3690Msdiv 86Anukampako kāruṇiko,
buddho lokagganāyako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3691Msdiv 87‘Yena chattamidaṃ dinnaṃ,
alaṅkataṃ manoramaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
duggatiṃ so na gacchati.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.266Ms 20Ap1_3692Msdiv 88Sattakkhattuñca devesu,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Bāttiṃsakkhattuñca rājā,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3693Msdiv 89Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3694Msdiv 90Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3695Msdiv 91Buddhassa giramaññāya,
vācāsabhimudīritaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3696Msdiv 92Jahitvā mānusaṃ yoniṃ,
Dibbaṃ yoniṃ samajjhagaṃ;
Vimānamuttamaṃ mayhaṃ,
Abbhuggataṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3697Msdiv 93Vimānā nikkhamantassa,
setacchattaṃ dharīyati;
Tadā saññaṃ paṭilabhiṃ,
pubbakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3698Msdiv 94Devalokā cavitvāna,
manussattañca āgamiṃ;
Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
sattakappasatamhito.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3699Msdiv 95Tamhā kāyā cavitvāna,
āgacchiṃ tidasaṃ puraṃ;
Saṃsaritvānupubbena,
mānusaṃ punarāgamiṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3700Msdiv 96Okkantaṃ mātukucchiṃ maṃ,
setacchattaṃ adhārayuṃ;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3701Msdiv 97Sunando nāma nāmena,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Phalikaṃ chattamādāya,
sāvakaggassa so tadā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3702Msdiv 98Anumodi mahāvīro,
sāriputto mahākathī;
Sutvānumodanaṃ tassa,
pubbakammamanussariṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3703Msdiv 99Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Saritvā purimaṃ kammaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3704Msdiv 100Uṭṭhāya āsanā tamhā,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
imaṃ vācaṃ udīrayiṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3705Msdiv 101Satasahassito kappe,
buddho loke anuttaro;
Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_3706Msdiv 102Tassa chattaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vicittaṃ samalaṅkataṃ;
Ubho hatthehi paggaṇhi,
sayambhū aggapuggalo.

SC 22PTS vp Pali 1.267Ms 20Ap1_3707Msdiv 103Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthusampadā;
Ekacchattassa dānena,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_3708Msdiv 104Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_3709Msdiv 105Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2925)

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_3710Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saparivārachattadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_3711Saparivārachattadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_3712Umāpupphiyavaggo tettiṃsatimo.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_3713Tassuddānaṃ

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_3714Umāpupphañca pulinaṃ,
hāso yañño nimittako;
Saṃsāvako nigguṇḍī ca,
sumanaṃ pupphachattako;
Saparivārachatto ca,
gāthā sattasatuttarāti.
