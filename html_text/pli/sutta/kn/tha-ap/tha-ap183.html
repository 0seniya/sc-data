1. Kuṭajapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2581Msdiv 1“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Sataraṃsiṃva uggataṃ;
Disaṃ anuvilokentaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2582Msdiv 2Kuṭajaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
saṃvitthatasamotthataṃ;
Rukkhato ocinitvāna,
phussassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2583Msdiv 3Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2584Msdiv 4Ito sattarase kappe,
tayo āsuṃ supupphitā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2585Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2586Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kuṭajapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2587Kuṭajapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
