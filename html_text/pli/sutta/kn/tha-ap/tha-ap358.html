6. Ambayāgudāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4001Msdiv 22“Sataraṃsī nāma sambuddho,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,
bhikkhāya mamupāgami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4002Msdiv 23Paccekabuddhaṃ disvāna,
ambayāguṃ adāsahaṃ;
Vippasannamanaṃ tassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4003Msdiv 24Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ambayāguyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4004Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4005Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambayāgudāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4006Ambayāgudāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
