3. Koraṇḍapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6448Msdiv 15“Vanakammiko pure āsiṃ,
pitumātumatenahaṃ;
Pasumārena jīvāmi,
kusalaṃ me na vijjati.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6449Msdiv 16Mama āsayasāmantā,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Padāni tīṇi dassesi,
anukampāya cakkhumā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6450Msdiv 17Akkante ca pade disvā,
tissanāmassa satthuno;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pade cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6451Msdiv 18Koraṇḍaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
pādapaṃ dharaṇīruhaṃ;
Sakosakaṃ gahetvāna,
padaseṭṭhamapūjayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6452Msdiv 19Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6453Msdiv 20Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Koraṇḍakachavi homi,
suppabhāso bhavāmahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6454Msdiv 21Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
padapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6455Msdiv 22Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6456Msdiv 23Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6457Msdiv 24Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6458Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koraṇḍapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6459Koraṇḍapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
