5. Naḷāgārikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3919Msdiv 31“Himavantassāvidūre,
hārito nāma pabbato;
Sayambhū nārado nāma,
rukkhamūle vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3920Msdiv 32Naḷāgāraṃ karitvāna,
tiṇena chādayiṃ ahaṃ;
Caṅkamaṃ sodhayitvāna,
sayambhussa adāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3921Msdiv 33Catuddasasu kappesu,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Catusattatikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3922Msdiv 34Catusattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3923Msdiv 35Ubbiddhaṃ bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Indalaṭṭhīva uggataṃ;
Sahassathambhaṃ atulaṃ,
Vimānaṃ sapabhassaraṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3924Msdiv 36Dve sampattī anubhotvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3925Msdiv 37Padhānapahitattomhi,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3926Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3927Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā naḷāgāriko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3928Naḷāgārikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
