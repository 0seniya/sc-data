1 Sakacintaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1491Msdiv 1“Pavanaṃ kānanaṃ disvā,
appasaddamanāvilaṃ;
Isīnaṃ anuciṇṇaṃva,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1492Msdiv 2Thūpaṃ katvāna pulinaṃ,
nānāpupphaṃ samokiriṃ;
Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
nimmitaṃ abhivandahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1493Msdiv 3Sattaratanasampanno,
rājā raṭṭhamhi issaro;
Sakakammābhiraddhohaṃ,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1494Msdiv 4Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1495Msdiv 5Asītikappenantayaso,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
catudīpamhi issaro.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1496Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1497Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sakacintaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1498Sakacintaniyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
