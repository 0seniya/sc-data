1. Naḷamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6066Msdiv 1“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Vipinaggena gacchantaṃ,
Addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6067Msdiv 2Naḷamālaṃ gahetvāna,
nikkhamanto ca tāvade;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6068Msdiv 3Pasannacitto sumano,
naḷamālamapūjayiṃ;
Dakkhiṇeyyaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
sabbalokānukampakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6069Msdiv 4Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ mālamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6070Msdiv 5Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6071Msdiv 6Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6072Msdiv 7Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6073Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā naḷamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6074Naḷamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
