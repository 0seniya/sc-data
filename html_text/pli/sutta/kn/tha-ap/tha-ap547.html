5. Nandakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7485Msdiv 143“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7486Msdiv 144Hitāya sabbasattānaṃ,
sukhāya vadataṃ varo;
Atthāya purisājañño,
paṭipanno sadevake.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7487Msdiv 145Yasaggapatto sirimā,
kittivaṇṇabhato jino;
Pūjito sabbalokassa,
disā sabbāsu vissuto.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7488Msdiv 146Uttiṇṇavicikiccho so,
vītivattakathaṃkatho;
Paripuṇṇamanasaṅkappo,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7489Msdiv 147Anuppannassa maggassa,
uppādetā naruttamo;
Anakkhātañca akkhāsi,
asañjātañca sañjanī.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7490Msdiv 148Maggaññū maggavidū ca,
maggakkhāyī narāsabho;
Maggassa kusalo satthā,
sārathīnaṃ varuttamo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7491Msdiv 149Tadā mahākāruṇiko,
dhammaṃ desesi nāyako;
Nimugge kāmapaṅkamhi,
samuddharati pāṇine.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7492Msdiv 150Bhikkhunīnaṃ ovadane,
sāvakaṃ seṭṭhasammataṃ;
Vaṇṇayaṃ etadaggamhi,
paññapesi mahāmuni.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7493Msdiv 151Taṃ sutvāhaṃ pamudito,
nimantetvā tathāgataṃ;
Bhojayitvā sasaṃghaṃ taṃ,
patthayiṃ ṭhānamuttamaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7494Msdiv 152Tadā pamudito nātho,
maṃ avoca mahāisi;
‘Sukhī bhavassu dīghāvu,
lacchase taṃ manorathaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7495Msdiv 153Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7496Msdiv 154Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Nandako nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7497Msdiv 155Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsūpago ahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7498Msdiv 156Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jāto seṭṭhikule ahaṃ;
Sāvatthiyaṃ pure vare,
iddhe phīte mahaddhane.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7499Msdiv 157Purappavese sugataṃ,
disvā vimhitamānaso;
Jetārāmapaṭiggāhe,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7500Msdiv 158Nacireneva kālena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Tatohaṃ tiṇṇasaṃsāro,
sāsito sabbadassinā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7501Msdiv 159Bhikkhunīnaṃ dhammakathaṃ,
paṭipucchākariṃ ahaṃ;
Sāsitā tā mayā sabbā,
abhaviṃsu anāsavā.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7502Msdiv 160Satāni pañcanūnāni,
tadā tuṭṭho mahāhito;
Bhikkhunīnaṃ ovadataṃ,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7503Msdiv 161Satasahasse kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha;
Sumutto saravegova,
kilese jhāpayiṃ mama.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7504Msdiv 162Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7505Msdiv 163Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7506Msdiv 164Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7507Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nandako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7508Nandakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
