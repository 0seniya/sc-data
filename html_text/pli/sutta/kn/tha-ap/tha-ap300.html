8. Saraṇagamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3407SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3407Msdiv 36“Pabbate himavantamhi,
ahosiṃ luddako tadā;
Vipassiṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3408SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3408Msdiv 37Upāsitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
veyyāvaccamakāsahaṃ;
Saraṇañca upāgacchiṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3409SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3409Msdiv 38Ekanavutito kappe,
saraṇaṃ upagacchahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saraṇāgamanapphalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3410SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3410Msdiv 39Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3411Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saraṇagamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3412Saraṇagamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
