11. Raṭṭhapālattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7869Msdiv 179“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Varanāgo mayā dinno,
Īsādanto urūḷhavā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7870Msdiv 180Setacchattopasobhito,
sakappano sahatthipo;
Agghāpetvāna taṃ sabbaṃ,
saṃghārāmaṃ akārayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7871Msdiv 181Catupaññāsasahassāni,
pāsāde kārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Mahoghadānaṃ karitvāna,
niyyādesiṃ mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7872Msdiv 182Anumodi mahāvīro,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Sabbe jane hāsayanto,
desesi amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7873Msdiv 183Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7874Msdiv 184‘Catupaññāsasahassāni,
pāsāde kārayī ayaṃ;
Kathayissāmi vipākaṃ,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7875Msdiv 185Aṭṭhārasasahassāni,
kūṭāgārā bhavissare;
Byamhuttamamhi nibbattā,
sabbasoṇṇamayā ca te.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7876Msdiv 186Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Aṭṭhapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7877Msdiv 187Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7878Msdiv 188Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Aḍḍhe kule mahābhoge,
nibbattissati tāvade.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7879Msdiv 189So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Raṭṭhapāloti nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7880Msdiv 190Padhānapahitatto so,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7881Msdiv 191Uṭṭhāya abhinikkhamma,
jahitā bhogasampadā;
Kheḷapiṇḍeva bhogamhi,
pemaṃ mayhaṃ na vijjati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7882Msdiv 192Vīriyaṃ me dhuradhorayhaṃ,
yogakkhemādhivāhanaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7883Msdiv 193Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7884Msdiv 194Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7885Msdiv 195Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (6440)

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7886Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṭṭhapālo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7887Raṭṭhapālattherassāpadānaṃ ekādasamaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7888Yasavaggo chapaññāsamo.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7889Tassuddānaṃ

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7890Yaso nadīkassapo ca,
gayākimilavajjino;
Duve uttarā bhaddajī,
sivako upavāhano;
Raṭṭhapālo ekasataṃ,
gāthānaṃ pañcanavuti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7891Therāpadānaṃ samattaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7892
