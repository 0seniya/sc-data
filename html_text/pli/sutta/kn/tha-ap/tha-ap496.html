4. Kiṃsukapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6460Msdiv 25“Kiṃsukaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
ākāse abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6461Msdiv 26Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6462Msdiv 27Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6463Msdiv 28Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6464Msdiv 29Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6465Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6466Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃsukapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6467Kiṃsukapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
