6. Kadambapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4062SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4062Msdiv 20“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4063SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4063Msdiv 21Nisajja pāsādavare,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Kadambapupphaṃ paggayha,
vipassiṃ abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4064SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4064Msdiv 22Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4065SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4065Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4066Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4067Kadambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
