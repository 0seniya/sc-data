1 Ekavihārikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5664Msdiv 1“Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5665Msdiv 2Nippapañco nirālambo,
ākāsasamamānaso;
Suññatābahulo tādī,
animittarato vasī.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5666Msdiv 3Asaṅgacitto nikleso,
asaṃsaṭṭho kule gaṇe;
Mahākāruṇiko vīro,
vinayopāyakovido.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5667Msdiv 4Uyyutto parakiccesu,
vinayanto sadevake;
Nibbānagamanaṃ maggaṃ,
gatiṃ paṅkavisosanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5668Msdiv 5Amataṃ paramassādaṃ,
jarāmaccunivāraṇaṃ;
Mahāparisamajjhe so,
nisinno lokatārako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5669Msdiv 6Karavīkaruto nātho,
brahmaghoso tathāgato;
Uddharanto mahāduggā,
vippanaṭṭhe anāyake.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5670Msdiv 7Desento virajaṃ dhammaṃ,
diṭṭho me lokanāyako;
Tassa dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5671Msdiv 8Pabbajitvā tadāpāhaṃ,
cintento jinasāsanaṃ;
Ekakova vane ramme,
vasiṃ saṃsaggapīḷito.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5672Msdiv 9Sakkāyavūpakāso me,
hetubhūto mamābhavī;
Manaso vūpakāsassa,
saṃsaggabhayadassino.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 2.391Ms 20Ap1_5673Msdiv 10Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5674Msdiv 11Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5675Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5676Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekavihāriko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5677Ekavihārikattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
