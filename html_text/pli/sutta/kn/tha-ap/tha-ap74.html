2. Padasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1595Msdiv 5“Akkantañca padaṃ disvā,
tissassādiccabandhuno;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pade cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1596Msdiv 6Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
padasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1597Msdiv 7Ito sattamake kappe,
sumedho nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1598Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1599Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1600Padasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
