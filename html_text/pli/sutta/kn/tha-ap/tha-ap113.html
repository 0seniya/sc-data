1 Mahāparivārakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1941Msdiv 1“Vipassī nāma bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Aṭṭhasaṭṭhisahassehi,
pāvisi bandhumaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1942Msdiv 2Nagarā abhinikkhamma,
agamaṃ dīpacetiyaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1943Msdiv 3Cullāsītisahassāni,
yakkhā mayhaṃ upantike;
Upaṭṭhahanti sakkaccaṃ,
indaṃva tidasā gaṇā.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.147Ms 20Ap1_1944Msdiv 4Bhavanā abhinikkhamma,
dussaṃ paggayhahaṃ tadā;
Sirasā abhivādesiṃ,
tañcādāsiṃ mahesino.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1945Msdiv 5Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthu sampadā;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
vasudhāyaṃ pakampatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1946Msdiv 6Tañca acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādemi,
dvipadindamhi tādine.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1947Msdiv 7Sohaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
dussaṃ datvāna satthuno;
Saraṇañca upāgacchiṃ,
sāmacco saparijjano.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1948Msdiv 8Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1949Msdiv 9Ito pannarase kappe,
soḷasāsuṃ suvāhanā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1950Msdiv 10Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1951Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahāparivārako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1952Mahāparivārakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
