1. Tissametteyyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4847Msdiv 1“Pabbhārakūṭaṃ nissāya,
sobhito nāma tāpaso;
Pavattaphalaṃ bhuñjitvā,
vasati pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4848Msdiv 2Aggiṃ dāruṃ āharitvā,
ujjālesiṃ ahaṃ tadā;
Uttamatthaṃ gavesanto,
brahmalokūpapattiyā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4849Msdiv 3Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santike.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4850Msdiv 4‘Kiṃ karosi mahāpuñña,
dehi me aggidārukaṃ;
Ahamaggiṃ paricare,
tato me suddhi hohiti’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4851Msdiv 5‘Subhaddako tvaṃ manuje,
devate tvaṃ pajānasi;
Tuvaṃ aggiṃ paricara,
handa te aggidārukaṃ’.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4852Msdiv 6Tato kaṭṭhaṃ gahetvāna,
aggiṃ ujjālayī jino;
Na tattha kaṭṭhaṃ pajjhāyi,
pāṭiheraṃ mahesino.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4853Msdiv 7‘Na te aggi pajjalati,
āhutī te na vijjati;
Niratthakaṃ vataṃ tuyhaṃ,
aggiṃ paricarassu me’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4854Msdiv 8‘Kīdiso so mahāvīra,
aggi tava pavuccati;
Mayhampi kathayassetaṃ,
ubho paricarāmase’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4855Msdiv 9‘Hetudhammanirodhāya,
kilesasamaṇāya ca;
Issāmacchariyaṃ hitvā,
tayo ete mamāhutī’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4856Msdiv 10‘Kīdiso tvaṃ mahāvīra,
kathaṃ gottosi mārisa;
Ācārapaṭipatti te,
bāḷhaṃ kho mama ruccati’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4857Msdiv 11‘Khattiyamhi kule jāto,
abhiññāpāramiṃ gato;
Sabbāsavaparikkhīṇo,
natthi dāni punabbhavo’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4858Msdiv 12‘Yadi buddhosi sabbaññū,
pabhaṅkara tamonuda;
Namassissāmi taṃ deva,
dukkhassantakaro tuvaṃ’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4859Msdiv 13Pattharitvājinacammaṃ,
nisīdanamadāsahaṃ;
Nisīda nātha sabbaññu,
upaṭṭhissāmahaṃ tuvaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4860Msdiv 14Nisīdi bhagavā tattha,
ajinamhi suvitthate;
Nimantayitvā sambuddhaṃ,
pabbataṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4861Msdiv 15Khāribhārañca pūretvā,
tindukaphalamāhariṃ;
Madhunā yojayitvāna,
phalaṃ buddhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4862Msdiv 16Mama nijjhāyamānassa,
paribhuñji tadā jino;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādesiṃ,
pekkhanto lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4863Msdiv 17Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mamassame nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4864Msdiv 18‘Yo maṃ phalena tappesi,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4865Msdiv 19Pañcavīsatikkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4866Msdiv 20Tassa saṅkappamaññāya,
pubbakammasamaṅgino;
Annaṃ pānañca vatthañca,
sayanañca mahārahaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4867Msdiv 21Puññakammena saṃyuttā,
nibbattissanti tāvade;
Sadā pamudito cāyaṃ,
bhavissati anāmayo.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4868Msdiv 22Upapajjati yaṃ yoniṃ,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbattha sukhito hutvā,
manussattaṃ gamissati.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4869Msdiv 23Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;
Sambuddhaṃ upagantvāna,
arahā so bhavissati’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4870Msdiv 24Yato sarāmi attānaṃ,
yato pattosmi viññutaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4871Msdiv 25Varadhammamanuppatto,
rāgadose samūhaniṃ;
Sabbāsavaparikkhīṇo,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4872Msdiv 26Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_4873Msdiv 27Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4874Msdiv 28Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4875Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tissametteyyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_4876Tissametteyyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
