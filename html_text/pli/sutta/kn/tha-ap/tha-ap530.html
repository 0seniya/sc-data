8. Bodhisammajjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6859Msdiv 48“Ahaṃ pure bodhipattaṃ,
ujjhitaṃ cetiyaṅgaṇe;
Taṃ gahetvāna chaḍḍesiṃ,
alabhiṃ vīsatīguṇe.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6860Msdiv 49Tassa kammassa tejena,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Duve bhave saṃsarāmi,
devatte cāpi mānuse.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6861Msdiv 50Devalokā cavitvāna,
āgantvā mānusaṃ bhavaṃ;
Duve kule pajāyāmi,
khattiye cāpi brāhmaṇe.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6862Msdiv 51Aṅgapaccaṅgasampanno,
ārohapariṇāhavā;
Abhirūpo suci homi,
sampuṇṇaṅgo anūnako.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6863Msdiv 52Devaloke manusse vā,
jāto vā yattha katthaci;
Bhave suvaṇṇavaṇṇo ca,
uttattakanakūpamo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6864Msdiv 53Mudukā maddavā sniddhā,
sukhumā sukumārikā;
Chavi me sabbadā hoti,
bodhipatte suchaḍḍite.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6865Msdiv 54Yato kutoci gatīsu,
sarīre samudāgate;
Na limpati rajojallaṃ,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6866Msdiv 55Uṇhe vātātape tassa,
aggitāpena vā pana;
Gatte sedā na muccanti,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6867Msdiv 56Kuṭṭhaṃ gaṇḍo kilāso ca,
tilakā piḷakā tathā;
Na honti kāye daddu ca,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6868Msdiv 57Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Rogā na honti kāyasmiṃ,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6869Msdiv 58Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Na hoti cittajā pīḷā,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6870Msdiv 59Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Amittā na bhavantassa,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6871Msdiv 60Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Anūnabhogo bhavati,
vipāko pattachaḍḍite.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6872Msdiv 61Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Aggirājūhi corehi,
na hoti udake bhayaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6873Msdiv 62Aparampi guṇaṃ tassa,
nibbattati bhavābhave;
Dāsidāsā anucarā,
honti cittānuvattakā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6874Msdiv 63Yamhi āyuppamāṇamhi,
jāyate mānuse bhave;
Tato na hāyate āyu,
tiṭṭhate yāvatāyukaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6875Msdiv 64Abbhantarā ca bāhirā,
negamā ca saraṭṭhakā;
Nuyuttā honti sabbepi,
vuddhikāmā sukhicchakā.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6876Msdiv 65Bhogavā yasavā homi,
sirimā ñātipakkhavā;
Apetabhayasantāso,
bhavehaṃ sabbato bhave.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6877Msdiv 66Devā manussā asurā,
gandhabbā yakkharakkhasā;
Sabbe te parirakkhanti,
bhave saṃsarato sadā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6878Msdiv 67Devaloke manusse ca,
anubhotvā ubho yase;
Avasāne ca nibbānaṃ,
sivaṃ patto anuttaraṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6879Msdiv 68Sambuddhamuddisitvāna,
bodhiṃ vā tassa satthuno;
Yo puññaṃ pasave poso,
tassa kiṃ nāma dullabhaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6880Msdiv 69Magge phale āgame ca,
jhānābhiññāguṇesu ca;
Aññesaṃ adhiko hutvā,
nibbāyāmi anāsavo.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6881Msdiv 70Purehaṃ bodhiyā pattaṃ,
chaḍḍetvā haṭṭhamānaso;
Imehi vīsataṅgehi,
samaṅgī homi sabbadā.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6882Msdiv 71Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6883Msdiv 72Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6884Msdiv 73Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6885Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhisammajjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6886Bodhisammajjakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
