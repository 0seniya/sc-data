7. Udakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6492Msdiv 51“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
bhikkhusaṃghe anuttare;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pānīghaṭamapūrayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6493Msdiv 52Pabbatagge dumagge vā,
ākāse vātha bhūmiyaṃ;
Yadā pānīyamicchāmi,
khippaṃ nibbattate mama.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6494Msdiv 53Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dakadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6495Msdiv 54Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6496Msdiv 55Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6497Msdiv 56Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6498Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6499Udakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
