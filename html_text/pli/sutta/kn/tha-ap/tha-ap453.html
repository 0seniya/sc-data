1. Jagatidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5879Msdiv 1“Dhammadassissa munino,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Pasannacitto sumano,
jagatiṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5880Msdiv 2Darito pabbatato vā,
rukkhato patito ahaṃ;
Cuto patiṭṭhaṃ vindāmi,
jagatiyā idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5881Msdiv 3Na me corā vihesanti,
nātimaññanti khattiyā;
Sabbāmittetikkamāmi,
jagatiyā idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5882Msdiv 4Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbattha pūjito homi,
jagatiyā idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5883Msdiv 5Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
jagatiṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
jagatidānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5884Msdiv 6Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5885Msdiv 7Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5886Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5887Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā jagatidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5888Jagatidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
