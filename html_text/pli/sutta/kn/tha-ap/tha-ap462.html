10. Gandhapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5959Msdiv 63“Citāsu kurumānāsu,
nānāgandhe samāhaṭe;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gandhamuṭṭhimapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5960Msdiv 64Satasahassito kappe,
citakaṃ yamapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5961Msdiv 65Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5962Msdiv 66Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 2.407Ms 20Ap1_5963Msdiv 67Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4771)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5964Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5965Gandhapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5966Jagatidāyakavaggo chacattālīsamo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5967Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5968Jagatī morahatthī ca,
āsanī ukkadhārako;
Akkami vanakoraṇḍi,
chattado jātipūjako.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5969Paṭṭipupphī ca yo thero,
dasamo gandhapūjako;
Sattasaṭṭhi ca gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.
