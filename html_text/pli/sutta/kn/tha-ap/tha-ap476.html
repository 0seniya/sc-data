4. Sumanabījaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6117Msdiv 46“Vipassino bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Sumano bījaniṃ gayha,
abījiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6118Msdiv 47Ekanavutito kappe,
abījiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bījanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6119Msdiv 48Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6120Msdiv 49Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6121Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6122Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sumanabījaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6123Sumanabījaniyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
