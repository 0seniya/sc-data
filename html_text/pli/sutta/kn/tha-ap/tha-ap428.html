6 Ghatamaṇḍadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5557Msdiv 78“Sucintitaṃ bhagavantaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Upaviṭṭhaṃ mahāraññaṃ,
vātābādhena pīḷitaṃ.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.384Ms 20Ap1_5558Msdiv 79Disvā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
ghatamaṇḍamupānayiṃ;
Katattā ācitattā ca,
gaṅgā bhāgīrathī ayaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5559Msdiv 80Mahāsamuddā cattāro,
ghataṃ sampajjare mama;
Ayañca pathavī ghorā,
appamāṇā asaṅkhiyā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5560Msdiv 81Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
bhavate madhusakkarā;
Cātuddīpā ime rukkhā,
pādapā dharaṇīruhā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5561Msdiv 82Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
kapparukkhā bhavanti te;
Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5562Msdiv 83Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5563Msdiv 84Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ghatamaṇḍassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5564Msdiv 85Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5565Msdiv 86Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5566Msdiv 87Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5567Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ghatamaṇḍadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5568Ghatamaṇḍadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
