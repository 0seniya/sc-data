1. Bandhujīvakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2353Msdiv 1“Candaṃva vimalaṃ suddhaṃ,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ;
Nandībhavaparikkhīṇaṃ,
tiṇṇaṃ loke visattikaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2354Msdiv 2Nibbāpayantaṃ janataṃ,
Tiṇṇaṃ Tiṇṇaṃ tārayataṃ varaṃ;
Muniṃ vanamhi jhāyantaṃ,
Ekaggaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2355Msdiv 3Bandhujīvakapupphāni,
lagetvā suttakenahaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropayiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.176Ms 20Ap1_2356Msdiv 4Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2357Msdiv 5Ito sattamake kappe,
manujindo mahāyaso;
Samantacakkhu nāmāsi,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2358Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2359Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bandhujīvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2360Bandhujīvakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
