1 Tuvaradāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3034Msdiv 1“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Bharitvā tuvaramādāya,
saṃghassa adadiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3035Msdiv 2Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tuvarassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3036Msdiv 3Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3037Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tuvaradāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3038Tuvaradāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
