10. Pāpanivāriyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2890Msdiv 51“Piyadassissa bhagavato,
caṅkamaṃ sodhitaṃ mayā;
Naḷakehi paṭicchannaṃ,
vātātapanivāraṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2891Msdiv 52Pāpaṃ vivajjanatthāya,
kusalassupasampadā;
Kilesānaṃ pahānāya,
padahiṃ satthu sāsane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2892Msdiv 53Ito ekādase kappe,
aggidevoti vissuto;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2893Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2368)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2894Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pāpanivāriyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2895Pāpanivāriyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2896Hatthivaggo bāvīsatimo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2897Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2898Hatthi pānadhi saccañca,
ekasaññi ca raṃsiko;
Sandhito tālavaṇṭañca,
tathā akkantasaññako;
Sappi pāpanivārī ca,
catuppaññāsa gāthakāti.
