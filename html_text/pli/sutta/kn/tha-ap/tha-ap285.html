3. Paccuggamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3301Msdiv 11“Sīhaṃ yathā vanacaraṃ,
nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
āgacchantaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3302Msdiv 12Siddhatthaṃ lokapajjotaṃ,
sabbalokatikicchakaṃ;
Akāsiṃ paccuggamanaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3303Msdiv 13Catunnavutito kappe,
paccuggacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paccuggamane idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3304Msdiv 14Sattatiṃse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Saparivāroti nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3305Msdiv 15Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3306Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paccuggamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3307Paccuggamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
