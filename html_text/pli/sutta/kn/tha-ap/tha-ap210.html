8. Koraṇḍapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2798Msdiv 35“Akkantañca padaṃ disvā,
cakkālaṅkārabhūsitaṃ;
Padenānupadaṃ yanto,
vipassissa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2799Msdiv 36Koraṇḍaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
samūlaṃ pūjitaṃ mayā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
avandiṃ padamuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2800Msdiv 37Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2801Msdiv 38Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
eko vītamalo ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2802Msdiv 39Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2803Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koraṇḍapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2804Koraṇḍapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
