2. Citakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5977SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5977Msdiv 6“Jhāyamānassa bhagavato,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Aṭṭha campakapupphāni,
citakaṃ abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5978SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5978Msdiv 7Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5979SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5979Msdiv 8Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5980SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5980Msdiv 9Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5981SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5981Msdiv 10Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5982Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5983Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
