1. Kuṇḍadhānattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1073Msdiv 1“Sattāhaṃ paṭisallīnaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aggapuggalaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ upaṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1074Msdiv 2Vuṭṭhitaṃ kālamaññāya,
padumuttaraṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Mahantiṃ kadalīkaṇṇiṃ,
gahetvā upagacchahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1075Msdiv 3Paṭiggahetvā bhagavā,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Mama cittaṃ pasādento,
paribhuñji mahāmuni.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1076Msdiv 4Paribhuñjitvā sambuddho,
satthavāho anuttaro;
Sakāsane nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 1.82Ms 20Ap1_1077Msdiv 5‘Ye ca santi samitāro,
yakkhā imamhi pabbate;
Araññe bhūtabhabyāni,
suṇantu vacanaṃ mama.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1078Msdiv 6Yo so buddhaṃ upaṭṭhāsi,
migarājaṃva kesariṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1079Msdiv 7Ekādasañcakkhattuṃ so,
devarājā bhavissati;
Catutiṃsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1080Msdiv 8Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1081Msdiv 9Akkositvāna samaṇe,
sīlavante anāsave;
Pāpakammavipākena,
nāmadheyyaṃ labhissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1082Msdiv 10Tassa dhamme sudāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Kuṇḍadhānoti nāmena,
sāvako so bhavissati’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1083Msdiv 11Pavivekamanuyutto,
jhāyī jhānarato ahaṃ;
Tosayitvāna satthāraṃ,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1084Msdiv 12Sāvakehi parivuto,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
salākaṃ gāhayī jino.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1085Msdiv 13Ekaṃsaṃ cīvaraṃ katvā,
vanditvā lokanāyakaṃ;
Vadataṃ varassa purato,
paṭhamaṃ aggahesahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1086Msdiv 14Tena kammena bhagavā,
dasasahassikampako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1087Msdiv 15Vīriyaṃ me dhuradhorayhaṃ,
yogakkhemādhivāhanaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1088Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1089Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kuṇḍadhāno thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1090Kuṇḍadhānattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
