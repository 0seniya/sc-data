8. Bhaddajittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7782Msdiv 98“Ogayha yaṃ pokkharaṇiṃ,
nānākuñjarasevitaṃ;
Uddharāmi bhisaṃ tattha,
ghāsahetu ahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7783Msdiv 99Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
padumuttarasavhayo;
Rattambaradharo buddho,
gacchate anilañjase.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7784Msdiv 100Dhunanto paṃsukūlāni,
saddaṃ assosahaṃ tadā;
Uddhaṃ nijjhāyamānohaṃ,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7785Msdiv 101Tattheva ṭhitako santo,
āyāciṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
‘Madhuṃ bhisehi sahitaṃ,
khīraṃ sappiṃ muḷālikaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7786Msdiv 102Paṭiggaṇhātu me buddho,
anukampāya cakkhumā’;
Tato kāruṇiko satthā,
orohitvā mahāyaso.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7787Msdiv 103Paṭiggaṇhi mama bhikkhaṃ,
anukampāya cakkhumā;
Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7788Msdiv 104‘Sukhī hotu mahāpuñña,
gati tuyhaṃ samijjhatu;
Iminā bhisadānena,
labhassu vipulaṃ sukhaṃ’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7789Msdiv 105Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Bhikkhamādāya sambuddho,
ākāsenāgamā jino.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7790Msdiv 106Tato bhisaṃ gahetvāna,
āgacchiṃ mama assamaṃ;
Bhisaṃ rukkhe laggetvāna,
mama dānaṃ anussariṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7791Msdiv 107Mahāvāto uṭṭhahitvā,
sañcālesi vanaṃ tadā;
Ākāso abhinādittha,
asanī ca phalī tadā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7792Msdiv 108Tato me asanīpāto,
matthake nipatī tadā;
Sohaṃ nisinnako santo,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7793Msdiv 109Puññakammena saññutto,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ;
Kaḷevaraṃ me patitaṃ,
devaloke ramāmahaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7794Msdiv 110Chaḷasītisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sāyaṃ pātaṃ upaṭṭhanti,
bhisadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7795Msdiv 111Manussayonimāgantvā,
sukhito homahaṃ tadā;
Bhogā me ūnatā natthi,
bhisadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7796Msdiv 112Anukampitako tena,
devadevena tādinā;
Sabbāsavaparikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7797Msdiv 113Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ bhisaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhisadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7798Msdiv 114Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7799Msdiv 115Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7800Msdiv 116Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7801Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhaddajitthero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7802Bhaddajittherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
