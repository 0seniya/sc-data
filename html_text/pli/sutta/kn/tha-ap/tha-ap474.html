2. Maṇipūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6075Msdiv 8“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
gacchate anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6076Msdiv 9Avidūre himavantassa,
mahājātassaro ahu;
Tattha me bhavanaṃ āsi,
puññakammena saṃyutaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6077Msdiv 10Bhavanā abhinikkhamma,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Indīvaraṃva jalitaṃ,
ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6078Msdiv 11Vicinaṃ naddasaṃ pupphaṃ,
pūjayissanti nāyakaṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
avandiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6079Msdiv 12Mama sīse maṇiṃ gayha,
pūjayiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Imāya maṇipūjāya,
vipāko hotu bhaddako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6080Msdiv 13Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6081Msdiv 14‘So te ijjhatu saṅkappo,
labhassu vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;
Imāya maṇipūjāya,
anubhohi mahāyasaṃ’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6082Msdiv 15Idaṃ vatvāna bhagavā,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Agamāsi buddhaseṭṭho,
yattha cittaṃ paṇīhitaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6083Msdiv 16Saṭṭhikappāni devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Anekasatakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6084Msdiv 17Pubbakammaṃ sarantassa,
devabhūtassa me sato;
Maṇi nibbattate mayhaṃ,
ālokakaraṇo mamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6085Msdiv 18Chaḷasītisahassāni,
nāriyo me pariggahā;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
āmukkamaṇikuṇḍalā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6086Msdiv 19Aḷārapamhā hasulā,
susaññā tanumajjhimā;
Parivārenti maṃ niccaṃ,
maṇipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6087Msdiv 20Soṇṇamayā maṇimayā,
lohitaṅgamayā tathā;
Bhaṇḍā me sukatā honti,
yadicchasi piḷandhanā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6088Msdiv 21Kūṭāgārā gahārammā,
sayanañca mahārahaṃ;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
nibbattanti yadicchakaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6089Msdiv 22Lābhā tesaṃ suladdhañca,
ye labhanti upassutiṃ;
Puññakkhettaṃ manussānaṃ,
osadhaṃ sabbapāṇinaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6090Msdiv 23Mayhampi sukataṃ kammaṃ,
yohaṃ adakkhi nāyakaṃ;
Vinipātā pamuttomhi,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6091Msdiv 24Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Divasañceva rattiñca,
āloko hoti me sadā.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6092Msdiv 25Tāyeva maṇipūjāya,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Ñāṇāloko mayā diṭṭho,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6093Msdiv 26Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ maṇiṃ abhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
maṇipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6094Msdiv 27Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6095Msdiv 28Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6096Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6097Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maṇipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6098Maṇipūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
