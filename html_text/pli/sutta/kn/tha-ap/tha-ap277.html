5 Ekañjalikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3249Msdiv 19“Udumbare vasantassa,
niyate paṇṇasanthare;
Vutthokāso mayā dinno,
samaṇassa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3250Msdiv 20Tissassa dvipadindassa,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
santhariṃ pupphasantharaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3251Msdiv 21Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kariṃ pupphasantharaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3252Msdiv 22Ito cuddasakappamhi,
ahosiṃ manujādhipo;
Ekaañjaliko nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3253Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3254Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekañjaliko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3255Ekañjalikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
