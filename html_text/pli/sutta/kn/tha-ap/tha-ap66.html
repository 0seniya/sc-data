4. Parappasādakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1516Msdiv 20“Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ vīraṃ,
Mahesiṃ vijitāvinaṃ;
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1517Msdiv 21Himavāvāparimeyyo,
sāgarova duruttaro;
Tatheva jhānaṃ buddhassa,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1518Msdiv 22Vasudhā yathāppameyyā,
cittā vanavaṭaṃsakā;
Tatheva sīlaṃ buddhassa,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1519Msdiv 23Anilañjasāsaṅkhubbho,
yathākāso asaṅkhiyo;
Tatheva ñāṇaṃ buddhassa,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 1.114Ms 20Ap1_1520Msdiv 24Imāhi catugāthāhi,
brāhmaṇo senasavhayo;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thavitvāna,
siddhatthaṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1521Msdiv 25Catunnavutikappāni,
duggatiṃ nupapajjatha;
Sugatiṃ sukhasampattiṃ,
anubhosimanappakaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1522Msdiv 26Catunnavutito kappe,
thavitvā lokanāyakaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
thomanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1523Msdiv 27Cātuddasamhi kappamhi,
caturo āsumuggatā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1524Msdiv 28Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1525Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā parappasādako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1526Parappasādakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
