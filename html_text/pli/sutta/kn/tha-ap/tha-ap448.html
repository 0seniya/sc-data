6. Ambāṭakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5820Msdiv 36“Supupphitaṃ sālavanaṃ,
ogayha vessabhū muni;
Nisīdi giriduggesu,
abhijātova kesarī.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5821Msdiv 37Pasannacitto sumano,
ambāṭakamapūjayiṃ;
Puññakkhettaṃ anuttaraṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5822Msdiv 38Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5823Msdiv 39Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5824Msdiv 40Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5825Msdiv 41Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5826Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambāṭakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5827Ambāṭakiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
