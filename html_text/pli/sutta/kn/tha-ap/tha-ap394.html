2. Selattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4507Msdiv 208“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
vīthisāmī ahosahaṃ;
Mama ñātī samānetvā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4508Msdiv 209‘Buddho loke samuppanno,
puññakkhetto anuttaro;
Āsi so sabbalokassa,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4509Msdiv 210Khattiyā negamā ceva,
mahāsālā ca brāhmaṇā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4510Msdiv 211Hatthārohā anīkaṭṭhā,
rathikā pattikārakā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4511Msdiv 212Uggā ca rājaputtā ca,
vesiyānā ca brāhmaṇā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4512Msdiv 213Āḷārikā kappakā ca,
nhāpakā mālakārakā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4513Msdiv 214Rajakā pesakārā ca,
cammakārā ca nhāpitā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4514Msdiv 215Usukārā bhamakārā,
cammakārā ca tacchakā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4515Msdiv 216Kammārā soṇṇakārā ca,
tipulohakarā tathā;
Pasannacittā sumanā,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4516Msdiv 217Bhatakā ceṭakā ceva,
dāsakammakarā bahū;
Yathāsakena thāmena,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4517Msdiv 218Udahārā kaṭṭhahārā,
kassakā tiṇahārakā;
Yathāsakena thāmena,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4518Msdiv 219Pupphikā mālikā ceva,
paṇṇikā phalahārakā;
Yathāsakena thāmena,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4519Msdiv 220Gaṇikā kumbhadāsī ca,
pūvikā macchikāpi ca;
Yathāsakena thāmena,
pūgadhammaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4520Msdiv 221Etha sabbe samāgantvā,
gaṇaṃ bandhāma ekato;
Adhikāraṃ karissāma,
puññakkhette anuttare’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4521Msdiv 222Te me sutvāna vacanaṃ,
gaṇaṃ bandhiṃsu tāvade;
‘Upaṭṭhānasālaṃ sukataṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghassa kārayuṃ’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4522Msdiv 223Niṭṭhāpetvāna taṃ sālaṃ,
udaggo tuṭṭhamānaso;
Pareto tehi sabbehi,
sambuddhamupasaṅkamiṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4523Msdiv 224Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
lokanāthaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4524Msdiv 225‘Ime tīṇi satā vīra,
Purisā ekato gaṇā;
Upaṭṭhānasālaṃ sukataṃ,
Niyyādenti Niyyādenti tuvaṃ muni.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4525Msdiv 226Bhikkhusaṃghassa purato,
sampaṭicchitva cakkhumā’;
Tiṇṇaṃ satānaṃ purato,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4526Msdiv 227‘Tisatāpi ca jeṭṭho ca,
anuvattiṃsu ekato;
Sampattiñhi karitvāna,
sabbe anubhavissatha.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4527Msdiv 228Pacchime bhave sampatte,
sītibhāvamanuttaraṃ;
Ajaraṃ amataṃ santaṃ,
nibbānaṃ phassayissatha’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4528Msdiv 229Evaṃ buddho viyākāsi,
sabbaññū samaṇuttaro;
Buddhassa vacanaṃ sutvā,
somanassaṃ pavedayiṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4529Msdiv 230Tiṃsa kappasahassāni,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Devādhipo pañcasataṃ,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4530Msdiv 231Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Devarajjaṃ karontassa,
mahādevā avandisuṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4531Msdiv 232Idha mānusake rajjaṃ,
parisā honti bandhavā;
Pacchime bhave sampatte,
vāseṭṭho nāma brāhmaṇo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4532Msdiv 233Asītikoṭi nicayo,
tassa putto ahosahaṃ;
Selo iti mamaṃ nāmaṃ,
chaḷaṅge pāramiṃ gato.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_4533Msdiv 234Jaṅghāvihāraṃ vicaraṃ,
sasissehi purakkhato;
Jaṭābhārikabharitaṃ,
keṇiyaṃ nāma tāpasaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4534Msdiv 235Paṭiyattāhutiṃ disvā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Āvāho vā vivāho vā,
rājā vā te nimantito’.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4535Msdiv 236‘Āhutiṃ yiṭṭhukāmohaṃ,
brāhmaṇe devasammate;
Na nimantemi rājānaṃ,
āhutī me na vijjati.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_4536Msdiv 237Na catthi mayhamāvāho,
vivāho me na vijjati;
Sakyānaṃ nandijanano,
seṭṭho loke sadevake.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_4537Msdiv 238Sabbalokahitatthāya,
sabbasattasukhāvaho;
So me nimantito ajja,
tassetaṃ paṭiyādanaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_4538Msdiv 239Timbarūsakavaṇṇābho,
appameyyo anūpamo;
Rūpenāsadiso buddho,
svātanāya nimantito.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_4539Msdiv 240Ukkāmukhapahaṭṭhova,
khadiraṅgārasannibho;
Vijjūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_4540Msdiv 241Pabbatagge yathā acci,
puṇṇamāyeva candimā;
Naḷaggivaṇṇasaṅkāso,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_4541Msdiv 242Asambhīto bhayātīto,
bhavantakaraṇo muni;
Sīhūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_4542Msdiv 243Kusalo buddhadhammehi,
apasayho parehi so;
Nāgūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_4543Msdiv 244Saddhammācārakusalo,
buddhanāgo asādiso;
Usabhūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_4544Msdiv 245Anantavaṇṇo amitayaso,
Vicittasabbalakkhaṇo;
Sakkūpamo mahāvīro,
So me buddho nimantito.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_4545Msdiv 246Vasī gaṇī patāpī ca,
tejassī ca durāsado;
Brahmūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_4546Msdiv 247Pattadhammo dasabalo,
balātibalapārago;
Dharaṇūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_4547Msdiv 248Sīlavīcisamākiṇṇo,
dhammaviññāṇakhobhito;
Udadhūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_4548Msdiv 249Durāsado duppasaho,
acalo uggato brahā;
Nerūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_4549Msdiv 250Anantañāṇo asamasamo,
Atulo aggataṃ gato;
Gaganūpamo mahāvīro,
So me buddho nimantito.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_4550Pannarasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_4551Msdiv 251Patiṭṭhā bhayabhītānaṃ,
tāṇo saraṇagāminaṃ;
Assāsako mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_4552Msdiv 252Āsayo buddhimantānaṃ,
puññakkhettaṃ sukhesinaṃ;
Ratanākaro mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_4553Msdiv 253Assāsako vedakaro,
sāmaññaphaladāyako;
Meghūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 48Ms 20Ap1_4554Msdiv 254Lokacakkhu mahātejo,
sabbatamavinodano;
Sūriyūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 49Ms 20Ap1_4555Msdiv 255Ārammaṇavimuttīsu,
sabhāvadassano muni;
Candūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 50Ms 20Ap1_4556Msdiv 256Buddho samussito loke,
lakkhaṇehi alaṅkato;
Appameyyo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 51Ms 20Ap1_4557Msdiv 257Yassa ñāṇaṃ appameyyaṃ,
sīlaṃ yassa anūpamaṃ;
Vimutti asadisā yassa,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 52Ms 20Ap1_4558Msdiv 258Yassa dhīti asadisā,
thāmo yassa acintiyo;
Yassa parakkamo jeṭṭho,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 53Ms 20Ap1_4559Msdiv 259Rāgo doso ca moho ca,
visā sabbe samūhatā;
Agadūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito.

SC 54Ms 20Ap1_4560Msdiv 260Klesabyādhibahudukkha,
sabbatamavinodano;
Vejjūpamo mahāvīro,
so me buddho nimantito’.

SC 55Ms 20Ap1_4561Msdiv 261Buddhoti bho yaṃ vadesi,
ghosopeso sudullabho;
Buddho buddhoti sutvāna,
pīti me udapajjatha.

SC 56Ms 20Ap1_4562Msdiv 262Abbhantaraṃ agaṇhantaṃ,
pīti me bahi nicchare;
Sohaṃ pītimano santo,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 57Ms 20Ap1_4563Msdiv 263‘Kahaṃ nu kho so bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Tattha gantvā namassissaṃ,
sāmaññaphaladāyakaṃ.

SC 58Ms 20Ap1_4564Msdiv 264Paggayha dakkhiṇaṃ bāhuṃ,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Ācikkhi me dhammarājaṃ,
sokasallavinodanaṃ’.

SC 59Ms 20Ap1_4565Msdiv 265‘Udentaṃva mahāmeghaṃ,
nīlaṃ añjanasannibhaṃ;
Sāgaraṃ viya dissantaṃ,
passasetaṃ mahāvanaṃ.

SC 60Ms 20Ap1_4566Msdiv 266Ettha so vasate buddho,
adantadamako muni;
Vinayanto ca veneyye,
bodhento bodhipakkhiye’.

SC 61Ms 20Ap1_4567Msdiv 267Pipāsitova udakaṃ,
bhojanaṃva jighacchito;
Gāvī yathā vacchagiddhā,
evāhaṃ viciniṃ jinaṃ.

SC 62Ms 20Ap1_4568Msdiv 268Ācāraupacāraññū,
dhammānucchavisaṃvaraṃ;
Sikkhāpemi sake sisse,
gacchante jinasantikaṃ.

SC 63Ms 20Ap1_4569Msdiv 269‘Durāsadā bhagavanto,
sīhāva ekacārino;
Pade padaṃ nikkhipantā,
āgaccheyyātha māṇavā.

SC 64Ms 20Ap1_4570Msdiv 270Āsīviso yathā ghoro,
migarājāva kesarī;
Mattova kuñjaro dantī,
evaṃ buddhā durāsadā.

SC 65Ms 20Ap1_4571Msdiv 271Ukkāsitañca khipitaṃ,
ajjhupekkhiya māṇavā;
Pade padaṃ nikkhipantā,
upetha buddhasantikaṃ.

SC 66Ms 20Ap1_4572Msdiv 272Paṭisallānagarukā,
appasaddā durāsadā;
Durūpasaṅkamā buddhā,
garū honti sadevake.

SC 67Ms 20Ap1_4573Msdiv 273Yadāhaṃ pañhaṃ pucchāmi,
paṭisammodayāmi vā;
Appasaddā tadā hotha,
munibhūtāva tiṭṭhatha.

SC 68Ms 20Ap1_4574Msdiv 274Yaṃ so deseti sambuddho,
khemaṃ nibbānapattiyā;
Tamevatthaṃ nisāmetha,
saddhammasavanaṃ sukhaṃ’.

SC 69Ms 20Ap1_4575Msdiv 275Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
sammodiṃ muninā ahaṃ;
Taṃ kathaṃ vītisāretvā,
lakkhaṇe upadhārayiṃ.

SC 70Ms 20Ap1_4576Msdiv 276Lakkhaṇe dve ca kaṅkhāmi,
passāmi tiṃsalakkhaṇe;
Kosohitavatthaguyhaṃ,
iddhiyā dassayī muni.

SC 71Ms 20Ap1_4577Msdiv 277Jivhaṃ ninnāmayitvāna,
kaṇṇasote ca nāsike;
Paṭimasi nalāṭantaṃ,
kevalaṃ chādayī jino.

SC 72Ms 20Ap1_4578Msdiv 278Tassāhaṃ lakkhaṇe disvā,
paripuṇṇe sabyañjane;
Buddhoti niṭṭhaṃ gantvāna,
saha sissehi pabbajiṃ.

SC 73Ms 20Ap1_4579Msdiv 279Satehi tīhi sahito,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Addhamāse asampatte,
sabbe pattāmha nibbutiṃ.

SC 74Ms 20Ap1_4580Msdiv 280Ekato kammaṃ katvāna,
puññakkhette anuttare;
Ekato saṃsaritvāna,
ekato vinivattayuṃ.

SC 75Ms 20Ap1_4581Msdiv 281Gopānasiyo datvāna,
pūgadhamme vasiṃ ahaṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
aṭṭha hetū labhāmahaṃ.

SC 76Ms 20Ap1_4582Msdiv 282Disāsu pūjito homi,
bhogā ca amitā mama;
Patiṭṭhā homi sabbesaṃ,
tāso mama na vijjati.

SC 77Ms 20Ap1_4583Msdiv 283Byādhayo me na vijjanti,
dīghāyuṃ pālayāmi ca;
Sukhumacchaviko homi,
āvāse patthite vase.

SC 78Ms 20Ap1_4584Msdiv 284Aṭṭha gopānasī datvā,
pūgadhamme vasiṃ ahaṃ;
Paṭisambhidārahattañca,
etaṃ me aparaṭṭhamaṃ.

SC 79Ms 20Ap1_4585Msdiv 285Sabbavositavosāno,
katakicco anāsavo;
Aṭṭhagopānasī nāma,
tava putto mahāmuni.

SC 80Ms 20Ap1_4586Msdiv 286Pañca thambhāni datvāna,
pūgadhamme vasiṃ ahaṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
pañca hetū labhāmahaṃ.

SC 81Ms 20Ap1_4587Msdiv 287Acalo homi mettāya,
anūnaṅgo bhavāmahaṃ;
Ādeyyavacano homi,
na dhaṃsemi yathā ahaṃ.

SC 82Ms 20Ap1_4588Msdiv 288Abhantaṃ hoti me cittaṃ,
akhilo homi kassaci;
Tena kammena sukatena,
vimalo homi sāsane.

SC 83Ms 20Ap1_4589Msdiv 289Sagāravo sappatisso,
katakicco anāsavo;
Sāvako te mahāvīra,
bhikkhu taṃ vandate muni.

SC 84Ms 20Ap1_4590Msdiv 290Katvā sukatapallaṅkaṃ,
sālāyaṃ paññapesahaṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
pañca hetū labhāmahaṃ.

SC 85Ms 20Ap1_4591Msdiv 291Ucce kule pajāyitvā,
mahābhogo bhavāmahaṃ;
Sabbasampattiko homi,
maccheraṃ me na vijjati.

SC 86Ms 20Ap1_4592Msdiv 292Gamane patthite mayhaṃ,
pallaṅko upatiṭṭhati;
Saha pallaṅkaseṭṭhena,
gacchāmi mama patthitaṃ.

SC 87Ms 20Ap1_4593Msdiv 293Tena pallaṅkadānena,
tamaṃ sabbaṃ vinodayiṃ;
Sabbābhiññābalappatto,
thero vandati taṃ muni.

SC 88Ms 20Ap1_4594Msdiv 294Parakiccattakiccāni,
sabbakiccāni sādhayiṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
pāvisiṃ abhayaṃ puraṃ.

SC 89Ms 20Ap1_4595Msdiv 295Pariniṭṭhitasālamhi,
paribhogamadāsahaṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
seṭṭhattaṃ ajjhupāgato.
