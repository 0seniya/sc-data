8. Uttareyyadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.272Ms 20Ap1_3818Msdiv 90“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;
Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3819Msdiv 91Purakkhato sasissehi,
jātimā ca susikkhito;
Toyābhisecanatthāya,
nagarā nikkhamiṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3820Msdiv 92Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
pāvisī nagaraṃ jino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3821Msdiv 93Sucārurūpaṃ disvāna,
āneñjakāritaṃ viya;
Parivutaṃ arahantehi,
disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3822Msdiv 94Sirasmiṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
namassitvāna subbataṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
uttarīyamadāsahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3823Msdiv 95Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
sāṭakaṃ ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Yāvatā buddhaparisā,
tāva chādesi sāṭako.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3824Msdiv 96Piṇḍacāraṃ carantassa,
mahābhikkhugaṇādino;
Chadaṃ karonto aṭṭhāsi,
hāsayanto mamaṃ tadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3825Msdiv 97Gharato nikkhamantassa,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Vīthiyaṃva ṭhito satthā,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3826Msdiv 98Pasannacitto sumano,
yo me adāsi sāṭakaṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇotha mama bhāsato.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3827Msdiv 99‘Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3828Msdiv 100Devaloke vasantassa,
puññakammasamaṅgino;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
dussacchannaṃ bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3829Msdiv 101Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3830Msdiv 102Bhave saṃsaramānassa,
puññakammasamaṅgino;
Manasā patthitaṃ sabbaṃ,
nibbattissati tāvade.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3831Msdiv 103Koseyyakambaliyāni,
khomakappāsikāni ca;
Mahagghāni ca dussāni,
paṭilacchatiyaṃ naro.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 1.273Ms 20Ap1_3832Msdiv 104Manasā patthitaṃ sabbaṃ,
paṭilacchatiyaṃ naro;
Ekadussassa vipākaṃ,
anubhossati sabbadā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3833Msdiv 105So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
dhammaṃ sacchikarissati’.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3834Msdiv 106Aho me sukataṃ kammaṃ,
sabbaññussa mahesino;
Ekāhaṃ sāṭakaṃ datvā,
pattomhi amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3835Msdiv 107Maṇḍape rukkhamūle vā,
vasato suññake ghare;
Dhāreti dussachadanaṃ,
samantā byāmato mama.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3836Msdiv 108Aviññattaṃ nivāsemi,
cīvaraṃ paccayañcahaṃ;
Lābhī Lābhī annassa pānassa,
uttareyyassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3837Msdiv 109Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vatthadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_3838Msdiv 110Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_3839Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttareyyadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_3840Uttareyyadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
