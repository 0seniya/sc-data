5. Bhisadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1527SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1527Msdiv 29“Vessabhū nāma nāmena,
isīnaṃ tatiyo ahu;
Kānanaṃ vanamogayha,
vihāsi purisuttamo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1528SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1528Msdiv 30Bhisamuḷālaṃ gaṇhitvā,
agamaṃ buddhasantikaṃ;
Tañca buddhassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1529SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1529Msdiv 31Karena ca parāmaṭṭho,
vessabhūvarabuddhinā;
Sukhāhaṃ nābhijānāmi,
samaṃ tena kutottariṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1530SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1530Msdiv 32Carimo vattate mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Hatthināgena santena,
kusalaṃ ropitaṃ mayā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1531SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1531Msdiv 33Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhisadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1532SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1532Msdiv 34Samodhānā ca rājāno,
soḷasa manujādhipā;
Kappamhi cuddase āsuṃ,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1533SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1533Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1534Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhisadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1535Bhisadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
