9. Sumanattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1568Msdiv 62“Sumano nāma nāmena,
mālākāro ahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
lokāhutipaṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1569Msdiv 63Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
sumanaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1570Msdiv 64Imāya pupphapūjāya,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1571Msdiv 65Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1572Msdiv 66Chabbīsatimhi kappamhi,
cattārosuṃ mahāyasā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
rājāno cakkavattino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1573Msdiv 67Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1574Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sumano thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1575Sumanattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
