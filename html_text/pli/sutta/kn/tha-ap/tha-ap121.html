9. Paccupaṭṭhānasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2028SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2028Msdiv 72“Atthadassimhi sugate,
nibbute samanantarā;
Yakkhayoniṃ upapajjiṃ,
yasaṃ patto cahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2029SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2029Msdiv 73Dulladdhaṃ vata me āsi,
duppabhātaṃ duruṭṭhitaṃ;
Yaṃ me bhoge vijjamāne,
parinibbāyi cakkhumā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2030SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2030Msdiv 74Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
sāgaro nāma sāvako;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2031SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2031Msdiv 75Kiṃ nu socasi mā bhāyi,
cara dhammaṃ sumedhasa;
Anuppadinnā buddhena,
sabbesaṃ bījasampadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2032SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2032Msdiv 76Yo ce pūjeyya sambuddhaṃ,
tiṭṭhantaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Dhātuṃ sāsapamattampi,
nibbutassāpi pūjaye.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2033SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2033Msdiv 77Same cittappasādamhi,
samaṃ puññaṃ mahaggataṃ;
Tasmā thūpaṃ karitvāna,
pūjehi jinadhātuyo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2034SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2034Msdiv 78Sāgarassa vaco sutvā,
buddhathūpaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Pañcavasse paricariṃ,
munino thūpamuttamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2035SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2035Msdiv 79Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2036SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2036Msdiv 80Bhūripaññā ca cattāro,
sattakappasate ito;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2037SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2037Msdiv 81Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2038Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paccupaṭṭhānasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
