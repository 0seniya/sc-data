9 Paṭṭipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5952Msdiv 58“Nīharante sarīramhi,
vajjamānāsu bherisu;
Pasannacitto sumano,
paṭṭipupphamapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5953Msdiv 59Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dehapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5954Msdiv 60Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5955Msdiv 61Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5956Msdiv 62Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5957Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṭṭipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5958Paṭṭipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
