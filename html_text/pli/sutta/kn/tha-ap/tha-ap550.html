8 Lomasakaṅgiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7573SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7573Msdiv 225“Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7574SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7574Msdiv 226Tadāhaṃ candano ceva,
pabbajitvāna sāsane;
Āpāṇakoṭikaṃ dhammaṃ,
pūrayitvāna sāsane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7575SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7575Msdiv 227Tato cutā santusitaṃ,
upapannā ubho mayaṃ;
Tattha dibbehi naccehi,
gītehi vāditehi ca.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7576SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7576Msdiv 228Rūpādidasahaṅgehi,
abhibhotvāna sesake;
Yāvatāyuṃ vasitvāna,
anubhotvā mahāsukhaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7577SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7577Msdiv 229Tato cavitvā tidasaṃ,
candano upapajjatha;
Ahaṃ kapilavatthusmiṃ,
ajāyiṃ sākiyatrajo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7578SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7578Msdiv 230Yadā udāyittherena,
ajjhiṭṭho lokanāyako;
Anukampiya sakyānaṃ,
upesi kapilavhayaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7579SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7579Msdiv 231Tadātimānino sakyā,
na buddhassa guṇaññuno;
Paṇamanti na sambuddhaṃ,
jātithaddhā anādarā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7580SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7580Msdiv 232Tesaṃ saṅkappamaññāya,
ākāse caṅkamī jino;
Pajjunno viya vassittha,
pajjalittha yathā sikhī.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7581SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7581Msdiv 233Dassetvā rūpamatulaṃ,
puna antaradhāyatha;
Ekopi hutvā bahudhā,
ahosi punarekako.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7582SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7582Msdiv 234Andhakāraṃ pakāsañca,
dassayitvā anekadhā;
Pāṭiheraṃ karitvāna,
vinayī ñātake muni.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7583SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7583Msdiv 235Cātuddīpo mahāmegho,
tāvadeva pavassatha;
Tadā hi jātakaṃ buddho,
vessantaramadesayi.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7584SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7584Msdiv 236Tadā te khattiyā sabbe,
nihantvā jātijaṃ madaṃ;
Upesuṃ saraṇaṃ buddhaṃ,
āha suddhodano tadā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7585SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7585Msdiv 237‘Idaṃ tatiyaṃ tava bhūripañña,
Pādāni vandāmi samantacakkhu;
Yadābhijāto pathavī pakampayī,
Yadā ca taṃ najjahi jambuchāyā’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7586SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7586Msdiv 238Tadā buddhānubhāvaṃ taṃ,
disvā vimhitamānaso;
Pabbajitvāna tattheva,
nivasiṃ mātupūjako.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7587SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7587Msdiv 239Candano devaputto maṃ,
upagantvānupucchatha;
Bhaddekarattassa tadā,
saṅkhepavitthāraṃ nayaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7588SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7588Msdiv 240Coditohaṃ tadā tena,
upecca naranāyakaṃ;
Bhaddekarattaṃ sutvāna,
saṃviggo vanamāmako.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7589SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7589Msdiv 241Tadā mātaramapucchiṃ,
‘vane vacchāmi ekako’;
‘Sukhumālo’ti me mātā,
vārayī taṃ tadā vacaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7590Msdiv 242‘SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7590Msdiv 242‘Kāsaṃ kusaṃ poṭakilaṃ,
usīraṃ muñjapabbajaṃ;
Urasā panudissāmi,
vivekamanubrūhayaṃ’.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7591SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7591Msdiv 243Tadā vanaṃ paviṭṭhohaṃ,
saritvā jinasāsanaṃ;
Bhaddekarattaovādaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7592SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7592Msdiv 244‘Atītaṃ nānvāgameyya,
nappaṭikaṅkhe anāgataṃ;
Yadatītaṃ pahīnaṃ taṃ,
appattañca anāgataṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7593SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7593Msdiv 245Paccuppannañca yo dhammaṃ,
tattha tattha vipassati;
Asaṃhīraṃ asaṃkuppaṃ,
taṃ vidvā manubrūhaye.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7594SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7594Msdiv 246Ajjeva kiccamātappaṃ,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Na hi no saṅgaraṃ tena,
mahāsenena maccunā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7595SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7595Msdiv 247Evaṃvihāriṃ ātāpiṃ,
ahorattamatanditaṃ;
Taṃ ve bhaddekarattoti,
santo ācikkhate muni’.

… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
