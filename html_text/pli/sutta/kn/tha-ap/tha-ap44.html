2 Soṇakoḷivisattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1235Msdiv 25“Anomadassissa munino,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Sudhāya lepanaṃ katvā,
caṅkamaṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1236Msdiv 26Nānāvaṇṇehi pupphehi,
caṅkamaṃ santhariṃ ahaṃ;
Ākāse vitānaṃ katvā,
bhojayiṃ buddhamuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1237Msdiv 27Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
abhivādetvāna subbataṃ;
Dīghasālaṃ bhagavato,
niyyādesimahaṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1238Msdiv 28Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
anukampāya cakkhumā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1239Msdiv 29Paṭiggahetvāna sambuddho,
dakkhiṇeyyo sadevake;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1240Msdiv 30‘Yo so haṭṭhena cittena,
dīghasālaṃ adāsi me;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.94Ms 20Ap1_1241Msdiv 31Imassa maccukālamhi,
puññakammasamaṅgino;
Sahassayuttassaratho,
upaṭṭhissati tāvade.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1242Msdiv 32Tena yānenayaṃ poso,
devalokaṃ gamissati;
Anumodissare devā,
sampatte kusalabbhave.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1243Msdiv 33Mahārahaṃ byamhaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
ratanamattikalepanaṃ;
Kūṭāgāravarūpetaṃ,
byamhaṃ ajjhāvasissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1244Msdiv 34Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Pañcavīsati kappāni,
devarājā bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1245Msdiv 35Sattasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Yasodharasanāmā te,
sabbepi ekanāmakā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1246Msdiv 36Dve sampattī anubhotvā,
Vaḍḍhetvā puññasañcayaṃ;
Aṭṭhavīsatikappamhi,
Cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1247Msdiv 37Tatrāpi byamhaṃ pavaraṃ,
vissakammena māpitaṃ;
Dasasaddāvivittaṃ taṃ,
puramajjhāvasissati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1248Msdiv 38Aparimeyye ito kappe,
bhūmipālo mahiddhiko;
Okkāko nāma nāmena,
rājā raṭṭhe bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1249Msdiv 39Soḷasitthisahassānaṃ,
sabbāsaṃ pavarā ca sā;
Abhijātā khattiyānī,
nava putte janessati.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1250Msdiv 40Nava putte janetvāna,
khattiyānī marissati;
Taruṇī ca piyā kaññā,
mahesittaṃ karissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1251Msdiv 41Okkākaṃ tosayitvāna,
varaṃ kaññā labhissati;
Varaṃ laddhāna sā kaññā,
putte pabbājayissati.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1252Msdiv 42Pabbājitā ca te sabbe,
gamissanti naguttamaṃ;
Jātibhedabhayā sabbe,
bhaginīhi vasissare.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1253Msdiv 43Ekā ca kaññā byādhīhi,
bhavissati parikkhatā;
Mā no jāti pabhijjīti,
nikhaṇissanti khattiyā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1254Msdiv 44Khattiyo nīharitvāna,
tāya saddhiṃ vasissati;
Bhavissati tadā bhedo,
okkākakulasambhavo.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 1.95Ms 20Ap1_1255Msdiv 45Tesaṃ pajā bhavissanti,
koḷiyā nāma jātiyā;
Tattha mānusakaṃ bhogaṃ,
anubhossatinappakaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1256Msdiv 46Tamhā kāyā cavitvāna,
devalokaṃ gamissati;
Tatrāpi pavaraṃ byamhaṃ,
labhissati manoramaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1257Msdiv 47Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Āgantvāna manussattaṃ,
soṇo nāma bhavissati.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_1258Msdiv 48Āraddhavīriyo pahitatto,
Padahaṃ satthu sāsane;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
Nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_1259Msdiv 49Anantadassī bhagavā,
gotamo sakyapuṅgavo;
Visesaññū mahāvīro,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapessati’.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_1260Msdiv 50Vuṭṭhamhi deve caturaṅgulamhi,
Tiṇe anileritaaṅgaṇamhi;
Ṭhatvāna yogassa payuttatādino,
Tatottariṃ pāramatā na vijjati.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_1261Msdiv 51Uttame damathe danto,
cittaṃ me supaṇīhitaṃ;
Bhāro me ohito sabbo,
nibbutomhi anāsavo.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_1262Msdiv 52Aṅgīraso mahānāgo,
abhijātova kesarī;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_1263Msdiv 53Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_1264Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā soṇo koḷiviso thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_1265Soṇakoḷivisattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
