10 Pulinacaṅkamiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6173SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6173Msdiv 90“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Vātamigaṃ gavesanto,
caṅkamaṃ addasaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6174Msdiv 91Ucchaṅgena SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6174Msdiv 91Ucchaṅgena pulinaṃ gayha,
caṅkame okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sugatassa sirīmato.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6175SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6175Msdiv 92Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
pulinaṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pulinassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6176SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6176Msdiv 93Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6177SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6177Msdiv 94Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6178SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6178Msdiv 95Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4938)
