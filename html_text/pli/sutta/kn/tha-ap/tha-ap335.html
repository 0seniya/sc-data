3. Punnāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3732Msdiv 14“Kānanaṃ vanamogayha,
vasāmi luddako ahaṃ;
Punnāgaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3733Msdiv 15Taṃ pupphaṃ ocinitvāna,
sugandhaṃ gandhitaṃ subhaṃ;
Thūpaṃ katvāna puline,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3734Msdiv 16Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3735Msdiv 17Ekamhi navute kappe,
eko āsiṃ tamonudo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3736Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3737Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā punnāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3738Punnāgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
