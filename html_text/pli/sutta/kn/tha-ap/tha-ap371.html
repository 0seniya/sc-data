9. Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4079Msdiv 31“Candabhāgānadītīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4080Msdiv 32Pasannacitto sumano,
paccekamunimuttamaṃ;
Gahetvā timiraṃ pupphaṃ,
matthake okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4081Msdiv 33Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4082Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4083Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā timirapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4084Timirapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
