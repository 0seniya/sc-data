5. Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6468Msdiv 31“Padumuttarabhagavato,
sujāto nāma sāvako;
Paṃsukūlaṃ gavesanto,
saṅkāre carate tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6469Msdiv 32Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
paresaṃ bhatako ahaṃ;
Upaḍḍhadussaṃ datvāna,
sirasā abhivādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6470Msdiv 33Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 2.436Ms 20Ap1_6471Msdiv 34Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Sattasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6472Msdiv 35Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Upaḍḍhadussadānena,
modāmi akutobhayo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6473Msdiv 36Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,
sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;
Khomadussehi chādeyyaṃ,
aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6474Msdiv 37Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
aḍḍhadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6475Msdiv 38Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6476Msdiv 39Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6477Msdiv 40Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6478Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaḍḍhadussadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6479Upaḍḍhadussadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
