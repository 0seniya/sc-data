8 Ñāṇasaññikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2151Msdiv 84“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vasāmi pabbatantare;
Pulinaṃ sobhanaṃ disvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2152Msdiv 85Ñāṇe upanidhā natthi,
saṅkhāraṃ natthi satthuno;
Sabbadhammaṃ abhiññāya,
ñāṇena adhimuccati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2153Msdiv 86Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Ñāṇena te samo natthi,
yāvatā ñāṇamuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2154Msdiv 87Ñāṇe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ;
Avasesesu kappesu,
kusalaṃ caritaṃ mayā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2155Msdiv 88Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2156Msdiv 89Ito sattatikappamhi,
eko pulinapupphiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.162Ms 20Ap1_2157Msdiv 90Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2158Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ñāṇasaññiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2159Ñāṇasaññikattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
