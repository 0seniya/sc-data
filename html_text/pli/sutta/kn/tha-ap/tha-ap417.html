5 Nāgapallavattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5375Msdiv 118“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
rājuyyāne vasāmahaṃ;
Mama assamasāmantā,
nisīdi lokanāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5376Msdiv 119Nāgapallavamādāya,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sugataṃ abhivādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5377Msdiv 120Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pallavamapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5378Msdiv 121Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5379Msdiv 122Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5380Msdiv 123Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5381Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgapallavo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5382Nāgapallavattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
