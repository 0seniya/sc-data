8. Anulepadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3484SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3484Msdiv 39“Atthadassissa munino,
addasaṃ sāvakaṃ ahaṃ;
Navakammaṃ karontassa,
sīmāya upagacchahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3485SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3485Msdiv 40Niṭṭhite navakamme ca,
anulepamadāsahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
puññakkhette anuttare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3486SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3486Msdiv 41Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
anulepassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3487SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3487Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3488Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anulepadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3489Anulepadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
