4. Ekadussadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3739SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3739Msdiv 19“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ tiṇahārako;
Tiṇahārena jīvāmi,
tena posemi dārake.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3740SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3740Msdiv 20Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Tamandhakāraṃ nāsetvā,
uppajji lokanāyako.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3741SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3741Msdiv 21Sake ghare nisīditvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
‘Buddho loke samuppanno,
deyyadhammo ca natthi me.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3742SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3742Msdiv 22Idaṃ me sāṭakaṃ ekaṃ,
natthi me koci dāyako;
Dukkho nirayasamphasso,
ropayissāmi dakkhiṇaṃ’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3743SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3743Msdiv 23Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Ekaṃ dussaṃ gahetvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3744SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3744Msdiv 24Ekaṃ dussaṃ daditvāna,
ukkuṭṭhiṃ sampavattayiṃ;
Yadi buddho tuvaṃ vīra,
tārehi maṃ mahāmuni.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3745SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3745Msdiv 25Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mama dānaṃ pakittento,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3746SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3746Msdiv 26‘Iminā ekadussena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Kappasatasahassāni,
vinipātaṃ na gacchati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3747SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3747Msdiv 27Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3748SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3748Msdiv 28Devaloke manusse vā,
saṃsaranto tuvaṃ bhave;
Rūpavā guṇasampanno,
anavakkantadehavā;
Akkhobhaṃ amitaṃ dussaṃ,
labhissati yadicchakaṃ’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3749SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3749Msdiv 29Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī dhīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3750SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3750Msdiv 30Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
ekadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3751SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3751Msdiv 31Paduddhāre paduddhāre,
dussaṃ nibbattate mamaṃ;
Heṭṭhā dussamhi tiṭṭhāmi,
upari chadanaṃ mama.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3752SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3752Msdiv 32Cakkavāḷamupādāya,
sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;
Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,
dussehi chādayeyyahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3753SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3753Msdiv 33Teneva ekadussena,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo hutvāna,
saṃsarāmi bhavābhave.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3754SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3754Msdiv 34Vipākaṃ ekadussassa,
najjhagaṃ katthacikkhayaṃ;
Ayaṃ me antimā jāti,
vipaccati idhāpi me.

yaṃ dussamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
