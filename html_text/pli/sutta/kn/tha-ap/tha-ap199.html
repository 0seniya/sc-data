7. Tindukadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2712Msdiv 35“Giriduggacaro āsiṃ,
makkaṭo thāmavegiko;
Phalinaṃ tindukaṃ disvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2713Msdiv 36Nikkhamitvā katipāhaṃ,
viciniṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
siddhatthaṃ tibhavantaguṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2714Msdiv 37Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2715Msdiv 38Pāmojjaṃ janayitvāna,
phalahattho upāgamiṃ;
Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
sabbaññū vadataṃ varo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2716Msdiv 39Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2717Msdiv 40Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
upanandasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2718Msdiv 41Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2719Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tindukadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2720Tindukadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
