1. Sereyyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2054Msdiv 1“Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;
Abbhokāse ṭhito santo,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2055Msdiv 2Sīhaṃ yathā vanacaraṃ,
byaggharājaṃva nittasaṃ;
Tidhāpabhinnamātaṅgaṃ,
kuñjaraṃva mahesinaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2056Msdiv 3Sereyyakaṃ gahetvāna,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
parivārenti sabbaso.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2057Msdiv 4Adhiṭṭhahi mahāvīro,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Samantā pupphacchadanā,
okiriṃsu narāsabhaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2058Msdiv 5Tato sā pupphakañcukā,
antovaṇṭā bahimukhā;
Sattāhaṃ chadanaṃ katvā,
tato antaradhāyatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2059Msdiv 6Tañca acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādesiṃ,
sugate lokanāyake.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2060Msdiv 7Tena cittappasādena,
sukkamūlena codito;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2061Msdiv 8Pannarasasahassamhi,
kappānaṃ pañcavīsati;
Vītamalā samānā ca,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2062Msdiv 9Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2063Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sereyyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2064Sereyyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
