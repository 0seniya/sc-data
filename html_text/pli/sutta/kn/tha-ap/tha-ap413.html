1. Bhaddālittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5249Msdiv 1“Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Vivekakāmo lokaggo,
himavantamupāgami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5250Msdiv 2Ajjhogāhetvā himavaṃ,
sumedho lokanāyako;
Pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvāna,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5251Msdiv 3Samādhiṃ so samāpanno,
sumedho lokanāyako;
Sattarattindivaṃ buddho,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5252Msdiv 4Khāribhāraṃ gahetvāna,
vanamajjhogahiṃ ahaṃ;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5253Msdiv 5Sammajjaniṃ gahetvāna,
sammajjitvāna assamaṃ;
Catudaṇḍe ṭhapetvāna,
akāsiṃ maṇḍapaṃ tadā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.366Ms 20Ap1_5254Msdiv 6Sālapupphaṃ āharitvā,
maṇḍapaṃ chādayiṃ ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
abhivandiṃ tathāgataṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5255Msdiv 7Yaṃ vadanti sumedhoti,
bhūripaññaṃ sumedhasaṃ;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5256Msdiv 8Buddhassa giramaññāya,
sabbe devā samāgamuṃ;
Asaṃsayaṃ buddhaseṭṭho,
dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5257Msdiv 9Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Devasaṅghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5258Msdiv 10‘Yo me sattāhaṃ maṇḍapaṃ,
dhārayī sālachāditaṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5259Msdiv 11Devabhūto manusso vā,
hemavaṇṇo bhavissati;
Pahūtabhogo hutvāna,
kāmabhogī bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5260Msdiv 12Saṭṭhi nāgasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Suvaṇṇakacchā mātaṅgā,
hemakappanavāsasā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5261Msdiv 13Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
tomaraṅkusapāṇibhi;
Sāyaṃ pāto upaṭṭhānaṃ,
āgamissantimaṃ naraṃ;
Tehi nāgehi parivuto,
ramissati ayaṃ naro.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5262Msdiv 14Saṭṭhi assasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Ājānīyāva jātiyā,
sindhavā sīghavāhino.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5263Msdiv 15Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
illiyācāpadhāribhi;
Parivāressantimaṃ niccaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5264Msdiv 16Saṭṭhi rathasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Dīpā athopi veyagghā,
sannaddhā ussitaddhajā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5265Msdiv 17Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
cāpahatthehi vammibhi;
Parivāressantimaṃ niccaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5266Msdiv 18Saṭṭhi gāmasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Pahūtadhanadhaññāni,
susamiddhāni sabbaso;
Sadā pātubhavissanti,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5267Msdiv 19Hatthī assā rathā pattī,
senā ca caturaṅginī;
Parivāressantimaṃ niccaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5268Msdiv 20Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
devaloke ramissati;
Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 2.367Ms 20Ap1_5269Msdiv 21Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_5270Msdiv 22Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5271Msdiv 23Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharissatināsavo’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_5272Msdiv 24Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Etthantaramupādāya,
gavesiṃ amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_5273Msdiv 25Lābhā mayhaṃ suladdhaṃ me,
yamahaññāsi sāsanaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_5274Msdiv 26Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Tava ñāṇaṃ pakittetvā,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_5275Msdiv 27Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbattha sukhito homi,
phalaṃ me ñāṇakittane.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_5276Msdiv 28Idaṃ pacchimakaṃ mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_5277Msdiv 29Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_5278Msdiv 30Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_5279Msdiv 31Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_5280Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhaddālitthero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_5281Bhaddālittherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
