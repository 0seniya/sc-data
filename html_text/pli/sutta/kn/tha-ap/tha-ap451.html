9 Vedikārakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5851Msdiv 61“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Vedikaṃ sukataṃ katvā,
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5852Msdiv 62Atoḷārāni bhaṇḍāni,
katāni akatāni ca;
Antalikkhā pavassanti,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5853Msdiv 63Ubhato byūḷhasaṅgāme,
pakkhandanto bhayānake;
Bhayabheravaṃ na passāmi,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 2.401Ms 20Ap1_5854Msdiv 64Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
byamhaṃ nibbattate subhaṃ;
Sayanāni mahagghāni,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5855Msdiv 65Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ vedikamakārayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5856Msdiv 66Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5857Msdiv 67Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5858Msdiv 68Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5859Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vedikārako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5860Vedikārakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
