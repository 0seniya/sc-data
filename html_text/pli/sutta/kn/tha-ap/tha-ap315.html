3. Gatasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3519Msdiv 10“Ākāseva padaṃ natthi,
ambare anilañjase;
Siddhatthaṃ jinamaddakkhiṃ,
gacchantaṃ tidivaṅgaṇe.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3520Msdiv 11Anileneritaṃ disvā,
sammāsambuddhacīvaraṃ;
Vitti mamāhu tāvade,
disvāna gamanaṃ muniṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3521Msdiv 12Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3522Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3523Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3524Gatasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
