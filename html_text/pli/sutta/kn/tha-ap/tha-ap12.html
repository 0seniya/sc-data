12. Ānandattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_670Msdiv 644“Ārāmadvārā nikkhamma,
padumuttaro mahāmuni;
Vassento amataṃ vuṭṭhiṃ,
nibbāpesi mahājanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_671Msdiv 645Satasahassaṃ te dhīrā,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Parivārenti sambuddhaṃ,
chāyāva anapāyinī.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_672Msdiv 646Hatthikkhandhagato āsiṃ,
setacchattaṃ varuttamaṃ;
Sucārurūpaṃ disvāna,
vitti me udapajjatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_673Msdiv 647Oruyha hatthikhandhamhā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Ratanāmayachattaṃ me,
buddhaseṭṭhassa dhārayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_674Msdiv 648Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
padumuttaro mahāisi;
Taṃ kathaṃ ṭhapayitvāna,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_675Msdiv 649‘Yo so chattamadhāresi,
soṇṇālaṅkārabhūsitaṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇotha mama bhāsato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_676Msdiv 650Ito gantvā ayaṃ poso,
tusitaṃ āvasissati;
Anubhossati sampattiṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_677Msdiv 651Catuttiṃsatikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Balādhipo aṭṭhasataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_678Msdiv 652Aṭṭhapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
mahiyā kārayissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_679Msdiv 653Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_680Msdiv 654Sakyānaṃ kulaketussa,
ñātibandhu bhavissati;
Ānando nāma nāmena,
upaṭṭhāko mahesino.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_681Msdiv 655Ātāpī nipako cāpi,
bāhusacce sukovido;
Nivātavutti atthaddho,
sabbapāṭhī bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_682Msdiv 656Padhānapahitatto so,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_683Msdiv 657Santi āraññakā nāgā,
kuñjarā saṭṭhihāyanā;
Tidhāpabhinnā mātaṅgā,
īsādantā urūḷhavā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_684Msdiv 658Anekasatasahassā,
paṇḍitāpi mahiddhikā;
Sabbe te buddhanāgassa,
na hontupaṇidhimhi te’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_685Msdiv 659Ādiyāme namassāmi,
majjhime atha pacchime;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ upaṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_686Msdiv 660Ātāpī nipako cāpi,
sampajāno patissato;
Sotāpattiphalaṃ patto,
sekhabhūmīsu kovido.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_687Msdiv 661Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamabhinīhariṃ;
Tāhaṃ bhūmimanuppatto,
ṭhitā saddhammamācalā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_688Msdiv 662Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
buddhaseṭṭhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_689Msdiv 663Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_690Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ānando thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_691Ānandattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_692Tassuddānaṃ

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_693Buddho paccekabuddho ca,
sāriputto ca kolito;
Kassapo anuruddho ca,
puṇṇatthero upāli ca.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_694Aññāsikoṇḍañño piṇḍolo,
revatānandapaṇḍito;
Chasatāni ca paññāsa,
gāthāyo sabbapiṇḍitā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_695Apadāne buddhavaggo paṭhamo.
