9 Aññāsikoṇḍaññattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_616Msdiv 596“Padumuttarasambuddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ vināyakaṃ;
Buddhabhūmimanuppattaṃ,
paṭhamaṃ addasaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_617Msdiv 597Yāvatā bodhiyā mūle,
yakkhā sabbe samāgatā;
Sambuddhaṃ parivāretvā,
vandanti pañjalīkatā.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.49Ms 20Ap1_618Msdiv 598Sabbe devā tuṭṭhamanā,
ākāse sañcaranti te;
Buddho ayaṃ anuppatto,
andhakāratamonudo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_619Msdiv 599Tesaṃ hāsaparetānaṃ,
mahānādo avattatha;
Kilese jhāpayissāma,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_620Msdiv 600Devānaṃ giramaññāya,
vācāsabhimudīrihaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
ādibhikkhamadāsahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_621Msdiv 601Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Devasaṅghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_622Msdiv 602Sattāhaṃ abhinikkhamma,
bodhiṃ ajjhagamaṃ ahaṃ;
Idaṃ me paṭhamaṃ bhattaṃ,
brahmacārissa yāpanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_623Msdiv 603Tusitā hi idhāgantvā,
yo me bhikkhaṃ upānayi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇotha mama bhāsato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_624Msdiv 604Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Sabbe deve abhibhotvā,
tidivaṃ āvasissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_625Msdiv 605Devalokā cavitvāna,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Sahassadhā cakkavattī,
tattha rajjaṃ karissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_626Msdiv 606Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_627Msdiv 607Tidasā so cavitvāna,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Agārā pabbajitvāna,
chabbassāni vasissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_628Msdiv 608‘Tato sattamake vasse,
buddho saccaṃ kathessati;
Koṇḍañño nāma nāmena,
paṭhamaṃ sacchikāhiti’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_629Msdiv 609Nikkhantenānupabbajiṃ,
padhānaṃ sukataṃ mayā;
Kilese jhāpanatthāya,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_630Msdiv 610Abhigantvāna sabbaññū,
buddho loke sadevake;
Isināme migāraññe,
amatabherimāhani.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_631Msdiv 611So dāni patto amataṃ,
santipadamanuttaraṃ;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 1.50Ms 20Ap1_632Msdiv 612Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_633Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aññāsikoṇḍañño thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_634Aññāsikoṇḍaññattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
