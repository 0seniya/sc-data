2. Ekāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3100Msdiv 5“Vijahitvā devavaṇṇaṃ,
sabhariyo idhāgamiṃ;
Adhikāraṃ kattukāmo,
buddhaseṭṭhassa sāsane.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3101Msdiv 6Devalo nāma nāmena,
padumuttarasāvako;
Tassa bhikkhā mayā dinnā,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3102Msdiv 7Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
piṇḍapātassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3103Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3104Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3105Ekāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
