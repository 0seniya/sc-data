5. Nāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6721Msdiv 23“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Nāgapupphaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6722Msdiv 24Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6723Msdiv 25Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6724Msdiv 26Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6725Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6726Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6727Nāgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
