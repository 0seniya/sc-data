5. Candanamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6268Msdiv 75“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
piyarūpe manorame;
Asītikoṭiyo hitvā,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6269Msdiv 76Pabbajitvāna kāyena,
pāpakammaṃ vivajjayiṃ;
Vacīduccaritaṃ hitvā,
nadīkūle vasāmahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6270Msdiv 77Ekakaṃ maṃ viharantaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭho upāgami;
Nāhaṃ jānāmi buddhoti,
akāsiṃ paṭisanthāraṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6271Msdiv 78Karitvā paṭisanthāraṃ,
nāmagottamapucchahaṃ;
‘Devatānusi gandhabbo,
adu sakko purindado.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6272Msdiv 79Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
mahābrahmā idhāgato;
Virocesi disā sabbā,
udayaṃ sūriyo yathā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.424Ms 20Ap1_6273Msdiv 80Sahassārāni cakkāni,
pāde dissanti mārisa;
Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ;
Nāmagottaṃ pavedehi,
saṃsayaṃ apanehi me.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6274Msdiv 81Namhi devo na gandhabbo,
namhi sakko purindado;
Brahmabhāvo ca me natthi,
etesaṃ uttamo ahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6275Msdiv 82Atīto visayaṃ tesaṃ,
dālayiṃ kāmabandhanaṃ;
Sabbe kilese jhāpetvā,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6276Msdiv 83Tassa vācaṃ suṇitvāhaṃ,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Yadi buddhoti sabbaññū,
nisīda tvaṃ mahāmune.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6277Msdiv 84Tamahaṃ pūjayissāmi,
dukkhassantakaro tuvaṃ’;
Pattharitvā jinacammaṃ,
adāsi satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6278Msdiv 85Nisīdi tattha bhagavā,
sīhova girigabbhare;
Khippaṃ pabbatamāruyha,
ambassa phalamaggahiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6279Msdiv 86Sālakalyāṇikaṃ pupphaṃ,
Candanañca mahārahaṃ;
Khippaṃ paggayha taṃ sabbaṃ,
Upetvā lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6280Msdiv 87Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
sālapupphamapūjayiṃ;
Candanaṃ anulimpitvā,
avandiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6281Msdiv 88Pasannacitto sumano,
vipulāya ca pītiyā;
Ajinamhi nisīditvā,
sumedho lokanāyako.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6282Msdiv 89Mama kammaṃ pakittesi,
hāsayanto mamaṃ tadā;
‘Iminā phaladānena,
gandhamālehi cūbhayaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6283Msdiv 90Pañcavīse kappasate,
devaloke ramissati;
Anūnamanasaṅkappo,
vasavattī bhavissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6284Msdiv 91Chabbīsatikappasate,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Bhavissati cakkavattī,
cāturanto mahiddhiko.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6285Msdiv 92Vebhāraṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
vissakammena māpitaṃ;
Hessati sabbasovaṇṇaṃ,
nānāratanabhūsitaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6286Msdiv 93Eteneva upāyena,
saṃsarissati so bhave;
Sabbattha pūjito hutvā,
devatte atha mānuse.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6287Msdiv 94Pacchime bhave sampatte,
brahmabandhu bhavissati;
Agārā abhinikkhamma,
anagārī bhavissati;
Abhiññāpāragū hutvā,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 2.425Ms 20Ap1_6288Msdiv 95Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
sumedho lokanāyako;
Mama nijjhāyamānassa,
pakkāmi anilañjase.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6289Msdiv 96Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6290Msdiv 97Tusitato cavitvāna,
nibbattiṃ mātukucchiyaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
yamhi gabbhe vasāmahaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6291Msdiv 98Mātukucchigate mayi,
annapānañca bhojanaṃ;
Mātuyā mama chandena,
nibbattati yadicchakaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6292Msdiv 99Jātiyā pañcavassena,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Oropitamhi kesamhi,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6293Msdiv 100Pubbakammaṃ gavesanto,
orena nāddasaṃ ahaṃ;
Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
mama kammamanussariṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6294Msdiv 101Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Tava sāsanamāgamma,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6295Msdiv 102Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6296Msdiv 103Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_6297Msdiv 104Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_6298Msdiv 105Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_6299Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā candanamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_6300Candanamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
