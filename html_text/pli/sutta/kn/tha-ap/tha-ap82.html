10 Tipadumiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.124Ms 20Ap1_1654Msdiv 48“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Danto dantaparivuto,
nagarā nikkhamī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1655Msdiv 49Nagare haṃsavatiyaṃ,
ahosiṃ māliko tadā;
Yaṃ tattha uttamaṃ tīṇi,
padmapupphāni aggahiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1656Msdiv 50‘Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
paṭimaggantarāpaṇe;
Saha disvāna sambuddhaṃ,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1657Msdiv 51Kiṃ me imehi pupphehi,
rañño upanitehi me;
Gāmaṃ vā gāmakhettaṃ vā,
sahassaṃ vā labheyyahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1658Msdiv 52Adantadamanaṃ vīraṃ,
sabbasattasukhāvahaṃ;
Lokanāthaṃ pūjayitvā,
lacchāmi amataṃ dhanaṃ’.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1659Msdiv 53Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Tīṇi lohitake gayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ tadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1660Msdiv 54Mayā ukkhittamattamhi,
ākāse patthariṃsu te;
Dhāriṃsu matthake tattha,
uddhaṃvaṇṭā adhomukhā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1661Msdiv 55Ye keci manujā disvā,
ukkuṭṭhiṃ sampavattayuṃ;
Devatā antalikkhamhi,
sādhukāraṃ pavattayuṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1662Msdiv 56Accheraṃ loke uppannaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhassa vāhasā;
Sabbe dhammaṃ suṇissāma,
pupphānaṃ vāhasā mayaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1663Msdiv 57Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Vīthiyañhi ṭhito santo,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1664Msdiv 58‘Yo so buddhaṃ apūjesi,
rattapadmehi māṇavo;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1665Msdiv 59Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Tiṃsakappāni devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1666Msdiv 60Mahāvitthārikaṃ nāma,
byamhaṃ hessati tāvade;
Tiyojanasatubbiddhaṃ,
diyaḍḍhasatavitthataṃ.

SC 14PTS vp Pali 1.125Ms 20Ap1_1667Msdiv 61Cattārisatasahassāni,
niyyūhā ca sumāpitā;
Kūṭāgāravarūpetā,
mahāsayanamaṇḍitā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1668Msdiv 62Koṭisatasahassiyo,
parivāressanti accharā;
Kusalā naccagītassa,
vāditepi padakkhiṇā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1669Msdiv 63Etādise byamhavare,
nārīgaṇasamākule;
Vassissati pupphavasso,
dibbo lohitako sadā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1670Msdiv 64Bhittikhīle nāgadante,
dvārabāhāya toraṇe;
Cakkamattā lohitakā,
olambissanti tāvade.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1671Msdiv 65Pattena pattasañchanne,
antobyamhavare imaṃ;
Attharitvā pārupitvā,
tuvaṭṭissanti tāvade.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1672Msdiv 66Bhavanaṃ parivāretvā,
samantā satayojane;
Tepi padmā lohitakā,
dibbagandhaṃ pavāyare.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1673Msdiv 67Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_1674Msdiv 68Sampattiyo duve bhutvā,
anīti anupaddavo;
Sampatte pariyosāne,
nibbānaṃ nibbānaṃ pāpuṇissati’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1675Msdiv 69Sudiṭṭho vata me buddho,
vāṇijjaṃ supayojitaṃ;
Padmāni tīṇi pūjetvā,
anubhosiṃ tisampadā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1676Msdiv 70Ajja me dhammappattassa,
vippamuttassa sabbaso;
Supupphitaṃ lohitakaṃ,
dhārayissati matthake.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_1677Msdiv 71Mama kammaṃ kathentassa,
padumuttarasatthuno;
Satapāṇasahassānaṃ,
dhammābhisamayo ahu.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_1678Msdiv 72Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tipadumānidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_1679Msdiv 73Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 27PTS vp Pali 1.126Ms 20Ap1_1680Msdiv 74Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1486)

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_1681Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tipadumiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_1682Tipadumiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_1683Nāgasamālavaggo aṭṭhamo.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_1684Tassuddānaṃ

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_1685Nāgasamālo padasaññī,
saññakāluvadāyako;
Ekasaññī tiṇasanthāro,
sūcipāṭalipupphiyo;
Ṭhitañjalī tipadumī,
gāthāyo pañcasattati.
