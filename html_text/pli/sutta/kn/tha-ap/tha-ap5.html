5. Mahākassapattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_409Msdiv 398“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
Pūjaṃ kubbanti satthuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_410Msdiv 399Udaggacittā janatā,
āmoditapamoditā;
Tesu saṃvegajātesu,
pīti me udapajjatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_411Msdiv 400Ñātimitte samānetvā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
Parinibbuto mahāvīro,
handa pūjaṃ karomase.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_412Msdiv 401Sādhūti te paṭissutvā,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janiṃsu me;
Buddhasmiṃ lokanāthamhi,
kāhāma puññasañcayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_413Msdiv 402Agghiyaṃ sukataṃ katvā,
satahatthasamuggataṃ;
Diyaḍḍhahatthapatthaṭaṃ,
vimānaṃ nabhamuggataṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_414Msdiv 403Katvāna hammiyaṃ tattha,
tālapantīhi cittitaṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
cetiyaṃ pūjayuttamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_415Msdiv 404Aggikkhandhova jalito,
kiṃsuko iva phullito;
Indalaṭṭhīva ākāse,
obhāsati catuddisā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_416Msdiv 405Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
katvāna kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Pubbakammaṃ saritvāna,
tidasaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 1.34Ms 20Ap1_417Msdiv 406Sahassayuttaṃ hayavāhiṃ,
Dibbayānamadhiṭṭhito;
Ubbiddhaṃ bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Sattabhūmaṃ samuggataṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_418Msdiv 407Kūṭāgārasahassāni,
sabbasoṇṇamayā ahuṃ;
Jalanti sakatejena,
disā sabbā pabhāsayaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_419Msdiv 408Santi aññepi niyyūhā,
lohitaṅgamayā tadā;
Tepi jotanti ābhāya,
samantā caturo disā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_420Msdiv 409Puññakammābhinibbattā,
kūṭāgārā sunimmitā;
Maṇimayāpi jotanti,
disā dasa samantato.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_421Msdiv 410Tesaṃ ujjotamānānaṃ,
obhāso vipulo ahu;
Sabbe deve abhibhomi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_422Msdiv 411Saṭṭhikappasahassamhi,
ubbiddho nāma khattiyo;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
pathaviṃ āvasiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_423Msdiv 412Tatheva bhaddake kappe,
tiṃsakkhattuṃ ahosahaṃ;
Sakakammābhiraddhomhi,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_424Msdiv 413Sattaratanasampanno,
catudīpamhi issaro;
Tatthāpi bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
indalaṭṭhīva uggataṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_425Msdiv 414Āyāmato catubbīsaṃ,
vitthārena ca dvādasa;
Rammaṇaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
daḷhapākāratoraṇaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_426Msdiv 415Āyāmato pañcasataṃ,
vitthārena tadaḍḍhakaṃ;
Ākiṇṇaṃ janakāyehi,
tidasānaṃ puraṃ viya.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_427Msdiv 416Yathā sūcighare sūcī,
pakkhittā paṇṇavīsati;
Aññamaññaṃ paghaṭṭenti,
ākiṇṇaṃ hoti laṅkataṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_428Msdiv 417Evampi nagaraṃ mayhaṃ,
hatthissarathasaṅkulaṃ;
Manussehi sadākiṇṇaṃ,
rammaṇaṃ nagaruttamaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_429Msdiv 418Tattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
puna devattanaṃ gato;
Bhave pacchimake mayhaṃ,
ahosi kulasampadā.

SC 22PTS vp Pali 1.35Ms 20Ap1_430Msdiv 419Brāhmaññakulasambhūto,
mahāratanasañcayo;
Asītikoṭiyo hitvā,
hiraññassāpi pabbajiṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_431Msdiv 420Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_432Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahākassapo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_433Mahākassapattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
