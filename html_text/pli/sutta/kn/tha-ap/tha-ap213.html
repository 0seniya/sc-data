1. Hatthidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2822Msdiv 1“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Nāgaseṭṭho mayā dinno,
īsādanto urūḷhavā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2823Msdiv 2Uttamatthaṃ anubhomi,
santipadamanuttaraṃ;
Nāgadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
sabbalokahitesino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2824Msdiv 3Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ nāga madadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
nāgadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2825Msdiv 4Aṭṭhasattatikappamhi,
soḷasāsiṃsu khattiyā;
Samantapāsādikā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2826Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2827Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā hatthidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2828Hatthidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
