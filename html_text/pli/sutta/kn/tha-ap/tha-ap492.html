10. Sālamaṇḍapiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6393Msdiv 190“Ajjhogāhetvā sālavanaṃ,
sukato assamo mama;
Sālapupphehi sañchanno,
vasāmi vipine tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6394Msdiv 191Piyadassī ca bhagavā,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
sālavanamupāgami.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6395Msdiv 192Assamā abhinikkhamma,
pavanaṃ agamāsahaṃ;
Mūlaphalaṃ gavesanto,
āhiṇḍāmi vane tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6396Msdiv 193Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
piyadassiṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Sunisinnaṃ samāpannaṃ,
virocantaṃ mahāvane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6397Msdiv 194Catudaṇḍe ṭhapetvāna,
buddhassa uparī ahaṃ;
Maṇḍapaṃ sukataṃ katvā,
sālapupphehi chādayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6398Msdiv 195Sattāhaṃ dhārayitvāna,
maṇḍapaṃ sālachāditaṃ;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
buddhaseṭṭhamavandahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6399Msdiv 196Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
vuṭṭhahitvā samādhito;
Yugamattaṃ pekkhamāno,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6400Msdiv 197Sāvako varuṇo nāma,
piyadassissa satthuno;
Vasīsatasahassehi,
upagacchi vināyakaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6401Msdiv 198Piyadassī ca bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
sitaṃ pātukarī jino.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6402Msdiv 199Anuruddho upaṭṭhāko,
piyadassissa satthuno;
Ekaṃsaṃ cīvaraṃ katvā,
apucchittha mahāmuniṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6403Msdiv 200‘Ko nu kho bhagavā hetu,
sitakammassa satthuno;
Kāraṇe vijjamānamhi,
satthā pātukare sitaṃ’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6404Msdiv 201‘Sattāhaṃ sālacchadanaṃ,
yo me dhāresi māṇavo;
Tassa kammaṃ saritvāna,
sitaṃ pātukariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6405Msdiv 202Anokāsaṃ na passāmi,
yattha puññaṃ vipaccati;
Devaloke manusse vā,
okāsova na sammati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6406Msdiv 203Devaloke vasantassa,
puññakammasamaṅgino;
Yāvatā parisā tassa,
sālacchannā bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6407Msdiv 204Tattha dibbehi naccehi,
gītehi vāditehi ca;
Ramissati sadā santo,
puññakammasamāhito.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6408Msdiv 205Yāvatā parisā tassa,
gandhagandhī bhavissati;
Sālassa pupphavasso ca,
pavassissati tāvade.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6409Msdiv 206Tato cutoyaṃ manujo,
mānusaṃ āgamissati;
Idhāpi sālacchadanaṃ,
sabbakālaṃ dharissati.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6410Msdiv 207Idha naccañca gītañca,
sammatāḷasamāhitaṃ;
Parivāressanti maṃ niccaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6411Msdiv 208Uggacchante ca sūriye,
sālavassaṃ pavassati;
Puññakammena saṃyuttaṃ,
vassate sabbakālikaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6412Msdiv 209Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6413Msdiv 210Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6414Msdiv 211Dhammaṃ abhisamentassa,
sālacchannaṃ bhavissati;
Citake jhāyamānassa,
chadanaṃ tattha hessati’.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6415Msdiv 212Vipākaṃ kittayitvāna,
piyadassī mahāmuni;
Parisāya dhammaṃ desesi,
tappento dhammavuṭṭhiyā.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6416Msdiv 213Tiṃsakappāni devesu,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Saṭṭhi ca sattakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6417Msdiv 214Devalokā idhāgantvā,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;
Idhāpi sālacchadanaṃ,
maṇḍapassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6418Msdiv 215Ayaṃ pacchimako mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Idhāpi sālacchadanaṃ,
hessati sabbakālikaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6419Msdiv 216Mahāmuniṃ tosayitvā,
gotamaṃ sakyapuṅgavaṃ;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6420Msdiv 217Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6421Msdiv 218Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_6422Msdiv 219Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_6423Msdiv 220Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5158)

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_6424Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sālamaṇḍapiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_6425Sālamaṇḍapiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_6426Paṃsukūlavaggo ekūnapaññāsamo.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_6427Tassuddānaṃ

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_6428Paṃsukūlaṃ buddhasaññī,
bhisado ñāṇakittako;
Candanī dhātupūjī ca,
pulinuppādakopi ca.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_6429Taraṇo dhammaruciko,
sālamaṇḍapiyo tathā;
Satāni dve honti gāthā,
ūnavīsatimeva ca.
