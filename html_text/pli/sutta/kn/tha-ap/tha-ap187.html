5. Isimuggadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2612SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2612Msdiv 24“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
padumuttaranāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2613Msdiv 25Isimuggāni SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2613Msdiv 25Isimuggāni pisitvā,
madhukhudde anīḷake;
Pāsādeva ṭhito santo,
adāsiṃ lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2614SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2614Msdiv 26Aṭṭhasatasahassāni,
ahesuṃ buddhasāvakā;
Sabbesaṃ pattapūrentaṃ,
tato cāpi bahuttaraṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2615SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2615Msdiv 27Tena cittappasādena,
sukkamūlena codito;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2616SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2616Msdiv 28Cattālīsamhi sahasse,
kappānaṃ aṭṭhatiṃsa te;
Isimuggasanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2617SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2617Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2618Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā isimuggadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2619Isimuggadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
