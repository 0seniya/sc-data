10. Padumapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2168Msdiv 97“Himavantassāvidūre,
gotamo nāma pabbato;
Nānārukkhehi sañchanno,
mahābhūtagaṇālayo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2169Msdiv 98Vemajjhamhi ca tassāsi,
assamo abhinimmito;
Purakkhato sasissehi,
vasāmi assame ahaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.163Ms 20Ap1_2170Msdiv 99Āyantu me sissagaṇā,
padumaṃ āharantu me;
Buddhapūjaṃ karissāmi,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2171Msdiv 100Evanti te paṭissutvā,
padumaṃ āhariṃsu me;
Tathā nimittaṃ katvāhaṃ,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2172Msdiv 101Sisse tadā samānetvā,
sādhukaṃ anusāsahaṃ;
Mā kho tumhe pamajjittha,
appamādo sukhāvaho.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2173Msdiv 102Evaṃ samanusāsitvā,
te sisse vacanakkhame;
Appamādaguṇe yutto,
tadā kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2174Msdiv 103Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2175Msdiv 104Ekapaññāsakappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ jaluttamo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2176Msdiv 105Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1863)

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2177Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2178Padumapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_2179Sereyyavaggo terasamo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_2180Tassuddānaṃ

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_2181Sereyyako pupphathūpi,
pāyaso gandhathomako;
Āsani phalasaññī ca,
gaṇṭhipadumapupphiyo;
Pañcuttarasatā gāthā,
gaṇitā atthadassibhi.
