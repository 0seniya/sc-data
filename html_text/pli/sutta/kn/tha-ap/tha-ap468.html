6 Avaṭaphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.409Ms 20Ap1_6005Msdiv 26“Sataraṃsī nāma bhagavā,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
gocarāyābhinikkhami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6006Msdiv 27Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
adāsiṃ avaṭaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6007Msdiv 28Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6008Msdiv 29Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6009Msdiv 30Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6010Msdiv 31Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6011Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6012Avaṭaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
