6. Upāhanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.228Ms 20Ap1_3123Msdiv 20“Ahosiṃ candano nāma,
sambuddhassatrajo tadā;
Ekopāhano mayā dinno,
bodhiṃ sampajja me tuvaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3124Msdiv 21Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pānadhiṃ dadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
upāhanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3125Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3126Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upāhanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3127Upāhanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
