3. Sabbakittikattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.323Ms 20Ap1_4606Msdiv 304“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
dīparukkhaṃva ujjalaṃ;
Osadhiṃva virocantaṃ,
vijjutaṃ gagane yathā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4607Msdiv 305Asambhītaṃ anuttāsiṃ,
migarājaṃva kesariṃ;
Ñāṇālokaṃ pakāsentaṃ,
maddantaṃ titthiye gaṇe.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4608Msdiv 306Uddharantaṃ imaṃ lokaṃ,
chindantaṃ sabbasaṃsayaṃ;
Gajjantaṃ migarājaṃva,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4609Msdiv 307Jaṭājinadharo āsiṃ,
brahā uju patāpavā;
Vākacīraṃ gahetvāna,
pādamūle apatthariṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4610Msdiv 308Kāḷānusāriyaṃ gayha,
anulimpiṃ tathāgataṃ;
Sambuddhamanulimpetvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4611Msdiv 309‘Samuddharasimaṃ lokaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamahāmuni;
Ñāṇālokena jotesi,
nāvaṭaṃ ñāṇamuttamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4612Msdiv 310Dhammacakkaṃ pavattesi,
maddase paratitthiye;
Usabho jitasaṅgāmo,
sampakampesi medaniṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4613Msdiv 311Mahāsamudde ūmiyo,
velantamhi pabhijjare;
Tatheva tava ñāṇamhi,
sabbadiṭṭhī pabhijjare.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4614Msdiv 312Sukhumacchikajālena,
saramhi sampatānite;
Antojālikatā pāṇā,
pīḷitā honti tāvade.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4615Msdiv 313Tatheva titthiyā loke,
puthupāsaṇḍanissitā;
Antoñāṇavare tuyhaṃ,
parivattanti mārisa.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4616Msdiv 314Patiṭṭhā vuyhataṃ oghe,
tvañhi nātho abandhunaṃ;
Bhayaṭṭitānaṃ saraṇaṃ,
muttitthīnaṃ parāyaṇaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4617Msdiv 315Ekavīro asadiso,
mettākaruṇasañcayo;
Asamo susamo santo,
vasī tādī jitañjayo.

SC 13PTS vp Pali 1.324Ms 20Ap1_4618Msdiv 316Dhīro vigatasammoho,
anejo akathaṃkathī;
Tusito vantadososi,
nimmalo saṃyato suci.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4619Msdiv 317Saṅgātigo Saṅgātigo hatamado,
tevijjo tibhavantago;
Sīmātigo dhammagaru,
gatattho hitavabbhuto.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4620Msdiv 318Tārako tvaṃ yathā nāvā,
nidhīvassāsakārako;
Asambhīto yathā sīho,
gajarājāva dappito’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4621Msdiv 319Thometvā dasagāthāhi,
padumuttaraṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
tuṇhī aṭṭhāsahaṃ tadā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4622Msdiv 320Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4623Msdiv 321‘Yo me sīlañca ñāṇañca,
saddhammañcāpi vaṇṇayi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4624Msdiv 322Saṭṭhi kappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Aññe devebhibhavitvā,
issaraṃ kārayissati.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4625Msdiv 323So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4626Msdiv 324Pabbajitvāna kāyena,
pāpakammaṃ vivajjiya;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4627Msdiv 325Yathāpi megho thanayaṃ,
tappeti mediniṃ imaṃ;
Tatheva tvaṃ mahāvīra,
dhammena tappayī mamaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4628Msdiv 326Sīlaṃ paññañca dhammañca,
thavitvā lokanāyakaṃ;
Pattomhi paramaṃ santiṃ,
nibbānaṃ padamaccutaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4629Msdiv 327Aho nūna sa bhagavā,
Ciraṃ tiṭṭheyya cakkhumā;
Aññātañca vijāneyyuṃ,
Phuseyyuṃ amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4630Msdiv 328Ayaṃ me pacchimā jāti,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4631Msdiv 329Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhithomayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 27PTS vp Pali 1.325Ms 20Ap1_4632Msdiv 330Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4633Msdiv 331Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4634Msdiv 332Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_4635Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sabbakittiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_4636Sabbakittikattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
