4. Kesarapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4050SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4050Msdiv 12“Vijjādharo tadā āsiṃ,
himavantamhi pabbate;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
caṅkamantaṃ mahāyasaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4051Msdiv 13Tīṇi SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4051Msdiv 13Tīṇi kesarapupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
vessabhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4052SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4052Msdiv 14Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4053SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4053Msdiv 15Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4054Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kesarapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4055Kesarapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
