7. Sīhāsanikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5828SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5828Msdiv 42“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Sabbabhūtahitesino;
Pasannacitto sumano,
Sīhāsanamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5829SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5829Msdiv 43Devaloke manusse vā,
yattha yattha vasāmahaṃ;
Labhāmi vipulaṃ byamhaṃ,
sīhāsanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5830SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5830Msdiv 44Soṇṇamayā rūpimayā,
lohitaṅgamayā bahū;
Maṇimayā ca pallaṅkā,
nibbattanti mamaṃ sadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5831SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5831Msdiv 45Bodhiyā āsanaṃ katvā,
jalajuttamanāmino;
Ucce kule pajāyāmi,
aho dhammasudhammatā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5832SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5832Msdiv 46Satasahassito kappe,
sīhāsanamakāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sīhāsanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5833SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5833Msdiv 47Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5834SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5834Msdiv 48Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5835SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5835Msdiv 49Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5836Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sīhāsaniko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5837Sīhāsanikattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
