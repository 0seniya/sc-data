2. Ekasaṅkhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5678Msdiv 13“Vipassino bhagavato,
mahābodhimaho ahu;
Mahājanā samāgamma,
pūjenti bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5679Msdiv 14Na hi taṃ orakaṃ maññe,
buddhaseṭṭho bhavissati;
Yassāyaṃ īdisā bodhi,
pūjanīyā ca satthuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5680Msdiv 15Tato saṅkhaṃ gahetvāna,
bodhirukkhamupaṭṭhahiṃ;
Dhamanto sabbadivasaṃ,
avandiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5681Msdiv 16Āsannake kataṃ kammaṃ,
devalokaṃ apāpayī;
Kaḷevaraṃ me patitaṃ,
devaloke ramāmahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5682Msdiv 17Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā;
Sadā mayhaṃ upaṭṭhanti,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5683Msdiv 18Ekasattatime kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sudassano;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
jambumaṇḍassa issaro.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5684Msdiv 19Tato aṅgasatā turiyā,
parivārenti maṃ sadā;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5685Msdiv 20Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Mātukucchigatassāpi,
vajjare bheriyo sadā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5686Msdiv 21Upaṭṭhitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
anubhutvāna sampadā;
Sivaṃ sukhemaṃ amataṃ,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5687Msdiv 22Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5688Msdiv 23Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5689Msdiv 24Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5690Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5691Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekasaṅkhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5692Ekasaṅkhiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
