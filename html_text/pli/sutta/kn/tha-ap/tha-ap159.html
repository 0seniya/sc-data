7. Tiṇasūlakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2399Msdiv 35“Himavantassāvidūre,
bhūtagaṇo nāma pabbato;
Vasateko jino tattha,
sayambhū lokanissaṭo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2400Msdiv 36Tiṇasūlaṃ gahetvāna,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;
Ekūnasatasahassaṃ,
kappaṃ na vinipātiko.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2401Msdiv 37Ito ekādase kappe,
ekosiṃ dharaṇīruho;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2402Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2403Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇasūlako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2404Tiṇasūlakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
