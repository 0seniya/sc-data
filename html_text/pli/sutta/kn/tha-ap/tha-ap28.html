6. Dhūpadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1030Msdiv 161“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Kuṭidhūpaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1031Msdiv 162Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbesampi piyo homi,
dhūpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1032Msdiv 163Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dhūpamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhūpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1033Msdiv 164Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1034Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhūpadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1035Dhūpadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
