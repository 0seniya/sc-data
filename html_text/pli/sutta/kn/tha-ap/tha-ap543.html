1 Lakuṇḍabhaddiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7334Msdiv 1“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7335Msdiv 2Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
seṭṭhiputto mahaddhano;
Jaṅghāvihāraṃ vicaraṃ,
saṃghārāmaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7336Msdiv 3Tadā so lokapajjoto,
dhammaṃ desesi nāyako;
Mañjussarānaṃ pavaraṃ,
sāvakaṃ abhikittayi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7337Msdiv 4Taṃ sutvā mudito hutvā,
kāraṃ katvā mahesino;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7338Msdiv 5Tadā buddho viyākāsi,
saṃghamajjhe vināyako;
‘Anāgatamhi addhāne,
lacchase taṃ manorathaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7339Msdiv 6Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7340Msdiv 7Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Bhaddiyo nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7341Msdiv 8Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7342Msdiv 9Dvenavute ito kappe,
phusso uppajji nāyako;
Durāsado duppasaho,
sabbalokuttamo jino.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7343Msdiv 10Caraṇena ca sampanno,
brahā uju patāpavā;
Hitesī sabbasattānaṃ,
bahuṃ mocesi bandhanā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7344Msdiv 11Nandārāmavane tassa,
ahosiṃ phussakokilo;
Gandhakuṭisamāsanne,
ambarukkhe vasāmahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7345Msdiv 12Tadā piṇḍāya gacchantaṃ,
dakkhiṇeyyaṃ jinuttamaṃ;
Disvā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
mañjunābhinikūjahaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7346Msdiv 13Rājuyyānaṃ tadā gantvā,
supakkaṃ kanakattacaṃ;
Ambapiṇḍaṃ gahetvāna,
sambuddhassopanāmayiṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7347Msdiv 14Tadā me cittamaññāya,
mahākāruṇiko jino;
Upaṭṭhākassa hatthato,
pattaṃ paggaṇhi nāyako.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7348Msdiv 15Adāsiṃ haṭṭhacittohaṃ,
ambapiṇḍaṃ mahāmune;
Patte pakkhippa pakkhehi,
pañjaliṃ katvāna mañjunā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7349Msdiv 16Sarena rajanīyena,
savanīyena vaggunā;
Vassanto buddhapūjatthaṃ,
nīḷaṃ gantvā nipajjahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7350Msdiv 17Tadā muditacittaṃ maṃ,
buddhapemagatāsayaṃ;
Sakuṇagghi upāgantvā,
ghātayī duṭṭhamānaso.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7351Msdiv 18Tato cutohaṃ tusite,
anubhotvā mahāsukhaṃ;
Manussayonimāgacchiṃ,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7352Msdiv 19Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7353Msdiv 20Sāsanaṃ jotayitvā so,
abhibhuyya kutitthiye;
Vinayitvāna veneyye,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7354Msdiv 21Nibbute tamhi lokagge,
pasannā janatā bahū;
Pūjanatthāya buddhassa,
thūpaṃ kubbanti satthuno.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7355Msdiv 22‘Sattayojanikaṃ thūpaṃ,
sattaratanabhūsitaṃ;
Karissāma mahesissa’,
iccevaṃ mantayanti te.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7356Msdiv 23Kikino kāsirājassa,
tadā senāya nāyako;
Hutvāhaṃ appamāṇassa,
pamāṇaṃ cetiye vadiṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7357Msdiv 24Tadā te mama vākyena,
cetiyaṃ yojanuggataṃ;
Akaṃsu naravīrassa,
nānāratanabhūsitaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7358Msdiv 25Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7359Msdiv 26Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jāto seṭṭhikule ahaṃ;
Sāvatthiyaṃ puravare,
iddhe phīte mahaddhane.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7360Msdiv 27Purappavese sugataṃ,
disvā vimhitamānaso;
Pabbajitvāna na ciraṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7361Msdiv 28Cetiyassa pamāṇaṃ yaṃ,
akariṃ tena kammunā;
Lakuṇḍakasarīrohaṃ,
jāto paribhavāraho.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7362Msdiv 29Sarena madhurenāhaṃ,
pūjitvā isisattamaṃ;
Mañjussarānaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ,
aggattamanupāpuṇiṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7363Msdiv 30Phaladānena buddhassa,
guṇānussaraṇena ca;
Sāmaññaphalasampanno,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7364Msdiv 31Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7365Msdiv 32Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
buddhaseṭṭhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7366Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7367Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā lakuṇḍabhaddiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7368Lakuṇḍabhaddiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
