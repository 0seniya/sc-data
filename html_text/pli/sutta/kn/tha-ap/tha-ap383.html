1 Avaṭaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4177Msdiv 1“Sataraṃsi nāma bhagavā,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
gocarāyābhinikkhami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4178Msdiv 2Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
avaṭaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4179Msdiv 3Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4180Msdiv 4Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4181Msdiv 5Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.295Ms 20Ap1_4182Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4183Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4184Avaṭaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
