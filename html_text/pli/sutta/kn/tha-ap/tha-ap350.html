8. Tiṇamuṭṭhidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3942Msdiv 48“Himavantassāvidūre,
lambako nāma pabbato;
Upatisso nāma sambuddho,
abbhokāsamhi caṅkami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3943Msdiv 49Migaluddo tadā āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Disvāna taṃ devadevaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3944Msdiv 50Vippasannena cittena,
tadā tassa mahesino;
Nisīdanatthaṃ buddhassa,
tiṇamuṭṭhimadāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3945Msdiv 51Datvāna devadevassa,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3946Msdiv 52Aciraṃ gatamattaṃ maṃ,
migarājā apothayi;
Sīhena pothito santo,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3947Msdiv 53Āsanne me kataṃ kammaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhe anāsave;
Sumutto saravegova,
devalokaṃ agañchahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3948Msdiv 54Yūpo tattha subho āsi,
puññakammābhinimmito;
Sahassakaṇḍo satabheṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3949Msdiv 55Pabhā niddhāvate tassa,
sataraṃsīva uggato;
Ākiṇṇo devakaññāhi,
āmodiṃ kāmakāmahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3950Msdiv 56Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Āgantvāna manussattaṃ,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3951Msdiv 57Catunnavutito kappe,
nisīdanamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇamuṭṭhiyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3952Msdiv 58Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3953Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇamuṭṭhidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3954Tiṇamuṭṭhidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
