7. Kuṭajapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6739Msdiv 37“Himavantassāvidūre,
vasalo nāma pabbato;
Buddho sudassano nāma,
vasate pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6740Msdiv 38Pupphaṃ hemavantaṃ gayha,
vehāsaṃ agamāsahaṃ;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6741Msdiv 39Pupphaṃ kuṭajamādāya,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sayambhussa mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6742Msdiv 40Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6743Msdiv 41Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6744Msdiv 42Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6745Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6746Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kuṭajapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6747Kuṭajapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
