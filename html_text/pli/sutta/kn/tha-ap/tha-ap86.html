4. Adhopupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1713Msdiv 22“Abhibhū nāma so bhikkhu,
sikhino aggasāvako;
Mahānubhāvo tevijjo,
himavantaṃ upāgami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1714Msdiv 23Ahampi himavantamhi,
ramaṇīyassame isi;
Vasāmi appamaññāsu,
iddhīsu ca tadā vasī.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1715Msdiv 24Pakkhijāto viyākāse,
pabbataṃ adhivatthayiṃ;
Adhopupphaṃ gahetvāna,
āgacchiṃ pabbataṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1716Msdiv 25Satta pupphāni gaṇhitvā,
matthake okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Ālokite ca vīrena,
pakkāmiṃ pācināmukho.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 1.129Ms 20Ap1_1717Msdiv 26Āvāsaṃ abhisambhosiṃ,
patvāna assamaṃ ahaṃ;
Khāribhāraṃ gahetvāna,
pāyāsiṃ pabbatantaraṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1718Msdiv 27Ajagaro maṃ pīḷesi,
ghorarūpo mahabbalo;
Pubbakammaṃ saritvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1719Msdiv 28Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1720Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1721Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā adhopupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1722Adhopupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
