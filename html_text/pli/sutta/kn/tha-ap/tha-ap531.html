9. Āmaṇḍaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6887SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6887Msdiv 74“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,
caṅkamī lokanāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6888SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6888Msdiv 75Khāribhāraṃ gahetvāna,
āharanto phalaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
caṅkamantaṃ mahāmuniṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6889SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6889Msdiv 76Pasannacitto sumano,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
āmaṇḍamadadiṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6890SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6890Msdiv 77Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
āmaṇḍassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6891SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6891Msdiv 78Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6892SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6892Msdiv 79Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6893SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6893Msdiv 80Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6894Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āmaṇḍaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6895Āmaṇḍaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
