5. Raṃsisaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1723Msdiv 30“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vāsaṃ kappesahaṃ pure;
Ajinuttaravāsohaṃ,
vasāmi pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1724Msdiv 31Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Sataraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Vanantaragataṃ disvā,
Sālarājaṃva pupphitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1725Msdiv 32Raṃsyā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
vipassissa mahesino;
Paggayha añjaliṃ vandiṃ,
sirasā ukkuṭī ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1726Msdiv 33Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
raṃsisaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1727Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1728Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1729Raṃsisaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
