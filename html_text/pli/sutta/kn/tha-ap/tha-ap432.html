10 Soṇṇakontarikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5645Msdiv 157“Manobhāvaniyaṃ buddhaṃ,
attadantaṃ samāhitaṃ;
Iriyamānaṃ brahmapathe,
cittavūpasame rataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5646Msdiv 158Nittiṇṇaoghaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
jhāyiṃ jhānarataṃ muniṃ;
Upatitthaṃ samāpannaṃ,
indivaradalappabhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5647Msdiv 159Alābunodakaṃ gayha,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ upāgamiṃ;
Buddhassa pāde dhovitvā,
alābukamadāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5648Msdiv 160Āṇāpesi ca sambuddho,
padumuttaranāmako;
‘Iminā dakamāhatvā,
pādamūle ṭhapehi me’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5649Msdiv 161Sādhūtihaṃ paṭissutvā,
satthugāravatāya ca;
Dakaṃ alābunāhatvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5650Msdiv 162Anumodi mahāvīro,
cittaṃ nibbāpayaṃ mama;
‘Iminālābudānena,
saṅkappo te samijjhatu’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5651Msdiv 163Pannarasesu kappesu,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Tiṃsatikkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5652Msdiv 164Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
caṅkamantassa tiṭṭhato;
Sovaṇṇaṃ kontaraṃ gayha,
tiṭṭhate purato mama.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5653Msdiv 165Buddhassa datvānalābuṃ,
labhāmi soṇṇakontaraṃ;
Appakampi kataṃ kāraṃ,
vipulaṃ hoti tādisu.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5654Msdiv 166Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃlābumadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
alābussa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5655Msdiv 167Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5656Msdiv 168Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5657Msdiv 169Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4537)

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5658Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā soṇṇakontariko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5659Soṇṇakontarikattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5660Sakiṃsammajjakavaggo tecattālīsamo.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5661Tassuddānaṃ

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5662Sakiṃsammajjako thero,
ekadussī ekāsanī;
Kadambakoraṇḍakado,
ghatassavanikopi ca.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5663Sucintiko kiṅkaṇiko,
soṇṇakontarikopi ca;
Ekagāthāsatañcettha,
ekasattatimeva ca.
