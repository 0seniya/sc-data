1 Mahākaccāyanattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6948Msdiv 1“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
anejo ajitaṃ jayo;
Satasahasse kappānaṃ,
ito uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6949Msdiv 2Vīro kamalapattakkho,
sasaṅkavimalānano;
Kanakācalasaṅkāso,
ravidittisamappabho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6950Msdiv 3Sattanettamanohārī,
varalakkhaṇabhūsito;
Sabbavākyapathātīto,
manujāmarasakkato.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6951Msdiv 4Sambuddho bodhayaṃ satte,
vāgīso madhurassaro;
Karuṇānibandhasantāno,
parisāsu visārado.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 2.464Ms 20Ap1_6952Msdiv 5Deseti madhuraṃ dhammaṃ,
catusaccūpasaṃhitaṃ;
Nimugge mohapaṅkamhi,
samuddharati pāṇine.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6953Msdiv 6Tadā ekacaro hutvā,
tāpaso himavālayo;
Nabhasā mānusaṃ lokaṃ,
gacchanto jinamaddasaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6954Msdiv 7Upecca santikaṃ tassa,
assosiṃ dhammadesanaṃ;
Vaṇṇayantassa vīrassa,
sāvakassa mahāguṇaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6955Msdiv 8‘Saṃkhittena mayā vuttaṃ,
vitthārena pakāsayaṃ;
Parisaṃ mañca toseti,
yathā kaccāyano ayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6956Msdiv 9Nāhaṃ evamidhekaccaṃ,
aññaṃ passāmi sāvakaṃ;
Tasmātadagge esaggo,
evaṃ dhāretha bhikkhavo’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6957Msdiv 10Tadāhaṃ vimhito hutvā,
sutvā vākyaṃ manoramaṃ;
Himavantaṃ gamitvāna,
āhitvā pupphasañcayaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6958Msdiv 11Pūjetvā lokasaraṇaṃ,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ;
Tadā mamāsayaṃ ñatvā,
byākāsi sa raṇañjaho.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6959Msdiv 12‘Passathetaṃ isivaraṃ,
niddhantakanakattacaṃ;
Uddhaggalomaṃ pīṇaṃsaṃ,
acalaṃ pañjaliṃ ṭhitaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6960Msdiv 13Hāsaṃ supuṇṇanayanaṃ,
buddhavaṇṇagatāsayaṃ;
Dhammajaṃ uggahadayaṃ,
amatāsittasannibhaṃ’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6961Msdiv 14Kaccānassa guṇaṃ sutvā,
taṃ ṭhānaṃ patthayaṃ ṭhito;
Anāgatamhi addhāne,
gotamassa mahāmune.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6962Msdiv 15Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Kaccāno nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6963Msdiv 16Bahussuto mahāñāṇī,
adhippāyavidū mune;
Pāpuṇissati taṃ ṭhānaṃ,
yathāyaṃ byākato mayā.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6964Msdiv 17Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6965Msdiv 18Duve bhave saṃsarāmi,
devatte atha mānuse;
Aññaṃ gatiṃ na gacchāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6966Msdiv 19Duve kule pajāyāmi,
khattiye atha brāhmaṇe;
Nīce kule na jāyāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 2.465Ms 20Ap1_6967Msdiv 20Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jāto ujjeniyaṃ pure;
Pajjotassa ca caṇḍassa,
purohitadijādhino.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6968Msdiv 21Putto tiriṭivacchassa,
nipuṇo vedapāragū;
Mātā ca candimā nāma,
kaccānohaṃ varattaco.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6969Msdiv 22Vīmaṃsanatthaṃ buddhassa,
bhūmipālena pesito;
Disvā mokkhapuradvāraṃ,
nāyakaṃ guṇasañcayaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6970Msdiv 23Sutvā ca vimalaṃ vākyaṃ,
gatipaṅkavisosanaṃ;
Pāpuṇiṃ amataṃ santaṃ,
sesehi saha sattahi.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6971Msdiv 24Adhippāyavidū jāto,
sugatassa mahāmate;
Ṭhapito etadagge ca,
susamiddhamanoratho.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6972Msdiv 25Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6973Msdiv 26Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6974Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6975Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahākaccāyano thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6976Mahākaccāyanattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
