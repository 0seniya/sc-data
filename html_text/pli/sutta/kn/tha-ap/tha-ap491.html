9 Dhammaruciyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6365Msdiv 164“Yadā dīpaṅkaro buddho,
sumedhaṃ byākarī jino;
‘Aparimeyye ito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6366Msdiv 165Imassa janikā mātā,
māyā nāma bhavissati;
Pitā suddhodano nāma,
ayaṃ hessati gotamo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6367Msdiv 166Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
katvā dukkarakārikaṃ;
Assatthamūle sambuddho,
bujjhissati mahāyaso.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6368Msdiv 167Upatisso kolito ca,
aggā hessanti sāvakā;
Ānando nāma nāmena,
upaṭṭhissatimaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6369Msdiv 168Khemā uppalavaṇṇā ca,
aggā hessanti sāvikā;
Citto āḷavako ceva,
aggā hessantupāsakā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6370Msdiv 169Khujjuttarā nandamātā,
aggā hessantupāsikā;
Bodhi imassa vīrassa,
assatthoti pavuccati’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6371Msdiv 170Idaṃ sutvāna vacanaṃ,
asamassa mahesino;
Āmoditā naramarū,
namassanti katañjalī.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6372Msdiv 171Tadāhaṃ māṇavo āsiṃ,
megho nāma susikkhito;
Sutvā byākaraṇaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
sumedhassa mahāmune.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6373Msdiv 172Saṃvisaṭṭho bhavitvāna,
sumedhe karuṇāsaye;
Pabbajantañca taṃ vīraṃ,
sahāva anupabbajiṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6374Msdiv 173Saṃvuto pātimokkhasmiṃ,
indriyesu ca pañcasu;
Suddhājīvo sato vīro,
jinasāsanakārako.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6375Msdiv 174Evaṃ viharamānohaṃ,
pāpamittena kenaci;
Niyojito anācāre,
sumaggā paridhaṃsito.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6376Msdiv 175Vitakkavasiko hutvā,
sāsanato apakkamiṃ;
Pacchā tena kumittena,
payutto mātughātanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6377Msdiv 176Akariṃ ānantariyaṃ,
ghātayiṃ duṭṭhamānaso;
Tato cuto mahāvīciṃ,
upapanno sudāruṇaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6378Msdiv 177Vinipātagato santo,
saṃsariṃ dukkhito ciraṃ;
Na puno addasaṃ vīraṃ,
sumedhaṃ narapuṅgavaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6379Msdiv 178Asmiṃ kappe samuddamhi,
maccho āsiṃ timiṅgalo;
Disvāhaṃ sāgare nāvaṃ,
gocaratthamupāgamiṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6380Msdiv 179Disvā maṃ vāṇijā bhītā,
buddhaseṭṭhamanussaruṃ;
Gotamoti mahāghosaṃ,
sutvā tehi udīritaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6381Msdiv 180Pubbasaññaṃ saritvāna,
tato kālaṅkato ahaṃ;
Sāvatthiyaṃ kule iddhe,
jāto brāhmaṇajātiyaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6382Msdiv 181Āsiṃ dhammaruci nāma,
sabbapāpajigucchako;
Disvāhaṃ lokapajjotaṃ,
jātiyā sattavassiko.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6383Msdiv 182Mahājetavanaṃ gantvā,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Upemi buddhaṃ tikkhattuṃ,
rattiyā divasassa ca.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6384Msdiv 183Tadā disvā muni āha,
ciraṃ dhammarucīti maṃ;
Tatohaṃ avacaṃ buddhaṃ,
pubbakammapabhāvitaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6385Msdiv 184Suciraṃ satapuññalakkhaṇaṃ,
Patipubbena visuddhapaccayaṃ;
Ahamajjasupekkhanaṃ vata,
Tava passāmi nirupamaṃ viggahaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6386Msdiv 185Suciraṃ vihatattamo mayā,
Sucirakkhena nadī visositā;
Suciraṃ amalaṃ visodhitaṃ,
Nayanaṃ ñāṇamayaṃ mahāmune.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6387Msdiv 186Cirakālasamaṅgito tayā,
Avinaṭṭho punarantaraṃ ciraṃ;
Punarajjasamāgato tayā,
Na hi nassanti katāni gotama.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6388Msdiv 187Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6389Msdiv 188Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6390Msdiv 189Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6391Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhammaruciyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6392Dhammaruciyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
