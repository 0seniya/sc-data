1. Supāricariyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2433SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2433Msdiv 1“Padumo nāma nāmena,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Pavanā abhinikkhamma,
dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2434SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2434Msdiv 2Yakkhānaṃ samayo āsi,
avidūre mahesino;
Yena kiccena sampattā,
ajjhāpekkhiṃsu tāvade.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2435SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2435Msdiv 3Buddhassa giramaññāya,
amatassa ca desanaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
apphoṭetvā upaṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2436SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2436Msdiv 4Suciṇṇassa phalaṃ passa,
upaṭṭhānassa satthuno;
Tiṃsakappasahassesu,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2437SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2437Msdiv 5Ūnatiṃse kappasate,
samalaṅkatanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2438SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2438Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2439Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā supāricariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2440Supāricariyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
