4. Desapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.183Ms 20Ap1_2456Msdiv 18“Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Abbhuggantvāna vehāsaṃ,
gacchate anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2457Msdiv 19Yamhi dese ṭhito satthā,
abbhuggacchi mahāmuni;
Tāhaṃ desaṃ apūjesiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2458Msdiv 20Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
addasaṃ yaṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
desapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2459Msdiv 21Ekādase kappasate,
gosujātasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2460Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2461Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā desapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2462Desapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
