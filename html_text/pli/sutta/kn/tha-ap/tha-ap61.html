9. Padumattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1458Msdiv 67“Catusaccaṃ pakāsento,
varadhammappavattako;
Vassate amataṃ vuṭṭhiṃ,
nibbāpento mahājanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1459Msdiv 68Sadhajaṃ padumaṃ gayha,
aḍḍhakose ṭhito ahaṃ;
Padumuttaramunissa,
pahaṭṭho ukkhipimambare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1460Msdiv 69Āgacchante ca padume,
abbhuto āsi tāvade;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
paggaṇhi vadataṃ varo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1461Msdiv 70Karaseṭṭhena paggayha,
jalajaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1462Msdiv 71‘Yenidaṃ padumaṃ khittaṃ,
sabbaññumhi vināyake;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.110Ms 20Ap1_1463Msdiv 72Tiṃsakappāni devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ sattasataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1464Msdiv 73Tattha pattaṃ gaṇetvāna,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Ākāsato pupphavuṭṭhi,
abhivassissatī tadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1465Msdiv 74Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1466Msdiv 75Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1467Msdiv 76Nikkhamitvāna kucchimhā,
sampajāno patissato;
Jātiyā pañcavassohaṃ,
arahattaṃ apāpuṇiṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1468Msdiv 77Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1469Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1470Padumattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
