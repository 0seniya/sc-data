8 Āyāgadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1180Msdiv 94“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
sikhimhi vadataṃ vare;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
avandiṃ thūpamuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1181Msdiv 95Vaḍḍhakīhi kathāpetvā,
mūlaṃ datvānahaṃ tadā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
āyāgaṃ kārapesahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1182Msdiv 96Aṭṭha kappāni devesu,
abbokiṇṇaṃ vasiṃ ahaṃ;
Avasesesu kappesu,
vokiṇṇaṃ saṃsariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1183Msdiv 97Kāye visaṃ na kamati,
satthāni na ca hanti me;
Udakehaṃ na miyyāmi,
āyāgassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1184Msdiv 98Yadicchāmi ahaṃ vassaṃ,
mahāmegho pavassati;
Devāpi me vasaṃ enti,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1185Msdiv 99Sattaratanasampanno,
tiṃsakkhattuṃ ahosahaṃ;
Na maṃ kecāvajānanti,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.90Ms 20Ap1_1186Msdiv 100Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
āyāgaṃ yamakārayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
āyāgassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1187Msdiv 101Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1188Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āyāgadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1189Āyāgadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
