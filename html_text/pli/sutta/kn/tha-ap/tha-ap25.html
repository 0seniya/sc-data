3 Tisaraṇagamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_969Msdiv 106“Nagare candavatiyā,
mātuupaṭṭhāko ahuṃ;
Andhā mātā pitā mayhaṃ,
te posemi ahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_970Msdiv 107Rahogato nisīditvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
Posento mātāpitaro,
pabbajjaṃ na labhāmahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_971Msdiv 108Mahandhakārapihitā,
tividhaggīhi ḍayhare;
Etādise bhave jāte,
natthi koci vināyako.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_972Msdiv 109Buddho loke samuppanno,
dippati dāni sāsanaṃ;
Sakkā uddharituṃ attā,
puññakāmena jantunā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_973Msdiv 110Uggayha tīṇi saraṇe,
paripuṇṇāni gopayiṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
paṭimokkhāmi duggatiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_974Msdiv 111Nisabho nāma samaṇo,
buddhassa aggasāvako;
Tamahaṃ upagantvāna,
saraṇagamanaṃ gahiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_975Msdiv 112Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā saraṇagamanaṃ,
paripuṇṇaṃ agopayiṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_976Msdiv 113Carime vattamānamhi,
saraṇaṃ taṃ anussariṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
tāvatiṃsaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_977Msdiv 114Devalokagato santo,
puññakammasamāhito;
Yaṃ desaṃ upapajjāmi,
aṭṭha hetū labhāmahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_978Msdiv 115Disāsu pūjito homi,
tikkhapañño bhavāmahaṃ;
Sabbe devānuvattanti,
amitabhogaṃ labhāmahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_979Msdiv 116Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sabbattha,
paṭikanto bhavāmahaṃ;
Mittānaṃ acalo homi,
yaso abbhuggato mamaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_980Msdiv 117Asītikkhattu devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Dibbasukhaṃ anubhaviṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_981Msdiv 118Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_982Msdiv 119Pacchime bhave sampatte,
puññakammasamāhito;
Pure sāvatthiyaṃ jāto,
mahāsāle suaḍḍhake.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_983Msdiv 120Nagarā nikkhamitvāna,
dārakehi purakkhato;
Hasakhiḍḍasamaṅgīhaṃ,
saṃghārāmaṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_984Msdiv 121Tatthaddasāsiṃ samaṇaṃ,
vippamuttaṃ nirūpadhiṃ;
So me dhammamadesesi,
saraṇañca adāsi me.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_985Msdiv 122Sohaṃ sutvāna saraṇaṃ,
saraṇaṃ me anussariṃ;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_986Msdiv 123Jātiyā sattame vasse,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Upasampādayi buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_987Msdiv 124Aparimeyye ito kappe,
saraṇāni agacchahaṃ;
Tato me sukataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_988Msdiv 125Sugopitaṃ me saraṇaṃ,
mānasaṃ suppaṇīhitaṃ;
Anubhotvā yasaṃ sabbaṃ,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_989Msdiv 126Yesaṃ sotāvadhānatthi,
suṇotha mama bhāsato;
Ahaṃ vo kathayissāmi,
sāmaṃ diṭṭhaṃ padaṃ mama.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_990Msdiv 127‘Buddho loke samuppanno,
vattate jinasāsanaṃ;
Amatā vāditā bherī,
sokasallavinodanā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_991Msdiv 128Yathāsakena thāmena,
puññakkhette anuttare;
Adhikāraṃ kareyyātha,
passayissatha nibbutiṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_992Msdiv 129Paggayha tīṇi saraṇe,
pañcasīlāni gopiya;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
dukkhassantaṃ karissatha.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_993Msdiv 130Sammā dhammaṃ bhāvetvāna,
Sīlāni parigopiya;
Aciraṃ arahattaṃ vo,
Sabbepi pāpuṇissatha.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_994Msdiv 131Tevijjo iddhipattomhi,
cetopariyakovido;
Sāvako te mahāvīra,
saraṇo vandati satthuno’.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_995Msdiv 132Aparimeyye ito kappe,
saraṇaṃ buddhassa gacchahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saraṇaṃ gamane phalaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_996Msdiv 133Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_997Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tisaraṇagamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_998Tisaraṇagamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
