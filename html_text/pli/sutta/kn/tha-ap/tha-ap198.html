6. Kisalayapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.200Ms 20Ap1_2703Msdiv 28“Nagare dvāravatiyā,
mālāvaccho mamaṃ ahu;
Udapāno ca tattheva,
pādapānaṃ virohano.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2704Msdiv 29Sabalena upatthaddho,
siddhattho aparājito;
Mamānukampamāno so,
gacchate anilañjase.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2705Msdiv 30Aññaṃ kiñci na passāmi,
pūjāyoggaṃ mahesino;
Asokaṃ pallavaṃ disvā,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2706Msdiv 31Buddhassa te kisalayā,
gacchato yanti pacchato;
Tāhaṃ disvāna saṃvijiṃ,
aho buddhassuḷāratā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2707Msdiv 32Catunnavutito kappe,
pallavaṃ abhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2708Msdiv 33Sattatiṃse ito kappe,
eko ekissaro ahu;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2709Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2710Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kisalayapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2711Kisalayapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
