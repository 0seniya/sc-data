7. Hemakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5041Msdiv 183“Pabbhārakūṭaṃ nissāya,
anomo nāma tāpaso;
Assamaṃ sukataṃ katvā,
paṇṇasāle vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5042Msdiv 184Siddhaṃ tassa tapo kammaṃ,
siddhipatto sake bale;
Sakasāmaññavikkanto,
ātāpī nipako muni.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5043Msdiv 185Visārado sasamaye,
paravāde ca kovido;
Paṭṭho bhūmantalikkhamhi,
uppātamhi ca kovido.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5044Msdiv 186Vītasoko nirārambho,
appāhāro alolupo;
Lābhālābhena santuṭṭho,
jhāyī jhānarato muni.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5045Msdiv 187Piyadassī nāma sambuddho,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Satte tāretukāmo so,
karuṇāya pharī tadā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.352Ms 20Ap1_5046Msdiv 188Bodhaneyyaṃ janaṃ disvā,
piyadassī mahāmuni;
Cakkavāḷasahassampi,
gantvā ovadate muni.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5047Msdiv 189Mamuddharitukāmo so,
mamassamamupāgami;
Na diṭṭho me jino pubbe,
na sutopi ca kassaci.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5048Msdiv 190Uppātā supinā mayhaṃ,
lakkhaṇā suppakāsitā;
Paṭṭho bhūmantalikkhamhi,
nakkhattapadakovido.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5049Msdiv 191Sohaṃ buddhassa sutvāna,
tattha cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Tiṭṭhanto vā nisinno vā,
sarāmi niccakālikaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5050Msdiv 192Mayi evaṃ sarantamhi,
bhagavāpi anussari;
Buddhaṃ anussarantassa,
pīti me hoti tāvade.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5051Msdiv 193Kālañca punarāgamma,
upesi maṃ mahāmuni;
Sampattepi na jānāmi,
ayaṃ buddho mahāmuni.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5052Msdiv 194Anukampako kāruṇiko,
piyadassī mahāmuni;
Sañjānāpesi attānaṃ,
‘ahaṃ buddho sadevake’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5053Msdiv 195Sañjānitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
piyadassiṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5054Msdiv 196‘Aññe pīṭhe ca pallaṅke,
āsandīsu nisīdare;
Tuvampi sabbadassāvī,
nisīda ratanāsane’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5055Msdiv 197Sabbaratanamayaṃ pīṭhaṃ,
nimminitvāna tāvade;
Piyadassissa munino,
adāsiṃ iddhinimmitaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5056Msdiv 198Ratane ca nisinnassa,
pīṭhake iddhinimmite;
Kumbhamattaṃ jambuphalaṃ,
adāsiṃ tāvade ahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5057Msdiv 199Mama hāsaṃ janetvāna,
paribhuñji mahāmuni;
Tadā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
satthāraṃ abhivādayiṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5058Msdiv 200Piyadassī tu bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Ratanāsanamāsīno,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5059Msdiv 201‘Yo me ratanamayaṃ pīṭhaṃ,
amatañca phalaṃ adā;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5060Msdiv 202Sattasattati kappāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5061Msdiv 203Dvattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 22PTS vp Pali 2.353Ms 20Ap1_5062Msdiv 204Soṇṇamayaṃ rūpimayaṃ,
pallaṅkaṃ sukataṃ bahuṃ;
Lohitaṅgamayañceva,
lacchati ratanāmayaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5063Msdiv 205Caṅkamantampi manujaṃ,
puññakammasamaṅginaṃ;
Pallaṅkāni anekāni,
parivāressare tadā.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_5064Msdiv 206Kūṭāgārā ca pāsādā,
sayanañca mahārahaṃ;
Imassa cittamaññāya,
nibbattissanti tāvade.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_5065Msdiv 207Saṭṭhi nāgasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Suvaṇṇakacchā mātaṅgā,
hemakappanavāsasā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_5066Msdiv 208Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
tomaraṅkusapāṇibhi;
Imaṃ paricarissanti,
ratnapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_5067Msdiv 209Saṭṭhi assasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Ājānīyāva jātiyā,
sindhavā sīghavāhino.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_5068Msdiv 210Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
illiyācāpadhāribhi;
Tepimaṃ paricarissanti,
ratnapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_5069Msdiv 211Saṭṭhi rathasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Dīpā athopi veyagghā,
sannaddhā ussitaddhajā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_5070Msdiv 212Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
cāpahatthehi vammibhi;
Parivāressantimaṃ niccaṃ,
ratnapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_5071Msdiv 213Saṭṭhi dhenusahassāni,
dohaññā puṅgavūsabhe;
Vacchake janayissanti,
ratnapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_5072Msdiv 214Soḷasitthisahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
āmukkamaṇikuṇḍalā.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_5073Msdiv 215Aḷārapamhā hasulā,
susaññā tanumajjhimā;
Parivāressantimaṃ niccaṃ,
ratnapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_5074Msdiv 216Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
gotamo nāma cakkhumā;
Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamitvā,
buddho loke bhavissati.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_5075Msdiv 217Tassa dassanamāgamma,
pabbajissatikiñcano;
Tosayitvāna satthāraṃ,
sāsanebhiramissati.

SC 36PTS vp Pali 2.354Ms 20Ap1_5076Msdiv 218Tassa dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
kilese ghātayissati;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_5077Msdiv 219Vīriyaṃ me dhuradhorayhaṃ,
yogakkhemādhivāhanaṃ;
Uttamatthaṃ patthayanto,
sāsane viharāmahaṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_5078Msdiv 220Idaṃ pacchimakaṃ mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_5079Msdiv 221Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_5080Msdiv 222Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_5081Msdiv 223Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_5082Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā hemako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_5083Hemakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
Sattarasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
