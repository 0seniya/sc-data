6 Pādapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.246Ms 20Ap1_3394Msdiv 27“Pabbate himavantamhi,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3395Msdiv 28Upetaṃ tamahaṃ buddhaṃ,
vipassiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Candanaṃ tagarañcāpi,
pāde osiñcahaṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3396Msdiv 29Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pādaṃ abhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pādapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3397Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3398Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pādapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3399Pādapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
