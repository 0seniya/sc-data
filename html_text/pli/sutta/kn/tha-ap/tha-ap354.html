2. Yavakalāpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3977SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3977Msdiv 6“Nagare aruṇavatiyā,
āsiṃ yavasiko tadā;
Panthe disvāna sambuddhaṃ,
yavakalāpaṃ santhariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3978SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3978Msdiv 7Anukampako kāruṇiko,
sikhī lokagganāyako;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
nisīdi yavasanthare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3979SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3979Msdiv 8Disvā nisinnaṃ vimalaṃ,
mahājhāyiṃ vināyakaṃ;
Pāmojjaṃ janayitvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3980SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3980Msdiv 9Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
yavatthare idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3981SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3981Msdiv 10Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3982Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yavakalāpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3983Yavakalāpiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
