3. Paccāgamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1507Msdiv 13“Sindhuyā nadiyā tīre,
cakkavāko ahaṃ tadā;
Suddhasevālabhakkhohaṃ,
pāpesu ca susaññato.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1508Msdiv 14Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase;
Tuṇḍena sālaṃ paggayha,
vipassissābhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1509Msdiv 15Yassa saddhā tathāgate,
acalā supatiṭṭhitā;
Tena cittappasādena,
duggatiṃ so na gacchati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1510Msdiv 16Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
buddhaseṭṭhassa santike;
Vihaṅgamena santena,
subījaṃ ropitaṃ mayā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1511Msdiv 17Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1512Msdiv 18Sucārudassanā nāma,
aṭṭhete ekanāmakā;
Kappe sattarase āsuṃ,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1513Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1514Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paccāgamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1515Paccāgamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
