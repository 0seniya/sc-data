5. Kuṭidhūpakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3058Msdiv 17“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
ahosiṃ kuṭigopako;
Kālena kālaṃ dhūpesiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3059Msdiv 18Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhūpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.224Ms 20Ap1_3060Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3061Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kuṭidhūpako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3062Kuṭidhūpakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
