4. Dhammasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3450Msdiv 13“Vipassino bhagavato,
Mahābodhimaho ahu;
Rukkhaṭṭhasseva sambuddho,
Lokajeṭṭho narāsabho.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3451Msdiv 14Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Catusaccaṃ pakāseti,
vācāsabhimudīrayaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3452Msdiv 15Saṃkhittena ca desento,
vitthārena ca desayaṃ;
Vivaṭṭacchado sambuddho,
nibbāpesi mahājanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3453Msdiv 16Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3454Msdiv 17Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dhammamasuṇiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhammasavassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3455Msdiv 18Tettiṃsamhi ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Sutavā nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3456Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3457Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhammasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3458Dhammasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
