10 Piyālaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2265Msdiv 66“Pārāvato tadā āsiṃ,
paraṃ anuparodhako;
Pabbhāre seyyaṃ kappemi,
avidūre sikhisatthuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2266Msdiv 67Sāyaṃ pātañca passāmi,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Deyyadhammo ca me natthi,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.170Ms 20Ap1_2267Msdiv 68Piyālaphalamādāya,
agamaṃ buddhasantikaṃ;
Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2268Msdiv 69Tato paraṃ upādāya,
paricāriṃ vināyakaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2269Msdiv 70Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2270Msdiv 71Ito pannarase kappe,
tayo āsuṃ piyālino;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2271Msdiv 72Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1935)

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2272Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā piyālaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2273Piyālaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2274Sobhitavaggo cuddasamo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2275Tassuddānaṃ

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_2276Sobhitasudassano ca,
candano pupphachadano;
Raho campakapupphī ca,
atthasandassakena ca.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_2277Ekapasādī sāladado,
dasamo phaladāyako;
Gāthāyo sattati dve ca,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.
