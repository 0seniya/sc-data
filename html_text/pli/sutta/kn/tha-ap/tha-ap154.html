2. Tambapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2361Msdiv 7“Parakammāyane yutto,
aparādhaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Vanantaṃ abhidhāvissaṃ,
bhayaverasamappito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2362Msdiv 8Pupphitaṃ pādapaṃ disvā,
piṇḍibandhaṃ sunimmitaṃ;
Tambapupphaṃ gahetvāna,
bodhiyaṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2363Msdiv 9Sammajjitvāna taṃ bodhiṃ,
pāṭaliṃ pādaputtamaṃ;
Pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvāna,
bodhimūle upāvisiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2364Msdiv 10Gatamaggaṃ gavesantā,
āgacchuṃ mama santikaṃ;
Te ca disvānahaṃ tattha,
āvajjiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2365Msdiv 11Vanditvāna ahaṃ bodhiṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Anekatāle papatiṃ,
giridugge bhayānake.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2366Msdiv 12Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2367Msdiv 13Ito ca tatiye kappe,
rājā susaññato ahaṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2368Msdiv 14Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2369Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2370Tambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
