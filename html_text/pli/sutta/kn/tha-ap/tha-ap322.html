10. Kumudamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3577Msdiv 54“Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ vīraṃ,
mahesiṃ vijitāvinaṃ;
Vipassinaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3578Msdiv 55Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Gahetvā kumudaṃ mālaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ samokiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3579Msdiv 56Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3580Msdiv 57Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2820)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3581Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kumudamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3582Kumudamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3583Ārakkhadāyakavaggo bāttiṃsatimo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3584Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3585Ārakkhado bhojanado,
gatasaññī padumiyo;
Pupphāsanī santhaviko,
saddasaññī tiraṃsiyo;
Kandaliko kumudī ca,
sattapaññāsa gāthakāti.
