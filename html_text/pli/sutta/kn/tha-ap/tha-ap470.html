8 Pilakkhaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6023Msdiv 40“Vanantare buddhaṃ disvā,
atthadassiṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pilakkhassa phalaṃ adā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6024Msdiv 41Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6025Msdiv 42Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6026Msdiv 43Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6027Msdiv 44Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6028Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilakkhaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6029Pilakkhaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
