8. Ambadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1557Msdiv 53“Anomadassī bhagavā,
nisinno pabbatantare;
Mettāya aphari loke,
appamāṇe nirūpadhi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1558Msdiv 54Kapi ahaṃ tadā āsiṃ,
himavante naguttame;
Disvā anomadassiṃ taṃ,
buddhe cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1559Msdiv 55Avidūre himavantassa,
ambāsuṃ phalino tadā;
Tato pakkaṃ gahetvāna,
ambaṃ samadhukaṃ adaṃ.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.117Ms 20Ap1_1560Msdiv 56Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
anomadassī mahāmuni;
Iminā madhudānena,
ambadānena cūbhayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1561Msdiv 57Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
devaloke ramissati;
Avasesesu kappesu,
vokiṇṇaṃ saṃsarissati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1562Msdiv 58Khepetvā pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
paripakkāya buddhiyā;
Vinipātamagantvāna,
kilese jhāpayissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1563Msdiv 59Damena uttamenāhaṃ,
damitomhi mahesinā;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1564Msdiv 60Sattasattatikappasate,
ambaṭṭhajasanāmakā;
Catuddasa te rājāno,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1565Msdiv 61Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1566Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1567Ambadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
