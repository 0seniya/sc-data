10. Kapparukkhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1198SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1198Msdiv 108“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
thūpaseṭṭhassa sammukhā;
Vicittadusse lagetvā,
kapparukkhaṃ ṭhapesahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1199SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1199Msdiv 109Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sobhayanto mama dvāraṃ,
kapparukkho patiṭṭhati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1200SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1200Msdiv 110Ahañca parisā ceva,
ye keci mama vassitā;
Tamhā dussaṃ gahetvāna,
nivāsema mayaṃ sadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1201SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1201Msdiv 111Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ rukkhaṃ ṭhapayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kapparukkhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1202SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1202Msdiv 112Ito ca sattame kappe,
suceḷā aṭṭha khattiyā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1203SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1203Msdiv 113Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1103)

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1204Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kapparukkhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1205Kapparukkhiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1206Kuṇḍadhānavaggo catuttho.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1207Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1208Kuṇḍasāgatakaccānā,
udāyī mogharājako;
Adhimutto lasuṇado,
āyāgī dhammacakkiko;
Kapparukkhī ca dasamo,
gāthā dvayadasasataṃ.
