7. Ārāmadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3475Msdiv 32“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
ārāmo ropito mayā;
Sandacchāyesu rukkhesu,
upāsantesu pakkhisu.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3476Msdiv 33Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Ārāmaṃ abhināmesiṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3477Msdiv 34Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
phalaṃ pupphamadāsahaṃ;
Tato jātappasādova,
taṃ vanaṃ pariṇāmayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3478Msdiv 35Buddhassa yamidaṃ dāsiṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
nibbattati phalaṃ mama.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3479Msdiv 36Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ ārāmamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ārāmassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3480Msdiv 37Sattatiṃse ito kappe,
sattāsuṃ mudusītalā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3481Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3482Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārāmadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3483Ārāmadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
