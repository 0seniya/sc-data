2. Kaṅkhārevatattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7369Msdiv 34“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7370Msdiv 35Sīhahanu brahmagiro,
haṃsadundubhinissano;
Nāgavikkantagamano,
candasūrādikappabho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7371Msdiv 36Mahāmatī mahāvīro,
mahājhāyī mahābalo;
Mahākāruṇiko nātho,
mahātamapanūdano.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7372Msdiv 37Sa kadāci tilokaggo,
veneyyaṃ vinayaṃ bahuṃ;
Dhammaṃ desesi sambuddho,
sattāsayavidū muni.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7373Msdiv 38Jhāyiṃ jhānarataṃ vīraṃ,
upasantaṃ anāvilaṃ;
Vaṇṇayanto parisatiṃ,
tosesi janataṃ jino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7374Msdiv 39Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
brāhmaṇo vedapāragū;
Dhammaṃ sutvāna mudito,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7375Msdiv 40Tadā jino viyākāsi,
saṃghamajjhe vināyako;
‘Mudito hohi tvaṃ brahme,
lacchase taṃ manorathaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7376Msdiv 41Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7377Msdiv 42Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Revato nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7378Msdiv 43Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7379Msdiv 44Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jātohaṃ koliye pure;
Khattiye kulasampanne,
iddhe phīte mahaddhane.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7380Msdiv 45Yadā kapilavatthusmiṃ,
buddho dhammamadesayi;
Tadā pasanno sugate,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7381Msdiv 46Kaṅkhā me bahulā āsi,
kappākappe tahiṃ tahiṃ;
Sabbaṃ taṃ vinayī buddho,
desetvā dhammamuttamaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7382Msdiv 47Tatohaṃ tiṇṇasaṃsāro,
sadā jhānasukhe rato;
Viharāmi tadā buddho,
maṃ disvā etadabravi.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7383Msdiv 48‘Yā kāci kaṅkhā idha vā huraṃ vā,
Sakavediyā vā paravediyā vā;
Ye jhāyino tā pajahanti sabbā,
Ātāpino brahmacariyaṃ carantā’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7384Msdiv 49Satasahasse kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha;
Sumutto saravegova,
kilese jhāpayiṃ mama.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7385Msdiv 50Tato jhānarataṃ disvā,
buddho lokantagū muni;
Jhāyīnaṃ bhikkhūnaṃ aggo,
paññāpeti mahāmati.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7386Msdiv 51Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7387Msdiv 52Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7388Msdiv 53Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7389Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṅkhārevato thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7390Kaṅkhārevatattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
