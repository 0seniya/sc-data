7. Javahaṃsakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3195Msdiv 26“Candabhāgānadītīre,
Āsiṃ vanacaro tadā;
Siddhatthaṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3196Msdiv 27Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
ullokento mahāmuniṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
avandiṃ nāyakaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3197Msdiv 28Catunnavutito kappe,
yamavandiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3198Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3199Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā javahaṃsako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3200Javahaṃsakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
