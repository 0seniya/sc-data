4 Ticampakapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3112Msdiv 13“Himavantassāvidūre,
vikato nāma pabbato;
Tassa vemajjhe vasati,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3113Msdiv 14Disvāna tassopasamaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Tīṇi campakapupphāni,
gahetvāna samokiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3114Msdiv 15Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3115Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3116Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ticampakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3117Ticampakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
