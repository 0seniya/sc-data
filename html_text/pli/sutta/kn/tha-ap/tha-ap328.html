6. Annasaṃsāvakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3629Msdiv 34“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3630Msdiv 35Siddhatthaṃ sabbasiddhatthaṃ,
anejaṃ aparājitaṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ atināmetvā,
bhojayiṃ taṃ mahāmuniṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3631Msdiv 36Muni kāruṇiko loke,
obhāsayi mamaṃ tadā;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3632Msdiv 37Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhikkhādānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3633Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3634Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā annasaṃsāvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3635Annasaṃsāvakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
