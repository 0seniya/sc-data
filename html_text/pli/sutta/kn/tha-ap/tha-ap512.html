10 Nāḷikeraphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6676SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6676Msdiv 91“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
ārāmiko ahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6677Msdiv 92SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6677Msdiv 92Nāḷikeraphalaṃ gayha,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Ākāse ṭhitako santo,
paṭiggaṇhi mahāyaso.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6678SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6678Msdiv 93Vittisañjanano mayhaṃ,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho;
Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6679SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6679Msdiv 94Adhigacchiṃ tadā pītiṃ,
vipulañca sukhuttamaṃ;
Uppajjateva ratanaṃ,
nibbattassa tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6680SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6680Msdiv 95Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6681SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6681Msdiv 96Dibbacakkhu visuddhaṃ me,
samādhikusalo ahaṃ;
Abhiññāpāramippatto,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6682SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6682Msdiv 97Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6683SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6683Msdiv 98Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6684SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6684Msdiv 99Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5366)

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6685Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6685Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāḷikeraphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6686Nāḷikeraphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 12SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6687Kaṇikāravaggo ekapaññāsamo.

SC 13SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6688Tassuddānaṃ

SC 14SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6689Kaṇikārekapattā ca,
kāsumārī tathāvaṭā;
Pādañca mātuluṅgañca,
ajelīmodameva ca.

SC 15SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6690Tālaṃ tathā nāḷikeraṃ,
gāthāyo gaṇitā viha;
Ekaṃ gāthāsataṃ hoti,
ūnādhikavivajjitaṃ.
