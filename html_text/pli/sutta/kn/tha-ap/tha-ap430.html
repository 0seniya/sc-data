8. Sucintitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5590Msdiv 107“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ kassako tadā;
Kasikammena jīvāmi,
tena posemi dārake.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.386Ms 20Ap1_5591Msdiv 108Susampannaṃ tadā khettaṃ,
Dhaññaṃ me phalinaṃ ahu;
Pākakāle ca sampatte,
Evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5592Msdiv 109Nacchannaṃ nappatirūpaṃ,
jānantassa guṇāguṇaṃ;
Yohaṃ saṃghe adatvāna,
aggaṃ bhuñjeyya ce tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5593Msdiv 110Ayaṃ buddho asamasamo,
dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Tato pabhāvito saṃgho,
puññakkhetto anuttaro.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5594Msdiv 111Tattha dassāmahaṃ dānaṃ,
navasassaṃ pure pure;
Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
haṭṭho pīṇitamānaso.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5595Msdiv 112Khettato dhaññamāhatvā,
sambuddhaṃ upasaṅkamiṃ;
Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5596Msdiv 113‘Navasassañca sampannaṃ,
āyāgosi ca tvaṃ mune;
Anukampamupādāya,
adhivāsehi cakkhuma’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5597Msdiv 114Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5598Msdiv 115‘Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṃgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito;
Yajantānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5599Msdiv 116Karotopadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ;
Tasmiṃ saṃgheva dātabbaṃ,
tava sassaṃ tathetaraṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5600Msdiv 117Saṃghato uddisitvāna,
bhikkhū netvāna saṃgharaṃ;
Paṭiyattaṃ ghare santaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghassa dehi tvaṃ’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5601Msdiv 118Saṃghato uddisitvāna,
bhikkhū netvāna saṃgharaṃ;
Yaṃ ghare paṭiyattaṃ me,
bhikkhusaṃghassadāsahaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5602Msdiv 119Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5603Msdiv 120Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ sappabhassaraṃ;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbedhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5604Ekūnavīsatimaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5605Msdiv 121Ākiṇṇaṃ bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
nārīgaṇasamākulaṃ;
Tattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
vasāmi tidase ahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5606Msdiv 122Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 18PTS vp Pali 2.387Ms 20Ap1_5607Msdiv 123Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
labhāmi amitaṃ dhanaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5608Msdiv 124Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
sivikaṃ sandamānikaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5609Msdiv 125Navavatthaṃ navaphalaṃ,
navaggarasabhojanaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5610Msdiv 126Koseyyakambaliyāni,
khomakappāsikāni ca;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_5611Msdiv 127Dāsīgaṇaṃ dāsagaṇaṃ,
nāriyo ca alaṅkatā;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5612Msdiv 128Na maṃ sītaṃ vā uṇhaṃ vā,
pariḷāho na vijjati;
Atho cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ,
hadaye me na vijjati.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_5613Msdiv 129Idaṃ khāda idaṃ bhuñja,
imamhi sayane saya;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_5614Msdiv 130Ayaṃ pacchimako dāni,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Ajjāpi deyyadhammo me,
phalaṃ tosesi sabbadā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_5615Msdiv 131Navasassaṃ daditvāna,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Aṭṭhānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_5616Msdiv 132Vaṇṇavā yasavā homi,
mahābhogo anītiko;
Mahāpakkho sadā homi,
abhejjapariso sadā.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_5617Msdiv 133Sabbe maṃ apacāyanti,
ye keci pathavissitā;
Deyyadhammā ca ye keci,
pure pure labhāmahaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_5618Msdiv 134Bhikkhusaṃghassa vā majjhe,
buddhaseṭṭhassa sammukhā;
Sabbepi samatikkamma,
denti mameva dāyakā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_5619Msdiv 135Paṭhamaṃ navasassañhi,
datvā saṃghe gaṇuttame;
Imānisaṃse anubhomi,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_5620Msdiv 136Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
navasassassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_5621Msdiv 137Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_5622Msdiv 138Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_5623Msdiv 139Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_5624Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sucintito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_5625Sucintitattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
