7. Ambāṭakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5730Msdiv 55“Vipine buddhaṃ disvāna,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Ambāṭakaṃ gahetvāna,
sayambhussa adāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5731Msdiv 56Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5732Msdiv 57Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5733Msdiv 58Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5734Msdiv 59Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5735Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambāṭakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5736Ambāṭakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
