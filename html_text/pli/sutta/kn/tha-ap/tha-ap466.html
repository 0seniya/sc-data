4. Setudāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5991SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5991Msdiv 16“Vipassino bhagavato,
caṅkamantassa sammukhā;
Pasannacitto sumano,
setuṃ kārāpayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5992SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5992Msdiv 17Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ setuṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5993SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5993Msdiv 18Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5994SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5994Msdiv 19Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5995SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5995Msdiv 20Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5996Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā setudāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5997Setudāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
