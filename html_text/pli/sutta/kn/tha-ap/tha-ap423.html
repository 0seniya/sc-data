1. Sakiṃsammajjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5470Msdiv 1“Vipassino bhagavato,
pāṭaliṃ bodhimuttamaṃ;
Disvāva taṃ pādapaggaṃ,
tattha cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5471Msdiv 2Sammajjaniṃ gahetvāna,
bodhiṃ sammajji tāvade;
Sammajjitvāna taṃ bodhiṃ,
avandiṃ pāṭaliṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5472Msdiv 3Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Namassamāno taṃ bodhiṃ,
gañchiṃ paṭikuṭiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5473Msdiv 4Tādimaggena gacchāmi,
saranto bodhimuttamaṃ;
Ajagaro maṃ pīḷesi,
ghorarūpo mahabbalo.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 2.379Ms 20Ap1_5474Msdiv 5Āsanne me kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalena tosayī mamaṃ;
Kaḷevaraṃ me gilati,
devaloke ramāmahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5475Msdiv 6Anāvilaṃ mama cittaṃ,
visuddhaṃ paṇḍaraṃ sadā;
Sokasallaṃ na jānāmi,
cittasantāpanaṃ mama.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5476Msdiv 7Kuṭṭhaṃ gaṇḍo kilāso ca,
apamāro vitacchikā;
Daddu kaṇḍu ca me natthi,
phalaṃ sammajjanāyidaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5477Msdiv 8Soko ca paridevo ca,
hadaye me na vijjati;
Abhantaṃ ujukaṃ cittaṃ,
phalaṃ sammajjanāyidaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5478Msdiv 9Samādhīsu na majjāmi,
visadaṃ hoti mānasaṃ;
Yaṃ yaṃ samādhimicchāmi,
so so sampajjate mamaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5479Msdiv 10Rajanīye na rajjāmi,
atho dussaniyesu ca;
Mohanīye na muyhāmi,
phalaṃ sammajjanāyidaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5480Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phalaṃ sammajjanāyidaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5481Msdiv 12Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5482Msdiv 13Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5483Msdiv 14Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5484Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sakiṃsammajjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5485Sakiṃsammajjakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
