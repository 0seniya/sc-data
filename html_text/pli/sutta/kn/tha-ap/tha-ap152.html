10. Pallaṅkadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2343Msdiv 48“Sumedhassa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Pallaṅko hi mayā dinno,
sauttarasapacchado.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2344Msdiv 49Sattaratanasampanno,
pallaṅko āsi so tadā;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
nibbattati sadā mama.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2345Msdiv 50Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
pallaṅkamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pallaṅkassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2346Msdiv 51Vīsakappasahassamhi,
suvaṇṇābhā tayo janā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2347Msdiv 52Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1987)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2348Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pallaṅkadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2349Pallaṅkadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2350Chattavaggo pannarasamo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2351Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2352Chattaṃ thambho ca vedi ca,
parivārumapupphiyo;
Anulepo ca maggo ca,
phalako ca vaṭaṃsako;
Pallaṅkadāyī ca gāthāyo,
chappaññāsa pakittitāti.
