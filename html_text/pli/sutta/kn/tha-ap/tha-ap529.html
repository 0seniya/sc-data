7. Girinelapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6851Msdiv 42“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
vipine vicaraṃ ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6852Msdiv 43Tasmiṃ mahākāruṇike,
sabbasattahite rate;
Pasannacitto sumano,
nelapupphamapūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6853Msdiv 44Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6854Msdiv 45Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6855Msdiv 46Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6856Msdiv 47Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6857Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā girinelapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6858Girinelapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
