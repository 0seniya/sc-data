9. Biḷālidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.145Ms 20Ap1_1920Msdiv 53“Himavantassāvidūre,
vasāmi paṇṇasanthare;
Ghāsesu gedhamāpanno,
seyyasīlo cahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1921Msdiv 54Khaṇantālu kalambāni,
biḷālitakkalāni ca;
Kolaṃ bhallātakaṃ billaṃ,
āhatvā paṭiyāditaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1922Msdiv 55Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1923Msdiv 56Upāgataṃ mahānāgaṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Biḷāliṃ paggahetvāna,
pattamhi okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1924Msdiv 57Paribhuñji mahāvīro,
tosayanto mamaṃ tadā;
Paribhuñjitvāna sabbaññū,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1925Msdiv 58‘Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
biḷāliṃ me adā tuvaṃ;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjasi’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1926Msdiv 59Carimaṃ vattate mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1927Msdiv 60Catupaññāsito kappe,
sumekhaliyasavhayo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1928Msdiv 61Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1929Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā biḷālidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1930Biḷālidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
