5 Vedikārakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2999SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2999Msdiv 19“Vipassino bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Pasannacitto sumano,
kāresiṃ vedikaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3000SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3000Msdiv 20Ekanavutito kappe,
kāresiṃ vedikaṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3001SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3001Msdiv 21Ito ekādase kappe,
ahosiṃ sūriyassamo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3002SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3002Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3003Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3003Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vedikārako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3004Vedikārakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
