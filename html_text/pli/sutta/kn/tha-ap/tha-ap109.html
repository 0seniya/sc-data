7. Naḷamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1899Msdiv 36“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Tiṇatthare nisinnassa,
upasantassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1900Msdiv 37Naḷamālaṃ gahetvāna,
bandhitvā bījaniṃ ahaṃ;
Buddhassa upanāmesiṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1901Msdiv 38Paṭiggahetvā sabbaññū,
bījaniṃ lokanāyako;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1902Msdiv 39‘Yathā me kāyo nibbāti,
pariḷāho na vijjati;
Tatheva tividhaggīhi,
cittaṃ tava vimuccatu’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1903Msdiv 40Sabbe devā samāgacchuṃ,
ye keci vananissitā;
Sossāma buddhavacanaṃ,
hāsayantañca dāyakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1904Msdiv 41Nisinno bhagavā tattha,
devasaṅghapurakkhato;
Dāyakaṃ sampahaṃsento,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1905Msdiv 42‘Iminā bījanidānena,
cittassa paṇidhīhi ca;
Subbato nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.144Ms 20Ap1_1906Msdiv 43Tena kammāvasesena,
sukkamūlena codito;
Māluto nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī bhavissati’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1907Msdiv 44Iminā bījanidānena,
sammānavipulena ca;
Kappasatasahassampi,
duggatiṃ nupapajjati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1908Msdiv 45Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
subbatā aṭṭhatiṃsa te;
Ekūnatiṃsasahasse,
aṭṭha mālutanāmakā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1909Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1910Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā naḷamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1911Naḷamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
Sattamabhāṇavāraṃ.
