2. Buddhasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6195Msdiv 9“Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;
Lakkhaṇe itihāse ca,
sanighaṇḍusakeṭubhe.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6196Msdiv 10Nadīsotapaṭibhāgā,
sissā āyanti me tadā;
Tesāhaṃ mante vācemi,
rattindivamatandito.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6197Msdiv 11Siddhattho nāma sambuddho,
loke uppajji tāvade;
Tamandhakāraṃ nāsetvā,
ñāṇālokaṃ pavattayi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6198Msdiv 12Mama aññataro sisso,
sissānaṃ so kathesi me;
Sutvāna te etamatthaṃ,
ārocesuṃ mamaṃ tadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6199Msdiv 13‘Buddho loke samuppanno,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Tassānuvattati jano,
lābho amhaṃ na vijjati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6200Msdiv 14Adhiccuppattikā buddhā,
cakkhumanto mahāyasā;
Yannūnāhaṃ buddhaseṭṭhaṃ,
passeyyaṃ lokanāyakaṃ’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6201Msdiv 15Ajinaṃ me gahetvāna,
vākacīraṃ kamaṇḍaluṃ;
Assamā abhinikkhamma,
sisse āmantayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6202Msdiv 16Odumbarikapupphaṃva,
candamhi sasakaṃ yathā;
Vāyasānaṃ yathā khīraṃ,
dullabho lokanāyako.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6203Msdiv 17Buddho lokamhi uppanno,
manussattampi dullabhaṃ;
Ubhosu vijjamānesu,
savanañca sudullabhaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6204Msdiv 18Buddho loke samuppanno,
cakkhuṃ lacchāma no bhavaṃ;
Etha sabbe gamissāma,
sammāsambuddhasantikaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6205Msdiv 19Kamaṇḍaludharā sabbe,
kharājinanivāsino;
Te jaṭā bhārabharitā,
nikkhamuṃ vipinā tadā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6206Msdiv 20Yugamattaṃ pekkhamānā,
uttamatthaṃ gavesino;
Āsattidosarahitā,
asambhītāva kesarī.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6207Msdiv 21Appakiccā aloluppā,
nipakā santavuttino;
Uñchāya caramānā te,
buddhaseṭṭhamupāgamuṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6208Msdiv 22Diyaḍḍhayojane sese,
byādhi me upapajjatha;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6209Msdiv 23Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6210Msdiv 24Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6211Msdiv 25Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6212Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6213Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6214Buddhasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
