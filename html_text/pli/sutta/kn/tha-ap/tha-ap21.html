9. Sopākattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_823Msdiv 112“Pabbhāraṃ sodhayantassa,
vipine pabbatuttame;
Siddhattho nāma bhagavā,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_824Msdiv 113Buddhaṃ upagataṃ disvā,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Santharaṃ paññapetvāna,
pupphāsanamadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_825Msdiv 114Pupphāsane nisīditvā,
siddhattho lokanāyako;
Mamañca gatimaññāya,
aniccatamudāhari.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_826Msdiv 115‘Aniccā vata saṅkhārā,
uppādavayadhammino;
Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti,
tesaṃ vūpasamo sukho’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_827Msdiv 116Idaṃ vatvāna sabbaññū,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggami vīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_828Msdiv 117Sakaṃ diṭṭhiṃ jahitvāna,
bhāvayāniccasaññahaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ bhāvayitvāna,
tattha kālaṃ kato ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_829Msdiv 118Dve sampattī anubhotvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Pacchime bhave sampatte,
sapākayonupāgamiṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_830Msdiv 119Agārā abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_831Msdiv 120Āraddhavīriyo pahitatto,
Sīlesu susamāhito;
Tosetvāna mahānāgaṃ,
Alatthaṃ upasampadaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_832Msdiv 121Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_833Msdiv 122Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññaṃ bhāvayiṃ tadā;
Taṃ saññaṃ bhāvayantassa,
patto me āsavakkhayo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_834Msdiv 123Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_835Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sopāko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_836Sopākattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
