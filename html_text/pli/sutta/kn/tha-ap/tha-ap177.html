5. Sīhāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2538Msdiv 21“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
padumuttaranāyake;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sīhāsanamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2539Msdiv 22Bahūhi gandhamālehi,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvahe;
Tattha pūjañca katvāna,
nibbāyati bahujjano.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2540Msdiv 23Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvā bodhimuttamaṃ;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2541Msdiv 24Pannarasasahassamhi,
kappānaṃ aṭṭha āsu te;
Siluccayasanāmā ca,
rājāno cakkavattino.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2542Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2543Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sīhāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2544Sīhāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
