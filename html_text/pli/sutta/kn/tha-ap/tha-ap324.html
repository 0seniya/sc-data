2 Pulinapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3594Msdiv 7“Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Osadhiṃva virocantaṃ,
obhāsantaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3595Msdiv 8Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
avandiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;
Satthāraṃ parivaṇṇesiṃ,
sakakammena tosayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3596Msdiv 9Susuddhaṃ pulinaṃ gayha,
gatamagge samokiriṃ;
Ucchaṅgena gahetvāna,
vipassissa mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3597Msdiv 10Tato upaḍḍhapulinaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Divāvihāre osiñciṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3598Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
pulinaṃ yamasiñcahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pulinassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3599Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3600Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pulinapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3601Pulinapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
