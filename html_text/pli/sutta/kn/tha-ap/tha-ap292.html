10 Pubbaṅgamiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3347Msdiv 43“Cullāsītisahassāni,
pabbajimha akiñcanā;
Tesaṃ pubbaṅgamo āsiṃ,
uttamatthassa pattiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3348Msdiv 44Sarāgā sabhavā cete,
vippasannamanāvilā;
Upaṭṭhahiṃsu sakkaccaṃ,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3349Msdiv 45Khīṇāsavā vantadosā,
katakiccā anāsavā;
Phariṃsu mettacittena,
sayambhū aparājitā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3350Msdiv 46Tesaṃ upaṭṭhahitvāna,
sambuddhānaṃ patissato;
Maraṇañca anuppatto,
devattañca agamhase.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3351Msdiv 47Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ sīlamanupālayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saññamassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3352Msdiv 48Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2666)

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3353Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pubbaṅgamiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3354Pubbaṅgamiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3355Paṇṇadāyakavaggo ekūnatiṃsatimo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3356Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3357Paṇṇaṃ phalaṃ paccuggamaṃ,
ekapupphi ca maghavā;
Upaṭṭhākāpadānañca,
pabbajjā buddhupaṭṭhāko;
Pubbaṅgamo ca gāthāyo,
aṭṭhatālīsa kittitā.
