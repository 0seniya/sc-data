9. Subhaddattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1325Msdiv 101“Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Janataṃ uddharitvāna,
nibbāyati mahāyaso.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1326Msdiv 102Nibbāyante ca sambuddhe,
dasasahassi kampatha;
Janakāyo mahā āsi,
devā sannipatuṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1327Msdiv 103Candanaṃ pūrayitvāna,
tagarāmallikāhi ca;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
āropayiṃ naruttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1328Msdiv 104Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Nipannakova sambuddho,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1329Msdiv 105‘Yo me pacchimake kāle,
gandhamālena chādayi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1330Msdiv 106Ito cuto ayaṃ poso,
tusitakāyaṃ gamissati;
Tattha rajjaṃ karitvāna,
nimmānaṃ so gamissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1331Msdiv 107Eteneva upāyena,
datvā mālaṃ varuttamaṃ;
Sakakammābhiraddho so,
sampattiṃ anubhossati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1332Msdiv 108Punāpi tusite kāye,
nibbattissatiyaṃ naro;
Tamhā kāyā cavitvāna,
manussattaṃ gamissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1333Msdiv 109Sakyaputto mahānāgo,
aggo loke sadevake;
Bodhayitvā bahū satte,
nibbāyissati cakkhumā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1334Msdiv 110Tadā sopagato santo,
sukkamūlena codito;
Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,
pañhaṃ pucchissati tadā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1335Msdiv 111Hāsayitvāna sambuddho,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Puññakammaṃ pariññāya,
saccāni vivarissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1336Msdiv 112Āraddho ca ayaṃ pañho,
tuṭṭho ekaggamānaso;
Satthāraṃ abhivādetvā,
pabbajjaṃ yācayissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1337Msdiv 113Pasannamānasaṃ disvā,
sakakammena tositaṃ;
Pabbājessati so buddho,
aggamaggassa kovido.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1338Msdiv 114Vāyamitvānayaṃ poso,
sammāsambuddhasāsane;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1339Pañcamabhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1340Msdiv 115Pubbakammena saṃyutto,
ekaggo susamāhito;
Buddhassa oraso putto,
dhammajomhi sunimmito.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1341Msdiv 116Dhammarājaṃ upagamma,
apucchiṃ pañhamuttamaṃ;
Kathayanto ca me pañhaṃ,
dhammasotaṃ upānayi.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1342Msdiv 117Tassāhaṃ dhammamaññāya,
vihāsiṃ sāsane rato;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1343Msdiv 118Satasahassito kappe,
jalajuttamanāyako;
Nibbāyi anupādāno,
dīpova telasaṅkhayā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1344Msdiv 119Sattayojanikaṃ āsi,
thūpañca ratanāmayaṃ;
Dhajaṃ tattha apūjesiṃ,
sabbabhaddaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_1345Msdiv 120Kassapassa ca buddhassa,
tisso nāmaggasāvako;
Putto me oraso āsi,
dāyādo jinasāsane.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1346Msdiv 121Tassa hīnena manasā,
vācaṃ bhāsiṃ abhaddakaṃ;
Tena kammavipākena,
pacchā me āsi bhaddakaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1347Msdiv 122Upavattane sālavane,
pacchime sayane muni;
Pabbājesi mahāvīro,
hito kāruṇiko jino.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_1348Msdiv 123Ajjeva dāni pabbajjā,
ajjeva upasampadā;
Ajjeva parinibbānaṃ,
sammukhā dvipaduttame.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_1349Msdiv 124Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_1350Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā subhaddo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_1351Subhaddattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
