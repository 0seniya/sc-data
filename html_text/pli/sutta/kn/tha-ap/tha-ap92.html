10. Padumapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.132Ms 20Ap1_1754Msdiv 51“Pokkharavanaṃ paviṭṭho,
Bhañjanto padumānihaṃ;
Tatthaddasaṃ phussaṃ buddhaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1755Msdiv 52Padumapupphaṃ gahetvāna,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Pāpakammaṃ saritvāna,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1756Msdiv 53Pabbajitvāna kāyena,
manasā saṃvutena ca;
Vacīduccaritaṃ hitvā,
ājīvaṃ parisodhayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1757Msdiv 54Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1758Msdiv 55Padumābhāsanāmā ca,
aṭṭhārasa mahīpatī;
Aṭṭhārasesu kappesu,
aṭṭhatālīsamāsisuṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1759Msdiv 56Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1542)

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1760Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1761Padumapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1762Timiravaggo navamo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1763Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1764Timiranaṅgalīpuppha,
nippannañjaliko adho;
Dve raṃsisaññī phalado,
saddasaññī ca secako;
Padmapupphī ca gāthāyo,
chappaññāsa pakittitā.
