1. Subhūtittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_860Msdiv 1“Himavantassāvidūre,
nisabho nāma pabbato;
Assamo sukato mayhaṃ,
paṇṇasālā sumāpitā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_861Msdiv 2Kosiyo nāma nāmena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Ekākiyo adutiyo,
vasāmi nisabhe tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_862Msdiv 3Phalaṃ mūlañca paṇṇañca,
na bhuñjāmi ahaṃ tadā;
Pavattaṃva supātāhaṃ,
upajīvāmi tāvade.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_863Msdiv 4Nāhaṃ kopemi ājīvaṃ,
cajamānopi jīvitaṃ;
Ārādhemi sakaṃ cittaṃ,
vivajjemi anesanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_864Msdiv 5Rāgūpasaṃhitaṃ cittaṃ,
yadā uppajjate mama;
Sayaṃva paccavekkhāmi,
ekaggo taṃ damemahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_865Msdiv 6‘Rajjase rajjanīye ca,
dussanīye ca dussase;
Muyhase mohanīye ca,
nikkhamassu vanā tuvaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_866Msdiv 7Visuddhānaṃ ayaṃ vāso,
nimmalānaṃ tapassinaṃ;
Mā kho visuddhaṃ dūsesi,
nikkhamassu vanā tuvaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_867Msdiv 8Agāriko bhavitvāna,
yadā puttaṃ labhissasi;
Ubhopi mā virādhesi,
nikkhamassu vanā tuvaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_868Msdiv 9Chavālātaṃ yathā kaṭṭhaṃ,
na kvaci kiccakārakaṃ;
Neva gāme araññe vā,
na hi taṃ kaṭṭhasammataṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_869Msdiv 10Chavālātūpamo tvaṃsi,
na gihī nāpi saññato;
Ubhato muttako ajja,
nikkhamassu vanā tuvaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_870Msdiv 11Siyā nu kho tava etaṃ,
ko pajānāti te idaṃ;
Saddhādhuraṃ vahisi me,
kosajjabahulāya ca.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_871Msdiv 12Jigucchissanti taṃ viññū,
asuciṃ nāgariko yathā;
Ākaḍḍhitvāna isayo,
codayissanti taṃ sadā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_872Msdiv 13Taṃ viññū pavadissanti,
samatikkantasāsanaṃ;
Saṃvāsaṃ alabhanto hi,
kathaṃ jīvihisi tuvaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_873Msdiv 14Tidhā pabhinnaṃ mātaṅgaṃ,
kuñjaraṃ saṭṭhihāyanaṃ;
Balī nāgo upagantvā,
yūthā nīharate gajaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_874Msdiv 15Yūthā vinissaṭo santo,
sukhaṃ sātaṃ na vindati;
Dukkhito vimano hoti,
pajjhāyanto pavedhati.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_875Msdiv 16Tatheva jaṭilā tampi,
nīharissanti dummatiṃ;
Tehi tvaṃ nissaṭo santo,
sukhaṃ sātaṃ na lacchasi.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_876Msdiv 17Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
sokasallasamappito;
Ḍayhasi pariḷāhena,
gajo yūthāva nissaṭo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_877Msdiv 18Jātarūpaṃ yathā kūṭaṃ,
neva jhāyati katthaci;
Tathā sīlavihīno tvaṃ,
na jhāyissasi katthaci.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_878Msdiv 19Agāraṃ vasamānopi,
kathaṃ jīvihisi tuvaṃ;
Mattikaṃ pettikañcāpi,
natthi te nihitaṃ dhanaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_879Msdiv 20Sayaṃ kammaṃ karitvāna,
gatte sedaṃ pamocayaṃ;
Evaṃ jīvihisi gehe,
sādhu te taṃ na ruccati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_880Msdiv 21Evāhaṃ tattha vāremi,
saṃkilesagataṃ manaṃ;
Nānādhammakathaṃ katvā,
pāpā cittaṃ nivārayiṃ’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_881Msdiv 22Evaṃ me viharantassa,
appamādavihārino;
Tiṃsavassasahassāni,
vipine me atikkamuṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_882Msdiv 23Appamādarataṃ disvā,
uttamatthaṃ gavesakaṃ;
Padumuttarasambuddho,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_883Msdiv 24Timbarūsakavaṇṇābho,
appameyyo anūpamo;
Rūpenāsadiso buddho,
ākāse caṅkamī tadā.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_884Msdiv 25Suphullo sālarājāva,
vijjūvabbhaghanantare;
Ñāṇenāsadiso buddho,
ākāse caṅkamī tadā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_885Msdiv 26Sīharājāvasambhīto,
gajarājāva dappito;
Lāsito byaggharājāva,
ākāse caṅkamī tadā.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_886Msdiv 27Siṅgīnikkhasavaṇṇābho,
khadiraṅgārasannibho;
Maṇi yathā jotiraso,
ākāse caṅkamī tadā.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_887Msdiv 28Visuddhakelāsanibho,
puṇṇamāyeva candimā;
Majjhanhikeva sūriyo,
ākāse caṅkamī tadā.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_888Msdiv 29Disvā nabhe caṅkamantaṃ,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
‘Devo nu kho ayaṃ satto,
udāhu manujo ayaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_889Msdiv 30Na me suto vā diṭṭho vā,
mahiyā ediso naro;
Api mantapadaṃ atthi,
ayaṃ satthā bhavissati’.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_890Msdiv 31Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Nānāpupphañca gandhañca,
sannipātesahaṃ tadā.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_891Msdiv 32Pupphāsanaṃ paññapetvā,
sādhucittaṃ manoramaṃ;
Narasārathinaṃ aggaṃ,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_892Msdiv 33‘Idaṃ me āsanaṃ vīra,
paññattaṃ tavanucchavaṃ;
Hāsayanto mamaṃ cittaṃ,
nisīda kusumāsane’.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_893Msdiv 34Nisīdi tattha bhagavā,
asambhītova kesarī;
Sattarattindivaṃ buddho,
pavare kusumāsane.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_894Msdiv 35Namassamāno aṭṭhāsiṃ,
sattarattindivaṃ ahaṃ;
Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Mama kammaṃ pakittento,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_895Msdiv 36‘Bhāvehi buddhānussatiṃ,
bhāvanānamanuttaraṃ;
Imaṃ satiṃ bhāvayitvā,
pūrayissasi mānasaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_896Msdiv 37Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissasi;
Asītikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissasi;
Sahassakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
rājā raṭṭhe bhavissasi.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_897Msdiv 38Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Anubhossasi taṃ sabbaṃ,
buddhānussatiyā phalaṃ.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_898Msdiv 39Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
mahābhogaṃ labhissasi;
Bhoge te ūnatā natthi,
buddhānussatiyā phalaṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_899Msdiv 40Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_900Msdiv 41Asītikoṭiṃ chaḍḍetvā,
dāse kammakare bahū;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissasi.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_901Msdiv 42Ārādhayitvā sambuddhaṃ,
gotamaṃ sakyapuṅgavaṃ;
Subhūti nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_902Msdiv 43Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
dakkhiṇeyyaguṇamhi taṃ;
Tathāraṇavihāre ca,
dvīsu agge ṭhapessati’.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_903Msdiv 44Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī vīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_904Msdiv 45Sāsito lokanāthena,
namassitvā tathāgataṃ;
Sadā bhāvemi mudito,
buddhānussatimuttamaṃ.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_905Msdiv 46Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_906Msdiv 47Asītikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 48Ms 20Ap1_907Msdiv 48Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Anubhomi susampattiṃ,
buddhānussatiyā phalaṃ.

SC 49Ms 20Ap1_908Msdiv 49Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
mahābhogaṃ labhāmahaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
buddhānussatiyā phalaṃ.

SC 50Ms 20Ap1_909Msdiv 50Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhānussatiyā phalaṃ.

SC 51Ms 20Ap1_910Msdiv 51Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 52Ms 20Ap1_911Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā subhūti thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 53Ms 20Ap1_912Subhūtittherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
