9 Naḷakuṭidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6539Msdiv 93“Himavantassāvidūre,
hārito nāma pabbato;
Sayambhū nārado nāma,
rukkhamūle vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6540Msdiv 94Naḷāgāraṃ karitvāna,
tiṇena chādayiṃ ahaṃ;
Caṅkamaṃ sodhayitvāna,
sayambhussa adāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6541Msdiv 95Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6542Msdiv 96Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
naḷakuṭikanimmitaṃ;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbedhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6543Msdiv 97Catuddasesu kappesu,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekasattatikkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6544Msdiv 98Catutiṃsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6545Msdiv 99Dhammapāsādamāruyha,
sabbākāravarūpamaṃ;
Yadicchakāhaṃ vihare,
sakyaputtassa sāsane.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6546Msdiv 100Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
naḷakuṭiyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6547Msdiv 101Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6548Msdiv 102Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6549Msdiv 103Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6550Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā naḷakuṭidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6551Naḷakuṭidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
