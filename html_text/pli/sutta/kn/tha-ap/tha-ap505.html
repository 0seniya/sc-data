3 Kāsumāraphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.445Ms 20Ap1_6622Msdiv 51“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6623Msdiv 52Pasannacitto sumano,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Kāsumārikamādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6624Msdiv 53Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6625Msdiv 54Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6626Msdiv 55Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6627Msdiv 56Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6628Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kāsumāraphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6629Kāsumāraphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
