1. Nāgasamālattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1589Msdiv 1“Āpāṭaliṃ ahaṃ pupphaṃ,
ujjhitaṃ sumahāpathe;
Thūpamhi abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1590Msdiv 2Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
thūpapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1591Msdiv 3Ito pannarase kappe,
bhūmiyo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1592Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1593Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgasamālo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1594Nāgasamālattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
