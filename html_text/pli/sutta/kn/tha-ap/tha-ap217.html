5. Raṃsisaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2850Msdiv 21“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Byagghūsabhaṃva pavaraṃ,
sujātaṃ pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2851Msdiv 22Buddhassa ānubhāvo so,
jalate pabbatantare;
Raṃse cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2852Msdiv 23Avasesesu kappesu,
kusalaṃ caritaṃ mayā;
Tena cittappasādena,
buddhānussatiyāpi ca.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2853Msdiv 24Tiṃsakappasahasseto,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2854Msdiv 25Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Sujāto nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2855Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2856Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2857Raṃsisaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
