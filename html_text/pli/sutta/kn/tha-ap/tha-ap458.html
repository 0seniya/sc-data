6. Vanakoraṇḍiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5920SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5920Msdiv 32“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Vanakoraṇḍamādāya,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5921SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5921Msdiv 33Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5922SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5922Msdiv 34Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5923SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5923Msdiv 35Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5924SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5924Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5925Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vanakoraṇḍiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5926Vanakoraṇḍiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
Vīsatimaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
