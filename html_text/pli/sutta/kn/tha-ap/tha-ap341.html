9 Dhammasavaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3841SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3841Msdiv 111“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Catusaccaṃ pakāsento,
santāresi bahuṃ janaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3842SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3842Msdiv 112Ahaṃ tena samayena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Dhunanto vākacīrāni,
gacchāmi ambare tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3843SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3843Msdiv 113Buddhaseṭṭhassa upari,
gantuṃ na visahāmahaṃ;
Pakkhīva selamāsajja,
gamanaṃ na labhāmahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3844SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3844Msdiv 114Na me idaṃ bhūtapubbaṃ,
iriyassa vikopanaṃ;
Dake yathā ummujjitvā,
evaṃ gacchāmi ambare.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3845SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3845Msdiv 115Uḷārabhūto manujo,
heṭṭhāsīno bhavissati;
Handa menaṃ gavesissaṃ,
api atthaṃ labheyyahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3846SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3846Msdiv 116Orohanto antalikkhā,
saddamassosi satthuno;
Aniccataṃ kathentassa,
tamahaṃ uggahiṃ tadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3847SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3847Msdiv 117Aniccasaññamuggayha,
agamāsiṃ mamassamaṃ;
Yāvatāyuṃ vasitvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3848SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3848Msdiv 118Carime vattamānamhi,
Taṃ dhammasavanaṃ sariṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
Tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3849SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3849Msdiv 119Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3850SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3850Msdiv 120Ekasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3851SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3851Msdiv 121Pitugehe nisīditvā,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo;
Gāthāya paridīpento,
aniccatamudāhari.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3852SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3852Msdiv 122Anussarāmi taṃ saññaṃ,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Na koṭiṃ paṭivijjhāmi,
nibbānaṃ accutaṃ padaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3853SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3853Msdiv 123Aniccā vata saṅkhārā,
uppādavayadhammino;
Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti,
tesaṃ vūpasamo sukho.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3854SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3854Msdiv 124Saha gāthaṃ suṇitvāna,
pubbakammaṃ anussariṃ;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3855SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3855Msdiv 125Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Upasampādayi buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3856SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3856Msdiv 126Dārakova ahaṃ santo,
karaṇīyaṃ samāpayiṃ;
Kiṃ me karaṇīyaṃ ajja,
sakyaputtassa sāsane.

yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saddhammasavane phalaṃ.
