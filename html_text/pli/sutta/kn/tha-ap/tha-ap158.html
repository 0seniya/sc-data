6. Kadambapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2392Msdiv 30“Himavantassāvidūre,
kukkuṭo nāma pabbato;
Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,
satta buddhā vasanti te.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2393Msdiv 31Kadambaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
dīparājaṃva uggataṃ;
Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
satta buddhe samokiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2394Msdiv 32Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2395Msdiv 33Dvenavute ito kappe,
sattāsuṃ pupphanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2396Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2397Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2398Kadambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
