1 Tikaṇikārapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6568Msdiv 1“Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Vivekakāmo sambuddho,
himavantamupāgamiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6569Msdiv 2Ajjhogayha himavantaṃ,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Pallaṅkamābhujitvāna,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6570Msdiv 3Vijjādharo tadā āsiṃ,
antalikkhacaro ahaṃ;
Tisūlaṃ sukataṃ gayha,
gacchāmi ambare tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6571Msdiv 4Pabbatagge yathā aggi,
puṇṇamāyeva candimā;
Vane obhāsate buddho,
sālarājāva phullito.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6572Msdiv 5Vanaggā nikkhamitvāna,
buddharaṃsībhidhāvare;
Naḷaggivaṇṇasaṅkāsā,
disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6573Msdiv 6Vicinaṃ addasaṃ pupphaṃ,
kaṇikāraṃ devagandhikaṃ;
Tīṇi pupphāni ādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhamapūjayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6574Msdiv 7Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
tīṇi pupphāni me tadā;
Uddhaṃvaṇṭā adhopattā,
chāyaṃ kubbanti satthuno.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6575Msdiv 8Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6576Msdiv 9Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
kaṇikārīti ñāyati;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbedhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6577Msdiv 10Sahassakaṇḍaṃ satabheṇḍu,
dhajāluharitāmayaṃ;
Satasahassaniyyūhā,
byamhe pātubhaviṃsu me.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6578Msdiv 11Soṇṇamayā maṇimayā,
lohitaṅgamayāpi ca;
Phalikāpi ca pallaṅkā,
yenicchakā yadicchakā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6579Msdiv 12Mahārahañca sayanaṃ,
tūlikāvikatīyutaṃ;
Uddhalomikaekantaṃ,
bibbohanasamāyutaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6580Msdiv 13Bhavanā nikkhamitvāna,
caranto devacārikaṃ;
Yadā icchāmi gamanaṃ,
devasaṅghapurakkhato.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6581Msdiv 14Pupphassa heṭṭhā tiṭṭhāmi,
uparicchadanaṃ mama;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
kaṇikārehi chāditaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6582Msdiv 15Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
sāyaṃ pātamupaṭṭhahuṃ;
Parivārenti maṃ niccaṃ,
rattindivamatanditā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6583Msdiv 16Tattha naccehi gītehi,
tāḷehi vāditehi ca;
Ramāmi khiḍḍāratiyā,
modāmi kāmakāmihaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6584Msdiv 17Tattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
modāmi tidase tadā;
Nārīgaṇehi sahito,
modāmi byamhamuttame.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6585Msdiv 18Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6586Msdiv 19Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
mahābhogaṃ labhāmahaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6587Msdiv 20Duve bhave saṃsarāmi,
devatte atha mānuse;
Aññaṃ gatiṃ na jānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6588Msdiv 21Duve kule pajāyāmi,
khattiye cāpi brāhmaṇe;
Nīce kule na jāyāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6589Msdiv 22Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
sivikaṃ sandamānikaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6590Msdiv 23Dāsīgaṇaṃ dāsagaṇaṃ,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6591Msdiv 24Koseyyakambaliyāni,
khomakappāsikāni ca;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6592Msdiv 25Navavatthaṃ navaphalaṃ,
navaggarasabhojanaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6593Msdiv 26Imaṃ khāda imaṃ bhuñja,
imamhi sayane saya;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6594Msdiv 27Sabbattha pūjito homi,
yaso abbhuggato mama;
Mahāpakkho sadā homi,
abhejjapariso sadā;
Ñātīnaṃ uttamo homi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6595Msdiv 28Sītaṃ uṇhaṃ na jānāmi,
pariḷāho na vijjati;
Atho cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ,
hadaye me na vijjati.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6596Msdiv 29Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo hutvāna,
saṃsarāmi bhavābhave;
Vevaṇṇiyaṃ na jānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_6597Msdiv 30Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Sāvatthiyaṃ pure jāto,
mahāsāle suaḍḍhake.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_6598Msdiv 31Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_6599Msdiv 32Upasampādayī buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā;
Taruṇo pūjanīyohaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_6600Msdiv 33Dibbacakkhu visuddhaṃ me,
samādhikusalo ahaṃ;
Abhiññāpāramippatto,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_6601Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā anuppatto,
iddhipādesu kovido;
Dhammesu pāramippatto,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_6602Msdiv 35Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_6603Msdiv 36Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_6604Msdiv 37Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_6605Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_6606Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tikaṇikārapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_6607Tikaṇikārapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
