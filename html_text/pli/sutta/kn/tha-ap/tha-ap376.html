4 Paṭṭipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4123Msdiv 22“Yadā nibbāyi sambuddho,
mahesī padumuttaro;
Samāgamma janā sabbe,
sarīraṃ nīharanti te.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4124Msdiv 23Nīharante sarīramhi,
vajjamānāsu bherisu;
Pasannacitto sumano,
paṭṭipupphaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4125Msdiv 24Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sarīrapūjite phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4126Msdiv 25Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4127Msdiv 26Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
Mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
Kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4128Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4129Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṭṭipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4130Paṭṭipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
