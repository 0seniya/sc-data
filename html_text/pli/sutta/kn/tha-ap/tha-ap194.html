2 Tiṇasanthārakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2673Msdiv 6“Yaṃ dāyavāsiko isi,
tiṇaṃ lāyati satthuno;
Sabbe padakkhiṇāvaṭṭā,
pathabyā nipatiṃsu te.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2674Msdiv 7Tamahaṃ tiṇamādāya,
santhariṃ dharaṇuttame;
Tīṇeva tālapattāni,
āharitvānahaṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2675Msdiv 8Tiṇena chadanaṃ katvā,
siddhatthassa adāsahaṃ;
Sattāhaṃ dhārayuṃ tassa,
devamānusasatthuno.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2676Msdiv 9Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ tiṇaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2677Msdiv 10Pañcasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
cattārosuṃ mahaddhanā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2678Msdiv 11Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2679Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇasanthārako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2680Tiṇasanthārakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
