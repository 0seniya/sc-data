2. Ekapattadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6608SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6608Msdiv 39“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
kumbhakāro ahosahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6609SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6609Msdiv 40Sukataṃ mattikāpattaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Pattaṃ datvā bhagavato,
ujubhūtassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6610SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6610Msdiv 41Bhave nibbattamānohaṃ,
soṇṇathāle labhāmahaṃ;
Rūpimaye ca sovaṇṇe,
taṭṭike ca maṇīmaye.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6611SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6611Msdiv 42Pātiyo paribhuñjāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ;
Yasānañca dhanānañca,
aggabhūto ca homahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6612SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6612Msdiv 43Yathāpi bhaddake khette,
bījaṃ appampi ropitaṃ;
Sammādhāraṃ pavecchante,
phalaṃ toseti kassakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6613SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6613Msdiv 44Tathevidaṃ pattadānaṃ,
buddhakhettamhi ropitaṃ;
Pītidhāre pavassante,
phalaṃ maṃ tosayissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6614SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6614Msdiv 45Yāvatā khettā vijjanti,
saṃghāpi ca gaṇāpi ca;
Buddhakhettasamo natthi,
sukhado sabbapāṇinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6615SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6615Msdiv 46Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Ekapattaṃ daditvāna,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6616SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6616Msdiv 47Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pattamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pattadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6617SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6617Msdiv 48Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6618SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6618Msdiv 49Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6619SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6619Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6620Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekapattadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6621Ekapattadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
