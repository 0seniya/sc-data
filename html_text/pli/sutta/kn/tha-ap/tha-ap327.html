5. Nimittasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.261Ms 20Ap1_3621Msdiv 28“Candabhāgānadītīre,
vasāmi assame ahaṃ;
Suvaṇṇamigamaddakkhiṃ,
carantaṃ vipine ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3622Msdiv 29Mige cittaṃ pasādetvā,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
aññe buddhe anussariṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3623Msdiv 30Abbhatītā ca ye buddhā,
vattamānā anāgatā;
Evamevaṃ virocanti,
migarājāva te tayo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3624Msdiv 31Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3625Msdiv 32Sattavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Araññasattho nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3626Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3627Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nimittasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3628Nimittasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
