3. Hāsajanakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3602Msdiv 13“Dumagge paṃsukūlakaṃ,
laggaṃ disvāna satthuno;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
bhiyyo uccāritaṃ mayā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3603Msdiv 14Dūrato pana disvāna,
hāso me udapajjatha;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3604Msdiv 15Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3605Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3606Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā hāsajanako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3607Hāsajanakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
