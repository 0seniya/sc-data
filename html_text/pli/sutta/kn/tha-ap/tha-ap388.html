6. Valliphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4217Msdiv 31“Sabbe janā samāgamma,
agamiṃsu vanaṃ tadā;
Phalamanvesamānā te,
alabhiṃsu phalaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4218Msdiv 32Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
valliphalamadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4219Msdiv 33Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4220Msdiv 34Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4221Msdiv 35Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4222Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4223Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā valliphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4224Valliphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
