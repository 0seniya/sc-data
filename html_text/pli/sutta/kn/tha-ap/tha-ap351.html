9. Tindukaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3955SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3955Msdiv 59“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3956SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3956Msdiv 60Tindukaṃ saphalaṃ disvā,
bhinditvāna sakosakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sayambhussa madāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3957SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3957Msdiv 61Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3958SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3958Msdiv 62Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3959Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tindukaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3960Tindukaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
