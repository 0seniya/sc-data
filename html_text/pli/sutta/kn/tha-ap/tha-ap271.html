9 Upāgatāsayattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3207Msdiv 34“Himavantassa vemajjhe,
saro āsi sunimmito;
Tatthāhaṃ rakkhaso āsiṃ,
heṭhasīlo bhayānako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3208Msdiv 35Anukampako kāruṇiko,
vipassī lokanāyako;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3209Msdiv 36Upāgataṃ mahāvīraṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Āsayā abhinikkhamma,
avandiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3210Msdiv 37Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ vandiṃ purisuttamaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3211Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3212Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upāgatāsayo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3213Upāgatāsayattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
