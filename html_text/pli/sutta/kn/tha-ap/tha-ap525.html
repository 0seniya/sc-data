3. Saraṇagamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6820SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6820Msdiv 19“Āruhimha tadā nāvaṃ,
bhikkhu cājīviko cahaṃ;
Nāvāya bhijjamānāya,
bhikkhu me saraṇaṃ adā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6821SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6821Msdiv 20Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ so me saraṇaṃ adā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saraṇagamane phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6822SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6822Msdiv 21Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6823SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6823Msdiv 22Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6824SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6824Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6825Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saraṇagamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6826Saraṇagamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
