8 Saḷalapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3201Msdiv 30“Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Vipassiṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
raṃsijālasamākulaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3202Msdiv 31Pasannacitto sumano,
paramāya ca pītiyā;
Paggayha saḷalaṃ pupphaṃ,
vipassiṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3203Msdiv 32Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3204Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3205Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saḷalapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3206Saḷalapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
