1. Bodhivandakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.290Ms 20Ap1_4096Msdiv 1“Pāṭaliṃ haritaṃ disvā,
pādapaṃ dharaṇīruhaṃ;
Ekaṃsaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
avandiṃ pāṭaliṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4097Msdiv 2Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
garuṃ katvāna mānasaṃ;
Antosuddhaṃ bahisuddhaṃ,
suvimuttamanāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4098Msdiv 3Vipassiṃ lokamahitaṃ,
karuṇāñāṇasāgaraṃ;
Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
avandiṃ pāṭaliṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4099Msdiv 4Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bodhimabhivandahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4100Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4101Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhivandako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4102Bodhivandakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
