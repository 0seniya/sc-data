1. Tamālapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2666Msdiv 1“Cullāsītisahassāni,
thambhā sovaṇṇayā ahū;
Devalaṭṭhipaṭibhāgaṃ,
vimānaṃ me sunimmitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2667Msdiv 2Tamālapupphaṃ paggayha,
vippasannena cetasā;
Buddhassa abhiropayiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2668Msdiv 3Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2669Msdiv 4Ito vīsatime kappe,
candatittoti ekako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2670Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2671Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tamālapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2672Tamālapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
