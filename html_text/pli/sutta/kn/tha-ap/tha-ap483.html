1. Paṃsukūlasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6185Msdiv 1“Tisso nāmāsi bhagavā,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Paṃsukūlaṃ ṭhapetvāna,
vihāraṃ pāvisī jino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6186Msdiv 2Vinataṃ dhanumādāya,
bhakkhatthāya cariṃ ahaṃ;
Maṇḍalaggaṃ gahetvāna,
kānanaṃ pāvisiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.419Ms 20Ap1_6187Msdiv 3Tatthaddasaṃ paṃsukūlaṃ,
dumagge laggitaṃ tadā;
Cāpaṃ tattheva nikkhippa,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6188Msdiv 4Pasannacitto sumano,
vipulāya ca pītiyā;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
paṃsukūlaṃ avandahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6189Msdiv 5Dvenavute ito kappe,
paṃsukūlamavandahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6190Msdiv 6Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6191Msdiv 7Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6192Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6193Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṃsukūlasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6194Paṃsukūlasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
