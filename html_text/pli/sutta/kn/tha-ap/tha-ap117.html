5. Suvaṇṇapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1988Msdiv 40“Vipassī nāma bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Nisinno janakāyassa,
desesi amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1989Msdiv 41Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Soṇṇapupphāni cattāri,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1990Msdiv 42Suvaṇṇacchadanaṃ āsi,
yāvatā parisā tadā;
Buddhābhā ca suvaṇṇābhā,
āloko vipulo ahu.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1991Msdiv 43Udaggacitto sumano,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Vittisañjanano tesaṃ,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1992Msdiv 44Āyācitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
vanditvāna ca subbataṃ;
Pāmojjaṃ janayitvāna,
sakaṃ bhavanupāgamiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1993Msdiv 45Bhavane upaviṭṭhohaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1994Msdiv 46Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1995Msdiv 47Soḷasāsiṃsu rājāno,
nemisammatanāmakā;
Tetālīse ito kappe,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1996Msdiv 48Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1997Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā suvaṇṇapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1998Suvaṇṇapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
