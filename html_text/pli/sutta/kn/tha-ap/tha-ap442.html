10 Ambaphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5762Msdiv 81“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Piṇḍāya vicarantassa,
dhārato uttamaṃ yasaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5763Msdiv 82Aggaphalaṃ gahetvāna,
vippasannena cetasā;
Dakkhiṇeyyassa vīrassa,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5764Msdiv 83Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5765Msdiv 84Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
aggadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5766Msdiv 85Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5767Msdiv 86Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5768Msdiv 87Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4624)

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5769Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ambaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5770Ambaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5771Ekavihārivaggo catucattālīsamo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5772Tassuddānaṃ

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5773Thero ekavihārī ca,
saṅkhiyo pāṭihīrako;
Thaviko ucchukhaṇḍī ca,
kaḷambaambāṭakado.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5774Harītakambapiṇḍī ca,
ambado dasamo yati;
Chaḷasīti ca gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.
