6. Ajjunapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6728SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6728Msdiv 28“Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6729SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6729Msdiv 29Pasannacitto sumano,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Gahetvā ajjunaṃ pupphaṃ,
sayambhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6730SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6730Msdiv 30Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā kinnaraṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6731SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6731Msdiv 31Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Dasakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
mahārajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6732SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6732Msdiv 32Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Sukhette vappitaṃ bījaṃ,
sayambhumhi aho mama.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6733SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6733Msdiv 33Kusalaṃ vijjate mayhaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Pūjāraho ahaṃ ajja,
sakyaputtassa sāsane.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6734SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6734Msdiv 34Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6735SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6735Msdiv 35Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6736SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6736Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6737Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ajjunapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6738Ajjunapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
