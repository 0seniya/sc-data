3 Kosambaphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6706Msdiv 12“Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
kosambaṃ adadiṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6707Msdiv 13Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6708Msdiv 14Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6709Msdiv 15Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6710Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6711Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kosambaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6712Kosambaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
