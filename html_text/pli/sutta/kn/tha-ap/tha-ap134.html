2. Sudassanattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2193Msdiv 10“SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2193Msdiv 10“Vinatā nadiyā tīre,
pilakkhu phalito ahu;
Tāhaṃ rukkhaṃ gavesanto,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2194SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2194Msdiv 11Ketakaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
vaṇṭe chetvānahaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2195SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2195Msdiv 12Yena ñāṇena pattosi,
accutaṃ amataṃ padaṃ;
Taṃ ñāṇaṃ abhipūjemi,
buddhaseṭṭha mahāmuni.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2196SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2196Msdiv 13Ñāṇamhi pūjaṃ katvāna,
pilakkhumaddasaṃ ahaṃ;
Paṭiladdhomhi taṃ paññaṃ,
ñāṇapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2197SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2197Msdiv 14Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2198SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2198Msdiv 15Ito terasakappamhi,
dvādasāsuṃ phaluggatā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahapphalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2199SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2199Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2200Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sudassano thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2201Sudassanattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
