1. Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.282Ms 20Ap1_3970Msdiv 1“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
devasaṅghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3971Msdiv 2Catusaccaṃ pakāsentaṃ,
uddharantaṃ mahājanaṃ;
Assosiṃ madhuraṃ vācaṃ,
karavīkarudopamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3972Msdiv 3Brahmasarassa munino,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Ghose cittaṃ pasādetvā,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3973Msdiv 4Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pasādassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3974Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3975Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3976Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
