2. Nāgakesariyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3039SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3039Msdiv 4“Dhanuṃ advejjhaṃ katvāna,
vanamajjhogahiṃ ahaṃ;
Kesaraṃ ogataṃ disvā,
patapattaṃ samuṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3040SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3040Msdiv 5Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
tissassa lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3041SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3041Msdiv 6Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3042Msdiv 7SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3042Msdiv 7Tesattatimhi kappamhi,
satta kesaranāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3043SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3043Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3044Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgakesariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3045Nāgakesariyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
