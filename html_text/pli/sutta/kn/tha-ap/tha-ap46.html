4. Sanniṭṭhāpakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1284SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1284Msdiv 70“Araññe kuṭikaṃ katvā,
vasāmi pabbatantare;
Lābhālābhena santuṭṭho,
yasena ayasena ca.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1285SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1285Msdiv 71Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Vasīsatasahassehi,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1286Msdiv 72Upāgataṃ SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1286Msdiv 72Upāgataṃ mahānāgaṃ,
jalajuttamanāmakaṃ;
Tiṇasantharaṃ paññāpetvā,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1287SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1287Msdiv 73Pasannacitto sumano,
āmaṇḍaṃ pānīyañcahaṃ;
Adāsiṃ ujubhūtassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

āmaṇḍassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

cakkavattī mahabbalo.
