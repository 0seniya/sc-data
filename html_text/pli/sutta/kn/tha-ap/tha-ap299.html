7 Desakittakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3400Msdiv 31“Upasālakanāmohaṃ,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;
Kānanaṃ vanamogāḷhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3401Msdiv 32Disvāna vandiṃ pādesu,
lokāhutipaṭiggahaṃ;
Pasannacittaṃ maṃ ñatvā,
buddho antaradhāyatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3402Msdiv 33Kānanā abhinikkhamma,
buddhaseṭṭhamanussariṃ;
Taṃ desaṃ kittayitvāna,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3403Msdiv 34Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ desamabhikittayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3404Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3405Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā desakittako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3406Desakittakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
