4. Ekasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2844Msdiv 17“Dumagge paṃsukūlikaṃ,
laggaṃ disvāna satthuno;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
paṃsukūlaṃ avandahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2845Msdiv 18Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.210Ms 20Ap1_2846Msdiv 19Pañcavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Amitābhoti nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2847Msdiv 20Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2848Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2849Ekasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
