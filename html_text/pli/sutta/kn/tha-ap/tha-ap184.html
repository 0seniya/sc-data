2. Bandhujīvakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.192Ms 20Ap1_2588Msdiv 6“Siddhattho nāma sambuddho,
sayambhū sabbhi vaṇṇito;
Samādhiṃ so samāpanno,
nisīdi pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2589Msdiv 7Jātassare gavesanto,
dakajaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Bandhujīvakapupphāni,
addasaṃ samanantaraṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2590Msdiv 8Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
upāgacchiṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
siddhatthassābhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2591Msdiv 9Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2592Msdiv 10Ito cātuddase kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Samuddakappo nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2593Msdiv 11Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2594Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bandhujīvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2595Bandhujīvakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
