2. Gatasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1697Msdiv 10“Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1698Msdiv 11Sattanaṅgalakīpupphe,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Tissaṃ buddhaṃ samuddissa,
anantaguṇasāgaraṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1699Msdiv 12Sugatānugataṃ maggaṃ,
pūjetvā haṭṭhamānaso;
Añjaliñca tadākāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1700Msdiv 13Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1701Msdiv 14Ito aṭṭhamake kappe,
tayo aggisikhā ahu;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1702Msdiv 15Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1703Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1704Gatasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
