9. Sappidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2881SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2881Msdiv 44“Nisinno pāsādavare,
nārīgaṇapurakkhato;
Byādhitaṃ samaṇaṃ disvā,
abhināmesahaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2882SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2882Msdiv 45Upaviṭṭhaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Sappitelaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
siddhatthassa mahesino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2883SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2883Msdiv 46Passaddhadarathaṃ disvā,
vippasannamukhindriyaṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
anusaṃsāvayiṃ pure.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2884Msdiv 47Disvā maṃ SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2884Msdiv 47Disvā maṃ suppasannattaṃ,
iddhiyā pāramiṅgato;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī dhīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2885SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2885Msdiv 48Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sappitelassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2886SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2886Msdiv 49Ito sattarase kappe,
jutideva sanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2887SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2887Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2888Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sappidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2889Sappidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
