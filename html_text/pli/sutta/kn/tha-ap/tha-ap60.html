8. Dhajadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1446SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1446Msdiv 57“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
dhajamāropayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1447SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1447Msdiv 58Patitapattāni gaṇhitvā,
bahiddhā chaḍḍayiṃ ahaṃ;
Antosuddhaṃ bahisuddhaṃ,
adhimuttamanāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1448SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1448Msdiv 59Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
avandiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ;
Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1449SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1449Msdiv 60Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha;
‘Iminā dhajadānena,
upaṭṭhānena cūbhayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1450SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1450Msdiv 61Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ so na gacchati;
Devesu devasobhagyaṃ,
anubhossatinappakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1451SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1451Msdiv 62Anekasatakkhattuñca,
rājā raṭṭhe bhavissati;
Uggato nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1452SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1452Msdiv 63Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsanebhiramissati’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1453SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1453Msdiv 64Padhānapahitattomhi,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1454SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1454Msdiv 65Ekapaññāsasahasse,
kappe uggatasavhayo;
Paññāsasatasahasse,
khattiyo meghasavhayo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1455SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1455Msdiv 66Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1456Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhajadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1457Dhajadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
