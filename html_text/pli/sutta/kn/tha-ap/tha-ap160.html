8. Nāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2405SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2405Msdiv 39“Suvaccho nāma nāmena,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Purakkhato sasissehi,
vasate pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2406SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2406Msdiv 40Padumuttaro nāma jino,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2407SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2407Msdiv 41Vehāsamhi caṅkamati,
dhūpāyati jalate tathā;
Hāsaṃ mamaṃ viditvāna,
pakkāmi pācināmukho.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2408SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2408Msdiv 42Tañca acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Nāgapupphaṃ gahetvāna,
gatamaggamhi okiriṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2409SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2409Msdiv 43Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphaṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2410Msdiv 44SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2410Msdiv 44Ekattiṃse kappasate,
rājā āsi mahāraho;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2411SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2411Msdiv 45Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2412Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2413Nāgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
