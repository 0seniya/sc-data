7. Giripunnāgiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6138Msdiv 61“Sobhito nāma sambuddho,
cittakūṭe vasī tadā;
Gahetvā giripunnāgaṃ,
sayambhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6139Msdiv 62Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6140Msdiv 63Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6141Msdiv 64Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6142Msdiv 65Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6143Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā giripunnāgiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6144Giripunnāgiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
