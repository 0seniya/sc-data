5. Biḷālidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3183SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3183Msdiv 18“Himavantassāvidūre,
romaso nāma pabbato;
Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3184SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3184Msdiv 19Biḷāliyo gahetvāna,
samaṇassa adāsahaṃ;
Anumodi mahāvīro,
sayambhū aparājito.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3185SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3185Msdiv 20Biḷālī te mama dinnā,
vippasannena cetasā;
Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
phalaṃ nibbattataṃ tava.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3186SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3186Msdiv 21Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ biḷālimadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
biḷāliyā idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3187SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3187Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3188Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā biḷālidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3189Biḷālidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
