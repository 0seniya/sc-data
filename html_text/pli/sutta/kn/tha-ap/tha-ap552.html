10. Cūḷasugandhattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.508Ms 20Ap1_7624Msdiv 272“Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7625Msdiv 273Anubyañjanasampanno,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Byāmappabhāparivuto,
raṃsijālasamotthaṭo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7626Msdiv 274Assāsetā yathā cando,
sūriyova pabhaṅkaro;
Nibbāpetā yathā megho,
sāgarova guṇākaro.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7627Msdiv 275Dharaṇīriva sīlena,
himavāva samādhinā;
Ākāso viya paññāya,
asaṅgo anilo yathā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7628Msdiv 276Tadāhaṃ bārāṇasiyaṃ,
upapanno mahākule;
Pahūtadhanadhaññasmiṃ,
nānāratanasañcaye.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7629Msdiv 277Mahatā parivārena,
nisinnaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Upecca dhammamassosiṃ,
amataṃva manoharaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7630Msdiv 278Dvattiṃsalakkhaṇadharo,
sanakkhattova candimā;
Anubyañjanasampanno,
sālarājāva phullito.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7631Msdiv 279Raṃsijālaparikkhitto,
dittova kanakācalo;
Byāmappabhāparivuto,
sataraṃsī divākaro.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7632Msdiv 280Soṇṇānano jinavaro,
samaṇīva siluccayo;
Karuṇā puṇṇahadayo,
guṇena viya sāgaro.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7633Msdiv 281Lokavissutakitti ca,
sinerūva naguttamo;
Yasasā vitthato vīro,
ākāsasadiso muni.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7634Msdiv 282Asaṅgacitto sabbattha,
anilo viya nāyako;
Patiṭṭhā sabbabhūtānaṃ,
mahīva munisattamo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7635Msdiv 283Anupalitto lokena,
toyena padumaṃ yathā;
Kuvādagacchadahano,
aggikhandhova sobhasi.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7636Msdiv 284Agadho viya sabbattha,
kilesavisanāsako;
Gandhamādanaselova,
guṇagandhavibhūsito.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7637Msdiv 285Guṇānaṃ ākaro vīro,
ratanānaṃva sāgaro;
Sindhūva vanarājīnaṃ,
kilesamalahārako.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 2.509Ms 20Ap1_7638Msdiv 286Vijayīva mahāyodho,
mārasenāvamaddano;
Cakkavattīva so rājā,
bojjhaṅgaratanissaro.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7639Msdiv 287Mahābhisakkasaṅkāso,
dosabyādhitikicchako;
Sallakatto yathā vejjo,
diṭṭhigaṇḍaviphālako.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7640Msdiv 288So tadā lokapajjoto,
sanarāmarasakkato;
Parisāsu narādicco,
dhammaṃ desayate jino.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7641Msdiv 289‘Dānaṃ datvā mahābhogo,
sīlena sugatūpago;
Bhāvanāya ca nibbāti’,
iccevamanusāsatha.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7642Msdiv 290Desanaṃ taṃ mahassādaṃ,
ādimajjhantasobhanaṃ;
Suṇanti parisā sabbā,
amataṃva mahārasaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7643Msdiv 291Sutvā sumadhuraṃ dhammaṃ,
pasanno jinasāsane;
Sugataṃ saraṇaṃ gantvā,
yāvajīvaṃ namassahaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7644Msdiv 292Munino gandhakuṭiyā,
opuñjesiṃ tadā mahiṃ;
Catujjātena gandhena,
māse aṭṭha dinesvahaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7645Msdiv 293Paṇidhāya sugandhattaṃ,
sarīravissagandhino;
Tadā jino viyākāsi,
sugandhatanulābhitaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7646Msdiv 294‘Yo yaṃ gandhakuṭibhūmiṃ,
gandhenopuñjate sakiṃ;
Tena kammavipākena,
upapanno tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7647Msdiv 295Sugandhadeho sabbattha,
bhavissati ayaṃ naro;
Guṇagandhayutto hutvā,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7648Msdiv 296Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7649Msdiv 297Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jāto vippakule ahaṃ;
Gabbhaṃ me vasato mātā,
dehenāsi sugandhitā.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7650Msdiv 298Yadā ca mātukucchimhā,
nikkhamāmi tadā purī;
Sāvatthisabbagandhehi,
vāsitā viya vāyatha.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7651Msdiv 299Pupphavassañca surabhi,
dibbagandhaṃ manoramaṃ;
Dhūpāni ca mahagghāni,
upavāyiṃsu tāvade.

SC 29PTS vp Pali 2.510Ms 20Ap1_7652Msdiv 300Devā ca sabbagandhehi,
dhūpapupphehi taṃ gharaṃ;
Vāsayiṃsu sugandhena,
yasmiṃ jāto ahaṃ ghare.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7653Msdiv 301Yadā ca taruṇo bhaddo,
paṭhame yobbane ṭhito;
Tadā selaṃ saparisaṃ,
vinetvā narasārathi.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7654Msdiv 302Tehi sabbehi parivuto,
sāvatthipuramāgato;
Tadā buddhānubhāvaṃ taṃ,
disvā pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7655Msdiv 303Sīlaṃ samādhipaññañca,
vimuttiñca anuttaraṃ;
Bhāvetvā caturo dhamme,
pāpuṇiṃ āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7656Msdiv 304Yadā pabbajito cāhaṃ,
yadā ca arahā ahuṃ;
Nibbāyissaṃ yadā cāhaṃ,
gandhavasso tadā ahu.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7657Msdiv 305Sarīragandho ca sadātiseti me,
Mahārahaṃ candanacampakuppalaṃ;
Tatheva gandhe itare ca sabbaso,
Pasayha vāyāmi tato tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7658Msdiv 306Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7659Msdiv 307Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7660Msdiv 308Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (6245)

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7661Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā cūḷasugandho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7662Cūḷasugandhattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7663Bhaddiyavaggo pañcapaññāsamo.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_7664Tassuddānaṃ

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_7665Bhaddiyo revato thero,
mahālābhī ca sīvali;
Vaṅgīso nandako ceva,
kāḷudāyī tathābhayo.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_7666Lomaso vanavaccho ca,
sugandho ceva dasamo;
Tīṇi gāthāsatā tattha,
soḷasā ca taduttari.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_7667Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_7668Kaṇikāravhayo vaggo,
phalado tiṇadāyako;
Kaccāno bhaddiyo vaggo,
gāthāyo gaṇitā cimā.

SC 46PTS vp Pali 2.511Ms 20Ap1_7669Navagāthāsatānīha,
caturāsītiyeva ca;
Sapaññāsaṃ pañcasataṃ,
apadānā pakāsitā.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_7670Saha udānagāthāhi,
chasahassāni hontimā;
Dvesatāni ca gāthānaṃ,
aṭṭhārasa taduttari.
