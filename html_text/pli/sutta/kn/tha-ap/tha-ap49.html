7. Sayanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1308Msdiv 88“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
mettacittassa tādino;
Sayanaggaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dussabhaṇḍehi atthataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1309Msdiv 89Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
kappiyaṃ sayanāsanaṃ;
Uṭṭhāya sayanā tamhā,
vehāsaṃ uggamī jino.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.99Ms 20Ap1_1310Msdiv 90Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ sayanamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sayanassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1311Msdiv 91Ekapaññāsito kappe,
varako devasavhayo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1312Msdiv 92Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1313Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sayanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1314Sayanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
