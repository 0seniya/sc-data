1. Kumudamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2510Msdiv 1“Pabbate himavantamhi,
mahājātassaro ahu;
Tatthajo rakkhaso āsiṃ,
ghorarūpo mahabbalo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2511Msdiv 2Kumudaṃ pupphate tattha,
cakkamattāni jāyare;
Ocināmi ca taṃ pupphaṃ,
balino samitiṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2512Msdiv 3Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Pupphasaṅkocitaṃ disvā,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2513Msdiv 4Upāgatañca sambuddhaṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Sabbañca pupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2514Msdiv 5Yāvatā himavantantā,
parisā sā tadā ahu;
Tāvacchadanasampanno,
agamāsi tathāgato.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2515Msdiv 6Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2516Msdiv 7Ito pannarase kappe,
sattāhesuṃ janādhipā;
Sahassarathanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2517Msdiv 8Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2518Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kumudamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2519Kumudamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
