4. Abbhañjanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.456Ms 20Ap1_6827Msdiv 24“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
rājuyyāne vasāmahaṃ;
Cammavāsī tadā āsiṃ,
kamaṇḍaludharo ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6828Msdiv 25Addasaṃ vimalaṃ buddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Padhānaṃ pahitattaṃ taṃ,
jhāyiṃ jhānarataṃ vasiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6829Msdiv 26Sabbakāmasamiddhiñca,
oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ;
Disvā pasanno sumano,
abbhañjanamadāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6830Msdiv 27Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
abbhañjanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6831Msdiv 28Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6832Msdiv 29Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6833Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6834Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā abbhañjanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6835Abbhañjanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
