10 Yūthikapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2736Msdiv 53“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,
vihāraṃ yāti cakkhumā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2737Msdiv 54Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
yūthikaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropayiṃ,
mettacittassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2738Msdiv 55Tena cittappasādena,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2739Msdiv 56Ito paññāsakappesu,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Samittanandano nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2740Msdiv 57Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2265)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2741Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yūthikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2742Yūthikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Tamālapupphiyavaggo vīsatimo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2743Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2744Tamālatiṇasanthāro,
khaṇḍaphulli asokiyo;
Aṅkolakī kisalayo,
tinduko nelapupphiyo;
Kiṃkaṇiko yūthiko ca,
gāthā paññāsa aṭṭha cāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2745Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2746Bhikkhādāyī parivāro,
sereyyo sobhito tathā;
Chattañca bandhujīvī ca,
supāricariyopi ca.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_2747Kumudo kuṭajo ceva,
tamāli dasamo kato;
Chasatāni ca gāthāni,
chasaṭṭhi ca tatuttari.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_2748Bhikkhāvaggadasakaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_2749Dutiyasatakaṃ samattaṃ.
