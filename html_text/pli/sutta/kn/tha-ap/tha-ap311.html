9 Buddhasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3490SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3490Msdiv 43“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Vanantaragataṃ santaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3491SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3491Msdiv 44Addasaṃ supinantena,
siddhatthaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
sugatiṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3492SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3492Msdiv 45Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3493SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3493Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3494Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3494Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3495Buddhasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
