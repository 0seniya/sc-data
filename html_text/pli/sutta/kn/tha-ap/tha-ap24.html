2 Upavānattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_913Msdiv 52 “Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
sambuddho parinibbuto.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_914Msdiv 53Mahājanā samāgamma,
pūjayitvā tathāgataṃ;
Citaṃ katvāna sukataṃ,
sarīraṃ abhiropayuṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_915Msdiv 54Sarīrakiccaṃ katvāna,
dhātuṃ tattha samānayuṃ;
Sadevamānusā sabbe,
buddhathūpaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.71Ms 20Ap1_916Msdiv 55Paṭhamā kañcanamayā,
dutiyāsi maṇīmayā;
Tatiyā rūpiyamayā,
catutthī phalikāmayā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_917Msdiv 56Tathā pañcamiyā Tathā pañcamiyā bhūmi,
lohitaṅgamayā ahu;
Chaṭṭhā masāragallassa,
sabbaratanamayūpari.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_918Msdiv 57Jaṅghā maṇimayā āsi,
vedikā ratanamayā;
Sabbasoṇṇamayo thūpo,
uddhaṃ yojanamuggato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_919Msdiv 58Devā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_920Msdiv 59Dhātu āveṇikā natthi,
sarīraṃ ekapiṇḍitaṃ;
Imamhi buddhathūpamhi,
kassāma kañcukaṃ mayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_921Msdiv 60Devā sattahi ratnehi,
aññaṃ vaḍḍhesu yojanaṃ;
Thūpo dviyojanubbedho,
timiraṃ byapahanti so.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_922Msdiv 61Nāgā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
Manussā ceva devā ca,
buddhathūpaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_923Msdiv 62Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_924Msdiv 63Indanīlaṃ mahānīlaṃ,
atho jotirasaṃ maṇiṃ;
Ekato sannipātetvā,
buddhathūpaṃ achādayuṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_925Msdiv 64Sabbaṃ maṇimayaṃ āsi,
tāvatā buddhacetiyaṃ;
Tiyojanasamubbiddhaṃ,
ālokakaraṇaṃ tadā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_926Msdiv 65Garuḷā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
Manussā devā nāgā ca,
buddhathūpaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_927Msdiv 66‘Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_928Msdiv 67Sabbaṃ maṇimayaṃ thūpaṃ,
akaruṃ te ca kañcukaṃ;
Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_929Msdiv 68Catuyojanamubbiddho,
buddhathūpo virocati;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
sataraṃsīva uggato.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_930Msdiv 69Kumbhaṇḍā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
Manussā ceva devā ca,
nāgā ca garuḷā tathā;
Paccekaṃ buddhaseṭṭhassa,
akaṃsu thūpamuttamaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_931Msdiv 70‘Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Ratanehi chādessāma,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ’.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_932Msdiv 71Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ;
Pañcayojanamubbiddho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 1.72Ms 20Ap1_933Msdiv 72Yakkhā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
Manussā devā nāgā ca,
garuḷā kumbhaaṇḍakā.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_934Msdiv 73Paccekaṃ buddhaseṭṭhassa,
akaṃsu thūpamuttamaṃ;
‘Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_935Msdiv 74Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Phalikāhi chādessāma,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_936Msdiv 75Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ;
Cha yojanāni ubbiddho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_937Msdiv 76Gandhabbā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manujā devatā nāgā,
garuḷā kumbhayakkhakā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_938Msdiv 77Sabbekaṃsu buddhathūpaṃ,
mayamettha akārakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino’.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_939Msdiv 78Vediyo satta katvāna,
chattamāropayiṃsu te;
Sabbasoṇṇamayaṃ thūpaṃ,
gandhabbā kārayuṃ tadā.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_940Msdiv 79Sattayojanamubbiddho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā;
Rattindivā na ñāyanti,
āloko hoti sabbadā.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_941Msdiv 80Abhibhonti na tassābhā,
candasūrā satārakā;
Samantā yojanasate,
padīpopi na pajjali.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_942Msdiv 81Tena kālena ye keci,
thūpaṃ pūjenti mānusā;
Na te thūpamāruhanti,
ambare ukkhipanti te.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_943Msdiv 82Devehi ṭhapito yakkho,
abhisammatanāmako;
Dhajaṃ vā pupphadāmaṃ vā,
abhiropeti uttari.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_944Msdiv 83Na te passanti taṃ yakkhaṃ,
dāmaṃ passanti gacchato;
Evaṃ passitvā gacchantā,
sabbe gacchanti suggatiṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_945Msdiv 84Viruddhā ye pāvacane,
pasannā ye ca sāsane;
Pāṭiheraṃ daṭṭhukāmā,
thūpaṃ pūjenti mānusā.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_946Msdiv 85Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ bhatako tadā;
Āmoditaṃ janaṃ disvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_947Msdiv 86‘Uḷāro bhagavā heso,
yassa dhātudharedisaṃ;
Imā ca janatā tuṭṭhā,
kāraṃ kubbaṃ na tappare.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_948Msdiv 87Ahampi kāraṃ kassāmi,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
bhavissāmi anāgate’.

SC 37PTS vp Pali 1.73Ms 20Ap1_949Msdiv 88Sudhotaṃ rajakenāhaṃ,
uttareyyapaṭaṃ mama;
Veḷagge ālaggetvāna,
dhajaṃ ukkhipimambare.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_950Msdiv 89Abhisammatako gayha,
ambarehāsi me dhajaṃ;
Vāteritaṃ dhajaṃ disvā,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_951Msdiv 90Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
samaṇaṃ upasaṅkamiṃ;
Taṃ bhikkhuṃ abhivādetvā,
vipākaṃ pucchahaṃ dhaje.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_952Msdiv 91So me kathesi ānanda,
pītisañjananaṃ mama;
‘Tassa dhajassa vipākaṃ,
anubhossasi sabbadā.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_953Msdiv 92Hatthī assā rathā pattī,
senā ca caturaṅginī;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_954Msdiv 93Saṭṭhitūriyasahassāni,
bheriyo samalaṅkatā;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_955Msdiv 94Chaḷāsītisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
āmuttamaṇikuṇḍalā.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_956Msdiv 95Aḷārapamhā hasulā,
susaññā tanumajjhimā;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_957Msdiv 96Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissasi;
Asītikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissasi.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_958Msdiv 97Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissasi;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_959Msdiv 98Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 48Ms 20Ap1_960Msdiv 99Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Puññakammena saṃyutto,
brahmabandhu bhavissasi.

SC 49Ms 20Ap1_961Msdiv 100Asītikoṭiṃ chaḍḍetvā,
dāse kammakare bahū;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissasi.

SC 50Ms 20Ap1_962Msdiv 101Ārādhayitvā sambuddhaṃ,
gotamaṃ sakyapuṅgavaṃ;
Upavānoti nāmena,
hessasi satthu sāvako’.

SC 51Ms 20Ap1_963Msdiv 102Satasahasse kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha;
Sumutto saravegova,
kilese jhāpayī mama.

SC 52Ms 20Ap1_964Msdiv 103Cakkavattissa santassa,
catudīpissarassa me;
Tiyojanāni samantā,
ussīsanti dhajā sadā.

SC 53PTS vp Pali 1.74Ms 20Ap1_965Msdiv 104Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 54Ms 20Ap1_966Msdiv 105Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 55Ms 20Ap1_967Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upavāno thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 56Ms 20Ap1_968Upavānattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
