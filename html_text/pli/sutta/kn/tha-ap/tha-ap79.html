7. Sūcidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1630Msdiv 30“Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
sambuddho lokanāyako;
Sumedho nāma nāmena,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1631Msdiv 31Tassa kañcanavaṇṇassa,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Pañca sūcī mayā dinnā,
sibbanatthāya cīvaraṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1632Msdiv 32Teneva sūcidānena,
nipuṇatthavipassakaṃ;
Tikkhaṃ lahuñca phāsuñca,
ñāṇaṃ me udapajjatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1633Msdiv 33Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1634Msdiv 34Dvipadādhipatī nāma,
rājāno caturo ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1635Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1636Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sūcidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1637Sūcidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
