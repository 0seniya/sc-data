5. Ekasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1614Msdiv 18“Khaṇḍo nāmāsi nāmena,
vipassissaggasāvako;
Ekā bhikkhā mayā dinnā,
lokāhutipaṭiggahe.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1615Msdiv 19Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadinda narāsabha;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekabhikkhāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1616Msdiv 20Cattālīsamhito kappe,
varuṇo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1617Msdiv 21Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1618Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1619Ekasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
