9. Vanavacchattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7601Msdiv 251“Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7602Msdiv 252Tadāhaṃ pabbajitvāna,
tassa buddhassa sāsane;
Yāvajīvaṃ caritvāna,
brahmacāraṃ tato cuto.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7603Msdiv 253Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7604Msdiv 254Tato cuto araññamhi,
kapoto āsahaṃ tahiṃ;
Vasate guṇasampanno,
bhikkhu jhānarato sadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7605Msdiv 255Mettacitto kāruṇiko,
sadā pamuditānano;
Upekkhako mahāvīro,
appamaññāsu kovido.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7606Msdiv 256Vinīvaraṇasaṅkappe,
sabbasattahitāsaye;
Visaṭṭho nacirenāsiṃ,
tasmiṃ sugatasāvake.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7607Msdiv 257Upecca pādamūlamhi,
nisinnassa tadāssame;
Kadāci sāmisaṃ deti,
dhammaṃ desesi cekadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7608Msdiv 258Tadā vipulapemena,
upāsitvā jinatrajaṃ;
Tato cuto gato saggaṃ,
pavāso sagharaṃ yathā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7609Msdiv 259Saggā cuto manussesu,
nibbatto puññakammunā;
Agāraṃ chaḍḍayitvāna,
pabbajiṃ bahuso ahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7610Msdiv 260Samaṇo tāpaso vippo,
paribbajo tathevahaṃ;
Hutvā vasiṃ araññamhi,
anekasataso ahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7611Msdiv 261Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
ramme kapilavatthave;
Vacchagotto dijo tassa,
jāyāya ahamokkamiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7612Msdiv 262Mātu me dohaḷo āsi,
tirokucchigatassa me;
Jāyamānasamīpamhi,
vanavāsāya nicchayo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7613Msdiv 263Tato me ajanī mātā,
ramaṇīye vanantare;
Gabbhato nikkhamantaṃ maṃ,
kāsāyena paṭiggahuṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7614Msdiv 264Tato kumāro siddhattho,
jāto sakyakuladdhajo;
Tassa mitto piyo āsiṃ,
saṃvisaṭṭho sumāniyo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7615Msdiv 265Sattasārebhinikkhante,
ohāya vipulaṃ yasaṃ;
Ahampi pabbajitvāna,
himavantamupāgamiṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7616Msdiv 266Vanālayaṃ bhāvanīyaṃ,
kassapaṃ dhutavādikaṃ;
Disvā sutvā jinuppādaṃ,
upesiṃ narasārathiṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7617Msdiv 267So me dhammamadesesi,
sabbatthaṃ sampakāsayaṃ;
Tatohaṃ pabbajitvāna,
vanameva punāgamaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7618Msdiv 268Tatthāppamatto viharaṃ,
chaḷabhiññā aphassayiṃ;
Aho suladdhalābhomhi,
sumittenānukampito.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7619Msdiv 269Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7620Msdiv 270Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7621Msdiv 271Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7622Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vanavaccho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7623Vanavacchattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
