8. Phalakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2328Msdiv 37“Yānakāro pure āsiṃ,
dārukamme susikkhito;
Candanaṃ phalakaṃ katvā,
adāsiṃ lokabandhuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2329Msdiv 38Pabhāsati idaṃ byamhaṃ,
suvaṇṇassa sunimmitaṃ;
Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
dibbayānaṃ upaṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2330Msdiv 39Pāsādā sivikā ceva,
Nibbattanti yadicchakaṃ;
Akkhobhaṃ ratanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Phalakassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2331Msdiv 40Ekanavutito kappe,
phalakaṃ yamahaṃ dadiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phalakassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2332Msdiv 41Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
caturo nimmitāvhayā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2333Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2334Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phalakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2335Phalakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
