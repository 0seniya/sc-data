1. Udakāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.218Ms 20Ap1_2973Msdiv 1“Ārāmadvārā nikkhamma,
phalakaṃ santhariṃ ahaṃ;
Udakañca upaṭṭhāsiṃ,
uttamatthassa pattiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2974Msdiv 2Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
āsane codake phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2975Msdiv 3Ito pannarase kappe,
abhisāmasamavhayo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2976Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2977Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2978Udakāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
