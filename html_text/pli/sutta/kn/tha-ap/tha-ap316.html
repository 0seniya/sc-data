4. Sattapadumiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3525Msdiv 14“Nadīkūle vasāmahaṃ,
nesādo nāma brāhmaṇo;
Satapattehi pupphehi,
sammajjitvāna assamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3526Msdiv 15Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Siddhatthaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Disvā nabhena gacchantaṃ,
Hāso me udapajjatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3527Msdiv 16Paccuggantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Assamaṃ atināmetvā,
jalajaggehi okiriṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3528Msdiv 17Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3529Msdiv 18Ito te sattame kappe,
caturo pādapāvarā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3530Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3531Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapadumiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3532Sattapadumiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
