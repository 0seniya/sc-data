10 Taraṇīyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3278Msdiv 38“Mahāpathamhi visame,
setu kārāpito mayā;
Taraṇatthāya lokassa,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3279Msdiv 39Ekanavutito kappe,
yo setu kārito mayā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3280Msdiv 40Pañcapaññāsito kappe,
eko āsiṃ samogadho;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3281Msdiv 41Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2618)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3282Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā taraṇīyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3283Taraṇīyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Suvaṇṇabibbohanavaggo aṭṭhavīsatimo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3284Tassuddānaṃ

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3285Suvaṇṇaṃ tilamuṭṭhi ca,
caṅkoṭabbhañjanañjalī;
Potthako citamāluvā,
ekapuṇḍarī setunā;
Dvecattālīsa gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhīti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3286Ekādasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
