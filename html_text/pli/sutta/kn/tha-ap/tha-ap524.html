2 Mañcadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6812SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6812Msdiv 13 “Vipassino bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Ekamañcaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
pasannena sapāṇinā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6813SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6813Msdiv 14Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
dibbayānaṃ samajjhagaṃ;
Tena mañcakadānena,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6814SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6814Msdiv 15Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ mañcamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
mañcadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6815SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6815Msdiv 16Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6816SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6816Msdiv 17Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6817SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6817Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6818Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6818Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mañcadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6819Mañcadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
