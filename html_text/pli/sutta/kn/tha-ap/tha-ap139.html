7. Atthasandassakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.168Ms 20Ap1_2240Msdiv 47“Visālamāḷe āsīno,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Khīṇāsavaṃ balappattaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2241Msdiv 48Satasahassā tevijjā,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Parivārenti sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2242Msdiv 49Ñāṇe upanidhā yassa,
na vijjati sadevake;
Anantañāṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2243Msdiv 50Dhammakāyañca dīpentaṃ,
kevalaṃ ratanākaraṃ;
Vikappetuṃ na sakkonti,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2244Msdiv 51Imāhi tīhi gāthāhi,
nāradovhayavacchalo;
Padumuttaraṃ thavitvāna,
sambuddhaṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2245Msdiv 52Tena cittappasādena,
buddhasanthavanena ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2246Msdiv 53Ito tiṃsakappasate,
sumitto nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2247Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2248Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā atthasandassako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2249Atthasandassakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
