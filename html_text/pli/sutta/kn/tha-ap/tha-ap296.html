4. Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3380Msdiv 17“Anuggatamhi ādicce,
panādo vipulo ahu;
Buddhaseṭṭhassa lokamhi,
pātubhāvo mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3381Msdiv 18Ghosamassosahaṃ tattha,
na ca passāmi taṃ jinaṃ;
Maraṇañca anuppatto,
buddhasaññamanussariṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3382Msdiv 19Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3383Msdiv 20Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3384Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3385Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
