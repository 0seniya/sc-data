1 Bhikkhadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.140Ms 20Ap1_1852Msdiv 1“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Pavarā abhinikkhantaṃ,
Vanā nibbanamāgataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1853Msdiv 2Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ pādāsiṃ,
siddhatthassa mahesino;
Paññāya upasantassa,
mahāvīrassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1854Msdiv 3Padenānupadāyantaṃ,
nibbāpente mahājanaṃ;
Uḷārā vitti me jātā,
buddhe ādiccabandhune.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1855Msdiv 4Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhikkhādānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1856Msdiv 5Sattāsītimhito kappe,
mahāreṇu sanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
sattete cakkavattino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1857Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1858Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhikkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1859Bhikkhadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
