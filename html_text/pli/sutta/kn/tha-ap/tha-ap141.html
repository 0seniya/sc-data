9 Sālapupphadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2257Msdiv 60“Migarājā tadā āsiṃ,
abhijāto sukesarī;
Giriduggaṃ gavesanto,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2258Msdiv 61Ayaṃ nu kho mahāvīro,
nibbāpeti mahājanaṃ;
Yannūnāhaṃ upāseyyaṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2259Msdiv 62Sākhaṃ sālassa bhañjitvā,
sakosaṃ pupphamāhariṃ;
Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
adāsiṃ pupphamuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2260Msdiv 63Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2261Msdiv 64Ito ca navame kappe,
virocanasanāmakā;
Tayo āsiṃsu rājāno,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2262Msdiv 65Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2263Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sālapupphadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2264Sālapupphadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
