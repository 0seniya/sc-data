6. Saparivārāsanattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1428Msdiv 43“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
piṇḍapātaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Gantvā kiliṭṭhakaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
mallikāhi parikkhitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1429Msdiv 44Tamhāsanamhi āsīno,
buddho lokagganāyako;
Akittayi piṇḍapātaṃ,
ujubhūto samāhito.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1430Msdiv 45Yathāpi bhaddake khette,
bījaṃ appampi ropitaṃ;
Sammā dhāraṃ pavecchante,
phalaṃ toseti kassakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1431Msdiv 46Tathevāyaṃ piṇḍapāto,
sukhette ropito tayā;
Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
phalaṃ te phalaṃ te tosayissati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1432Msdiv 47Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Piṇḍapātaṃ gahetvāna,
pakkāmi uttarāmukho.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1433Msdiv 48Saṃvuto pātimokkhasmiṃ,
indriyesu ca pañcasu;
Pavivekamanuyutto,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1434Msdiv 49Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1435Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saparivārāsano thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1436Saparivārāsanattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
