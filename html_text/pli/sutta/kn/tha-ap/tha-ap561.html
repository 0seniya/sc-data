9. Sivakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7803SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7803Msdiv 117“Esanāya carantassa,
vipassissa mahesino;
Rittakaṃ pattaṃ disvāna,
kummāsaṃ pūrayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7804SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7804Msdiv 118Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bhikkhamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kummāsassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7805SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7805Msdiv 119Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7806SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7806Msdiv 120Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7807SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7807Msdiv 121Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7808Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sivakatthero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7809Sivakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
