4. Dhotakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4924Msdiv 72“Gaṅgā bhāgīrathī nāma,
himavantā pabhāvitā;
Haṃsavatiyā dvārena,
anusandati tāvade.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4925Msdiv 73Sobhito nāma ārāmo,
gaṅgākūle sumāpito;
Tattha padumuttaro buddho,
vasate lokanāyako.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.344Ms 20Ap1_4926Msdiv 74Tidasehi yathā indo,
manujehi purakkhato;
Nisīdi tattha bhagavā,
asambhītova kesarī.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4927Msdiv 75Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
vasāmi brāhmaṇo ahaṃ;
Chaḷaṅgo nāma nāmena,
evaṃnāmo mahāmuni.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4928Msdiv 76Aṭṭhārasa sissasatā,
parivārenti maṃ tadā;
Tehi sissehi samito,
gaṅgātīraṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4929Msdiv 77Tatthaddasāsiṃ samaṇe,
nikkuhe dhotapāpake;
Bhāgīrathiṃ tarantehaṃ,
evaṃ cintesi tāvade.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4930Msdiv 78‘Sāyaṃ pātaṃ tarantāme,
buddhaputtā mahāyasā;
Vihesayanti attānaṃ,
tesaṃ attā vihaññati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4931Msdiv 79Sadevakassa lokassa,
buddho aggo pavuccati;
Natthi me dakkhiṇe kāraṃ,
gatimaggavisodhanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4932Msdiv 80Yannūna buddhaseṭṭhassa,
setuṃ gaṅgāya kāraye;
Kārāpetvā imaṃ kammaṃ,
santarāmi imaṃ bhavaṃ’.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4933Msdiv 81Satasahassaṃ datvāna,
setuṃ kārāpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Saddahanto kataṃ kāraṃ,
vipulaṃ me bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4934Msdiv 82Kārāpetvāna taṃ setuṃ,
upesiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Sirasi añjaliṃ katvā,
imaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4935Msdiv 83‘Satasahassassa vayaṃ,
datvā kārāpito mayā;
Tavatthāya mahāsetu,
paṭiggaṇha mahāmune’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4936Msdiv 84Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4937Msdiv 85‘Yo me setuṃ akāresi,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

4.1. Setudānaānisaṃsa

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4938Msdiv 86Darito pabbatato vā,
rukkhato patitopiyaṃ;
Cutopi lacchatī ṭhānaṃ,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4939Msdiv 87Virūḷhamūlasantānaṃ,
nigrodhamiva māluto;
Amittā nappasahanti,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4940Msdiv 88Nāssa corā pasahanti,
nātimaññanti khattiyā;
Sabbe tarissatāmitte,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 18PTS vp Pali 2.345Ms 20Ap1_4941Msdiv 89Abbhokāsagataṃ santaṃ,
kaṭhinātapatāpitaṃ;
Puññakammena saṃyuttaṃ,
na bhavissati vedanā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4942Msdiv 90Devaloke manusse vā,
hatthiyānaṃ sunimmitaṃ;
Tassa saṅkappamaññāya,
nibbattissati tāvade.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4943Msdiv 91Sahassassā vātajavā,
sindhavā sīghavāhanā;
Sāyaṃ pātaṃ upessanti,
setudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4944Msdiv 92Āgantvāna manussattaṃ,
sukhitoyaṃ bhavissati;
Vehāsaṃ manujasseva,
hatthiyānaṃ bhavissati.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4945Msdiv 93Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4946Msdiv 94Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4947Msdiv 95Aho me sukataṃ kammaṃ,
jalajuttamanāmake;
Tattha kāraṃ karitvāna,
pattohaṃ āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4948Msdiv 96Padhānaṃ pahitattomhi,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.
