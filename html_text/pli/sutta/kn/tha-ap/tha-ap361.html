9 Saraṇagamaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4019SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4019Msdiv 34“Āruhimha tadā nāvaṃ,
bhikkhu cājīvako cahaṃ;
Nāvāya bhijjamānāya,
bhikkhu me saraṇaṃ adā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4020SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4020Msdiv 35Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yañca me saraṇaṃ adā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saraṇāgamane phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4021SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4021Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4022Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4022Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saraṇagamaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4023Saraṇagamaniyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
