3. Khaṇḍaphulliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2681SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2681Msdiv 12“Phussassa kho bhagavato,
thūpo āsi mahāvane;
Kuñjarehi tadā bhinno,
parūḷho pādapo tahiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2682SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2682Msdiv 13Visamañca samaṃ katvā,
sudhāpiṇḍaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Tilokagaruno tassa,
guṇehi paritosito.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2683SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2683Msdiv 14Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sudhāpiṇḍassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2684SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2684Msdiv 15Sattasattatikappamhi,
jitasenāsuṃ soḷasa;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2685SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2685Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2686Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā khaṇḍaphulliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2687Khaṇḍaphulliyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
