10. Pupphacaṅkoṭiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1576Msdiv 68“Abhītarūpaṃ sīhaṃva,
garuḷaggaṃva pakkhinaṃ;
Byagghūsabhaṃva pavaraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1577Msdiv 69Sikhiṃ tilokasaraṇaṃ,
anejaṃ aparājitaṃ;
Nisinnaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1578Msdiv 70Caṅkoṭake ṭhapetvāna,
anojaṃ pupphamuttamaṃ;
Saha caṅkoṭakeneva,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ samokiriṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1579Msdiv 71Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadinda narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1580Msdiv 72Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1581Msdiv 73Sampuṇṇe tiṃsakappamhi,
devabhūtisanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
pañcāsuṃ cakkavattino.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1582Msdiv 74Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1412)

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1583Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphacaṅkoṭiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1584Pupphacaṅkoṭiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1585Sakacintaniyavaggo sattamo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1586Tassuddānaṃ

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1587Sakacintī avopupphī,
sapaccāgamanena ca;
Parappasādī bhisado,
sucinti vatthadāyako.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1588Ambadāyī ca sumano,
pupphacaṅkoṭakīpi ca;
Gāthekasattati vuttā,
gaṇitā atthadassibhi.
