3 Billiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5793Msdiv 15“Candabhāgānadītīre,
assamo sukato mama;
Billarukkhehi ākiṇṇo,
nānādumanisevito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5794Msdiv 16Sugandhaṃ beluvaṃ disvā,
buddhaseṭṭhamanussariṃ;
Khāribhāraṃ pūrayitvā,
tuṭṭho saṃviggamānaso.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5795Msdiv 17Kakusandhaṃ upāgamma,
billapakkamadāsahaṃ;
Puññakkhettassa vīrassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5796Msdiv 18Imasmiṃyeva kappasmiṃ,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5797Msdiv 19Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5798Msdiv 20Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5799Msdiv 21Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5800Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā billiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5801Billiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
