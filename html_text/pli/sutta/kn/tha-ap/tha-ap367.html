5. Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4056SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4056Msdiv 16“Padumo nāma sambuddho,
cittakūṭe vasī tadā;
Disvāna taṃ ahaṃ buddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4057SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4057Msdiv 17Aṅkolaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;
Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
pūjayiṃ padumaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4058SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4058Msdiv 18Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4059SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4059Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4060Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4061Aṅkolapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
