6 Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2937Msdiv 29“Nārado iti me nāmaṃ,
kassapo iti maṃ vidū;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
vipassiṃ devasakkataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2938Msdiv 30Anubyañjanadharaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Aṅkolapupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2939Msdiv 31Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2940Msdiv 32Catusattatito kappe,
romaso nāma khattiyo;
Āmukkamālābharaṇo,
sayoggabalavāhano.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2941Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2942Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2943Aṅkolapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
