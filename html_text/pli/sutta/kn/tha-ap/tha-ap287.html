5. Maghavapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3314Msdiv 20“Nammadānadiyā tīre,
sayambhū aparājito;
Samādhiṃ so samāpanno,
vippasanno anāvilo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3315Msdiv 21Disvā pasannasumano,
sambuddhaṃ aparājitaṃ;
Tāhaṃ maghavapupphena,
sayambhuṃ pūjayiṃ tadā.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.241Ms 20Ap1_3316Msdiv 22Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3317Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3318Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maghavapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3319Maghavapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
