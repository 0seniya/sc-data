10. Upavānattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7810Msdiv 122“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
sambuddho parinibbuto.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7811Msdiv 123Mahājanā samāgamma,
pūjayitvā tathāgataṃ;
Citakaṃ katvā sugataṃ,
sarīraṃ abhiropayuṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7812Msdiv 124Sarīrakiccaṃ katvāna,
dhātuṃ tattha samānayuṃ;
Sadevamanussā sabbe,
buddhathūpaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7813Msdiv 125Paṭhamā kañcanamayā,
dutiyā ca maṇimayā;
Tatiyā rūpiyamayā,
catutthī phalikāmayā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7814Msdiv 126Tattha pañcamikā ceva,
lohitaṅkamayā ahu;
Chaṭṭhā masāragallassa,
sabbaṃ ratanamayūpari.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7815Msdiv 127Jaṅghā maṇimayā āsi,
vedikā ratanāmayā;
Sabbasoṇṇamayo thūpo,
uddhaṃ yojanamuggato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7816Msdiv 128Devā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7817Msdiv 129Dhātu āveṇikā natthi,
sarīraṃ ekapiṇḍitaṃ;
Imamhi buddhathūpamhi,
kassāma kañcukaṃ mayaṃ’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7818Msdiv 130Devā sattahi ratnehi,
aññaṃ vaḍḍhesuṃ yojanaṃ;
Thūpo dviyojanubbedho,
timiraṃ byapahanti so.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7819Msdiv 131Nāgā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manussā ceva devā ca,
buddhathūpaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7820Msdiv 132Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7821Msdiv 133Indanīlaṃ mahānīlaṃ,
atho jotirasaṃ maṇiṃ;
Ekato sannipātetvā,
buddhathūpaṃ achādayuṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7822Msdiv 134Sabbaṃ maṇimayaṃ āsi,
yāvatā buddhacetiyaṃ;
Tiyojanasamubbedhaṃ,
ālokakaraṇaṃ tadā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7823Msdiv 135Garuḷā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manussā devanāgā ca,
buddhapūjaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7824Msdiv 136Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7825Msdiv 137Sabbaṃ maṇimayaṃ thūpaṃ,
akaruṃ te ca kañcukaṃ;
Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7826Msdiv 138Catuyojanamubbedho,
buddhathūpo virocati;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
sataraṃsīva uggato.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7827Msdiv 139Kumbhaṇḍā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manussā ceva devā ca,
nāgā ca garuḷā tathā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7828Msdiv 140Paccekaṃ buddhaseṭṭhassa,
akaṃsu thūpamuttamaṃ;
Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7829Msdiv 141Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Ratanehi chādessāma,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ’.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7830Msdiv 142Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ;
Pañcayojanamubbedho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7831Msdiv 143Yakkhā tattha samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manussā devanāgā ca,
garuḷā ca kumbhaṇḍakā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7832Msdiv 144Paccekaṃ buddhaseṭṭhassa,
akaṃsu thūpamuttamaṃ;
Mā no pamattā assumha,
appamattā sadevakā.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7833Msdiv 145Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Phalikā chādayissāma,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7834Msdiv 146Yojanaṃ tepi vaḍḍhesuṃ,
āyataṃ buddhacetiyaṃ;
Chayojanikamubbedho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7835Msdiv 147Gandhabbā ca samāgantvā,
ekato mantayuṃ tadā;
‘Manujā devatā nāgā,
kumbhaṇḍā guyhakā tathā.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7836Msdiv 148Sabbe akaṃsu buddhathūpaṃ,
mayamettha akārakā;
Mayampi thūpaṃ kassāma,
lokanāthassa tādino’.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7837Msdiv 149Vediyo satta katvāna,
dhajaṃ chattaṃ akaṃsu te;
Sabbasoṇṇamayaṃ thūpaṃ,
gandhabbā kārayuṃ tadā.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7838Msdiv 150Sattayojanamubbedho,
thūpo obhāsate tadā;
Rattindivā na ñāyanti,
āloko hoti sabbadā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7839Msdiv 151Abhibhonti na tassābhā,
candasūrā satārakā;
Samantā yojanasate,
padīpopi na pajjali.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7840Msdiv 152Tena kālena ye keci,
thūpaṃ pūjenti mānusā;
Na te thūpaṃ āruhanti,
ambare ukkhipanti te.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7841Msdiv 153Devehi ṭhapito yakkho,
abhisammatanāmako;
Dhajaṃ vā pupphadāmaṃ vā,
abhiropeti uttariṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7842Msdiv 154Na te passanti taṃ yakkhaṃ,
dāmaṃ passanti gacchato;
Evaṃ passitvā gacchantā,
sabbe gacchanti suggatiṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7843Msdiv 155Viruddhā ye pāvacane,
pasannā ye ca sāsane;
Pāṭihīraṃ daṭṭhukāmā,
thūpaṃ pūjenti mānusā.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7844Msdiv 156Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ bhatako tadā;
Āmoditaṃ janaṃ disvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7845Msdiv 157‘Uḷāro bhagavā neso,
yassa dhātughare disaṃ;
Imā ca janatā tuṭṭhā,
kāraṃ kubbaṃ na tappare.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7846Msdiv 158Ahampi kāraṃ kassāmi,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
bhavissāmi anāgate’.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7847Msdiv 159Sudhotaṃ rajakenāhaṃ,
uttareyyaṃ paṭaṃ mama;
Veḷagge ālaggetvāna,
dhajaṃ ukkhipimambare.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7848Msdiv 160Abhisammatako gayha,
ambare hāsi me dhajaṃ;
Vāteritaṃ dhajaṃ disvā,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7849Msdiv 161Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
samaṇaṃ upasaṅkamiṃ;
Taṃ bhikkhuṃ abhivādetvā,
vipākaṃ pucchahaṃ dhaje.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_7850Msdiv 162So me kathesi ānandī,
pītisañjananaṃ mama;
‘Tassa dhajassa vipākaṃ,
anubhossasi sabbadā.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_7851Msdiv 163Hatthiassarathāpattī,
senā ca caturaṅginī;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_7852Msdiv 164Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
bheriyo samalaṅkatā;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_7853Msdiv 165Chaḷasītisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
āmukkamaṇikuṇḍalā.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_7854Msdiv 166Aḷārapamhā hasulā,
susaññā tanumajjhimā;
Parivāressanti taṃ niccaṃ,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_7855Msdiv 167Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissasi;
Asītikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissasi.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_7856Msdiv 168Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 48Ms 20Ap1_7857Msdiv 169Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 49Ms 20Ap1_7858Msdiv 170Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Puññakammena saññutto,
brahmabandhu bhavissasi.

SC 50Ms 20Ap1_7859Msdiv 171Asītikoṭiṃ chaḍḍetvā,
dāse kammakare bahū;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissasi.

SC 51Ms 20Ap1_7860Msdiv 172Ārādhayitvā sambuddhaṃ,
gotamaṃ sakyapuṅgavaṃ;
Upavānoti nāmena,
hessasi satthu sāvako’.

SC 52Ms 20Ap1_7861Msdiv 173Satasahasse kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha;
Sumutto saravegova,
kilese jhāpayiṃ mama.

SC 53Ms 20Ap1_7862Msdiv 174Cakkavattissa santassa,
cātuddīpissarassa me;
Tīṇi yojanāni sāmantā,
ussīyanti dhajā sadā.

SC 54Ms 20Ap1_7863Msdiv 175Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 55Ms 20Ap1_7864Msdiv 176Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 56Ms 20Ap1_7865Msdiv 177Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 57Ms 20Ap1_7866Msdiv 178Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 58Ms 20Ap1_7867Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upavānatthero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 59Ms 20Ap1_7868Upavānattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.
