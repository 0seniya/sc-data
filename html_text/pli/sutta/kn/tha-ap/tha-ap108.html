6. Udakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1890Msdiv 29“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ anilañjase;
Ghatāsanaṃva jalitaṃ,
Ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1891Msdiv 30Pāṇinā udakaṃ gayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Sampaṭicchi mahāvīro,
buddho kāruṇiko isi.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.143Ms 20Ap1_1892Msdiv 31Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
padumuttaranāmako;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1893Msdiv 32‘Iminā dakadānena,
pītiuppādanena ca;
Kappasatasahassampi,
duggatiṃ nupapajjati’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1894Msdiv 33Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1895Msdiv 34Sahassarājanāmena,
tayo te cakkavattino;
Pañcasaṭṭhikappasate,
cāturantā janādhipā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1896Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1897Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1898Udakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
