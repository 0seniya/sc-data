4. Gandhodakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2094Msdiv 35“Nisajja pāsādavare,
vipassiṃ addasaṃ jinaṃ;
Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
sabbaññuṃ tamanāsakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2095Msdiv 36Pāsādassāvidūre ca,
gacchati lokanāyako;
Pabhā niddhāvate tassa,
yathā ca sataraṃsino.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.158Ms 20Ap1_2096Msdiv 37Gandhodakañca paggayha,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ samokiriṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2097Msdiv 38Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ gandhodakamākiriṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2098Msdiv 39Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
sugandho nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2099Msdiv 40Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2100Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhodakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2101Gandhodakiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
