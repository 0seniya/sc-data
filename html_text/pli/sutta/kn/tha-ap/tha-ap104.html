2 Ñāṇasaññikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1860Msdiv 7“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Tidhāpabhinnaṃ mātaṅgaṃ,
Kuñjaraṃva mahesinaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1861Msdiv 8Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
uḷurājaṃva pūritaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ apassahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1862Msdiv 9Ñāṇe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
siddhatthamabhivādayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1863Msdiv 10Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1864Msdiv 11Tesattatimhito kappe,
soḷasāsuṃ naruttamā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.141Ms 20Ap1_1865Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1866Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ñāṇasaññiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1867Ñāṇasaññikattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
