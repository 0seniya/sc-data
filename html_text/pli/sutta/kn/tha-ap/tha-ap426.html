4. Sattakadambapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5536Msdiv 61“Himavantassāvidūre,
kukkuṭo nāma pabbato;
Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,
satta buddhā vasiṃsu te.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5537Msdiv 62Kadambaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Satta mālā gahetvāna,
puññacittena okiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5538Msdiv 63Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 2.383Ms 20Ap1_5539Msdiv 64Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5540Msdiv 65Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5541Msdiv 66Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5542Msdiv 67Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5543Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattakadambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5544Sattakadambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
