4. Ārakkhadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2923Msdiv 19“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
vedi kārāpitā mayā;
Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,
sugatassa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2924Msdiv 20Tena kammavisesena,
na passiṃ bhayabheravaṃ;
Kuhiñci upapannassa,
tāso mayhaṃ na vijjati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2925Msdiv 21Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ vediṃ kārayiṃ pure;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vedikāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2926Msdiv 22Ito chaṭṭhamhi kappamhi,
apassenasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2927Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2928Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2929Ārakkhadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
