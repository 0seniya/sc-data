10. Piṇḍapātikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4024SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4024Msdiv 37“Tisso nāmāsi sambuddho,
vihāsi vipine tadā;
Tusitā hi idhāgantvā,
piṇḍapātaṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4025SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4025Msdiv 38Sambuddhamabhivādetvā,
tissaṃ nāma mahāyasaṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
tusitaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4026SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4026Msdiv 39Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
piṇḍapātassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4027SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4027Msdiv 40Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3173)

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4028Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā piṇḍapātiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4029Piṇḍapātikattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4030SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4030Saddasaññakavaggo chattiṃsatimo.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4031Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4032Saddasaññī yavasiko,
kiṃsukoraṇḍapupphiyo;
Ālambano ambayāgu,
supuṭī mañcadāyako;
Saraṇaṃ piṇḍapāto ca,
gāthā tālīsameva ca.
