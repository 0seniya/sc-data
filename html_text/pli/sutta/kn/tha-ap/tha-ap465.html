3. Citakanibbāpakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5984Msdiv 11“Dayhamāne sarīramhi,
vessabhussa mahesino;
Gandhodakaṃ gahetvāna,
citaṃ nibbāpayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5985Msdiv 12Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
citaṃ nibbāpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
gandhodakassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5986Msdiv 13Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5987Msdiv 14Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5988Msdiv 15Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5989Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakanibbāpako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5990Citakanibbāpakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
