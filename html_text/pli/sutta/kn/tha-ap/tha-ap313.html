1. Ārakkhadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3506Msdiv 1“Dhammadassissa munino,
vati kārāpitā mayā;
Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3507Msdiv 2Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Tena kammavisesena,
patto me āsavakkhayo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3508Msdiv 3Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3509Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3510Ārakkhadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
