1. Citakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3358Msdiv 1“Ajito nāma nāmena,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;
Āhutiṃ yiṭṭhukāmohaṃ,
nānāpupphaṃ samānayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3359Msdiv 2Jalantaṃ citakaṃ disvā,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Tañca pupphaṃ samānetvā,
citake okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.244Ms 20Ap1_3360Msdiv 3Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3361Msdiv 4Sattavīse ito kappe,
sattāsuṃ manujādhipā;
Supajjalitanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3362Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3363Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3364Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
