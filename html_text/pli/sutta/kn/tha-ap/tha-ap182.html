10. Saṃghupaṭṭhākattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2572SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2572Msdiv 45“Vessabhumhi bhagavati,
ahosārāmiko ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
upaṭṭhiṃ saṃghamuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2573SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2573Msdiv 46Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2574SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2574Msdiv 47Ito te sattame kappe,
sattevāsuṃ samodakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2575SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2575Msdiv 48Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2146)

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2576Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saṃghupaṭṭhāko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2577Saṃghupaṭṭhākattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2578Kumudavaggo aṭṭhārasamo.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2579Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2580Kumudo atha nisseṇī,
rattiko udapānado;
Sīhāsanī maggadado,
ekadīpī maṇippado;
Tikicchako upaṭṭhāko,
ekapaññāsa gāthakāti.
