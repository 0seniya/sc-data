4 Viravapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3053Msdiv 14“Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
niyyāti lokanāyako;
Viravapupphamādāya,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3054Msdiv 15Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3055Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3056Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā viravapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3057Viravapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
