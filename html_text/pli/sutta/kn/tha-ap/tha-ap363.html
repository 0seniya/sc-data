1 Mandāravapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4033Msdiv 1“Tāvatiṃsā idhāgantvā,
maṅgalo nāma māṇavo;
Mandāravaṃ gahetvāna,
vipassissa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4034Msdiv 2Samādhinā nisinnassa,
matthake dhārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Sattāhaṃ dhārayitvāna,
devalokaṃ punāgamiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4035Msdiv 3Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4036Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4037Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mandāravapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4038Mandāravapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
