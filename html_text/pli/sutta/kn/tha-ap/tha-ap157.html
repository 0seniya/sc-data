5 Mandāravapupphapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2385Msdiv 25“Devaputto ahaṃ santo,
pūjayiṃ sikhināyakaṃ;
Mandāravena pupphena,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2386Msdiv 26Sattāhaṃ chadanaṃ āsi,
dibbaṃ mālaṃ tathāgate;
Sabbe janā samāgantvā,
namassiṃsu tathāgataṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2387Msdiv 27Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2388Msdiv 28Ito ca dasame kappe,
rājāhosiṃ jutindharo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2389Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2390Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mandāravapupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2391Mandāravapupphapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
