6 Anulepadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2313SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2313Msdiv 26“Anomadassīmunino,
bodhivedimakāsahaṃ;
Sudhāya piṇḍaṃ datvāna,
pāṇikammaṃ akāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2314SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2314Msdiv 27Disvā taṃ sukataṃ kammaṃ,
anomadassī naruttamo;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2315SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2315Msdiv 28‘Iminā sudhakammena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
dukkhassantaṃ karissati’.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2316SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2316Msdiv 29Pasannamukhavaṇṇomhi,
ekaggo susamāhito;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2317SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2317Msdiv 30Ito kappasate āsiṃ,
paripuṇṇe anūnake;
Rājā sabbaghano nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2318SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2318Msdiv 31Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2319Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2319Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anulepadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
