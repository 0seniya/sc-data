10. Anusaṃsāvakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3418Msdiv 43“Piṇḍāya caramānāhaṃ,
vipassimaddasaṃ jinaṃ;
Uḷuṅgabhikkhaṃ pādāsiṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3419Msdiv 44Pasannacitto sumano,
abhivādesahaṃ tadā;
Anusaṃsāvayiṃ buddhaṃ,
uttamatthassa pattiyā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3420Msdiv 45Ekanavutito kappe,
anusaṃsāvayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
anusaṃsāvanā phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3421Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2712)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3422Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anusaṃsāvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3423Anusaṃsāvakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Citakapūjakavaggo tiṃsatimo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3424Tassuddānaṃ

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3425Citakaṃ pārichatto ca,
saddagosīsasantharaṃ;
Pādo padesaṃ saraṇaṃ,
ambo saṃsāvakopi ca;
Aṭṭhatālīsa gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3426Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3427Kaṇikāro hatthidado,
ālambaṇudakāsanaṃ;
Tuvaraṃ thomako ceva,
ukkhepaṃ sīsupadhānaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3428Paṇṇado citapūjī ca,
gāthāyo ceva sabbaso;
Cattāri ca satānīha,
ekapaññāsameva ca.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3429Pañcavīsasatā sabbā,
dvāsattati taduttari;
Tisataṃ apadānānaṃ,
gaṇitā atthadassibhi.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3430Kaṇikāravaggadasakaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3431Tatiyasatakaṃ samattaṃ.
