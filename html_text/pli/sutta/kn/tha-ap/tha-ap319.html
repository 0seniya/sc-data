7. Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3551SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3551Msdiv 34“Sudassano mahāvīro,
deseti amataṃ padaṃ;
Parivuto sāvakehi,
vasati gharamuttame.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3552SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3552Msdiv 35Tāya vācāya madhurāya,
saṅgaṇhāti mahājanaṃ;
Ghoso ca vipulo āsi,
āsīso devamānuse.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3553SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3553Msdiv 36Nigghosasaddaṃ sutvāna,
siddhatthassa mahesino;
Sadde cittaṃ pasādetvā,
avandiṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3554SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3554Msdiv 37Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3555SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3555Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3556Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3557Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
