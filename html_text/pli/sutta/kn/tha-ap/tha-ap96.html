4. Sūcidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1789Msdiv 19“Kammārohaṃ pure āsiṃ,
bandhumāyaṃ puruttame;
Sūcidānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vipassissa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1790Msdiv 20Vajiraggasamaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
hoti kammena tādisaṃ;
Virāgomhi vimuttomhi,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.135Ms 20Ap1_1791Msdiv 21Atīte ca bhave sabbe,
vattamāne canāgate;
Ñāṇena viciniṃ sabbaṃ,
sūcidānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1792Msdiv 22Ekanavutito kappe,
sattāsuṃ vajiravhayā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1793Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1794Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sūcidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1795Sūcidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
