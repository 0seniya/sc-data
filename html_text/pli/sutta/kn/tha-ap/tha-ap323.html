1 Umāpupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3586SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3586Msdiv 1“Samāhitaṃ samāpannaṃ,
siddhatthamaparājitaṃ;
Samādhinā upaviṭṭhaṃ,
addasāhaṃ naruttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3587SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3587Msdiv 2Umāpupphaṃ gahetvāna,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;
Sabbapupphā ekasīsā,
uddhaṃvaṇṭā adhomukhā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3588SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3588Msdiv 3Sucittā viya tiṭṭhante,
ākāse pupphasantharā;
Tena cittappasādena,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3589SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3589Msdiv 4Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3590SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3590Msdiv 5Pañcapaññāsito kappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Samantachadano nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3591SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3591Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3592Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3592Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā umāpupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3593Umāpupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
