1 Bhāgineyyupālittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1209Msdiv 1“Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
parivuto lokanāyako;
Vivekamanuyutto so,
gacchate paṭisallituṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1210Msdiv 2Ajinena nivatthohaṃ,
tidaṇḍaparidhārako;
Bhikkhusaṃghaparibyūḷhaṃ,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.92Ms 20Ap1_1211Msdiv 3Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1212Msdiv 4Yathāṇḍajā ca saṃsedā,
opapātī jalābujā;
Kākādipakkhino sabbe,
antalikkhacarā sadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1213Msdiv 5‘Ye keci pāṇabhūtatthi,
saññino vā asaññino;
Sabbe te tava ñāṇamhi,
anto honti samogadhā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1214Msdiv 6Gandhā ca pabbateyyā ye,
himavantanaguttame;
Sabbe te tava sīlamhi,
kalāyapi na yujjare.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1215Msdiv 7Mohandhakārapakkhando,
ayaṃ loko sadevako;
Tava ñāṇamhi jotante,
andhakārā vidhaṃsitā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1216Msdiv 8Yathā atthaṅgate sūriye,
honti sattā tamogatā;
Evaṃ buddhe anuppanne,
hoti loko tamogato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1217Msdiv 9Yathodayanto ādicco,
vinodeti tamaṃ sadā;
Tatheva tvaṃ buddhaseṭṭha,
viddhaṃsesi tamaṃ sadā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1218Msdiv 10Padhānapahitattosi,
buddho loke sadevake;
Tava kammābhiraddhena,
tosesi janataṃ bahuṃ’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1219Msdiv 11Taṃ sabbaṃ anumoditvā,
padumuttaro mahāmuni;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī dhīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1220Msdiv 12Abbhuggantvāna sambuddho,
mahesi padumuttaro;
Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1221Msdiv 13Yenidaṃ thavitaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
opammehi samāyutaṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1222Msdiv 14‘Aṭṭhārasañca khattuṃ so,
devarājā bhavissati;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ tisataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1223Msdiv 15Pañcavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1224Msdiv 16Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1225Msdiv 17Tusitā hi cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Hīnova jātiyā santo,
upāli nāma hessati.

SC 18PTS vp Pali 1.93Ms 20Ap1_1226Msdiv 18So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
virājetvāna pāpakaṃ;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1227Msdiv 19Tuṭṭho ca gotamo buddho,
sakyaputto mahāyaso;
Vinayādhigataṃ tassa,
etadagge ṭhapessati’.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1228Msdiv 20Saddhāyāhaṃ pabbajito,
katakicco anāsavo;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_1229Msdiv 21Bhagavā cānukampī maṃ,
vinayehaṃ visārado;
Sakakammābhiraddho ca,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1230Msdiv 22Saṃvuto pātimokkhamhi,
indriyesu ca pañcasu;
Dhāremi vinayaṃ sabbaṃ,
kevalaṃ ratanākaraṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1231Msdiv 23Mamañca guṇamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_1232Msdiv 24Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_1233Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upāli thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_1234Bhāgineyyupālittherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
