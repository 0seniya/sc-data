7. Abhayattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7541Msdiv 195“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7542Msdiv 196Saraṇagamane kiñci,
nivesesi tathāgato;
Kiñci sīle nivesesi,
dasakammapathuttame.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7543Msdiv 197Deti kassaci so vīro,
sāmaññaphalamuttamaṃ;
Samāpattī tathā aṭṭha,
tisso vijjā pavecchati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7544Msdiv 198Chaḷabhiññāsu yojesi,
kiñci sattaṃ naruttamo;
Deti kassaci nātho so,
catasso paṭisambhidā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7545Msdiv 199Bodhaneyyaṃ pajaṃ disvā,
asaṅkheyyampi yojanaṃ;
Khaṇena upagantvāna,
vineti narasārathi.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7546Msdiv 200Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇatrajo;
Pāragū sabbavedānaṃ,
veyyākaraṇasammato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7547Msdiv 201Niruttiyā ca kusalo,
nighaṇḍumhi visārado;
Padako keṭubhavidū,
chandovicitikovido.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7548Msdiv 202Jaṅghāvihāraṃ vicaraṃ,
haṃsārāmamupeccahaṃ;
Addasaṃ varadaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
mahājanapurakkhataṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7549Msdiv 203Desentaṃ virajaṃ dhammaṃ,
paccanīkamatī ahaṃ;
Upetvā tassa kalyāṇaṃ,
sutvāna vimalaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7550Msdiv 204Byāhataṃ punaruttaṃ vā,
apatthaṃ vā niratthakaṃ;
Nāddasaṃ tassa munino,
tato pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7551Msdiv 205Nacireneva kālena,
sabbasattavisārado;
Nipuṇo buddhavacane,
ahosiṃ guṇisammato.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7552Msdiv 206Tadā catasso gāthāyo,
ganthayitvā subyañjanā;
Santhavitvā tilokaggaṃ,
desayissaṃ dine dine.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7553Msdiv 207‘Virattosi mahāvīro,
saṃsāre sabhaye vasaṃ;
Karuṇāya na nibbāyi,
tato kāruṇiko muni.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7554Msdiv 208Puthujjano vayo santo,
na kilesavaso ahu;
Sampajāno satiyutto,
tasmā eso acintiyo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7555Msdiv 209Dubbalāni kilesāni,
yassāsayagatāni me;
Ñāṇaggiparidaḍḍhāni,
na khīyiṃsu tamabbhutaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7556Msdiv 210Yo sabbalokassa garu,
loko yassa tathā garu;
Tathāpi lokācariyo,
loko tassānuvattako’.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7557Msdiv 211Evamādīhi sambuddhaṃ,
kittayaṃ dhammadesanaṃ;
Yāvajīvaṃ karitvāna,
gato saggaṃ tato cuto.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7558Msdiv 212Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhikittayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7559Msdiv 213Devaloke mahārajjaṃ,
pādesiṃ kañcanagghiyaṃ;
Cakkavattī mahārajjaṃ,
bahusonubhaviṃ ahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7560Msdiv 214Duve bhave pajāyāmi,
devatte atha mānuse;
Aññaṃ gatiṃ na jānāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7561Msdiv 215Duve kule pajāyāmi,
khattiye atha brāhmaṇe;
Nīce kule na jāyāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7562Msdiv 216Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
giribbajapuruttame;
Raññohaṃ bimbisārassa,
putto nāmena cābhayo.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7563Msdiv 217Pāpamittavasaṃ gantvā,
nigaṇṭhena vimohito;
Pesito nāṭaputtena,
buddhaseṭṭhamupeccahaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7564Msdiv 218Pucchitvā nipuṇaṃ pañhaṃ,
sutvā byākaraṇuttamaṃ;
Pabbajitvāna naciraṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7565Msdiv 219Kittayitvā jinavaraṃ,
kittito homi sabbadā;
Sugandhadehavadano,
āsiṃ sukhasamappito.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7566Msdiv 220Tikkhahāsalahupañño,
mahāpañño tathevahaṃ;
Vicittapaṭibhāno ca,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7567Msdiv 221Abhitthavitvā padumuttarāhaṃ,
Pasannacitto asamaṃ sayambhuṃ;
Na gacchi kappāni apāyabhūmiṃ,
Sataṃsahassāni balena tassa.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7568Msdiv 222Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7569Msdiv 223Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7570Msdiv 224Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7571Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā abhayo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7572Abhayattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
