3 Mahākaccānattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1107Msdiv 31“Padumuttaranāthassa,
padumaṃ nāma cetiyaṃ;
Silāsanaṃ kārayitvā,
suvaṇṇenābhilepayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1108Msdiv 32Ratanāmayachattañca,
paggayha vāḷabījaniṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
lokabandhussa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1109Msdiv 33Yāvatā devatā bhummā,
sabbe sannipatuṃ tadā;
Ratanāmayachattānaṃ,
vipākaṃ kathayissati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1110Msdiv 34Tañca sabbaṃ suṇissāma,
kathayantassa satthuno;
Bhiyyo hāsaṃ janeyyāma,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1111Msdiv 35Hemāsane nisīditvā,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Bhikkhusaṃghaparibyūḷho,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1112Msdiv 36‘Yenidaṃ āsanaṃ dinnaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ ratanāmayaṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1113Msdiv 37Tiṃsa kappāni devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
ābhāyābhibhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1114Msdiv 38Manussalokamāgantvā,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Pabhassaroti nāmena,
uggatejo bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1115Msdiv 39Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
sataraṃsīva uggato;
Samantā aṭṭharatanaṃ,
ujjotissati khattiyo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1116Msdiv 40Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1117Msdiv 41Tusitā hi cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Kaccāno nāma nāmena,
brahmabandhu bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1118Msdiv 42So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
arahā hessatināsavo;
Gotamo lokapajjoto,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapessati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1119Msdiv 43Saṃkhittapucchitaṃ pañhaṃ,
Vitthārena kathessati;
Kathayanto ca taṃ pañhaṃ,
Ajjhāsayaṃ pūrayissati’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1120Msdiv 44Aḍḍhe kule abhijāto,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Ohāya dhanadhaññāni,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1121Msdiv 45Saṃkhittenapi pucchante,
vitthārena kathemahaṃ;
Ajjhāsayaṃ tesaṃ pūremi,
tosemi dvipaduttamaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1122Msdiv 46Tosito me mahāvīro,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1123Msdiv 47Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1124Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahākaccāno thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1125Mahākaccānattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
