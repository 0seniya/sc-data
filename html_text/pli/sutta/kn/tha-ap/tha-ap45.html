3 Kāḷigodhāputtabhaddiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1266SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1266Msdiv 54“Padumuttarasambuddhaṃ,
mettacittaṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Upeti janatā sabbā,
sabbalokagganāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1267Msdiv 55SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1267Msdiv 55Sattukañca baddhakañca,
āmisaṃ pānabhojanaṃ;
Dadanti satthuno sabbe,
puññakkhette anuttare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1268SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1268Msdiv 56Ahampi dānaṃ dassāmi,
devadevassa tādino;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ nimantetvā,
saṃghampi ca anuttaraṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1269SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1269Msdiv 57Uyyojitā mayā cete,
nimantesuṃ tathāgataṃ;
Kevalaṃ bhikkhusaṃghañca,
puññakkhettaṃ anuttaraṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1270SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1270Msdiv 58Satasahassapallaṅkaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ gonakatthataṃ;
Tūlikāpaṭalikāya,
khomakappāsikehi ca;
Mahārahaṃ paññāpayiṃ,
āsanaṃ buddhayuttakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1271SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1271Msdiv 59Padumuttaro lokavidū,
devadevo narāsabho;
Bhikkhusaṃghaparibyūḷho,
mama dvāramupāgami.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1272SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1272Msdiv 60Paccuggantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
lokanāthaṃ yasassinaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
abhināmayiṃ saṃgharaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1273SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1273Msdiv 61Bhikkhūnaṃ satasahassaṃ,
buddhañca lokanāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
paramannena tappayiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1274SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1274Msdiv 62Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1275SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1275Msdiv 63‘Yenidaṃ āsanaṃ dinnaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ gonakatthataṃ;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1276SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1276Msdiv 64Catusattatikkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Anubhossati sampattiṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1277SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1277Msdiv 65Padesarajjaṃ sahassaṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati;
Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1278SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1278Msdiv 66Sabbāsu bhavayonīsu,
uccākulī bhavissati;
So ca pacchā pabbajitvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Bhaddiyo nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1279SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1279Msdiv 67Vivekamanuyuttomhi,
pantasenanivāsahaṃ;
Phalañcādhigataṃ sabbaṃ,
cattaklesomhi ajjahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1280Msdiv 68Mama SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1280Msdiv 68Mama sabbaṃ abhiññāya,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1281SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1281Msdiv 69Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1282Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhaddiyo kāḷigodhāya putto thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1283Bhaddiyassa kāḷigodhāya puttattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
