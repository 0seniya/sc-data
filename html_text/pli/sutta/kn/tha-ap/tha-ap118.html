6. Citakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1999Msdiv 49“Vasāmi rājāyatane,
sāmacco saparijjano;
Parinibbute bhagavati,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2000Msdiv 50Pasannacitto sumano,
citakaṃ agamāsahaṃ;
Tūriyaṃ tattha vādetvā,
gandhamālaṃ samokiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2001Msdiv 51Citamhi pūjaṃ katvāna,
vanditvā citakaṃ ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sakaṃ bhavanupāgamiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2002Msdiv 52Bhavane upaviṭṭhohaṃ,
citapūjaṃ anussariṃ;
Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2003Msdiv 53Anubhotvāna sampattiṃ,
devesu mānusesu ca;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2004Msdiv 54Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2005Msdiv 55Ekūnatiṃsakappamhi,
ito soḷasa rājāno;
Uggatā nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2006Msdiv 56Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2007Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2008Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
