10. Reṇupūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1931Msdiv 62“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Sataraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
Uḷurājaṃva pūritaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1932Msdiv 63Purakkhataṃ sāvakehi,
sāgareheva medaniṃ;
Nāgaṃ paggayha reṇūhi,
vipassissābhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1933Msdiv 64Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ reṇumabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1934Msdiv 65Paṇṇatālīsito kappe,
reṇu nāmāsi khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1935Msdiv 66Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1668)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1936Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā reṇupūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1937Reṇupūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1938Bhikkhadāyivaggo ekādasamo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1939Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1940Bhikkhadāyī ñāṇasaññī,
hatthiyo padapūjako;
Muṭṭhipupphī udakado,
naḷamāli upaṭṭhako;
Biḷālidāyī reṇu ca,
gāthāyo cha ca saṭṭhi ca.
