6 Sampasādakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3465Msdiv 24“‘Namo te buddha vīratthu,
vippamuttosi sabbadhi;
Byasanamhi anuppatto,
tassa me saraṇaṃ bhava’.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3466Msdiv 25Siddhattho tassa byākāsi,
loke appaṭipuggalo;
‘Mahodadhisamo saṃgho,
appameyyo anuttaro.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3467Msdiv 26Tattha tvaṃ viraje khette,
anantaphaladāyake;
Saṃghe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
subījaṃ vāpa ropaya’.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3468Msdiv 27Idaṃ vatvāna sabbaññū,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Mameva anusāsitvā,
vehāsaṃ nabhamuggami.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3469Msdiv 28Aciraṃ gatamattamhi,
sabbaññumhi narāsabhe;
Maraṇaṃ samanuppatto,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3470Msdiv 29Tadāhaṃ viraje khette,
anantaphaladāyake;
Saṃghe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3471Msdiv 30Catunnavutito kappe,
pasādamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pasādassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3472Msdiv 31Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3473Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sampasādako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3474Sampasādakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
