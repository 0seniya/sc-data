9. Vaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2336SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2336Msdiv 43“Sumedho nāma nāmena,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekamanubrūhanto,
ajjhogāhi mahāvanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2337SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2337Msdiv 44Saḷalaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
ganthitvāna vaṭaṃsakaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sammukhā lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2338SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2338Msdiv 45Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2339SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2339Msdiv 46Ūnavīse kappasate,
soḷasāsuṃ sunimmitā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2340SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2340Msdiv 47Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2341Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vaṭaṃsakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2342Vaṭaṃsakiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
