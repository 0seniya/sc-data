8 Muṭṭhipūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2721SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2721Msdiv 42“Sumedho nāma bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Pacchime anukampāya,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2722SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2722Msdiv 43Tassa caṅkamamānassa,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Girinelassa pupphānaṃ,
muṭṭhiṃ buddhassa ropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2723SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2723Msdiv 44Tena cittappasādena,
sukkamūlena codito;
Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2724SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2724Msdiv 45Tevīsatikappasate,
sunelo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
eko āsiṃ mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2725SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2725Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2726Itthaṃ SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2726Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā muṭṭhipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
