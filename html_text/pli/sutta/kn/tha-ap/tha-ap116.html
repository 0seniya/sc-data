4. Ekāsaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1977Msdiv 31“Varuṇo nāma nāmena,
devarājā ahaṃ tadā;
Upaṭṭhahesiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sayoggabalavāhano.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1978Msdiv 32Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
atthadassīnaruttame;
Tūriyaṃ sabbamādāya,
agamaṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1979Msdiv 33Vāditena ca naccena,
sammatāḷasamāhito;
Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
upaṭṭhiṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1980Msdiv 34Upaṭṭhahitvā taṃ bodhiṃ,
dharaṇīruhapādapaṃ;
Pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1981Msdiv 35Sakakammābhiraddhohaṃ,
pasanno bodhimuttame;
Tena cittappasādena,
nimmānaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1982Msdiv 36Saṭṭhitūriyasahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ sadā;
Manussesu ca devesu,
vattamānaṃ bhavābhave.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1983Msdiv 37Tividhaggī nibbutā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.150Ms 20Ap1_1984Msdiv 38Subāhū nāma nāmena,
catuttiṃsāsu khattiyā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
pañcakappasate ito.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1985Msdiv 39Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1986Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekāsaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1987Ekāsaniyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
