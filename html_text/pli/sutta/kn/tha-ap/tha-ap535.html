ratanānaṃva sāgaro;
Pajjunnoriva bhūtāni,
dhammameghena vassati.

nagare haṃsasavhaye;

jalajuttamanāmino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7022SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7022Msdiv 71Ovādakassa bhikkhūnaṃ,
sāvakassa katāvino;

tappayantassa me manaṃ.

nimantetvā tathāgataṃ;
Sasissaṃ bhojayitvāna,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ.

haṃsadundubhinissano;
‘Passathetaṃ mahāmattaṃ,
vinicchayavisāradaṃ.

samuggatatanūruhaṃ;

pasannanayanānanaṃ.

rājayuttaṃ mahāyasaṃ;

pattheti muditāsayo.

cāgena paṇidhīhi ca;
Kappasatasahassāni,
nupapajjati duggatiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7028SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7028Msdiv 77Devesu devasobhaggaṃ,
manussesu mahantataṃ;
Anubhotvāna sesena,
nibbānaṃ pāpuṇissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7029SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7029Msdiv 78Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7030SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7030Msdiv 79Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Kappino nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7031SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7031Msdiv 80Tatohaṃ sukataṃ kāraṃ,
katvāna jinasāsane;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tusitaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7032SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7032Msdiv 81Devamānusarajjāni,
sataso anusāsiya;
Bārāṇasiyamāsanne,
jāto keniyajātiyaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7033Msdiv 82SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7033Msdiv 82Sahassaparivārena,
sapajāpatiko ahaṃ;
Pañca paccekabuddhānaṃ,
satāni samupaṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7034SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7034Msdiv 83Temāsaṃ bhojayitvāna,
pacchādamha ticīvaraṃ;
Tato cutā mayaṃ sabbe,
ahumha tidasūpagā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7035SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7035Msdiv 84Puno sabbe manussattaṃ,
agamimha tato cutā;
Kukkuṭamhi pure jātā,
himavantassa passato.

Sesāmaccakule jātā,
mameva parivārayuṃ.

Vāṇijehi samakkhātaṃ,
buddhuppādamahaṃ suṇiṃ.

asamo ekapuggalo;
So pakāseti saddhammaṃ,
amataṃ sukhamuttamaṃ.

sumuttā ca anāsavā’;

sakkaritvāna vāṇije.

nikkhamiṃ buddhamāmako;

pūritaṃ samatittikaṃ.

Guṇaṃ saritvā buddhassa,
sotthinā samatikkamiṃ.

gamanaṃ me samijjhatu.

gamanaṃ me samijjhatu’.
