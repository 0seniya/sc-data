6 Vaṇṇakārattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3005SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3005Msdiv 23“Nagare aruṇavatiyā,
vaṇṇakāro ahaṃ tadā;
Cetiye dussabhaṇḍāni,
nānāvaṇṇaṃ rajesahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3006SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3006Msdiv 24Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ vaṇṇaṃ rajayiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vaṇṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3007SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3007Msdiv 25Ito tevīsatikappe,
vaṇṇasamasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3008SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3008Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3009Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3009Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vaṇṇakāro thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
