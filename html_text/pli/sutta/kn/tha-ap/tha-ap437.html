5. Ucchukhaṇḍikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5715Msdiv 44“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
dvārapālo ahosahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5716Msdiv 45Ucchukhaṇḍikamādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vipassissa mahesino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5717Msdiv 46Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ ucchumadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ucchukhaṇḍassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5718Msdiv 47Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5719Msdiv 48Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5720Msdiv 49Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5721Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ucchukhaṇḍiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5722Ucchukhaṇḍikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
