8. Paṇṇadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3135Msdiv 28“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vākacīradharo ahaṃ;
Aloṇapaṇṇabhakkhomhi,
niyamesu ca saṃvuto.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3136Msdiv 29Pātarāse anuppatte,
siddhattho upagacchi maṃ;
Tāhaṃ buddhassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.229Ms 20Ap1_3137Msdiv 30Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ paṇṇamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paṇṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3138Msdiv 31Sattavīsatikappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ sadatthiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3139Msdiv 32Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3140Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṇṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3141Paṇṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
