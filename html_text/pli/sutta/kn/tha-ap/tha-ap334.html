2. Udakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3723Msdiv 7“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ anilañjase;
Ghatāsanaṃva jalitaṃ,
Ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3724Msdiv 8Pāṇinā udakaṃ gayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;
Sampaṭicchi mahāvīro,
buddho kāruṇiko isi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3725Msdiv 9Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
padumuttaranāmako;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
imaṃ gāthamabhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3726Msdiv 10‘Iminā dakadānena,
pītiuppādanena ca;
Kappasatasahassampi,
duggatiṃ nupapajjati’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3727Msdiv 11Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3728Msdiv 12Sahassarājanāmena,
tayo ca cakkavattino;
Pañcasaṭṭhikappasate,
cāturantā janādhipā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3729Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3730Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3731Udakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
