7. Ekachattiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5927SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5927Msdiv 37“Aṅgārajātā pathavī,
kukkuḷānugatā mahī;
Padumuttaro bhagavā,
abbhokāsamhi caṅkami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5928SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5928Msdiv 38Paṇḍaraṃ chattamādāya,
addhānaṃ paṭipajjahaṃ;
Tattha disvāna sambuddhaṃ,
vitti me upapajjatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5929Msdiv 39SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5929Msdiv 39Marīciyotthaṭā bhūmi,
aṅgārāva mahī ayaṃ;
Upahanti mahāvātā,
sarīrassāsukhepanā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5930Msdiv 40Sītaṃ uṇhaṃ SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5930Msdiv 40Sītaṃ uṇhaṃ vihanantaṃ,
vātātapanivāraṇaṃ;
Paṭiggaṇha imaṃ chattaṃ,
phassayissāmi nibbutiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5931SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5931Msdiv 41Anukampako kāruṇiko,
padumuttaro mahāyaso;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
paṭiggaṇhi tadā jino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5932SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5932Msdiv 42Tiṃsa kappāni devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5933SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5933Msdiv 43Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5934SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5934Msdiv 44Ayaṃ me pacchimā jāti,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Ajjāpi setacchattaṃ me,
sabbakālaṃ dharīyati.

yaṃ chattamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
chattadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.
