8. Pādapīṭhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5838Msdiv 50“Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Tārayitvā bahū satte,
nibbuto so mahāyaso.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5839Msdiv 51Sīhāsanassa sāmantā,
sumedhassa mahesino;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pādapīṭhamakārayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5840Msdiv 52Katvāna kusalaṃ kammaṃ,
sukhapākaṃ sukhudrayaṃ;
Puññakammena saṃyutto,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5841Msdiv 53Tattha me vasamānassa,
puññakammasamaṅgino;
Padāni uddharantassa,
soṇṇapīṭhā bhavanti me.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5842Msdiv 54Lābhā tesaṃ suladdhaṃ vo,
ye labhanti upassutiṃ;
Nibbute kāraṃ katvāna,
labhanti vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5843Msdiv 55Mayāpi sukataṃ kammaṃ,
vāṇijjaṃ suppayojitaṃ;
Pādapīṭhaṃ karitvāna,
soṇṇapīṭhaṃ labhāmahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5844Msdiv 56Yaṃ yaṃ disaṃ pakkamāmi,
kenaci kiccayenahaṃ;
Soṇṇapīṭhe akkamāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5845Msdiv 57Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pādapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5846Msdiv 58Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5847Msdiv 59Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5848Msdiv 60Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5849Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pādapīṭhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5850Pādapīṭhiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
