7. Nigguṇḍipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3636SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3636Msdiv 39“Yadā devo devakāyā,
cavate āyusaṅkhayā;
Tayo saddā niccharanti,
devānaṃ anumodataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3637SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3637Msdiv 40‘Ito bho sugatiṃ gaccha,
manussānaṃ sahabyataṃ;
Manussabhūto saddhamme,
labha saddhaṃ anuttaraṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3638SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3638Msdiv 41Sā te saddhā niviṭṭhāssa,
mūlajātā patiṭṭhitā;
Yāvajīvaṃ asaṃhīrā,
saddhamme suppavedite.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3639SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3639Msdiv 42Kāyena kusalaṃ katvā,
vācāya kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Manasā kusalaṃ katvā,
abyāpajjaṃ nirūpadhiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3640SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3640Msdiv 43Tato opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
katvā dānena taṃ bahuṃ;
Aññepi macce saddhamme,
brahmacariye nivesaya’.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3641SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3641Msdiv 44Imāya anukampāya,
devādevaṃ yadā vidū;
Cavantaṃ anumodanti,
ehi deva punappunaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3642Msdiv 45‘SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3642Msdiv 45‘Saṃvego me tadā āsi,
devasaṅghe samāgate;
Kaṃsu nāma ahaṃ yoniṃ,
gamissāmi ito cuto’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3643SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3643Msdiv 46Mama saṃvegamaññāya,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3644SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3644Msdiv 47Sumano nāma nāmena,
padumuttarasāvako;
Atthadhammānusāsitvā,
saṃvejesi mamaṃ tadā.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3645Dvādasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3646SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3646Msdiv 48Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
buddhe cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Taṃ dhīraṃ abhivādetvā,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3647Msdiv 49SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3647Msdiv 49Upapajjiṃ sa tattheva,
sukkamūlena codito;
Vasanto mātukucchimhi,
puna dhāreti mātuyā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3648SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3648Msdiv 50Tamhā kāyā cavitvāna,
tidase upapajjahaṃ;
Etthantare na passāmi,
domanassamahaṃ tadā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3649SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3649Msdiv 51Tāvatiṃsā cavitvāna,
mātukucchiṃ samokkamiṃ;
Nikkhamitvāna kucchimhā,
kaṇhasukkaṃ ajānahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3650Msdiv 52Jātiyā SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3650Msdiv 52Jātiyā sattavassova,
ārāmaṃ pāvisiṃ ahaṃ;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sakyaputtassa tādino.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3651Msdiv 53SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3651Msdiv 53Vitthārike pāvacane,
bāhujaññamhi sāsane;
Addasaṃ sāsanakare,
bhikkhavo tattha satthuno.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3652SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3652Msdiv 54Sāvatthi nāma nagaraṃ,
rājā tatthāsi kosalo;
Rathena nāgayuttena,
upesi bodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3653SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3653Msdiv 55Tassāhaṃ nāgaṃ disvāna,
pubbakammaṃ anussariṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
samayaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3654SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3654Msdiv 56Jātiyā sattavassova,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Yo so buddhaṃ upaṭṭhāsi,
ānando nāma sāvako.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3655SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3655Msdiv 57Gatimā dhitimā ceva,
satimā ca bahussuto;
Rañño cittaṃ pasādento,
niyyādesi mahājuti.

pubbakammaṃ anussariṃ;
Tattheva ṭhitako santo,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_3657SC 22Ms 20Ap1_3657Msdiv 59Ekaṃsaṃ cīvaraṃ katvā,
sire katvāna añjaliṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
imaṃ vācaṃ udīrayiṃ.

dvipadindassa satthuno;
Nigguṇḍipupphaṃ paggayha,
sīhāsane ṭhapesahaṃ.

hitvā jayaparājayaṃ’.

aṭṭhaṭṭhāsiṃsu khattiyā.
