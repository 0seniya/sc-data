10. Aggapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3148Msdiv 37“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Obhāsayantaṃ raṃsena,
Sikhinaṃ sikhinaṃ yathā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3149Msdiv 38Aggajaṃ pupphamādāya,
upāgacchiṃ naruttamaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3150Msdiv 39Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3151Msdiv 40Pañcavīsatikappamhi,
ahosi amitogato;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 1.230Ms 20Ap1_3152Msdiv 41Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2535)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3153Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aggapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3154Aggapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3155Thomakavaggo chabbīsatimo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3156Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3157Thomakekāsanacitakaṃ,
campako sattapāṭali;
Pānadhi mañjarī paṇṇaṃ,
kuṭido aggapupphiyo;
Gāthāyo gaṇitā cettha,
ekatālīsameva cāti.
