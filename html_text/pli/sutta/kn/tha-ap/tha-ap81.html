9. Ṭhitañjaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1646SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1646Msdiv 42“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
Araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Tattha addasaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1647SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1647Msdiv 43Tatthāhaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
pakkāmiṃ pācināmukho;
Avidūre nisinnassa,
niyake paṇṇasanthare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1648SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1648Msdiv 44Tato me asanīpāto,
matthake nipatī tadā;
Sohaṃ maraṇakālamhi,
akāsiṃ punarañjaliṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1649SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1649Msdiv 45Dvenavute ito kappe,
añjaliṃ akariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
añjalissa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1650SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1650Msdiv 46Catupaṇṇāsakappamhi,
migaketusanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1651SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1651Msdiv 47Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1652Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ṭhitañjaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1653Ṭhitañjaliyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
