3. Aḍḍhacandiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3171SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3171Msdiv 10“Tissassa kho bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Aḍḍhacandaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dharaṇīruhapādape.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3172SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3172Msdiv 11Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ candamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3173SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3173Msdiv 12Pañcavīse ito kappe,
devalo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3174SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3174Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3175Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aḍḍhacandiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3176Aḍḍhacandiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
