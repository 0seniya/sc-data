3. Nandattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_726SC 1Ms 20Ap1_726Msdiv 27“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Vatthaṃ khomaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
Sayambhussa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_727SC 2Ms 20Ap1_727Msdiv 28Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
jalajuttaranāmako;
‘Iminā vatthadānena,
hemavaṇṇo bhavissasi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_728SC 3Ms 20Ap1_728Msdiv 29Dve sampattī anubhotvā,
kusalamūlehi codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
kaniṭṭho tvaṃ bhavissasi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_729SC 4Ms 20Ap1_729Msdiv 30Rāgaratto sukhasīlo,
kāmesu gedhamāyuto;
Buddhena codito santo,
tadā tvaṃ pabbajissasi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_730SC 5Ms 20Ap1_730Msdiv 31Pabbajitvāna tvaṃ tattha,
kusalamūlena codito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissasināsavo’.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_731Msdiv 32SC 6Ms 20Ap1_731Msdiv 32Satta kappasahassamhi,
caturo ceḷanāmakā;
Saṭṭhi kappasahassamhi,
upacelā catujjanā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_732SC 7Ms 20Ap1_732Msdiv 33Pañca kappasahassamhi,
ceḷāva caturo janā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
catudīpamhi issarā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_733SC 8Ms 20Ap1_733Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_734Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nando thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_735Nandattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
