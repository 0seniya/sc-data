1. Vidhūpanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1376SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1376Msdiv 1“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Bījanikā mayā dinnā,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1377SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1377Msdiv 2Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Sambuddhamabhivādetvā,
pakkamiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1378SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1378Msdiv 3Bījaniṃ paggahetvāna,
satthā lokagganāyako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito santo,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1379SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1379Msdiv 4‘Iminā bījanidānena,
cittassa paṇidhīhi ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
vinipātaṃ na gacchati’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1380SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1380Msdiv 5Āraddhavīriyo pahitatto,
Cetoguṇasamāhito;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
Arahattaṃ apāpuṇiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1381SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1381Msdiv 6Saṭṭhikappasahassamhi,
bījamānasanāmakā;
Soḷasāsiṃsu rājāno,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1382SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1382Msdiv 7Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1383Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vidhūpanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1384Vidhūpanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
