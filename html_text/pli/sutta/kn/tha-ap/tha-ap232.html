10 Ekavandaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2964SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2964Msdiv 48“Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ vīraṃ,
vessabhuṃ vijitāvinaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhaseṭṭhamavandahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2965SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2965Msdiv 49Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2966SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2966Msdiv 50Catuvīsatikappamhi,
vikatānandanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2967SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2967Msdiv 51Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2419)

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2968Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekavandaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2969Ekavandaniyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2970SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2970Ālambaṇadāyakavaggo tevīsatimo.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2971Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2972Ālambaṇañca ajinaṃ,
maṃsadārakkhadāyako;
Abyādhi aṅkolaṃ soṇṇaṃ,
miñjaāveḷavandanaṃ;
Pañcapaññāsa gāthāyo,
gaṇitā atthadassibhi.
