7. Padumapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3936Msdiv 44“Himavantassāvidūre,
romaso nāma pabbato;
Buddhopi sambhavo nāma,
abbhokāse vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3937Msdiv 45Bhavanā nikkhamitvāna,
padumaṃ dhārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ dhārayitvāna,
puna bhavanupāgamiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3938Msdiv 46Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3939Msdiv 47Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3940Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3941Padumapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
Terasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
