4 Bhallātadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5802SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5802Msdiv 22“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ;
Vipinaggena gacchantaṃ,
Sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5803SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5803Msdiv 23Tiṇattharaṃ paññāpetvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ ayācahaṃ;
‘Anukampatu maṃ buddho,
bhikkhaṃ icchāmi dātave’.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5804SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5804Msdiv 24Anukampako kāruṇiko,
atthadassī mahāyaso;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
orūhi mama assame.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5805SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5805Msdiv 25Orohitvāna sambuddho,
nisīdi paṇṇasanthare;
Bhallātakaṃ gahetvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5806SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5806Msdiv 26Mama nijjhāyamānassa,
paribhuñji tadā jino;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
abhivandiṃ tadā jinaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5807SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5807Msdiv 27Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5808SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5808Msdiv 28Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5809SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5809Msdiv 29Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5810SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5810Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5811Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5811Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhallātadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
