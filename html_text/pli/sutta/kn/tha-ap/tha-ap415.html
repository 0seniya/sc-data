3 Tiṇasūlakachādaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5331Msdiv 79“Jātiṃ jarañca maraṇaṃ,
paccavekkhiṃ ahaṃ tadā;
Ekako abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5332Msdiv 80Caramānonupubbena,
gaṅgātīraṃ upāgamiṃ;
Tatthaddasāsiṃ pathaviṃ,
gaṅgātīre samunnataṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5333Msdiv 81Assamaṃ tattha māpetvā,
vasāmi assame ahaṃ;
Sukato caṅkamo mayhaṃ,
nānādijagaṇāyuto.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5334Msdiv 82Mamupenti ca vissatthā,
kūjanti ca manoharaṃ;
Ramamāno saha tehi,
vasāmi assame ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5335Msdiv 83Mama assamasāmantā,
migarājā catukkamo;
Āsayā abhinikkhamma,
gajji so asanī viya.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5336Msdiv 84Nadite migarāje ca,
hāso me udapajjatha;
Migarājaṃ gavesanto,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5337Msdiv 85Disvānāhaṃ devadevaṃ,
tissaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pūjayiṃ nāgakesaraṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5338Msdiv 86Uggacchantaṃva sūriyaṃ,
sālarājaṃva pupphitaṃ;
Osadhiṃva virocantaṃ,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5339Msdiv 87‘Tava ñāṇena sabbaññu,
mocesimaṃ sadevakaṃ;
Tavaṃ ārādhayitvāna,
jātiyā parimuccare.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5340Msdiv 88Adassanena sabbaññu,
buddhānaṃ sabbadassinaṃ;
Patantivīcinirayaṃ,
rāgadosehi ophuṭā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5341Msdiv 89Tava dassanamāgamma,
sabbaññu lokanāyaka;
Pamuccanti bhavā sabbā,
phusanti amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5342Msdiv 90Yadā buddhā cakkhumanto,
uppajjanti pabhaṅkarā;
Kilese jhāpayitvāna,
ālokaṃ dassayanti te’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5343Msdiv 91Kittayitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
tissaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
tiṇasūlaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5344Msdiv 92Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Sakāsane nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5345Msdiv 93‘Yo maṃ pupphehi chādesi,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5346Msdiv 94Pañcavīsatikkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5347Msdiv 95Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Tassa kammanissandena,
pupphānaṃ pūjanāya ca.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5348Msdiv 96Sīsaṃnhāto cayaṃ poso,
pupphamākaṅkhate yadi;
Puññakammena saṃyuttaṃ,
purato pātubhavissati.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5349Msdiv 97Yaṃ yaṃ icchati kāmehi,
taṃ taṃ pātubhavissati;
Saṅkappaṃ paripūretvā,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5350Aṭṭhārasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5351Msdiv 98Kilese jhāpayitvāna,
sampajāno patissato;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_5352Msdiv 99Caṅkamanto nipajjanto,
nisinno uda vā ṭhito;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
viharāmi ahaṃ sadā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5353Msdiv 100Cīvare piṇḍapāte ca,
paccaye sayanāsane;
Tattha me ūnatā natthi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_5354Msdiv 101So dāni patto amataṃ,
santaṃ padamanuttaraṃ;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_5355Msdiv 102Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_5356Msdiv 103Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_5357Msdiv 104Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_5358Msdiv 105Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_5359Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇasūlakachādaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_5360Tiṇasūlakachādaniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
