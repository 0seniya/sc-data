3 Candanapūjanakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2202Msdiv 17“Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Pupphabhakkho cahaṃ āsiṃ,
pupphānivasano tathā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2203Msdiv 18Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Vipinaggena niyyāsi,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2204Msdiv 19Namo te purisājañña,
cittaṃ te suvisodhitaṃ;
Pasannamukhavaṇṇosi,
vippasannamukhindriyo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2205Msdiv 20Orohitvāna ākāsā,
bhūripañño sumedhaso;
Saṅghāṭiṃ pattharitvāna,
pallaṅkena upāvisi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2206Msdiv 21Vilīnaṃ candanādāya,
agamāsiṃ jinantikaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2207Msdiv 22Abhivādetvāna sambuddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pāmojjaṃ janayitvāna,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2208Msdiv 23Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
candanaṃ yaṃ apūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2209Msdiv 24Catuddase kappasate,
ito āsiṃsu te tayo;
Rohaṇī nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2210Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2211Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā candanapūjanako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2212Candanapūjanakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
Aṭṭhamabhāṇavāraṃ.
