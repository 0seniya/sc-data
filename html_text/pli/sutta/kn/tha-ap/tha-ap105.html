3. Uppalahatthiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1868SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1868Msdiv 13“Tivarāyaṃ nivāsīhaṃ,
ahosiṃ māliko tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
siddhatthaṃ lokapūjitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1869SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1869Msdiv 14Pasannacitto sumano,
pupphahatthamadāsahaṃ;
Yattha yatthupapajjāmi,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1870SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1870Msdiv 15Anubhomi phalaṃ iṭṭhaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano;
Parikkhitto sumallehi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1871SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1871Msdiv 16Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1872SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1872Msdiv 17Catunnavutupādāya,
ṭhapetvā vattamānakaṃ;
Pañcarājasatā tattha,
najjasamasanāmakā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1873SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1873Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1874Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uppalahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1875Uppalahatthiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
