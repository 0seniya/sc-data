1. Tiṇamuṭṭhidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6798Msdiv 1“Himavantassāvidūre,
lambako nāma pabbato;
Tattheva tisso sambuddho,
abbhokāsamhi caṅkami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6799Msdiv 2Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Disvāna taṃ devadevaṃ,
tiṇamuṭṭhimadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6800Msdiv 3Nisīdanatthaṃ buddhassa,
datvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6801Msdiv 4Aciraṃ gatamattassa,
migarājā apothayi;
Sīhena pothito santo,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6802Msdiv 5Āsanne me kataṃ kammaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhe anāsave;
Sumutto saravegova,
devalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6803Msdiv 6Yūpo tattha subho āsi,
puññakammābhinimmito;
Sahassakaṇḍo satabheṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6804Msdiv 7Pabhā niddhāvate tassa,
sataraṃsīva uggato;
Ākiṇṇo devakaññāhi,
āmodiṃ kāmakāmihaṃ.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 2.455Ms 20Ap1_6805Msdiv 8Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Āgantvāna manussattaṃ,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6806Msdiv 9Catunnavutito kappe,
nisīdanamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇamuṭṭhe idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6807Msdiv 10Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6808Msdiv 11Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6809Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6810Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇamuṭṭhidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6811Tiṇamuṭṭhidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
