4 Cūḷapanthakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_736SC 1Ms 20Ap1_736Msdiv 35 “Padumuttaro nāma jino,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Gaṇamhā vūpakaṭṭho so,
himavante vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_737SC 2Ms 20Ap1_737Msdiv 36Ahampi himavantamhi,
vasāmi assame tadā;
Acirāgataṃ mahāvīraṃ,
upesiṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_738SC 3Ms 20Ap1_738Msdiv 37Pupphacchattaṃ gahetvāna,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Samādhiṃ samāpajjantaṃ,
antarāyamakāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_739SC 4Ms 20Ap1_739Msdiv 38Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
pupphacchattaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
padumuttaro mahāmuni.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_740SC 5Ms 20Ap1_740Msdiv 39Sabbe devā attamanā,
himavantaṃ upenti te;
Sādhukāraṃ pavattesuṃ,
anumodissati cakkhumā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_741SC 6Ms 20Ap1_741Msdiv 40Idaṃ vatvāna te devā,
upagacchuṃ naruttamaṃ;
Ākāse dhārayantassa,
padumacchattamuttamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_742SC 7Ms 20Ap1_742Msdiv 41Satapattachattaṃ paggayha,
adāsi tāpaso mama;
‘Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_743SC 8Ms 20Ap1_743Msdiv 42Pañcavīsatikappāni,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Catuttiṃsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_744SC 9Ms 20Ap1_744Msdiv 43Yaṃ yaṃ yoniṃ saṃsarati,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Abbhokāse patiṭṭhantaṃ,
padumaṃ dhārayissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_745SC 10Ms 20Ap1_745Msdiv 44Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_746SC 11Ms 20Ap1_746Msdiv 45Pakāsite pāvacane,
manussattaṃ labhissati;
Manomayamhi kāyamhi,
uttamo so bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_747SC 12Ms 20Ap1_747Msdiv 46Dve bhātaro bhavissanti,
ubhopi panthakavhayā;
Anubhotvā uttamatthaṃ,
jotayissanti sāsanaṃ’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_748Msdiv 47SC 13Ms 20Ap1_748Msdiv 47Sohaṃ aṭṭhārasavasso,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Visesāhaṃ na vindāmi,
sakyaputtassa sāsane.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_749SC 14Ms 20Ap1_749Msdiv 48Dandhā mayhaṃ gatī āsi,
paribhūto pure ahuṃ;
Bhātā ca maṃ paṇāmesi,
gaccha dāni sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_750SC 15Ms 20Ap1_750Msdiv 49Sohaṃ paṇāmito santo,
saṃghārāmassa koṭṭhake;
Dummano tattha aṭṭhāsiṃ,
sāmaññasmiṃ apekkhavā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_751Msdiv 50SC 16Ms 20Ap1_751Msdiv 50Bhagavā tattha āgacchi,
sīsaṃ mayhaṃ parāmasi;
Bāhāya maṃ gahetvāna,
saṃghārāmaṃ pavesayi.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_752SC 17Ms 20Ap1_752Msdiv 51Anukampāya me satthā,
adāsi pādapuñchaniṃ;
Evaṃ suddhaṃ adhiṭṭhehi,
ekamantamadhiṭṭhahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_753SC 18Ms 20Ap1_753Msdiv 52Hatthehi tamahaṃ gayha,
sariṃ kokanadaṃ ahaṃ;
Tattha cittaṃ vimucci me,
arahattaṃ apāpuṇiṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_754SC 19Ms 20Ap1_754Msdiv 53Manomayesu kāyesu,
sabbattha pāramiṃ gato;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_755SC 20Ms 20Ap1_755Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
