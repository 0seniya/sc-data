10. Ekañjaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3961Msdiv 63“Romaso nāma sambuddho,
nadīkūle vasī tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3962Msdiv 64Ukkāmukhapahaṭṭhaṃva,
khadiraṅgārasannibhaṃ;
Osadhiṃva virocantaṃ,
ekañjalimakāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3963Msdiv 65Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ añjalimakāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
añjaliyā idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3964Msdiv 66Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3133)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3965Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekañjaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3966Ekañjaliyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3967Ekapadumiyavaggo pañcatiṃsatimo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3968Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3969Padumī uppalamālī,
dhajo kiṅkaṇikaṃ naḷaṃ;
Campako padumo muṭṭhi,
tindukekañjalī tathā;
Cha ca saṭṭhi ca gāthāyo,
gaṇitāyo vibhāvibhi.
