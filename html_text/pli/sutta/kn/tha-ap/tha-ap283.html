1. Paṇṇadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3287Msdiv 1“Paṇṇasāle nisinnomhi,
paṇṇabhojanabhojano;
Upaviṭṭhañca maṃ santaṃ,
upāgacchi mahāisi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3288Msdiv 2Siddhattho lokapajjoto,
sabbalokatikicchako;
Tassa paṇṇaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
nisinnaṃ paṇṇasanthare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3289Msdiv 3Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ paṇṇamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paṇṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3290Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3291Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṇṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3292Paṇṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
