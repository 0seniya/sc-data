2. Labujadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4185Msdiv 7“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
āsiṃ ārāmiko tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4186Msdiv 8Labujassa phalaṃ gayha,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Ākāse ṭhitako santo,
paṭigaṇhi mahāyaso.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4187Msdiv 9Vittisañjananaṃ mayhaṃ,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvahaṃ;
Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4188Msdiv 10Adhigacchiṃ tadā pītiṃ,
vipulañca sukhuttamaṃ;
Uppajjateva ratanaṃ,
nibbattassa tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4189Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4190Msdiv 12Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4191Msdiv 13Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4192Msdiv 14Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4193Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā labujadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4194Labujadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
