9 Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2728Msdiv 47“Sumaṅgaloti nāmena,
sayambhū aparājito;
Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,
nagaraṃ pāvisī jino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2729Msdiv 48Piṇḍacāraṃ caritvāna,
nikkhamma nagarā muni;
Katakiccova sambuddho,
so vasī vanamantare.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2730Msdiv 49Kiṃkaṇipupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sayambhussa mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2731Msdiv 50Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2732Msdiv 51Chaḷāsītimhito kappe,
apilāsisanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2733Msdiv 52Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2734Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃkaṇikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2735Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
