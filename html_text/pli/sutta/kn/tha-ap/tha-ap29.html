7 Pulinapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1036SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1036Msdiv 165“Vipassissa bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Purāṇapulinaṃ Purāṇapulinaṃ hitvā,
suddhapulinamākiriṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1037SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1037Msdiv 166Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pulinamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pulinadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1038Msdiv 167SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1038Msdiv 167Tiṃsatime ito kappe,
rājā āsiṃ janādhibhū;
Mahāpulinanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1039SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1039Msdiv 168Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1040Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1040Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pulinapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1041Pulinapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
