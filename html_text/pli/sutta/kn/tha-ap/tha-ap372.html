10 Saḷalapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4085SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4085Msdiv 35“Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Tatthaddasaṃ devadevaṃ,
caṅkamantaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4086SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4086Msdiv 36Ocinitvāna saḷalaṃ,
pupphaṃ buddhassadāsahaṃ;
Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,
saḷalaṃ devagandhikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4087SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4087Msdiv 37Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
vipassī lokanāyako;
Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,
pekkhamānassa me sato.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4088SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4088Msdiv 38Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvā dvipaduttamaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
puna pabbatamāruhiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4089SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4089Msdiv 39Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4090SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4090Msdiv 40Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3213)

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4091Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4091Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saḷalapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4093Mandāravapupphiyavaggo sattatiṃsatimo.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4094Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4095Mandāravañca kakkāru,

Aṅkolako kadambī ca,
uddālī ekacampako;
Timiraṃ saḷalañceva,
gāthā tālīsameva ca.
