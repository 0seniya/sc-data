1. Sīhāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_696Msdiv 1“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
siddhatthe dvipaduttame;
Vitthārike pāvacane,
bāhujaññamhi sāsane.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_697Msdiv 2Pasannacitto sumano,
sīhāsanamakāsahaṃ;
Sīhāsanaṃ karitvāna,
pādapīṭhamakāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_698Msdiv 3Sīhāsane ca vassante,
gharaṃ tattha akāsahaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_699Msdiv 4Āyāmena catubbīsa-
yojanaṃ āsi tāvade;
Vimānaṃ sukataṃ mayhaṃ,
vitthārena catuddasa.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_700Msdiv 5Sataṃ kaññāsahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ sadā;
Soṇṇamayañca pallaṅkaṃ,
byamhe āsi sunimmitaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_701Msdiv 6Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
dibbayānaṃ upaṭṭhitaṃ;
Pāsādā sivikā ceva,
nibbattanti yadicchakaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_702Msdiv 7Maṇimayā ca pallaṅkā,
aññe sāramayā bahū;
Nibbattanti mamaṃ sabbe,
sīhāsanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_703Msdiv 8Soṇṇamayā rūpimayā,
phalikāveḷuriyāmayā;
Pādukā abhirūhāmi,
pādapīṭhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_704Msdiv 9Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_705Msdiv 10Tesattatimhito kappe,
indanāmā tayo janā;
Dvesattatimhito kappe,
tayo sumananāmakā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_706Msdiv 11Samasattatito kappe,
tayo varuṇanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
catudīpamhi issarā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_707Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_708Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sīhāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_709Sīhāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
