9. Bodhisiñcakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1747SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1747Msdiv 46“Vipassissa bhagavato,
mahābodhimaho ahu;
Pabbajjupagato santo,
upagacchiṃ ahaṃ tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1748SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1748Msdiv 47Kusumodakamādāya,
bodhiyā okiriṃ ahaṃ;
Mocayissati no mutto,
nibbāpessati nibbuto.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1749SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1749Msdiv 48Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bodhimabhisiñcayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhisiñcāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1750SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1750Msdiv 49Tettiṃse vattamānamhi,
kappe āsuṃ janādhipā;
Udakasecanā nāma,
aṭṭhete cakkavattino.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1751SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1751Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1752Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhisiñcako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1753Bodhisiñcakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
