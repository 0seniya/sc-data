6. Uttarattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7735Msdiv 55“Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Vivekakāmo bhagavā,
himavantamupāgami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7736Msdiv 56Ajjhogāhetvā himavantaṃ,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvāna,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7737Msdiv 57Vijjādharo tadā āsiṃ,
antalikkhacaro ahaṃ;
Tisūlaṃ sukataṃ gayha,
gacchāmi ambare tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7738Msdiv 58Pabbatagge yathā aggi,
puṇṇamāyeva candimā;
Vanaṃ obhāsate buddho,
sālarājāva phullito.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7739Msdiv 59Vanaggā nikkhamitvāna,
buddharaṃsībhidhāvare;
Naḷaggivaṇṇasaṅkāsā,
disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7740Msdiv 60Vicinaṃ addasaṃ pupphaṃ,
kaṇikāraṃ devagandhikaṃ;
Tīṇi pupphāni ādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhamapūjayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7741Msdiv 61Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
tīṇi pupphāni me tadā;
Uddhaṃ vaṇṭā adhopattā,
chāyaṃ kubbanti satthuno.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7742Msdiv 62Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7743Msdiv 63Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
kaṇikārīti ñāyati;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbedhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7744Msdiv 64Sahassakaṇḍaṃ satabheṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayaṃ;
Satasahassaniyyūhā,
byamhe pātubhaviṃsu me.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7745Msdiv 65Soṇṇamayā maṇimayā,
lohitaṅkamayāpi ca;
Phalikāpi ca pallaṅkā,
yenicchakā yadicchakā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7746Msdiv 66Mahārahañca sayanaṃ,
tūlikā vikatīyutaṃ;
Uddhalomiñca ekantaṃ,
bimbohanasamāyutaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7747Msdiv 67Bhavanā nikkhamitvāna,
caranto devacārikaṃ;
Yathā icchāmi gamanaṃ,
devasaṅghapurakkhato.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7748Msdiv 68Pupphassa heṭṭhā tiṭṭhāmi,
uparicchadanaṃ mama;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
kaṇikārehi chāditaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7749Msdiv 69Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
sāyapātaṃ upaṭṭhahuṃ;
Parivārenti maṃ niccaṃ,
rattindivamatanditā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7750Msdiv 70Tattha naccehi gītehi,
tālehi vāditehi ca;
Ramāmi khiḍḍā ratiyā,
modāmi kāmakāmahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7751Msdiv 71Tattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
modāmi tidase tadā;
Nārīgaṇehi sahito,
modāmi byamhamuttame.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7752Msdiv 72Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7753Msdiv 73Bhave bhave saṃsaranto,
mahābhogaṃ labhāmahaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7754Msdiv 74Duve bhave saṃsarāmi,
devatte atha mānuse;
Aññaṃ gatiṃ na jānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7755Msdiv 75Duve kule pajāyāmi,
khattiye cāpi brāhmaṇe;
Nīce kule na jānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7756Msdiv 76Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
sivikaṃ sandamānikaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7757Msdiv 77Dāsīgaṇaṃ dāsagaṇaṃ,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7758Msdiv 78Koseyyakambaliyāni,
khomakappāsikāni ca;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7759Msdiv 79Navavatthaṃ navaphalaṃ,
navaggarasabhojanaṃ;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7760Msdiv 80Imaṃ khāda imaṃ bhuñja,
imamhi sayane saya;
Labhāmi sabbamevetaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7761Msdiv 81Sabbattha pūjito homi,
yaso accuggato mama;
Mahāpakkho sadā homi,
abhejjapariso sadā;
Ñātīnaṃ uttamo homi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7762Msdiv 82Sītaṃ uṇhaṃ na jānāmi,
pariḷāho na vijjati;
Atho cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ,
hadaye me na vijjati.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7763Msdiv 83Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo hutvāna,
saṃsarāmi bhavābhave;
Vevaṇṇiyaṃ na jānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7764Msdiv 84Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Sāvatthiyaṃ pure jāto,
mahāsālesu aḍḍhake.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7765Msdiv 85Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7766Msdiv 86Upasampadāyī buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā;
Taruṇo pūjanīyohaṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7767Msdiv 87Dibbacakkhuvisuddhaṃ me,
samādhikusalo ahaṃ;
Abhiññāpāramippatto,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7768Msdiv 88Paṭisambhidā anuppatto,
iddhipādesu kovido;
Dhammesu pāramippatto,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7769Msdiv 89Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7770Msdiv 90Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7771Msdiv 91Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7772Msdiv 92Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7773Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttaro thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7774Uttarattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
