7. Dhātupūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3068Msdiv 23“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
siddhatthamhi naruttame;
Ekā dhātu mayā laddhā,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3069Msdiv 24Tāhaṃ dhātuṃ gahetvāna,
buddhassādiccabandhuno;
Pañcavasse paricariṃ,
tiṭṭhantaṃva naruttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3070Msdiv 25Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dhātuṃ pūjayiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhātupaṭṭhahane phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3071Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3072Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhātupūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3073Dhātupūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
