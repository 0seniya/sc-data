1. Ekapadumiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3881Msdiv 1“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Bhavābhave vibhāvento,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3882Msdiv 2Haṃsarājā tadā homi,
dijānaṃ pavaro ahaṃ;
Jātassaraṃ samogayha,
kīḷāmi haṃsakīḷitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3883Msdiv 3Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Jātassarassa upari,
āgacchi tāvade jino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3884Msdiv 4Disvānahaṃ devadevaṃ,
sayambhuṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Vaṇṭe chetvāna padumaṃ,
satapattaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3885Msdiv 5Mukhatuṇḍena paggayha,
pasanno lokanāyake;
Ukkhipitvāna gagane,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3886Msdiv 6Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3887Msdiv 7‘Iminā ekapadumena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
vinipātaṃ na gacchasi’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3888Msdiv 8Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Mama kammaṃ pakittetvā,
agamā yena patthitaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3889Msdiv 9Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3890Msdiv 10Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3891Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekapadumiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3892Ekapadumiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
