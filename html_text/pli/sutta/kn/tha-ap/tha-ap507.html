5 Pādaphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6638Msdiv 63“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Pādaphalaṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6639Msdiv 64Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6640Msdiv 65Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6641Msdiv 66Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6642Msdiv 67Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6643Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pādaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6644Pādaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
