11. Khadiravaniyarevatattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_652Msdiv 628“Gaṅgā bhāgīrathī nāma,
himavantā pabhāvitā;
Kutitthe nāviko āsiṃ,
orime ca tariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_653Msdiv 629Padumuttaro nāyako,
sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Vasī satasahassehi,
gaṅgātīramupāgato.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_654Msdiv 630Bahū nāvā samānetvā,
vaḍḍhakīhi susaṅkhataṃ;
Nāvāya chadanaṃ katvā,
paṭimāniṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_655Msdiv 631Āgantvāna ca sambuddho,
ārūhi tañca nāvakaṃ;
Vārimajjhe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_656Msdiv 632‘Yo so tāresi sambuddhaṃ,
saṃghañcāpi anāsavaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
devaloke ramissati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_657Msdiv 633Nibbattissati te byamhaṃ,
sukataṃ nāvasaṇṭhitaṃ;
Ākāse pupphachadanaṃ,
dhārayissati sabbadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_658Msdiv 634Aṭṭhapaññāsakappamhi,
tārako nāma khattiyo;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.52Ms 20Ap1_659Msdiv 635Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
cammako nāma khattiyo;
Uggacchantova sūriyo,
jotissati mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_660Msdiv 636Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_661Msdiv 637Tidasā so cavitvāna,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Revato nāma nāmena,
brahmabandhu bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_662Msdiv 638Agārā nikkhamitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_663Msdiv 639So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
yuttayogo vipassako;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_664Msdiv 640Vīriyaṃ me dhuradhorayhaṃ,
Yogakkhemādhivāhanaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
Sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_665Msdiv 641Satasahasse kataṃ kammaṃ,
phalaṃ dassesi me idha;
Sumutto saravegova,
kilese jhāpayī mama.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_666Msdiv 642Tato maṃ vananirataṃ,
disvā lokantagū muni;
Vanavāsibhikkhūnaggaṃ,
paññapesi mahāmati.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_667Msdiv 643Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_668Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā khadiravaniyo revato thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_669Khadiravaniyarevatattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
