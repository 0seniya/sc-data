6. Kusaṭṭhakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6131Msdiv 56“Kassapassa bhagavato,
brāhmaṇassa vusīmato;
Pasannacitto sumano,
kusaṭṭhakamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6132Msdiv 57Imasmiṃyeva kappasmiṃ,
kusaṭṭhakamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kusaṭṭhakassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6133Msdiv 58Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6134Msdiv 59Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6135Msdiv 60Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6136Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kusaṭṭhakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6137Kusaṭṭhakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
