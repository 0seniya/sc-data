1 Suvaṇṇabibbohaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3223Msdiv 1“Ekāsanaṃ ahamadaṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Bibbohanañca pādāsiṃ,
uttamatthassa pattiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3224Msdiv 2Ekanavutito kappe,
bibbohanamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bibbohanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3225Msdiv 3Ito tesaṭṭhime kappe,
asamo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3226Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3227Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā suvaṇṇabibbohaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3228Suvaṇṇabibbohaniyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
