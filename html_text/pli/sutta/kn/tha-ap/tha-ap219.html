7. Tālavaṇṭadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2864Msdiv 31“Tālavaṇṭaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
tissassādiccabandhuno;
Gimhanibbāpanatthāya,
pariḷāhopasantiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2865Msdiv 32Sannibbāpemi rāgaggiṃ,
dosaggiñca taduttariṃ;
Nibbāpemi ca mohaggiṃ,
tālavaṇṭassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2866Msdiv 33Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2867Msdiv 34Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tālavaṇṭassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2868Msdiv 35Tesaṭṭhimhi ito kappe,
mahānāmasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2869Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2870Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tālavaṇṭadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2871Tālavaṇṭadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
