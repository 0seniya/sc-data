9. Dhammacakkikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1190SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1190Msdiv 102“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
sīhāsanassa sammukhā;
Dhammacakkaṃ me ṭhapitaṃ,
sukataṃ viññuvaṇṇitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1191SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1191Msdiv 103Cāruvaṇṇova sobhāmi,
sayoggabalavāhano;
Parivārenti maṃ niccaṃ,
anuyantā bahujjanā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1192SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1192Msdiv 104Saṭṭhitūriyasahassehi,
paricāremahaṃ sadā;
Parivārena sobhāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1193SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1193Msdiv 105Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ cakkaṃ ṭhapayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhammacakkassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1194SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1194Msdiv 106Ito ekādase kappe,
aṭṭhāsiṃsu janādhipā;
Sahassarājanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1195SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1195Msdiv 107Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1196Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhammacakkiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1197Dhammacakkikattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
