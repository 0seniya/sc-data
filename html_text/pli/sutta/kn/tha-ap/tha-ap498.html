6. Ghatamaṇḍadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6480SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6480Msdiv 41“Sucintitaṃ bhagavantaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Upaviṭṭhaṃ mahāraññaṃ,
vātābādhena pīḷitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6481SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6481Msdiv 42Disvā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
ghatamaṇḍamupānayiṃ;
Katattā ācitattā ca,
gaṅgā bhāgīrathī ayaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6482SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6482Msdiv 43Mahāsamuddā cattāro,
ghataṃ sampajjare mama;
Ayañca pathavī ghorā,
appamāṇā asaṅkhiyā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6483SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6483Msdiv 44Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
bhavate madhusakkarā;
Cātuddīpā ime rukkhā,
pādapā dharaṇīruhā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6484SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6484Msdiv 45Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
kapparukkhā bhavanti te;
Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6485SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6485Msdiv 46Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6486Msdiv 47SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6486Msdiv 47Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ghatamaṇḍassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6487SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6487Msdiv 48Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6488SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6488Msdiv 49Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6489SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6489Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6490Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ghatamaṇḍadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
