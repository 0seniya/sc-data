9. Rādhattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7259Msdiv 296“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbalokavidū muni;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji cakkhumā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7260Msdiv 297Ovādako viññāpako,
tārako sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Desanākusalo buddho,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7261Msdiv 298Anukampako kāruṇiko,
hitesī sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Sampatte titthiye sabbe,
pañcasīle patiṭṭhapi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7262Msdiv 299Evaṃ nirākulaṃ āsi,
suññataṃ titthiyehi ca;
Vicittaṃ arahantehi,
vasībhūtehi tādibhi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7263Msdiv 300Ratanānaṭṭhapaññāsaṃ,
uggato so mahāmuni;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7264Msdiv 301Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7265Msdiv 302Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū;
Upecca taṃ naravaraṃ,
assosiṃ dhammadesanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7266Msdiv 303Paññāpentaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
parisāsu visāradaṃ;
Paṭibhāneyyakaṃ bhikkhuṃ,
etadagge vināyakaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7267Msdiv 304Tadāhaṃ kāraṃ katvāna,
sasaṃghe lokanāyake;
Nipacca sirasā pāde,
taṃ ṭhānaṃ abhipatthayiṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7268Msdiv 305Tato maṃ bhagavā āha,
siṅgīnikkhasamappabho;
Sarena rajanīyena,
kilesamalahārinā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7269Msdiv 306‘Sukhī bhavassu dīghāyu,
sijjhatu paṇidhī tava;
Sasaṃghe me kataṃ kāraṃ,
atīva vipulaṃ tayā.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 2.485Ms 20Ap1_7270Msdiv 307Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7271Msdiv 308Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Rādhoti nāmadheyyena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7272Msdiv 309Sa te hetuguṇe tuṭṭho,
sakyaputto narāsabho;
Paṭibhāneyyakānaggaṃ,
paññapessati nāyako’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7273Msdiv 310Taṃ sutvā mudito hutvā,
yāvajīvaṃ tadā jinaṃ;
Mettacitto paricariṃ,
sato paññāsamāhito.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7274Msdiv 311Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7275Msdiv 312Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7276Msdiv 313Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Sabbattha sukhito āsiṃ,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7277Msdiv 314Pacchime bhave sampatte,
giribbajapuruttame;
Jāto vippakule niddhe,
vikalacchādanāsane.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7278Msdiv 315Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ pādāsiṃ,
sāriputtassa tādino;
Yadā jiṇṇo ca vuddho ca,
tadārāmamupāgamiṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7279Msdiv 316Pabbajati na maṃ koci,
jiṇṇadubbalathāmakaṃ;
Tena dīno vivaṇṇaṅgo,
soko cāsiṃ tadā ahaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7280Msdiv 317Disvā mahākāruṇiko,
mamamāha mahāmuni;
‘Kimatthaṃ puttasokaṭṭo,
brūhi te cittajaṃ rujaṃ’.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7281Msdiv 318‘Pabbajjaṃ na labhe vīra,
svākkhāte tava sāsane;
Tena sokena dīnosmi,
saraṇaṃ hohi nāyaka’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7282Msdiv 319Tadā bhikkhū samānetvā,
apucchi munisattamo;
‘Imassa adhikāraṃ ye,
saranti byāharantu te’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7283Msdiv 320Sāriputto tadāvoca,
‘kāramassa sarāmahaṃ;
Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ dāpesi,
piṇḍāya carato mama’.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7284Msdiv 321‘Sādhu sādhu kataññūsi,
sāriputta imaṃ tuvaṃ;
Pabbājehi dijaṃ vuḍḍhaṃ,
hessatājāniyo ayaṃ’.

SC 27PTS vp Pali 2.486Ms 20Ap1_7285Msdiv 322Tato alatthaṃ pabbajjaṃ,
kammavācopasampadaṃ;
Na cireneva kālena,
pāpuṇiṃ āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7286Msdiv 323Sakkaccaṃ munino vākyaṃ,
suṇāmi mudito yato;
Paṭibhāneyyakānaggaṃ,
tato maṃ ṭhapayī jino.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7287Msdiv 324Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7288Msdiv 325Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7289Msdiv 326Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7290Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā rādho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7291Rādhattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
