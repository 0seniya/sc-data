10. Ajitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4800Msdiv 484“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Ajjhogāhetvā himavantaṃ,
nisīdi lokanāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4801Msdiv 485Nāhaṃ addakkhiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Napi saddaṃ suṇomahaṃ;
Mama bhakkhaṃ gavesanto,
Āhiṇḍāmi vane ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4802Msdiv 486Tatthaddassāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ;
Disvāna vittimāpajjiṃ,
satto ko nāmayaṃ bhave.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4803Msdiv 487Lakkhaṇāni viloketvā,
mama vijjaṃ anussariṃ;
Sutañhi metaṃ vuḍḍhānaṃ,
paṇḍitānaṃ subhāsitaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4804Msdiv 488Tesaṃ yathā taṃ vacanaṃ,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati;
Yannūnāhaṃ sakkareyyaṃ,
gatiṃ me sodhayissati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4805Msdiv 489Khippaṃ assamamāgantvā,
madhutelaṃ gahiṃ ahaṃ;
Kolambakaṃ gahetvāna,
upagacchiṃ vināyakaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4806Msdiv 490Tidaṇḍake gahetvāna,
abbhokāse ṭhapesahaṃ;
Padīpaṃ pajjalitvāna,
aṭṭhakkhattuṃ avandahaṃ.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.336Ms 20Ap1_4807Msdiv 491Sattarattindivaṃ buddho,
nisīdi purisuttamo;
Tato ratyā vivasāne,
vuṭṭhāsi lokanāyako.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4808Msdiv 492Pasannacitto sumano,
sabbarattindivaṃ ahaṃ;
Dīpaṃ buddhassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4809Msdiv 493Sabbe vanā gandhamayā,
Pabbate gandhamādane;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
Āgacchuṃ buddhasantikaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4810Msdiv 494Ye keci pupphagandhāse,
pupphitā dharaṇīruhā;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
sabbe sannipatuṃ tadā.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4811Msdiv 495Yāvatā himavantamhi,
nāgā ca garuḷā ubho;
Dhammañca sotukāmā te,
āgacchuṃ buddhasantikaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4812Msdiv 496Devalo nāma samaṇo,
buddhassa aggasāvako;
Vasīsatasahassehi,
buddhasantikupāgami.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4813Msdiv 497Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4814Msdiv 498‘Yo me dīpaṃ padīpesi,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4815Msdiv 499Saṭṭhi kappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati;
Sahassakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4816Soḷasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4817Msdiv 500Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Pathaviyaṃ sattasataṃ,
vipulaṃ rajjaṃ karissati.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4818Msdiv 501Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Iminā dīpadānena,
dibbacakkhu bhavissati.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4819Msdiv 502Samantato aṭṭhakosaṃ,
passissati ayaṃ sadā;
Devalokā cavantassa,
nibbattantassa jantuno.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4820Msdiv 503Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
padīpaṃ dhārayissati;
Jāyamānassa sattassa,
puññakammasamaṅgino.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4821Msdiv 504Yāvatā nagaraṃ āsi,
tāvatā jotayissati;
Upapajjati yaṃ yoniṃ,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4822Msdiv 505Asseva dīpadānassa,
aṭṭhadīpaphalena hi;
Na jayissantimaṃ jantū,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24PTS vp Pali 1.337Ms 20Ap1_4823Msdiv 506Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4824Msdiv 507Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4825Msdiv 508Tosayitvāna sambuddhaṃ,
gotamaṃ sakyapuṅgavaṃ;
Ajito nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_4826Msdiv 509Saṭṭhi kappasahassāni,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Tatrāpi me dīpasataṃ,
jotate niccakālikaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4827Msdiv 510Devaloke manusse vā,
niddhāvanti pabhā mama;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4828Msdiv 511Tusitāhaṃ cavitvāna,
okkamiṃ mātukucchiyaṃ;
Jāyamānassa santassa,
āloko vipulo ahu.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_4829Msdiv 512Agārā abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Bāvariṃ upasaṅkamma,
sissattaṃ ajjhupāgamiṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_4830Msdiv 513Himavante vasantohaṃ,
assosiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Uttamatthaṃ gavesanto,
upagacchiṃ vināyakaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_4831Msdiv 514Danto buddho dametāvī,
oghatiṇṇo nirūpadhi;
Nibbānaṃ kathayī buddho,
sabbadukkhappamocanaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_4832Msdiv 515Taṃ me āgamanaṃ siddhaṃ,
tositohaṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_4833Msdiv 516Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dīpamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_4834Msdiv 517Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_4835Msdiv 518Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_4836Msdiv 519Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3886)

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_4837Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ajito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_4838Ajitattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Pilindavacchavaggo cattālīsamo.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_4839Tassuddānaṃ

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_4840Pilindavaccho selo ca,
sabbakittī madhundado;
Kūṭāgārī bākulo ca,
giri saḷalasavhayo.

SC 42PTS vp Pali 1.338Ms 20Ap1_4841Sabbado ajito ceva,
gāthāyo gaṇitā iha;
Satāni pañca gāthānaṃ,
vīsati ca taduttarīti.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_4842Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_4843Padumārakkhado ceva,
umā gandhodakena ca;
Ekapadma saddasaññī,
mandāraṃ bodhivandako.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_4844Avaṭañca pilindi ca,
gāthāyo gaṇitā iha;
Catusattati gāthāyo,
ekādasa satāni ca.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_4845Padumavaggadasakaṃ.

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_4846Catutthasatakaṃ samattaṃ.
