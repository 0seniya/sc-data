6. Mātuluṅgaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6645Msdiv 68“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;
Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6646Msdiv 69Mātuluṅgaphalaṃ gayha,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;
Dakkhiṇeyyassa vīrassa,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6647Msdiv 70Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6648Msdiv 71Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6649Msdiv 72Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6650Msdiv 73Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6651Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mātuluṅgaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6652Mātuluṅgaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
