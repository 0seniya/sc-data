10. Khomadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1061Msdiv 184“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
ahosiṃ vāṇijo tadā;
Teneva dāraṃ posemi,
ropemi bījasampadaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1062Msdiv 185Rathiyaṃ paṭipannassa,
vipassissa mahesino;
Ekaṃ khomaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
kusalatthāya satthuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1063Msdiv 186Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ khomamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
khomadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1064Msdiv 187Sattarase ito kappe,
eko sindhavasandhano;
Sattaratanasampanno,
catudīpamhi issaro.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1065Msdiv 188Paṭisambhidā catasso,
Vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
Kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (991)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1066Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā khomadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1067Khomadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1068Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1069Subhūti upavāno ca,
saraṇo sīlagāhako;
Annasaṃsāvako dhūpo,
pulino uttiyena ca.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1070Añjalī khomadāyī ca,
daseva tatiye gaṇe;
Pañcāsītisataṃ vuttā,
gāthāyo sabbapiṇḍitā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1071Subhūtivaggo tatiyo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1072Catutthabhāṇavāraṃ.
