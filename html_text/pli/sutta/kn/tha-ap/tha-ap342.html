10. Ukkhittapadumiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3861Msdiv 129“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ māliko tadā;
Ogāhetvā padumasaraṃ,
satapattaṃ ocināmahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3862Msdiv 130Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Saha satasahassehi,
santacittehi tādibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3863Msdiv 131Khīṇāsavehi suddhehi,
chaḷabhiññehi jhāyibhi;
Mama vuddhiṃ samanvesaṃ,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3864Msdiv 132Disvānahaṃ devadevaṃ,
sayambhuṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Vaṇṭe chetvā satapattaṃ,
ukkhipimambare tadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3865Msdiv 133Yadi buddho tuvaṃ vīra,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Sayaṃ gantvā satapattā,
matthake dhārayantu te.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3866Msdiv 134Adhiṭṭhahi mahāvīro,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
matthake dhārayiṃsu te.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3867Msdiv 135Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3868Msdiv 136Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
satapattanti vuccati;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbiddhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3869Msdiv 137Sahassakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3870Msdiv 138Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3871Msdiv 139Tenevekapadumena,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
dhammaṃ sacchikariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3872Msdiv 140Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3873Msdiv 141Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekapadumassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3874Msdiv 142Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (3067)

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3875Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ukkhittapadumiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3876Ukkhittapadumiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3877Gandhodakavaggo catutiṃsatimo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3878Tassuddānaṃ

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3879Gandhadhūpo udakañca,
punnāga ekadussakā;
Phusito ca pabhaṅkaro,
kuṭido uttarīyako.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3880Savanī ekapadumī,
gāthāyo sabbapiṇḍitā;
Ekaṃ gāthāsatañceva,
catutālīsameva ca.
