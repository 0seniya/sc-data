4. Madhumaṃsadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5361Msdiv 106“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
sūkariko ahosahaṃ;
Ukkoṭakaṃ randhayitvā,
madhumaṃsamhi okiriṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5362Msdiv 107Sannipātaṃ ahaṃ gantvā,
ekaṃ pattaṃ gahesahaṃ;
Pūrayitvāna taṃ pattaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghassadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5363Msdiv 108Yottha therataro bhikkhu,
niyyādesi mamaṃ tadā;
Iminā pattapūrena,
labhassu vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5364Msdiv 109Duve sampattiyo bhutvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Pacchime vattamānamhi,
kilese jhāpayissati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5365Msdiv 110Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ;
Tattha bhutvā pivitvā ca,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5366Msdiv 111Maṇḍape rukkhamūle vā,
pubbakammaṃ anussariṃ;
Annapānābhivasso me,
abhivassati tāvade.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5367Msdiv 112Idaṃ pacchimakaṃ mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Idhāpi annapānaṃ me,
vassate sabbakālikaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5368Msdiv 113Teneva madhudānena,
sandhāvitvā bhave ahaṃ;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 2.373Ms 20Ap1_5369Msdiv 114Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
madhudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5370Msdiv 115Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5371Msdiv 116Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5372Msdiv 117Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5373Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā madhumaṃsadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5374Madhumaṃsadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
