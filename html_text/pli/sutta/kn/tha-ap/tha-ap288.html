6 Upaṭṭhākadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3320Msdiv 24“Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Dvipadindaṃ mahānāgaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3321Msdiv 25Pakkosāpiya tassāhaṃ,
sabbalokahitesino;
Upaṭṭhāko mayā dinno,
siddhatthassa mahesino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3322Msdiv 26Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
niyyādesi mahāmuni;
Uṭṭhāya āsanā tamhā,
pakkāmi pācināmukho.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3323Msdiv 27Catunnavutito kappe,
upaṭṭhākamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3324Msdiv 28Sattapaññāsito kappe,
balasenasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3325Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3326Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaṭṭhākadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3327Upaṭṭhākadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
