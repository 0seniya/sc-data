7. Mañjaripūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3128Msdiv 23“Mañjarikaṃ karitvāna,
rathiyaṃ paṭipajjahaṃ;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3129Msdiv 24Pasannacitto sumano,
paramāya ca pītiyā;
Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3130Msdiv 25Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3131Msdiv 26Ito tesattatikappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Jotiyo nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3132Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3133Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mañjaripūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3134Mañjaripūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
