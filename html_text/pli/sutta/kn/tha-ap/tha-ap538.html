6. Bāhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7135Msdiv 178“Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako;
Mahappabho tilokaggo,
nāmena padumuttaro.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7136Msdiv 179Khippābhiññassa bhikkhussa,
guṇaṃ kittayato mune;
Sutvā udaggacittohaṃ,
kāraṃ katvā mahesino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7137Msdiv 180Datvā sattāhikaṃ dānaṃ,
sasissassa mune ahaṃ;
Abhivādiya sambuddhaṃ,
taṃ ṭhānaṃ patthayiṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7138Msdiv 181Tato maṃ byākari buddho,
‘etaṃ passatha brāhmaṇaṃ;
Patitaṃ pādamūle me,
cariyaṃ paccavekkhaṇaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7139Msdiv 182Hemayaññopacitaṅgaṃ,
avadātatanuttacaṃ;
Palambabimbatamboṭṭhaṃ,
setatiṇhasamaṃ dijaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7140Msdiv 183Guṇathāmabahutaraṃ,
samuggatatanūruhaṃ;
Guṇoghāyatanībhūtaṃ,
pītisamphullitānanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7141Msdiv 184Eso patthayate ṭhānaṃ,
khippābhiññassa bhikkhuno;
Anāgate mahāvīro,
gotamo nāma hessati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7142Msdiv 185Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Bāhiyo nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7143Msdiv 186Tadā hi tuṭṭho vuṭṭhāya,
yāvajīvaṃ mahāmune;
Kāraṃ katvā cuto saggaṃ,
agaṃ sabhavanaṃ yathā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7144Msdiv 187Devabhūto manusso vā,
sukhito tassa kammuno;
Vāhasā saṃsaritvāna,
sampattimanubhomahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7145Msdiv 188Puna kassapavīrassa,
atthamentamhi sāsane;
Āruyha selasikharaṃ,
yuñjitvā jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7146Msdiv 189Visuddhasīlo sappañño,
jinasāsanakārako;
Tato cutā pañca janā,
devalokaṃ agamhase.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7147Msdiv 190Tatohaṃ bāhiyo jāto,
bhārukacche puruttame;
Tato nāvāya pakkhando,
sāgaraṃ appasiddhiyaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7148Msdiv 191Tato nāvā abhijjittha,
gantvāna katipāhakaṃ;
Tadā bhīsanake ghore,
patito makarākare.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7149Msdiv 192Tadāhaṃ vāyamitvāna,
santaritvā mahodadhiṃ;
Suppārapaṭṭanavaraṃ,
sampatto mandavedhito.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7150Msdiv 193Dārucīraṃ nivāsetvā,
gāmaṃ piṇḍāya pāvisiṃ;
Tadāha so jano tuṭṭho,
‘arahāyamidhāgato.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7151Msdiv 194Imaṃ annena pānena,
vatthena sayanena ca;
Bhesajjena ca sakkatvā,
hessāma sukhitā mayaṃ’.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7152Msdiv 195Paccayānaṃ tadā lābhī,
tehi sakkatapūjito;
Arahāhanti saṅkappaṃ,
uppādesiṃ ayoniso.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7153Msdiv 196Tato me cittamaññāya,
codayī pubbadevatā;
‘Na tvaṃ upāyamaggaññū,
kuto tvaṃ arahā bhave’.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7154Msdiv 197Codito tāya saṃviggo,
tadāhaṃ paripucchi taṃ;
‘Ke vā ete kuhiṃ loke,
arahanto naruttamā’.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7155Msdiv 198‘Sāvatthiyaṃ kosalamandire jino,
Pahūtapañño varabhūrimedhaso;
So sakyaputto arahā anāsavo,
Deseti dhammaṃ arahattapattiyā’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7156Msdiv 199Tadassa sutvā vacanaṃ supīṇito,
Nidhiṃva laddhā kapaṇoti vimhito;
Udaggacitto arahattamuttamaṃ,
Sudassanaṃ daṭṭhumanantagocaraṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7157Msdiv 200Tadā tato nikkhamitvāna ‘satthuno,
Sadā jinaṃ passāmi vimalānanaṃ’;
Upecca rammaṃ vijitavhayaṃ vanaṃ,
Dije apucchiṃ ‘kuhiṃ lokanandano’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7158Msdiv 201‘Tato avocuṃ naradevavandito,
Puraṃ paviṭṭho asanesanāya so;
Sasova khippaṃ munidassanussuko,
Upecca vandāhi tamaggapuggalaṃ’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7159Msdiv 202Tatohaṃ tuvaṭaṃ gantvā,
sāvatthiṃ puramuttamaṃ;
Vicarantaṃ tamaddakkhiṃ,
piṇḍatthaṃ apihāgidhaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7160Msdiv 203Pattapāṇiṃ alolakkhaṃ,
pācayantaṃ pītākaraṃ;
Sirīnilayasaṅkāsaṃ,
ravidittiharānanaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7161Msdiv 204Taṃ samecca nipaccāhaṃ,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ;
‘Kupathe vippanaṭṭhassa,
saraṇaṃ hohi gotama’.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7162Msdiv 205‘Pāṇasantāraṇatthāya,
piṇḍāya vicarāmahaṃ;
Na te dhammakathākālo’,
iccāha munisattamo.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7163Msdiv 206Tadā punappunaṃ buddhaṃ,
āyāciṃ dhammalālaso;
Yo me dhammamadesesi,
gambhīraṃ suññataṃ padaṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7164Msdiv 207Tassa dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
pāpuṇiṃ āsavakkhayaṃ;
Parikkhīṇāyuko santo,
aho satthānukampako.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7165Msdiv 208Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7166Msdiv 209Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7167Msdiv 210Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7168Msdiv 211Evaṃ thero viyākāsi,
bāhiyo dārucīriyo;
Saṅkārakūṭe patito,
bhūtāviṭṭhāya gāviyā.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7169Msdiv 212Attano pubbacariyaṃ,
kittayitvā mahāmati;
Parinibbāyi so thero,
sāvatthiyaṃ puruttame.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7170Msdiv 213Nagarā nikkhamanto taṃ,
disvāna isisattamo;
Dārucīradharaṃ dhīraṃ,
bāhiyaṃ bāhitāgamaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7171Msdiv 214Bhūmiyaṃ patitaṃ dantaṃ,
indaketūva pātitaṃ;
Gatāyuṃ sukkhakilesaṃ,
jinasāsanakārakaṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7172Msdiv 215Tato āmantayī satthā,
sāvake sāsane rate;
‘Gaṇhatha netvā jhāpetha,
tanuṃ sabrahmacārino.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7173Msdiv 216Thūpaṃ karotha pūjetha,
nibbuto so mahāmati;
Khippābhiññānamesaggo,
sāvako me vacokaro.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7174Msdiv 217Sahassamapi ce gāthā,
anatthapadasañhitā;
Ekaṃ gāthāpadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_7175Msdiv 218Yattha āpo ca pathavī,
tejo vāyo na gādhati;
Na tattha sukkā jotanti,
ādicco na pakāsati.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_7176Msdiv 219Na tattha candimā bhāti,
tamo tattha na vijjati;
Yadā ca attanā vedi,
munimonena brāhmaṇo.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_7177Msdiv 220Atha rūpā arūpā ca,
sukhadukkhā vimuccati’;
Iccevaṃ abhaṇī nātho,
tilokasaraṇo muni”.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_7178Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bāhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_7179Bāhiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
