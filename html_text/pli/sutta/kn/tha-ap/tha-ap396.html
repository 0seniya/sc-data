4. Madhudāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4637Msdiv 333“Sindhuyā nadiyā tīre,
sukato assamo mama;
Tattha vācemahaṃ sisse,
itihāsaṃ salakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4638Msdiv 334Dhammakāmā vinītā te,
sotukāmā susāsanaṃ;
Chaḷaṅge pāramippattā,
sindhukūle vasanti te.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4639Msdiv 335Uppātagamane ceva,
lakkhaṇesu ca kovidā;
Uttamatthaṃ gavesantā,
vasanti vipine tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4640Msdiv 336Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
loke uppajji tāvade;
Amhākaṃ anukampanto,
upāgacchi vināyako.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4641Msdiv 337Upāgataṃ mahāvīraṃ,
sumedhaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Tiṇasanthārakaṃ katvā,
lokajeṭṭhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4642Msdiv 338Vipināto madhuṃ gayha,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Sambuddho paribhuñjitvā,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4643Msdiv 339‘Yo taṃ adāsi madhuṃ me,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4644Msdiv 340Iminā madhudānena,
tiṇasanthārakena ca;
Tiṃsa kappasahassāni,
devaloke ramissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4645Msdiv 341Tiṃsa kappasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4646Msdiv 342Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4647Msdiv 343Devalokā idhāgantvā,
mātukucchiṃ upāgate;
Madhuvassaṃ pavassittha,
chādayaṃ madhunā mahiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4648Msdiv 344Mayi nikkhantamattamhi,
kucchiyā ca suduttarā;
Tatrāpi madhuvassaṃ me,
vassate niccakālikaṃ.

SC 13PTS vp Pali 1.326Ms 20Ap1_4649Msdiv 345Agārā abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Lābhī annassa pānassa,
madhudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4650Msdiv 346Sabbakāmasamiddhohaṃ,
bhavitvā devamānuse;
Teneva madhudānena,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4651Msdiv 347Vuṭṭhamhi deve caturaṅgule tiṇe,
Sampupphite dharaṇīruhe Sampupphite dharaṇīruhe sañchanne;
Suññe ghare maṇḍaparukkhamūlake,
Vasāmi niccaṃ sukhito anāsavo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4652Msdiv 348Majjhe mahante hīne ca,
bhave sabbe atikkamiṃ;
Ajja me āsavā khīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4653Msdiv 349Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
madhudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4654Msdiv 350Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4655Msdiv 351Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4656Msdiv 352Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4657Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā madhudāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4658Madhudāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
