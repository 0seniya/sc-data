2. Morahatthiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.403Ms 20Ap1_5889Msdiv 9“Morahatthaṃ gahetvāna,
upesiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
morahatthamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5890Msdiv 10Iminā morahatthena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Nibbāyiṃsu tayo aggī,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5891Msdiv 11Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthusampadā;
Datvānahaṃ morahatthaṃ,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5892Msdiv 12Tiyaggī nibbutā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5893Msdiv 13Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
morahatthassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5894Msdiv 14Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5895Msdiv 15Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5896Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5897Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā morahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5898Morahatthiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
