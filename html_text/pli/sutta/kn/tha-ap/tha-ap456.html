4 Tiṇukkadhāriyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5906SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5906Msdiv 22“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Pasannacitto sumano,
tayo ukke adhārayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5907SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5907Msdiv 23Satasahassito kappe,
sohaṃ ukkamadhārayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ukkadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5908SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5908Msdiv 24Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5909SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5909Msdiv 25Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5910SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5910Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5911Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5911Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiṇukkadhāriyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5912Tiṇukkadhāriyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
