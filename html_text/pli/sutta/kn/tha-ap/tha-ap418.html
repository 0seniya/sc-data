6. Ekadīpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5383SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5383Msdiv 124“Parinibbute sugate,
siddhatthe lokanāyake;
Sadevamānusā sabbe,
pūjenti dvipaduttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5384SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5384Msdiv 125Āropite ca citake,
siddhatthe lokanāyake;
Yathāsakena thāmena,
citaṃ pūjenti satthuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5385SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5385Msdiv 126Avidūre citakassa,
dīpaṃ ujjālayiṃ ahaṃ;
Yāva udeti sūriyo,
dīpaṃ me tāva ujjali.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5386SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5386Msdiv 127Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5387SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5387Msdiv 128Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
ekadīpīti ñāyati;
Dīpasatasahassāni,
byamhe pajjalare mama.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5388SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5388Msdiv 129Udayantova sūriyo,
deho me jotate sadā;
Sappabhāhi sarīrassa,
āloko hoti me sadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5389Msdiv 130SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5389Msdiv 130Tirokuṭṭaṃ tiroselaṃ,
samatiggayha pabbataṃ;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
passāmi cakkhunā ahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5390SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5390Msdiv 131Sattasattatikkhattuñca,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekatiṃsatikkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5391SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5391Msdiv 132Aṭṭhavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5392SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5392Msdiv 133Devalokā cavitvāna,
nibbattiṃ mātukucchiyaṃ;
Mātukucchigatassāpi,
akkhi me na nimīlati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5393SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5393Msdiv 134Jātiyā catuvassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Aḍḍhamāse asampatte,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5394SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5394Msdiv 135Dibbacakkhuṃ visodhesiṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Sabbe kilesā sañchinnā,
ekadīpassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5395SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5395Msdiv 136Tirokuṭṭaṃ tiroselaṃ,
pabbatañcāpi kevalaṃ;
Samatikkamma passāmi,
ekadīpassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5396SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5396Msdiv 137Visamā me samā honti,
andhakāro na vijjati;
Nāhaṃ passāmi timiraṃ,
ekadīpassidaṃ phalaṃ.

yaṃ dīpamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekadīpassidaṃ phalaṃ.
