7. Phaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2140Msdiv 75“Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;
Himavantassāvidūre,
vasāmi assame ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2141Msdiv 76Aggihuttañca me atthi,
puṇḍarīkaphalāni ca;
Puṭake nikkhipitvāna,
dumagge laggitaṃ mayā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2142Msdiv 77Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
bhikkhanto mamupāgami.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2143Msdiv 78Pasannacitto sumano,
phalaṃ buddhassadāsahaṃ;
Vittisañjanano mayhaṃ,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2144Msdiv 79Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2145Msdiv 80‘Iminā phaladānena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjati’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2146Msdiv 81Teneva sukkamūlena,
anubhotvāna sampadā;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2147Msdiv 82Ito sattasate kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sumaṅgalo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2148Msdiv 83Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2149Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2150Phaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
