4. Kilañjadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2992Msdiv 14“Tivarāyaṃ pure ramme,
naḷakāro ahaṃ tadā;
Siddhatthe lokapajjote,
pasannā janatā tahiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2993Msdiv 15Pūjatthaṃ lokanāthassa,
kilañjaṃ pariyesati;
Buddhapūjaṃ karontānaṃ,
kilañjaṃ adadiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2994Msdiv 16Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kilañjassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2995Msdiv 17Sattasattatikappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ jaladdharo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2996Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2997Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kilañjadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2998Kilañjadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
