4. Mahāmoggallānattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_384Msdiv 375“Anomadassī bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Vihāsi himavantamhi,
devasaṅghapurakkhato.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_385Msdiv 376Varuṇo nāma nāmena,
nāgarājā ahaṃ tadā;
Kāmarūpī vikubbāmi,
mahodadhinivāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_386Msdiv 377Saṅgaṇiyaṃ gaṇaṃ hitvā,
tūriyaṃ paṭṭhapesahaṃ;
Sambuddhaṃ parivāretvā,
vādesuṃ accharā tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_387Msdiv 378Vajjamānesu tūresu,
devā tūrāni vajjayuṃ;
Ubhinnaṃ saddaṃ sutvāna,
buddhopi sampabujjhatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_388Msdiv 379Nimantetvāna sambuddhaṃ,
sakaṃ bhavanupāgamiṃ;
Āsanaṃ paññapetvāna,
kālamārocayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_389Msdiv 380Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
parivuto lokanāyako;
Obhāsento disā sabbā,
bhavanaṃ me upāgami.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.32Ms 20Ap1_390Msdiv 381Upaviṭṭhaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
Devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Sabhikkhusaṃghaṃ tappesiṃ,
Annapānenahaṃ tadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_391Msdiv 382Anumodi mahāvīro,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_392Msdiv 383Yo so saṃghaṃ apūjesi,
buddhañca lokanāyakaṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
devalokaṃ gamissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_393Msdiv 384Sattasattatikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ aṭṭhasataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_394Msdiv 385Pañcapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Bhogā asaṅkhiyā tassa,
uppajjissanti tāvade.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_395Msdiv 386Aparimeyye ito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_396Msdiv 387Nirayā so cavitvāna,
manussataṃ gamissati;
Kolito nāma nāmena,
brahmabandhu bhavissati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_397Msdiv 388So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
kusalamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
dutiyo hessati sāvako.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_398Msdiv 389Āraddhavīriyo pahitatto,
Iddhiyā pāramiṃ gato;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
Nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_399Msdiv 390Pāpamittopanissāya,
kāmarāgavasaṃ gato;
Mātaraṃ pitarañcāpi,
ghātayiṃ duṭṭhamānaso.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_400Msdiv 391Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
nirayaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Pāpakammasamaṅgitā,
bhinnasīso marāmahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_401Msdiv 392Idaṃ pacchimakaṃ mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Idhāpi ediso mayhaṃ,
maraṇakāle bhavissati.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_402Msdiv 393Pavivekamanuyutto,
samādhibhāvanārato;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_403Msdiv 394Dharaṇimpi sugambhīraṃ,
bahalaṃ duppadhaṃsiyaṃ;
Vāmaṅguṭṭhena khobheyyaṃ,
iddhiyā pāramiṃ gato.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_404Msdiv 395Asmimānaṃ na passāmi,
māno mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Sāmaṇere upādāya,
garucittaṃ karomahaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_405Msdiv 396Aparimeyye ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamabhinīhariṃ;
Tāhaṃ bhūmimanuppatto,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 23PTS vp Pali 1.33Ms 20Ap1_406Msdiv 397Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_407Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahāmoggallāno thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_408Mahāmoggallānattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
