10 Bodhigharadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5861Msdiv 69“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Pasannacitto sumano,
bodhigharamakārayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5862Msdiv 70Tusitaṃ upapannomhi,
vasāmi ratane ghare;
Na me sītaṃ vā uṇhaṃ vā,
vāto gatte na samphuse.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5863Msdiv 71Pañcasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Kāsikaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
vissakammena māpitaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5864Msdiv 72Dasayojanaāyāmaṃ,
aṭṭhayojanavitthataṃ;
Na tamhi nagare atthi,
kaṭṭhaṃ vallī ca mattikā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5865Msdiv 73Tiriyaṃ yojanaṃ āsi,
addhayojanavitthataṃ;
Maṅgalo nāma pāsādo,
vissakammena māpito.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5866Msdiv 74Cullāsītisahassāni,
thambhā soṇṇamayā ahuṃ;
Maṇimayā ca niyyūhā,
chadanaṃ rūpiyaṃ ahu.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5867Msdiv 75Sabbasoṇṇamayaṃ gharaṃ,
vissakammena māpitaṃ;
Ajjhāvutthaṃ mayā etaṃ,
gharadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5868Msdiv 76Te sabbe anubhotvāna,
devamānusake bhave;
Ajjhapattomhi nibbānaṃ,
santipadamanuttaraṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5869Msdiv 77Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
bodhigharamakārayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
gharadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5870Msdiv 78Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5871Msdiv 79Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 2.402Ms 20Ap1_5872Msdiv 80Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4704)

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5873Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhigharadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5874Bodhigharadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5875Vibhītakavaggo pañcacattālīsamo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5876Tassuddānaṃ

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5877Vibhītakī kolaphalī,
billabhallātakappado;
Uttalambaṭakī ceva,
āsanī pādapīṭhako.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5878Vediko bodhighariko,
gāthāyo gaṇitāpi ca;
Ekūnāsītikā sabbā,
asmiṃ vagge pakittitā.
