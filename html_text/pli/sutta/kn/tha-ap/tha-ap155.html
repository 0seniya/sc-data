3. Vīthisammajjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2371Msdiv 15“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Pannarase yathā candaṃ,
niyyantaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2372Msdiv 16Aṭṭhasaṭṭhisahassāni,
sabbe khīṇāsavā ahuṃ;
Parivāriṃsu sambuddhaṃ,
dvipadindaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2373Msdiv 17Sammajjitvāna taṃ vīthiṃ,
niyyante lokanāyake;
Ussāpesiṃ dhajaṃ tattha,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2374Msdiv 18Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dhajaṃ abhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2375Msdiv 19Ito catutthake kappe,
rājāhosiṃ mahabbalo;
Sabbākārena sampanno,
sudhajo iti vissuto.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2376Msdiv 20Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2377Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīthisammajjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2378Vīthisammajjakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
