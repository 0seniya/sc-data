9. Ekacāriyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.196Ms 20Ap1_2647Msdiv 51“Tāvatiṃsesu devesu,
mahāghoso tadā ahu;
Buddho ca loke nibbāti,
mayañcamha sarāgino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2648Msdiv 52Tesaṃ saṃvegajātānaṃ,
sokasallasamaṅginaṃ;
Sabalena upatthaddho,
agamaṃ buddhasantikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2649Msdiv 53Mandāravaṃ gahetvāna,
saṅgīti abhinimmitaṃ;
Parinibbutakālamhi,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2650Msdiv 54Sabbe devānumodiṃsu,
accharāyo ca me tadā;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2651Msdiv 55Saṭṭhikappasahassamhi,
ito soḷasa te janā;
Mahāmallajanā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2652Msdiv 56Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2653Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekacāriyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2654Ekacāriyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
