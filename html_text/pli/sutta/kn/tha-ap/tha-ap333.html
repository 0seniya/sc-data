1. Gandhadhūpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3715Msdiv 1“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
gandhadhūpaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Sumanehi paṭicchannaṃ,
buddhānucchavikañca taṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3716Msdiv 2Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Indīvaraṃva jalitaṃ,
ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3717Msdiv 3Byagghusabhaṃva pavaraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ;
Nisinnaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3718Msdiv 4Disvā cittaṃ pasādetvā,
paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3719Msdiv 5Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ gandhamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
gandhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3720Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3721Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhadhūpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3722Gandhadhūpiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
