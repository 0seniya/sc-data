8 Sattalipupphapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3074SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3074Msdiv 27“Satta sattalipupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
vessabhumhi naruttame.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3075SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3075Msdiv 28Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3076SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3076Msdiv 29Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3077Itthaṃ sudaṃ SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3077Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattalipupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3078Sattalipupphapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
