6. Campakapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2232Msdiv 41“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
osadhiṃ viya tārakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2233Msdiv 42Tayo māṇavakā āsuṃ,
sake sippe susikkhitā;
Khāribhāraṃ gahetvāna,
anventi mama pacchato.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2234Msdiv 43Puṭake satta pupphāni,
nikkhittāni tapassinā;
Gahetvā tāni ñāṇamhi,
vessabhussābhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2235Msdiv 44Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2236Msdiv 45Ekūnatiṃsakappamhi,
vipulābhasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2237Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2238Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā campakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2239Campakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
