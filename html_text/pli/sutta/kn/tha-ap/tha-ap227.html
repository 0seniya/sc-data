5. Abyādhikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2930Msdiv 24“Vipassissa bhagavato,
aggisālaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Byādhikānañca āvāsaṃ,
uṇhodakapaṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2931Msdiv 25Tena kammenayaṃ mayhaṃ,
attabhāvo sunimmito;
Byādhāhaṃ nābhijānāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2932Msdiv 26Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ sālamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
aggisālāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2933Msdiv 27Ito ca sattame kappe,
ekosiṃ aparājito;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2934Msdiv 28Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2935Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā abyādhiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2936Abyādhikattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
