2. Kakkārupupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4039Msdiv 5“Yāmā devā idhāgantvā,
gotamaṃ sirivacchasaṃ;
Kakkārumālaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4040Msdiv 6Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4041Msdiv 7Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4042Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kakkārupupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4043Kakkārupupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
