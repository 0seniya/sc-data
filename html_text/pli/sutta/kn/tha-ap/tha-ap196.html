4. Asokapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2688Msdiv 17“Tivarāyaṃ pure ramme,
rājuyyānaṃ ahu tadā;
Uyyānapālo tatthāsiṃ,
rañño baddhacaro ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2689Msdiv 18Padumo nāma nāmena,
sayambhū sappabho ahu;
Nisinnaṃ puṇḍarīkamhi,
chāyā na jahi taṃ muniṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2690Msdiv 19Asokaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
piṇḍibhāraṃ sudassanaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
jalajuttamanāmino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2691Msdiv 20Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2692Msdiv 21Sattatiṃsamhito kappe,
soḷasa araṇañjahā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2693Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2694Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā asokapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2695Asokapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
