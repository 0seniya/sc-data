9 Buddhupaṭṭhāyikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3340Msdiv 38“Veṭambhinīti me nāmaṃ,
pitusantaṃ mamaṃ tadā;
Mama hatthaṃ gahetvāna,
upānayi mahāmuniṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3341Msdiv 39Imemaṃ uddisissanti,
buddhā lokagganāyakā;
Tehaṃ upaṭṭhiṃ sakkaccaṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3342Msdiv 40Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
buddhe upaṭṭhahiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3343Msdiv 41Tevīsamhi ito kappe,
caturo āsu khattiyā;
Samaṇupaṭṭhākā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3344Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3345Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhupaṭṭhāyiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3346Buddhupaṭṭhāyikattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
