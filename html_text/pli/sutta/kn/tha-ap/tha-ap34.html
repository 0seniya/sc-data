2. Sāgatattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.83Ms 20Ap1_1091Msdiv 17“Sobhito nāma nāmena,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;
Purakkhato sasissehi,
ārāmaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1092Msdiv 18Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Ārāmadvārā nikkhamma,
aṭṭhāsi purisuttamo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1093Msdiv 19Tamaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
dantaṃ dantapurakkhataṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1094Msdiv 20Ye keci pādapā sabbe,
mahiyā te virūhare;
Buddhimanto tathā sattā,
ruhanti jinasāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1095Msdiv 21Satthavāhosi sappañño,
mahesi bahuke jane;
Vipathā uddharitvāna,
pathaṃ ācikkhase tuvaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1096Msdiv 22Danto dantaparikiṇṇo,
jhāyī jhānaratehi ca;
Ātāpī pahitattehi,
upasantehi tādibhi.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1097Msdiv 23Alaṅkato parisāhi,
puññañāṇehi sobhati;
Pabhā niddhāvate tuyhaṃ,
sūriyodayane yathā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1098Msdiv 24Pasannacittaṃ disvāna,
mahesī padumuttaro;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1099Msdiv 25‘Yo so hāsaṃ janetvāna,
mamaṃ kittesi brāhmaṇo;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
devaloke ramissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1100Msdiv 26Tusitā hi cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
sāsane pabbajissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1101Msdiv 27Tena kammena sukatena,
arahattaṃ arahattaṃ labhissati;
Sāgato nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1102Msdiv 28Pabbajitvāna kāyena,
pāpakammaṃ vivajjayiṃ;
Vacīduccaritaṃ hitvā,
ājīvaṃ parisodhayiṃ.

SC 13PTS vp Pali 1.84Ms 20Ap1_1103Msdiv 29Evaṃ viharamānohaṃ,
tejodhātūsu kovido;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1104Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1105Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sāgato thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1106Sāgatattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
