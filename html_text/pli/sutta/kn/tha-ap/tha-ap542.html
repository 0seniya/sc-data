10. Mogharājattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7292Msdiv 327“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbalokavidū muni;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji cakkhumā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7293Msdiv 328Ovādako viññāpako,
tārako sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Desanākusalo buddho,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7294Msdiv 329Anukampako kāruṇiko,
hitesī sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Sampatte titthiye sabbe,
pañcasīle patiṭṭhapi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7295Msdiv 330Evaṃ nirākulaṃ āsi,
suññataṃ titthiyehi ca;
Vicittaṃ arahantehi,
vasībhūtehi tādibhi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7296Msdiv 331Ratanānaṭṭhapaññāsaṃ,
uggato so mahāmuni;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7297Msdiv 332Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7298Msdiv 333Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
kule aññatare ahuṃ;
Parakammāyane yutto,
natthi me kiñci sandhanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7299Msdiv 334Paṭikkamanasālāyaṃ,
vasanto katabhūmiyaṃ;
Aggiṃ ujjālayiṃ tattha,
daḷhaṃ kaṇhāsi sā mahī.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7300Msdiv 335Tadā parisatiṃ nātho,
catusaccapakāsako;
Sāvakaṃ sampakittesi,
lūkhacīvaradhārakaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7301Msdiv 336Tassa tamhi guṇe tuṭṭho,
paṇipacca tathāgataṃ;
Lūkhacīvaradhāraggaṃ,
patthayiṃ ṭhānamuttamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7302Msdiv 337Tadā avoca bhagavā,
sāvake padumuttaro;
‘Passathetaṃ purisakaṃ,
kucelaṃ tanudehakaṃ.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 2.487Ms 20Ap1_7303Msdiv 338Pītippasannavadanaṃ,
saddhādhanasamanvitaṃ;
Udaggatanujaṃ haṭṭhaṃ,
acalaṃ sālapiṇḍitaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7304Msdiv 339Eso pattheti taṃ ṭhānaṃ,
saccasenassa bhikkhuno;
Lūkhacīvaradhārissa,
tassa vaṇṇasitāsayo’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7305Msdiv 340Taṃ sutvā mudito hutvā,
nipacca sirasā jinaṃ;
Yāvajīvaṃ subhaṃ kammaṃ,
karitvā jinasāsane.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7306Msdiv 341Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsūpago ahaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7307Msdiv 342Paṭikkamanasālāyaṃ,
bhūmidāhakakammunā;
Samasahassaṃ niraye,
adayhiṃ vedanāṭṭito.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7308Msdiv 343Tena kammāvasesena,
pañca jātisatānihaṃ;
Manusso kulajo hutvā,
jātiyā lakkhaṇaṅkito.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7309Msdiv 344Pañca jātisatāneva,
kuṭṭharogasamappito;
Mahādukkhaṃ anubhaviṃ,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7310Msdiv 345Imasmiṃ bhaddake kappe,
upariṭṭhaṃ yasassinaṃ;
Piṇḍapātena tappesiṃ,
pasannamānaso ahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7311Msdiv 346Tena kammavisesena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7312Msdiv 347Pacchime bhave sampatte,
ajāyiṃ khattiye kule;
Pituno accayenāhaṃ,
mahārajjasamappito.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7313Msdiv 348Kuṭṭharogādhibhūtohaṃ,
na ratiṃ na sukhaṃ labhe;
Moghaṃ rajjaṃ sukhaṃ yasmā,
mogharājā tato ahaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7314Msdiv 349Kāyassa dosaṃ disvāna,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Bāvarissa dijaggassa,
sissattaṃ ajjhupāgamiṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7315Msdiv 350Mahatā parivārena,
upecca naranāyakaṃ;
Apucchiṃ nipuṇaṃ pañhaṃ,
taṃ vīraṃ vādisūdanaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7316Msdiv 351‘Ayaṃ loko paro loko,
brahmaloko sadevako;
Diṭṭhiṃ no nābhijānāmi,
gotamassa yasassino.

SC 26PTS vp Pali 2.488Ms 20Ap1_7317Msdiv 352Evābhikkantadassāviṃ,
atthi pañhena āgamaṃ;
Kathaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
maccurājā na passati’.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7318Msdiv 353‘Suññato lokaṃ avekkhassu,
mogharāja sadā sato;
Attānudiṭṭhiṃ uhacca,
evaṃ maccutaro siyā.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7319Msdiv 354Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
maccurājā na passati’;
Iti maṃ abhaṇi buddho,
sabbarogatikicchako.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7320Msdiv 355Saha gāthāvasānena,
kesamassuvivajjito;
Kāsāvavatthavasano,
āsiṃ bhikkhu tathārahā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7321Msdiv 356Saṃghikesu vihāresu,
na vasiṃ rogapīḷito;
Mā vihāro padussīti,
vātarogehi pīḷito.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7322Msdiv 357Saṅkārakūṭā āhitvā,
susānā rathikāhi ca;
Tato saṅghāṭiṃ karitvā,
dhārayiṃ lūkhacīvaraṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7323Msdiv 358Mahābhisakko tasmiṃ me,
guṇe tuṭṭho vināyako;
Lūkhacīvaradhārīnaṃ,
etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7324Msdiv 359Puññapāpaparikkhīṇo,
sabbarogavivajjito;
Sikhīva anupādāno,
nibbāyissamanāsavo.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7325Msdiv 360Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7326Msdiv 361Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_7327Msdiv 362Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5937)

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7328Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mogharājā thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7329Mogharājattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7330Kaccāyanavaggo catupaññāsamo.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7331Tassuddānaṃ

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_7332Kaccāno vakkalī thero,
mahākappinasavhayo;
Dabbo kumāranāmo ca,
bāhiyo koṭṭhiko vasī.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_7333Uruveḷakassapo rādho,
mogharājā ca paṇḍito;
Tīṇi gāthāsatānettha,
bāsaṭṭhi ceva piṇḍitā.
