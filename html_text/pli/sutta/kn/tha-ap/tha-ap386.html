4 Pilakkhaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.296Ms 20Ap1_4203Msdiv 21“Vanantare buddhaṃ disvā,
atthadassiṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pilakkhassādadiṃ phalaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4204Msdiv 22Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4205Msdiv 23Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4206Msdiv 24Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4207Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4208Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilakkhaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4209Pilakkhaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
