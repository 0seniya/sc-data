6 Kaḷambadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5723SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5723Msdiv 50“Romaso nāma sambuddho,
vasate pabbatantare;
Kaḷambaṃ tassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5724SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5724Msdiv 51Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kaḷambassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5725SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5725Msdiv 52Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5726SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5726Msdiv 53Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5727SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5727Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5728Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5728Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaḷambadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5729Kaḷambadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
