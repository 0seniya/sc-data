8. Akkantasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2872SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2872Msdiv 37“Kusāṭakaṃ gahetvāna,
upajjhāyassahaṃ pure;
Mantañca anusikkhāmi,
ganthādosassa pattiyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2873SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2873Msdiv 38Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ aggaṃ,
Tissaṃ buddhaṃ gaṇuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2874SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2874Msdiv 39Kusāṭakaṃ pattharitaṃ,
akkamantaṃ naruttamaṃ;
Samuggataṃ mahāvīraṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2875SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2875Msdiv 40Disvā taṃ lokapajjotaṃ,
vimalaṃ candasannibhaṃ;
Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2876SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2876Msdiv 41Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ adāsiṃ kusāṭakaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kusāṭakassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2877SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2877Msdiv 42Sattatiṃse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Sunando nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2878SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2878Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2879Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā akkantasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2880Akkantasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
