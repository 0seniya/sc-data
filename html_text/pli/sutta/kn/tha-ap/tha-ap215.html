3. Saccasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2836Msdiv 11“Vessabhū tamhi samaye,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Deseti ariyasaccāni,
nibbāpento mahājanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2837Msdiv 12Paramakāruññapattomhi,
samitiṃ agamāsahaṃ;
Sohaṃ nisinnako santo,
dhammamassosi satthuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2838Msdiv 13Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
devalokaṃ agacchahaṃ;
Tiṃsakappāni devesu,
avasiṃ tatthahaṃ pure.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2839Msdiv 14Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
saccasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2840Msdiv 15Chabbīsamhi ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Ekaphusitanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2841Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2842Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saccasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2843Saccasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
