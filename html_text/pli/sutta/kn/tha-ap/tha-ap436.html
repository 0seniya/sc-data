4. Ñāṇatthavikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5704Msdiv 35“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
dīparukkhaṃva jotitaṃ;
Kañcanaṃva virocantaṃ,
addasaṃ dvipaduttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5705Msdiv 36Kamaṇḍaluṃ ṭhapetvāna,
vākacīrañca kuṇḍikaṃ;
Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5706Msdiv 37‘Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamaṃ,
mohajālasamākulaṃ;
Ñāṇālokaṃ dassetvāna,
nittiṇṇosi mahāmuni.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5707Msdiv 38Samuddharasimaṃ lokaṃ,
sabbāvantamanuttaraṃ;
Ñāṇe te upamā natthi,
yāvatājagatogati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5708Msdiv 39Tena ñāṇena sabbaññū,
iti buddho pavuccati;
Vandāmi taṃ mahāvīraṃ,
sabbaññutamanāvaraṃ’.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.393Ms 20Ap1_5709Msdiv 40Satasahassito kappe,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ñāṇatthavāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5710Msdiv 41Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5711Msdiv 42Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5712Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5713Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ñāṇatthaviko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5714Ñāṇatthavikattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
