2. Pupphathūpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2065Msdiv 10“Himavantassāvidūre,
kukkuro nāma pabbato;
Vemajjhe tassa vasati,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2066Msdiv 11Pañca sissasahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ sadā;
Pubbuṭṭhāyī ca te āsuṃ,
mantesu ca visāradā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2067Msdiv 12Buddho loke samuppanno,
taṃ vijānātha no bhavaṃ;
Asītibyañjanānassa,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2068Msdiv 13Byāmappabho jinavaro,
ādiccova virocati;
Sissānaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2069Msdiv 14Assamā abhinikkhamma,
disaṃ pucchati sissake;
Yamhi dese mahāvīro,
vasati lokanāyako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2070Msdiv 15Tāhaṃ disaṃ namassissaṃ,
jinaṃ appaṭipuggalaṃ;
Udaggacitto sumano,
pūjesiṃ taṃ tathāgataṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2071Msdiv 16Etha sissā gamissāma,
dakkhissāma tathāgataṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
sossāma jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2072Msdiv 17Ekāhaṃ abhinikkhamma,
byādhiṃ paṭilabhiṃ ahaṃ;
Byādhinā pīḷito santo,
sālaṃ vāsayituṃ gamiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2073Msdiv 18Sabbe sisse samānetvā,
apucchiṃ te tathāgataṃ;
Kīdisaṃ lokanāthassa,
guṇaṃ paramabuddhino.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2074Msdiv 19Te me puṭṭhā viyākaṃsu,
Yathā dassāvino tathā;
Sakkaccaṃ buddhaseṭṭhaṃ taṃ,
Desesuṃ mama sammukhā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2075Msdiv 20Tesāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Pupphehi thūpaṃ katvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_2076Msdiv 21Te me sarīraṃ jhāpetvā,
agamuṃ buddhasantikaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
satthāramabhivādayuṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_2077Msdiv 22Pupphehi thūpaṃ katvāna,
sugatassa mahesino;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_2078Msdiv 23Cattālīsasahassamhi,
kappe soḷasa khattiyā;
Nāmenaggisamā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_2079Msdiv 24Vīsakappasahassamhi,
rājāno cakkavattino;
Ghatāsanasanāmāva,
aṭṭhattiṃsa mahīpatī.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_2080Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_2081Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphathūpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_2082Pupphathūpiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
