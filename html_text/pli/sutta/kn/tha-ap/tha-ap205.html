3 Kiṅkaṇipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2763Msdiv 10“Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
sabbaññū lokanāyako;
Odakaṃ dahamoggayha,
sināyi lokanāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2764Msdiv 11Paggayha kiṅkaṇiṃ pupphaṃ,
Vipassissābhiropayiṃ;
Udaggacitto sumano,
Dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2765Msdiv 12Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2766Msdiv 13Sattavīsatikappamhi,
rājā bhīmaratho ahu;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2767Msdiv 14Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2768Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṅkaṇipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2769Kiṅkaṇipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
