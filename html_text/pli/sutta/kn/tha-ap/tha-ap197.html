5. Aṅkolakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2696SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2696Msdiv 23“Aṅkolaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
mālāvaraṃ sakosakaṃ;
Ocinitvāna taṃ pupphaṃ,
agamaṃ buddhasantikaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2697SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2697Msdiv 24Siddhattho tamhi samaye,
patilīno mahāmuni;
Muhuttaṃ paṭimānetvā,
guhāyaṃ pupphamokiriṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2698SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2698Msdiv 25Catunnavutito kappe,
Yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2699SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2699Msdiv 26Chattiṃsamhi ito kappe,
āseko devagajjito;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2700SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2700Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2701Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2702Aṅkolakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
