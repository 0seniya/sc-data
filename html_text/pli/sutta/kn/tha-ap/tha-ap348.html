6. Campakapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3929Msdiv 39“Himavantassāvidūre,
jāpalo nāma pabbato;
Buddho sudassano nāma,
vihāsi pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3930Msdiv 40Pupphaṃ hemavantaṃ gayha,
Gacchaṃ vehāyasenahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
Oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3931Msdiv 41Satta campakapupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sayambhussa mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3932Msdiv 42Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3933Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3934Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā campakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3935Campakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
