5. Koraṇḍapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5545Msdiv 68“Vanakammiko pure āsiṃ,
pitumātumatenahaṃ;
Pasumārena jīvāmi,
kusalaṃ me na vijjati.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5546Msdiv 69Mama āsayasāmantā,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Padāni tīṇi dassesi,
anukampāya cakkhumā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5547Msdiv 70Akkante ca pade disvā,
tissanāmassa satthuno;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pade cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5548Msdiv 71Koraṇḍaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
pādapaṃ dharaṇīruhaṃ;
Sakosakaṃ gahetvāna,
padaseṭṭhamapūjayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5549Msdiv 72Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5550Msdiv 73Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Koraṇḍavaṇṇakoyeva,
suppabhāso bhavāmahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5551Msdiv 74Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
padapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5552Msdiv 75Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5553Msdiv 76Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5554Msdiv 77Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5555Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koraṇḍapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5556Koraṇḍapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
