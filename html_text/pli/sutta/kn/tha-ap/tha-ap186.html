4. Pañcahatthiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2604Msdiv 18“Tisso nāmāsi bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
rathiyaṃ paṭipajjatha.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2605Msdiv 19Pañca uppalahatthā ca,
cāturā ṭhapitā mayā;
Āhutiṃ dātukāmohaṃ,
paggaṇhiṃ vatasiddhiyā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2606Msdiv 20Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Buddharaṃsīhi phuṭṭhosmi,
Pūjesiṃ dvipaduttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2607Msdiv 21Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2608Msdiv 22Ito terasakappamhi,
pañca susabhasammatā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2609Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2610Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pañcahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2611Pañcahatthiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
