10 Padumadhārikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6786Msdiv 78“Himavantassāvidūre,
romaso nāma pabbato;
Buddhopi sambhavo nāma,
abbhokāse vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6787Msdiv 79Bhavanā nikkhamitvāna,
padumaṃ dhārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ dhārayitvāna,
bhavanaṃ punarāgamiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6788Msdiv 80Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6789Msdiv 81Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6790Msdiv 82Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6791Msdiv 83Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5449)

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6792Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumadhāriko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6793Padumadhārikattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6794Phaladāyakavaggo dvepaññāsamo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6795Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6796Kurañciyaṃ kapitthañca,
kosambamatha ketakaṃ;
Nāgapupphajjunañceva,
kuṭajī ghosasaññako.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6797Thero ca sabbaphalado,
tathā padumadhāriko;
Asīti cettha gāthāyo,
tisso gāthā taduttari.
