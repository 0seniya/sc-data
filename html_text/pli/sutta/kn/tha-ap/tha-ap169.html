7 Yūthikapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2477SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2477Msdiv 33“Candabhāgānadītīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Sayambhuṃ addasaṃ tattha,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2478Msdiv 34Pupphaṃ SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2478Msdiv 34Pupphaṃ yūthikamādāya,
upagacchiṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2479SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2479Msdiv 35Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2480SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2480Msdiv 36Sattasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
eko sāmuddharo ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2481SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2481Msdiv 37Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2482Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2482Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yūthikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2483Yūthikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
