10. Sugandhattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6896Msdiv 81“Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6897Msdiv 82Anubyañjanasampanno,
bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Byāmappabhāparivuto,
raṃsijālasamotthaṭo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6898Msdiv 83Assāsetā yathā cando,
sūriyova pabhaṅkaro;
Nibbāpetā yathā megho,
sāgarova guṇākaro.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6899Msdiv 84Dharaṇīriva sīlena,
himavāva samādhinā;
Ākāso viya paññāya,
asaṅgo anilo yathā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6900Msdiv 85Sa kadāci mahāvīro,
parisāsu visārado;
Saccāni sampakāseti,
uddharanto mahājanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6901Msdiv 86Tadā hi bārāṇasiyaṃ,
seṭṭhiputto mahāyaso;
Āsahaṃ dhanadhaññassa,
pahūtassa bahū tadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6902Msdiv 87Jaṅghāvihāraṃ vicaraṃ,
migadāyamupeccahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
desentaṃ amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6903Msdiv 88Visaṭṭhakantavacanaṃ,
karavīkasamassaraṃ;
Haṃsarutehi nigghosaṃ,
viññāpentaṃ mahājanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6904Msdiv 89Disvā devātidevaṃ taṃ,
sutvāva madhuraṃ giraṃ;
Pahāyanappake bhoge,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6905Msdiv 90Evaṃ pabbajito cāhaṃ,
na cirena bahussuto;
Ahosiṃ dhammakathiko,
vicittapaṭibhānavā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6906Msdiv 91Mahāparisamajjhehaṃ,
haṭṭhacitto punappunaṃ;
Vaṇṇayiṃ hemavaṇṇassa,
vaṇṇaṃ vaṇṇavisārado.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6907Msdiv 92‘Esa khīṇāsavo buddho,
anīgho chinnasaṃsayo;
Sabbakammakkhayaṃ patto,
vimuttopadhisaṅkhaye.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6908Msdiv 93Esa so bhagavā buddho,
esa sīho anuttaro;
Sadevakassa lokassa,
brahmacakkappavattako.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6909Msdiv 94Danto dametā santo ca,
sametā nibbuto isi;
Nibbāpetā ca assattho,
assāsetā mahājanaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6910Msdiv 95Vīro sūro ca vikkanto,
pañño kāruṇiko vasī;
Vijitāvī ca sa jino,
appagabbo anālayo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6911Msdiv 96Aneñjo acalo dhīmā,
amoho asamo muni;
Dhorayho usabho nāgo,
sīho sakko garūsupi.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6912Msdiv 97Virāgo vimalo brahmā,
vādī sūro raṇañjaho;
Akhilo ca visallo ca,
asamo saṃyato suci.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6913Msdiv 98Brāhmaṇo samaṇo nātho,
bhisakko sallakattako;
Yodho buddho sutāsuto,
acalo mudito sito.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6914Msdiv 99Dhātā dhatā ca santi ca,
kattā netā pakāsitā;
Sampahaṃsitā bhettā ca,
chettā sotā pasaṃsitā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6915Msdiv 100Akhilo ca visallo ca,
anīgho akathaṃkathī;
Anejo virajo kattā,
gandhā vattā pasaṃsitā.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6916Msdiv 101Tāretā atthakāretā,
kāretā sampadāritā;
Pāpetā sahitā kantā,
hantā ātāpī tāpaso.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6917Msdiv 102Samacitto samasamo,
asahāyo dayālayo;
Accherasatto akuho,
katāvī isisattamo.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6918Msdiv 103Nittiṇṇakaṅkho nimmāno,
appameyyo anūpamo;
Sabbavākyapathātīto,
saccaneyyantagū jino.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_6919Msdiv 104Sattasāravare tasmiṃ,
Pasādo amatāvaho;
Tasmā buddhe ca dhamme ca,
Saṃghe saddhā mahatthikā’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6920Msdiv 105Guṇehi evamādīhi,
tilokasaraṇuttamaṃ;
Vaṇṇento parisāmajjhe,
akaṃ dhammakathaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6921Msdiv 106Tato cutāhaṃ tusite,
anubhotvā mahāsukhaṃ;
Tato cuto manussesu,
jāto homi sugandhiko.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6922Msdiv 107Nissāso mukhagandho ca,
dehagandho tatheva me;
Sedagandho ca satataṃ,
sabbagandhova hoti me.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6923Msdiv 108Mukhagandho sadā mayhaṃ,
padumuppalacampako;
Parisanto sadā vāti,
sarīro ca tatheva me.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6924Msdiv 109Guṇatthavassa sabbantaṃ,
Phalaṃ tu paramabbhutaṃ;
Ekaggamanasā sabbe,
Vaṇṇayissaṃ suṇātha me.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_6925Msdiv 110Guṇaṃ buddhassa vatvāna,
hitāya ca na sadisaṃ;
Sukhito homi sabbattha,
saṃgho vīrasamāyuto.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_6926Msdiv 111Yasassī sukhito kanto,
jutimā piyadassano;
Vattā aparibhūto ca,
niddoso paññavā tathā.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_6927Msdiv 112Khīṇe āyusi nibbānaṃ,
sulabhaṃ buddhabhattino;
Tesaṃ hetuṃ pavakkhāmi,
taṃ suṇātha yathātathaṃ.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_6928Msdiv 113Santaṃ yasaṃ bhagavato,
vidhinā abhivādayaṃ;
Tattha tatthūpapannopi,
yasassī tena homahaṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_6929Msdiv 114Dukkhassantakaraṃ buddhaṃ,
dhammaṃ santamasaṅkhataṃ;
Vaṇṇayaṃ sukhado āsiṃ,
sattānaṃ sukhito tato.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_6930Msdiv 115Guṇaṃ vadanto buddhassa,
buddhapītisamāyuto;
Sakantiṃ parakantiñca,
janayiṃ tena kantimā.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_6931Msdiv 116Jino te titthikākiṇṇe,
abhibhuyya kutitthiye;
Guṇaṃ vadanto jotesiṃ,
nāyakaṃ jutimā tato.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_6932Msdiv 117Piyakārī janassāpi,
sambuddhassa guṇaṃ vadaṃ;
Saradova sasaṅkohaṃ,
tenāsiṃ piyadassano.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_6933Msdiv 118Yathāsattivasenāhaṃ,
sabbavācāhi santhaviṃ;
Sugataṃ tena vāgiso,
vicittapaṭibhānavā.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_6934Msdiv 119Ye bālā vimatiṃ pattā,
paribhonti mahāmuniṃ;
Niggahiṃ te saddhammena,
paribhūto na tenahaṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_6935Msdiv 120Buddhavaṇṇena sattānaṃ,
kilese apanesahaṃ;
Nikkilesamano homi,
tassa kammassa vāhasā.

SC 41Ms 20Ap1_6936Msdiv 121Sotūnaṃ vuddhimajaniṃ,
buddhānussatidesako;
Tenāhamāsiṃ sappañño,
nipuṇatthavipassako.

SC 42Ms 20Ap1_6937Msdiv 122Sabbāsavaparikkhīṇo,
tiṇṇasaṃsārasāgaro;
Sikhīva anupādāno,
pāpuṇissāmi nibbutiṃ.

SC 43Ms 20Ap1_6938Msdiv 123Imasmiṃyeva kappasmiṃ,
yamahaṃ santhaviṃ jinaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhavaṇṇassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 44Ms 20Ap1_6939Msdiv 124Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 45Ms 20Ap1_6940Msdiv 125Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 46Ms 20Ap1_6941Msdiv 126Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (5575)

SC 47Ms 20Ap1_6942Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sugandho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 48Ms 20Ap1_6943Sugandhattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 49Ms 20Ap1_6944Tiṇadāyakavaggo tepaññāsamo.

SC 50Ms 20Ap1_6945Tassuddānaṃ

SC 51Ms 20Ap1_6946Tiṇado mañcado ceva,
saraṇabbhañjanappado;
Supaṭo daṇḍadāyī ca,
nelapūjī tatheva ca.

SC 52Ms 20Ap1_6947Bodhisammajjako maṇḍo,
sugandho dasamoti ca;
Gāthāsataṃ satevīsaṃ,
gaṇitañcettha sabbaso.
