3. Sayanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1399Msdiv 20“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
sabbalokānukampino;
Sayanaṃ tassa pādāsiṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1400Msdiv 21Tena sayanadānena,
sukhette bījasampadā;
Bhogā nibbattare tassa,
sayanassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1401Msdiv 22Ākāse seyyaṃ kappemi,
dhāremi pathaviṃ imaṃ;
Pāṇesu me issariyaṃ,
sayanassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1402Msdiv 23Pañcakappasahassamhi,
aṭṭha āsuṃ mahātejā;
Catuttiṃse kappasate,
caturo ca mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1403Msdiv 24Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1404Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sayanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1405Sayanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
