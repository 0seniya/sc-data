8. Maggasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2020Msdiv 66“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
sāvakā vanacārino;
Vippanaṭṭhā brahāraññe,
andhāva anusuyyare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2021Msdiv 67Anussaritvā sambuddhaṃ,
padumuttaranāyakaṃ;
Tassa te munino puttā,
vippanaṭṭhā mahāvane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2022Msdiv 68Bhavanā oruhitvāna,
agamiṃ bhikkhusantikaṃ;
Tesaṃ maggañca ācikkhiṃ,
bhojanañca adāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2023Msdiv 69Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2024Msdiv 70Sacakkhū nāma nāmena,
dvādasa cakkavattino;
Sattaratanasampannā,
pañcakappasate ito.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2025Msdiv 71Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2026Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maggasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2027Maggasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
