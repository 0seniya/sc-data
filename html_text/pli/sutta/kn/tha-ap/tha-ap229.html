7. Sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2944Msdiv 34“Uyyānabhūmiṃ niyyanto,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Vaṭaṃsakaṃ gahetvāna,
sovaṇṇaṃ sādhunimmitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2945Msdiv 35Sīghaṃ tato samāruyha,
hatthikkhandhagato ahaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2946Msdiv 36Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2947Msdiv 37Sattavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Mahāpatāpanāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2948Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2949Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2950Sovaṇṇavaṭaṃsakiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
