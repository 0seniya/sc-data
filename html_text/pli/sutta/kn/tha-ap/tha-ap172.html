10. Pañcaṅguliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2500Msdiv 50“Tisso nāmāsi bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Pavisati gandhakuṭiṃ,
vihārakusalo muni.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2501Msdiv 51Sugandhamālamādāya,
agamāsiṃ jinantikaṃ;
Apasaddo ca sambuddhe,
pañcaṅgulimadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2502Msdiv 52Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ gandhamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pañcaṅgulissidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2503Msdiv 53Dvesattatimhito kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sayampabho;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2504Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2098)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2505Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pañcaṅguliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2506Pañcaṅguliyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2507Supāricariyavaggo sattarasamo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2508Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2509Supāricari kaṇaverī,
khajjako desapūjako;
Kaṇikāro sappidado,
yūthiko dussadāyako;
Māḷo ca pañcaṅguliko,
catupaññāsa gāthakāti.
