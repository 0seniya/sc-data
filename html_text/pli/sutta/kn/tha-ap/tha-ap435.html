3 Pāṭihīrasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5693SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5693Msdiv 26“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Vasīsatasahassehi,
nagaraṃ pāvisī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5694SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5694Msdiv 27Nagaraṃ pavisantassa,
upasantassa tādino;
Ratanāni pajjotiṃsu,
nigghoso āsi tāvade.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5695SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5695Msdiv 28Buddhassa ānubhāvena,
bherī vajjumaghaṭṭitā;
Sayaṃ vīṇā pavajjanti,
buddhassa pavisato puraṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5696Msdiv 29Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5696Msdiv 29Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ namassāmi,
padumuttaramahāmuniṃ;
Pāṭihīrañca passitvā,
tattha cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5697Msdiv 30SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5697Msdiv 30Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthusampadā;
Acetanāpi turiyā,
sayameva pavajjare.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5698SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5698Msdiv 31Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5699SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5699Msdiv 32Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5700SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5700Msdiv 33Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5701SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5701Msdiv 34Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
