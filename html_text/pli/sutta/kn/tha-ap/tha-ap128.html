6. Kusumāsaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2128Msdiv 65“Nagare dhaññavatiyā,
ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;
Lakkhaṇe itihāse ca,
sanighaṇḍusakeṭubhe.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2129Msdiv 66Padako veyyākaraṇo,
nimittakovido ahaṃ;
Mante ca sisse vācesiṃ,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2130Msdiv 67Pañca uppalahatthāni,
piṭṭhiyaṃ ṭhapitāni me;
Āhutiṃ yiṭṭhukāmohaṃ,
pitumātusamāgame.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2131Msdiv 68Tadā vipassī bhagavā,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Obhāsento disā sabbā,
āgacchati narāsabho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2132Msdiv 69Āsanaṃ paññapetvāna,
nimantetvā mahāmuniṃ;
Santharitvāna taṃ pupphaṃ,
abhinesiṃ sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2133Msdiv 70Yaṃ me atthi sake gehe,
āmisaṃ paccupaṭṭhitaṃ;
Tāhaṃ buddhassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2134Msdiv 71Bhuttāviṃ kālamaññāya,
pupphahatthamadāsahaṃ;
Anumoditvāna sabbaññū,
pakkāmi uttarāmukho.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2135Msdiv 72Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2136Msdiv 73Anantaraṃ ito kappe,
rājāhuṃ varadassano;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2137Msdiv 74Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2138Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kusumāsaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_2139Kusumāsaniyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
