3. Paramannadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3444Msdiv 9“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
udayantaṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Vipassiṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3445Msdiv 10Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
abhinesiṃ sakaṃ gharaṃ;
Abhinetvāna sambuddhaṃ,
paramannamadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3446Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
paramannamadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paramannassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3447Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3448Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paramannadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3449Paramannadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
