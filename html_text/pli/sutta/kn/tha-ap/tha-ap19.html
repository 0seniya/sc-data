7 Upasenavaṅgantaputtattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_793Msdiv 86“Padumuttaraṃ bhagavantaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pabbhāramhi nisīdantaṃ,
upagacchiṃ naruttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_794Msdiv 87Kaṇikārapupphaṃ disvā,
vaṇṭe chetvānahaṃ tadā;
Alaṅkaritvā chattamhi,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_795Msdiv 88Piṇḍapātañca pādāsiṃ,
paramannaṃ subhojanaṃ;
Buddhena navame tattha,
samaṇe aṭṭha bhojayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_796Msdiv 89Anumodi mahāvīro,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Iminā chattadānena,
paramannapavecchanā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_797Msdiv 90Tena cittappasādena,
sampattimanubhossasi;
Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_798Msdiv 91Ekavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_799Msdiv 92Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_800Msdiv 93Sāsane dibbamānamhi,
manussattaṃ gamissati;
Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_801Msdiv 94Upasenoti nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako;
Samantapāsādikattā,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapessati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_802Msdiv 95Carimaṃ vattate mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhiniṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_803Msdiv 96Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_804Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaseno vaṅgantaputto thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_805Upasenavaṅgantaputtattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
Tatiyabhāṇavāraṃ.
