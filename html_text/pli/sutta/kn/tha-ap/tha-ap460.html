8. Jātipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5941SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5941Msdiv 49“Parinibbute bhagavati,
padumuttare mahāyase;
Pupphavaṭaṃsake katvā,
sarīramabhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5942SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5942Msdiv 50Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
nimmānaṃ agamāsahaṃ;
Devalokagato santo,
puññakammaṃ sarāmahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5943SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5943Msdiv 51Ambarā pupphavasso me,
sabbakālaṃ pavassati;
Saṃsarāmi manusse ce,
rājā homi mahāyaso.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5944SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5944Msdiv 52Tahiṃ kusumavasso me,
abhivassati sabbadā;
Tasseva pupphapūjāya,
vāhasā sabbadassino.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5945SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5945Msdiv 53Ayaṃ pacchimako mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Ajjāpi pupphavasso me,
abhivassati sabbadā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5946SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5946Msdiv 54Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dehapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5947SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5947Msdiv 55Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5948SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5948Msdiv 56Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5949SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5949Msdiv 57Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5950Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā jātipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5951Jātipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
