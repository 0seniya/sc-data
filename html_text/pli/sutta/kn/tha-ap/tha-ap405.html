3. Mettaguttheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4895Msdiv 45“Himavantassāvidūre,
asoko nāma pabbato;
Tatthāsi assamo mayhaṃ,
vissakammena māpito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4896Msdiv 46Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
aggo kāruṇiko muni;
Nivāsayitvā pubbaṇhe,
piṇḍāya me upāgami.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4897Msdiv 47Upāgataṃ mahāvīraṃ,
sumedhaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Paggayha sugatapattaṃ,
sappitelaṃ apūrayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4898Msdiv 48Datvānahaṃ buddhaseṭṭhe,
sumedhe lokanāyake;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4899Msdiv 49Iminā sappidānena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Devabhūto manusso vā,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4900Msdiv 50Vinipātaṃ vivajjetvā,
saṃsarāmi bhavābhave;
Tattha cittaṃ paṇidhitvā,
labhāmi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4901Msdiv 51‘Lābhā tuyhaṃ suladdhaṃ te,
yaṃ maṃ addakkhi brāhmaṇa;
Mama dassanamāgamma,
arahattaṃ bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4902Msdiv 52Vissattho hohi mā bhāyi,
adhigantvā mahāyasaṃ;
Mamañhi sappiṃ datvāna,
parimokkhasi jātiyā’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4903Msdiv 53Iminā sappidānena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Devabhūto manusso vā,
labhase vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4904Msdiv 54Iminā sappidānena,
mettacittavatāya ca;
Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
devaloke ramissasi.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4905Msdiv 55Aṭṭhatiṃsatikkhattuñca,
devarājā bhavissasi;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4906Msdiv 56Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissasi;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
jambumaṇḍassa issaro.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4907Msdiv 57Mahāsamuddovakkhobho,
duddharo pathavī yathā;
Evamevaṃ ca te bhogā,
appameyyā bhavissare.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4908Msdiv 58Saṭṭhikoṭī hiraññassa,
cajitvā pabbajiṃ ahaṃ;
Kiṃ kusalaṃ gavesanto,
bāvariṃ upasaṅkamiṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4909Msdiv 59Tattha mante adhīyāmi,
chaḷaṅgaṃ nāma lakkhaṇaṃ;
Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamaṃ,
uppajji tvaṃ mahāmuni.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4910Msdiv 60Tava dassanakāmohaṃ,
āgatomhi mahāmuni;
Tava dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4911Msdiv 61Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
sappiṃ buddhassadāsahaṃ;
Etthantare nābhijāne,
sappiṃ viññāpitaṃ mayā.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_4912Msdiv 62Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
uppajjati yadicchakaṃ;
Cittamaññāya nibbattaṃ,
sabbe santappayāmahaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_4913Msdiv 63Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthu sampadā;
Thokañhi sappiṃ datvāna,
appameyyaṃ labhāmahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_4914Msdiv 64Mahāsamudde udakaṃ,
yāvatā nerupassato;
Mama sappiṃ upādāya,
kalabhāgaṃ na hessati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_4915Msdiv 65Yāvatā cakkavāḷassa,
kariyantassa rāsito;
Mama nibbattavatthānaṃ,
okāso so na sammati.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_4916Msdiv 66Pabbatarājā himavā,
pavaropi siluccayo;
Mamānulittagandhassa,
upanidhiṃ na hessati.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_4917Msdiv 67Vatthaṃ gandhañca sappiñca,
aññañca diṭṭhadhammikaṃ;
Asaṅkhatañca nibbānaṃ,
sappidānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_4918Msdiv 68Satipaṭṭhānasayano,
samādhijhānagocaro;
Bojjhaṅgabhojano ajja,
sappidānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_4919Msdiv 69Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_4920Msdiv 70Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_4921Msdiv 71Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_4922Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mettagū thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_4923Mettaguttherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
