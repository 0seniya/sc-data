6. Macchadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3190SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3190Msdiv 23“Candabhāgānadītīre,
ukkuso āsahaṃ tadā;
Mahantaṃ macchaṃ paggayha,
siddhatthamunino adaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3191SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3191Msdiv 24Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ macchamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
macchadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3192SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3192Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3193Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā macchadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3194Macchadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
