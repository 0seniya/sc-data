6 Sandhitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2858Msdiv 27“Assatthe haritobhāse,
saṃvirūḷhamhi pādape;
Ekaṃ buddhagataṃ saññaṃ,
alabhiṃhaṃ patissato.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2859Msdiv 28Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Tassā saññāya vāhasā,
patto me āsavakkhayo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2860Msdiv 29Ito terasakappamhi,
dhaniṭṭho nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.211Ms 20Ap1_2861Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2862Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sandhito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2863Sandhitattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
