4 Tikiṅkaṇipūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3913Msdiv 27“Himavantassāvidūre,
bhūtagaṇo nāma pabbato;
Tatthaddasaṃ paṃsukūlaṃ,
dumaggamhi vilaggitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3914Msdiv 28Tīṇi kiṅkaṇipupphāni,
ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
paṃsukūlaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3915Msdiv 29Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tiṇṇaṃ pupphānidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3916Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3917Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tikiṅkaṇipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3918Tikiṅkaṇipūjakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
