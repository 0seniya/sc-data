9. Veyyāvaccakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1831SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1831Msdiv 51“Vipassissa bhagavato,
mahāpūgagaṇo ahu;
Veyyāvaccakaro āsiṃ,
sabbakiccesu vāvaṭo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1832SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1832Msdiv 52Deyyadhammo ca me natthi,
sugatassa mahesino;
Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1833SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1833Msdiv 53Ekanavutito kappe,
veyyāvaccaṃ akāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
veyyāvaccassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1834SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1834Msdiv 54Ito ca aṭṭhame kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sucintito;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1835SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1835Msdiv 55Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1836Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā veyyāvaccako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1837Veyyāvaccakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
