4. Pañcasīlasamādāniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_999Msdiv 134“Nagare candavatiyā,
bhatako āsahaṃ tadā;
Parakammāyane yutto,
pabbajjaṃ na labhāmahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1000Msdiv 135Mahandhakārapihitā,
tividhaggīhi ḍayhare;
Kena nu kho upāyena,
visaṃyutto bhave ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1001Msdiv 136Deyyadhammo ca me natthi,
varāko bhatako ahaṃ;
Yannūnāhaṃ pañcasīlaṃ,
rakkheyyaṃ paripūrayaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1002Msdiv 137Anomadassissa munino,
nisabho nāma sāvako;
Tamahaṃ upasaṅkamma,
pañcasikkhāpadaggahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1003Msdiv 138Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā pañcasīlāni,
paripuṇṇāni gopayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1004Msdiv 139Maccukāle ca sampatte,
devā assāsayanti maṃ;
‘Ratho sahassayutto te,
mārisāyaṃ upaṭṭhito’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1005Msdiv 140Vattante carime citte,
mama sīlaṃ anussariṃ;
Tena kammena sukatena,
tāvatiṃsaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.77Ms 20Ap1_1006Msdiv 141Tiṃsakkhattuñca devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Dibbasukhaṃ anubhaviṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1007Msdiv 142Pañcasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1008Msdiv 143Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Pure vesāliyaṃ jāto,
mahāsāle suaḍḍhake.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1009Msdiv 144Vassūpanāyike kāle,
dippante jinasāsane;
Mātā ca me pitā ceva,
pañcasikkhāpadaggahuṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1010Msdiv 145Saha sutvānahaṃ sīlaṃ,
mama sīlaṃ anussariṃ;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1011Msdiv 146Jātiyā pañcavassena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Upasampādayi buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1012Msdiv 147Paripuṇṇāni gopetvā,
pañcasikkhāpadānahaṃ;
Aparimeyye ito kappe,
vinipātaṃ na gacchahaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_1013Msdiv 148Svāhaṃ yasamanubhaviṃ,
tesaṃ sīlāna vāhasā;
Kappakoṭimpi kittento,
kittaye ekadesakaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_1014Msdiv 149Pañca sīlāni gopetvā,
tayo hetū labhāmahaṃ;
Dīghāyuko mahābhogo,
tikkhapañño bhavāmahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_1015Msdiv 150Saṅkittento ca sabbesaṃ,
adhimattañca porisaṃ;
Bhavābhave saṃsaritvā,
ete ṭhāne labhāmahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_1016Msdiv 151Aparimeyyasīlesu,
vattantā jinasāvakā;
Bhavesu yadi rajjeyyuṃ,
vipāko kīdiso bhave.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_1017Msdiv 152Suciṇṇaṃ me pañcasīlaṃ,
bhatakena tapassinā;
Tena sīlenahaṃ ajja,
mocayiṃ sabbabandhanā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_1018Msdiv 153Aparimeyye ito kappe,
pañca sīlāni gopayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pañcasīlānidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_1019Msdiv 154Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_1020Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pañcasīlasamādāniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_1021Pañcasīlasamādāniyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
