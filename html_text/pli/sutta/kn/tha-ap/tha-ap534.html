2. Vakkalittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6977Msdiv 28“Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako;
Anomanāmo amito,
nāmena padumuttaro.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6978Msdiv 29Padumākāravadano,
padumāmalasucchavī;
Lokenānupalittova,
toyena padumaṃ yathā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6979Msdiv 30Vīro padumapattakkho,
kanto ca padumaṃ yathā;
Padumuttaragandhova,
tasmā so padumuttaro.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6980Msdiv 31Lokajeṭṭho ca nimmāno,
andhānaṃ nayanūpamo;
Santaveso guṇanidhi,
karuṇāmatisāgaro.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6981Msdiv 32Sa kadāci mahāvīro,
brahmāsurasuraccito;
Sadevamanujākiṇṇe,
janamajjhe jinuttamo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6982Msdiv 33Vadanena sugandhena,
madhurena rutena ca;
Rañjayaṃ parisaṃ sabbaṃ,
santhavī sāvakaṃ sakaṃ.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 2.466Ms 20Ap1_6983Msdiv 34Saddhādhimutto sumati,
mama dassanalālaso;
Natthi etādiso añño,
yathāyaṃ bhikkhu vakkali.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6984Msdiv 35Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
nagare brāhmaṇatrajo;
Hutvā sutvā ca taṃ vākyaṃ,
taṃ ṭhānamabhirocayiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6985Msdiv 36Sasāvakaṃ taṃ vimalaṃ,
nimantetvā tathāgataṃ;
Sattāhaṃ bhojayitvāna,
dussehacchādayiṃ tadā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6986Msdiv 37Nipacca sirasā tassa,
anantaguṇasāgare;
Nimuggo pītisampuṇṇo,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6987Msdiv 38‘Yo so tayā santhavito,
ito sattamake muni;
Bhikkhu saddhāvataṃ aggo,
tādiso homahaṃ mune’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6988Msdiv 39Evaṃ vutte, mahāvīro,
anāvaraṇadassano;
Imaṃ vākyaṃ udīresi,
parisāya mahāmuni.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6989Msdiv 40‘Passathetaṃ māṇavakaṃ,
pītamaṭṭhanivāsanaṃ;
Hemayaññopacitaṅgaṃ,
jananettamanoharaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6990Msdiv 41Eso anāgataddhāne,
gotamassa mahesino;
Aggo saddhādhimuttānaṃ,
sāvakoyaṃ bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6991Msdiv 42Devabhūto manusso vā,
sabbasantāpavajjito;
Sabbabhogaparibyūḷho,
sukhito saṃsarissati.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6992Msdiv 43Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6993Msdiv 44Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Vakkali nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6994Msdiv 45Tena kammavisesena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6995Msdiv 46Sabbattha sukhito hutvā,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Sāvatthiyaṃ pure jāto,
kule aññatare ahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6996Msdiv 47Nonītasukhumālaṃ maṃ,
jātapallavakomalaṃ;
Mandaṃ uttānasayanaṃ,
pisācībhayatajjitā.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6997Msdiv 48Pādamūle mahesissa,
sāyesuṃ dīnamānasā;
‘Imaṃ dadāma te nātha,
saraṇaṃ hohi nāyaka’.

SC 22PTS vp Pali 2.467Ms 20Ap1_6998Msdiv 49Tadā paṭiggahi so maṃ,
bhītānaṃ saraṇo muni;
Jālinā cakkaṅkitena,
mudukomalapāṇinā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6999Msdiv 50Tadā pabhuti tenāhaṃ,
arakkheyyena rakkhito;
Sabbaveravinimutto,
sukhena parivuddhito.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7000Msdiv 51Sugatena vinā bhūto,
ukkaṇṭhāmi muhuttakaṃ;
Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7001Msdiv 52Sabbapāramisambhūtaṃ,
nīlakkhinayanaṃ varaṃ;
Rūpaṃ sabbasubhākiṇṇaṃ,
atitto viharāmahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7002Msdiv 53Buddharūparatiṃ ñatvā,
tadā ovadi maṃ jino;
‘Alaṃ vakkali kiṃ rūpe,
ramase bālanandite.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7003Msdiv 54Yo hi passati saddhammaṃ,
so maṃ passati paṇḍito;
Apassamāno saddhammaṃ,
maṃ passampi na passati.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7004Msdiv 55Anantādīnavo kāyo,
visarukkhasamūpamo;
Āvāso sabbarogānaṃ,
puñjo dukkhassa kevalo.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7005Msdiv 56Nibbindiya tato rūpe,
khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ;
Passa upakkilesānaṃ,
sukhenantaṃ gamissasi’.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7006Msdiv 57Evaṃ tenānusiṭṭhohaṃ,
nāyakena hitesinā;
Gijjhakūṭaṃ samāruyha,
jhāyāmi girikandare.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_7007Msdiv 58Ṭhito pabbatapādamhi,
assāsayi mahāmuni;
Vakkalīti jino vācaṃ,
taṃ sutvā mudito ahaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_7008Msdiv 59Pakkhandiṃ selapabbhāre,
anekasataporise;
Tadā buddhānubhāvena,
sukheneva mahiṃ gato.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_7009Msdiv 60Punopi dhammaṃ deseti,
khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ;
Tamahaṃ dhammamaññāya,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_7010Msdiv 61Sumahāparisamajjhe,
tadā maṃ caraṇantago;
Aggaṃ saddhādhimuttānaṃ,
paññapesi mahāmati.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_7011Msdiv 62Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 36PTS vp Pali 2.468Ms 20Ap1_7012Msdiv 63Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_7013Msdiv 64Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_7014Msdiv 65Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_7015Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vakkalitthero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_7016Vakkalittherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
