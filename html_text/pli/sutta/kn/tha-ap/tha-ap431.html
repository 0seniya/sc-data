9 Sovaṇṇakiṅkaṇiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.388Ms 20Ap1_5626Msdiv 140“Saddhāya abhinikkhamma,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Vākacīradharo āsiṃ,
tapokammamapassito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5627Msdiv 141Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Uppajji tamhi samaye,
tārayanto mahājanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5628Msdiv 142Balañca vata me khīṇaṃ,
byādhinā paramena taṃ;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ saritvāna,
puline thūpamuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5629Msdiv 143Karitvā haṭṭhacittohaṃ,
sahatthena samokiriṃ;
Soṇṇakiṅkaṇipupphāni,
udaggamanaso ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5630Msdiv 144Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
thūpaṃ paricariṃ ahaṃ;
Tena cetopasādena,
atthadassissa tādino.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5631Msdiv 145Devalokaṃ gato santo,
labhāmi vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo tatthāsiṃ,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5632Msdiv 146Asītikoṭiyo mayhaṃ,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sadā mayhaṃ upaṭṭhanti,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5633Msdiv 147Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
bheriyo paṇavāni ca;
Saṅkhā ca ḍiṇḍimā tattha,
vaggū vajjanti dundubhī.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5634Msdiv 148Cullāsītisahassāni,
hatthino samalaṅkatā;
Tidhāpabhinnamātaṅgā,
kuñjarā saṭṭhihāyanā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5635Msdiv 149Hemajālābhisañchannā,
upaṭṭhānaṃ karonti me;
Balakāye gaje ceva,
ūnatā me na vijjati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5636Msdiv 150Soṇṇakiṅkaṇipupphānaṃ,
vipākaṃ anubhomahaṃ;
Aṭṭhapaññāsakkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5637Msdiv 151Ekasattatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ ekasataṃ,
mahiyā kārayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5638Msdiv 152So dāni amataṃ patto,
asaṅkhataṃ sududdasaṃ;
Saṃyojanaparikkhīṇo,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5639Msdiv 153Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 2.389Ms 20Ap1_5640Msdiv 154Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5641Msdiv 155Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5642Msdiv 156Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5643Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sovaṇṇakiṅkaṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5644Sovaṇṇakiṅkaṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
