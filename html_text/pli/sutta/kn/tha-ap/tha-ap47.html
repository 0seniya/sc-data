5. Pañcahatthiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1293Msdiv 77“Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
gacchate antarāpaṇe;
Okkhittacakkhu mitabhāṇī,
satimā saṃvutindriyo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1294Msdiv 78Pañca uppalahatthāni,
āveḷatthaṃ ahaṃsu me;
Tena buddhaṃ apūjesiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.98Ms 20Ap1_1295Msdiv 79Āropitā ca te pupphā,
chadanaṃ assu satthuno;
Samādhiṃsu mahānāgaṃ,
sissā ācariyaṃ yathā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1296Msdiv 80Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1297Msdiv 81Ito vīsakappasate,
ahesuṃ pañca khattiyā;
Hatthiyā nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1298Msdiv 82Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1299Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pañcahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1300Pañcahatthiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
