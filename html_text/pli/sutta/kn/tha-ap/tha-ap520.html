8. Ghosasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6748Msdiv 44“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe vipine ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
devasaṅghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6749Msdiv 45Catusaccaṃ pakāsentaṃ,
desentaṃ amataṃ padaṃ;
Assosiṃ madhuraṃ dhammaṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6750Msdiv 46Ghose cittaṃ pasādesiṃ,
asamappaṭipuggale;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
uttariṃ duttaraṃ bhavaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6751Msdiv 47Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ghosasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6752Msdiv 48Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6753Msdiv 49Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6754Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6755Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ghosasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6756Ghosasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
