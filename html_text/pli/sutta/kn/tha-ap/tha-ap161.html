9. Punnāgapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2414Msdiv 46“Kānanaṃ vanamogayha,
vasāmi luddako ahaṃ;
Punnāgaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ anussariṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2415Msdiv 47Taṃ pupphaṃ ocinitvāna,
sugandhaṃ gandhitaṃ subhaṃ;
Thūpaṃ karitvā puline,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2416Msdiv 48Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2417Msdiv 49Ekamhi navute kappe,
eko āsiṃ tamonudo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2418Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2419Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā punnāgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2420Punnāgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
