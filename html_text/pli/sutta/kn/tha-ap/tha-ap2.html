2. Paccekabuddhaapadāna

SC 1SC 1Ms 20Ap1_86Atha paccekabuddhāpadānaṃ suṇātha—

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_87SC 2Ms 20Ap1_87Msdiv 83“Tathāgataṃ jetavane vasantaṃ,
Apucchi vedehamunī nataṅgo;
‘Paccekabuddhā kira nāma honti,
Bhavanti te hetubhi kehi vīra’.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_88SC 3Ms 20Ap1_88Msdiv 84Tadāha sabbaññuvaro mahesi,
Ānandabhaddaṃ madhurassarena;
‘Ye pubbabuddhesu katādhikārā,
Aladdhamokkhā jinasāsanesu.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_89SC 4Ms 20Ap1_89Msdiv 85Teneva saṃvegamukhena dhīrā,
Vināpi buddhehi sutikkhapaññā;
Ārammaṇenāpi parittakena,
Paccekabodhiṃ anupāpuṇanti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_90SC 5Ms 20Ap1_90Msdiv 86Sabbamhi lokamhi mamaṃ ṭhapetvā,
Paccekabuddhehi samova natthi;
Tesaṃ imaṃ vaṇṇapadesamattaṃ,
Vakkhāmahaṃ sādhu mahāmunīnaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_91SC 6Ms 20Ap1_91Msdiv 87Sayameva buddhānaṃ mahāisīnaṃ,
Sādhūni vākyāni madhūva khuddaṃ;
Anuttaraṃ bhesajaṃ patthayantā,
Suṇātha sabbesu pasannacittā’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_92SC 7Ms 20Ap1_92Msdiv 88Paccekabuddhānaṃ samāgatānaṃ,
Paramparaṃ byākaraṇāni yāni;
Ādīnavo yañca virāgavatthuṃ,
Yathā ca bodhiṃ anupāpuṇiṃsu.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_93SC 8Ms 20Ap1_93Msdiv 89Sarāgavatthūsu virāgasaññī,
Rattamhi lokamhi virattacittā;
Hitvā papañce jiya phanditāni,
Tatheva bodhiṃ anupāpuṇiṃsu.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_94SC 9Ms 20Ap1_94Msdiv 90Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṃ,
Aviheṭhayaṃ aññatarampi tesaṃ;
Mettena cittena hitānukampī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_95SC 10Ms 20Ap1_95Msdiv 91Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṃ,
Aviheṭhayaṃ aññatarampi tesaṃ;
Na puttamiccheyya kuto sahāyaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_96SC 11Ms 20Ap1_96Msdiv 92‘Saṃsaggajātassa bhavanti snehā,
Snehanvayaṃ dukkhamidaṃ pahoti;
Ādīnavaṃ snehajaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_97SC 12Ms 20Ap1_97Msdiv 93‘Mitte suhajje anukampamāno,
Hāpeti atthaṃ paṭibaddhacitto;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ santhave pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_98SC 13Ms 20Ap1_98Msdiv 94‘Vaṃso visālova yathā visatto,
Puttesu dāresu ca yā apekkhā;
Vaṃse kaḷīrova asajjamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_99SC 14Ms 20Ap1_99Msdiv 95‘Migo araññamhi yathā abaddho,
Yenicchakaṃ gacchati gocarāya;
Viññū naro seritaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_100SC 15Ms 20Ap1_100Msdiv 96‘Āmantanā hoti sahāyamajjhe,
Vāse ca ṭhāne gamane cārikāya;
Anabhijjhitaṃ seritaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_101SC 16Ms 20Ap1_101Msdiv 97‘Khiḍḍā ratī hoti sahāyamajjhe,
Puttesu pemaṃ vipulañca hoti;
Piyavippayogaṃ vijigucchamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_102SC 17Ms 20Ap1_102Msdiv 98‘Cātuddiso appaṭigho ca hoti,
Santussamāno itarītarena;
Parissayānaṃ sahitā achambhī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_103SC 18Ms 20Ap1_103Msdiv 99‘Dussaṅgahā pabbajitāpi eke,
Atho gahaṭṭhā gharamāvasantā;
Appossukko paraputtesu hutvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_104SC 19Ms 20Ap1_104Msdiv 100‘Oropayitvā gihibyañjanāni,
Sañchinnapatto yathā koviḷāro;
Chetvāna vīro gihibandhanāni,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_105SC 20Ms 20Ap1_105Msdiv 101‘Sace labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāridhīraṃ;
Abhibhuyya sabbāni parissayāni,
Careyya tenattamano satīmā’.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_106SC 21Ms 20Ap1_106Msdiv 102‘No ce labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāri dhīraṃ;
Rājāva raṭṭhaṃ vijitaṃ pahāya,
Eko care mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo’.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_107SC 22Ms 20Ap1_107Msdiv 103‘Addhā pasaṃsāma sahāyasampadaṃ,
Seṭṭhā samā sevitabbā sahāyā;
Ete aladdhā anavajjabhojī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_108SC 23Ms 20Ap1_108Msdiv 104‘Disvā suvaṇṇassa pabhassarāni,
Kammāraputtena suniṭṭhitāni;
Saṅghaṭṭamānāni duve bhujasmiṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_109SC 24Ms 20Ap1_109Msdiv 105‘Evaṃ dutīyena sahā mamassa,
Vācābhilāpo abhisajjanā vā;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ āyatiṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_110SC 25Ms 20Ap1_110Msdiv 106‘Kāmā hi citrā madhurā manoramā,
Virūparūpena mathenti cittaṃ;
Ādīnavaṃ kāmaguṇesu disvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_111SC 26Ms 20Ap1_111Msdiv 107‘Ītī ca gaṇḍo ca upaddavo ca,
Rogo ca sallañca bhayañca metaṃ;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ kāmaguṇesu disvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_112SC 27Ms 20Ap1_112Msdiv 108‘Sītañca uṇhañca khudaṃ pipāsaṃ,
Vātātape ḍaṃsasarīsape ca;
Sabbānipetāni abhibbhavitvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_113SC 28Ms 20Ap1_113Msdiv 109‘Nāgova yūthāni vivajjayitvā,
Sañjātakhandho padumī uḷāro;
Yathābhirantaṃ viharaṃ araññe,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_114SC 29Ms 20Ap1_114Msdiv 110‘Aṭṭhāna taṃ saṅgaṇikāratassa,
Yaṃ phassaye sāmayikaṃ vimuttiṃ;
Ādiccabandhussa vaco nisamma,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_115SC 30Ms 20Ap1_115Msdiv 111‘Diṭṭhīvisūkāni upātivatto,
Patto niyāmaṃ paṭiladdhamaggo;
Uppannañāṇomhi anaññaneyyo,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_116SC 31Ms 20Ap1_116Msdiv 112‘Nillolupo nikkuho nippipāso,
Nimmakkha niddhantakasāvamoho;
Nirāsayo sabbaloke bhavitvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_117SC 32Ms 20Ap1_117Msdiv 113‘Pāpaṃ sahāyaṃ parivajjayetha,
Anatthadassiṃ visame niviṭṭhaṃ;
Sayaṃ na seve pasutaṃ pamattaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_118SC 33Ms 20Ap1_118Msdiv 114‘Bahussutaṃ dhammadharaṃ bhajetha,
Mittaṃ uḷāraṃ paṭibhānavantaṃ;
Aññāya atthāni vineyya kaṅkhaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_119SC 34Ms 20Ap1_119Msdiv 115‘Khiḍḍaṃ ratiṃ kāmasukhañca loke,
Analaṅkaritvā anapekkhamāno;
Vibhūsaṭṭhānā virato saccavādī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_120SC 35Ms 20Ap1_120Msdiv 116‘Puttañca dāraṃ pitarañca mātaraṃ,
Dhanāni dhaññāni ca bandhavāni;
Hitvāna kāmāni yathodhikāni,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_121SC 36Ms 20Ap1_121Msdiv 117‘Saṅgo eso parittamettha sokhyaṃ,
Appassādo dukkhamevettha bhiyyo;
Gaḷo eso iti ñatvā matimā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_122SC 37Ms 20Ap1_122Msdiv 118‘Sandālayitvāna saṃyojanāni,
Jālaṃva bhetvā salilambucārī;
Aggīva daḍḍhaṃ anivattamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_123SC 38Ms 20Ap1_123Msdiv 119‘Okkhittacakkhū na ca pādalolo,

Anavassuto apariḍayhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo’.

SC 40Ms 20Ap1_125SC 40Ms 20Ap1_125Msdiv 121‘Rasesu gedhaṃ akaraṃ alolo,
