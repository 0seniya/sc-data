8. Sattāhapabbajitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3333Msdiv 33“Vipassissa bhagavato,
saṃgho sakkatamānito;
Byasanaṃ me anuppattaṃ,
ñātibhedo pure ahu.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3334Msdiv 34Pabbajjaṃ upagantvāna,
byasanupasamāyahaṃ;
Sattāhābhirato tattha,
satthusāsanakamyatā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3335Msdiv 35Ekanavutito kappe,
yamahaṃ pabbajiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pabbajjāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3336Msdiv 36Sattasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
satta āsuṃ mahīpatī;
Sunikkhamāti ñāyanti,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3337Msdiv 37Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3338Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattāhapabbajito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3339Sattāhapabbajitattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
