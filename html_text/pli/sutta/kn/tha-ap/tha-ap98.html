6. Tipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.136Ms 20Ap1_1805Msdiv 31“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Pāṭaliṃ haritaṃ disvā,
tīṇi pupphāni okiriṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1806Msdiv 32Patitapattāni gaṇhitvā,
bahi chaḍḍesahaṃ tadā;
Antosuddhaṃ bahisuddhaṃ,
suvimuttaṃ anāsavaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1807Msdiv 33Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
vipassiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Pāṭaliṃ abhivādetvā,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1808Msdiv 34Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ bodhimabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1809Msdiv 35Samantapāsādikā nāma,
terasāsiṃsu rājino;
Ito tettiṃsakappamhi,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1810Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1811Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1812Tipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
