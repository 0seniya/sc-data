9. Tikicchakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2564SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2564Msdiv 39“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
vejjo āsiṃ susikkhito;
Āturānaṃ sadukkhānaṃ,
mahājanasukhāvaho.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2565SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2565Msdiv 40Byādhitaṃ samaṇaṃ disvā,
sīlavantaṃ mahājutiṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
bhesajjamadadiṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2566SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2566Msdiv 41Arogo āsi teneva,
samaṇo saṃvutindriyo;
Asoko nāma nāmena,
upaṭṭhāko vipassino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2567SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2567Msdiv 42Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ osadhamadāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhesajjassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2568SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2568Msdiv 43Ito ca aṭṭhame kappe,
sabbosadhasanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2569SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2569Msdiv 44Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2570Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tikicchako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2571Tikicchakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
