4. Padapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1876Msdiv 19“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
jātipupphamadāsahaṃ;
Pādesu satta pupphāni,
hāsenokiritāni me.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1877Msdiv 20Tena kammenahaṃ ajja,
abhibhomi narāmare;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1878Msdiv 21Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1879Msdiv 22Samantagandhanāmāsuṃ,
terasa cakkavattino;
Ito pañcamake kappe,
cāturantā janādhipā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1880Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1881Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1882Padapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
