2 Kapitthaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6699SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6699Msdiv 7“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Kapitthaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6700SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6700Msdiv 8Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6701SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6701Msdiv 9Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6702SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6702Msdiv 10Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6703SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6703Msdiv 11Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6704Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6704Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kapitthaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6705Kapitthaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
