9 Gaṇṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2160Msdiv 91“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
ārāmā abhinikkhami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2161Msdiv 92Disvānahaṃ buddhaseṭṭhaṃ,
sabbaññuṃ tamanāsakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gaṇṭhipupphaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2162Msdiv 93Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
puna vandiṃ tathāgataṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2163Msdiv 94Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2164Msdiv 95Ekatālīsito kappe,
caraṇo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2165Msdiv 96Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2166Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gaṇṭhipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2167Gaṇṭhipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
