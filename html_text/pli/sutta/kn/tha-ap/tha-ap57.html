5 Opavayhattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1416SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1416Msdiv 33“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
ājānīyamadāsahaṃ;
Niyyādetvāna sambuddhe,
agamāsiṃ sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1417SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1417Msdiv 34Devalo nāma nāmena,
satthuno aggasāvako;
Varadhammassa dāyādo,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1418SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1418Msdiv 35Sapattabhāro bhagavā,
ājāneyyo na kappati;
Tava saṅkappamaññāya,
adhivāsesi cakkhumā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1419SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1419Msdiv 36Agghāpetvā vātajavaṃ,
sindhavaṃ sīghavāhanaṃ;
Padumuttarabuddhassa,
khamanīyamadāsahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1420SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1420Msdiv 37Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Khamanīyaṃ vātajavaṃ,
cittaṃ nibbattate mama.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1421SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1421Msdiv 38Lābhaṃ tesaṃ suladdhaṃva,
ye labhantupasampadaṃ;
Punapi payirupāseyyaṃ,
buddho loke sace bhave.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1422SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1422Msdiv 39Aṭṭhavīsatikkhattuṃhaṃ,
rājā āsiṃ mahabbalo;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
jambusaṇḍassa issaro.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1423SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1423Msdiv 40Idaṃ pacchimakaṃ mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Pattosmi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1424SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1424Msdiv 41Catutiṃsasahassamhi,
mahātejosi khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1425SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1425Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.
