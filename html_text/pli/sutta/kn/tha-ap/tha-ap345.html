3. Dhajadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3904Msdiv 20“Tisso nāma ahu satthā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Tayopadhikkhaye disvā,
dhajaṃ āropitaṃ mayā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3905Msdiv 21Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3906Msdiv 22Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3907Msdiv 23Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3908Msdiv 24Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3909Msdiv 25Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,
sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;
Khomadussena chādeyyaṃ,
tadā mayhaṃ kate phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3910Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3911Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhajadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3912Dhajadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
