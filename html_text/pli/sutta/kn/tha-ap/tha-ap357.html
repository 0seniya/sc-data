5. Daṇḍadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3995SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3995Msdiv 18“Kānanaṃ vanamogayha,
veḷuṃ chetvānahaṃ tadā;
Ālambanaṃ karitvāna,
saṃghassa adadaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3996SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3996Msdiv 19Tena cittappasādena,
subbate abhivādiya;
Ālambanampi datvāna,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3997SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3997Msdiv 20Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ daṇḍamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
daṇḍadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3998SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3998Msdiv 21Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3999Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā daṇḍadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4000Daṇḍadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
