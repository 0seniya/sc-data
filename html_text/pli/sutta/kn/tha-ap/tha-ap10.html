10. Piṇḍolabhāradvājattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_635SC 1Ms 20Ap1_635Msdiv 613“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Purato himavantassa,
cittakūṭe vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_636SC 2Ms 20Ap1_636Msdiv 614Abhītarūpo tatthāsiṃ,
migarājā catukkamo;
Tassa saddaṃ suṇitvāna,
vikkhambhanti bahujjanā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_637SC 3Ms 20Ap1_637Msdiv 615Suphullaṃ padumaṃ gayha,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Vuṭṭhitassa samādhimhā,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_638Msdiv 616SC 4Ms 20Ap1_638Msdiv 616Catuddisaṃ namassitvā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ naruttamaṃ;
Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
sīhanādaṃ nadiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_639SC 5Ms 20Ap1_639Msdiv 617Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Sakāsane nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_640SC 6Ms 20Ap1_640Msdiv 618Buddhassa giramaññāya,
Sabbe devā samāgatā;
‘Āgato vadataṃ seṭṭho,
Dhammaṃ sossāma taṃ mayaṃ’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_641SC 7Ms 20Ap1_641Msdiv 619Tesaṃ hāsaparetānaṃ,
purato lokanāyako;
Mama saddaṃ pakittesi,
dīghadassī mahāmuni.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_642SC 8Ms 20Ap1_642Msdiv 620Yenidaṃ padumaṃ dinnaṃ,
sīhanādo ca nādito;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_643SC 9Ms 20Ap1_643Msdiv 621Ito aṭṭhamake kappe,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Sattaratanasampanno,
catudīpamhi issaro.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_644Msdiv 622Kārayissati SC 10Ms 20Ap1_644Msdiv 622Kārayissati issariyaṃ,
mahiyā catusaṭṭhiyā;
Padumo nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_645SC 11Ms 20Ap1_645Msdiv 623Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_646SC 12Ms 20Ap1_646Msdiv 624Pakāsite pāvacane,
brahmabandhu bhavissati;
Brahmaññā abhinikkhamma,
pabbajissati tāvade.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_647SC 13Ms 20Ap1_647Msdiv 625Padhānapahitatto so,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_648SC 14Ms 20Ap1_648Msdiv 626Vijane pantaseyyamhi,
vāḷamigasamākule;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_649SC 15Ms 20Ap1_649Msdiv 627Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 16SC 16Ms 20Ap1_650Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā piṇḍolabhāradvājo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 17SC 17Ms 20Ap1_651Piṇḍolabhāradvājattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
