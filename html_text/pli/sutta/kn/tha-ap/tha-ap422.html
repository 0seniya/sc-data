10. Mañcadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5451SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5451Msdiv 184“Parinibbute kāruṇike,
siddhatthe lokanāyake;
Vitthārike pāvacane,
devamānusasakkate.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5452SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5452Msdiv 185Caṇḍālo āsahaṃ tattha,
āsandipīṭhakārako;
Tena kammena jīvāmi,
tena posemi dārake.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5453SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5453Msdiv 186Āsandiṃ sukataṃ katvā,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Sayamevupagantvāna,
bhikkhusaṃghassadāsahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5454SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5454Msdiv 187Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5455SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5455Msdiv 188Devalokagato santo,
modāmi tidase gaṇe;
Sayanāni mahagghāni,
nibbattanti yadicchakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5456SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5456Msdiv 189Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Asītikkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5457SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5457Msdiv 190Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Sukhito yasavā homi,
mañcadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5458SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5458Msdiv 191Devalokā cavitvāna,
emi ce mānusaṃ bhavaṃ;
Mahārahā susayanā,
sayameva bhavanti me.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5459SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5459Msdiv 192Ayaṃ pacchimako mayhaṃ,
carimo vattate bhavo;
Ajjāpi sayane kāle,
sayanaṃ upatiṭṭhati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5460SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5460Msdiv 193Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
mañcadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5461SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5461Msdiv 194Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5462SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5462Msdiv 195Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5463SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5463Msdiv 196Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (4460)

Nāgapallaviko dīpī,
ucchaṅgi yāgudāyako.

gāthā cekā taduttari.
