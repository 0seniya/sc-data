2. Pānadhidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2829Msdiv 6“Āraññikassa isino,
cirarattatapassino;
Vuddhassa bhāvitattassa,
adāsiṃ pānadhiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2830Msdiv 7Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Dibbayānaṃ anubhomi,
pubbakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2831Msdiv 8Catunnavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pānadhissa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2832Msdiv 9Sattasattatito kappe,
aṭṭha āsiṃsu khattiyā;
Suyānā nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2833Msdiv 10Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2834Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pānadhidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2835Pānadhidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
