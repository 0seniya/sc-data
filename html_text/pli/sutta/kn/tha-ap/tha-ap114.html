2. Sumaṅgalattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1953Msdiv 11“Atthadassī jinavaro,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Vihārā abhinikkhamma,
taḷākaṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1954Msdiv 12Nhatvā pitvā ca sambuddho,
uttaritvekacīvaro;
Aṭṭhāsi bhagavā tattha,
vilokento disodisaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1955Msdiv 13Bhavane upaviṭṭhohaṃ,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
apphoṭesiṃ ahaṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1956Msdiv 14Sataraṃsiṃva jotantaṃ,
pabhāsantaṃva kañcanaṃ;
Naccagīte payuttohaṃ,
pañcaṅgatūriyamhi ca.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1957Msdiv 15Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbe satte abhibhomi,
vipulo hoti me yaso.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.148Ms 20Ap1_1958Msdiv 16Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Attānaṃ tosayitvāna,
pare tosesi tvaṃ muni.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1959Msdiv 17Pariggahe nisīditvā,
hāsaṃ katvāna subbate;
Upaṭṭhahitvā sambuddhaṃ,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1960Msdiv 18Soḷaseto kappasate,
dvinavaekacintitā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1961Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1962Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sumaṅgalo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1963Sumaṅgalattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
