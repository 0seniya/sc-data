9 Soṇakoṭivīsattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4241SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4241Msdiv 49“Vipassino pāvacane,
ekaṃ leṇaṃ mayā kataṃ;
Cātuddisassa saṃghassa,
bandhumārājadhāniyā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4242SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4242Msdiv 50Dussehi bhūmiṃ leṇassa,
santharitvā pariccajiṃ;
Udaggacitto sumano,
akāsiṃ paṇidhiṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4243SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4243Msdiv 51Ārādhayeyyaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
pabbajjañca labheyyahaṃ;
Anuttarañca nibbānaṃ,
phuseyyaṃ santimuttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4244SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4244Msdiv 52Teneva sukkamūlena,
kappe navuti saṃsariṃ;
Devabhūto manusso ca,
katapuñño virocahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4245SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4245Msdiv 53Tato kammāvasesena,
idha pacchimake bhave;
Campāyaṃ aggaseṭṭhissa,
jātomhi ekaputtako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4246SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4246Msdiv 54Jātamattassa me sutvā,
pitu chando ayaṃ ahu;
Dadāmahaṃ kumārassa,
vīsakoṭī anūnakā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4247SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4247Msdiv 55Caturaṅgulā ca me lomā,
jātā pādatale ubho;
Sukhumā mudusamphassā,
tūlāpicusamā subhā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4248SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4248Msdiv 56Atītā navuti kappā,
ayaṃ eko ca uttari;
Nābhijānāmi nikkhitte,
pāde bhūmyā asanthate.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4249SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4249Msdiv 57Ārādhito me sambuddho,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Arahattañca me pattaṃ,
sītibhūtomhi nibbuto.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4250SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4250Msdiv 58Aggo āraddhavīriyānaṃ,
niddiṭṭho sabbadassinā;
Khīṇāsavomhi arahā,
chaḷabhiñño mahiddhiko.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4251SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4251Msdiv 59Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
leṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4252SC 12Ms 20Ap1_4252Msdiv 60Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4253SC 13Ms 20Ap1_4253Msdiv 61Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4254SC 14Ms 20Ap1_4254Msdiv 62Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4255Msdiv 63Thero SC 15Ms 20Ap1_4255Msdiv 63Thero koṭivīso soṇo,
bhikkhusaṃghassa aggato;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
anotatte mahāsareti.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4256Itthaṃ sudaṃ SC 16Ms 20Ap1_4256Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā soṇo koṭivīso thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 17SC 17Ms 20Ap1_4257Soṇakoṭivīsattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
