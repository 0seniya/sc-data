4. Ketakapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6713Msdiv 17“Vinatānadiyā tīre,
vihāsi purisuttamo;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
ekaggaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6714Msdiv 18Madhugandhassa pupphena,
ketakassa ahaṃ tadā;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhaseṭṭhamapūjayiṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.450Ms 20Ap1_6715Msdiv 19Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6716Msdiv 20Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6717Msdiv 21Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6718Msdiv 22Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6719Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ketakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6720Ketakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
