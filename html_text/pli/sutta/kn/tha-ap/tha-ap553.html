1. Yasattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7671SC 1Ms 20Ap1_7671Msdiv 1 “Mahāsamuddaṃ oggayha,
bhavanaṃ me sunimmitaṃ;
Sunimmitā pokkharaṇī,
cakkavākūpakūjitā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7672SC 2Ms 20Ap1_7672Msdiv 2Mandārakehi sañchannā,
padumuppalakehi ca;
Nadī ca sandate tattha,
supatitthā manoramā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7673SC 3Ms 20Ap1_7673Msdiv 3Macchakacchapasañchannā,
nānādijasamotthaṭā;
Mayūrakoñcābhirudā,
kokilādīhi vagguhi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7674SC 4Ms 20Ap1_7674Msdiv 4Pārevatā ravihaṃsā,
cakkavākā nadīcarā;
Tittirā sālikā cettha,
pāvakā jīvaṃjīvakā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7675SC 5Ms 20Ap1_7675Msdiv 5Haṃsākoñcābhinaditā,
kosiyā piṅgalā bahū;
Sattaratanasampannā,
maṇimuttapavāḷikā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7676SC 6Ms 20Ap1_7676Msdiv 6Sabbe soṇṇamayā rukkhā,
nānākhandhasameritā;
Ujjotenti divārattiṃ,
bhavanaṃ sabbakālikaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7677SC 7Ms 20Ap1_7677Msdiv 7Saṭṭhituriyasahassāni,
sāyaṃ pāto pavajjare;
Soḷasitthisahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ sadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7678SC 8Ms 20Ap1_7678Msdiv 8Abhinikkhamma bhavanā,
sumedhaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vandayiṃ taṃ vandayiṃ taṃ mahāyasaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7679SC 9Ms 20Ap1_7679Msdiv 9Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
sasaṃghaṃ taṃ nimantayiṃ;
Adhivāsesi so dhīro,
sumedho lokanāyako.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7680SC 10Ms 20Ap1_7680Msdiv 10Mama dhammakathaṃ katvā,
uyyojesi mahāmuni;
Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,
bhavanaṃ me upāgamiṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7681SC 11Ms 20Ap1_7681Msdiv 11Āmantayiṃ parijanaṃ,
sabbe sannipatuṃ tadā;
‘Pubbaṇhasamayaṃ buddho,
bhavanaṃ āgamissati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7682SC 12Ms 20Ap1_7682Msdiv 12Lābhā amhaṃ suladdhā no,
ye vasāma tavantike;
Mayampi buddhaseṭṭhassa,
pūjayissāma satthuno’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7683SC 13Ms 20Ap1_7683Msdiv 13Annaṃ pānaṃ paṭṭhapetvā,
kālaṃ ārocayiṃ ahaṃ;
Vasīsatasahassehi,
upesi lokanāyako.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7684SC 14Ms 20Ap1_7684Msdiv 14Pañcaṅgikehi turiyehi,
paccuggamamakāsahaṃ;
Sabbasoṇṇamaye pīṭhe,
nisīdi purisuttamo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7685SC 15Ms 20Ap1_7685Msdiv 15Uparicchadanaṃ āsi,
sabbasoṇṇamayaṃ tadā;
Bījanīyo pavāyanti,
bhikkhusaṃghaṃ anuttaraṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7686SC 16Ms 20Ap1_7686Msdiv 16Pahūtenannapānena,
bhikkhusaṃghaṃ atappayiṃ;
Paccekadussayugale,
bhikkhusaṃghassadāsahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7687SC 17Ms 20Ap1_7687Msdiv 17Yaṃ vadeti sumedho so,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7688SC 18Ms 20Ap1_7688Msdiv 18‘Yo maṃ annena pānena,
sabbe ime ca tappayi;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7689SC 19Ms 20Ap1_7689Msdiv 19Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
devaloke ramissati;
Sahassakkhattuṃ rājāyaṃ,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7690SC 20Ms 20Ap1_7690Msdiv 20Upagacchati yaṃ yoniṃ,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbasoṇṇamayaṃ tassa,
chadanaṃ dhārayissati.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7691SC 21Ms 20Ap1_7691Msdiv 21Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7692SC 22Ms 20Ap1_7692Msdiv 22Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7693SC 23Ms 20Ap1_7693Msdiv 23Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
sīhanādaṃ nadissati;
Citake chattaṃ dhārenti,
heṭṭhā chattamhi ḍayhatha’.

SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7694SC 24Ms 20Ap1_7694Msdiv 24Sāmaññaṃ me anuppattaṃ,
kilesā jhāpitā mayā;
Maṇḍape rukkhamūle vā,
santāso me na vijjati.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7695SC 25Ms 20Ap1_7695Msdiv 25Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sabbadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7696SC 26Ms 20Ap1_7696Msdiv 26Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7697SC 27Ms 20Ap1_7697Msdiv 27Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7698SC 28Ms 20Ap1_7698Msdiv 28Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 29SC 29Ms 20Ap1_7699Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yaso thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 30SC 30Ms 20Ap1_7700Yasattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
