1 Kurañciyaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6691SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6691Msdiv 1“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
vipine vicaraṃ ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6692Msdiv 2SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6692Msdiv 2Kurañciyaphalaṃ gayha,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Puññakkhettassa tādino,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6693SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6693Msdiv 3Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6694SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6694Msdiv 4Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6695SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6695Msdiv 5Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
buddhaseṭṭhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6696SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6696Msdiv 6Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6697Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6697Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kurañciyaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6698Kurañciyaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
