3 Koṭumbariyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2596Msdiv 12“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Appameyyaṃva udadhiṃ,
vitthataṃ dharaṇiṃ yathā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2597Msdiv 13Pūjitaṃ devasaṅghena,
nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
upāgacchiṃ naruttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2598Msdiv 14Sattapupphāni paggayha,
koṭumbarasamākulaṃ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2599Msdiv 15Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2600Msdiv 16Ito vīsatikappamhi,
mahānelasanāmako;
Eko āsi mahātejo,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2601Msdiv 17Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2602Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koṭumbariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2603Koṭumbariyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
