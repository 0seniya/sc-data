9 Pupphacchattiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3675Msdiv 73“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Saccaṃ pakāsayantassa,
nibbāpentassa pāṇino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3676Msdiv 74Jalajaṃ āharitvāna,
satapattaṃ manoramaṃ;
Pupphassa chattaṃ katvāna,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.265Ms 20Ap1_3677Msdiv 75Siddhattho ca lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3678Msdiv 76‘Yo me cittaṃ pasādetvā,
pupphacchattaṃ adhārayiṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
duggatiṃ so na gacchati’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3679Msdiv 77Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
siddhattho lokanāyako;
Uyyojetvāna parisaṃ,
vehāsaṃ nabhamuggami.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3680Msdiv 78Vuṭṭhite naradevamhi,
setacchattampi vuṭṭhahi;
Purato buddhaseṭṭhassa,
gacchati chattamuttamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3681Msdiv 79Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ chattaṃ abhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphacchattassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3682Msdiv 80Catusattatikappamhi,
aṭṭha jalasikhā ahū;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3683Msdiv 81Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3684Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphacchattiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3685Pupphacchattiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
