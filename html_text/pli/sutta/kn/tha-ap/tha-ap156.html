4 Kakkārupupphapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2379Msdiv 21“Devaputto ahaṃ santo,
pūjayiṃ sikhināyakaṃ;
Kakkārupupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2380Msdiv 22Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2381Msdiv 23Ito ca navame kappe,
rājā sattuttamo ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2382Msdiv 24Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2383Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kakkārupupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2384Kakkārupupphapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
