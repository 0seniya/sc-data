8. Dussadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.185Ms 20Ap1_2484Msdiv 38“Tivarāyaṃ pure ramme,
rājaputtosahaṃ tadā;
Paṇṇākāraṃ labhitvāna,
upasantassadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2485Msdiv 39Adhivāsesi bhagavā,
vatthaṃ hatthena āmasi;
Siddhattho adhivāsetvā,
vehāsaṃ nabhamuggami.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2486Msdiv 40Buddhassa gacchamānassa,
dussā dhāvanti pacchato;
Tattha cittaṃ pasādesiṃ,
buddho no aggapuggalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2487Msdiv 41Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dussamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dussadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2488Msdiv 42Sattasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
cakkavattī tadā ahuṃ;
Parisuddhoti nāmena,
manujindo mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2489Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2490Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dussadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2491Dussadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
