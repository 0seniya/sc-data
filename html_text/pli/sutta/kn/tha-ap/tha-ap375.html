3 Tīṇuppalamāliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4112Msdiv 13 “Candabhāgānadītīre,
ahosiṃ vānaro tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4113Msdiv 14Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ;
Lakkhaṇabyañjanūpetaṃ,
disvānattamano ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4114Msdiv 15Udaggacitto sumano,
pītiyā haṭṭhamānaso;
Tīṇi uppalapupphāni,
matthake abhiropayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4115Msdiv 16Pūjayitvāna pupphāni,
phussassāhaṃ mahesino;
Sagāravo bhavitvāna,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4116Msdiv 17Gacchanto paṭikuṭiko,
vippasannena cetasā;
Selantare patitvāna,
pāpuṇiṃ jīvitakkhayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4117Msdiv 18Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā purimaṃ jātiṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4118Msdiv 19Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4119Msdiv 20Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4120Msdiv 21Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_4121Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tīṇuppalamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_4122Tīṇuppalamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
