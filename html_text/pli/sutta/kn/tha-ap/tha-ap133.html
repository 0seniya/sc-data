1. Sobhitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2182Msdiv 1“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Mahato janakāyassa,
deseti amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2183Msdiv 2Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
vācāsabhimudīritaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
ekaggo āsahaṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2184Msdiv 3‘Yathā samuddo udadhīnamaggo,
Nerū nagānaṃ pavaro siluccayo;
Tatheva ye cittavasena vattare’,
Na buddhañāṇassa kalaṃ upenti te.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2185Msdiv 4Dhammavidhiṃ ṭhapetvāna,
buddho kāruṇiko isi;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2186Msdiv 5‘Yo so ñāṇaṃ pakittesi,
buddhamhi lokanāyake;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ na gamissati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2187Msdiv 6Kilese jhāpayitvāna,
ekaggo susamāhito;
Sobhito nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2188Msdiv 7Paññāse kappasahasse,
sattevāsuṃ yasuggatā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2189Msdiv 8Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2190Msdiv 9Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2191Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sobhito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2192Sobhitattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
