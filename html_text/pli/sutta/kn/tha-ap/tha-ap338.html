6. Pabhaṅkarattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3777Msdiv 53“Padumuttarabhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Vipine cetiyaṃ āsi,
vāḷamigasamākule.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3778Msdiv 54Na koci visahi gantuṃ,
cetiyaṃ abhivandituṃ;
Tiṇakaṭṭhalatonaddhaṃ,
paluggaṃ āsi cetiyaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3779Msdiv 55Vanakammiko tadā āsiṃ,
pitumātumatenahaṃ;
Addasaṃ vipine thūpaṃ,
luggaṃ tiṇalatākulaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3780Msdiv 56Disvānāhaṃ buddhathūpaṃ,
garucittaṃ upaṭṭhahiṃ;
Buddhaseṭṭhassa thūpoyaṃ,
paluggo acchatī vane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3781Msdiv 57Nacchannaṃ nappatirūpaṃ,
jānantassa guṇāguṇaṃ;
Buddhathūpaṃ asodhetvā,
aññaṃ kammaṃ payojaye.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3782Msdiv 58Tiṇakaṭṭhañca valliñca,
sodhayitvāna cetiye;
Vanditvā aṭṭha vārāni,
paṭikuṭiko agacchahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3783Msdiv 59Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3784Msdiv 60Tattha me sukataṃ byamhaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ sapabhassaraṃ;
Saṭṭhiyojanamubbiddhaṃ,
tiṃsayojanavitthataṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3785Msdiv 61Tisatāni ca vārāni,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Pañcavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3786Msdiv 62Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
mahābhogaṃ labhāmahaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
sodhanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11PTS vp Pali 1.270Ms 20Ap1_3787Msdiv 63Sivikā hatthikhandhena,
vipine gacchato mama;
Yaṃ yaṃ disāhaṃ gacchāmi,
saraṇaṃ sampate vanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3788Msdiv 64Khāṇuṃ vā kaṇṭakaṃ vāpi,
nāhaṃ passāmi cakkhunā;
Puññakammena saṃyutto,
sayamevāpanīyare.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3789Msdiv 65Kuṭṭhaṃ gaṇḍo kilāso ca,
apamāro vitacchikā;
Daddu kacchu ca me natthi,
sodhanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3790Msdiv 66Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpassa sodhane;
Nābhijānāmi me kāye,
jātaṃ piḷakabindukaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3791Msdiv 67Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpamhi sodhite;
Duve bhave saṃsarāmi,
devatte atha mānuse.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3792Msdiv 68Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpamhi sodhite;
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sabbattha,
sappabhāso bhavāmahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3793Msdiv 69Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpamhi sodhite;
Amanāpaṃ vivajjati,
manāpaṃ upatiṭṭhati.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_3794Msdiv 70Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpamhi sodhite;
Visuddhaṃ hoti me cittaṃ,
ekaggaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_3795Msdiv 71Aññampi me acchariyaṃ,
buddhathūpamhi sodhite;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_3796Msdiv 72Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sodhanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_3797Msdiv 73Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_3798Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pabhaṅkaro thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_3799Pabhaṅkarattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
