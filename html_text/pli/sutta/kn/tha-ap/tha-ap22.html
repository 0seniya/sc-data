10. Sumaṅgalattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_837Msdiv 124“Āhutiṃ yiṭṭhukāmohaṃ,
paṭiyādetvāna bhojanaṃ;
Brāhmaṇe paṭimānento,
visāle māḷake ṭhito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_838Msdiv 125Athaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
piyadassiṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Sabbalokavinetāraṃ,
sayambhuṃ aggapuggalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_839Msdiv 126Bhagavantaṃ jutimantaṃ,
sāvakehi purakkhataṃ;
Ādiccamiva rocantaṃ,
rathiyaṃ paṭipannakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_840Msdiv 127Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Manasāva nimantesiṃ,
‘āgacchatu mahāmuni’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_841Msdiv 128Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
satthā loke anuttaro;
Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
mama dvāraṃ upāgami.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_842Msdiv 129Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Pāsādaṃ abhirūhitvā,
sīhāsane nisīdataṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_843Msdiv 130Danto dantaparivāro,
tiṇṇo tārayataṃ varo;
Pāsādaṃ abhirūhitvā,
nisīdi pavarāsane.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_844Msdiv 131Yaṃ me atthi sake gehe,
āmisaṃ paccupaṭṭhitaṃ;
Tāhaṃ buddhassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_845Msdiv 132Pasannacitto sumano,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Buddhaseṭṭhaṃ namassāmi,
aho buddhassuḷāratā.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 1.66Ms 20Ap1_846Msdiv 133Aṭṭhannaṃ payirūpāsataṃ,
bhuñjaṃ khīṇāsavā bahū;
Tuyheveso ānubhāvo,
saraṇaṃ taṃ upemahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_847Msdiv 134Piyadassī ca bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_848Msdiv 135‘Yo so saṃghaṃ abhojesi,
ujubhūtaṃ samāhitaṃ;
Tathāgatañca sambuddhaṃ,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_849Msdiv 136Sattavīsatikkhattuṃ so,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Sakakammābhiraddho so,
devaloke ramissati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_850Msdiv 137Dasa aṭṭha cakkhattuṃ so,
cakkavattī bhavissati;
Pathabyā rajjaṃ pañcasataṃ,
vasudhaṃ āvasissati’.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_851Msdiv 138Araññavanamoggayha,
kānanaṃ byagghasevitaṃ;
Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
kilesā jhāpitā mayā.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_852Msdiv 139Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhattadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_853Msdiv 140Paṭisambhidā catasso,
Vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
Kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (803)

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_854Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sumaṅgalo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_855Sumaṅgalattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_856Tassuddānaṃ

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_857Sīhāsanī ekathambhī,
nando ca cūḷapanthako;
Pilindarāhulo ceva,
vaṅganto raṭṭhapālako.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_858Sopāko maṅgalo ceva,
daseva dutiye vagge;
Satañca aṭṭhatiṃsa ca,
gāthā cettha pakāsitā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_859Sīhāsaniyavaggo dutiyo.
