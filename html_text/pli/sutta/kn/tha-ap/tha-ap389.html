7. Kadaliphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4225SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4225Msdiv 37“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;
Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4226SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4226Msdiv 38Kadaliphalaṃ paggayha,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvāna apakkamiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4227SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4227Msdiv 39Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4228SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4228Msdiv 40Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4229SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4229Msdiv 41Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4230SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4230Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4231Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadaliphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4232Kadaliphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
