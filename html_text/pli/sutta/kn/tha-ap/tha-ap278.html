6 Potthakadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.237Ms 20Ap1_3256Msdiv 24“Satthāraṃ dhammamārabbha,
saṃghañcāpi mahesinaṃ;
Potthadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dakkhiṇeyye anuttare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3257Msdiv 25Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
potthadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3258Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3259Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā potthakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3260Potthakadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
