10. Buddhupaṭṭhākattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1838Msdiv 56“Vipassissa bhagavato,
ahosiṃ saṅkhadhammako;
Niccupaṭṭhānayuttomhi,
sugatassa mahesino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1839Msdiv 57Upaṭṭhānaphalaṃ passa,
lokanāthassa tādino;
Saṭṭhitūriyasahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ sadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1840Msdiv 58Ekanavutito kappe,
upaṭṭhahiṃ mahāisiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1841Msdiv 59Catuvīse ito kappe,
mahānigghosanāmakā;
Soḷasāsiṃsu rājāno,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1842Msdiv 60Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1602)

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1843Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhupaṭṭhāko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1844Buddhupaṭṭhākattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

Sudhāvaggo dasamo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1845Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1846Sudhā sucinti ceḷañca,
sūcī ca gandhamāliyo;
Tipupphiyo madhusenā,
veyyāvacco cupaṭṭhako;
Samasaṭṭhi ca gāthāyo,
asmiṃ vagge pakittitā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1847Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1848Buddhavaggo hi paṭhamo,
sīhāsani subhūti ca;
Kuṇḍadhāno upāli ca,
bījanisakacinti ca.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1849Nāgasamālo timiro,
sudhāvaggena te dasa;
Catuddasasatā gāthā,
pañcapaññāsameva ca.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1850Buddhavaggadasakaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1851Paṭhamasatakaṃ samattaṃ.
