8. Ekacampakapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4073Msdiv 27“Upasanto ca sambuddho,
vasatī pabbatantare;
Ekacampakamādāya,
upagacchiṃ naruttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4074Msdiv 28Pasannacitto sumano,
paccekamunimuttamaṃ;
Ubhohatthehi paggayha,
pūjayiṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4075Msdiv 29Pañcasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4076Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4077Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekacampakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4078Ekacampakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
