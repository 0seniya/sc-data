5 Phusitakampiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3760Msdiv 38“Vipassī nāma sambuddho,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Khīṇāsavehi sahito,
saṃghārāme vasī tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3761Msdiv 39Ārāmadvārā nikkhamma,
vipassī lokanāyako;
Saha satasahassehi,
aṭṭha khīṇāsavehi so.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3762Msdiv 40Ajinena nivatthohaṃ,
vākacīradharopi ca;
Kusumodakamādāya,
sambuddhaṃ upasaṅkamiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3763Msdiv 41Sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādetvā,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Kusumodakamādāya,
buddhamabbhukkiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3764Msdiv 42Tena kammena sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Mama kammaṃ pakittetvā,
agamā yena patthitaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3765Msdiv 43Phusitā pañcasahassā,
yehi pūjesahaṃ jinaṃ;
Aḍḍhateyyasahassehi,
devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3766Msdiv 44Aḍḍhateyyasahassehi,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Avasesena kammena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3767Msdiv 45Devarājā yadā homi,
manujādhipatī yadā;
Tameva nāmadheyyaṃ me,
phusito nāma homahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3768Msdiv 46Devabhūtassa santassa,
athāpi mānusassa vā;
Samantā byāmato mayhaṃ,
phusitaṃva pavassati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3769Msdiv 47Bhavā ugghāṭitā mayhaṃ,
kilesā jhāpitā mama;
Sabbāsavaparikkhīṇo,
phusitassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3770Msdiv 48Candanasseva me kāyā,
tathā gandho pavāyati;
Sarīrato mama gandho,
aḍḍhakose pavāyati.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3771Msdiv 49Dibbagandhaṃ sampavantaṃ,
puññakammasamaṅginaṃ;
Gandhaṃ ghatvāna jānanti,
phusito āgato idha.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3772Msdiv 50Sākhāpalāsakaṭṭhāni,
tiṇānipi ca sabbaso;
Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
gandho sampajjate khaṇe.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_3773Msdiv 51Satasahassito kappe,
candanaṃ abhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phusitassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_3774Msdiv 52Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_3775Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phusitakampiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_3776Phusitakampiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
