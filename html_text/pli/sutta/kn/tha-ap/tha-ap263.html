1. Ākāsukkhipiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3158Msdiv 1“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ siddhatthaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Jalajagge duve gayha,
Upāgacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3159Msdiv 2Ekañca pupphaṃ pādesu,
buddhaseṭṭhassa nikkhipiṃ;
Ekañca pupphaṃ paggayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3160Msdiv 3Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3161Msdiv 4Ito chattiṃsakappamhi,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Antalikkhakaro nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3162Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3163Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ākāsukkhipiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3164Ākāsukkhipiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
