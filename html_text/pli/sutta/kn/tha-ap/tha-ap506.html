4. Avaṭaphaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6630SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6630Msdiv 57“Sahassaraṃsī bhagavā,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekā vuṭṭhahitvāna,
gocarāyābhinikkhami.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6631SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6631Msdiv 58Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
avaṭaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6632SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6632Msdiv 59Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6633SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6633Msdiv 60Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6634SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6634Msdiv 61Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6635SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6635Msdiv 62Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6636Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6637Avaṭaphaliyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
