8. Saddasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1742Msdiv 43“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vasāmi paṇṇasanthare;
Phussassa dhammaṃ bhaṇato,
sadde cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1743Msdiv 44Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1744Msdiv 45Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1745Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1746Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
