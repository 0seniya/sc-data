3. Naḷinakesariyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.223Ms 20Ap1_3046Msdiv 9“Jātassarassa vemajjhe,
vasāmi jalakukkuṭo;
Addasāhaṃ devadevaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3047Msdiv 10Tuṇḍena kesariṃ gayha,
vippasannena cetasā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
tissassa lokabandhuno.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3048Msdiv 11Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3049Msdiv 12Tesattatimhi kappamhi,
satta kesaranāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3050Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3051Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā naḷinakesariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3052Naḷinakesariyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
