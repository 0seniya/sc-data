5. Sattapāṭaliyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3118SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3118Msdiv 17“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,
nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;
Satta pāṭalipupphāni,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3119SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3119Msdiv 18Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3120SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3120Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 4SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3121Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapāṭaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3122Sattapāṭaliyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
