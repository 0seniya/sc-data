7 Ekadhammassavaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5569Msdiv 88“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Catusaccaṃ pakāsento,
santāresi bahuṃ janaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5570Msdiv 89Ahaṃ tena samayena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Dhunanto vākacīrāni,
gacchāmi ambare tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5571Msdiv 90Buddhaseṭṭhassa upari,
gantuṃ na visahāmahaṃ;
Pakkhīva selamāsajja,
gamanaṃ na labhāmahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5572Msdiv 91Udake vokkamitvāna,
evaṃ gacchāmi ambare;
Na me idaṃ bhūtapubbaṃ,
iriyāpathavikopanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5573Msdiv 92Handa metaṃ gavesissaṃ,
appevatthaṃ labheyyahaṃ;
Orohanto antalikkhā,
saddamassosi satthuno.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5574Msdiv 93Sarena rajanīyena,
savanīyena vaggunā;
Aniccataṃ kathentassa,
taññeva uggahiṃ tadā;
Aniccasaññamuggayha,
agamāsiṃ mamassamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5575Msdiv 94Yāvatāyuṃ vasitvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ;
Carime vattamānamhi,
saddhammassavanaṃ sariṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5576Msdiv 95Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5577Msdiv 96Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ;
Ekapaññāsakkhattuñca,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5578Msdiv 97Ekavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5579Msdiv 98Anubhomi sakaṃ puññaṃ,
sukhitohaṃ bhavābhave;
Anussarāmi taṃ saññaṃ,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Na koṭiṃ paṭivijjhāmi,
nibbānaṃ accutaṃ padaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5580Msdiv 99Pitugehe nisīditvā,
samaṇo bhāvitindriyo;
Kathaṃsa paridīpento,
aniccatamudāhari.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5581Msdiv 100‘Aniccā vata saṅkhārā,
uppādavayadhammino;
Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti,
tesaṃ vūpasamo sukho’.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5582Msdiv 101Saha gāthaṃ suṇitvāna,
pubbasaññamanussariṃ;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5583Msdiv 102Jātiyā sattavassena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Upasampādayī buddho,
dhammassavanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5584Msdiv 103Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dhammamasuṇiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dhammassavanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5585Msdiv 104Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5586Msdiv 105Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5587Msdiv 106Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5588Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekadhammassavaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5589Ekadhammassavaniyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
