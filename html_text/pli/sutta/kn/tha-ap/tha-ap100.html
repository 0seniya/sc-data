8. Senāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1823Msdiv 45“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
adāsiṃ paṇṇasantharaṃ;
Samantā upahārañca,
kusumaṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1824Msdiv 46Pāsādevaṃ guṇaṃ rammaṃ,
Anubhomi mahārahaṃ;
Mahagghāni ca pupphāni,
Sayanebhisavanti me.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1825Msdiv 47Sayanehaṃ tuvaṭṭāmi,
vicitte pupphasanthate;
Pupphavuṭṭhi ca sayane,
abhivassati tāvade.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1826Msdiv 48Catunnavutito kappe,
adāsiṃ paṇṇasantharaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1827Msdiv 49Tiṇasantharakā nāma,
sattete cakkavattino;
Ito te pañcame kappe,
uppajjiṃsu janādhipā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.138Ms 20Ap1_1828Msdiv 50Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1829Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā senāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1830Senāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
