2. Telamakkhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3165Msdiv 6“Siddhatthamhi bhagavati,
nibbutamhi narāsabhe;
Bodhiyā vedikāyāhaṃ,
telaṃ makkhesi tāvade.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3166Msdiv 7Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ telaṃ makkhayiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
makkhanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3167Msdiv 8Catuvīse ito kappe,
succhavi nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3168Msdiv 9Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3169Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā telamakkhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3170Telamakkhiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
