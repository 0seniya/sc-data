8. Tiraṃsiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3558Msdiv 39“Kesariṃ abhijātaṃva,
aggikkhandhaṃva pabbate;
Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
siddhatthaṃ pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3559Msdiv 40Sūriyassa ca ālokaṃ,
candālokaṃ tatheva ca;
Buddhālokañca disvāna,
vitti me udapajjatha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3560Msdiv 41Tayo āloke disvāna,
sambuddhaṃ sāvakuttamaṃ;
Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3561Msdiv 42Tayo hi ālokakarā,
loke lokatamonudā;
Cando ca sūriyo cāpi,
buddho ca lokanāyako.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3562Msdiv 43Opammaṃ upadassetvā,
kittito me mahāmuni;
Buddhassa vaṇṇaṃ kittetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3563Msdiv 44Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ buddhamabhikittayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kittanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 1.257Ms 20Ap1_3564Msdiv 45Ekasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
eko ñāṇadharo ahu;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3565Msdiv 46Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3566Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tiraṃsiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3567Tiraṃsiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
