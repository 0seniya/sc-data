6. Padumacchadaniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1301Msdiv 83“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,
vipassimhaggapuggale;
Suphullapadumaṃ gayha,
citamāropayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1302Msdiv 84Āropite ca citake,
vehāsaṃ nabhamuggami;
Ākāse chadanaṃ katvā,
citakamhi adhārayi.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1303Msdiv 85Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1304Msdiv 86Sattatālīsito kappe,
padumissaranāmako;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1305Msdiv 87Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1306Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumacchadaniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1307Padumacchadaniyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
