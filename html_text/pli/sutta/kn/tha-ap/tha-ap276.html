4. Abbhañjanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3242Msdiv 14“Koṇḍaññassa bhagavato,
vītarāgassa tādino;
Ākāsasamacittassa,
nippapañcassa jhāyino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3243Msdiv 15Sabbamohātivattassa,
sabbalokahitesino;
Abbhañjanaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3244Msdiv 16Aparimeyye ito kappe,
abbhañjanamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
abbhañjanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3245Msdiv 17Ito pannarase kappe,
cirappo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3246Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3247Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā abbhañjanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3248Abbhañjanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
