7. Citakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3261Msdiv 27“Candabhāgānadītīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Satta māluvapupphāni,
citamāropayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3262Msdiv 28Catunnavutito kappe,
citakaṃ yamapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3263Msdiv 29Sattasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
paṭijaggasanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
sattāsuṃ cakkavattino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3264Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3265Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3266Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
