10. Taraṇiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.234Ms 20Ap1_3214Msdiv 39“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;
Nadītīre ṭhito satthā,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3215Msdiv 40Nāvā na vijjate tattha,
santāraṇī mahaṇṇave;
Nadiyā abhinikkhamma,
tāresiṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3216Msdiv 41Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ tāresiṃ naruttamaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
taraṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3217Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2577)

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3218Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā taraṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3219Taraṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3220Padumukkhipavaggo sattavīsatimo.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3221Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3222Ukkhipī telacandī ca,
dīpado ca biḷālido;
Maccho javo saḷalado,
rakkhaso taraṇo dasa;
Gāthāyo cettha saṅkhātā,
tālīsaṃ cekameva cāti.
