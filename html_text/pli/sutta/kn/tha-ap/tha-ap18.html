6. Rāhulattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_773Msdiv 68“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Sattabhūmamhi pāsāde,
Ādāsaṃ santhariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_774Msdiv 69Khīṇāsavasahassehi,
parikiṇṇo mahāmuni;
Upāgami gandhakuṭiṃ,
dvipadindo narāsabho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_775Msdiv 70Virocento gandhakuṭiṃ,
devadevo narāsabho;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_776Msdiv 71‘Yenāyaṃ jotitā seyyā,
ādāsova susanthato;
Tamahaṃ kittayissāmi,
suṇātha mama bhāsato.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_777Msdiv 72Soṇṇamayā rūpimayā,
atho veḷuriyāmayā;
Nibbattissanti pāsādā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 1.61Ms 20Ap1_778Msdiv 73Catusaṭṭhikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Sahassakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
bhavissati anantarā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_779Msdiv 74Ekavīsatikappamhi,
vimalo nāma khattiyo;
Cāturanto vijitāvī,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_780Msdiv 75Nagaraṃ reṇuvatī nāma,
iṭṭhakāhi sumāpitaṃ;
Āyāmato tīṇi sataṃ,
caturassasamāyutaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_781Msdiv 76Sudassano nāma pāsādo,
vissakammena māpito;
Kūṭāgāravarūpeto,
sattaratanabhūsito.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_782Msdiv 77Dasasaddāvivittaṃ taṃ,
vijjādharasamākulaṃ;
Sudassanaṃva nagaraṃ,
devatānaṃ bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_783Msdiv 78Pabhā niggacchate tassa,
uggacchanteva sūriye;
Virocessati taṃ niccaṃ,
samantā aṭṭhayojanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_784Msdiv 79Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_785Msdiv 80Tusitā so cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Gotamassa bhagavato,
atrajo so bhavissati.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_786Msdiv 81Sacevaseyya agāraṃ,
cakkavattī bhaveyya so;
Aṭṭhānametaṃ yaṃ tādī,
agāre ratimajjhagā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_787Msdiv 82Nikkhamitvā agāramhā,
pabbajissati subbato;
Rāhulo nāma nāmena,
arahā so bhavissati’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_788Msdiv 83Kikīva aṇḍaṃ rakkheyya,
cāmarī viya vāladhiṃ;
Nipako sīlasampanno,
evaṃ rakkhiṃ mahāmuni.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_789Msdiv 84Tassāhaṃ dhammamaññāya,
vihāsiṃ sāsane rato;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_790Msdiv 85Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_791Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā rāhulo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_792Rāhulattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
