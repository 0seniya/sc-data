4. Yaññasāmikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3608SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3608Msdiv 17“Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
ahosiṃ mantapāragū;
Kulavattaṃ adhāresiṃ,
yañño ussāhito mayā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3609SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3609Msdiv 18Cullāsītisahassāni,
pasū haññanti me tadā;
Sārathambhupanītāni,
yaññatthāya upaṭṭhitā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3610SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3610Msdiv 19Ukkāmukhapahaṭṭhova,
khadiraṅgārasannibho;
Udayantova sūriyo,
puṇṇamāyeva candimā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3611SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3611Msdiv 20Siddhattho sabbasiddhattho,
tilokamahito hito;
Upagantvāna sambuddho,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3612SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3612Msdiv 21‘Ahiṃsā sabbapāṇīnaṃ,
kumāra mama ruccati;
Theyyā ca aticārā ca,
majjapānā ca ārati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3613SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3613Msdiv 22Rati ca samacariyāya,
bāhusaccaṃ kataññutā;
Diṭṭhe dhamme parattha ca,
dhammā ete pasaṃsiyā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3614SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3614Msdiv 23Ete dhamme bhāvayitvā,
sabbasattahite rato;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
bhāvehi maggamuttamaṃ’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3615SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3615Msdiv 24Idaṃ vatvāna sabbaññū,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Mamevaṃ anusāsitvā,
vehāsaṃ uggato gato.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3616SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3616Msdiv 25Pubbe cittaṃ visodhetvā,
pacchā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tusitaṃ upapajjahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3617SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3617Msdiv 26Catunnavutito kappe,
yadā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3618SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3618Msdiv 27Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12SC 12Ms 20Ap1_3619Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yaññasāmiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13SC 13Ms 20Ap1_3620Yaññasāmikattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
