2. Pāṭalipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4103Msdiv 6“Vipassī nāma bhagavā,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Purakkhato sasissehi,
pāvisi bandhumaṃ jino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4104Msdiv 7Tīṇi pāṭalipupphāni,
ucchaṅge ṭhapitāni me;
Sīsaṃ nhāyitukāmova,
nadītitthaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4105Msdiv 8Nikkhamma bandhumatiyā,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Indīvaraṃva jalitaṃ,
ādittaṃva hutāsanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4106Msdiv 9Byagghūsabhaṃva pavaraṃ,
abhijātaṃva kesariṃ;
Gacchantaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4107Msdiv 10Tasmiṃ pasanno sugate,
kilesamaladhovane;
Gahetvā tīṇi pupphāni,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4108Msdiv 11Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4109Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4110Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pāṭalipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_4111Pāṭalipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
