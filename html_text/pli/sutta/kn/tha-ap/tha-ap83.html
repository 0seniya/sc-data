1. Timirapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1686Msdiv 1“Candabhāgānadītīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Nisinnaṃ samaṇaṃ disvā,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1687Msdiv 2Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
Tārayissati tiṇṇoyaṃ,
dantoyaṃ damayissati.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1688Msdiv 3Assāsissati assattho,
santo ca samayissati;
Mocayissati mutto ca,
nibbāpessati nibbuto.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1689Msdiv 4Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
siddhatthassa mahesino;
Gahetvā timirapupphaṃ,
matthake okiriṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1690Msdiv 5Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
katvā ca naṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ aparaṃ disaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1691Msdiv 6Aciraṃ gatamattaṃ maṃ,
migarājā viheṭhayi;
Papātamanugacchanto,
tattheva papatiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1692Msdiv 7Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.127Ms 20Ap1_1693Msdiv 8Chappaññāsamhi kappamhi,
sattevāsuṃ mahāyasā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1694Msdiv 9Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1695Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā timirapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1696Timirapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
