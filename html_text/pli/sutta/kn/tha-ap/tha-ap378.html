6. Gandhamuṭṭhiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4138Msdiv 33“Citake karīyamāne,
nānāgandhe samāhate;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gandhamuṭṭhimapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4139Msdiv 34Satasahassito kappe,
citakaṃ yaṃ apūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4140Msdiv 35Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4141Msdiv 36Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4142Msdiv 37Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4143Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhamuṭṭhiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4144Gandhamuṭṭhiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
