2 Sataraṃsittheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1385Msdiv 8“Ubbiddhaṃ selamāruyha,
nisīdi purisuttamo;
Pabbatassāvidūramhi,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1386Msdiv 9Upaviṭṭhaṃ mahāvīraṃ,
devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1387Msdiv 10‘Esa buddho mahāvīro,
varadhammappakāsako;
Jalati aggikhandhova,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1388Msdiv 11Mahāsamuddovakkhubbho,
aṇṇavova duruttaro;
Migarājāvasambhīto,
dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1389Msdiv 12Mama saṅkappamaññāya,
padumuttaranāyako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe ṭhito satthā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1390Msdiv 13‘Yenāyaṃ añjalī dinno,
buddhaseṭṭho ca thomito;
Tiṃsakappasahassāni,
devarajjaṃ karissati.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1391Msdiv 14Kappasatasahassamhi,
aṅgīrasasanāmako;
Vivaṭṭacchado sambuddho,
uppajjissati tāvade.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1392Msdiv 15Tassa dhammesu dāyādo,
oraso dhammanimmito;
Sataraṃsīti nāmena,
arahā so bhavissati’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1393Msdiv 16Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Sataraṃsimhi nāmena,
pabhā niddhāvate mama.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1394Msdiv 17Maṇḍape rukkhamūle vā,
jhāyī jhānarato ahaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1395Msdiv 18Saṭṭhikappasahassamhi,
caturo rāmanāmakā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 1.105Ms 20Ap1_1396Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1397Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sataraṃsi thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_1398Sataraṃsittherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
