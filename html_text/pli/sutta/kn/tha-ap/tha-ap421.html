9. Patthodanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5435SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5435Msdiv 170“Vanacārī pure āsiṃ,
satataṃ vanakammiko;
Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,
kammantaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5436SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5436Msdiv 171Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ;
Vanā piṇḍāya nikkhantaṃ,
disvā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5437Msdiv 172SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5437Msdiv 172Parakammāyane yutto,
puññañca me na vijjati;
Ayaṃ patthodano atthi,
bhojayissāmahaṃ muniṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5438SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5438Msdiv 173Patthodanaṃ gahetvāna,
sayambhussa adāsahaṃ;
Mama nijjhāyamānassa,
paribhuñji tadā muni.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5439SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5439Msdiv 174Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5440SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5440Msdiv 175Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5441SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5441Msdiv 176Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Sukhito yasavā homi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5442SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5442Msdiv 177Bhavābhave saṃsaranto,
labhāmi amitaṃ dhanaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5443SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5443Msdiv 178Nadīsotapaṭibhāgā,
bhogā nibbattare mama;

patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
patthodanassidaṃ phalaṃ.
