1 Ālambaṇadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2899Msdiv 1“Atthadassissa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Ālambaṇaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2900Msdiv 2Dharaṇiṃ paṭipajjāmi,
vipulaṃ sāgarapparaṃ;
Pāṇesu ca issariyaṃ,
vattemi vasudhāya ca.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2901Msdiv 3Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2902Msdiv 4Ito dvesaṭṭhikappamhi,
tayo āsiṃsu khattiyā;
Ekāpassitanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2903Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2904Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ālambaṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2905Ālambaṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
