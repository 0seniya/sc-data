8 Sumanāveḷiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.264Ms 20Ap1_3664Msdiv 64“Vessabhussa bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Sabbe janā samāgamma,
mahāpūjaṃ karonti te.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3665Msdiv 65Sudhāya piṇḍaṃ katvāna,
āveḷaṃ sumanāyahaṃ;
Sīhāsanassa purato,
abhiropesahaṃ tadā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3666Msdiv 66Sabbe janā samāgamma,
pekkhanti pupphamuttamaṃ;
Kenidaṃ pūjitaṃ pupphaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhassa tādino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3667Msdiv 67Tena cittappasādena,
nimmānaṃ upapajjahaṃ;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3668Msdiv 68Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Sabbesānaṃ piyo homi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3669Msdiv 69Nābhijānāmi kāyena,
vācāya uda cetasā;
Saṃyatānaṃ tapassīnaṃ,
kataṃ akkositaṃ mayā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3670Msdiv 70Tena sucaritenāhaṃ,
cittassa paṇidhīhi ca;
Sabbesaṃ pūjito homi,
anakkosassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3671Msdiv 71Ito ekādase kappe,
sahassārosi khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3672Msdiv 72Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3673Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sumanāveḷiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_3674Sumanāveḷiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
