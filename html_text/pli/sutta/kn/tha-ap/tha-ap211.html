9. Ādhāradāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.207Ms 20Ap1_2805Msdiv 40“Ādhārakaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Dhāremi pathaviṃ sabbaṃ,
kevalaṃ vasudhaṃ imaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2806Msdiv 41Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2807Msdiv 42Sattavīse ito kappe,
ahesuṃ caturo janā;
Samantavaraṇā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2808Msdiv 43Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2809Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ādhāradāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2810Ādhāradāyakattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
