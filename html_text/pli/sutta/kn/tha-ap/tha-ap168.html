6. Sappidāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2470Msdiv 28“Phusso nāmāsi bhagavā,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Gacchate vīthiyaṃ vīro,
nibbāpento mahājanaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2471Msdiv 29Anupubbena bhagavā,
āgacchi mama santikaṃ;
Tato taṃ pattaṃ paggayha,
sappitelamadāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2472Msdiv 30Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ sappimadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sappidānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2473Msdiv 31Chappaññāse ito kappe,
eko āsi samodako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2474Msdiv 32Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2475Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sappidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2476Sappidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
