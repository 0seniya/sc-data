2. Ekadussadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5486Msdiv 15“Nagare haṃsavatiyā,
ahosiṃ tiṇahārako;
Tiṇahārena jīvāmi,
tena posemi dārake.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5487Msdiv 16Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Tamandhakāraṃ nāsetvā,
uppajji lokanāyako.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5488Msdiv 17Sake ghare nisīditvā,
evaṃ cintesi tāvade;
‘Buddho loke samuppanno,
deyyadhammo na vijjati.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5489Msdiv 18Idaṃ me sāṭakaṃ ekaṃ,
natthi me koci dāyako;
Dukkho nirayasamphasso,
ropayissāmi dakkhiṇaṃ’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5490Msdiv 19Evāhaṃ cintayitvāna,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Ekaṃ dussaṃ gahetvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 2.380Ms 20Ap1_5491Msdiv 20Ekaṃ dussaṃ daditvāna,
ukkuṭṭhiṃ sampavattayiṃ;
‘Yadi buddho tuvaṃ vīra,
tārehi maṃ mahāmuni’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5492Msdiv 21Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;
Mama dānaṃ pakittento,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5493Msdiv 22‘Iminā ekadussena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Kappasatasahassāni,
vinipātaṃ na gacchasi.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5494Msdiv 23Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissasi;
Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ rājā ca,
cakkavattī bhavissasi.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5495Msdiv 24Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Devaloke manusse vā,
saṃsaranto tuvaṃ bhave.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_5496Msdiv 25Rūpavā guṇasampanno,
anavakkantadehavā;
Akkhobhaṃ amitaṃ dussaṃ,
labhissasi yadicchakaṃ’.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5497Msdiv 26Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
jalajuttamanāmako;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī vīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5498Msdiv 27Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Bhoge me ūnatā natthi,
ekadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5499Msdiv 28Paduddhāre paduddhāre,
dussaṃ nibbattate mamaṃ;
Heṭṭhā dussamhi tiṭṭhāmi,
uparicchadanaṃ mama.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5500Msdiv 29Cakkavāḷaṃ upādāya,
sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;
Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,
dussehacchādayeyya taṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5501Msdiv 30Teneva ekadussena,
saṃsaranto bhavābhave;
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo hutvāna,
saṃsarāmi bhavābhave.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5502Msdiv 31Vipākaṃ ekadussassa,
nājjhagaṃ katthacikkhayaṃ;
Ayaṃ me antimā jāti,
vipaccati idhāpi me.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5503Msdiv 32Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ dussamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekadussassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5504Msdiv 33Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5505Msdiv 34Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5506Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_5507Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekadussadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5508Ekadussadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
