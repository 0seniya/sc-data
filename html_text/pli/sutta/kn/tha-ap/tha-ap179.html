7. Ekadīpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2551Msdiv 30“Padumuttarassa munino,
saḷale bodhimuttame;
Pasannacitto sumano,
ekadīpaṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2552Msdiv 31Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
nibbatte puññasañcaye;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2553Msdiv 32Soḷase kappasahasse,
ito te caturo janā;
Candābhā nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2554Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2555Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekadīpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2556Ekadīpiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
Navamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
