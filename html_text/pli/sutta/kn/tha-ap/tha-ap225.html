3 Dverataniyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2914Msdiv 12“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
araññe kānane ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2915Msdiv 13Maṃsapesi mayā dinnā,
vipassissa mahesino;
Sadevakasmiṃ lokasmiṃ,
issaraṃ kārayāmahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2916Msdiv 14Iminā maṃsadānena,
ratanaṃ nibbattate mama;
Duveme ratanā loke,
diṭṭhadhammassa pattiyā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2917Msdiv 15Tehaṃ sabbe anubhomi,
maṃsadānassa sattiyā;
Gattañca mudukaṃ mayhaṃ,
paññā nipuṇavedanī.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2918Msdiv 16Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ maṃsamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
maṃsadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2919Msdiv 17Ito catutthake kappe,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Mahārohitanāmo so,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2920Msdiv 18Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2921Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dverataniyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2922Dverataniyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
Dasamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
