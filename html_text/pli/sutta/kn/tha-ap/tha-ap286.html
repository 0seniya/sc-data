4. Ekapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3308Msdiv 16“Dakkhiṇamhi duvāramhi,
pisāco āsahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3309Msdiv 17Vipassissa naraggassa,
sabbalokahitesino;
Ekapupphaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dvipadindassa tādino.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3310Msdiv 18Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3311Msdiv 19Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3312Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3313Ekapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
