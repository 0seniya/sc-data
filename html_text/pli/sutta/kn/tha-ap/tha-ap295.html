3. Chattadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3372Msdiv 11“Putto mama pabbajito,
kāsāyavasano tadā;
So ca buddhattaṃ sampatto,
nibbuto lokapūjito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3373Msdiv 12Vicinanto sakaṃ puttaṃ,
agamaṃ pacchato ahaṃ;
Nibbutassa mahantassa,
citakaṃ agamāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3374Msdiv 13Paggayha añjaliṃ tattha,
vanditvā citakaṃ ahaṃ;
Setacchattañca paggayha,
āropesiṃ ahaṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3375Msdiv 14Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ chattamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
chattadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3376Msdiv 15Pañcavīse ito kappe,
satta āsuṃ janādhipā;
Mahārahasanāmā te,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3377Msdiv 16Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3378Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā chattadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3379Chattadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
