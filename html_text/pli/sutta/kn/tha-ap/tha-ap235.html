3. Sālapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2985Msdiv 9“Aruṇavatiyā nagare,
ahosiṃ pūpiko tadā;
Mama dvārena gacchantaṃ,
sikhinaṃ addasaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2986Msdiv 10Buddhassa pattaṃ paggayha,
sālapupphaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Sammaggatassa buddhassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.219Ms 20Ap1_2987Msdiv 11Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
Yaṃ pupphamabhidāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Sālapupphassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2988Msdiv 12Ito cuddasakappamhi,
ahosiṃ amitañjalo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2989Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2990Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sālapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2991Sālapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
