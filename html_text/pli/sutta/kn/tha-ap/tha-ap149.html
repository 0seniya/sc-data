7. Maggadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2321Msdiv 32“Uttaritvāna nadikaṃ,
vanaṃ gacchati cakkhumā;
Tamaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
siddhatthaṃ varalakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2322Msdiv 33Kudāla piṭakamādāya,
samaṃ katvāna taṃ pathaṃ;
Satthāraṃ abhivādetvā,
sakaṃ cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2323Msdiv 34Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
maggadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2324Msdiv 35Sattapaññāsakappamhi,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Nāmena suppabuddhoti,
nāyako so narissaro.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2325Msdiv 36Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2326Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maggadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2327Maggadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
