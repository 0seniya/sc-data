5. Phaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.250Ms 20Ap1_3459Msdiv 20“Bhāgīrathīnadītīre,
ahosi assamo tadā;
Tamahaṃ assamaṃ gacchiṃ,
phalahattho apekkhavā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3460Msdiv 21Vipassiṃ tattha addakkhiṃ,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Yaṃ me atthi phalaṃ sabbaṃ,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3461Msdiv 22Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3462Msdiv 23Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3463Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3464Phaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
