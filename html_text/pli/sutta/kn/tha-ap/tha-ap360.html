8 Mañcadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4013Msdiv 30 “Vipassino bhagavato,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Ekaṃ mañcaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
pasannena sapāṇinā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4014Msdiv 31Hatthiyānaṃ assayānaṃ,
dibbayānaṃ samajjhagaṃ;
Tena mañcakadānena,
pattomhi āsavakkhayaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4015Msdiv 32Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ mañcamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
mañcadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4016Msdiv 33Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4017Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mañcadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4018Mañcadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
