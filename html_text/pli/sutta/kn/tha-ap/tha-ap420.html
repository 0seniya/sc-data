8. Yāgudāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5412Msdiv 149“Atithiṃ me gahetvāna,
agacchiṃ gāmakaṃ tadā;
Sampuṇṇanadikaṃ disvā,
saṃghārāmaṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5413Msdiv 150Āraññakā dhutadharā,
jhāyino lūkhacīvarā;
Vivekābhiratā dhīrā,
saṃghārāme vasanti te.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5414Msdiv 151Gati tesaṃ upacchinnā,
suvimuttāna tādinaṃ;
Piṇḍāya te na gacchanti,
oruddhanaditāya hi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5415Msdiv 152Pasannacitto sumano,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Taṇḍulaṃ me gahetvāna,
yāgudānaṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5416Msdiv 153Pañcannaṃ yāguṃ datvāna,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Sakakammābhiraddhohaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5417Msdiv 154Maṇimayañca me byamhaṃ,
nibbatti tidase gaṇe;
Nārīgaṇehi sahito,
modāmi byamhamuttame.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5418Msdiv 155Tettiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Tiṃsakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
mahārajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5419Msdiv 156Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Devaloke manusse vā,
anubhotvā sayaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5420Msdiv 157Pacchime bhave sampatte,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;
Saha oropite kese,
sabbaṃ sampaṭivijjhahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_5421Msdiv 158Khayato vayato cāpi,
sammasanto kaḷevaraṃ;
Pure sikkhāpadādānā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 11PTS vp Pali 2.376Ms 20Ap1_5422Msdiv 159Sudinnaṃ me dānavaraṃ,
vāṇijjaṃ sampayojitaṃ;
Teneva yāgudānena,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_5423Msdiv 160Sokaṃ pariddavaṃ byādhiṃ,
darathaṃ cittatāpanaṃ;
Nābhijānāmi uppannaṃ,
yāgudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_5424Msdiv 161Yāguṃ saṃghassa datvāna,
puññakkhette anuttare;
Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,
aho yāgusuyiṭṭhatā.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_5425Msdiv 162Abyādhitā rūpavatā,
khippaṃ dhammanisantitā;
Lābhitā annapānassa,
āyu pañcamakaṃ mama.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_5426Msdiv 163Yo koci vedaṃ janayaṃ,
saṃghe yāguṃ dadeyya so;
Imāni pañca ṭhānāni,
paṭigaṇheyya paṇḍito.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_5427Msdiv 164Karaṇīyaṃ kataṃ sabbaṃ,
bhavā ugghāṭitā mayā;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_5428Msdiv 165So ahaṃ vicarissāmi,
gāmā gāmaṃ purā puraṃ;
Namassamāno sambuddhaṃ,
dhammassa ca sudhammataṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_5429Msdiv 166Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
yāgudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_5430Msdiv 167Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_5431Msdiv 168Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_5432Msdiv 169Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_5433Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yāgudāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_5434Yāgudāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
