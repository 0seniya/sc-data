8. Pāṭalipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1638Msdiv 36“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāsaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1639Msdiv 37Seṭṭhiputto tadā āsiṃ,
sukhumālo sukhedhito;
Ucchaṅge pāṭalipupphaṃ,
katvāna abhisaṃhariṃ.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.123Ms 20Ap1_1640Msdiv 38Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
pupphehi abhipūjayiṃ;
Tissaṃ lokaviduṃ nāthaṃ,
naradevaṃ namassahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1641Msdiv 39Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1642Msdiv 40Ito tesaṭṭhikappamhi,
abhisammatanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1643Msdiv 41Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1644Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pāṭalipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1645Pāṭalipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
