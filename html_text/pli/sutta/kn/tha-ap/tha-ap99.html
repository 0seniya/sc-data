7. Madhupiṇḍikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1813Msdiv 37“Vipine kānane disvā,
appasadde nirākule;
Siddhatthaṃ isinaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1814Msdiv 38Nibbutattaṃ mahānāgaṃ,
nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;
Osadhiṃva virocantaṃ,
devasaṅghanamassitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1815Msdiv 39Vitti mamāhu tāvade,
ñāṇaṃ uppajji tāvade;
Vuṭṭhitassa samādhimhā,
madhuṃ datvāna satthuno.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.137Ms 20Ap1_1816Msdiv 40Vanditvā satthuno pāde,
pakkāmiṃ pācināmukho;
Catuttiṃsamhi kappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ sudassano.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1817Msdiv 41Madhu bhisehi savati,
bhojanamhi ca tāvade;
Madhuvassaṃ pavassittha,
pubbakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1818Msdiv 42Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ madhuṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
madhudānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1819Msdiv 43Catuttiṃse ito kappe,
cattāro te sudassanā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1820Msdiv 44Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1821Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā madhupiṇḍiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1822Madhupiṇḍikattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
