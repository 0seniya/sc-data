2 Sucintikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1774Msdiv 8“Tissassa lokanāthassa,
suddhapīṭhamadāsahaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
buddhassādiccabandhuno.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1775Msdiv 9Aṭṭhārase ito kappe,
rājā āsiṃ mahāruci;
Bhogo ca vipulo āsi,
sayanañca anappakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1776Msdiv 10Pīṭhaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe sukatamattano.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.134Ms 20Ap1_1777Msdiv 11Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pīṭhamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pīṭhadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1778Msdiv 12Aṭṭhatiṃse ito kappe,
tayo te cakkavattino;
Ruci uparuci ceva,
mahāruci tatiyako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1779Msdiv 13Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1780Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sucintiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1781Sucintikattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
