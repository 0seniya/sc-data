7. Ucchaṅgapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5403Msdiv 142“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
ahosiṃ māliko tadā;
Ucchaṅgaṃ pūrayitvāna,
agamaṃ antarāpaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5404Msdiv 143Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Mahatā ānubhāvena,
niyyāti lokanāyako.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5405Msdiv 144Disvāna lokapajjotaṃ,
vipassiṃ lokatāraṇaṃ;
Pupphaṃ paggayha ucchaṅgā,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ apūjayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5406Msdiv 145Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5407Msdiv 146Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5408Msdiv 147Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5409Msdiv 148Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_5410Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ucchaṅgapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_5411Ucchaṅgapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
