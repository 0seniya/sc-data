2. Ekatthambhikattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.56Ms 20Ap1_710Msdiv 13“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
mahāpūgagaṇo ahu;
Saraṇaṃ gatā ca te buddhaṃ,
saddahanti tathāgataṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_711Msdiv 14Sabbe saṅgamma mantetvā,
māḷaṃ kubbanti satthuno;
Ekatthambhaṃ alabhantā,
vicinanti brahāvane.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_712Msdiv 15Tehaṃ araññe disvāna,
upagamma gaṇaṃ tadā;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
paripucchiṃ gaṇaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_713Msdiv 16Te me puṭṭhā viyākaṃsu,
sīlavanto upāsakā;
Māḷaṃ mayaṃ kattukāmā,
ekatthambho na labbhati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_714Msdiv 17Ekatthambhaṃ mamaṃ detha,
ahaṃ dassāmi satthuno;
Āharissāmahaṃ thambhaṃ,
appossukkā bhavantu te.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_715Msdiv 18Te me thambhaṃ pavecchiṃsu,
pasannā tuṭṭhamānasā;
Tato paṭinivattitvā,
agamaṃsu sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_716Msdiv 19Aciraṃ gate pūgagaṇe,
thambhaṃ ahāsahaṃ tadā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
paṭhamaṃ ussapesahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_717Msdiv 20Tena cittappasādena,
Vimānaṃ upapajjahaṃ;
Ubbiddhaṃ bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Sattabhūmaṃ samuggataṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_718Msdiv 21Vajjamānāsu bherīsu,
paricāremahaṃ sadā;
Pañcapaññāsakappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ yasodharo.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_719Msdiv 22Tatthāpi bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
sattabhūmaṃ samuggataṃ;
Kūṭāgāravarūpetaṃ,
ekatthambhaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_720Msdiv 23Ekavīsatikappamhi,
udeno nāma khattiyo;
Tatrāpi bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
sattabhūmaṃ samuggataṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_721Msdiv 24Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,
devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;
Anubhomi sukhaṃ sabbaṃ,
ekatthambhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 13PTS vp Pali 1.57Ms 20Ap1_722Msdiv 25Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ thambhamadadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ekatthambhassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_723Msdiv 26Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_724Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekatthambhiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_725Ekatthambhikattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
