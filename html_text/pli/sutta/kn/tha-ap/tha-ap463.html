1. Sālakusumiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5970Msdiv 1“Parinibbute bhagavati,
jalajuttamanāmake;
Āropitamhi citake,
sālapupphamapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5971Msdiv 2Satasahassito kappe,
Yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5972Msdiv 3Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5973Msdiv 4Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5974Msdiv 5Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5975Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sālakusumiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5976Sālakusumiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
