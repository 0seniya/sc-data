2. Kaṇaverapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2441Msdiv 7“Siddhattho nāma bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
nagaraṃ paṭipajjatha.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2442Msdiv 8Rañño antepure āsiṃ,
gopako abhisammato;
Pāsāde upaviṭṭhohaṃ,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2443Msdiv 9Kaṇaveraṃ gahetvāna,
bhikkhusaṃghe samokiriṃ;
Buddhassa visuṃ katvāna,
tato bhiyyo samokiriṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2444Msdiv 10Catunnavutito kappe,
Yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2445Msdiv 11Sattāsītimhito kappe,
caturāsuṃ mahiddhikā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2446Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2447Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṇaverapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2448Kaṇaverapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
