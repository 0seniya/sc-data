8 Caṅkamanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1315Msdiv 93“Atthadassissa munino,
lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Iṭṭhakāhi cinitvāna,
caṅkamaṃ kārayiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1316Msdiv 94Uccato pañcaratanaṃ,
caṅkamaṃ sādhumāpitaṃ;
Āyāmato hatthasataṃ,
bhāvanīyyaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1317Msdiv 95Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
atthadassī naruttamo;
Hatthena pulinaṃ gayha,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1318Msdiv 96‘Iminā pulinadānena,
caṅkamaṃ sukatena ca;
Sattaratanasampannaṃ,
pulinaṃ anubhossati.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1319Msdiv 97Tīṇi kappāni devesu,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Anubhossati sampattiṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1320Msdiv 98Manussalokamāgantvā,
rājā raṭṭhe bhavissati;
Tikkhattuṃ cakkavattī ca,
mahiyā so bhavissati’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1321Msdiv 99Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
caṅkamassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1322Msdiv 100Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1323Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā caṅkamanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1324Caṅkamanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
