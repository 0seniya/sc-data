7 Labujaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6013SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6013Msdiv 32“Nagare bandhumatiyā,
ārāmiko ahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
gacchantaṃ anilañjase.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6014SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6014Msdiv 33Labujaṃ phalamādāya,
buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;
Ākāseva ṭhito santo,
paṭiggaṇhi mahāyaso.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6015SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6015Msdiv 34Vittisañjanano mayhaṃ,
diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho;
Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6016SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6016Msdiv 35Adhigañchiṃ tadā pītiṃ,
vipulaṃ sukhamuttamaṃ;
Uppajjateva ratanaṃ,
nibbattassa tahiṃ tahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6017SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6017Msdiv 36Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6018SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6018Msdiv 37Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6019SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6019Msdiv 38Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6020SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6020Msdiv 39Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6021Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 9Ms 20Ap1_6021Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā labujaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6022Labujaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
