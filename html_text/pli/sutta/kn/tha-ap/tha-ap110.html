8 Āsanupaṭṭhāhakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1912Msdiv 47“Kānanaṃ vanamogayha,
appasaddaṃ nirākulaṃ;
Sīhāsanaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
atthadassissa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1913Msdiv 48Mālāhatthaṃ gahetvāna,
katvā ca naṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Satthāraṃ payirupāsitvā,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1914Msdiv 49Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Sannibbāpemi attānaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1915Msdiv 50Aṭṭhārasakappasate,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sīhāsanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1916Msdiv 51Ito sattakappasate,
sannibbāpaka khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1917Msdiv 52Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1918Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āsanupaṭṭhāhako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1919Āsanupaṭṭhāhakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
