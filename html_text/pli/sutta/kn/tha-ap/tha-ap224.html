2. Ajinadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2906Msdiv 6“Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
gaṇasatthārako ahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2907Msdiv 7Cammakhaṇḍaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2908Msdiv 8Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
kilese jhāpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2909Msdiv 9Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
ajinaṃ yaṃ adāsahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
ajinassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2910Msdiv 10Ito pañcamake kappe,
rājā āsiṃ sudāyako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2911Msdiv 11Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2912Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ajinadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2913Ajinadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
