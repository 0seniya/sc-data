1. Kaṇikārapupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2750SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2750Msdiv 1“Kaṇikāraṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,
ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;
Tissassa abhiropesiṃ,
oghatiṇṇassa tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2751SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2751Msdiv 2Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2752SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2752Msdiv 3Pañcattiṃse ito kappe,
aruṇapāṇīti vissuto;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2753SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2753Msdiv 4Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2754Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṇikārapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2755Kaṇikārapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
