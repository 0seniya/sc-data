9 Kandalipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3568SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3568Msdiv 47“Sindhuyā nadiyā tīre,
ahosiṃ kassako tadā;
Parakammāyane yutto,
parabhattaṃ apassito.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3569SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3569Msdiv 48Sindhuṃ anucarantohaṃ,
siddhatthaṃ jinamaddasaṃ;
Samādhinā nisinnaṃva,
satapattaṃva pupphitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3570Msdiv 49SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3570Msdiv 49Satta kandalipupphāni,
vaṇṭe chetvānahaṃ tadā;
Matthake abhiropesiṃ,
buddhassādiccabandhuno.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3571SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3571Msdiv 50Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Anukūle samāhitaṃ;
Tidhāpabhinnamātaṅgaṃ,
Kuñjaraṃva durāsadaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3572SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3572Msdiv 51Tamahaṃ upagantvāna,
nipakaṃ bhāvitindriyaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
avandiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3573SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3573Msdiv 52Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3574SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3574Msdiv 53Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3575Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3575Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kandalipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3576Kandalipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
