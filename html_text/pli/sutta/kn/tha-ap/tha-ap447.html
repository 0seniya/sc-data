5 Uttalipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_5813Msdiv 31“Nigrodhe haritobhāse,
saṃviruḷhamhi pādape;
Uttalimālaṃ paggayha,
bodhiyā abhiropayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_5814Msdiv 32Imasmiṃyeva kappamhi,
yaṃ bodhimabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_5815Msdiv 33Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_5816Msdiv 34Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_5817Msdiv 35Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_5818Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttalipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_5819Uttalipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
