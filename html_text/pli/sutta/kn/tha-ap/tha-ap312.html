10. Pabbhāradāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3496SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3496Msdiv 47“Piyadassino bhagavato,
pabbhāro sodhito mayā;
Ghaṭakañca upaṭṭhāsiṃ,
paribhogāya tādino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3497SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3497Msdiv 48Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
piyadassī mahāmuni;
Sahassakaṇḍo satabheṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayo.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3498SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3498Msdiv 49Nibbattissati so yūpo,
ratanañca anappakaṃ;
Pabbhāradānaṃ datvāna,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3499SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3499Msdiv 50Ito bāttiṃsakappamhi,
susuddho nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3500SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3500Msdiv 51Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (2763)

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3501Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pabbhāradāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3502Pabbhāradāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3503SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3503Padumakesaravaggo ekatiṃsatimo.

SC 9SC 9Ms 20Ap1_3504Tassuddānaṃ

SC 10SC 10Ms 20Ap1_3505Kesaraṃ gandhamannañca,
dhammasaññī phalena ca;
Pasādārāmadāyī ca,
lepako buddhasaññako;
Pabbhārado ca gāthāyo,
ekapaññāsa kittitā.
