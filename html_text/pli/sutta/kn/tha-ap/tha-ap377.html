5. Sattapaṇṇiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4131Msdiv 28“Sumano nāma sambuddho,
uppajji lokanāyako;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4132Msdiv 29Satasahassito kappe,
Sattapaṇṇimapūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Sattapaṇṇipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4133Msdiv 30Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4134Msdiv 31Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4135Msdiv 32Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4136Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapaṇṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4137Sattapaṇṇiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
