4 Gandhodakiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1406Msdiv 25“Padumuttarabuddhassa,
mahābodhimaho ahu;
Vicittaṃ ghaṭamādāya,
gandhodakamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1407Msdiv 26Nhānakāle ca bodhiyā,
mahāmegho pavassatha;
Ninnādo ca mahā āsi,
asaniyā phalantiyā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1408Msdiv 27Tenevāsanivegena,
tattha kālaṅkato kālaṅkato ahaṃ;
Devaloke ṭhito santo,
imā gāthā abhāsahaṃ.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 1.106Ms 20Ap1_1409Msdiv 28‘Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthusampadā;
Kaḷevaraṃ me patitaṃ,
devaloke ramāmahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1410Msdiv 29Ubbiddhaṃ bhavanaṃ mayhaṃ,
Satabhūmaṃ samuggataṃ;
Kaññāsatasahassāni,
Parivārenti maṃ sadā.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1411Msdiv 30Ābādhā me na vijjanti,
soko mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Pariḷāhaṃ na passāmi,
puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1412Msdiv 31Aṭṭhavīse kappasate,
rājā saṃvasito ahuṃ;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo’.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1413Msdiv 32Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1414Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhodakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1415Gandhodakiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
