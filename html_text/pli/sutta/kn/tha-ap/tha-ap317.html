5. Pupphāsanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3533Msdiv 20“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Avidūrena gacchantaṃ,
Siddhatthaṃ aparājitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3534Msdiv 21Tassa paccuggamitvāna,
pavesetvāna assamaṃ;
Pupphāsanaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3535Msdiv 22Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
vedajāto tadā ahaṃ;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
taṃ kammaṃ pariṇāmayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3536Msdiv 23Yaṃ me atthi kataṃ puññaṃ,
sayambhumhaparājite;
Sabbena tena kusalena,
vimalo homi sāsane.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3537Msdiv 24Catunnavutito kappe,
pupphāsanamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
pupphāsanassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3538Msdiv 25Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3539Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pupphāsanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3540Pupphāsanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
