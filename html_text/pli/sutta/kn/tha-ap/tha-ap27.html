5. Annasaṃsāvakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1022Msdiv 155“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Kañcanagghiyasaṃkāsaṃ,
Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1023Msdiv 156Siddhatthaṃ lokapajjotaṃ,
appameyyaṃ anopamaṃ;
Alatthaṃ paramaṃ pītiṃ,
disvā dantaṃ jutindharaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1024Msdiv 157Sambuddhaṃ abhināmetvā,
bhojayiṃ taṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Mahākāruṇiko Mahākāruṇiko loke,
anumodi mamaṃ tadā.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1025Msdiv 158Tasmiṃ mahākāruṇike,
paramassāsakārake;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1026Msdiv 159Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhikkhādānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1027Msdiv 160Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1028Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā annasaṃsāvako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1029Annasaṃsāvakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
