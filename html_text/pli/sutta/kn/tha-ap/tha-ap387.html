5. Phārusaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4210SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4210Msdiv 26“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Phārusaphalamadāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4211SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4211Msdiv 27Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4212SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4212Msdiv 28Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4213SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4213Msdiv 29Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4214SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4214Msdiv 30Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4215Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phārusaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4216Phārusaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
