5. Muṭṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1883Msdiv 24“Sudassanoti nāmena,
mālākāro ahaṃ tadā;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1884Msdiv 25Jātipupphaṃ gahetvāna,
pūjayiṃ padumuttaraṃ;
Visuddhacakkhu sumano,
dibbacakkhuṃ samajjhagaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1885Msdiv 26Etissā pupphapūjāya,
cittassa paṇidhīhi ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1886Msdiv 27Soḷasāsiṃsu rājāno,
devuttarasanāmakā;
Chattiṃsamhi ito kappe,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1887Msdiv 28Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1888Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā muṭṭhipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1889Muṭṭhipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
