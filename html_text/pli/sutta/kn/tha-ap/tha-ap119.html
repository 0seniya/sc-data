7. Buddhasaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2009Msdiv 57“Yadā vipassī lokaggo,
āyusaṅkhāramossaji;
Pathavī sampakampittha,
medanī jalamekhalā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2010Msdiv 58Otataṃ vitthataṃ mayhaṃ,
suvicittavaṭaṃsakaṃ;
Bhavanampi pakampittha,
buddhassa āyusaṅkhaye.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 1.152Ms 20Ap1_2011Msdiv 59Tāso mayhaṃ samuppanno,
bhavane sampakampite;
Uppādo nu kimatthāya,
āloko vipulo ahu.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2012Msdiv 60Vessavaṇo idhāgamma,
nibbāpesi mahājanaṃ;
Pāṇabhūte bhayaṃ natthi,
ekaggā hotha saṃvutā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2013Msdiv 61Aho buddhā aho dhammā,
aho no satthu sampadā;
Yasmiṃ uppajjamānamhi,
pathavī sampakampati.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2014Msdiv 62Buddhānubhāvaṃ kittetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ;
Avasesesu kappesu,
kusalaṃ caritaṃ mayā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2015Msdiv 63Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_2016Msdiv 64Ito cuddasakappamhi,
rājā āsiṃ patāpavā;
Samito nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_2017Msdiv 65Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_2018Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā buddhasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_2019Buddhasaññakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
