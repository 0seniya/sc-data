8 Pulinathūpiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_6500Msdiv 57“Himavantassāvidūre,
yamako nāma pabbato;
Assamo sukato mayhaṃ,
paṇṇasālā sumāpitā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_6501Msdiv 58Nārado nāma nāmena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Catuddasasahassāni,
sissā paricaranti maṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_6502Msdiv 59Paṭisallīnako santo,
evaṃ cintesahaṃ tadā;
‘Sabbo jano maṃ pūjeti,
nāhaṃ pūjemi kiñcanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_6503Msdiv 60Na me ovādako atthi,
vattā koci na vijjati;
Anācariyupajjhāyo,
vane vāsaṃ upemahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_6504Msdiv 61Upāsamāno yamahaṃ,
garucittaṃ upaṭṭhahe;
So me ācariyo natthi,
vanavāso niratthako.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_6505Msdiv 62Āyāgaṃ me gavesissaṃ,
garuṃ bhāvaniyaṃ tathā;
Sāvassayo vasissāmi,
na koci garahissati’.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_6506Msdiv 63Uttānakūlā nadikā,
supatitthā manoramā;
Saṃsuddhapulinākiṇṇā,
avidūre mamassamaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_6507Msdiv 64Nadiṃ amarikaṃ nāma,
upagantvānahaṃ tadā;
Saṃvaḍḍhayitvā pulinaṃ,
akaṃ pulinacetiyaṃ.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 2.438Ms 20Ap1_6508Msdiv 65Ye te ahesuṃ sambuddhā,
bhavantakaraṇā munī;
Tesaṃ etādiso thūpo,
taṃ nimittaṃ karomahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_6509Msdiv 66Karitvā pulinaṃ thūpaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ māpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Soṇṇakiṅkaṇipupphāni,
sahasse tīṇi pūjayiṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_6510Msdiv 67Sāyaṃ pātaṃ namassāmi,
vedajāto katañjalī;
Sammukhā viya sambuddhaṃ,
vandiṃ pulinacetiyaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_6511Msdiv 68Yadā kilesā jāyanti,
vitakkā gehanissitā;
Sarāmi sukataṃ thūpaṃ,
paccavekkhāmi tāvade.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_6512Msdiv 69Upanissāya viharaṃ,
satthavāhaṃ vināyakaṃ;
Kilese saṃvaseyyāsi,
na yuttaṃ tava mārisa.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_6513Msdiv 70Saha āvajjite thūpe,
gāravaṃ hoti me tadā;
Kuvitakke vinodesiṃ,
nāgo tuttaṭṭito yathā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_6514Msdiv 71Evaṃ viharamānaṃ maṃ,
maccurājābhimaddatha;
Tattha kālaṅkato santo,
brahmalokamagacchahaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_6515Msdiv 72Yāvatāyuṃ vasitvāna,
tidive upapajjahaṃ;
Asītikkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_6516Msdiv 73Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ.

SC 18Ms 20Ap1_6517Msdiv 74Soṇṇakiṅkaṇipupphānaṃ,
vipākaṃ anubhomahaṃ;
Dhātīsatasahassāni,
parivārenti maṃ bhave.

SC 19Ms 20Ap1_6518Msdiv 75Thūpassa pariciṇṇattā,
rajojallaṃ na limpati;
Gatte sedā na muccanti,
suppabhāso bhavāmahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 20Ap1_6519Msdiv 76Aho me sukato thūpo,
sudiṭṭhā marikā nadī;
Thūpaṃ katvāna pulinaṃ,
pattomhi acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap1_6520Msdiv 77Kusalaṃ kattukāmena,
jantunā sāragāhinā;
Natthi khettaṃ akhettaṃ vā,
paṭipattīva sādhakā.

SC 22Ms 20Ap1_6521Msdiv 78Yathāpi balavā poso,
aṇṇavaṃ taritussahe;
Parittaṃ kaṭṭhamādāya,
pakkhandeyya mahāsaraṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap1_6522Msdiv 79Imāhaṃ kaṭṭhaṃ nissāya,
tarissāmi mahodadhiṃ;
Ussāhena vīriyena,
tareyya udadhiṃ naro.

SC 24PTS vp Pali 2.439Ms 20Ap1_6523Msdiv 80Tatheva me kataṃ kammaṃ,
parittaṃ thokakañca yaṃ;
Taṃ kammaṃ upanissāya,
saṃsāraṃ samatikkamiṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap1_6524Msdiv 81Pacchime bhave sampatte,
sukkamūlena codito;
Sāvatthiyaṃ pure jāto,
mahāsāle suaḍḍhake.

SC 26Ms 20Ap1_6525Msdiv 82Saddhā mātā pitā mayhaṃ,
buddhassa saraṇaṃ gatā;
Ubho diṭṭhapadā ete,
anuvattanti sāsanaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap1_6526Msdiv 83Bodhipapaṭikaṃ gayha,
soṇṇathūpamakārayuṃ;
Sāyaṃ pātaṃ namassanti,
sakyaputtassa sammukhā.

SC 28Ms 20Ap1_6527Msdiv 84Uposathamhi divase,
soṇṇathūpaṃ vinīharuṃ;
Buddhassa vaṇṇaṃ kittentā,
tiyāmaṃ vītināmayuṃ.

SC 29Ms 20Ap1_6528Msdiv 85Saha disvānahaṃ thūpaṃ,
sariṃ pulinacetiyaṃ;
Ekāsane nisīditvā,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap1_6529Dvāvīsatimaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.

SC 31Ms 20Ap1_6530Msdiv 86Gavesamāno taṃ vīraṃ,
dhammasenāpatiddasaṃ;
Agārā nikkhamitvāna,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 32Ms 20Ap1_6531Msdiv 87Jātiyā sattavassena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ;
Upasampādayī buddho,
guṇamaññāya cakkhumā.

SC 33Ms 20Ap1_6532Msdiv 88Dārakeneva santena,
kiriyaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ mayā;
Kataṃ me karaṇīyajja,
sakyaputtassa sāsane.

SC 34Ms 20Ap1_6533Msdiv 89Sabbaverabhayātīto,
sabbasaṅgātigo isi;
Sāvako te mahāvīra,
soṇṇathūpassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 35Ms 20Ap1_6534Msdiv 90Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 36Ms 20Ap1_6535Msdiv 91Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap1_6536Msdiv 92Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 38Ms 20Ap1_6537Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pulinathūpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 39Ms 20Ap1_6538Pulinathūpiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
