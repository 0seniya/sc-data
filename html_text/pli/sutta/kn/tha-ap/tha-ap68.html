6. Sucintitattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1536Msdiv 36“Giriduggacaro āsiṃ,
abhijātova kesarī;
Migasaṅghaṃ vadhitvāna,
jīvāmi pabbatantare.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1537Msdiv 37Atthadassī tu bhagavā,
sabbaññū vadataṃ varo;
Mamuddharitukāmo so,
āgacchi pabbatuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1538Msdiv 38Pasadañca migaṃ hantvā,
bhakkhituṃ samupāgamiṃ;
Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
bhikkhamāno upāgami.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1539Msdiv 39Varamaṃsāni paggayha,
adāsiṃ tassa satthuno;
Anumodi mahāvīro,
nibbāpento mamaṃ tadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1540Msdiv 40Tena cittappasādena,
giriduggaṃ pavisiṃ ahaṃ;
Pītiṃ uppādayitvāna,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1541Msdiv 41Etena maṃsadānena,
cittassa paṇidhīhi ca;
Pannarase kappasate,
devaloke ramiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1542Msdiv 42Avasesesu kappesu,
kusalaṃ cintitaṃ mayā;
Teneva maṃsadānena,
buddhānussaraṇena ca.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1543Msdiv 43Aṭṭhattiṃsamhi kappamhi,
aṭṭha dīghāyunāmakā;
Saṭṭhimhito kappasate,
duve varuṇanāmakā.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1544Msdiv 44Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1545Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sucintito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1546Sucintitattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
