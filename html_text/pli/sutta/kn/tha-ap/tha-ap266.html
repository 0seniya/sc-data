4 Padīpadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3177SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3177Msdiv 14“Devabhūto ahaṃ santo,
oruyha pathaviṃ tadā;
Padīpe pañca pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3178SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3178Msdiv 15Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ padīpamadaṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3179SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3179Msdiv 16Pañcapaññāsake kappe,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Samantacakkhunāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3180SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3180Msdiv 17Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3181Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3181Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padīpadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3182Padīpadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
