2. Bhojanadāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3511SC 1Ms 20Ap1_3511Msdiv 4“Sujāto sālalaṭṭhīva,
sobhañjanamivuggato;
Indalaṭṭhirivākāse,
virocati sadā jino.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3512SC 2Ms 20Ap1_3512Msdiv 5Tassa devātidevassa,
vessabhussa mahesino;
Adāsi bhojanamahaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3513SC 3Ms 20Ap1_3513Msdiv 6Taṃ me buddho anumodi,
sayambhū aparājito;
Bhave nibbattamānamhi,
phalaṃ nibbattatū tava.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3514SC 4Ms 20Ap1_3514Msdiv 7Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhojanassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3515SC 5Ms 20Ap1_3515Msdiv 8Pañcavīse ito kappe,
eko āsiṃ amittako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3516SC 6Ms 20Ap1_3516Msdiv 9Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_3517Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bhojanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_3518Bhojanadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
