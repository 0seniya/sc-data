8. Panasaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4233SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4233Msdiv 43“Ajjuno nāma sambuddho,
himavante vasī tadā;
Caraṇena ca sampanno,
samādhikusalo muni.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4234SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4234Msdiv 44Kumbhamattaṃ gahetvāna,
panasaṃ jīvajīvakaṃ;
Chattapaṇṇe ṭhapetvāna,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4235SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4235Msdiv 45Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4236SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4236Msdiv 46Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4237SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4237Msdiv 47Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4238SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4238Msdiv 48Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 7SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4239Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā panasaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 20Ap1_4240Panasaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
