6 Dutiyaraṃsisaññakattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.130Ms 20Ap1_1730Msdiv 35“Pabbate himavantamhi,
vākacīradharo ahaṃ;
Caṅkamañca samārūḷho,
nisīdiṃ pācināmukho.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1731Msdiv 36Pabbate sugataṃ disvā,
phussaṃ jhānarataṃ tadā;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
raṃsyā cittaṃ pasādayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1732Msdiv 37Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
raṃsisaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1733Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1734Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1735Dutiyaraṃsisaññakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
