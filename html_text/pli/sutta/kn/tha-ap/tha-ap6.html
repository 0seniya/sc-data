6. Anuruddhattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_434Msdiv 421“Sumedhaṃ bhagavantāhaṃ,
lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Vūpakaṭṭhaṃ viharantaṃ,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_435Msdiv 422Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
sumedhaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
buddhaseṭṭhamayācahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_436Msdiv 423Anukampa mahāvīra,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Padīpaṃ te padassāmi,
rukkhamūlamhi jhāyato.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_437Msdiv 424Adhivāsesi so dhīro,
sayambhū vadataṃ varo;
Dumesu vinivijjhitvā,
yantaṃ yojiyahaṃ tadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_438Msdiv 425Sahassavaṭṭiṃ pādāsiṃ,
buddhassa lokabandhuno;
Sattāhaṃ pajjalitvāna,
dīpā vūpasamiṃsu me.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_439Msdiv 426Tena cittappasādena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
vimānamupapajjahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_440Msdiv 427Upapannassa devattaṃ,
byamhaṃ āsi sunimmitaṃ;
Samantato pajjalati,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 1.36Ms 20Ap1_441Msdiv 428Samantā yojanasataṃ,
virocesimahaṃ tadā;
Sabbe deve abhibhomi,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_442Msdiv 429Tiṃsakappāni devindo,
devarajjamakārayiṃ;
Na maṃ kecītimaññanti,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_443Msdiv 430Aṭṭhavīsatikkhattuñca,
cakkavattī ahosahaṃ;
Divā rattiñca passāmi,
samantā yojanaṃ tadā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_444Msdiv 431Sahassalokaṃ ñāṇena,
passāmi satthu sāsane;
Dibbacakkhumanuppatto,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_445Msdiv 432Sumedho nāma sambuddho,
tiṃsakappasahassito;
Tassa dīpo mayā dinno,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_446Msdiv 433Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 14Ms 20Ap1_447Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anuruddho thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_448Anuruddhattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
