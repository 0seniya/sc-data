1. Sudhāpiṇḍiyattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.133Ms 20Ap1_1765Msdiv 1“Pūjārahe pūjayato,
buddhe yadi va sāvake;
Papañcasamatikkante,
tiṇṇasokapariddave.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1766Msdiv 2Te tādise pūjayato,
nibbute akutobhaye;
Na sakkā puññaṃ saṅkhātuṃ,
imettamapi kenaci.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1767Msdiv 3Catunnamapi dīpānaṃ,
issaraṃ yodha kāraye;
Ekissā pūjanāyetaṃ,
kalaṃ nāgghati soḷasiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1768Msdiv 4Siddhatthassa naraggassa,
cetiye phalitantare;
Sudhāpiṇḍo mayā dinno,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1769Msdiv 5Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
paṭisaṅkhārassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1770Msdiv 6Ito tiṃsatikappamhi,
paṭisaṅkhārasavhayā;
Sattaratanasampannā,
terasa cakkavattino.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1771Msdiv 7Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1772Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sudhāpiṇḍiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1773Sudhāpiṇḍiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
