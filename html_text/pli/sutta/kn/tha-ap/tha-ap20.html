8. Raṭṭhapālattheraapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.63Ms 20Ap1_806Msdiv 97“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;
Varanāgo mayā dinno,
Īsādanto urūḷhavā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_807Msdiv 98Setacchatto pasobhito,
sakappano sahatthipo;
Agghāpetvāna taṃ sabbaṃ,
saṃghārāmaṃ akārayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_808Msdiv 99Catupaññāsasahassāni,
pāsāde kārayiṃ ahaṃ;
Mahoghadānaṃ karitvāna,
niyyādesiṃ mahesino.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_809Msdiv 100Anumodi mahāvīro,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Sabbe jane hāsayanto,
desesi amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_810Msdiv 101Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
jalajuttaranāmako;
Bhikkhusaṃghe nisīditvā,
imā gāthā abhāsatha.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_811Msdiv 102‘Catupaññāsasahassāni,
pāsāde kārayī ayaṃ;
Kathayissāmi vipākaṃ,
suṇotha mama bhāsato.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_812Msdiv 103Aṭṭhārasasahassāni,
kūṭāgārā bhavissare;
Byamhuttamamhi nibbattā,
sabbasoṇṇamayā ca te.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_813Msdiv 104Paññāsakkhattuṃ devindo,
devarajjaṃ karissati;
Aṭṭhapaññāsakkhattuñca,
cakkavattī bhavissati.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_814Msdiv 105Kappasatasahassamhi,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_815Msdiv 106Devalokā cavitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Aḍḍhe kule mahābhoge,
nibbattissati tāvade.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_816Msdiv 107So pacchā pabbajitvāna,
sukkamūlena codito;
Raṭṭhapāloti nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvako.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_817Msdiv 108Padhānapahitatto so,
upasanto nirūpadhi;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissatināsavo’.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_818Msdiv 109Uṭṭhāya abhinikkhamma,
jahitā bhogasampadā;
Kheḷapiṇḍeva bhogamhi,
pemaṃ mayhaṃ na vijjati.

SC 14PTS vp Pali 1.64Ms 20Ap1_819Msdiv 110Vīriyaṃ me dhuradhorayhaṃ,
yogakkhemādhivāhanaṃ;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 15Ms 20Ap1_820Msdiv 111Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 16Ms 20Ap1_821Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṭṭhapālo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 17Ms 20Ap1_822Raṭṭhapālattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
