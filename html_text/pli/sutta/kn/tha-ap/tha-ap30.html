8 Uttiyattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1042Msdiv 169“Candabhāgānadītīre,
susumāro ahaṃ tadā;
Sagocarappasutohaṃ,
nadītitthaṃ agacchahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1043Msdiv 170Siddhattho tamhi samaye,
sayambhū aggapuggalo;
Nadiṃ taritukāmo so,
nadītitthaṃ upāgami.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1044Msdiv 171Upāgate ca sambuddhe,
ahampi tatthupāgamiṃ;
Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,
imaṃ vācaṃ udīrayiṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1045Msdiv 172‘Abhirūha mahāvīra,
tāressāmi ahaṃ tuvaṃ;
Pettikaṃ visayaṃ mayhaṃ,
anukampa mahāmuni’.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1046Msdiv 173Mama uggajjanaṃ sutvā,
abhirūhi mahāmuni;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
tāresiṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1047Msdiv 174Nadiyā pārime tīre,
siddhattho lokanāyako;
Assāsesi mamaṃ tattha,
amataṃ pāpuṇissasi.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_1048Msdiv 175Tamhā kāyā cavitvāna,
devalokaṃ āgacchahaṃ;
Dibbasukhaṃ anubhaviṃ,
accharāhi purakkhato.

SC 8Ms 20Ap1_1049Msdiv 176Sattakkhattuñca devindo,
devarajjamakāsahaṃ;
Tīṇikkhattuṃ cakkavattī,
mahiyā issaro ahuṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap1_1050Msdiv 177Vivekamanuyuttohaṃ,
nipako ca susaṃvuto;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammāsambuddhasāsane.

SC 10Ms 20Ap1_1051Msdiv 178Catunnavutito kappe,
tāresiṃ yaṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
taraṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap1_1052Msdiv 179Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap1_1053Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap1_1054Uttiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
