8. Maṇipūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_2557Msdiv 34“Orena himavantassa,
nadikā sampavattatha;
Tassā cānupakhettamhi,
sayambhū vasate tadā.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_2558Msdiv 35Maṇiṃ paggayha pallaṅkaṃ,
sādhucittaṃ manoramaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_2559Msdiv 36Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ maṇiṃ abhiropayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_2560Msdiv 37Ito ca dvādase kappe,
sataraṃsīsanāmakā;
Aṭṭha te āsuṃ rājāno,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_2561Msdiv 38Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_2562Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā maṇipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_2563Maṇipūjakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
