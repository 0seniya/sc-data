9. Ekañjalikattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_1055Msdiv 180“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Vipassiṃ satthavāhaggaṃ,
Naravaraṃ vināyakaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_1056Msdiv 181Adantadamanaṃ tādiṃ,
mahāvādiṃ mahāmatiṃ;
Disvā pasanno sumano,
ekañjalimakāsahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_1057Msdiv 182Ekanavutito kappe,
yamañjaliṃ kariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
añjalissa idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_1058Msdiv 183Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_1059Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekañjaliko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_1060Ekañjalikattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
