7. Citakapūjakattheraapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap1_4145Msdiv 38“Parinibbute bhagavati,
jalajuttamanāmake;
Āropitamhi citake,
sālapupphamapūjayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap1_4146Msdiv 39Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap1_4147Msdiv 40Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

SC 4Ms 20Ap1_4148Msdiv 41Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap1_4149Msdiv 42Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 20Ap1_4150Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 7Ms 20Ap1_4151Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
