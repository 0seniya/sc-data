21. Caṇḍālivimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 193Ms 19Vv_219Msdiv 195“Caṇḍāli vanda pādāni,
gotamassa yasassino;
Tameva anukampāya,
aṭṭhāsi isisattamo.

SC 2SC Verse 194Ms 19Vv_220Msdiv 196Abhippasādehi manaṃ,
arahantamhi tādini;
Khippaṃ pañjalikā vanda,
parittaṃ tava jīvitan”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 195Ms 19Vv_221Msdiv 197Coditā bhāvitattena,
sarīrantimadhārinā;
Caṇḍālī vandi pādāni,
gotamassa yasassino.

SC 4SC Verse 196Ms 19Vv_222Msdiv 198Tamenaṃ avadhī gāvī,
caṇḍāliṃ pañjaliṃ ṭhitaṃ;
Namassamānaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
andhakāre pabhaṅkaranti.

SC 5SC Verse 197Ms 19Vv_223Msdiv 199“Khīṇāsavaṃ vigatarajaṃ anejaṃ,
Ekaṃ araññamhi raho nisinnaṃ;
Deviddhipattā upasaṅkamitvā,
Vandāmi taṃ vīra mahānubhāvan”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 198Ms 19Vv_224Msdiv 200“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇā jalitā mahāyasā,
Vimānamoruyha anekacittā;
Parivāritā accharāsaṅgaṇena,
Kā tvaṃ subhe devate vandase maman”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 199PTS 2nd ed. 26 Ms 19Vv_225Msdiv 201“Ahaṃ bhaddante caṇḍālī,
tayā vīrena pesitā;
Vandiṃ arahato pāde,
gotamassa yasassino.

SC 8SC Verse 200Ms 19Vv_226Msdiv 202Sāhaṃ vanditvā pādāni,
cutā caṇḍālayoniyā;
Vimānaṃ sabbato bhaddaṃ,
upapannamhi nandane.

SC 9SC Verse 201Ms 19Vv_227Msdiv 203Accharānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
purakkhatvāna tiṭṭhati;
Tāsāhaṃ pavarā seṭṭhā,
vaṇṇena yasasāyunā.

SC 10SC Verse 202Ms 19Vv_228Msdiv 204Pahūtakatakalyāṇā,
Sampajānā paṭissatā;
Muniṃ kāruṇikaṃ loke,
Taṃ bhante vanditumāgatā”ti.

SC 11SC Verse 203Ms 19Vv_229Msdiv 205Idaṃ vatvāna caṇḍālī,
kataññū katavedinī;
Vanditvā arahato pāde,
tatthevantaradhāyathāti.

SC 12Ms 19Vv_230Caṇḍālivimānaṃ catutthaṃ.
