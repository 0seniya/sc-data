61. Dutiyanāgavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 731PTS 2nd ed. 89 Ms 19Vv_811Msdiv 968“Mahantaṃ nāgaṃ abhiruyha,
Sabbasetaṃ gajuttamaṃ;
Vanā vanaṃ anupariyāsi,
Nārīgaṇapurakkhato;
Obhāsento disā sabbā,
Osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 2SC Verse 732Ms 19Vv_812Msdiv 969-970Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 733Ms 19Vv_813Msdiv 971So devaputto attamano,
vaṅgīseneva pucchito;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 734Ms 19Vv_814Msdiv 972“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Upāsako cakkhumato ahosiṃ;
Pāṇātipātā virato ahosiṃ,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayissaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 735Ms 19Vv_815Msdiv 973Amajjapo no ca musā abhāṇiṃ,
Sakena dārena ca tuṭṭho ahosiṃ;
Annañca pānañca pasannacitto,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adāsiṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 736Ms 19Vv_816Msdiv 974-975Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 7Ms 19Vv_817Dutiyanāgavimānaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
