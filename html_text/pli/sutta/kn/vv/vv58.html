58. Paṭhamasūcivimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 712Ms 19Vv_789Msdiv 944“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 713Ms 19Vv_790Msdiv 945Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,
Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;
Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,
Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā.

SC 3SC Verse 714Ms 19Vv_791Msdiv 946-947Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 715Ms 19Vv_792Msdiv 948So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 716Ms 19Vv_793Msdiv 949“Yaṃ dadāti na taṃ hoti,
Yañceva dajjā tañceva seyyo;
Sūci dinnā sūcimeva seyyo.

SC 6SC Verse 717Ms 19Vv_794Msdiv 950-951Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 7Ms 19Vv_795Paṭhamasūcivimānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
