76. Nandanavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 865PTS 2nd ed. 112 Ms 19Vv_969Msdiv 1120“Yathā vanaṃ SC 1SC Verse 865PTS 2nd ed. 112 Ms 19Vv_969Msdiv 1120“Yathā vanaṃ nandanaṃ pabhāsati,
Uyyānaseṭṭhaṃ tidasānamuttamaṃ;
Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.
