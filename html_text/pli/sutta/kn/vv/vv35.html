35. Pesavatīvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 438PTS 2nd ed. 51 Ms 19Vv_485SC 1SC Verse 438PTS 2nd ed. 51 Ms 19Vv_485Msdiv 646“Phalikarajatahemajālachannaṃ,
Vividhacitratalamaddasaṃ surammaṃ;
Byamhaṃ sunimmitaṃ toraṇūpapannaṃ,
Rucakupakiṇṇamidaṃ subhaṃ vimānaṃ.

Tathā tapati midaṃ tava vimānaṃ,
Jalamiva dhūmasikho nise nabhagge.

SC 3SC Verse 440Ms 19Vv_487Msdiv 648Musatīva nayanaṃ SC 3SC Verse 440Ms 19Vv_487Msdiv 648Musatīva nayanaṃ sateratāva,
Ākāse ṭhapitamidaṃ manuññaṃ;
Vīṇāmurajasammatāḷaghuṭṭhaṃ,
Iddhaṃ indapuraṃ yathā tavedaṃ.

Yodhika bandhukanojakā ca santi;
Sālakusumitapupphitā asokā,
Vividhadumaggasugandhasevitamidaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 442Ms 19Vv_489Msdiv 650Saḷalalabujabhujaka SC 5SC Verse 442Ms 19Vv_489Msdiv 650Saḷalalabujabhujaka saṃyuttā,
Kusakasuphullitalatāvalambinīhi;
Maṇijālasadisā yasassinī,
Rammā pokkharaṇī upaṭṭhitā te.

SC 6SC Verse 443Ms 19Vv_490SC 6SC Verse 443Ms 19Vv_490Msdiv 651Udakaruhā ca yetthi pupphajātā,
Thalajā ye ca santi rukkhajātā;
Mānusakāmānussakā ca dibbā,
Sabbe tuyhaṃ nivesanamhi jātā.

SC 7SC Verse 444Ms 19Vv_491SC 7SC Verse 444Ms 19Vv_491Msdiv 652Kissa saṃyamadamassayaṃ vipāko,
Kenāsi kammaphalenidhūpapannā;
Yathā ca te adhigatamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Tadanupadaṃ avacāsiḷārapamhe”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 445Ms 19Vv_492SC 8SC Verse 445Ms 19Vv_492Msdiv 653“Yathā ca me adhigatamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Koñcamayūracakorasaṅghacaritaṃ;
Dibyapilavahaṃsarājaciṇṇaṃ,
Dijakāraṇḍavakokilābhinaditaṃ.

Yathā ca me adhigatamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Taṃ te pavedayāmi suṇohi bhante.

SC 10SC Verse 447PTS 2nd ed. 52 Ms 19Vv_494SC 10SC Verse 447PTS 2nd ed. 52 Ms 19Vv_494Msdiv 655Magadhavarapuratthimena,
Nāḷakagāmo nāma atthi bhante;
Tattha ahosiṃ pure suṇisā,
Pesavatīti tattha jāniṃsu mamaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 448Ms 19Vv_495SC 11SC Verse 448Ms 19Vv_495Msdiv 656Sāhamapacitatthadhammakusalaṃ,

Muditamanā kusumehi abbhukiriṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 449Ms 19Vv_496SC 12SC Verse 449Ms 19Vv_496Msdiv 657Paramagatigatañca pūjayitvā,

Pahāya mānusakaṃ samussayaṃ,
Tidasagatā idha māvasāmi ṭhānan”ti.
