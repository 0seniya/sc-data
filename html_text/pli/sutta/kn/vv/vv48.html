48. Ucchuvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 583Ms 19Vv_646SC 1SC Verse 583Ms 19Vv_646Msdiv 808“Obhāsayitvā pathaviṃ sadevakaṃ,
Atirocasi candimasūriyā viya;
Siriyā ca vaṇṇena yasena tejasā,
Brahmāva deve tidase sahindake.

SC 2SC Verse 584PTS 2nd ed. 70 Ms 19Vv_647SC 2SC Verse 584PTS 2nd ed. 70 Ms 19Vv_647Msdiv 809Pucchāmi taṃ uppalamāladhārinī,
Āveḷinī kañcanasannibhattace;
Alaṅkate uttamavatthadhārinī,
Kā tvaṃ subhe devate vandase mamaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 585Ms 19Vv_648SC 3SC Verse 585Ms 19Vv_648Msdiv 810Kiṃ tvaṃ pure kammamakāsi attanā,
Manussabhūtā purimāya jātiyā;
Dānaṃ suciṇṇaṃ atha sīlasaññamaṃ,
Kenupapannā sugatiṃ yasassinī;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
Kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 586Ms 19Vv_649SC 4SC Verse 586Ms 19Vv_649Msdiv 811“Idāni bhante imameva gāmaṃ,
Piṇḍāya amhāka gharaṃ upāgami;
Tato te ucchussa adāsi khaṇḍikaṃ,
Pasannacittā atulāya pītiyā.

SC 5SC Verse 587Ms 19Vv_650SC 5SC Verse 587Ms 19Vv_650Msdiv 812Sassu ca pacchā anuyuñjate mamaṃ,
Kahaṃ nu ucchuṃ vadhuke avākiri;
Na chaḍḍitaṃ no pana khāditaṃ mayā,
Santassa bhikkhussa sayaṃ adāsahaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 588Ms 19Vv_651SC 6SC Verse 588Ms 19Vv_651Msdiv 813Tuyhaṃ nvidaṃ issariyaṃ atho mama,
Itissā sassu paribhāsate mamaṃ;
Leḍḍuṃ gahetvā pahāraṃ adāsi me,
Tato cutā kālakatāmhi devatā.

SC 7SC Verse 589Ms 19Vv_652SC 7SC Verse 589Ms 19Vv_652Msdiv 814Tadeva kammaṃ kusalaṃ kataṃ mayā,
Sukhañca kammaṃ anubhomi attanā;
Devehi saddhiṃ paricārayāmahaṃ,
Modāmahaṃ kāmaguṇehi pañcahi.

SC 8SC Verse 590Ms 19Vv_653SC 8SC Verse 590Ms 19Vv_653Msdiv 815Tadeva kammaṃ kusalaṃ kataṃ mayā,
Sukhañca kammaṃ anubhomi attanā;
Devindaguttā tidasehi rakkhitā,
Samappitā kāmaguṇehi pañcahi.

SC 9SC Verse 591Ms 19Vv_654SC 9SC Verse 591Ms 19Vv_654Msdiv 816Etādisaṃ puññaphalaṃ anappakaṃ,
Mahāvipākā mama ucchudakkhiṇā;
Devehi saddhiṃ paricārayāmahaṃ,
Modāmahaṃ kāmaguṇehi pañcahi.

SC 10SC Verse 592Ms 19Vv_655SC 10SC Verse 592Ms 19Vv_655Msdiv 817Etādisaṃ puññaphalaṃ anappakaṃ,
Mahājutikā mama ucchudakkhiṇā;
Devindaguttā tidasehi rakkhitā,
Sahassanettoriva nandane vane.

SC 11SC Verse 593PTS 2nd ed. 71 Ms 19Vv_656SC 11SC Verse 593PTS 2nd ed. 71 Ms 19Vv_656Msdiv 818Tuvañca bhante anukampakaṃ viduṃ,
Upecca vandiṃ kusalañca pucchisaṃ;
Tato te ucchussa adāsiṃ khaṇḍikaṃ,
Pasannacittā atulāya pītiyā”ti.
