14. Dutiyasuṇisāvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 116Ms 19Vv_130SC 1SC Verse 116Ms 19Vv_130Msdiv 116“Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
yā tvaṃ tiṭṭhasi devate;
Obhāsentī disā sabbā,
osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 2SC Verse 117PTS 2nd ed. 15 Ms 19Vv_131SC 2SC Verse 117PTS 2nd ed. 15 Ms 19Vv_131Msdiv 117Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 3SC Verse 118Ms 19Vv_132SC 3SC Verse 118Ms 19Vv_132Msdiv 118Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 119Ms 19Vv_133SC 4SC Verse 119Ms 19Vv_133Msdiv 119Sā devatā attamanā,
moggallānena pucchitā;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭhā viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 120Ms 19Vv_134SC 5SC Verse 120Ms 19Vv_134Msdiv 120“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Suṇisā ahosiṃ sasurassa gehe.

SC 6SC Verse 121Ms 19Vv_135SC 6SC Verse 121Ms 19Vv_135Msdiv 121Addasaṃ virajaṃ bhikkhuṃ,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ;
Tassa adāsahaṃ bhāgaṃ,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi;
Kummāsapiṇḍaṃ datvāna,
modāmi nandane vane.

SC 7SC Verse 122Ms 19Vv_136SC 7SC Verse 122Ms 19Vv_136Msdiv 122Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
tena me idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca me bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 8SC Verse 123Ms 19Vv_137SC 8SC Verse 123Ms 19Vv_137Msdiv 123Akkhāmi te bhikkhu mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūtā yamakāsi puññaṃ;
Tenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 19Vv_138Dutiyasuṇisāvimānaṃ cuddasamaṃ.
