85. Sunikkhittavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 1023PTS 2nd ed. 134 Ms 19Vv_1136Msdiv 1282“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 1024Ms 19Vv_1137Msdiv 1283Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,
Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;
Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,
Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā.

SC 3SC Verse 1025Ms 19Vv_1138Msdiv 1284Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 4SC Verse 1026Ms 19Vv_1139Msdiv 1285Pucchāmi taṃ deva mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 1027PTS 2nd ed. 135 Ms 19Vv_1140Msdiv 1286So devaputto attamano,
moggallānena pucchito;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 1028Ms 19Vv_1141Msdiv 1287“Dunnikkhittaṃ mālaṃ sunikkhipitvā,
Patiṭṭhapetvā sugatassa thūpe;
Mahiddhiko camhi mahānubhāvo,
Dibbehi kāmehi samaṅgibhūto.

SC 7SC Verse 1029Ms 19Vv_1142Msdiv 1288Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
tena me idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca me bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 8SC Verse 1030Ms 19Vv_1143Msdiv 1289Akkhāmi te bhikkhu mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūto yamahaṃ akāsiṃ;
Tenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Vv_1144Sunikkhittavimānaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
Sunikkhittavaggo sattamo.

SC 10Ms 19Vv_1145Tassuddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 19Vv_1146Dve daliddā vanavihārā,
Bhatako gopālakaṇḍako;
Anekavaṇṇamaṭṭhakuṇḍalī,
Serīsako sunikkhittaṃ;
Purisānaṃ tatiyo vaggo pavuccatīti.

SC 12Ms 19Vv_1147Bhāṇavāraṃ catutthaṃ.

SC 13Ms 19Vv_1148Vimānavatthupāḷi niṭṭhitā.
