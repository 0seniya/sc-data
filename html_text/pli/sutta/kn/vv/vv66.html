66. Dutiyaagāriyavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 814PTS 2nd ed. 102 Ms 19Vv_902Msdiv 1054“Yathā vanaṃ cittalataṃ pabhāsati,
Uyyānaseṭṭhaṃ tidasānamuttamaṃ;
Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

SC 2SC Verse 815Ms 19Vv_903Msdiv 1055Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 816Ms 19Vv_904Msdiv 1056So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 817Ms 19Vv_905Msdiv 1057“Ahañca bhariyā ca manussaloke,
Opānabhūtā gharamāvasimha;
Annañca pānañca pasannacittā,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adamha.

SC 5SC Verse 818Ms 19Vv_906Msdiv 1058-1059Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_907Dutiyaagāriyavimānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
