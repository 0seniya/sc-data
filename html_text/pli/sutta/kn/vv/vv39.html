39. Mañjiṭṭhakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 481PTS 2nd ed. 57 Ms 19Vv_535Msdiv 689“Mañjiṭṭhake vimānasmiṃ,
soṇṇavālukasanthate;
Pañcaṅgikena tūriyena,
ramasi suppavādite.

SC 2SC Verse 482Ms 19Vv_536Msdiv 690Tamhā vimānā oruyha,
nimmitā ratanāmayā;
Ogāhasi sālavanaṃ,
pupphitaṃ sabbakālikaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 483Ms 19Vv_537Msdiv 691Yassa yasseva sālassa,
mūle tiṭṭhasi devate;
So so muñcati pupphāni,
onamitvā dumuttamo.

SC 4SC Verse 484Ms 19Vv_538Msdiv 692Vāteritaṃ sālavanaṃ,
ādhutaṃ dijasevitaṃ;
Vāti gandho disā sabbā,
rukkho mañjūsako yathā.

SC 5SC Verse 485Ms 19Vv_539Msdiv 693Ghāyase taṃ sucigandhaṃ,
rūpaṃ passasi amānusaṃ;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 486Ms 19Vv_540Msdiv 694“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Dāsī ayirakule ahuṃ;
Buddhaṃ nisinnaṃ disvāna,
Sālapupphehi okiriṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 487Ms 19Vv_541Msdiv 695Vaṭaṃsakañca sukataṃ,
sālapupphamayaṃ ahaṃ;
Buddhassa upanāmesiṃ,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 8SC Verse 488Ms 19Vv_542Msdiv 696Tāhaṃ kammaṃ karitvāna,
kusalaṃ buddhavaṇṇitaṃ;
Apetasokā sukhitā,
sampamodāmanāmayā”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Vv_543Mañjiṭṭhakavimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
