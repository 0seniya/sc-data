83. Maṭṭhakuṇḍalīvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 948PTS 2nd ed. 123 Ms 19Vv_1059Msdiv 1207“Alaṅkato maṭṭhakuṇḍalī,
Māladhārī haricandanussado;
Bāhā paggayha kandasi,
Vanamajjhe kiṃ dukkhito tuvan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 949Ms 19Vv_1060Msdiv 1208“Sovaṇṇamayo pabhassaro,
Uppanno rathapañjaro mama;
Tassa cakkayugaṃ na vindāmi,
Tena dukkhena jahāmi jīvitan”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 950Ms 19Vv_1061Msdiv 1209“Sovaṇṇamayaṃ maṇimayaṃ,
Lohitakamayaṃ atha rūpiyamayaṃ;
Ācikkha me bhaddamāṇava,
Cakkayugaṃ paṭipādayāmi te”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 951Ms 19Vv_1062Msdiv 1210So māṇavo tassa pāvadi,
“Candasūriyā ubhayettha dissare;
Sovaṇṇamayo ratho mama,
Tena cakkayugena sobhatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 952Ms 19Vv_1063Msdiv 1211“Bālo kho tvaṃ asi māṇava,
Yo tvaṃ patthayase apatthiyaṃ;
Maññāmi tuvaṃ marissasi,
Na hi tvaṃ lacchasi candasūriye”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 953Ms 19Vv_1064Msdiv 1212“Gamanāgamanampi dissati,
Vaṇṇadhātu ubhayattha vīthiyā;
Peto kālakato na dissati,
Ko nidha kandataṃ bālyataro”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 954Ms 19Vv_1065Msdiv 1213“Saccaṃ kho vadesi māṇava,
Ahameva kandataṃ bālyataro;
Candaṃ viya dārako rudaṃ,
Petaṃ kālakatābhipatthayinti.

SC 8SC Verse 955Ms 19Vv_1066Msdiv 1214Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 956Ms 19Vv_1067Msdiv 1215Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
sokaṃ hadayanissitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 10SC Verse 957PTS 2nd ed. 124 Ms 19Vv_1068Msdiv 1216Svāhaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
sītibhūtosmi nibbuto;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna māṇavā”ti.

SC 11SC Verse 958Ms 19Vv_1069Msdiv 1217“Devatā nusi gandhabbo,
adu sakko purindado;
Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayan”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 959Ms 19Vv_1070Msdiv 1218“Yañca kandasi yañca rodasi,
Puttaṃ āḷāhane sayaṃ dahitvā;
Svāhaṃ kusalaṃ karitvā kammaṃ,
Tidasānaṃ sahabyataṃ gato”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 960Ms 19Vv_1071Msdiv 1219“Appaṃ vā bahuṃ vā nāddasāma,
Dānaṃ dadantassa sake agāre;
Uposathakammaṃ vā tādisaṃ,
Kena kammena gatosi devalokan”ti.

SC 14SC Verse 961Ms 19Vv_1072Msdiv 1220“Ābādhikohaṃ dukkhito gilāno,
Āturarūpomhi sake nivesane;
Buddhaṃ vigatarajaṃ vitiṇṇakaṅkhaṃ,
Addakkhiṃ sugataṃ anomapaññaṃ.

SC 15SC Verse 962Ms 19Vv_1073Msdiv 1221Svāhaṃ muditamano pasannacitto,
Añjaliṃ akariṃ tathāgatassa;
Tāhaṃ kusalaṃ karitvāna kammaṃ,
Tidasānaṃ sahabyataṃ gato”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 963Ms 19Vv_1074Msdiv 1222“Acchariyaṃ vata abbhutaṃ vata,
Añjalikammassa ayamīdiso vipāko;
Ahampi muditamano pasannacitto,
Ajjeva buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ vajāmī”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 964Ms 19Vv_1075Msdiv 1223“Ajjeva buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ vajāhi,
Dhammañca saṃghañca pasannacitto;
Tatheva sikkhāya padāni pañca,
Akhaṇḍaphullāni samādiyassu.

SC 18SC Verse 965Ms 19Vv_1076Msdiv 1224Pāṇātipātā viramassu khippaṃ,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayassu;
Amajjapo mā ca musā bhaṇāhi,
Sakena dārena ca hohi tuṭṭho”ti.

SC 19SC Verse 966Ms 19Vv_1077Msdiv 1225“Atthakāmosi me yakkha,
hitakāmosi devate;
Karomi tuyhaṃ vacanaṃ,
tvaṃsi ācariyo mamāti.

SC 20SC Verse 967Ms 19Vv_1078Msdiv 1226Upemi saraṇaṃ buddhaṃ,
dhammañcāpi anuttaraṃ;
Saṃghañca naradevassa,
gacchāmi saraṇaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 21SC Verse 968PTS 2nd ed. 125 Ms 19Vv_1079Msdiv 1227Pāṇātipātā viramāmi khippaṃ,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayāmi;
Amajjapo no ca musā bhaṇāmi,
Sakena dārena ca homi tuṭṭho”ti.

SC 22Ms 19Vv_1080Maṭṭhakuṇḍalīvimānaṃ navamaṃ.
