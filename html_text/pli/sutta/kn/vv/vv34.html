34. Daddallavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 412PTS 2nd ed. 48 Ms 19Vv_458Msdiv 619“Daddallamānā vaṇṇena,
yasasā ca yasassinī;
Sabbe deve tāvatiṃse,
vaṇṇena atirocasi.

SC 2SC Verse 413Ms 19Vv_459Msdiv 620Dassanaṃ nābhijānāmi,
idaṃ paṭhamadassanaṃ;
Kasmā kāyā nu āgamma,
nāmena bhāsase maman”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 414Ms 19Vv_460Msdiv 621“Ahaṃ bhadde subhaddāsiṃ,
pubbe mānusake bhave;
Sahabhariyā ca te āsiṃ,
bhaginī ca kaniṭṭhikā.

SC 4SC Verse 415Ms 19Vv_461Msdiv 622Sā ahaṃ kāyassa bhedā,
vippamuttā tato cutā;
Nimmānaratīnaṃ devānaṃ,
upapannā sahabyatan”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 416Ms 19Vv_462Msdiv 623“Pahūtakatakalyāṇā,
te deve yanti pāṇino;
Yesaṃ tvaṃ kittayissasi,
subhadde jātimattano.

SC 6SC Verse 417Ms 19Vv_463Msdiv 624Atha tvaṃ kena vaṇṇena,
kena vā anusāsitā;
Kīdiseneva dānena,
subbatena yasassinī.

SC 7SC Verse 418Ms 19Vv_464Msdiv 625Yasaṃ etādisaṃ pattā,
visesaṃ vipulamajjhagā;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 419Ms 19Vv_465Msdiv 626“Aṭṭheva piṇḍapātāni,
yaṃ dānaṃ adadaṃ pure;
Dakkhiṇeyyassa saṃghassa,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 9SC Verse 420Ms 19Vv_466Msdiv 627-628Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 10SC Verse 421Ms 19Vv_467Msdiv 629“Ahaṃ tayā bahutare bhikkhū,
Saññate brahmacārayo;
Tappesiṃ annapānena,
Pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 11SC Verse 422Ms 19Vv_468Msdiv 630Tayā bahutaraṃ datvā,
hīnakāyūpagā ahaṃ;
Kathaṃ tvaṃ appataraṃ datvā,
visesaṃ vipulamajjhagā;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 423PTS 2nd ed. 49 Ms 19Vv_469Msdiv 631“Manobhāvanīyo bhikkhu,
sandiṭṭho me pure ahu;
Tāhaṃ bhattena nimantesiṃ,
revataṃ attanaṭṭhamaṃ.

SC 13SC Verse 424Ms 19Vv_470Msdiv 632So me atthapurekkhāro,
anukampāya revato;
Saṃghe dehīti maṃvoca,
tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ kariṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 425Ms 19Vv_471Msdiv 633Sā dakkhiṇā saṃghagatā,
appameyye patiṭṭhitā;
Puggalesu tayā dinnaṃ,
na taṃ tava mahapphalan”ti.

SC 15SC Verse 426Ms 19Vv_472Msdiv 634“Idānevāhaṃ jānāmi,
saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ;
Sāhaṃ gantvā manussattaṃ,
vadaññū vītamaccharā;
Saṃghe dānāni dassāmi,
appamattā punappunan”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 427Ms 19Vv_473Msdiv 635“Kā esā devatā bhadde,
tayā mantayate saha;
Sabbe deve tāvatiṃse,
vaṇṇena atirocatī”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 428Ms 19Vv_474Msdiv 636“Manussabhūtā devinda,
pubbe mānusake bhave;
Sahabhariyā ca me āsi,
bhaginī ca kaniṭṭhikā;
Saṃghe dānāni datvāna,
katapuññā virocatī”ti.

SC 18SC Verse 429Ms 19Vv_475Msdiv 637“Dhammena pubbe bhaginī,
tayā bhadde virocati;
Yaṃ saṃghamhi appameyye,
patiṭṭhāpesi dakkhiṇaṃ”.

SC 19SC Verse 430Ms 19Vv_476Msdiv 638“Pucchito hi mayā buddho,
gijjhakūṭamhi pabbate;
Vipākaṃ saṃvibhāgassa,
yattha dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ.

SC 20SC Verse 431Ms 19Vv_477Msdiv 639Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
yattha dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ.

SC 21SC Verse 432Ms 19Vv_478Msdiv 640Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,
jānaṃ kammaphalaṃ sakaṃ;
Vipākaṃ saṃvibhāgassa,
yattha dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ.

SC 22SC Verse 433Ms 19Vv_479Msdiv 641Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṃgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito.

SC 23SC Verse 434Ms 19Vv_480Msdiv 642Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ.

SC 24SC Verse 435Ms 19Vv_481Msdiv 643Eso hi saṃgho vipulo mahaggato,
Esappameyyo udadhīva sāgaro;
Ete hi seṭṭhā naravīrasāvakā,
Pabhaṅkarā dhammamudīrayanti.

SC 25SC Verse 436PTS 2nd ed. 50 Ms 19Vv_482Msdiv 644Tesaṃ sudinnaṃ suhutaṃ suyiṭṭhaṃ,
Ye saṃghamuddissa dadanti dānaṃ;
Sā dakkhiṇā saṃghagatā patiṭṭhitā,
Mahapphalā lokavidūna vaṇṇitā.

SC 26SC Verse 437Ms 19Vv_483Msdiv 645Etādisaṃ yaññamanussarantā,
Ye vedajātā vicaranti loke;
Vineyya maccheramalaṃ samūlaṃ,
Aninditā saggamupenti ṭhānan”ti.

SC 27Ms 19Vv_484Daddallavimānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
