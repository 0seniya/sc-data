3. Tatiyapīṭhavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 15PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 19Vv_18Msdiv 15“Pīṭhaṃ te sovaṇṇamayaṃ uḷāraṃ,
Manojavaṃ gacchati yenakāmaṃ;
Alaṅkate malyadhare suvatthe,
Obhāsasi vijjurivabbhakūṭaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 16Ms 19Vv_19Msdiv 16Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 3SC Verse 17Ms 19Vv_20Msdiv 17Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 18Ms 19Vv_21Msdiv 18Sā devatā attamanā,
moggallānena pucchitā;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭhā viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 19Ms 19Vv_22Msdiv 19“Appassa kammassa phalaṃ mamedaṃ,
Yenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā;
Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Purimāya jātiyā manussaloke.

SC 6SC Verse 20Ms 19Vv_23Msdiv 20Addasaṃ virajaṃ bhikkhuṃ,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ;
Tassa adāsahaṃ pīṭhaṃ,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 7SC Verse 21Ms 19Vv_24Msdiv 21Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
tena me idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca me bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 8SC Verse 22Ms 19Vv_25Msdiv 22Akkhāmi te bhikkhu mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūtā yamakāsi puññaṃ;
Tenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Vv_26Tatiyapīṭhavimānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
