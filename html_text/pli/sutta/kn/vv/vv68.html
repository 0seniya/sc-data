68. Paṭhamaupassayadāyakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 827PTS 2nd ed. 104 Ms 19Vv_917Msdiv 1069“Cando yathā vigatavalāhake nabhe,
Obhāsayaṃ gacchati antalikkhe;
Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

SC 2SC Verse 828Ms 19Vv_918Msdiv 1070Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 829Ms 19Vv_919Msdiv 1071So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 830Ms 19Vv_920Msdiv 1072“Ahañca bhariyā ca manussaloke,
Upassayaṃ arahato adamha;
Annañca pānañca pasannacittā,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adamha.

SC 5SC Verse 831Ms 19Vv_921Msdiv 1073-1074Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_922Paṭhamaupassayadāyakavimānaṃ catutthaṃ.
