47. Pītavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 570Ms 19Vv_632Msdiv 795“Pītavatthe pītadhaje,
pītālaṅkārabhūsite;
Pītacandanalittaṅge,
pītauppalamālinī.

SC 2SC Verse 571Ms 19Vv_633Msdiv 796Pītapāsādasayane,
pītāsane pītabhājane;
Pītachatte pītarathe,
pītasse pītabījane.

SC 3SC Verse 572Ms 19Vv_634Msdiv 797Kiṃ kammamakarī bhadde,
pubbe mānusake bhave;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 573Ms 19Vv_635Msdiv 798“Kosātakī nāma latatthi bhante,
Tittikā anabhicchitā;
Tassā cattāri pupphāni,
Thūpaṃ abhihariṃ ahaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 574Ms 19Vv_636Msdiv 799Satthu sarīramuddissa,
vippasannena cetasā;
Nāssa maggaṃ avekkhissaṃ,
na taggamanasā satī.

SC 6SC Verse 575Ms 19Vv_637Msdiv 800Tato maṃ avadhī gāvī,
thūpaṃ apattamānasaṃ;
Tañcāhaṃ abhisañceyyaṃ,
bhiyyo nūna ito siyā.

SC 7SC Verse 576Ms 19Vv_638Msdiv 801Tena kammena devinda,
maghavā devakuñjara;
Pahāya mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tava sahabyamāgatā”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 577PTS 2nd ed. 69 Ms 19Vv_639Msdiv 802Idaṃ sutvā tidasādhipati,
Maghavā devakuñjaro;
Tāvatiṃse pasādento,
Mātaliṃ etadabravi.

SC 9SC Verse 578Ms 19Vv_640Msdiv 803“Passa mātali accheraṃ,
cittaṃ kammaphalaṃ idaṃ;
Appakampi kataṃ deyyaṃ,
puññaṃ hoti mahapphalaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 579Ms 19Vv_641Msdiv 804Natthi citte pasannamhi,
appakā nāma dakkhiṇā;
Tathāgate vā sambuddhe,
atha vā tassa sāvake.

SC 11SC Verse 580Ms 19Vv_642Msdiv 805Ehi mātali amhepi,
bhiyyo bhiyyo mahemase;
Tathāgatassa dhātuyo,
sukho puññāna muccayo.

SC 12SC Verse 581Ms 19Vv_643Msdiv 806Tiṭṭhante nibbute cāpi,
same citte samaṃ phalaṃ;
Cetopaṇidhihetu hi,
sattā gacchanti suggatiṃ.

SC 13SC Verse 582Ms 19Vv_644Msdiv 807Bahūnaṃ vata atthāya,
uppajjanti tathāgatā;
Yattha kāraṃ karitvāna,
saggaṃ gacchanti dāyakā”ti.

SC 14Ms 19Vv_645Pītavimānaṃ navamaṃ.
