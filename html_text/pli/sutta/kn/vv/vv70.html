70. Bhikkhādāyakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 833Ms 19Vv_927Msdiv 1081“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 834PTS 2nd ed. 106 Ms 19Vv_928Msdiv 1082Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 835Ms 19Vv_929Msdiv 1083So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 836Ms 19Vv_930Msdiv 1084“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Disvāna bhikkhuṃ tasitaṃ kilantaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ bhikkhaṃ paṭipādayissaṃ,
Samaṅgi bhattena tadā akāsiṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 837Ms 19Vv_931Msdiv 1085-1086Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_932Bhikkhādāyakavimānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
