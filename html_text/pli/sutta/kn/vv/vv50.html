50. Rajjumālāvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 599Ms 19Vv_664Msdiv 826“Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
yā tvaṃ tiṭṭhasi devate;
Hatthapāde ca viggayha,
naccasi suppavādite.

SC 2SC Verse 600Ms 19Vv_665Msdiv 827Tassā te naccamānāya,
aṅgamaṅgehi sabbaso;
Dibbā saddā niccharanti,
savanīyā manoramā.

SC 3SC Verse 601Ms 19Vv_666Msdiv 828Tassā te naccamānāya,
aṅgamaṅgehi sabbaso;
Dibbā gandhā pavāyanti,
sucigandhā manoramā.

SC 4SC Verse 602Ms 19Vv_667Msdiv 829Vivattamānā kāyena,
yā veṇīsu piḷandhanā;
Tesaṃ suyyati nigghoso,
tūriye pañcaṅgike yathā.

SC 5SC Verse 603Ms 19Vv_668Msdiv 830Vaṭaṃsakā vātadhutā,
vātena sampakampitā;
Tesaṃ suyyati nigghoso,
tūriye pañcaṅgike yathā.

SC 6SC Verse 604Ms 19Vv_669Msdiv 831Yāpi te sirasmiṃ mālā,
sucigandhā manoramā;
Vāti gandho disā sabbā,
rukkho mañjūsako yathā.

SC 7SC Verse 605PTS 2nd ed. 73 Ms 19Vv_670Msdiv 832Ghāyase taṃ sucigandhaṃ,
rūpaṃ passasi amānusaṃ;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 606Ms 19Vv_671Msdiv 833“Dāsī ahaṃ pure āsiṃ,
gayāyaṃ brāhmaṇassahaṃ;
Appapuññā alakkhikā,
rajjumālāti maṃ viduṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 607Ms 19Vv_672Msdiv 834Akkosānaṃ vadhānañca,
tajjanāya ca uggatā;
Kuṭaṃ gahetvā nikkhamma,
agañchiṃ agañchiṃ udahāriyā.

SC 10SC Verse 608Ms 19Vv_673Msdiv 835Vipathe kuṭaṃ nikkhipitvā,
vanasaṇḍaṃ upāgamiṃ;
Idhevāhaṃ marissāmi,
ko attho jīvitena me.

SC 11SC Verse 609Ms 19Vv_674Msdiv 836Daḷhaṃ pāsaṃ karitvāna,
āsumbhitvāna pādape;
Tato disā vilokesiṃ,
‘ko nu kho vanamassito’.

SC 12SC Verse 610Ms 19Vv_675Msdiv 837Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sabbalokahitaṃ muniṃ;
Nisinnaṃ rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
jhāyantaṃ akutobhayaṃ.

SC 13SC Verse 611Ms 19Vv_676Msdiv 838Tassā me ahu saṃvego,
abbhuto lomahaṃsano;
Ko nu kho vanamassito,
manusso udāhu devatā.

SC 14SC Verse 612Ms 19Vv_677Msdiv 839Pāsādikaṃ pasādanīyaṃ,
vanā nibbanamāgataṃ;
Disvā mano me pasīdi,
nāyaṃ yādisakīdiso.

SC 15SC Verse 613Ms 19Vv_678Msdiv 840Guttindriyo jhānarato,
abahiggatamānaso;
Hito sabbassa lokassa,
buddho ayaṃ bhavissati.

SC 16SC Verse 614Ms 19Vv_679Msdiv 841Bhayabheravo durāsado,
sīhova guhamassito;
Dullabhāyaṃ dassanāya,
pupphaṃ odumbaraṃ yathā.

SC 17SC Verse 615Ms 19Vv_680Msdiv 842So maṃ mudūhi vācāhi,
ālapitvā tathāgato;
Rajjumāleti maṃvoca,
saraṇaṃ gaccha tathāgataṃ.

SC 18SC Verse 616Ms 19Vv_681Msdiv 843Tāhaṃ giraṃ suṇitvāna,
nelaṃ atthavatiṃ suciṃ;
Saṇhaṃ muduñca vagguñca,
sabbasokāpanūdanaṃ.

SC 19SC Verse 617Ms 19Vv_682Msdiv 844Kallacittañca maṃ ñatvā,
pasannaṃ suddhamānasaṃ;
Hito sabbassa lokassa,
anusāsi tathāgato.

SC 20SC Verse 618Ms 19Vv_683Msdiv 845Idaṃ dukkhanti maṃvoca,
Ayaṃ dukkhassa sambhavo;
Dukkha Dukkha nirodho maggo ca,
Añjaso amatogadho.

SC 21SC Verse 619Ms 19Vv_684Msdiv 846Anukampakassa kusalassa,
Ovādamhi ahaṃ ṭhitā;
Ajjhagā amataṃ santiṃ,
Nibbānaṃ padamaccutaṃ.

SC 22SC Verse 620Ms 19Vv_685Msdiv 847Sāhaṃ avaṭṭhitāpemā,
dassane avikampinī;
Mūlajātāya saddhāya,
dhītā buddhassa orasā.

SC 23SC Verse 621PTS 2nd ed. 74 Ms 19Vv_686Msdiv 848Sāhaṃ ramāmi kīḷāmi,
modāmi akutobhayā;
Dibbamālaṃ dhārayāmi,
pivāmi madhumaddavaṃ.

SC 24SC Verse 622Ms 19Vv_687Msdiv 849Saṭṭhitūriyasahassāni,
paṭibodhaṃ karonti me;
Āḷambo gaggaro bhīmo,
sādhuvādī ca saṃsayo.

SC 25SC Verse 623Ms 19Vv_688Msdiv 850Pokkharo ca suphasso ca,
vīṇāmokkhā ca nāriyo;
Nandā ceva sunandā ca,
soṇadinnā sucimhitā.

SC 26SC Verse 624Ms 19Vv_689Msdiv 851Alambusā missakesī ca,
puṇḍarīkātidāruṇī;
Eṇīphassā suphassā ca,
subhaddā muduvādinī.

SC 27SC Verse 625Ms 19Vv_690Msdiv 852Etā caññā ca seyyāse,
accharānaṃ pabodhikā;
Tā maṃ kālenupāgantvā,
abhibhāsanti devatā.

SC 28SC Verse 626Ms 19Vv_691Msdiv 853Handa naccāma gāyāma,
handa taṃ ramayāmase;
Nayidaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
katapuññānamevidaṃ.

SC 29SC Verse 627Ms 19Vv_692Msdiv 854Asokaṃ nandanaṃ rammaṃ,
tidasānaṃ mahāvanaṃ;
Sukhaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
idha natthi parattha ca.

SC 30SC Verse 628Ms 19Vv_693Msdiv 855Sukhañca katapuññānaṃ,
idha ceva parattha ca;
Tesaṃ sahabyakāmānaṃ,
kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Katapuññā hi modanti,
sagge bhogasamaṅgino.

SC 31SC Verse 629Ms 19Vv_694Msdiv 856Bahūnaṃ vata atthāya,
uppajjanti tathāgatā;
Dakkhiṇeyyā manussānaṃ,
puññakhettānamākarā;
Yattha kāraṃ karitvāna,
sagge modanti dāyakā”ti.

SC 32Ms 19Vv_695Rajjumālāvimānaṃ dvādasamaṃ.

SC 33Ms 19Vv_696Tassuddānaṃ

SC 34PTS 2nd ed. 76 Ms 19Vv_697Mañjiṭṭhā pabhassarā nāgā,
Alomākañjikadāyikā;
Vihāracaturitthambā,
Pītā ucchuvandanarajjumālā ca;
Vaggo tena pavuccatīti.

SC 35Ms 19Vv_698Mañjiṭṭhakavaggo catuttho.

SC 36Ms 19Vv_699Itthivimānaṃ samattaṃ.
