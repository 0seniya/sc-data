72. Paṭhamakuṇḍalīvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 843Ms 19Vv_939SC 1SC Verse 843Ms 19Vv_939Msdiv 1094“Alaṅkato malyadharo suvattho,
Sukuṇḍalī kappitakesamassu;
Āmuttahatthābharaṇo yasassī,
Dibbe vimānamhi yathāpi candimā.

SC 2SC Verse 844Ms 19Vv_940SC 2SC Verse 844Ms 19Vv_940Msdiv 1095Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ,
Aṭṭhaṭṭhakā sikkhitā sādhurūpā;
Dibbā ca kaññā tidasacarā uḷārā,
Naccanti gāyanti pamodayanti.

SC 3SC Verse 845Ms 19Vv_941SC 3SC Verse 845Ms 19Vv_941Msdiv 1096Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,

Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.
