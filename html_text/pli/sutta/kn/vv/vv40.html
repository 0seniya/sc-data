40. Pabhassaravimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 489PTS 2nd ed. 58 Ms 19Vv_544Msdiv 697“Pabhassaravaravaṇṇanibhe,
Surattavatthavasane;
Mahiddhike candanaruciragatte,
Kā tvaṃ subhe devate vandase mamaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 490Ms 19Vv_545Msdiv 698Pallaṅko ca te mahaggho,
Nānāratanacittito ruciro;
Yattha tvaṃ nisinnā virocasi,
Devarājāriva nandane vane.

SC 3SC Verse 491Ms 19Vv_546Msdiv 699Kiṃ tvaṃ pure sucaritamācarī bhadde,
Kissa kammassa vipākaṃ;
Anubhosi devalokasmiṃ,
Devate pucchitācikkha;
Kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 492Ms 19Vv_547Msdiv 700“Piṇḍāya te carantassa,
Mālaṃ phāṇitañca adadaṃ bhante;
Tassa kammassidaṃ vipākaṃ,
Anubhomi devalokasmiṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 493Ms 19Vv_548Msdiv 701Hoti ca me anutāpo,
Aparaddhaṃ dukkhitañca me bhante;
Sāhaṃ dhammaṃ nāssosiṃ,
Sudesitaṃ dhammarājena.

SC 6SC Verse 494Ms 19Vv_549Msdiv 702Taṃ taṃ vadāmi bhaddante,
‘Yassa me anukampiyo koci;
Dhammesu taṃ samādapetha’,
Sudesitaṃ dhammarājena.

SC 7SC Verse 495Ms 19Vv_550Msdiv 703Yesaṃ atthi saddhā buddhe,
dhamme ca saṃgharatane;
Te maṃ ativirocanti,
āyunā yasasā siriyā.

SC 8SC Verse 496Ms 19Vv_551Msdiv 704Patāpena vaṇṇena uttaritarā,
Aññe mahiddhikatarā mayā devā”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Vv_552Pabhassaravimānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
