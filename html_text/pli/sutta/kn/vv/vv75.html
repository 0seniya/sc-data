75. Cittalatāvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 860PTS 2nd ed. 111 Ms 19Vv_963Msdiv 1114“Yathā vanaṃ cittalataṃ pabhāsati,
Uyyānaseṭṭhaṃ tidasānamuttamaṃ;
Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

SC 2SC Verse 861Ms 19Vv_964Msdiv 1115Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 862Ms 19Vv_965Msdiv 1116So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 863Ms 19Vv_966Msdiv 1117“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Daliddo atāṇo kapaṇo kammakaro ahosiṃ;
Jiṇṇe ca mātāpitaro abhāriṃ,
Piyā ca me sīlavanto ahesuṃ;
Annañca pānañca pasannacitto,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adāsiṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 864Ms 19Vv_967Msdiv 1118-1119Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_968Cittalatāvimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
