67. Phaladāyakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 819PTS 2nd ed. 103 Ms 19Vv_908Msdiv 1060“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato soḷasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 820Ms 19Vv_909Msdiv 1061Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,
Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;
Aṭṭhaṭṭhakā sikkhitā sādhurūpā,
Dibbā ca kaññā tidasacarā uḷārā;
Naccanti gāyanti pamodayanti.

SC 3SC Verse 821Ms 19Vv_910Msdiv 1062Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 822Ms 19Vv_911Msdiv 1063So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
Yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 823Ms 19Vv_912Msdiv 1064“Phaladāyī phalaṃ vipulaṃ labhati,
Dadamujugatesu pasannamānaso;
So hi pamodati saggagato pamodati saggagato tidive,
Anubhoti ca puññaphalaṃ vipulaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 824Ms 19Vv_913Msdiv 1065Tavevāhaṃ mahāmuni,
Adāsiṃ caturo phale.

SC 7SC Verse 825Ms 19Vv_914Msdiv 1066Tasmā hi phalaṃ alameva dātuṃ,
Niccaṃ manussena sukhatthikena;
Dibbāni vā patthayatā sukhāni,
Manussasobhaggatamicchatā vā.

SC 8SC Verse 826Ms 19Vv_915Msdiv 1067-1068Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Vv_916Phaladāyakavimānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
