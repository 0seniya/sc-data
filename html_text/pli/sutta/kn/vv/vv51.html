51. Maṇḍūkadevaputtavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 630PTS 2nd ed. 77 Ms 19Vv_700Msdiv 857“Ko me vandati pādāni,
iddhiyā yasasā jalaṃ;
Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
sabbā obhāsayaṃ disā”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 631Ms 19Vv_701Msdiv 858“Maṇḍūkohaṃ pure āsiṃ,
udake vārigocaro;
Tava dhammaṃ suṇantassa,
avadhī vacchapālako.

SC 3SC Verse 632Ms 19Vv_702Msdiv 859Muhuttaṃ cittapasādassa,
iddhiṃ passa yasañca me;
Ānubhāvañca me passa,
vaṇṇaṃ passa jutiñca me.

SC 4SC Verse 633Ms 19Vv_703Msdiv 860Ye ca te dīghamaddhānaṃ,
dhammaṃ assosuṃ gotama;
Pattā te acalaṭṭhānaṃ,
yattha gantvā na socare”ti.

SC 5Ms 19Vv_704Maṇḍūkadevaputtavimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
