18. Dāsivimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 156PTS 2nd ed. 21 Ms 19Vv_178Msdiv 157“Api sakkova devindo,
ramme cittalatāvane;
Samantā anupariyāsi,
nārīgaṇapurakkhatā;
Obhāsentī disā sabbā,
osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 2SC Verse 157Ms 19Vv_179Msdiv 158Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 3SC Verse 158Ms 19Vv_180Msdiv 159Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 159Ms 19Vv_181Msdiv 160Sā devatā attamanā,
moggallānena pucchitā;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭhā viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 160Ms 19Vv_182Msdiv 161“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Dāsī ahosiṃ parapessiyā kule.

SC 6SC Verse 161Ms 19Vv_183Msdiv 162Upāsikā cakkhumato,
gotamassa yasassino;
Tassā me nikkamo āsi,
sāsane tassa tādino.

SC 7SC Verse 162Ms 19Vv_184Msdiv 163Kāmaṃ bhijjatuyaṃ kāyo,
neva atthettha saṇṭhanaṃ;
Sikkhāpadānaṃ pañcannaṃ,
maggo sovatthiko sivo.

SC 8SC Verse 163Ms 19Vv_185Msdiv 164Akaṇṭako agahano,
uju sabbhi pavedito;
Nikkamassa phalaṃ passa,
yathidaṃ pāpuṇitthikā.

SC 9SC Verse 164PTS 2nd ed. 22 Ms 19Vv_186Msdiv 165Āmantanikā raññomhi,
sakkassa vasavattino;
Saṭṭhi tūriyasahassāni,
paṭibodhaṃ karonti me.

SC 10SC Verse 165Ms 19Vv_187Msdiv 166Ālambo gaggaro gaggaro bhīmo,
sādhuvādī ca saṃsayo;
Pokkharo ca suphasso ca,
viṇāmokkhā ca nāriyo.

SC 11SC Verse 166Ms 19Vv_188Msdiv 167Nandā ceva sunandā ca,
soṇadinnā sucimhitā;
Alambusā missakesī ca,
puṇḍarīkāti dāruṇī.

SC 12SC Verse 167Ms 19Vv_189Msdiv 168Eṇīphassā suphassā ca,
subhaddā muduvādinī;
Etā caññā ca seyyāse,
accharānaṃ pabodhikā.

SC 13SC Verse 168Ms 19Vv_190Msdiv 169Tā maṃ kālenupāgantvā,
abhibhāsanti devatā;
Handa naccāma gāyāma,
handa taṃ ramayāmase.

SC 14SC Verse 169Ms 19Vv_191Msdiv 170Nayidaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
katapuññānamevidaṃ;
Asokaṃ nandanaṃ rammaṃ,
tidasānaṃ mahāvanaṃ.

SC 15SC Verse 170Ms 19Vv_192Msdiv 171Sukhaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
idha natthi parattha ca;
Sukhañca katapuññānaṃ,
idha ceva parattha ca.

SC 16SC Verse 171Ms 19Vv_193Msdiv 172Tesaṃ sahabyakāmānaṃ,
kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Katapuññā hi modanti,
sagge bhogasamaṅgino”ti.

SC 17Ms 19Vv_194Dāsivimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
