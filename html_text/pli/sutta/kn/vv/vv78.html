78. Suvaṇṇavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 876PTS 2nd ed. 114 Ms 19Vv_982Msdiv 1134“Sovaṇṇamaye pabbatasmiṃ,
vimānaṃ sabbatopabhaṃ;
Hemajālapaṭicchannaṃ,
kiṅkiṇijālakappitaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 877Ms 19Vv_983Msdiv 1135Aṭṭhaṃsā sukatā thambhā,
sabbe veḷuriyāmayā;
Ekamekāya aṃsiyā,
ratanā satta nimmitā.

SC 3SC Verse 878Ms 19Vv_984Msdiv 1136Veḷuriyasuvaṇṇassa,
phalikā rūpiyassa ca;
Masāragallamuttāhi,
lohitaṅgamaṇīhi ca.

SC 4SC Verse 879Ms 19Vv_985Msdiv 1137Citrā manoramā bhūmi,
na tatthuddhaṃsatī rajo;
Gopānasīgaṇā pītā,
kūṭaṃ dhārenti nimmitā.

SC 5SC Verse 880Ms 19Vv_986Msdiv 1138Sopānāni ca cattāri,
nimmitā caturo disā;
Nānāratanagabbhehi,
ādiccova virocati.

SC 6SC Verse 881Ms 19Vv_987Msdiv 1139Vediyā catasso tattha,
vibhattā bhāgaso mitā;
Daddallamānā ābhanti,
samantā caturo disā.

SC 7SC Verse 882Ms 19Vv_988Msdiv 1140Tasmiṃ vimāne pavare,
devaputto mahappabho;
Atirocasi vaṇṇena,
udayantova bhāṇumā.

SC 8SC Verse 883Ms 19Vv_989Msdiv 1141Dānassa te idaṃ phalaṃ,
atho sīlassa vā pana;
Atho añjalikammassa,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 9SC Verse 884Ms 19Vv_990Msdiv 1142So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 885Ms 19Vv_991Msdiv 1143“Ahaṃ andhakavindasmiṃ,
buddhassādiccabandhuno;
Vihāraṃ satthu kāresiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 11SC Verse 886Ms 19Vv_992Msdiv 1144Tattha gandhañca mālañca,
paccayañca vilepanaṃ;
Vihāraṃ satthu adāsiṃ,
vippasannena cetasā;
Tena mayhaṃ idaṃ laddhaṃ,
vasaṃ vattemi nandane.

SC 12SC Verse 887Ms 19Vv_993Msdiv 1145Nandane ca vane ramme,
nānādijagaṇāyute;
Ramāmi naccagītehi,
accharāhi purakkhato”ti.

SC 13Ms 19Vv_994Suvaṇṇavimānaṃ catutthaṃ.
