46. Ambavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 560Ms 19Vv_621Msdiv 783“Dibbaṃ te ambavanaṃ rammaṃ,
Pāsādettha mahallako;
Nānātūriyasaṅghuṭṭho,
Accharāgaṇaghosito.

SC 2SC Verse 561Ms 19Vv_622Msdiv 784Padīpo cettha jalati,
niccaṃ sovaṇṇayo mahā;
Dussaphalehi rukkhehi,
samantā parivārito.

SC 3SC Verse 562Ms 19Vv_623Msdiv 785-786Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 563Ms 19Vv_624Msdiv 787Sā devatā attamanā,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 564Ms 19Vv_625Msdiv 788“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Purimāya jātiyā manussaloke;
Vihāraṃ saṃghassa kāresiṃ,
Ambehi parivāritaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 565Ms 19Vv_626Msdiv 789Pariyosite vihāre,
kārente niṭṭhite mahe;
Ambehi chādayitvāna,
katvā dussamaye phale.

SC 7SC Verse 566Ms 19Vv_627Msdiv 790Padīpaṃ tattha jāletvā,
bhojayitvā gaṇuttamaṃ;
Niyyādesiṃ taṃ saṃghassa,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 8SC Verse 567Ms 19Vv_628Msdiv 791Tena me ambavanaṃ rammaṃ,
pāsādettha mahallako;
Nānātūriyasaṅghuṭṭho,
accharāgaṇaghosito.

SC 9SC Verse 568PTS 2nd ed. 68 Ms 19Vv_629Msdiv 792Padīpo cettha jalati,
niccaṃ sovaṇṇayo mahā;
Dussaphalehi rukkhehi,
samantā parivārito.

SC 10SC Verse 569Ms 19Vv_630Msdiv 793-794Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 11Ms 19Vv_631Ambavimānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
