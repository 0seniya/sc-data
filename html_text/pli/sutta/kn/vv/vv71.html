71. Yavapālakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 838Ms 19Vv_933Msdiv 1087-1088“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 839Ms 19Vv_934Msdiv 1089So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 840Ms 19Vv_935Msdiv 1090“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Ahosiṃ yavapālako;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ bhikkhuṃ,
Vippasannamanāvilaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 841PTS 2nd ed. 107 Ms 19Vv_936Msdiv 1091Tassa adāsahaṃ bhāgaṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi;
Kummāsapiṇḍaṃ datvāna,
modāmi nandane vane.

SC 5SC Verse 842Ms 19Vv_937Msdiv 1092-1093Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_938Yavapālakavimānaṃ sattamaṃ.
