52. Revatīvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 634Ms 19Vv_705Msdiv 861“Cirappavāsiṃ purisaṃ,
dūrato sotthimāgataṃ;
Ñātimittā suhajjā ca,
abhinandanti āgataṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 635PTS 2nd ed. 78 Ms 19Vv_706Msdiv 862Tatheva katapuññampi,
asmā lokā paraṃ gataṃ;
Puññāni paṭigaṇhanti,
piyaṃ ñātīva āgataṃ”.

SC 3SC Verse 636Ms 19Vv_707Msdiv 863“Uṭṭhehi revate supāpadhamme,
Apārutadvāre adānasīle;
Nessāma taṃ yattha thunanti duggatā,
Samappitā nerayikā dukkhenā”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 637Ms 19Vv_708Msdiv 864Icceva vatvāna yamassa dūtā,
Te dve yakkhā lohitakkhā brahantā;
Paccekabāhāsu gahetvā revataṃ,
Pakkāmayuṃ devagaṇassa santike.

SC 5SC Verse 638Ms 19Vv_709Msdiv 865“Ādiccavaṇṇaṃ ruciraṃ pabhassaraṃ,
Byamhaṃ subhaṃ kañcanajālachannaṃ;
Kassetamākiṇṇajanaṃ vimānaṃ,
Suriyassa raṃsīriva jotamānaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 639Ms 19Vv_710Msdiv 866Nārīgaṇā candanasāralittā,
Ubhato vimānaṃ upasobhayanti;
Taṃ dissati suriyasamānavaṇṇaṃ,
Ko modati saggapatto vimāne”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 640Ms 19Vv_711Msdiv 867“Bārāṇasiyaṃ nandiyo nāmāsi,
Upāsako amaccharī dānapati vadaññū;
Tassetamākiṇṇajanaṃ vimānaṃ,
Suriyassa raṃsīriva jotamānaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 641Ms 19Vv_712Msdiv 868Nārīgaṇā candanasāralittā,
Ubhato vimānaṃ upasobhayanti;
Taṃ dissati suriyasamānavaṇṇaṃ,
So modati saggapatto vimāne”ti.

SC 9SC Verse 642Ms 19Vv_713Msdiv 869“Nandiyassāhaṃ bhariyā,
Agārinī sabbakulassa issarā;
Bhattu vimāne ramissāmi dānahaṃ,
Na patthaye nirayaṃ dassanāyā”ti.

SC 10SC Verse 643Ms 19Vv_714Msdiv 870“Eso te nirayo supāpadhamme,
Puññaṃ tayā akataṃ jīvaloke;
Na hi maccharī rosako pāpadhammo,
Saggūpagānaṃ labhati sahabyatan”ti.

SC 11SC Verse 644Ms 19Vv_715Msdiv 871“Kiṃ nu gūthañca muttañca,
asucī paṭidissati;
Duggandhaṃ kimidaṃ mīḷhaṃ,
kimetaṃ upavāyatī”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 645Ms 19Vv_716Msdiv 872“Esa saṃsavako nāma,
gambhīro sataporiso;
Yattha vassasahassāni,
tuvaṃ paccasi revate”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 646PTS 2nd ed. 79 Ms 19Vv_717Msdiv 873“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kena saṃsavako laddho,
gambhīro sataporiso”ti.

SC 14SC Verse 647Ms 19Vv_718Msdiv 874“Samaṇe brāhmaṇe cāpi,
aññe vāpi vanibbake;
Musāvādena vañcesi,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ tayā.

SC 15SC Verse 648Ms 19Vv_719Msdiv 875Tena saṃsavako laddho,
gambhīro sataporiso;
Tattha vassasahassāni,
tuvaṃ paccasi revate.

SC 16SC Verse 649Ms 19Vv_720Msdiv 876Hatthepi chindanti athopi pāde,
Kaṇṇepi chindanti athopi nāsaṃ;
Athopi kākoḷagaṇā samecca,
Saṅgamma khādanti viphandamānan”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 650Ms 19Vv_721Msdiv 877“Sādhu kho maṃ paṭinetha,
kāhāmi kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Dānena samacariyāya,
saṃyamena damena ca;
Yaṃ katvā sukhitā honti,
na ca pacchānutappare”ti.

SC 18SC Verse 651Ms 19Vv_722Msdiv 878“Pure tuvaṃ pamajjitvā,
idāni paridevasi;
Sayaṃ katānaṃ kammānaṃ,
vipākaṃ anubhossasī”ti.

SC 19SC Verse 652Ms 19Vv_723Msdiv 879“Ko devalokato manussalokaṃ,
Gantvāna puṭṭho me evaṃ vadeyya;
‘Nikkhittadaṇḍesu dadātha dānaṃ,
Acchādanaṃ seyyamathannapānaṃ;
Na hi maccharī rosako pāpadhammo,
Saggūpagānaṃ labhati sahabyataṃ’.

SC 20SC Verse 653Ms 19Vv_724Msdiv 880Sāhaṃ nūna ito gantvā,
yoniṃ laddhāna mānusiṃ;
Vadaññū sīlasampannā,
kāhāmi kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Dānena samacariyāya,
saṃyamena damena ca.

SC 21SC Verse 654Ms 19Vv_725Msdiv 881Ārāmāni ca ropissaṃ,
dugge saṅkamanāni ca;
Papañca udapānañca,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 22SC Verse 655Ms 19Vv_726Msdiv 882Cātuddasiṃ pañcadasiṃ,
yā ca pakkhassa aṭṭhamī;
Pāṭihāriyapakkhañca,
aṭṭhaṅgasusamāgataṃ.

SC 23SC Verse 656Ms 19Vv_727Msdiv 883Uposathaṃ upavasissaṃ,
sadā sīlesu saṃvutā;
Na ca dāne pamajjissaṃ,
sāmaṃ diṭṭhamidaṃ mayā”ti.

SC 24SC Verse 657Ms 19Vv_728Msdiv 884Iccevaṃ vippalapantiṃ,
phandamānaṃ tato tato;
Khipiṃsu niraye ghore,
uddhampādaṃ avaṃsiraṃ.

SC 25SC Verse 658Ms 19Vv_729Msdiv 885“Ahaṃ pure maccharinī ahosiṃ,
Paribhāsikā samaṇabrāhmaṇānaṃ;
Vitathena ca sāmikaṃ vañcayitvā,
Paccāmahaṃ niraye ghorarūpe”ti.

SC 26Ms 19Vv_730Revatīvimānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
