79. Ambavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 888PTS 2nd ed. 115 Ms 19Vv_995Msdiv 1146“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 889Ms 19Vv_996Msdiv 1147Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,
Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;
Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,
Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā.

SC 3SC Verse 890Ms 19Vv_997Msdiv 1148-1149Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 891Ms 19Vv_998Msdiv 1150So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 892Ms 19Vv_999Msdiv 1151“Gimhānaṃ pacchime māse,
patapante divaṅkare;
Paresaṃ bhatako poso,
ambārāmamasiñcati.

SC 6SC Verse 893Ms 19Vv_1000Msdiv 1152Atha tenāgamā bhikkhu,
sāriputtoti vissuto;
Kilantarūpo kāyena,
akilantova cetasā.

SC 7SC Verse 894PTS 2nd ed. 116 Ms 19Vv_1001Msdiv 1153Tañca disvāna āyantaṃ,
Avocaṃ ambasiñcako;
Sādhu taṃ bhante nhāpeyyaṃ,
Yaṃ mamassa sukhāvahaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 895Ms 19Vv_1002Msdiv 1154Tassa me anukampāya,
nikkhipi pattacīvaraṃ;
Nisīdi rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
chāyāya ekacīvaro.

SC 9SC Verse 896Ms 19Vv_1003Msdiv 1155Tañca acchena vārinā,
pasannamānaso naro;
Nhāpayī rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
chāyāya ekacīvaraṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 897Ms 19Vv_1004Msdiv 1156Ambo ca sitto samaṇo ca nhāpito,
Mayā ca puññaṃ pasutaṃ anappakaṃ;
Iti so pītiyā kāyaṃ,
Sabbaṃ pharati attano.

SC 11SC Verse 898Ms 19Vv_1005Msdiv 1157Tadeva ettakaṃ kammaṃ,
akāsiṃ tāya jātiyā;
Pahāya mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
upapannomhi nandanaṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 899Ms 19Vv_1006Msdiv 1158Nandane ca vane ramme,
nānādijagaṇāyute;
Ramāmi naccagītehi,
accharāhi purakkhato”ti.

SC 13Ms 19Vv_1007Ambavimānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
