80. Gopālavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 900Ms 19Vv_1008Msdiv 1159“Disvāna devaṃ paṭipucchi bhikkhu,
Ucce vimānamhi ciraṭṭhitike;
Āmuttahatthābharaṇaṃ yasassiṃ,
Dibbe vimānamhi yathāpi candimā.

SC 2SC Verse 901PTS 2nd ed. 117 Ms 19Vv_1009Msdiv 1160Alaṅkato malyadharo suvattho,
Sukuṇḍalī kappitakesamassu;
Āmuttahatthābharaṇo yasassī,
Dibbe vimānamhi yathāpi candimā.

SC 3SC Verse 902Ms 19Vv_1010Msdiv 1161Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ,
Aṭṭhaṭṭhakā sikkhitā sādhurūpā;
Dibbā ca kaññā tidasacarā uḷārā,
Naccanti gāyanti pamodayanti.

SC 4SC Verse 903Ms 19Vv_1011Msdiv 1162Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 904Ms 19Vv_1012Msdiv 1163So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 905Ms 19Vv_1013Msdiv 1164“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Saṅgamma rakkhissaṃ paresaṃ dhenuyo;
Tato ca āgā samaṇo mamantike,
Gāvo ca māse agamaṃsu khādituṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 906Ms 19Vv_1014Msdiv 1165Dvayajja kiccaṃ ubhayañca kāriyaṃ,
Iccevahaṃ bhante tadā vicintayiṃ;
Tato ca saññaṃ paṭiladdha yoniso,
Dadāmi bhanteti khipiṃ anantakaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 907Ms 19Vv_1015Msdiv 1166So māsakhettaṃ turito avāsariṃ,
Purā ayaṃ bhañjati yassidaṃ dhanaṃ;
Tato ca kaṇho urago mahāviso,
Aḍaṃsi pāde turitassa me sato.

SC 9SC Verse 908Ms 19Vv_1016Msdiv 1167Svāhaṃ aṭṭomhi dukkhena pīḷito,
Bhikkhu ca taṃ sāmaṃ muñcitvānantakaṃ;
Ahāsi kummāsaṃ mamānukampayā,
Tato cuto kālakatomhi devatā.

SC 10SC Verse 909Ms 19Vv_1017Msdiv 1168Tadeva kammaṃ kusalaṃ kataṃ mayā,
Sukhañca kammaṃ anubhomi attanā;
Tayā hi bhante anukampito bhusaṃ,
Kataññutāya abhivādayāmi taṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 910Ms 19Vv_1018Msdiv 1169Sadevake loke samārake ca,
Añño muni natthi tayānukampako;
Tayā hi bhante anukampito bhusaṃ,
Kataññutāya abhivādayāmi taṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 911PTS 2nd ed. 118 Ms 19Vv_1019Msdiv 1170Imasmiṃ loke parasmiṃ vā pana,
Añño munī natthi tayānukampako;
Tayā hi bhante anukampito bhusaṃ,
Kataññutāya abhivādayāmi tan”ti.

SC 13Ms 19Vv_1020Gopālavimānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
