4. Catutthapīṭhavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 23PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 19Vv_27SC 1SC Verse 23PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 19Vv_27Msdiv 23“Pīṭhaṃ te veḷuriyamayaṃ uḷāraṃ,
Manojavaṃ gacchati yenakāmaṃ;
Alaṅkate malyadhare suvatthe,
Obhāsasi vijjurivabbhakūṭaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 24Ms 19Vv_28SC 2SC Verse 24Ms 19Vv_28Msdiv 24Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 3SC Verse 25Ms 19Vv_29SC 3SC Verse 25Ms 19Vv_29Msdiv 25Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 26Ms 19Vv_30SC 4SC Verse 26Ms 19Vv_30Msdiv 26Sā devatā attamanā,
moggallānena pucchitā;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭhā viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 27Ms 19Vv_31SC 5SC Verse 27Ms 19Vv_31Msdiv 27“Appassa kammassa phalaṃ mamedaṃ,
Yenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā;
Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Purimāya jātiyā manussaloke.

SC 6SC Verse 28Ms 19Vv_32SC 6SC Verse 28Ms 19Vv_32Msdiv 28Addasaṃ virajaṃ bhikkhuṃ,
vippasannamanāvilaṃ;
Tassa adāsahaṃ pīṭhaṃ,
pasannā sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 7SC Verse 29Ms 19Vv_33SC 7SC Verse 29Ms 19Vv_33Msdiv 29Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
tena me idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca me bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 8SC Verse 30Ms 19Vv_34SC 8SC Verse 30Ms 19Vv_34Msdiv 30Akkhāmi te bhikkhu mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūtā yamakāsi puññaṃ;
Tenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 9SC 9Ms 19Vv_35Catutthapīṭhavimānaṃ catutthaṃ.
