65. Paṭhamaagāriyavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 809PTS 2nd ed. 101 Ms 19Vv_896Msdiv 1048“Yathā vanaṃ cittalataṃ pabhāsati,
Uyyānaseṭṭhaṃ tidasānamuttamaṃ;
Tathūpamaṃ tuyhamidaṃ vimānaṃ,
Obhāsayaṃ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

SC 2SC Verse 810Ms 19Vv_897Msdiv 1049Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 811Ms 19Vv_898Msdiv 1050So devaputto attamano,
moggallānena pucchito;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 812Ms 19Vv_899Msdiv 1051“Ahañca bhariyā ca manussaloke,
Opānabhūtā gharamāvasimha;
Annañca pānañca pasannacittā,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adamha.

SC 5SC Verse 813Ms 19Vv_900Msdiv 1052-1053Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 19Vv_901Paṭhamaagāriyavimānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
