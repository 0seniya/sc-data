73. Dutiyakuṇḍalīvimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 849Ms 19Vv_946Msdiv 1101“Alaṅkato malyadharo suvattho,
Sukuṇḍalī kappitakesamassu;
Āmuttahatthābharaṇo yasassī,
Dibbe vimānamhi yathāpi candimā.

SC 2SC Verse 850Ms 19Vv_947Msdiv 1102Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ,
Aṭṭhaṭṭhakā sikkhitā sādhurūpā;
Dibbā ca kaññā tidasacarā uḷārā,
Naccanti gāyanti pamodayanti.

SC 3SC Verse 851Ms 19Vv_948Msdiv 1103Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 852Ms 19Vv_949Msdiv 1104So devaputto attamano,
… pe …
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 853Ms 19Vv_950Msdiv 1105“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,
Disvāna samaṇe sādhurūpe;
Sampannavijjācaraṇe yasassī,
Bahussute sīlavante pasanne;
Annañca pānañca pasannacitto,
Sakkacca dānaṃ vipulaṃ adāsiṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 854PTS 2nd ed. 109 Ms 19Vv_951Msdiv 1106-1107Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 7Ms 19Vv_952Dutiyakuṇḍalīvimānaṃ navamaṃ.
