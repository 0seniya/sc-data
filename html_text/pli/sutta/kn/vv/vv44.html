44. Vihāravimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 518Ms 19Vv_577Msdiv 729“Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
… pe …
osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 2SC Verse 519Ms 19Vv_578Msdiv 730Tassā te naccamānāya,
aṅgamaṅgehi sabbaso;
Dibbā saddā niccharanti,
savanīyā manoramā.

SC 3SC Verse 520Ms 19Vv_579Msdiv 731Tassā te naccamānāya,
aṅgamaṅgehi sabbaso;
Dibbā gandhā pavāyanti,
sucigandhā manoramā.

SC 4SC Verse 521Ms 19Vv_580Msdiv 732Vivattamānā kāyena,
yā veṇīsu piḷandhanā;
Tesaṃ suyyati nigghoso,
tūriye pañcaṅgike yathā.

SC 5SC Verse 522Ms 19Vv_581Msdiv 733Vaṭaṃsakā vātadhutā,
vātena sampakampitā;
Tesaṃ suyyati nigghoso,
tūriye pañcaṅgike yathā.

SC 6SC Verse 523Ms 19Vv_582Msdiv 734Yāpi te sirasmiṃ mālā,
sucigandhā manoramā;
Vāti gandho disā sabbā,
rukkho mañjūsako yathā.

SC 7SC Verse 524Ms 19Vv_583Msdiv 735Ghāyase taṃ sucigandhaṃ,
rūpaṃ passasi amānusaṃ;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 525Ms 19Vv_584Msdiv 736“Sāvatthiyaṃ mayhaṃ sakhī bhadante,
Saṃghassa kāresi mahāvihāraṃ;
Tatthappasannā ahamānumodiṃ,
Disvā agārañca piyañca metaṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 526PTS 2nd ed. 63 Ms 19Vv_585Msdiv 737Tāyeva me suddhanumodanāya,
Laddhaṃ vimānabbhutadassaneyyaṃ;
Samantato soḷasayojanāni,
Vehāyasaṃ gacchati iddhiyā mama.

SC 10SC Verse 527Ms 19Vv_586Msdiv 738Kūṭāgārā nivesā me,
vibhattā bhāgaso mitā;
Daddallamānā ābhanti,
samantā satayojanaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 528Ms 19Vv_587Msdiv 739Pokkharañño ca me ettha,
puthulomanisevitā;
Acchodakā vippasannā,
soṇṇavālukasanthatā.

SC 12SC Verse 529Ms 19Vv_588Msdiv 740Nānāpadumasañchannā,
puṇḍarīkasamotatā;
Surabhī sampavāyanti,
manuññā māluteritā.

SC 13SC Verse 530Ms 19Vv_589Msdiv 741Jambuyo panasā tālā,
nāḷikeravanāni ca;
Antonivesane jātā,
nānārukkhā aropimā.

SC 14SC Verse 531Ms 19Vv_590Msdiv 742Nānātūriyasaṅghuṭṭhaṃ,
accharāgaṇaghositaṃ;
Yopi maṃ supine passe,
sopi vitto siyā naro.

SC 15SC Verse 532Ms 19Vv_591Msdiv 743Etādisaṃ abbhutadassaneyyaṃ,
Vimānaṃ sabbasopabhaṃ;
Mama kammehi nibbattaṃ,
Alaṃ puññāni kātave”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 533Ms 19Vv_592Msdiv 744“Tāyeva te suddhanumodanāya,
Laddhaṃ vimānabbhutadassaneyyaṃ;
Yā ceva sā dānamadāsi nārī,
Tassā gatiṃ brūhi kuhiṃ uppannā sā”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 534Ms 19Vv_593Msdiv 745“Yā sā ahu mayhaṃ sakhī bhadante,
Saṃghassa kāresi mahāvihāraṃ;
Viññātadhammā sā adāsi dānaṃ,
Uppannā nimmānaratīsu devesu.

SC 18SC Verse 535Ms 19Vv_594Msdiv 746Pajāpatī tassa sunimmitassa,
Acintiyā kammavipākā tassā;
Yametaṃ pucchasi kuhiṃ uppannā sāti,
Taṃ te viyākāsiṃ anaññathā ahaṃ.

SC 19SC Verse 536Ms 19Vv_595Msdiv 747Tenahaññepi samādapetha,
Saṃghassa dānāni dadātha vittā;
Dhammañca suṇātha pasannamānasā,
Sudullabho laddho manussalābho.

SC 20SC Verse 537Ms 19Vv_596Msdiv 748Yaṃ maggaṃ maggādhipatī adesayi,
Brahmassaro kañcanasannibhattaco;
Saṃghassa dānāni dadātha vittā,
Mahapphalā yattha bhavanti dakkhiṇā.

SC 21SC Verse 538PTS 2nd ed. 64 Ms 19Vv_597Msdiv 749Ye puggalā aṭṭha sataṃ pasatthā,
Cattāri etāni yugāni honti;
Te dakkhiṇeyyā sugatassa sāvakā,
Etesu dinnāni mahapphalāni.

SC 22SC Verse 539Ms 19Vv_598Msdiv 750Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṃgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito.

SC 23SC Verse 540Ms 19Vv_599Msdiv 751Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
saṃghe dinnaṃ mahapphalaṃ.

SC 24SC Verse 541Ms 19Vv_600Msdiv 752Eso hi saṃgho vipulo mahaggato,
Esappameyyo udadhīva sāgaro;
Etehi seṭṭhā naravīrasāvakā,
Pabhaṅkarā dhammamudīrayanti.

SC 25SC Verse 542Ms 19Vv_601Msdiv 753Tesaṃ sudinnaṃ suhutaṃ suyiṭṭhaṃ,
Ye saṃghamuddissa dadanti dānaṃ;
Sā dakkhiṇā saṃghagatā patiṭṭhitā,
Mahapphalā lokavidūna vaṇṇitā.

SC 26SC Verse 543Ms 19Vv_602Msdiv 754Etādisaṃ yaññamanussarantā,
Ye vedajātā vicaranti loke;
Vineyya maccheramalaṃ samūlaṃ,
Aninditā saggamupenti ṭhānan”ti.

SC 27Ms 19Vv_603Vihāravimānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
Bhāṇavāraṃ dutiyaṃ.
