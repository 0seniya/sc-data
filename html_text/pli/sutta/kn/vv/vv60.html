60. Paṭhamanāgavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 725PTS 2nd ed. 88 Ms 19Vv_804Msdiv 961“Susukkakhandhaṃ abhiruyha nāgaṃ,
Akācinaṃ dantiṃ baliṃ mahājavaṃ;
Abhiruyha gajavaraṃ sukappitaṃ,
Idhāgamā vehāyasaṃ antalikkhe.

SC 2SC Verse 726Ms 19Vv_805Msdiv 962Nāgassa dantesu duvesu nimmitā,
Acchodakā paduminiyo suphullā;
Padumesu ca tūriyagaṇā pavajjare,
Imā ca naccanti manoharāyo.

SC 3SC Verse 727Ms 19Vv_806Msdiv 963Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 728Ms 19Vv_807Msdiv 964So devaputto attamano,
moggallānena pucchito;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 729Ms 19Vv_808Msdiv 965“Aṭṭheva muttapupphāni,
kassapassa mahesino;
Thūpasmiṃ abhiropesiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 6SC Verse 730Ms 19Vv_809Msdiv 966-967Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
… pe …
vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 7Ms 19Vv_810Paṭhamanāgavimānaṃ dasamaṃ.
