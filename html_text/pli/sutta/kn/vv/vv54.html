54. Kakkaṭakarasadāyakavimānavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 683PTS 2nd ed. 84 Ms 19Vv_756SC 1SC Verse 683PTS 2nd ed. 84 Ms 19Vv_756Msdiv 910“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,
Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;
Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,
Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā.

SC 2SC Verse 684Ms 19Vv_757SC 2SC Verse 684Ms 19Vv_757Msdiv 911Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,
Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;
Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,
Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā.

SC 3SC Verse 685Ms 19Vv_758SC 3SC Verse 685Ms 19Vv_758Msdiv 912Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 4SC Verse 686Ms 19Vv_759SC 4SC Verse 686Ms 19Vv_759Msdiv 913Pucchāmi taṃ deva mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 687Ms 19Vv_760SC 5SC Verse 687Ms 19Vv_760Msdiv 914So devaputto attamano,
moggallānena pucchito;
Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,
yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 688Ms 19Vv_761SC 6SC Verse 688Ms 19Vv_761Msdiv 915“Satisamuppādakaro,
dvāre kakkaṭako ṭhito;
Niṭṭhito jātarūpassa,
sobhati dasapādako.

SC 7SC Verse 689Ms 19Vv_762SC 7SC Verse 689Ms 19Vv_762Msdiv 916Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
tena me idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca me bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 8SC Verse 690Ms 19Vv_763SC 8SC Verse 690Ms 19Vv_763Msdiv 917Akkhāmi te bhikkhu mahānubhāva,
Manussabhūto yamakāsi puññaṃ;
Tenamhi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,
Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.
