3.5. Cariyākathā

SC 1Ms 26Ps_1854Msdiv 28Cariyāti, aṭṭha cariyāyo—iriyāpathacariyā, āyatanacariyā, saticariyā, samādhicariyā, ñāṇacariyā, maggacariyā, patticariyā, lokatthacariyāti.

SC 2Ms 26Ps_1855Iriyāpathacariyāti catūsu iriyāpathesu. Āyatanacariyāti chasu ajjhattikabāhiresu āyatanesu. Saticariyāti catūsu satipaṭṭhānesu. Samādhicariyāti catūsu jhānesu. Ñāṇacariyāti catūsu ariyasaccesu. Maggacariyāti catūsu ariyamaggesu. Patticariyāti catūsu sāmaññaphalesu. Lokatthacariyāti tathāgatesu arahantesu sammāsambuddhesu padese paccekabuddhesu padese sāvakesu.

SC 3Ms 26Ps_1856Iriyāpathacariyā ca paṇidhisampannānaṃ. Āyatanacariyā ca indriyesu guttadvārānaṃ. Saticariyā ca appamādavihārīnaṃ. Samādhicariyā ca adhicittamanuyuttānaṃ. Ñāṇacariyā ca buddhisampannānaṃ. Maggacariyā ca sammāpaṭipannānaṃ. Patticariyā ca adhigataphalānaṃ. Lokatthacariyā ca tathāgatānaṃ arahantānaṃ sammāsambuddhānaṃ padese paccekabuddhānaṃ padese sāvakānaṃ. Imā aṭṭha cariyāyo.

SC 4Ms 26Ps_1857Msdiv 29Aparāpi aṭṭha cariyāyo. Adhimuccanto saddhāya carati, paggaṇhanto vīriyena carati, upaṭṭhāpento satiyā carati, avikkhepaṃ karonto samādhinā carati, pajānanto paññāya carati, vijānanto viññāṇacariyāya carati, evaṃ paṭipannassa kusalā dhammā āyāpentīti āyatanacariyāya carati, evaṃ paṭipanno visesamadhigacchatīti visesacariyāya carati. Imā aṭṭha cariyāyo.

SC 5Ms 26Ps_1858Aparāpi aṭṭha cariyāyo. Dassanacariyā ca sammādiṭṭhiyā, abhiniropanacariyā ca sammāsaṅkappassa, pariggahacariyā ca sammāvācāya, samuṭṭhānacariyā ca sammākammantassa, vodānacariyā ca sammāājīvassa, paggahacariyā ca sammāvāyāmassa, upaṭṭhānacariyā ca sammāsatiyā, avikkhepacariyā ca sammāsamādhissa. Imā aṭṭha cariyāyoti.

Ms 26Ps_1859Cariyākathā niṭṭhitā.
