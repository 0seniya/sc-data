Vinicchayamahāmattavatthu

Chabbaggiyavatthu

yāvatā bahu bhāsati;
Khemī averī abhayo,
“ paṇḍito”ti pavuccati.

Ekudānakhīṇāsavavatthu

SC 259Vatthugāthā 258.1Ms 18Dh_280SC 259Vatthugāthā 258.1Ms 18Dh_280Msdiv 259Na tāvatā dhammadharo,
yāvatā bahu bhāsati;
Yo ca appampi sutvāna,
dhammaṃ kāyena passati;
Sa ve dhammadharo hoti,
yo dhammaṃ nappamajjati.

Lakuṇḍakabhaddiyattheravatthu

yenassa palitaṃ siro;
Paripakko vayo tassa,
“ moghajiṇṇo”ti vuccati.

ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo;

“ thero” iti pavuccati.

Sambahulabhikkhuvatthu

vaṇṇapokkharatāya vā;
Sādhurūpo naro hoti,
issukī maccharī saṭho.

mūlaghaccaṃ samūhataṃ;
Sa vantadoso medhāvī,
“ sādhurūpo”ti vuccati.

Hatthakavatthu

abbato alikaṃ bhaṇaṃ;
Icchālobhasamāpanno,
samaṇo kiṃ bhavissati.

SC 265Vatthugāthā 261.2PTS 1st ed. 39 Ms 18Dh_286SC 265Vatthugāthā 261.2PTS 1st ed. 39 Ms 18Dh_286Msdiv 265Yo ca sameti pāpāni,
aṇuṃthūlāni sabbaso;
Samitattā hi pāpānaṃ,
“ samaṇo”ti pavuccati.

Aññatarabrāhmaṇavatthu

yāvatā bhikkhate pare;
Vissaṃ dhammaṃ samādāya,
bhikkhu hoti na tāvatā.

bāhetvā brahmacariyavā;
Saṅkhāya loke carati,
sa ve “bhikkhū”ti vuccati.

Titthiyavatthu

mūḷharūpo aviddasu;
Yo ca tulaṃva paggayha,
varamādāya paṇḍito.

Bālisikavatthu

“ ariyo”ti pavuccati.

Sambahulasīlādisampannabhikkhuvatthu

vivittasayanena vā.

Dhammaṭṭhavaggo ekūnavīsatimo.
