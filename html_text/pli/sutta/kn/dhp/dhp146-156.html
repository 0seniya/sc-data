Jarāvagga

Visākhāyasahāyikānaṃvatthu

SC 146Vatthugāthā 146.1PTS 1st ed. 22 PTS 2nd ed. 42 Ms 18Dh_157Msdiv 146Ko nu hāso kimānando,
niccaṃ pajjalite sati;
Andhakārena onaddhā,
padīpaṃ na gavesatha.

Sirimāvatthu

SC 147Vatthugāthā 147.1Ms 18Dh_158Msdiv 147Passa cittakataṃ bimbaṃ,
arukāyaṃ samussitaṃ;
Āturaṃ bahusaṅkappaṃ,
yassa natthi dhuvaṃ ṭhiti.

Uttarātherīvatthu

SC 148Vatthugāthā 148.1Ms 18Dh_159Msdiv 148Parijiṇṇamidaṃ rūpaṃ,
roganīḷaṃ pabhaṅguraṃ;
Bhijjati pūtisandeho,
maraṇantañhi jīvitaṃ.

Sambahulaadhimānikabhikkhuvatthu

SC 149Vatthugāthā 149.1Ms 18Dh_160Msdiv 149Yānimāni apatthāni,
alābūneva sārade;
Kāpotakāni aṭṭhīni,
tāni disvāna kā rati.

Janapadakalyāṇīrūpanandātherīvatthu

SC 150Vatthugāthā 150.1PTS 2nd ed. 43 Ms 18Dh_161Msdiv 150Aṭṭhīnaṃ nagaraṃ kataṃ,
maṃsalohitalepanaṃ;
Yattha jarā ca maccu ca,
māno makkho ca ohito.

Mallikādevīvatthu

SC 151Vatthugāthā 151.1Ms 18Dh_162Msdiv 151Jīranti ve rājarathā sucittā,
Atho sarīrampi jaraṃ upeti;
Satañca dhammo na jaraṃ upeti,
Santo have sabbhi pavedayanti.

Lāḷudāyītheravatthu

SC 152Vatthugāthā 152.1Ms 18Dh_163Msdiv 152Appassutāyaṃ puriso,
balībaddhova jīrati;
Maṃsāni tassa vaḍḍhanti,
paññā tassa na vaḍḍhati.

Udānavatthu

SC 153Vatthugāthā 153.1PTS 1st ed. 23 Ms 18Dh_164Msdiv 153Anekajātisaṃsāraṃ,
sandhāvissaṃ anibbisaṃ;
Gahakāraṃ gavesanto,
dukkhā jāti punappunaṃ.

SC 154Vatthugāthā 153.2PTS 2nd ed. 44 Ms 18Dh_165Msdiv 154Gahakāraka diṭṭhosi,
puna gehaṃ na kāhasi;
Sabbā te phāsukā bhaggā,
gahakūṭaṃ visaṅkhataṃ;
Visaṅkhāragataṃ cittaṃ,
taṇhānaṃ khayamajjhagā.

Mahādhanaseṭṭhiputtavatthu

SC 155Vatthugāthā 154.1Ms 18Dh_166Msdiv 155Acaritvā brahmacariyaṃ,
aladdhā yobbane dhanaṃ;
Jiṇṇakoñcāva jhāyanti,
khīṇamaccheva pallale.

SC 156Vatthugāthā 154.2Ms 18Dh_167Msdiv 156Acaritvā brahmacariyaṃ,
aladdhā yobbane dhanaṃ;
Senti cāpātikhīṇāva,
purāṇāni anutthunaṃ.

Jarāvaggo ekādasamo.
