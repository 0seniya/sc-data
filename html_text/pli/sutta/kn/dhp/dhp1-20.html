Yamakavagga

Cakkhupālattheravatthu

SC 1Vatthugāthā 1.1PTS 1st ed. 1 PTS 2nd ed. 1 Ms 18Dh_2Msdiv 1Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,
manoseṭṭhā manomayā;
Manasā ce paduṭṭhena,
bhāsati vā karoti vā;
Tato naṃ dukkhamanveti,
cakkaṃva vahato padaṃ.

Maṭṭhakuṇḍalīvatthu

SC 2Vatthugāthā 2.1Ms 18Dh_3Msdiv 2Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,
manoseṭṭhā manomayā;
Manasā ce pasannena,
bhāsati vā karoti vā;
Tato naṃ sukhamanveti,
chāyāva anapāyinī.

Tissattheravatthu

SC 3Vatthugāthā 3.1Ms 18Dh_4Msdiv 3Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ upanayhanti,
veraṃ tesaṃ na sammati.

SC 4Vatthugāthā 3.2PTS 2nd ed. 2 Ms 18Dh_5Msdiv 4Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ nupanayhanti,
veraṃ tesūpasammati.

Kāḷayakkhinīvatthu

SC 5Vatthugāthā 4.1PTS 1st ed. 2 Ms 18Dh_6Msdiv 5Na hi verena verāni,
sammantīdha kudācanaṃ;
Averena ca sammanti,
esa dhammo sanantano.

Kosambakavatthu

SC 6Vatthugāthā 5.1Ms 18Dh_7Msdiv 6Pare ca na vijānanti,
mayamettha yamāmase;
Ye ca tattha vijānanti,
tato sammanti medhagā.

Mahākāḷattheravatthu

SC 7Vatthugāthā 6.1Ms 18Dh_8Msdiv 7Subhānupassiṃ viharantaṃ,
indriyesu asaṃvutaṃ;
Bhojanamhi cāmattaññuṃ,
kusītaṃ hīnavīriyaṃ;
Taṃ ve pasahati māro,
vāto rukkhaṃva dubbalaṃ.

SC 8Vatthugāthā 6.2PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 18Dh_9Msdiv 8Asubhānupassiṃ viharantaṃ,
Indriyesu susaṃvutaṃ;
Bhojanamhi ca mattaññuṃ,
Saddhaṃ āraddhavīriyaṃ;
Taṃ ve nappasahati māro,
Vāto selaṃva pabbataṃ.

Devadattavatthu

SC 9Vatthugāthā 7.1Ms 18Dh_10Msdiv 9Anikkasāvo kāsāvaṃ,
yo vatthaṃ paridahissati;
Apeto damasaccena,
na so kāsāvamarahati.

SC 10Vatthugāthā 7.2Ms 18Dh_11Msdiv 10Yo ca vantakasāvassa,
sīlesu susamāhito;
Upeto damasaccena,
sa ve kāsāvamarahati.

Sāriputtattheravatthu

SC 11Vatthugāthā 8.1Ms 18Dh_12Msdiv 11Asāre sāramatino,
sāre cāsāradassino;
Te sāraṃ nādhigacchanti,
micchāsaṅkappagocarā.

SC 12Vatthugāthā 8.2Ms 18Dh_13Msdiv 12Sārañca sārato ñatvā,
asārañca asārato;
Te sāraṃ adhigacchanti,
sammāsaṅkappagocarā.

Nandattheravatthu

SC 13Vatthugāthā 9.1PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 18Dh_14Msdiv 13Yathā agāraṃ ducchannaṃ,
vuṭṭhī samativijjhati;
Evaṃ abhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,
rāgo samativijjhati.

SC 14Vatthugāthā 9.2PTS 1st ed. 3 Ms 18Dh_15Msdiv 14Yathā agāraṃ suchannaṃ,
vuṭṭhī na samativijjhati;
Evaṃ subhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,
rāgo na samativijjhati.

Cundasūkarikavatthu

SC 15Vatthugāthā 10.1Ms 18Dh_16Msdiv 15Idha socati pecca socati,
Pāpakārī ubhayattha socati;
So socati so vihaññati,
Disvā kammakiliṭṭhamattano.

Dhammikaupāsakavatthu

SC 16Vatthugāthā 11.1Ms 18Dh_17Msdiv 16Idha modati pecca modati,
Katapuñño ubhayattha modati;
So modati so pamodati,
Disvā kammavisuddhimattano.

Devadattavatthu

SC 17Vatthugāthā 12.1PTS 2nd ed. 5 Ms 18Dh_18Msdiv 17Idha tappati pecca tappati,
Pāpakārī ubhayattha tappati;
“ Pāpaṃ me katan”ti tappati,
Bhiyyo tappati duggatiṃ gato.

Sumanadevīvatthu

SC 18Vatthugāthā 13.1Ms 18Dh_19Msdiv 18Idha nandati pecca nandati,
Katapuñño ubhayattha nandati;
“ Puññaṃ me katan”ti nandati,
Bhiyyo nandati suggatiṃ gato.

Dvesahāyakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 19Vatthugāthā 14.1Ms 18Dh_20Msdiv 19Bahumpi ce saṃhita bhāsamāno,
Na takkaro hoti naro pamatto;
Gopova gāvo gaṇayaṃ paresaṃ,
Na bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti.

SC 20Vatthugāthā 14.2PTS 2nd ed. 6 Ms 18Dh_21Msdiv 20Appampi ce saṃhita bhāsamāno,
Dhammassa hoti anudhammacārī;
Rāgañca dosañca pahāya mohaṃ,
Sammappajāno suvimuttacitto;
Anupādiyāno idha vā huraṃ vā,
Sa bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti.

Yamakavaggo paṭhamo.
