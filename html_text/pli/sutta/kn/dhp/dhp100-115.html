Sahassavagga

Tambadāṭhikacoraghātakavatthu

SC 100Vatthugāthā 100.1PTS 2nd ed. 29 Ms 18Dh_108Msdiv 100Sahassamapi ce vācā,
anatthapadasaṃhitā;
Ekaṃ atthapadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.

Bāhiyadārucīriyattheravatthu

SC 101Vatthugāthā 101.1Ms 18Dh_109Msdiv 101Sahassamapi ce gāthā,
anatthapadasaṃhitā;
Ekaṃ gāthāpadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.

Kuṇḍalakesitherīvatthu

SC 102Vatthugāthā 102.1Ms 18Dh_110Msdiv 102Yo ca gāthā sataṃ bhāse,
anatthapadasaṃhitā;
Ekaṃ dhammapadaṃ seyyo,
yaṃ sutvā upasammati.

SC 103Vatthugāthā 102.2Ms 18Dh_111Msdiv 103Yo sahassaṃ sahassena,
saṅgāme mānuse jine;
Ekañca jeyyamattānaṃ,
sa ve saṅgāmajuttamo.

Anatthapucchakabrāhmaṇavatthu

SC 104Vatthugāthā 103.1PTS 2nd ed. 30 Ms 18Dh_112Msdiv 104Attā have jitaṃ seyyo,
yā cāyaṃ itarā pajā;
Attadantassa posassa,
niccaṃ saññatacārino.

SC 105Vatthugāthā 103.2Ms 18Dh_113Msdiv 105Neva devo na gandhabbo,
na māro saha brahmunā;
Jitaṃ apajitaṃ kayirā,
tathārūpassa jantuno.

Sāriputtattherassa mātulabrāhmaṇavatthu

SC 106Vatthugāthā 104.1PTS 1st ed. 16 Ms 18Dh_114Msdiv 106Māse māse sahassena,
yo yajetha sataṃ samaṃ;
Ekañca bhāvitattānaṃ,
muhuttamapi pūjaye;
Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,
yañce vassasataṃ hutaṃ.

Sāriputtattherassa bhāgineyyavatthu

SC 107Vatthugāthā 105.1Ms 18Dh_115Msdiv 107Yo ca vassasataṃ jantu,
aggiṃ paricare vane;
Ekañca bhāvitattānaṃ,
muhuttamapi pūjaye;
Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,
yañce vassasataṃ hutaṃ.

Sāriputtatherassa sahāyakabrāhmaṇavatthu

SC 108Vatthugāthā 106.1PTS 2nd ed. 31 Ms 18Dh_116Msdiv 108Yaṃ kiñci yiṭṭhaṃ va hutaṃ va loke,
Saṃvaccharaṃ yajetha puññapekkho;
Sabbampi taṃ na catubhāgameti,
Abhivādanā ujjugatesu seyyo.

Āyuvaḍḍhanakumāravatthu

SC 109Vatthugāthā 107.1Ms 18Dh_117Msdiv 109Abhivādanasīlissa,
niccaṃ vuḍḍhāpacāyino;
Cattāro dhammā vaḍḍhanti,
āyu vaṇṇo sukhaṃ balaṃ.

Saṅkiccasāmaṇeravatthu

SC 110Vatthugāthā 108.1Ms 18Dh_118Msdiv 110Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
dussīlo asamāhito;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
sīlavantassa jhāyino.

Khāṇukoṇḍaññattheravatthu

SC 111Vatthugāthā 109.1Ms 18Dh_119Msdiv 111Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
duppañño asamāhito;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
paññavantassa jhāyino.

Sabbadāsattheravatthu

SC 112Vatthugāthā 110.1PTS 2nd ed. 32 Ms 18Dh_120Msdiv 112Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
kusīto hīnavīriyo;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
vīriyamārabhato daḷhaṃ.

Paṭācārātherīvatthu

SC 113Vatthugāthā 111.1Ms 18Dh_121Msdiv 113Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
apassaṃ udayabbayaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
passato udayabbayaṃ.

Kisāgotamīvatthu

SC 114Vatthugāthā 112.1PTS 1st ed. 17 Ms 18Dh_122Msdiv 114Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
apassaṃ amataṃ padaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
passato amataṃ padaṃ.

Bahuputtikattherīvatthu

SC 115Vatthugāthā 113.1Ms 18Dh_123Msdiv 115Yo ca vassasataṃ jīve,
apassaṃ dhammamuttamaṃ;
Ekāhaṃ jīvitaṃ seyyo,
passato dhammamuttamaṃ.

Sahassavaggo aṭṭhamo.
