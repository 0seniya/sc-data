Maggavagga

Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

SC 273Vatthugāthā 273.1PTS 1st ed. 40 PTS 2nd ed. 77 Ms 18Dh_295Msdiv 273Maggānaṭṭhaṅgiko seṭṭho,
saccānaṃ caturo padā;
Virāgo seṭṭho dhammānaṃ,
dvipadānañca cakkhumā.

SC 274Vatthugāthā 273.2Ms 18Dh_296Msdiv 274Eseva maggo natthañño,
Dassanassa visuddhiyā;
Etañhi tumhe paṭipajjatha,
Mārassetaṃ pamohanaṃ.

SC 275Vatthugāthā 273.3Ms 18Dh_297Msdiv 275Etañhi tumhe paṭipannā,
dukkhassantaṃ karissatha;
Akkhāto vo mayā maggo,
aññāya sallakantanaṃ.

SC 276Vatthugāthā 273.4Ms 18Dh_298Msdiv 276Tumhehi kiccamātappaṃ,
akkhātāro tathāgatā;
Paṭipannā pamokkhanti,
jhāyino mārabandhanā.

Aniccalakkhaṇavatthu

SC 277Vatthugāthā 274.1PTS 2nd ed. 78 Ms 18Dh_299Msdiv 277“Sabbe saṅkhārā aniccā”ti,
yadā paññāya passati;
Atha nibbindati dukkhe,
esa maggo visuddhiyā.

Dukkhalakkhaṇavatthu

SC 278Vatthugāthā 275.1Ms 18Dh_300Msdiv 278“Sabbe saṅkhārā dukkhā”ti,
yadā paññāya passati;
Atha nibbindati dukkhe,
esa maggo visuddhiyā.

Anattalakkhaṇavatthu

SC 279Vatthugāthā 276.1Ms 18Dh_301Msdiv 279“Sabbe dhammā anattā”ti,
yadā paññāya passati;
Atha nibbindati dukkhe,
esa maggo visuddhiyā.

Padhānakammikatissattheravatthu

SC 280Vatthugāthā 277.1Ms 18Dh_302Msdiv 280Uṭṭhānakālamhi anuṭṭhahāno,
Yuvā balī ālasiyaṃ upeto;
Saṃsannasaṅkappamano kusīto,
Paññāya maggaṃ alaso na vindati.

Sūkarapetavatthu

SC 281Vatthugāthā 278.1PTS 1st ed. 41 PTS 2nd ed. 79 Ms 18Dh_303Msdiv 281Vācānurakkhī manasā susaṃvuto,
Kāyena ca nākusalaṃ kayirā;
Ete tayo kammapathe visodhaye,
Ārādhaye maggamisippaveditaṃ.

Poṭṭhilattheravatthu

SC 282Vatthugāthā 279.1Ms 18Dh_304Msdiv 282Yogā ve jāyatī bhūri,
ayogā bhūrisaṅkhayo;
Etaṃ dvedhāpathaṃ ñatvā,
bhavāya vibhavāya ca;
Tathāttānaṃ niveseyya,
yathā bhūri pavaḍḍhati.

Pañcamahallakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 283Vatthugāthā 280.1Ms 18Dh_305Msdiv 283Vanaṃ chindatha mā rukkhaṃ,
vanato jāyate bhayaṃ;
Chetvā vanañca vanathañca,
nibbanā hotha bhikkhavo.

SC 284Vatthugāthā 280.2PTS 2nd ed. 80 Ms 18Dh_306Msdiv 284Yāva hi vanatho na chijjati,
Aṇumattopi narassa nārisu;
Paṭibaddhamanova tāva so,
Vaccho khīrapakova mātari.

Suvaṇṇakārattheravatthu

SC 285Vatthugāthā 281.1Ms 18Dh_307Msdiv 285Ucchinda sinehamattano,
Kumudaṃ sāradikaṃva pāṇinā;
Santimaggameva brūhaya,
Nibbānaṃ sugatena desitaṃ.

Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

SC 286Vatthugāthā 282.1Ms 18Dh_308Msdiv 286Idha vassaṃ vasissāmi,
idha hemantagimhisu;
Iti bālo vicinteti,
antarāyaṃ na bujjhati.

Kisāgotamīvatthu

SC 287Vatthugāthā 283.1PTS 2nd ed. 81 Ms 18Dh_309Msdiv 287Taṃ puttapasusammattaṃ,
byāsattamanasaṃ naraṃ;
Suttaṃ gāmaṃ mahoghova,
maccu ādāya gacchati.

Paṭācārāvatthu

SC 288Vatthugāthā 284.1PTS 1st ed. 42 Ms 18Dh_310Msdiv 288Na santi puttā tāṇāya,
na pitā nāpi bandhavā;
Antakenādhipannassa,
natthi ñātīsu tāṇatā.

SC 289Vatthugāthā 284.2Ms 18Dh_311Msdiv 289Etamatthavasaṃ ñatvā,
paṇḍito sīlasaṃvuto;
Nibbānagamanaṃ maggaṃ,
khippameva visodhaye.

Maggavaggo vīsatimo.
