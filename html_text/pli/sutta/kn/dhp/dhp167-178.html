Lokavagga

Daharabhikkhuvatthu

SC 167Vatthugāthā 167.1PTS 2nd ed. 48 Ms 18Dh_180Msdiv 167Hīnaṃ dhammaṃ na seveyya,
pamādena na saṃvase;
Micchādiṭṭhiṃ na seveyya,
na siyā lokavaḍḍhano.

Suddhodanavatthu

SC 168Vatthugāthā 168.1Ms 18Dh_181Msdiv 168Uttiṭṭhe nappamajjeyya,
dhammaṃ sucaritaṃ care;
Dhammacārī sukhaṃ seti,
asmiṃ loke paramhi ca.

SC 169Vatthugāthā 168.2Ms 18Dh_182Msdiv 169Dhammaṃ care sucaritaṃ,
na naṃ duccaritaṃ care;
Dhammacārī sukhaṃ seti,
asmiṃ loke paramhi ca.

Pañcasatavipassakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 170Vatthugāthā 169.1Ms 18Dh_183Msdiv 170Yathā pubbuḷakaṃ passe,
yathā passe marīcikaṃ;
Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
maccurājā na passati.

Abhayarājakumāravatthu

SC 171Vatthugāthā 170.1PTS 2nd ed. 49 Ms 18Dh_184Msdiv 171Etha passathimaṃ lokaṃ,
cittaṃ rājarathūpamaṃ;
Yattha bālā visīdanti,
natthi saṅgo vijānataṃ.

Sammajjanattheravatthu

SC 172Vatthugāthā 171.1PTS 1st ed. 26 Ms 18Dh_185Msdiv 172Yo ca pubbe pamajjitvā,
pacchā so nappamajjati;
Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,
abbhā muttova candimā.

Aṅgulimālattheravatthu

SC 173Vatthugāthā 172.1Ms 18Dh_186Msdiv 173Yassa pāpaṃ kataṃ kammaṃ,
kusalena pidhīyati;
Somaṃ lokaṃ pabhāseti,
abbhā muttova candimā.

Pesakāradhītāvatthu

SC 174Vatthugāthā 173.1Ms 18Dh_187Msdiv 174Andhabhūto ayaṃ loko,
tanukettha vipassati;
Sakuṇo jālamuttova,
appo saggāya gacchati.

Tiṃsabhikkhuvatthu

SC 175Vatthugāthā 174.1Ms 18Dh_188Msdiv 175Haṃsādiccapathe yanti,
ākāse yanti iddhiyā;
Nīyanti dhīrā lokamhā,
jetvā māraṃ savāhiniṃ.

Ciñcamāṇavikāvatthu

SC 176Vatthugāthā 175.1PTS 2nd ed. 50 Ms 18Dh_189Msdiv 176Ekaṃ dhammaṃ atītassa,
musāvādissa jantuno;
Vitiṇṇaparalokassa,
natthi pāpaṃ akāriyaṃ.

Asadisadānavatthu

SC 177Vatthugāthā 176.1Ms 18Dh_190Msdiv 177Na ve kadariyā devalokaṃ vajanti,
Bālā have nappasaṃsanti dānaṃ;
Dhīro ca dānaṃ anumodamāno,
Teneva so hoti sukhī parattha.

Anāthapiṇḍikaputtakālavatthu

SC 178Vatthugāthā 177.1Ms 18Dh_191Msdiv 178Pathabyā ekarajjena,
saggassa gamanena vā;
Sabbalokādhipaccena,
sotāpattiphalaṃ varaṃ.

Lokavaggo terasamo.
