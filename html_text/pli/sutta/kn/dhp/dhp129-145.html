Daṇḍavagga

Chabbaggiyabhikkhuvatthu

SC 129Vatthugāthā 129.1PTS 2nd ed. 37 Ms 18Dh_139Msdiv 129Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,
sabbe bhāyanti maccuno;
Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,
na haneyya na ghātaye.

Chabbaggiyavatthu

SC 130Vatthugāthā 130.1Ms 18Dh_140Msdiv 130Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,
sabbesaṃ jīvitaṃ piyaṃ;
Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,
na haneyya na ghātaye.

Sambahulakumārakavatthu

SC 131Vatthugāthā 131.1Ms 18Dh_141Msdiv 131Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,
yo daṇḍena vihiṃsati;
Attano sukhamesāno,
pecca so na labhate sukhaṃ.

SC 132Vatthugāthā 131.2PTS 1st ed. 20 Ms 18Dh_142Msdiv 132Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,
yo daṇḍena na hiṃsati;
Attano sukhamesāno,
pecca so labhate sukhaṃ.

Koṇḍadhānattheravatthu

SC 133Vatthugāthā 132.1PTS 2nd ed. 38 Ms 18Dh_143Msdiv 133Māvoca pharusaṃ kañci,
vuttā paṭivadeyyu taṃ;
Dukkhā hi sārambhakathā,
paṭidaṇḍā phuseyyu taṃ.

SC 134Vatthugāthā 132.2Ms 18Dh_144Msdiv 134Sace neresi attānaṃ,
kaṃso upahato yathā;
Esa pattosi nibbānaṃ,
sārambho te na vijjati.

Uposathikaitthīnaṃvatthu

SC 135Vatthugāthā 133.1Ms 18Dh_145Msdiv 135Yathā daṇḍena gopālo,
gāvo pājeti gocaraṃ;
Evaṃ jarā ca maccu ca,
āyuṃ pājenti pāṇinaṃ.

Ajagarapetavatthu

SC 136Vatthugāthā 134.1Ms 18Dh_146Msdiv 136Atha pāpāni kammāni,
karaṃ bālo na bujjhati;
Sehi kammehi dummedho,
aggidaḍḍhova tappati.

Mahāmoggallānattheravatthu

SC 137Vatthugāthā 135.1Ms 18Dh_147Msdiv 137Yo daṇḍena adaṇḍesu,
appaduṭṭhesu dussati;
Dasannamaññataraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
khippameva nigacchati.

SC 138Vatthugāthā 135.2PTS 2nd ed. 39 Ms 18Dh_148Msdiv 138Vedanaṃ pharusaṃ jāniṃ,
sarīrassa va bhedanaṃ;
Garukaṃ vāpi ābādhaṃ,
cittakkhepaṃ va pāpuṇe.

SC 139Vatthugāthā 135.3Ms 18Dh_149Msdiv 139Rājato vā upasaggaṃ,
Abbhakkhānaṃ va dāruṇaṃ;
Parikkhayaṃ va ñātīnaṃ,
Bhogānaṃ va Bhogānaṃ va pabhaṅguraṃ.

SC 140Vatthugāthā 135.4Ms 18Dh_150Msdiv 140Atha vāssa agārāni,
aggi ḍahati pāvako;
Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,
nirayaṃ sopapajjati.

Bahubhaṇḍikabhikkhuvatthu

SC 141Vatthugāthā 136.1PTS 1st ed. 21 PTS 2nd ed. 40 Ms 18Dh_151Msdiv 141Na naggacariyā na jaṭā na paṅkā,
Nānāsakā thaṇḍilasāyikā vā;
Rajojallaṃ ukkuṭikappadhānaṃ,
Sodhenti maccaṃ avitiṇṇakaṅkhaṃ.

Santatimahāmattavatthu

SC 142Vatthugāthā 137.1Ms 18Dh_152Msdiv 142Alaṅkato cepi samaṃ careyya,
Santo danto niyato brahmacārī;
Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṃ,
So brāhmaṇo so samaṇo sa bhikkhu.

Pilotikatissattheravatthu

SC 143Vatthugāthā 138.1Ms 18Dh_153Msdiv 143Hirīnisedho puriso,
koci lokasmi vijjati;
Yo niddaṃ niddaṃ apabodheti,
asso bhadro kasāmiva.

SC 144Vatthugāthā 138.2PTS 2nd ed. 41 Ms 18Dh_154Msdiv 144Asso yathā bhadro kasāniviṭṭho,
Ātāpino saṃvegino bhavātha;
Saddhāya sīlena ca vīriyena ca,
Samādhinā dhammavinicchayena ca;
Sampannavijjācaraṇā patissatā,
Jahissatha dukkhamidaṃ anappakaṃ.

Sukhasāmaṇeravatthu

SC 145Vatthugāthā 139.1Ms 18Dh_155Msdiv 145Udakañhi nayanti nettikā,
Usukārā namayanti tejanaṃ;
Dāruṃ namayanti tacchakā,
Attānaṃ damayanti subbatā.

Daṇḍavaggo dasamo.
