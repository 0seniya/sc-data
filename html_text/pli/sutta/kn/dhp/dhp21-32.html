Appamādavagga

Sāmāvatīvatthu

SC 21Vatthugāthā 21.1PTS 1st ed. 4 PTS 2nd ed. 7 Ms 18Dh_23Msdiv 21Appamādo amatapadaṃ,
pamādo maccuno padaṃ;
Appamattā na mīyanti,
ye pamattā yathā matā.

SC 22Vatthugāthā 21.2Ms 18Dh_24Msdiv 22Evaṃ visesato ñatvā,
appamādamhi paṇḍitā;
Appamāde pamodanti,
ariyānaṃ gocare ratā.

SC 23Vatthugāthā 21.3Ms 18Dh_25Msdiv 23Te jhāyino sātatikā,
niccaṃ daḷhaparakkamā;
Phusanti dhīrā nibbānaṃ,
yogakkhemaṃ anuttaraṃ.

Kumbaghosakaseṭṭhivatthu

SC 24Vatthugāthā 22.1Ms 18Dh_26Msdiv 24Uṭṭhānavato satīmato,
Sucikammassa nisammakārino;
Saññatassa dhammajīvino,
Appamattassa yasobhivaḍḍhati.

Cūḷapanthakavatthu

SC 25Vatthugāthā 23.1PTS 2nd ed. 8 Ms 18Dh_27Msdiv 25Uṭṭhānenappamādena,
saṃyamena damena ca;
Dīpaṃ kayirātha medhāvī,
yaṃ ogho nābhikīrati.

Bālanakkhattasaṅghuṭṭhavatthu

SC 26Vatthugāthā 24.1Ms 18Dh_28Msdiv 26Pamādamanuyuñjanti,
bālā dummedhino janā;
Appamādañca medhāvī,
dhanaṃ seṭṭhaṃva rakkhati.

SC 27Vatthugāthā 24.2Ms 18Dh_29Msdiv 27Mā pamādamanuyuñjetha,
mā kāmaratisanthavaṃ;
Appamatto hi jhāyanto,
pappoti vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

Mahākassapattheravatthu

SC 28Vatthugāthā 25.1Ms 18Dh_30Msdiv 28Pamādaṃ appamādena,
yadā nudati paṇḍito;
Paññāpāsādamāruyha,
asoko sokiniṃ pajaṃ;
Pabbataṭṭhova bhūmaṭṭhe,
dhīro bāle avekkhati.

Pamattāpamattadvesahāyakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 29Vatthugāthā 26.1PTS 1st ed. 5 PTS 2nd ed. 9 Ms 18Dh_31Msdiv 29Appamatto pamattesu,
suttesu bahujāgaro;
Abalassaṃva sīghasso,
hitvā yāti sumedhaso.

Maghavatthu

SC 30Vatthugāthā 27.1Ms 18Dh_32Msdiv 30Appamādena maghavā,
devānaṃ seṭṭhataṃ gato;
Appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti,
pamādo garahito sadā.

Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu

SC 31Vatthugāthā 28.1Ms 18Dh_33Msdiv 31Appamādarato bhikkhu,
pamāde bhayadassi vā;
Saṃyojanaṃ aṇuṃ thūlaṃ,
ḍahaṃ aggīva gacchati.

Nigamavāsitissattheravatthu

SC 32Vatthugāthā 29.1Ms 18Dh_34Msdiv 32Appamādarato bhikkhu,
pamāde bhayadassi vā;
Abhabbo parihānāya,
nibbānasseva santike.

Appamādavaggo dutiyo.
