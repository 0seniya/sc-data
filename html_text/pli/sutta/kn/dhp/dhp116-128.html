Pāpavagga

Cūḷekasāṭakabrāhmaṇavatthu

SC 116Vatthugāthā 116.1PTS 2nd ed. 33 Ms 18Dh_125Msdiv 116Abhittharetha kalyāṇe,
pāpā cittaṃ nivāraye;
Dandhañhi karoto puññaṃ,
pāpasmiṃ ramatī mano.

Seyyasakattheravatthu

SC 117Vatthugāthā 117.1Ms 18Dh_126Msdiv 117Pāpañce puriso kayirā,
Na naṃ kayirā punappunaṃ;
Na tamhi chandaṃ kayirātha,
Dukkho pāpassa uccayo.

Lājadevadhītāvatthu

SC 118Vatthugāthā 118.1Ms 18Dh_127Msdiv 118Puññañce puriso kayirā,
kayirā naṃ punappunaṃ;
Tamhi chandaṃ kayirātha,
sukho puññassa uccayo.

Anāthapiṇḍikaseṭṭhivatthu

SC 119Vatthugāthā 119.1Ms 18Dh_128Msdiv 119Pāpopi passati bhadraṃ,
Yāva pāpaṃ na paccati;
Yadā ca paccati pāpaṃ,
Atha pāpo pāpāni passati.

SC 120Vatthugāthā 119.2PTS 1st ed. 18 PTS 2nd ed. 34 Ms 18Dh_129Msdiv 120Bhadropi passati pāpaṃ,
Yāva bhadraṃ na paccati;
Yadā ca paccati bhadraṃ,
Atha bhadro bhadrāni passati.

Asaññataparikkhāravatthu

SC 121Vatthugāthā 120.1Ms 18Dh_130Msdiv 121Māvamaññetha pāpassa,
na mantaṃ āgamissati;
Udabindunipātena,
udakumbhopi pūrati;
Bālo pūrati pāpassa,
thokaṃ thokampi ācinaṃ.

Biḷālapādakaseṭṭhivatthu

SC 122Vatthugāthā 121.1Ms 18Dh_131Msdiv 122Māvamaññetha puññassa,
na mandaṃ āgamissati;
Udabindunipātena,
udakumbhopi pūrati;
Dhīro pūrati puññassa,
thokaṃ thokampi ācinaṃ.

Mahādhanavāṇijavatthu

SC 123Vatthugāthā 122.1PTS 2nd ed. 35 Ms 18Dh_132Msdiv 123Vāṇijova bhayaṃ maggaṃ,
appasattho mahaddhano;
Visaṃ jīvitukāmova,
pāpāni parivajjaye.

Kukkuṭamittanesādavatthu

SC 124Vatthugāthā 123.1Ms 18Dh_133Msdiv 124Pāṇimhi ce vaṇo nāssa,
hareyya pāṇinā visaṃ;
Nābbaṇaṃ visamanveti,
natthi pāpaṃ akubbato.

Kokasunakhaluddakavatthu

SC 125Vatthugāthā 124.1Ms 18Dh_134Msdiv 125Yo appaduṭṭhassa narassa dussati,
Suddhassa posassa anaṅgaṇassa;
Tameva bālaṃ pacceti pāpaṃ,
Sukhumo rajo paṭivātaṃva khitto.

Maṇikārakulūpakatissattheravatthu

SC 126Vatthugāthā 125.1Ms 18Dh_135Msdiv 126Gabbhameke uppajjanti,
nirayaṃ pāpakammino;
Saggaṃ sugatino yanti,
parinibbanti anāsavā.

Tayojanavatthu

SC 127Vatthugāthā 126.1PTS 1st ed. 19 PTS 2nd ed. 36 Ms 18Dh_136Msdiv 127Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,
Na pabbatānaṃ vivaraṃ pavissa;
Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,
Yatthaṭṭhito mucceyya pāpakammā.

Suppabuddhasakyavatthu

SC 128Vatthugāthā 127.1Ms 18Dh_137Msdiv 128Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,
Na pabbatānaṃ vivaraṃ pavissa;
Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,
Yatthaṭṭhitaṃ nappasaheyya maccu.

Pāpavaggo navamo.
