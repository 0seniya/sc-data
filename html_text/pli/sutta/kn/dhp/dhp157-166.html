Attavagga

Bodhirājakumāravatthu

SC 157Vatthugāthā 157.1PTS 2nd ed. 45 Ms 18Dh_169Msdiv 157Attānañce piyaṃ jaññā,
rakkheyya naṃ surakkhitaṃ;
Tiṇṇaṃ aññataraṃ yāmaṃ,
paṭijaggeyya paṇḍito.

Upanandasakyaputtattheravatthu

SC 158Vatthugāthā 158.1Ms 18Dh_170Msdiv 158Attānameva paṭhamaṃ,
patirūpe nivesaye;
Athaññamanusāseyya,
na kilisseyya paṇḍito.

Padhānikatissattheravatthu

SC 159Vatthugāthā 159.1PTS 1st ed. 24 Ms 18Dh_171Msdiv 159Attānañce tathā kayirā,
yathāññamanusāsati;
Sudanto vata dametha,
attā hi kira duddamo.

Kumārakassapamātuttherivatthu

SC 160Vatthugāthā 160.1Ms 18Dh_172Msdiv 160Attā hi attano nātho,
ko hi nātho paro siyā;
Attanā hi sudantena,
nāthaṃ labhati dullabhaṃ.

Mahākālaupāsakavatthu

SC 161Vatthugāthā 161.1PTS 2nd ed. 46 Ms 18Dh_173Msdiv 161Attanā hi kataṃ pāpaṃ,
Attajaṃ attasambhavaṃ;
Abhimatthati dummedhaṃ,
Vajiraṃvasmamayaṃ maṇiṃ.

Devadattavatthu

SC 162Vatthugāthā 162.1Ms 18Dh_174Msdiv 162Yassa accantadussilyaṃ,
māluvā sālamivotthataṃ;
Karoti so tathattānaṃ,
yathā naṃ icchatī diso.

Saṅghabhedaparisakkanavatthu

SC 163Vatthugāthā 163.1Ms 18Dh_175Msdiv 163Sukarāni asādhūni,
attano ahitāni ca;
Yaṃ ve hitañca sādhuñca,
taṃ ve paramadukkaraṃ.

Kālattheravatthu

SC 164Vatthugāthā 164.1Ms 18Dh_176Msdiv 164Yo sāsanaṃ arahataṃ,
ariyānaṃ dhammajīvinaṃ;
Paṭikkosati dummedho,
diṭṭhiṃ nissāya pāpikaṃ;
Phalāni kaṭṭhakasseva,
attaghātāya phallati.

Cūḷakālaupāsakavatthu

SC 165Vatthugāthā 165.1PTS 2nd ed. 47 Ms 18Dh_177Msdiv 165Attanā hi kataṃ pāpaṃ,
attanā saṅkilissati;
Attanā akataṃ pāpaṃ,
attanāva visujjhati;
Suddhī asuddhi paccattaṃ,
nāñño aññaṃ visodhaye.

Attadatthattheravatthu

SC 166Vatthugāthā 166.1PTS 1st ed. 25 Ms 18Dh_178Msdiv 166Attadatthaṃ paratthena,
bahunāpi na hāpaye;
Attadatthamabhiññāya,
sadatthapasuto siyā.

Attavaggo dvādasamo.
