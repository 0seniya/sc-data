Bālavagga

Aññatarapurisavatthu

SC 60Vatthugāthā 60.1PTS 2nd ed. 17 Ms 18Dh_65Msdiv 60Dīghā jāgarato ratti,
dīghaṃ santassa yojanaṃ;
Dīgho bālāna saṃsāro,
saddhammaṃ avijānataṃ.

Mahākassapasaddhivihārikavatthu

SC 61Vatthugāthā 61.1Ms 18Dh_66Msdiv 61Carañce nādhigaccheyya,
Seyyaṃ sadisamattano;
Ekacariyaṃ daḷhaṃ kayirā,
Natthi bāle sahāyatā.

Ānandaseṭṭhivatthu

SC 62Vatthugāthā 62.1Ms 18Dh_67Msdiv 62Puttā matthi dhanaṃ matthi,
iti bālo vihaññati;
Attā hi attano natthi,
kuto puttā kuto dhanaṃ.

Gaṇṭhibhedakacoravatthu

SC 63Vatthugāthā 63.1Ms 18Dh_68Msdiv 63Yo bālo maññati bālyaṃ,
paṇḍito vāpi tena so;
Bālo ca paṇḍitamānī,
sa ve “bālo”ti vuccati.

Udāyittheravatthu

SC 64Vatthugāthā 64.1PTS 2nd ed. 18 Ms 18Dh_69Msdiv 64Yāvajīvampi ce bālo,
paṇḍitaṃ payirupāsati;
Na so dhammaṃ vijānāti,
dabbī sūparasaṃ yathā.

Tiṃsapāveyyakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 65Vatthugāthā 65.1PTS 1st ed. 10 Ms 18Dh_70Msdiv 65Muhuttamapi ce viññū,
paṇḍitaṃ payirupāsati;
Khippaṃ dhammaṃ vijānāti,
jivhā sūparasaṃ yathā.

Suppabuddhakuṭṭhivatthu

SC 66Vatthugāthā 66.1Ms 18Dh_71Msdiv 66Caranti bālā dummedhā,
amitteneva attanā;
Karontā pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
yaṃ hoti kaṭukapphalaṃ.

Kassakavatthu

SC 67Vatthugāthā 67.1Ms 18Dh_72Msdiv 67Na taṃ kammaṃ kataṃ sādhu,
yaṃ katvā anutappati;
Yassa assumukho rodaṃ,
vipākaṃ paṭisevati.

Sumanamālākāravatthu

SC 68Vatthugāthā 68.1Ms 18Dh_73Msdiv 68Tañca kammaṃ kataṃ sādhu,
yaṃ katvā nānutappati;
Yassa patīto sumano,
vipākaṃ paṭisevati.

Uppalavaṇṇattherīvatthu

SC 69Vatthugāthā 69.1PTS 2nd ed. 19 Ms 18Dh_74Msdiv 69Madhuṃvā maññati bālo,
yāva pāpaṃ na paccati;
Yadā ca paccati pāpaṃ,
atha dukkhaṃ nigacchati.

Jambukattheravatthu

SC 70Vatthugāthā 70.1Ms 18Dh_75Msdiv 70Māse māse kusaggena,
bālo bhuñjeyya bhojanaṃ;
Na so saṅkhātadhammānaṃ,
kalaṃ agghati soḷasiṃ.

Ahipetavatthu

SC 71Vatthugāthā 71.1Ms 18Dh_76Msdiv 71Na hi pāpaṃ kataṃ kammaṃ,
Sajjukhīraṃva muccati;
Ḍahantaṃ bālamanveti,
Bhasmacchannova pāvako.

Saṭṭhikūṭapetavatthu

SC 72Vatthugāthā 72.1PTS 2nd ed. 20 Ms 18Dh_77Msdiv 72Yāvadeva anatthāya,
ñattaṃ bālassa jāyati;
Hanti bālassa sukkaṃsaṃ,
muddhamassa vipātayaṃ.

Cittagahapativatthu

SC 73Vatthugāthā 73.1PTS 1st ed. 11 Ms 18Dh_78Msdiv 73Asantaṃ bhāvanamiccheyya,
Purekkhārañca bhikkhusu;
Āvāsesu ca issariyaṃ,
Pūjaṃ parakulesu ca.

SC 74Vatthugāthā 73.2Ms 18Dh_79Msdiv 74Mameva kata maññantu,
gihī pabbajitā ubho;
Mamevātivasā assu,
kiccākiccesu kismici;
Iti bālassa saṅkappo,
icchā māno ca vaḍḍhati.

Vanavāsitissasāmaṇeravatthu

SC 75Vatthugāthā 74.1Ms 18Dh_80Msdiv 75Aññā hi lābhūpanisā,
aññā nibbānagāminī;
Evametaṃ abhiññāya,
bhikkhu buddhassa sāvako;
Sakkāraṃ nābhinandeyya,
vivekamanubrūhaye.

Bālavaggo pañcamo.
