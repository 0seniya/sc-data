Pakiṇṇakavagga

Attanopubbakammavatthu

SC 290Vatthugāthā 290.1PTS 2nd ed. 82 Ms 18Dh_313Msdiv 290Mattāsukhapariccāgā,
passe ce vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;
Caje mattāsukhaṃ dhīro,
sampassaṃ vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

Kukkuṭaṇḍakhādikāvatthu

SC 291Vatthugāthā 291.1Ms 18Dh_314Msdiv 291Paradukkhūpadhānena,
attano sukhamicchati;
Verasaṃsaggasaṃsaṭṭho,
verā so na parimuccati.

Bhaddiyānaṃbhikkhūnaṃvatthu

SC 292Vatthugāthā 292.1Ms 18Dh_315Msdiv 292Yañhi kiccaṃ apaviddhaṃ,
akiccaṃ pana karīyati;
Unnaḷānaṃ pamattānaṃ,
tesaṃ vaḍḍhanti āsavā.

SC 293Vatthugāthā 292.2Ms 18Dh_316Msdiv 293Yesañca susamāraddhā,
niccaṃ kāyagatā sati;
Akiccaṃ te na sevanti,
kicce sātaccakārino;
Satānaṃ sampajānānaṃ,
atthaṃ gacchanti āsavā.

Lakuṇḍakabhaddiyavatthu

SC 294Vatthugāthā 293.1PTS 1st ed. 43 PTS 2nd ed. 83 Ms 18Dh_317Msdiv 294Mātaraṃ pitaraṃ hantvā,
rājāno dve ca khattiye;
Raṭṭhaṃ sānucaraṃ hantvā,
anīgho yāti brāhmaṇo.

SC 295Vatthugāthā 293.2Ms 18Dh_318Msdiv 295Mātaraṃ pitaraṃ hantvā,
rājāno dve ca sotthiye;
Veyagghapañcamaṃ hantvā,
anīgho yāti brāhmaṇo.

Vajjiputtakabhikkhuvatthu

SC 302Vatthugāthā 295.1Ms 18Dh_325Msdiv 302Duppabbajjaṃ durabhiramaṃ,
Durāvāsā gharā dukhā;
Dukkhosamānasaṃvāso,
Dukkhānupatitaddhagū;
Tasmā na caddhagū siyā,
Na ca dukkhānupatito siyā.

Cittagahapativatthu

SC 303Vatthugāthā 296.1Ms 18Dh_326Msdiv 303Saddho sīlena sampanno,
yasobhogasamappito;
Yaṃ yaṃ padesaṃ bhajati,
tattha tattheva pūjito.

Cūḷasubhaddāvatthu

SC 304Vatthugāthā 297.1PTS 2nd ed. 85 Ms 18Dh_327Msdiv 304Dūre santo pakāsenti,
himavantova pabbato;
Asantettha na dissanti,
rattiṃ khittā yathā sarā.

Ekavihārittheravatthu

SC 305Vatthugāthā 298.1PTS 1st ed. 44 Ms 18Dh_328Msdiv 305Ekāsanaṃ ekaseyyaṃ,
eko caramatandito;
Eko damayamattānaṃ,
vanante ramito siyā.

Pakiṇṇakavaggo ekavīsatimo.
