Cittavagga

Meghiyattheravatthu

SC 33Vatthugāthā 33.1PTS 2nd ed. 10 Ms 18Dh_36Msdiv 33Phandanaṃ capalaṃ cittaṃ,
dūrakkhaṃ dunnivārayaṃ;
Ujuṃ karoti medhāvī,
usukārova tejanaṃ.

SC 34Vatthugāthā 33.2Ms 18Dh_37Msdiv 34Vārijova thale khitto,
okamokataubbhato;
Pariphandatidaṃ cittaṃ,
māradheyyaṃ pahātave.

Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu

SC 35Vatthugāthā 34.1Ms 18Dh_38Msdiv 35Dunniggahassa lahuno,
yatthakāmanipātino;
Cittassa damatho sādhu,
cittaṃ dantaṃ sukhāvahaṃ.

Aññataraukkaṇṭhitabhikkhuvatthu

SC 36Vatthugāthā 35.1PTS 1st ed. 6 Ms 18Dh_39Msdiv 36Sududdasaṃ sunipuṇaṃ,
yatthakāmanipātinaṃ;
Cittaṃ rakkhetha medhāvī,
cittaṃ guttaṃ sukhāvahaṃ.

Saṅgharakkhitabhāgineyyattheravatthu

SC 37Vatthugāthā 36.1PTS 2nd ed. 11 Ms 18Dh_40Msdiv 37Dūraṅgamaṃ ekacaraṃ,
asarīraṃ guhāsayaṃ;
Ye cittaṃ saṃyamissanti,
mokkhanti mārabandhanā.

Cittahatthattheravatthu

SC 38Vatthugāthā 37.1Ms 18Dh_41Msdiv 38Anavaṭṭhitacittassa,
saddhammaṃ avijānato;
Pariplavapasādassa,
paññā na paripūrati.

SC 39Vatthugāthā 37.2Ms 18Dh_42Msdiv 39Anavassutacittassa,
ananvāhatacetaso;
Puññapāpapahīnassa,
natthi jāgarato bhayaṃ.

Pañcasatabhikkhuvatthu

SC 40Vatthugāthā 38.1Ms 18Dh_43Msdiv 40Kumbhūpamaṃ kāyamimaṃ viditvā,
Nagarūpamaṃ cittamidaṃ ṭhapetvā;
Yodhetha māraṃ paññāvudhena,
Jitañca rakkhe anivesano siyā.

Pūtigattatissattheravatthu

SC 41Vatthugāthā 39.1PTS 2nd ed. 12 Ms 18Dh_44Msdiv 41Aciraṃ vatayaṃ kāyo,
pathaviṃ adhisessati;
Chuddho apetaviññāṇo,
niratthaṃva kaliṅgaraṃ.

Nandagopālakavatthu

SC 42Vatthugāthā 40.1Ms 18Dh_45Msdiv 42Diso disaṃ yaṃ taṃ kayirā,
verī vā pana verinaṃ;
Micchāpaṇihitaṃ cittaṃ,
pāpiyo naṃ tato kare.

Soreyyavatthu

SC 43Vatthugāthā 41.1Ms 18Dh_46Msdiv 43Na taṃ mātā pitā kayirā,
aññe vāpi ca ñātakā;
Sammāpaṇihitaṃ cittaṃ,
seyyaso naṃ tato kare.

Cittavaggo tatiyo.
