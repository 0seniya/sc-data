28. Buddhapakiṇṇakakaṇḍa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.100 Ms 21Bu_1061Msdiv 1Aparimeyyito kappe,
caturo āsuṃ vināyakā;
Taṇhaṅkaro medhaṅkaro,
athopi saraṇaṅkaro;
Dīpaṅkaro ca sambuddho,
ekakappamhi te jinā.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_1062Msdiv 2Dīpaṅkarassa aparena,
koṇḍañño nāma nāyako;
Ekova ekakappamhi,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_1063Msdiv 3Dīpaṅkarassa bhagavato,
koṇḍaññassa ca satthuno;
Etesaṃ antarā kappā,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyā.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_1064Msdiv 4Koṇḍaññassa aparena,
maṅgalo nāma nāyako;
Tesampi antarā kappā,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyā.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_1065Msdiv 5Maṅgalo ca sumano ca,
revato sobhito muni;
Tepi buddhā ekakappe,
cakkhumanto pabhaṅkarā.

SC 6PTS 1st ed. .67 Ms 21Bu_1066Msdiv 6Sobhitassa aparena,
anomadassī mahāyaso;
Tesampi antarā kappā,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyā.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_1067Msdiv 7Anomadassī padumo,
nārado cāpi nāyako;
Tepi buddhā ekakappe,
tamantakārakā munī.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_1068Msdiv 8Nāradassa aparena,
Padumuttaro nāma nāyako;
Ekakappamhi uppanno,
Tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_1069Msdiv 9Nāradassa bhagavato,
padumuttarassa satthuno;
Tesampi antarā kappā,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyā.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_1070Msdiv 10Kappasatasahassamhi,
eko āsi mahāmuni;
Padumuttaro lokavidū,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_1071Msdiv 11Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
duve āsuṃ vināyakā;
Sumedho ca sujāto ca,
orato padumuttarā.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_1072Msdiv 12Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
tayo āsuṃ vināyakā;
Piyadassī atthadassī,
dhammadassī ca nāyakā.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_1073Msdiv 13Orato ca sujātassa,
sambuddhā dvipaduttamā;
Ekakappamhi te buddhā,
loke appaṭipuggalā.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_1074Msdiv 14Catunnavutito kappe,
eko āsi mahāmuni;
Siddhattho so lokavidū,
sallakatto anuttaro.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.101 Ms 21Bu_1075Msdiv 15Dvenavute ito kappe,
duve āsuṃ vināyakā;
Tisso phusso ca sambuddhā,
asamā appaṭipuggalā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_1076Msdiv 16Ekanavutito kappe,
vipassī nāma nāyako;
Sopi buddho kāruṇiko,
satte mocesi bandhanā.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_1077Msdiv 17Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
duve āsuṃ vināyakā;
Sikhī ca vessabhū ceva,
asamā appaṭipuggalā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_1078Msdiv 18Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
tayo āsuṃ vināyakā;
Kakusandho koṇāgamano,
kassapo cāpi nāyako.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_1079Msdiv 19Ahametarahi sambuddho,
metteyyo cāpi hessati;
Etepime pañca buddhā,
dhīrā lokānukampakā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_1080Msdiv 20Etesaṃ dhammarājūnaṃ,
Aññesaṃnekakoṭinaṃ;
Ācikkhitvāna taṃ maggaṃ,
Nibbutā te sasāvakāti. (1052)

SC 21Ms 21Bu_1081Buddhapakiṇṇakakaṇḍaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
