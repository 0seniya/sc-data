10. Padumabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .31 PTS 2nd ed. 1.44 Ms 21Bu_502Msdiv 1“Anomadassissa aparena,
Sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Padumo nāma nāmena,
Asamo appaṭipuggalo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_503Msdiv 2Tassāpi asamaṃ sīlaṃ,
samādhipi anantako;
Asaṅkheyyaṃ ñāṇavaraṃ,
vimuttipi anūpamā.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_504Msdiv 3Tassāpi atulatejassa,
dhammacakkappavattane;
Abhisamayā tayo āsuṃ,
mahātamapavāhanā.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_505Msdiv 4Paṭhamābhisamaye buddho,
koṭisatamabodhayi;
Dutiyābhisamaye dhīro,
navutikoṭimabodhayi.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_506Msdiv 5Yadā ca padumo buddho,
ovadī sakamatrajaṃ;
Tadā asītikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_507Msdiv 6Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
padumassa mahesino;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_508Msdiv 7Kathinatthārasamaye,
uppanne kathinacīvare;
Dhammasenāpatitthāya,
bhikkhū sibbiṃsu cīvaraṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_509Msdiv 8Tadā te vimalā bhikkhū,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Tīṇi satasahassāni,
samiṃsu aparājitā.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_510Msdiv 9Punāparaṃ so narāsabho,
pavane vāsaṃ upāgami;
Tadā samāgamo āsi,
dvinnaṃ satasahassinaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_511Msdiv 10Ahaṃ tena samayena,
sīho āsiṃ migādhibhū;
Vivekamanubrūhantaṃ,
pavane addasaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_512Msdiv 11Vanditvā sirasā pāde,
katvāna taṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Tikkhattuṃ abhināditvā,
sattāhaṃ jinamupaṭṭhahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_513Msdiv 12Sattāhaṃ varasamāpattiyā,
Vuṭṭhahitvā tathāgato;
Manasā cintayitvāna,
Koṭibhikkhū samānayi.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_514Msdiv 13Tadāpi so mahāvīro,
tesaṃ majjhe viyākari;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.45 Ms 21Bu_515Msdiv 14Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_516Msdiv 15Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_517Msdiv 16Campakaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
asamo nāma khattiyo;
Asamā nāma janikā,
padumassa mahesino.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .32 Ms 21Bu_518Msdiv 17Dasavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Nandā-vasu-yasuttarā,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_519Msdiv 18Tettiṃsa ca sahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Uttarā nāma sā nārī,
rammo nāmāsi atrajo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_520Msdiv 19Nimitte caturo disvā,
rathayānena nikkhami;
Anūnaaṭṭhamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_521Msdiv 20Brahmunā yācito santo,
padumo lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
dhanañcuyyānamuttame.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_522Msdiv 21Sālo ca upasālo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Varuṇo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
padumassa mahesino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_523Msdiv 22Rādhā ceva surādhā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
mahāsoṇoti vuccati.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_524Msdiv 23Bhiyyo ceva asamo ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Rucī ca nandarāmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_525Msdiv 24Aṭṭhapaṇṇāsaratanaṃ,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Pabhā niddhāvatī tassa,
asamā sabbaso disā.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_526Msdiv 25Candappabhā sūriyappabhā,
ratanaggimaṇippabhā;
Sabbāpi tā hatā honti,
patvā jinapabhuttamaṃ.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_527Msdiv 26Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_528Msdiv 27Paripakkamānase satte,
bodhayitvā asesato;
Sesake anusāsitvā,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_529Msdiv 28Uragova tacaṃ jiṇṇaṃ,
vaddhapattaṃva pādapo;
Jahitvā sabbasaṅkhāre,
nibbuto so yathā sikhī.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.46 Ms 21Bu_530Msdiv 29Padumo jinavaro satthā,
Dhammārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (520)

SC 30Ms 21Bu_531Padumassa bhagavato vaṃso aṭṭhamo.
