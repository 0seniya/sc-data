19. Tissabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.71 Ms 21Bu_773SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.71 Ms 21Bu_773Msdiv 1“Siddhatthassa aparena,
Asamo appaṭipuggalo;
Anantatejo amitayaso,
Tisso lokagganāyako.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_774SC 2Ms 21Bu_774Msdiv 2Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamitvā,
obhāsetvā sadevakaṃ;
Anukampako mahāvīro,
loke uppajji cakkhumā.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_775SC 3Ms 21Bu_775Msdiv 3Tassāpi atulā iddhi,
atulaṃ sīlaṃ samādhi ca;
Sabbattha pāramiṃ gantvā,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_776SC 4Ms 21Bu_776Msdiv 4So buddho dasasahassimhi,
Viññāpesi giraṃ suciṃ;
Koṭisatāni abhisamiṃsu,
Paṭhame dhammadesane.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_777SC 5Ms 21Bu_777Msdiv 5Dutiyo navutikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyo saṭṭhikoṭiyo;
Bandhanāto pamocesi,
satte naramarū tadā.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_778SC 6Ms 21Bu_778Msdiv 6Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
tisse lokagganāyake;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_779SC 7Ms 21Bu_779Msdiv 7Khīṇāsavasatasahassānaṃ,
Paṭhamo āsi samāgamo;
Navutisatasahassānaṃ,
Dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 8PTS 1st ed. .49 Ms 21Bu_780SC 8PTS 1st ed. .49 Ms 21Bu_780Msdiv 8Asītisatasahassānaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
pupphitānaṃ vimuttiyā.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_781SC 9Ms 21Bu_781Msdiv 9Ahaṃ tena samayena,
sujāto nāma khattiyo;
Mahābhogaṃ chaḍḍayitvā,
pabbajiṃ isipabbajaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_782SC 10Ms 21Bu_782Msdiv 10Mayi pabbajite sante,
uppajji lokanāyako;
Buddhoti saddaṃ sutvāna,
pīti me upapajjatha.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_783SC 11Ms 21Bu_783Msdiv 11Dibbaṃ mandāravaṃ pupphaṃ,
padumaṃ pārichattakaṃ;
Ubho hatthehi paggayha,
dhunamāno upāgamiṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_784SC 12Ms 21Bu_784Msdiv 12Catuvaṇṇaparivutaṃ,
tissaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Tamahaṃ pupphaṃ gahetvā,
matthake dhārayiṃ jinaṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_785SC 13Ms 21Bu_785Msdiv 13Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
janamajjhe nisīdiya;
‘Dvenavute ito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.72 Ms 21Bu_786SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.72 Ms 21Bu_786Msdiv 14Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_787SC 15Ms 21Bu_787Msdiv 15Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_788SC 16Ms 21Bu_788Msdiv 16Khemakaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
janasandho nāma khattiyo;
Padumā nāma janikā,
tissassa ca mahesino.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_789SC 17Ms 21Bu_789Msdiv 17Sattavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Guhāsela nārisaya nisabhā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_790SC 18Ms 21Bu_790Msdiv 18Samatiṃsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Subhaddānāmikā nārī,
ānando nāma atrajo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_791SC 19Ms 21Bu_791Msdiv 19Nimitte caturo disvā,
assayānena nikkhami;
Anūnaaṭṭhamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_792SC 20Ms 21Bu_792Msdiv 20Brahmunā yācito santo,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
yasavatiyamuttame.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_793SC 21Ms 21Bu_793Msdiv 21Brahmadevo udayo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Samaṅgo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
tissassa ca mahesino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_794SC 22Ms 21Bu_794Msdiv 22Phussā ceva sudattā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
asanoti pavuccati.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_795SC 23Ms 21Bu_795Msdiv 23Sambalo ca sirimā ceva,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Kisāgotamī upasenā,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_796SC 24Ms 21Bu_796Msdiv 24So buddho saṭṭhiratano,
ahu uccattane jino;
Anūpamo asadiso,
himavā viya dissati.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_797SC 25Ms 21Bu_797Msdiv 25Tassāpi atulatejassa,
āyu āsi anuttaro;
Vassasatasahassāni,
loke aṭṭhāsi cakkhumā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_798SC 26Ms 21Bu_798Msdiv 26Uttamaṃ pavaraṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
anubhotvā mahāyasaṃ;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 27PTS 1st ed. .50 Ms 21Bu_799SC 27PTS 1st ed. .50 Ms 21Bu_799Msdiv 27Valāhakova anilena,
sūriyena viya ussavo;
Andhakārova padīpena,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_800SC 28Ms 21Bu_800Msdiv 28Tisso jinavaro buddho,
Nandārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa jinathūpo,
Tīṇiyojanamuggato”ti. (781)

SC 29SC 29Ms 21Bu_801Tissassa bhagavato vaṃso sattarasamo.
