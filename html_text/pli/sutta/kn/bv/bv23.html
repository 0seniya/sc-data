23. Vessabhūbuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .56 PTS 2nd ed. 1.83 Ms 21Bu_895Msdiv 1“Tattheva maṇḍakappamhi,
asamo appaṭipuggalo;
Vessabhū nāma nāmena,
loke uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_896Msdiv 2Ādittaṃ vata rāgaggi,
taṇhānaṃ vijitaṃ tadā;
Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_897Msdiv 3Dhammacakkaṃ pavattente,
vessabhūlokanāyake;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_898Msdiv 4Pakkante cārikaṃ raṭṭhe,
lokajeṭṭhe narāsabhe;
Sattatikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_899Msdiv 5Mahādiṭṭhiṃ vinodento,
pāṭiheraṃ karoti so;
Samāgatā naramarū,
dasasahassī sadevake.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_900Msdiv 6Mahāacchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Devā ceva manussā ca,
bujjhare saṭṭhikoṭiyo.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_901Msdiv 7Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
vessabhussa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_902Msdiv 8Asītibhikkhusahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo;
Sattatibhikkhusahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_903Msdiv 9Saṭṭhibhikkhusahassānaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo;
Jarādibhayabhītānaṃ,
orasānaṃ mahesino.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_904Msdiv 10Ahaṃ tena samayena,
sudassano nāma khattiyo;
Nimantetvā mahāvīraṃ,
dānaṃ datvā mahārahaṃ;
Annapānena vatthena,
sasaṃghaṃ jinapūjayiṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_905Msdiv 11Tassa buddhassa asamassa,
cakkaṃ vattitamuttamaṃ;
Sutvāna paṇitaṃ dhammaṃ,
pabbajjamabhirocayiṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_906Msdiv 12Mahādānaṃ pavattetvā,
rattindivamatandito;
Pabbajjaṃ guṇasampannaṃ,
pabbajiṃ jinasantike.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_907Msdiv 13Ācāraguṇasampanno,
vattasīlasamāhito;
Sabbaññutaṃ gavesanto,
ramāmi jinasāsane.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.84 Ms 21Bu_908Msdiv 14Saddhāpītiṃ upagantvā,
buddhaṃ vandāmi sattharaṃ;
Pīti uppajjati mayhaṃ,
bodhiyāyeva kāraṇā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_909Msdiv 15Anivattamānasaṃ ñatvā,
sambuddho etadabravi;
‘Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_910Msdiv 16Ahu kapilavhayā rammā,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .57 Ms 21Bu_911Msdiv 17Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_912Msdiv 18Anomaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
suppatīto nāma khattiyo;
Mātā yasavatī nāma,
vessabhussa mahesino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_913Msdiv 19Cha ca vassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Ruci suruci rativaḍḍhano,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_914Msdiv 20Anūnatiṃsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sucittā nāma sā nārī,
suppabuddho nāma atrajo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_915Msdiv 21Nimitte caturo disvā,
sivikāyābhinikkhami;
Chamāsaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acarī purisuttamo.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_916Msdiv 22Brahmunā yācito santo,
vessabhūlokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
aruṇārāme naruttamo.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_917Msdiv 23Soṇo ca uttaro ceva,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Upasanto nāmupaṭṭhāko,
vessabhussa mahesino.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_918Msdiv 24Rāmā ceva samālā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
mahāsāloti vuccati.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_919Msdiv 25Sotthiko ceva rambho ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Gotamī sirimā ceva,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_920Msdiv 26Saṭṭhiratanamubbedho,
hemayūpasamūpamo;
Kāyā niccharati rasmi,
rattiṃva pabbate sikhī.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_921Msdiv 27Saṭṭhivassasahassāni,
āyu tassa mahesino;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_922Msdiv 28Dhammaṃ vitthārikaṃ katvā,
vibhajitvā mahājanaṃ;
Dhammanāvaṃ ṭhapetvāna,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.85 Ms 21Bu_923Msdiv 29Dassaneyyaṃ sabbajanaṃ,
vihāraṃ iriyāpathaṃ;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 30Ms 21Bu_924Msdiv 30Vessabhū jinavaro satthā,
Khemārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (901)

SC 31Ms 21Bu_925Vessabhussa bhagavato vaṃso ekavīsatimo.
