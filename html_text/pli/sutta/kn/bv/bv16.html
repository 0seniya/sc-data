16 Atthadassībuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .43 PTS 2nd ed. 1.62 Ms 21Bu_695Msdiv 1“Tattheva maṇḍakappamhi,
atthadassī mahāyaso;
Mahātamaṃ nihantvāna,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_696Msdiv 2Brahmunā yācito santo,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi;
Amatena tappayī lokaṃ,
dasasahassisadevakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_697Msdiv 3Tassāpi lokanāthassa,
Ahesuṃ abhisamayā tayo;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_698Msdiv 4Yadā buddho atthadassī,
carate devacārikaṃ;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_699Msdiv 5Punāparaṃ yadā buddho,
desesi pitusantike;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_700Msdiv 6Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
tassāpi ca mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_701Msdiv 7Aṭṭhanavutisahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo;
Aṭṭhāsītisahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_702Msdiv 8Aṭṭhasattatisatasahassānaṃ,
Tatiyo āsi samāgamo;
Anupādā vimuttānaṃ,
Vimalānaṃ mahesinaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_703Msdiv 9Ahaṃ tena samayena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Susīmo nāma nāmena,
mahiyā seṭṭhasammato.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_704Msdiv 10Dibbaṃ mandāravaṃ pupphaṃ,
padumaṃ pārichattakaṃ;
Devalokāharitvāna,
sambuddhamabhipūjayiṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_705Msdiv 11Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
atthadassī mahāmuni;
‘Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_706Msdiv 12Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_707Msdiv 13Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_708Msdiv 14Sobhanaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
sāgaro nāma khattiyo;
Sudassanā nāma janikā,
atthadassissa satthuno.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.63 Ms 21Bu_709Msdiv 15Dasavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Amaragiri sugiri vāhanā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_710Msdiv 16Tettiṃsañca sahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Visākhā nāma nārī ca,
selo nāmāsi atrajo.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .44 Ms 21Bu_711Msdiv 17Nimitte caturo disvā,
assayānena nikkhami;
Anūnaaṭṭhamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_712Msdiv 18Brahmunā yācito santo,
atthadassī mahāyaso;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
anomuyyāne narāsabho.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_713Msdiv 19Santo ca upasanto ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Abhayo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
atthadassissa satthuno.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_714Msdiv 20Dhammā ceva sudhammā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
campakoti pavuccati.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_715Msdiv 21Nakulo ca nisabho ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Makilā ca sunandā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_716Msdiv 22Sopi buddho asamasamo,
asītihatthamuggato;
Sobhate sālarājāva,
uḷurājāva pūrito.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_717Msdiv 23Tassa pākatikā raṃsī,
anekasatakoṭiyo;
Uddhaṃ adho dasa disā,
pharanti yojanaṃ sadā.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_718Msdiv 24Sopi buddho narāsabho,
sabbasattuttamo muni;
Vassasatasahassāni,
loke aṭṭhāsi cakkhumā.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_719Msdiv 25Atulaṃ dassetvā obhāsaṃ,
virocetvā sadevake;
Sopi aniccataṃ patto,
yathaggupādānasaṅkhayā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_720Msdiv 26Atthadassī jinavaro,
Anomārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (704)

SC 27Ms 21Bu_721Atthadassissa bhagavato vaṃso cuddasamo.
