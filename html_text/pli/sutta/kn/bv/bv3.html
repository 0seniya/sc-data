3. Dīpaṅkarabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .17 Ms 21Bu_272SC 1PTS 1st ed. .17 Ms 21Bu_272Msdiv 1“Tadā te bhojayitvāna,
sasaṃghaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Upagacchuṃ saraṇaṃ tassa,
dīpaṅkarassa satthuno.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_273SC 2Ms 21Bu_273Msdiv 2Saraṇāgamane kañci,
nivesesi tathāgato;
Kañci pañcasu sīlesu,
sīle dasavidhe paraṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_274SC 3Ms 21Bu_274Msdiv 3Kassaci deti sāmaññaṃ,
caturo phalamuttame;
Kassaci asame dhamme,
deti so paṭisambhidā.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_275SC 4Ms 21Bu_275Msdiv 4Kassaci varasamāpattiyo,
Aṭṭha deti narāsabho;
Tisso kassaci vijjāyo,
Chaḷabhiññā pavecchati.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_276SC 5Ms 21Bu_276Msdiv 5Tena yogena janakāyaṃ,
ovadati mahāmuni;
Tena vitthārikaṃ āsi,
lokanāthassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_277SC 6Ms 21Bu_277Msdiv 6Mahāhanusabhakkhandho,
dīpaṅkarassa nāmako;
Bahū jane tārayati,
parimoceti duggatiṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_278SC 7Ms 21Bu_278Msdiv 7Bodhaneyyaṃ janaṃ disvā,
satasahassepi yojane;
Khaṇena upagantvāna,
bodheti taṃ mahāmuni.

SC 8PTS 2nd ed. 1.22 Ms 21Bu_279SC 8PTS 2nd ed. 1.22 Ms 21Bu_279Msdiv 8Paṭhamābhisamaye buddho,
koṭisatamabodhayi;
Dutiyābhisamaye nātho,
navutikoṭimabodhayi.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_280SC 9Ms 21Bu_280Msdiv 9Yadā ca devabhavanamhi,
buddho dhammamadesayi;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_281SC 10Ms 21Bu_281Msdiv 10Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
dīpaṅkarassa satthuno;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_282SC 11Ms 21Bu_282Msdiv 11Puna nāradakūṭamhi,
pavivekagate jine;
Khīṇāsavā vītamalā,
samiṃsu satakoṭiyo.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_283SC 12Ms 21Bu_283Msdiv 12Yamhi kāle mahāvīro,
sudassanasiluccaye;
Navakoṭisahassehi,
pavāresi mahāmuni.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_284SC 13Ms 21Bu_284Msdiv 13Dasavīsasahassānaṃ,
dhammābhisamayo ahu;
Ekadvinnaṃ abhisamayā,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyā.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_285SC 14Ms 21Bu_285Msdiv 14Vitthārikaṃ bāhujaññaṃ,
iddhaṃ phītaṃ ahū tadā;
Dīpaṅkarassa bhagavato,
sāsanaṃ suvisodhitaṃ.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_286SC 15Ms 21Bu_286Msdiv 15Cattāri satasahassāni,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Dīpaṅkaraṃ lokaviduṃ,
parivārenti sabbadā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_287SC 16Ms 21Bu_287Msdiv 16Ye keci tena samayena,
jahanti mānusaṃ bhavaṃ;
Apattamānasā sekhā,
garahitā bhavanti te.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_288SC 17Ms 21Bu_288Msdiv 17Supupphitaṃ pāvacanaṃ,
arahantehi tādihi;
Khīṇāsavehi vimalehi,
upasobhati sabbadā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_289SC 18Ms 21Bu_289Msdiv 18Nagaraṃ rammavatī nāma,
sudevo nāma khattiyo;
Sumedhā nāma janikā,
dīpaṅkarassa satthuno.

SC 19PTS 1st ed. .18 Ms 21Bu_290SC 19PTS 1st ed. .18 Ms 21Bu_290Msdiv 19Dasavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Haṃsā koñcā mayūrā ca,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_291SC 20Ms 21Bu_291Msdiv 20Tīṇi satasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Padumā nāma sā nārī,
usabhakkhandho atrajo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_292SC 21Ms 21Bu_292Msdiv 21Nimitte caturo disvā,
hatthiyānena nikkhami;
Anūnadasamāsāni,
padhāne padahī jino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_293SC 22Ms 21Bu_293Msdiv 22Padhānacāraṃ caritvāna,
abujjhi mānasaṃ muni;
Brahmunā yācito santo,
dīpaṅkaro mahāmuni.

SC 23PTS 2nd ed. 1.23 Ms 21Bu_294SC 23PTS 2nd ed. 1.23 Ms 21Bu_294Msdiv 23Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
nandārāme sirīghare;
Nisinno sirīsamūlamhi,
akā titthiyamaddanaṃ.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_295SC 24Ms 21Bu_295Msdiv 24Sumaṅgalo ca tisso ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sāgato nāmupaṭṭhāko,
dīpaṅkarassa satthuno.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_296SC 25Ms 21Bu_296Msdiv 25Nandā ceva sunandā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
pipphalīti pavuccati.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_297Msdiv 26SC 26Ms 21Bu_297Msdiv 26Tapussabhallikā nāma,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Sirimā koṇā upaṭṭhikā,
dīpaṅkarassa satthuno.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_298SC 27Ms 21Bu_298Msdiv 27Asītihatthamubbedho,
dīpaṅkaro mahāmuni;
Sobhati dīparukkhova,
sālarājāva phullito.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_299SC 28Ms 21Bu_299Msdiv 28Satasahassavassāni,
āyu tassa mahesino;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_300SC 29Ms 21Bu_300Msdiv 29Jotayitvāna saddhammaṃ,
santāretvā mahājanaṃ;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 30Ms 21Bu_301SC 30Ms 21Bu_301Msdiv 30Sā ca iddhi so ca yaso,
Tāni ca pādesu cakkaratanāni;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_302SC 31Ms 21Bu_302Msdiv 31Dīpaṅkaro jino satthā,
Nandārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa jinathūpo,
Chattiṃsubbedhayojano”ti. (299)

SC 32Ms 21Bu_303SC 32Ms 21Bu_303Dīpaṅkarassa bhagavato vaṃso paṭhamo.
