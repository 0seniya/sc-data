14. Sujātabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .39 PTS 2nd ed. 1.56 Ms 21Bu_630Msdiv 1“Tattheva maṇḍakappamhi,
sujāto nāma nāyako;
Sīhahanusabhakkhandho,
appameyyo durāsado.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_631Msdiv 2Candova vimalo suddho,
sataraṃsīva patāpavā;
Evaṃ sobhati sambuddho,
jalanto siriyā sadā.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_632Msdiv 3Pāpuṇitvāna sambuddho,
kevalaṃ bodhimuttamaṃ;
Sumaṅgalamhi nagare,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_633Msdiv 4Desente pavaraṃ dhammaṃ,
Sujāte lokanāyake;
Asītikoṭī abhisamiṃsu,
Paṭhame dhammadesane.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_634Msdiv 5Yadā sujāto amitayaso,
Deve vassaṃ upāgami;
Sattatiṃsasatasahassānaṃ,
Dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_635Msdiv 6Yadā sujāto asamasamo,
Upagacchi pitusantikaṃ;
Saṭṭhisatasahassānaṃ,
Tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_636Msdiv 7Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
sujātassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_637Msdiv 8Abhiññābalappattānaṃ,
appattānaṃ bhavābhave;
Saṭṭhisatasahassāni,
paṭhamaṃ sannipatiṃsu te.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_638Msdiv 9Punāparaṃ sannipāte,
tidivorohaṇe jine;
Paññāsasatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_639Msdiv 10Upasaṅkamanto narāsabhaṃ,
Tassa yo aggasāvako;
Catūhi satasahassehi,
Sambuddhaṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_640Msdiv 11Ahaṃ tena samayena,
catudīpamhi issaro;
Antalikkhacaro āsiṃ,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_641Msdiv 12Loke acchariyaṃ disvā,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Upagantvāna vandiṃ so,
sujātaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_642Msdiv 13Catudīpe mahārajjaṃ,
ratane satta uttame;
Buddhe niyyādayitvāna,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_643Msdiv 14Ārāmikā janapade,
uṭṭhānaṃ paṭipiṇḍiya;
Upanenti bhikkhusaṃghassa,
paccayaṃ sayanāsanaṃ.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.57 Ms 21Bu_644Msdiv 15Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
dasasahassimhi issaro;
‘Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 16PTS 1st ed. .40 Ms 21Bu_645Msdiv 16Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_646Msdiv 17Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo hāsaṃ janesahaṃ;
Adhiṭṭhahiṃ vataṃ uggaṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_647Msdiv 18Suttantaṃ vinayañcāpi,
navaṅgaṃ satthusāsanaṃ;
Sabbaṃ pariyāpuṇitvāna,
sobhayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_648Msdiv 19Tatthappamatto viharanto,
brahmaṃ bhāvetva bhāvanaṃ;
Abhiññāpāramiṃ gantvā,
brahmalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_649Msdiv 20Sumaṅgalaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
uggato nāma khattiyo;
Mātā pabhāvatī nāma,
sujātassa mahesino.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_650Msdiv 21Navavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Sirī upasirī nando,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_651Msdiv 22Tevīsatisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sirinandā nāma nārī,
upaseno nāma atrajo.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_652Msdiv 23Nimitte caturo disvā,
assayānena nikkhami;
Anūnanavamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_653Msdiv 24Brahmunā yācito santo,
sujāto lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
sumaṅgaluyyānamuttame.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_654Msdiv 25Sudassano sudevo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Nārado nāmupaṭṭhāko,
sujātassa mahesino.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_655Msdiv 26Nāgā ca nāgasamālā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
mahāveḷūti vuccati.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_656Msdiv 27So ca rukkho ghanakkhandho,
Acchiddo hoti pattiko;
Uju vaṃso brahā hoti,
Dassanīyo manoramo.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_657Msdiv 28Ekakkhandho pavaḍḍhitvā,
tato sākhā pabhijjati;
Yathā subaddho morahattho,
evaṃ sobhati so dumo.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_658Msdiv 29Na tassa kaṇṭakā honti,
nāpi chiddaṃ mahā ahu;
Vitthiṇṇasākho aviralo,
sandacchāyo manoramo.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.58 Ms 21Bu_659Msdiv 30Sudatto ceva citto ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Subhaddā ca padumā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_660Msdiv 31Paññāsaratano āsi,
uccattanena so jino;
Sabbākāravarūpeto,
sabbaguṇamupāgato.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_661Msdiv 32Tassa pabhā asamasamā,
niddhāvati samantato;
Appamāṇo atuliyo,
opammehi anūpamo.

SC 33Ms 21Bu_662Msdiv 33Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 34Ms 21Bu_663Msdiv 34Yathāpi sāgare ūmī,
gagane tārakā yathā;
Evaṃ tadā pāvacanaṃ,
arahantehi cittitaṃ.

SC 35PTS 1st ed. .41 Ms 21Bu_664Msdiv 35So ca buddho asamasamo,
Guṇāni ca tāni atuliyāni;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 36Ms 21Bu_665Msdiv 36Sujāto jinavaro buddho,
Silārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tattheva tassa cetiyo,
Tīṇigāvutamuggato”ti. (651)

SC 37Ms 21Bu_666Sujātassa bhagavato vaṃso dvādasamo.
