• Buddhavaṃsa

1. Ratanacaṅkamanakaṇḍa

SC 1Ms 21Bu_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2PTS 1st ed. 1.1 PTS 2nd ed. 1.1 Ms 21Bu_2Msdiv 1Brahmā ca lokādhipatī sahampatī,
Katañjalī anadhivaraṃ ayācatha;
“Santīdha sattāpparajakkhajātikā,
Desehi dhammaṃ anukampimaṃ pajaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_3Msdiv 2Sampannavijjācaraṇassa tādino,
Jutindharassantimadehadhārino;
Tathāgatassappaṭipuggalassa,
Uppajji kāruññatā sabbasatte.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_4Msdiv 3“Na hete jānanti sadevamānusā,
Buddho ayaṃ kīdisako naruttamo;
Iddhibalaṃ paññābalañca kīdisaṃ,
Buddhabalaṃ lokahitassa kīdisaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_5Msdiv 4Na hete jānanti sadevamānusā,
Buddho ayaṃ edisako naruttamo;
Iddhibalaṃ paññābalañca edisaṃ,
Buddhabalaṃ lokahitassa edisaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_6Msdiv 5Handāhaṃ dassayissāmi,
buddhabalamanuttaraṃ;
Caṅkamaṃ māpayissāmi,
nabhe ratanamaṇḍitaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_7Msdiv 6Bhummā mahārājikā tāvatiṃsā,
Yāmā ca devā tusitā ca nimmitā;
Paranimmitā yepi ca brahmakāyikā,
Ānanditā vipulamakaṃsu ghosaṃ.

SC 8PTS 1st ed. 1.2 Ms 21Bu_8Msdiv 7Obhāsitā ca pathavī sadevakā,
Puthū ca lokantarikā asaṃvutā;
Tamo ca tibbo vihato tadā ahu,
Disvāna accherakaṃ pāṭihīraṃ.

SC 9PTS 2nd ed. 1.2 Ms 21Bu_9Msdiv 8Sadevagandhabbamanussarakkhase,
Ābhā uḷārā vipulā ajāyatha;
Imasmiṃ loke parasmiñcobhayasmiṃ,
Adho ca uddhaṃ tiriyañca vitthataṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_10Msdiv 9Sattuttamo anadhivaro vināyako,
Satthā ahū devamanussapūjito;
Mahānubhāvo satapuññalakkhaṇo,
Dassesi accherakaṃ pāṭihīraṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_11Msdiv 10So yācito devavarena cakkhumā,
Atthaṃ samekkhitvā tadā naruttamo;
Caṅkamaṃ māpayi lokanāyako,
Suniṭṭhitaṃ sabbaratananimmitaṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_12Msdiv 11Iddhī ca ādesanānusāsanī,
Tipāṭihīre bhagavā vasī ahu;
Caṅkamaṃ māpayi lokanāyako,
Suniṭṭhitaṃ sabbaratananimmitaṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_13Msdiv 12Dasasahassīlokadhātuyā,
Sinerupabbatuttame;
Thambheva dassesi paṭipāṭiyā,
Caṅkame ratanāmaye.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_14Msdiv 13Dasasahassī atikkamma,
caṅkamaṃ māpayī jino;
Sabbasoṇṇamayā passe,
caṅkame ratanāmaye.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_15Msdiv 14Tulāsaṅghāṭānuvaggā,
sovaṇṇaphalakatthatā;
Vedikā sabbasovaṇṇā,
dubhato passesu nimmitā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_16Msdiv 15Maṇimuttāvālikākiṇṇā,
nimmito ratanāmayo;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
sataraṃsīva uggato.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_17Msdiv 16Tasmiṃ caṅkamane dhīro,
dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Virocamāno sambuddho,
caṅkame caṅkamī jino.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_18Msdiv 17Dibbaṃ mandāravaṃ pupphaṃ,
padumaṃ pārichattakaṃ;
Caṅkamane okiranti,
sabbe devā samāgatā.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_19Msdiv 18Passanti taṃ devasaṅghā,
dasasahassī pamoditā;
Namassamānā nipatanti,
tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_20Msdiv 19Tāvatiṃsā ca yāmā ca,
tusitā cāpi devatā;
Nimmānaratino devā,
ye devā vasavattino;
Udaggacittā sumanā,
passanti lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 21PTS 1st ed. 1.3 PTS 2nd ed. 1.3 Ms 21Bu_21Msdiv 20Sadevagandhabbamanussarakkhasā,
Nāgā supaṇṇā atha vāpi kinnarā;
Passanti taṃ lokahitānukampakaṃ,
Nabheva accuggatacandamaṇḍalaṃ.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_22Msdiv 21Ābhassarā subhakiṇhā,
Vehapphalā akaniṭṭhā ca devatā;
Susuddhasukkavatthavasanā,
Tiṭṭhanti pañjalīkatā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_23Msdiv 22Muñcanti pupphaṃ pana pañcavaṇṇikaṃ,
Mandāravaṃ candanacuṇṇamissitaṃ;
Bhamenti celāni ca ambare tadā,
“Aho jino lokahitānukampako.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_24Msdiv 23Tuvaṃ satthā ca ketū ca,
dhajo yūpo ca pāṇinaṃ;
Parāyaṇo patiṭṭhā ca,
dīpo ca dvipaduttamo”.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_25Msdiv 24Dasasahassīlokadhātuyā,
Devatāyo mahiddhikā;
Parivāretvā namassanti,
Tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_26Msdiv 25Devatā devakaññā ca,
pasannā tuṭṭhamānasā;
Pañcavaṇṇikapupphehi,
pūjayanti narāsabhaṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_27Msdiv 26Passanti taṃ devasaṅghā,
pasannā tuṭṭhamānasā;
Pañcavaṇṇikapupphehi,
pūjayanti narāsabhaṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_28Msdiv 27“Aho acchariyaṃ loke,
abbhutaṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Na medisaṃ bhūtapubbaṃ,
accheraṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ”.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_29Msdiv 28Sakasakamhi bhavane,
nisīditvāna devatā;
Hasanti tā mahāhasitaṃ,
disvānaccherakaṃ nabhe.

SC 30Ms 21Bu_30Msdiv 29Ākāsaṭṭhā ca bhūmaṭṭhā,
tiṇapanthanivāsino;
Katañjalī namassanti,
tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_31Msdiv 30Yepi dīghāyukā nāgā,
puññavanto mahiddhikā;
Pamoditā namassanti,
pūjayanti naruttamaṃ.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_32Msdiv 31Saṅgītiyo pavattenti,
ambare anilañjase;
Cammanaddhāni vādenti,
disvānaccherakaṃ nabhe.

SC 33Ms 21Bu_33Msdiv 32Saṅkhā ca paṇavā ceva,
athopi ḍiṇḍimā bahū;
Antalikkhasmiṃ vajjanti,
disvānaccherakaṃ nabhe.

SC 34Ms 21Bu_34Msdiv 33“Abbhuto vata no ajja,
uppajji lomahaṃsano;
Dhuvamatthasiddhiṃ labhāma,
khaṇo no paṭipādito”.

SC 35PTS 2nd ed. 1.4 Ms 21Bu_35Msdiv 34Buddhoti tesaṃ sutvāna,
pīti uppajji tāvade;
Buddho buddhoti kathayantā,
tiṭṭhanti pañjalīkatā.

SC 36Ms 21Bu_36Msdiv 35Hiṅkārā sādhukārā ca,
ukkuṭṭhi sampahaṃsanaṃ;
Pajā ca vividhā gagane,
vattenti pañjalīkatā.

SC 37Ms 21Bu_37Msdiv 36Gāyanti seḷenti ca vādayanti ca,
Bhujāni pothenti ca naccayanti ca;
Muñcanti pupphaṃ pana pañcavaṇṇikaṃ,
Mandāravaṃ candanacuṇṇamissitaṃ.

SC 38PTS 1st ed. 1.4 Ms 21Bu_38Msdiv 37“Yathā tuyhaṃ mahāvīra,
pādesu cakkalakkhaṇaṃ;
Dhajavajirapaṭākā,
vaḍḍhamānaṅkusācitaṃ.

SC 39Ms 21Bu_39Msdiv 38Rūpe sīle samādhimhi,
paññāya ca asādiso;
Vimuttiyā asamasamo,
dhammacakkappavattane.

SC 40Ms 21Bu_40Msdiv 39Dasanāgabalaṃ kāye,
tuyhaṃ pākatikaṃ balaṃ;
Iddhibalena asamo,
dhammacakkappavattane.

SC 41Ms 21Bu_41Msdiv 40Evaṃ sabbaguṇūpetaṃ,
sabbaṅgasamupāgataṃ;
Mahāmuniṃ kāruṇikaṃ,
lokanāthaṃ namassatha.

SC 42Ms 21Bu_42Msdiv 41Abhivādanaṃ thomanañca,
vandanañca pasaṃsanaṃ;
Namassanañca pūjañca,
sabbaṃ arahasī tuvaṃ.

SC 43Ms 21Bu_43Msdiv 42Ye keci loke vandaneyyā,
vandanaṃ arahanti ye;
Sabbaseṭṭho mahāvīra,
sadiso te na vijjati.

SC 44Ms 21Bu_44Msdiv 43Sāriputto mahāpañño,
samādhijjhānakovido;
Gijjhakūṭe ṭhitoyeva,
passati lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 45Ms 21Bu_45Msdiv 44Suphullaṃ sālarājaṃva,
candaṃva gagane yathā;
Majjhanhikeva sūriyaṃ,
olokesi narāsabhaṃ.

SC 46Ms 21Bu_46Msdiv 45Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
Taruṇasūriyaṃva uggataṃ;
Byāmappabhānurañjitaṃ,
Dhīraṃ passati lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 47Ms 21Bu_47Msdiv 46Pañcannaṃ bhikkhusatānaṃ,
katakiccāna tādinaṃ;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
khaṇena sannipātayi.

SC 48Ms 21Bu_48Msdiv 47Lokappasādanaṃ nāma,
pāṭihīraṃ nidassayi;
Amhepi tattha gantvāna,
vandissāma mayaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 49Ms 21Bu_49Msdiv 48Etha sabbe gamissāma,
pucchissāma mayaṃ jinaṃ;
Kaṅkhaṃ vinodayissāma,
passitvā lokanāyakaṃ”.

SC 50PTS 2nd ed. 1.5 Ms 21Bu_50Msdiv 49Sādhūti te paṭissutvā,
nipakā saṃvutindriyā;
Pattacīvaramādāya,
taramānā upāgamuṃ.

SC 51Ms 21Bu_51Msdiv 50Khīṇāsavehi vimalehi,
dantehi uttame dame;
Sāriputto mahāpañño,
iddhiyā upasaṅkami.

SC 52Ms 21Bu_52Msdiv 51Tehi bhikkhūhi parivuto,
sāriputto mahāgaṇī;
Laḷanto devova gagane,
iddhiyā upasaṅkami.

SC 53Ms 21Bu_53Msdiv 52Ukkāsitañca khipitaṃ,
ajjhupekkhiya subbatā;
Sagāravā sappatissā,
sambuddhaṃ upasaṅkamuṃ.

SC 54Ms 21Bu_54Msdiv 53Upasaṅkamitvā passanti,
sayambhuṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Nabhe accuggataṃ dhīraṃ,
candaṃva gagane yathā.

SC 55Ms 21Bu_55Msdiv 54Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
vijjuṃva gagane yathā;
Majjhanhikeva sūriyaṃ,
passanti lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 56Ms 21Bu_56Msdiv 55Pañcabhikkhusatā sabbe,
passanti lokanāyakaṃ;
Rahadamiva vippasannaṃ,
suphullaṃ padumaṃ yathā.

SC 57PTS 1st ed. 1.5 Ms 21Bu_57Msdiv 56Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
tuṭṭhahaṭṭhā pamoditā;
Namassamānā nipatanti,
satthuno cakkalakkhaṇe.

SC 58Ms 21Bu_58Msdiv 57Sāriputto mahāpañño,
koraṇḍasamasādiso;
Samādhijjhānakusalo,
vandate lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 59Ms 21Bu_59Msdiv 58Gajjitā kālameghova,
nīluppalasamasādiso;
Iddhibalena asamo,
moggallāno mahiddhiko.

SC 60Ms 21Bu_60Msdiv 59Mahākassapopi ca thero,
uttattakanakasannibho;
Dhutaguṇe agganikkhitto,
thomito satthuvaṇṇito.

SC 61Ms 21Bu_61Msdiv 60Dibbacakkhūnaṃ yo aggo,
anuruddho mahāgaṇī;
Ñātiseṭṭho bhagavato,
avidūreva tiṭṭhati.

SC 62Ms 21Bu_62Msdiv 61Āpattianāpattiyā,
satekicchāya kovido;
Vinaye agganikkhitto,
upāli satthuvaṇṇito.

SC 63Ms 21Bu_63Msdiv 62Sukhumanipuṇatthapaṭividdho,
Kathikānaṃ pavaro gaṇī;
Isi mantāniyā putto,
Puṇṇo nāmāti vissuto.

SC 64Ms 21Bu_64Msdiv 63Etesaṃ cittamaññāya,
opammakusalo muni;
Kaṅkhacchedo mahāvīro,
kathesi attano guṇaṃ.

SC 65PTS 2nd ed. 1.6 Ms 21Bu_65Msdiv 64“Cattāro te asaṅkheyyā,
koṭi yesaṃ na nāyati;
Sattakāyo ca ākāso,
cakkavāḷā canantakā;
Buddhañāṇaṃ appameyyaṃ,
na sakkā ete vijānituṃ.

SC 66Ms 21Bu_66Msdiv 65Kimetaṃ acchariyaṃ loke,
yaṃ me iddhivikubbanaṃ;
Aññe bahū acchariyā,
abbhutā lomahaṃsanā.

SC 67Ms 21Bu_67Msdiv 66Yadāhaṃ tusite kāye,
santusito nāmahaṃ tadā;
Dasasahassī samāgamma,
yācanti pañjalī mamaṃ.

SC 68Ms 21Bu_68Msdiv 67‘Kālo kho te mahāvīra,
uppajja mātukucchiyaṃ;
Sadevakaṃ tārayanto,
bujjhassu amataṃ padaṃ’.

SC 69Ms 21Bu_69Msdiv 68Tusitā kāyā cavitvāna,
yadā okkami kucchiyaṃ;
Dasasahassīlokadhātu,
kampittha dharaṇī tadā.

SC 70Ms 21Bu_70Msdiv 69Yadāhaṃ mātukucchito,
sampajānova nikkhamiṃ;
Sādhukāraṃ pavattenti,
dasasahassī pakampatha.

SC 71Ms 21Bu_71Msdiv 70Okkantiṃ me samo natthi,
jātito abhinikkhame;
Sambodhiyaṃ ahaṃ seṭṭho,
dhammacakkappavattane.

SC 72Ms 21Bu_72Msdiv 71Aho acchariyaṃ loke,
buddhānaṃ guṇamahantatā;
Dasasahassīlokadhātu,
chappakāraṃ pakampatha;
Obhāso ca mahā āsi,
accheraṃ lomahaṃsanaṃ”.

SC 73Ms 21Bu_73Msdiv 72Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Sadevakaṃ dassayanto,
iddhiyā caṅkamī jino.

SC 74Ms 21Bu_74Msdiv 73Caṅkame caṅkamantova,
kathesi lokanāyako;
Antarā na nivatteti,
catuhatthe caṅkame yathā.

SC 75PTS 1st ed. 1.6 Ms 21Bu_75Msdiv 74Sāriputto mahāpañño,
samādhijjhānakovido;
Paññāya pāramippatto,
pucchati lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 76Ms 21Bu_76Msdiv 75“Kīdiso te mahāvīra,
abhinīhāro naruttama;
Kamhi kāle tayā dhīra,
patthitā bodhimuttamā.

SC 77Ms 21Bu_77Msdiv 76Dānaṃ sīlañca nekkhammaṃ,
paññāvīriyañca kīdisaṃ;
Khantisaccamadhiṭṭhānaṃ,
mettupekkhā ca kīdisā.

SC 78Ms 21Bu_78Msdiv 77Dasa pāramī tayā dhīra,
kīdisī lokanāyaka;
Kathaṃ upapāramī puṇṇā,
paramatthapāramī kathaṃ”.

SC 79Ms 21Bu_79Msdiv 78Tassa puṭṭho viyākāsi,
karavīkamadhuragiro;
Nibbāpayanto hadayaṃ,
hāsayanto sadevakaṃ.

SC 80PTS 2nd ed. 1.7 Ms 21Bu_80Msdiv 79Atītabuddhānaṃ jinānaṃ desitaṃ,
Nikīlitaṃ buddhaparamparāgataṃ;
Pubbenivāsānugatāya buddhiyā,
Pakāsayī lokahitaṃ sadevake.

SC 81Ms 21Bu_81Msdiv 80“Pītipāmojjajananaṃ,
sokasallavinodanaṃ;
Sabbasampattipaṭilābhaṃ,
cittīkatvā suṇātha me.

SC 82Ms 21Bu_82Msdiv 81Madanimmadanaṃ sokanudaṃ,
Saṃsāraparimocanaṃ;
Sabbadukkhakkhayaṃ maggaṃ,
Sakkaccaṃ paṭipajjathā”ti.

SC 83Ms 21Bu_83Ratanacaṅkamanakaṇḍo niṭṭhito.
