7. Revatabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.35 Ms 21Bu_411Msdiv 1Sumanassa aparena,
revato nāma nāyako;
Anūpamo asadiso,
atulo uttamo jino.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_412Msdiv 2Sopi dhammaṃ pakāsesi,
brahmunā abhiyācito;
Khandhadhātuvavatthānaṃ,
appavattaṃ bhavābhave.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_413Msdiv 3Tassābhisamayā tīṇi,
ahesuṃ dhammadesane;
Gaṇanāya na vattabbo,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_414Msdiv 4Yadā arindamaṃ rājaṃ,
vinesi revato muni;
Tadā koṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_415Msdiv 5Sattāhaṃ paṭisallānā,
vuṭṭhahitvā narāsabho;
Koṭisataṃ naramarūnaṃ,
vinesi uttame phale.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_416Msdiv 6Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
revatassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
suvimuttāna tādinaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_417Msdiv 7Atikkantā gaṇanapathaṃ,
paṭhamaṃ ye samāgatā;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_418Msdiv 8Yopi paññāya asamo,
tassa cakkānuvattako;
So tadā byādhito āsi,
patto jīvitasaṃsayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_419Msdiv 9Tassa gilānapucchāya,
ye tadā upagatā munī;
Koṭisahassā arahanto,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_420Msdiv 10Ahaṃ tena samayena,
atidevo nāma brāhmaṇo;
Upagantvā revataṃ buddhaṃ,
saraṇaṃ tassa gañchahaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_421Msdiv 11Tassa sīlaṃ samādhiñca,
paññāguṇamanuttamaṃ;
Thomayitvā yathāthāmaṃ,
uttarīyamadāsahaṃ.

SC 12PTS 1st ed. .26 Ms 21Bu_422Msdiv 12Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
revato lokanāyako;
“Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_423Msdiv 13‘Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.36 Ms 21Bu_424Msdiv 14Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_425Msdiv 15Tadāpi taṃ buddhadhammaṃ,
saritvā anubrūhayiṃ;
Āharissāmi taṃ dhammaṃ,
yaṃ mayhaṃ abhipatthitaṃ.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_426Msdiv 16Nagaraṃ sudhaññavatī nāma,
Vipulo nāma khattiyo;
Vipulā nāma janikā,
Revatassa mahesino.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_427Msdiv 17Cha ca vassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Sudassano ratanagghi,
āveḷo ca vibhūsito;
Puññakammābhinibbattā,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_428Msdiv 18Tettiṃsa ca sahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sudassanā nāma nārī,
varuṇo nāma atrajo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_429Msdiv 19Nimitte caturo disvā,
rathayānena nikkhami;
Anūnasattamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_430Msdiv 20Brahmunā yācito santo,
revato lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
varuṇārāme sirīghare.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_431Msdiv 21Varuṇo brahmadevo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sambhavo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
revatassa mahesino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_432Msdiv 22Bhaddā ceva subhaddā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Sopi buddho asamasamo,
nāgamūle abujjhatha.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_433Msdiv 23Padumo kuñjaro ceva,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Sirīmā ceva yasavatī,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_434Msdiv 24Uccattanena so buddho,
asītihatthamuggato;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
indaketuva uggato.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_435Msdiv 25Tassa sarīre nibbattā,
pabhāmālā anuttarā;
Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
samantā pharati yojanaṃ.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_436Msdiv 26Saṭṭhivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_437Msdiv 27Dassayitvā buddhabalaṃ,
amataṃ loke pakāsayaṃ;
Nibbāyi anupādāno,
yathaggupādānasaṅkhayā.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_438Msdiv 28So ca kāyo ratananibho,
so ca dhammo asādiso;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.37 Ms 21Bu_439Msdiv 29Revato yasadharo buddho,
Nibbuto so mahāpure;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (432)

SC 30Ms 21Bu_440Revatassa bhagavato vaṃso pañcamo.
