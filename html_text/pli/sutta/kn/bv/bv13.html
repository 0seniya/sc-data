13. Sumedhabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .37 PTS 2nd ed. 1.53 Ms 21Bu_598Msdiv 1“Padumuttarassa aparena,
Sumedho nāma nāyako;
Durāsado uggatejo,
Sabbalokuttamo muni.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_599Msdiv 2Pasannanetto sumukho,
brahā uju patāpavā;
Hitesī sabbasattānaṃ,
bahū mocesi bandhanā.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_600Msdiv 3Yadā buddho pāpuṇitvā,
kevalaṃ bodhimuttamaṃ;
Sudassanamhi nagare,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_601Msdiv 4Tassāpi abhisamayā tīṇi,
Ahesuṃ dhammadesane;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_602Msdiv 5Punāparaṃ kumbhakaṇṇaṃ,
yakkhaṃ so damayī jino;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_603Msdiv 6Punāparaṃ amitayaso,
catusaccaṃ pakāsayi;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_604Msdiv 7Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
sumedhassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_605Msdiv 8Sudassanaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
upagañchi jino yadā;
Tadā khīṇāsavā bhikkhū,
samiṃsu satakoṭiyo.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_606Msdiv 9Punāparaṃ devakūṭe,
bhikkhūnaṃ kathinatthate;
Tadā navutikoṭīnaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_607Msdiv 10Punāparaṃ dasabalo,
yadā carati cārikaṃ;
Tadā asītikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_608Msdiv 11Ahaṃ tena samayena,
uttaro nāma māṇavo;
Asītikoṭiyo mayhaṃ,
ghare sannicitaṃ dhanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_609Msdiv 12Kevalaṃ sabbaṃ datvāna,
sasaṃghe lokanāyake;
Saraṇaṃ tassupagañchiṃ,
pabbajjañcābhirocayiṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_610Msdiv 13Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
karonto anumodanaṃ;
‘Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_611Msdiv 14Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.54 Ms 21Bu_612Msdiv 15Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_613Msdiv 16Suttantaṃ vinayañcāpi,
navaṅgaṃ satthusāsanaṃ;
Sabbaṃ pariyāpuṇitvāna,
sobhayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .38 Ms 21Bu_614Msdiv 17Tatthappamatto viharanto,
Nisajjaṭṭhānacaṅkame;
Abhiññāsu pāramiṃ gantvā,
Brahmalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_615Msdiv 18Sudassanaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
sudatto nāma khattiyo;
Sudattā nāma janikā,
sumedhassa mahesino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_616Msdiv 19Navavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Sucandakañcanasirivaḍḍhā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_617Msdiv 20Tisoḷasasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sumanā nāma sā nārī,
punabbasu nāma atrajo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_618Msdiv 21Nimitte caturo disvā,
hatthiyānena nikkhami;
Anūnakaṃ aḍḍhamāsaṃ,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_619Msdiv 22Brahmunā yācito santo,
sumedho lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
sudassanuyyānamuttame.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_620Msdiv 23Saraṇo sabbakāmo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sāgaro nāmupaṭṭhāko,
sumedhassa mahesino.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_621Msdiv 24Rāmā ceva surāmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
mahānīpoti vuccati.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_622Msdiv 25Uruvelā yasavā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Yasodharā sirimā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_623Msdiv 26Aṭṭhāsītiratanāni,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
cando tāragaṇe yathā.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_624Msdiv 27Cakkavattimaṇī nāma,
yathā tapati yojanaṃ;
Tatheva tassa ratanaṃ,
samantā pharati yojanaṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_625Msdiv 28Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_626Msdiv 29Tevijjachaḷabhiññehi,
balappattehi tādihi;
Samākulamidaṃ āsi,
arahantehi sādhuhi.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.55 Ms 21Bu_627Msdiv 30Tepi sabbe amitayasā,
vippamuttā nirūpadhī;
Ñāṇālokaṃ dassayitvā,
nibbutā te mahāyasā.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_628Msdiv 31Sumedho jinavaro buddho,
Medhārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (615)

SC 32Ms 21Bu_629Sumedhassa bhagavato vaṃso ekādasamo.
