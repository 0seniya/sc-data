15 Piyadassībuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.59 Ms 21Bu_667Msdiv 1“Sujātassa aparena,
sayambhū lokanāyako;
Durāsado asamasamo,
piyadassī mahāyaso.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_668Msdiv 2Sopi buddho amitayaso,
ādiccova virocati;
Sabbaṃ tamaṃ nihantvāna,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_669Msdiv 3Tassāpi atulatejassa,
Ahesuṃ abhisamayā tayo;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_670Msdiv 4Sudassano devarājā,
micchādiṭṭhimarocayi;
Tassa diṭṭhiṃ vinodento,
satthā dhammamadesayi.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_671Msdiv 5Janasannipāto atulo,
mahāsannipatī tadā;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_672Msdiv 6Yadā doṇamukhaṃ hatthiṃ,
vinesi narasārathi;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_673Msdiv 7Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
tassāpi piyadassino;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_674Msdiv 8Tato paraṃ navutikoṭī,
samiṃsu ekato munī;
Tatiye sannipātamhi,
asītikoṭiyo ahū.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_675Msdiv 9Ahaṃ tena samayena,
kassapo nāma brāhmaṇo;
Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_676Msdiv 10Tassa dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,
pasādaṃ janayiṃ ahaṃ;
Koṭisatasahassehi,
saṃghārāmaṃ amāpayiṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_677Msdiv 11Tassa datvāna ārāmaṃ,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
Saraṇe pañca sīle ca,
daḷhaṃ katvā samādiyiṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_678Msdiv 12Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
saṃghamajjhe nisīdiya;
‘Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 13PTS 1st ed. .42 Ms 21Bu_679Msdiv 13Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_680Msdiv 14Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.60 Ms 21Bu_681Msdiv 15Sudhaññaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
sudatto nāma khattiyo;
Candā nāmāsi janikā,
piyadassissa satthuno.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_682Msdiv 16Navavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Sunimmalavimalagiriguhā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_683Msdiv 17Tettiṃsasahassāni ca,
Nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vimalā nāma nārī ca,
Kañcanāveḷo nāma atrajo.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_684Msdiv 18Nimitte caturo disvā,
rathayānena nikkhami;
Chamāsaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acarī purisuttamo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_685Msdiv 19Brahmunā yācito santo,
piyadassī mahāmuni;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
usabhuyyāne manorame.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_686Msdiv 20Pālito sabbadassī ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sobhito nāmupaṭṭhāko,
piyadassissa satthuno.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_687Msdiv 21Sujātā dhammadinnā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
kakudhoti pavuccati.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_688Msdiv 22Sandhako dhammako ceva,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Visākhā dhammadinnā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_689Msdiv 23Sopi buddho amitayaso,
dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇo;
Asītihatthamubbedho,
sālarājāva dissati.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_690Msdiv 24Aggicandasūriyānaṃ,
natthi tādisikā pabhā;
Yathā ahu pabhā tassa,
asamassa mahesino.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_691Msdiv 25Tassāpi devadevassa,
āyu tāvatakaṃ ahu;
Navutivassasahassāni,
loke aṭṭhāsi cakkhumā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_692Msdiv 26Sopi buddho asamasamo,
Yugānipi tāni atuliyāni;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_693Msdiv 27Piyadassī munivaro,
Assatthārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa jinathūpo,
Tīṇiyojanamuggato”ti. (678)

SC 28Ms 21Bu_694Piyadassissa bhagavato vaṃso terasamo.
