20. Phussabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.74 Ms 21Bu_802Msdiv 1“Tattheva maṇḍakappamhi,
ahu satthā anuttaro;
Anūpamo asamasamo,
phusso lokagganāyako.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_803Msdiv 2Sopi sabbaṃ tamaṃ hantvā,
vijaṭetvā mahājaṭaṃ;
Sadevakaṃ tappayanto,
abhivassi amatambunā.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_804Msdiv 3Dhammacakkaṃ pavattente,
phusse nakkhattamaṅgale;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_805Msdiv 4Navutisatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu;
Asītisatasahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_806Msdiv 5Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
phussassāpi mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_807Msdiv 6Saṭṭhisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo;
Paññāsasatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_808Msdiv 7Cattārīsasatasahassānaṃ,
Tatiyo āsi samāgamo;
Anupādā vimuttānaṃ,
Vocchinnapaṭisandhinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_809Msdiv 8Ahaṃ tena samayena,
vijitāvī nāma khattiyo;
Chaḍḍayitvā mahārajjaṃ,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_810Msdiv 9Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
phusso lokagganāyako;
‘Dvenavute ito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_811Msdiv 10Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_812Msdiv 11Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_813Msdiv 12Suttantaṃ vinayañcāpi,
navaṅgaṃ satthusāsanaṃ;
Sabbaṃ pariyāpuṇitvā,
sobhayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 13PTS 1st ed. .51 Ms 21Bu_814Msdiv 13Tatthappamatto viharanto,
brahmaṃ bhāvetva bhāvanaṃ;
Abhiññāsu pāramiṃ gantvā,
brahmalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_815Msdiv 14Kāsikaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
jayaseno nāma khattiyo;
Sirimā nāma janikā,
phussassāpi mahesino.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.75 Ms 21Bu_816Msdiv 15Navavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Garuḷapakkha haṃsa suvaṇṇabhārā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_817Msdiv 16Tiṃsaitthisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Kisāgotamī nāma nārī,
anūpamo nāma atrajo.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_818Msdiv 17Nimitte caturo disvā,
hatthiyānena nikkhami;
Chamāsaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acarī purisuttamo.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_819Msdiv 18Brahmunā yācito santo,
phusso lokagganāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
migadāye naruttamo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_820Msdiv 19Surakkhito dhammaseno,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sabhiyo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
phussassāpi mahesino.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_821Msdiv 20Cālā ca upacālā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
āmaṇḍoti pavuccati.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_822Msdiv 21Dhanañcayo visākho ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Padumā ceva nāgā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_823Msdiv 22Aṭṭhapaṇṇāsaratanaṃ,
sopi accuggato muni;
Sobhate sataraṃsīva,
uḷurājāva pūrito.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_824Msdiv 23Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_825Msdiv 24Ovaditvā bahū satte,
santāretvā bahū jane;
Sopi satthā atulayaso,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_826Msdiv 25Phusso jinavaro satthā,
Senārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (806)

SC 26Ms 21Bu_827Phussassa bhagavato vaṃso aṭṭhārasamo.
