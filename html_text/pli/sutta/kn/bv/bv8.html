8. Sobhitabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .27 PTS 2nd ed. 1.38 Ms 21Bu_441Msdiv 1“Revatassa aparena,
sobhito nāma nāyako;
Samāhito santacitto,
asamo appaṭipuggalo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_442Msdiv 2So jino sakagehamhi,
mānasaṃ vinivattayi;
Patvāna kevalaṃ bodhiṃ,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_443Msdiv 3Yāva heṭṭhā avīcito,
bhavaggā cāpi uddhato;
Etthantare ekaparisā,
ahosi dhammadesane.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_444Msdiv 4Tāya parisāya sambuddho,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi;
Gaṇanāya na vattabbo,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_445Msdiv 5Tato parampi desente,
marūnañca samāgame;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_446Msdiv 6Punāparaṃ rājaputto,
jayaseno nāma khattiyo;
Ārāmaṃ ropayitvāna,
buddhe niyyādayī tadā.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_447Msdiv 7Tassa yāgaṃ pakittento,
dhammaṃ desesi cakkhumā;
Tadā koṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_448Msdiv 8Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
sobhitassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_449Msdiv 9Uggato nāma so rājā,
dānaṃ deti naruttame;
Tamhi dāne samāgañchuṃ,
arahantā satakoṭiyo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_450Msdiv 10Punāparaṃ puragaṇo,
deti dānaṃ naruttame;
Tadā navutikoṭīnaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_451Msdiv 11Devaloke vasitvāna,
yadā orohatī jino;
Tadā asītikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_452Msdiv 12Ahaṃ tena samayena,
sujāto nāma brāhmaṇo;
Tadā sasāvakaṃ buddhaṃ,
annapānena tappayiṃ.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_453Msdiv 13Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
sobhito lokanāyako;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_454Msdiv 14Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.39 Ms 21Bu_455Msdiv 15Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
Tamevatthamanuppattiyā,
uggaṃ dhitimakāsahaṃ.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_456Msdiv 16Sudhammaṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
sudhammo nāma khattiyo;
Sudhammā nāma janikā,
sobhitassa mahesino.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .28 Ms 21Bu_457Msdiv 17Navavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Kumudo nāḷino padumo,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_458Msdiv 18Sattatiṃsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Maṇilā nāma sā nārī,
sīho nāmāsi atrajo.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_459Msdiv 19Nimitte caturo disvā,
pāsādenābhinikkhami;
Sattāhaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
caritvā purisuttamo.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_460Msdiv 20Brahmunā yācito santo,
sobhito lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
sudhammuyyānamuttame.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_461Msdiv 21Asamo ca sunetto ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Anomo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
sobhitassa mahesino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_462Msdiv 22Nakulā ca sujātā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bujjhamāno ca so buddho,
nāgamūle abujjhatha.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_463Msdiv 23Rammo ceva sudatto ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Nakulā ceva cittā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_464Msdiv 24Aṭṭhapaṇṇāsaratanaṃ,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Obhāseti disā sabbā,
sataraṃsīva uggato.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_465Msdiv 25Yathā suphullaṃ pavanaṃ,
nānāgandhehi dhūpitaṃ;
Tatheva tassa pāvacanaṃ,
sīlagandhehi dhūpitaṃ.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_466Msdiv 26Yathāpi sāgaro nāma,
dassanena atappiyo;
Tatheva tassa pāvacanaṃ,
savanena atappiyaṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_467Msdiv 27Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_468Msdiv 28Ovādaṃ anusiṭṭhiñca,
datvāna sesake jane;
Hutāsanova tāpetvā,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_469Msdiv 29So ca buddho asamasamo,
tepi sāvakā balappattā;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.40 Ms 21Bu_470Msdiv 30Sobhito varasambuddho,
Sīhārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (462)

SC 31Ms 21Bu_471Sobhitassa bhagavato vaṃso chaṭṭho.
