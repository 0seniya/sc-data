25. Koṇāgamanabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .60 PTS 2nd ed. 1.89 Ms 21Bu_954SC 1PTS 1st ed. .60 PTS 2nd ed. 1.89 Ms 21Bu_954Msdiv 1“Kakusandhassa aparena,
sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Koṇāgamano nāma jino,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_955SC 2Ms 21Bu_955Msdiv 2Dasa dhamme pūrayitvāna,
kantāraṃ samatikkami;
Pavāhiya malaṃ sabbaṃ,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_956SC 3Ms 21Bu_956Msdiv 3Dhammacakkaṃ pavattente,
koṇāgamananāyake;
Tiṃsakoṭisahassānaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_957SC 4Ms 21Bu_957Msdiv 4Pāṭihīraṃ karonte ca,
paravādappamaddane;
Vīsatikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_958SC 5Ms 21Bu_958Msdiv 5Tato vikubbanaṃ katvā,
jino devapuraṃ gato;
Vasate tattha sambuddho,
silāya paṇḍukambale.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_959SC 6Ms 21Bu_959Msdiv 6Pakaraṇe satta desento,
vassaṃ vasati so muni;
Dasakoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_960SC 7Ms 21Bu_960Msdiv 7Tassāpi devadevassa,
eko āsi samāgamo;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_961SC 8Ms 21Bu_961Msdiv 8Tiṃsabhikkhusahassānaṃ,
tadā āsi samāgamo;
Oghānamatikkantānaṃ,
bhijjitānañca maccuyā.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_962SC 9Ms 21Bu_962Msdiv 9Ahaṃ tena samayena,
pabbato nāma khattiyo;
Mittāmaccehi sampanno,
anantabalavāhano.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_963SC 10Ms 21Bu_963Msdiv 10Sambuddhadassanaṃ gantvā,
sutvā dhammamanuttaraṃ;
Nimantetvā sajinasaṃghaṃ,
dānaṃ datvā yadicchakaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_964SC 11Ms 21Bu_964Msdiv 11Pattuṇṇaṃ cīnapaṭṭañca,
koseyyaṃ kambalampi ca;
Sovaṇṇapādukañceva,
adāsiṃ satthusāvake.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_965SC 12Ms 21Bu_965Msdiv 12Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
saṃghamajjhe nisīdiya;
‘Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_966SC 13Ms 21Bu_966Msdiv 13Ahu kapilavhayā rammā,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.90 Ms 21Bu_967SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.90 Ms 21Bu_967Msdiv 14Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_968SC 15Ms 21Bu_968Msdiv 15Sabbaññutaṃ gavesanto,
dānaṃ datvā naruttame;
Ohāyāhaṃ mahārajjaṃ,
pabbajiṃ jinasantike.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_969SC 16Ms 21Bu_969Msdiv 16Nagaraṃ sobhavatī nāma,
sobho nāmāsi khattiyo;
Vasate tattha nagare,
sambuddhassa mahākulaṃ.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .61 Ms 21Bu_970SC 17PTS 1st ed. .61 Ms 21Bu_970Msdiv 17Brāhmaṇo yaññadatto ca,
āsi buddhassa so pitā;
Uttarā nāma janikā,
koṇāgamanassa satthuno.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_971SC 18Ms 21Bu_971Msdiv 18Tīṇi vassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Tusitasantusitasantuṭṭhā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_972SC 19Ms 21Bu_972Msdiv 19Anūnasoḷasasahassāni,
Nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Rucigattā nāma nārī,
Satthavāho nāma atrajo.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_973SC 20Ms 21Bu_973Msdiv 20Nimitte caturo disvā,
hatthiyānena nikkhami;
Chamāsaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acarī purisuttamo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_974SC 21Ms 21Bu_974Msdiv 21Brahmunā yācito santo,
koṇāgamananāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
migadāye naruttamo.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_975SC 22Ms 21Bu_975Msdiv 22Bhiyyaso uttaro nāma,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sotthijo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
koṇāgamanassa satthuno.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_976SC 23Ms 21Bu_976Msdiv 23Samuddā uttarā ceva,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
udumbaroti pavuccati.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_977SC 24Ms 21Bu_977Msdiv 24Uggo ca somadevo ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Sīvalā ceva sāmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_978SC 25Ms 21Bu_978Msdiv 25Uccattanena so buddho,
tiṃsahatthasamuggato;
Ukkāmukhe yathā kambu,
evaṃ raṃsīhi maṇḍito.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_979SC 26Ms 21Bu_979Msdiv 26Tiṃsavassasahassāni,
āyu buddhassa tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_980SC 27Ms 21Bu_980Msdiv 27Dhammacetiṃ samussetvā,
dhammadussavibhūsitaṃ;
Dhammapupphaguḷaṃ katvā,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_981SC 28Ms 21Bu_981Msdiv 28Mahāvilāso tassa jano,
siridhammappakāsano;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.91 Ms 21Bu_982SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.91 Ms 21Bu_982Msdiv 29Koṇāgamano sambuddho,
Pabbatārāmamhi nibbuto;
Dhātuvitthārikaṃ āsi,
Tesu tesu padesato”ti. (957)

SC 30Ms 21Bu_983SC 30Ms 21Bu_983Koṇāgamanassa bhagavato vaṃso tevīsatimo.
