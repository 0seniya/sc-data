5. Maṅgalabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.29 Ms 21Bu_343SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.29 Ms 21Bu_343Msdiv 1“Koṇḍaññassa aparena,
maṅgalo nāma nāyako;
Tamaṃ loke nihantvāna,
dhammokkamabhidhārayi.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_344SC 2Ms 21Bu_344Msdiv 2Atulāsi pabhā tassa,
jinehaññehi uttariṃ;
Candasūriyapabhaṃ hantvā,
dasasahassī virocati.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_345SC 3Ms 21Bu_345Msdiv 3Sopi buddho pakāsesi,
caturo saccavaruttame;
Te te saccarasaṃ pītvā,
vinodenti mahātamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_346SC 4Ms 21Bu_346Msdiv 4Patvāna bodhimatulaṃ,
paṭhame dhammadesane;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
dhammābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_347SC 5Ms 21Bu_347Msdiv 5Surindadevabhavane,
buddho dhammamadesayi;
Tadā koṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyo samayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_348SC 6Ms 21Bu_348Msdiv 6Yadā sunando cakkavattī,
sambuddhaṃ upasaṅkami;
Tadā āhani sambuddho,
dhammabheriṃ varuttamaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_349SC 7Ms 21Bu_349Msdiv 7Sunandassānucarā janatā,
Tadāsuṃ navutikoṭiyo;
Sabbepi te niravasesā,
Ahesuṃ ehi bhikkhukā.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_350SC 8Ms 21Bu_350Msdiv 8Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
maṅgalassa mahesino;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_351SC 9Ms 21Bu_351Msdiv 9Dutiyo koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Tatiyo navutikoṭinaṃ;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
Tadā āsi samāgamo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_352SC 10Ms 21Bu_352Msdiv 10Ahaṃ tena samayena,
surucī nāma brāhmaṇo;
Ajjhāyako mantadharo,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū.

SC 11PTS 1st ed. .22 Ms 21Bu_353SC 11PTS 1st ed. .22 Ms 21Bu_353Msdiv 11Tamahaṃ upasaṅkamma,
Saraṇaṃ gantvāna satthuno;
Sambuddhappamukhaṃ saṃghaṃ,
Gandhamālena pūjayiṃ;
Pūjetvā gandhamālena,
Gavapānena tappayiṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_354SC 12Ms 21Bu_354Msdiv 12Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
maṅgalo dvipaduttamo;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_355SC 13Ms 21Bu_355Msdiv 13Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.30 Ms 21Bu_356SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.30 Ms 21Bu_356Msdiv 14Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_357SC 15Ms 21Bu_357Msdiv 15Tadā pītimanubrūhanto,
sambodhivarapattiyā;
Buddhe datvāna maṃ gehaṃ,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_358SC 16Ms 21Bu_358Msdiv 16Suttantaṃ vinayañcāpi,
navaṅgaṃ satthusāsanaṃ;
Sabbaṃ pariyāpuṇitvā,
sobhayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_359SC 17Ms 21Bu_359Msdiv 17Tatthappamatto viharanto,
brahmaṃ bhāvetva bhāvanaṃ;
Abhiññāpāramiṃ gantvā,
brahmalokamagacchahaṃ.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_360SC 18Ms 21Bu_360Msdiv 18Uttaraṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
uttaro nāma khattiyo;
Uttarā nāma janikā,
maṅgalassa mahesino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_361SC 19Ms 21Bu_361Msdiv 19Navavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Yasavā sucimā sirīmā,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_362SC 20Ms 21Bu_362Msdiv 20Samatiṃsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Yasavatī nāma nārī,
sīvalo nāma atrajo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_363SC 21Ms 21Bu_363Msdiv 21Nimitte caturo disvā,
assayānena nikkhami;
Anūnaaṭṭhamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_364SC 22Ms 21Bu_364Msdiv 22Brahmunā yācito santo,
maṅgalo nāma nāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
vane sirīvaruttame.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_365SC 23Ms 21Bu_365Msdiv 23Sudevo dhammaseno ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Pālito nāmupaṭṭhāko,
maṅgalassa mahesino.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_366SC 24Ms 21Bu_366Msdiv 24Sīvalā ca asokā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
nāgarukkhoti vuccati.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_367SC 25Ms 21Bu_367Msdiv 25Nando ceva visākho ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Anulā ceva sutanā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_368SC 26Ms 21Bu_368Msdiv 26Aṭṭhāsīti ratanāni,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Tato niddhāvatī raṃsī,
anekasatasahassiyo.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_369SC 27Ms 21Bu_369Msdiv 27Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_370SC 28Ms 21Bu_370Msdiv 28Yathāpi sāgare ūmī,
na sakkā tā gaṇetuye;
Tatheva sāvakā tassa,
na sakkā te gaṇetuye.

SC 29PTS 1st ed. .23 PTS 2nd ed. 1.31 Ms 21Bu_371SC 29PTS 1st ed. .23 PTS 2nd ed. 1.31 Ms 21Bu_371Msdiv 29Yāva aṭṭhāsi sambuddho,
maṅgalo lokanāyako;
Na tassa sāsane atthi,
sakilesamaraṇaṃ tadā.

SC 30Ms 21Bu_372SC 30Ms 21Bu_372Msdiv 30Dhammokkaṃ dhārayitvāna,
santāretvā mahājanaṃ;
Jalitvā dhūmaketūva,
nibbuto so mahāyaso.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_373SC 31Ms 21Bu_373Msdiv 31Saṅkhārānaṃ sabhāvatthaṃ,
dassayitvā sadevake;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
sūriyo atthaṅgato yathā.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_374SC 32Ms 21Bu_374Msdiv 32Uyyāne vassare nāma,
Buddho nibbāyi maṅgalo;
Tatthevassa jinathūpo,
Tiṃsayojanamuggato”ti. (369)

SC 33SC 33Ms 21Bu_375Maṅgalassa bhagavato vaṃso tatiyo.
