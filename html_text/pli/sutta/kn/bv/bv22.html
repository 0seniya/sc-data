dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

santacittāna tādinaṃ.

dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

toyamhi sampavaḍḍhitaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_875SC 10Ms 21Bu_875Msdiv 10Bahuṃ dussavaraṃ datvā,

sambuddhassa adāsahaṃ.

niccaṃ daḷhamupaṭṭhitaṃ.

bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;

dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_880SC 15Ms 21Bu_880Msdiv 15Nagaraṃ aruṇavatī nāma,

Sikhissāpi mahesino.

tayo pāsādamuttamā.

atulo nāma atrajo.

acarī purisuttamo.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_885SC 20Ms 21Bu_885Msdiv 20Abhibhū sambhavo ceva,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;

sikhissāpi mahesino.
