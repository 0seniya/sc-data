9 Anomadassībuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .29 PTS 2nd ed. 1.41 Ms 21Bu_472Msdiv 1“Sobhitassa aparena,
Sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Anomadassī amitayaso,
Tejassī duratikkamo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_473Msdiv 2So chetvā bandhanaṃ sabbaṃ,
viddhaṃsetvā tayo bhave;
Anivattigamanaṃ maggaṃ,
desesi devamānuse.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_474Msdiv 3Sāgarova asaṅkhobbho,
pabbatova durāsado;
Ākāsova ananto so,
sālarājāva phullito.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_475Msdiv 4Dassanenapi taṃ buddhaṃ,
tositā honti pāṇino;
Byāharantaṃ giraṃ sutvā,
amataṃ pāpuṇanti te.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_476Msdiv 5Dhammābhisamayo tassa,
Iddho phīto tadā ahu;
Koṭisatāni abhisamiṃsu,
Paṭhame dhammadesane.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_477Msdiv 6Tato paraṃ abhisamaye,
Vassante dhammavuṭṭhiyo;
Asītikoṭiyobhisamiṃsu,
Dutiye dhammadesane.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_478Msdiv 7Tatoparañhi vassante,
tappayante ca pāṇinaṃ;
Aṭṭhasattatikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_479Msdiv 8Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
tassāpi ca mahesino;
Abhiññābalappattānaṃ,
pupphitānaṃ vimuttiyā.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_480Msdiv 9Aṭṭhasatasahassānaṃ,
sannipāto tadā ahu;
Pahīnamadamohānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_481Msdiv 10Sattasatasahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo;
Anaṅgaṇānaṃ virajānaṃ,
upasantāna tādinaṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_482Msdiv 11Channaṃ satasahassānaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo;
Abhiññābalappattānaṃ,
nibbutānaṃ tapassinaṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_483Msdiv 12Ahaṃ tena samayena,
yakkho āsiṃ mahiddhiko;
Nekānaṃ yakkhakoṭīnaṃ,
vasavattimhi issaro.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_484Msdiv 13Tadāpi taṃ buddhavaraṃ,
upagantvā mahesinaṃ;
Annapānena tappesiṃ,
sasaṃghaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.42 Ms 21Bu_485Msdiv 14Sopi maṃ tadā byākāsi,
visuddhanayano muni;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_486Msdiv 15Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_487Msdiv 16Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
haṭṭho saṃviggamānaso;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .30 Ms 21Bu_488Msdiv 17Nagaraṃ candavatī nāma,
yasavā nāma khattiyo;
Mātā yasodharā nāma,
anomadassissa satthuno.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_489Msdiv 18Dasavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Sirī upasirī vaḍḍho,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_490Msdiv 19Tevīsatisahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Sirimā nāma sā nārī,
upavāṇo nāma atrajo.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_491Msdiv 20Nimitte caturo disvā,
sivikāyābhinikkhami;
Anūnadasamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_492Msdiv 21Brahmunā yācito santo,
anomadassī mahāmuni;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
uyyāne so sudassane.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_493Msdiv 22Nisabho ca anomo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Varuṇo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
anomadassissa satthuno.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_494Msdiv 23Sundarī ca sumanā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
ajjunoti pavuccati.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_495Msdiv 24Nandivaḍḍho sirivaḍḍho,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Uppalā ceva padumā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_496Msdiv 25Aṭṭhapaṇṇāsaratanaṃ,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Pabhā niddhāvatī tassa,
sataraṃsīva uggato.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_497Msdiv 26Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_498Msdiv 27Supupphitaṃ pāvacanaṃ,
arahantehi tādihi;
Vītarāgehi vimalehi,
sobhittha jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_499Msdiv 28So ca satthā amitayaso,
Yugāni tāni atuliyāni;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.43 Ms 21Bu_500Msdiv 29Anomadassī jino satthā,
Dhammārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa jinathūpo,
Ubbedho pañcavīsatī”ti. (491)

SC 30Ms 21Bu_501Anomadassissa bhagavato vaṃso sattamo.
