4. Koṇḍaññabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .19 PTS 2nd ed. 1.26 Ms 21Bu_304Msdiv 1“Dīpaṅkarassa aparena,
Koṇḍañño nāma nāyako;
Anantatejo amitayaso,
Appameyyo durāsado.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_305Msdiv 2Dharaṇūpamo khamanena,
sīlena sāgarūpamo;
Samādhinā merūpamo,
ñāṇena gaganūpamo.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_306Msdiv 3Indriyabalabojjhaṅga-
maggasaccappakāsanaṃ;
Pakāsesi sadā buddho,
hitāya sabbapāṇinaṃ.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_307Msdiv 4Dhammacakkaṃ pavattente,
koṇḍaññe lokanāyake;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_308Msdiv 5Tato parampi desente,
naramarūnaṃ samāgame;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_309Msdiv 6Titthiye abhimaddanto,
yadā dhammamadesayi;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_310Msdiv 7Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
koṇḍaññassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_311Msdiv 8Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo;
Dutiyo koṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyo navutikoṭinaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_312Msdiv 9Ahaṃ tena samayena,
vijitāvī nāma khattiyo;
Samuddaṃ antamantena,
issariyaṃ vattayāmahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_313Msdiv 10Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
vimalānaṃ mahesinaṃ;
Saha lokagganāthena,
paramannena tappayiṃ.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_314Msdiv 11Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
koṇḍañño lokanāyako;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
buddho loke bhavissati.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_315Msdiv 12Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
katvā dukkarakārikaṃ;
Assatthamūle sambuddho,
bujjhissati mahāyaso.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_316Msdiv 13Imassa janikā mātā,
māyā nāma bhavissati;
Pitā suddhodano nāma,
ayaṃ hessati gotamo.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_317Msdiv 14Kolito upatisso ca,
aggā hessanti sāvakā;
Ānando nāmupaṭṭhāko,
upaṭṭhissatimaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.27 Ms 21Bu_318Msdiv 15Khemā uppalavaṇṇā ca,
aggā hessanti sāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
assatthoti pavuccati.

SC 16PTS 1st ed. .20 Ms 21Bu_319Msdiv 16Citto ca hatthāḷavako,
aggā hessantupaṭṭhakā;
Nandamātā ca uttarā,
aggā hessantupaṭṭhikā;
Āyu vassasataṃ tassa,
gotamassa yasassino’.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_320Msdiv 17Idaṃ sutvāna vacanaṃ,
asamassa mahesino;
Āmoditā naramarū,
‘buddhabījaṃ kira ayaṃ’.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_321Msdiv 18Ukkuṭṭhisaddā vattanti,
apphoṭenti hasanti ca;
Katañjalī namassanti,
dasasahassidevatā.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_322Msdiv 19‘Yadimassa lokanāthassa,
virajjhissāma sāsanaṃ;
Anāgatamhi addhāne,
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_323Msdiv 20Yathā manussā nadiṃ tarantā,
Paṭititthaṃ virajjhiya;
Heṭṭhātitthe gahetvāna,
Uttaranti mahānadiṃ.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_324Msdiv 21Evamevaṃ mayaṃ sabbe,
yadi muñcāmimaṃ jinaṃ;
Anāgatamhi addhāne,
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_325Msdiv 22Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Tameva atthaṃ sādhento,
mahārajjaṃ jine adaṃ;
Mahārajjaṃ daditvāna,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_326Msdiv 23Suttantaṃ vinayañcāpi,
navaṅgaṃ satthusāsanaṃ;
Sabbaṃ pariyāpuṇitvāna,
sobhayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_327Msdiv 24Tatthappamatto viharanto,
nisajjaṭṭhānacaṅkame;
Abhiññāpāramiṃ gantvā,
brahmalokamagañchahaṃ.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_328Msdiv 25Nagaraṃ rammavatī nāma,
sunando nāma khattiyo;
Sujātā nāma janikā,
koṇḍaññassa mahesino.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_329Msdiv 26Dasavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Suci suruci subho ca,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_330Msdiv 27Tīṇi satasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Rucidevī nāma nārī,
vijitaseno atrajo.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_331Msdiv 28Nimitte caturo disvā,
rathayānena nikkhami;
Anūnadasamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_332Msdiv 29Brahmunā yācito santo,
koṇḍañño dvipaduttamo;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
devānaṃ nagaruttame.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.28 Ms 21Bu_333Msdiv 30Bhaddo ceva subhaddo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Anuruddho nāmupaṭṭhāko,
koṇḍaññassa mahesino.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_334Msdiv 31Tissā ca upatissā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Sālakalyāṇiko bodhi,
koṇḍaññassa mahesino.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_335Msdiv 32Soṇo ca upasoṇo ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Nandā ceva sirīmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 33Ms 21Bu_336Msdiv 33So aṭṭhāsīti hatthāni,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Sobhate uḷurājāva,
sūriyo majjhanhike yathā.

SC 34Ms 21Bu_337Msdiv 34Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 35PTS 1st ed. .21 Ms 21Bu_338Msdiv 35Khīṇāsavehi vimalehi,
vicittā āsi medanī;
Yathā gaganamuḷūhi,
evaṃ so upasobhatha.

SC 36Ms 21Bu_339Msdiv 36Tepi nāgā appameyyā,
asaṅkhobbhā durāsadā;
Vijjupātaṃva dassetvā,
nibbutā te mahāyasā.

SC 37Ms 21Bu_340Msdiv 37Sā ca atuliyā jinassa iddhi,
Ñāṇaparibhāvito ca samādhi;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 38Ms 21Bu_341Msdiv 38Koṇḍañño pavaro buddho,
Candārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tattheva cetiyo citto,
Satta yojanamussito”ti. (337)

SC 39Ms 21Bu_342Koṇḍaññassa bhagavato vaṃso dutiyo.
