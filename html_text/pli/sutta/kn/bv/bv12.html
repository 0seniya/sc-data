12. Padumuttarabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .35 PTS 2nd ed. 1.50 Ms 21Bu_566Msdiv 1“Nāradassa aparena,
sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Padumuttaro nāma jino,
akkhobbho sāgarūpamo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_567Msdiv 2Maṇḍakappova so āsi,
yamhi buddho ajāyatha;
Ussannakusalā janatā,
tamhi kappe ajāyatha.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_568Msdiv 3Padumuttarassa bhagavato,
Paṭhame dhammadesane;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Dhammābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_569Msdiv 4Tato parampi vassante,
Tappayante ca pāṇine;
Sattatiṃsasatasahassānaṃ,
Dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_570Msdiv 5Yamhi kāle mahāvīro,
ānandaṃ upasaṅkami;
Pitusantikaṃ upagantvā,
āhanī amatadundubhiṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_571Msdiv 6Āhate amatabherimhi,
vassante dhammavuṭṭhiyā;
Paññāsasatasahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_572Msdiv 7Ovādako viññāpako,
tārako sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Desanākusalo buddho,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_573Msdiv 8Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
padumuttarassa satthuno;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_574Msdiv 9Yadā buddho asamasamo,
vasi vebhārapabbate;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_575Msdiv 10Puna cārikaṃ pakkante,
gāmanigamaraṭṭhato;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_576Msdiv 11Ahaṃ tena samayena,
Jaṭilo nāma raṭṭhiko;
Sambuddhappamukhaṃ saṃghaṃ,
Sabhattaṃ dussamadāsahaṃ.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_577Msdiv 12Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
saṃghamajjhe nisīdiya;
‘Satasahassito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_578Msdiv 13Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.51 Ms 21Bu_579Msdiv 14Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhahiṃ;
Akāsiṃ uggadaḷhaṃ dhitiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_580Msdiv 15Byāhatā titthiyā sabbe,
Vimanā dummanā tadā;
Na tesaṃ keci paricaranti,
Raṭṭhato nicchubhanti te.

SC 16PTS 1st ed. .36 Ms 21Bu_581Msdiv 16Sabbe tattha samāgantvā,
upagacchuṃ buddhasantike;
Tuvaṃ nātho mahāvīra,
saraṇaṃ hohi cakkhuma.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_582Msdiv 17Anukampako kāruṇiko,
hitesī sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Sampatte titthiye sabbe,
pañcasīle patiṭṭhapi.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_583Msdiv 18Evaṃ nirākulaṃ āsi,
suññataṃ titthiyehi taṃ;
Vicittaṃ arahantehi,
vasībhūtehi tādihi.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_584Msdiv 19Nagaraṃ haṃsavatī nāma,
ānando nāma khattiyo;
Sujātā nāma janikā,
padumuttarassa satthuno.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_585Msdiv 20Dasavassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Naravāhano yaso vasavattī,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_586Msdiv 21Ticattārīsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vasudattā nāma nārī,
uttamo nāma atrajo.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_587Msdiv 22Nimitte caturo disvā,
pāsādenābhinikkhami;
Sattāhaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acarī purisuttamo.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_588Msdiv 23Brahmunā yācito santo,
padumuttaro vināyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
mithiluyyānamuttame.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_589Msdiv 24Devalo ca sujāto ca,
Ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Sumano nāmupaṭṭhāko,
Padumuttarassa mahesino.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_590Msdiv 25Amitā ca asamā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
salaloti pavuccati.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_591Msdiv 26Vitiṇṇo ceva tisso ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Haṭṭhā ceva vicittā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_592Msdiv 27Aṭṭhapaṇṇāsaratanaṃ,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇo.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_593Msdiv 28Kuṭṭā kavāṭā bhittī ca,
rukkhā nagasiluccayā;
Na tassāvaraṇaṃ atthi,
samantā dvādasayojane.

SC 29PTS 2nd ed. 1.52 Ms 21Bu_594Msdiv 29Vassasatasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 30Ms 21Bu_595Msdiv 30Santāretvā bahujanaṃ,
chinditvā sabbasaṃsayaṃ;
Jalitvā aggikkhandhova,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_596Msdiv 31Padumuttaro jino buddho,
Nandārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa thūpavaro,
Dvādasubbedhayojano”ti. (584)

SC 32Ms 21Bu_597Padumuttarassa bhagavato vaṃso dasamo.
