6. Sumanabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 2nd ed. 1.32 Ms 21Bu_376Msdiv 1“Maṅgalassa aparena,
sumano nāma nāyako;
Sabbadhammehi asamo,
sabbasattānamuttamo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_377Msdiv 2Tadā amatabheriṃ so,
āhanī mekhale pure;
Dhammasaṅkhasamāyuttaṃ,
navaṅgaṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_378Msdiv 3Nijjinitvā kilese so,
patvā sambodhimuttamaṃ;
Māpesi nagaraṃ satthā,
saddhammapuravaruttamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_379Msdiv 4Nirantaraṃ akuṭilaṃ,
ujuṃ vipulavitthataṃ;
Māpesi so mahāvīthiṃ,
satipaṭṭhānavaruttamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_380Msdiv 5Phale cattāri sāmaññe,
catasso paṭisambhidā;
Chaḷabhiññāṭṭhasamāpattī,
pasāresi tattha vīthiyaṃ.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_381Msdiv 6Ye appamattā akhilā,
hirivīriyehupāgatā;
Te te ime guṇavare,
ādiyanti yathā sukhaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_382Msdiv 7Evametena yogena,
uddharanto mahājanaṃ;
Bodhesi paṭhamaṃ satthā,
koṭisatasahassiyo.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_383Msdiv 8Yamhi kāle mahāvīro,
ovadī titthiye gaṇe;
Koṭisahassābhisamiṃsu,
dutiye dhammadesane.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_384Msdiv 9Yadā devā manussā ca,
samaggā ekamānasā;
Nirodhapañhaṃ pucchiṃsu,
saṃsayañcāpi mānasaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_385Msdiv 10Tadāpi dhammadesane,
nirodhaparidīpane;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_386Msdiv 11Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
sumanassa mahesino;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 12PTS 1st ed. .24 Ms 21Bu_387Msdiv 12Vassaṃvutthassa bhagavato,
abhighuṭṭhe pavāraṇe;
Koṭisatasahassehi,
pavāresi tathāgato.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_388Msdiv 13Tatoparaṃ sannipāte,
vimale kañcanapabbate;
Navutikoṭisahassānaṃ,
dutiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_389Msdiv 14Yadā sakko devarājā,
buddhadassanupāgami;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyo āsi samāgamo.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.33 Ms 21Bu_390Msdiv 15Ahaṃ tena samayena,
nāgarājā mahiddhiko;
Atulo nāma nāmena,
ussannakusalasañcayo.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_391Msdiv 16Tadāhaṃ nāgabhavanā,
nikkhamitvā sañātibhi;
Nāgānaṃ dibbaturiyehi,
sasaṃghaṃ jinamupaṭṭhahiṃ.

SC 17Ms 21Bu_392Msdiv 17Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
annapānena tappayiṃ;
Paccekadussayugaṃ datvā,
saraṇaṃ tamupāgamiṃ.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_393Msdiv 18Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
sumano lokanāyako;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_394Msdiv 19Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_395Msdiv 20Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_396Msdiv 21Nagaraṃ mekhalaṃ nāma,
sudatto nāma khattiyo;
Sirimā nāma janikā,
sumanassa mahesino.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_397Msdiv 22Navavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Cando sucando vaṭaṃso ca,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_398Msdiv 23Tesaṭṭhisatasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vaṭaṃsikā nāma nārī,
anūpamo nāma atrajo.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_399Msdiv 24Nimitte caturo disvā,
hatthiyānena nikkhami;
Anūnadasamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_400Msdiv 25Brahmunā yācito santo,
sumano lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
mekhale puramuttame.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_401Msdiv 26Saraṇo bhāvitatto ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Udeno nāmupaṭṭhāko,
sumanassa mahesino.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_402Msdiv 27Soṇā ca upasoṇā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Sopi buddho amitayaso,
nāgamūle abujjhatha.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_403Msdiv 28Varuṇo ceva saraṇo ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Cālā ca upacālā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_404Msdiv 29Uccattanena so buddho,
navutihatthamuggato;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
dasasahassī virocati.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.34 Ms 21Bu_405Msdiv 30Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 31PTS 1st ed. .25 Ms 21Bu_406Msdiv 31Tāraṇīye tārayitvā,
bodhanīye ca bodhayi;
Parinibbāyi sambuddho,
uḷurājāva atthami.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_407Msdiv 32Te ca khīṇāsavā bhikkhū,
so ca buddho asādiso;
Atulappabhaṃ dassayitvā,
nibbutā ye mahāyasā.

SC 33Ms 21Bu_408Msdiv 33Tañca ñāṇaṃ atuliyaṃ,
Tāni ca atulāni ratanāni;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 34Ms 21Bu_409Msdiv 34Sumano yasadharo buddho,
Aṅgārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tattheva tassa jinathūpo,
Catuyojanamuggato”ti. (403)

SC 35Ms 21Bu_410Sumanassa bhagavato vaṃso catuttho.
