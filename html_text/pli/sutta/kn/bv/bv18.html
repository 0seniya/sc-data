18. Siddhatthabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .47 PTS 2nd ed. 1.68 Ms 21Bu_748Msdiv 1“Dhammadassissa aparena,
siddhattho nāma nāyako;
Nihanitvā tamaṃ sabbaṃ,
sūriyo abbhuggato yathā.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_749Msdiv 2Sopi patvāna sambodhiṃ,
santārento sadevakaṃ;
Abhivassi dhammameghena,
nibbāpento sadevakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_750Msdiv 3Tassāpi atulatejassa,
Ahesuṃ abhisamayā tayo;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
Paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_751Msdiv 4Punāparaṃ bhīmarathe,
yadā āhani dundubhiṃ;
Tadā navutikoṭīnaṃ,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_752Msdiv 5Yadā buddho dhammaṃ desesi,
vebhāre so puruttame;
Tadā navutikoṭīnaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_753Msdiv 6Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
tasmimpi dvipaduttame;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_754Msdiv 7Koṭisatānaṃ navutīnaṃ,
asītiyāpi ca koṭinaṃ;
Ete āsuṃ tayo ṭhānā,
vimalānaṃ samāgame.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_755Msdiv 8Ahaṃ tena samayena,
maṅgalo nāma tāpaso;
Uggatejo duppasaho,
abhiññābalasamāhito.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_756Msdiv 9Jambuto phalamānetvā,
siddhatthassa adāsahaṃ;
Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_757Msdiv 10‘Passatha imaṃ tāpasaṃ,
jaṭilaṃ uggatāpanaṃ;
Catunnavutito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_758Msdiv 11Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_759Msdiv 12Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_760Msdiv 13Vebhāraṃ nāma nagaraṃ,
udeno nāma khattiyo;
Suphassā nāma janikā,
siddhatthassa mahesino.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.69 Ms 21Bu_761Msdiv 14Dasavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Kokāsuppalakokanadā,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_762Msdiv 15Tisoḷasasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Somanassā nāma sā nārī,
anūpamo nāma atrajo.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_763Msdiv 16Nimitte caturo disvā,
sivikāyābhinikkhami;
Anūnadasamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .48 Ms 21Bu_764Msdiv 17Brahmunā yācito santo,
siddhattho lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
migadāye naruttamo.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_765Msdiv 18Sambalo ca sumitto ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Revato nāmupaṭṭhāko,
siddhatthassa mahesino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_766Msdiv 19Sīvalā ca surāmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
kaṇikāroti vuccati.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_767Msdiv 20Suppiyo ca samuddo ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Rammā ceva surammā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_768Msdiv 21So buddho saṭṭhiratanaṃ,
ahosi nabhamuggato;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
dasasahassī virocati.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_769Msdiv 22Sopi buddho asamasamo,
atulo appaṭipuggalo;
Vassasatasahassāni,
loke aṭṭhāsi cakkhumā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_770Msdiv 23Vipulaṃ pabhaṃ dassayitvā,
pupphāpetvāna sāvake;
Vilāsetvā samāpatyā,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_771Msdiv 24Siddhattho munivaro buddho,
Anomārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa thūpavaro,
Catuyojanamuggato”ti. (753)

SC 25Ms 21Bu_772Siddhatthassa bhagavato vaṃso soḷasamo.
