11. Nāradabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .33 PTS 2nd ed. 1.47 Ms 21Bu_532Msdiv 1“Padumassa aparena,
sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Nārado nāma nāmena,
asamo appaṭipuggalo.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_533Msdiv 2So buddho cakkavattissa,
jeṭṭho dayitaoraso;
Āmukkamālābharaṇo,
uyyānaṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_534Msdiv 3Tatthāsi rukkho yasavipulo,
Abhirūpo brahā suci;
Tamajjhapatvā upanisīdi,
Mahāsoṇassa heṭṭhato.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_535Msdiv 4Tattha ñāṇavaruppajji,
anantaṃ vajirūpamaṃ;
Tena vicini saṅkhāre,
ukkujjamavakujjakaṃ.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_536Msdiv 5Tattha sabbakilesāni,
asesamabhivāhayi;
Pāpuṇī kevalaṃ bodhiṃ,
buddhañāṇe ca cuddasa.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_537Msdiv 6Pāpuṇitvāna sambodhiṃ,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayi;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_538Msdiv 7Mahādoṇaṃ nāgarājaṃ,
vinayanto mahāmuni;
Pāṭiheraṃ tadākāsi,
dassayanto sadevake.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_539Msdiv 8Tadā devamanussānaṃ,
tamhi dhammappakāsane;
Navutikoṭisahassāni,
tariṃsu sabbasaṃsayaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_540Msdiv 9Yamhi kāle mahāvīro,
ovadī sakamatrajaṃ;
Asītikoṭisahassānaṃ,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_541Msdiv 10Sannipātā tayo āsuṃ,
nāradassa mahesino;
Koṭisatasahassānaṃ,
paṭhamo āsi samāgamo.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_542Msdiv 11Yadā buddho buddhaguṇaṃ,
sanidānaṃ pakāsayi;
Navutikoṭisahassāni,
samiṃsu vimalā tadā.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_543Msdiv 12Yadā verocano nāgo,
dānaṃ dadāti satthuno;
Tadā samiṃsu jinaputtā,
asītisatasahassiyo.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_544Msdiv 13Ahaṃ tena samayena,
jaṭilo uggatāpano;
Antalikkhacaro āsiṃ,
pañcābhiññāsu pāragū.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_545Msdiv 14Tadāpāhaṃ asamasamaṃ,
sasaṃghaṃ saparijjanaṃ;
Annapānena tappetvā,
candanenābhipūjayiṃ.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.48 Ms 21Bu_546Msdiv 15Sopi maṃ tadā byākāsi,
nārado lokanāyako;
‘Aparimeyyito kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_547Msdiv 16Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .34 Ms 21Bu_548Msdiv 17Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo hāsetva mānasaṃ;
Adhiṭṭhahiṃ vataṃ uggaṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_549Msdiv 18Nagaraṃ dhaññavatī nāma,
sudevo nāma khattiyo;
Anomā nāma janikā,
nāradassa mahesino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_550Msdiv 19Navavassasahassāni,
agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Jito vijitābhirāmo,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_551Msdiv 20Ticattārīsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Vijitasenā nāma nārī,
nanduttaro nāma atrajo.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_552Msdiv 21Nimitte caturo disvā,
Padasā gamanena nikkhami;
Sattāhaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
Acarī purisuttamo.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_553Msdiv 22Brahmunā yācito santo,
nārado lokanāyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
dhanañcuyyānamuttame.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_554Msdiv 23Bhaddasālo jitamitto,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Vāseṭṭho nāmupaṭṭhāko,
nāradassa mahesino.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_555Msdiv 24Uttarā phaggunī ceva,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
mahāsoṇoti vuccati.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_556Msdiv 25Uggarindo vasabho ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Indāvarī ca vaṇḍī ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_557Msdiv 26Aṭṭhāsītiratanāni,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Kañcanagghiyasaṅkāso,
dasasahassī virocati.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_558Msdiv 27Tassa byāmappabhā kāyā,
niddhāvati disodisaṃ;
Nirantaraṃ divārattiṃ,
yojanaṃ pharate sadā.

SC 28Ms 21Bu_559Msdiv 28Na keci tena samayena,
samantā yojane janā;
Ukkāpadīpe ujjālenti,
buddharaṃsīhi otthaṭā.

SC 29Ms 21Bu_560Msdiv 29Navutivassasahassāni,
āyu vijjati tāvade;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 30PTS 2nd ed. 1.49 Ms 21Bu_561Msdiv 30Yathā uḷūhi gaganaṃ,
vicittaṃ upasobhati;
Tatheva sāsanaṃ tassa,
arahantehi sobhati.

SC 31Ms 21Bu_562Msdiv 31Saṃsārasotaṃ taraṇāya,
sesake paṭipannake;
Dhammasetuṃ daḷhaṃ katvā,
nibbuto so narāsabho.

SC 32Ms 21Bu_563Msdiv 32Sopi buddho asamasamo,
Tepi khīṇāsavā atulatejā;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 33Ms 21Bu_564Msdiv 33Nārado jinavasabho,
Nibbuto sudassane pure;
Tatthevassa thūpavaro,
Catuyojanamuggato”ti. (553)

SC 34Ms 21Bu_565Nāradassa bhagavato vaṃso navamo.
