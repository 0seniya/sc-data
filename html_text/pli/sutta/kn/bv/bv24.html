24. Kakusandhabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .58 PTS 2nd ed. 1.86 Ms 21Bu_926Msdiv 1“Vessabhussa aparena,
sambuddho dvipaduttamo;
Kakusandho nāma nāmena,
appameyyo durāsado.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_927Msdiv 2Ugghāṭetvā sabbabhavaṃ,
cariyāya pāramiṃ gato;
Sīhova pañjaraṃ bhetvā,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_928Msdiv 3Dhammacakkaṃ pavattente,
Kakusandhe lokanāyake;
Cattārīsakoṭisahassānaṃ,
Dhammābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_929Msdiv 4Antalikkhamhi ākāse,
yamakaṃ katvā vikubbanaṃ;
Tiṃsakoṭisahassānaṃ,
bodhesi devamānuse.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_930Msdiv 5Naradevassa yakkhassa,
catusaccappakāsane;
Dhammābhisamayo tassa,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyo.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_931Msdiv 6Kakusandhassa bhagavato,
eko āsi samāgamo;
Khīṇāsavānaṃ vimalānaṃ,
santacittāna tādinaṃ.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_932Msdiv 7Cattālīsasahassānaṃ,
tadā āsi samāgamo;
Dantabhūmimanuppattānaṃ,
āsavārigaṇakkhayā.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_933Msdiv 8Ahaṃ tena samayena,
khemo nāmāsi khattiyo;
Tathāgate jinaputte,
dānaṃ datvā anappakaṃ.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_934Msdiv 9Pattañca cīvaraṃ datvā,
añjanaṃ madhulaṭṭhikaṃ;
Imetaṃ patthitaṃ sabbaṃ,
paṭiyādemi varaṃ varaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_935Msdiv 10Sopi maṃ buddho byākāsi,
kakusandho vināyako;
‘Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
ayaṃ buddho bhavissati.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_936Msdiv 11Ahu kapilavhayā rammā,
… pe …
hessāma sammukhā imaṃ’.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_937Msdiv 12Tassāpi vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ;
Uttariṃ vatamadhiṭṭhāsiṃ,
dasapāramipūriyā.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_938Msdiv 13Nagaraṃ khemāvatī nāma,
khemo nāmāsahaṃ tadā;
Sabbaññutaṃ gavesanto,
pabbajiṃ tassa santike.

SC 14Ms 21Bu_939Msdiv 14Brāhmaṇo aggidatto ca,
āsi buddhassa so pitā;
Visākhā nāma janikā,
kakusandhassa satthuno.

SC 15PTS 2nd ed. 1.87 Ms 21Bu_940Msdiv 15Vasate tattha kheme pure,
sambuddhassa mahākulaṃ;
Narānaṃ pavaraṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
jātimantaṃ mahāyasaṃ.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_941Msdiv 16Catuvassasahassāni,
Agāraṃ ajjha so vasi;
Kāmakāmavaṇṇakāmasuddhināmā,
Tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .59 Ms 21Bu_942Msdiv 17Samattiṃsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Rocinī nāma sā nārī,
uttaro nāma atrajo.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_943Msdiv 18Nimitte caturo disvā,
rathayānena nikkhami;
Anūnaaṭṭhamāsāni,
padhānaṃ padahī jino.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_944Msdiv 19Brahmunā yācito santo,
kakusandho vināyako;
Vatti cakkaṃ mahāvīro,
migadāye naruttamo.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_945Msdiv 20Vidhuro ca sañjīvo ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvakā;
Buddhijo nāmupaṭṭhāko,
kakusandhassa satthuno.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_946Msdiv 21Sāmā ca campānāmā ca,
ahesuṃ aggasāvikā;
Bodhi tassa bhagavato,
sirīsoti pavuccati.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_947Msdiv 22Accuto ca sumano ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhakā;
Nandā ceva sunandā ca,
ahesuṃ aggupaṭṭhikā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_948Msdiv 23Cattālīsaratanāni,
accuggato mahāmuni;
Kanakappabhā niccharati,
samantā dasayojanaṃ.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_949Msdiv 24Cattālīsavassasahassāni,
Āyu tassa mahesino;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamāno so,
Tāresi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 25Ms 21Bu_950Msdiv 25Dhammāpaṇaṃ pasāretvā,
naranārīnaṃ sadevake;
Naditvā sīhanādaṃva,
nibbuto so sasāvako.

SC 26Ms 21Bu_951Msdiv 26Aṭṭhaṅgavacanasampanno,
acchiddāni nirantaraṃ;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahitaṃ,
nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā.

SC 27Ms 21Bu_952Msdiv 27Kakusandho jinavaro,
Khemārāmamhi nibbuto;
Tatthevassa thūpavaro,
Gāvutaṃ nabhamuggato”ti. (928)

SC 28Ms 21Bu_953Kakusandhassa bhagavato vaṃso dvāvīsatimo.
