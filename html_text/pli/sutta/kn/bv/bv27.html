27. Gotamabuddhavaṃsa

SC 1PTS 1st ed. .65 PTS 2nd ed. 1.97 Ms 21Bu_1036Msdiv 1“Ahametarahi sambuddho,
gotamo sakyavaḍḍhano;
Padhānaṃ padahitvāna,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 2Ms 21Bu_1037Msdiv 2Brahmunā yācito santo,
dhammacakkaṃ pavattayiṃ;
Aṭṭhārasannaṃ koṭīnaṃ,
paṭhamābhisamayo ahu.

SC 3Ms 21Bu_1038Msdiv 3Tato parañca desente,
naradevasamāgame;
Gaṇanāya na vattabbo,
dutiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 4Ms 21Bu_1039Msdiv 4Idhevāhaṃ etarahi,
ovadiṃ mama atrajaṃ;
Gaṇanāya na vattabbo,
tatiyābhisamayo ahu.

SC 5Ms 21Bu_1040Msdiv 5Ekosi sannipāto me,
sāvakānaṃ mahesinaṃ;
Aḍḍhateḷasasatānaṃ,
bhikkhūnāsi samāgamo.

SC 6Ms 21Bu_1041Msdiv 6Virocamāno vimalo,
bhikkhusaṃghassa majjhago;
Dadāmi patthitaṃ sabbaṃ,
maṇīva sabbakāmado.

SC 7Ms 21Bu_1042Msdiv 7Phalamākaṅkhamānānaṃ,
bhavacchandajahesinaṃ;
Catusaccaṃ pakāsemi,
anukampāya pāṇinaṃ.

SC 8Ms 21Bu_1043Msdiv 8Dasavīsasahassānaṃ,
dhammābhisamayo ahu;
Ekadvinnaṃ abhisamayo,
gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyo.

SC 9Ms 21Bu_1044Msdiv 9Vitthārikaṃ bāhujaññaṃ,
iddhaṃ phītaṃ suphullitaṃ;
Idha mayhaṃ sakyamunino,
sāsanaṃ suvisodhitaṃ.

SC 10Ms 21Bu_1045Msdiv 10Anāsavā vītarāgā,
santacittā samāhitā;
Bhikkhūnekasatā sabbe,
parivārenti maṃ sadā.

SC 11Ms 21Bu_1046Msdiv 11Idāni ye etarahi,
jahanti mānusaṃ bhavaṃ;
Appattamānasā sekhā,
te bhikkhū viññugarahitā.

SC 12Ms 21Bu_1047Msdiv 12Ariyañca santhomayantā,
sadā dhammaratā janā;
Bujjhissanti satimanto,
saṃsārasaritaṃ gatā.

SC 13Ms 21Bu_1048Msdiv 13Nagaraṃ kapilavatthu me,
rājā suddhodano pitā;
Mayhaṃ janettikā mātā,
māyādevīti vuccati.

SC 14PTS 2nd ed. 1.98 Ms 21Bu_1049Msdiv 14Ekūnatiṃsavassāni,
agāraṃ ajjhahaṃ vasiṃ;
Rammo surammo subhako,
tayo pāsādamuttamā.

SC 15Ms 21Bu_1050Msdiv 15Cattārīsasahassāni,
nāriyo samalaṅkatā;
Bhaddakañcanā nāma nārī,
rāhulo nāma atrajo.

SC 16Ms 21Bu_1051Msdiv 16Nimitte caturo disvā,
assayānena nikkhamiṃ;
Chabbassaṃ padhānacāraṃ,
acariṃ dukkaraṃ ahaṃ.

SC 17PTS 1st ed. .66 Ms 21Bu_1052Msdiv 17Bārāṇasiyaṃ isipatane,
Cakkaṃ pavattitaṃ mayā;
Ahaṃ gotamasambuddho,
Saraṇaṃ sabbapāṇinaṃ.

SC 18Ms 21Bu_1053Msdiv 18Kolito upatisso ca,
dve bhikkhū aggasāvakā;
Ānando nāmupaṭṭhāko,
santikāvacaro mama;
Khemā uppalavaṇṇā ca,
bhikkhunī aggasāvikā.

SC 19Ms 21Bu_1054Msdiv 19Citto hatthāḷavako ca,
aggupaṭṭhākupāsakā;
Nandamātā ca uttarā,
aggupaṭṭhākupāsikā.

SC 20Ms 21Bu_1055Msdiv 20Ahaṃ assatthamūlamhi,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ;
Byāmappabhā sadā mayhaṃ,
soḷasahatthamuggatā.

SC 21Ms 21Bu_1056Msdiv 21Appaṃ vassasataṃ āyu,
idānetarahi vijjati;
Tāvatā tiṭṭhamānohaṃ,
tāremi janataṃ bahuṃ.

SC 22Ms 21Bu_1057Msdiv 22Ṭhapayitvāna dhammukkaṃ,
pacchimaṃ janabodhanaṃ;
Ahampi nacirasseva,
saddhiṃ sāvakasaṃghato;
Idheva parinibbissaṃ,
aggīvāhārasaṅkhayā.

SC 23Ms 21Bu_1058Msdiv 23Tāni ca atulatejāni,
Imāni ca dasabalāni;
Ayañca guṇadhāraṇo deho,
Dvattiṃsavaralakkhaṇavicitto.

SC 24Ms 21Bu_1059Msdiv 24Dasa disā pabhāsetvā,
Sataraṃsīva chappabhā;
Sabbaṃ tamantarahissanti,
Nanu rittā sabbasaṅkhārā”ti. (1032)

SC 25Ms 21Bu_1060Gotamassa bhagavato vaṃso pañcavīsatimo.
