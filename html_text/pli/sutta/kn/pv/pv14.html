14. Sāriputtattheramātupetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 116PTS 2nd ed. 17 Ms 19Pv_133Msdiv 116“Naggā dubbaṇṇarūpāsi,
kisā dhamanisanthatā;
Upphāsulike kisike,
kā nu tvaṃ idha tiṭṭhasi”.

SC 2SC Verse 117PTS 2nd ed. 18 Ms 19Pv_134Msdiv 117“Ahaṃ te sakiyā mātā,
pubbe aññāsu jātīsu;
Upapannā pettivisayaṃ,
khuppipāsasamappitā.

SC 3SC Verse 118Ms 19Pv_135Msdiv 118Chaḍḍitaṃ khipitaṃ kheḷaṃ,
siṅghāṇikaṃ silesumaṃ;
Vasañca ḍayhamānānaṃ,
vijātānañca lohitaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 119Ms 19Pv_136Msdiv 119Vaṇikānañca yaṃ ghāna-
sīsacchinnāna lohitaṃ;
Khudāparetā bhuñjāmi,
itthipurisanissitaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 120Ms 19Pv_137Msdiv 120Pubbalohitaṃ bhakkhāmi,
pasūnaṃ mānusāna ca;
Aleṇā anagārā ca,
nīlamañcaparāyaṇā.

SC 6SC Verse 121Ms 19Pv_138Msdiv 121Dehi puttaka me dānaṃ,
datvā anvādisāhi me;
Appeva nāma mucceyyaṃ,
pubbalohitabhojanā”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 122Ms 19Pv_139Msdiv 122Mātuyā vacanaṃ sutvā,
upatissonukampako;
Āmantayi moggallānaṃ,
anuruddhañca kappinaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 123Ms 19Pv_140Msdiv 123Catasso kuṭiyo katvā,
saṃghe cātuddise adā;
Kuṭiyo annapānañca,
mātu dakkhiṇamādisī.

SC 9SC Verse 124Ms 19Pv_141Msdiv 124Samanantarānuddiṭṭhe,
vipāko udapajjatha;
Bhojanaṃ pānīyaṃ vatthaṃ,
dakkhiṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 125Ms 19Pv_142Msdiv 125Tato suddhā sucivasanā,
kāsikuttamadhārinī;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
kolitaṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 11SC Verse 126Ms 19Pv_143Msdiv 126“Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
yā tvaṃ tiṭṭhasi devate;
Obhāsentī disā sabbā,
osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 12SC Verse 127Ms 19Pv_144Msdiv 127Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 13SC Verse 128Ms 19Pv_145Msdiv 128Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 14SC Verse 129Ms 19Pv_146Msdiv 129“Sāriputtassāhaṃ mātā,
pubbe aññāsu jātīsu;
Upapannā pettivisayaṃ,
khuppipāsasamappitā.

SC 15SC Verse 130Ms 19Pv_147Msdiv 130Chaḍḍitaṃ khipitaṃ kheḷaṃ,
siṅghāṇikaṃ silesumaṃ;
Vasañca ḍayhamānānaṃ,
vijātānañca lohitaṃ.

SC 16SC Verse 131Ms 19Pv_148Msdiv 131Vaṇikānañca yaṃ ghāna-
sīsacchinnāna lohitaṃ;
Khudāparetā bhuñjāmi,
itthipurisanissitaṃ.

SC 17SC Verse 132Ms 19Pv_149Msdiv 132Pubbalohitaṃ bhakkhissaṃ,
pasūnaṃ mānusāna ca;
Aleṇā anagārā ca,
nīlamañcaparāyaṇā.

SC 18SC Verse 133PTS 2nd ed. 19 Ms 19Pv_150Msdiv 133Sāriputtassa dānena,
modāmi akutobhayā;
Muniṃ kāruṇikaṃ loke,
bhante vanditumāgatā”ti.

SC 19Ms 19Pv_151Sāriputtattherassa mātupetivatthu dutiyaṃ.
