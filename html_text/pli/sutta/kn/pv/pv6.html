6. Pañcaputtakhādapetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 26PTS 2nd ed. 5 Ms 19Pv_32SC 1SC Verse 26PTS 2nd ed. 5 Ms 19Pv_32Msdiv 26“Naggā dubbaṇṇarūpāsi,
duggandhā pūti vāyasi;
Makkhikāhi parikiṇṇā,
kā nu tvaṃ idha tiṭṭhasī”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 27Ms 19Pv_33Msdiv 27“Ahaṃ SC 2SC Verse 27Ms 19Pv_33Msdiv 27“Ahaṃ bhadante petīmhi,
duggatā yamalokikā;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā.

SC 3SC Verse 28Ms 19Pv_34SC 3SC Verse 28Ms 19Pv_34Msdiv 28Kālena pañca puttāni,
sāyaṃ pañca punāpare;
Vijāyitvāna khādāmi,
tepi nā honti me alaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 29Ms 19Pv_35SC 4SC Verse 29Ms 19Pv_35Msdiv 29Pariḍayhati dhūmāyati,
khudāya hadayaṃ mama;
Pānīyaṃ na labhe pātuṃ,
passa maṃ byasanaṃ gatan”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 30Ms 19Pv_36SC 5SC Verse 30Ms 19Pv_36Msdiv 30“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
puttamaṃsāni khādasī”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 31Ms 19Pv_37Msdiv 31“SC 6SC Verse 31Ms 19Pv_37Msdiv 31“Sapatī me gabbhinī āsi,
tassā pāpaṃ acetayiṃ;
Sāhaṃ paduṭṭhamanasā,
akariṃ gabbhapātanaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 32Ms 19Pv_38SC 7SC Verse 32Ms 19Pv_38Msdiv 32Tassā dvemāsiko gabbho,
lohitaññeva pagghari;
Tadassā mātā kupitā,
mayhaṃ ñātī samānayi;
Sapathañca maṃ kāresi,
paribhāsāpayī ca maṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 33Ms 19Pv_39SC 8SC Verse 33Ms 19Pv_39Msdiv 33Sāhaṃ ghorañca sapathaṃ,
musāvādaṃ abhāsisaṃ;
Puttamaṃsāni khādāmi,
sace taṃ pakataṃ mayā.

SC 9SC Verse 34Ms 19Pv_40Msdiv 34Tassa SC 9SC Verse 34Ms 19Pv_40Msdiv 34Tassa kammassa vipākena,
musāvādassa cūbhayaṃ;
Puttamaṃsāni khādāmi,
pubbalohitamakkhitā”ti.
