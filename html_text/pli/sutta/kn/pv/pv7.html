7. Sattaputtakhādapetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 35PTS 2nd ed. 6 Ms 19Pv_42Msdiv 35“Naggā dubbaṇṇarūpāsi,
duggandhā pūti vāyasi;
Makkhikāhi parikiṇṇā,
kā nu tvaṃ idha tiṭṭhasī”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 36Ms 19Pv_43Msdiv 36“Ahaṃ bhadante petīmhi,
duggatā yamalokikā;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā.

SC 3SC Verse 37Ms 19Pv_44Msdiv 37Kālena satta puttāni,
sāyaṃ satta punāpare;
Vijāyitvāna khādāmi,
tepi nā honti me alaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 38Ms 19Pv_45Msdiv 38Pariḍayhati dhūmāyati,
khudāya hadayaṃ mama;
Nibbutiṃ nādhigacchāmi,
aggidaḍḍhāva ātape”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 39Ms 19Pv_46Msdiv 39“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
puttamaṃsāni khādasī”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 40Ms 19Pv_47Msdiv 40“Ahū mayhaṃ duve puttā,
ubho sampattayobbanā;
Sāhaṃ puttabalūpetā,
sāmikaṃ atimaññisaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 41Ms 19Pv_48Msdiv 41Tato me sāmiko kuddho,
sapatiṃ mayhamānayi;
Sā ca gabbhaṃ alabhittha,
tassā pāpaṃ acetayiṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 42Ms 19Pv_49Msdiv 42Sāhaṃ paduṭṭhamanasā,
akariṃ gabbhapātanaṃ;
Tassā temāsiko gabbho,
pūtilohitako pati.

SC 9SC Verse 43Ms 19Pv_50Msdiv 43Tadassā mātā kupitā,
mayhaṃ ñātī samānayi;
Sapathañca maṃ kāresi,
paribhāsāpayī ca maṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 44Ms 19Pv_51Msdiv 44Sāhaṃ ghorañca sapathaṃ,
musāvādaṃ abhāsisaṃ;
‘Puttamaṃsāni khādāmi,
sace taṃ pakataṃ mayā’.

SC 11SC Verse 45Ms 19Pv_52Msdiv 45Tassa kammassa vipākena,
musāvādassa cūbhayaṃ;
Puttamaṃsāni khādāmi,
pubbalohitamakkhitā”ti.

SC 12Ms 19Pv_53Sattaputtakhādapetivatthu sattamaṃ.
