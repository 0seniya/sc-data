24. Kaṇṇamuṇḍapetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 349Ms 19Pv_376Msdiv 348“Soṇṇasopānaphalakā,
soṇṇavālukasanthatā;
Tattha sogandhiyā vaggū,
sucigandhā manoramā.

SC 2SC Verse 350Ms 19Pv_377Msdiv 349Nānārukkhehi sañchannā,
nānāgandhasameritā;
Nānāpadumasañchannā,
puṇḍarīkasamotatā.

SC 3SC Verse 351Ms 19Pv_378Msdiv 350Surabhiṃ sampavāyanti,
manuññā māluteritā;
Haṃsakoñcābhirudā ca,
cakkavakkābhikūjitā.

SC 4SC Verse 352PTS 2nd ed. 42 Ms 19Pv_379Msdiv 351Nānādijagaṇākiṇṇā,
nānāsaragaṇāyutā;
Nānāphaladharā rukkhā,
nānāpupphadharā vanā.

SC 5SC Verse 353Ms 19Pv_380Msdiv 352Na manussesu īdisaṃ,
nagaraṃ yādisaṃ idaṃ;
Pāsādā bahukā tuyhaṃ,
sovaṇṇarūpiyāmayā;
Daddallamānā ābhenti,
samantā caturo disā.

SC 6SC Verse 354Ms 19Pv_381Msdiv 353Pañca dāsisatā tuyhaṃ,
yā temā paricārikā;
Tā kambukāyūradharā,
kañcanāveḷabhūsitā.

SC 7SC Verse 355Ms 19Pv_382Msdiv 354Pallaṅkā bahukā tuyhaṃ,
sovaṇṇarūpiyāmayā;
Kadalimigasañchannā,
sajjā gonakasanthatā.

SC 8SC Verse 356Ms 19Pv_383Msdiv 355Yattha tvaṃ vāsūpagatā,
sabbakāmasamiddhinī;
Sampattāyaḍḍharattāya,
tato uṭṭhāya gacchasi.

SC 9SC Verse 357Ms 19Pv_384Msdiv 356Uyyānabhūmiṃ gantvāna,
pokkharaññā samantato;
Tassā tīre tuvaṃ ṭhāsi,
harite saddale subhe.

SC 10SC Verse 358Ms 19Pv_385Msdiv 357Tato te kaṇṇamuṇḍo sunakho,
Aṅgamaṅgāni khādati;
Yadā ca khāyitā āsi,
Aṭṭhisaṅkhalikā katā;
Ogāhasi pokkharaṇiṃ,
Hoti kāyo yathā pure.

SC 11SC Verse 359Ms 19Pv_386Msdiv 358Tato tvaṃ aṅgapaccaṅgī,
sucāru piyadassanā;
Vatthena pārupitvāna,
āyāsi mama santikaṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 360Ms 19Pv_387Msdiv 359Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
kaṇṇamuṇḍo sunakho tava;
Aṅgamaṅgāni khādatī”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 361Ms 19Pv_388Msdiv 360“Kimilāyaṃ gahapati,
saddho āsi upāsako;
Tassāhaṃ bhariyā āsiṃ,
dussīlā aticārinī.

SC 14SC Verse 362Ms 19Pv_389Msdiv 361So maṃ aticaramānāya,
sāmiko etadabravi;
‘Netaṃ channaṃ patirūpaṃ,
yaṃ tvaṃ aticarāsi maṃ’.

SC 15SC Verse 363Ms 19Pv_390Msdiv 362Sāhaṃ ghorañca sapathaṃ,
musāvādañca bhāsisaṃ;
‘Nāhaṃ taṃ aticarāmi,
kāyena uda cetasā.

SC 16SC Verse 364Ms 19Pv_391Msdiv 363Sacāhaṃ taṃ aticarāmi,
kāyena uda cetasā;
Kaṇṇamuṇḍoyaṃ sunakho,
aṅgamaṅgāni khādatu’.

SC 17SC Verse 365Ms 19Pv_392Msdiv 364Tassa kammassa vipākaṃ,
musāvādassa cūbhayaṃ;
Satteva vassasatāni,
anubhūtaṃ yato hi me;
Kaṇṇamuṇḍo ca sunakho,
aṅgamaṅgāni khādati.

SC 18SC Verse 366Ms 19Pv_393Msdiv 365Tvañca deva bahukāro,
atthāya me idhāgato;
Sumuttāhaṃ kaṇṇamuṇḍassa,
asokā akutobhayā.

SC 19SC Verse 367PTS 2nd ed. 43 Ms 19Pv_394Msdiv 366Tāhaṃ deva namassāmi,
yācāmi pañjalīkatā;
Bhuñja amānuse kāme,
rama deva mayā sahā”ti.

SC 20SC Verse 368Ms 19Pv_395Msdiv 367“Bhuttā amānusā kāmā,
ramitomhi tayā saha;
Tāhaṃ subhage yācāmi,
khippaṃ paṭinayāhi man”ti.

SC 21Ms 19Pv_396Kaṇṇamuṇḍapetivatthu dvādasamaṃ.
