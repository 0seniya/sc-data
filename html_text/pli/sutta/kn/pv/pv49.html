49. Bhogasaṃharaṇapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 803Ms 19Pv_862Msdiv 801“Mayaṃ bhoge saṃharimhā,
samena visamena ca;
Te aññe paribhuñjanti,
mayaṃ dukkhassa bhāginī”ti.
