42. Rājaputtapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 755PTS 2nd ed. 88 Ms 19Pv_807Msdiv 753Pubbe katānaṃ kammānaṃ,
vipāko mathaye manaṃ;
Rūpe sadde rase gandhe,
phoṭṭhabbe ca manorame.

SC 2SC Verse 756Ms 19Pv_808Msdiv 754Naccaṃ gītaṃ ratiṃ khiḍḍaṃ,
anubhutvā anappakaṃ;
Uyyāne paricaritvā,
pavisanto giribbajaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 757Ms 19Pv_809Msdiv 755Isiṃ sunetta maddakkhi,
attadantaṃ samāhitaṃ;
Appicchaṃ hirisampannaṃ,
uñche pattagate rataṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 758Ms 19Pv_810Msdiv 756Hatthikkhandhato oruyha,
laddhā bhanteti cābravi;
Tassa pattaṃ gahetvāna,
uccaṃ paggayha khattiyo.

SC 5SC Verse 759Ms 19Pv_811Msdiv 757Thaṇḍile pattaṃ bhinditvā,
hasamāno apakkami;
“Rañño kitavassāhaṃ putto,
kiṃ maṃ bhikkhu karissasi”.

SC 6SC Verse 760Ms 19Pv_812Msdiv 758Tassa kammassa pharusassa,
vipāko kaṭuko ahu;
Yaṃ rājaputto vedesi,
nirayamhi samappito.

SC 7SC Verse 761Ms 19Pv_813Msdiv 759Chaḷeva caturāsīti,
vassāni navutāni ca;
Bhusaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchittho,
niraye katakibbiso.

SC 8SC Verse 762Ms 19Pv_814Msdiv 760Uttānopi ca paccittha,
nikujjo vāmadakkhiṇo;
Uddhampādo ṭhito ceva,
ciraṃ bālo apaccatha.

SC 9SC Verse 763Ms 19Pv_815Msdiv 761Bahūni vassasahassāni,
pūgāni nahutāni ca;
Bhusaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchittho,
niraye katakibbiso.

SC 10SC Verse 764Ms 19Pv_816Msdiv 762Etādisaṃ kho kaṭukaṃ,
appaduṭṭhappadosinaṃ;
Paccanti pāpakammantā,
isimāsajja subbataṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 765Ms 19Pv_817Msdiv 763So tattha bahuvassāni,
vedayitvā bahuṃ dukhaṃ;
Khuppipāsahato nāma,
peto āsi tato cuto.

SC 12SC Verse 766Ms 19Pv_818Msdiv 764Etamādīnavaṃ Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
issaramadasambhavaṃ;
Pahāya issaramadaṃ,
nivātamanuvattaye.

SC 13SC Verse 767Ms 19Pv_819Msdiv 765Diṭṭheva dhamme pāsaṃso,
yo buddhesu sagāravo;
Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,
saggaṃ so upapajjatīti.

SC 14Ms 19Pv_820Rājaputtapetavatthu sattamaṃ.
