43. Gūthakhādakapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 768PTS 2nd ed. 89 Ms 19Pv_821Msdiv 766“Gūthakūpato uggantvā,
ko nu dīno patiṭṭhasi;
Nissaṃsayaṃ pāpakammanto,
kiṃ nu saddahase tuvan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 769Ms 19Pv_822Msdiv 767“Ahaṃ bhadante petomhi,
duggato yamalokiko;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gato”.

SC 3SC Verse 770Ms 19Pv_823Msdiv 768“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
idaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchasī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 771Ms 19Pv_824Msdiv 769“Ahu āvāsiko mayhaṃ,
issukī kulamaccharī;
Ajjhāsito mayhaṃ ghare,
kadariyo paribhāsako.

SC 5SC Verse 772Ms 19Pv_825Msdiv 770Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhikkhavo paribhāsisaṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
petalokaṃ ito gato”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 773Ms 19Pv_826Msdiv 771“Amitto mittavaṇṇena,
yo te āsi kulūpako;
Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,
kiṃ nu pecca gatiṃ gato”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 774Ms 19Pv_827Msdiv 772“Tassevāhaṃ pāpakammassa,
sīse tiṭṭhāmi matthake;
So ca paravisayaṃ patto,
mameva paricārako.

SC 8SC Verse 775Ms 19Pv_828Msdiv 773Yaṃ bhadante hadantaññe,
etaṃ me hoti bhojanaṃ;
Ahañca kho yaṃ hadāmi,
etaṃ so upajīvatī”ti.

SC 9Ms 19Pv_829Gūthakhādakapetavatthu aṭṭhamaṃ.
