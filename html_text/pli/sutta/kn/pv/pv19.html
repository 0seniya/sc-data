19. Dhanapālaseṭṭhipetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 227PTS 2nd ed. 27 Ms 19Pv_249Msdiv 227“Naggo dubbaṇṇarūposi,
kiso dhamanisanthato;
Upphāsuliko kisiko,
ko nu tvamasi mārisa”.

SC 2SC Verse 228Ms 19Pv_250Msdiv 228“Ahaṃ bhadante petomhi,
duggato yamalokiko;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gato”.

SC 3SC Verse 229Ms 19Pv_251Msdiv 229“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
petalokaṃ ito gato”.

SC 4SC Verse 230Ms 19Pv_252Msdiv 230“Nagaraṃ atthi paṇṇānaṃ,
erakacchanti vissutaṃ;
Tattha seṭṭhi pure āsiṃ,
dhanapāloti maṃ vidū.

SC 5SC Verse 231Ms 19Pv_253Msdiv 231Asīti sakaṭavāhānaṃ,
hiraññassa ahosi me;
Pahūtaṃ me jātarūpaṃ,
muttā veḷuriyā bahū.

SC 6SC Verse 232Ms 19Pv_254Msdiv 232Tāva mahaddhanassāpi,
na me dātuṃ piyaṃ ahu;
Pidahitvā dvāraṃ bhuñjiṃ,
mā maṃ yācanakāddasuṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 233Ms 19Pv_255Msdiv 233Assaddho maccharī cāsiṃ,
kadariyo paribhāsako;
Dadantānaṃ karontānaṃ,
vārayissaṃ bahū jane.

SC 8SC Verse 234PTS 2nd ed. 28 Ms 19Pv_256Msdiv 234Vipāko natthi dānassa,
saṃyamassa kuto phalaṃ;
Pokkharaññodapānāni,
ārāmāni ca ropite;
Papāyo ca vināsesiṃ,
dugge saṅkamanāni ca.

SC 9SC Verse 235Ms 19Pv_257Msdiv 235Svāhaṃ akatakalyāṇo,
katapāpo tato cuto;
Upapanno pettivisayaṃ,
khuppipāsasamappito.

SC 10SC Verse 236Ms 19Pv_258Msdiv 236Pañcapaṇṇāsavassāni,
yato kālaṅkato ahaṃ;
Nābhijānāmi bhuttaṃ vā,
pītaṃ vā pana pāniyaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 237Ms 19Pv_259Msdiv 237Yo saṃyamo so vināso,
yo vināso so saṃyamo;
Petā hi kira jānanti,
yo saṃyamo so vināso.

SC 12SC Verse 238Ms 19Pv_260Msdiv 238Ahaṃ pure saṃyamissaṃ,
nādāsiṃ bahuke dhane;
Santesu deyyadhammesu,
dīpaṃ nākāsimattano;
Svāhaṃ pacchānutappāmi,
attakammaphalūpago.

SC 13SC Verse 239Ms 19Pv_261Msdiv 239Uddhaṃ catūhi māsehi,
kālaṃkiriyā bhavissati;
Ekantakaṭukaṃ ghoraṃ,
nirayaṃ papatissahaṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 240Ms 19Pv_262Msdiv 240Catukkaṇṇaṃ catudvāraṃ,
vibhattaṃ bhāgaso mitaṃ;
Ayopākārapariyantaṃ,
ayasā paṭikujjitaṃ.

SC 15SC Verse 241Ms 19Pv_263Msdiv 241Tassa ayomayā bhūmi,
jalitā tejasā yutā;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
pharitvā tiṭṭhati sabbadā.

SC 16SC Verse 242Ms 19Pv_264Msdiv 242Tatthāhaṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ,
Dukkhaṃ vedissa vedanaṃ;
Phalaṃ pāpassa kammassa,
Tasmā socāmahaṃ bhusaṃ.

SC 17SC Verse 243Ms 19Pv_265Msdiv 243Taṃ vo vadāmi bhaddaṃ vo,
yāvantettha samāgatā;
Mākattha pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
āvi vā yadi vā raho.

SC 18SC Verse 244Ms 19Pv_266Msdiv 244Sace taṃ pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
karissatha karotha vā;
Na vo dukkhā pamutyatthi,
uppaccāpi palāyataṃ.

SC 19SC Verse 245Ms 19Pv_267Msdiv 245Matteyyā hotha petteyyā,
kule jeṭṭhāpacāyikā;
Sāmaññā hotha brahmaññā,
evaṃ saggaṃ gamissathā”ti.

SC 20Ms 19Pv_268Dhanapālaseṭṭhipetavatthu sattamaṃ.
