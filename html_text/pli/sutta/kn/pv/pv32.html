32. Migaluddakapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 480PTS 2nd ed. 59 Ms 19Pv_518Msdiv 478“Naranāripurakkhato yuvā,
Rajanīyehi kāmaguṇehi sobhasi;
Divasaṃ anubhosi kāraṇaṃ,
Kimakāsi purimāya jātiyā”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 481Ms 19Pv_519Msdiv 479“Ahaṃ rājagahe ramme,
ramaṇīye giribbaje;
Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,
lohitapāṇi dāruṇo.

SC 3SC Verse 482Ms 19Pv_520Msdiv 480Avirodhakaresu pāṇisu,
Puthusattesu paduṭṭhamānaso;
Vicariṃ atidāruṇo sadā,
Parahiṃsāya rato asaññato.

SC 4SC Verse 483Ms 19Pv_521Msdiv 481Tassa me sahāyo suhadayo,
Saddho āsi upāsako;
Sopi maṃ anukampanto,
Nivāresi punappunaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 484Ms 19Pv_522Msdiv 482‘Mākāsi pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
Mā tāta duggatiṃ agā;
Sace icchasi pecca sukhaṃ,
Virama pāṇavadhā asaṃyamā’.

SC 6SC Verse 485Ms 19Pv_523Msdiv 483Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
Sukhakāmassa hitānukampino;
Nākāsiṃ sakalānusāsaniṃ,
Cirapāpābhirato abuddhimā.

SC 7SC Verse 486Ms 19Pv_524Msdiv 484So maṃ puna bhūrisumedhaso,
Anukampāya saṃyame nivesayi;
‘Sace divā hanasi pāṇino,
Atha te rattiṃ bhavatu saṃyamo’.

SC 8SC Verse 487PTS 2nd ed. 60 Ms 19Pv_525Msdiv 485Svāhaṃ divā hanitvā pāṇino,
Virato rattimahosi saññato;
Rattāhaṃ paricāremi,
Divā khajjāmi duggato.

SC 9SC Verse 488Ms 19Pv_526Msdiv 486Tassa kammassa kusalassa,
Anubhomi rattiṃ amānusiṃ;
Divā paṭihatāva kukkurā,
Upadhāvanti samantā khādituṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 489Ms 19Pv_527Msdiv 487Ye ca te satatānuyogino,
Dhuvaṃ payuttā sugatassa sāsane;
Maññāmi te amatameva kevalaṃ,
Adhigacchanti padaṃ asaṅkhatan”ti.

SC 11Ms 19Pv_528Migaluddakapetavatthu sattamaṃ.
