4. Piṭṭhadhītalikapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 10PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 19Pv_14SC 1SC Verse 10PTS 2nd ed. 3 Ms 19Pv_14Msdiv 10“Yaṃ kiñcārammaṇaṃ katvā,
dajjā dānaṃ amaccharī;
Pubbapete ca ārabbha,
atha vā vatthudevatā.

SC 2SC Verse 11Ms 19Pv_15SC 2SC Verse 11Ms 19Pv_15Msdiv 11Cattāro ca mahārāje,
lokapāle yasassine;
Kuveraṃ dhataraṭṭhañca,
virūpakkhaṃ virūḷhakaṃ;
Te ceva pūjitā honti,
dāyakā ca anipphalā.

SC 3SC Verse 12Ms 19Pv_16SC 3SC Verse 12Ms 19Pv_16Msdiv 12Na hi ruṇṇaṃ vā soko vā,
yā caññā paridevanā;
Na taṃ petassa atthāya,
evaṃ tiṭṭhanti ñātayo.

SC 4SC Verse 13Ms 19Pv_17SC 4SC Verse 13Ms 19Pv_17Msdiv 13Ayañca kho dakkhiṇā dinnā,
saṃghamhi suppatiṭṭhitā;
Dīgharattaṃ hitāyassa,
ṭhānaso upakappatī”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 19Pv_18Piṭṭhadhītalikapetavatthu catutthaṃ.
