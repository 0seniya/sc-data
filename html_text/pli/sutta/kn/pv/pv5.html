5. Tirokuṭṭapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 14Ms 19Pv_19Msdiv 14“SC 1SC Verse 14Ms 19Pv_19Msdiv 14“Tirokuṭṭesu tiṭṭhanti,
sandhisiṅghāṭakesu ca;
Dvārabāhāsu tiṭṭhanti,
āgantvāna sakaṃ gharaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 15Ms 19Pv_20SC 2SC Verse 15Ms 19Pv_20Msdiv 15Pahūte annapānamhi,
khajjabhojje upaṭṭhite;
Na tesaṃ koci sarati,
sattānaṃ kammapaccayā.

SC 3SC Verse 16PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 19Pv_21SC 3SC Verse 16PTS 2nd ed. 4 Ms 19Pv_21Msdiv 16Evaṃ dadanti ñātīnaṃ,
ye honti anukampakā;
Suciṃ paṇītaṃ kālena,
kappiyaṃ pānabhojanaṃ;
‘Idaṃ vo ñātīnaṃ hotu,
sukhitā hontu ñātayo’.

SC 4SC Verse 17Ms 19Pv_22SC 4SC Verse 17Ms 19Pv_22Msdiv 17Te ca tattha samāgantvā,
ñātipetā samāgatā;
Pahūte annapānamhi,
sakkaccaṃ anumodare.

SC 5SC Verse 18Ms 19Pv_23SC 5SC Verse 18Ms 19Pv_23Msdiv 18‘Ciraṃ jīvantu no ñātī,
yesaṃ hetu labhāmase;
Amhākañca katā pūjā,
dāyakā ca anipphalā’.

SC 6SC Verse 19Ms 19Pv_24SC 6SC Verse 19Ms 19Pv_24Msdiv 19Na hi tattha kasī atthi,
gorakkhettha na vijjati;
Vaṇijjā tādisī natthi,
hiraññena kayākayaṃ;
Ito dinnena yāpenti,
petā kālagatā tahiṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 20Ms 19Pv_25SC 7SC Verse 20Ms 19Pv_25Msdiv 20Unname udakaṃ vuṭṭhaṃ,
yathā ninnaṃ pavattati;
Evamevaṃ ito dinnaṃ,
petānaṃ upakappati.

SC 8SC Verse 21Ms 19Pv_26SC 8SC Verse 21Ms 19Pv_26Msdiv 21Yathā vārivahā pūrā,
paripūrenti sāgaraṃ;
Evamevaṃ ito dinnaṃ,
petānaṃ upakappati.

SC 9SC Verse 22Ms 19Pv_27SC 9SC Verse 22Ms 19Pv_27Msdiv 22‘Adāsi me akāsi me,
ñāti mittā sakhā ca me’;
Petānaṃ dakkhiṇaṃ dajjā,
pubbe katamanussaraṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 23Ms 19Pv_28SC 10SC Verse 23Ms 19Pv_28Msdiv 23Na hi ruṇṇaṃ vā soko vā,
yā caññā paridevanā;
Na taṃ petānamatthāya,
evaṃ tiṭṭhanti ñātayo.

SC 11SC Verse 24Ms 19Pv_29SC 11SC Verse 24Ms 19Pv_29Msdiv 24Ayañca kho dakkhiṇā dinnā,
saṃghamhi suppatiṭṭhitā;
Dīgharattaṃ hitāyassa,
ṭhānaso upakappati.

SC 12SC Verse 25Ms 19Pv_30SC 12SC Verse 25Ms 19Pv_30Msdiv 25So ñātidhammo ca ayaṃ nidassito,
Petāna pūjā ca katā uḷārā;
Balañca bhikkhūnamanuppadinnaṃ,
Tumhehi puññaṃ pasutaṃ anappakan”ti.
