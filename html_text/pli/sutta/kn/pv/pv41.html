41. Kumārapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 748PTS 2nd ed. 87 Ms 19Pv_799Msdiv 746“Sāvatthi nāma nagaraṃ,
himavantassa passato;
Tattha āsuṃ dve kumārā,
rājaputtāti me sutaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 749Ms 19Pv_800Msdiv 747Sammattā rajanīyesu,
kāmassādābhinandino;
Paccuppannasukhe giddhā,
na te passiṃsunāgataṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 750Ms 19Pv_801Msdiv 748Te cutā ca manussattā,
paralokaṃ ito gatā;
Tedha ghosentyadissantā,
pubbe dukkaṭamattano.

SC 4SC Verse 751Ms 19Pv_802Msdiv 749Bahūsu vata santesu,
deyyadhamme upaṭṭhite;
Nāsakkhimhā ca attānaṃ,
parittaṃ kātuṃ sukhāvahaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 752Ms 19Pv_803Msdiv 750Kiṃ tato pāpakaṃ assa,
yaṃ no rājakulā cutā;
Upapannā pettivisayaṃ,
khuppipāsasamappitā.

SC 6SC Verse 753Ms 19Pv_804Msdiv 751Sāmino idha hutvāna,
honti asāmino tahiṃ;
Bhamanti khuppipāsāya,
manussā unnatonatā.

SC 7SC Verse 754Ms 19Pv_805Msdiv 752Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
issaramadasambhavaṃ;
Pahāya issaramadaṃ,
bhave saggagato naro;
Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,
saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti.

SC 8Ms 19Pv_806Kumārapetavatthu chaṭṭhaṃ.
