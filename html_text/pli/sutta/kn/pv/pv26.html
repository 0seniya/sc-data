26. Abhijjamānapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 388PTS 2nd ed. 47 Ms 19Pv_420Msdiv 387“Abhijjamāne vārimhi,
gaṅgāya idha gacchasi;
Naggo pubbaddhapetova,
māladhārī alaṅkato;
Kuhiṃ gamissasi peta,
kattha vāso bhavissatī”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 389Ms 19Pv_421Msdiv 388“Cundaṭṭhilaṃ gamissāmi,
peto so iti bhāsati;
Antare vāsabhagāmaṃ,
bārāṇasiṃ ca santike”.

SC 3SC Verse 390Ms 19Pv_422Msdiv 389Tañca disvā mahāmatto,
koliyo iti vissuto;
Sattuṃ bhattañca petassa,
pītakañca yugaṃ adā.

SC 4SC Verse 391Ms 19Pv_423Msdiv 390Nāvāya tiṭṭhamānāya,
kappakassa adāpayi;
Kappakassa padinnamhi,
ṭhāne petassa dissatha.

SC 5SC Verse 392Ms 19Pv_424Msdiv 391Tato suvatthavasano,
māladhārī alaṅkato;
Ṭhāne ṭhitassa petassa,
dakkhiṇā upakappatha;
Tasmā dajjetha petānaṃ,
anukampāya punappunaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 393Ms 19Pv_425Msdiv 392Sātunnavasanā eke,
aññe kesanivāsanā;
Petā bhattāya gacchanti,
pakkamanti disodisaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 394Ms 19Pv_426Msdiv 393Dūre eke padhāvitvā,
aladdhāva nivattare;
Chātā pamucchitā bhantā,
bhūmiyaṃ paṭisumbhitā.

SC 8SC Verse 395Ms 19Pv_427Msdiv 394Te ca tattha papatitā,
bhūmiyaṃ paṭisumbhitā;
Pubbe akatakalyāṇā,
aggidaḍḍhāva ātape.

SC 9SC Verse 396Ms 19Pv_428Msdiv 395“Mayaṃ pubbe pāpadhammā,
gharaṇī kulamātaro;
Santesu deyyadhammesu,
dīpaṃ nākamha attano.

SC 10SC Verse 397PTS 2nd ed. 48 Ms 19Pv_429Msdiv 396Pahūtaṃ annapānampi,
apissu avakirīyati;
Sammaggate pabbajite,
na ca kiñci adamhase.

SC 11SC Verse 398Ms 19Pv_430Msdiv 397Akammakāmā alasā,
Sādukāmā mahagghasā;
Ālopapiṇḍadātāro,
Paṭiggahe paribhāsimhase.

SC 12SC Verse 399Ms 19Pv_431Msdiv 398Te gharā tā ca dāsiyo,
tānevābharaṇāni no;
Te aññe paricārenti,
mayaṃ dukkhassa bhāgino.

SC 13SC Verse 400Ms 19Pv_432Msdiv 399Veṇī vā avaññā honti,
rathakārī ca dubbhikā;
Caṇḍālī kapaṇā honti,
kappakā ca punappunaṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 401Ms 19Pv_433Msdiv 400Yāni yāni nihīnāni,
kulāni kapaṇāni ca;
Tesu tesveva jāyanti,
esā maccharino gati.

SC 15SC Verse 402Ms 19Pv_434Msdiv 401Pubbe ca katakalyāṇā,
dāyakā vītamaccharā;
Saggaṃ te paripūrenti,
obhāsenti ca nandanaṃ.

SC 16SC Verse 403Ms 19Pv_435Msdiv 402Vejayante ca pāsāde,
ramitvā kāmakāmino;
Uccākulesu jāyanti,
sabhogesu tato cutā.

SC 17SC Verse 404Ms 19Pv_436Msdiv 403Kūṭāgāre ca pāsāde,
pallaṅke gonakatthate;
Bījitaṅgā morahatthehi,
kule jātā yasassino.

SC 18SC Verse 405Ms 19Pv_437Msdiv 404Aṅkato aṅkaṃ gacchanti,
māladhārī alaṅkatā;
Dhātiyo upatiṭṭhanti,
sāyaṃ pātaṃ sukhesino.

SC 19SC Verse 406Ms 19Pv_438Msdiv 405Nayidaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
katapuññānamevidaṃ;
Asokaṃ nandanaṃ rammaṃ,
tidasānaṃ mahāvanaṃ.

SC 20SC Verse 407Ms 19Pv_439Msdiv 406Sukhaṃ akatapuññānaṃ,
idha natthi parattha ca;
Sukhañca katapuññānaṃ,
idha ceva parattha ca.

SC 21SC Verse 408Ms 19Pv_440Msdiv 407Tesaṃ sahabyakāmānaṃ,
kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ bahuṃ;
Katapuññā hi modanti,
sagge bhogasamaṅgino”ti.

SC 22Ms 19Pv_441Abhijjamānapetavatthu paṭhamaṃ.
