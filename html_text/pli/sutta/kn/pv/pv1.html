1. Khettūpamapetavatthu

SC 1Ms 19Pv_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2SC Verse 1PTS 2nd ed. 1 Ms 19Pv_2Msdiv 1“Khettūpamā arahanto,
dāyakā kassakūpamā;
Bījūpamaṃ deyyadhammaṃ,
etto nibbattate phalaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 2Ms 19Pv_3Msdiv 2Etaṃ bījaṃ kasi khettaṃ,
petānaṃ dāyakassa ca;
Taṃ petā paribhuñjanti,
dātā puññena vaḍḍhati.

SC 4SC Verse 3Ms 19Pv_4Msdiv 3Idheva kusalaṃ katvā,
Pete ca paṭipūjiya;
Saggañca kamatiṭṭhānaṃ,
Kammaṃ katvāna bhaddakan”ti.

SC 5Ms 19Pv_5Khettūpamapetavatthu paṭhamaṃ.
