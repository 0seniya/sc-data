47. Ambavanapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 798Ms 19Pv_855Msdiv 796“Ayañca te pokkharaṇī surammā,
Samā sutitthā ca mahodakā ca;
Supupphitā bhamaragaṇānukiṇṇā,
Kathaṃ tayā laddhā ayaṃ manuññā.

SC 2SC Verse 799Ms 19Pv_856Msdiv 797Idañca te ambavanaṃ surammaṃ,
Sabbotukaṃ dhārayate phalāni;
Supupphitaṃ bhamaragaṇānukiṇṇaṃ,
Kathaṃ tayā laddhamidaṃ vimānaṃ”.

SC 3SC Verse 800Ms 19Pv_857Msdiv 798“Ambapakkaṃ dakaṃ yāgu,
sītacchāyā manoramā;
Dhītāya dinnadānena,
tena me idha labbhati”.

SC 4SC Verse 801Ms 19Pv_858Msdiv 799“Sandiṭṭhikaṃ kammaṃ evaṃ passatha,
Dānassa damassa saṃyamassa vipākaṃ;
Dāsī ahaṃ ayyakulesu hutvā,
Suṇisā homi agārassa issarā”ti.

SC 5Ms 19Pv_859Ambavanapetavatthu dvādasamaṃ.
