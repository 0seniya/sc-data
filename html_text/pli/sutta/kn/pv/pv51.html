51. Saṭṭhikūṭapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 808Ms 19Pv_869Msdiv 806“Kiṃ nu ummattarūpova,
Migo bhantova dhāvasi;
Nissaṃsayaṃ pāpakammanto,
Kiṃ nu saddāyase tuvan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 809Ms 19Pv_870Msdiv 807“Ahaṃ bhadante petomhi,
duggato yamalokiko;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gato.

SC 3SC Verse 810Ms 19Pv_871Msdiv 808Saṭṭhi kūṭasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Sīse mayhaṃ nipatanti,
te bhindanti ca matthakan”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 811Ms 19Pv_872Msdiv 809“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
idaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchasi.

SC 5SC Verse 812Ms 19Pv_873Msdiv 810Saṭṭhi kūṭasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Sīse tuyhaṃ nipatanti,
te bhindanti ca matthakan”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 813Ms 19Pv_874Msdiv 811“Athaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,
sunettaṃ bhāvitindriyaṃ;
Nisinnaṃ rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
jhāyantaṃ akutobhayaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 814Ms 19Pv_875Msdiv 812Sālittakappahārena,
bhindissaṃ tassa matthakaṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
idaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchisaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 815Ms 19Pv_876Msdiv 813Saṭṭhi kūṭasahassāni,
Paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Sīse mayhaṃ nipatanti,
Te bhindanti ca matthakan”ti.

SC 9SC Verse 816Ms 19Pv_877Msdiv 814“Dhammena te kāpurisa,
saṭṭhikūṭasahassāni;
Paripuṇṇāni sabbaso,
sīse tuyhaṃ nipatanti;
Te bhindanti ca matthakan”ti.

SC 10Ms 19Pv_878Saṭṭhikūṭapetavatthu soḷasamaṃ.

SC 11Ms 19Pv_879Mahāvaggo catuttho.

SC 12Ms 19Pv_880Tassuddānaṃ

SC 13PTS 2nd ed. 95 Ms 19Pv_881Ambasakkaro serīsako,
piṅgalo revati ucchu;
Dve kumārā duve gūthā,
gaṇapāṭaliambavanaṃ.

SC 14Ms 19Pv_882Akkharukkhabhogasaṃharā,
seṭṭhiputtasaṭṭhikūṭā;
Iti soḷasavatthūni,
vaggo tena pavuccati.

SC 15Ms 19Pv_883Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 16Ms 19Pv_884Urago uparivaggo,
cūḷamahāti catudhā;
Vatthūni ekapaññāsaṃ,
catudhā bhāṇavārato.

SC 17Ms 19Pv_885Petavatthupāḷi niṭṭhitā.
