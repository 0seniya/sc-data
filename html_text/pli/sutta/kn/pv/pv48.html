48. Akkharukkhapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 802PTS 2nd ed. 93 Ms 19Pv_860SC 1SC Verse 802PTS 2nd ed. 93 Ms 19Pv_860Msdiv 800“Yaṃ dadāti na taṃ hoti,
Detheva dānaṃ datvā ubhayaṃ tarati;
Ubhayaṃ tena dānena gacchati,
Jāgaratha māpamajjathā”ti.
