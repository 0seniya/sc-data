29. Bhusapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 448PTS 2nd ed. 55 Ms 19Pv_483Msdiv 447“Bhusāni eko sāliṃ punāparo,
Ayañca nārī sakamaṃsalohitaṃ;
Tuvañca gūthaṃ asuciṃ akantaṃ,
Paribhuñjasi kissa ayaṃ vipāko”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 449Ms 19Pv_484Msdiv 448“Ayaṃ pure mātaraṃ hiṃsati,
Ayaṃ pana kūṭavāṇijo;
Ayaṃ maṃsāni khāditvā,
Musāvādena vañceti.

SC 3SC Verse 450Ms 19Pv_485Msdiv 449Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūtā,
Agārinī sabbakulassa issarā;
Santesu pariguhāmi,
Mā ca kiñci ito adaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 451Ms 19Pv_486Msdiv 450Musāvādena chādemi,
‘natthi etaṃ mama gehe;
Sace santaṃ niguhāmi,
gūtho me hotu bhojanaṃ’.

SC 5SC Verse 452Ms 19Pv_487Msdiv 451Tassa kammassa vipākena,
musāvādassa cūbhayaṃ;
Sugandhaṃ sālino bhattaṃ,
gūthaṃ me parivattati.

SC 6SC Verse 453Ms 19Pv_488Msdiv 452Avañjhāni ca kammāni,
Na hi kammaṃ vinassati;
Duggandhaṃ kiminaṃ kiminaṃ mīḷaṃ,
Bhuñjāmi ca pivāmi cā”ti.

SC 7Ms 19Pv_489Bhusapetavatthu catutthaṃ.
