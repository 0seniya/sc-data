46. Pāṭaliputtapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 795PTS 2nd ed. 92 Ms 19Pv_851Msdiv 793“Diṭṭhā tayā nirayā tiracchānayoni,
Petā asurā athavāpi mānusā devā;
Sayamaddasa kammavipākamattano,
Nessāmi taṃ pāṭaliputtamakkhataṃ;
Tattha gantvā kusalaṃ karohi kammaṃ”.

SC 2SC Verse 796Ms 19Pv_852Msdiv 794“Atthakāmosi me yakkha,
hitakāmosi devate;
Karomi tuyhaṃ vacanaṃ,
tvaṃsi ācariyo mama.

SC 3SC Verse 797Ms 19Pv_853Msdiv 795Diṭṭhā mayā nirayā tiracchānayoni,
Petā asurā athavāpi mānusā devā;
Sayamaddasaṃ kammavipākamattano,
Kāhāmi puññāni anappakānī”ti.

SC 4Ms 19Pv_854Pāṭaliputtapetavatthu ekādasamaṃ.
