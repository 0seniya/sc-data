18. Kaṇhapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 207PTS 2nd ed. 25 Ms 19Pv_228Msdiv 207“Uṭṭhehi kaṇha kiṃ sesi,
Ko attho supanena te;
Yo ca tuyhaṃ sako bhātā,
Hadayaṃ cakkhu ca dakkhiṇaṃ;
Tassa vātā balīyanti,
Sasaṃ jappati kesavā”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 208Ms 19Pv_229Msdiv 208“Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
rohiṇeyyassa kesavo;
Taramānarūpo vuṭṭhāsi,
bhātusokena aṭṭito.

SC 3SC Verse 209Ms 19Pv_230Msdiv 209Kiṃ nu ummattarūpova,
kevalaṃ dvārakaṃ imaṃ;
Saso sasoti lapasi,
kīdisaṃ sasamicchasi.

SC 4SC Verse 210Ms 19Pv_231Msdiv 210Sovaṇṇamayaṃ maṇimayaṃ,
Lohamayaṃ atha rūpiyamayaṃ;
Saṅkhasilāpavāḷamayaṃ,
Kārayissāmi te sasaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 211Ms 19Pv_232Msdiv 211Santi aññepi sasakā,
araññavanagocarā;
Tepi te ānayissāmi,
kīdisaṃ sasamicchasī”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 212Ms 19Pv_233Msdiv 212“Nāhamete sase icche,
ye sasā pathavissitā;
Candato sasamicchāmi,
taṃ me ohara kesavā”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 213Ms 19Pv_234Msdiv 213“So nūna madhuraṃ ñāti,
jīvitaṃ vijahissasi;
Apatthiyaṃ patthayasi,
candato sasamicchasī”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 214Ms 19Pv_235Msdiv 214“Evañce kaṇha jānāsi,
yathaññamanusāsasi;
Kasmā pure mataṃ puttaṃ,
ajjāpi manusocasi.

SC 9SC Verse 215Ms 19Pv_236Msdiv 215Na yaṃ labbhā manussena,
amanussena vā pana;
Jāto me mā mari putto,
kuto labbhā alabbhiyaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 216Ms 19Pv_237Msdiv 216Na mantā mūlabhesajjā,
osadhehi dhanena vā;
Sakkā ānayituṃ kaṇha,
yaṃ petamanusocasi.

SC 11SC Verse 217PTS 2nd ed. 26 Ms 19Pv_238Msdiv 217Mahaddhanā mahābhogā,
raṭṭhavantopi khattiyā;
Pahūtadhanadhaññāse,
tepi no ajarāmarā.

SC 12SC Verse 218Ms 19Pv_239Msdiv 218Khattiyā brāhmaṇā vessā,
suddā caṇḍālapukkusā;
Ete caññe ca jātiyā,
tepi no ajarāmarā.

SC 13SC Verse 219Ms 19Pv_240Msdiv 219Ye mantaṃ parivattenti,
chaḷaṅgaṃ brahmacintitaṃ;
Ete caññe ca vijjāya,
tepi no ajarāmarā.

SC 14SC Verse 220Ms 19Pv_241Msdiv 220Isayo vāpi ye santā,
saññatattā tapassino;
Sarīraṃ tepi kālena,
vijahanti tapassino.

SC 15SC Verse 221Ms 19Pv_242Msdiv 221Bhāvitattā arahanto,
katakiccā anāsavā;
Nikkhipanti imaṃ dehaṃ,
puññapāpaparikkhayā”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 222Ms 19Pv_243Msdiv 222“Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 17SC Verse 223Ms 19Pv_244Msdiv 223Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
sokaṃ hadayanissitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 18SC Verse 224Ms 19Pv_245Msdiv 224Svāhaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
sītibhūtosmi nibbuto;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna bhātika”.

SC 19SC Verse 225Ms 19Pv_246Msdiv 225Evaṃ karonti sappaññā,
ye honti anukampakā;
Nivattayanti sokamhā,
ghaṭo jeṭṭhaṃva bhātaraṃ.

SC 20SC Verse 226Ms 19Pv_247Msdiv 226Yassa etādisā honti,
amaccā paricārakā;
Subhāsitena anventi,
ghaṭo jeṭṭhaṃva bhātaranti.

SC 21Ms 19Pv_248Kaṇhapetavatthu chaṭṭhaṃ.
