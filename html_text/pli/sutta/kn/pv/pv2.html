2. Sūkaramukhapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 4PTS 2nd ed. 2 Ms 19Pv_6SC 1SC Verse 4PTS 2nd ed. 2 Ms 19Pv_6Msdiv 4“Kāyo te sabbasovaṇṇo,
sabbā obhāsate disā;
Mukhaṃ te sūkarasseva,
kiṃ kammamakarī pure”.

SC 2SC Verse 5Ms 19Pv_7SC 2SC Verse 5Ms 19Pv_7Msdiv 5“Kāyena saññato āsiṃ,
vācāyāsimasaññato;
Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,
yathā passasi nārada.

SC 3SC Verse 6Ms 19Pv_8Msdiv 6SC 3SC Verse 6Ms 19Pv_8Msdiv 6Taṃ tyāhaṃ nārada brūmi,
Sāmaṃ diṭṭhamidaṃ tayā;
Mākāsi mukhasā pāpaṃ,
Mā kho sūkaramukho ahū”ti.

SC 4SC 4Ms 19Pv_9Sūkaramukhapetavatthu dutiyaṃ.
