17. Maṭṭhakuṇḍalīpetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 186Ms 19Pv_206Msdiv 186“Alaṅkato maṭṭhakuṇḍalī,
Māladhārī haricandanussado;
Bāhā paggayha kandasi,
Vanamajjhe kiṃ dukkhito tuvan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 187Ms 19Pv_207Msdiv 187“Sovaṇṇamayo pabhassaro,
Uppanno rathapañjaro mama;
Tassa cakkayugaṃ na vindāmi,
Tena dukkhena jahāmi jīvitan”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 188Ms 19Pv_208Msdiv 188“Sovaṇṇamayaṃ maṇimayaṃ,
Lohitakamayaṃ atha rūpiyamayaṃ;
Ācikkha me bhaddamāṇava,
Cakkayugaṃ paṭipādayāmi te”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 189Ms 19Pv_209Msdiv 189So māṇavo tassa pāvadi,
“Candasūriyā ubhayettha dissare;
Sovaṇṇamayo ratho mama,
Tena cakkayugena sobhatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 190Ms 19Pv_210Msdiv 190“Bālo kho tvaṃ asi māṇava,
Yo tvaṃ patthayase apatthiyaṃ;
Maññāmi tuvaṃ marissasi,
Na hi tvaṃ lacchasi candasūriye”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 191Ms 19Pv_211Msdiv 191“Gamanāgamanampi dissati,
Vaṇṇadhātu ubhayattha vīthiyā;
Peto kālakato na dissati,
Ko nidha kandataṃ bālyataro”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 192Ms 19Pv_212Msdiv 192“Saccaṃ kho vadesi māṇava,
Ahameva kandataṃ bālyataro;
Candaṃ viya dārako rudaṃ,
Petaṃ kālakatābhipatthayinti.

SC 8SC Verse 193Ms 19Pv_213Msdiv 193Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 194Ms 19Pv_214Msdiv 194Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
sokaṃ hadayanissitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 10SC Verse 195Ms 19Pv_215Msdiv 195Svāhaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
sītibhūtosmi nibbuto;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna māṇavā”ti.

SC 11SC Verse 196Ms 19Pv_216Msdiv 196“Devatā nusi gandhabbo,
adu sakko purindado;
Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayan”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 197Ms 19Pv_217Msdiv 197“Yañca kandasi yañca rodasi,
Puttaṃ āḷāhane sayaṃ dahitvā;
Svāhaṃ kusalaṃ karitvā kammaṃ,
Tidasānaṃ sahabyataṃ gato”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 198Ms 19Pv_218Msdiv 198“Appaṃ vā bahuṃ vā nāddasāma,
Dānaṃ dadantassa sake agāre;
Uposathakammaṃ vā tādisaṃ,
Kena kammena gatosi devalokan”ti.

SC 14SC Verse 199Ms 19Pv_219Msdiv 199“Ābādhikohaṃ dukkhito gilāno,
Āturarūpomhi sake nivesane;
Buddhaṃ vigatarajaṃ vitiṇṇakaṅkhaṃ,
Addakkhiṃ sugataṃ anomapaññaṃ.

SC 15SC Verse 200Ms 19Pv_220Msdiv 200Svāhaṃ muditamano pasannacitto,
Añjaliṃ akariṃ tathāgatassa;
Tāhaṃ kusalaṃ karitvāna kammaṃ,
Tidasānaṃ sahabyataṃ gato”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 201Ms 19Pv_221Msdiv 201“Acchariyaṃ vata abbhutaṃ vata,
Añjalikammassa ayamīdiso vipāko;
Ahampi muditamano pasannacitto,
Ajjeva buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ vajāmī”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 202Ms 19Pv_222Msdiv 202“Ajjeva buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ vajāhi,
Dhammañca saṃghañca pasannacitto;
Tatheva sikkhāya padāni pañca,
Akhaṇḍaphullāni samādiyassu.

SC 18SC Verse 203Ms 19Pv_223Msdiv 203Pāṇātipātā viramassu khippaṃ,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayassu;
Amajjapo mā ca musā bhaṇāhi,
Sakena dārena ca hohi tuṭṭho”ti.

SC 19SC Verse 204Ms 19Pv_224Msdiv 204“Atthakāmosi me yakkha,
hitakāmosi devate;
Karomi tuyhaṃ vacanaṃ,
tvaṃsi ācariyo mamāti.

SC 20SC Verse 205Ms 19Pv_225Msdiv 205Upemi saraṇaṃ buddhaṃ,
dhammañcāpi anuttaraṃ;
Saṃghañca naradevassa,
gacchāmi saraṇaṃ ahaṃ.

SC 21SC Verse 206Ms 19Pv_226Msdiv 206Pāṇātipātā viramāmi khippaṃ,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayāmi;
Amajjapo no ca musā bhaṇāmi,
Sakena dārena ca homi tuṭṭho”ti.

SC 22Ms 19Pv_227Maṭṭhakuṇḍalīpetavatthu pañcamaṃ.
