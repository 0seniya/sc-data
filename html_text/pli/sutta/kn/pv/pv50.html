50. Seṭṭhiputtapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 804Ms 19Pv_864Msdiv 802“Saṭṭhivassasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Niraye paccamānānaṃ,
kadā anto bhavissati.

SC 2SC Verse 805Ms 19Pv_865Msdiv 803Natthi anto kuto anto,
na anto paṭidissati;
Tathā hi pakataṃ pāpaṃ,
tuyhaṃ mayhañca mārisā.

SC 3SC Verse 806PTS 2nd ed. 94 Ms 19Pv_866Msdiv 804Dujjīvitamajīvamha,
ye sante na dadamhase;
Santesu deyyadhammesu,
dīpaṃ nākamha attano.

SC 4SC Verse 807Ms 19Pv_867Msdiv 805Sohaṃ nūna ito gantvā,
yoniṃ laddhāna mānusiṃ;
Vadaññū sīlasampanno,
kāhāmi kusalaṃ bahun”ti.

SC 5Ms 19Pv_868Seṭṭhiputtapetavatthu pannarasamaṃ.
