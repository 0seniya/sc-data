10. Khallāṭiyapetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 58PTS 2nd ed. 9 Ms 19Pv_68Msdiv 58“Kā nu antovimānasmiṃ,
tiṭṭhantī nūpanikkhami;
Upanikkhamassu bhadde,
passāma taṃ bahiṭṭhitan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 59Ms 19Pv_69Msdiv 59“Aṭṭīyāmi harāyāmi,
naggā nikkhamituṃ bahi;
Kesehamhi paṭicchannā,
puññaṃ me appakaṃ katan”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 60Ms 19Pv_70Msdiv 60“Handuttarīyaṃ dadāmi te,
idaṃ dussaṃ nivāsaya;
Idaṃ dussaṃ nivāsetvā,
ehi nikkhama sobhane;
Upanikkhamassu bhadde,
passāma taṃ bahiṭṭhitan”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 61Ms 19Pv_71Msdiv 61“Hatthena hatthe te dinnaṃ,
na mayhaṃ upakappati;
Esetthupāsako saddho,
sammāsambuddhasāvako.

SC 5SC Verse 62Ms 19Pv_72Msdiv 62Etaṃ acchādayitvāna,
mama dakkhiṇamādisa;
Tathāhaṃ sukhitā hessaṃ,
sabbakāmasamiddhinī”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 63Ms 19Pv_73Msdiv 63Tañca te nhāpayitvāna,
vilimpetvāna vāṇijā;
Vatthehacchādayitvāna,
tassā dakkhiṇamādisuṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 64Ms 19Pv_74Msdiv 64Samanantarānuddiṭṭhe,
vipāko udapajjatha;
Bhojanacchādanapānīyaṃ,
dakkhiṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 65Ms 19Pv_75Msdiv 65Tato suddhā sucivasanā,
kāsikuttamadhārinī;
Hasantī vimānā nikkhami,
dakkhiṇāya idaṃ phalanti.

SC 9SC Verse 66Ms 19Pv_76Msdiv 66“Sucittarūpaṃ ruciraṃ,
vimānaṃ te pabhāsati;
Devate pucchitācikkha,
kissa kammassidaṃ phalan”ti.

SC 10SC Verse 67Ms 19Pv_77Msdiv 67“Bhikkhuno caramānassa,
doṇinimmajjaniṃ ahaṃ;
Adāsiṃ ujubhūtassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 11SC Verse 68Ms 19Pv_78Msdiv 68Tassa kammassa kusalassa,
vipākaṃ dīghamantaraṃ;
Anubhomi vimānasmiṃ,
tañca dāni parittakaṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 69PTS 2nd ed. 10 Ms 19Pv_79Msdiv 69Uddhaṃ catūhi māsehi,
kālakiriyā bhavissati;
Ekantakaṭukaṃ ghoraṃ,
nirayaṃ papatissahaṃ.

SC 13SC Verse 70Ms 19Pv_80Msdiv 70Catukkaṇṇaṃ catudvāraṃ,
vibhattaṃ bhāgaso mitaṃ;
Ayopākārapariyantaṃ,
ayasā paṭikujjitaṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 71Ms 19Pv_81Msdiv 71Tassa ayomayā bhūmi,
jalitā tejasā yutā;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
pharitvā tiṭṭhati sabbadā.

SC 15SC Verse 72Ms 19Pv_82Msdiv 72Tatthāhaṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ,
dukkhaṃ vedissa vedanaṃ;
Phalañca pāpakammassa,
tasmā socāmahaṃ bhusan”ti.

SC 16Ms 19Pv_83Khallāṭiyapetivatthu dasamaṃ.
