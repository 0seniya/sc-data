44. Gūthakhādakapetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 776PTS 2nd ed. 90 Ms 19Pv_830SC 1SC Verse 776PTS 2nd ed. 90 Ms 19Pv_830Msdiv 774“Gūthakūpato uggantvā,
kā nu dīnā patiṭṭhasi;
Nissaṃsayaṃ pāpakammantā,
kiṃ nu saddahase tuvan”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 777Ms 19Pv_831SC 2SC Verse 777Ms 19Pv_831Msdiv 775“Ahaṃ bhadante petīmhi,
duggatā yamalokikā;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 778Ms 19Pv_832SC 3SC Verse 778Ms 19Pv_832Msdiv 776“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
idaṃ dukkhaṃ nigacchasī”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 779Ms 19Pv_833SC 4SC Verse 779Ms 19Pv_833Msdiv 777“Ahu āvāsiko mayhaṃ,
issukī kulamaccharī;
Ajjhāsito mayhaṃ ghare,
kadariyo paribhāsako.

SC 5SC Verse 780Ms 19Pv_834SC 5SC Verse 780Ms 19Pv_834Msdiv 778Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bhikkhavo paribhāsisaṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
petalokaṃ ito gatā”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 781Ms 19Pv_835SC 6SC Verse 781Ms 19Pv_835Msdiv 779“Amitto mittavaṇṇena,
yo te āsi kulūpako;
Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,
kiṃ nu pecca gatiṃ gato”ti.

SC 7SC Verse 782Ms 19Pv_836SC 7SC Verse 782Ms 19Pv_836Msdiv 780“Tassevāhaṃ pāpakammassa,
sīse tiṭṭhāmi matthake;
So ca paravisayaṃ patto,
mameva paricārako.

SC 8SC Verse 783Ms 19Pv_837SC 8SC Verse 783Ms 19Pv_837Msdiv 781Yaṃ bhadante hadantaññe,
etaṃ me hoti bhojanaṃ;
Ahañca kho yaṃ hadāmi,
etaṃ so upajīvatī”ti.
