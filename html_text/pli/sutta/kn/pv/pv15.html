15. Mattāpetivatthu

SC 1SC Verse 134Ms 19Pv_152Msdiv 134“Naggā dubbaṇṇarūpāsi,
kisā dhamanisanthatā;
Upphāsulike kisike,
kā nu tvaṃ idha tiṭṭhasī”ti.

SC 2SC Verse 135Ms 19Pv_153Msdiv 135“Ahaṃ mattā tuvaṃ tissā,
sapattī te pure ahuṃ;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 136Ms 19Pv_154Msdiv 136“Kiṃ nu kāyena vācāya,
manasā dukkaṭaṃ kataṃ;
Kissa kammavipākena,
petalokaṃ ito gatā”ti.

SC 4SC Verse 137PTS 2nd ed. 20 Ms 19Pv_155Msdiv 137“Caṇḍī ca pharusā cāsiṃ,
issukī maccharī saṭhā;
Tāhaṃ duruttaṃ vatvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 138Ms 19Pv_156Msdiv 138“Sabbaṃ ahampi jānāmi,
yathā tvaṃ caṇḍikā ahu;
Aññañca kho taṃ pucchāmi,
kenāsi paṃsukunthitā”ti.

SC 6SC Verse 139Ms 19Pv_157Msdiv 139“Sīsaṃnhātā tuvaṃ āsi,
sucivatthā alaṅkatā;
Ahañca kho adhimattaṃ,
samalaṅkatatarā tayā.

SC 7SC Verse 140Ms 19Pv_158Msdiv 140Tassā me pekkhamānāya,
sāmikena samantayi;
Tato me issā vipulā,
kodho me samajāyatha.

SC 8SC Verse 141Ms 19Pv_159Msdiv 141Tato paṃsuṃ gahetvāna,
paṃsunā taṃ hi okiriṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
tenamhi paṃsukunthitā”ti.

SC 9SC Verse 142Ms 19Pv_160Msdiv 142“Sabbaṃ ahampi jānāmi,
paṃsunā maṃ tvamokiri;
Aññañca kho taṃ pucchāmi,
kena khajjasi kacchuyā”ti.

SC 10SC Verse 143Ms 19Pv_161Msdiv 143“Bhesajjahārī ubhayo,
vanantaṃ agamimhase;
Tvañca bhesajjamāhari,
ahañca kapikacchuno.

SC 11SC Verse 144Ms 19Pv_162Msdiv 144Tassā tyājānamānāya,
seyyaṃ tyāhaṃ samokiriṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
tena khajjāmi kacchuyā”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 145Ms 19Pv_163Msdiv 145“Sabbaṃ ahampi jānāmi,
seyyaṃ me tvaṃ samokiri;
Aññañca kho taṃ pucchāmi,
kenāsi naggiyā tuvan”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 146Ms 19Pv_164Msdiv 146“Sahāyānaṃ samayo āsi,
ñātīnaṃ samitī ahu;
Tvañca āmantitā āsi,
sasāminī no ca kho ahaṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 147Ms 19Pv_165Msdiv 147Tassā tyājānamānāya,
dussaṃ tyāhaṃ apānudiṃ;
Tassa kammavipākena,
tenamhi naggiyā ahan”ti.

SC 15SC Verse 148Ms 19Pv_166Msdiv 148“Sabbaṃ ahampi jānāmi,
dussaṃ me tvaṃ apānudi;
Aññañca kho taṃ pucchāmi,
kenāsi gūthagandhinī”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 149Ms 19Pv_167Msdiv 149“Tava gandhañca mālañca,
paccagghañca vilepanaṃ;
Gūthakūpe atāresiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Tassa kammavipākena,
tenamhi gūthagandhinī”ti.

SC 17SC Verse 150Ms 19Pv_168Msdiv 150“Sabbaṃ ahampi jānāmi,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ tayā;
Aññañca kho taṃ pucchāmi,
kenāsi duggatā tuvan”ti.

SC 18SC Verse 151Ms 19Pv_169Msdiv 151“Ubhinnaṃ samakaṃ āsi,
yaṃ gehe vijjate dhanaṃ;
Santesu deyyadhammesu,
dīpaṃ nākāsimattano;
Tassa kammavipākena,
tenamhi duggatā ahaṃ.

SC 19SC Verse 152PTS 2nd ed. 21 Ms 19Pv_170Msdiv 152Tadeva maṃ tvaṃ avaca,
‘pāpakammaṃ nisevasi;
Na hi pāpehi kammehi,
sulabhā hoti suggatī’”ti.

SC 20SC Verse 153Ms 19Pv_171Msdiv 153“Vāmato maṃ tvaṃ paccesi,
athopi maṃ usūyasi;
Passa pāpānaṃ kammānaṃ,
vipāko hoti yādiso.

SC 21SC Verse 154Ms 19Pv_172Msdiv 154Te gharā tā ca dāsiyo,
tānevābharaṇānime;
Te aññe paricārenti,
na bhogā honti sassatā.

SC 22SC Verse 155Ms 19Pv_173Msdiv 155Idāni bhūtassa pitā,
āpaṇā gehamehiti;
Appeva te dade kiñci,
mā su tāva ito agā”ti.

SC 23SC Verse 156Ms 19Pv_174Msdiv 156“Naggā dubbaṇṇarūpāmhi,
kisā dhamanisanthatā;
Kopīnametaṃ itthīnaṃ,
mā maṃ bhūtapitāddasā”ti.

SC 24SC Verse 157Ms 19Pv_175Msdiv 157“Handa kiṃ vā tyāhaṃ dammi,
Kiṃ vā tedha karomahaṃ;
Yena tvaṃ sukhitā assa,
Sabbakāmasamiddhinī”ti.

SC 25SC Verse 158Ms 19Pv_176Msdiv 158“Cattāro bhikkhū saṃghato,
cattāro pana puggale;
Aṭṭha bhikkhū bhojayitvā,
mama dakkhiṇamādisa;
Tadāhaṃ sukhitā hessaṃ,
sabbakāmasamiddhinī”ti.

SC 26SC Verse 159Ms 19Pv_177Msdiv 159Sādhūti sā paṭissutvā,
bhojayitvāṭṭha bhikkhavo;
Vatthehacchādayitvāna,
tassā dakkhiṇamādisī.

SC 27SC Verse 160Ms 19Pv_178Msdiv 160Samanantarānuddiṭṭhe,
vipāko udapajjatha;
Bhojanacchādanapānīyaṃ,
dakkhiṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 28SC Verse 161Ms 19Pv_179Msdiv 161Tato suddhā sucivasanā,
kāsikuttamadhārinī;
Vicittavatthābharaṇā,
sapattiṃ upasaṅkami.

SC 29SC Verse 162Ms 19Pv_180Msdiv 162“Abhikkantena vaṇṇena,
yā tvaṃ tiṭṭhasi devate;
Obhāsentī disā sabbā,
osadhī viya tārakā.

SC 30SC Verse 163Ms 19Pv_181Msdiv 163Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,
kena te idha mijjhati;
Uppajjanti ca te bhogā,
ye keci manaso piyā.

SC 31SC Verse 164Ms 19Pv_182Msdiv 164Pucchāmi taṃ devi mahānubhāve,
Manussabhūtā kimakāsi puññaṃ;
Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvā,
Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.

SC 32SC Verse 165Ms 19Pv_183Msdiv 165“Ahaṃ mattā tuvaṃ tissā,
sapattī te pure ahuṃ;
Pāpakammaṃ karitvāna,
petalokaṃ ito gatā.

SC 33SC Verse 166Ms 19Pv_184Msdiv 166Tava dinnena dānena,
modāmi akutobhayā;
Ciraṃ jīvāhi bhagini,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Asokaṃ virajaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
āvāsaṃ vasavattinaṃ.

SC 34SC Verse 167PTS 2nd ed. 22 Ms 19Pv_185Msdiv 167Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
Dānaṃ datvāna sobhane;
Vineyya maccheramalaṃ samūlaṃ,
Aninditā saggamupehi ṭhānan”ti.

SC 35Ms 19Pv_186Mattāpetivatthu tatiyaṃ.
