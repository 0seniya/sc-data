8. Goṇapetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 46PTS 2nd ed. 7 Ms 19Pv_54SC 1SC Verse 46PTS 2nd ed. 7 Ms 19Pv_54Msdiv 46“Kiṃ nu ummattarūpova,
lāyitvā haritaṃ tiṇaṃ;
Khāda khādāti lapasi,
gatasattaṃ jaraggavaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 47Ms 19Pv_55SC 2SC Verse 47Ms 19Pv_55Msdiv 47Na hi annena pānena,
mato goṇo samuṭṭhahe;
Tvaṃsi bālo ca dummedho,
yathā taññova dummatī”ti.

SC 3SC Verse 48Ms 19Pv_56SC 3SC Verse 48Ms 19Pv_56Msdiv 48“Ime pādā idaṃ sīsaṃ,
ayaṃ kāyo savāladhi;
Nettā tatheva tiṭṭhanti,
ayaṃ goṇo samuṭṭhahe.

SC 4SC Verse 49Ms 19Pv_57SC 4SC Verse 49Ms 19Pv_57Msdiv 49Nāyyakassa hatthapādā,
kāyo sīsañca dissati;
Rudaṃ mattikathūpasmiṃ,
nanu tvaññeva dummatī”ti.

SC 5SC Verse 50Ms 19Pv_58SC 5SC Verse 50Ms 19Pv_58Msdiv 50“Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 51Ms 19Pv_59Msdiv 51SC 6SC Verse 51Ms 19Pv_59Msdiv 51Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
sokaṃ hadayanissitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
pitusokaṃ apānudi.

SC 7SC Verse 52Ms 19Pv_60SC 7SC Verse 52Ms 19Pv_60Msdiv 52Svāhaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
sītibhūtosmi nibbuto;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna māṇava”.

SC 8SC Verse 53Ms 19Pv_61SC 8SC Verse 53Ms 19Pv_61Msdiv 53Evaṃ karonti sappaññā,
ye honti anukampakā;
Vinivattayanti sokamhā,
sujāto pitaraṃ yathāti.
