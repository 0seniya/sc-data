25. Ubbaripetavatthu

SC 1SC Verse 369PTS 2nd ed. 44 Ms 19Pv_397Msdiv 368Ahu rājā brahmadatto,
pañcālānaṃ rathesabho;
Ahorattānamaccayā,
rājā kālamakrubbatha.

SC 2SC Verse 370Ms 19Pv_398Msdiv 369Tassa āḷāhanaṃ gantvā,
bhariyā kandati ubbarī;
Brahmadattaṃ apassantī,
brahmadattāti kandati.

SC 3SC Verse 371Ms 19Pv_399Msdiv 370Isi ca tattha āgacchi,
sampannacaraṇo muni;
So ca tattha apucchittha,
ye tattha susamāgatā.

SC 4SC Verse 372Ms 19Pv_400Msdiv 371“Kassa idaṃ āḷāhanaṃ,
nānāgandhasameritaṃ;
Kassāyaṃ kandati bhariyā,
ito dūragataṃ patiṃ;
Brahmadattaṃ apassantī,
‘brahmadattā’ti kandati”.

SC 5SC Verse 373Ms 19Pv_401Msdiv 372Te ca tattha viyākaṃsu,
ye tattha susamāgatā;
“Brahmadattassa bhadante,
brahmadattassa mārisa.

SC 6SC Verse 374Ms 19Pv_402Msdiv 373Tassa idaṃ āḷāhanaṃ,
nānāgandhasameritaṃ;
Tassāyaṃ kandati bhariyā,
ito dūragataṃ patiṃ;
Brahmadattaṃ apassantī,
‘brahmadattā’ti kandati”.

SC 7SC Verse 375Ms 19Pv_403Msdiv 374“Chaḷāsītisahassāni,
brahmadattassanāmakā;
Imasmiṃ āḷāhane daḍḍhā,
tesaṃ kamanusocasī”ti.

SC 8SC Verse 376Ms 19Pv_404Msdiv 375“Yo rājā cūḷanīputto,
pañcālānaṃ rathesabho;
Taṃ bhante anusocāmi,
bhattāraṃ sabbakāmadan”ti.

SC 9SC Verse 377Ms 19Pv_405Msdiv 376“Sabbe vāhesuṃ rājāno,
brahmadattassanāmakā;
Sabbeva cūḷanīputtā,
pañcālānaṃ rathesabhā.

SC 10SC Verse 378PTS 2nd ed. 45 Ms 19Pv_406Msdiv 377Sabbesaṃ anupubbena,
mahesittamakārayi;
Kasmā purimake hitvā,
pacchimaṃ anusocasī”ti.

SC 11SC Verse 379Ms 19Pv_407Msdiv 378“Ātume itthibhūtāya,
dīgharattāya mārisa;
Yassā me itthibhūtāya,
saṃsāre bahubhāsasī”ti.

SC 12SC Verse 380Ms 19Pv_408Msdiv 379“Ahu itthī ahu puriso,
pasuyonimpi āgamā;
Evametaṃ atītānaṃ,
pariyanto na dissatī”ti.

SC 13SC Verse 381Ms 19Pv_409Msdiv 380“Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 14SC Verse 382Ms 19Pv_410Msdiv 381Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
sokaṃ hadayanissitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetāya,
patisokaṃ apānudi.

SC 15SC Verse 383Ms 19Pv_411Msdiv 382Sāhaṃ abbūḷhasallāsmi,
sītibhūtāsmi nibbutā;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvā mahāmunī”ti.

SC 16SC Verse 384Ms 19Pv_412Msdiv 383Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
samaṇassa subhāsitaṃ;
Pattacīvaramādāya,
pabbaji anagāriyaṃ.

SC 17SC Verse 385Ms 19Pv_413Msdiv 384Sā ca pabbajitā santā,
agārasmā anagāriyaṃ;
Mettacittaṃ abhāvesi,
brahmalokūpapattiyā.

SC 18SC Verse 386Ms 19Pv_414Msdiv 385Gāmā gāmaṃ vicarantī,
nigame rājadhāniyo;
Uruvelā nāma so gāmo,
yattha kālamakrubbatha.

SC 19SC Verse 387Ms 19Pv_415Msdiv 386Mettacittaṃ ābhāvetvā,
brahmalokūpapattiyā;
Itthicittaṃ virājetvā,
brahmalokūpagā ahūti.

SC 20Ms 19Pv_416Ubbaripetavatthu terasamaṃ.

SC 21Ms 19Pv_417Ubbarivaggo dutiyo.

SC 22Ms 19Pv_418Tassuddānaṃ

SC 23Ms 19Pv_419Mocakaṃ mātā mattā ca,
Nandā kuṇḍalīnā ghaṭo;
Dve seṭṭhī tunnavāyo ca,
Uttara suttakaṇṇa ubbarīti.
