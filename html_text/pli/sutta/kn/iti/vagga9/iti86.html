• Itivuttaka 86

Dhammānudhammapaṭipannasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_597Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_598“Dhammānudhammapaṭipannassa bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti veyyākaraṇāya—dhammānudhammapaṭipannoyanti bhāsamāno dhammaññeva bhāsati no adhammaṃ, vitakkayamāno vā dhammavitakkaññeva vitakketi no adhammavitakkaṃ, tadubhayaṃ vā pana abhinivejjetvā upekkhako viharati sato sampajāno”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 179PTS vp Pali 82Ms 18It_599“Dhammārāmo dhammarato,
dhammaṃ anuvicintayaṃ;
Dhammaṃ anussaraṃ bhikkhu,
saddhammā na parihāyati.

SC 4SC Verse 180Ms 18It_600Caraṃ vā yadi vā tiṭṭhaṃ,
nisinno uda vā sayaṃ;
Ajjhattaṃ samayaṃ cittaṃ,
santimevādhigacchatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_601Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Sattamaṃ.
