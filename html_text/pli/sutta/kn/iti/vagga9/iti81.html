• Itivuttaka 81

Sakkārasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_541Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_542“Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā sakkārena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā.

SC 3Ms 18It_543Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā asakkārena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 74Ms 18It_544Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā sakkārena ca asakkārena ca tadubhayena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā.

SC 5Ms 18It_545Taṃ kho panāhaṃ, bhikkhave, nāññassa samaṇassa vā brāhmaṇassa vā sutvā vadāmi; () api ca, bhikkhave, yadeva me sāmaṃ ñātaṃ sāmaṃ diṭṭhaṃ sāmaṃ viditaṃ tamevāhaṃ vadāmi.

SC 6Ms 18It_546Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā sakkārena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā.

SC 7Ms 18It_547Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā asakkārena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā.

SC 8Ms 18It_548Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, sattā sakkārena ca asakkārena ca tadubhayena abhibhūtā, pariyādinnacittā, kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapannā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 9SC Verse 162Ms 18It_549“Yassa sakkariyamānassa,
asakkārena cūbhayaṃ;
Samādhi na vikampati,
appamādavihārino.

SC 10SC Verse 163PTS vp Pali 75Ms 18It_550Taṃ jhāyinaṃ sātatikaṃ,
sukhumadiṭṭhivipassakaṃ;
Upādānakkhayārāmaṃ,
āhu sappuriso itī”ti.

SC 11Ms 18It_551Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dutiyaṃ.
