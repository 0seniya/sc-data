• Itivuttaka 88

Antarāmalasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_609Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_610“Tayome, bhikkhave, antarāmalā antarāamittā antarāsapattā antarāvadhakā antarāpaccatthikā. Katame tayo? Lobho, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko. Doso, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko. Moho, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo antarāmalā antarāamittā antarāsapattā antarāvadhakā antarāpaccatthikā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 182Ms 18It_611“Anatthajanano lobho,
lobho cittappakopano;
Bhayamantarato jātaṃ,
taṃ jano nāvabujjhati.

SC 4SC Verse 183Ms 18It_612Luddho atthaṃ na jānāti,
luddho dhammaṃ na passati;
Andhatamaṃ tadā hoti,
yaṃ lobho sahate naraṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 184Ms 18It_613Yo ca lobhaṃ pahantvāna,
lobhaneyye na lubbhati;
Lobho pahīyate tamhā,
udabindūva pokkharā.

SC 6SC Verse 185Ms 18It_614Anatthajanano doso,
doso cittappakopano;
Bhayamantarato jātaṃ,
taṃ jano nāvabujjhati.

SC 7SC Verse 186Ms 18It_615Duṭṭho atthaṃ na jānāti,
duṭṭho dhammaṃ na passati;
Andhatamaṃ tadā hoti,
yaṃ doso sahate naraṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 187Ms 18It_616Yo ca dosaṃ pahantvāna,
dosaneyye na dussati;
Doso pahīyate tamhā,
tālapakkaṃva bandhanā.

SC 9SC Verse 188Ms 18It_617Anatthajanano moho,
moho cittappakopano;
Bhayamantarato jātaṃ,
taṃ jano nāvabujjhati.

SC 10SC Verse 189Ms 18It_618Mūḷho atthaṃ na jānāti,
mūḷho dhammaṃ na passati;
Andhatamaṃ tadā hoti,
yaṃ moho sahate naraṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 190Ms 18It_619Yo ca mohaṃ pahantvāna,
mohaneyye na muyhati;
Mohaṃ vihanti so sabbaṃ,
ādiccovudayaṃ taman”ti.

SC 12Ms 18It_620Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
