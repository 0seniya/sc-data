• Itivuttaka 89

Devadattasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_622Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_623“Tīhi, bhikkhave, asaddhammehi abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho. Katamehi tīhi? Pāpicchatāya, bhikkhave, abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho. Pāpamittatāya, bhikkhave, abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho. Sati kho pana uttarikaraṇīye oramattakena uttarikaraṇīye oramattakena visesādhigamena antarā vosānaṃ āpādi. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhi asaddhammehi abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 191Ms 18It_624“Mā jātu koci lokasmiṃ,
pāpiccho udapajjatha;
Tadamināpi jānātha,
pāpicchānaṃ yathā gati.

SC 4SC Verse 192PTS vp Pali 86Ms 18It_625Paṇḍitoti samaññāto,
bhāvitattoti sammato;
Jalaṃva yasasā aṭṭhā,
devadattoti vissuto.

SC 5SC Verse 193Ms 18It_626So samānamanuciṇṇo,
āsajja naṃ tathāgataṃ;
Avīcinirayaṃ patto,
catudvāraṃ bhayānakaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 194Ms 18It_627Aduṭṭhassa hi yo dubbhe,
pāpakammaṃ akubbato;
Tameva pāpaṃ phusati,
duṭṭhacittaṃ anādaraṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 195Ms 18It_628Samuddaṃ visakumbhena,
yo maññeyya padūsituṃ;
Na so tena padūseyya,
bhesmā hi udadhi mahā.

SC 8SC Verse 196PTS vp Pali 87Ms 18It_629Evamevaṃ tathāgataṃ,
yo vādena vihiṃsati;
Sammaggataṃ santacittaṃ,
vādo tamhi na rūhati.

SC 9SC Verse 197Ms 18It_630Tādisaṃ mittaṃ kubbetha,
tañca seveyya paṇḍito;
Yassa maggānugo bhikkhu,
khayaṃ dukkhassa pāpuṇe”ti.

SC 10Ms 18It_631Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dasamaṃ.

Catuttho vaggo.

SC 11Ms 18It_635Vitakkāsakkārasadda,
cavanaloke asubhaṃ;
Dhammaandhakāramalaṃ,
devadattena te dasāti.
