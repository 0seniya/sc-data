• Itivuttaka 80

Vitakkasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_535Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_536“Tayome, bhikkhave, akusalavitakkā. Katame tayo? Anavaññattipaṭisaṃyutto vitakko, lābhasakkārasilokapaṭisaṃyutto vitakko, parānuddayatāpaṭisaṃyutto vitakko. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo akusalavitakkā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 160Ms 18It_537“Anavaññattisaṃyutto,
lābhasakkāragāravo;
Sahanandī amaccehi,
ārā saṃyojanakkhayā.

SC 4SC Verse 161Ms 18It_538Yo ca puttapasuṃ hitvā,
Vivāhe saṃharāni ca;
Bhabbo so tādiso bhikkhu,
Phuṭṭhuṃ sambodhimuttaman”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_539Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
