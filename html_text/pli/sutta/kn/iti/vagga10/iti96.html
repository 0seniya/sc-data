• Itivuttaka 96

Kāmayogasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_685Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_686“Kāmayogayutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogayutto āgāmī hoti āgantā itthattaṃ. Kāmayogavisaṃyutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogayutto anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṃ. Kāmayogavisaṃyutto, bhikkhave, bhavayogavisaṃyutto arahā hoti, khīṇāsavo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 220PTS vp Pali 96Ms 18It_687“Kāmayogena saṃyuttā,
bhavayogena cūbhayaṃ;
Sattā gacchanti saṃsāraṃ,
jātimaraṇagāmino.

SC 4SC Verse 221Ms 18It_688Ye ca kāme pahantvāna,
appattā āsavakkhayaṃ;
Bhavayogena saṃyuttā,
anāgāmīti vuccare.

SC 5SC Verse 222Ms 18It_689Ye ca kho chinnasaṃsayā,
khīṇamānapunabbhavā;
Te ve pāraṅgatā loke,
ye pattā āsavakkhayan”ti.

SC 6Ms 18It_690Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Sattamaṃ.

Tatiyabhāṇavāraṃ.
