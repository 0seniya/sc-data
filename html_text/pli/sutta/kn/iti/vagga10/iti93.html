• Itivuttaka 93

Aggisutta

SC 1Ms 18It_661Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_662“Tayome, bhikkhave, aggī. Katame tayo? Rāgaggi, dosaggi, mohaggi—ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo aggī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 208Ms 18It_663“Rāgaggi dahati macce,
ratte kāmesu mucchite;
Dosaggi pana byāpanne,
nare pāṇātipātino.

SC 4SC Verse 209Ms 18It_664Mohaggi pana sammūḷhe,
ariyadhamme akovide;
Ete aggī ajānantā,
sakkāyābhiratā pajā.

SC 5SC Verse 210Ms 18It_665Te vaḍḍhayanti nirayaṃ,
tiracchānañca yoniyo;
Asuraṃ pettivisayaṃ,
amuttā mārabandhanā.

SC 6SC Verse 211Ms 18It_666Ye ca rattindivā yuttā,
sammāsambuddhasāsane;
Te nibbāpenti rāgaggiṃ,
niccaṃ asubhasaññino.

SC 7SC Verse 212Ms 18It_667Dosaggiṃ pana mettāya,
nibbāpenti naruttamā;
Mohaggiṃ pana paññāya,
yāyaṃ nibbedhagāminī.

SC 8SC Verse 213Ms 18It_668Te nibbāpetvā nipakā,
rattindivamatanditā;
Asesaṃ parinibbanti,
asesaṃ dukkhamaccaguṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 214Ms 18It_669Ariyaddasā vedaguno,
sammadaññāya paṇḍitā;
Jātikkhayamabhiññāya,
nāgacchanti punabbhavan”ti.

SC 10Ms 18It_670Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Catutthaṃ.
