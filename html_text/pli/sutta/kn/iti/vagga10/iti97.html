• Itivuttaka 97

Kalyāṇasīlasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_693Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_694“Kalyāṇasīlo, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇadhammo kalyāṇapañño imasmiṃ dhammavinaye ‘kevalī vusitavā uttamapuriso’ti vuccati—

SC 3Ms 18It_695Kathañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇasīlo hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sīlavā hoti, pātimokkhasaṃvarasaṃvuto viharati, ācāragocarasampanno aṇumattesu vajjesu bhayadassāvī, samādāya sikkhati sikkhāpadesu. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇasīlo hoti. Iti kalyāṇasīlo.

SC 4Ms 18It_696Kalyāṇadhammo ca kathaṃ hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sattannaṃ bodhipakkhiyānaṃ dhammānaṃ bhāvanānuyogamanuyutto viharati. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇadhammo hoti. Iti kalyāṇasīlo, kalyāṇadhammo.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 97Ms 18It_697Kalyāṇapañño ca kathaṃ hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharati. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇapañño hoti.

SC 6Ms 18It_698Iti kalyāṇasīlo kalyāṇadhammo kalyāṇapañño imasmiṃ dhammavinaye ‘kevalī vusitavā uttamapuriso’ti vuccatī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 7SC Verse 223Ms 18It_699“Yassa kāyena vācāya,
manasā natthi dukkaṭaṃ;
Taṃ ve kalyāṇasīloti,
āhu bhikkhuṃ hirīmanaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 224Ms 18It_700Yassa dhammā subhāvitā,
satta sambodhigāmino;
Taṃ ve kalyāṇadhammoti,
āhu bhikkhuṃ anussadaṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 225Ms 18It_701Yo dukkhassa pajānāti,
idheva khayamattano;
Taṃ ve kalyāṇapaññoti,
āhu bhikkhuṃ anāsavaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 226Ms 18It_702Tehi dhammehi sampannaṃ,
anīghaṃ chinnasaṃsayaṃ;
Asitaṃ sabbalokassa,
āhu sabbapahāyinan”ti.

SC 11Ms 18It_703Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
