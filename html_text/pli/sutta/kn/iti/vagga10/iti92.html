• Itivuttaka 92

Saṅghāṭikaṇṇasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_653Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2PTS vp Pali 91Ms 18It_654“Saṅghāṭikaṇṇe cepi, bhikkhave, bhikkhu gahetvā piṭṭhito piṭṭhito anubandho assa pāde pādaṃ nikkhipanto, so ca hoti abhijjhālu kāmesu tibbasārāgo byāpannacitto paduṭṭhamanasaṅkappo muṭṭhassati asampajāno asamāhito vibbhantacitto pākatindriyo; atha kho so ārakāva mayhaṃ, ahañca tassa. Taṃ kissa hetu? Dhammañhi so, bhikkhave, bhikkhu na passati. Dhammaṃ apassanto na maṃ passati.

SC 3Ms 18It_655Yojanasate cepi so, bhikkhave, bhikkhu vihareyya. So ca hoti anabhijjhālu kāmesu na tibbasārāgo abyāpannacitto apaduṭṭhamanasaṅkappo upaṭṭhitassati sampajāno samāhito ekaggacitto saṃvutindriyo; atha kho so santikeva mayhaṃ, ahañca tassa. Taṃ kissa hetu? Dhammañhi so, bhikkhave, bhikkhu passati; dhammaṃ passanto maṃ passatī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 4SC Verse 205Ms 18It_656“Anubandhopi ce assa,
mahiccho ca vighātavā;
Ejānugo anejassa,
nibbutassa anibbuto;
Giddho so vītagedhassa,
passa yāvañca ārakā.

SC 5SC Verse 206PTS vp Pali 92Ms 18It_657Yo ca dhammamabhiññāya,
dhammamaññāya paṇḍito;
Rahadova nivāte ca,
anejo vūpasammati.

SC 6SC Verse 207Ms 18It_658Anejo so anejassa,
nibbutassa ca nibbuto;
Agiddho vītagedhassa,
passa yāvañca santike”ti.

SC 7Ms 18It_659Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
