• Itivuttaka 98

Dānasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_705Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_706“Dvemāni, bhikkhave, dānāni—āmisadānañca dhammadānañca. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ dānānaṃ yadidaṃ—dhammadānaṃ.

SC 3Ms 18It_707Dveme, bhikkhave, saṃvibhāgā—āmisasaṃvibhāgo ca dhammasaṃvibhāgo ca. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ saṃvibhāgānaṃ yadidaṃ—dhammasaṃvibhāgo.

SC 4Ms 18It_708Dveme, bhikkhave, anuggahā—āmisānuggaho ca dhammānuggaho ca. Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, imesaṃ dvinnaṃ anuggahānaṃ yadidaṃ—dhammānuggaho”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 5SC Verse 227Ms 18It_709“Yamāhu dānaṃ paramaṃ anuttaraṃ,
Yaṃ saṃvibhāgaṃ bhagavā avaṇṇayi;
Aggamhi khettamhi pasannacitto,
Viññū pajānaṃ ko na yajetha kāle.

SC 6SC Verse 228Ms 18It_710Ye ceva bhāsanti suṇanti cūbhayaṃ,
Pasannacittā sugatassa sāsane;
Tesaṃ so attho paramo visujjhati,
Ye appamattā sugatassa sāsane”ti.

SC 7Ms 18It_711Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
