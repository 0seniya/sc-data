• Itivuttaka 28

Dukkhavihārasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_172() Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_173“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhaṃ viharati savighātaṃ saupāyāsaṃ sapariḷāhaṃ; kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā duggati pāṭikaṅkhā. Katamehi dvīhi? Indriyesu aguttadvāratāya ca, bhojane aguttadvāratāya ca, bhojane amattaññutāya ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhaṃ viharati savighātaṃ saupāyāsaṃ sapariḷāhaṃ; kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā duggati pāṭikaṅkhā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 47Ms 18It_174“Cakkhu sotañca ghānañca,
jivhā kāyo tathā mano;
Etāni yassa dvārāni,
aguttāni ca bhikkhuno.

SC 4SC Verse 48Ms 18It_175Bhojanamhi amattaññū,
indriyesu asaṃvuto;
Kāyadukkhaṃ cetodukkhaṃ,
dukkhaṃ so adhigacchati.

SC 5SC Verse 49Ms 18It_176Ḍayhamānena kāyena,
Ḍayhamānena cetasā;
Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,
Dukkhaṃ viharati tādiso”ti.

SC 6Ms 18It_177Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
