• Itivuttaka 33

Dutiyasīlasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_203Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2PTS vp Pali 27Ms 18It_204“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge. Katamehi dvīhi? Bhaddakena ca sīlena, bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 58Ms 18It_205“Bhaddakena ca sīlena,
bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;
Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,
yo samannāgato naro;
Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,
saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_206Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
