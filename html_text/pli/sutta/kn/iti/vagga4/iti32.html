• Itivuttaka 32

Paṭhamasīlasutta

SC 1SC 1Ms 18It_198Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2SC 2Ms 18It_199“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye. Katamehi dvīhi? Pāpakena ca sīlena, pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 57SC 3SC Verse 57Ms 18It_200“Pāpakena ca sīlena,
pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;
Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,
yo samannāgato naro;
Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,
nirayaṃ sopapajjatī”ti.

SC 4SC 4Ms 18It_201Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Pañcamaṃ.
