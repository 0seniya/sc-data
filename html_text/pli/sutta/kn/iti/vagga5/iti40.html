• Itivuttaka 40

Vijjāsutta

SC 1SC 1Ms 18It_251Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2SC 2Ms 18It_252“Avijjā, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ samāpattiyā anvadeva ahirikaṃ anottappaṃ; vijjā ca kho, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ samāpattiyā anvadeva hirottappan”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 72SC 3SC Verse 72Ms 18It_253“Yā kācimā duggatiyo,
asmiṃ loke paramhi ca;
Avijjāmūlikā sabbā,
icchālobhasamussayā.

SC 4SC Verse 73SC 4SC Verse 73Ms 18It_254Yato ca hoti pāpiccho,
ahirīko anādaro;
Tato pāpaṃ pasavati,
apāyaṃ tena gacchati.

SC 5SC Verse 74SC 5SC Verse 74Ms 18It_255Tasmā chandañca lobhañca,
avijjañca virājayaṃ;
Vijjaṃ uppādayaṃ bhikkhu,
sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 18It_256Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.

Paṭhamabhāṇavāro.
