• Itivuttaka 38

Vitakkasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_235Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_236“Tathāgataṃ, bhikkhave, arahantaṃ sammāsambuddhaṃ dve vitakkā bahulaṃ samudācaranti—khemo ca vitakko, paviveko ca. Abyāpajjhārāmo, bhikkhave, tathāgato abyāpajjharato. Tamenaṃ, bhikkhave, tathāgataṃ abyāpajjhārāmaṃ abyāpajjharataṃ eseva vitakko bahulaṃ samudācarati: ‘imāyāhaṃ iriyāya na vitakko, paviveko ca. Abyāpajjhārāmo, bhikkhave, tathāgato abyāpajjharato. Tamenaṃ, bhikkhave, tathāgataṃ abyāpajjhārāmaṃ abyāpajjharataṃ eseva vitakko bahulaṃ samudācarati: ‘imāyāhaṃ iriyāya na kañci byābādhemi tasaṃ vā thāvaraṃ vā’ti.

SC 3Ms 18It_237Pavivekārāmo, bhikkhave, tathāgato pavivekarato. Tamenaṃ, bhikkhave, tathāgataṃ pavivekārāmaṃ pavivekarataṃ eseva vitakko bahulaṃ samudācarati: ‘yaṃ akusalaṃ taṃ pahīnan’ti.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 32Ms 18It_238Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, tumhepi abyāpajjhārāmā viharatha abyāpajjharatā. Tesaṃ vo, bhikkhave, tumhākaṃ abyāpajjhārāmānaṃ viharataṃ abyāpajjharatānaṃ eseva vitakko bahulaṃ samudācarissati: ‘imāya mayaṃ iriyāya na kañci byābādhema tasaṃ vā thāvaraṃ vā’ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_239Pavivekārāmā, bhikkhave, viharatha pavivekaratā. Tesaṃ vo, bhikkhave, tumhākaṃ pavivekārāmānaṃ viharataṃ pavivekaratānaṃ eseva vitakko bahulaṃ samudācarissati: ‘kiṃ akusalaṃ, kiṃ appahīnaṃ, kiṃ pajahāmā’”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 6SC Verse 67Ms 18It_240“Tathāgataṃ buddhamasayhasāhinaṃ,
Duve vitakkā samudācaranti naṃ;
Khemo vitakko paṭhamo udīrito,
Tato viveko dutiyo pakāsito.

SC 7SC Verse 68PTS vp Pali 33Ms 18It_241Tamonudaṃ pāragataṃ mahesiṃ,
Taṃ pattipattaṃ vasimaṃ anāsavaṃ;
Visantaraṃ taṇhakkhaye vimuttaṃ,
Taṃ ve muniṃ antimadehadhāriṃ;
Mārañjahaṃ brūmi jarāya pāraguṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 69Ms 18It_242Sele yathā pabbatamuddhaniṭṭhito,
Yathāpi passe janataṃ samantato;
Tathūpamaṃ dhammamayaṃ sumedho,
Pāsādamāruyha samantacakkhu;
Sokāvatiṇṇaṃ janatamapetasoko,
Avekkhati jātijarābhibhūtan”ti.

SC 9Ms 18It_243Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
