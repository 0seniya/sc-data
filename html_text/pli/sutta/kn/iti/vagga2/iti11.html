• Itivuttaka 11

Mohapariññāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_56Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_57“Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mohañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 12Ms 18It_58“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_59Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
