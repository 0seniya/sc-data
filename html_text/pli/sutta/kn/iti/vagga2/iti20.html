• Itivuttaka 20

Paduṭṭhacittasutta

SC 1SC 1Ms 18It_103Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2SC 2Ms 18It_104“Idhāhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekaccaṃ puggalaṃ paduṭṭhacittaṃ evaṃ cetasā ceto paricca pajānāmi: ‘imamhi cāyaṃ samaye puggalo kālaṃ kareyya yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye’. Taṃ kissa hetu? Cittaṃ hissa, bhikkhave, paduṭṭhaṃ. Cetopadosahetu kho pana, bhikkhave, evamidhekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjantī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 23SC 3SC Verse 23Ms 18It_105“Paduṭṭhacittaṃ ñatvāna,
ekaccaṃ idha puggalaṃ;
Etamatthañca byākāsi,
buddho bhikkhūna santike.

SC 4SC Verse 24SC 4SC Verse 24Ms 18It_106Imamhi cāyaṃ samaye,
kālaṃ kayirātha puggalo;
Nirayaṃ upapajjeyya,
cittaṃ hissa padūsitaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 25SC 5SC Verse 25Ms 18It_107Yathā haritvā nikkhipeyya,
evamevaṃ tathāvidho;
Cetopadosahetu hi,
sattā gacchanti duggatin”ti.

SC 6SC 6Ms 18It_108Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dasamaṃ.

Dutiyo vaggo.

SC 7SC 7Ms 18It_112Moho kodho atha makkho,
Vijjā taṇhā sekhaduve ca;
Bhedo sāmaggipuggalo,
Vaggamāhu dutiyanti vuccatīti.
