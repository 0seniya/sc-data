• Itivuttaka 62

Indriyasutta

SC 1PTS vp Pali 53Ms 18It_399Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_400“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṃ, aññindriyaṃ, aññātāvindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 116Ms 18It_401“Sekhassa sikkhamānassa,
ujumaggānusārino;
Khayasmiṃ paṭhamaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
tato aññā anantarā.

SC 4SC Verse 117Ms 18It_402Tato aññā vimuttassa,
ñāṇaṃ ve hoti tādino;
Akuppā me vimuttīti,
bhavasaṃyojanakkhayā.

SC 5SC Verse 118Ms 18It_403Sa ve indriyasampanno,
santo santipade rato;
Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhinin”ti.

SC 6Ms 18It_404Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
