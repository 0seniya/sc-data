• Itivuttaka 60

Puññakiriyavatthusutta

SC 1Ms 18It_387Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_388“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, puññakiriyavatthūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Dānamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, sīlamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, bhāvanāmayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu—imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi puññakiriyavatthūnī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 112PTS vp Pali 52Ms 18It_389“Puññameva so sikkheyya,
āyataggaṃ sukhudrayaṃ;
Dānañca samacariyañca,
mettacittañca bhāvaye.

SC 4SC Verse 113Ms 18It_390Ete dhamme bhāvayitvā,
tayo sukhasamuddaye;
Abyābajjhaṃ sukhaṃ lokaṃ,
paṇḍito upapajjatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_391Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
