• Itivuttaka 66

Soceyyasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_425Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_426“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, soceyyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Kāyasoceyyaṃ, vacīsoceyyaṃ, manosoceyyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi soceyyānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 126Ms 18It_427“Kāyasuciṃ vacīsuciṃ,
cetosucimanāsavaṃ;
Suciṃ soceyyasampannaṃ,
āhu sabbappahāyinan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_428Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Sattamaṃ.
