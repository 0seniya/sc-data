• Itivuttaka 63

Addhāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_406Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_407“Tayome, bhikkhave, addhā. Katame tayo? Atīto addhā, anāgato addhā, paccuppanno addhā—ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo addhā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 119Ms 18It_408“Akkheyyasaññino sattā,
akkheyyasmiṃ patiṭṭhitā;
Akkheyyaṃ apariññāya,
yogamāyanti maccuno.

SC 4SC Verse 120Ms 18It_409Akkheyyañca pariññāya,
akkhātāraṃ na maññati;
Phuṭṭho vimokkho manasā,
santipadamanuttaraṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 121Ms 18It_410Sa ve akkheyyasampanno,
santo santipade rato;
Saṅkhāyasevī dhammaṭṭho,
saṅkhyaṃ nopeti vedagū”ti.

SC 6Ms 18It_411Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Catutthaṃ.
