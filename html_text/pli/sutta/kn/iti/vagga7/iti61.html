• Itivuttaka 61

Cakkhusutta

SC 1Ms 18It_393Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_394“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, cakkhūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Maṃsacakkhu, dibbacakkhu, paññācakkhu—imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi cakkhūnī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 114Ms 18It_395“Maṃsacakkhu dibbacakkhu,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṃ;
Etāni tīṇi cakkhūni,
akkhāsi purisuttamo.

SC 4SC Verse 115Ms 18It_396Maṃsacakkhussa uppādo,
maggo dibbassa cakkhuno;
Yato ñāṇaṃ udapādi,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṃ;
Yassa cakkhussa paṭilābhā,
sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_397Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dutiyaṃ.
