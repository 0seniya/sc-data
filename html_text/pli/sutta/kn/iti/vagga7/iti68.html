• Itivuttaka 68

Paṭhamarāgasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_435Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_436“Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo appahīno, doso appahīno, moho appahīno—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘baddho mārassa paṭimukkassa mārapāso yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato’. Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo pahīno, doso pahīno, moho pahīno—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘abaddho mārassa omukkassa mārapāso baddho mārassa paṭimukkassa mārapāso yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato’. Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo pahīno, doso pahīno, moho pahīno—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘abaddho mārassa omukkassa mārapāso na yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato’”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 128Ms 18It_437“Yassa rāgo ca doso ca,
avijjā ca virājitā;
Taṃ bhāvitattaññataraṃ,
brahmabhūtaṃ tathāgataṃ;
Buddhaṃ verabhayātītaṃ,
āhu sabbappahāyinan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_438Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
