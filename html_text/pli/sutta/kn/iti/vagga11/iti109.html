• Itivuttaka 109

Nadīsotasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_792Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_793“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, puriso nadiyā sotena ovuyheyya piyarūpasātarūpena. Tamenaṃ cakkhumā puriso tīre ṭhito disvā evaṃ vadeyya: ‘kiñcāpi kho tvaṃ, ambho purisa, nadiyā sotena ovuyhasi piyarūpasātarūpena, atthi cettha heṭṭhā rahado saūmi sāvaṭṭo sagaho sarakkhaso yaṃ tvaṃ, ambho purisa, rahadaṃ pāpuṇitvā maraṇaṃ vā nigacchasi maraṇamattaṃ vā dukkhan’ti. Atha kho so, bhikkhave, puriso tassa purisassa saddaṃ sutvā hatthehi ca pādehi ca paṭisotaṃ vāyameyya.

SC 3Ms 18It_794Upamā kho me ayaṃ, bhikkhave, katā atthassa viññāpanāya. Ayañcettha attho: ‘nadiyā soto’ti kho, bhikkhave, taṇhāyetaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 18It_795‘Piyarūpaṃ sātarūpan’ti kho, bhikkhave, channetaṃ ajjhattikānaṃ āyatanānaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 18It_796‘Heṭṭhā rahado’ti kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ orambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 6Ms 18It_797‘Ūmibhayan’ti kho, bhikkhave, kodhupāyāsassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 18It_798‘Āvaṭṭan’ti kho, bhikkhave, pañcannetaṃ kāmaguṇānaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 18It_799‘Gaharakkhaso’ti kho, bhikkhave, mātugāmassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 18It_800‘Paṭisoto’ti kho, bhikkhave, nekkhammassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 18It_801‘Hatthehi ca pādehi ca vāyāmo’ti kho, bhikkhave, vīriyārambhassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ.

SC 11Ms 18It_802‘Cakkhumā puriso tīre ṭhito’ti kho, bhikkhave, tathāgatassetaṃ adhivacanaṃ arahato sammāsambuddhassā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 12SC Verse 255Ms 18It_803“Sahāpi dukkhena jaheyya kāme,
Yogakkhemaṃ āyatiṃ patthayāno;
Sammappajāno suvimuttacitto,
Vimuttiyā phassaye tattha tattha;
Sa vedagū vūsitabrahmacariyo,
Lokantagū pāragatoti vuccatī”ti.

SC 13Ms 18It_804Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dasamaṃ.
