• Itivuttaka 111

Sampannasīlasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_820Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_821“Sampannasīlā, bhikkhave, viharatha sampannapātimokkhā; pātimokkhasaṃvarasaṃvutā viharatha ācāragocarasampannā aṇumattesu vajjesu bhayadassāvino; samādāya sikkhatha sikkhāpadesu.

SC 3Ms 18It_822Sampannasīlānaṃ vo, bhikkhave, viharataṃ sampannapātimokkhānaṃ pātimokkhasaṃvarasaṃvutānaṃ viharataṃ ācāragocarasampannānaṃ aṇumattesu vajjesu bhayadassāvīnaṃ samādāya sikkhataṃ sikkhāpadesu, viharataṃ sampannapātimokkhānaṃ pātimokkhasaṃvarasaṃvutānaṃ viharataṃ ācāragocarasampannānaṃ aṇumattesu vajjesu bhayadassāvīnaṃ samādāya sikkhataṃ sikkhāpadesu, kimassa uttari karaṇīyaṃ?

SC 4PTS vp Pali 119Ms 18It_823Carato cepi, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno abhijjhā, byāpādo vigato hoti, thinamiddhaṃ vigataṃ hoti, uddhaccakukkuccaṃ vigataṃ hoti, vicikicchā pahīnā hoti, āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ, upaṭṭhitā sati abhijjhā, byāpādo vigato hoti, thinamiddhaṃ vigataṃ hoti, uddhaccakukkuccaṃ vigataṃ hoti, vicikicchā pahīnā hoti, āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ, upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā, passaddho kāyo asāraddho, samāhitaṃ cittaṃ ekaggaṃ. Carampi, bhikkhave, bhikkhu evaṃbhūto ātāpī ottāpī satataṃ samitaṃ āraddhavīriyo pahitattoti vuccati.

SC 5Ms 18It_824Ṭhitassa cepi, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno abhijjhā byāpādo vigato hoti … pe … thinamiddhaṃ … uddhaccakukkuccaṃ … vicikicchā pahīnā hoti, āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ, upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā, passaddho kāyo asāraddho, samāhitaṃ cittaṃ ekaggaṃ. Ṭhitopi, bhikkhave, bhikkhu evaṃbhūto ātāpī ottāpī satataṃ samitaṃ āraddhavīriyo pahitattoti vuccati.

SC 6Ms 18It_825Nisinnassa cepi, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno abhijjhā byāpādo vigato hoti … pe … thinamiddhaṃ … uddhaccakukkuccaṃ … vicikicchā pahīnā hoti, āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ, upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā, passaddho kāyo asāraddho, samāhitaṃ cittaṃ ekaggaṃ. Nisinnopi, bhikkhave, bhikkhu evaṃbhūto ātāpī ottāpī satataṃ samitaṃ āraddhavīriyo pahitattoti vuccati.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 120Ms 18It_826Sayānassa cepi, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno jāgarassa abhijjhā byāpādo vigato hoti … pe … thinamiddhaṃ … uddhaccakukkuccaṃ … vicikicchā pahīnā hoti, āraddhaṃ hoti vīriyaṃ asallīnaṃ, upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā, passaddho kāyo asāraddho, samāhitaṃ cittaṃ ekaggaṃ. Sayānopi, bhikkhave, bhikkhu jāgaro evaṃbhūto ātāpī ottāpī satataṃ samitaṃ āraddhavīriyo pahitattoti vuccatī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 8SC Verse 259Ms 18It_827“Yataṃ care yataṃ tiṭṭhe,
yataṃ acche yataṃ saye;
Yataṃ samiñjaye bhikkhu,
yatamenaṃ pasāraye.

SC 9SC Verse 260Ms 18It_828Uddhaṃ tiriyaṃ apācīnaṃ,
yāvatājagatogati;
Samavekkhitā ca dhammānaṃ,
khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 261PTS vp Pali 121Ms 18It_829Evaṃ vihārimātāpiṃ,
santavuttimanuddhataṃ;
Cetosamathasāmīciṃ,
sikkhamānaṃ sadā sataṃ;
Satataṃ pahitattoti,
āhu bhikkhuṃ tathāvidhan”ti.

SC 11Ms 18It_830Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dvādasamaṃ.
