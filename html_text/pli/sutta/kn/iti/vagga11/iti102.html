• Itivuttaka 102

Āsavakkhayasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_741Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2PTS vp Pali 104Ms 18It_742“Jānatohaṃ, bhikkhave, passato āsavānaṃ khayaṃ vadāmi, no ajānato no apassato. Kiñca, bhikkhave, jānato, kiṃ passato āsavānaṃ khayo hoti? Idaṃ dukkhanti, bhikkhave, jānato passato āsavānaṃ khayo hoti. Ayaṃ dukkhasamudayoti, bhikkhave, jānato passato āsavānaṃ khayo hoti. Ayaṃ dukkhanirodhoti, bhikkhave, jānato passato āsavānaṃ khayo hoti. Ayaṃ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadāti, bhikkhave, jānato passato āsavānaṃ khayo hoti. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, jānato evaṃ passato āsavānaṃ khayo hotī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 234Ms 18It_743“Sekhassa sikkhamānassa,
ujumaggānusārino;
Khayasmiṃ paṭhamaṃ ñāṇaṃ,
tato aññā anantarā.

SC 4SC Verse 235Ms 18It_744Tato aññā vimuttassa,
vimuttiñāṇamuttamaṃ;
Uppajjati khaye ñāṇaṃ,
khīṇā saṃyojanā iti.

SC 5SC Verse 236Ms 18It_745Na tvevidaṃ kusītena,
bālenamavijānatā;
Nibbānaṃ adhigantabbaṃ,
sabbaganthappamocanan”ti.

SC 6Ms 18It_746Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
