• Itivuttaka 54

Paṭhamaesanāsutta

SC 1PTS vp Pali 48Ms 18It_350Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_351“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 102Ms 18It_352“Samāhito sampajāno,
sato buddhassa sāvako;
Esanā ca pajānāti,
esanānañca sambhavaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 103Ms 18It_353Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,
maggañca khayagāminaṃ;
Esanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,
nicchāto parinibbuto”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_354Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Pañcamaṃ.
