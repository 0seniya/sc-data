• Itivuttaka 52

Paṭhamavedanāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_338Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_339“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 98Ms 18It_340“Samāhito sampajāno,
sato buddhassa sāvako;
Vedanā ca pajānāti,
vedanānañca sambhavaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 99Ms 18It_341Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,
maggañca khayagāminaṃ;
Vedanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,
nicchāto parinibbuto”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_342Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
