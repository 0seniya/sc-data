• Itivuttaka 53

Dutiyavedanāsutta

SC 1PTS vp Pali 47Ms 18It_344Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_345“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā. Sukhā, bhikkhave, vedanā dukkhato daṭṭhabbā; dukkhā vedanā sallato daṭṭhabbā; adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato daṭṭhabbā. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno sukhā vedanā dukkhato diṭṭhā hoti, dukkhā vedanā sallato diṭṭhā hoti, adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato diṭṭhā hoti; ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘bhikkhu ariyo sammaddaso acchecchi, taṇhaṃ, acchecchi, taṇhaṃ, vivattayi saṃyojanaṃ, sammā mānābhisamayā antamakāsi dukkhassā’”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 100Ms 18It_346“Yo sukhaṃ dukkhato adda,
Dukkhamaddakkhi sallato;
Adukkhamasukhaṃ santaṃ,
Adakkhi naṃ aniccato.

SC 4SC Verse 101Ms 18It_347Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,
yato tattha vimuccati;
Abhiññāvosito santo,
sa ve yogātigo munī”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_348Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Catutthaṃ.
