• Itivuttaka 57

Dutiyaāsavasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_368Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_369“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 108Ms 18It_370“Yassa kāmāsavo khīṇo,
avijjā ca virājitā;
Bhavāsavo parikkhīṇo,
vippamutto nirūpadhi;
Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhinin”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_371Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
