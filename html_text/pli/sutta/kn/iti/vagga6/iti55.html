• Itivuttaka 55

Dutiyaesanāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_356Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_357“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 104Ms 18It_358“Kāmesanā bhavesanā,
brahmacariyesanā saha;
Iti saccaparāmāso,
diṭṭhiṭṭhānā samussayā.

SC 4SC Verse 105Ms 18It_359Sabbarāgavirattassa,
taṇhakkhayavimuttino;
Esanā paṭinissaṭṭhā,
diṭṭhiṭṭhānā samūhatā;
Esanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,
nirāso akathaṅkathī”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_360Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
