• Itivuttaka 56

Paṭhamaāsavasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_362Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_363“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 106Ms 18It_364“Samāhito sampajāno,
sato buddhassa sāvako;
Āsave ca pajānāti,
āsavānañca sambhavaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 107Ms 18It_365Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,
maggañca khayagāminaṃ;
Āsavānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,
nicchāto parinibbuto”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_366Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Sattamaṃ.
