• Itivuttaka 51

Dhātusutta

SC 1Ms 18It_332Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_333“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, dhātuyo. Katamā tisso? Rūpadhātu, arūpadhātu, nirodhadhātu—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso dhātuyo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 96Ms 18It_334“Rūpadhātuṃ pariññāya,
āruppesu asaṇṭhitā;
Nirodhe ye vimuccanti,
te janā maccuhāyino.

SC 4SC Verse 97Ms 18It_335Kāyena amataṃ dhātuṃ,
phusayitvā nirūpadhiṃ;
Upadhippaṭinissaggaṃ,
sacchikatvā anāsavo;
Deseti sammāsambuddho,
asokaṃ virajaṃ padan”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_336Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dutiyaṃ.
