• Itivuttaka 72

Nissaraṇiyasutta

SC 1SC 1Ms 18It_465Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_466“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, SC 2Ms 18It_466“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo. Katamā tisso? Kāmānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ nekkhammaṃ, rūpānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ āruppaṃ, yaṃ kho pana kiñci bhūtaṃ saṅkhataṃ paṭiccasamuppannaṃ nirodho tassa nissaraṇaṃ—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 135SC 3SC Verse 135Ms 18It_467“Kāmanissaraṇaṃ ñatvā,
rūpānañca atikkamaṃ;
Sabbasaṅkhārasamathaṃ,
phusaṃ ātāpi sabbadā.

SC 4SC Verse 136SC 4SC Verse 136Ms 18It_468Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,
yato tattha vimuccati;
Abhiññāvosito santo,
sa ve yogātigo munī”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 18It_469Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
