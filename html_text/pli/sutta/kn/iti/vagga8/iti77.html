• Itivuttaka 77

Bhidurasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_510Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_511“Bhidurāyaṃ, bhikkhave, kāyo, viññāṇaṃ virāgadhammaṃ, sabbe upadhī aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāmadhammā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 154Ms 18It_512“Kāyañca bhiduraṃ ñatvā,
Viññāṇañca virāgunaṃ;
Upadhīsu bhayaṃ disvā,
Jātimaraṇamaccagā;
Sampatvā paramaṃ santiṃ,
Kālaṃ kaṅkhati bhāvitatto”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_513Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
