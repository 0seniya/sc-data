• Itivuttaka 75

Avuṭṭhikasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_487Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_488“Tayome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame tayo? Avuṭṭhikasamo, padesavassī, sabbatthābhivassī.

SC 3Ms 18It_489Kathañca, bhikkhave, puggalo avuṭṭhikasamo hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo sabbesaññeva na dātā hoti, samaṇabrāhmaṇakapaṇaddhikavanibbakayācakānaṃ annaṃ pānaṃ vatthaṃ yānaṃ mālāgandhavilepanaṃ seyyāvasathapadīpeyyaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, puggalo avuṭṭhikasamo hoti.

SC 4Ms 18It_490Kathañca, bhikkhave, puggalo padesavassī hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo ekaccānaṃ dātā hoti, ekaccānaṃ na dātā hoti samaṇabrāhmaṇakapaṇaddhikavanibbakayācakānaṃ annaṃ pānaṃ vatthaṃ yānaṃ mālāgandhavilepanaṃ seyyāvasathapadīpeyyaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, puggalo padesavassī hoti.

SC 5Ms 18It_491Kathañca, bhikkhave, puggalo sabbatthābhivassī hoti? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo sabbesaṃva deti, samaṇabrāhmaṇakapaṇaddhikavanibbakayācakānaṃ annaṃ pānaṃ vatthaṃ yānaṃ mālāgandhavilepanaṃ seyyāvasathapadīpeyyaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, puggalo sabbatthābhivassī hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 6SC Verse 142Ms 18It_492“Na samaṇe na brāhmaṇe,
Na kapaṇaddhikavanibbake;
Laddhāna saṃvibhājeti,
Annaṃ pānañca bhojanaṃ;
Taṃ ve avuṭṭhikasamoti,
Āhu naṃ purisādhamaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 143Ms 18It_493Ekaccānaṃ na dadāti,
ekaccānaṃ pavecchati;
Taṃ ve padesavassīti,
āhu medhāvino janā.

SC 8SC Verse 144Ms 18It_494Subhikkhavāco puriso,
sabbabhūtānukampako;
Āmodamāno pakireti,
detha dethāti bhāsati.

SC 9SC Verse 145Ms 18It_495Yathāpi megho thanayitvā,
gajjayitvā pavassati;
Thalaṃ ninnañca pūreti,
abhisandantova vārinā.

SC 10SC Verse 146Ms 18It_496Evamevaṃ idhekacco,
puggalo hoti tādiso;
Dhammena saṃharitvāna,
uṭṭhānādhigataṃ dhanaṃ;
Tappeti annapānena,
sammā patte vanibbake”ti.

SC 11Ms 18It_497Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
