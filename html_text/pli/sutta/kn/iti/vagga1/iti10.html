• Itivuttaka 10

Dosapariññāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_48Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_49“Dosaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Dosañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 11Ms 18It_50“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ dosaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_51Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dasamaṃ.

Pāṭibhogavaggo paṭhamo.

SC 5Ms 18It_55Rāgadosā atha moho,
Kodhamakkhā mānaṃ sabbaṃ;
Mānato rāgadosā puna dve,
Pakāsitā vaggamāhu paṭhamanti.
