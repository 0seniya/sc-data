• Itivuttaka 7

Sabbapariññāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_32Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2PTS vp Pali 4Ms 18It_33“Sabbaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Sabbañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 7Ms 18It_34“Yo sabbaṃ sabbato ñatvā,
sabbatthesu na rajjati;
Sa ve sabbapariññā so,
sabbadukkhamupaccagā”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_35Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Sattamaṃ.
