• Itivuttaka 3

Mohasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_12Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_13“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 3Ms 18It_14“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_15Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
