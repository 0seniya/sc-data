• Itivuttaka 2

Dosasutta

SC 1Ms 18It_7Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2Ms 18It_8“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Dosaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 2Ms 18It_9“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ dosaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_10Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Dutiyaṃ.
