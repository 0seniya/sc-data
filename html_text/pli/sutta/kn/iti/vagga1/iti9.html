• Itivuttaka 9

Lobhapariññāsutta

SC 1SC 1Ms 18It_43Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2SC 2Ms 18It_44“Lobhaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Lobhañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 10SC 3SC Verse 10Ms 18It_45“Yena lobhena luddhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ lobhaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4SC 4Ms 18It_46Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
