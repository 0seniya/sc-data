• Itivuttaka 5

Makkhasutta

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3Ms 18It_22Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_23“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Makkhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 5Ms 18It_24“Yena makkhena makkhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;
Taṃ makkhaṃ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18It_25Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Pañcamaṃ.
