• Itivuttaka 8

Mānapariññāsutta

SC 1Ms 18It_37Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ:

SC 2Ms 18It_38“Mānaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mānañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

SC 3SC Verse 8Ms 18It_39“Mānupetā ayaṃ pajā,
mānaganthā bhave ratā;
Mānaṃ aparijānantā,
āgantāro punabbhavaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 9Ms 18It_40Ye ca mānaṃ pahantvāna,
vimuttā mānasaṅkhaye;
Te mānaganthābhibhuno,
sabbadukkhamupaccagun”ti.

SC 5Ms 18It_41Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
