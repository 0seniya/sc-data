3. Niddesavāra

SC 1Ms 27Ne_24Msdiv 4Tattha saṅkhepato netti kittitā.

3.1. Hārasaṅkhepa

SC 2Ms 27Ne_25Assādādīnavatā,
Nissaraṇampi ca phalaṃ upāyo ca;
Āṇattī ca bhagavato,
Yogīnaṃ desanāhāro.

SC 3Ms 27Ne_26Yaṃ pucchitañca vissajjitañca,
Suttassa yā ca anugīti;
Suttassa yo pavicayo,
Hāro vicayoti niddiṭṭho.

SC 4 Ms 27Ne_27Sabbesaṃ hārānaṃ,
Yā bhūmī yo ca gocaro tesaṃ;
Yuttāyuttaparikkhā,
Hāro yuttīti niddiṭṭho.

SC 5 Ms 27Ne_28Dhammaṃ deseti jino,
Tassa ca dhammassa yaṃ padaṭṭhānaṃ;
Iti yāva sabbadhammā,
Eso hāro padaṭṭhāno.

SC 6 Ms 27Ne_29Vuttamhi ekadhamme,
Ye dhammā ekalakkhaṇā keci;
Vuttā bhavanti sabbe,
So hāro lakkhaṇo nāma.

SC 7Ms 27Ne_30Neruttamadhippāyo,
Byañjanamatha desanānidānañca;
Pubbāparānusandhī,
Eso hāro catubyūho.

SC 8 Ms 27Ne_31Ekamhi padaṭṭhāne,
Pariyesati sesakaṃ padaṭṭhānaṃ;
Āvaṭṭati paṭipakkhe,
Āvaṭṭo nāma so hāro.

SC 9Ms 27Ne_32Dhammañca padaṭṭhānaṃ,
Bhūmiñca vibhajjate ayaṃ hāro;
Sādhāraṇe asādhā-
Raṇe ca neyyo vibhattīti.

SC 10Ms 27Ne_33Kusalākusale dhamme,
Niddiṭṭhe bhāvite pahīne ca;
Parivattati paṭipakkhe,
Hāro parivattano nāma.

SC 11PTS vp Pali 4Ms 27Ne_34Vevacanāni bahūni tu,
Sutte vuttāni ekadhammassa;
Yo jānāti suttavidū,
Vevacano nāma so hāro.

SC 12Ms 27Ne_35Ekaṃ bhagavā dhammaṃ,
Paññattīhi vividhāhi deseti;
So ākāro ñeyyo,
Paññattī nāma hāroti.

SC 13Ms 27Ne_36Yo ca paṭiccuppādo,
Indriyakhandhā ca dhātu āyatanā;
Etehi otarati yo,
Otaraṇo nāma so hāro.

SC 14Ms 27Ne_37Vissajjitamhi pañhe,
Gāthāyaṃ pucchitā yamārabbha;
Suddhāsuddhaparikkhā,
Hāro so sodhano nāma.

SC 15Ms 27Ne_38Ekattatāya dhammā,
Yepi ca vemattatāya niddiṭṭhā;
Te na vikappayitabbā,
Eso hāro adhiṭṭhāno.

SC 16Ms 27Ne_39Ye dhammā yaṃ dhammaṃ,
Janayantippaccayā paramparato;
Hetumavakaḍḍhayitvā,
Eso hāro parikkhāro.

SC 17Ms 27Ne_40Ye dhammā yaṃmūlā,
Ye cekatthā pakāsitā muninā;
Te samaropayitabbā,
Esa samāropano hāro.

3.2. Nayasaṅkhepa

SC 18Ms 27Ne_41Taṇhañca avijjampi ca,
Samathena vipassanāya yo neti;
Saccehi yojayitvā,
Ayaṃ nayo nandiyāvaṭṭo.

SC 19Ms 27Ne_42Yo akusale samūlehi,
Neti kusale ca kusalamūlehi;
Bhūtaṃ tathaṃ avitathaṃ,
Tipukkhalaṃ taṃ nayaṃ āhu.

SC 20Ms 27Ne_43Yo neti vipallāsehi,
Kilese indriyehi saddhamme;
Etaṃ nayaṃ nayavidū,
Sīhavikkīḷitaṃ āhu.

SC 21Ms 27Ne_44Veyyākaraṇesu hi ye,
Kusalākusalā tahiṃ tahiṃ vuttā;
Manasā volokayate,
Taṃ khu disālocanaṃ āhu.

SC 22 Ms 27Ne_45Oloketvā disalocanena,
Ukkhipiya yaṃ samāneti;
Sabbe kusalākusale,
Ayaṃ nayo aṅkuso nāma.

SC 23Ms 27Ne_46Soḷasa hārā paṭhamaṃ,
Disalocanato disā viloketvā;
Saṅkhipiya aṅkusena hi,
Nayehi tīhi niddise suttaṃ.

3.3. Dvādasapada

SC 24Ms 27Ne_47Akkharaṃ padaṃ byañjanaṃ,
nirutti tatheva niddeso;
Ākārachaṭṭhavacanaṃ,
ettāva byañjanaṃ sabbaṃ.

SC 25PTS vp Pali 5Ms 27Ne_48Saṅkāsanā pakāsanā,
Vivaraṇā vibhajanuttānīkammapaññatti;
Etehi chahi padehi,
Attho kammañca niddiṭṭhaṃ.

SC 26Ms 27Ne_49Tīṇi ca nayā anūnā,
Atthassa ca chappadāni gaṇitāni;
Navahi padehi bhagavato,
Vacanassattho samāyutto.

SC 27Ms 27Ne_50Atthassa navappadāni,
Byañjanapariyeṭṭhiyā catubbīsa;
Ubhayaṃ saṅkalayitvā,
Tettiṃsā ettikā nettīti.

Ms 27Ne_51Niddesavāro.
