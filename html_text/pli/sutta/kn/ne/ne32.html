Sodhanahārasampāta

SC 1Ms 27Ne_531SC 1Ms 27Ne_531Msdiv 75Tattha katamo sodhano hārasampāto?

SC 2SC 2Ms 27Ne_532“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. Yattha ārambho suddho, so pañho visajjito bhavati. Yattha pana ārambho na suddho, na tāva so pañho visajjito bhavati.

Ms 27Ne_533Niyutto sodhano hārasampāto.
