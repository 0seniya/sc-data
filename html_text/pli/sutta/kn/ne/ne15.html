  • Vibhaṅga 12

Otaraṇahāravibhaṅga

SC 1Ms 27Ne_327Msdiv 42Tattha katamo otaraṇo hāro? “Yo ca paṭiccuppādo”ti.

SC 2 Ms 27Ne_328“Uddhaṃ adho sabbadhi vippamutto,
Ayaṃ ahasmīti anānupassī;
Evaṃ vimutto udatāri oghaṃ,
Atiṇṇapubbaṃ apunabbhavāyā”ti.

SC 3Ms 27Ne_329“Uddhan”ti rūpadhātu ca arūpadhātu ca. “Adho”ti kāmadhātu. “Sabbadhi vippamutto”ti tedhātuke ayaṃ asekkhāvimutti. Tāniyeva asekkhāni pañcindriyāni, ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 4Ms 27Ne_330Tāniyeva asekkhāni pañcindriyāni vijjā, vijjuppādā avijjānirodho, avijjānirodhā saṅkhāranirodho, saṅkhāranirodhā viññāṇanirodho, viññāṇanirodhā nāmarūpanirodho, nāmarūpanirodhā saḷāyatananirodho, saḷāyatananirodhā phassanirodho, phassanirodhā vedanānirodho, vedanānirodhā taṇhānirodho, taṇhānirodhā upādānanirodho, upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho, bhavanirodhā jātinirodho, jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṃ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 5Ms 27Ne_331Tāniyeva asekkhāni pañcindriyāni tīhi khandhehi saṅgahitāni—sīlakkhandhena samādhikkhandhena paññākkhandhena, ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 6Ms 27Ne_332Tāniyeva asekkhāni pañcindriyāni saṅkhārapariyāpannāni ye saṅkhārā anāsavā, no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 7Ms 27Ne_333Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ, no ca bhavaṅgaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 8Ms 27Ne_334“Ayaṃ ahasmīti anānupassī”ti ayaṃ sakkāyadiṭṭhiyā samugghāto, sā sekkhāvimutti, tāniyeva sekkhāni pañcindriyāni. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 9Ms 27Ne_335Tāniyeva sekkhāni pañcindriyāni vijjā, vijjuppādā avijjānirodho, avijjānirodhā saṅkhāranirodho, evaṃ sabbo paṭiccasamuppādo. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 10Ms 27Ne_336Sāyeva vijjā paññākkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 11Ms 27Ne_337Sāyeva vijjā saṅkhārapariyāpannā, ye saṅkhārā anāsavā, no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā, ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 12Ms 27Ne_338Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ, no ca bhavaṅgaṃ, ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 13Ms 27Ne_339Sekkhāya ca vimuttiyā asekkhāya ca vimuttiyā vimutto udatāri oghaṃ atiṇṇapubbaṃ apunabbhavāya. Tenāha bhagavā “uddhaṃ adho”ti.

SC 14 Ms 27Ne_340Msdiv 43“Nissitassa calitaṃ, anissitassa calitaṃ natthi, calite asati passaddhi, passaddhiyā sati nati na hoti, natiyā asati āgatigati na hoti, āgatigatiyā asati cutūpapāto na hoti, cutūpapāte asati nevidha na huraṃ na ubhayamantarena esevanto dukkhassā”ti.

SC 15Ms 27Ne_341“Nissitassa calitan”ti nissayo nāma duvidho taṇhānissayo ca diṭṭhinissayo ca. Tattha yā rattassa cetanā, ayaṃ taṇhānissayo; yā mūḷhassa cetanā, ayaṃ diṭṭhinissayo. Cetanā pana saṅkhārā, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṃ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṃ, evaṃ sabbo paṭiccasamuppādo. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 16Ms 27Ne_342Tattha yā rattassa vedanā, ayaṃ sukhā vedanā. Yā sammūḷhassa vedanā, ayaṃ adukkhamasukhā vedanā, imā dve vedanā vedanākkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 17Ms 27Ne_343Tattha sukhā vedanā dve indriyāni sukhindriyaṃ somanassindriyañca, adukkhamasukhā vedanā upekkhindriyaṃ. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 18Ms 27Ne_344Tāniyeva indriyāni saṅkhārapariyāpannāni, ye saṅkhārā sāsavā bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 19Ms 27Ne_345Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ sāsavaṃ bhavaṅgaṃ, ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 20Ms 27Ne_346“Anissitassa calitaṃ natthī”ti samathavasena vā taṇhāya anissito vipassanāvasena vā diṭṭhiyā anissito. Yā vipassanā ayaṃ vijjā, vijjuppādā avijjānirodho, avijjānirodhā saṅkhāranirodho, saṅkhāranirodhā viññāṇanirodho, evaṃ sabbo paṭiccasamuppādo. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 21Ms 27Ne_347Sāyeva vipassanā paññākkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 22Ms 27Ne_348Sāyeva vipassanā dve indriyāni—vīriyindriyañca paññindriyañca. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 23Ms 27Ne_349Sāyeva vipassanā saṅkhārapariyāpannā, ye saṅkhārā anāsavā, no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 24Ms 27Ne_350Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ, no ca bhavaṅgaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 25Ms 27Ne_351“Passaddhiyā satī”ti duvidhā passaddhi kāyikā ca cetasikā ca. Yaṃ kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ, ayaṃ kāyapassaddhi. Yaṃ cetasikaṃ sukhaṃ, ayaṃ cetasikā passaddhi. Passaddhakāyo sukhaṃ vediyati, sukhino cittaṃ samādhiyati, samāhito yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, yathābhūtaṃ pajānanto nibbindati, nibbindanto virajjati, virāgā vimuccati, vimuttasmiṃ “vediyati, sukhino cittaṃ samādhiyati, samāhito yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, yathābhūtaṃ pajānanto nibbindati, nibbindanto virajjati, virāgā vimuccati, vimuttasmiṃ “vimuttam”iti ñāṇaṃ hoti, “khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā”ti pajānāti. So na namati rūpesu, na saddesu, na gandhesu, na rasesu, na phoṭṭhabbesu, na dhammesu khayā rāgassa khayā dosassa khayā mohassa yena rūpena tathāgataṃ tiṭṭhantaṃ carantaṃ paññāpayamāno paññāpeyya, tassa rūpassa khayā virāgā nirodhā cāgā paṭinissaggā rūpasaṅkhaye vimutto, tathāgato atthītipi na upeti, natthītipi na upeti, atthi natthītipi na upeti, nevatthi no natthītipi na upeti. Atha kho gambhīro appameyyo asaṅkheyyo nibbutotiyeva saṅkhaṃ gacchati khayā rāgassa, khayā dosassa, khayā mohassa.

SC 26Ms 27Ne_352Yāya vedanāya … pe … yāya saññāya. Yehi saṅkhārehi. Yena viññāṇena tathāgataṃ tiṭṭhantaṃ carantaṃ paññāpayamāno paññāpeyya, tassa viññāṇassa khayā virāgā nirodhā cāgā paṭinissaggā viññāṇasaṅkhaye vimutto, tathāgato atthītipi na upeti, natthītipi na upeti, atthi natthītipi na upeti, nevatthi no natthītipi na upeti. Atha kho gambhīro appameyyo asaṅkheyyo nibbutotiyeva saṅkhaṃ gacchati khayā rāgassa, khayā dosassa, khayā mohassa. “Āgatī”ti idhāgati. “Gatī”ti peccabhavo. Āgatigatīpi na bhavanti, “nevidhā”ti chasu ajjhattikesu āyatanesu. “Na huran”ti chasu bāhiresu āyatanesu. “Na ubhayamantarenā”ti phassasamuditesu dhammesu attānaṃ na passati. “Esevanto dukkhassā”ti paṭiccasamuppādo. So duvidho lokiyo ca lokuttaro ca. Tattha lokiyo avijjāpaccayā saṅkhārā, yāva jarāmaraṇā. Lokuttaro sīlavato avippaṭisāro jāyati, yāva nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānāti. Tenāha bhagavā “nissitassa calitaṃ anissitassa calitaṃ natthi … pe … esevanto dukkhassā”ti.

SC 27 Ms 27Ne_353Msdiv 44“Ye keci sokā paridevitā vā,
Dukkhā ca lokasmimanekarūpā;
Piyaṃ paṭiccappabhavanti ete,
Piye asante na bhavanti ete.

SC 28 Ms 27Ne_354Tasmā hi te sukhino vītasokā,
Yesaṃ piyaṃ natthi kuhiñci loke;
Tasmā asokaṃ virajaṃ patthayāno,
Piyaṃ na kayirātha kuhiñci loke”ti.

SC 29Ms 27Ne_355“Ye keci sokā paridevitā vā, dukkhā ca lokasmimanekarūpā piyaṃ paṭiccappabhavanti ete”ti—ayaṃ dukkhā vedanā. “Piye asante na bhavanti ete”ti—ayaṃ sukhā vedanā. Vedanā vedanākkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 30Ms 27Ne_356Vedanāpaccayā taṇhā, taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṃ, upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṃ, evaṃ sabbaṃ. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 31Ms 27Ne_357Tattha sukhā vedanā dve indriyāni—sukhindriyaṃ somanassindriyañca. Dukkhā vedanā dve indriyāni—dukkhindriyaṃ domanassindriyañca. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 32Ms 27Ne_358Tāniyeva indriyāni saṅkhārapariyāpannāni, ye saṅkhārā sāsavā bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 33Ms 27Ne_359Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ sāsavaṃ bhavaṅgaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 34Ms 27Ne_360“Tasmā hi te sukhino vītasokā,
Yesaṃ piyaṃ natthi kuhiñci loke;
Tasmā asokaṃ virajaṃ patthayāno,
Piyaṃ na kayirātha kuhiñci loke”ti.

SC 35Ms 27Ne_361Idaṃ taṇhāpahānaṃ. Taṇhānirodhā upādānanirodho, upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho, evaṃ sabbaṃ. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 36Ms 27Ne_362Taṃyeva taṇhāpahānaṃ samatho. So samatho dve indriyāni satindriyaṃ samādhindriyañca. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 37Ms 27Ne_363Soyeva samatho samādhikkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 38Ms 27Ne_364Soyeva samatho saṅkhārapariyāpanno, ye saṅkhārā anāsavā, no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 39Ms 27Ne_365Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ, no ca bhavaṅgaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā. Tenāha bhagavā “ye keci sokā”ti.

SC 40 Ms 27Ne_366“Kāmaṃ kāmayamānassa,
tassa ce taṃ samijjhati;
Addhā pītimano hoti,
laddhā macco yadicchati.

SC 41Ms 27Ne_367Tassa ce kāmayānassa,
chandajātassa jantuno;
Te kāmā parihāyanti,
sallaviddhova ruppati.

SC 42Ms 27Ne_368Yo kāme parivajjeti,
sappasseva padā siro;
Somaṃ visattikaṃ loke,
sato samativattatī”ti.

SC 43Ms 27Ne_369Tattha yā pītimanatā, ayaṃ anunayo. Yadāha sallaviddhova ruppatīti, idaṃ paṭighaṃ. Anunayaṃ paṭighañca pana taṇhāpakkho, taṇhāya ca pana dasarūpīni āyatanāni padaṭṭhānaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

SC 44Ms 27Ne_370Tāniyeva dasa rūpīni rūpakāyo nāmasampayutto, tadubhayaṃ nāmarūpaṃ, nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ, saḷāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayā taṇhā, evaṃ sabbaṃ. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 45Ms 27Ne_371Tadeva nāmarūpaṃ pañcakkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 46Ms 27Ne_372Tadeva nāmarūpaṃ aṭṭhārasa dhātuyo. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 47Ms 27Ne_373Tattha yo rūpakāyo imāni pañca rūpīni indriyāni, yo nāmakāyo imāni pañca arūpīni indriyāni, imāni dasa indriyāni. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 48Ms 27Ne_374Tattha yadāha—

SC 49Ms 27Ne_375“Yo kāme parivajjeti,
sappasseva padā siro;
Somaṃ visattikaṃ loke,
sato samativattatī”ti.

SC 50Ms 27Ne_376Ayaṃ saupādisesā nibbānadhātu, ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 51Ms 27Ne_377Sāyeva saupādisesā nibbānadhātu vijjā, vijjuppādā avijjānirodho, avijjānirodhā saṅkhāranirodho, evaṃ sabbaṃ. Ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādehi otaraṇā.

SC 52Ms 27Ne_378Sāyeva vijjā paññākkhandho. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 53Ms 27Ne_379Sāyeva vijjā dve indriyāni—vīriyindriyaṃ paññindriyañca. Ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 54Ms 27Ne_380Sāyeva vijjā saṅkhārapariyāpannā, ye saṅkhārā anāsavā, no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā. Ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 55Ms 27Ne_381Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ, no ca bhavaṅgaṃ. Ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā. Tenāha bhagavā “kāmaṃ kāmayamānassā”ti.

SC 56Ms 27Ne_382Ettāvatā paṭicca indriyakhandhadhātuāyatanāni samosaraṇotaraṇāni bhavanti. Evaṃ paṭicca indriyakhandhadhātuāyatanāni otāretabbāni. Tenāha āyasmā mahākaccāyano “yo ca paṭiccuppādo”ti.

Ms 27Ne_383Niyutto otaraṇo hāro.
