Vibhattihārasampāta

SC 1Ms 27Ne_512Msdiv 70Tattha katamo vibhattihārasampāto?

SC 2Ms 27Ne_513“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. Kusalapakkho kusalapakkhena niddisitabbo. Akusalapakkho akusalapakkhena niddisitabbo.
