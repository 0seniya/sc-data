Āvaṭṭahārasampāta

SC 1Ms 27Ne_509Msdiv 69Tattha katamo āvaṭṭo hārasampāto?

SC 2Ms 27Ne_510“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. “Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti samatho. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro”ti vipassanā. “Ñatvāna udayabbayan”ti dukkhapariññā. “Thinamiddhābhibhū bhikkhū”ti samudayapahānaṃ. “Sabbā duggatiyo samatho. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro”ti vipassanā. “Ñatvāna udayabbayan”ti dukkhapariññā. “Thinamiddhābhibhū bhikkhū”ti samudayapahānaṃ. “Sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti nirodho. Imāni cattāri saccāni.

Ms 27Ne_511Niyutto āvaṭṭo hārasampāto.
