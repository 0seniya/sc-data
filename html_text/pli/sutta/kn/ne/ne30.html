Paññattihārasampāta

SC 1Ms 27Ne_521Msdiv 73Tattha katamo paññattihārasampāto?

SC 2Ms 27Ne_522“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. “Tasmā rakkhitacittassā”ti padaṭṭhānapaññatti satiyā. “Sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti bhāvanāpaññatti samathassa. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro, ñatvāna udayabbayan”ti dassanabhūmiyā nikkhepapaññatti. “Thinamiddhābhibhū bhikkhū”ti samudayassa anavasesappahānapaññatti, “sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti bhāvanāpaññatti maggassa.

Ms 27Ne_523Niyutto paññattihārasampāto.
