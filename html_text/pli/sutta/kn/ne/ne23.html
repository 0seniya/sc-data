Padaṭṭhānahārasampāta

SC 1PTS vp Pali 104Ms 27Ne_500Msdiv 66Tattha katamo padaṭṭhāno hārasampāto?

SC 2Ms 27Ne_501“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. “Tasmā rakkhitacittassā”ti tiṇṇaṃ sucaritānaṃ padaṭṭhānaṃ. “Sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti samathassa padaṭṭhānaṃ. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro”ti vipassanāya padaṭṭhānaṃ. “Ñatvāna udayabbayan”ti dassanabhūmiyā padaṭṭhānaṃ. “Thinamiddhābhibhū bhikkhū”ti vīriyassa padaṭṭhānaṃ. “Sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti bhāvanāya padaṭṭhānaṃ.

Ms 27Ne_502Niyutto padaṭṭhāno hārasampāto.
