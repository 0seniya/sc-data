1. Saṅgahavāra

Ms 27Ne_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1Ms 27Ne_2Msdiv 0Yaṃ loko pūjayate,
Salokapālo sadā namassati ca;
Tasseta sāsanavaraṃ,
Vidūhi ñeyyaṃ naravarassa.

SC 2Ms 27Ne_3Dvādasa padāni suttaṃ,
Taṃ sabbaṃ byañjanañca attho ca;
Taṃ viññeyyaṃ ubhayaṃ,
Ko attho byañjanaṃ katamaṃ.

SC 3 Ms 27Ne_4Soḷasahārā netti,
Pañcanayā sāsanassa pariyeṭṭhi;
Aṭṭhārasamūlapadā,
Mahakaccānena niddiṭṭhā.

SC 4Ms 27Ne_5Hārā byañjanavicayo,
Suttassa nayā tayo ca suttattho;
Ubhayaṃ pariggahītaṃ,
Vuccati suttaṃ yathāsuttaṃ.

SC 5Ms 27Ne_6Yā ceva desanā yañca,
Desitaṃ ubhayameva viññeyyaṃ;
Tatrāyamānupubbī,
Navavidhasuttantapariyeṭṭhīti.

Ms 27Ne_7Saṅgahavāro.
