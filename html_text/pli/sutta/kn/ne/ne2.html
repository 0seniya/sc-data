2. Uddesavāra

SC 1Ms 27Ne_8Msdiv 1Tattha katame soḷasa hārā? Desanā vicayo yutti padaṭṭhāno lakkhaṇo catubyūho āvaṭṭo vibhatti parivattano vevacano paññatti otaraṇo sodhano adhiṭṭhāno parikkhāro samāropano iti.

SC 2Ms 27Ne_9Tassānugīti

SC 3 Ms 27Ne_10Desanā vicayo yutti,
padaṭṭhāno ca lakkhaṇo;
Catubyūho ca āvaṭṭo,
vibhatti parivattano.

SC 4Ms 27Ne_11Vevacano ca paññatti,
otaraṇo ca sodhano;
Adhiṭṭhāno parikkhāro,
samāropano soḷaso.

SC 5Ms 27Ne_12Ete soḷasa hārā,
Pakittitā atthato asaṃkiṇṇā;
Etesañceva bhavati,
Vitthāratayā nayavibhattīti.

SC 6Ms 27Ne_13Msdiv 2Tattha katame pañca nayā? Nandiyāvaṭṭo tipukkhalo sīhavikkīḷito disālocano aṅkuso iti.

SC 7Ms 27Ne_14Tassānugīti

SC 8 Ms 27Ne_15Paṭhamo nandiyāvaṭṭo,
dutiyo ca tipukkhalo;
Sīhavikkīḷito nāma,
tatiyo nayalañjako.

SC 9Ms 27Ne_16Disālocanamāhaṃsu,
catutthaṃ nayamuttamaṃ;
Pañcamo aṅkuso nāma,
sabbe pañca nayā gatāti.

SC 10Ms 27Ne_17Msdiv 3Tattha katamāni aṭṭhārasa mūlapadāni? Nava padāni kusalāni nava padāni akusalāni. Tattha katamāni nava padāni akusalāni, taṇhā avijjā lobho doso moho subhasaññā sukhasaññā niccasaññā attasaññāti, imāni nava padāni akusalāni, yattha sabbo akusalapakkho saṅgahaṃ samosaraṇaṃ gacchati.

SC 11Ms 27Ne_18Tattha katamāni nava padāni kusalāni? Samatho vipassanā alobho adoso amoho asubhasaññā dukkhasaññā aniccasaññā anattasaññāti, imāni nava padāni kusalāni, yattha sabbo kusalapakkho saṅgahaṃ samosaraṇaṃ gacchati.

Ms 27Ne_19Tatridaṃ uddānaṃ

SC 12Ms 27Ne_20Taṇhā ca avijjāpi ca,
Lobho doso tatheva moho ca;
Caturo ca vipallāsā,
Kilesabhūmī nava padāni.

SC 13Ms 27Ne_21Samatho ca vipassanā ca,
Kusalāni ca yāni tīṇi mūlāni;
Caturo satipaṭṭhānā,
Indriyabhūmī nava padāni.

SC 14 Ms 27Ne_22Navahi ca padehi kusalā,
Navahi ca yujjanti akusalapakkhā;
Ete kho mūlapadā,
Bhavanti aṭṭhārasa padānīti.

Ms 27Ne_23Uddesavāro.
