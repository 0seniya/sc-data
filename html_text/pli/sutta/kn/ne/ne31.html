Otaraṇahārasampāta

SC 1Ms 27Ne_524SC 1Ms 27Ne_524Msdiv 74Tattha katamo otaraṇo hārasampāto?

SC 2SC 2Ms 27Ne_525“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. “Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro”ti sammādiṭṭhiyā gahitāya gahitāni bhavanti pañcindriyāni, ayaṃ indriyehi otaraṇā.

SC 3SC 3Ms 27Ne_526Tāniyeva indriyāni vijjā, vijjuppādā avijjānirodho, avijjānirodhā saṅkhāranirodho, saṅkhāranirodhā viññāṇanirodho, evaṃ sabbaṃ, ayaṃ paṭiccasamuppādena otaraṇā.

SC 4SC 4Ms 27Ne_527Tāniyeva pañcindriyāni tīhi khandhehi saṅgahitāni—sīlakkhandhena samādhikkhandhena paññākkhandhena. Ayaṃ khandhehi otaraṇā.

SC 5SC 5Ms 27Ne_528Tāni yeva pañcindriyāni saṅkhārapariyāpannāni. Ye saṅkhārā anāsavā no ca bhavaṅgā, te saṅkhārā dhammadhātusaṅgahitā, ayaṃ dhātūhi otaraṇā.

SC 6SC 6Ms 27Ne_529Sā dhammadhātu dhammāyatanapariyāpannā, yaṃ āyatanaṃ anāsavaṃ no ca bhavaṅgaṃ, ayaṃ āyatanehi otaraṇā.

Ms 27Ne_530Niyutto otaraṇo hārasampāto.
