360. Suyonandījātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1209SC 1Ms 22J_1209Msdiv 55“Vāti gandho timirānaṃ,
kusamuddo ca ghosavā;
Dūre ito suyonandī,
tamba kāmā tudanti maṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1210SC 2Ms 22J_1210Msdiv 56“Kathaṃ samuddamatari,
Kathaṃ addakkhi sedumaṃ;
Kathaṃ tassā ca tuyhañca,
Ahu saggasamāgamo”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1211Msdiv 57“SC 3Ms 22J_1211Msdiv 57“Kurukacchā payātānaṃ,
vāṇijānaṃ dhanesinaṃ;
Makarehi abhidā nāvā,
phalakenāhamaplaviṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1212SC 4Ms 22J_1212Msdiv 58Sā maṃ saṇhena mudunā,
niccaṃ candanagandhinī;
Aṅgena uddharī bhaddā,
mātā puttaṃva orasaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1213SC 5Ms 22J_1213Msdiv 59Sā maṃ annena pānena,
vatthena sayanena ca;
Attanāpi ca mandakkhī,
evaṃ tamba vijānahī”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1214SC 6Ms 22J_1214Suyonandījātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Maṇikuṇḍalavaggo paṭhamo.

Dariyā puna kuñjara bhūnahatā,
