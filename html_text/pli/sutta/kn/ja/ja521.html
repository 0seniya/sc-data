521. Tesakuṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 23J_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 5.112Ms 23J_2Msdiv 1“Vessantaraṃ taṃ pucchāmi,
sakuṇa bhaddamatthu te;
Rajjaṃ kāretukāmena,
kiṃ su kiccaṃ kataṃ varaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 23J_3Msdiv 2“Cirassaṃ vata maṃ tāto,
kaṃso bārāṇasiggaho;
Pamatto appamattaṃ maṃ,
pitā puttaṃ acodayi.

SC 4Ms 23J_4Msdiv 3Paṭhameneva vitathaṃ,
kodhaṃ hāsaṃ nivāraye;
Tato kiccāni kāreyya,
taṃ vataṃ āhu khattiya.

SC 5Ms 23J_5Msdiv 4Yaṃ tvaṃ tāta tapokammaṃ,
Pubbe katamasaṃsayaṃ;
Ratto duṭṭho ca yaṃ kayirā,
Na taṃ kayirā tato puna.

SC 6Ms 23J_6Msdiv 5Khattiyassa pamattassa,
raṭṭhasmiṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhana;
Sabbe bhogā vinassanti,
rañño taṃ vuccate aghaṃ.

SC 7Ms 23J_7Msdiv 6Sirī tāta alakkhī ca,
pucchitā etadabravuṃ;
Uṭṭhānavīriye pose,
ramāhaṃ anusūyake.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 5.113Ms 23J_8Msdiv 7Usūyake duhadaye,
purise kammadussake;
Kāḷakaṇṇī mahārāja,
ramati cakkabhañjanī.

SC 9Ms 23J_9Msdiv 8So tvaṃ sabbesu suhadayo,
sabbesaṃ rakkhito bhava;
Alakkhiṃ nuda mahārāja,
lakkhyā bhava nivesanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 23J_10Msdiv 9Sa lakkhīdhitisampanno,
puriso hi mahaggato;
Amittānaṃ kāsipati,
mūlaṃ aggañca chindati.

SC 11Ms 23J_11Msdiv 10Sakkopi hi bhūtapati,
uṭṭhāne nappamajjati;
Sa kalyāṇe dhitiṃ katvā,
uṭṭhāne kurute mano.

SC 12Ms 23J_12Msdiv 11Gandhabbā pitaro devā,
sājīvā honti tādino;
Uṭṭhāhato Uṭṭhāhato appamajjato,
anutiṭṭhanti devatā.

SC 13Ms 23J_13Msdiv 12So appamatto akkuddho,
tāta kiccāni kāraya;
Vāyamassu ca kiccesu,
nālaso vindate sukhaṃ.

SC 14Ms 23J_14Msdiv 13Tattheva te vattapadā,
esāva anusāsanī;
Alaṃ mitte sukhāpetuṃ,
amittānaṃ dukhāya ca”.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 5.116Ms 23J_15Msdiv 14“Sakkhisi tvaṃ kuṇḍalini,
maññasi khattabandhuni;
Rajjaṃ kāretukāmena,
kiṃ su kiccaṃ kataṃ varaṃ”.

SC 16Ms 23J_16Msdiv 15“Dveva tāta padakāni,
yattha sabbaṃ patiṭṭhitaṃ;
Aladdhassa ca yo lābho,
laddhassa cānurakkhaṇā.

SC 17Ms 23J_17Msdiv 16Amacce tāta jānāhi,
dhīre atthassa kovide;
Anakkhākitave tāta,
asoṇḍe avināsake.

SC 18Ms 23J_18Msdiv 17Yo ca taṃ tāta rakkheyya,
dhanaṃ yañceva te siyā;
Sūtova rathaṃ saṅgaṇhe,
so te kiccāni kāraye.

SC 19Ms 23J_19Msdiv 18Susaṅgahitantajano,
sayaṃ vittaṃ avekkhiya;
Nidhiñca iṇadānañca,
na kare parapattiyā.

SC 20Ms 23J_20Msdiv 19Sayaṃ āyaṃ vayaṃ jaññā,
sayaṃ jaññā katākataṃ;
Niggaṇhe niggahārahaṃ,
paggaṇhe paggahārahaṃ.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 5.117Ms 23J_21Msdiv 20Sayaṃ jānapadaṃ atthaṃ,
anusāsa rathesabha;
Mā te adhammikā yuttā,
dhanaṃ raṭṭhañca nāsayuṃ.

SC 22Ms 23J_22Msdiv 21Mā ca vegena kiccāni,
karosi kārayesi vā;
Vegasā hi kataṃ kammaṃ,
mando pacchānutappati.

SC 23Ms 23J_23Msdiv 22Mā te adhisare muñca,
subāḷhamadhikopitaṃ;
Kodhasā hi bahū phītā,
kulā akulataṃ gatā.

SC 24Ms 23J_24Msdiv 23Mā tāta issaromhīti,
anatthāya patārayi;
Itthīnaṃ purisānañca,
mā te āsi dukhudrayo.

SC 25Ms 23J_25Msdiv 24Apetalomahaṃsassa,
rañño kāmānusārino;
Sabbe bhogā vinassanti,
rañño taṃ vuccate aghaṃ.

SC 26Ms 23J_26Msdiv 25Tattheva te vattapadā,
esāva anusāsanī;
Dakkhassudāni puññakaro,
asoṇḍo avināsako;
Sīlavāssu mahārāja,
dussīlo vinipātiko”.

SC 27PTS vp Pali 5.120Ms 23J_27Msdiv 26“Apucchimha kosiyagottaṃ,
kuṇḍaliniṃ tatheva ca;
Tvaṃ dāni vadehi jambuka,
balānaṃ balamuttamaṃ”.

SC 28Ms 23J_28Msdiv 27“Balaṃ pañcavidhaṃ loke,
purisasmiṃ mahaggate;
Tattha bāhubalaṃ nāma,
carimaṃ vuccate balaṃ.

SC 29Ms 23J_29Msdiv 28Bhogabalañca dīghāvu,
dutiyaṃ vuccate balaṃ;
Amaccabalañca dīghāvu,
tatiyaṃ vuccate balaṃ.

SC 30Ms 23J_30Msdiv 29Abhijaccabalaṃ ceva,
taṃ catutthaṃ asaṃsayaṃ;
Yāni cetāni sabbāni,
adhigaṇhāti paṇḍito.

SC 31PTS vp Pali 5.121Ms 23J_31Msdiv 30Taṃ balānaṃ balaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
aggaṃ paññābalaṃ balaṃ;
Paññābalenupatthaddho,
atthaṃ vindati paṇḍito.

SC 32Ms 23J_32Msdiv 31Api ce labhati mando,
phītaṃ dharaṇimuttamaṃ;
Akāmassa pasayhaṃ vā,
añño taṃ paṭipajjati.

SC 33Ms 23J_33Msdiv 32Abhijātopi ce hoti,
rajjaṃ laddhāna khattiyo;
Duppañño hi kāsipati,
sabbenapi na jīvati.

SC 34Ms 23J_34Msdiv 33Paññāva sutaṃ vinicchinī,
Paññā kitti silokavaḍḍhanī;
Paññāsahito naro idha,
Api dukkhe sukhāni vindati.

SC 35Ms 23J_35Msdiv 34Paññañca kho asussūsaṃ,
na koci adhigacchati;
Bahussutaṃ anāgamma,
dhammaṭṭhaṃ avinibbhujaṃ.

SC 36Ms 23J_36Msdiv 35Yo ca dhammavibhaṅgaññū,
kāluṭṭhāyī matandito;
Anuṭṭhahati kālena,
kammaphalaṃ tassijjhati.

SC 37Ms 23J_37Msdiv 36Anāyatanasīlassa,
anāyatanasevino;
Na nibbindiyakārissa,
sammadattho vipaccati.

SC 38Ms 23J_38Msdiv 37Ajjhattañca payuttassa,
tathāyatanasevino;
Anibbindiyakārissa,
sammadattho vipaccati.

SC 39Ms 23J_39Msdiv 38Yogappayogasaṅkhātaṃ,
sambhatassānurakkhaṇaṃ;
Tāni tvaṃ tāta sevassu,
mā akammāya randhayi;
Akammunā hi dummedho,
naḷāgāraṃva sīdati”.

SC 40PTS vp Pali 5.123Ms 23J_40Msdiv 39“Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
mātāpitūsu khattiya;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 41Ms 23J_41Msdiv 40Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
puttadāresu khattiya;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 42Ms 23J_42Msdiv 41Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
mittāmaccesu khattiya;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 43Ms 23J_43Msdiv 42Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
vāhanesu balesu ca;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 44Ms 23J_44Msdiv 43Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
gāmesu nigamesu ca;
… pe …

SC 45Ms 23J_45Msdiv 44Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
raṭṭhesu janapadesu ca;
… pe …

SC 46Ms 23J_46Msdiv 45Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
samaṇabrāhmaṇesu ca;
… pe …

SC 47Ms 23J_47Msdiv 46Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
migapakkhīsu khattiya;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 48Ms 23J_48Msdiv 47Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
dhammo ciṇṇo sukhāvaho;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
rāja saggaṃ gamissasi.

SC 49Ms 23J_49Msdiv 48Dhammaṃ cara mahārāja,
Saindā devā sabrahmakā;
Suciṇṇena divaṃ pattā,
Mā dhammaṃ rāja pāmado.

SC 50PTS vp Pali 5.124Ms 23J_50Msdiv 49Tattheva te vattapadā,
Esāva anusāsanī;
Sappaññasevī kalyāṇī,
Samattaṃ sāma taṃ vidū”ti.

SC 51Ms 23J_51Tesakuṇajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
