483. Sarabhamigajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.269Ms 22J_2526Msdiv 134“Āsīsetheva puriso,
na nibbindeyya paṇḍito;
Passāmi vohaṃ attānaṃ,
yathā icchiṃ tathā ahu.

SC 2Ms 22J_2527Msdiv 135Āsīsetheva puriso,
na nibbindeyya paṇḍito;
Passāmi vohaṃ attānaṃ,
udakā thalamubbhataṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_2528Msdiv 136Vāyametheva puriso,
na nibbindeyya paṇḍito;
Passāmi vohaṃ attānaṃ,
yathā icchiṃ tathā ahu.

SC 4Ms 22J_2529Msdiv 137Vāyametheva puriso,
na nibbindeyya paṇḍito;
Passāmi vohaṃ attānaṃ,
udakā thalamubbhataṃ.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 4.270Ms 22J_2530Msdiv 138Dukkhūpanītopi naro sapañño,
Āsaṃ na chindeyya sukhāgamāya;
Bahū hi phassā ahitā hitā ca,
Avitakkitā maccamupabbajanti.

SC 6Ms 22J_2531Msdiv 139Acintitampi bhavati,
cintitampi vinassati;
Na hi cintāmayā bhogā,
itthiyā purisassa vā”.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 4.271Ms 22J_2532Msdiv 140“Sarabhaṃ giriduggasmiṃ,
yaṃ tvaṃ anusarī pure;
Alīnacittassa tuvaṃ,
vikkantamanujīvasi.

SC 8Ms 22J_2533Msdiv 141Yo taṃ viduggā narakā samuddhari,
Silāya yoggaṃ sarabho karitvā;
Dukkhūpanītaṃ maccumukhā pamocayi,
Alīnacittaṃ ta migaṃ vadesi”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2534Msdiv 142“Kiṃ tvaṃ nu tattheva tadā ahosi,
Udāhu te koci naṃ etadakkhā;
Vivaṭṭacchaddo nusi sabbadassī,
Ñāṇaṃ nu te brāhmaṇa bhiṃsarūpaṃ”.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 4.272Ms 22J_2535Msdiv 143“Na cevahaṃ tattha tadā ahosiṃ,
Na cāpi me koci naṃ etadakkhā;
Gāthāpadānañca subhāsitānaṃ,
Atthaṃ tadānenti janinda dhīrā”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2536Msdiv 144“Ādāya pattiṃ paraviriyaghātiṃ,
Cāpe saraṃ kiṃ vicikicchase tuvaṃ;
Nunno saro sarabhaṃ hantu khippaṃ,
Annañhi etaṃ varapañña rañño”.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 4.273Ms 22J_2537Msdiv 145“Addhā pajānāmi ahampi etaṃ,
Annaṃ migo brāhmaṇa khattiyassa;
Pubbe katañca apacāyamāno,
Tasmā migaṃ sarabhaṃ no hanāmi”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2538Msdiv 146“Neso migo mahārāja,
asureso disampati;
Etaṃ hantvā manussinda,
bhavassu amarādhipo.

SC 14Ms 22J_2539Msdiv 147Sace ca rājā vicikicchase tuvaṃ,
Hantuṃ migaṃ sarabhaṃ sahāyakaṃ;
Saputtadāro naravīraseṭṭha,
Gantā tuvaṃ vetaraṇiṃ yamassa”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2540Msdiv 148“Kāmaṃ ahaṃ jānapadā ca sabbe,
Puttā ca dārā ca sahāyasaṅghā;
Gacchemu taṃ vetaraṇiṃ yamassa,
Na tveva hañño mama pāṇado yo.

SC 16PTS vp Pali 4.274Ms 22J_2541Msdiv 149Ayaṃ migo kicchagatassa mayhaṃ,
Ekassa kattā vivanasmi ghore;
Taṃ tādisaṃ pubbakiccaṃ saranto,
Jānaṃ mahābrahme kathaṃ haneyyaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 22J_2542Msdiv 150“Mittābhirādhī cirameva jīva,
Rajjaṃ imaṃ dhammaguṇe pasāsa;
Nārīgaṇehi paricāriyanto,
Modassu raṭṭhe tidiveva vāsavo.

SC 18Ms 22J_2543Msdiv 151Akkodhano niccapasannacitto,
Sabbātithī yācayogo bhavitvā;
Datvā ca bhutvā ca yathānubhāvaṃ,
Anindito saggamupehi ṭhānan”ti. (1930)

SC 19Ms 22J_2544Sarabhamigajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 20Ms 22J_2545Terasakanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 21Ms 22J_2546Tassuddānaṃ

SC 22Ms 22J_2547Varaamba kuṭhāri sahaṃsavaro,
Atharaññasmiṃ dūtakapañcamako;
Atha bodhi akitti sutakkarinā,
Atha rurumigenaparo sarabhoti.
