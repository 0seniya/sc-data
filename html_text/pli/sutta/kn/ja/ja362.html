362. Sīlavīmaṃsajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1223Msdiv 65“Sīlaṃ seyyo sutaṃ seyyo,
iti me saṃsayo ahu;
Sīlameva sutā seyyo,
iti me natthi saṃsayo.

SC 2Ms 22J_1224Msdiv 66Moghā jāti ca vaṇṇo ca,
sīlameva kiruttamaṃ;
Sīlena anupetassa,
sutenattho na vijjati.

SC 3Ms 22J_1225Msdiv 67Khattiyo ca adhammaṭṭho,
vesso cādhammanissito;
Te pariccajjubho loke,
upapajjanti duggatiṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1226Msdiv 68Khattiyā brāhmaṇā vessā,
suddā caṇḍālapukkusā;
Idha dhammaṃ caritvāna,
bhavanti tidive samā.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 3.195Ms 22J_1227Msdiv 69Na vedā samparāyāya,
Na jāti nāpi bandhavā;
Sakañca sīlaṃ saṃsuddhaṃ,
Samparāyāya sukhāya cā”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1228Sīlavīmaṃsajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
