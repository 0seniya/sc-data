459. Pānīyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2169Msdiv 59“Mitto mittassa pānīyaṃ,
adinnaṃ paribhuñjisaṃ;
Tena pacchā vijigucchiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Mā puna akaraṃ pāpaṃ,
tasmā pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_2170Msdiv 60Paradārañca disvāna,
chando me udapajjatha;
Tena pacchā vijigucchiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Mā puna akaraṃ pāpaṃ,
tasmā pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_2171Msdiv 61Pitaraṃ me mahārāja,
corā agaṇhu kānane;
Tesāhaṃ pucchito jānaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākariṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_2172Msdiv 62Tena pacchā vijigucchiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Mā puna akaraṃ pāpaṃ,
tasmā pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_2173Msdiv 63Pāṇātipātamakaruṃ,
somayāge upaṭṭhite;
Tesāhaṃ samanuññāsiṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_2174Msdiv 64Tena pacchā vijigucchiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Mā puna akaraṃ pāpaṃ,
tasmā pabbajito ahaṃ.

SC 7Ms 22J_2175Msdiv 65Surāmerayamādhukā,
ye janā paṭhamāsu no;
Bahūnaṃ te anatthāya,
majjapānamakappayuṃ;
Tesāhaṃ samanuññāsiṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_2176Msdiv 66Tena pacchā vijigucchiṃ,
taṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Mā puna akaraṃ pāpaṃ,
tasmā pabbajito ahaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2177Msdiv 67“Dhiratthu subahū kāme,
duggandhe bahukaṇṭake;
Ye ahaṃ paṭisevanto,
nālabhiṃ tādisaṃ sukhaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2178Msdiv 68“Mahassādā sukhā kāmā,
Natthi kāmā paraṃ sukhaṃ;
Ye kāme paṭisevanti,
Saggaṃ te upapajjare”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2179Msdiv 69“Appassādā dukhā kāmā,
natthi kāmā paraṃ dukhaṃ;
Ye kāme paṭisevanti,
nirayaṃ te upapajjare.

SC 12Ms 22J_2180Msdiv 70Asī yathā sunisito,
nettiṃsova supāyiko;
Sattīva urasi khittā,
kāmā dukkhatarā tato.

SC 13Ms 22J_2181Msdiv 71Aṅgārānaṃva jalitaṃ,
kāsuṃ sādhikaporisaṃ;
Phālaṃva divasantattaṃ,
kāmā dukkhatarā tato.

SC 14Ms 22J_2182Msdiv 72Visaṃ yathā halāhalaṃ,
telaṃ pakkuthitaṃ yathā;
Tambaloha vilīnaṃva,
kāmā dukkhatarā tato”ti.

SC 15Ms 22J_2183Pānīyajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
