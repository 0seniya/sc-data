165. Nakulajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_386Msdiv 29“Sandhiṃ katvā amittena,
aṇḍajena jalābuja;
Vivariya dāṭhaṃ sesi,
kuto te bhayamāgataṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_387Msdiv 30“Saṅketheva amittasmiṃ,
mittasmimpi na vissase;
Abhayā bhayamuppannaṃ,
api mūlāni kantatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_388Nakulajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
