348. Araññajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1126Msdiv 189“Araññā gāmamāgamma,
kiṃsīlaṃ kiṃvataṃ ahaṃ;
Purisaṃ tāta seveyyaṃ,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1127Msdiv 190“Yo taṃ vissāsaye tāta,
vissāsañca khameyya te;
Sussūsī ca titikkhī ca,
taṃ bhajehi ito gato.

SC 3Ms 22J_1128Msdiv 191Yassa kāyena vācāya,
manasā natthi dukkaṭaṃ;
Urasīva patiṭṭhāya,
taṃ bhajehi ito gato.

SC 4Ms 22J_1129Msdiv 192Haliddirāgaṃ kapicittaṃ,
purisaṃ rāgavirāginaṃ;
Tādisaṃ tāta mā sevi,
nimmanussampi ce siyā”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1130Araññajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
