67. Ucchaṅgajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_147Msdiv 67“Ucchaṅge deva me putto,
Pathe dhāvantiyā pati;
Tañca desaṃ na passāmi,
Yato sodariyamānayeti.

SC 2Ms 22J_148Ucchaṅgajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
