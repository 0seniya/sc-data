320. Succajajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.69Ms 22J_980Msdiv 77“Succajaṃ vata naccaji,
vācāya adadaṃ giriṃ;
Kiṃ hitassa cajantassa,
vācāya adada pabbataṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_981Msdiv 78“Yañhi kayirā tañhi vade,
yaṃ na kayirā na taṃ vade;
Akarontaṃ bhāsamānaṃ,
parijānanti paṇḍitā”.

SC 3Ms 22J_982Msdiv 79“Rājaputta namo tyatthu,
sacce dhamme ṭhito casi;
Yassa te byasanaṃ patto,
saccasmiṃ ramate mano”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 3.70Ms 22J_983Msdiv 80“Yā daliddī daliddassa,
aḍḍhā aḍḍhassa kittima;
Sā hissa paramā bhariyā,
sahiraññassa itthiyo”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_984Succajajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Pucimandavaggo dutiyo.

SC 6Ms 22J_985Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 22J_986Atha cora sakassapa khantīvaro,
Dujjīvitatā ca varā pharusā;
Atha sasa matañca vasanta sukhaṃ,
Succajaṃvatanaccajinā ca dasāti.
