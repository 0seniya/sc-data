58. Tayodhammajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.282Ms 22J_127Msdiv 58“Yassa ete tayo dhammā,
vānarinda yathā tava;
Dakkhiyaṃ sūriyaṃ paññā,
diṭṭhaṃ so ativattatī”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_128Tayodhammajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
