422. Cetiyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1722Msdiv 45“Dhammo have hato hanti,
Nāhato hanti kiñcanaṃ;
Tasmā hi dhammaṃ na hane,
Mā tvaṃ dhammo hato hani. (1)

SC 2Ms 22J_1723Msdiv 46Alikaṃ bhāsamānassa,
apakkamanti devatā;
Pūtikañca mukhaṃ vāti,
sakaṭṭhānā ca dhaṃsati;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 3Ms 22J_1724Msdiv 47Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhūmiyaṃ tiṭṭha cetiya. (2)

SC 4Ms 22J_1725Msdiv 48Akāle vassatī tassa,
kāle tassa na vassati;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 5Ms 22J_1726Msdiv 49Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhūmiṃ pavisa cetiya. (3)

SC 6Ms 22J_1727Msdiv 50Jivhā tassa dvidhā hoti,
uragasseva disampati;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 7Ms 22J_1728Msdiv 51Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhiyyo pavisa cetiya. (4)

SC 8Ms 22J_1729Msdiv 52Jivhā tassa na bhavati,
macchasseva disampati;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 9Ms 22J_1730Msdiv 53Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhiyyo pavisa cetiya. (5)

SC 10Ms 22J_1731Msdiv 54Thiyova tassa jāyanti,
na pumā jāyare kule;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 11Ms 22J_1732Msdiv 55Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhiyyo pavisa cetiya. (6)

SC 12Ms 22J_1733Msdiv 56Puttā tassa na bhavanti,
pakkamanti disodisaṃ;
Yo jānaṃ pucchito pañhaṃ,
aññathā naṃ viyākare.

SC 13Ms 22J_1734Msdiv 57Sace hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
hohi rāja yathā pure;
Musā ce bhāsase rāja,
bhiyyo pavisa cetiya”. (7)

SC 14Ms 22J_1735Msdiv 58“Sa rājā isinā satto,
antalikkhacaro pure;
Pāvekkhi pathaviṃ cecco,
hīnatto patva pariyāyaṃ.

SC 15Ms 22J_1736Msdiv 59Tasmā hi chandāgamanaṃ,
Nappasaṃsanti paṇḍitā;
Aduṭṭhacitto bhāseyya,
Giraṃ saccūpasaṃhitanti”. (8)

SC 16Ms 22J_1737Cetiyajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
