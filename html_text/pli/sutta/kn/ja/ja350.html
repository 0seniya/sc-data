350. Devatāpañhajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1136Msdiv 197“Hanti hatthehi pādehi,
mukhañca parisumbhati;
Sa ve rāja piyo hoti,
kaṃ tena tvābhipassasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1137Msdiv 198“Akkosati yathākāmaṃ,
āgamañcassa icchati;
Sa ve rāja piyo hoti,
kaṃ tena tvābhipassasi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1138Msdiv 199“Abbhakkhāti abhūtena,
alikenābhisāraye;
Sa ve rāja piyo hoti,
kaṃ tena tvābhipassasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1139Msdiv 200“Haraṃ annañca pānañca,
Vatthasenāsanāni ca;
Aññadatthuharā santā,
Te ve rāja piyā honti;
Kaṃ tena tvābhipassasī”ti. (701)

SC 5Ms 22J_1140Devatāpañhajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Cūḷakuṇālavaggo pañcamo.

SC 6Ms 22J_1141Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 22J_1142Narānaṃ asakkhivasimhavaro,
Nīliyamaggivarañca puna;
Puna rasāyasakūṭavaro,
Tathārañña sārathi hanti dasāti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1143Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 22J_1144Kāliṅgaṃ pucimandañca,
kuṭidūsaka kokilā;
Cūḷakuṇālavaggo so,
pañcamo suppakāsitoti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1145Catukkanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
