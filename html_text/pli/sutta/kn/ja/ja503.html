503. Sattigumbajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.432Ms 22J_3017Msdiv 159Migaluddo mahārājā,
pañcālānaṃ rathesabho;
Nikkhanto saha senāya,
ogaṇo vanamāgamā.

SC 2Ms 22J_3018Msdiv 160Tatthaddasā araññasmiṃ,
takkarānaṃ kuṭiṃ kataṃ;
Tassā kuṭiyā nikkhamma,
suvo luddāni bhāsati.

SC 3Ms 22J_3019Msdiv 161“Sampannavāhano poso,
yuvā sammaṭṭhakuṇḍalo;
Sobhati lohituṇhīso,
divā sūriyova bhāsati.

SC 4Ms 22J_3020Msdiv 162Majjhanhike sampatike,
sutto rājā sasārathi;
Handassābharaṇaṃ sabbaṃ,
gaṇhāma sāhasā mayaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_3021Msdiv 163Nisīthepi raho dāni,
Sutto rājā sasārathi;
Ādāya vatthaṃ maṇikuṇḍalañca,
Hantvāna sākhāhi avattharāma”.

SC 6Ms 22J_3022Msdiv 164“Kiṃ nu ummattarūpova,
sattigumba pabhāsasi;
Durāsadā hi rājāno,
aggi pajjalito yathā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_3023Msdiv 165“Atha tvaṃ patikolamba,
matto thullāni gajjasi;
Mātari mayhaṃ naggāya,
kiṃ nu tvaṃ vijigucchase”.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 4.433Ms 22J_3024Msdiv 166“Uṭṭhehi samma taramāno,
rathaṃ yojehi sārathi;
Sakuṇo me na ruccati,
aññaṃ gacchāma assamaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_3025Msdiv 167“Yutto ratho mahārāja,
yutto ca balavāhano;
Adhitiṭṭha mahārāja,
aññaṃ gacchāma assamaṃ”.

SC 10Ms 22J_3026Msdiv 168“Ko numeva gatā sabbe,
ye asmiṃ paricārakā;
Esa gacchati pañcālo,
mutto tesaṃ adassanā.

SC 11Ms 22J_3027Msdiv 169Kodaṇḍakāni gaṇhatha,
sattiyo tomarāni ca;
Esa gacchati pañcālo,
mā vo muñcittha jīvataṃ”.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 4.434Ms 22J_3028Msdiv 170Athāparo paṭinandittha,
suvo lohitatuṇḍako;
“Svāgataṃ te mahārāja,
atho te adurāgataṃ;
Issarosi anuppatto,
yaṃ idhatthi pavedaya.

SC 13Ms 22J_3029Msdiv 171Tindukāni piyālāni,
madhuke kāsumāriyo;
Phalāni khuddakappāni,
bhuñja rāja varaṃ varaṃ.

SC 14Ms 22J_3030Msdiv 172Idampi pānīyaṃ sītaṃ,
ābhataṃ girigabbharā;
Tato piva mahārāja,
sace tvaṃ abhikaṅkhasi.

SC 15Ms 22J_3031Msdiv 173Araññaṃ uñchāya gatā,
ye asmiṃ paricārakā;
Sayaṃ uṭṭhāya gaṇhavho,
hatthā me natthi dātave”.

SC 16Ms 22J_3032Msdiv 174“Bhaddako vatayaṃ pakkhī,
dijo paramadhammiko;
Atheso itaro pakkhī,
suvo luddāni bhāsati.

SC 17Ms 22J_3033Msdiv 175‘Etaṃ hanatha bandhatha,
Mā vo muñcittha jīvataṃ’;
Iccevaṃ vilapantassa,
Sotthiṃ pattosmi assamaṃ”.

SC 18Ms 22J_3034Msdiv 176“Bhātarosma mahārāja,
sodariyā ekamātukā;
Ekarukkhasmiṃ saṃvaḍḍhā,
nānākhettagatā ubho.

SC 19Ms 22J_3035Msdiv 177Sattigumbo ca corānaṃ,
ahañca isīnaṃ idha;
Asataṃ so, sataṃ ahaṃ,
tena dhammena no vinā”.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 4.435Ms 22J_3036Msdiv 178“Tattha vadho ca bandho ca,
nikatī vañcanāni ca;
Ālopā sāhasākārā,
tāni so tattha sikkhati.

SC 21Ms 22J_3037Msdiv 179Idha saccañca dhammo ca,
Ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo;
Āsanūdakadāyīnaṃ,
Aṅke vaddhosmi bhāradha”.

SC 22Ms 22J_3038Msdiv 180“Yaṃ yañhi rāja bhajati,
santaṃ vā yadi vā asaṃ;
Sīlavantaṃ visīlaṃ vā,
vasaṃ tasseva gacchati.

SC 23Ms 22J_3039Msdiv 181Yādisaṃ kurute mittaṃ,
yādisaṃ cūpasevati;
Sopi tādisako hoti,
sahavāso hi tādiso.

SC 24Ms 22J_3040Msdiv 182Sevamāno sevamānaṃ,
samphuṭṭho samphusaṃ paraṃ;
Saro diddho kalāpaṃva,
alittamupalimpati;
Upalepabhayā dhīro,
neva pāpasakhā siyā.

SC 25Ms 22J_3041Msdiv 183Pūtimacchaṃ kusaggena,
yo naro upanayhati;
Kusāpi pūti vāyanti,
evaṃ bālūpasevanā.

SC 26PTS vp Pali 4.436Ms 22J_3042Msdiv 184Tagarañca palāsena,
yo naro upanayhati;
Pattāpi surabhi vāyanti,
evaṃ dhīrūpasevanā.

SC 27Ms 22J_3043Msdiv 185Tasmā pattapuṭasseva,
ñatvā sampākamattano;
Asante nopaseveyya,
sante seveyya paṇḍito;
Asanto nirayaṃ nenti,
santo pāpenti suggatin”ti.

SC 28Ms 22J_3044Sattigumbajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
