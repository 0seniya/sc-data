110. Sabbasaṃhārakapañhajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.424Ms 22J_243Msdiv 110“Sabbasaṃhārako natthi,
suddhaṃ kaṅgu pavāyati;
Alikaṃ bhāyatiyaṃ dhuttī,
saccamāha mahallikā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_244Sabbasaṃhārakapañhajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Parosatavaggo ekādasamo.

SC 3Ms 22J_245Tassuddānaṃ

SC 4Ms 22J_246Saparosatatāyitaverī puna,
Bhamacakkatha nāgasirivhayano;
Sukhakañca vata sippaka bāhiyā,
Kuṇḍapūva mahallikakā ca dasāti.
