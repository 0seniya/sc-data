414. Jāgarajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1639SC 1Ms 22J_1639Msdiv 135“Kodha jāgarataṃ sutto,
kodha suttesu jāgaro;
Ko mametaṃ vijānāti,
ko taṃ paṭibhaṇāti me”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1640SC 2Ms 22J_1640Msdiv 136“Ahaṃ jāgarataṃ sutto,
ahaṃ suttesu jāgaro;
Ahametaṃ vijānāmi,
ahaṃ paṭibhaṇāmi te”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1641SC 3Ms 22J_1641Msdiv 137“Kathaṃ jāgarataṃ sutto,
kathaṃ suttesu jāgaro;
Kathaṃ etaṃ vijānāsi,
kathaṃ paṭibhaṇāsi me”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1642SC 4Ms 22J_1642Msdiv 138“Ye dhammaṃ nappajānanti,
saṃyamoti damoti ca;
Tesu suppamānesu,
ahaṃ jaggāmi devate.

SC 5Ms 22J_1643SC 5Ms 22J_1643Msdiv 139Yesaṃ rāgo ca doso ca,
avijjā ca virājitā;
Tesu jāgaramānesu,
ahaṃ suttosmi devate.

SC 6Ms 22J_1644SC 6Ms 22J_1644Msdiv 140Evaṃ jāgarataṃ sutto,
evaṃ suttesu jāgaro;
Evametaṃ vijānāmi,
evaṃ paṭibhaṇāmi te”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1645SC 7Ms 22J_1645Msdiv 141“Sādhu jāgarataṃ sutto,
sādhu suttesu jāgaro;
Sādhumetaṃ vijānāsi,
sādhu paṭibhaṇāsi me”ti.

SC 8SC 8Ms 22J_1646Jāgarajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
