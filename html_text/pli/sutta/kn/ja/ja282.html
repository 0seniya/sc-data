282. Seyyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_800Msdiv 94“Seyyaṃso seyyaso hoti,
yo seyyamupasevati;
Ekena sandhiṃ katvāna,
sataṃ vajjhe amocayiṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_801Msdiv 95Tasmā sabbena lokena,
sandhiṃ katvāna ekato;
Pecca saggaṃ nigaccheyya,
idaṃ suṇātha kāsiyā”.

SC 3Ms 22J_802Msdiv 96Idaṃ vatvā mahārājā,
Kaṃso bārāṇasiggaho;
Dhanuṃ kaṇḍañca nikkhippa,
Saṃyamaṃ ajjhupāgamīti.

SC 4Ms 22J_803Seyyajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
