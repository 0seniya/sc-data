395. Pārāvatajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1473Msdiv 135“Cirassaṃ vata passāmi,
sahāyaṃ maṇidhārinaṃ;
Sukatā massukuttiyā,
sobhate vata me sakhā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1474Msdiv 136“Parūḷhakacchanakhalomo,
ahaṃ kammesu byāvaṭo;
Cirassaṃ nhāpitaṃ laddhā,
lomaṃ taṃ ajja hārayiṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1475Msdiv 137“Yannu lomaṃ ahāresi,
dullabhaṃ laddha kappakaṃ;
Atha kiñcarahi te samma,
kaṇṭhe kiṇikiṇāyati”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1476Msdiv 138“Manussasukhumālānaṃ,
maṇi kaṇṭhesu lambati;
Tesāhaṃ anusikkhāmi,
mā tvaṃ maññi davā kataṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1477Msdiv 139Sacepimaṃ pihayasi,
massukuttiṃ sukāritaṃ;
Kārayissāmi te samma,
maṇiñcāpi dadāmi te”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1478Msdiv 140“Tvaññeva maṇinā channo,
Sukatāya ca massuyā;
Āmanta kho taṃ gacchāmi,
Piyaṃ me tavadassanan”ti. (980)

SC 7Ms 22J_1479Pārāvatajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kharaputtavaggo dutiyo.

SC 8Ms 22J_1480Tassuddānaṃ

SC 9Ms 22J_1481Atha passa sasūci ca tuṇḍilako,
Miga mayhakapañcamapakkhivaro;
Atha pañjali vārija mejjha puna,
Atha vaṭṭa kapotavarena dasāti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1482Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 11Ms 22J_1483Atha vaggaṃ pakittissaṃ,
chanipātaṃ varuttame;
Avāriyā ca kharo ca,
dve ca vuttā subyañjanāti.

SC 12Ms 22J_1484Chakkanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
