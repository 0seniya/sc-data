450. Bilārakosiyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2044Msdiv 125“Apacantāpi dicchanti,
santo laddhāna bhojanaṃ;
Kimeva tvaṃ pacamāno,
yaṃ na dajjā na taṃ samaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_2045Msdiv 126Maccherā ca pamādā ca,
evaṃ dānaṃ na diyyati;
Puññaṃ ākaṅkhamānena,
deyyaṃ hoti vijānatā.

SC 3Ms 22J_2046Msdiv 127Yasseva bhīto na dadāti maccharī,
Tadevādadato bhayaṃ;
Jighacchā ca pipāsā ca,
Yassa bhāyati maccharī;
Tameva bālaṃ phusati,
Asmiṃ loke paramhi ca.

SC 4Ms 22J_2047Msdiv 128Tasmā vineyya maccheraṃ,
dajjā dānaṃ malābhibhū;
Puññāni paralokasmiṃ,
patiṭṭhā honti pāṇinaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2048Msdiv 129“Duddadaṃ dadamānānaṃ,
dukkaraṃ kamma kubbataṃ;
Asanto nānukubbanti,
sataṃ dhammo durannayo.

SC 6Ms 22J_2049Msdiv 130Tasmā satañca asataṃ,
nānā hoti ito gati;
Asanto nirayaṃ yanti,
santo saggaparāyaṇā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2050Msdiv 131“Appasmeke pavecchanti,
bahuneke na dicchare;
Appasmā dakkhiṇā dinnā,
sahassena samaṃ mitā”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2051Msdiv 132“Dhammaṃ care yopi samuñchakaṃ care,
Dārañca posaṃ dadamappakasmiṃ;
Sataṃ sahassānaṃ sahassayāginaṃ,
Kalampi nāgghanti tathāvidhassa te”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2052Msdiv 133“Kenesa yañño vipulo mahagghato,
Samena dinnassa na agghameti;
Kathaṃ sataṃ sahassānaṃ sahassayāginaṃ,
Kalampi nāgghanti tathāvidhassa te”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2053Msdiv 134“Dadanti heke visame niviṭṭhā,
Chetvā vadhitvā atha socayitvā;
Sā dakkhiṇā assumukhā sadaṇḍā,
Samena dinnassa na agghameti;
Evaṃ sataṃ sahassānaṃ sahassayāginaṃ,
Kalampi nāgghanti tathāvidhassa te”ti.

SC 11Ms 22J_2054Bilārakosiyajātakaṃ dvādasamaṃ.
