308. Sakuṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_918Msdiv 29“Akaramhasa te kiccaṃ,
yaṃ balaṃ ahuvamhase;
Migarāja namo tyatthu,
api kiñci labhāmase”.

SC 2Ms 22J_919Msdiv 30“Mama lohitabhakkhassa,
niccaṃ luddāni kubbato;
Dantantaragato santo,
taṃ bahuṃ yampi jīvasi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_920Msdiv 31“Akataññumakattāraṃ,
katassa appaṭikārakaṃ;
Yasmiṃ kataññutā natthi,
niratthā tassa sevanā.

SC 4Ms 22J_921Msdiv 32Yassa sammukhaciṇṇena,
Mittadhammo na labbhati;
Anusūyamanakkosaṃ,
Saṇikaṃ tamhā apakkame”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_922Sakuṇajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
