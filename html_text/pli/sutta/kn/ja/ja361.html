361. Vaṇṇārohajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1217Msdiv 60“Vaṇṇārohena jātiyā,
balanikkamanena ca;
Subāhu na mayā seyyo,
sudāṭha iti bhāsasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1218Msdiv 61“Vaṇṇārohena jātiyā,
balanikkamanena ca;
Sudāṭho na mayā seyyo,
subāhu iti bhāsasi.

SC 3Ms 22J_1219Msdiv 62Evañce maṃ viharantaṃ,
subāhu samma dubbhasi;
Na dānāhaṃ tayā saddhiṃ,
saṃvāsamabhirocaye.

SC 4Ms 22J_1220Msdiv 63Yo paresaṃ vacanāni,
saddaheyya yathātathaṃ;
Khippaṃ bhijjetha mittasmiṃ,
verañca pasave bahuṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1221Msdiv 64Na so mitto yo sadā appamatto,
Bhedāsaṅkī randhamevānupassī;
Yasmiñca setī urasīva putto,
Sa ve mitto yo abhejjo parehī”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1222Vaṇṇārohajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
