530. Saṅkiccajātaka

SC 1Ms 23J_512Msdiv 69Disvā nisinnaṃ rājānaṃ,
brahmadattaṃ rathesabhaṃ;
Athassa paṭivedesi,
“yassāsi anukampako.

SC 2Ms 23J_513Msdiv 70Saṅkiccāyaṃ anuppatto,
isīnaṃ sādhusammato;
Taramānarūpo niyyāhi,
khippaṃ passa mahesinaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 23J_514Msdiv 71Tato ca rājā taramāno,
yuttamāruyha sandanaṃ;
Mittāmaccaparibyūḷho,
agamāsi rathesabho.

SC 4Ms 23J_515Msdiv 72Nikkhippa pañca kakudhāni,
kāsīnaṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhano;
Vālabījanimuṇhīsaṃ,
khaggaṃ chattañcupāhanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 23J_516Msdiv 73Oruyha rājā yānamhā,
ṭhapayitvā paṭicchadaṃ;
Āsīnaṃ dāyapassasmiṃ,
saṅkiccamupasaṅkami.

SC 6Ms 23J_517Msdiv 74Upasaṅkamitvā so rājā,
sammodi isinā saha;
Taṃ kathaṃ vītisāretvā,
ekamantaṃ upāvisi.

SC 7Ms 23J_518Msdiv 75Ekamantaṃ nisinnova,
atha kālaṃ amaññatha;
Tato pāpāni kammāni,
pucchituṃ paṭipajjatha.

SC 8Ms 23J_519Msdiv 76“Isiṃ pucchāma saṅkiccaṃ,
isīnaṃ sādhusammataṃ;
Āsīnaṃ dāyapassasmiṃ,
isisaṃghapurakkhataṃ.

SC 9Ms 23J_520Msdiv 77Kaṃ gatiṃ pecca gacchanti,
narā dhammāticārino;
Aticiṇṇo mayā dhammo,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 10Ms 23J_521Msdiv 78Isī avaca saṅkicco,
kāsīnaṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhanaṃ;
Āsīnaṃ dāyapassasmiṃ,
“mahārāja suṇohi me.

SC 11Ms 23J_522Msdiv 79Uppathena vajantassa,
yo maggamanusāsati;
Tassa ce vacanaṃ kayirā,
nāssa maggeyya kaṇṭako.

SC 12Ms 23J_523Msdiv 80Adhammaṃ paṭipannassa,
yo dhammamanusāsati;
Tassa ce vacanaṃ kayirā,
na so gaccheyya duggatiṃ”.

SC 13Ms 23J_524Msdiv 81“Dhammo patho mahārāja,
adhammo pana uppatho;
Adhammo nirayaṃ neti,
dhammo pāpeti suggatiṃ.

SC 14Ms 23J_525Msdiv 82Adhammacārino rāja,
narā visamajīvino;
Yaṃ gatiṃ pecca gacchanti,
niraye te suṇohi me.

SC 15Ms 23J_526Msdiv 83Sañjīvo kāḷasutto ca,
saṅghāto dve ca roruvā;
Athāparo mahāvīci,
tāpano ca patāpano.

SC 16Ms 23J_527Msdiv 84Iccete aṭṭha nirayā,
akkhātā duratikkamā;
Ākiṇṇā luddakammehi,
paccekā soḷasussadā.

SC 17Ms 23J_528Msdiv 85Kadariyatāpanā ghorā,
accimanto mahabbhayā;
Lomahaṃsanarūpā ca,
bhesmā paṭibhayā dukhā.

SC 18Ms 23J_529Msdiv 86Catukkaṇṇā catudvārā,
vibhattā bhāgaso mitā;
Ayopākārapariyantā,
ayasā paṭikujjitā.

SC 19Ms 23J_530Msdiv 87Tesaṃ ayomayā bhūmi,
jalitā tejasā yutā;
Samantā yojanasataṃ,
phuṭā tiṭṭhanti sabbadā.

SC 20Ms 23J_531Msdiv 88Ete patanti niraye,
uddhampādā avaṃsirā;
Isīnaṃ ativattāro,
saññatānaṃ tapassinaṃ.

SC 21Ms 23J_532Msdiv 89Te bhūnahuno paccanti,
macchā bilakatā yathā;
Saṃvacchare asaṅkheyye,
narā kibbisakārino.

SC 22Ms 23J_533Msdiv 90Ḍayhamānena gattena,
niccaṃ santarabāhiraṃ;
Nirayā nādhigacchanti,
dvāraṃ nikkhamanesino.

SC 23Ms 23J_534Msdiv 91Puratthimena dhāvanti,
tato dhāvanti pacchato;
Uttarenapi dhāvanti,
tato dhāvanti dakkhiṇaṃ;
Yaṃ yañhi dvāraṃ gacchanti,
taṃ tadeva pidhīyare.

SC 24Ms 23J_535Msdiv 92Bahūni vassasahassāni,
janā nirayagāmino;
Bāhā paggayha kandanti,
patvā dukkhaṃ anappakaṃ.

SC 25Ms 23J_536Msdiv 93Āsīvisaṃva kupitaṃ,
tejassiṃ duratikkamaṃ;
Na sādhurūpe āsīde,
saññatānaṃ tapassinaṃ.

SC 26Ms 23J_537Msdiv 94Atikāyo mahissāso,
ajjuno kekakādhipo;
Sahassabāhu ucchinno,
isimāsajja gotamaṃ.

SC 27Ms 23J_538Msdiv 95Arajaṃ rajasā vacchaṃ,
Kisaṃ avakiriya daṇḍakī;
Tālova mūlato chinno,
Sa rājā vibhavaṅgato.

SC 28Ms 23J_539Msdiv 96Upahacca manaṃ majjho,
mātaṅgasmiṃ yasassine;
Sapārisajjo ucchinno,
majjhāraññaṃ tadā ahu.

SC 29Ms 23J_540Msdiv 97Kaṇhadīpāyanāsajja,
Isiṃ andhakaveṇḍayo;
Aññoññaṃ Aññoññaṃ musalā hantvā,
Sampattā yamasādhanaṃ.

SC 30Ms 23J_541Msdiv 98Athāyaṃ isinā satto,
antalikkhacaro pure;
Pāvekkhi pathaviṃ cecco,
hīnatto pattapariyāyaṃ.

SC 31Ms 23J_542Msdiv 99Tasmā hi chandāgamanaṃ,
nappasaṃsanti paṇḍitā;
Aduṭṭhacitto bhāseyya,
giraṃ saccūpasaṃhitaṃ.

SC 32Ms 23J_543Msdiv 100Manasā ce paduṭṭhena,
yo naro pekkhate muniṃ;
Vijjācaraṇasampannaṃ,
gantā so nirayaṃ adho.

SC 33Ms 23J_544Msdiv 101Ye vuḍḍhe paribhāsanti,
pharusūpakkamā janā;
Anapaccā adāyādā,
tālavatthu bhavanti te.

SC 34Ms 23J_545Msdiv 102Yo ca pabbajitaṃ hanti,
katakiccaṃ mahesinaṃ;
Sa kāḷasutte niraye,
cirarattāya paccati.

SC 35Ms 23J_546Msdiv 103Yo ca rājā adhammaṭṭho,
raṭṭhaviddhaṃsano mago;
Tāpayitvā janapadaṃ,
tāpane pecca paccati.

SC 36Ms 23J_547Msdiv 104So ca vassasahassāni,
sataṃ dibbāni paccati;
Accisaṃghapareto so,
dukkhaṃ vedeti vedanaṃ.

SC 37Ms 23J_548Msdiv 105Tassa aggisikhā kāyā,
niccharanti pabhassarā;
Tejobhakkhassa gattāni,
lomehi ca nakhehi ca.

SC 38Ms 23J_549Msdiv 106Ḍayhamānena gattena,
niccaṃ santarabāhiraṃ;
Dukkhābhitunno nadati,
nāgo tuttaṭṭito yathā.

SC 39Ms 23J_550Msdiv 107Yo lobhā pitaraṃ hanti,
dosā vā purisādhamo;
Sa kāḷasutte niraye,
cirarattāya paccati.

SC 40Ms 23J_551Msdiv 108Sa tādiso paccati lohakumbhiyaṃ,
Pakkañca sattīhi hananti nittacaṃ;
Andhaṃ karitvā muttakarīsabhakkhaṃ,
Khāre nimujjanti tathāvidhaṃ naraṃ.

SC 41Ms 23J_552Msdiv 109Tattaṃ pakkuthitamayoguḷañca,
Dīghe ca phāle cirarattatāpite;
Vikkhambhamādāya vibandharajjubhi,
Vivaṭe mukhe sampavisanti rakkhasā.

SC 42Ms 23J_553Msdiv 110Sāmā ca soṇā sabalā ca gijjhā,
Kākoḷasaṅghā ca dijā ayomukhā;
Saṅgamma khādanti vipphandamānaṃ,
Jivhaṃ vibhajja vighāsaṃ salohitaṃ.

SC 43Ms 23J_554Msdiv 111Taṃ daḍḍhatālaṃ paribhinnagattaṃ,
Nippothayantā anuvicaranti rakkhasā;
Ratī hi nesaṃ dukhino panītare,
Etādisasmiṃ niraye vasanti;
Ye keci loke idha pettighātino.

SC 44Ms 23J_555Msdiv 112Putto ca mātaraṃ hantvā,
ito gantvā yamakkhayaṃ;
Bhusamāpajjate dukkhaṃ,
attakammaphalūpago.

SC 45Ms 23J_556Msdiv 113Amanussā atibalā,
hantāraṃ janayantiyā;
Ayomayehi vālehi,
pīḷayanti punappunaṃ.

SC 46Ms 23J_557Msdiv 114Tamassavaṃ sakā gattā,
rudhiraṃ attasambhavaṃ;
Tambalohavilīnaṃva,
tattaṃ pāyenti mattighaṃ.

SC 47Ms 23J_558Msdiv 115Jigucchaṃ kuṇapaṃ pūtiṃ,
Duggandhaṃ gūthakaddamaṃ;
Pubbalohitasaṅkāsaṃ,
Rahadamogayha tiṭṭhati.

SC 48Ms 23J_559Msdiv 116Tamenaṃ kimayo tattha,
atikāyā ayomukhā;
Chaviṃ bhetvāna khādanti,
saṅgiddhā maṃsalohite.

SC 49Ms 23J_560Msdiv 117So ca taṃ nirayaṃ patto,
nimuggo sataporisaṃ;
Pūtikaṃ kuṇapaṃ vāti,
samantā satayojanaṃ.

SC 50Ms 23J_561Msdiv 118Cakkhumāpi hi cakkhūhi,
tena gandhena jīyati;
Etādisaṃ brahmadatta,
mātugho labhate dukhaṃ.

SC 51Ms 23J_562Msdiv 119Khuradhāramanukkamma,
tikkhaṃ durabhisambhavaṃ;
Patanti gabbhapātiyo,
duggaṃ vetaraṇiṃ nadiṃ.

SC 52Ms 23J_563Msdiv 120Ayomayā simbaliyo,
soḷasaṅgulakaṇṭakā;
Ubhato abhilambanti,
duggaṃ vetaraṇiṃ nadiṃ.

SC 53Ms 23J_564Msdiv 121Te accimanto tiṭṭhanti,
aggikkhandhāva ārakā;
Ādittā jātavedena,
uddhaṃ yojanamuggatā.

SC 54Ms 23J_565Msdiv 122Ete vajanti niraye,
tatte tikhiṇakaṇṭake;
Nāriyo ca aticārā,
narā ca paradāragū.

SC 55Ms 23J_566Msdiv 123Te patanti adhokkhandhā,
vivattā vihatā puthū;
Sayanti vinividdhaṅgā,
dīghaṃ jagganti sabbadā.

SC 56Ms 23J_567Msdiv 124Tato ratyā vivasāne,
mahatiṃ pabbatūpamaṃ;
Lohakumbhiṃ pavajjanti,
tattaṃ aggisamūdakaṃ.

SC 57Ms 23J_568Msdiv 125Evaṃ divā ca ratto ca,
dussīlā mohapārutā;
Anubhonti sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe dukkaṭamattano.

SC 58Ms 23J_569Msdiv 126Yā ca bhariyā dhanakkītā,
sāmikaṃ atimaññati;
Sassuṃ vā sasuraṃ vāpi,
jeṭṭhaṃ vāpi nanandaraṃ.

SC 59Ms 23J_570Msdiv 127Tassā vaṅkena jivhaggaṃ,
nibbahanti sabandhanaṃ;
Sa byāmamattaṃ kiminaṃ,
jivhaṃ passati attani;
Viññāpetuṃ na sakkoti,
tāpane pecca paccati.

SC 60Ms 23J_571Msdiv 128Orabbhikā sūkarikā,
macchikā migabandhakā;
Corā goghātakā luddā,
avaṇṇe vaṇṇakārakā.

SC 61Ms 23J_572Msdiv 129Sattīhi lohakūṭehi,
nettiṃsehi usūhi ca;
Haññamānā khāranadiṃ,
papatanti avaṃsirā.

SC 62Ms 23J_573Msdiv 130Sāyaṃ pāto kūṭakārī,
ayokūṭehi haññati;
Tato vantaṃ durattānaṃ,
paresaṃ bhuñjare sadā.

SC 63Ms 23J_574Msdiv 131Dhaṅkā bheraṇḍakā gijjhā,
kākoḷā ca ayomukhā;
Vipphandamānaṃ khādanti,
naraṃ kibbisakārakaṃ.

SC 64Ms 23J_575Msdiv 132Ye migena migaṃ hanti,
pakkhiṃ vā pana pakkhinā;
Asanto rajasā channā,
gantā te gantā te nirayussadaṃ.

SC 65Ms 23J_576Msdiv 133Santo ca uddhaṃ gacchanti,
suciṇṇenidha kammunā;
Suciṇṇassa phalaṃ passa,
saindā devā sabrahmakā.

SC 66Ms 23J_577Msdiv 134Taṃ taṃ brūmi mahārāja,
Dhammaṃ raṭṭhapatī cara;
Tathā rāja carāhi dhammaṃ,
Yathā taṃ suciṇṇaṃ nānutappeyya pacchā”ti. (3535)

SC 67Ms 23J_578Saṅkiccajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.

SC 68Ms 23J_579Saṭṭhinipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 69Ms 23J_580Tassuddānaṃ

SC 70Ms 23J_581Atha saṭṭhinipātamhi,
Suṇātha mama bhāsitaṃ;
Jātakasavhayano pavaro,
Soṇakaarindamasavhayano;
Tathā vuttarathesabhakiccavaroti.
