419. Sulasājātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.437Ms 22J_1692Msdiv 18“Idaṃ suvaṇṇakāyūraṃ,
muttā veḷuriyā bahū;
Sabbaṃ harassu bhaddante,
mañca dāsīti sāvaya”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1693Msdiv 19“Oropayassu kalyāṇi,
mā bāḷhaṃ paridevasi;
Na cāhaṃ abhijānāmi,
ahantvā dhanamābhataṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1694Msdiv 20“Yato sarāmi attānaṃ,
yato pattāsmi viññutaṃ;
Na cāhaṃ abhijānāmi,
aññaṃ piyataraṃ tayā.

SC 4Ms 22J_1695Msdiv 21Ehi taṃ upagūhissaṃ,
karissañca padakkhiṇaṃ;
Na hi dāni puna atthi,
mama tuyhañca saṅgamo”.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 3.438Ms 22J_1696Msdiv 22“Na hi sabbesu ṭhānesu,
puriso hoti paṇḍito;
Itthīpi paṇḍitā hoti,
tattha tattha vicakkhaṇā.

SC 6Ms 22J_1697Msdiv 23Na hi sabbesu ṭhānesu,
puriso hoti paṇḍito;
Itthīpi paṇḍitā hoti,
lahuṃ atthaṃ vicintikā.

SC 7Ms 22J_1698Msdiv 24Lahuñca vata khippañca,
nikaṭṭhe samacetayi;
Migaṃ puṇṇāyateneva,
sulasā sattukaṃ vadhi.

SC 8Ms 22J_1699Msdiv 25Yodha uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
na khippamanubujjhati;
So haññati mandamati,
corova girigabbhare.

SC 9Ms 22J_1700Msdiv 26Yo ca uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
khippameva nibodhati;
Muccate sattusambādhā,
sulasā sattukāmivā”ti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1701Sulasājātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
