349. Sandhibhedajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1131SC 1Ms 22J_1131Msdiv 193“Neva itthīsu sāmaññaṃ,
nāpi bhakkhesu sārathi;
Athassa sandhibhedassa,
passa yāva sucintitaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_1132SC 2Ms 22J_1132Msdiv 194Asi tikkhova maṃsamhi,
pesuññaṃ parivattati;
Yatthūsabhañca sīhañca,
bhakkhayanti migādhamā.

SC 3Ms 22J_1133SC 3Ms 22J_1133Msdiv 195Imaṃ so sayanaṃ seti,
yamimaṃ passasi sārathi;
Yo vācaṃ sandhibhedassa,
pisuṇassa nibodhati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1134SC 4Ms 22J_1134Msdiv 196Te janā sukhamedhanti,
narā saggagatāriva;
Ye vācaṃ sandhibhedassa,
nāvabodhanti sārathī”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 22J_1135Sandhibhedajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
