404. Kapijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1553Msdiv 61“Yattha verī nivasati,
na vase tattha paṇḍito;
Ekarattaṃ dirattaṃ vā,
dukkhaṃ vasati verisu.

SC 2Ms 22J_1554Msdiv 62Diso ve lahucittassa,
posassānuvidhīyato;
Ekassa kapino hetu,
yūthassa anayo kato.

SC 3Ms 22J_1555Msdiv 63Bālova paṇḍitamānī,
yūthassa parihārako;
Sacittassa vasaṃ gantvā,
sayethāyaṃ yathā kapi.

SC 4Ms 22J_1556Msdiv 64Na sādhu balavā bālo,
yūthassa parihārako;
Ahito bhavati ñātīnaṃ,
sakuṇānaṃva cetako.

SC 5Ms 22J_1557Msdiv 65Dhīrova balavā sādhu,
yūthassa parihārako;
Hito bhavati ñātīnaṃ,
tidasānaṃva vāsavo.

SC 6Ms 22J_1558Msdiv 66Yo ca sīlañca paññañca,
sutañcattani passati;
Ubhinnamatthaṃ carati,
attano ca parassa ca.

SC 7Ms 22J_1559Msdiv 67Tasmā tuleyya mattānaṃ,
sīlapaññāsutāmiva;
Gaṇaṃ vā parihare dhīro,
eko vāpi paribbaje”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1560Kapijātakaṃ navamaṃ.
