355. Ghaṭajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1178SC 1Ms 22J_1178Msdiv 29“Aññe socanti rodanti,
aññe assumukhā janā;
Pasannamukhavaṇṇosi,
kasmā ghaṭa na socasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1179SC 2Ms 22J_1179Msdiv 30“Nābbhatītaharo soko,
nānāgatasukhāvaho;
Tasmā dhaṅka na socāmi,
natthi soke dutīyatā.

SC 3Ms 22J_1180SC 3Ms 22J_1180Msdiv 31Socaṃ paṇḍu kiso hoti,
bhattañcassa na ruccati;
Amittā sumanā honti,
sallaviddhassa ruppato.

SC 4Ms 22J_1181SC 4Ms 22J_1181Msdiv 32Gāme vā yadi vāraññe,
ninne vā yadi vā thale;
Ṭhitaṃ maṃ nāgamissati,
evaṃ diṭṭhapado ahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1182SC 5Ms 22J_1182Msdiv 33Yassattā nālamekova,
sabbakāmarasāharo;
Sabbāpi pathavī tassa,
na sukhaṃ āvahissatī”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1183SC 6Ms 22J_1183Ghaṭajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
