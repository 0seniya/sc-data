141. Godhajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.488Ms 22J_313Msdiv 141“Na pāpajanasaṃsevī,
accantasukhamedhati;
Godhākulaṃ Godhākulaṃ kakaṇṭāva,
kaliṃ pāpeti attanan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_314Godhajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
