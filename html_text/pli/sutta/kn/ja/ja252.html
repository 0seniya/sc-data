252. Tilamuṭṭhijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_674Msdiv 4“Ajjāpi me taṃ manasi,
yaṃ maṃ tvaṃ tilamuṭṭhiyā;
Bāhāya maṃ gahetvāna,
laṭṭhiyā anutāḷayi.

SC 2Ms 22J_675Msdiv 5Nanu jīvite na ramasi,
yenāsi brāhmaṇāgato;
Yaṃ maṃ bāhā gahetvāna,
tikkhattuṃ anutāḷayi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_676Msdiv 6“Ariyo anariyaṃ kubbantaṃ,
Yo daṇḍena nisedhati;
Sāsanaṃ taṃ na taṃ veraṃ,
Iti naṃ paṇḍitā vidū”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_677Tilamuṭṭhijātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
