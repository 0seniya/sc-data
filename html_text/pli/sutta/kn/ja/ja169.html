169. Arakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.61Ms 22J_398Msdiv 37“Yo ve mettena cittena,
sabbalokānukampati;
Uddhaṃ adho ca tiriyaṃ,
appamāṇena sabbaso.

SC 2Ms 22J_399Msdiv 38Appamāṇaṃ hitaṃ cittaṃ,
paripuṇṇaṃ subhāvitaṃ;
Yaṃ pamāṇakataṃ kammaṃ,
na taṃ tatrāvasissatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_400Arakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
