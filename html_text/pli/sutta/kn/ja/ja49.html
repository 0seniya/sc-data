49. Nakkhattajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.258Ms 22J_106Msdiv 49Nakkhattaṃ paṭimānentaṃ,
attho bālaṃ upaccagā;
Attho atthassa nakkhattaṃ,
kiṃ karissanti tārakā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_107Nakkhattajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
