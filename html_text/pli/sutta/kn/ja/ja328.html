328. Ananusociyajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.95Ms 22J_1022Msdiv 109“Bahūnaṃ vijjatī bhotī,
tehi me kiṃ bhavissati;
Tasmā etaṃ na socāmi,
piyaṃ sammillahāsiniṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_1023Msdiv 110Taṃ tañce anusoceyya,
yaṃ yaṃ tassa na vijjati;
Attānamanusoceyya,
sadā maccuvasaṃ pataṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1024Msdiv 111Na heva ṭhitaṃ nāsīnaṃ,
Na sayānaṃ na paddhaguṃ;
Yāva byāti nimisati,
Tatrāpi rasatī vayo.

SC 4Ms 22J_1025Msdiv 112Tatthattani vatappaddhe,
vinābhāve asaṃsaye;
Bhūtaṃ sesaṃ dayitabbaṃ,
vītaṃ ananusociyan”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1026Ananusociyajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
