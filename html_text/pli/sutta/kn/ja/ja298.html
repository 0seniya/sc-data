298. Udumbarajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_866Msdiv 142“Udumbarā cime pakkā,
nigrodhā ca kapitthanā;
Ehi nikkhama bhuñjassu,
kiṃ jighacchāya miyyasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_867Msdiv 143“Evaṃ so suhito hoti,
yo vuḍḍhamapacāyati;
Yathāhamajja suhito,
dumapakkāni māsito”.

SC 3Ms 22J_868Msdiv 144“Yaṃ vanejo vanejassa,
vañceyya kapino kapi;
Daharo kapi saddheyya,
na hi jiṇṇo jarākapī”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_869Udumbarajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
