330. Sīlavīmaṃsajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1032Msdiv 117“Sīlaṃ kireva kalyāṇaṃ,
sīlaṃ loke anuttaraṃ;
Passa ghoraviso nāgo,
sīlavāti na haññati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1033Msdiv 118“Yāvadevassahū kiñci,
tāvadeva akhādisuṃ;
Saṅgamma kulalā loke,
na hiṃsanti akiñcanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1034Msdiv 119Sukhaṃ nirāsā supati,
āsā phalavatī sukhā;
Āsaṃ nirāsaṃ katvāna,
sukhaṃ supati piṅgalā.

SC 4Ms 22J_1035Msdiv 120Na samādhiparo atthi,
asmiṃ loke paramhi ca;
Na paraṃ nāpi attānaṃ,
vihiṃsati samāhito”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1036Sīlavīmaṃsajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kuṭidūsakavaggo tatiyo.

SC 6Ms 22J_1037Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 22J_1038Samanussa saduddubha yācanako,
Atha meṇḍavaruttama godhavaro;
Atha kāyasakepuka bhotīvaro,
Atha rādhasusīlavarena dasāti.
