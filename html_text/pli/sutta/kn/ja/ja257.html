257. Gāmaṇicandajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_694SC 1Ms 22J_694Msdiv 19“Nāyaṃ gharānaṃ kusalo,
lolo ayaṃ valīmukho;
Kataṃ kataṃ kho dūseyya,
evaṃdhammamidaṃ kulaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_695SC 2Ms 22J_695Msdiv 20Nayidaṃ cittavato lomaṃ,
nāyaṃ assāsiko migo;
Siṭṭhaṃ me janasandhena,
nāyaṃ kiñci vijānati.

SC 3Ms 22J_696SC 3Ms 22J_696Msdiv 21Na mātaraṃ pitaraṃ vā,
bhātaraṃ bhaginiṃ sakaṃ;
Bhareyya tādiso poso,
siṭṭhaṃ dasarathena me”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_697SC 4Ms 22J_697Gāmaṇicanda jātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
