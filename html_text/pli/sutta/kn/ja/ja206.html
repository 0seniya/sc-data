206. Kuruṅgamigajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.153Ms 22J_521Msdiv 111“Iṅgha vaṭṭamayaṃ pāsaṃ,
chinda dantehi kacchapa;
Ahaṃ tathā karissāmi,
yathā nehiti luddako”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.155Ms 22J_522Msdiv 112“Kacchapo pāvisī vāriṃ,
kuruṅgo pāvisī vanaṃ;
Satapatto dumaggamhā,
dūre putte apānayī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_523Kuruṅgamigajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
