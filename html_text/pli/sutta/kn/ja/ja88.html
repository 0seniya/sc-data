88. Sārambhajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.375Ms 22J_194Msdiv 88“Kalyāṇimeva muñceyya,
na hi muñceyya pāpikaṃ;
Mokkho kalyāṇiyā sādhu,
mutvā tappati pāpikan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_195Sārambhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
