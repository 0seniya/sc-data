273. Kacchapajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.360Ms 22J_762Msdiv 67“Ko nu uddhitabhattova,
pūrahatthova brāhmaṇo;
Kahaṃ nu bhikkhaṃ acari,
kaṃ saddhaṃ upasaṅkami”.

SC 2Ms 22J_763Msdiv 68“Ahaṃ kapismi dummedho,
anāmāsāni āmasiṃ;
Tvaṃ maṃ mocaya bhaddaṃ te,
mutto gaccheyya pabbataṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_764Msdiv 69“Kacchapā kassapā honti,
Koṇḍaññā honti makkaṭā;
Muñca kassapa koṇḍaññaṃ,
Kataṃ methunakaṃ tayā”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_765Kacchapajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
