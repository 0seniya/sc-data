437. Pūtimaṃsajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1886Msdiv 96“Na kho me ruccati āḷi,
pūtimaṃsassa pekkhanā;
Etādisā sakhārasmā,
ārakā parivajjaye”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1887Msdiv 97“Ummattikā ayaṃ veṇī,
vaṇṇeti patino sakhiṃ;
Pajjhāyi paṭigacchantiṃ,
āgataṃ meṇḍamātaraṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1888Msdiv 98“Tvaṃ khosi samma ummatto,
dummedho avicakkhaṇo;
Yo tvaṃ matālayaṃ katvā,
akālena vipekkhasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1889Msdiv 99“Na akāle vipekkheyya,
kāle pekkheyya paṇḍito;
Pūtimaṃsova pajjhāyi,
yo akāle vipekkhati”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1890Msdiv 100“Piyaṃ kho āḷi me hotu,
puṇṇapattaṃ dadāhi me;
Pati sañjīvito mayhaṃ,
eyyāsi piyapucchikā”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1891Msdiv 101“Piyaṃ kho āḷi te hotu,
puṇṇapattaṃ dadāmi te;
Mahatā parivārena,
essaṃ kayirāhi bhojanaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1892Msdiv 102“Kīdiso tuyhaṃ parivāro,
yesaṃ kāhāmi bhojanaṃ;
Kiṃnāmakā ca te sabbe,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchitā”.

SC 8Ms 22J_1893Msdiv 103“Māliyo caturakkho ca,
piṅgiyo atha jambuko;
Ediso mayhaṃ parivāro,
tesaṃ kayirāhi bhojanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1894Msdiv 104“Nikkhantāya agārasmā,
bhaṇḍakampi vinassati;
Ārogyaṃ āḷino vajjaṃ,
idheva vasa māgamā”ti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1895Pūtimaṃsajātakaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
