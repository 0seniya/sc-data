243. Guttilajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_641Msdiv 186“Sattatantiṃ sumadhuraṃ,
rāmaṇeyyaṃ avācayiṃ;
So maṃ raṅgamhi avheti,
saraṇaṃ me hohi kosiya”.

SC 2Ms 22J_642Msdiv 187“Ahaṃ taṃ saraṇaṃ samma,
Ahamācariyapūjako;
Na taṃ jayissati sisso,
Sissamācariya jessasī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_643Guttilajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
