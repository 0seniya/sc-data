55. Pañcāvudhajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_121Msdiv 55“Yo alīnena cittena,
alīnamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_122Pañcāvudhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
