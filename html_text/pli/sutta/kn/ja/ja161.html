161. Indasamānagottajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_374Msdiv 21“Na santhavaṃ kāpurisena kayirā,
Ariyo anariyena pajānamatthaṃ;
Cirānuvutthopi karoti pāpaṃ,
Gajo yathā indasamānagottaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_375Msdiv 22Yaṃ tveva jaññā sadiso mamanti,
Sīlena paññāya sutena cāpi;
Teneva mettiṃ kayirātha saddhiṃ,
Sukho have sappurisena saṅgamo”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_376Indasamānagottajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
