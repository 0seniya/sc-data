1 Soṇakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 5.250Ms 23J_443Msdiv 1“Tassa sutvā sataṃ dammi,
sahassaṃ diṭṭha soṇakaṃ;
Ko me soṇakamakkhāti,
sahāyaṃ paṃsukīḷitaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 23J_444Msdiv 2“Athabravī māṇavako,
daharo pañcacūḷako;
Mayhaṃ sutvā sataṃ dehi,
sahassaṃ diṭṭha soṇakaṃ;
Ahaṃ te soṇakakkhissaṃ,
sahāyaṃ paṃsukīḷitaṃ”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 5.251Ms 23J_445Msdiv 3“Katamasmiṃ so janapade,
raṭṭhesu nigamesu ca;
Kattha soṇakamaddakkhi,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 4Ms 23J_446Msdiv 4“Taveva deva vijite,
tavevuyyānabhūmiyā;
Ujuvaṃsā mahāsālā,
nīlobhāsā manoramā.

SC 5Ms 23J_447Msdiv 5Tiṭṭhanti meghasamānā,
rammā aññoññanissitā;
Tesaṃ mūlamhi soṇako,
jhāyatī anupādano;
Upādānesu lokesu,
ḍayhamānesu nibbuto”.

SC 6Ms 23J_448Msdiv 6Tato ca rājā pāyāsi,
senāya caturaṅgiyā;
Kārāpetvā samaṃ maggaṃ,
agamā yena soṇako.

SC 7Ms 23J_449Msdiv 7Uyyānabhūmiṃ gantvāna,
vicaranto brahāvane;
Āsīnaṃ soṇakaṃ dakkhi,
ḍayhamānesu nibbutaṃ.

SC 8Ms 23J_450Msdiv 8“Kapaṇo vatayaṃ bhikkhu,
muṇḍo saṅghāṭipāruto;
Amātiko apitiko,
rukkhamūlasmi jhāyati”.

SC 9Ms 23J_451Msdiv 9Imaṃ vākyaṃ nisāmetvā,
Soṇako etadabravi;
“Na rāja kapaṇo hoti,
Dhammaṃ kāyena phassayaṃ.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 5.252Ms 23J_452Msdiv 10Yo ca dhammaṃ ca dhammaṃ niraṃkatvā,
Adhammamanuvattati;
Sa rāja kapaṇo hoti,
Pāpo pāpaparāyaṇo”.

SC 11Ms 23J_453Msdiv 11“Arindamoti me nāmaṃ,
kāsirājāti maṃ vidū;
Kacci bhoto sukhasseyyā,
idha pattassa soṇaka”.

SC 12Ms 23J_454Msdiv 12“Sadāpi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Na tesaṃ koṭṭhe openti,
Na kumbhiṃ na khaḷopiyaṃ;
Paraniṭṭhitamesānā,
Tena yāpenti subbatā.

SC 13Ms 23J_455Msdiv 13Dutiyampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Anavajjapiṇḍo bhottabbo,
Na ca kocūparodhati.

SC 14Ms 23J_456Msdiv 14Tatiyampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Nibbuto piṇḍo bhottabbo,
Na ca kocūparodhati.

SC 15Ms 23J_457Msdiv 15Catutthampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Muttassa raṭṭhe carato,
Saṅgo yassa na vijjati.

SC 16Ms 23J_458Msdiv 16Pañcamampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Nagaramhi ḍayhamānamhi,
Nāssa kiñci aḍayhatha.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 5.253Ms 23J_459Msdiv 17Chaṭṭhampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Raṭṭhe vilumpamānamhi,
Nāssa kiñci ahīratha.

SC 18Ms 23J_460Msdiv 18Sattamampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Corehi rakkhitaṃ maggaṃ,
Ye caññe paripanthikā;
Pattacīvaramādāya,
Sotthiṃ gacchati subbato.

SC 19Ms 23J_461Msdiv 19Aṭṭhamampi bhadramadhanassa,
Anāgārassa bhikkhuno;
Yaṃ yaṃ disaṃ pakkamati,
Anapekkhova gacchati”.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 5.254Ms 23J_462Msdiv 20“Bahūni samaṇabhadrāni,
ye tvaṃ bhikkhu pasaṃsasi;
Ahañca giddho kāmesu,
kathaṃ kāhāmi soṇaka.

SC 21Ms 23J_463Msdiv 21Piyā me mānusā kāmā,
atho dibyāpi me piyā;
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,
ubho loke labhāmase”.

SC 22PTS vp Pali 5.255Ms 23J_464Msdiv 22“Kāme giddhā kāmaratā,
kāmesu adhimucchitā;
Narā pāpāni katvāna,
upapajjanti duggatiṃ.

SC 23Ms 23J_465Msdiv 23Ye ca kāme pahantvāna,
nikkhantā akutobhayā;
Ekodibhāvādhigatā,
na te gacchanti duggatiṃ.

SC 24Ms 23J_466Msdiv 24Upamaṃ te karissāmi,
taṃ suṇohi arindama;
Upamāya midhekacce,
atthaṃ jānanti paṇḍitā.

SC 25Ms 23J_467Msdiv 25Gaṅgāya kuṇapaṃ disvā,
vuyhamānaṃ mahaṇṇave;
Vāyaso samacintesi,
appapañño acetaso.

SC 26Ms 23J_468Msdiv 26‘Yānañca vatidaṃ laddhaṃ,
bhakkho cāyaṃ anappako’;
Tattha rattiṃ tattha divā,
tattheva nirato mano.

SC 27Ms 23J_469Msdiv 27Khādaṃ nāgassa maṃsāni,
pivaṃ bhāgīrathodakaṃ;
Sampassaṃ vanacetyāni,
na palettha vihaṅgamo.

SC 28Ms 23J_470Msdiv 28Tañca otaraṇī gaṅgā,
pamattaṃ kuṇape rataṃ;
Samuddaṃ ajjhagāhāsi,
agatī yattha pakkhinaṃ.

SC 29Ms 23J_471Msdiv 29So ca bhakkhaparikkhīṇo,
udapatvā vihaṅgamo;
Na pacchato na purato,
nuttaraṃ nopi dakkhiṇaṃ.

SC 30Ms 23J_472Msdiv 30Dīpaṃ so najjhagāgañchi,
agatī yattha pakkhinaṃ;
So ca tattheva pāpattha,
yathā dubbalako tathā.

SC 31Ms 23J_473Msdiv 31Tañca sāmuddikā macchā,
kumbhīlā makarā susū;
Pasayhakārā khādiṃsu,
phandamānaṃ vipakkhakaṃ.

SC 32Ms 23J_474Msdiv 32Evameva tuvaṃ rāja,
ye caññe kāmabhogino;
Giddhā ce na vamissanti,
kākapaññāva te vidū.

SC 33Ms 23J_475Msdiv 33Esā te upamā rāja,
atthasandassanī katā;
Tvañca paññāyase tena,
yadi kāhasi vā na vā.

SC 34PTS vp Pali 5.257Ms 23J_476Msdiv 34Ekavācampi dvivācaṃ,
bhaṇeyya anukampako;
Tatuttariṃ na bhāseyya,
dāsovayyassa santike”.

SC 35Ms 23J_477Msdiv 35Idaṃ vatvāna pakkāmi,
soṇako amitabuddhimā;
Vehāse antalikkhasmiṃ,
anusāsitvāna khattiyaṃ.

SC 36PTS vp Pali 5.258Ms 23J_478Msdiv 36“Ko nume rājakattāro,
suddā veyyattamāgatā;
Rajjaṃ niyyādayissāmi,
nāhaṃ rajjena matthiko.

SC 37Ms 23J_479Msdiv 37Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
Ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
Kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ”.

SC 38Ms 23J_480Msdiv 38“Atthi te daharo putto,
dīghāvu raṭṭhavaḍḍhano;
Taṃ rajje abhisiñcassu,
so no rājā bhavissati”.

SC 39Ms 23J_481Msdiv 39“Khippaṃ kumāramānetha,
dīghāvuṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhanaṃ;
Taṃ rajje abhisiñcissaṃ,
so vo rājā bhavissati”.

SC 40Ms 23J_482Msdiv 40Tato kumāramānesuṃ,
dīghāvuṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhanaṃ;
Taṃ disvā ālapī rājā,
ekaputtaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 41Ms 23J_483Msdiv 41“Saṭṭhi gāmasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;
Te putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 42Ms 23J_484Msdiv 42Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ.

SC 43Ms 23J_485Msdiv 43Saṭṭhi nāgasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Suvaṇṇakacchā mātaṅgā,
hemakappanavāsasā.

SC 44Ms 23J_486Msdiv 44Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
tomaraṅkusapāṇibhi;
Te putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 45PTS vp Pali 5.259Ms 23J_487Msdiv 45Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ.

SC 46Ms 23J_488Msdiv 46Saṭṭhi assasahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Ājānīyāva jātiyā,
sindhavā sīghavāhino.

SC 47Ms 23J_489Msdiv 47Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
illiyācāpadhāribhi;
Te putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 48Ms 23J_490Msdiv 48Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ.

SC 49Ms 23J_491Msdiv 49Saṭṭhi rathasahassāni,
sannaddhā ussitaddhajā;
Dīpā athopi veyyagghā,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā.

SC 50Ms 23J_492Msdiv 50Ārūḷhā gāmaṇīyehi,
cāpahatthehi vammibhi;
Te putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 51Ms 23J_493Msdiv 51Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ.

SC 52Ms 23J_494Msdiv 52Saṭṭhi dhenusahassāni,
rohaññā puṅgavūsabhā;
Tā putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 53Ms 23J_495Msdiv 53Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ.

SC 54Ms 23J_496Msdiv 54Soḷasitthisahassāni,
sabbālaṅkārabhūsitā;
Vicitravatthābharaṇā,
āmuttamaṇikuṇḍalā;
Tā putta paṭipajjassu,
rajjaṃ niyyādayāmi te.

SC 55Ms 23J_497Msdiv 55Ajjeva pabbajissāmi,
ko jaññā maraṇaṃ suve;
Māhaṃ kākova dummedho,
kāmānaṃ vasamanvagaṃ”.

SC 56Ms 23J_498Msdiv 56“Daharasseva me tāta,
mātā matāti me sutaṃ;
Tayā vinā ahaṃ tāta,
jīvitumpi na ussahe.

SC 57Ms 23J_499Msdiv 57Yathā āraññakaṃ nāgaṃ,
poto anveti pacchato;
Jessantaṃ giriduggesu,
samesu visamesu ca.

SC 58Ms 23J_500Msdiv 58Evaṃ taṃ anugacchāmi,
pattamādāya pacchato;
Subharo te bhavissāmi,
na te hessāmi dubbharo”.

SC 59Ms 23J_501Msdiv 59“Yathā sāmuddikaṃ nāvaṃ,
vāṇijānaṃ dhanesinaṃ;
Vohāro tattha gaṇheyya,
vāṇijā byasanī siyā.

SC 60Ms 23J_502Msdiv 60Evamevāyaṃ puttakali,
antarāyakaro mama;
Imaṃ kumāraṃ pāpetha,
pāsādaṃ rativaḍḍhanaṃ.

SC 61PTS vp Pali 5.260Ms 23J_503Msdiv 61Tattha kambusahatthāyo,
yathā sakkaṃva accharā;
Tā naṃ tattha ramessanti,
tāhi ceso ramissati.

SC 62Ms 23J_504Msdiv 62Tato kumāraṃ pāpesuṃ,
pāsādaṃ rativaḍḍhanaṃ;
Taṃ disvā avacuṃ kaññā,
dīghāvuṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhanaṃ.

SC 63Ms 23J_505Msdiv 63Devatā nusi gandhabbo,
adu sakko purindado;
Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 64Ms 23J_506Msdiv 64“Namhi devo na gandhabbo,
Nāpi sakko purindado;
Kāsirañño ahaṃ putto,
Dīghāvu raṭṭhavaḍḍhano;
Mamaṃ bharatha Mamaṃ bharatha bhaddaṃ vo,
Ahaṃ bhattā bhavāmi vo”.

SC 65Ms 23J_507Msdiv 65Taṃ tattha avacuṃ kaññā,
dīghāvuṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhanaṃ;
“Kuhiṃ rājā anuppatto,
ito rājā kuhiṃ gato”.

SC 66Ms 23J_508Msdiv 66“Paṅkaṃ rājā atikkanto,
thale rājā patiṭṭhito;
Akaṇṭakaṃ agahanaṃ,
paṭipanno mahāpathaṃ.

SC 67Ms 23J_509Msdiv 67Ahañca paṭipannosmi,
maggaṃ duggatigāminaṃ;
Sakaṇṭakaṃ sagahanaṃ,
yena gacchanti duggatiṃ”.

SC 68Ms 23J_510Msdiv 68“Tassa te svāgataṃ rāja,
sīhasseva giribbajaṃ;
Anusāsa mahārāja,
tvaṃ no sabbāsamissaro”ti.

SC 69Ms 23J_511Soṇakajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
