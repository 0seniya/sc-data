76. Asaṃkiyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_167Msdiv 76“Asaṅkiyomhi gāmamhi,
araññe natthi me bhayaṃ;
Ujuṃ maggaṃ samārūḷho,
mettāya karuṇāya cā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_168Asaṃkiyajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
