369. Mittavindakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1265Msdiv 100“Kyāhaṃ devānamakaraṃ,
kiṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā;
Yaṃ me sirasmiṃ ohacca,
cakkaṃ bhamati matthake”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1266Msdiv 101“Atikkamma ramaṇakaṃ,
sadāmattañca dūbhakaṃ;
Brahmattarañca pāsādaṃ,
kenatthena idhāgato”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1267Msdiv 102“Ito bahutarā bhogā,
atra maññe bhavissare;
Iti etāya saññāya,
passa maṃ byasanaṃ gataṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1268Msdiv 103“Catubbhi aṭṭhajjhagamā,
aṭṭhāhipi ca soḷasa;
Soḷasāhi ca bāttiṃsa,
atricchaṃ cakkamāsado;
Icchāhatassa posassa,
cakkaṃ bhamati matthake.

SC 5Ms 22J_1269Msdiv 104Uparivisālā duppūrā,
icchā visaṭagāminī;
Ye ca taṃ anugijjhanti,
te honti cakkadhārino”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1270Mittavindakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
