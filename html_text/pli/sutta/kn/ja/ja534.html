gacchathūbho yathāsukhaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 23J_880Msdiv 109“Yassa tvaṃ bhatako rañño,

pañjare ajjhavodahi.

SC 23Ms 23J_882Msdiv 111Te pañjaragate pakkhī,

SC 26Ms 23J_885Msdiv 114“Evaṃ mahanto lokassa,

lolo andhova āmisaṃ.

SC 28Ms 23J_887Msdiv 116Avinicchayaññu atthesu,

sampatto kālapariyāyaṃ.

SC 29Ms 23J_888Msdiv 117Aḍḍhummatto udīresi,

SC 30Ms 23J_889Msdiv 118Māyā cesā marīcī ca,

so naresu narādhamo”.

yadidaṃ sattā pajāyare;

pāṇamāsajja pāṇibhi.

SC 33Ms 23J_892Msdiv 121Tvameva nañño sumukha,

bhītena jāyate mati.

SC 34Ms 23J_893Msdiv 122Sabbo hi saṃsayaṃ patto,

atthe yuñjanti duyyuje.

āpadaṃ attapariyāyaṃ.
