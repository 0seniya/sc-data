528. Mahābodhijātaka

SC 1Ms 23J_380Msdiv 124“Kiṃ nu daṇḍaṃ kimajinaṃ,
kiṃ chattaṃ kimupāhanaṃ;
Kimaṅkusañca pattañca,
saṅghāṭiñcāpi brāhmaṇa;
Taramānarūpohāsi,
kiṃ nu patthayase disaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 23J_381Msdiv 125“Dvādasetāni vassāni,
vusitāni tavantike;

piṅgalenābhikūjitaṃ.

SC 3Ms 23J_382Msdiv 126Svāyaṃ dittova nadati,
sukkadāṭhaṃ vidaṃsayaṃ;

vītasaddhassa maṃ pati”.

SC 12Ms 23J_391Msdiv 135Tasmā nābhikkhaṇaṃ gacche,

SC 13Ms 23J_392Msdiv 136Aticiraṃ nivāsena,

SC 14Ms 23J_393Msdiv 137“Evañce yācamānānaṃ,
