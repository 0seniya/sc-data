254. Kuṇḍakakucchisindhavajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_682Msdiv 10“Bhutvā tiṇaparighāsaṃ,
bhutvā ācāmakuṇḍakaṃ;
Etaṃ te bhojanaṃ āsi,
kasmā dāni na bhuñjasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_683Msdiv 11“Yattha posaṃ na jānanti,
jātiyā vinayena vā;
Bahuṃ tattha mahābrahme,
api ācāmakuṇḍakaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_684Msdiv 12Tvañca kho maṃ pajānāsi,
yādisāyaṃ hayuttamo;
Jānanto jānamāgamma,
na te bhakkhāmi kuṇḍakan”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_685Kuṇḍakakucchisindhavajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
