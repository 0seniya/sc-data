232. Vīṇāguṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_605Msdiv 163“Ekacintito yamattho,
bālo apariṇāyako;
Na hi khujjena vāmena,
bhoti saṅgantumarahasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_606Msdiv 164“Purisūsabhaṃ maññamānā,
ahaṃ khujjamakāmayiṃ;
Soyaṃ saṅkuṭito seti,
chinnatanti yathā viṇā”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_607Vīṇāguṇajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
