215. Kacchapajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.177Ms 22J_550Msdiv 129“Avadhī vata attānaṃ,
kacchapo byāharaṃ giraṃ;
Suggahītasmiṃ kaṭṭhasmiṃ,
vācāya sakiyāvadhi.

SC 2Ms 22J_551Msdiv 130Etampi disvā naravīriyaseṭṭha,
Vācaṃ pamuñce kusalaṃ nātivelaṃ;
Passasi bahubhāṇena,
Kacchapaṃ byasanaṃ gatan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_552Kacchapajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
