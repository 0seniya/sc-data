153. Sūkarajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.10Ms 22J_346Msdiv 5“Catuppado ahaṃ samma,
tvampi samma catuppado;
Ehi samma nivattassu,
kiṃ nu bhīto palāyasi”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.11Ms 22J_347Msdiv 6“Asuci pūtilomosi,
duggandho vāsi sūkara;
Sace yujjhitukāmosi,
jayaṃ samma dadāmi te”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_348Sūkarajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
