80. Bhīmasenajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_176Msdiv 80“Yaṃ te pavikatthitaṃ pure,
Atha te pūtisarā sajanti pacchā;
Ubhayaṃ na sameti bhīmasena,
Yuddhakathā ca idañca te vihaññan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_177Bhīmasenajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Varuṇavaggo aṭṭhamo.

SC 3Ms 22J_178Tassuddānaṃ

SC 4Ms 22J_179Varuṇā akataññūvare tu saccavaraṃ,
Savanappatinā ca abhitthanaya;
Karuṇāya silāplava illisato,
Puna ḍiṇḍimapūtisarena dasāti.
