302. Mahāassārohajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.12Ms 22J_888Msdiv 5“Adeyyesu dadaṃ dānaṃ,
deyyesu nappavecchati;
Āpāsu byasanaṃ patto,
sahāyaṃ nādhigacchati.

SC 2Ms 22J_889Msdiv 6Nādeyyesu dadaṃ dānaṃ,
deyyesu yo pavecchati;
Āpāsu byasanaṃ patto,
sahāyamadhigacchati.

SC 3Ms 22J_890Msdiv 7Saññogasambhogavisesadassanaṃ,
Anariyadhammesu saṭhesu nassati;
Katañca ariyesu ca ajjavesu,
Mahapphalaṃ hoti aṇumpi tādisu.

SC 4Ms 22J_891Msdiv 8Yo pubbe katakalyāṇo,
Akā loke sudukkaraṃ;
Pacchā kayirā na vā kayirā,
Accantaṃ pūjanāraho”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_892Mahāassārohajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
