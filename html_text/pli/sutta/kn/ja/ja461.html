461. Dasarathajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2196SC 1Ms 22J_2196Msdiv 84“Etha lakkhaṇa sītā ca,
ubho otarathodakaṃ;
Evāyaṃ bharato āha,
‘rājā dasaratho mato’”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2197SC 2Ms 22J_2197Msdiv 85“Kena rāmappabhāvena,
socitabbaṃ na socasi;
Pitaraṃ kālakataṃ sutvā,
na taṃ pasahate dukhaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2198SC 3Ms 22J_2198Msdiv 86“Yaṃ na sakkā nipāletuṃ,
posena lapataṃ bahuṃ;
Sa kissa viññū medhāvī,
attānamupatāpaye.

SC 4Ms 22J_2199Msdiv 87Daharā ca SC 4Ms 22J_2199Msdiv 87Daharā ca hi vuddhā ca,
ye bālā ye ca paṇḍitā;
Aḍḍhā ceva daliddā ca,
sabbe maccuparāyaṇā.

SC 5Ms 22J_2200SC 5Ms 22J_2200Msdiv 88Phalānamiva pakkānaṃ,
niccaṃ patanato bhayaṃ;
Evaṃ jātāna maccānaṃ,
nicca maraṇato bhayaṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_2201SC 6Ms 22J_2201Msdiv 89Sāyameke na dissanti,
pāto diṭṭhā bahujjanā;
Pāto eke na dissanti,
sāyaṃ diṭṭhā bahujjanā.

SC 7Ms 22J_2202SC 7Ms 22J_2202Msdiv 90Paridevayamāno ce,
kiñcidatthaṃ udabbahe;
Sammūḷho hiṃsamattānaṃ,
kayirā taṃ vicakkhaṇo.

SC 8Ms 22J_2203SC 8Ms 22J_2203Msdiv 91Kiso vivaṇṇo bhavati,
hiṃsamattānamattano;
Na tena petā pālenti,
niratthā paridevanā.

SC 9Ms 22J_2204SC 9Ms 22J_2204Msdiv 92Yathā saraṇamādittaṃ,
vārinā parinibbaye;
Evampi dhīro sutavā,
medhāvī paṇḍito naro;
Khippamuppatitaṃ sokaṃ,
vāto tūlaṃva dhaṃsaye.

SC 10Ms 22J_2205Msdiv 93Macco SC 10Ms 22J_2205Msdiv 93Macco ekova acceti,
ekova jāyate kule;
Saṃyogaparamā tveva,
sambhogā sabbapāṇinaṃ.

SC 11Ms 22J_2206SC 11Ms 22J_2206Msdiv 94Tasmā hi dhīrassa bahussutassa,
Sampassato lokamimaṃ parañca;
Aññāya dhammaṃ hadayaṃ manañca,
Sokā mahantāpi na tāpayanti.

SC 12Ms 22J_2207SC 12Ms 22J_2207Msdiv 95Sohaṃ dassañca bhokkhañca,
bharissāmi ca ñātake;
Sesañca pālayissāmi,
kiccametaṃ vijānato.

SC 13Ms 22J_2208SC 13Ms 22J_2208Msdiv 96Dasa vassasahassāni,
saṭṭhi vassasatāni ca;
Kambugīvo mahābāhu,
rāmo rajjamakārayī”ti.

SC 14SC 14Ms 22J_2209Dasarathajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
