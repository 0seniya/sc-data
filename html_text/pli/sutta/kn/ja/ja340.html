340. Visayhajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1084Msdiv 157“Adāsi dānāni pure visayha,
Dadato ca te khayadhammo ahosi;
Ito parañce na dadeyya dānaṃ,
Tiṭṭheyyuṃ te saṃyamantassa bhogā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1085Msdiv 158“Anariyamariyena sahassanetta,
Suduggatenāpi akiccamāhu;
Mā vo dhanaṃ taṃ ahu devarāja,
Yaṃ bhogahetu vijahemu saddhaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1086Msdiv 159Yena eko ratho yāti,
yāti tenāparo ratho;
Porāṇaṃ nihitaṃ vattaṃ,
vattataññeva vāsava.

SC 4Ms 22J_1087Msdiv 160Yadi hessati dassāma,
asante kiṃ dadāmase;
Evaṃbhūtāpi dassāma,
mā dānaṃ pamadamhase”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1088Visayhajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kokilavaggo catuttho.

SC 6Ms 22J_1089Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 22J_1090Ativelapabhāsati jītavaro,
Vanamajjha rathesabha jimhagamo;
Atha jambu tiṇāsanapīṭhavaraṃ,
Atha taṇḍula mora visayha dasāti.
