374. Cūḷadhanuggahajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1298Msdiv 128“Sabbaṃ bhaṇḍaṃ samādāya,
pāraṃ tiṇṇosi brāhmaṇa;
Paccāgaccha lahuṃ khippaṃ,
mampi tārehi dānito”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1299Msdiv 129“Asanthutaṃ maṃ cirasanthutena,
Nimīni bhotī addhuvaṃ dhuvena;
Mayāpi bhotī nimineyya aññaṃ,
Ito ahaṃ dūrataraṃ gamissaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1300Msdiv 130“Kāyaṃ eḷagalāgumbe,
karoti ahuhāsiyaṃ;
Nayīdha naccagītaṃ vā,
tāḷaṃ vā susamāhitaṃ;
Anamhikāle Anamhikāle susoṇi,
kiṃ nu jagghasi sobhane”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1301Msdiv 131“Siṅgāla bāla dummedha,
appapaññosi jambuka;
Jīno macchañca pesiñca,
kapaṇo viya jhāyasi”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1302Msdiv 132“Sudassaṃ vajjamaññesaṃ,
attano pana duddasaṃ;
Jīnā patiñca jārañca,
maññe tvaññeva jhāyasi”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1303Msdiv 133“Evametaṃ migarāja,
yathā bhāsasi jambuka;
Sā nūnāhaṃ ito gantvā,
bhattu hessaṃ vasānugā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1304Msdiv 134“Yo hare mattikaṃ thālaṃ,
kaṃsathālampi so hare;
Katañceva tayā pāpaṃ,
punapevaṃ karissasī”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1305Cūḷadhanuggahajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
