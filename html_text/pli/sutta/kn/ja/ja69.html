69. Visavantajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.311Ms 22J_151Msdiv 69“Dhiratthu taṃ visaṃ vantaṃ,
yamahaṃ jīvitakāraṇā;
Vantaṃ paccāvamissāmi,
mataṃ me jīvitā varan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_152Visavantajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
