68. Sāketajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_149SC 1Ms 22J_149Msdiv 68Yasmiṃ mano nivisati,
cittañcāpi pasīdati;
Adiṭṭhapubbake pose,
kāmaṃ tasmimpi vissase”ti.

SC 2SC 2Ms 22J_150Sāketajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
