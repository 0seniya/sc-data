290. Sīlavīmaṃsakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_832Msdiv 118“Sīlaṃ kireva kalyāṇaṃ,
sīlaṃ loke anuttaraṃ;
Passa ghoraviso nāgo,
sīlavāti na haññati.

SC 2Ms 22J_833Msdiv 119Sohaṃ sīlaṃ samādissaṃ,
loke anumataṃ sivaṃ;
Ariyavuttisamācāro,
yena vuccati sīlavā.

SC 3Ms 22J_834Msdiv 120Ñātīnañca piyo hoti,
mittesu ca virocati;
Kāyassa bhedā sugatiṃ,
upapajjati sīlavā”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_835Sīlavīmaṃsakajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Abbhantaravaggo catuttho.

SC 5Ms 22J_836Tassuddānaṃ

SC 6Ms 22J_837Duma kaṃsavaruttamabyagghamigā,
Maṇayo maṇi sālukamavhayano;
Anusāsaniyopi ca macchavaro,
Maṇikuṇḍalakena kirena dasāti.
