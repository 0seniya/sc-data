279. Satapattajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.389Ms 22J_786Msdiv 85“Yathā māṇavako panthe,
siṅgāliṃ vanagocariṃ;
Atthakāmaṃ pavedentiṃ,
anatthakāmāti maññati;
Anatthakāmaṃ satapattaṃ,
atthakāmoti maññati.

SC 2Ms 22J_787Msdiv 86Evameva idhekacco,
puggalo hoti tādiso;
Hitehi vacanaṃ vutto,
paṭigaṇhāti vāmato.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.390Ms 22J_788Msdiv 87Ye ca kho naṃ pasaṃsanti,
bhayā ukkaṃsayanti vā;
Tañhi so maññate mittaṃ,
satapattaṃva māṇavo”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_789Satapattajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
