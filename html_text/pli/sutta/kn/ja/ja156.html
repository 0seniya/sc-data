156. Alīnacittajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_355Msdiv 11“Alīnacittaṃ nissāya,
pahaṭṭhā mahatī camū;
Kosalaṃ senāsantuṭṭhaṃ,
jīvaggāhaṃ agāhayi.

SC 2Ms 22J_356Msdiv 12Evaṃ nissayasampanno,
bhikkhu āraddhavīriyo;
Bhāvayaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_357Alīnacittajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
