394. Vaṭṭakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1466Msdiv 129“Paṇītaṃ bhuñjase bhattaṃ,
sappitelañca mātula;
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,
kiso tvamasi vāyasa”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1467Msdiv 130“Amittamajjhe vasato,
tesu āmisamesato;
Niccaṃ ubbiggahadayassa,
kuto kākassa daḷhiyaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1468Msdiv 131Niccaṃ ubbegino kākā,
dhaṅkā pāpena kammunā;
Laddho piṇḍo na pīṇeti,
kiso tenasmi vaṭṭaka.

SC 4Ms 22J_1469Msdiv 132Lūkhāni tiṇabījāni,
appasnehāni bhuñjasi;
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,
thūlo tvamasi vaṭṭaka”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1470Msdiv 133“Appicchā appacintāya,
adūragamanena ca;
Laddhāladdhena yāpento,
thūlo tenasmi vāyasa.

SC 6Ms 22J_1471Msdiv 134Appicchassa hi posassa,
appacintasukhassa ca;
Susaṅgahitamānassa,
vuttī susamudānayā”ti.

SC 7Ms 22J_1472Vaṭṭakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
