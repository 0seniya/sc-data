368. Tacasārajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1259Msdiv 95“Amittahatthatthagatā,
tacasārasamappitā;
Pasannamukhavaṇṇāttha,
kasmā tumhe na socatha”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1260Msdiv 96“Na socanāya paridevanāya,
Atthova labbho api appakopi;
Socantamenaṃ dukhitaṃ viditvā,
Paccatthikā attamanā bhavanti.

SC 3Ms 22J_1261Msdiv 97Yato ca kho paṇḍito āpadāsu,
Na vedhatī atthavinicchayaññū;
Paccatthikāssa dukhitā bhavanti,
Disvā mukhaṃ avikāraṃ purāṇaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1262Msdiv 98Jappena mantena subhāsitena,
Anuppadānena paveṇiyā vā;
Yathā yathā yattha labhetha atthaṃ,
Tathā tathā tattha parakkameyya.

SC 5Ms 22J_1263Msdiv 99Yato ca jāneyya alabbhaneyyo,
Mayā va aññena vā esa attho;
Asocamāno adhivāsayeyya,
Kammaṃ daḷhaṃ kinti karomi dānī”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1264Tacasārajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
