318. Kaṇaverajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.62Ms 22J_970Msdiv 69“Yaṃ taṃ vasantasamaye,
kaṇaveresu bhāṇusu;
Sāmaṃ bāhāya pīḷesi,
sā taṃ ārogyamabravi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_971Msdiv 70“Ambho na kira saddheyyaṃ,
yaṃ vāto pabbataṃ vahe;
Pabbatañce vahe vāto,
sabbampi pathaviṃ vahe;
Yattha sāmā kālakatā,
sā maṃ ārogyamabravi”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 3.63Ms 22J_972Msdiv 71“Na ceva sā kālakatā,
na ca sā aññamicchati;
Ekabhattikinī sāmā,
tameva abhikaṅkhati”.

SC 4Ms 22J_973Msdiv 72“Asanthutaṃ maṃ cirasanthutena,
Nimīni sāmā adhuvaṃ dhuvena;
Mayāpi sāmā nimineyya aññaṃ,
Ito ahaṃ dūrataraṃ gamissan”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_974Kaṇaverajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
