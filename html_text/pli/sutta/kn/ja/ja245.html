245. Mūlapariyāyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_647SC 1Ms 22J_647Msdiv 190“Kālo ghasati bhūtāni,
sabbāneva sahattanā;
Yo ca kālaghaso bhūto,
sa bhūtapacaniṃ paci”.

SC 2Ms 22J_648SC 2Ms 22J_648Msdiv 191“Bahūni narasīsāni,
lomasāni brahāni ca;
Gīvāsu paṭimukkāni,
kocidevettha kaṇṇavā”ti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 22J_649Mūlapariyāyajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
