108. Bāhiyajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.421Ms 22J_239Msdiv 108“Sikkheyya sikkhitabbāni,
santi sacchandino janā;
Bāhiyā hi suhannena,
rājānamabhirādhayī”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_240Bāhiyajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
