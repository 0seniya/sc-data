102. Paṇṇikajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.412Ms 22J_227Msdiv 102“Yo dukkhaphuṭṭhāya bhaveyya tāṇaṃ,
So me pitā dubbhi vane karoti;
Sā kassa kandāmi vanassa majjhe,
Yo tāyitā so sahasaṃ karotī”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_228Paṇṇikajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
