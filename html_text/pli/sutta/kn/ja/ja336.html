336. Brahāchattajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1064Msdiv 141“Tiṇaṃ tiṇanti lapasi,
ko nu te tiṇamāhari;
Kiṃ nu te tiṇakiccatthi,
tiṇameva pabhāsasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1065Msdiv 142“Idhāgamā brahmacārī,
brahā chatto bahussuto;
So me sabbaṃ samādāya,
tiṇaṃ nikkhippa gacchati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1066Msdiv 143“Evetaṃ hoti kattabbaṃ,
appena bahumicchatā;
Sabbaṃ sakassa ādānaṃ,
anādānaṃ tiṇassa ca”.
()

SC 4Ms 22J_1067Msdiv 144“Sīlavanto na kubbanti,
bālo sīlāni kubbati;
Aniccasīlaṃ dussīlyaṃ,
kiṃ paṇḍiccaṃ karissatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1068Brahāchattajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
