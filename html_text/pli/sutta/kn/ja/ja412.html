412. Koṭasimbalijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1623Msdiv 121“Ahaṃ dasasataṃbyāmaṃ,
uragamādāya āgato;
Tañca mañca mahākāyaṃ,
dhārayaṃ nappavedhasi.

SC 2Ms 22J_1624Msdiv 122Athimaṃ khuddakaṃ pakkhiṃ,
Appamaṃsataraṃ mayā;
Dhārayaṃ byathasi bhītā,
Kamatthaṃ koṭasimbali”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1625Msdiv 123“Maṃsabhakkho tuvaṃ rāja,
phalabhakkho ayaṃ dijo;
Ayaṃ nigrodhabījāni,
pilakkhudumbarāni ca;
Assatthāni ca bhakkhitvā,
khandhe me ohadissati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1626Msdiv 124Te rukkhā saṃvirūhanti,
mama passe nivātajā;
Te maṃ pariyonandhissanti,
arukkhaṃ maṃ karissare.

SC 5Ms 22J_1627Msdiv 125Santi aññepi rukkhā se,
mūlino khandhino dumā;
Iminā sakuṇajātena,
bījamāharitā hatā.

SC 6Ms 22J_1628Msdiv 126Ajjhārūhābhivaḍḍhanti,
brahantampi vanappatiṃ;
Tasmā rāja pavedhāmi,
sampassaṃnāgataṃ bhayaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1629Msdiv 127“Saṅkeyya saṅkitabbāni,
rakkheyyānāgataṃ bhayaṃ;
Anāgatabhayā dhīro,
ubho loke avekkhatī”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1630Koṭasimbalijātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
