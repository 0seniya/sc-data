267. Kakkaṭakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.343Ms 22J_736Msdiv 49“Siṅgīmigo āyatacakkhunetto,
Aṭṭhittaco vārisayo alomo;
Tenābhibhūto kapaṇaṃ rudāmi,
Mā heva maṃ pāṇasamaṃ jaheyya”.

SC 2Ms 22J_737Msdiv 50“Ayya na taṃ jahissāmi,
kuñjaraṃ saṭṭhihāyanaṃ;
Pathabyā cāturantāya,
suppiyo hosi me tuvaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_738Msdiv 51“Ye kuḷīrā samuddasmiṃ,
gaṅgāya yamunāya ca;
Tesaṃ tvaṃ vārijo seṭṭho,
muñca rodantiyā patin”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_739Kakkaṭakajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
