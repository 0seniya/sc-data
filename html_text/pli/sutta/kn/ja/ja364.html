364. Khajjopanakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.197Ms 22J_1235Msdiv 75“Ko nu santamhi pajjote,
aggipariyesanaṃ caraṃ;
Addakkhi ratti khajjotaṃ,
jātavedaṃ amaññatha.

SC 2Ms 22J_1236Msdiv 76Svassa gomayacuṇṇāni,
abhimatthaṃ tiṇāni ca;
Viparītāya saññāya,
nāsakkhi pajjaletave.

SC 3Ms 22J_1237Msdiv 77Evampi anupāyena,
atthaṃ na labhate migo;
Visāṇato gavaṃ dohaṃ,
yattha khīraṃ na vindati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1238Msdiv 78Vividhehi upāyehi,
atthaṃ papponti māṇavā;
Niggahena amittānaṃ,
mittānaṃ paggahena ca.

SC 5Ms 22J_1239Msdiv 79Senāmokkhapalābhena,
vallabhānaṃ nayena ca;
Jagatiṃ jagatipālā,
āvasanti vasundharan”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1240Khajjopanakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
