157. Guṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_358Msdiv 13“Yena kāmaṃ paṇāmeti,
dhammo balavataṃ migī;
Unnadantī vijānāhi,
jātaṃ saraṇato bhayaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_359Msdiv 14“Api cepi dubbalo mitto,
mittadhammesu tiṭṭhati;
So ñātako ca bandhu ca,
so mitto so ca me sakhā;
Dāṭhini mātimaññittho,
siṅgālo mama pāṇado”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_360Guṇajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
