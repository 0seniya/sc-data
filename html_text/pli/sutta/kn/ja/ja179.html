179. Satadhammajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_430Msdiv 57“Tañca appañca ucchiṭṭhaṃ,
tañca kicchena no adā;
Sohaṃ brāhmaṇajātiko,
yaṃ bhuttaṃ tampi uggataṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_431Msdiv 58“Evaṃ dhammaṃ niraṃkatvā,
Yo adhammena jīvati;
Satadhammova lābhena,
Laddhenapi na nandatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_432Satadhammajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
