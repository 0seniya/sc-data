136. Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.476Ms 22J_301Msdiv 136“Yaṃ laddhaṃ tena tuṭṭhabbaṃ,
Atilobho hi pāpako;
Haṃsarājaṃ gahetvāna,
Suvaṇṇā parihāyathā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_302Suvaṇṇahaṃsajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
