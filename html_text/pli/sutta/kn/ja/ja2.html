2. Vaṇṇupathajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.109Ms 22J_4Msdiv 2“Akilāsuno vaṇṇupathe khaṇantā,
Udaṅgaṇe tattha papaṃ avinduṃ;
Evaṃ munī viriyabalūpapanno,
Akilāsu vinde hadayassa santin”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_5Vaṇṇupathajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
