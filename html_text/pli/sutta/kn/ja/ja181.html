181. Asadisajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_438SC 1Ms 22J_438Msdiv 61Dhanuggaho asadiso,
rājaputto mahabbalo;
Dūrepātī akkhaṇavedhī,
mahākāyappadālano.

SC 2Ms 22J_439SC 2Ms 22J_439Msdiv 62Sabbāmitte raṇaṃ katvā,
na ca kañci viheṭhayi;
Bhātaraṃ sotthiṃ katvāna,
saṃyamaṃ ajjhupāgamīti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 22J_440Asadisajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
