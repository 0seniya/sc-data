449. Maṭṭhakuṇḍalījātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2033Msdiv 115“Alaṅkato maṭṭhakuṇḍalī,
Māladhārī haricandanussado;
Bāhā paggayha kandasi,
Vanamajjhe kiṃ dukkhito tuvaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2034Msdiv 116“Sovaṇṇamayo pabhassaro,
Uppanno rathapañjaro mama;
Tassa cakkayugaṃ na vindāmi,
Tena dukkhena jahāmi jīvitaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2035Msdiv 117“Sovaṇṇamayaṃ maṇīmayaṃ,
Lohamayaṃ atha rūpiyāmayaṃ;
Pāvada rathaṃ Pāvada rathaṃ karissāmi te,
Cakkayugaṃ paṭipādayāmi taṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2036Msdiv 118“So māṇavo tassa pāvadi,
Candasūriyā ubhayettha Candasūriyā ubhayettha bhātaro;
Sovaṇṇamayo ratho mama,
Tena cakkayugena sobhati”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2037Msdiv 119“Bālo kho tvaṃsi māṇava,
Yo tvaṃ patthayase apatthiyaṃ;
Maññāmi tuvaṃ marissasi,
Na hi tvaṃ lacchasi candasūriye”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2038Msdiv 120“Gamanāgamanampi dissati,
Vaṇṇadhātu ubhayettha vīthiyo;
Peto pana neva dissati,
Ko nu kho kandataṃ bālyataro”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2039Msdiv 121“Saccaṃ kho vadesi māṇava,
Ahameva kandataṃ bālyataro;
Candaṃ viya dārako rudaṃ,
Petaṃ kālakatābhipatthaye.

SC 8Ms 22J_2040Msdiv 122Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 9Ms 22J_2041Msdiv 123Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
yamāsi hadayassitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 10Ms 22J_2042Msdiv 124Sohaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
vītasoko anāvilo;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna māṇavā”ti.

SC 11Ms 22J_2043Maṭṭhakuṇḍalījātakaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
