346. Kesavajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.144Ms 22J_1116Msdiv 181“Manussindaṃ jahitvāna,
sabbakāmasamiddhinaṃ;
Kathaṃ nu bhagavā kesī,
kappassa ramati assame”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1117Msdiv 182“Sādūni ramaṇīyāni,
santi vakkhā manoramā;
Subhāsitāni kappassa,
nārada ramayanti maṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1118Msdiv 183“Sālīnaṃ odanaṃ bhuñje,
suciṃ maṃsūpasecanaṃ;
Kathaṃ sāmākanīvāraṃ,
aloṇaṃ chādayanti taṃ”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 3.145Ms 22J_1119Msdiv 184“Sāduṃ vā yadi vāsāduṃ,
appaṃ vā yadi vā bahuṃ;
Vissattho yattha bhuñjeyya,
vissāsaparamā rasā”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1120Kesavajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
