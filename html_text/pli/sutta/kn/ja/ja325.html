325. Godharājajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1007Msdiv 97“Samaṇaṃ taṃ maññamāno,
upagacchimasaññataṃ;
So maṃ daṇḍena pāhāsi,
yathā assamaṇo tathā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1008Msdiv 98“Kiṃ te jaṭāhi dummedha,
kiṃ te ajinasāṭiyā;
Abbhantaraṃ te gahanaṃ,
bāhiraṃ parimajjasi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1009Msdiv 99“Ehi godha nivattassu,
bhuñja sālīnamodanaṃ;
Telaṃ loṇañca me atthi,
pahūtaṃ mayha pipphali.

SC 4Ms 22J_1010Msdiv 100Esa bhiyyo pavekkhāmi,
vammikaṃ sataporisaṃ;
Telaṃ loṇañca kittesi,
ahitaṃ mayha pipphalī”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1011Godharājajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
