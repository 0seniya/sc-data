480. Akittijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2472Msdiv 83“Akittiṃ disvā sammantaṃ,
sakko bhūtapatī bravi;
Kiṃ patthayaṃ mahābrahme,
eko sammasi ghammani”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2473Msdiv 84“Dukkho punabbhavo sakka,
sarīrassa ca bhedanaṃ;
Sammohamaraṇaṃ dukkhaṃ,
tasmā sammāmi vāsava”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2474Msdiv 85“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2475Msdiv 86“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Yena putte ca dāre ca,
dhanadhaññaṃ piyāni ca;
Laddhā narā na tappanti,
so lobho na mayī vase”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2476Msdiv 87“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2477Msdiv 88“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Khettaṃ vatthuṃ hiraññañca,
gavāssaṃ dāsaporisaṃ;
Yena jātena jīyanti,
so doso na mayī vase”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2478Msdiv 89“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2479Msdiv 90“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Bālaṃ na passe na suṇe,
na ca bālena saṃvase;
Bālenallāpasallāpaṃ,
na kare na ca rocaye”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2480Msdiv 91“Kiṃ nu te akaraṃ bālo,
vada kassapa kāraṇaṃ;
Kena kassapa bālassa,
dassanaṃ nābhikaṅkhasi”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2481Msdiv 92“Anayaṃ nayati dummedho,
adhurāyaṃ niyuñjati;
Dunnayo seyyaso hoti,
sammā vutto pakuppati;
Vinayaṃ so na jānāti,
sādhu tassa adassanaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2482Msdiv 93“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2483Msdiv 94“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Dhīraṃ passe suṇe dhīraṃ,
dhīrena saha saṃvase;
Dhīrenallāpasallāpaṃ,
taṃ kare tañca rocaye”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2484Msdiv 95“Kiṃ nu te akaraṃ dhīro,
vada kassapa kāraṇaṃ;
Kena kassapa dhīrassa,
dassanaṃ abhikaṅkhasi”.

SC 14Ms 22J_2485Msdiv 96“Nayaṃ nayati medhāvī,
adhurāyaṃ na yuñjati;
Sunayo seyyaso hoti,
sammā vutto na kuppati;
Vinayaṃ so pajānāti,
sādhu tena samāgamo”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2486Msdiv 97“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 16Ms 22J_2487Msdiv 98“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Tato ratyā vivasāne,
sūriyuggamanaṃ pati;
Dibbā bhakkhā pātubhaveyyuṃ,
sīlavanto ca yācakā.

SC 17Ms 22J_2488Msdiv 99Dadato me na khīyetha,
datvā nānutapeyyahaṃ;
Dadaṃ cittaṃ pasādeyyaṃ,
etaṃ sakka varaṃ vare”.

SC 18Ms 22J_2489Msdiv 100“Etasmiṃ te sulapite,
patirūpe subhāsite;
Varaṃ kassapa te dammi,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 19Ms 22J_2490Msdiv 101“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Na maṃ puna upeyyāsi,
etaṃ sakka varaṃ vare”.

SC 20Ms 22J_2491Msdiv 102“Bahūhi vatacariyāhi,
narā ca atha nāriyo;
Dassanaṃ abhikaṅkhanti,
kiṃ nu me dassane bhayaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 22J_2492Msdiv 103“Taṃ tādisaṃ devavaṇṇaṃ,
sabbakāmasamiddhinaṃ;
Disvā tapo pamajjeyyaṃ,
etaṃ te dassane bhayan”ti.

SC 22Ms 22J_2493Akittijātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
