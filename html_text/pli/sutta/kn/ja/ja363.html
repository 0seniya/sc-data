363. Hirijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.196Ms 22J_1229Msdiv 70“Hiriṃ tarantaṃ vijigucchamānaṃ,
Tavāhamasmī iti bhāsamānaṃ;
Seyyāni kammāni anādiyantaṃ,
Neso mamanti iti naṃ vijaññā.

SC 2Ms 22J_1230Msdiv 71Yañhi kayirā tañhi vade,
yaṃ na kayirā na taṃ vade;
Akarontaṃ bhāsamānaṃ,
parijānanti paṇḍitā.

SC 3Ms 22J_1231Msdiv 72Na so mitto yo sadā appamatto,
Bhedāsaṅkī randhamevānupassī;
Yasmiñca setī urasīva putto,
Sa ve mitto yo abhejjo parehi.

SC 4Ms 22J_1232Msdiv 73Pāmojjakaraṇaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
pasaṃsāvahanaṃ sukhaṃ;
Phalānisaṃso bhāveti,
vahanto porisaṃ dhuraṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1233Msdiv 74Pavivekarasaṃ pitvā,
rasaṃ upasamassa ca;
Niddaro hoti nippāpo,
dhammappītirasaṃ pivan”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1234Hirijātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
