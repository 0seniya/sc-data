467. Kāmajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.172Ms 22J_2278Msdiv 37“Kāmaṃ kāmayamānassa,
tassa ce taṃ samijjhati;
Addhā pītimano hoti,
laddhā macco yadicchati.

SC 2Ms 22J_2279Msdiv 38Kāmaṃ kāmayamānassa,
tassa ce taṃ samijjhati;
Tato naṃ aparaṃ kāme,
ghamme taṇhaṃva vindati.

SC 3Ms 22J_2280Msdiv 39Gavaṃva siṅgino siṅgaṃ,
vaḍḍhamānassa vaḍḍhati;
Evaṃ mandassa posassa,
bālassa avijānato;
Bhiyyo taṇhā pipāsā ca,
vaḍḍhamānassa vaḍḍhati.

SC 4Ms 22J_2281Msdiv 40Pathabyā sāliyavakaṃ,
gavāssaṃ dāsaporisaṃ;
Datvā ca nālamekassa,
iti vidvā samaṃ care.

SC 5Ms 22J_2282Msdiv 41Rājā pasayha pathaviṃ vijitvā,
Sasāgarantaṃ mahimāvasanto;
Oraṃ samuddassa atittarūpo,
Pāraṃ samuddassapi patthayetha.

SC 6Ms 22J_2283Msdiv 42Yāva anussaraṃ kāme,
Manasā titti nājjhagā;
Tato nivattā paṭikkamma disvā,
Te ve sutittā ye paññāya tittā.

SC 7Ms 22J_2284Msdiv 43Paññāya tittinaṃ seṭṭhaṃ,
na so kāmehi tappati;
Paññāya tittaṃ purisaṃ,
taṇhā na kurute vasaṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_2285Msdiv 44Apacinetheva kāmānaṃ,
appicchassa alolupo;
Samuddamatto puriso,
na so kāmehi tappati.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 4.173Ms 22J_2286Msdiv 45Rathakārova cammassa,
parikantaṃ upāhanaṃ;
Yaṃ yaṃ cajati kāmānaṃ,
taṃ taṃ sampajjate sukhaṃ;
Sabbañce sukhamiccheyya,
sabbe kāme pariccaje”.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 4.175Ms 22J_2287Msdiv 46“Aṭṭha te bhāsitā gāthā,
sabbā honti sahassiyo;
Paṭigaṇha mahābrahme,
sādhetaṃ tava bhāsitaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2288Msdiv 47“Na me attho sahassehi,
satehi nahutehi vā;
Pacchimaṃ bhāsato gāthaṃ,
kāme me na rato mano”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2289Msdiv 48“Bhadrako vatāyaṃ māṇavako,
Sabbalokavidū muni;
Yo imaṃ taṇhaṃ dukkhajananiṃ,
Parijānāti paṇḍito”ti.

SC 13Ms 22J_2290Kāmajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
