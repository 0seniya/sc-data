234. Asitābhūjātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_611Msdiv 167“Tvameva dānimakara,
Yaṃ kāmo byagamā tayi;
Soyaṃ appaṭisandhiko,
Kharachinnaṃva renukaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_612Msdiv 168“Atricchaṃ atilobhena,
atilobhamadena ca;
Evaṃ hāyati atthamhā,
ahaṃva asitābhuyā”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_613Asitābhūjātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
