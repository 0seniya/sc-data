223. Puṭabhattajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.205Ms 22J_576Msdiv 145“Name namantassa bhaje bhajantaṃ,
Kiccānukubbassa kareyya kiccaṃ;
Nānatthakāmassa kareyya atthaṃ,
Asambhajantampi na sambhajeyya.

SC 2Ms 22J_577Msdiv 146Caje cajantaṃ vanathaṃ na kayirā,
Apetacittena na sambhajeyya;
Dijo dumaṃ khīṇaphalanti ñatvā,
Aññaṃ samekkheyya mahā hi loko”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_578Puṭabhattajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
