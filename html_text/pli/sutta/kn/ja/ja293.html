293. Kāyanibbindajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_846Msdiv 127“Phuṭṭhassa me aññatarena byādhinā,
Rogena bāḷhaṃ dukhitassa ruppato;
Parisussati khippamidaṃ kaḷevaraṃ,
Pupphaṃ yathā paṃsuni ātape kataṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_847Msdiv 128Ajaññaṃ jaññasaṅkhātaṃ,
asuciṃ sucisammataṃ;
Nānākuṇapaparipūraṃ,
jaññarūpaṃ apassato.

SC 3Ms 22J_848Msdiv 129Dhiratthumaṃ āturaṃ pūtikāyaṃ,
Jegucchiyaṃ assuciṃ byādhidhammaṃ;
Yatthappamattā adhimucchitā pajā,
Hāpenti maggaṃ sugatūpapattiyā”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_849Kāyanibbindajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
