515. Sambhavajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_3397Msdiv 138“Rajjañca paṭipannāsma,
ādhipaccaṃ sucīrata;
Mahattaṃ pattumicchāmi,
vijetuṃ pathaviṃ imaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_3398Msdiv 139Dhammena no adhammena,
adhammo me na ruccati;
Kiccova dhammo carito,
rañño hoti sucīrata.

SC 3Ms 22J_3399Msdiv 140Idha cevāninditā yena,
pecca yena aninditā;
Yasaṃ devamanussesu,
yena pappomu brāhmaṇa.

SC 4Ms 22J_3400Msdiv 141Yohaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
kattumicchāmi brāhmaṇa;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
brāhmaṇakkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 5Ms 22J_3401Msdiv 142“Nāññatra vidhurā rāja,
etadakkhātumarahati;
Yaṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
kattumicchasi khattiya”.

SC 6Ms 22J_3402Msdiv 143“Ehi kho pahito gaccha,
vidhurassa upantikaṃ;
Nikkhañcimaṃ suvaṇṇassa,
haraṃ gaccha sucīrata;
Abhihāraṃ imaṃ dajjā,
atthadhammānusiṭṭhiyā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_3403Msdiv 144Svādhippāgā bhāradvājo,
vidhurassa upantikaṃ;
Tamaddasa mahābrahmā,
asamānaṃ sake ghare.

SC 8Ms 22J_3404Msdiv 145“Raññohaṃ pahito dūto,
korabyassa yasassino;
‘Atthaṃ dhammañca pucchesi’,
iccabravi yudhiṭṭhilo;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
vidhurakkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 9Ms 22J_3405Msdiv 146“Gaṅgaṃ me pidahissanti,
na taṃ sakkomi brāhmaṇa;
Apidhetuṃ mahāsindhuṃ,
taṃ kathaṃ so bhavissati;
Na te sakkomi akkhātuṃ,
atthaṃ dhammañca pucchito.

SC 10Ms 22J_3406Msdiv 147Bhadrakāro ca me putto,
oraso mama atrajo;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
gantvā pucchassu brāhmaṇa”.

SC 11Ms 22J_3407Msdiv 148Svādhippāgā bhāradvājo,
bhadrakārassupantikaṃ;
Tamaddasa mahābrahmā,
nisinnaṃ samhi vesmani.

SC 12Ms 22J_3408Msdiv 149“Raññohaṃ pahito dūto,
korabyassa yasassino;
‘Atthaṃ dhammañca pucchesi’,
iccabravi yudhiṭṭhilo;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
bhadrakāra pabrūhi me”.

SC 13Ms 22J_3409Msdiv 150“Maṃsakājaṃ avahāya,
godhaṃ anupatāmahaṃ;
Na te sakkomi akkhātuṃ,
atthaṃ dhammañca pucchito.

SC 14Ms 22J_3410Msdiv 151Sañcayo nāma me bhātā,
kaniṭṭho me sucīrata;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
gantvā pucchassu brāhmaṇa”.

SC 15Ms 22J_3411Msdiv 152Svādhippāgā bhāradvājo,
sañcayassa upantikaṃ;
Tamaddasa mahābrahmā,
nisinnaṃ samhi vesmani.

SC 16Ms 22J_3412Msdiv 153“Raññohaṃ pahito dūto,
korabyassa yasassino;
‘Atthaṃ dhammañca pucchesi’,
iccabravi yudhiṭṭhilo;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
sañcayakkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 17Ms 22J_3413Msdiv 154“Sadā maṃ gilate maccu,
sāyaṃ pāto sucīrata;
Na te sakkomi akkhātuṃ,
atthaṃ dhammañca pucchito.

SC 18Ms 22J_3414Msdiv 155Sambhavo nāma me bhātā,
kaniṭṭho me sucīrata;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
gantvā pucchassu brāhmaṇa”.

SC 19Ms 22J_3415Msdiv 156“Abbhuto vata bho dhammo,
nāyaṃ asmāka ruccati;
Tayo janā pitāputtā,
te su paññāya no vidū.

SC 20Ms 22J_3416Msdiv 157Na taṃ sakkotha akkhātuṃ,
atthaṃ dhammañca pucchitā;
Kathaṃ nu daharo jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca pucchito”.

SC 21Ms 22J_3417Msdiv 158“Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa.

SC 22Ms 22J_3418Msdiv 159Yathāpi cando vimalo,
gacchaṃ ākāsadhātuyā;
Sabbe tārāgaṇe loke,
ābhāya atirocati.

SC 23Ms 22J_3419Msdiv 160Evampi daharūpeto,
paññāyogena sambhavo;
Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa.

SC 24Ms 22J_3420Msdiv 161Yathāpi rammako māso,
gimhānaṃ hoti brāhmaṇa;
Atevaññehi māsehi,
dumapupphehi sobhati.

SC 25Ms 22J_3421Msdiv 162Evampi daharūpeto,
paññāyogena sambhavo;
Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa.

SC 26Ms 22J_3422Msdiv 163Yathāpi himavā brahme,
pabbato gandhamādano;
Nānārukkhehi sañchanno,
mahābhūtagaṇālayo;
Osadhehi ca dibbehi,
disā bhāti pavāti ca.

SC 27Ms 22J_3423Msdiv 164Evampi daharūpeto,
paññāyogena sambhavo;
Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa.

SC 28Ms 22J_3424Msdiv 165Yathāpi pāvako brahme,
accimālī yasassimā;
Jalamāno vane gacche,
analo kaṇhavattanī.

SC 29Ms 22J_3425Msdiv 166Ghatāsano dhūmaketu,
uttamāhevanandaho;
Nisīthe pabbataggasmiṃ,
pahūtedho virocati.

SC 30Ms 22J_3426Msdiv 167Evampi daharūpeto,
paññāyogena sambhavo;
Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa.

SC 31Ms 22J_3427Msdiv 168Javena bhadraṃ jānanti,
balībaddañca vāhiye;
Dohena dhenuṃ jānanti,
bhāsamānañca paṇḍitaṃ.

SC 32Ms 22J_3428Msdiv 169Evampi daharūpeto,
paññāyogena sambhavo;
Mā naṃ daharoti uññāsi,
apucchitvāna sambhavaṃ;
Pucchitvā sambhavaṃ jaññā,
atthaṃ dhammañca brāhmaṇa”.

SC 33Ms 22J_3429Msdiv 170Svādhippāgā bhāradvājo,
sambhavassa upantikaṃ;
Tamaddasa mahābrahmā,
kīḷamānaṃ bahīpure.

SC 34Ms 22J_3430Msdiv 171“Raññohaṃ pahito dūto,
korabyassa yasassino;
‘Atthaṃ dhammañca pucchesi’,
iccabravi yudhiṭṭhilo;
Taṃ tvaṃ atthañca dhammañca,
sambhavakkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 35Ms 22J_3431Msdiv 172“Taggha te ahamakkhissaṃ,
yathāpi kusalo tathā;
Rājā ca kho taṃ jānāti,
yadi kāhati vā na vā.

SC 36Ms 22J_3432Msdiv 173‘Ajja suve’ti saṃseyya,
raññā puṭṭho sucīrata;
Mā katvā avasī rājā,
atthe jāte yudhiṭṭhilo.

SC 37Ms 22J_3433Msdiv 174Ajjhattaññeva saṃseyya,
raññā puṭṭho sucīrata;
Kummaggaṃ na niveseyya,
yathā mūḷho acetaso.

SC 38Ms 22J_3434Msdiv 175Attānaṃ nātivatteyya,
adhammaṃ na samācare;
Atitthe nappatāreyya,
anatthe na yuto siyā.

SC 39Ms 22J_3435Msdiv 176Yo ca etāni ṭhānāni,
kattuṃ jānāti khattiyo;
Sadā so vaḍḍhate rājā,
sukkapakkheva candimā.

SC 40Ms 22J_3436Msdiv 177Ñātīnañca piyo hoti,
mittesu ca virocati;
Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,
saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti.

SC 41Ms 22J_3437Sambhavajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
