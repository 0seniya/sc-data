387. Sūcijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.282Ms 22J_1413Msdiv 83“Akakkasaṃ apharusaṃ,
kharadhotaṃ supāsiyaṃ;
Sukhumaṃ tikhiṇaggañca,
ko sūciṃ ketumicchati.

SC 2Ms 22J_1414Msdiv 84Sumajjañca supāsañca,
anupubbaṃ suvaṭṭitaṃ;
Ghanaghātimaṃ paṭithaddhaṃ,
ko sūciṃ ketumicchati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1415Msdiv 85“Ito dāni patāyanti,
sūciyo baḷisāni ca;
Koyaṃ kammāragāmasmiṃ,
sūciṃ vikketumicchati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1416Msdiv 86Ito satthāni gacchanti,
Kammantā vividhā puthū;
Koyaṃ kammāragāmasmiṃ,
Sūciṃ Sūciṃ vikketumicchati”.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 3.284Ms 22J_1417Msdiv 87“Sūciṃ kammāragāmasmiṃ,
vikketabbā pajānatā;
Ācariyāva jānanti,
kammaṃ sukatadukkaṭaṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_1418Msdiv 88Imañce te pitā bhadde,
Sūciṃ jaññā mayā kataṃ;
Tayā ca maṃ nimanteyya,
Yañcatthaññaṃ ghare dhanan”ti.

SC 7Ms 22J_1419Sūcijātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
