473. Mittāmittajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2368Msdiv 121“Kāni kammāni kubbānaṃ,
kathaṃ viññū parakkame;
Amittaṃ jāneyya medhāvī,
disvā sutvā ca paṇḍito”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2369Msdiv 122“Na naṃ umhayate disvā,
na ca naṃ paṭinandati;
Cakkhūni cassa na dadāti,
paṭilomañca vattati.

SC 3Ms 22J_2370Msdiv 123Amitte tassa bhajati,
mitte tassa na sevati;
Vaṇṇakāme nivāreti,
akkosante pasaṃsati.

SC 4Ms 22J_2371Msdiv 124Guyhañca tassa nakkhāti,
tassa guyhaṃ na gūhati;
Kammaṃ tassa na vaṇṇeti,
paññassa nappasaṃsati.

SC 5Ms 22J_2372Msdiv 125Abhave nandati tassa,
bhave tassa na nandati;
Accheraṃ bhojanaṃ laddhā,
tassa nuppajjate sati;
Tato naṃ nānukampati,
aho sopi labheyyito.

SC 6Ms 22J_2373Msdiv 126Iccete soḷasākārā,
amittasmiṃ patiṭṭhitā;
Yehi amittaṃ jāneyya,
disvā sutvā ca paṇḍito”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2374Msdiv 127“Kāni kammāni kubbānaṃ,
kathaṃ viññū parakkame;
Mittaṃ jāneyya medhāvī,
disvā sutvā ca paṇḍito.

SC 8Ms 22J_2375Msdiv 128Pavutthaṃ tassa sarati,
āgataṃ abhinandati;
Tato kelāyito hoti,
vācāya paṭinandati.

SC 9Ms 22J_2376Msdiv 129Mitte tasseva bhajati,
amitte tassa na sevati;
Akkosante nivāreti,
vaṇṇakāme pasaṃsati.

SC 10Ms 22J_2377Msdiv 130Guyhañca tassa akkhāti,
tassa guyhañca gūhati;
Kammañca tassa vaṇṇeti,
paññaṃ tassa pasaṃsati.

SC 11Ms 22J_2378Msdiv 131Bhave ca nandati tassa,
abhave tassa na nandati;
Accheraṃ bhojanaṃ laddhā,
tassa uppajjate sati;
Tato naṃ anukampati,
aho sopi labheyyito.

SC 12Ms 22J_2379Msdiv 132Iccete soḷasākārā,
Mittasmiṃ suppatiṭṭhitā;
Yehi mittañca jāneyya,
Disvā sutvā ca paṇḍito”ti. (1779)

SC 13Ms 22J_2380Mittāmittajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.

SC 14Ms 22J_2381Dvādasakanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 15Ms 22J_2382Tassuddānaṃ

SC 16Ms 22J_2383Lahucitta sasāla kasanti puna,
Atha kāma dasakhaluṭṭhānavaro;
Atha kaṇha sukosiya meṇḍavaro,
Padumo puna mittavarena dasāti.
