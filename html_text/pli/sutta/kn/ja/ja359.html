359. Suvaṇṇamigajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.184Ms 22J_1203Msdiv 50“Vikkama re haripāda,
vikkama re mahāmiga;
Chinda vārattikaṃ pāsaṃ,
nāhaṃ ekā vane rame”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1204Msdiv 51“Vikkamāmi na pāremi,
bhūmiṃ sumbhāmi vegasā;
Daḷho vārattiko pāso,
pādaṃ me parikantati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1205Msdiv 52“Attharassu palāsāni,
asiṃ nibbāha luddaka;
Paṭhamaṃ maṃ vadhitvāna,
hana pacchā mahāmigaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1206Msdiv 53“Na me sutaṃ vā diṭṭhaṃ vā,
Bhāsantiṃ mānusiṃ migiṃ;
Tvañca bhadde sukhī hohi,
Eso cāpi mahāmigo”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1207Msdiv 54“Evaṃ luddaka nandassu,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Yathāhamajja nandāmi,
muttaṃ disvā mahāmigan”ti.

SC 6Ms 22J_1208Suvaṇṇamigajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
