5 Cūḷasutasomajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 5.178Ms 23J_200Msdiv 195“Āmantayāmi nigamaṃ,
mittāmacce parissaje;
Sirasmiṃ palitaṃ jātaṃ,
pabbajjaṃ dāni rocahaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 23J_201Msdiv 196“Abhumme kathaṃ nu bhaṇasi,
Sallaṃ me deva urasi kappesi;
Sattasatā te bhariyā,
Kathaṃ nu te tā bhavissanti”.

SC 3Ms 23J_202Msdiv 197“Paññāyihinti etā,
Daharā aññampi tā gamissanti;
Saggañcassa patthayāno,
Tena ahaṃ pabbajissāmi”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 5.179Ms 23J_203Msdiv 198“Dulladdhaṃ me āsi sutasoma,
Yassa te homahaṃ mātā;
Yaṃ me vilapantiyā,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva.

SC 5Ms 23J_204Msdiv 199Dulladdhaṃ me āsi sutasoma,
Yaṃ taṃ ahaṃ vijāyissaṃ;
Yaṃ me vilapantiyā,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva”.

SC 6Ms 23J_205Msdiv 200“Ko nāmeso dhammo,
Sutasoma kā ca nāma pabbajjā;
Yaṃ no amhe jiṇṇe,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva.

SC 7Ms 23J_206Msdiv 201Puttāpi tuyhaṃ bahavo,
daharā appattayobbanā;
Mañjū tepi taṃ apassantā,
maññe dukkhaṃ nigacchanti”.

SC 8Ms 23J_207Msdiv 202“Puttehi ca me etehi,
Daharehi appattayobbanehi;
Mañjūhi sabbehipi tumhehi,
Cirampi ṭhatvā vināsabhāvo”.

SC 9Ms 23J_208Msdiv 203“Chinnaṃ nu tuyhaṃ hadayaṃ,
Adu te karuṇā ca natthi amhesu;
Yaṃ no vikandantiyo,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva”.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 5.181Ms 23J_209Msdiv 204“Na ca mayhaṃ chinnaṃ hadayaṃ,
Atthi karuṇāpi mayhaṃ tumhesu;
Saggañca patthayāno,
Tena ahaṃ pabbajissāmi”.

SC 11Ms 23J_210Msdiv 205“Dulladdhaṃ me āsi,
Sutasoma yassa te ahaṃ bhariyā;
Yaṃ me vilapantiyā,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva.

SC 12Ms 23J_211Msdiv 206Dulladdhaṃ me āsi,
Sutasoma yassa te ahaṃ bhariyā;
Yaṃ me kucchipaṭisandhiṃ,
Anapekkho pabbajasi deva.

SC 13Ms 23J_212Msdiv 207Paripakko me gabbho,
Kucchigato yāva naṃ vijāyāmi;
Māhaṃ ekā vidhavā,
Pacchā dukkhāni addakkhiṃ”.

SC 14PTS vp Pali 5.182Ms 23J_213Msdiv 208“Paripakko te gabbho,
Kucchigato iṅgha tvaṃ vijāyassu;
Puttaṃ anomavaṇṇaṃ,
Taṃ hitvā pabbajissāmi”.

SC 15Ms 23J_214Msdiv 209“Mā tvaṃ cande rudi,
Mā soci vanatimiramattakkhi;
Āroha varapāsādaṃ,
Anapekkho ahaṃ gamissāmi”.

SC 16Ms 23J_215Msdiv 210“Ko taṃ amma kopesi,
Kiṃ rodasi pekkhasi ca maṃ bāḷhaṃ;
Kaṃ avajjhaṃ ghātemi,
Ñātīnaṃ udikkhamānānaṃ”.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 5.183Ms 23J_216Msdiv 211“Na hi so sakkā hantuṃ,
Vijitāvī yo maṃ tāta kopesi;
Pitā te maṃ tāta avaca,
Anapekkho ahaṃ gamissāmi”.

SC 18Ms 23J_217Msdiv 212“Yohaṃ pubbe niyyāmi,

Sutasome pabbajite,
Kathaṃ nu dāni karissāmi”.

SC 19Ms 23J_218Msdiv 213“Mātucca me Mātucca me rudantyā,

Hatthepi te gahessaṃ,
Na hi gacchasi no akāmānaṃ”.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 5.184Ms 23J_219Msdiv 214“Uṭṭhehi tvaṃ dhāti,
Imaṃ kumāraṃ ramehi aññattha;
Mā me paripanthamakāsi,
Saggaṃ mama patthayānassa”.

SC 21Ms 23J_220Msdiv 215“Yaṃnūnimaṃ dadeyyaṃ pabhaṅkaraṃ,
Ko nu me imināttho;
Sutasome pabbajite,
Kiṃ nu menaṃ karissāmi”.

SC 22Ms 23J_221Msdiv 216“Koso ca tuyhaṃ vipulo,

Ramassu mā pabbaji deva”.

SC 23Ms 23J_222Msdiv 217“Koso ca mayhaṃ vipulo,

SC 24PTS vp Pali 5.185Ms 23J_223Msdiv 218“Mayhampi dhanaṃ pahūtaṃ,

Taṃ te dadāmi sabbampi,
Ramassu mā pabbaji deva”.

SC 25Ms 23J_224Msdiv 219“Jānāmi dhanaṃ pahūtaṃ,

Saggañca patthayāno,
Tena ahaṃ pabbajissāmi.

SC 26Ms 23J_225Msdiv 220Ukkaṇṭhitosmi bāḷhaṃ,

Bahukāpi me antarāyā,
Ajjevāhaṃ pabbajissāmi”.

SC 27Ms 23J_226Msdiv 221“Idañca tuyhaṃ rucitaṃ,
Sutasoma ajjeva dāni tvaṃ pabbaja;
Ahampi pabbajissāmi,
Na ussahe tayā vinā ahaṃ ṭhātuṃ”.

SC 28PTS vp Pali 5.186Ms 23J_227Msdiv 222“Na hi sakkā pabbajituṃ,

Sutasome pabbajite,
Kathaṃ nu dāni karissāma.
