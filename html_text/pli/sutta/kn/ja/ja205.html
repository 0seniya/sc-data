205. Gaṅgeyyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_518Msdiv 109“Sobhati maccho gaṅgeyyo,
atho sobhati yāmuno;
Catuppadoyaṃ puriso,
nigrodhaparimaṇḍalo;
Īsakāyatagīvo ca,
sabbeva atirocati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_519Msdiv 110“Yaṃ pucchito na taṃ akkhāsi,
Aññaṃ akkhāsi pucchito;
Attappasaṃsako poso,
Nāyaṃ asmāka ruccatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_520Gaṅgeyyajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
