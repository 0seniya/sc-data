443. Cūḷabodhijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1961Msdiv 49“Yo te imaṃ visālakkhiṃ,
piyaṃ sammhitabhāsiniṃ;
Ādāya balā gaccheyya,
kiṃ nu kayirāsi brāhmaṇa”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1962Msdiv 50“Uppajje me na mucceyya,
na me mucceyya jīvato;
Rajaṃva vipulā vuṭṭhi,
khippameva nivāraye”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1963Msdiv 51“Yannu pubbe vikatthittho,
balamhiva apassito;
Svajja Svajja tuṇhikato dāni,
saṅghāṭiṃ sibbamacchasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1964Msdiv 52“Uppajji me na muccittha,
na me muccittha jīvato;
Rajaṃva vipulā vuṭṭhi,
khippameva nivārayiṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1965Msdiv 53“Kiṃ te uppajji no mucci,
kiṃ te na mucci jīvato;
Rajaṃva vipulā vuṭṭhi,
katamaṃ tvaṃ nivārayi”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1966Msdiv 54“Yamhi jāte na passati,
ajāte sādhu passati;
So me uppajji no mucci,
kodho dummedhagocaro.

SC 7Ms 22J_1967Msdiv 55Yena jātena nandanti,
amittā dukkhamesino;
So me uppajji no mucci,
kodho dummedhagocaro.

SC 8Ms 22J_1968Msdiv 56Yasmiñca jāyamānamhi,
sadatthaṃ nāvabujjhati;
So me uppajji no mucci,
kodho dummedhagocaro.

SC 9Ms 22J_1969Msdiv 57Yenābhibhūto kusalaṃ jahāti,
Parakkare vipulañcāpi atthaṃ;
Sa bhīmaseno balavā pamaddī,
Kodho mahārāja na me amuccatha.

SC 10Ms 22J_1970Msdiv 58Kaṭṭhasmiṃ matthamānasmiṃ,
Pāvako nāma jāyati;
Tameva kaṭṭhaṃ ḍahati,
Yasmā so jāyate gini.

SC 11Ms 22J_1971Msdiv 59Evaṃ mandassa posassa,
bālassa avijānato;
Sārambhā jāyate kodho,
sopi teneva ḍayhati.

SC 12Ms 22J_1972Msdiv 60Aggīva tiṇakaṭṭhasmiṃ,
kodho yassa pavaḍḍhati;
Nihīyati tassa yaso,
kāḷapakkheva candimā.

SC 13Ms 22J_1973Msdiv 61Anedho dhūmaketūva,
kodho yassūpasammati;
Āpūrati tassa yaso,
sukkapakkheva candimā”ti.

SC 14Ms 22J_1974Cūḷabodhijātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
