233. Vikaṇṇajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.228Ms 22J_608Msdiv 165“Kāmaṃ yahiṃ icchasi tena gaccha,
Viddhosi mammamhi vikaṇṇakena;
Hatosi bhattena suvāditena,
Lolo ca macche anubandhamāno”.

SC 2Ms 22J_609Msdiv 166“Evampi lokāmisaṃ opatanto,
Vihaññatī cittavasānuvattī;
So haññatī ñātisakhāna majjhe,
Macchānugo soriva suṃsumāro”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_610Vikaṇṇajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
