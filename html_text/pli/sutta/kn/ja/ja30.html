30. Munikajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_64Msdiv 30“Mā munikassa pihayi,
āturannāni bhuñjati;
Appossukko bhusaṃ khāda,
etaṃ dīghāyulakkhaṇan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_65Munikajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kuruṅgavaggo tatiyo.

SC 3Ms 22J_66Tassuddānaṃ

SC 4Ms 22J_67Kuruṅgassa kukkuragojavaro,
Puna vāḷavassasirivhayano;
Mahiḷāmukhanāmanuññavaro,
Vahate dhura munikena dasāti.
