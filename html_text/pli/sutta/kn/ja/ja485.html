485. Candakinnarījātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2566Msdiv 18“Upanīyatidaṃ maññe,
cande lohitamaddane;
Ajja jahāmi jīvitaṃ,
pāṇā me cande nirujjhanti.

SC 2Ms 22J_2567Msdiv 19Osīdi me dukkhaṃ hadayaṃ,
Me ḍayhate nitammāmi;
Tava candiyā socantiyā,
Na naṃ aññehi sokehi.

SC 3Ms 22J_2568Msdiv 20Tiṇamiva vanamiva milāyāmi,
Nadī aparipuṇṇāva sussāmi;
Tava candiyā socantiyā,
Na naṃ aññehi sokehi.

SC 4Ms 22J_2569Msdiv 21Vassamiva sare pāde,
Imāni assūni vattare mayhaṃ;
Tava candiyā socantiyā,
Na naṃ aññehi sokehi”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2570Msdiv 22“Pāpo khosi rājaputta,
Yo me icchitaṃ patiṃ varākiyā;
Vijjhasi vanamūlasmiṃ,
Soyaṃ viddho chamā seti.

SC 6Ms 22J_2571Msdiv 23Imaṃ mayhaṃ hadayasokaṃ,
Paṭimuñcatu rājaputta tava mātā;
Yo mayhaṃ hadayasoko,
Kimpurisaṃ avekkhamānāya.

SC 7Ms 22J_2572Msdiv 24Imaṃ mayhaṃ hadayasokaṃ,
Paṭimuñcatu rājaputta tava jāyā;
Yo mayhaṃ hadayasoko,
Kimpurisaṃ avekkhamānāya.

SC 8Ms 22J_2573Msdiv 25Mā ca puttaṃ mā ca patiṃ,
Addakkhi rājaputta tava mātā;
Yo kimpurisaṃ avadhi,
Adūsakaṃ mayha kāmā hi.

SC 9Ms 22J_2574Msdiv 26Mā ca puttaṃ mā ca patiṃ,
Addakkhi rājaputta tava jāyā;
Yo kimpurisaṃ avadhi,
Adūsakaṃ mayha kāmā hi”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2575Msdiv 27“Mā tvaṃ cande rodi,
Mā soci vanatimiramattakkhi;
Mama tvaṃ hehisi bhariyā,
Rājakule pūjitā nārībhi”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2576Msdiv 28“Api nūnahaṃ marissaṃ,
Nāhaṃ rājaputta tava hessaṃ;
Yo kimpurisaṃ avadhi,
Adūsakaṃ mayha kāmā hi”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2577Msdiv 29“Api bhīruke api jīvitukāmike,
Kimpurisi gaccha himavantaṃ;
Tālīsatagarabhojanā,
Aññe taṃ migā ramissanti”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2578Msdiv 30“Te pabbatā tā ca kandarā,
Tā ca giriguhāyo tatheva tiṭṭhanti;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 14Ms 22J_2579Msdiv 31Te paṇṇasanthatā ramaṇīyā,
Vāḷamigehi anuciṇṇā;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 15Ms 22J_2580Msdiv 32Te pupphasanthatā ramaṇīyā,
Vāḷamigehi anuciṇṇā;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 16Ms 22J_2581Msdiv 33Acchā savanti girivananadiyo,
Kusumābhikiṇṇasotāyo;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 17Ms 22J_2582Msdiv 34Nīlāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 18Ms 22J_2583Msdiv 35Pītāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 19Ms 22J_2584Msdiv 36Tambāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 20Ms 22J_2585Msdiv 37Tuṅgāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 21Ms 22J_2586Msdiv 38Setāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 22Ms 22J_2587Msdiv 39Citrāni himavato pabbatassa,
Kūṭāni dassanīyāni;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 23Ms 22J_2588Msdiv 40Yakkhagaṇasevite gandhamādane,
Osadhebhi sañchanne;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ.

SC 24Ms 22J_2589Msdiv 41Kimpurisasevite gandhamādane,
Osadhebhi sañchanne;
Tattheva taṃ apassantī,
Kimpurisa kathaṃ ahaṃ kassaṃ”.

SC 25Ms 22J_2590Msdiv 42“Vande te ayirabrahme,
Yo me icchitaṃ patiṃ varākiyā;
Amatena abhisiñci,
Samāgatāsmi piyatamena.

SC 26Ms 22J_2591Msdiv 43Vicarāma dāni girivana nadiyo,
Kusumābhikiṇṇasotāyo;
Nānādumavasanāyo,
Piyaṃvadā aññamaññassā”ti.

SC 27Ms 22J_2592Candakinnarījātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
