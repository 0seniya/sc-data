309. Chavakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_923Msdiv 33“Sabbamidaṃ carimaṃ kataṃ,
Ubho dhammaṃ na passare;
Ubho pakatiyā cutā,
Yo cāyaṃ mantejjhāpeti;
Yo ca mantaṃ adhīyati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_924Msdiv 34“Sālīnaṃ odanaṃ bhuñje,
suciṃ maṃsūpasecanaṃ;
Tasmā etaṃ na sevāmi,
dhammaṃ isīhi sevitaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_925Msdiv 35“Paribbaja mahā loko,
pacantaññepi pāṇino;
Mā taṃ adhammo ācarito,
asmā kumbhamivābhidā.

SC 4Ms 22J_926Msdiv 36Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ,
dhanalābhañca brāhmaṇa;
Yā vutti vinipātena,
adhammacaraṇena vā”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_927Chavakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
