428. Kosambiyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1791Msdiv 10“Puthusaddo samajano,
na bālo koci maññatha;
Saṃghasmiṃ bhijjamānasmiṃ,
nāññaṃ bhiyyo amaññaruṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_1792Msdiv 11Parimuṭṭhā paṇḍitābhāsā,
vācāgocarabhāṇino;
Yāvicchanti mukhāyāmaṃ,
yena nītā na taṃ vidū.

SC 3Ms 22J_1793Msdiv 12Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ upanayhanti,
veraṃ tesaṃ na sammati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1794Msdiv 13Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ nupanayhanti,
veraṃ tesūpasammati.

SC 5Ms 22J_1795Msdiv 14Na hi verena verāni,
sammantīdha kudācanaṃ;
Averena ca sammanti,
esa dhammo sanantano.

SC 6Ms 22J_1796Msdiv 15Pare ca na vijānanti,
mayamettha yamāmase;
Ye ca tattha vijānanti,
tato sammanti medhagā.

SC 7Ms 22J_1797Msdiv 16Aṭṭhicchinnā pāṇaharā,
gavāssa dhanahārino;
Raṭṭhaṃ vilumpamānānaṃ,
tesampi hoti saṅgati;
Kasmā tumhāka no siyā.

SC 8Ms 22J_1798Msdiv 17Sace labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāridhīraṃ;
Abhibhuyya sabbāni parissayāni,
Careyya tenattamano satīmā.

SC 9Ms 22J_1799Msdiv 18No ce labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāridhīraṃ;
Rājāva raṭṭhaṃ vijitaṃ pahāya,
Eko care mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo.

SC 10Ms 22J_1800Msdiv 19Ekassa caritaṃ seyyo,
Natthi bāle sahāyatā;
Eko care na pāpāni kayirā,
Appossukko mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo”ti.

SC 11Ms 22J_1801Kosambiyajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
