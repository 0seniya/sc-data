266. Vātaggasindhavajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.340Ms 22J_732Msdiv 46“Yenāsi kisiyā paṇḍu,
yena bhattaṃ na ruccati;
Ayaṃ so āgato bhattā,
kasmā dāni palāyasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_733Msdiv 47“Sace panādikeneva,
santhavo nāma jāyati;
Yaso hāyati itthīnaṃ,
tasmā tāta palāyahaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_734Msdiv 48Yasassinaṃ kule jātaṃ,
āgataṃ yā na icchati;
Socati cirarattāya,
vātaggamiva bhaddalīti.

SC 4Ms 22J_735Vātaggasindhavajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
