310. Seyyajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_928SC 1Ms 22J_928Msdiv 37“Sasamuddapariyāyaṃ,
mahiṃ sāgarakuṇḍalaṃ;
Na icche saha nindāya,
evaṃ seyya vijānahi.

SC 2Ms 22J_929SC 2Ms 22J_929Msdiv 38Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ,
dhanalābhañca brāhmaṇa;
Yā vutti vinipātena,
adhammacaraṇena vā.

SC 3Ms 22J_930SC 3Ms 22J_930Msdiv 39Api ce pattamādāya,
anagāro paribbaje;
Sāyeva jīvikā seyyo,
yā cādhammena esanā.

SC 4Ms 22J_931SC 4Ms 22J_931Msdiv 40Api ce pattamādāya,
anagāro paribbaje;
Aññaṃ ahiṃsayaṃ loke,
api rajjena taṃ varan”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_932SC 5Ms 22J_932Seyyajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kāliṅgavaggo paṭhamo.

SC 6SC 6Ms 22J_933Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7SC 7Ms 22J_934Vivarañca adeyya samiddhavaraṃ,
Atha daddara pāpamahātiraho;
Atha koli palāsavarañca kara,
Carimaṃ sasamuddavarena dasāti.
