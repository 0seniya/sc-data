288. Macchuddānajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_824SC 1Ms 22J_824Msdiv 112“Agghanti macchā adhikaṃ sahassaṃ,
Na so atthi yo imaṃ saddaheyya;
Mayhañca assu idha satta māsā,
Ahampi taṃ macchuddānaṃ kiṇeyyaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_825SC 2Ms 22J_825Msdiv 113“Macchānaṃ bhojanaṃ datvā,
mama dakkhiṇamādisi;
Taṃ dakkhiṇaṃ sarantiyā,
kataṃ apacitiṃ tayā”.

SC 3Ms 22J_826SC 3Ms 22J_826Msdiv 114“Paduṭṭhacittassa na phāti hoti,
Na cāpi taṃ devatā pūjayanti;
Yo bhātaraṃ pettikaṃ sāpateyyaṃ,
Avañcayī dukkaṭakammakārī”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_827SC 4Ms 22J_827Macchuddānajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
