60. Saṅkhadhamajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_131Msdiv 60“Dhame dhame nātidhame,
atidhantañhi pāpakaṃ;
Dhantenādhigatā bhogā,
te tāto vidhamī dhaman”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_132Saṅkhadhamajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Āsīsavaggo chaṭṭho.

SC 3Ms 22J_133Tassuddānaṃ

SC 4Ms 22J_134Yathā icchiṃ tathāhudakā thalā,
Sura sāduphalo ca alīnamano;
Sampahaṭṭhamano caturo ca tayo,
Sataladdhaka bhogadhanena dasāti.
