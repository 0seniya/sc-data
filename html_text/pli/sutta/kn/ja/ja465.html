465. Bhaddasālajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2252Msdiv 13“Kā tvaṃ suddhehi vatthehi,
aghe vehāyasaṃ ṭhitā;
Kena tyāssūni vattanti,
kuto taṃ bhayamāgataṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2253Msdiv 14“Taveva deva vijite,
bhaddasāloti maṃ vidū;
Saṭṭhi vassasahassāni,
tiṭṭhato pūjitassa me.

SC 3Ms 22J_2254Msdiv 15Kārayantā nagarāni,
agāre ca disampati;
Vividhe cāpi pāsāde,
na maṃ te accamaññisuṃ;
Yatheva maṃ te pūjesuṃ,
tatheva tvampi pūjaya”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2255Msdiv 16“Taṃ ivāhaṃ na passāmi,
thūlaṃ kāyena te dumaṃ;
Ārohapariṇāhena,
abhirūposi jātiyā.

SC 5Ms 22J_2256Msdiv 17Pāsādaṃ kārayissāmi,
ekatthambhaṃ manoramaṃ;
Tattha taṃ upanessāmi,
ciraṃ te yakkha jīvitaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2257Msdiv 18“Evaṃ cittaṃ udapādi,
sarīrena vinābhāvo;
Puthuso maṃ vikantitvā,
khaṇḍaso avakantatha.

SC 7Ms 22J_2258Msdiv 19Agge ca chetvā majjhe ca,
pacchā mūlamhi chindatha;
Evaṃ me chijjamānassa,
na dukkhaṃ maraṇaṃ siyā”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2259Msdiv 20“Hatthapādaṃ yathā Hatthapādaṃ yathā chinde,
Kaṇṇanāsañca jīvato;
Tato pacchā siro chinde,
Taṃ dukkhaṃ maraṇaṃ siyā.

SC 9Ms 22J_2260Msdiv 21Sukhaṃ nu khaṇḍaso chinnaṃ,
bhaddasāla vanappati;
Kiṃhetu kiṃ upādāya,
khaṇḍaso chinnamicchasi”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2261Msdiv 22“Yañca hetumupādāya,
hetuṃ dhammūpasaṃhitaṃ;
Khaṇḍaso chinnamicchāmi,
mahārāja suṇohi me.

SC 11Ms 22J_2262Msdiv 23Ñātī me sukhasaṃvaddhā,
mama passe nivātajā;
Tepihaṃ upahiṃseyyaṃ,
paresaṃ asukhocitaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2263Msdiv 24“Ceteyyarūpaṃ cetesi,
bhaddasāla vanappati;
Hitakāmosi ñātīnaṃ,
abhayaṃ samma dammi te”ti.

SC 13Ms 22J_2264Bhaddasālajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
