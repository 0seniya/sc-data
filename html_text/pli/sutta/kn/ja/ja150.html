150. Sañjīvajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_331Msdiv 150“Asantaṃ yo paggaṇhāti,
asantaṃ cūpasevati;
Tameva ghāsaṃ kurute,
byaggho sañjīvako yathā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_332Sañjīvajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kakaṇṭakavaggo pannarasamo.

SC 3Ms 22J_333Tassuddānaṃ

SC 4Ms 22J_334Sukhamedhati daṇḍavaro ca puna,
Lasi vāladhi pañcamarādhavaro;
Samahodadhi kattika bondi puna,
Caturaṅgulabyagghavarena dasāti.

SC 5Ms 22J_335Uparimapaṇṇāsako.

SC 6Ms 22J_336Atha vagguddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 22J_337Apaṇṇakaṃ sīlavaggakuruṅga,
Kulāvakaṃ atthakāmena pañcamaṃ;
Āsīso itthivaruṇaṃ apāyi,
Littavaggena te dasa;
Parosataṃ haṃci kusanāḷi,
Asampadaṃ kakaṇṭakavaggo.

SC 8Ms 22J_338Ekanipātamhilaṅkatanti.

SC 9Ms 22J_339Ekakanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
