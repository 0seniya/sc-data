509. Hatthipālajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.476Ms 22J_3201Msdiv 337“Cirassaṃ vata passāma,
brāhmaṇaṃ devavaṇṇinaṃ;
Mahājaṭaṃ khāridharaṃ,
paṅkadantaṃ rajassiraṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_3202Msdiv 338Cirassaṃ vata passāma,
isiṃ dhammaguṇe rataṃ;
Kāsāyavatthavasanaṃ,
vākacīraṃ paṭicchadaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_3203Msdiv 339Āsanaṃ udakaṃ pajjaṃ,
paṭigaṇhātu no bhavaṃ;
Agghe bhavantaṃ pucchāma,
agghaṃ kurutu no bhavaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_3204Msdiv 340“Adhicca vede pariyesa vittaṃ,
Putte gahe tāta patiṭṭhapetvā;
Gandhe rase paccanubhuyya sabbaṃ,
Araññaṃ sādhu muni so pasattho”.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 4.478Ms 22J_3205Msdiv 341“Vedā na saccā na ca vittalābho,
Na puttalābhena jaraṃ vihanti;
Gandhe rase muccanamāhu santo,
Sakammunā hoti phalūpapatti”.

SC 6Ms 22J_3206Msdiv 342“Addhā hi saccaṃ vacanaṃ tavetaṃ,
Sakammunā hoti phalūpapatti;
Jiṇṇā ca mātāpitaro tavīme,
Passeyyuṃ taṃ vassasataṃ arogaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_3207Msdiv 343“Yassassa sakkhī maraṇena rāja,
Jarāya mettī naravīraseṭṭha;
Yo cāpi jaññā na marissaṃ kadāci,
Passeyyuṃ taṃ vassasataṃ arogaṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_3208Msdiv 344Yathāpi nāvaṃ puriso dakamhi,
Ereti ce naṃ upaneti tīraṃ;
Evampi byādhī satataṃ jarā ca,
Upaneti maccaṃ vasamantakassa”.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 4.480Ms 22J_3209Msdiv 345“Paṅko ca kāmā palipo ca kāmā,
Manoharā duttarā maccudheyyā;
Etasmiṃ paṅke palipe byasannā,
Hīnattarūpā na taranti pāraṃ.

SC 10Ms 22J_3210Msdiv 346Ayaṃ pure luddamakāsi kammaṃ,
Svāyaṃ gahīto na hi mokkhito me;
Orundhiyā naṃ parirakkhissāmi,
Māyaṃ puna luddamakāsi kammaṃ”.

SC 11PTS vp Pali 4.481Ms 22J_3211Msdiv 347“Gavaṃva naṭṭhaṃ puriso yathā vane,
Anvesatī rāja apassamāno;
Evaṃ naṭṭho esukārī mamattho,
Sohaṃ kathaṃ na gaveseyyaṃ rāja.

SC 12Ms 22J_3212Msdiv 348Hiyyoti hiyyati poso,
Pareti parihāyati;
Anāgataṃ netamatthīti ñatvā,
Uppannachandaṃ ko panudeyya dhīro”.

SC 13Ms 22J_3213Msdiv 349“Passāmi vohaṃ daharaṃ kumāriṃ,
Mattūpamaṃ ketakapupphanettaṃ;
Abhuttabhoge paṭhame vayasmiṃ,
Ādāya maccu vajate kumāriṃ.

SC 14Ms 22J_3214Msdiv 350Yuvā sujāto sumukho sudassano,
Sāmo kusumbhaparikiṇṇamassu;
Hitvāna kāme paṭikacca gehaṃ,
Anujāna maṃ pabbajissāmi deva”.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 4.483Ms 22J_3215Msdiv 351“Sākhāhi rukkho labhate samaññaṃ,
Pahīnasākhaṃ pana khāṇumāhu;
Pahīnaputtassa mamajja bhoti,
Vāseṭṭhi bhikkhācariyāya kālo”.

SC 16PTS vp Pali 4.484Ms 22J_3216Msdiv 352“Aghasmi koñcāva yathā himaccaye,
Katāni jālāni Katāni jālāni padāliya haṃsā;
Gacchanti puttā ca patī ca mayhaṃ,
Sāhaṃ kathaṃ nānuvaje pajānaṃ”.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 4.485Ms 22J_3217Msdiv 353“Ete bhutvā vamitvā ca,
pakkamanti vihaṅgamā;
Ye ca bhutvāna vamiṃsu,
te me hatthattamāgatā.

SC 18PTS vp Pali 4.486Ms 22J_3218Msdiv 354Avamī brāhmaṇo kāme,
so tvaṃ paccāvamissasi;
Vantādo puriso rāja,
na so hoti pasaṃsiyo”.

SC 19Ms 22J_3219Msdiv 355“Paṅke ca posaṃ palipe byasannaṃ,
Balī yathā dubbalamuddhareyya;
Evampi maṃ tvaṃ udatāri bhoti,
Pañcāli gāthāhi subhāsitāhi”.

SC 20Ms 22J_3220Msdiv 356Idaṃ vatvā mahārājā,
esukārī disampati;
Raṭṭhaṃ hitvāna pabbaji,
nāgo chetvāva bandhanaṃ.

SC 21Ms 22J_3221Msdiv 357“Rājā ca pabbajjamarocayittha,
Raṭṭhaṃ pahāya naravīraseṭṭho;
Tuvampi no hohi yatheva rājā,
Amhehi guttā anusāsa rajjaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 22J_3222Msdiv 358“Rājā ca pabbajjamarocayittha,
Raṭṭhaṃ pahāya naravīraseṭṭho;
Ahampi ekā carissāmi loke,
Hitvāna kāmāni manoramāni.

SC 23Ms 22J_3223Msdiv 359Rājā ca pabbajjamarocayittha,
Raṭṭhaṃ pahāya naravīraseṭṭho;
Ahampi ekā carissāmi loke,
Hitvāna kāmāni yathodhikāni.

SC 24Ms 22J_3224Msdiv 360Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,
Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;
Ahampi ekā carissāmi loke,
Hitvāna kāmāni manoramāni.

SC 25Ms 22J_3225Msdiv 361Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,
Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;
Ahampi ekā carissāmi loke,
Hitvāna kāmāni yathodhikāni.

SC 26Ms 22J_3226Msdiv 362Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,
Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;
Ahampi ekā carissāmi loke,
Sītibhūtā sabbamaticca saṅgan”ti.

SC 27Ms 22J_3227Hatthipālajātakaṃ terasamaṃ.
