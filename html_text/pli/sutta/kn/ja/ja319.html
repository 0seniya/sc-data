319. Tittirajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_975Msdiv 73“Susukhaṃ vata jīvāmi,
labhāmi ceva bhuñjituṃ;
Paripanthe ca tiṭṭhāmi,
kā nu bhante gatī mama”.

SC 2Ms 22J_976Msdiv 74“Mano ce te nappaṇamati,
pakkhi pāpassa kammuno;
Abyāvaṭassa bhadrassa,
na pāpamupalimpati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_977Msdiv 75“Ñātako no nisinnoti,
bahu āgacchate jano;
Paṭicca kammaṃ phusati,
tasmiṃ me saṅkate mano”.

SC 4Ms 22J_978Msdiv 76“Na paṭicca kammaṃ phusati,
mano ce nappadussati;
Appossukkassa bhadrassa,
na pāpamupalimpatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_979Tittirajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
