207. Assakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.157Ms 22J_524Msdiv 113“Ayamassakarājena,
deso vicarito mayā;
Anukāmayakāmena,
piyena patinā saha.

SC 2Ms 22J_525Msdiv 114Navena sukhadukkhena,
porāṇaṃ apidhīyati;
Tasmā assakaraññāva,
kīṭo piyataro mamā”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_526Assakajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
