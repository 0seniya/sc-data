36. Sakuṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_78Msdiv 36“Yaṃ nissitā jagatiruhaṃ vihaṅgamā,
Svāyaṃ aggiṃ pamuñcati;
Disā bhajatha vakkaṅgā,
Jātaṃ saraṇato bhayan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_79Sakuṇajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
