278. Mahiṃsarājajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.386Ms 22J_782Msdiv 82“Kimatthamabhisandhāya,
lahucittassa dubbhino;
Sabbakāmadadasseva,
imaṃ dukkhaṃ titikkhasi.

SC 2Ms 22J_783Msdiv 83Siṅgena nihanāhetaṃ,
padasā ca adhiṭṭhaha;
Bhiyyo bālā pakujjheyyuṃ,
no cassa paṭisedhako”.

SC 3Ms 22J_784Msdiv 84“Mamevāyaṃ maññamāno,
aññepevaṃ karissati;
Te naṃ tattha vadhissanti,
sā me mutti bhavissatī”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_785Mahiṃsarājajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
