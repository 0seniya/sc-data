272. Byagghajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.357Ms 22J_758Msdiv 64“Yena mittena saṃsaggā,
yogakkhemo vihiyyati;
Pubbevajjhābhavaṃ tassa,
rukkhe akkhīva paṇḍito.

SC 2Ms 22J_759Msdiv 65Yena mittena saṃsaggā,
yogakkhemo pavaḍḍhati;
Kareyyattasamaṃ vuttiṃ,
sabbakiccesu paṇḍito”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.358Ms 22J_760Msdiv 66“Etha byagghā nivattavho,
Paccupetha mahāvanaṃ;
Mā vanaṃ chindi nibyagghaṃ,
Byagghā māhesu nibbanā”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_761Byagghajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
