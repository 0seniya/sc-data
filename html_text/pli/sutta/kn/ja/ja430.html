430. Cūḷasuvajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1813Msdiv 30“Santi rukkhā SC 1Ms 22J_1813Msdiv 30“Santi rukkhā haripattā,
dumā nekaphalā bahū;
Kasmā nu sukkhe koḷāpe,
suvassa nirato mano”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1814SC 2Ms 22J_1814Msdiv 31“Phalassa upabhuñjimhā,
nekavassagaṇe bahū;
Aphalampi viditvāna,
sāva metti yathā pure”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1815SC 3Ms 22J_1815Msdiv 32“Sukkhañca rukkhaṃ koḷāpaṃ,
opattamaphalaṃ dumaṃ;
Ohāya sakuṇā yanti,
kiṃ dosaṃ passase dija”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1816SC 4Ms 22J_1816Msdiv 33“Ye phalatthā sambhajanti,
aphaloti jahanti naṃ;
Attatthapaññā dummedhā,
te honti pakkhapātino”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1817SC 5Ms 22J_1817Msdiv 34“Sādhu sakkhi kataṃ hoti,
metti saṃsati santhavo;
Sacetaṃ dhammaṃ rocesi,
pāsaṃsosi vijānataṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_1818SC 6Ms 22J_1818Msdiv 35So te suva varaṃ dammi,
pattayāna vihaṅgama;
Varaṃ varassu vakkaṅga,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1819Msdiv 36“SC 7Ms 22J_1819Msdiv 36“Api nāma naṃ passeyyaṃ,
sapattaṃ saphalaṃ dumaṃ;
Daliddova nidhiṃ laddhā,
nandeyyāhaṃ punappunaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 22J_1820SC 8Ms 22J_1820Msdiv 37“Tato amatamādāya,
abhisiñci mahīruhaṃ;
Tassa sākhā virūhiṃsu,
sītacchāyā manoramā”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1821SC 9Ms 22J_1821Msdiv 38“Evaṃ sakka sukhī hohi,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Yathāhamajja sukhito,
disvāna saphalaṃ dumaṃ.

SC 10Ms 22J_1822SC 10Ms 22J_1822Msdiv 39Suvassa ca varaṃ datvā,
katvāna saphalaṃ dumaṃ;
Pakkāmi saha bhariyāya,
devānaṃ nandanaṃ vanan”ti.
