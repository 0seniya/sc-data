335. Jambukajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1059SC 1Ms 22J_1059Msdiv 137“Brahā pavaḍḍhakāyo so,
dīghadāṭho ca jambuka;
Na tvaṃ tattha kule jāto,
yattha gaṇhanti kuñjaraṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1060SC 2Ms 22J_1060Msdiv 138“Asīho sīhamānena,
yo attānaṃ vikubbati;
Kotthūva gajamāsajja,
seti bhūmyā anutthunaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1061SC 3Ms 22J_1061Msdiv 139Yasassino uttamapuggalassa,
Sañjātakhandhassa mahabbalassa;
Asamekkhiya thāmabalūpapattiṃ,
Sa seti nāgena hatoyaṃ jambuko.

SC 4Ms 22J_1062SC 4Ms 22J_1062Msdiv 140Yo cīdha kammaṃ kurute pamāya,
Thāmabbalaṃ attani saṃviditvā;
Jappena mantena subhāsitena,
Parikkhavā so vipulaṃ jinātī”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 22J_1063Jambukajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
