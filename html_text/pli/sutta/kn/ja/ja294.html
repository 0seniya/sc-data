294. Jambukhādakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.439Ms 22J_850Msdiv 130“Koyaṃ bindussaro vaggu,
saravantānamuttamo;
Accuto jambusākhāya,
moracchāpova kūjati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_851Msdiv 131“Kulaputtova jānāti,
kulaputtaṃ pasaṃsituṃ;
Byagghacchāpasarīvaṇṇa,
bhuñja samma dadāmi te”.

SC 3Ms 22J_852Msdiv 132“Cirassaṃ vata passāmi,
musāvādī samāgate;
Vantādaṃ kuṇapādañca,
aññamaññaṃ pasaṃsake”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_853Jambukhādakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
