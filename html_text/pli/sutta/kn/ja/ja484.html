484. Sālikedārajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.278Ms 22J_2548Msdiv 1“Sampannaṃ sālikedāraṃ,
suvā bhuñjanti kosiya;
Paṭivedemi te brahme,
na ne vāretumussahe.

SC 2Ms 22J_2549Msdiv 2Eko ca tattha sakuṇo,
yo nesaṃ sabbasundaro;
Bhutvā sāliṃ yathākāmaṃ,
tuṇḍenādāya gacchati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2550Msdiv 3“Oḍḍentu vāḷapāsāni,
yathā bajjhetha so dijo;
Jīvañca naṃ gahetvāna,
ānayehi mamantike”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 4.279Ms 22J_2551Msdiv 4“Ete bhutvā pivitvā ca,
pakkamanti vihaṅgamā;
Eko baddhosmi pāsena,
kiṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2552Msdiv 5“Udaraṃ nūna aññesaṃ,
suva accodaraṃ tava;
Bhutvā sāliṃ yathākāmaṃ,
tuṇḍenādāya gacchasi.

SC 6Ms 22J_2553Msdiv 6Koṭṭhaṃ nu tattha pūresi,
suva veraṃ nu te mayā;
Puṭṭho me samma akkhāhi,
kuhiṃ sāliṃ nidāhasi”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2554Msdiv 7“Na me veraṃ tayā saddhiṃ,
koṭṭho mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Iṇaṃ muñcāmiṇaṃ dammi,
sampatto koṭasimbaliṃ;
Nidhimpi tattha nidahāmi,
evaṃ jānāhi kosiya”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2555Msdiv 8“Kīdisaṃ te iṇadānaṃ,
iṇamokkho ca kīdiso;
Nidhinidhānamakkhāhi,
atha pāsā pamokkhasi”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2556Msdiv 9“Ajātapakkhā taruṇā,
puttakā mayha kosiya;
Te maṃ bhatā bharissanti,
tasmā tesaṃ iṇaṃ dade.

SC 10Ms 22J_2557Msdiv 10Mātā pitā ca me vuddhā,
jiṇṇakā gatayobbanā;
Tesaṃ tuṇḍena hātūna,
muñce pubbakataṃ iṇaṃ.

SC 11Ms 22J_2558Msdiv 11Aññepi tattha sakuṇā,
khīṇapakkhā sudubbalā;
Tesaṃ puññatthiko dammi,
taṃ nidhiṃ āhu paṇḍitā.

SC 12Ms 22J_2559Msdiv 12Īdisaṃ me iṇadānaṃ,
iṇamokkho ca īdiso;
Nidhinidhānamakkhāmi,
evaṃ jānāhi kosiya”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2560Msdiv 13“Bhaddako vatayaṃ pakkhī,
dijo paramadhammiko;
Ekaccesu manussesu,
ayaṃ dhammo na vijjati.

SC 14PTS vp Pali 4.281Ms 22J_2561Msdiv 14Bhuñja sāliṃ yathākāmaṃ,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Punāpi suva passemu,
piyaṃ me tava dassanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2562Msdiv 15“Bhuttañca pītañca tavassamamhi,
Ratī ca no kosiya te sakāse;
Nikkhittadaṇḍesu dadāhi dānaṃ,
Jiṇṇe ca mātāpitaro bharassu”.

SC 16Ms 22J_2563Msdiv 16“Lakkhī vata me udapādi ajja,
Yo addasāsiṃ pavaraṃ dijānaṃ;
Suvassa sutvāna subhāsitāni,
Kāhāmi puññāni anappakāni”.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 4.282Ms 22J_2564Msdiv 17So kosiyo attamano udaggo,
Annañca pānañcabhisaṅkharitvā;
Annena pānena pasannacitto,
Santappayi samaṇabrāhmaṇe cāti.

SC 18Ms 22J_2565Sālikedārajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
