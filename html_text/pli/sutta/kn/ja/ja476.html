476. Javanahaṃsajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2412Msdiv 27“Idheva haṃsa nipata,
piyaṃ me tava dassanaṃ;
Issarosi anuppatto,
yamidhatthi pavedaya”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2413Msdiv 28“Savanena ekassa piyā bhavanti,
Disvā panekassa viyeti chando;
Disvā ca sutvā ca piyā bhavanti,
Kaccinnu me pīyasi dassanena.

SC 3Ms 22J_2414Msdiv 29Savanena piyo mesi,
bhiyyo cāgamma dassanaṃ;
Evaṃ piyadassano me,
vasa haṃsa mamantike”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2415Msdiv 30“Vaseyyāma tavāgāre,
niccaṃ sakkatapūjitā;
Matto ca ekadā vajje,
‘haṃsarājaṃ pacantu me’”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2416Msdiv 31“Dhiratthu taṃ majjapānaṃ,
yaṃ me piyataraṃ tayā;
Na cāpi majjaṃ pissāmi,
yāva me vacchasī ghare”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2417Msdiv 32“Suvijānaṃ siṅgālānaṃ,
sakuṇānañca vassitaṃ;
Manussavassitaṃ rāja,
dubbijānataraṃ tato.

SC 7Ms 22J_2418Msdiv 33Api ce maññatī poso,
ñāti mitto sakhāti vā;
Yo pubbe sumano hutvā,
pacchā sampajjate diso.

SC 8Ms 22J_2419Msdiv 34Yasmiṃ mano nivisati,
avidūre sahāpi so;
Santikepi hi so dūre,
yasmiṃ nāvisate mano.

SC 9Ms 22J_2420Msdiv 35Antopi so hoti pasannacitto,
Pāraṃ samuddassa pasannacitto;
Antopi so hoti paduṭṭhacitto,
Pāraṃ samuddassa paduṭṭhacitto.

SC 10Ms 22J_2421Msdiv 36Saṃvasantā vivasanti,
ye disā te rathesabha;
Ārā santo saṃvasanti,
manasā raṭṭhavaḍḍhana.

SC 11Ms 22J_2422Msdiv 37Aticiraṃ nivāsena,
piyo bhavati appiyo;
Āmanta kho taṃ gacchāma,
purā te homa appiyā”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2423Msdiv 38“Evañce yācamānānaṃ,
Añjaliṃ nāvabujjhasi;
Paricārakānaṃ sataṃ,
Vacanaṃ na karosi no;
Evaṃ taṃ abhiyācāma,
Puna kayirāsi pariyāyaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2424Msdiv 39“Evañce no viharataṃ,
antarāyo na hessati;
Tuyhañcāpi mahārāja,
mayhañca raṭṭhavaḍḍhana;
Appeva nāma passemu,
ahorattānamaccaye”ti.

SC 14Ms 22J_2425Javanahaṃsajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
