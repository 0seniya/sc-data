401. Paṇṇakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.338Ms 22J_1528Msdiv 39“Paṇṇakaṃ tikhiṇadhāraṃ,
Asiṃ sampannapāyinaṃ;
Parisāyaṃ puriso gilati,
Kiṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Yadaññaṃ dukkaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
Taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 3.339Ms 22J_1529Msdiv 40“Gileyya puriso lobhā,
asiṃ sampannapāyinaṃ;
Yo ca vajjā dadāmīti,
taṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Sabbaññaṃ sukaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
evaṃ jānāhi maddava”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1530Msdiv 41“Byākāsi āyuro pañhaṃ,
atthaṃ dhammassa kovido;
Pukkusaṃ dāni pucchāmi,
kiṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Yadaññaṃ dukkaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1531Msdiv 42“Na vācamupajīvanti,
aphalaṃ giramudīritaṃ;
Yo ca datvā avākayirā,
taṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Sabbaññaṃ sukaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
evaṃ jānāhi maddava”.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 3.340Ms 22J_1532Msdiv 43“Byākāsi pukkuso pañhaṃ,
atthaṃ dhammassa kovido;
Senakaṃ dāni pucchāmi,
kiṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Yadaññaṃ dukkaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1533Msdiv 44“Dadeyya puriso dānaṃ,
appaṃ vā yadi vā bahuṃ;
Yo ca datvā nānutappe,
taṃ dukkarataraṃ tato;
Sabbaññaṃ sukaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,
evaṃ jānāhi maddava”.

SC 7PTS vp Pali 3.341Ms 22J_1534Msdiv 45“Byākāsi āyuro pañhaṃ,
atho pukkusaporiso;
Sabbe pañhe atibhoti,
yathā bhāsati senako”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1535Paṇṇakajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
