431. Haritacajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1824Msdiv 40“Sutaṃ metaṃ mahābrahme,
kāme bhuñjati hārito;
Kaccetaṃ vacanaṃ tucchaṃ,
kacci suddho iriyyasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1825Msdiv 41“Evametaṃ mahārāja,
yathā te vacanaṃ sutaṃ;
Kummaggaṃ paṭipannosmi,
mohaneyyesu mucchito”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1826Msdiv 42“Adu paññā kimatthiyā,
nipuṇā sādhucintinī;
Yāya uppatitaṃ rāgaṃ,
kiṃ mano na vinodaye”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1827Msdiv 43“Cattārome mahārāja,
loke atibalā bhusā;
Rāgo doso mado moho,
yattha paññā na gādhati”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1828Msdiv 44“Arahā sīlasampanno,
suddho carati hārito;
Medhāvī paṇḍito ceva,
iti no sammato bhavaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1829Msdiv 45“Medhāvīnampi hiṃsanti,
isiṃ dhammaguṇe rataṃ;
Vitakkā pāpakā rāja,
subhā rāgūpasaṃhitā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1830Msdiv 46“Uppannāyaṃ sarīrajo,
Rāgo vaṇṇavidūsano tava;
Taṃ pajaha bhaddamatthu te,
Bahunnāsi medhāvisammato”.

SC 8Ms 22J_1831Msdiv 47“Te andhakārake kāme,
Bahudukkhe mahāvise;
Tesaṃ mūlaṃ gavesissaṃ,
Checchaṃ rāgaṃ sabandhanaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1832Msdiv 48“Idaṃ vatvāna hārito,
isi saccaparakkamo;
Kāmarāgaṃ virājetvā,
brahmalokūpago ahū”ti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1833Haritacajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
