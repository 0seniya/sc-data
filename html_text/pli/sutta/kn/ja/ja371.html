371. Dīghītikosalajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.211Ms 22J_1277Msdiv 110“Evaṃbhūtassa te rāja,
āgatassa vase mama;
Atthi nu koci pariyāyo,
yo taṃ dukkhā pamocaye”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1278Msdiv 111“Evaṃbhūtassa me tāta,
āgatassa vase tava;
Natthi no koci pariyāyo,
yo maṃ dukkhā pamocaye”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 3.217Ms 22J_1279Msdiv 112“Nāññaṃ sucaritaṃ rāja,
nāññaṃ rāja subhāsitaṃ;
Tāyate maraṇakāle,
evamevitaraṃ dhanaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1280Msdiv 113Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ upanayhanti,
veraṃ tesaṃ na sammati.

SC 5Ms 22J_1281Msdiv 114Akkocchi maṃ avadhi maṃ,
ajini maṃ ahāsi me;
Ye ca taṃ nupanayhanti,
veraṃ tesūpasammati.

SC 6Ms 22J_1282Msdiv 115Na hi verena verāni,
sammantīdha kudācanaṃ;
Averena ca sammanti,
esa dhammo sanantano”ti.

SC 7Ms 22J_1283Dīghītikosalajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
