416. Parantapajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1660SC 1Ms 22J_1660Msdiv 154“Āgamissati me pāpaṃ,
āgamissati me bhayaṃ;
Tadā hi calitā sākhā,
manussena migena vā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1661SC 2Ms 22J_1661Msdiv 155“Bhīruyā nūna me kāmo,
avidūre vasantiyā;
Karissati kisaṃ paṇḍuṃ,
sāva sākhā parantapaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1662SC 3Ms 22J_1662Msdiv 156Socayissati maṃ kantā,
gāme vasamaninditā;
Karissati kisaṃ paṇḍuṃ,
sāva sākhā parantapaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1663Msdiv 157Tayā maṃ SC 4Ms 22J_1663Msdiv 157Tayā maṃ asitāpaṅgi,
sitāni bhaṇitāni ca;
Kisaṃ paṇḍuṃ karissanti,
sāva sākhā parantapaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1664SC 5Ms 22J_1664Msdiv 158“Agamā nūna so saddo,
asaṃsi nūna so tava;
Akkhātaṃ nūna taṃ tena,
yo taṃ sākhamakampayi.

SC 6Ms 22J_1665SC 6Ms 22J_1665Msdiv 159Idaṃ kho taṃ samāgamma,
mama bālassa cintitaṃ;
Tadā hi calitā sākhā,
manussena migena vā”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1666SC 7Ms 22J_1666Msdiv 160“Tatheva tvaṃ avedesi,
Avañci pitaraṃ mama;
Hantvā sākhāhi chādento,
Āgamissati me bhayan”ti. (1140)

SC 8SC 8Ms 22J_1667Parantapajātakaṃ ekādasamaṃ.

Urago vidhuro puna jāgarataṃ,
