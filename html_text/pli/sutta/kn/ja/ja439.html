439. Catudvārajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1909Msdiv 1“Catudvāramidaṃ nagaraṃ,
āyasaṃ daḷhapākāraṃ;
Oruddhapaṭiruddhosmi,
kiṃ pāpaṃ pakataṃ mayā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1910Msdiv 2“Sabbe apihitā dvārā,
oruddhosmi yathā dijo;
Kimādhikaraṇaṃ yakkha,
cakkābhinihato ahaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_1911Msdiv 3Laddhā satasahassāni,
atirekāni vīsati;
Anukampakānaṃ ñātīnaṃ,
vacanaṃ samma nākari.

SC 4Ms 22J_1912Msdiv 4Laṅghiṃ samuddaṃ pakkhandi,
sāgaraṃ appasiddhikaṃ;
Catubbhi aṭṭhajjhagamā,
aṭṭhāhipi ca soḷasa.

SC 5Ms 22J_1913Msdiv 5Soḷasāhi ca bāttiṃsa,
atricchaṃ cakkamāsado;
Icchāhatassa posassa,
cakkaṃ bhamati matthake.

SC 6Ms 22J_1914Msdiv 6Uparivisālā duppūrā,
icchā visaṭagāminī;
Ye ca taṃ anugijjhanti,
te honti cakkadhārino.

SC 7Ms 22J_1915Msdiv 7Bahubhaṇḍaṃ avahāya,
maggaṃ appaṭivekkhiya;
Yesañcetaṃ asaṅkhātaṃ,
te honti cakkadhārino.

SC 8Ms 22J_1916Msdiv 8Kammaṃ samekkhe vipulañca bhogaṃ,
Icchaṃ na seveyya anatthasaṃhitaṃ;
Kareyya vākyaṃ anukampakānaṃ,
Taṃ tādisaṃ nātivatteyya cakkaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1917Msdiv 9“Kīvaciraṃ nu me yakkha,
cakkaṃ sirasi ṭhassati;
Kati vassasahassāni,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 10Ms 22J_1918Msdiv 10“Atisaro paccasaro,
mittavinda suṇohi me;
Cakkaṃ te sirasi māviddhaṃ,
na taṃ jīvaṃ pamokkhasī”ti.

SC 11Ms 22J_1919Catudvārajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
