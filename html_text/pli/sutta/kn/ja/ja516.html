516. Mahākapijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 5.68Ms 22J_3438Msdiv 178Bārāṇasyaṃ ahū rājā,
kāsīnaṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhano;
Mittāmaccaparibyūḷho,
agamāsi migājinaṃ.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 5.69Ms 22J_3439Msdiv 179Tattha brāhmaṇamaddakkhi,
setaṃ citraṃ kilāsinaṃ;
Viddhastaṃ koviḷāraṃva,
kisaṃ dhamanisanthataṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_3440Msdiv 180Paramakāruññataṃ pattaṃ,
disvā kicchagataṃ naraṃ;
Avaca byamhito rājā,
“yakkhānaṃ katamo nusi.

SC 4Ms 22J_3441Msdiv 181Hatthapādā ca te setā,
tato setataraṃ siro;
Gattaṃ kammāsavaṇṇaṃ te,
kilāsabahulo casi.

SC 5Ms 22J_3442Msdiv 182Vaṭṭanāvaḷi saṅkāsā,
piṭṭhi te ninnatunnatā;
Kāḷapabbāva te aṅgā,
nāññaṃ passāmi edisaṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_3443Msdiv 183Ugghaṭṭapādo tasito,
kiso dhamanisanthato;
Chāto ātattarūposi,
kutosi kattha gacchasi.

SC 7Ms 22J_3444Msdiv 184Duddasī appakārosi,
dubbaṇṇo bhīmadassano;
Janetti yāpi te mātā,
na taṃ iccheyya passituṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_3445Msdiv 185Kiṃ kammamakaraṃ pubbe,
Kaṃ avajjhaṃ aghātayi;
Kibbisaṃ yaṃ karitvāna,
Idaṃ dukkhaṃ upāgami”.

SC 9Ms 22J_3446Msdiv 186“Taggha te ahamakkhissaṃ,
yathāpi kusalo tathā;
Saccavādiñhi lokasmiṃ,
pasaṃsantīdha paṇḍitā.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 5.70Ms 22J_3447Msdiv 187Eko caraṃ gogaveso,
mūḷho accasariṃ vane;
Araññe irīṇe vivane,
nānākuñjarasevite.

SC 11Ms 22J_3448Msdiv 188Vāḷamigānucarite,
vippanaṭṭhosmi kānane;
Acariṃ tattha sattāhaṃ,
khuppipāsasamappito.

SC 12Ms 22J_3449Msdiv 189Tattha tindukamaddakkhiṃ,
visamaṭṭhaṃ bubhukkhito;
Papātamabhilambantaṃ,
sampannaphaladhārinaṃ.

SC 13Ms 22J_3450Msdiv 190Vātassitāni bhakkhesiṃ,
tāni rucciṃsu me bhusaṃ;
Atitto rukkhamārūhiṃ,
tattha hessāmi āsito.

SC 14Ms 22J_3451Msdiv 191Ekaṃ me bhakkhitaṃ āsi,
dutiyaṃ abhipatthitaṃ;
Tato sā bhañjatha sākhā,
chinnā pharasunā viya.

SC 15Ms 22J_3452Msdiv 192Sohaṃ sahāva sākhāhi,
uddhampādo avaṃsiro;
Appatiṭṭhe anālambe,
giriduggasmi pāpataṃ.

SC 16Ms 22J_3453Msdiv 193Yasmā ca vāri gambhīraṃ,
tasmā na samapajjisaṃ;
Tattha sesiṃ nirānando,
anūnā dasa rattiyo.

SC 17Ms 22J_3454Msdiv 194Athettha kapi māgañchi,
gonaṅgulo darīcaro;
Sākhāhi sākhaṃ vicaranto,
khādamāno dumapphalaṃ.

SC 18Ms 22J_3455Msdiv 195So maṃ disvā kisaṃ paṇḍuṃ,
kāruññamakaraṃ mayi;
Ambho ko nāma so ettha,
evaṃ dukkhena aṭṭito.

SC 19Ms 22J_3456Msdiv 196Manusso amanusso vā,
attānaṃ me pavedaya;
Tassañjaliṃ paṇāmetvā,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 20Ms 22J_3457Msdiv 197Manussohaṃ byasampatto,
sā me natthi ito gati;
Taṃ vo vadāmi bhaddaṃ vo,
tvañca me saraṇaṃ bhava.

SC 21Ms 22J_3458Msdiv 198Garuṃ silaṃ gahetvāna,
vicarī pabbate kapi;
Silāya yoggaṃ katvāna,
nisabho etadabravi.

SC 22Ms 22J_3459Msdiv 199Ehi me piṭṭhimāruyha,
gīvaṃ gaṇhāhi bāhubhi;
Ahaṃ taṃ uddharissāmi,
giriduggata vegasā.

SC 23Ms 22J_3460Msdiv 200Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
vānarindassa sirīmato;
Piṭṭhimāruyha dhīrassa,
gīvaṃ bāhāhi aggahiṃ.

SC 24Ms 22J_3461Msdiv 201So maṃ tato samuṭṭhāsi,
tejassī balavā kapi;
Vihaññamāno kicchena,
giriduggata vegasā.

SC 25Ms 22J_3462Msdiv 202Uddharitvāna maṃ santo,
nisabho etadabravi;
Iṅgha maṃ samma rakkhassu,
pasupissaṃ muhuttakaṃ.

SC 26PTS vp Pali 5.71Ms 22J_3463Msdiv 203Sīhā byagghā ca dīpī ca,
acchakokataracchayo;
Te maṃ pamattaṃ hiṃseyyuṃ,
te tvaṃ disvā nivāraya.

SC 27Ms 22J_3464Msdiv 204Evaṃ me parittātūna,
pasupī so muhuttakaṃ;
Tadāhaṃ pāpikaṃ diṭṭhiṃ,
paṭilacchiṃ ayoniso.

SC 28Ms 22J_3465Msdiv 205Bhakkho ayaṃ manussānaṃ,
yathā caññe vane migā;
Yaṃ nūnimaṃ vadhitvāna,
chāto khādeyya vānaraṃ.

SC 29Ms 22J_3466Msdiv 206Asito ca gamissāmi,
maṃsamādāya sambalaṃ;
Kantāraṃ nittharissāmi,
pātheyyaṃ me bhavissati.

SC 30Ms 22J_3467Msdiv 207Tato silaṃ gahetvāna,
matthakaṃ sannitāḷayiṃ;
Mama gattakilantassa,
pahāro dubbalo ahu.

SC 31Ms 22J_3468Msdiv 208So ca vegenudappatto,
kapi ruhira makkhito;
Assupuṇṇehi nettehi,
rodanto maṃ udikkhati.

SC 32Ms 22J_3469Msdiv 209‘Māyyomaṃ kari bhaddante,
tvañca nāmedisaṃ kari;
Tvañca kho nāma dīghāvu,
aññe vāretumarahasi.

SC 33Ms 22J_3470Msdiv 210Aho vata re purisa,
tāvadukkarakāraka;
Edisā visamā duggā,
papātā uddhato mayā.

SC 34Ms 22J_3471Msdiv 211Ānīto paralokāva,
dubbheyyaṃ maṃ amaññatha;
Taṃ tena pāpadhammena,
pāpaṃ pāpena cintitaṃ.

SC 35Ms 22J_3472Msdiv 212Mā heva tvaṃ adhammaṭṭha,
vedanaṃ kaṭukaṃ phusi;
Mā heva pāpakammaṃ taṃ,
phalaṃ veḷuṃva taṃ vadhi.

SC 36Ms 22J_3473Msdiv 213Tayime natthi vissāso,
pāpadhamma asaññata;
Ehi me piṭṭhito gaccha,
dissamānova santike.

SC 37Ms 22J_3474Msdiv 214Muttosi hatthā vāḷānaṃ,
pattosi mānusiṃ padaṃ;
Esa maggo adhammaṭṭha,
tena gaccha yathāsukhaṃ’.

SC 38Ms 22J_3475Msdiv 215Idaṃ vatvā giricaro,
rahade pakkhalya matthakaṃ;
Assūni sampamajjitvā,
tato pabbatamāruhi.

SC 39Ms 22J_3476Msdiv 216Sohaṃ tenābhisattosmi,
pariḷāhena aṭṭito;
Ḍayhamānena gattena,
vāriṃ pātuṃ upāgamiṃ.

SC 40Ms 22J_3477Msdiv 217Agginā viya santatto,
rahado ruhiramakkhito;
Pubbalohitasaṅkāso,
sabbo me samapajjatha.

SC 41PTS vp Pali 5.72Ms 22J_3478Msdiv 218Yāvanto udabindūni,
kāyasmiṃ nipatiṃsu me;
Tāvanto gaṇḍa jāyetha,
addhabeluvasādisā.

SC 42Ms 22J_3479Msdiv 219Pabhinnā pagghariṃsu me,
kuṇapā pubbalohitā;
Yena yeneva gacchāmi,
gāmesu nigamesu ca.

SC 43Ms 22J_3480Msdiv 220Daṇḍahatthā nivārenti,
itthiyo purisā ca maṃ;
Okkitā pūtigandhena,
māssu orena āgamā.

SC 44Ms 22J_3481Msdiv 221Etādisaṃ idaṃ dukkhaṃ,
satta vassāni dāni me;
Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,
pubbe dukkaṭamattano.

SC 45Ms 22J_3482Msdiv 222Taṃ vo vadāmi bhaddante,
yāvantettha samāgatā;
Māssu mittāna dubbhittho,
mittadubbho hi pāpako.

SC 46Ms 22J_3483Msdiv 223Kuṭṭhī kilāsī bhavati,
yo mittānidha dubbhati;
Kāyassa bhedā mittaddu,
nirayaṃ sopapajjatī”ti.

SC 47Ms 22J_3484Mahākapijātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
