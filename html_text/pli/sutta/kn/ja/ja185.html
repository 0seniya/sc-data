185. Anabhiratijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_450Msdiv 69“Yathodake āvile appasanne,
Na passati sippikasambukañca;
Sakkharaṃ vālukaṃ macchagumbaṃ,
Evaṃ āvilamhi citte;
Na passati attadatthaṃ paratthaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_451Msdiv 70Yathodake acche vippasanne,
So passati sippikasambukañca;
Sakkharaṃ vālukaṃ macchagumbaṃ,
Evaṃ anāvilamhi citte;
So passati attadatthaṃ paratthan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_452Anabhiratijātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
