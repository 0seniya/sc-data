482. Rurumigarājajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.257Ms 22J_2508Msdiv 117“Tassa gāmavaraṃ dammi,
Nāriyo ca alaṅkatā;
Yo metaṃ Yo metaṃ migamakkhāti,
Migānaṃ migamuttamaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2509Msdiv 118“Mayhaṃ gāmavaraṃ dehi,
nāriyo ca alaṅkatā;
Ahaṃ te migamakkhissaṃ,
migānaṃ migamuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_2510Msdiv 119Etasmiṃ vanasaṇḍasmiṃ,
ambā sālā ca pupphitā;
Indagopakasañchannā,
ettheso tiṭṭhate migo”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2511Msdiv 120“Dhanuṃ advejjhaṃ katvāna,
usuṃ sannayhupāgami;
Migo ca disvā rājānaṃ,
dūrato ajjhabhāsatha.

SC 5Ms 22J_2512Msdiv 121Āgamehi mahārāja,
mā maṃ vijjhi rathesabha;
Ko nu te idamakkhāsi,
ettheso tiṭṭhate migo”.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 4.259Ms 22J_2513Msdiv 122“Esa pāpacaro poso,
samma tiṭṭhati ārakā;
Soyaṃ me idamakkhāsi,
ettheso tiṭṭhate migo”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2514Msdiv 123“Saccaṃ kireva māhaṃsu,
narā ekacciyā idha;
Kaṭṭhaṃ niplavitaṃ seyyo,
na tvevekacciyo naro”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2515Msdiv 124“Kiṃ nu ruru garahasi migānaṃ,
Kiṃ pakkhīnaṃ kiṃ pana mānusānaṃ;
Bhayañhi maṃ vindatinapparūpaṃ,
Sutvāna taṃ mānusiṃ bhāsamānaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2516Msdiv 125“Yamuddhariṃ vāhane vuyhamānaṃ,
Mahodake salile sīghasote;
Tatonidānaṃ bhayamāgataṃ mama,
Dukkho have rāja asabbhi saṅgamo”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2517Msdiv 126“Sohaṃ catuppattamimaṃ vihaṅgamaṃ,
Tanucchidaṃ hadaye ossajāmi;
Hanāmi taṃ mittadubbhiṃ akiccakāriṃ,
Yo tādisaṃ kammakataṃ na jāne”.

SC 11PTS vp Pali 4.261Ms 22J_2518Msdiv 127“Dhīrassa bālassa have janinda,
Santo vadhaṃ nappasaṃsanti jātu;
Kāmaṃ gharaṃ gacchatu pāpadhammo,
Yañcassa bhaṭṭhaṃ tadetassa dehi;
Ahañca te kāmakaro bhavāmi”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2519Msdiv 128“Addhā rurū aññataro sataṃ so,
Yo dubbhato mānusassa na dubbhi;
Kāmaṃ gharaṃ gacchatu pāpadhammo,
Yañcassa bhaṭṭhaṃ tadetassa dammi;
Ahañca te kāmacāraṃ dadāmi”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2520Msdiv 129“Suvijānaṃ siṅgālānaṃ,
sakuṇānañca vassitaṃ;
Manussavassitaṃ rāja,
dubbijānataraṃ tato.

SC 14Ms 22J_2521Msdiv 130Api ce maññatī poso,
ñāti mitto sakhāti vā;
Yo pubbe sumano hutvā,
pacchā sampajjate diso”.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 4.262Ms 22J_2522Msdiv 131“Samāgatā jānapadā,
negamā ca samāgatā;
Migā sassāni khādanti,
taṃ devo paṭisedhatu”.

SC 16Ms 22J_2523Msdiv 132“Kāmaṃ janapado māsi,
raṭṭhañcāpi vinassatu;
Na tvevāhaṃ ruruṃ dubbhe,
datvā abhayadakkhiṇaṃ.

SC 17Ms 22J_2524Msdiv 133Mā me janapado āsi,
raṭṭhañcāpi vinassatu;
Na tvevāhaṃ migarājassa,
varaṃ datvā musā bhaṇe”ti.

SC 18Ms 22J_2525Rurumigarājajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
