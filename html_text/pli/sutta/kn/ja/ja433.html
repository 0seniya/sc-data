433. Lomasakassapajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1846Msdiv 60“Assa indasamo rāja,
accantaṃ ajarāmaro;
Sace tvaṃ yaññaṃ yājeyya,
isiṃ lomasakassapaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1847Msdiv 61“Sasamuddapariyāyaṃ,
mahiṃ sāgarakuṇḍalaṃ;
Na icche saha nindāya,
evaṃ seyya vijānahi.

SC 3Ms 22J_1848Msdiv 62Dhiratthu taṃ yasalābhaṃ,
dhanalābhañca brāhmaṇa;
Yā vutti vinipātena,
adhammacaraṇena vā.

SC 4Ms 22J_1849Msdiv 63Api ce pattamādāya,
anagāro paribbaje;
Sāyeva jīvikā seyyo,
yā cādhammena esanā.

SC 5Ms 22J_1850Msdiv 64Api ce pattamādāya,
anagāro paribbaje;
Aññaṃ ahiṃsayaṃ loke,
api rajjena taṃ varaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1851Msdiv 65“Balaṃ cando balaṃ suriyo,
balaṃ samaṇabrāhmaṇā;
Balaṃ velā samuddassa,
balātibalamitthiyo.

SC 7Ms 22J_1852Msdiv 66Yathā uggatapaṃ santaṃ,
isiṃ lomasakassapaṃ;
Pitu atthā candavatī,
vājapeyyaṃ ayājayi”.

SC 8Ms 22J_1853Msdiv 67“Taṃ lobhapakataṃ kammaṃ,
Kaṭukaṃ kāmahetukaṃ;
Tassa mūlaṃ gavesissaṃ,
Checchaṃ rāgaṃ sabandhanaṃ.

SC 9Ms 22J_1854Msdiv 68Dhiratthu kāme subahūpi loke,
Tapova seyyo kāmaguṇehi rāja;
Tapo karissāmi pahāya kāme,
Taveva raṭṭhaṃ candavatī ca hotū”ti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1855Lomasakassapajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
