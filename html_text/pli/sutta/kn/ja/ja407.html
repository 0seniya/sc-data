407. Mahākapijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1580Msdiv 83“Attānaṃ saṅkamaṃ katvā,
Yo sotthiṃ samatārayi;
Kiṃ tvaṃ tesaṃ kime tuyhaṃ,
Honti ete mahākapi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1581Msdiv 84“Rājāhaṃ issaro tesaṃ,
yūthassa parihārako;
Tesaṃ sokaparetānaṃ,
bhītānaṃ te arindama.

SC 3Ms 22J_1582Msdiv 85Ullaṅghayitvā attānaṃ,
vissaṭṭhadhanuno sataṃ;
Tato aparapādesu,
daḷhaṃ bandhaṃ latāguṇaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_1583Msdiv 86Chinnabbhamiva vātena,
Nuṇṇo rukkhaṃ upāgamiṃ;
Sohaṃ appabhavaṃ tattha,
Sākhaṃ hatthehi aggahiṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1584Msdiv 87Taṃ maṃ viyāyataṃ santaṃ,
sākhāya ca latāya ca;
Samanukkamantā pādehi,
sotthiṃ sākhāmigā gatā.

SC 6Ms 22J_1585Msdiv 88Taṃ maṃ na tapate bandho,
mato me na tapessati;
Sukhamāharitaṃ tesaṃ,
yesaṃ rajjamakārayiṃ.

SC 7Ms 22J_1586Msdiv 89Esā te upamā rāja,
taṃ suṇohi arindama;
Raññā raṭṭhassa yoggassa,
balassa nigamassa ca;
Sabbesaṃ sukhameṭṭhabbaṃ,
khattiyena pajānatā”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1587Mahākapijātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
