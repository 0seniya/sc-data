311. Pucimandajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_935SC 1Ms 22J_935Msdiv 41“Uṭṭhehi cora kiṃ sesi,
ko attho supanena te;
Mā taṃ gahesuṃ rājāno,
gāme kibbisakārakaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_936Msdiv 42“SC 2Ms 22J_936Msdiv 42“Yannu coraṃ gahessanti,
gāme kibbisakārakaṃ;
Kiṃ tattha pucimandassa,
vane jātassa tiṭṭhato”.

SC 3Ms 22J_937SC 3Ms 22J_937Msdiv 43“Na tvaṃ assattha jānāsi,
mama corassa cantaraṃ;
Coraṃ gahetvā rājāno,
gāme kibbisakārakaṃ;
Appenti nimbasūlasmiṃ,
tasmiṃ me saṅkate mano”.

SC 4Ms 22J_938SC 4Ms 22J_938Msdiv 44“Saṅkeyya saṅkitabbāni,
rakkheyyānāgataṃ bhayaṃ;
Anāgatabhayā dhīro,
ubho loke avekkhatī”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 22J_939Pucimandajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
