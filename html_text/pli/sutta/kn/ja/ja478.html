478. Dūtajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2441Msdiv 54“Dūte te SC 1Ms 22J_2441Msdiv 54“Dūte te brahme pāhesiṃ,
Gaṅgātīrasmi jhāyato;
Tesaṃ puṭṭho na byākāsi,
Dukkhaṃ guyhamataṃ nu te”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2442SC 2Ms 22J_2442Msdiv 55“Sace te dukkhamuppajje,
kāsīnaṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhana;
Mā kho naṃ tassa akkhāhi,
yo taṃ dukkhā na mocaye.

SC 3Ms 22J_2443Msdiv 56Yo SC 3Ms 22J_2443Msdiv 56Yo tassa dukkhajātassa,
ekaṅgamapi bhāgaso;
Vippamoceyya dhammena,
kāmaṃ tassa pavedaya.

SC 4Ms 22J_2444SC 4Ms 22J_2444Msdiv 57Suvijānaṃ siṅgālānaṃ,
sakuṇānañca vassitaṃ;
Manussavassitaṃ rāja,
dubbijānataraṃ tato.

SC 5Ms 22J_2445SC 5Ms 22J_2445Msdiv 58Api ce maññatī poso,
ñāti mitto sakhāti vā;
Yo pubbe sumano hutvā,
pacchā sampajjate diso.

SC 6Ms 22J_2446SC 6Ms 22J_2446Msdiv 59Yo attano dukkhamanānupuṭṭho,
Pavedaye jantu akālarūpe;
Ānandino tassa bhavantimittā,
Hitesino tassa dukhī bhavanti.

SC 7Ms 22J_2447SC 7Ms 22J_2447Msdiv 60Kālañca ñatvāna tathāvidhassa,
Medhāvīnaṃ ekamanaṃ viditvā;
Akkheyya tibbāni parassa dhīro,
Saṇhaṃ giraṃ atthavatiṃ pamuñce.

SC 8Ms 22J_2448SC 8Ms 22J_2448Msdiv 61Sace ca jaññā avisayhamattano,
Na te hi mayhaṃ sukhāgamāya;
Ekova tibbāni saheyya dhīro,
Saccaṃ hirottappamapekkhamāno.

SC 9Ms 22J_2449SC 9Ms 22J_2449Msdiv 62Ahaṃ raṭṭhe vicaranto,
nigame rājadhāniyo;
Bhikkhamāno mahārāja,
ācariyassa dhanatthiko.

SC 10Ms 22J_2450SC 10Ms 22J_2450Msdiv 63Gahapatī rājapurise,
mahāsāle ca brāhmaṇe;
Alatthaṃ satta nikkhāni,
suvaṇṇassa janādhipa;
Te me naṭṭhā mahārāja,
tasmā socāmahaṃ bhusaṃ.

SC 11Ms 22J_2451SC 11Ms 22J_2451Msdiv 64Purisā te mahārāja,
manasānuvicintitā;
Nālaṃ dukkhā pamocetuṃ,
tasmā tesaṃ na byāhariṃ.

SC 12Ms 22J_2452SC 12Ms 22J_2452Msdiv 65Tvañca kho me mahārāja,
manasānuvicintito;
Alaṃ dukkhā pamocetuṃ,
tasmā tuyhaṃ pavedayiṃ”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2453SC 13Ms 22J_2453Msdiv 66“Tassādāsi pasannatto,
kāsīnaṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhano;
Jātarūpamaye nikkhe,
suvaṇṇassa catuddasā”ti.

SC 14SC 14Ms 22J_2454Dūtajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
