382. Sirikāḷakaṇṇijātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1363SC 1Ms 22J_1363Msdiv 40“Kā nu kāḷena vaṇṇena,
na cāpi piyadassanā;
Kā vā tvaṃ kassa vā dhītā,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1364SC 2Ms 22J_1364Msdiv 41“Mahārājassahaṃ dhītā,
virūpakkhassa caṇḍiyā;
Ahaṃ kāḷī alakkhikā,
kāḷakaṇṇīti maṃ vidū;
Okāsaṃ yācito dehi,
vasemu tava santike”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1365SC 3Ms 22J_1365Msdiv 42“Kiṃsīle kiṃsamācāre,
Purise nivisase tuvaṃ;
Puṭṭhā me kāḷi akkhāhi,
Kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1366SC 4Ms 22J_1366Msdiv 43“Makkhī paḷāsī sārambhī,
issukī maccharī saṭho;
So mayhaṃ puriso kanto,
laddhaṃ yassa vinassati.

SC 5Ms 22J_1367SC 5Ms 22J_1367Msdiv 44Kodhano upanāhī ca,
pisuṇo ca vibhedako;
Kaṇḍakavāco pharuso,
so me kantataro tato.

SC 6Ms 22J_1368SC 6Ms 22J_1368Msdiv 45Ajja suveti puriso,
sadatthaṃ nāvabujjhati;
Ovajjamāno kuppati,
seyyaṃ so atimaññati.

SC 7Ms 22J_1369Msdiv 46SC 7Ms 22J_1369Msdiv 46Davappaluddho puriso,
sabbamittehi dhaṃsati;
So mayhaṃ puriso kanto,
tasmiṃ homi anāmayā”.

SC 8Ms 22J_1370SC 8Ms 22J_1370Msdiv 47“Apehi etto tvaṃ kāḷi,
netaṃ amhesu vijjati;
Aññaṃ janapadaṃ gaccha,
nigame rājadhāniyo”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1371SC 9Ms 22J_1371Msdiv 48“Ahampi kho taṃ jānāmi,
netaṃ tumhesu vijjati;
Santi loke alakkhikā,
saṃgharanti bahuṃ dhanaṃ;
Ahaṃ devo ca me bhātā,
ubho naṃ vidhamāmase”.

SC 10Ms 22J_1372SC 10Ms 22J_1372Msdiv 49“Kā nu dibbena vaṇṇena,
pathabyā supatiṭṭhitā;
Kā vā tvaṃ kassa vā dhītā,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_1373SC 11Ms 22J_1373Msdiv 50“Mahārājassahaṃ dhītā,
dhataraṭṭhassa sirīmato;
Ahaṃ sirī ca lakkhī ca,
bhūripaññāti maṃ vidū;
Okāsaṃ yācito dehi,
vasemu tava santike”.

SC 12Ms 22J_1374SC 12Ms 22J_1374Msdiv 51“Kiṃsīle kiṃsamācāre,
Purise nivisase tuvaṃ;
Puṭṭhā me lakkhi akkhāhi,
Kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 22J_1375SC 13Ms 22J_1375Msdiv 52“Yo cāpi sīte atha vāpi uṇhe,
Vātātape ḍaṃsasarīsape ca;
Khudhaṃ pipāsaṃ abhibhuyya sabbaṃ,
Rattindivaṃ yo satataṃ niyutto.

SC 14Ms 22J_1376SC 14Ms 22J_1376Msdiv 53Kālāgatañca na hāpeti atthaṃ,
So me manāpo nivise ca tamhi;
Akkodhano mittavā cāgavā ca,
Sīlūpapanno asaṭhojubhūto.

SC 15Ms 22J_1377SC 15Ms 22J_1377Msdiv 54Saṅgāhako sakhilo saṇhavāco,
Mahattapattopi nivātavutti;
Tasmiṃhaṃ pose vipulā bhavāmi,
Ūmi samuddassa yathāpi vaṇṇaṃ.

SC 16Ms 22J_1378SC 16Ms 22J_1378Msdiv 55Yo cāpi mitte atha vā amitte,
Seṭṭhe sarikkhe atha vāpi hīne;
Atthaṃ carantaṃ atha vā anatthaṃ,
Āvī raho saṅgahameva vatte.

SC 17Ms 22J_1379SC 17Ms 22J_1379Msdiv 56Vācaṃ na vajjā pharusaṃ kadāci,
Matassa jīvassa ca tassa homi;
Etesaṃ yo aññataraṃ labhitvā,
Kantā sirī majjati appapañño;
Taṃ dittarūpaṃ visamaṃ carantaṃ,
Karīsaṭhānaṃva vivajjayāmi.

SC 18Ms 22J_1380SC 18Ms 22J_1380Msdiv 57Attanā kurute lakkhiṃ,
alakkhiṃ kurutattanā;
Na hi lakkhiṃ alakkhiṃ vā,
añño aññassa kārako”ti.

SC 19Ms 22J_1381SC 19Ms 22J_1381Sirikāḷakaṇṇijātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
