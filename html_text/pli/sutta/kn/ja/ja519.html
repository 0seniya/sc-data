519. Sambulājātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_3560Msdiv 297“Kā vedhamānā girikandarāyaṃ,
Ekā tuvaṃ tiṭṭhasi saṃhitūru;
Puṭṭhāsi me pāṇipameyyamajjhe,
Akkhāhi me nāmañca bandhave ca.

SC 2Ms 22J_3561Msdiv 298Obhāsayaṃ vanaṃ rammaṃ,
sīhabyagghanisevitaṃ;
Kā vā tvamasi kalyāṇi,
kassa vā tvaṃ sumajjhime;
Abhivādemi taṃ bhadde,
dānavāhaṃ namatthu te”.

SC 3Ms 22J_3562Msdiv 299“Yo putto kāsirājassa,
sotthisenoti taṃ vidū;
Tassāhaṃ sambulā bhariyā,
evaṃ jānāhi dānava;
Abhivādemi taṃ bhante,
sambulāhaṃ namatthu te.

SC 4Ms 22J_3563Msdiv 300Vedehaputto bhaddante,
vane vasati āturo;
Tamahaṃ rogasammattaṃ,
ekā ekaṃ upaṭṭhahaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_3564Msdiv 301Ahañca vanamuñchāya,
Madhumaṃsaṃ migābilaṃ;
Yadāharāmi taṃ bhakkho,
Tassa nūnajja nādhati”.

SC 6Ms 22J_3565Msdiv 302“Kiṃ vane rājaputtena,
āturena karissasi;
Sambule pariciṇṇena,
ahaṃ bhattā bhavāmi te”.

SC 7Ms 22J_3566Msdiv 303“Sokaṭṭāya durattāya,
kiṃ rūpaṃ vijjate mama;
Aññaṃ pariyesa bhaddante,
abhirūpataraṃ mayā”.

SC 8Ms 22J_3567Msdiv 304“Ehimaṃ girimāruyha,
bhariyā me catussatā;
Tāsaṃ tvaṃ pavarā hohi,
sabbakāmasamiddhinī.

SC 9Ms 22J_3568Msdiv 305Nūna Nūna tārakavaṇṇābhe,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchasi;
Sabbaṃ taṃ pacuraṃ mayhaṃ,
ramassvajja mayā saha.

SC 10Ms 22J_3569Msdiv 306No ce tuvaṃ maheseyyaṃ,
Sambule kārayissasi;
Alaṃ tvaṃ pātarāsāya,
Paṇhe bhakkhā bhavissasi”.

SC 11Ms 22J_3570Msdiv 307Tañca sattajaṭo luddo,
kaḷāro purisādako;
Vane nāthaṃ apassantiṃ,
sambulaṃ aggahī bhuje.

SC 12Ms 22J_3571Msdiv 308Adhipannā pisācena,
luddenāmisacakkhunā;
Sā ca sattuvasaṃ pattā,
patimevānusocati.

SC 13Ms 22J_3572Msdiv 309“Na me idaṃ tathā dukkhaṃ,
yaṃ maṃ khādeyya rakkhaso;
Yañca me ayyaputtassa,
mano hessati aññathā.

SC 14Ms 22J_3573Msdiv 310Na santi devā pavasanti nūna,
Na hi nūna santi idha lokapālā;
Sahasā karontānamasaññatānaṃ,
Na hi nūna santi paṭisedhitāro”.

SC 15Ms 22J_3574Msdiv 311“Itthīnamesā pavarā yasassinī,
Santā samā aggirivuggatejā;
Tañce tuvaṃ rakkhasādesi kaññaṃ,
Muddhā ca hi sattadhā te phaleyya;
Mā tvaṃ dahī muñca dahī muñca patibbatāya”.

SC 16Ms 22J_3575Msdiv 312Sā ca assamamāgacchi,
pamuttā purisādakā;
Nīḷaṃ Nīḷaṃ paḷinaṃ sakuṇīva,
gatasiṅgaṃva ālayaṃ.

SC 17Ms 22J_3576Msdiv 313Sā tattha paridevesi,
rājaputtī yasassinī;
Sambulā utumattakkhā,
vane nāthaṃ apassantī.

SC 18Ms 22J_3577Msdiv 314Samaṇe brāhmaṇe vande,
sampannacaraṇe ise;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 19Ms 22J_3578Msdiv 315Vande sīhe ca byagghe ca,
ye ca aññe vane migā;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 20Ms 22J_3579Msdiv 316Tiṇā latāni osadhyo,
pabbatāni vanāni ca;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 21Ms 22J_3580Msdiv 317Vande indīvarīsāmaṃ,
rattiṃ nakkhattamāliniṃ;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 22Ms 22J_3581Msdiv 318Vande bhāgīrathiṃ gaṅgaṃ,
savantīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 23Ms 22J_3582Msdiv 319Vande ahaṃ pabbatarājaseṭṭhaṃ,
Himavantaṃ siluccayaṃ;
Rājaputtaṃ apassantī,
“Tumhaṃmhi saraṇaṃ gatā”.

SC 24Ms 22J_3583Msdiv 320“Atisāyaṃ vatāgacchi,
rājaputti yasassini;
Kena nujja samāgacchi,
ko te piyataro mayā”.

SC 25Ms 22J_3584Msdiv 321“Idaṃ khohaṃ tadāvocaṃ,
gahitā tena sattunā;
Na me idaṃ tathā dukkhaṃ,
yaṃ maṃ khādeyya rakkhaso;
Yañca me ayyaputtassa,
mano hessati aññathā”.

SC 26Ms 22J_3585Msdiv 322“Corīnaṃ bahubuddhīnaṃ,
yāsu saccaṃ sudullabhaṃ;
Thīnaṃ bhāvo durājāno,
macchassevodake gataṃ”.

SC 27Ms 22J_3586Msdiv 323“Tathā maṃ saccaṃ pāletu,
pālayissati ce mamaṃ;
Yathāhaṃ nābhijānāmi,
aññaṃ piyataraṃ tayā;
Etena saccavajjena,
byādhi te vūpasammatu”.

SC 28Ms 22J_3587Msdiv 324“Ye kuñjarā sattasatā uḷārā,
Rakkhanti rattindivamuyyutāvudhā;
Dhanuggahānañca satāni soḷasa,
Kathaṃvidhe passasi bhadde sattavo”.

SC 29Ms 22J_3588Msdiv 325“Alaṅkatāyo padumuttarattacā,
Virāgitā passati haṃsagaggarā;
Tāsaṃ suṇitvā mitagītavāditaṃ,
Na dāni me tāta tathā yathā pure.

SC 30Ms 22J_3589Msdiv 326Suvaṇṇasaṅkaccadharā suviggahā,
Alaṅkatā mānusiyaccharūpamā;
Senopiyā tāta aninditaṅgiyo,
Khattiyakaññā paṭilobhayanti naṃ.

SC 31Ms 22J_3590Msdiv 327Sace ahaṃ tāta tathā yathā pure,
Patiṃ tamuñchāya punā vane bhare;
Sammānaye maṃ na ca maṃ vimānaye,
Itopi me tāta tato varaṃ siyā.

SC 32Ms 22J_3591Msdiv 328Yamannapāne vipulasmi ohite,
Nārī vimaṭṭhābharaṇā alaṅkatā;
Sabbaṅgupetā patino ca appiyā,
Abajjha tassā maraṇaṃ tato varaṃ.

SC 33Ms 22J_3592Msdiv 329Api ce daliddā kapaṇā anāḷhiyā,
Kaṭādutīyā patino ca sā piyā;
Sabbaṅgupetāyapi appiyāya,
Ayameva seyyā seyyā kapaṇāpi yā piyā”.

SC 34Ms 22J_3593Msdiv 330“Sudullabhitthī purisassa yā hitā,
Bhattitthiyā dullabho yo hito ca;
Hitā ca te sīlavatī ca bhariyā,
Janinda dhammaṃ cara sambulāya”.

SC 35Ms 22J_3594Msdiv 331“Sace tuvaṃ vipule laddhabhoge,
Issāvatiṇṇā maraṇaṃ upesi;
Ahañca te bhadde imā rājakaññā,
Sabbe te vacanakarā bhavāmā”ti.

SC 36Ms 22J_3595Sambulājātakaṃ navamaṃ.
