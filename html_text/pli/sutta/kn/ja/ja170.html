170. Kakaṇṭakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_401Msdiv 39“Nāyaṃ pure SC 1Ms 22J_401Msdiv 39“Nāyaṃ pure uṇṇamati,
toraṇagge kakaṇṭako;
Mahosadha vijānāhi,
kena thaddho kakaṇṭako”.

SC 2Ms 22J_402SC 2Ms 22J_402Msdiv 40“Aladdhapubbaṃ laddhāna,
aḍḍhamāsaṃ kakaṇṭako;
Atimaññati rājānaṃ,
vedehaṃ mithilaggahan”ti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 22J_403Kakaṇṭakajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Santhavavaggo dutiyo.

SC 4SC 4Ms 22J_404Tassuddānaṃ

SC 5SC 5Ms 22J_405Atha indasamāna sapaṇṇakuṭi,
Susimuttama gijjha jalābujako;
Upasāḷaka bhikkhu salāpavaro,
Atha mettavaro dasapuṇṇamatīti.
