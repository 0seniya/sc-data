270. Ulūkajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.353Ms 22J_748Msdiv 58“Sabbehi kira ñātīhi,
kosiyo issaro kato;
Sace ñātīhi anuññāto,
bhaṇeyyāhaṃ ekavācikaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_749Msdiv 59“Bhaṇa samma anuññāto,
atthaṃ dhammañca kevalaṃ;
Santi hi daharā pakkhī,
paññavanto jutindharā”.

SC 3Ms 22J_750Msdiv 60“Na me ruccati bhaddaṃ vo,
ulūkassābhisecanaṃ;
Akkuddhassa mukhaṃ passa,
kathaṃ kuddho karissatī”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_751Ulūkajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Padumavaggo dutiyo.

SC 5Ms 22J_752Tassuddānaṃ

SC 6Ms 22J_753Padumuttama nāgasirivhayano,
Sa-mahaṇṇava yūpa khurappavaro;
Atha bhaddalī kuñjara rukkha puna,
Kharavāca ulūkavarena dasāti.
