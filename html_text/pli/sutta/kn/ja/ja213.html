213. Bharujātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_544SC 1Ms 22J_544Msdiv 125“Isīnamantaraṃ katvā,
bharurājāti me sutaṃ;
Ucchinno saha raṭṭhehi,
sarājā vibhavaṅgato.

SC 2Ms 22J_545SC 2Ms 22J_545Msdiv 126Tasmā hi chandāgamanaṃ,
nappasaṃsanti paṇḍitā;
Aduṭṭhacitto bhāseyya,
giraṃ saccupasaṃhitan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_546SC 3Ms 22J_546Bharujātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
