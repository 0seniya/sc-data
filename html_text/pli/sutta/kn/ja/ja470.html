470. Kosiyajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.186Ms 22J_2317Msdiv 73“Neva kiṇāmi napi vikkiṇāmi,
Na cāpi me sannicayo ca atthi;
Sukiccharūpaṃ vatidaṃ parittaṃ,
Patthodano nālamayaṃ duvinnaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2318Msdiv 74“Appamhā appakaṃ dajjā,
anumajjhato majjhakaṃ;
Bahumhā bahukaṃ dajjā,
adānaṃ nupapajjati.

SC 3Ms 22J_2319Msdiv 75Taṃ taṃ vadāmi kosiya,
dehi dānāni bhuñja ca”.
Ariyamaggaṃ samārūha,
nekāsī labhate sukhaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_2320Msdiv 76“Moghañcassa hutaṃ hoti,
moghañcāpi samīhitaṃ;
Atithismiṃ yo nisinnasmiṃ,
eko bhuñjati bhojanaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_2321Msdiv 77Taṃ taṃ vadāmi kosiya,
dehi dānāni bhuñja ca;
Ariyamaggaṃ samārūha,
nekāsī labhate sukhaṃ”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2322Msdiv 78“Saccañcassa hutaṃ hoti,
saccañcāpi samīhitaṃ;
Atithismiṃ yo nisinnasmiṃ,
neko bhuñjati bhojanaṃ.

SC 7Ms 22J_2323Msdiv 79Taṃ taṃ vadāmi kosiya,
dehi dānāni bhuñja ca;
Ariyamaggaṃ samārūha,
nekāsī labhate sukhaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2324Msdiv 80“Sarañca juhati poso,
bahukāya gayāya ca;
Doṇe timbarutitthasmiṃ,
sīghasote mahāvahe.

SC 9Ms 22J_2325Msdiv 81Atra cassa hutaṃ hoti,
atra cassa samīhitaṃ;
Atithismiṃ yo nisinnasmiṃ,
neko bhuñjati bhojanaṃ.

SC 10Ms 22J_2326Msdiv 82Taṃ taṃ vadāmi kosiya,
dehi dānāni bhuñja ca;
Ariyamaggaṃ samārūha,
nekāsī labhate sukhaṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2327Msdiv 83“Baḷisañhi so nigilati,
dīghasuttaṃ sabandhanaṃ;
Atithismiṃ yo nisinnasmiṃ,
eko bhuñjati bhojanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 22J_2328Msdiv 84Taṃ taṃ vadāmi kosiya,
dehi dānāni bhuñja ca;
Ariyamaggaṃ samārūha,
nekāsī labhate sukhaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2329Msdiv 85“Uḷāravaṇṇā vata brāhmaṇā ime,
Ayañca vo sunakho kissa hetu;
Uccāvacaṃ vaṇṇanibhaṃ vikubbati,
Akkhātha no brāhmaṇā ke nu tumhe”.

SC 14Ms 22J_2330Msdiv 86“Cando ca suriyo ca ubho idhāgatā,
Ayaṃ pana mātali devasārathi;
Sakkohamasmi tidasānamindo,
Eso ca kho pañcasikhoti vuccati”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2331Msdiv 87“Pāṇissarā mudiṅgā ca,
murajālambarāni ca;
Suttamenaṃ pabodhenti,
paṭibuddho ca nandati”.

SC 16Ms 22J_2332Msdiv 88“Ye kecime maccharino kadariyā,
Paribhāsakā samaṇabrāhmaṇānaṃ;
Idheva nikkhippa sarīradehaṃ,
Kāyassa bhedā nirayaṃ vajanti”.

SC 17Ms 22J_2333Msdiv 89“Ye kecime suggatimāsamānā,
Dhamme ṭhitā saṃyame saṃvibhāge;
Idheva nikkhippa sarīradehaṃ,
Kāyassa bhedā sugatiṃ vajanti.

SC 18Ms 22J_2334Msdiv 90Tvaṃ nosi ñāti purimāsu jātisu,
So maccharī rosako pāpadhammo;
Taveva atthāya idhāgatamhā,
Mā pāpadhammo nirayaṃ gamittha”.

SC 19Ms 22J_2335Msdiv 91“Addhā hi maṃ vo hitakāmā,
yaṃ maṃ samanusāsatha;
Sohaṃ tathā karissāmi,
sabbaṃ vuttaṃ hitesibhi.

SC 20Ms 22J_2336Msdiv 92Esāhamajjeva upāramāmi,
Na cāpihaṃ kiñci kareyya pāpaṃ;
Na cāpi me kiñci adeyyamatthi,
Na cāpidatvā udakaṃ pivāmi.

SC 21Ms 22J_2337Msdiv 93Evañca me dadato sabbakālaṃ,
Bhogā ime vāsava khīyissanti;
Tato ahaṃ pabbajissāmi sakka,
Hitvāna kāmāni yathodhikānī”ti.

SC 22Ms 22J_2338Kosiyajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
