502. Cūḷahaṃsajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.424Ms 22J_2990Msdiv 133“Ete haṃsā pakkamanti,
vakkaṅgā bhayameritā;
Harittaca hemavaṇṇa,
kāmaṃ sumukha pakkama.

SC 2Ms 22J_2991Msdiv 134Ohāya maṃ ñātigaṇā,
ekaṃ pāsavasaṃ gataṃ;
Anapekkhamānā gacchanti,
kiṃ eko avahiyyasi.

SC 3Ms 22J_2992Msdiv 135Pateva patataṃ seṭṭha,
natthi baddhe sahāyatā;
Mā anīghāya hāpesi,
kāmaṃ sumukha pakkama”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 4.425Ms 22J_2993Msdiv 136“Nāhaṃ ‘dukkhapareto’ti,
dhataraṭṭha tuvaṃ jahe;
Jīvitaṃ maraṇaṃ vā me,
tayā saddhiṃ bhavissati”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2994Msdiv 137“Etadariyassa kalyāṇaṃ,
yaṃ tvaṃ sumukha bhāsasi;
Tañca vīmaṃsamānohaṃ,
‘patatetaṃ’ avassajiṃ”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2995Msdiv 138“Apadena padaṃ yāti,
antalikkhacaro dijo;
Ārā pāsaṃ na bujjhi tvaṃ,
haṃsānaṃ pavaruttama”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2996Msdiv 139“Yadā parābhavo hoti,
poso jīvitasaṅkhaye;
Atha jālañca pāsañca,
āsajjāpi na bujjhati”.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 4.426Ms 22J_2997Msdiv 140“Ete haṃsā pakkamanti,
vakkaṅgā bhayameritā;
Harittaca hemavaṇṇa,
tvaññeva avahiyyasi.

SC 9Ms 22J_2998Msdiv 141Ete bhutvā ca pitvā ca,
pakkamanti vihaṅgamā;
Anapekkhamānā vakkaṅgā,
tvaññeveko upāsasi.

SC 10Ms 22J_2999Msdiv 142Kiṃ nu tyāyaṃ dijo hoti,
mutto baddhaṃ upāsasi;
Ohāya sakuṇā yanti,
kiṃ eko avahiyyasi”.

SC 11Ms 22J_3000Msdiv 143“Rājā me so dijo mitto,
sakhā pāṇasamo ca me;
Neva naṃ vijahissāmi,
yāva kālassa pariyāyaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 22J_3001Msdiv 144“Yo ca tvaṃ sakhino hetu,
pāṇaṃ cajitumicchasi;
So te sahāyaṃ muñcāmi,
hotu rājā tavānugo”.

SC 13PTS vp Pali 4.427Ms 22J_3002Msdiv 145“Evaṃ luddaka nandassu,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Yathāhamajja nandāmi,
muttaṃ disvā dijādhipaṃ”.

SC 14Ms 22J_3003Msdiv 146“Kaccinnu bhoto kusalaṃ,
kacci bhoto anāmayaṃ;
Kacci raṭṭhamidaṃ phītaṃ,
dhammena manusāsasi”.

SC 15Ms 22J_3004Msdiv 147“Kusalañceva me haṃsa,
atho haṃsa anāmayaṃ;
Atho raṭṭhamidaṃ phītaṃ,
dhammena manusāsahaṃ”.

SC 16Ms 22J_3005Msdiv 148“Kacci bhoto amaccesu,
doso koci na vijjati;
Kacci ārā amittā te,
chāyā dakkhiṇatoriva”.

SC 17Ms 22J_3006Msdiv 149“Athopi me amaccesu,
doso koci na vijjati;
Atho ārā amittā me,
chāyā dakkhiṇatoriva”.

SC 18Ms 22J_3007Msdiv 150“Kacci te sādisī bhariyā,
assavā piyabhāṇinī;
Puttarūpayasūpetā,
tava chandavasānugā”.

SC 19Ms 22J_3008Msdiv 151“Atho me sādisī bhariyā,
assavā piyabhāṇinī;
Puttarūpayasūpetā,
mama chandavasānugā”.

SC 20Ms 22J_3009Msdiv 152“Kacci te bahavo puttā,
sujātā raṭṭhavaḍḍhana;
Paññājavena sampannā,
sammodanti tato tato”.

SC 21Ms 22J_3010Msdiv 153“Satameko ca me puttā,
dhataraṭṭha mayā sutā;
Tesaṃ tvaṃ kiccamakkhāhi,
nāvarujjhanti te vaco”.

SC 22Ms 22J_3011Msdiv 154“Upapannopi ce hoti,
jātiyā vinayena vā;
Atha pacchā kurute yogaṃ,
kicche kicche āpāsu sīdati.

SC 23PTS vp Pali 4.429Ms 22J_3012Msdiv 155Tassa saṃhīrapaññassa,
vivaro jāyate mahā;
Rattimandhova rūpāni,
thūlāni manupassati.

SC 24Ms 22J_3013Msdiv 156Asāre sārayogaññū,
matiṃ na tveva vindati;
Sarabhova giriduggasmiṃ,
antarāyeva sīdati.

SC 25Ms 22J_3014Msdiv 157Hīnajaccopi ce hoti,
uṭṭhātā dhitimā naro;
Ācārasīlasampanno,
nise aggīva bhāsati.

SC 26Ms 22J_3015Msdiv 158Etaṃ me upamaṃ katvā,
putte vijjāsu vācaya;
Saṃvirūḷhetha medhāvī,
khette bījaṃva vuṭṭhiyā”ti.

SC 27Ms 22J_3016Cūḷahaṃsajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
