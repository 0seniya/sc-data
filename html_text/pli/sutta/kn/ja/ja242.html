242. Sunakhajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.247Ms 22J_638Msdiv 184“Bālo vatāyaṃ sunakho,
yo varattaṃ na khādati;
Bandhanā ca pamuñceyya,
asito ca gharaṃ vaje”.

SC 2Ms 22J_639Msdiv 185“Aṭṭhitaṃ me manasmiṃ me,
atho me hadaye kataṃ;
Kālañca paṭikaṅkhāmi,
yāva passupatū jano”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_640Sunakhajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
