248. Kiṃsukopamajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_656SC 1Ms 22J_656Msdiv 196“Sabbehi kiṃsuko diṭṭho,
kinnvettha vicikicchatha;
Na hi sabbesu ṭhānesu,
sārathī paripucchito”.

SC 2Ms 22J_657SC 2Ms 22J_657Msdiv 197“Evaṃ sabbehi ñāṇehi,
yesaṃ dhammā ajānitā;
Te ve dhammesu kaṅkhanti,
kiṃsukasmiṃva bhātaro”ti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 22J_658Kiṃsukopamajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
