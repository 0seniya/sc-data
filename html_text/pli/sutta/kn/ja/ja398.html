398. Sutanujātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.327Ms 22J_1501Msdiv 15“Rājā te bhattaṃ pāhesi,
Suciṃ maṃsūpasecanaṃ;
Maghadevasmiṃ adhivatthe,
Ehi nikkhamma bhuñjasu”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 3.328Ms 22J_1502Msdiv 16“Ehi māṇava orena,
bhikkhamādāya sūpitaṃ;
Tvañca māṇava bhikkhā ca,
ubho bhakkhā bhavissatha”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1503Msdiv 17“Appakena tuvaṃ yakkha,
thullamatthaṃ jahissasi;
Bhikkhaṃ te nāharissanti,
janā maraṇasaññino.

SC 4Ms 22J_1504Msdiv 18Laddhāya yakkhā tava niccabhikkhaṃ,
Suciṃ paṇītaṃ rasasā upetaṃ;
Bhikkhañca te āhariyo naro idha,
Sudullabho hehiti bhakkhite mayi”.

SC 5PTS vp Pali 3.329Ms 22J_1505Msdiv 19“Mameva sutano attho,
yathā bhāsasi māṇava;
Mayā tvaṃ samanuññāto,
sotthiṃ passāhi mātaraṃ.

SC 6Ms 22J_1506Msdiv 20Khaggaṃ chattañca pātiñca,
gacchamādāya māṇava;
Sotthiṃ passatu te mātā,
tvañca passāhi mātaraṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1507Msdiv 21“Evaṃ yakkha sukhī hohi,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Dhanañca me adhigataṃ,
rañño ca vacanaṃ katan”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1508Sutanujātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
