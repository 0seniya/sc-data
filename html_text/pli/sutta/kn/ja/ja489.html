489. Surucijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.319Ms 22J_2654Msdiv 102“Mahesī surucino bhariyā,
Ānītā paṭhamaṃ ahaṃ;
Dasa vassasahassāni,
Yaṃ maṃ surucimānayi.

SC 2Ms 22J_2655Msdiv 103Sāhaṃ brāhmaṇa rājānaṃ,
vedehaṃ mithilaggahaṃ;
Nābhijānāmi kāyena,
vācāya uda cetasā;
Suruciṃ atimaññittha,
āvi vā yadi vā raho.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 4.320Ms 22J_2656Msdiv 104Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā. (1)

SC 4Ms 22J_2657Msdiv 105Bhattu mama sassu mātā,
pitā cāpi ca sassuro;
Te maṃ brahme vinetāro,
yāva aṭṭhaṃsu jīvitaṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_2658Msdiv 106Sāhaṃ ahiṃsāratinī,
kāmasā kāmasā dhammacārinī;
Sakkaccaṃ te upaṭṭhāsiṃ,
rattindivamatanditā.

SC 6Ms 22J_2659Msdiv 107Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā. (2)

SC 7Ms 22J_2660Msdiv 108Soḷasitthisahassāni,
sahabhariyāni brāhmaṇa;
Tāsu issā vā kodho vā,
nāhu mayhaṃ kudācanaṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_2661Msdiv 109Hitena tāsaṃ nandāmi,
na ca me kāci appiyā;
Attānaṃvānukampāmi,
sadā sabbā sapattiyo.

SC 9Ms 22J_2662Msdiv 110Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā. (3)

SC 10Ms 22J_2663Msdiv 111Dāse kammakare pesse,
ye caññe anujīvino;
Pesemi sahadhammena,
sadā pamuditindriyā.

SC 11Ms 22J_2664Msdiv 112Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā. (4)

SC 12Ms 22J_2665Msdiv 113Samaṇe brāhmaṇe cāpi,
aññe cāpi vanibbake;
Tappemi annapānena,
sadā payatapāṇinī.

SC 13Ms 22J_2666Msdiv 114Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā. (5)

SC 14Ms 22J_2667Msdiv 115Cātuddasiṃ Cātuddasiṃ pañcaddasiṃ,
Yā ca pakkhassa aṭṭhamī;
Pāṭihāriyapakkhañca,
Aṭṭhaṅgasusamāgataṃ;
Uposathaṃ upavasāmi,
Sadā sīlesu saṃvutā.

SC 15Ms 22J_2668Msdiv 116Etena saccavajjena,
putto uppajjataṃ ise;
Musā me bhaṇamānāya,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā”. (6)

SC 16PTS vp Pali 4.321Ms 22J_2669Msdiv 117“Sabbeva te dhammaguṇā,
rājaputti yasassini;
Saṃvijjanti tayi bhadde,
ye tvaṃ kittesi attani.

SC 17Ms 22J_2670Msdiv 118Khattiyo jātisampanno,
abhijāto yasassimā;
Dhammarājā videhānaṃ,
putto uppajjate tava”.

SC 18Ms 22J_2671Msdiv 119“Dummī rajojalladharo,
aghe vehāyasaṃ ṭhito;
Manuññaṃ bhāsase vācaṃ,
yaṃ mayhaṃ hadayaṅgamaṃ.

SC 19Ms 22J_2672Msdiv 120Devatānusi saggamhā,
isi vāsi mahiddhiko;
Ko vāsi tvaṃ anuppatto,
attānaṃ me pavedaya”.

SC 20Ms 22J_2673Msdiv 121“Yaṃ devasaṅghā vandanti,
sudhammāyaṃ samāgatā;
Sohaṃ sakko sahassakkho,
āgatosmi tavantike.

SC 21Ms 22J_2674Msdiv 122Itthiyo jīvalokasmiṃ,
yā hoti samacārinī;
Medhāvinī sīlavatī,
sassudevā patibbatā.

SC 22Ms 22J_2675Msdiv 123Tādisāya sumedhāya,
sucikammāya nāriyā;
Devā dassanamāyanti,
mānusiyā amānusā.

SC 23Ms 22J_2676Msdiv 124Tvañca bhadde suciṇṇena,
pubbe sucaritena ca;
Idha rājakule jātā,
sabbakāmasamiddhinī.

SC 24Ms 22J_2677Msdiv 125Ayañca te rājaputti,
ubhayattha kaṭaggaho;
Devalokūpapattī ca,
kittī ca idha jīvite.

SC 25Ms 22J_2678Msdiv 126Ciraṃ sumedhe sukhinī,
dhammamattani pālaya;
Esāhaṃ tidivaṃ yāmi,
piyaṃ me tava dassanan”ti.

SC 26Ms 22J_2679Surucijātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
