400. Dabbhapupphajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1517Msdiv 29“Anutīracārī bhaddante,
sahāyamanudhāva maṃ;
Mahā me gahito maccho,
so maṃ harati vegasā”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1518Msdiv 30“Gambhīracārī bhaddante,
daḷhaṃ gaṇhāhi thāmasā;
Ahaṃ taṃ uddharissāmi,
supaṇṇo uragāmiva”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1519Msdiv 31“Vivādo no samuppanno,
dabbhapuppha suṇohi me;
Samehi medhagaṃ samma,
vivādo vūpasammataṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1520Msdiv 32“Dhammaṭṭhohaṃ pure āsiṃ,
bahū aḍḍā me tīritā;
Samemi medhagaṃ samma,
vivādo vūpasammataṃ.

SC 5Ms 22J_1521Msdiv 33Anutīracāri naṅguṭṭhaṃ,
sīsaṃ gambhīracārino;
Accāyaṃ majjhimo khaṇḍo,
dhammaṭṭhassa bhavissati”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1522Msdiv 34“Cirampi bhakkho abhavissa,
sace na vivademase;
Asīsakaṃ anaṅguṭṭhaṃ,
siṅgālo harati rohitaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1523Msdiv 35“Yathāpi rājā nandeyya,
rajjaṃ laddhāna khattiyo;
Evāhamajja nandāmi,
disvā puṇṇamukhaṃ patiṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_1524Msdiv 36Kathaṃ nu thalajo santo,
udake macchaṃ parāmasi;
Puṭṭho me samma akkhāhi,
kathaṃ adhigataṃ tayā”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1525Msdiv 37“Vivādena kisā honti,
vivādena dhanakkhayā;
Jīnā uddā vivādena,
bhuñja māyāvi rohitaṃ.

SC 10Ms 22J_1526Msdiv 38Evameva manussesu,
vivādo yattha jāyati;
Dhammaṭṭhaṃ paṭidhāvanti,
so hi nesaṃ vināyako;
Dhanāpi tattha jīyanti,
rājakoso pavaḍḍhatī”ti.

SC 11Ms 22J_1527Dabbhapupphajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
