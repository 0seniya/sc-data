149. Ekapaṇṇajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.507Ms 22J_329Msdiv 149“Ekapaṇṇo ayaṃ rukkho,
na bhūmyā caturaṅgulo;
Phalena visakappena,
mahāyaṃ kiṃ bhavissatī”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_330Ekapaṇṇajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
