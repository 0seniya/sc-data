454. Ghaṭapaṇḍitajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2088Msdiv 165“Uṭṭhehi kaṇha kiṃ sesi,
Ko attho supanena te;
Yopi tuyhaṃ sako bhātā,
Hadayaṃ cakkhu ca dakkhiṇaṃ;
Tassa vātā balīyanti,
Ghaṭo jappati kesava”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2089Msdiv 166“Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
rohiṇeyyassa kesavo;
Taramānarūpo vuṭṭhāsi,
bhātusokena aṭṭito”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2090Msdiv 167“Kiṃ nu ummattarūpova,
kevalaṃ dvārakaṃ imaṃ;
Saso sasoti lapasi,
ko nu te sasamāhari”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2091Msdiv 168“Sovaṇṇamayaṃ maṇīmayaṃ,
Lohamayaṃ atha rūpiyāmayaṃ;
Saṅkhasilāpavāḷamayaṃ,
Kārayissāmi te sasaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2092Msdiv 169“Santi aññepi sasakā,
araññe vanagocarā;
Tepi te ānayissāmi,
kīdisaṃ sasamicchasi”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2093Msdiv 170“Na cāhamete icchāmi,
ye sasā pathavissitā;
Candato sasamicchāmi,
taṃ me ohara kesava”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2094Msdiv 171“So nūna madhuraṃ ñāti,
jīvitaṃ vijahissasi;
Apatthiyaṃ yo patthayasi,
candato sasamicchasi”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2095Msdiv 172“Evañce kaṇha jānāsi,
yadaññamanusāsasi;
Kasmā pure mataṃ puttaṃ,
ajjāpi manusocasi”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2096Msdiv 173“Yaṃ na labbhā manussena,
amanussena vā puna;
Jāto me mā marī putto,
kuto labbhā alabbhiyaṃ.

SC 10Ms 22J_2097Msdiv 174Na mantā mūlabhesajjā,
osadhehi dhanena vā;
Sakkā ānayituṃ kaṇha,
yaṃ petamanusocasi”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2098Msdiv 175“Yassa etādisā assu,
amaccā purisapaṇḍitā;
Yathā nijjhāpaye ajja,
ghaṭo purisapaṇḍito.

SC 12Ms 22J_2099Msdiv 176Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 13Ms 22J_2100Msdiv 177Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
yamāsi hadayassitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 14Ms 22J_2101Msdiv 178Sohaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
vītasoko anāvilo;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna māṇava”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2102Msdiv 179“Evaṃ karonti sappaññā,
Ye honti anukampakā;
Nivattayanti sokamhā,
Ghaṭo jeṭṭhaṃva bhātaran”ti. (1527)

SC 16Ms 22J_2103Ghaṭapaṇḍitajātakaṃ soḷasamaṃ.

SC 17Ms 22J_2104Dasakanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

SC 18Ms 22J_2105Tassuddānaṃ

SC 19Ms 22J_2106Daḷha kaṇha dhanañjaya saṅkhavaro,
Raja sattaha kassa ca takkalinā;
Dhammaṃ kukkuṭa kuṇḍali bhojanadā,
Cakkavāka subhūrisa sotthi ghaṭoti.
