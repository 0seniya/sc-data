447. Mahādhammapālajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2008Msdiv 92“Kiṃ te vataṃ kiṃ pana brahmacariyaṃ,
Kissa suciṇṇassa ayaṃ vipāko;
Akkhāhi me brāhmaṇa etamatthaṃ,
Kasmā nu tumhaṃ daharā na miyyare”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2009Msdiv 93“Dhammaṃ carāma na musā bhaṇāma,
Pāpāni kammāni parivajjayāma;
Anariyaṃ parivajjemu sabbaṃ,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 3Ms 22J_2010Msdiv 94Suṇoma dhammaṃ asataṃ satañca,
Na cāpi dhammaṃ asataṃ rocayāma;
Hitvā asante na jahāma sante,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 4Ms 22J_2011Msdiv 95Pubbeva dānā sumanā bhavāma,
Dadampi ve attamanā bhavāma;
Datvāpi ve nānutappāma pacchā,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 5Ms 22J_2012Msdiv 96Samaṇe mayaṃ brāhmaṇe addhike ca,
Vanibbake yācanake dalidde;
Annena pānena abhitappayāma,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 6Ms 22J_2013Msdiv 97Mayañca bhariyaṃ nātikkamāma,
Amhe ca bhariyā nātikkamanti;
Aññatra tāhi brahmacariyaṃ carāma,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 7Ms 22J_2014Msdiv 98Pāṇātipātā viramāma sabbe,
Loke adinnaṃ parivajjayāma;
Amajjapā nopi musā bhaṇāma,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 8Ms 22J_2015Msdiv 99Etāsu ve jāyare suttamāsu,
Medhāvino honti pahūtapaññā;
Bahussutā vedaguno ca honti,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 9Ms 22J_2016Msdiv 100Mātā pitā ca bhaginī bhātaro ca,
Puttā ca dārā ca mayañca sabbe;
Dhammaṃ carāma paralokahetu,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare.

SC 10Ms 22J_2017Msdiv 101Dāsā ca dāsyo anujīvino ca,
Paricārakā kammakarā ca sabbe;
Dhammaṃ caranti paralokahetu,
Tasmā hi amhaṃ daharā na miyyare”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2018Msdiv 102“Dhammo have rakkhati dhammacāriṃ,
Dhammo suciṇṇo sukhamāvahāti;
Esānisaṃso dhamme suciṇṇe,
Na duggatiṃ gacchati dhammacārī.

SC 12Ms 22J_2019Msdiv 103Dhammo have rakkhati dhammacāriṃ,
Chattaṃ mahantaṃ viya vassakāle;
Dhammena gutto mama dhammapālo,
Aññassa aṭṭhīni sukhī kumāro”ti.

SC 13Ms 22J_2020Mahādhammapālajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
