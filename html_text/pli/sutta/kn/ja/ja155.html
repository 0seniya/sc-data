155. Bhaggajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_352Msdiv 9“Jīva vassasataṃ bhagga,
aparāni ca vīsatiṃ;
Mā maṃ pisācā khādantu,
jīva tvaṃ sarado sataṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_353Msdiv 10“Tvampi vassasataṃ jīva,
aparāni ca vīsatiṃ;
Visaṃ pisācā khādantu,
jīva tvaṃ sarado satan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_354Bhaggajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
