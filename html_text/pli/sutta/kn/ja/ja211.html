211. Somadattajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.166Ms 22J_538Msdiv 121“Akāsi yoggaṃ dhuvamappamatto,
Saṃvaccharaṃ bīraṇathambhakasmiṃ;
Byākāsi saññaṃ parisaṃ vigayha,
Na niyyamo tāyati appapaññaṃ”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.167Ms 22J_539Msdiv 122“Dvayaṃ yācanako tāta,
somadatta nigacchati;
Alābhaṃ dhanalābhaṃ vā,
evaṃ dhammā hi yācanā”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_540Somadattajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
