406. Gandhārajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.367Ms 22J_1572Msdiv 76“Hitvā gāmasahassāni,
paripuṇṇāni soḷasa;
Koṭṭhāgārāni phītāni,
sannidhiṃ dāni kubbasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1573Msdiv 77“Hitvā gandhāravisayaṃ,
pahūtadhanadhāriyaṃ;
Pasāsanato nikkhanto,
idha dāni pasāsasi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1574Msdiv 78“Dhammaṃ bhaṇāmi vedeha,
adhammo me na ruccati;
Dhammaṃ me bhaṇamānassa,
na pāpamupalimpati”.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 3.368Ms 22J_1575Msdiv 79“Yena kenaci vaṇṇena,
paro labhati ruppanaṃ;
Mahatthiyampi ce vācaṃ,
na taṃ bhāseyya paṇḍito”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1576Msdiv 80“Kāmaṃ ruppatu vā mā vā,
bhusaṃva vikirīyatu;
Dhammaṃ me bhaṇamānassa,
na pāpamupalimpati”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1577Msdiv 81“No ce assa sakā buddhi,
vinayo vā susikkhito;
Vane andhamahiṃsova,
careyya bahuko jano.

SC 7Ms 22J_1578Msdiv 82Yasmā ca panidhekacce,
āceramhi susikkhitā;
Tasmā vinītavinayā,
caranti susamāhitā”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1579Gandhārajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
