291. Surāghaṭajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_838Msdiv 121“Sabbakāmadadaṃ kumbhaṃ,
kuṭaṃ laddhāna dhuttako;
Yāva naṃ anupāleti,
tāva so sukhamedhati.

SC 2Ms 22J_839Msdiv 122Yadā matto ca ditto ca,
pamādā kumbhamabbhidā;
Tadā naggo ca pottho ca,
pacchā bālo vihaññati.

SC 3Ms 22J_840Msdiv 123Evameva yo dhanaṃ laddhā,
Pamatto paribhuñjati;
Pacchā tappati dummedho,
Kuṭaṃ bhitvāva dhuttako”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_841Surāghaṭajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
