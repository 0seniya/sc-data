296. Samuddajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_858Msdiv 136“Ko nāyaṃ loṇatoyasmiṃ,
samantā paridhāvati;
Macche makare ca vāreti,
ūmīsu ca vihaññati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_859Msdiv 137“Anantapāyī sakuṇo,
atittoti disāsuto;
Samuddaṃ pātumicchāmi,
sāgaraṃ saritaṃ patiṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_860Msdiv 138“So ayaṃ hāyati ceva,
pūrate ca mahodadhi;
Nāssa nāyati pītanto,
apeyyo kira sāgaro”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_861Samuddajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
