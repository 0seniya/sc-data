392. Siṅghapupphajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1451Msdiv 116“Yametaṃ vārijaṃ pupphaṃ,
Adinnaṃ upasiṅghasi;
Ekaṅgametaṃ theyyānaṃ,
Gandhathenosi mārisa”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1452Msdiv 117“Na harāmi na bhañjāmi,
ārā siṅghāmi vārijaṃ;
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,
gandhathenoti vuccati.

SC 3Ms 22J_1453Msdiv 118Yoyaṃ bhisāni khaṇati,
puṇḍarīkāni bhañjati;
Evaṃ ākiṇṇakammanto,
kasmā eso na vuccati”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1454Msdiv 119“Ākiṇṇaluddo puriso,
dhāticelaṃva makkhito;
Tasmiṃ me vacanaṃ natthi,
tañcārahāmi vattave.

SC 5Ms 22J_1455Msdiv 120Anaṅgaṇassa posassa,
niccaṃ sucigavesino;
Vālaggamattaṃ pāpassa,
abbhāmattaṃva khāyati”.

SC 6Ms 22J_1456Msdiv 121“Addhā maṃ yakkha jānāsi,
atho maṃ anukampasi;
Punapi yakkha vajjāsi,
yadā passasi edisaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1457Msdiv 122“Neva taṃ upajīvāmi,
napi te bhatakāmhase;
Tvameva bhikkhu jāneyya,
yena gaccheyya suggatin”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1458Siṅghapupphajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
