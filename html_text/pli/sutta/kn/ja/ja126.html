126. Asilakkhaṇajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_279Msdiv 126“SC 1Ms 22J_279Msdiv 126“Tadevekassa kalyāṇaṃ,
tadevekassa pāpakaṃ;
Tasmā sabbaṃ na kalyāṇaṃ,
sabbaṃ vāpi na pāpakan”ti.
