492. Tacchasūkarajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.344Ms 22J_2715Msdiv 160“Yadesamānā vicarimha,
pabbatāni vanāni ca;
Anvesaṃ vicariṃ ñātī,
teme adhigatā mayā.

SC 2Ms 22J_2716Msdiv 161Bahuñcidaṃ mūlaphalaṃ,
bhakkho cāyaṃ anappako;
Rammā cimā girīnajjo,
phāsuvāso bhavissati.

SC 3Ms 22J_2717Msdiv 162Idhevāhaṃ vasissāmi,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Appossukko nirāsaṅkī,
asoko akutobhayo”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2718Msdiv 163“Aññampi leṇaṃ pariyesa,
sattu no idha vijjati;
So taccha sūkare hanti,
idhāgantvā varaṃ varaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2719Msdiv 164“Ko numhākaṃ idha sattu,
ko ñātī susamāgate;
Duppadhaṃse padhaṃseti,
taṃ me akkhātha pucchitā”.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 4.345Ms 22J_2720Msdiv 165“Uddhaggarājī migarājā,
balī dāṭhāvudho migo;
So taccha sūkare hanti,
idhāgantvā varaṃ varaṃ”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2721Msdiv 166“Na no dāṭhā na vijjanti,
balaṃ kāye samohitaṃ;
Sabbe samaggā hutvāna,
vasaṃ kāhāma ekakaṃ”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2722Msdiv 167“Hadayaṅgamaṃ kaṇṇasukhaṃ,
Vācaṃ bhāsasi tacchaka;
Yopi yuddhe palāyeyya,
Tampi pacchā hanāmase”.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 4.346Ms 22J_2723Msdiv 168“Pāṇātipātā virato nu ajja,
Abhayaṃ nu te sabbabhūtesu dinnaṃ;
Dāṭhā nu te migavadhāya na santi,
Yo saṃghapatto kapaṇova jhāyasi”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2724Msdiv 169“Na me dāṭhā na vijjanti,
Balaṃ kāye samohitaṃ;
Ñātī ca disvāna sāmaggī ekato,
Tasmā ca jhāyāmi vanamhi ekako.

SC 11Ms 22J_2725Msdiv 170Imassudaṃ yanti disodisaṃ pure,
Bhayaṭṭitā leṇagavesino puthu;
Te dāni saṅgamma vasanti ekato,
Yatthaṭṭhitā duppasahajja te mayā.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 4.347Ms 22J_2726Msdiv 171Pariṇāyakasampannā,
sahitā ekavādino;
Te maṃ samaggā hiṃseyyuṃ,
tasmā nesaṃ na patthaye”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2727Msdiv 172“Ekova indo asure jināti,
Ekova seno hanti dije pasayha;
Ekova byaggho migasaṅghapatto,
Varaṃ varaṃ hanti balañhi tādisaṃ”.

SC 14Ms 22J_2728Msdiv 173“Na heva indo na seno,
napi byaggho migādhipo;
Samagge sahite ñātī,
na byagghe kurute vase”.

SC 15Ms 22J_2729Msdiv 174“Kumbhīlakā sakuṇakā,
saṃghino gaṇacārino;
Sammodamānā ekajjhaṃ,
uppatanti ḍayanti ca.

SC 16Ms 22J_2730Msdiv 175Tesañca ḍayamānānaṃ,
ekettha apasakkati;
Tañca seno nitāḷeti,
veyyagghiyeva sā gati”.

SC 17PTS vp Pali 4.348Ms 22J_2731Msdiv 176“Ussāhito jaṭilena,
luddenāmisacakkhunā;
Dāṭhī dāṭhīsu pakkhandi,
maññamāno yathā pure”.

SC 18PTS vp Pali 4.349Ms 22J_2732Msdiv 177“Sādhu sambahulā ñātī,
api rukkhā araññajā;
Sūkarehi samaggehi,
byaggho ekāyane hato”.

SC 19Ms 22J_2733Msdiv 178Brāhmaṇañceva byagghañca,
ubho hantvāna sūkarā.
Ānandino pamuditā,
mahānādaṃ panādisuṃ.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 4.350Ms 22J_2734Msdiv 179Te su udumbaramūlasmiṃ,
sūkarā susamāgatā;
Tacchakaṃ abhisiñciṃsu,
“tvaṃ no rājāsi issaro”ti.

SC 21Ms 22J_2735Tacchasūkarajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
