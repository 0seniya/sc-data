384. Dhammadhajajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.268Ms 22J_1390Msdiv 65“Dhammaṃ caratha ñātayo,
Dhammaṃ caratha bhaddaṃ vo;
Dhammacārī sukhaṃ seti,
Asmiṃ loke paramhi ca”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1391Msdiv 66“Bhaddako vatayaṃ pakkhī,
dijo paramadhammiko;
Ekapādena tiṭṭhanto,
dhammamevānusāsati”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 3.269Ms 22J_1392Msdiv 67“Nāssa sīlaṃ vijānātha,
anaññāya pasaṃsatha;
Bhutvā aṇḍañca potañca,
dhammo dhammoti bhāsati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1393Msdiv 68Aññaṃ bhaṇati vācāya,
aññaṃ kāyena kubbati;
Vācāya no ca kāyena,
na taṃ dhammaṃ adhiṭṭhito.

SC 5Ms 22J_1394Msdiv 69Vācāya sakhilo manoviduggo,
Channo kūpasayova kaṇhasappo;
Dhammadhajo gāmanigamāsu sādhu,
Dujjāno purisena bālisena.

SC 6Ms 22J_1395Msdiv 70Imaṃ tuṇḍehi pakkhehi,
pādā cimaṃ viheṭhatha;
Chavañhimaṃ vināsetha,
nāyaṃ saṃvāsanāraho”ti.

SC 7Ms 22J_1396Dhammadhajajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
