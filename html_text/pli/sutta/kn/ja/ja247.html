247. Pādañjalījātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_653Msdiv 194“Addhā pādañjalī sabbe,
paññāya atirocati;
Tathā hi oṭṭhaṃ bhañjati,
uttariṃ nūna passati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_654Msdiv 195“Nāyaṃ dhammaṃ adhammaṃ vā,
Atthānatthañca bujjhati;
Aññatra oṭṭhanibbhogā,
Nāyaṃ jānāti kiñcanan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_655Pādañjalījātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
