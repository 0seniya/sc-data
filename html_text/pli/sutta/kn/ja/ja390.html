390. Mayhakajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1436SC 1Ms 22J_1436Msdiv 103“Sakuṇo mayhako nāma,
girisānudarīcaro;
Pakkaṃ pipphalimāruyha,
mayhaṃ mayhanti kandati.

SC 2Ms 22J_1437SC 2Ms 22J_1437Msdiv 104Tassevaṃ vilapantassa,
dijasaṅghā samāgatā;
Bhutvāna pipphaliṃ yanti,
vilapatveva so dijo.

SC 3Ms 22J_1438SC 3Ms 22J_1438Msdiv 105Evameva idhekacco,
saṅgharitvā bahuṃ dhanaṃ;
Nevattano na ñātīnaṃ,
yathodhiṃ paṭipajjati.

SC 4Ms 22J_1439SC 4Ms 22J_1439Msdiv 106Na so acchādanaṃ bhattaṃ,
na mālaṃ na vilepanaṃ;
Anubhoti sakiṃ kiñci,
na saṅgaṇhāti ñātake.

SC 5Ms 22J_1440SC 5Ms 22J_1440Msdiv 107Tassevaṃ vilapantassa,
mayhaṃ mayhanti rakkhato;
Rājāno atha vā corā,
dāyādā ye va appiyā;
Dhanamādāya gacchanti,
vilapatveva so naro.

SC 6Ms 22J_1441Msdiv 108SC 6Ms 22J_1441Msdiv 108Dhīro bhoge adhigamma,
saṅgaṇhāti ca ñātake;
Tena so kittiṃ pappoti,
pecca sagge pamodatī”ti.

SC 7Ms 22J_1442SC 7Ms 22J_1442Mayhakajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
