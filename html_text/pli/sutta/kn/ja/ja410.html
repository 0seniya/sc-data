410. Somadattajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1605Msdiv 105“Yo maṃ pure paccuḍḍeti,
araññe dūramāyato;
So na dissati mātaṅgo,
somadatto kuhiṃ gato.

SC 2Ms 22J_1606Msdiv 106Ayaṃ vā so mato seti,
allasiṅgaṃva vacchito;
Bhumyā nipatito seti,
amarā vata kuñjaro”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1607Msdiv 107“Anagāriyupetassa,
vippamuttassa te sato;
Samaṇassa na taṃ sādhu,
yaṃ petamanusocasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1608Msdiv 108“Saṃvāsena have sakka,
manussassa migassa vā;
Hadaye jāyate pemaṃ,
taṃ na sakkā asocituṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_1609Msdiv 109“Mataṃ marissaṃ rodanti,
ye rudanti lapanti ca;
Tasmā tvaṃ isi mā rodi,
roditaṃ moghamāhu santo.

SC 6Ms 22J_1610Msdiv 110Kanditena have brahme,
mato peto samuṭṭhahe;
Sabbe saṅgamma rodāma,
aññamaññassa ñātake”.

SC 7Ms 22J_1611Msdiv 111“Ādittaṃ vata maṃ santaṃ,
ghatasittaṃva pāvakaṃ;
Vārinā viya osiñcaṃ,
sabbaṃ nibbāpaye daraṃ.

SC 8Ms 22J_1612Msdiv 112Abbahī vata me sallaṃ,
yamāsi hadayassitaṃ;
Yo me sokaparetassa,
puttasokaṃ apānudi.

SC 9Ms 22J_1613Msdiv 113Sohaṃ abbūḷhasallosmi,
vītasoko anāvilo;
Na socāmi na rodāmi,
tava sutvāna vāsavā”ti.

SC 10Ms 22J_1614Somadattajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
