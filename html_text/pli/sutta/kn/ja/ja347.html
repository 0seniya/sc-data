347. Ayakūṭajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1121SC 1Ms 22J_1121Msdiv 185“Sabbāyasaṃ kūṭamatippamāṇaṃ,
Paggayha yo tiṭṭhasi antalikkhe;
Rakkhāya me tvaṃ vihito nusajja,
Udāhu me cetayase vadhāya”.

SC 2Ms 22J_1122SC 2Ms 22J_1122Msdiv 186“Dūto ahaṃ rājidha rakkhasānaṃ,
Vadhāya tuyhaṃ pahitohamasmi;
Indo ca taṃ rakkhati devarājā,
Tenuttamaṅgaṃ na te phālayāmi”.

SC 3Ms 22J_1123SC 3Ms 22J_1123Msdiv 187“Sace ca maṃ rakkhati devarājā,
Devānamindo maghavā sujampati;
Kāmaṃ pisācā vinadantu sabbe,
Na santase rakkhasiyā pajāya.

SC 4Ms 22J_1124Msdiv 188Kāmaṃ SC 4Ms 22J_1124Msdiv 188Kāmaṃ kandantu kumbhaṇḍā,
Sabbe paṃsupisācakā;
Nālaṃ pisācā yuddhāya,
Mahatī sā vibhiṃsikā”ti.

SC 5SC 5Ms 22J_1125Ayakūṭajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
