137. Babbujātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_303Msdiv 137“Yattheko labhate babbu,
dutiyo tattha jāyati;
Tatiyo ca catuttho ca,
idaṃ te babbukā bilan”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_304Babbujātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
