342. Vānarajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.133Ms 22J_1096Msdiv 165“Asakkhiṃ vata attānaṃ,
uddhātuṃ udakā thalaṃ;
Na dānāhaṃ puna tuyhaṃ,
vasaṃ gacchāmi vārija.

SC 2Ms 22J_1097Msdiv 166Alametehi ambehi,
jambūhi panasehi ca;
Yāni pāraṃ samuddassa,
varaṃ mayhaṃ udumbaro.

SC 3Ms 22J_1098Msdiv 167Yo ca uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
na khippamanubujjhati;
Amittavasamanveti,
pacchā ca anutappati.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 3.134Ms 22J_1099Msdiv 168Yo ca uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
khippameva nibodhati;
Muccate sattusambādhā,
na ca pacchānutappatī”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1100Vānarajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
