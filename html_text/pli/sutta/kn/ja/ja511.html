511. Kiṃchandajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_3256SC 1Ms 22J_3256Msdiv 1“Kiṃchando kimadhippāyo,
eko sammasi ghammani;
Kiṃ patthayāno kiṃ esaṃ,
kena atthena brāhmaṇa”.

SC 2Ms 22J_3257SC 2Ms 22J_3257Msdiv 2“Yathā mahā vāridharo,
kumbho supariṇāhavā;
Tathūpamaṃ ambapakkaṃ,
vaṇṇagandharasuttamaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_3258SC 3Ms 22J_3258Msdiv 3Taṃ vuyhamānaṃ sotena,
disvānāmalamajjhime;
Pāṇīhi naṃ gahetvāna,
agyāyatanamāhariṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_3259SC 4Ms 22J_3259Msdiv 4Tato kadalipattesu,
nikkhipitvā sayaṃ ahaṃ;
Satthena naṃ vikappetvā,
khuppipāsaṃ ahāsi me.

SC 5Ms 22J_3260SC 5Ms 22J_3260Msdiv 5Sohaṃ apetadaratho,
Byantībhūto dukhakkhamo;
Assādaṃ nādhigacchāmi,
Phalesvaññesu kesuci.

SC 6Ms 22J_3261SC 6Ms 22J_3261Msdiv 6Sosetvā nūna maraṇaṃ,
taṃ mamaṃ āvahissati;
Ambaṃ yassa phalaṃ sādu,
madhuraggaṃ manoramaṃ;
Yamuddhariṃ vuyhamānaṃ,
udadhismā mahaṇṇave.

SC 7Ms 22J_3262SC 7Ms 22J_3262Msdiv 7Akkhātaṃ te mayā sabbaṃ,
yasmā upavasāmahaṃ;
Rammaṃ pati nisinnosmi,
puthulomāyutā puthu.

SC 8Ms 22J_3263Msdiv 8Tvañca kho SC 8Ms 22J_3263Msdiv 8Tvañca kho meva akkhāhi,
attānamapalāyini;
Kā vā tvamasi kalyāṇi,
kissa vā tvaṃ sumajjhime.

SC 9Ms 22J_3264Msdiv 9SC 9Ms 22J_3264Msdiv 9Ruppapaṭṭapalimaṭṭhīva,
byagghīva girisānujā;
Yā santi nāriyo devesu,
devānaṃ paricārikā.

SC 10Ms 22J_3265SC 10Ms 22J_3265Msdiv 10Yā ca manussalokasmiṃ,
Rūpenānvāgatitthiyo;
Rūpena te sadisī natthi,
Devesu gandhabbamanussaloke;
Puṭṭhāsi me cārupubbaṅgi,
Brūhi nāmañca bandhave”.

SC 11Ms 22J_3266SC 11Ms 22J_3266Msdiv 11“Yaṃ tvaṃ pati nisinnosi,
rammaṃ brāhmaṇa kosikiṃ;
Sāhaṃ bhusālayāvutthā,
varavārivahoghasā.

SC 12Ms 22J_3267SC 12Ms 22J_3267Msdiv 12Nānādumagaṇākiṇṇā,
bahukā girikandarā;
Mameva pamukhā honti,
abhisandanti pāvuse.

SC 13Ms 22J_3268SC 13Ms 22J_3268Msdiv 13Atho bahū vanatodā,
nīlavārivahindharā;
Bahukā nāgavittodā,
abhisandanti vārinā.

SC 14Ms 22J_3269SC 14Ms 22J_3269Msdiv 14Tā ambajambulabujā,
nīpā tālā cudumbarā;
Bahūni phalajātāni,
āvahanti abhiṇhaso.

SC 15Ms 22J_3270SC 15Ms 22J_3270Msdiv 15Yaṃ kiñci ubhato tīre,
phalaṃ patati ambuni;
Asaṃsayaṃ taṃ sotassa,
phalaṃ hoti vasānugaṃ.

SC 16Ms 22J_3271SC 16Ms 22J_3271Msdiv 16Etadaññāya medhāvi,
puthupañña suṇohi me;
Mā rocaya mabhisaṅgaṃ,
paṭisedha janādhipa.

SC 17Ms 22J_3272Msdiv 17Na vāhaṃ SC 17Ms 22J_3272Msdiv 17Na vāhaṃ vaḍḍhavaṃ maññe,
Yaṃ tvaṃ raṭṭhābhivaḍḍhana;
Āceyyamāno rājisi,
Maraṇaṃ abhikaṅkhasi.

SC 18Ms 22J_3273SC 18Ms 22J_3273Msdiv 18Tassa jānanti pitaro,
gandhabbā ca sadevakā;
Ye cāpi isayo loke,
saññatattā tapassino;
Asaṃsayaṃ tepi jānanti,
paṭṭhabhūtā yasassino”.

SC 19Ms 22J_3274SC 19Ms 22J_3274Msdiv 19“Evaṃ viditvā vidū sabbadhammaṃ,
Viddhaṃsanaṃ cavanaṃ jīvitassa;
Na cīyatī tassa narassa pāpaṃ,
Sace na ceteti vadhāya tassa.

SC 20Ms 22J_3275SC 20Ms 22J_3275Msdiv 20Isipūgasamaññāte,
evaṃ lokyā viditā sati;
Anariyaparisambhāse,
pāpakammaṃ jigīsasi.

SC 21Ms 22J_3276SC 21Ms 22J_3276Msdiv 21Sace ahaṃ marissāmi,
tīre te puthusussoṇi;
Asaṃsayaṃ taṃ asiloko,
mayi pete āgamissati.

SC 22Ms 22J_3277SC 22Ms 22J_3277Msdiv 22Tasmā hi pāpakaṃ kammaṃ,
rakkhasseva sumajjhime;
Mā taṃ sabbo jano pacchā,
pakuṭṭhāyi mayi mate”.

SC 23Ms 22J_3278SC 23Ms 22J_3278Msdiv 23“Aññātametaṃ avisayhasāhi,
Attānamambañca dadāmi te taṃ;
So duccaje kāmaguṇe pahāya,
Santiñca dhammañca adhiṭṭhitosi.

SC 24Ms 22J_3279SC 24Ms 22J_3279Msdiv 24Yo hitvā pubbasaññogaṃ,
pacchā saṃyojane ṭhito;
Adhammañceva carati,
pāpañcassa pavaḍḍhati.

SC 25Ms 22J_3280SC 25Ms 22J_3280Msdiv 25Ehi taṃ pāpayissāmi,
kāmaṃ appossuko bhava;
Upānayāmi sītasmiṃ,
viharāhi anussuko.

SC 26Ms 22J_3281SC 26Ms 22J_3281Msdiv 26Taṃ puppharasamattebhi,
vakkaṅgehi arindama;
Koñcā mayūrā diviyā,
kolaṭṭhimadhusāḷikā;
Kūjitā haṃsapūgehi,
kokilettha pabodhare.

SC 27Ms 22J_3282Msdiv 27Ambettha SC 27Ms 22J_3282Msdiv 27Ambettha vippasākhaggā,
palālakhalasannibhā;
Kosambasaḷalā nīpā,
pakkatālavilambino”.

SC 28Ms 22J_3283SC 28Ms 22J_3283Msdiv 28“Mālī tiriṭī kāyūrī,
aṅgadī candanussado;
Rattiṃ tvaṃ paricāresi,
divā vedesi vedanaṃ.

SC 29Ms 22J_3284SC 29Ms 22J_3284Msdiv 29Soḷasitthisahassāni,
yā temā paricārikā;
Evaṃ mahānubhāvosi,
abbhuto lomahaṃsano.

SC 30Ms 22J_3285SC 30Ms 22J_3285Msdiv 30Kiṃ kammamakarī pubbe,
pāpaṃ attadukhāvahaṃ;
Yaṃ karitvā manussesu,
piṭṭhimaṃsāni khādasi”.

SC 31Ms 22J_3286SC 31Ms 22J_3286Msdiv 31“Ajjhenāni paṭiggayha,
kāmesu gadhito ahaṃ;
Acariṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ,
paresaṃ ahitāyahaṃ.

SC 32Ms 22J_3287SC 32Ms 22J_3287Msdiv 32Yo piṭṭhimaṃsiko hoti,
evaṃ ukkacca khādati;
Yathāhaṃ ajja khādāmi,
piṭṭhimaṃsāni attano”ti.
