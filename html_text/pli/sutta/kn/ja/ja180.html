180. Duddadajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.86Ms 22J_433Msdiv 59“Duddadaṃ dadamānānaṃ,
dukkaraṃ kamma kubbataṃ;
Asanto nānukubbanti,
sataṃ dhammo durannayo”.

SC 2Ms 22J_434Msdiv 60“Tasmā satañca asataṃ,
nānā hoti ito gati;
Asanto nirayaṃ yanti,
santo saggaparāyaṇāti.

SC 3Ms 22J_435Duddadajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kalyāṇavaggo tatiyo.

SC 4Ms 22J_436Tassuddānaṃ

SC 5Ms 22J_437Susamaññamigādhibhū māṇavako,
Vāripahūtarūpādiccupaṭṭhānā;
Sakaḷāyasatindukapaṅka puna,
Satadhamma sududdadakena dasā”ti.
