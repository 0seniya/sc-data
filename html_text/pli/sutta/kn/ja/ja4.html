4. Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.122Ms 22J_8Msdiv 4“Appakenapi medhāvī,
pābhatena vicakkhaṇo;
Samuṭṭhāpeti attānaṃ,
aṇuṃ aggiṃva sandhaman”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_9Cūḷaseṭṭhijātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
