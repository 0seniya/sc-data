225. Khantivaṇṇajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.207Ms 22J_582Msdiv 149“Atthi me puriso deva,
sabbakiccesu byāvaṭo;
Tassa cekoparādhatthi,
tattha tvaṃ kinti maññasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_583Msdiv 150“Amhākampatthi puriso,
ediso idha vijjati;
Dullabho aṅgasampanno,
khantirasmāka ruccatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_584Khantivaṇṇajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
