415. Kummāsapiṇḍijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.408Ms 22J_1647Msdiv 142“Na kiratthi anomadassisu,
Pāricariyā buddhesu appikā;
Sukkhāya aloṇikāya ca,
Passaphalaṃ kummāsapiṇḍiyā.

SC 2Ms 22J_1648Msdiv 143Hatthigavassā cime bahū,
Dhanadhaññaṃ pathavī ca kevalā;
Nāriyo cimā accharūpamā,
Passa phalaṃ kummāsapiṇḍiyā”.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 3.410Ms 22J_1649Msdiv 144“Abhikkhaṇaṃ rājakuñjara,
Gāthā bhāsasi kosalādhipa;
Pucchāmi taṃ raṭṭhavaḍḍhana,
Bāḷhaṃ pītimano pabhāsasi”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1650Msdiv 145“Imasmiññeva nagare,
kule aññatare ahuṃ;
Parakammakaro āsiṃ,
bhatako sīlasaṃvuto.

SC 5Ms 22J_1651Msdiv 146Kammāya nikkhamantohaṃ,
caturo samaṇeddasaṃ;
Ācārasīlasampanne,
sītibhūte anāsave.

SC 6Ms 22J_1652Msdiv 147Tesu cittaṃ pasādetvā,
nisīdetvā paṇṇasanthate;
Adaṃ buddhāna kummāsaṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

SC 7Ms 22J_1653Msdiv 148Tassa kammassa kusalassa,
idaṃ me edisaṃ phalaṃ;
Anubhomi idaṃ rajjaṃ,
phītaṃ dharaṇimuttamaṃ”.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 3.412Ms 22J_1654Msdiv 149“Dadaṃ bhuñja mā ca pamādo,
Cakkaṃ vattaya kosalādhipa;
Mā rāja adhammiko ahu,
Dhammaṃ pālaya kosalādhipa”.

SC 9Ms 22J_1655Msdiv 150“Sohaṃ tadeva punappunaṃ,
Vaṭumaṃ ācarissāmi sobhane;
Ariyācaritaṃ sukosale,
Arahanto me manāpāva passituṃ.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 3.413Ms 22J_1656Msdiv 151Devī viya accharūpamā,
Majjhe nārigaṇassa sobhasi;
Kiṃ kammamakāsi bhaddakaṃ,
Kenāsi vaṇṇavatī sukosale”.

SC 11Ms 22J_1657Msdiv 152“Ambaṭṭhakulassa khattiya,
Dāsyāhaṃ parapesiyā ahuṃ;
Saññatā ca dhammajīvinī,
Sīlavatī ca apāpadassanā.

SC 12Ms 22J_1658Msdiv 153Uddhaṭabhattaṃ ahaṃ tadā,
Caramānassa adāsi bhikkhuno;
Vittā sumanā sayaṃ ahaṃ,
Tassa kammassa phalaṃ mamedisan”ti.

SC 13Ms 22J_1659Kummāsapiṇḍijātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
