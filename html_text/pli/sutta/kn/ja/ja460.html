460. Yudhañcayajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2184Msdiv 73“Mittāmaccaparibyūḷhaṃ,
ahaṃ vande rathesabhaṃ;
Pabbajissāmahaṃ rāja,
taṃ devo anumaññatu”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2185Msdiv 74“Sace te ūnaṃ kāmehi,
ahaṃ paripūrayāmi te;
Yo taṃ hiṃsati vāremi,
mā pabbaja pabbaja yudhañcaya”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2186Msdiv 75“Na matthi ūnaṃ kāmehi,
hiṃsitā me na vijjati;
Dīpañca kātumicchāmi,
yaṃ jarā nābhikīrati”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2187Msdiv 76“Putto vā pitaraṃ yāce,
pitā vā puttamorasaṃ;
Negamo taṃ yāce tāta,
mā pabbaja yudhañcaya”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2188Msdiv 77“Mā maṃ deva nivārehi,
pabbajantaṃ rathesabha;
Māhaṃ kāmehi sammatto,
jarāya vasamanvagū”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2189Msdiv 78“Ahaṃ taṃ tāta yācāmi,
ahaṃ putta nivāraye;
Ciraṃ taṃ daṭṭhumicchāmi,
mā pabbaja yudhañcaya”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2190Msdiv 79“Ussāvova tiṇaggamhi,
sūriyuggamanaṃ pati;
Evamāyu manussānaṃ,
mā maṃ amma nivāraya”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2191Msdiv 80“Taramāno imaṃ yānaṃ,
āropetu rathesabha;
Mā me mātā tarantassa,
antarāyakarā ahu”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2192Msdiv 81“Abhidhāvatha bhaddante,
suññaṃ hessati rammakaṃ;
Yudhañcayo anuññāto,
sabbadattena rājinā.

SC 10Ms 22J_2193Msdiv 82Yohu seṭṭho sahassassa,
yuvā kañcanasannibho;
Soyaṃ kumāro pabbajito,
kāsāyavasano balī”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2194Msdiv 83“Ubho kumārā pabbajitā,
yudhañcayo yudhiṭṭhilo;
Pahāya mātāpitaro,
saṅgaṃ chetvāna maccuno”ti.

SC 12Ms 22J_2195Yudhañcayajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
