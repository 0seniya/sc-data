214. Puṇṇanadījātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.174Ms 22J_547Msdiv 127“Puṇṇaṃ nadiṃ yena ca peyyamāhu,
Jātaṃ yavaṃ yena ca guyhamāhu;
Dūraṃ gataṃ yena ca avhayanti,
So tyāgato handa ca bhuñja brāhmaṇa”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 2.175Ms 22J_548Msdiv 128“Yato maṃ saratī rājā,
vāyasampi pahetave;
Haṃsā koñcā mayūrā ca,
asatīyeva pāpiyā”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_549Puṇṇanadījātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
