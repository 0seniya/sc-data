472. Mahāpadumajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 4.192Ms 22J_2352Msdiv 106“Nādaṭṭhā parato dosaṃ,
aṇuṃthūlāni sabbaso;
Issaro paṇaye daṇḍaṃ,
sāmaṃ appaṭivekkhiya.

SC 2Ms 22J_2353Msdiv 107Yo ca appaṭivekkhitvā,
daṇḍaṃ kubbati khattiyo;
Sakaṇṭakaṃ so gilati,
jaccandhova samakkhikaṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_2354Msdiv 108Adaṇḍiyaṃ daṇḍayati,
daṇḍiyañca adaṇḍiyaṃ;
Andhova visamaṃ maggaṃ,
na jānāti samāsamaṃ.

SC 4Ms 22J_2355Msdiv 109Yo ca etāni ṭhānāni,
Aṇuṃthūlāni sabbaso;
Sudiṭṭhamanusāseyya,
Sa ve voharitumarahati.

SC 5Ms 22J_2356Msdiv 110Nekantamudunā sakkā,
Ekantatikhiṇena vā;
Attaṃ mahante mahante ṭhapetuṃ,
Tasmā ubhayamācare.

SC 6Ms 22J_2357Msdiv 111Paribhūto mudu hoti,
atitikkho ca veravā;
Etañca ubhayaṃ ñatvā,
anumajjhaṃ samācare.

SC 7Ms 22J_2358Msdiv 112Bahumpi ratto bhāseyya,
duṭṭhopi bahu bhāsati;
Na itthikāraṇā rāja,
puttaṃ ghātetumarahasi”.

SC 8PTS vp Pali 4.193Ms 22J_2359Msdiv 113“Sabbova loko Sabbova loko ekato,
Itthī ca ayamekikā;
Tenāhaṃ paṭipajjissaṃ,
Gacchatha pakkhipatheva taṃ”.

SC 9PTS vp Pali 4.195Ms 22J_2360Msdiv 114“Anekatāle narake,
gambhīre ca suduttare;
Pātito giriduggasmiṃ,
kena tvaṃ tattha nāmari”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2361Msdiv 115“Nāgo jātaphaṇo tattha,
thāmavā girisānujo;
Paccaggahi maṃ bhogehi,
tenāhaṃ tattha nāmariṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2362Msdiv 116“Ehi taṃ paṭinessāmi,
rājaputta sakaṃ gharaṃ;
Rajjaṃ kārehi bhaddante,
kiṃ araññe karissasi”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2363Msdiv 117“Yathā gilitvā baḷisaṃ,
Uddhareyya salohitaṃ;
Uddharitvā sukhī assa,
Evaṃ passāmi Evaṃ passāmi attanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2364Msdiv 118“Kiṃ nu tvaṃ baḷisaṃ brūsi,
kiṃ tvaṃ brūsi salohitaṃ;
Kiṃ nu tvaṃ ubbhataṃ brūsi,
taṃ me akkhāhi pucchito”.

SC 14Ms 22J_2365Msdiv 119“Kāmāhaṃ baḷisaṃ brūmi,
hatthiassaṃ salohitaṃ;
Cattāhaṃ ubbhataṃ brūmi,
evaṃ jānāhi khattiya”.

SC 15PTS vp Pali 4.196Ms 22J_2366Msdiv 120“Ciñcamāṇavikā mātā,
devadatto ca me pitā;
Ānando paṇḍito nāgo,
sāriputto ca devatā;
Rājaputto ahaṃ āsiṃ,
evaṃ dhāretha jātakan”ti.

SC 16Ms 22J_2367Mahāpadumajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
