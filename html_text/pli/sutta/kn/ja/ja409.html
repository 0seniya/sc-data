409. Daḷhadhammajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1597Msdiv 98“Ahañce daḷhadhammassa,
vahantī nābhirādhayiṃ;
Dharantī urasi sallaṃ,
yuddhe vikkantacārinī.

SC 2Ms 22J_1598Msdiv 99Nūna rājā na jānāti,
mama vikkamaporisaṃ;
Saṅgāme sukatantāni,
dūtavippahitāni ca.

SC 3Ms 22J_1599Msdiv 100Sā nūnāhaṃ marissāmi,
abandhu aparāyinī;
Tadā hi kumbhakārassa,
dinnā chakaṇahārikā”.

SC 4Ms 22J_1600Msdiv 101“Yāvatāsīsatī poso,
tāvadeva pavīṇati;
Atthāpāye jahanti naṃ,
oṭṭhibyādhiṃva khattiyo.

SC 5Ms 22J_1601Msdiv 102Yo pubbe katakalyāṇo,
katattho nāvabujjhati;
Atthā tassa palujjanti,
ye honti abhipatthitā.

SC 6Ms 22J_1602Msdiv 103Yo pubbe katakalyāṇo,
katattho manubujjhati;
Atthā tassa pavaḍḍhanti,
ye honti abhipatthitā.

SC 7Ms 22J_1603Msdiv 104Taṃ vo vadāmi bhaddante,
yāvantettha samāgatā;
Sabbe kataññuno hotha,
ciraṃ saggamhi ṭhassathā”ti.

SC 8Ms 22J_1604Daḷhadhammajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
