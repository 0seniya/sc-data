200. Sādhusīlajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_497Msdiv 99“Sarīradabyaṃ vuḍḍhabyaṃ,
sojaccaṃ sādhusīliyaṃ;
Brāhmaṇaṃ teva pucchāma,
kaṃ nu tesaṃ kaṃ nu tesaṃ vanimhase”.

SC 2Ms 22J_498Msdiv 100“Attho atthi sarīrasmiṃ,
vuḍḍhabyassa namo kare;
Attho atthi sujātasmiṃ,
sīlaṃ asmāka ruccatī”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_499Sādhusīlajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Ruhakavaggo pañcamo.

SC 4Ms 22J_500Tassuddānaṃ

SC 5Ms 22J_501Apiruhaka rūpavatī musalo,
Pavasanti sapañcamapokkharaṇī;
Atha muttimavāṇija umhayate,
Ciraāgata koṭṭha sarīra dasāti.
