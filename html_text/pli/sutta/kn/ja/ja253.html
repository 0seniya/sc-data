253. Maṇikaṇṭhajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_678Msdiv 7“Mamannapānaṃ vipulaṃ uḷāraṃ,
Uppajjatīmassa maṇissa hetu;
Taṃ te na dassaṃ atiyācakosi,
Na cāpi te assamamāgamissaṃ.

SC 2Ms 22J_679Msdiv 8Susū yathā sakkharadhotapāṇī,
Tāsesi maṃ selaṃ yācamāno;
Taṃ te na dassaṃ atiyācakosi,
Na cāpi te assamamāgamissaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_680Msdiv 9“Na taṃ yāce yassa piyaṃ jigīse,
Desso hoti atiyācanāya;
Nāgo maṇiṃ yācito brāhmaṇena,
Adassanaṃyeva tadajjhagamā”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_681Maṇikaṇṭhajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
