523. Alambusājātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 5.153Ms 23J_98Msdiv 95Atha bravi brahā indo,
vatrabhū jayataṃ pitā;
Devakaññaṃ parābhetvā,
sudhammāyaṃ alambusaṃ.

SC 2Ms 23J_99Msdiv 96“Misse devā taṃ yācanti,
tāvatiṃsā saindakā;
Isippalobhane gaccha,
isisiṅgaṃ alambuse.

SC 3Ms 23J_100Msdiv 97Purāyaṃ amhe acceti,
vattavā brahmacariyavā;
Nibbānābhirato vuddho,
tassa maggāni āvara”.

SC 4Ms 23J_101Msdiv 98“Devarāja kimeva tvaṃ,
mameva tuvaṃ sikkhasi;
Isippalobhane gaccha,
santi aññāpi accharā.

SC 5Ms 23J_102Msdiv 99Mādisiyo pavarā ceva,
asoke nandane vane;
Tāsampi hotu pariyāyo,
tāpi yantu palobhanā”.

SC 6PTS vp Pali 5.154Ms 23J_103Msdiv 100“Addhā hi saccaṃ bhaṇasi,
santi aññāpi accharā;
Tādisiyo pavarā ceva,
asoke nandane vane.

SC 7Ms 23J_104Msdiv 101Na tā evaṃ pajānanti,
pāricariyaṃ pumaṃ gatā;
Yādisaṃ tvaṃ pajānāsi,
nāri sabbaṅgasobhane.

SC 8Ms 23J_105Msdiv 102Tvameva gaccha kalyāṇi,
itthīnaṃ pavarā casi;
Taveva vaṇṇarūpena,
savasamānayissasi”.

SC 9Ms 23J_106Msdiv 103“Na vāhaṃ na gamissāmi,
devarājena pesitā;
Vibhemi cetaṃ āsāduṃ,
uggatejo hi brāhmaṇo.

SC 10Ms 23J_107Msdiv 104Aneke nirayaṃ pattā,
isimāsādiyā janā;
Āpannā mohasaṃsāraṃ,
tasmā lomāni haṃsaye”.

SC 11Ms 23J_108Msdiv 105Idaṃ vatvāna pakkāmi,
accharā kāmavaṇṇinī;
Missā missitumicchantī,
isisiṅgaṃ alambusā.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 5.155Ms 23J_109Msdiv 106Sā ca taṃ vanamogayha,
isisiṅgena rakkhitaṃ;
Bimbajālakasañchannaṃ,
samantā addhayojanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 23J_110Msdiv 107Pātova pātarāsamhi,
udaṇhasamayaṃ pati;
Aggiṭṭhaṃ parimajjantaṃ,
isisiṅgaṃ upāgami.

SC 14Ms 23J_111Msdiv 108“Kā nu vijjurivābhāsi,
osadhī viya tārakā;
Vicittahatthābharaṇā,
āmuttamaṇikuṇḍalā.

SC 15Ms 23J_112Msdiv 109Ādiccavaṇṇasaṅkāsā,
hemacandanagandhinī;
Saññatūrū mahāmāyā,
kumārī cārudassanā.

SC 16Ms 23J_113Msdiv 110Vilaggā mudukā suddhā,
pādā te suppatiṭṭhitā;
Gamanā kāmanīyā te,
harantiyeva me mano.

SC 17Ms 23J_114Msdiv 111Anupubbāva te ūrū,
nāganāsasamūpamā;
Vimaṭṭhā tuyhaṃ sussoṇī,
akkhassa phalakaṃ yathā.

SC 18Ms 23J_115Msdiv 112Uppalasseva kiñjakkhā,
nābhi te sādhu saṇṭhitā;
Pūrā kaṇhañjanasseva,
dūrato paṭidissati.

SC 19Ms 23J_116Msdiv 113Duvidhā jātā urajā,
avaṇṭā sādhupaccudā;
Payodharā apatitā,
aḍḍhalābusamā thanā.

SC 20Ms 23J_117Msdiv 114Dīghā kambutalābhāsā,
gīvā eṇeyyakā yathā;
Paṇḍarāvaraṇā vaggu,
catutthamanasannibhā.

SC 21PTS vp Pali 5.156Ms 23J_118Msdiv 115Uddhaggā ca adhaggā ca,
dumaggaparimajjitā;
Duvijā nelasambhūtā,
dantā tava sudassanā.

SC 22Ms 23J_119Msdiv 116Apaṇḍarā lohitantā,
jiñjūkaphalasannibhā;
Āyatā ca visālā ca,
nettā tava sudassanā.

SC 23Ms 23J_120Msdiv 117Nātidīghā susammaṭṭhā,
kanakabyā samocitā;
Uttamaṅgaruhā tuyhaṃ,
kesā candanagandhikā.

SC 24Ms 23J_121Msdiv 118Yāvatā kasigorakkhā,
vāṇijānaṃ ca yā gati;
Isīnañca parakkantaṃ,
saññatānaṃ tapassinaṃ.

SC 25Ms 23J_122Msdiv 119Na te samasamaṃ passe,
asmiṃ pathavimaṇḍale;
Ko vā tvaṃ kassa vā putto,
kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 26PTS vp Pali 5.157Ms 23J_123Msdiv 120“Na pañhakālo bhaddante,
kassapevaṃ gate sati;
Ehi samma ramissāma,
ubho asmākamassame;
Ehi taṃ upagūhissaṃ,
ratīnaṃ kusalo bhava”.

SC 27Ms 23J_124Msdiv 121Idaṃ vatvāna pakkāmi,
accharā kāmavaṇṇinī;
Missā missitumicchantī,
isisiṅgaṃ alambusā.

SC 28Ms 23J_125Msdiv 122So ca vegena nikkhamma,
Chetvā dandhaparakkamaṃ;
Tamuttamāsu veṇīsu,
Ajjhappatto parāmasi.

SC 29Ms 23J_126Msdiv 123Tamudāvatta kalyāṇī,
palissaji susobhanā;
Cavitamhi brahmacariyā,
yathā taṃ atha tositā.

SC 30Ms 23J_127Msdiv 124Manasā agamā indaṃ,
vasantaṃ nandane vane;
Tassā saṅkappamaññāya,
maghavā devakuñjaro.

SC 31Ms 23J_128Msdiv 125Pallaṅkaṃ pahiṇī khippaṃ,
sovaṇṇaṃ sopavāhanaṃ;
Sauttaracchadapaññāsaṃ,
sahassapaṭiyatthataṃ.

SC 32Ms 23J_129Msdiv 126Tamenaṃ tattha dhāresi,
ure katvāna sobhanā;
Yathā ekamuhuttaṃva,
tīṇi vassāni dhārayi.

SC 33Ms 23J_130Msdiv 127Vimado tīhi vassehi,
pabujjhitvāna brāhmaṇo;
Addasāsi haritarukkhe,
samantā aggiyāyanaṃ.

SC 34Ms 23J_131Msdiv 128Navapattavanaṃ phullaṃ,
kokilaggaṇaghositaṃ;
Samantā paviloketvā,
rudaṃ assūni vattayi.

SC 35Ms 23J_132Msdiv 129“Na juhe na jape mante,
aggihuttaṃ pahāpitaṃ;
Ko nu me pāricariyāya,
pubbe cittaṃ palobhayi.

SC 36Ms 23J_133Msdiv 130Araññe me viharato,
yo me tejā ha sambhutaṃ;
Nānāratnaparipūraṃ,
nāvaṃva gaṇhi aṇṇave”.

SC 37PTS vp Pali 5.159Ms 23J_134Msdiv 131“Ahaṃ te pāricariyāya,
devarājena pesitā;
Avadhiṃ cittaṃ cittena,
pamādo tvaṃ na bujjhasi”.

SC 38Ms 23J_135Msdiv 132“Imāni kira maṃ tāto,
kassapo anusāsati;
Kamalāsadisitthiyo,
tāyo bujjhesi māṇava.

SC 39Ms 23J_136Msdiv 133Uregaṇḍāyo bujjhesi,
tāyo bujjhesi māṇava;
Iccānusāsi maṃ tāto,
yathā maṃ anukampako.

SC 40PTS vp Pali 5.160Ms 23J_137Msdiv 134Tassāhaṃ vacanaṃ nākaṃ,
pitu vuddhassa sāsanaṃ;
Araññe nimmanussamhi,
svajja jhāyāmi ekako.

SC 41Ms 23J_138Msdiv 135Sohaṃ tathā karissāmi,
dhiratthu jīvitena me;
Puna vā tādiso hessaṃ,
maraṇaṃ me bhavissati”.

SC 42Ms 23J_139Msdiv 136Tassa tejaṃ vīriyañca,
Dhitiṃ ñatvā Dhitiṃ ñatvā avaṭṭhitaṃ;
Sirasā aggahī pāde,
Isisiṅgaṃ alambusā.

SC 43Ms 23J_140Msdiv 137“Mā me kujjha mahāvīra,
mā me kujjha mahāise;
Mahā attho mayā ciṇṇo,
tidasānaṃ yasassinaṃ;
Tayā saṅkampitaṃ āsi,
sabbaṃ devapuraṃ tadā”.

SC 44Ms 23J_141Msdiv 138“Tāvatiṃsā ca ye devā,
tidasānañca vāsavo;
Tvañca bhadde sukhī hohi,
gaccha kaññe yathāsukhaṃ”.

SC 45PTS vp Pali 5.161Ms 23J_142Msdiv 139Tassa pāde gahetvāna,
katvā ca naṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,
tamhā ṭhānā apakkami.

SC 46Ms 23J_143Msdiv 140Yo ca tassāsi pallaṅko,
sovaṇṇo sopavāhano;
Sauttaracchadapaññāso,
sahassapaṭiyatthato;
Tameva pallaṅkamāruyha,
agā devāna santike.

SC 47Ms 23J_144Msdiv 141Tamokkamiva āyantiṃ,
jalantiṃ vijjutaṃ yathā;
Patīto sumano vitto,
devindo adadā varaṃ.

SC 48Ms 23J_145Msdiv 142“Varañce me ado sakka,
sabbabhūtānamissara;
Nisippalobhikā gacche,
etaṃ sakka varaṃ vare”ti.

SC 49Ms 23J_146Alambusājātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
