531. Kusajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 5.289Ms 23J_582Msdiv 1“Idaṃ te raṭṭhaṃ sadhanaṃ sayoggaṃ,
Sakāyuraṃ sabbakāmūpapannaṃ;
Idaṃ te rajjaṃ anusāsa amma,
Gacchāmahaṃ yattha piyā pabhāvatī”.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 5.293Ms 23J_583Msdiv 2“Anujjubhūtena haraṃ mahantaṃ,

Paṭigaccha tvaṃ khippaṃ kusāvatiṃ kusa,
Nicchāmi dubbaṇṇamahaṃ vasantaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 23J_585Msdiv 4Pabhāvatī vaṇṇapalobhito tava,
