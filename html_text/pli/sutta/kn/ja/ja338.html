338. Thusajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_1074Msdiv 149“Viditaṃ thusaṃ undurānaṃ,
Viditaṃ pana taṇḍulaṃ;
Thusaṃ thusaṃ vivajjetvā,
Taṇḍulaṃ pana khādare.

SC 2Ms 22J_1075Msdiv 150Yā mantanā araññasmiṃ,
yā ca gāme nikaṇṇikā;
Yañcetaṃ iti cīti ca,
etampi viditaṃ mayā.

SC 3Ms 22J_1076Msdiv 151Dhammena kira jātassa,
pitā puttassa makkaṭo;
Daharasseva santassa,
dantehi phalamacchidā.

SC 4Ms 22J_1077Msdiv 152Yametaṃ parisappasi,
ajakāṇova sāsape;
Yopāyaṃ heṭṭhato seti,
etampi viditaṃ mayā”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_1078Thusajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
