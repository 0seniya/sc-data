249. Sālakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.267Ms 22J_659Msdiv 198“Ekaputtako bhavissasi,
Tvañca no hessasi issaro kule;
Oroha dumasmā sālaka,
Ehi dāni gharakaṃ vajemase”.

SC 2Ms 22J_660Msdiv 199“Nanu maṃ suhadayoti maññasi,
Yañca maṃ hanasi veḷuyaṭṭhiyā;
Pakkambavane ramāmase,
Gaccha tvaṃ gharakaṃ yathāsukhan”ti.

SC 3Ms 22J_661Sālakajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
