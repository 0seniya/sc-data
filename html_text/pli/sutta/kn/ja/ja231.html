231. Upāhanajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_602SC 1Ms 22J_602Msdiv 161“Yathāpi kītā purisassupāhanā,
Sukhassa atthāya dukhaṃ udabbahe;
Ghammābhitattā thalasā papīḷitā,
Tasseva pāde purisassa khādare.

SC 2Ms 22J_603SC 2Ms 22J_603Msdiv 162Evameva yo dukkulīno anariyo,
Tammāka vijjañca sutañca ādiya;
Tameva so tattha sutena khādati,
Anariyo vuccati dupāhanūpamo”ti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 22J_604Upāhanajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
