265. Khurappajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_728Msdiv 43“Disvā khurappe dhanuveganunne,
Khagge gahīte tikhiṇe teladhote;
Tasmiṃ bhayasmiṃ maraṇe viyūḷhe,
Kasmā nu te nāhu chambhitattaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_729Msdiv 44“Disvā khurappe dhanuveganunne,
Khagge gahīte tikhiṇe teladhote;
Tasmiṃ bhayasmiṃ maraṇe viyūḷhe,
Vedaṃ alatthaṃ vipulaṃ uḷāraṃ.

SC 3Ms 22J_730Msdiv 45So vedajāto ajjhabhaviṃ amitte,
Pubbeva me jīvitamāsi cattaṃ;
Na hi jīvite ālayaṃ kubbamāno,
Sūro kayirā sūrakiccaṃ kadācī”ti.

SC 4Ms 22J_731Khurappajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
