72. Sīlavahatthijātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.322Ms 22J_159Msdiv 72“Akataññussa posassa,
niccaṃ vivaradassino;
Sabbañce pathaviṃ dajjā,
neva naṃ abhirādhaye”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_160Sīlavahatthijātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
