139. Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.483Ms 22J_307Msdiv 139“Akkhī bhinnā paṭo naṭṭho,
sakhigehe ca bhaṇḍanaṃ;
Ubhato paduṭṭhā kammantā,
udakamhi thalamhi cā”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_308Ubhatobhaṭṭhajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
