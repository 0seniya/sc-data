475. Phandanajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2398Msdiv 14“SC 1Ms 22J_2398Msdiv 14“Kuṭhārihattho puriso,
vanamogayha tiṭṭhasi;
Puṭṭho me samma akkhāhi,
kiṃ dāruṃ chetumicchasi”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2399Msdiv 15“SC 2Ms 22J_2399Msdiv 15“Isso vanāni carasi,
samāni visamāni ca;
Puṭṭho me samma akkhāhi,
kiṃ dāruṃ nemiyā daḷhaṃ”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2400SC 3Ms 22J_2400Msdiv 16“Neva sālo na khadiro,
nāssakaṇṇo kuto dhavo;
Rukkho ca phandano nāma,
taṃ dāruṃ nemiyā daḷhaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2401SC 4Ms 22J_2401Msdiv 17“Kīdisānissa pattāni,
khandho vā pana kīdiso;
Puṭṭho me samma akkhāhi,
yathā jānemu phandanaṃ”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2402SC 5Ms 22J_2402Msdiv 18“Yassa sākhā palambanti,
namanti na ca bhañjare;
So rukkho phandano nāma,
yassa mūle ahaṃ ṭhito.

SC 6Ms 22J_2403SC 6Ms 22J_2403Msdiv 19Arānaṃ cakkanābhīnaṃ,
īsānemirathassa ca;
Sabbassa te kammaniyo,
ayaṃ hessati phandano”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2404SC 7Ms 22J_2404Msdiv 20“Iti phandanarukkhopi,
tāvade ajjhabhāsatha;
Mayhampi vacanaṃ atthi,
bhāradvāja suṇohi me.

SC 8Ms 22J_2405Msdiv 21Issassa SC 8Ms 22J_2405Msdiv 21Issassa upakkhandhamhā,
Ukkacca caturaṅgulaṃ;
Tena nemiṃ pasāresi,
Evaṃ daḷhataraṃ siyā.

SC 9Ms 22J_2406SC 9Ms 22J_2406Msdiv 22Iti phandanarukkhopi,
veraṃ appesi tāvade;
Jātānañca ajātānaṃ,
issānaṃ dukkhamāvahi.

SC 10Ms 22J_2407Msdiv 23SC 10Ms 22J_2407Msdiv 23Iccevaṃ phandano issaṃ,
isso ca pana phandanaṃ;
Aññamaññaṃ vivādena,
aññamaññamaghātayuṃ.

SC 11Ms 22J_2408SC 11Ms 22J_2408Msdiv 24Evameva manussānaṃ,
vivādo yattha jāyati;
Mayūranaccaṃ naccanti,
yathā te issaphandanā.

SC 12Ms 22J_2409Msdiv 25Taṃ vo vadāmi SC 12Ms 22J_2409Msdiv 25Taṃ vo vadāmi bhaddaṃ vo,
yāvantettha samāgatā;
Sammodatha mā vivadatha,
mā hotha issaphandanā.

SC 13Ms 22J_2410Msdiv 26SC 13Ms 22J_2410Msdiv 26Sāmaggimeva sikkhetha,
buddhehetaṃ pasaṃsitaṃ;
Sāmaggirato dhammaṭṭho,
yogakkhemā na dhaṃsatī”ti.
