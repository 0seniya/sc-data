104. Mittavindakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 1.414Ms 22J_231Msdiv 104“Catubbhi aṭṭhajjhagamā,
aṭṭhāhipi ca soḷasa;
Soḷasāhi ca bāttiṃsa,
atricchaṃ cakkamāsado;
Icchāhatassa posassa,
cakkaṃ bhamati matthake”ti.

SC 2Ms 22J_232Mittavindakajātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
