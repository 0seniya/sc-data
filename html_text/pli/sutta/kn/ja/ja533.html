1 Cūḷahaṃsajātaka

SC 1Ms 23J_771Msdiv 1“Sumukha anupacinantā,
pakkamanti vihaṅgamā;
Gaccha tuvampi mā kaṅkhi,
natthi baddhe sahāyatā”.

SC 2Ms 23J_772Msdiv 2“Gacche vāhaṃ na vā gacche,
na tena amaro siyaṃ;
Sukhitaṃ taṃ upāsitvā,
dukkhitaṃ taṃ kathaṃ jahe.

SC 3Ms 23J_773Msdiv 3Maraṇaṃ vā tayā saddhiṃ,
jīvitaṃ vā tayā vinā;
Tadeva maraṇaṃ seyyo,
yañce jīve tayā vinā.

SC 4Ms 23J_774Msdiv 4Nesa dhammo mahārāja,
yaṃ taṃ evaṃ gataṃ jahe;
Yā gati tuyhaṃ sā mayhaṃ,
ruccate vihagādhipa”.

SC 5Ms 23J_775Msdiv 5“Kā nu pāsena baddhassa,
gati aññā mahānasā;
Sā kathaṃ cetayānassa,
muttassa tava ruccati.

SC 6Ms 23J_776Msdiv 6Kaṃ vā tvaṃ passase atthaṃ,
mama tuyhañca pakkhima;
Ñātīnaṃ vāvasiṭṭhānaṃ,
ubhinnaṃ jīvitakkhaye.

SC 7Ms 23J_777Msdiv 7Yaṃ na kañcanadepiñcha,
Andhena tamasā gataṃ;
Tādise sañcajaṃ pāṇaṃ,
Kamatthamabhijotaye”.

SC 8Ms 23J_778Msdiv 8“Kathaṃ nu patataṃ seṭṭha,
Dhamme atthaṃ na bujjhasi;
Dhammo apacito santo,
Atthaṃ dasseti pāṇinaṃ.

SC 9Ms 23J_779Msdiv 9Sohaṃ dhammaṃ apekkhāno,
dhammā catthaṃ samuṭṭhitaṃ;
Bhattiñca tayi sampassaṃ,
nāvakaṅkhāmi jīvitaṃ.

SC 10Ms 23J_780Msdiv 10Addhā eso sataṃ dhammo,
yo mitto mittamāpade;
Na caje jīvitassāpi,
hetudhammamanussaraṃ”.

SC 11Ms 23J_781Msdiv 11“Svāyaṃ dhammo ca te ciṇṇo,
bhatti ca viditā mayi;
Kāmaṃ karassu mayhetaṃ,
gacchevānumato mayā.

SC 12Ms 23J_782Msdiv 12Api tvevaṃ gate kāle,
yaṃ khaṇḍaṃ ñātinaṃ mayā;
Tayā taṃ buddhisampannaṃ,
assa paramasaṃvutaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 23J_783Msdiv 13Iccevaṃ mantayantānaṃ,
Ariyānaṃ ariyavuttinaṃ;
Paccadissatha nesādo,
Āturānamivantako.

SC 14Ms 23J_784Msdiv 14Te sattumabhisañcikkha,
dīgharattaṃ hitā dijā;
Tuṇhīmāsittha ubhayo,
na sañcalesumāsanā.

SC 15Ms 23J_785Msdiv 15Dhataraṭṭhe ca disvāna,
samuḍḍente tato tato;
Abhikkamatha vegena,
dijasattu dijādhipe.

SC 16Ms 23J_786Msdiv 16So ca vegenabhikkamma,
āsajja parame dije;
Paccakamittha nesādo,
baddhā iti vicintayaṃ.

SC 17Ms 23J_787Msdiv 17Ekaṃva baddhamāsīnaṃ,
abaddhañca punāparaṃ;
Āsajja baddhamāsīnaṃ,
pekkhamānamadīnavaṃ.

SC 18Ms 23J_788Msdiv 18Tato so vimatoyeva,
paṇḍare ajjhabhāsatha;
Pavaḍḍhakāye āsīne,
dijasaṅghagaṇādhipe.

SC 19Ms 23J_789Msdiv 19“Yannu pāsena mahatā,
baddho na kurute disaṃ;
Atha kasmā abaddho tvaṃ,
balī pakkhi na gacchasi.

SC 20Ms 23J_790Msdiv 20Kiṃ nu tyāyaṃ dijo hoti,
mutto baddhaṃ upāsasi;
Ohāya sakuṇā yanti,
kiṃ eko avahīyasi”.

SC 21Ms 23J_791Msdiv 21“Rājā me so dijāmitta,
sakhā pāṇasamo ca me;
Neva naṃ vijahissāmi,
yāva kālassa pariyāyaṃ”.

SC 22Ms 23J_792Msdiv 22“Kathaṃ panāyaṃ vihaṅgo,
nāddasa pāsamoḍḍitaṃ;
Padañhetaṃ mahantānaṃ,
boddhumarahanti āpadaṃ”.

SC 23Ms 23J_793Msdiv 23“Yadā parābhavo hoti,
poso jīvitasaṅkhaye;
Atha jālañca pāsañca,
āsajjāpi na bujjhati.

SC 24Ms 23J_794Msdiv 24Api tveva mahāpañña,
pāsā bahuvidhā tatā;
Guyhamāsajja bajjhanti,
athevaṃ jīvitakkhaye”.

SC 25Ms 23J_795Msdiv 25“Api nāyaṃ tayā saddhiṃ,
saṃvāsassa sukhudrayo;
Api no anumaññāsi,
api no jīvitaṃ dade”.

SC 26Ms 23J_796Msdiv 26“Na ceva me tvaṃ baddhosi,
napi icchāmi te vadhaṃ;
Kāmaṃ khippamito gantvā,
jīva tvaṃ anigho ciraṃ”.

SC 27Ms 23J_797Msdiv 27“Nevāhametamicchāmi,
aññatretassa jīvitā;
Sace ekena tuṭṭhosi,
muñcetaṃ mañca bhakkhaya.

SC 28Ms 23J_798Msdiv 28Ārohapariṇāhena,
tulyāsmā vayasā ubho;
Na te lābhena jīvatthi,
etena niminā tuvaṃ.

SC 29Ms 23J_799Msdiv 29Tadiṅgha samapekkhassu,
hotu giddhi tavamhasu;
Maṃ pubbe bandha pāsena,
pacchā muñca dijādhipaṃ.

SC 30Ms 23J_800Msdiv 30Tāvadeva ca te lābho,
katāssa yācanāya ca;
Mitti ca dhataraṭṭhehi,
yāvajīvāya te siyā”.

SC 31Ms 23J_801Msdiv 31“Passantu no mahāsaṃghā,
tayā muttaṃ ito gataṃ;
Mittāmaccā ca bhaccā ca,
puttadārā ca bandhavā.

SC 32Ms 23J_802Msdiv 32Na ca te tādisā mittā,
bahūnaṃ idha vijjati;
Yathā tvaṃ dhataraṭṭhassa,
pāṇasādhāraṇo sakhā.

SC 33Ms 23J_803Msdiv 33So te sahāyaṃ muñcāmi,
hotu rājā tavānugo;
Kāmaṃ khippamito gantvā,
ñātimajjhe virocatha”.

SC 34Ms 23J_804Msdiv 34“So patīto pamuttena,
bhattunā bhattugāravo;
Ajjhabhāsatha vakkaṅgo,
vācaṃ kaṇṇasukhaṃ bhaṇaṃ.

SC 35Ms 23J_805Msdiv 35Evaṃ luddaka nandassu,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Yathāhamajja nandāmi,
muttaṃ disvā dijādhipaṃ”.

SC 36Ms 23J_806Msdiv 36“Ehi taṃ anusikkhāmi,
Yathā tvamapi lacchase;
Lābhaṃ tavāyaṃ dhataraṭṭho,
Pāpaṃ kiñci na dakkhati.

SC 37Ms 23J_807Msdiv 37Khippamantepuraṃ netvā,
rañño dassehi no ubho;
Abaddhe pakatibhūte,
kāje ubhayato ṭhite.

SC 38Ms 23J_808Msdiv 38Dhataraṭṭhā mahārāja,
haṃsādhipatino ime;
Ayañhi rājā haṃsānaṃ,
ayaṃ senāpatītaro.

SC 39Ms 23J_809Msdiv 39Asaṃsayaṃ imaṃ disvā,
haṃsarājaṃ narādhipo;
Patīto sumano vitto,
bahuṃ dassati te dhanaṃ”.

SC 40Ms 23J_810Msdiv 40Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
kammunā upapādayi;
Khippamantepuraṃ gantvā,
rañño haṃse adassayi;
Abaddhe pakatibhūte,
kāje ubhayato ṭhite.

SC 41Ms 23J_811Msdiv 41“Dhataraṭṭhā mahārāja,
haṃsādhipatino ime;
Ayañhi rājā haṃsānaṃ,
ayaṃ senāpatītaro”.

SC 42Ms 23J_812Msdiv 42“Kathaṃ panime vihaṅgā,
tava hatthattamāgatā;
Kathaṃ luddo mahantānaṃ,
issare idha ajjhagā”.

SC 43Ms 23J_813Msdiv 43“Vihitā santime pāsā,
pallalesu janādhipa;
Yaṃ yadāyatanaṃ maññe,
dijānaṃ pāṇarodhanaṃ.

SC 44Ms 23J_814Msdiv 44Tādisaṃ pāsamāsajja,
haṃsarājā abajjhatha;
Taṃ abaddho upāsīno,
mamāyaṃ ajjhabhāsatha.

SC 45Ms 23J_815Msdiv 45Sudukkaraṃ anariyehi,
dahate bhāvamuttamaṃ;
Bhatturatthe parakkanto,
dhammayutto vihaṅgamo.

SC 46Ms 23J_816Msdiv 46Attanāyaṃ cajitvāna,
jīvitaṃ jīvitāraho;
Anutthunanto āsīno,
bhattu yācittha jīvitaṃ.

SC 47Ms 23J_817Msdiv 47Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
pasādamahamajjhagā;
Tato naṃ pāmuciṃ pāsā,
anuññāsiṃ sukhena ca.

SC 48Ms 23J_818Msdiv 48So patīto pamuttena,
bhattunā bhattugāravo;
Ajjhabhāsatha vakkaṅgo,
vācaṃ kaṇṇasukhaṃ bhaṇaṃ.

SC 49Ms 23J_819Msdiv 49‘Evaṃ luddaka nandassu,
saha sabbehi ñātibhi;
Yathāhamajja nandāmi,
muttaṃ disvā dijādhipaṃ.

SC 50Ms 23J_820Msdiv 50Ehi taṃ anusikkhāmi,
yathā tvamapi lacchase;
Lābhaṃ tavāyaṃ dhataraṭṭho,
pāpaṃ kiñci na dakkhati.

SC 51Ms 23J_821Msdiv 51Khippamantepuraṃ netvā,
rañño dassehi no ubho;
Abaddhe pakatibhūte,
kāje ubhayato ṭhite.

SC 52Ms 23J_822Msdiv 52“Dhataraṭṭhā mahārāja,
haṃsādhipatino ime;
Ayañhi rājā haṃsānaṃ,
ayaṃ senāpatītaro”.

SC 53Ms 23J_823Msdiv 53Asaṃsayaṃ imaṃ disvā,
haṃsarājaṃ narādhipo;
Patīto sumano vitto,
bahuṃ dassati te dhanaṃ’.

SC 54Ms 23J_824Msdiv 54Evametassa vacanā,
ānītāme ubho mayā;
Ettheva hi ime āsuṃ,
ubho anumatā mayā.

SC 55Ms 23J_825Msdiv 55Soyaṃ evaṃ gato pakkhī,
dijo paramadhammiko;
Mādisassa hi luddassa,
janayeyyātha maddavaṃ.

SC 56Ms 23J_826Msdiv 56Upāyanañca te deva,
nāññaṃ passāmi edisaṃ;
Sabbasākuṇikāgāme,
taṃ passa manujādhipa”.

SC 57Ms 23J_827Msdiv 57Disvā nisinnaṃ rājānaṃ,
pīṭhe sovaṇṇaye subhe;
Ajjhabhāsatha vakkaṅgo,
vācaṃ kaṇṇasukhaṃ bhaṇaṃ.

SC 58Ms 23J_828Msdiv 58“Kaccinnu bhoto kusalaṃ,
kacci bhoto anāmayaṃ;
Kacci raṭṭhamidaṃ phītaṃ,
dhammena manusāsasi”.

SC 59Ms 23J_829Msdiv 59“Kusalañceva me haṃsa,
Atho haṃsa anāmayaṃ;
Atho raṭṭhamidaṃ phītaṃ,
Dhammena manusāsahaṃ”.

SC 60Ms 23J_830Msdiv 60“Kacci bhoto amaccesu,
doso koci na vijjati;
Kacci ca te tavatthesu,
nāvakaṅkhanti jīvitaṃ”.

SC 61Ms 23J_831Msdiv 61“Athopi me amaccesu,
doso koci na vijjati;
Athopi te mamatthesu,
nāvakaṅkhanti jīvitaṃ”.

SC 62Ms 23J_832Msdiv 62“Kacci te sādisī bhariyā,
assavā piyabhāṇinī;
Puttarūpayasūpetā,
tava chandavasānugā”.

SC 63Ms 23J_833Msdiv 63“Atho me sādisī bhariyā,
assavā piyabhāṇinī;
Puttarūpayasūpetā,
mama chandavasānugā.

SC 64Ms 23J_834Msdiv 64Bhavantaṃ kacci nu mahā-
sattuhatthattataṃ gato;
Dukkhamāpajji vipulaṃ,
tasmiṃ paṭhamamāpade.

SC 65Ms 23J_835Msdiv 65Kacci yantāpatitvāna,
daṇḍena samapothayi;
Evametesaṃ jammānaṃ,
pātikaṃ bhavati tāvade”.

SC 66Ms 23J_836Msdiv 66“Khemamāsi mahārāja,
evamāpadiyā sati;
Na cāyaṃ kiñci rasmāsu,
sattūva samapajjatha.

SC 67Ms 23J_837Msdiv 67Paccagamittha nesādo,
pubbeva ajjhabhāsatha;
Tadāyaṃ sumukhoyeva,
paṇḍito paccabhāsatha.

SC 68Ms 23J_838Msdiv 68Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
pasādamayamajjhagā;
Tato maṃ pāmucī pāsā,
anuññāsi sukhena ca.

SC 69Ms 23J_839Msdiv 69Idañca sumukheneva,
etadatthāya cintitaṃ;
Bhoto sakāsegamanaṃ,
etassa dhanamicchatā”.

SC 70Ms 23J_840Msdiv 70“Svāgatañcevidaṃ bhavataṃ,
patīto casmi dassanā;
Eso cāpi bahuṃ vittaṃ,
labhataṃ yāvadicchati”.

SC 71Ms 23J_841Msdiv 71Santappayitvā nesādaṃ,
bhogehi manujādhipo;
Ajjhabhāsatha vakkaṅgaṃ,
vācaṃ kaṇṇasukhaṃ bhaṇaṃ.

SC 72Ms 23J_842Msdiv 72“Yaṃ khalu dhammamādhīnaṃ,
vaso vattati kiñcanaṃ;
Sabbatthissariyaṃ tava,
taṃ pasāsa yadicchatha.

SC 73Ms 23J_843Msdiv 73Dānatthaṃ upabhottuṃ vā,
yaṃ caññaṃ upakappati;
Etaṃ dadāmi vo vittaṃ,
issariyaṃ vissajāmi vo”.

SC 74Ms 23J_844Msdiv 74“Yathā ca myāyaṃ sumukho,
ajjhabhāseyya paṇḍito;
Kāmasā buddhisampanno,
taṃ myāssa paramappiyaṃ”.

SC 75Ms 23J_845Msdiv 75“Ahaṃ khalu mahārāja,
nāgarājārivantaraṃ;
Paṭivattuṃ na sakkomi,
na me so vinayo siyā.

SC 76Ms 23J_846Msdiv 76Amhākañceva so seṭṭho,
tvañca uttamasattavo;
Bhūmipālo manussindo,
pūjā bahūhi hetuhi.

SC 77Ms 23J_847Msdiv 77Tesaṃ ubhinnaṃ bhaṇataṃ,
vattamāne vinicchaye;
Nantaraṃ paṭivattabbaṃ,
pessena manujādhipa”.

SC 78Ms 23J_848Msdiv 78“Dhammena kira nesādo,
paṇḍito aṇḍajo iti;
Na heva akatattassa,
nayo etādiso siyā.

SC 79Ms 23J_849Msdiv 79Evaṃ aggapakatimā,
evaṃ uttamasattavo;
Yāvatatthi mayā diṭṭhā,
nāññaṃ passāmi edisaṃ.

SC 80Ms 23J_850Msdiv 80Tuṭṭhosmi vo pakatiyā,
vākyena madhurena ca;
Eso cāpi mamacchando,
ciraṃ passeyya vo ubho”.

SC 81Ms 23J_851Msdiv 81“Yaṃ kiccaṃ parame mitte,
katamasmāsu taṃ tayā;
Pattā nissaṃsayaṃ tyāmhā,
bhattirasmāsu yā tava.

SC 82Ms 23J_852Msdiv 82Aduñca nūna sumahā,
ñātisaṅghassa mantaraṃ;
Adassanena asmākaṃ,
dukkhaṃ bahūsu pakkhisu.

SC 83Ms 23J_853Msdiv 83Tesaṃ sokavighātāya,
Tayā anumatā mayaṃ;
Taṃ padakkhiṇato katvā,
Ñātiṃ passemurindama.

SC 84Ms 23J_854Msdiv 84Addhāhaṃ vipulaṃ pītiṃ,
bhavataṃ vindāmi dassanā;
Eso cāpi mahā attho,
ñātivissāsanā siyā”.

SC 85Ms 23J_855Msdiv 85Idaṃ vatvā dhataraṭṭho,
haṃsarājā narādhipaṃ;
Uttamaṃ javamanvāya,
ñātisaṅghaṃ upāgamuṃ.

SC 86Ms 23J_856Msdiv 86Te aroge anuppatte,
disvāna parame dije;
Kekāti makaruṃ haṃsā,
puthusaddo ajāyatha.

SC 87Ms 23J_857Msdiv 87Te patītā pamuttena,
bhattunā bhattugāravā;
Samantā parikiriṃsu,
aṇḍajā laddhapaccayā.

SC 88Ms 23J_858Msdiv 88Evaṃ mittavataṃ atthā,
sabbe honti padakkhiṇā;
Haṃsā yathā dhataraṭṭhā,
ñātisaṅghaṃ upāgamunti.

SC 89Ms 23J_859Cūḷahaṃsajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
