321. Kuṭidūsakajātaka

SC 1PTS vp Pali 3.73Ms 22J_987Msdiv 81“Manussasseva te sīsaṃ,
hatthapādā ca vānara;
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,
agāraṃ te na vijjati”.

SC 2Ms 22J_988Msdiv 82“Manussasseva me sīsaṃ,
hatthapādā ca siṅgila;
Yāhu seṭṭhā manussesu,
sā me paññā na vijjati”.

SC 3Ms 22J_989Msdiv 83“Anavaṭṭhitacittassa,
lahucittassa dubbhino;
Niccaṃ addhuvasīlassa,
sukhabhāvo na vijjati.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 3.74Ms 22J_990Msdiv 84So karassu ānubhāvaṃ,
vītivattassu sīliyaṃ;
Sītavātaparittāṇaṃ,
karassu kuṭavaṃ kapī”ti.

SC 5Ms 22J_991Kuṭidūsakajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
