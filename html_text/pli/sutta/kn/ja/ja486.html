486. Mahāukkusajātaka

SC 1Ms 22J_2593Msdiv 44“Ukkā cilācā bandhanti cilācā bandhanti dīpe,
Pajā mamaṃ khādituṃ patthayanti;
Mittaṃ sahāyañca vadehi senaka,
Ācikkha ñātibyasanaṃ dijānaṃ”.

SC 2Ms 22J_2594Msdiv 45“Dijo dijānaṃ pavarosi pakkhima,
Ukkusarāja saraṇaṃ taṃ upema;
Pajā mamaṃ khādituṃ patthayanti,
Luddā cilācā bhava me sukhāya”.

SC 3Ms 22J_2595Msdiv 46“Mittaṃ sahāyañca karonti paṇḍitā,
Kāle akāle sukhamesamānā;
Karomi te senaka etamatthaṃ,
Ariyo hi ariyassa karoti kiccaṃ”.

SC 4Ms 22J_2596Msdiv 47“Yaṃ hoti kiccaṃ anukampakena,
Ariyassa ariyena kataṃ tayīdaṃ;
Attānurakkhī bhava mā adayhi,
Lacchāma putte tayi jīvamāne”.

SC 5Ms 22J_2597Msdiv 48“Taveva rakkhāvaraṇaṃ karonto,
Sarīrabhedāpi na santasāmi;
Karonti heke sakhīnaṃ sakhāro,
Pāṇaṃ cajantā cajantā satamesa dhammo”.

SC 6Ms 22J_2598Msdiv 49“Sudukkaraṃ kammamakāsi,
aṇḍajāyaṃ vihaṅgamo;
Atthāya kuraro putte,
aḍḍharatte anāgate”.

SC 7Ms 22J_2599Msdiv 50“Cutāpi heke khalitā sakammunā,
Mittānukampāya patiṭṭhahanti;
Puttā mamaṭṭā gatimāgatosmi,
Atthaṃ caretho mama caretho mama vāricara”.

SC 8Ms 22J_2600Msdiv 51“Dhanena dhaññena ca attanā ca,
Mittaṃ sahāyañca karonti paṇḍitā;
Karomi te senaka etamatthaṃ,
Ariyo hi ariyassa karoti kiccaṃ”.

SC 9Ms 22J_2601Msdiv 52“Appossukko tāta tuvaṃ nisīda,
Putto pitu carati atthacariyaṃ;
Ahaṃ carissāmi tavetamatthaṃ,
Senassa putte paritāyamāno”.

SC 10Ms 22J_2602Msdiv 53“Addhā hi tāta satamesa dhammo,
Putto pitu yaṃ care atthacariyaṃ;
Appeva maṃ disvāna pavaḍḍhakāyaṃ,
Senassa puttā na viheṭhayeyyuṃ”.

SC 11Ms 22J_2603Msdiv 54“Pasū manussā migavīraseṭṭha,
Bhayaṭṭitā seṭṭhamupabbajanti;
Puttā mamaṭṭā gatimāgatosmi,
Tvaṃ nosi rājā bhava me sukhāya”.

SC 12Ms 22J_2604Msdiv 55“Karomi te senaka etamatthaṃ,
Āyāmi te taṃ disataṃ vadhāya;
Kathañhi viññū pahu sampajāno,
Na vāyame attajanassa guttiyā”.

SC 13Ms 22J_2605Msdiv 56“Mittañca kayirātha suhadayañca,
Ayirañca kayirātha sukhāgamāya;
Nivatthakocova sarebhihantvā,
Modāma puttehi samaṅgibhūtā.

SC 14Ms 22J_2606Msdiv 57Sakamittassa kammena,
sahāyassāpalāyino;
Kūjantamupakūjanti,
lomasā hadayaṅgamaṃ.

SC 15Ms 22J_2607Msdiv 58Mittaṃ sahāyaṃ adhigamma paṇḍito,
So bhuñjatī putta pasuṃ dhanaṃ vā;
Ahañca puttā ca patī ca mayhaṃ,
Mittānukampāya samaṅgibhūtā.

SC 16Ms 22J_2608Msdiv 59Rājavatā sūravatā ca attho,
Sampannasakhissa bhavanti hete;
So mittavā yasavā uggatatto,
Asmiṃdhaloke modati kāmakāmī.

SC 17Ms 22J_2609Msdiv 60Karaṇīyāni mittāni,
daliddenāpi senaka;
Passa mittānukampāya,
samaggamhā sañātake.

SC 18Ms 22J_2610Msdiv 61Sūrena balavantena,
yo mitte kurute dijo;
Evaṃ so sukhito hoti,
yathāhaṃ tvañca senakāti”.

SC 19Ms 22J_2611Mahāukkusajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
