10. Tacchakaṅgapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_1978Msdiv 284“Bhante nāgasena, ‘tacchakassa dve aṅgāni gahetabbānī’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamāni tāni dve aṅgāni gahetabbānī”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, tacchako kāḷasuttaṃ anulometvā rukkhaṃ tacchati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena jinasāsanamanulomayitvā sīlapathaviyaṃ patiṭṭhahitvā saddhāhatthena paññāvāsiṃ gahetvā kilesā tacchetabbā. Idaṃ, mahārāja, tacchakassa paṭhamaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ.

SC 2Ms 28Mi_1979Puna caparaṃ, mahārāja, tacchako phegguṃ apaharitvā sāramādiyati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena sassataṃ ucchedaṃ taṃ jīvaṃ taṃ sarīraṃ aññaṃ jīvaṃ aññaṃ sarīraṃ taduttamaṃ aññaduttamaṃ akatamabhabbaṃ apurisakāraṃ abrahmacariyavāsaṃ sattavināsaṃ navasattapātubhāvaṃ saṅkhārasassatabhāvaṃ yo karoti, so paṭisaṃvedeti, añño karoti, añño paṭisaṃvedeti, kammaphaladassanā ca kiriyaphaladiṭṭhi ca iti evarūpāni ceva aññāni ca vivādapathāni apanetvā saṅkhārānaṃ sabhāvaṃ paramasuññataṃ nirīhanijjīvataṃ accantaṃ suññataṃ ādiyitabbaṃ. Idaṃ, mahārāja, tacchakassa dutiyaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ. Bhāsitampetaṃ, mahārāja, bhagavatā devātidevena suttanipāte—

SC 3Ms 28Mi_1980‘Kāraṇḍavaṃ niddhamatha,
Kasambuṃ apakassatha;
Tato palāpe vāhetha,
Assamaṇe samaṇamānine.

SC 4Ms 28Mi_1981Niddhamitvāna pāpicche,
pāpaācāragocare;
Suddhā suddhehi saṃvāsaṃ,
kappayavho patissatā;
Tato samaggā nipakā,
dukkhassantaṃ karissathā’”ti.

SC 5Ms 28Mi_1982Tacchakaṅgapañho dasamo.

SC 6Ms 28Mi_1983Makkaṭakavaggo chaṭṭho.

SC 7Ms 28Mi_1984Tassuddānaṃ

SC 8Ms 28Mi_1985Makkaṭo dārako kummo,
vanaṃ rukkho ca pañcamo;
Megho maṇi māgaviko,
bāḷisī tacchakena cāti.
