4. Vicchikaṅgapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_1846SC 1Ms 28Mi_1846Msdiv 258“Bhante nāgasena, ‘vicchikassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamaṃ taṃ ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, vicchiko naṅgulāvudho naṅgulaṃ ussāpetvā carati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena ñāṇāvudhena bhavitabbaṃ, ñāṇaṃ ussāpetvā viharitabbaṃ. Idaṃ, mahārāja, vicchikassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ. Bhāsitampetaṃ, mahārāja, therena upasenena vaṅgantaputtena—

SC 2SC 2Ms 28Mi_1847‘Ñāṇakhaggaṃ gahetvāna,
viharanto vipassako;
Parimuccati sabbabhayā,
duppasaho ca so bhave’”ti.

SC 3SC 3Ms 28Mi_1848Vicchikaṅgapañho catuttho.
