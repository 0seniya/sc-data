3. Cittakadharakummaṅgapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_1941Msdiv 277“Bhante nāgasena, ‘cittakadharakummassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamaṃ taṃ ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, cittakadharakummo udakabhayā udakaṃ parivajjetvā vicarati, tāya ca pana udakaṃ parivajjanāya āyunā na parihāyati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena pamāde bhayadassāvinā bhavitabbaṃ, appamāde guṇavisesadassāvinā.
