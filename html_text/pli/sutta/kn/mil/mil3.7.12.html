12. Samuddapañha

SC 1PTS vp Pali 86Ms 28Mi_495Msdiv 105Rājā āha—“bhante nāgasena, ‘samuddo samuddo’ti vuccati, kena kāraṇena udakaṃ ‘samuddo’ti vuccatī”ti? Thero āha—“yattakaṃ, mahārāja, udakaṃ, tattakaṃ loṇaṃ. Yattakaṃ loṇaṃ, tattakaṃ udakaṃ. Tasmā ‘samuddo’ti vuccatī”ti.

SC 2Ms 28Mi_496“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_497Samuddapañho dvādasamo.
