10. Ajagaraṅgapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_1928Msdiv 274“Bhante nāgasena, ‘ajagarassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamaṃ taṃ ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, ajagaro mahatimahākāyo bahūpi divase ūnūdaro dīnataro kucchipūraṃ āhāraṃ na labhati, aparipuṇṇo yeva yāvadeva sarīrayāpanamattakena yāpeti; evameva kho, mahārāja, yogino yogāvacarassa bhikkhācariyappasutassa parapiṇḍamupagatassa paradinnappāṭikaṅkhissa sayaṅgāhappaṭiviratassa dullabhaṃ udaraparipūraṃ āhāraṃ, api ca atthavasikena kulaputtena cattāro pañca ālope abhuñjitvā avasesaṃ udakena paripūretabbaṃ. Idaṃ, mahārāja, ajagarassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ. Bhāsitampetaṃ, mahārāja, therena sāriputtena dhammasenāpatinā—

SC 2PTS vp Pali 407Ms 28Mi_1929‘Allaṃ sukkhaṃ vā bhuñjanto,
Na bāḷhaṃ suhito siyā;
Ūnūdaro mitāhāro,
Sato bhikkhu paribbaje.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_1930Cattāro pañca ālope,
abhutvā udakaṃ pive;
Alaṃ phāsu vihārāya,
pahitattassa bhikkhuno’”ti.

SC 4Ms 28Mi_1931Ajagaraṅgapañho dasamo.

SC 5Ms 28Mi_1932Sīhavaggo pañcamo.

SC 6Ms 28Mi_1933Tassuddānaṃ

SC 7Ms 28Mi_1934Kesarī cakkavāko ca,
peṇāhi gharakapotako;
Ulūko satapatto ca,
vagguli ca jalūpikā;
Sappo ajagaro ceva,
vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
