14. Sukhumapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_501SC 1Ms 28Mi_501Msdiv 107Rājā āha—“bhante nāgasena, sakkā sabbaṃ sukhumaṃ chinditun”ti? “Āma, mahārāja, sakkā sabbaṃ sukhumaṃ chinditun”ti. “Kiṃ pana, bhante, sabbaṃ sukhuman”ti? “Dhammo kho, mahārāja, sabbasukhumo, na kho, mahārāja, dhammā sabbe sukhumā, ‘sukhuman’ti vā ‘thūlan’ti vā dhammānametamadhivacanaṃ. Yaṃ kiñci chinditabbaṃ, sabbaṃ taṃ paññāya chindati, natthi dutiyaṃ paññāya chedanan”ti.

SC 2SC 2Ms 28Mi_502“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_503SC 3Ms 28Mi_503Sukhumapañho cuddasamo.
