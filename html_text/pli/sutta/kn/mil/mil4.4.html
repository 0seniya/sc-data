4.4. Aṭṭhapaññāpaṭilābhakāraṇa

SC 1Ms 28Mi_540Msdiv 117“Bhante nāgasena, aṭṭhahi kāraṇehi buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati. Katamehi aṭṭhahi? Vayapariṇāmena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, yasapariṇāmena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, paripucchāya buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, titthasaṃvāsena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, yoniso manasikārena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, sākacchāya buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, snehūpasevanena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati, patirūpadesavāsena buddhi pariṇamati paripākaṃ gacchati. Bhavatīha—

SC 2Ms 28Mi_541‘Vayena yasapucchāhi,
titthavāsena yoniso;
Sākacchā snehasaṃsevā,
patirūpavasena ca.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_542Etāni aṭṭha ṭhānāni,
buddhivisadakāraṇā;
Yesaṃ etāni sambhonti,
tesaṃ buddhi pabhijjatī’”ti.

SC 4Ms 28Mi_543Aṭṭha paññāpaṭilābhakāraṇāni.
