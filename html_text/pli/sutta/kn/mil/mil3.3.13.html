13. Vitakkalakkhaṇapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_305Msdiv 61“Bhante nāgasena, kiṃlakkhaṇo vitakko”ti? “Appanālakkhaṇo, mahārāja, vitakko”ti.

SC 2Ms 28Mi_306“Opammaṃ karohī”ti. “Yathā, mahārāja, vaḍḍhakī suparikammakataṃ dāruṃ sandhismiṃ appeti; evameva kho, mahārāja, appanālakkhaṇo vitakko”ti.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_307“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

SC 4Ms 28Mi_308Vitakkalakkhaṇapañho terasamo.
