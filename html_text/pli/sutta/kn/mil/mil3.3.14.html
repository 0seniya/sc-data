14. Vicāralakkhaṇapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_309Msdiv 62“Bhante nāgasena, kiṃlakkhaṇo vicāro”ti? “Anumajjanalakkhaṇo, mahārāja, vicāro”ti.

SC 2PTS vp Pali 63Ms 28Mi_310“Opammaṃ karohī”ti. “Yathā, mahārāja, kaṃsathālaṃ ākoṭitaṃ pacchā anuravati anusandahati, yathā, mahārāja, ākoṭanā, evaṃ vitakko daṭṭhabbo. Yathā anusandahati, yathā, mahārāja, ākoṭanā, evaṃ vitakko daṭṭhabbo. Yathā anuravanā, evaṃ vicāro daṭṭhabbo”ti.

SC 3Ms 28Mi_311“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

SC 4Ms 28Mi_312Vicāralakkhaṇapañho cuddasamo.

SC 5Ms 28Mi_313Vicāravaggo tatiyo.

SC 6Ms 28Mi_314Imasmiṃ vagge cuddasa pañhā.
