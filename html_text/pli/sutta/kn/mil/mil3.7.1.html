1. Satiuppajjanapañha

SC 1Ms 28Mi_435SC 1Ms 28Mi_435Msdiv 94Rājā āha—“bhante nāgasena, katihākārehi sati uppajjatī”ti? “Sattarasahākārehi, mahārāja, sati uppajjatī”ti. “Katamehi sattarasahākārehī”ti? “Abhijānatopi, mahārāja, sati uppajjati, kaṭumikāyapi sati uppajjati, oḷārikaviññāṇatopi sati uppajjati, hitaviññāṇatopi sati uppajjati, ahitaviññāṇatopi sati uppajjati, sabhāganimittatopi sati uppajjati, visabhāganimittatopi sati uppajjati, kathābhiññāṇatopi sati uppajjati, lakkhaṇatopi sati uppajjati, sāraṇatopi sati uppajjati, muddātopi sati uppajjati, gaṇanātopi sati uppajjati, dhāraṇatopi sati uppajjati, bhāvanatopi sati uppajjati, potthakanibandhanatopi sati uppajjati, upanikkhepatopi sati uppajjati, anubhūtatopi sati uppajjatīti.

SC 2SC 2Ms 28Mi_436Kathaṃ abhijānato sati uppajjati? Yathā, mahārāja, āyasmā ca ānando khujjuttarā ca upāsikā, ye vā pana aññepi keci jātissarā jātiṃ saranti, evaṃ abhijānato sati uppajjati.

SC 3SC 3Ms 28Mi_437Kathaṃ kaṭumikāya sati uppajjati? Yo pakatiyā muṭṭhassatiko, pare ca taṃ sarāpanatthaṃ nibandhanti, evaṃ kaṭumikāya sati uppajjati.

SC 4SC 4Ms 28Mi_438Kathaṃ oḷārikaviññāṇato sati uppajjati? Yadā rajje vā abhisitto hoti, sotāpattiphalaṃ vā patto hoti, evaṃ oḷārikaviññāṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 5SC 5Ms 28Mi_439Kathaṃ hitaviññāṇato sati uppajjati? Yamhi sukhāpito, ‘amukasmiṃ evaṃ sukhāpito’ti sarati, evaṃ hitaviññāṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 6SC 6Ms 28Mi_440Kathaṃ ahitaviññāṇato sati uppajjati? Yamhi dukkhāpito, ‘amukasmiṃ evaṃ dukkhāpito’ti sarati, evaṃ ahitaviññāṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 7SC 7Ms 28Mi_441Kathaṃ sabhāganimittato sati uppajjati? Sadisaṃ puggalaṃ disvā mātaraṃ vā pitaraṃ vā bhātaraṃ vā bhaginiṃ vā sarati, oṭṭhaṃ vā goṇaṃ vā gadrabhaṃ vā disvā aññaṃ tādisaṃ oṭṭhaṃ vā goṇaṃ vā gadrabhaṃ vā sarati, evaṃ sabhāganimittato sati uppajjati.

SC 8SC 8Ms 28Mi_442Kathaṃ visabhāganimittato sati uppajjati? Asukassa nāma vaṇṇo ediso, saddo ediso, gandho ediso, raso ediso, phoṭṭhabbo edisoti sarati, evampi visabhāganimittatopi sati uppajjati.

SC 9SC 9Ms 28Mi_443Kathaṃ kathābhiññāṇato sati uppajjati? Yo pakatiyā muṭṭhassatiko hoti, taṃ pare sarāpenti, tena so sarati, evaṃ kathābhiññāṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 10SC 10Ms 28Mi_444Kathaṃ lakkhaṇato sati uppajjati? Yo pakatiyā balībaddānaṃ aṅgena jānāti, lakkhaṇena jānāti, evaṃ lakkhaṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 11SC 11Ms 28Mi_445Kathaṃ sāraṇato sati uppajjati? Yo pakatiyā muṭṭhassatiko hoti, yo taṃ ‘sarāhi bho, sarāhi bho’ti punappunaṃ sarāpeti, evaṃ sāraṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 12SC 12Ms 28Mi_446Kathaṃ muddāto sati uppajjati? Lipiyā sikkhitattā jānāti ‘imassa akkharassa anantaraṃ imaṃ akkharaṃ kātabban’ti evaṃ muddāto sati uppajjati.

SC 13SC 13Ms 28Mi_447Kathaṃ gaṇanāto sati uppajjati? Gaṇanāya sikkhitattā gaṇakā bahumpi gaṇenti, evaṃ gaṇanāto sati uppajjati.

SC 14SC 14Ms 28Mi_448Kathaṃ dhāraṇato sati uppajjati? Dhāraṇāya sikkhitattā dhāraṇakā bahumpi dhārenti, evaṃ dhāraṇato sati uppajjati.

SC 15SC 15Ms 28Mi_449Kathaṃ bhāvanāto sati uppajjati? Idha bhikkhu anekavihitaṃ pubbenivāsaṃ anussarati, seyyathidaṃ—ekampi jātiṃ dvepi jātiyo … pe … iti sākāraṃ sauddesaṃ pubbenivāsaṃ anussarati, evaṃ bhāvanāto sati uppajjati.

SC 16Ms 28Mi_450Kathaṃ potthakanibandhanato sati uppajjati? Rājāno anusāsaniyaṃ SC 16Ms 28Mi_450Kathaṃ potthakanibandhanato sati uppajjati? Rājāno anusāsaniyaṃ assarantā etaṃ potthakaṃ āharathāti, tena potthakena anussaranti, evaṃ potthakanibandhanato sati uppajjati.

SC 17SC 17Ms 28Mi_451Kathaṃ upanikkhepato sati uppajjati? Upanikkhittaṃ bhaṇḍaṃ disvā sarati, evaṃ upanikkhepato sati uppajjati.

SC 18SC 18Ms 28Mi_452Kathaṃ anubhūtato sati uppajjati? Diṭṭhattā rūpaṃ sarati, sutattā saddaṃ sarati, ghāyitattā gandhaṃ sarati, sāyitattā rasaṃ sarati, phuṭṭhattā phoṭṭhabbaṃ sarati, viññātattā dhammaṃ sarati, evaṃ anubhūtato sati uppajjati. Imehi kho, mahārāja, sattarasahākārehi sati uppajjatī”ti.

SC 19SC 19Ms 28Mi_453“Kallosi, bhante nāgasenā”ti.

SC 20Ms 28Mi_454SC 20Ms 28Mi_454Satiuppajjanapañho paṭhamo.
