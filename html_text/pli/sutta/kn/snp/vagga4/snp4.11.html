• Sutta Nipāta 4.11

Kalahavivādasutta

SC 1SC Verse 869PTS Verse 862Ms 18Sn_1040“Kutopahūtā kalahā vivādā,
Paridevasokā sahamaccharā ca;
Mānātimānā sahapesuṇā ca,
Kutopahūtā te tadiṅgha brūhi”.

SC 2SC Verse 870PTS Verse 863Ms 18Sn_1041“Piyappahūtā kalahā vivādā,
Paridevasokā sahamaccharā ca;
Mānātimānā sahapesuṇā ca,
Maccherayuttā kalahā vivādā;
Vivādajātesu ca pesuṇāni”.

SC 3SC Verse 871PTS Verse 864PTS vp Pali 169Ms 18Sn_1042“Piyā su lokasmiṃ kutonidānā,su lokasmiṃ kutonidānā,
Ye cāpi lobhā vicaranti loke;
Āsā ca niṭṭhā ca kutonidānā,
Ye samparāyāya narassa honti”.

SC 4SC Verse 872PTS Verse 865Ms 18Sn_1043“Chandānidānāni piyāni loke,
Ye cāpi lobhā vicaranti loke;
Āsā ca niṭṭhā ca itonidānā,
Ye samparāyāya narassa honti”.

SC 5SC Verse 873PTS Verse 866Ms 18Sn_1044“Chando nu lokasmiṃ kutonidāno,
Vinicchayā cāpi kutopahūtā;
Kodho mosavajjañca kathaṃkathā ca,
Ye vāpi dhammā samaṇena vuttā”.

SC 6SC Verse 874PTS Verse 867Ms 18Sn_1045“Sātaṃ asātanti yamāhu loke,
Tamūpanissāya pahoti chando;
Rūpesu disvā vibhavaṃ bhavañca,
Vinicchayaṃ kubbati jantu loke.

SC 7SC Verse 875PTS Verse 868Ms 18Sn_1046Kodho mosavajjañca kathaṅkathā ca,
Etepi dhammā dvayameva sante;
Kathaṅkathī ñāṇapathāya sikkhe,
Ñatvā pavuttā samaṇena dhammā”.

SC 8SC Verse 876PTS Verse 869Ms 18Sn_1047“Sātaṃ asātañca kutonidānā,
Kismiṃ asante na bhavanti hete;
Vibhavaṃ bhavañcāpi yametamatthaṃ,
Etaṃ me pabrūhi yatonidānaṃ”.

SC 9SC Verse 877PTS Verse 870PTS vp Pali 170Ms 18Sn_1048“Phassanidānaṃ sātaṃ asātaṃ,
Phasse asante na bhavanti hete;
Vibhavaṃ bhavañcāpi yametamatthaṃ,
Etaṃ te pabrūmi itonidānaṃ”.

SC 10SC Verse 878PTS Verse 871Ms 18Sn_1049“Phasso nu lokasmi kutonidāno,
Pariggahā cāpi kutopahūtā;
Kismiṃ asante na mamattamatthi,
Kismiṃ vibhūte na phusanti phassā”.

SC 11SC Verse 879PTS Verse 872Ms 18Sn_1050“Nāmañca rūpañca paṭicca phasso,
Icchānidānāni pariggahāni;
Icchāyasantyā na mamattamatthi,
Rūpe vibhūte na phusanti phassā”.

SC 12SC Verse 880PTS Verse 873Ms 18Sn_1051“Kathaṃ sametassa vibhoti rūpaṃ,
Sukhaṃ dukhañcāpi kathaṃ vibhoti;
Etaṃ me pabrūhi yathā vibhoti,
Taṃ jāniyāmāti me mano ahu”.

SC 13SC Verse 881PTS Verse 874Ms 18Sn_1052“Na saññasaññī na visaññasaññī,
Nopi asaññī na vibhūtasaññī;
Evaṃ sametassa vibhoti rūpaṃ,
Saññānidānā hi papañcasaṅkhā”.

SC 14SC Verse 882PTS Verse 875PTS vp Pali 171Ms 18Sn_1053“Yaṃ taṃ apucchimha akittayī no,
Aññaṃ taṃ pucchāma tadiṅgha brūhi;
Ettāvataggaṃ nu vadanti heke,
Yakkhassa suddhiṃ idha paṇḍitāse;
Udāhu aññampi vadanti etto”.

SC 15SC Verse 883PTS Verse 876Ms 18Sn_1054“Ettāvataggampi vadanti heke,
Yakkhassa suddhiṃ idha paṇḍitāse;
Tesaṃ paneke samayaṃ vadanti,
Anupādisese kusalā vadānā.

SC 16SC Verse 884PTS Verse 877Ms 18Sn_1055Ete ca ñatvā upanissitāti,
Ñatvā munī nissaye so vimaṃsī;
Ñatvā vimutto na vivādameti,
Bhavābhavāya na sameti dhīro”ti.

Kalahavivādasuttaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
