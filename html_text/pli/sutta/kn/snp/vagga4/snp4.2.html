• Sutta Nipāta 4.2

Guhaṭṭhakasutta

SC 1SC Verse 779PTS Verse 772Ms 18Sn_941Satto guhāyaṃ bahunābhichanno,
Tiṭṭhaṃ naro mohanasmiṃ pagāḷho;
Dūre vivekā hi tathāvidho so,
Kāmā hi loke na hi suppahāyā.

SC 2SC Verse 780PTS Verse 773Ms 18Sn_942Icchānidānā bhavasātabaddhā,
Te duppamuñcā na hi aññamokkhā;
Pacchā pure vāpi apekkhamānā,
Ime va kāme purime va jappaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 781PTS Verse 774Ms 18Sn_943Kāmesu giddhā pasutā pamūḷhā,
Avadāniyā te visame niviṭṭhā;
Dukkhūpanītā paridevayanti,
Kiṃsū bhavissāma ito cutāse.

SC 4SC Verse 782PTS Verse 775Ms 18Sn_944Tasmā hi sikkhetha idheva jantu,
Yaṃ kiñci jaññā visamanti loke;
Na tassa hetū visamaṃ careyya,
Appañhidaṃ jīvitamāhu dhīrā.

SC 5SC Verse 783PTS Verse 776Ms 18Sn_945Passāmi loke pariphandamānaṃ,
Pajaṃ imaṃ taṇhagataṃ bhavesu;
Hīnā narā maccumukhe lapanti,
Avītataṇhāse bhavābhavesu.

SC 6SC Verse 784PTS Verse 777Ms 18Sn_946Mamāyite passatha phandamāne,
Maccheva appodake khīṇasote;
Etampi disvā amamo careyya,
Bhavesu āsattimakubbamāno.

SC 7SC Verse 785PTS Verse 778Ms 18Sn_947Ubhosu antesu vineyya chandaṃ,
Phassaṃ pariññāya anānugiddho;
Yadattagarahī tadakubbamāno,
Na lippatī diṭṭhasutesu dhīro.

SC 8SC Verse 786PTS Verse 779Ms 18Sn_948Saññaṃ pariññā vitareyya oghaṃ,
Pariggahesu muni nopalitto;
Abbūḷhasallo caramappamatto,
Nāsīsatī lokamimaṃ parañcāti.

Guhaṭṭhakasuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.
