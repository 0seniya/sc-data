• Sutta Nipāta 4.3

Duṭṭhaṭṭhakasutta

SC 1SC Verse 787PTS Verse 780Ms 18Sn_950Vadanti ve duṭṭhamanāpi eke,
Athopi ve saccamanā vadanti;
Vādañca jātaṃ muni no upeti,
Tasmā munī natthi khilo kuhiñci.

SC 2SC Verse 788PTS Verse 781Ms 18Sn_951Sakañhi diṭṭhiṃ kathamaccayeyya,
Chandānunīto ruciyā niviṭṭho;
Sayaṃ samattāni pakubbamāno,
Yathā hi jāneyya tathā vadeyya.

SC 3SC Verse 789PTS Verse 782Ms 18Sn_952Yo attano sīlavatāni jantu,
Anānupuṭṭhova paresa paresa pāva;
Anariyadhammaṃ kusalā tamāhu,
Yo ātumānaṃ sayameva pāva.

SC 4SC Verse 790PTS Verse 783Ms 18Sn_953Santo ca bhikkhu abhinibbutatto,
Itihanti sīlesu akatthamāno;
Tamariyadhammaṃ kusalā vadanti,
Yassussadā natthi kuhiñci loke.

SC 5SC Verse 791PTS Verse 784Ms 18Sn_954Pakappitā saṅkhatā yassa dhammā,
Purakkhatā santi avīvadātā;
Yadattani passati ānisaṃsaṃ,
Taṃ nissito kuppapaṭiccasantiṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 792PTS Verse 785Ms 18Sn_955Diṭṭhīnivesā na hi svātivattā,
Dhammesu niccheyya samuggahītaṃ;
Tasmā naro tesu nivesanesu,
Nirassatī ādiyatī ca dhammaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 793PTS Verse 786Ms 18Sn_956Dhonassa hi natthi kuhiñci loke,
Pakappitā diṭṭhi bhavābhavesu;
Māyañca mānañca pahāya dhono,
Sa kena gaccheyya anūpayo so.

SC 8SC Verse 794PTS Verse 787Ms 18Sn_957Upayo hi dhammesu upeti vādaṃ,
Anūpayaṃ kena kathaṃ vadeyya;
Attā nirattā na hi tassa atthi,
Adhosi so diṭṭhimidheva sabbanti.

Duṭṭhaṭṭhakasuttaṃ tatiyaṃ.
