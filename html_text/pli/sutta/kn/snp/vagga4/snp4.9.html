• Sutta Nipāta 4.9

Māgaṇḍiyasutta

SC 1SC Verse 842PTS Verse 835Ms 18Sn_1011“Disvāna taṇhaṃ aratiṃ ragañca,
Nāhosi chando api methunasmiṃ;
Kimevidaṃ muttakarīsapuṇṇaṃ,
Pādāpi naṃ samphusituṃ na icche”.

SC 2SC Verse 843PTS Verse 836Ms 18Sn_1012“Etādisañce ratanaṃ na icchasi,
Nāriṃ narindehi bahūhi patthitaṃ;
Diṭṭhigataṃ sīlavataṃ nu jīvitaṃ,
Bhavūpapattiñca vadesi kīdisaṃ”.

SC 3SC Verse 844PTS Verse 837Ms 18Sn_1013“Idaṃ vadāmīti na tassa hoti,
(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā):
Dhammesu niccheyya samuggahītaṃ;
Passañca diṭṭhīsu anuggahāya,
Ajjhattasantiṃ pacinaṃ adassaṃ”.

SC 4SC Verse 845PTS Verse 838Ms 18Sn_1014“Vinicchayā yāni pakappitāni,
(iti māgaṇḍiyo):
Te ve munī brūsi anuggahāya;
Ajjhattasantīti yametamatthaṃ,
Kathaṃ nu dhīrehi paveditaṃ taṃ”.

SC 5SC Verse 846PTS Verse 839Ms 18Sn_1015“Na diṭṭhiyā na sutiyā na ñāṇena,
(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā):
Sīlabbatenāpi na suddhimāha;
Adiṭṭhiyā assutiyā añāṇā,
Asīlatā abbatā nopi tena;
Ete ca nissajja anuggahāya,
Santo anissāya bhavaṃ na jappe”.

SC 6SC Verse 847PTS Verse 840Ms 18Sn_1016“No ce kira diṭṭhiyā na sutiyā na ñāṇena,
(iti māgaṇḍiyo):
Sīlabbatenāpi na suddhimāha;
Adiṭṭhiyā assutiyā añāṇā,
Asīlatā abbatā nopi tena;
Maññāmahaṃ momuhameva dhammaṃ,
Diṭṭhiyā eke paccenti suddhiṃ”.

SC 7SC Verse 848PTS Verse 841Ms 18Sn_1017“Diṭṭhañca nissāya anupucchamāno,
(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā):
Samuggahītesu pamohamāgā;
Ito ca nāddakkhi aṇumpi saññaṃ,
Tasmā tuvaṃ momuhato dahāsi.

SC 8SC Verse 849PTS Verse 842Ms 18Sn_1018Samo visesī uda vā nihīno,
Yo maññati so vivadetha tena;
Tīsu vidhāsu avikampamāno,
Samo visesīti na tassa hoti.

SC 9SC Verse 850PTS Verse 843Ms 18Sn_1019Saccanti so brāhmaṇo kiṃ vadeyya,
Musāti vā so vivadetha kena;
Yasmiṃ samaṃ visamaṃ vāpi natthi,
Sa kena vādaṃ paṭisaṃyujeyya.

SC 10SC Verse 851PTS Verse 844Ms 18Sn_1020Okaṃ pahāya aniketasārī,
Gāme akubbaṃ muni santhavāni;
Kāmehi ritto apurakkharāno,
Kathaṃ na viggayha janena kayirā.

SC 11SC Verse 852PTS Verse 845Ms 18Sn_1021Yehi vivitto vicareyya loke,
Na tāni uggayha vadeyya nāgo;
Jalambujaṃ kaṇḍakavārijaṃ yathā,
Jalena paṅkena canūpalittaṃ;
Evaṃ munī santivādo agiddho,
Kāme ca loke ca anūpalitto.

SC 12SC Verse 853PTS Verse 846Ms 18Sn_1022Na vedagū diṭṭhiyāyako na mutiyā,
Sa mānameti na hi tammayo so;
Na kammunā nopi sutena neyyo,
Anūpanīto sa nivesanesu.

SC 13SC Verse 854PTS Verse 847Ms 18Sn_1023Saññāvirattassa na santi ganthā,
Paññāvimuttassa na santi mohā;
Saññañca diṭṭhiñca ye aggahesuṃ,
Te ghaṭṭayantā vicaranti loke”ti.

Māgaṇḍiyasuttaṃ navamaṃ.
