• Sutta Nipāta 4.1

Kāmasutta

SC 1SC Verse 773PTS Verse 766Ms 18Sn_934Kāmaṃ kāmayamānassa,
tassa ce taṃ samijjhati;
Addhā pītimano hoti,
laddhā macco yadicchati.

SC 2SC Verse 774PTS Verse 767Ms 18Sn_935Tassa ce kāmayānassa,
chandajātassa jantuno;
Te kāmā parihāyanti,
sallaviddhova ruppati.

SC 3SC Verse 775PTS Verse 768Ms 18Sn_936Yo kāme parivajjeti,
sappasseva padā siro;
Somaṃ visattikaṃ loke,
sato samativattati.

SC 4SC Verse 776PTS Verse 769Ms 18Sn_937Khettaṃ vatthuṃ hiraññaṃ vā,
gavāssaṃ dāsaporisaṃ;
Thiyo bandhū puthu kāme,
yo naro anugijjhati.

SC 5SC Verse 777PTS Verse 770Ms 18Sn_938Abalā naṃ balīyanti,
maddantenaṃ parissayā;
Tato naṃ dukkhamanveti,
nāvaṃ bhinnamivodakaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 778PTS Verse 771Ms 18Sn_939Tasmā jantu sadā sato,
Kāmāni parivajjaye;
Te pahāya tare oghaṃ,
Nāvaṃ sitvāva pāragūti.

Kāmasuttaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
