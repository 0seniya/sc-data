• Sutta Nipāta 4.16

Sāriputtasutta

SC 1SC Verse 962PTS Verse 955Ms 18Sn_1138“Na me diṭṭho ito pubbe,
(iccāyasmā sāriputto):
Na suto uda kassaci;
Evaṃ vagguvado satthā,
Tusitā gaṇimāgato.

SC 2SC Verse 963PTS Verse 956Ms 18Sn_1139Sadevakassa lokassa,
yathā dissati cakkhumā;
Sabbaṃ tamaṃ vinodetvā,
ekova ratimajjhagā.

SC 3SC Verse 964PTS Verse 957PTS vp Pali 186Ms 18Sn_1140Taṃ buddhaṃ asitaṃ tādiṃ,
akuhaṃ gaṇimāgataṃ;
Bahūnamidha baddhānaṃ,
atthi pañhena āgamaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 965PTS Verse 958Ms 18Sn_1141Bhikkhuno vijigucchato,
bhajato rittamāsanaṃ;
Rukkhamūlaṃ susānaṃ vā,
pabbatānaṃ guhāsu vā.

SC 5SC Verse 966PTS Verse 959Ms 18Sn_1142Uccāvacesu sayanesu,
kīvanto tattha bheravā;
Yehi bhikkhu na vedheyya,
nigghose sayanāsane.

SC 6SC Verse 967PTS Verse 960Ms 18Sn_1143Katī parissayā loke,
gacchato agataṃ disaṃ;
Ye bhikkhu abhisambhave,
pantamhi sayanāsane.

SC 7SC Verse 968PTS Verse 961Ms 18Sn_1144Kyāssa byappathayo assu,
kyāssassu idha gocarā;
Kāni sīlabbatānāssu,
pahitattassa bhikkhuno.

SC 8SC Verse 969PTS Verse 962Ms 18Sn_1145Kaṃ so sikkhaṃ samādāya,
ekodi nipako sato;
Kammāro rajatasseva,
niddhame malamattano”.

SC 9SC Verse 970PTS Verse 963Ms 18Sn_1146“Vijigucchamānassa yadidaṃ phāsu,
(sāriputtāti bhagavā):
Rittāsanaṃ sayanaṃ sevato ce;
Sambodhikāmassa yathānudhammaṃ,
Taṃ te pavakkhāmi yathā pajānaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 971PTS Verse 964Ms 18Sn_1147Pañcannaṃ dhīro bhayānaṃ na bhāye,
Bhikkhu sato sapariyantacārī;
Ḍaṃsādhipātānaṃ sarīsapānaṃ,
Manussaphassānaṃ catuppadānaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 972PTS Verse 965PTS vp Pali 187Ms 18Sn_1148Paradhammikānampi na santaseyya,
Disvāpi tesaṃ bahubheravāni;
Athāparāni abhisambhaveyya,
Parissayāni kusalānuesī.

SC 12SC Verse 973PTS Verse 966Ms 18Sn_1149Ātaṅkaphassena khudāya phuṭṭho,
Sītaṃ athuṇhaṃ adhivāsayeyya;
So tehi phuṭṭho bahudhā anoko,
Viriyaṃ parakkammadaḷhaṃ kareyya.

SC 13SC Verse 974PTS Verse 967Ms 18Sn_1150Theyyaṃ na kāre na musā bhaṇeyya,na kāre na musā bhaṇeyya,
Mettāya phasse tasathāvarāni;
Yadāvilattaṃ manaso vijaññā,
Kaṇhassa pakkhoti vinodayeyya.

SC 14SC Verse 975PTS Verse 968Ms 18Sn_1151Kodhātimānassa vasaṃ na gacche,
Mūlampi tesaṃ palikhañña tiṭṭhe;
Athappiyaṃ vā pana appiyaṃ vā,
Addhā bhavanto abhisambhaveyya.

SC 15SC Verse 976PTS Verse 969Ms 18Sn_1152Paññaṃ purakkhatvā kalyāṇapīti,
Vikkhambhaye tāni parissayāni;
Aratiṃ sahetha sayanamhi pante,
Caturo sahetha paridevadhamme.

SC 16SC Verse 977PTS Verse 970Ms 18Sn_1153Kiṃsū asissāmi kuva vā asissaṃ,kuva vā asissaṃ,
Dukkhaṃ vata settha kvajja sessaṃ;
Ete vitakke paridevaneyye,
Vinayetha sekho aniketacārī.

SC 17SC Verse 978PTS Verse 971PTS vp Pali 188Ms 18Sn_1154Annañca laddhā vasanañca kāle,
Mattaṃ so jaññā idha tosanatthaṃ;
So tesu gutto yatacāri gāme,
Rusitopi vācaṃ pharusaṃ na vajjā.

SC 18SC Verse 979PTS Verse 972Ms 18Sn_1155Okkhittacakkhu na ca pādalolo,
Jhānānuyutto bahujāgarassa;
Upekkhamārabbha samāhitatto,
Takkāsayaṃ kukkucciyūpachinde.

SC 19SC Verse 980PTS Verse 973Ms 18Sn_1156Cudito vacībhi satimābhinande,
Sabrahmacārīsu khilaṃ pabhinde;
Vācaṃ pamuñce kusalaṃ nātivelaṃ,
Janavādadhammāya na cetayeyya.

SC 20SC Verse 981PTS Verse 974Ms 18Sn_1157Athāparaṃ pañca rajāni loke,
Yesaṃ satīmā vinayāya sikkhe;
Rūpesu saddesu atho rasesu,
Gandhesu phassesu sahetha rāgaṃ.

SC 21SC Verse 982PTS Verse 975PTS vp Pali 189Ms 18Sn_1158Etesu dhammesu vineyya chandaṃ,
Bhikkhu satimā suvimuttacitto;
Kālena so sammā dhammaṃ parivīmaṃsamāno,
Ekodibhūto vihane tamaṃ so”ti.

Sāriputtasuttaṃ soḷasamaṃ.

Aṭṭhakavaggo catuttho.

SC 22Ms 18Sn_1162Kāmaṃ guhañca duṭṭhā ca,
suddhañca paramā jarā;
Metteyyo ca pasūro ca,
māgaṇḍi purābhedanaṃ.

SC 23Ms 18Sn_1163Kalahaṃ dve ca byūhāni,
punadeva tuvaṭṭakaṃ;
Attadaṇḍavaraṃ suttaṃ,
therapuṭṭhena soḷasa;
Iti etāni suttāni,
sabbānaṭṭhakavaggikāti.
