• Sutta Nipāta 4.10

Purābhedasutta

SC 1SC Verse 855PTS Verse 848Ms 18Sn_1025“Kathaṃdassī kathaṃsīlo,
upasantoti vuccati;
Taṃ me gotama pabrūhi,
pucchito uttamaṃ naraṃ”.

SC 2SC Verse 856PTS Verse 849Ms 18Sn_1026“Vītataṇho purā bhedā,
(iti bhagavā):
Pubbamantamanissito;
Vemajjhe nupasaṅkheyyo,
Tassa natthi purakkhataṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 857PTS Verse 850PTS vp Pali 167Ms 18Sn_1027Akkodhano asantāsī,
avikatthī akukkuco;
Mantabhāṇī anuddhato,
sa ve vācāyato muni.

SC 4SC Verse 858PTS Verse 851Ms 18Sn_1028Nirāsatti anāgate,
atītaṃ nānusocati;
Vivekadassī phassesu,
diṭṭhīsu ca na nīyati.

SC 5SC Verse 859PTS Verse 852Ms 18Sn_1029Patilīno akuhako,
apihālu amaccharī;
Appagabbho ajeguccho,
pesuṇeyye ca no yuto.

SC 6SC Verse 860PTS Verse 853Ms 18Sn_1030Sātiyesu anassāvī,
atimāne ca no yuto;
Saṇho ca paṭibhānavā,
na saddho na virajjati.

SC 7SC Verse 861PTS Verse 854Ms 18Sn_1031Lābhakamyā na sikkhati,
alābhe ca na kuppati;
Aviruddho ca taṇhāya,
rasesu nānugijjhati.

SC 8SC Verse 862PTS Verse 855Ms 18Sn_1032Upekkhako sadā sato,
na loke maññate samaṃ;
Na visesī na nīceyyo,
tassa no santi ussadā.

SC 9SC Verse 863PTS Verse 856Ms 18Sn_1033Yassa nissayanā natthi,
ñatvā dhammaṃ anissito;
Bhavāya vibhavāya vā,
taṇhā yassa na vijjati.

SC 10SC Verse 864PTS Verse 857Ms 18Sn_1034Taṃ brūmi upasantoti,
Kāmesu anapekkhinaṃ;
Ganthā tassa na vijjanti,
Atarī so visattikaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 865PTS Verse 858PTS vp Pali 168Ms 18Sn_1035Na tassa puttā pasavo,
Khettaṃ vatthuñca vijjati;
Attā vāpi nirattā vā,
Na tasmiṃ upalabbhati.

SC 12SC Verse 866PTS Verse 859Ms 18Sn_1036Yena naṃ vajjuṃ puthujjanā,
Atho samaṇabrāhmaṇā;
Taṃ tassa apurakkhataṃ,
Tasmā vādesu nejati.

SC 13SC Verse 867PTS Verse 860Ms 18Sn_1037Vītagedho amaccharī,
Na ussesu vadate muni;
Na samesu na omesu,
Kappaṃ neti akappiyo.

SC 14SC Verse 868PTS Verse 861Ms 18Sn_1038Yassa loke sakaṃ natthi,
Asatā ca na socati;
Dhammesu ca na gacchati,
Sa ve santoti vuccatī”ti.

Purābhedasuttaṃ dasamaṃ.
