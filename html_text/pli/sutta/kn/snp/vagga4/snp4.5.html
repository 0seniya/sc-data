• Sutta Nipāta 4.5

Paramaṭṭhakasutta

SC 1SC Verse 803PTS Verse 796SC 1SC Verse 803PTS Verse 796Ms 18Sn_968Paramanti diṭṭhīsu paribbasāno,
Yaduttari kurute jantu loke;
Hīnāti aññe tato sabbamāha,
Tasmā vivādāni avītivatto.

SC 2SC Verse 804PTS Verse 797Ms 18Sn_969Yadattanī passati ānisaṃsaṃ,SC 2SC Verse 804PTS Verse 797Ms 18Sn_969Yadattanī passati ānisaṃsaṃ,
Diṭṭhe sute sīlavate mute vā;
Tadeva so tattha samuggahāya,
Nihīnato passati sabbamaññaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 805PTS Verse 798Ms 18Sn_970Taṃ vāpi ganthaṃ kusalā vadanti,SC 3SC Verse 805PTS Verse 798Ms 18Sn_970Taṃ vāpi ganthaṃ kusalā vadanti,
Yaṃ nissito passati hīnamaññaṃ;
Tasmā hi diṭṭhaṃ va sutaṃ mutaṃ vā,
Sīlabbataṃ bhikkhu na nissayeyya.

SC 4SC Verse 806PTS Verse 799Ms 18Sn_971Diṭṭhimpi lokasmiṃ na kappayeyya,SC 4SC Verse 806PTS Verse 799Ms 18Sn_971Diṭṭhimpi lokasmiṃ na kappayeyya,
Ñāṇena vā sīlavatena vāpi;
Samoti attānamanūpaneyya,
Hīno na maññetha visesi vāpi.

SC 5SC Verse 807PTS Verse 800Ms 18Sn_972Attaṃ pahāya anupādiyāno,SC 5SC Verse 807PTS Verse 800Ms 18Sn_972Attaṃ pahāya anupādiyāno,
Ñāṇepi so nissayaṃ no karoti;
Sa ve viyattesu na vaggasārī,
Diṭṭhimpi so na pacceti kiñci.

SC 6SC Verse 808PTS Verse 801Ms 18Sn_973Yassūbhayante paṇidhīdha natthi,SC 6SC Verse 808PTS Verse 801Ms 18Sn_973Yassūbhayante paṇidhīdha natthi,
Bhavābhavāya idha vā huraṃ vā;
Nivesanā tassa na santi keci,
Dhammesu niccheyya samuggahītaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 809PTS Verse 802Ms 18Sn_974Tassīdha diṭṭhe va sute mute vā,SC 7SC Verse 809PTS Verse 802Ms 18Sn_974Tassīdha diṭṭhe va sute mute vā,
Pakappitā natthi aṇūpi saññā;
Taṃ brāhmaṇaṃ diṭṭhimanādiyānaṃ,
Kenīdha lokasmiṃ vikappayeyya.

SC 8SC Verse 810PTS Verse 803Ms 18Sn_975Na kappayanti na purekkharonti,SC 8SC Verse 810PTS Verse 803Ms 18Sn_975Na kappayanti na purekkharonti,
Dhammāpi tesaṃ na paṭicchitāse;
Na brāhmaṇo sīlavatena neyyo,
Pāraṅgato na pacceti tādīti.

Paramaṭṭhakasuttaṃ pañcamaṃ.
