• Sutta Nipāta 4.7

Tissametteyyasutta

SC 1SC Verse 821PTS Verse 814Ms 18Sn_988“Methunamanuyuttassa,
(iccāyasmā tisso metteyyo):
Vighātaṃ brūhi mārisa;
Sutvāna tava sāsanaṃ,
Viveke sikkhissāmase”.

SC 2SC Verse 822PTS Verse 815Ms 18Sn_989“Methunamanuyuttassa,
(metteyyāti bhagavā):
Mussate vāpi sāsanaṃ;
Micchā ca paṭipajjati,
Etaṃ tasmiṃ anāriyaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 823PTS Verse 816Ms 18Sn_990Eko pubbe caritvāna,
methunaṃ yo nisevati;
Yānaṃ bhantaṃva taṃ loke,
hīnamāhu puthujjanaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 824PTS Verse 817Ms 18Sn_991Yaso kitti ca yā pubbe,
hāyate vāpi tassa sā;
Etampi disvā sikkhetha,
methunaṃ vippahātave.

SC 5SC Verse 825PTS Verse 818Ms 18Sn_992Saṅkappehi pareto so,
kapaṇo viya jhāyati;
Sutvā paresaṃ nigghosaṃ,
maṅku hoti tathāvidho.

SC 6SC Verse 826PTS Verse 819Ms 18Sn_993Atha satthāni kurute,
paravādehi codito;
Esa khvassa mahāgedho,
mosavajjaṃ pagāhati.

SC 7SC Verse 827PTS Verse 820Ms 18Sn_994Paṇḍitoti samaññāto,
ekacariyaṃ adhiṭṭhito;
Athāpi methune yutto,
mandova parikissati.

SC 8SC Verse 828PTS Verse 821Ms 18Sn_995Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
Muni pubbāpare idha;
Ekacariyaṃ daḷhaṃ kayirā,
Na nisevetha methunaṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 829PTS Verse 822Ms 18Sn_996Vivekaññeva sikkhetha,
etaṃ ariyānamuttamaṃ;
Na tena seṭṭho maññetha,
sa ve nibbānasantike.

SC 10SC Verse 830PTS Verse 823Ms 18Sn_997Rittassa munino carato,
Kāmesu anapekkhino;
Oghatiṇṇassa pihayanti,
Kāmesu gadhitā pajā”ti.

Tissametteyyasuttaṃ sattamaṃ.
