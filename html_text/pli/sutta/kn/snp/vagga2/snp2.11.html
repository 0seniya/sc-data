• Sutta Nipāta 2.11

Rāhulasutta

SC 1SC Verse 338PTS Verse 335Ms 18Sn_391“Kacci abhiṇhasaṃvāsā,
nāvajānāsi paṇḍitaṃ;
Ukkādhāro manussānaṃ,
kacci apacito tayā”.

SC 2SC Verse 339PTS Verse 336Ms 18Sn_392“Nāhaṃ abhiṇhasaṃvāsā,
avajānāmi paṇḍitaṃ;
Ukkādhāro manussānaṃ,
niccaṃ apacito mayā”.

SC 3SC Verse 340PTS Verse 337Ms 18Sn_393“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,
piyarūpe manorame;
Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,
dukkhassantakaro bhava.

SC 4SC Verse 341PTS Verse 338Ms 18Sn_394Mitte bhajassu kalyāṇe,
pantañca sayanāsanaṃ;
Vivittaṃ appanigghosaṃ,
mattaññū hohi bhojane.

SC 5SC Verse 342PTS Verse 339Ms 18Sn_395Cīvare piṇḍapāte ca,
paccaye sayanāsane;
Etesu taṇhaṃ mākāsi,
mā lokaṃ punarāgami.

SC 6SC Verse 343PTS Verse 340Ms 18Sn_396Saṃvuto pātimokkhasmiṃ,
indriyesu ca pañcasu;
Sati kāyagatā tyatthu,
nibbidābahulo bhava.

SC 7SC Verse 344PTS Verse 341Ms 18Sn_397Nimittaṃ parivajjehi,
subhaṃ rāgūpasañhitaṃ;
Asubhāya cittaṃ bhāvehi,
ekaggaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 345PTS Verse 342Ms 18Sn_398Animittañca bhāvehi,
Mānānusayamujjaha;
Tato mānābhisamayā,
Upasanto carissatī”ti.

SC 9Ms 18Sn_399Itthaṃ sudaṃ bhagavā āyasmantaṃ rāhulaṃ imāhi gāthāhi abhiṇhaṃ ovadatīti.

Rāhulasuttaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
