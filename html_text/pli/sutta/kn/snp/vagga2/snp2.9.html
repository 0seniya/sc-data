• Sutta Nipāta 2.9

Kiṃsīlasutta

SC 1SC Verse 327PTS Verse 324Ms 18Sn_378“Kiṃsīlo kiṃsamācāro,
kāni kammāni brūhayaṃ;
Naro sammā niviṭṭhassa,
uttamatthañca pāpuṇe”.

SC 2SC Verse 328PTS Verse 325Ms 18Sn_379“Vuḍḍhāpacāyī anusūyako siyā,
Kālaññū cassa Kālaññū cassa garūnaṃ dassanāya;
Dhammiṃ kathaṃ erayitaṃ khaṇaññū,
Suṇeyya sakkacca subhāsitāni.

SC 3SC Verse 329PTS Verse 326PTS vp Pali 57Ms 18Sn_380Kālena gacche garūnaṃ sakāsaṃ,
Thambhaṃ niraṃkatvā nivātavutti;
Atthaṃ dhammaṃ saṃyamaṃ brahmacariyaṃ,
Anussare ceva samācare ca.

SC 4SC Verse 330PTS Verse 327Ms 18Sn_381Dhammārāmo dhammarato,
Dhamme ṭhito dhammavinicchayaññū;
Nevācare dhammasandosavādaṃ,
Tacchehi nīyetha subhāsitehi.

SC 5SC Verse 331PTS Verse 328Ms 18Sn_382Hassaṃ jappaṃ paridevaṃ padosaṃ,
Māyākataṃ kuhanaṃ giddhi mānaṃ;
Sārambhaṃ kakkasaṃ kasāvañca mucchaṃ,
Hitvā care vītamado ṭhitatto.

SC 6SC Verse 332PTS Verse 329Ms 18Sn_383Viññātasārāni subhāsitāni,
Sutañca viññātasamādhisāraṃ;
Na tassa paññā ca sutañca vaḍḍhati,
Yo sāhaso hoti naro pamatto.

SC 7SC Verse 333PTS Verse 330Ms 18Sn_384Dhamme ca ye ariyapavedite ratā,
Anuttarā te vacasā manasā kammunā ca;
Te santisoraccasamādhisaṇṭhitā,
Sutassa paññāya ca sāramajjhagū”ti.

Kiṃsīlasuttaṃ navamaṃ.
