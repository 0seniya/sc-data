• Sutta Nipāta 2.1

Ratanasutta

SC 1SC Verse 224PTS Verse 222Ms 18Sn_260Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Sabbeva bhūtā sumanā bhavantu,
Athopi sakkacca suṇantu bhāsitaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 225PTS Verse 223Ms 18Sn_261Tasmā hi bhūtā nisāmetha sabbe,
Mettaṃ karotha mānusiyā pajāya;
Divā ca ratto ca haranti ye baliṃ,
Tasmā hi ne rakkhatha appamattā.

SC 3SC Verse 226PTS Verse 224Ms 18Sn_262Yaṃ kiñci vittaṃ idha vā huraṃ vā,
Saggesu vā yaṃ ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Na no samaṃ atthi tathāgatena,
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 4SC Verse 227PTS Verse 225Ms 18Sn_263Khayaṃ virāgaṃ amataṃ paṇītaṃ,
Yadajjhagā sakyamunī samāhito;
Na tena dhammena samatthi kiñci,
Idampi dhamme ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 5SC Verse 228PTS Verse 226Ms 18Sn_264Yaṃ buddhaseṭṭho parivaṇṇayī suciṃ,
Samādhimānantarikaññamāhu;
Samādhinā tena samo na vijjati,
Idampi dhamme ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 6SC Verse 229PTS Verse 227Ms 18Sn_265Ye puggalā aṭṭha sataṃ pasatthā,
Cattāri etāni yugāni honti;
Te dakkhiṇeyyā sugatassa sāvakā,
Etesu dinnāni mahapphalāni;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 7SC Verse 230PTS Verse 228Ms 18Sn_266Ye suppayuttā manasā daḷhena,
Nikkāmino gotamasāsanamhi;
Te pattipattā amataṃ vigayha,
Laddhā mudhā nibbutiṃ bhuñjamānā;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 8SC Verse 231PTS Verse 229Ms 18Sn_267Yathindakhīlo pathavissito siyā,pathavissito siyā,
Catubbhi vātehi asampakampiyo;
Tathūpamaṃ sappurisaṃ vadāmi,
Yo ariyasaccāni avecca passati;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 9SC Verse 232PTS Verse 230Ms 18Sn_268Ye ariyasaccāni vibhāvayanti,
Gambhīrapaññena sudesitāni;
Kiñcāpi te honti bhusaṃ pamattā,
Na te bhavaṃ aṭṭhamamādiyanti;
Idampi saṅghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 10SC Verse 233PTS Verse 231Ms 18Sn_269Sahāvassa dassanasampadāya,Sahāvassa dassanasampadāya,
Tayassu dhammā jahitā bhavanti;
Sakkāyadiṭṭhī vicikicchitañca,
Sīlabbataṃ vāpi yadatthi kiñci.

SC 11SC Verse 234Ms 18Sn_270Catūhapāyehi ca vippamutto,
Chaccābhiṭhānāni Chaccābhiṭhānāni abhabba kātuṃ;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 12SC Verse 235PTS Verse 232Ms 18Sn_271Kiñcāpi so kamma karoti pāpakaṃ,kamma karoti pāpakaṃ,
Kāyena vācā uda cetasā vā;
Abhabba so tassa Abhabba so tassa paṭicchadāya,
Abhabbatā diṭṭhapadassa vuttā;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 13SC Verse 236PTS Verse 233Ms 18Sn_272Vanappagumbe yatha phussitagge,yatha phussitagge,
Gimhānamāse paṭhamasmiṃ gimhe;
Tathūpamaṃ dhammavaraṃ adesayi,
Nibbānagāmiṃ paramaṃ hitāya;
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 14SC Verse 237PTS Verse 234Ms 18Sn_273Varo varaññū varado varāharo,
Anuttaro dhammavaraṃ adesayi;
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 15SC Verse 238PTS Verse 235Ms 18Sn_274Khīṇaṃ purāṇaṃ nava natthi sambhavaṃ,
Virattacittāyatike bhavasmiṃ;
Te khīṇabījā avirūḷhichandā,
Nibbanti dhīrā yathāyaṃ padīpo;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

SC 16SC Verse 239PTS Verse 236Ms 18Sn_275Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Buddhaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotu.

SC 17SC Verse 240PTS Verse 237Ms 18Sn_276Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Dhammaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotu.

SC 18SC Verse 241PTS Verse 238Ms 18Sn_277Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Saṅghaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotūti.

Ratanasuttaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
