• Sutta Nipāta 2.3

Hirisutta

SC 1SC Verse 256PTS Verse 253Ms 18Sn_294Hiriṃ tarantaṃ vijigucchamānaṃ,
Tavāhamasmi iti bhāsamānaṃ;
Sayhāni kammāni anādiyantaṃ,
Neso mamanti iti naṃ vijaññā.

SC 2SC Verse 257PTS Verse 254Ms 18Sn_295Ananvayaṃ piyaṃ vācaṃ,Ananvayaṃ piyaṃ vācaṃ,
yo mittesu pakubbati;
Akarontaṃ bhāsamānaṃ,
parijānanti paṇḍitā.

SC 3SC Verse 258PTS Verse 255Ms 18Sn_296Na so mitto yo sadā appamatto,
Bhedāsaṅkī randhamevānupassī;
Yasmiñca seti urasīva putto,
Sa ve mitto yo parehi abhejjo.

SC 4SC Verse 259PTS Verse 256Ms 18Sn_297Pāmujjakaraṇaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
pasaṃsāvahanaṃ sukhaṃ;
Phalānisaṃso bhāveti,
vahanto porisaṃ dhuraṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 260PTS Verse 257Ms 18Sn_298Pavivekarasaṃ pitvā,
Rasaṃ upasamassa ca;
Niddaro hoti nippāpo,
Dhammapītirasaṃ pivanti.

Hirisuttaṃ tatiyaṃ.
