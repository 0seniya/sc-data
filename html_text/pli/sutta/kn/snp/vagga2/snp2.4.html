• Sutta Nipāta 2.4

Maṅgalasutta

SC 1Ms 18Sn_300Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​ ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Atha kho aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

SC 2SC Verse 261PTS Verse 258Ms 18Sn_301“Bahū devā manussā ca,
maṅgalāni acintayuṃ;
Ākaṅkhamānā sotthānaṃ,
brūhi maṅgalamuttamaṃ”.

SC 3SC Verse 262PTS Verse 259Ms 18Sn_302“Asevanā ca bālānaṃ,
paṇḍitānañca sevanā;
Pūjā ca pūjaneyyānaṃ,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 263PTS Verse 260Ms 18Sn_303Patirūpadesavāso ca,
pubbe ca katapuññatā;
Attasammāpaṇidhi ca,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 264PTS Verse 261Ms 18Sn_304Bāhusaccañca sippañca,
vinayo ca susikkhito;
Subhāsitā ca yā vācā,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 6SC Verse 265PTS Verse 262Ms 18Sn_305Mātāpitu upaṭṭhānaṃ,
puttadārassa saṅgaho;
Anākulā ca kammantā,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 7SC Verse 266PTS Verse 263Ms 18Sn_306Dānañca dhammacariyā ca,
ñātakānañca saṅgaho;
Anavajjāni kammāni,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 8SC Verse 267PTS Verse 264Ms 18Sn_307Āratī viratī pāpā,
majjapānā ca saṃyamo;
Appamādo ca dhammesu,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 268PTS Verse 265Ms 18Sn_308Gāravo ca nivāto ca,
santuṭṭhi ca kataññutā;
Kālena dhammassavanaṃ,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 269PTS Verse 266Ms 18Sn_309Khantī ca sovacassatā,
samaṇānañca dassanaṃ;
Kālena dhammasākacchā,
etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 11SC Verse 270PTS Verse 267Ms 18Sn_310Tapo ca brahmacariyañca,
Ariyasaccāna dassanaṃ;
Nibbānasacchikiriyā ca,
Etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 271PTS Verse 268Ms 18Sn_311Phuṭṭhassa lokadhammehi,
Cittaṃ yassa na kampati;
Asokaṃ virajaṃ khemaṃ,
Etaṃ maṅgalamuttamaṃ.

SC 13SC Verse 272PTS Verse 269Ms 18Sn_312Etādisāni katvāna,
Sabbattha maparājitā;
Sabbattha sotthiṃ gacchanti,
Taṃ tesaṃ maṅgalamuttaman”ti.

Maṅgalasuttaṃ catutthaṃ.
