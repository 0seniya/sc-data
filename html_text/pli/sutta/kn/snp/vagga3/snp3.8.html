• Sutta Nipāta 3.8

Sallasutta

SC 1SC Verse 580PTS Verse 574Ms 18Sn_695Animittamanaññātaṃ,
maccānaṃ idha jīvitaṃ;
Kasirañca parittañca,
tañca dukkhena saṃyutaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 581PTS Verse 575PTS vp Pali 113Ms 18Sn_696Na hi so upakkamo atthi,
yena jātā na miyyare;
Jarampi patvā maraṇaṃ,
evaṃdhammā hi pāṇino.

SC 3SC Verse 582PTS Verse 576Ms 18Sn_697Phalānamiva pakkānaṃ,
pāto patanato bhayaṃ;
Evaṃ jātāna maccānaṃ,
niccaṃ maraṇato bhayaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 583PTS Verse 577Ms 18Sn_698Yathāpi kumbhakārassa,
katā mattikabhājanā;
Sabbe bhedanapariyantā,
evaṃ maccāna jīvitaṃ.

SC 5SC Verse 584PTS Verse 578Ms 18Sn_699Daharā ca mahantā ca,
ye bālā ye ca paṇḍitā;
Sabbe maccuvasaṃ yanti,
sabbe maccuparāyaṇā.

SC 6SC Verse 585PTS Verse 579Ms 18Sn_700Tesaṃ maccuparetānaṃ,
gacchataṃ paralokato;
Na pitā tāyate puttaṃ,
ñātī vā pana ñātake.

SC 7SC Verse 586PTS Verse 580Ms 18Sn_701Pekkhataṃyeva ñātīnaṃ,
passa lālapataṃ puthu;
Ekamekova maccānaṃ,
govajjho viya nīyati.

SC 8SC Verse 587PTS Verse 581Ms 18Sn_702Evamabbhāhato loko,
Maccunā ca jarāya ca;
Tasmā dhīrā na socanti,
Viditvā lokapariyāyaṃ.

SC 9SC Verse 588PTS Verse 582Ms 18Sn_703Yassa maggaṃ na jānāsi,
āgatassa gatassa vā;
Ubho ante asampassaṃ,
niratthaṃ paridevasi.

SC 10SC Verse 589PTS Verse 583Ms 18Sn_704Paridevayamāno ce,
Kiñcidatthaṃ udabbahe;
Sammūḷho hiṃsamattānaṃ,
Kayirā ce naṃ vicakkhaṇo.

SC 11SC Verse 590PTS Verse 584Ms 18Sn_705Na hi ruṇṇena sokena,
Santiṃ pappoti cetaso;
Bhiyyassuppajjate dukkhaṃ,
Sarīraṃ cupahaññati.

SC 12SC Verse 591PTS Verse 585PTS vp Pali 114Ms 18Sn_706Kiso vivaṇṇo bhavati,
Hiṃsamattānamattanā;
Na tena petā pālenti,
Niratthā paridevanā.

SC 13SC Verse 592PTS Verse 586Ms 18Sn_707Sokamappajahaṃ jantu,
Bhiyyo dukkhaṃ nigacchati;
Anutthunanto kālaṅkataṃ,
Sokassa vasamanvagū.

SC 14SC Verse 593PTS Verse 587Ms 18Sn_708Aññepi passa gamine,
Yathākammupage nare;
Maccuno vasamāgamma,
Phandantevidha pāṇino.

SC 15SC Verse 594PTS Verse 588Ms 18Sn_709Yena yena hi maññanti,
Tato taṃ hoti aññathā;
Etādiso vinābhāvo,
Passa lokassa pariyāyaṃ.

SC 16SC Verse 595PTS Verse 589Ms 18Sn_710Api vassasataṃ jīve,
Bhiyyo vā pana māṇavo;
Ñātisaṅghā vinā hoti,
Jahāti idha jīvitaṃ.

SC 17SC Verse 596PTS Verse 590Ms 18Sn_711Tasmā arahato sutvā,
Vineyya paridevitaṃ;
Petaṃ kālaṅkataṃ disvā,
Neso labbhā mayā iti.

SC 18SC Verse 597PTS Verse 591Ms 18Sn_712Yathā saraṇamādittaṃ,
Vārinā parinibbaye;
Evampi dhīro sapañño,
Paṇḍito kusalo naro;
Khippamuppatitaṃ sokaṃ,
Vāto tūlaṃva dhaṃsaye.

SC 19SC Verse 598PTS Verse 592Ms 18Sn_713Paridevaṃ pajappañca,
Domanassañca attano;
Attano sukhamesāno,
Abbahe sallamattano.

SC 20SC Verse 599PTS Verse 593Ms 18Sn_714Abbūḷhasallo asito,
santiṃ pappuyya cetaso;
Sabbasokaṃ atikkanto,
asoko hoti nibbutoti.

Sallasuttaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
