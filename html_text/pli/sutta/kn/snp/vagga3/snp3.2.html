• Sutta Nipāta 3.2

Padhānasutta

SC 1SC Verse 428PTS Verse 425Ms 18Sn_493“Taṃ maṃ padhānapahitattaṃ,
nadiṃ nerañjaraṃ pati;
Viparakkamma jhāyantaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā.

SC 2SC Verse 429PTS Verse 426Ms 18Sn_494Namucī karuṇaṃ vācaṃ,
bhāsamāno upāgami;
‘ Kiso tvamasi dubbaṇṇo,
santike maraṇaṃ tava.

SC 3SC Verse 430PTS Verse 427Ms 18Sn_495Sahassabhāgo maraṇassa,
ekaṃso tava jīvitaṃ;
Jīva bho jīvitaṃ seyyo,
jīvaṃ puññāni kāhasi.

SC 4SC Verse 431PTS Verse 428PTS vp Pali 75Ms 18Sn_496Carato ca te brahmacariyaṃ,
Aggihuttañca jūhato;
Pahūtaṃ cīyate puññaṃ,
Kiṃ padhānena kāhasi.

SC 5SC Verse 432PTS Verse 429Ms 18Sn_497Duggo maggo padhānāya,
dukkaro durabhisambhavo’”;
Imā gāthā bhaṇaṃ māro,
aṭṭhā buddhassa santike.

SC 6SC Verse 433PTS Verse 430Ms 18Sn_498Taṃ tathāvādinaṃ māraṃ,
bhagavā etadabravi;
“ Pamattabandhu pāpima,
yenatthena idhāgato.

SC 7SC Verse 434PTS Verse 431Ms 18Sn_499Aṇumattopi puññena,
Attho mayhaṃ na vijjati;
Yesañca attho puññena,
Te māro vattumarahati.

SC 8SC Verse 435PTS Verse 432Ms 18Sn_500Atthi saddhā tathā viriyaṃ,
paññā ca mama vijjati;
Evaṃ maṃ pahitattampi,
kiṃ jīvamanupucchasi.

SC 9SC Verse 436PTS Verse 433Ms 18Sn_501Nadīnamapi sotāni,
ayaṃ vāto visosaye;
Kiñca me pahitattassa,
lohitaṃ nupasussaye.

SC 10SC Verse 437PTS Verse 434Ms 18Sn_502Lohite sussamānamhi,
Pittaṃ semhañca sussati;
Maṃsesu khīyamānesu,
Bhiyyo cittaṃ pasīdati;
Bhiyyo sati ca paññā ca,
Samādhi mama tiṭṭhati.

SC 11SC Verse 438PTS Verse 435Ms 18Sn_503Tassa mevaṃ viharato,
Pattassuttamavedanaṃ;
Kāmesu nāpekkhate cittaṃ,
Passa sattassa suddhataṃ.

SC 12SC Verse 439PTS Verse 436PTS vp Pali 76Ms 18Sn_504Kāmā te paṭhamā senā,
Dutiyā arati vuccati;
Tatiyā khuppipāsā te,
Catutthī taṇhā pavuccati.

SC 13SC Verse 440PTS Verse 437Ms 18Sn_505Pañcamaṃ thinamiddhaṃ te,Pañcamaṃ thinamiddhaṃ te,
Chaṭṭhā bhīrū pavuccati;
Sattamī vicikicchā te,
Makkho thambho te aṭṭhamo.

SC 14SC Verse 441PTS Verse 438Ms 18Sn_506Lābho siloko sakkāro,
Micchāladdho ca yo yaso;
Yo cattānaṃ samukkaṃse,
Pare ca avajānati.

SC 15SC Verse 442PTS Verse 439Ms 18Sn_507Esā namuci te senā,
Kaṇhassābhippahārinī;
Na naṃ asūro jināti,
Jetvā ca labhate sukhaṃ.

SC 16SC Verse 443PTS Verse 440Ms 18Sn_508Esa muñjaṃ parihare,
Dhiratthu mama jīvitaṃ;
Saṅgāme me mataṃ seyyo,
Yañce jīve parājito.

SC 17SC Verse 444PTS Verse 441Ms 18Sn_509Pagāḷhettha na dissanti,
Eke samaṇabrāhmaṇā;
Tañca maggaṃ na jānanti,
Yena gacchanti subbatā.

SC 18SC Verse 445PTS Verse 442Ms 18Sn_510Samantā dhajiniṃ disvā,
Yuttaṃ māraṃ savāhanaṃ;
Yuddhāya paccuggacchāmi,
Mā maṃ ṭhānā acāvayi.

SC 19SC Verse 446PTS Verse 443PTS vp Pali 77Ms 18Sn_511Yaṃ te taṃ nappasahati,
Senaṃ loko sadevako;
Taṃ te paññāya bhecchāmi,
Āmaṃ pattaṃva asmanā.

SC 20SC Verse 447PTS Verse 444Ms 18Sn_512Vasīkaritvā saṅkappaṃ,Vasīkaritvā saṅkappaṃ,
Satiñca sūpatiṭṭhitaṃ;
Raṭṭhā raṭṭhaṃ vicarissaṃ,
Sāvake vinayaṃ puthū.

SC 21SC Verse 448PTS Verse 445Ms 18Sn_513Te appamattā pahitattā,
Mama sāsanakārakā;
Akāmassa te gamissanti,
Yattha gantvā na socare”.

SC 22SC Verse 449PTS Verse 446Ms 18Sn_514“Satta vassāni bhagavantaṃ,
Anubandhiṃ padāpadaṃ;
Otāraṃ nādhigacchissaṃ,
Sambuddhassa satīmato.

SC 23SC Verse 450PTS Verse 447Ms 18Sn_515Medavaṇṇaṃva pāsāṇaṃ,
Vāyaso anupariyagā;
Apettha muduṃ vindema,
Api assādanā siyā.

SC 24SC Verse 451PTS Verse 448Ms 18Sn_516Aladdhā tattha assādaṃ,
Vāyasetto apakkami;
Kākova selamāsajja,
Nibbijjāpema gotamaṃ”.

SC 25SC Verse 452PTS Verse 449PTS vp Pali 78Ms 18Sn_517Tassa sokaparetassa,
Vīṇā kacchā abhassatha;
Tato so dummano yakkho,
Tatthevantaradhāyathāti.

Padhānasuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.
