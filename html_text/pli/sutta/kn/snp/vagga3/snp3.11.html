• Sutta Nipāta 3.11

Nālakasutta

SC 1SC Verse 685PTS Verse 679Ms 18Sn_817Ānandajāte tidasagaṇe patīte,
Sakkañca indaṃ sucivasane ca deve;
Dussaṃ gahetvā atiriva thomayante,
Asito isi addasa divāvihāre.

SC 2SC Verse 686PTS Verse 680Ms 18Sn_818Disvāna deve muditamane udagge,
Cittiṃ karitvāna idamavoca tattha;
“ Kiṃ devasaṅgho atiriva kalyarūpo,
Dussaṃ gahetvā ramayatha kiṃ paṭicca.

SC 3SC Verse 687PTS Verse 681Ms 18Sn_819Yadāpi āsī asurehi saṅgamo,
Jayo surānaṃ asurā parājitā;
Tadāpi netādiso lomahaṃsano,
Kimabbhutaṃ daṭṭhu marū pamoditā.

SC 4SC Verse 688PTS Verse 682Ms 18Sn_820Seḷenti gāyanti ca vādayanti ca,
Bhujāni phoṭenti ca naccayanti ca;
Pucchāmi vohaṃ merumuddhavāsine,
Dhunātha me saṃsayaṃ khippa mārisā”.

SC 5SC Verse 689PTS Verse 683Ms 18Sn_821“So bodhisatto ratanavaro atulyo,
Manussaloke hitasukhatthāya jāto;
Sakyāna gāme janapade lumbineyye,
Tenamha tuṭṭhā atiriva kalyarūpā.

SC 6SC Verse 690PTS Verse 684Ms 18Sn_822So sabbasattuttamo aggapuggalo,
Narāsabho sabbapajānamuttamo;
Vattessati cakkamisivhaye vane,
Nadaṃva sīho balavā migābhibhū”.

SC 7SC Verse 691PTS Verse 685Ms 18Sn_823Taṃ saddaṃ sutvā turitamavasarī so,
Suddhodanassa tada bhavanaṃ upāvisi;
Nisajja tattha idamavocāsi sakye,
“ Kuhiṃ kumāro ahamapi daṭṭhukāmo”.

SC 8SC Verse 692PTS Verse 686Ms 18Sn_824Tato kumāraṃ jalitamiva suvaṇṇaṃ,
Ukkāmukheva sukusalasampahaṭṭhaṃ;
Daddallamānaṃ siriyā anomavaṇṇaṃ,
Dassesu puttaṃ asitavhayassa sakyā.

SC 9SC Verse 693PTS Verse 687Ms 18Sn_825Disvā kumāraṃ sikhimiva pajjalantaṃ,
Tārāsabhaṃva nabhasigamaṃ visuddhaṃ;
Suriyaṃ tapantaṃ saradarivabbhamuttaṃ,
Ānandajāto vipulamalattha pītiṃ.

SC 10SC Verse 694PTS Verse 688Ms 18Sn_826Anekasākhañca sahassamaṇḍalaṃ,
Chattaṃ marū dhārayumantalikkhe;
Suvaṇṇadaṇḍā vītipatanti cāmarā,
Na dissare cāmarachattagāhakā.

SC 11SC Verse 695PTS Verse 689Ms 18Sn_827Disvā jaṭī kaṇhasirivhayo isi,
Suvaṇṇanikkhaṃ viya paṇḍukambale;
Setañca chattaṃ dhariyanta muddhani,
Udaggacitto sumano paṭiggahe.

SC 12SC Verse 696PTS Verse 690Ms 18Sn_828Paṭiggahetvā pana sakyapuṅgavaṃ,
Jigīsako lakkhaṇamantapāragū;
Pasannacitto giramabbhudīrayi,
“ Anuttarāyaṃ dvipadānamuttamo”.

SC 13SC Verse 697PTS Verse 691Ms 18Sn_829Athattano gamanamanussaranto,
Akalyarūpo gaḷayati assukāni;
Disvāna sakyā isimavocuṃ rudantaṃ,
“ No ce kumāre bhavissati antarāyo”.

SC 14SC Verse 698PTS Verse 692Ms 18Sn_830Disvāna sakye isimavoca akalye,
“ Nāhaṃ kumāre ahitamanussarāmi;
Na cāpimassa bhavissati antarāyo,
Na orakāyaṃ adhimānasā bhavātha.

SC 15SC Verse 699PTS Verse 693Ms 18Sn_831Sambodhiyaggaṃ phusissatāyaṃ kumāro,
So dhammacakkaṃ paramavisuddhadassī;
Vattessatāyaṃ bahujanahitānukampī,
Vitthārikassa bhavissati brahmacariyaṃ.

SC 16SC Verse 700PTS Verse 694Ms 18Sn_832Mamañca āyu na ciramidhāvaseso,
Athantarā me bhavissati kālakiriyā;
Sohaṃ na sossaṃ asamadhurassa dhammaṃ,
Tenamhi aṭṭo byasanaṅgato aghāvī”.

SC 17SC Verse 701PTS Verse 695Ms 18Sn_833So sākiyānaṃ vipulaṃ janetvā pītiṃ,
Antepuramhā niggamā brahmacārī;
So bhāgineyyaṃ sayaṃ anukampamāno,
Samādapesi asamadhurassa dhamme.

SC 18SC Verse 702PTS Verse 696Ms 18Sn_834“Buddhoti ghosaṃ yada parato suṇāsi,yada parato suṇāsi,
Sambodhipatto vivarati dhammamaggaṃ;
Gantvāna tattha samayaṃ paripucchamāno,
Carassu tasmiṃ bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ”.

SC 19SC Verse 703PTS Verse 697Ms 18Sn_835Tenānusiṭṭho hitamanena tādinā,
Anāgate paramavisuddhadassinā;
So nālako upacitapuññasañcayo,
Jinaṃ patikkhaṃ parivasi rakkhitindriyo.

SC 20SC Verse 704PTS Verse 698Ms 18Sn_836Sutvāna ghosaṃ jinavaracakkavattane,
Gantvāna disvā isinisabhaṃ pasanno;
Moneyyaseṭṭhaṃ munipavaraṃ apucchi,
Samāgate asitāvhayassa sāsaneti.

Vatthugāthā niṭṭhitā.

SC 21SC Verse 705PTS Verse 699Ms 18Sn_838“Aññātametaṃ vacanaṃ,
Asitassa yathātathaṃ;
Taṃ taṃ gotama pucchāmi,
Sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ.

SC 22SC Verse 706PTS Verse 700Ms 18Sn_839Anagāriyupetassa,
Bhikkhācariyaṃ jigīsato;
Muni pabrūhi me puṭṭho,
Moneyyaṃ uttamaṃ padaṃ”.

SC 23SC Verse 707PTS Verse 701Ms 18Sn_840“Moneyyaṃ te upaññissaṃ,
(iti bhagavā):
Dukkaraṃ durabhisambhavaṃ;
Handa te naṃ pavakkhāmi,
Santhambhassu daḷho bhava.

SC 24SC Verse 708PTS Verse 702Ms 18Sn_841Samānabhāgaṃ kubbetha,
Gāme akkuṭṭhavanditaṃ;
Manopadosaṃ rakkheyya,
Santo anuṇṇato care.

SC 25SC Verse 709PTS Verse 703Ms 18Sn_842Uccāvacā niccharanti,
Dāye aggisikhūpamā;
Nāriyo muniṃ palobhenti,
Tā su taṃ mā palobhayuṃ.

SC 26SC Verse 710PTS Verse 704Ms 18Sn_843Virato methunā dhammā,
Hitvā kāme paropare;
Aviruddho asāratto,
Pāṇesu tasathāvare.

SC 27SC Verse 711PTS Verse 705Ms 18Sn_844Yathā ahaṃ tathā ete,
Yathā ete tathā ahaṃ;
Attānaṃ upamaṃ katvā,
Na haneyya na ghātaye.

SC 28SC Verse 712PTS Verse 706Ms 18Sn_845Hitvā icchañca lobhañca,
Yattha satto puthujjano;
Cakkhumā paṭipajjeyya,
Tareyya narakaṃ imaṃ.

SC 29SC Verse 713PTS Verse 707Ms 18Sn_846Ūnūdaro mitāhāro,
Appicchassa alolupo;
Sadā icchāya nicchāto,
Aniccho hoti nibbuto.

SC 30SC Verse 714PTS Verse 708Ms 18Sn_847Sa piṇḍacāraṃ caritvā,
Vanantamabhihāraye;
Upaṭṭhito rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
Āsanūpagato muni.

SC 31SC Verse 715PTS Verse 709Ms 18Sn_848Sa jhānapasuto dhīro,
Vanante ramito siyā;
Jhāyetha rukkhamūlasmiṃ,
Attānamabhitosayaṃ.

SC 32SC Verse 716PTS Verse 710Ms 18Sn_849Tato ratyā vivasāne,vivasāne,
Gāmantamabhihāraye;
Avhānaṃ nābhinandeyya,
Abhihārañca gāmato.

SC 33SC Verse 717PTS Verse 711Ms 18Sn_850Na munī gāmamāgamma,
Kulesu sahasā care;
Ghāsesanaṃ chinnakatho,
Na vācaṃ payutaṃ bhaṇe.

SC 34SC Verse 718PTS Verse 712Ms 18Sn_851Alatthaṃ yadidaṃ sādhu,
Nālatthaṃ kusalaṃ iti;
Ubhayeneva so tādī,
Rukkhaṃvupanivattati.

SC 35SC Verse 719PTS Verse 713Ms 18Sn_852Sa pattapāṇi vicaranto,
Amūgo mūgasammato;
Appaṃ dānaṃ na hīḷeyya,
Dātāraṃ nāvajāniyā.

SC 36SC Verse 720PTS Verse 714Ms 18Sn_853Uccāvacā hi paṭipadā,
Samaṇena pakāsitā;
Na pāraṃ diguṇaṃ yanti,
Nayidaṃ ekaguṇaṃ mutaṃ.

SC 37SC Verse 721PTS Verse 715Ms 18Sn_854Yassa ca visatā natthi,
Chinnasotassa bhikkhuno;
Kiccākiccappahīnassa,
Pariḷāho na vijjati.

SC 38SC Verse 722PTS Verse 716Ms 18Sn_855Moneyyaṃ te upaññissaṃ,
Khuradhārūpamo bhave;
Jivhāya tālumāhacca,
Udare saññato siyā.

SC 39SC Verse 723PTS Verse 717Ms 18Sn_856Alīnacitto ca siyā,
Na cāpi bahu cintaye;
Nirāmagandho asito,
Brahmacariyaparāyaṇo.

SC 40SC Verse 724PTS Verse 718Ms 18Sn_857Ekāsanassa sikkhetha,
Samaṇūpāsanassa ca;
Ekattaṃ monamakkhātaṃ,
Eko ce abhiramissasi;
Atha bhāhisi dasadisā.

SC 41SC Verse 725PTS Verse 719Ms 18Sn_858Sutvā dhīrānaṃ nighosaṃ,
Jhāyīnaṃ kāmacāginaṃ;
Tato hiriñca saddhañca,
Bhiyyo kubbetha māmako.

SC 42SC Verse 726PTS Verse 720Ms 18Sn_859Taṃ nadīhi vijānātha,
Sobbhesu padaresu ca;
Saṇantā yanti kusobbhā,
Tuṇhī yanti mahodadhī.

SC 43SC Verse 727PTS Verse 721Ms 18Sn_860Yadūnakaṃ taṃ saṇati,
Yaṃ pūraṃ santameva taṃ;
Aḍḍhakumbhūpamo bālo,
Rahado pūrova paṇḍito.

SC 44SC Verse 728PTS Verse 722Ms 18Sn_861Yaṃ samaṇo bahuṃ bhāsati,
Upetaṃ atthasañhitaṃ;
Jānaṃ so dhammaṃ deseti,
Jānaṃ so bahu bhāsati.

SC 45SC Verse 729PTS Verse 723Ms 18Sn_862Yo ca jānaṃ saṃyatatto,
Jānaṃ na bahu bhāsati;
Sa munī monamarahati,
Sa munī monamajjhagā”ti.

Nālakasuttaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
