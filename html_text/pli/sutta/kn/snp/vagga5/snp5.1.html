SC 10SC Verse 992PTS Verse 985Ms 18Sn_1173Ussussati anāhāro,

SC 13SC Verse 995PTS Verse 988Ms 18Sn_1176“Bhotī carahi jānāsi,

SC 14SC Verse 996PTS Verse 989Ms 18Sn_1177“Ahampetaṃ na jānāmi,

SC 15SC Verse 997PTS Verse 990Ms 18Sn_1178“Atha ko carahi jānāti,

Muddhaṃ muddhādhipātañca,
Taṃ me akkhāhi devate”.

SC 16SC Verse 998PTS Verse 991PTS vp Pali 192Ms 18Sn_1179“Purā kapilavatthumhā,
Nikkhanto lokanāyako;
Apacco okkākarājassa,
Sakyaputto pabhaṅkaro.

SC 17SC Verse 999PTS Verse 992Ms 18Sn_1180So hi brāhmaṇa sambuddho,
Sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Sabbābhiññābalappatto,
Sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Sabbakammakkhayaṃ patto,
Vimutto upadhikkhaye.

SC 18SC Verse 1000PTS Verse 993Ms 18Sn_1181Buddho so bhagavā loke,
Dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā;
Taṃ tvaṃ gantvāna pucchassu,
So te taṃ byākarissati”.

SC 19SC Verse 1001PTS Verse 994Ms 18Sn_1182Sambuddhoti vaco sutvā,
Udaggo bāvarī ahu;
Sokassa tanuko āsi,
Pītiñca vipulaṃ labhi.

SC 20SC Verse 1002PTS Verse 995Ms 18Sn_1183So bāvarī attamano udaggo,
Taṃ devataṃ pucchati vedajāto;
“Katamamhi gāme nigamamhi vā pana,
Katamamhi vā janapade lokanātho;
Yattha gantvāna passemu,
Sambuddhaṃ dvipaduttamaṃ”.

SC 21SC Verse 1003PTS Verse 996Ms 18Sn_1184“Sāvatthiyaṃ kosalamandire jino,
Pahūtapañño varabhūrimedhaso;
So sakyaputto vidhuro anāsavo,
Muddhādhipātassa vidū narāsabho”.

SC 22SC Verse 1004PTS Verse 997Ms 18Sn_1185Tato āmantayī sisse,
Brāhmaṇe mantapārage;
“Etha māṇavā akkhissaṃ,
Suṇātha vacanaṃ mama.

Passavho dvipaduttamaṃ”.
