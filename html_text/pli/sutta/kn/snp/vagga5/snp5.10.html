• Sutta Nipāta 5.10

Todeyyamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1095PTS Verse 1088Ms 18Sn_1289“Yasmiṃ kāmā na vasanti,
(iccāyasmā todeyyo):
Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;
Kathaṃkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,
Vimokkho tassa kīdiso”.

SC 2SC Verse 1096PTS Verse 1089Ms 18Sn_1290“Yasmiṃ kāmā na vasanti,
(todeyyāti bhagavā):
Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;
Kathaṅkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,
Vimokkho tassa nāparo”.

SC 3SC Verse 1097PTS Verse 1090Ms 18Sn_1291“Nirāsaso so uda āsasāno,
Paññāṇavā so uda paññakappī;
Muniṃ ahaṃ sakka yathā vijaññaṃ,
Taṃ me viyācikkha samantacakkhu”.

SC 4SC Verse 1098PTS Verse 1091Ms 18Sn_1292“Nirāsaso so na ca āsasāno,
Paññāṇavā so na ca paññakappī;
Evampi todeyya muniṃ vijāna,
Akiñcanaṃ kāmabhave asattan”ti.

Todeyyamāṇavapucchā navamā.
