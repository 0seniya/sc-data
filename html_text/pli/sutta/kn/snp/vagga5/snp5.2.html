• Sutta Nipāta 5.2

Ajitamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1039PTS Verse 1032Ms 18Sn_1221“Kenassu nivuto loko,
(iccāyasmā ajito):
Kenassu nappakāsati;
Kissābhilepanaṃ brūsi,
Kiṃsu tassa mahabbhayaṃ”.

SC 2SC Verse 1040PTS Verse 1033Ms 18Sn_1222“Avijjāya nivuto loko,
(ajitāti bhagavā):
Vevicchā pamādā nappakāsati;
Jappābhilepanaṃ brūmi,
Dukkhamassa mahabbhayaṃ”.

SC 3SC Verse 1041PTS Verse 1034PTS vp Pali 198Ms 18Sn_1223“Savanti sabbadhi sotā,
(iccāyasmā ajito):
Sotānaṃ kiṃ nivāraṇaṃ;
Sotānaṃ saṃvaraṃ brūhi,
Kena sotā pidhiyyare”.

SC 4SC Verse 1042PTS Verse 1035Ms 18Sn_1224“Yāni sotāni lokasmiṃ,
(ajitāti bhagavā):
Sati tesaṃ nivāraṇaṃ;
Sotānaṃ saṃvaraṃ brūmi,
Paññāyete pidhiyyare”.

SC 5SC Verse 1043PTS Verse 1036Ms 18Sn_1225“Paññā ceva sati yañca,sati yañca,
(iccāyasmā ajito):
Nāmarūpañca mārisa;
Etaṃ me puṭṭho pabrūhi,
Katthetaṃ uparujjhati”.

SC 6SC Verse 1044PTS Verse 1037Ms 18Sn_1226“Yametaṃ pañhaṃ apucchi,
ajita taṃ vadāmi te;
Yattha nāmañca rūpañca,
asesaṃ uparujjhati;
Viññāṇassa nirodhena,
etthetaṃ uparujjhati”.

SC 7SC Verse 1045PTS Verse 1038Ms 18Sn_1227“Ye ca saṅkhātadhammāse,
ye ca sekhā puthū idha;
Tesaṃ me nipako iriyaṃ,
puṭṭho pabrūhi mārisa”.

SC 8SC Verse 1046PTS Verse 1039Ms 18Sn_1228“Kāmesu nābhigijjheyya,
Manasānāvilo siyā;
Kusalo sabbadhammānaṃ,
Sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

Ajitamāṇavapucchā paṭhamā.
