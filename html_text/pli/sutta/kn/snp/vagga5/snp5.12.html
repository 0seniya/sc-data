• Sutta Nipāta 5.12

Jatukaṇṇimāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1103PTS Verse 1096SC 1SC Verse 1103PTS Verse 1096Ms 18Sn_1299“Sutvānahaṃ vīramakāmakāmiṃ,
(iccāyasmā jatukaṇṇi):
Oghātigaṃ puṭṭhumakāmamāgamaṃ;
Santipadaṃ brūhi sahajanetta,
Yathātacchaṃ bhagavā brūhi metaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 1104PTS Verse 1097Ms 18Sn_1300Bhagavā hi kāme abhibhuyya iriyati,SC 2SC Verse 1104PTS Verse 1097Ms 18Sn_1300Bhagavā hi kāme abhibhuyya iriyati,

Parittapaññassa me bhūripañña,
Ācikkha dhammaṃ yamahaṃ vijaññaṃ;
Jātijarāya idha vippahānaṃ”.

Nekkhammaṃ daṭṭhu khemato;
Uggahītaṃ nirattaṃ vā,
Mā te vijjittha kiñcanaṃ.

Majjhe ce no gahessasi,
upasanto carissasi.

Jatukaṇṇimāṇavapucchā ekādasamā.
