• Sutta Nipāta 5.16

Mogharājamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1123PTS Verse 1116Ms 18Sn_1323“Dvāhaṃ sakkaṃ apucchissaṃ,
(iccāyasmā mogharājā):
Na me byākāsi cakkhumā;
Yāvatatiyañca devīsi,
Byākarotīti me sutaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 1124PTS Verse 1117Ms 18Sn_1324Ayaṃ loko paro loko,
brahmaloko sadevako;
Diṭṭhiṃ te nābhijānāti,
gotamassa yasassino.

SC 3SC Verse 1125PTS Verse 1118Ms 18Sn_1325Evaṃ abhikkantadassāviṃ,
atthi pañhena āgamaṃ;
Kathaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
maccurājā na passati”.

SC 4SC Verse 1126PTS Verse 1119Ms 18Sn_1326“Suññato lokaṃ avekkhassu,
Mogharāja sadā sato;
Attānudiṭṭhiṃ ūhacca,
Evaṃ maccutaro siyā;
Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,
Maccurājā na passatī”ti.

Mogharājamāṇavapucchā pannarasamā.
