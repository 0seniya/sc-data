• Sutta Nipāta 5.14

Udayamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1112PTS Verse 1105Ms 18Sn_1310“Jhāyiṃ virajamāsīnaṃ,
(iccāyasmā udayo):
Katakiccaṃ anāsavaṃ;
Pāraguṃ sabbadhammānaṃ,
Atthi pañhena āgamaṃ;
Aññāvimokkhaṃ pabrūhi,
Avijjāya pabhedanaṃ”.

SC 2SC Verse 1113PTS Verse 1106Ms 18Sn_1311“Pahānaṃ kāmacchandānaṃ,
(udayāti bhagavā):
Domanassāna cūbhayaṃ;
Thinassa ca panūdanaṃ,
Kukkuccānaṃ nivāraṇaṃ.

SC 3SC Verse 1114PTS Verse 1107Ms 18Sn_1312Upekkhāsatisaṃsuddhaṃ,
dhammatakkapurejavaṃ;
Aññāvimokkhaṃ pabrūmi,
avijjāya pabhedanaṃ”.

SC 4SC Verse 1115PTS Verse 1108PTS vp Pali 215Ms 18Sn_1313“Kiṃsu saṃyojano loko,
kiṃsu tassa vicāraṇaṃ;
Kissassa vippahānena,
nibbānaṃ iti vuccati”.

SC 5SC Verse 1116PTS Verse 1109Ms 18Sn_1314“Nandisaṃyojano loko,
vitakkassa vicāraṇaṃ;
Taṇhāya vippahānena,
nibbānaṃ iti vuccati”.

SC 6SC Verse 1117PTS Verse 1110Ms 18Sn_1315“Kathaṃ satassa carato,
viññāṇaṃ uparujjhati;
Bhagavantaṃ puṭṭhumāgamma,
taṃ suṇoma vaco tava”.

SC 7SC Verse 1118PTS Verse 1111Ms 18Sn_1316“Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,
Vedanaṃ nābhinandato;
Evaṃ satassa carato,
Viññāṇaṃ uparujjhatī”ti.

Udayamāṇavapucchā terasamā.
