• Sutta Nipāta 5.11

Kappamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1099PTS Verse 1092Ms 18Sn_1294“Majjhe sarasmiṃ tiṭṭhataṃ,
(iccāyasmā kappo):
Oghe jāte mahabbhaye;
Jarāmaccuparetānaṃ,
Dīpaṃ pabrūhi mārisa;
Tvañca me dīpamakkhāhi,
Yathāyidaṃ nāparaṃ siyā”.

SC 2SC Verse 1100PTS Verse 1093PTS vp Pali 212Ms 18Sn_1295“Majjhe sarasmiṃ tiṭṭhataṃ,
(kappāti bhagavā):
Oghe jāte mahabbhaye;
Jarāmaccuparetānaṃ,
Dīpaṃ pabrūmi kappa te.

SC 3SC Verse 1101PTS Verse 1094Ms 18Sn_1296Akiñcanaṃ anādānaṃ,
etaṃ dīpaṃ anāparaṃ;
Nibbānaṃ iti naṃ brūmi,
jarāmaccuparikkhayaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 1102PTS Verse 1095Ms 18Sn_1297Etadaññāya ye satā,
Diṭṭhadhammābhinibbutā;
Na te māravasānugā,
Na te mārassa paddhagū”ti.

Kappamāṇavapucchā dasamā.
