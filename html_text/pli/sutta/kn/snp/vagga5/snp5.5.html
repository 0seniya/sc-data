• Sutta Nipāta 5.5

Mettagūmāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1056PTS Verse 1049Ms 18Sn_1241“Pucchāmi taṃ bhagavā brūhi me taṃ,
(iccāyasmā mettagū):
Maññāmi taṃ vedaguṃ bhāvitattaṃ;
Kuto nu dukkhā samudāgatā ime,
Ye keci lokasmimanekarūpā”.

SC 2SC Verse 1057PTS Verse 1050Ms 18Sn_1242“Dukkhassa ve maṃ pabhavaṃ apucchasi,
(mettagūti bhagavā):
Taṃ te pavakkhāmi yathā pajānaṃ;
Upadhinidānā pabhavanti dukkhā,
Ye keci lokasmimanekarūpā.

SC 3SC Verse 1058PTS Verse 1051Ms 18Sn_1243Yo ve avidvā upadhiṃ karoti,
Punappunaṃ dukkhamupeti mando;
Tasmā pajānaṃ upadhiṃ na kayirā,
Dukkhassa jātippabhavānupassī”.

SC 4SC Verse 1059PTS Verse 1052Ms 18Sn_1244“Yaṃ taṃ apucchimha akittayī no,
Aññaṃ taṃ pucchāma tadiṅgha brūhi;
Kathaṃ nu dhīrā vitaranti oghaṃ,
Jātiṃ jaraṃ sokapariddavañca;
Taṃ me muni sādhu viyākarohi,
Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo”.

SC 5SC Verse 1060PTS Verse 1053Ms 18Sn_1245“Kittayissāmi te dhammaṃ,
(mettagūti bhagavā):
Diṭṭhe dhamme anītihaṃ;
Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,
Tare loke visattikaṃ”.

SC 6SC Verse 1061PTS Verse 1054Ms 18Sn_1246“Tañcāhaṃ abhinandāmi,
mahesi dhammamuttamaṃ;
Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,
tare loke visattikaṃ”.

SC 7SC Verse 1062PTS Verse 1055Ms 18Sn_1247“Yaṃ kiñci sampajānāsi,
(mettagūti bhagavā):
Uddhaṃ adho tiriyañcāpi majjhe;
Etesu nandiñca nivesanañca,
Panujja viññāṇaṃ bhave na tiṭṭhe.

SC 8SC Verse 1063PTS Verse 1056Ms 18Sn_1248Evaṃvihārī sato appamatto,
Bhikkhu caraṃ hitvā mamāyitāni;
Jātiṃ jaraṃ sokapariddavañca,
Idheva vidvā pajaheyya dukkhaṃ”.

SC 9SC Verse 1064PTS Verse 1057Ms 18Sn_1249“Etābhinandāmi vaco mahesino,
Sukittitaṃ gotamanūpadhīkaṃ;
Addhā hi bhagavā pahāsi dukkhaṃ,
Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo.

SC 10SC Verse 1065PTS Verse 1058Ms 18Sn_1250Te cāpi nūnappajaheyyu dukkhaṃ,
Ye tvaṃ muni aṭṭhitaṃ ovadeyya;
Taṃ taṃ namassāmi samecca nāga,
Appeva maṃ bhagavā aṭṭhitaṃ ovadeyya”.

SC 11SC Verse 1066PTS Verse 1059Ms 18Sn_1251“Yaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ vedagumābhijaññā,
Akiñcanaṃ kāmabhave asattaṃ;
Addhā hi so oghamimaṃ atāri,
Tiṇṇo ca pāraṃ akhilo akaṅkho.

SC 12SC Verse 1067PTS Verse 1060Ms 18Sn_1252Vidvā ca yo vedagū naro idha,yo vedagū naro idha,
Bhavābhave saṅgamimaṃ visajja;
So vītataṇho anīgho nirāso,
Atāri so jātijaranti brūmī”ti.

Mettagūmāṇavapucchā catutthī.
