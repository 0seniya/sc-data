• Sutta Nipāta 5.7

Upasīvamāṇavapucchā

SC 1SC Verse 1076PTS Verse 1069Ms 18Sn_1263“Eko ahaṃ sakka mahantamoghaṃ,
(iccāyasmā upasīvo):
Anissito no visahāmi tārituṃ;
Ārammaṇaṃ brūhi samantacakkhu,
Yaṃ nissito oghamimaṃ tareyyaṃ”.

SC 2SC Verse 1077PTS Verse 1070PTS vp Pali 206Ms 18Sn_1264“Ākiñcaññaṃ pekkhamāno satimā,
(upasīvāti bhagavā):
Natthīti nissāya tarassu oghaṃ;
Kāme pahāya virato kathāhi,
Taṇhakkhayaṃ nattamahābhipassa”.

SC 3SC Verse 1078PTS Verse 1071Ms 18Sn_1265“Sabbesu kāmesu yo vītarāgo,
(iccāyasmā upasīvo):
Ākiñcaññaṃ nissito hitvā maññaṃ;
Saññāvimokkhe parame vimutto,
Tiṭṭhe nu so tattha anānuyāyī”.

SC 4SC Verse 1079PTS Verse 1072Ms 18Sn_1266“Sabbesu kāmesu yo vītarāgo,
(upasīvāti bhagavā):
Ākiñcaññaṃ nissito hitvā maññaṃ;
Saññāvimokkhe parame vimutto,
Tiṭṭheyya so tattha anānuyāyī”.

SC 5SC Verse 1080PTS Verse 1073Ms 18Sn_1267“Tiṭṭhe ce so tattha anānuyāyī,
Pūgampi vassānaṃ samantacakkhu;
Tattheva so sītisiyā vimutto,
Cavetha viññāṇaṃ tathāvidhassa”.

SC 6SC Verse 1081PTS Verse 1074PTS vp Pali 207Ms 18Sn_1268“Accī yathā vātavegena khittā,
(upasīvāti bhagavā):
Atthaṃ paleti na upeti saṅkhaṃ;
Evaṃ munī nāmakāyā vimutto,
Atthaṃ paleti na upeti saṅkhaṃ”.

SC 7SC Verse 1082PTS Verse 1075Ms 18Sn_1269“Atthaṅgato so uda vā so natthi,
Udāhu ve sassatiyā arogo;
Taṃ me munī sādhu viyākarohi,
Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo”.

SC 8SC Verse 1083PTS Verse 1076Ms 18Sn_1270“Atthaṅgatassa na pamāṇamatthi,
(upasīvāti bhagavā):
Yena naṃ vajjuṃ taṃ tassa natthi;
Sabbesu dhammesu samohatesu,
Samūhatā vādapathāpi sabbe”ti.

Upasīvamāṇavapucchā chaṭṭhī.
