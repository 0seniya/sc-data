• Sutta Nipāta 1.12

Munisutta

SC 1SC Verse 209PTS Verse 207Ms 18Sn_240Santhavāto bhayaṃ jātaṃ,
niketā jāyate rajo;
Aniketamasanthavaṃ,
etaṃ ve munidassanaṃ.

SC 2SC Verse 210PTS Verse 208Ms 18Sn_241Yo jātamucchijja na ropayeyya,
Jāyantamassa nānuppavecche;
Tamāhu ekaṃ muninaṃ carantaṃ,
Addakkhi so santipadaṃ mahesi.

SC 3SC Verse 211PTS Verse 209Ms 18Sn_242Saṅkhāya vatthūni pamāya bījaṃ,
Sinehamassa nānuppavecche;
Sa ve munī jātikhayantadassī,
Takkaṃ pahāya na upeti saṅkhaṃ.

SC 4SC Verse 212PTS Verse 210Ms 18Sn_243Aññāya sabbāni nivesanāni,
Anikāmayaṃ aññatarampi tesaṃ;
Sa ve munī vītagedho agiddho,
Nāyūhatī pāragato hi hoti.

SC 5SC Verse 213PTS Verse 211Ms 18Sn_244Sabbābhibhuṃ sabbaviduṃ sumedhaṃ,
Sabbesu dhammesu anūpalittaṃ;
Sabbañjahaṃ taṇhakkhaye vimuttaṃ,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 6SC Verse 214PTS Verse 212Ms 18Sn_245Paññābalaṃ sīlavatūpapannaṃ,
Samāhitaṃ jhānarataṃ satīmaṃ;
Saṅgā pamuttaṃ akhilaṃ anāsavaṃ,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 7SC Verse 215PTS Verse 213Ms 18Sn_246Ekaṃ carantaṃ munimappamattaṃ,
Nindāpasaṃsāsu avedhamānaṃ;
Sīhaṃva saddesu asantasantaṃ,
Vātaṃva jālamhi asajjamānaṃ;
Padmaṃva toyena alippamānaṃ,
Netāramaññesamanaññaneyyaṃ;
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 8SC Verse 216PTS Verse 214Ms 18Sn_247Yo ogahaṇe thambhorivābhijāyati,
Yasmiṃ pare vācāpariyantaṃ vadanti;
Taṃ vītarāgaṃ susamāhitindriyaṃ,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 9SC Verse 217PTS Verse 215Ms 18Sn_248Yo ve ṭhitatto tasaraṃva ujju,
Jigucchati kammehi pāpakehi;
Vīmaṃsamāno visamaṃ samañca,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 10SC Verse 218PTS Verse 216Ms 18Sn_249Yo saññatatto na karoti pāpaṃ,
Daharo majjhimo ca muni yatatto;
Arosaneyyo na so roseti kañci,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 11SC Verse 219PTS Verse 217Ms 18Sn_250Yadaggato majjhato sesato vā,
Piṇḍaṃ labhetha paradattūpajīvī;
Nālaṃ thutuṃ nopi nipaccavādī,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 12SC Verse 220PTS Verse 218Ms 18Sn_251Muniṃ carantaṃ virataṃ methunasmā,
Yo yobbane nopanibajjhate kvaci;
Madappamādā virataṃ vippamuttaṃ,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 13SC Verse 221PTS Verse 219Ms 18Sn_252Aññāya lokaṃ paramatthadassiṃ,
Oghaṃ samuddaṃ atitariya tādiṃ;
Taṃ chinnaganthaṃ asitaṃ anāsavaṃ,
Taṃ vāpi dhīrā muni vedayanti.

SC 14SC Verse 222PTS Verse 220Ms 18Sn_253Asamā ubho dūravihāravuttino,
Gihī dāraposī amamo ca subbato;
Parapāṇarodhāya gihī asaññato,
Niccaṃ munī rakkhati pāṇine yato.

SC 15SC Verse 223PTS Verse 221Ms 18Sn_254Sikhī yathā nīlagīvo vihaṅgamo,
Haṃsassa nopeti javaṃ kudācanaṃ;
Evaṃ gihī nānukaroti bhikkhuno,
Munino vivittassa vanamhi jhāyatoti.

Munisuttaṃ dvādasamaṃ.

Uragavaggo paṭhamo.

SC 16Ms 18Sn_258Urago dhaniyo ceva,
visāṇañca tathā kasi;
Cundo parābhavo ceva,
vasalo mettabhāvanā.

SC 17Ms 18Sn_259Sātāgiro āḷavako,
Vijayo ca tathā muni;
Dvādasetāni suttāni,
Uragavaggoti vuccatīti.
