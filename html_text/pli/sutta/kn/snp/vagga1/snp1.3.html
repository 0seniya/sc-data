• Sutta Nipāta 1.3

Khaggavisāṇasutta

SC 1SC Verse 35PTS Verse 35Ms 18Sn_38“Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṃ,
Aviheṭhayaṃ aññatarampi tesaṃ;
Na puttamiccheyya kuto sahāyaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 2SC Verse 36PTS Verse 36Ms 18Sn_39“Saṃsaggajātassa bhavanti snehā,
Snehanvayaṃ dukkhamidaṃ pahoti;
Ādīnavaṃ snehajaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 3SC Verse 37PTS Verse 37Ms 18Sn_40“Mitte suhajje anukampamāno,
Hāpeti atthaṃ paṭibaddhacitto;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ santhave pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 4SC Verse 38PTS Verse 38PTS vp Pali 7Ms 18Sn_41“Vaṃso visālova yathā visatto,
Puttesu dāresu ca yā apekkhā;
Vaṃsakkaḷīrova asajjamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 5SC Verse 39PTS Verse 39Ms 18Sn_42“Migo araññamhi yathā abaddho,abaddho,
Yenicchakaṃ gacchati gocarāya;
Viññū naro seritaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 6SC Verse 40PTS Verse 40Ms 18Sn_43“Āmantanā hoti sahāyamajjhe,
Vāse ṭhāne gamane cārikāya;
Anabhijjhitaṃ seritaṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 7SC Verse 41PTS Verse 41Ms 18Sn_44“Khiḍḍā ratī hoti sahāyamajjhe,
Puttesu ca vipulaṃ hoti pemaṃ;
Piyavippayogaṃ vijigucchamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 8SC Verse 42PTS Verse 42Ms 18Sn_45“Cātuddiso appaṭigho ca hoti,
Santussamāno itarītarena;
Parissayānaṃ sahitā achambhī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 9SC Verse 43PTS Verse 43Ms 18Sn_46“Dussaṅgahā pabbajitāpi eke,
Atho gahaṭṭhā gharamāvasantā;
Appossukko paraputtesu hutvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 10SC Verse 44PTS Verse 44PTS vp Pali 8Ms 18Sn_47“Oropayitvā gihibyañjanāni,
Sañchinnapatto yathā koviḷāro;
Chetvāna vīro gihibandhanāni,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 11SC Verse 45PTS Verse 45Ms 18Sn_48“Sace labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāridhīraṃ;
Abhibhuyya sabbāni parissayāni,
Careyya tenattamano satīmā”.

SC 12SC Verse 46PTS Verse 46Ms 18Sn_49“No ce labhetha nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ,
Saddhiṃ caraṃ sādhuvihāridhīraṃ;
Rājāva raṭṭhaṃ vijitaṃ pahāya,
Eko care mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo”.

SC 13SC Verse 47PTS Verse 47Ms 18Sn_50“Addhā pasaṃsāma sahāyasampadaṃ,
Seṭṭhā samā sevitabbā sahāyā;
Ete aladdhā anavajjabhojī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 14SC Verse 48PTS Verse 48Ms 18Sn_51“Disvā suvaṇṇassa pabhassarāni,
Kammāraputtena suniṭṭhitāni;
Saṅghaṭṭamānāni duve bhujasmiṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 15SC Verse 49PTS Verse 49Ms 18Sn_52“Evaṃ dutīyena sahā mamassa,dutīyena sahā mamassa,
Vācābhilāpo abhisajjanā vā;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ āyatiṃ pekkhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 16SC Verse 50PTS Verse 50Ms 18Sn_53“Kāmā hi citrā madhurā manoramā,
Virūparūpena mathenti cittaṃ;
Ādīnavaṃ kāmaguṇesu disvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 17SC Verse 51PTS Verse 51Ms 18Sn_54“Ītī ca gaṇḍo ca upaddavo ca,
Rogo ca sallañca bhayañca metaṃ;
Etaṃ bhayaṃ kāmaguṇesu disvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 18SC Verse 52PTS Verse 52PTS vp Pali 9Ms 18Sn_55“Sītañca uṇhañca khudaṃ pipāsaṃ,
Vātātape ḍaṃsasarīsape ca;
Sabbānipetāni abhisambhavitvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 19SC Verse 53PTS Verse 53Ms 18Sn_56“Nāgova yūthāni vivajjayitvā,
Sañjātakhandho padumī uḷāro;
Yathābhirantaṃ viharaṃ araññe,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 20SC Verse 54PTS Verse 54Ms 18Sn_57“Aṭṭhānataṃ saṅgaṇikāratassa,
Yaṃ phassaye sāmayikaṃ vimuttiṃ;
Ādiccabandhussa vaco nisamma,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 21SC Verse 55PTS Verse 55Ms 18Sn_58“Diṭṭhīvisūkāni upātivatto,
Patto niyāmaṃ paṭiladdhamaggo;
Uppannañāṇomhi anaññaneyyo,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 22SC Verse 56PTS Verse 56Ms 18Sn_59“Nillolupo nikkuho nippipāso,
Nimmakkho niddhantakasāvamoho;
Nirāsayo sabbaloke bhavitvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 23SC Verse 57PTS Verse 57Ms 18Sn_60“Pāpaṃ sahāyaṃ parivajjayetha,
Anatthadassiṃ visame niviṭṭhaṃ;
Sayaṃ na seve pasutaṃ pamattaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 24SC Verse 58PTS Verse 58PTS vp Pali 10Ms 18Sn_61“Bahussutaṃ dhammadharaṃ bhajetha,
Mittaṃ uḷāraṃ paṭibhānavantaṃ;
Aññāya atthāni vineyya kaṅkhaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 25SC Verse 59PTS Verse 59Ms 18Sn_62“Khiḍḍaṃ ratiṃ kāmasukhañca loke,
Analaṅkaritvā anapekkhamāno;
Vibhūsanaṭṭhānā virato saccavādī,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 26SC Verse 60PTS Verse 60Ms 18Sn_63“Puttañca dāraṃ pitarañca mātaraṃ,
Dhanāni dhaññāni ca bandhavāni;
Hitvāna kāmāni yathodhikāni,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 27SC Verse 61PTS Verse 61Ms 18Sn_64“Saṅgo eso parittamettha sokhyaṃ,
Appassādo dukkhamettha bhiyyo;
Gaḷo eso iti ñatvā matīmā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 28SC Verse 62PTS Verse 62Ms 18Sn_65“Sandālayitvāna saṃyojanāni,Sandālayitvāna saṃyojanāni,
Jālaṃva bhetvā salilambucārī;
Aggīva daḍḍhaṃ anivattamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 29SC Verse 63PTS Verse 63Ms 18Sn_66“Okkhittacakkhu na ca pādalolo,
Guttindriyo rakkhitamānasāno;
Anavassuto apariḍayhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 30SC Verse 64PTS Verse 64PTS vp Pali 11Ms 18Sn_67“Ohārayitvā gihibyañjanāni,
Sañchannapatto yathā Sañchannapatto yathā pārichatto;
Kāsāyavattho abhinikkhamitvā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 31SC Verse 65PTS Verse 65Ms 18Sn_68“Rasesu gedhaṃ akaraṃ alolo,
Anaññaposī sapadānacārī;
Kule kule appaṭibaddhacitto,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 32SC Verse 66PTS Verse 66Ms 18Sn_69“Pahāya pañcāvaraṇāni cetaso,
Upakkilese byapanujja sabbe;
Anissito chetva chetva sinehadosaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 33SC Verse 67PTS Verse 67Ms 18Sn_70“Vipiṭṭhikatvāna sukhaṃ dukhañca,
Pubbeva ca somanassadomanassaṃ;
Laddhānupekkhaṃ samathaṃ visuddhaṃ,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 34SC Verse 68PTS Verse 68Ms 18Sn_71“Āraddhaviriyo paramatthapattiyā,
Alīnacitto akusītavutti;
Daḷhanikkamo thāmabalūpapanno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 35SC Verse 69PTS Verse 69Ms 18Sn_72“Paṭisallānaṃ jhānamariñcamāno,
Dhammesu niccaṃ anudhammacārī;
Ādīnavaṃ sammasitā bhavesu,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 36SC Verse 70PTS Verse 70Ms 18Sn_73“Taṇhakkhayaṃ patthayamappamatto,
Aneḷamūgo sutavā satīmā;
Saṅkhātadhammo niyato padhānavā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 37SC Verse 71PTS Verse 71PTS vp Pali 12Ms 18Sn_74“Sīhova saddesu asantasanto,
Vātova jālamhi asajjamāno;
Padumaṃva toyena alippamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 38SC Verse 72PTS Verse 72Ms 18Sn_75“Sīho yathā dāṭhabalī pasayha,
Rājā migānaṃ abhibhuyya cārī;
Sevetha pantāni senāsanāni,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 39SC Verse 73PTS Verse 73Ms 18Sn_76“Mettaṃ upekkhaṃ karuṇaṃ vimuttiṃ,
Āsevamāno muditañca kāle;
Sabbena lokena avirujjhamāno,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 40SC Verse 74PTS Verse 74Ms 18Sn_77“Rāgañca dosañca pahāya mohaṃ,
Sandālayitvāna saṃyojanāni;
Asantasaṃ jīvitasaṅkhayamhi,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

SC 41SC Verse 75PTS Verse 75Ms 18Sn_78“Bhajanti sevanti ca kāraṇatthā,
Nikkāraṇā dullabhā ajja mittā;
Attaṭṭhapaññā asucī manussā,
Eko care khaggavisāṇakappo”.

Khaggavisāṇasuttaṃ tatiyaṃ.
