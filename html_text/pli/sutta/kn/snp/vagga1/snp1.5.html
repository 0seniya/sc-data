Cundasutta

SC 1SC Verse 83PTS Verse 83SC 1SC Verse 83PTS Verse 83Ms 18Sn_97“Pucchāmi muniṃ pahūtapaññaṃ,
(iti cundo kammāraputto):
Buddhaṃ dhammassāmiṃ vītataṇhaṃ;
Dvipaduttamaṃ sārathīnaṃ pavaraṃ,
Kati loke samaṇā tadiṅgha brūhi”.

SC 2SC Verse 84PTS Verse 84Ms 18Sn_98“Caturo samaṇā na pañcamatthi,SC 2SC Verse 84PTS Verse 84Ms 18Sn_98“Caturo samaṇā na pañcamatthi,

Te te āvikaromi sakkhipuṭṭho;
Maggajino maggadesako ca,
Magge jīvati yo ca maggadūsī”.

Magge jīvati me brūhi puṭṭho,
Atha me āvikarohi maggadūsiṃ”.

SC 8SC Verse 90PTS Verse 90Ms 18Sn_104Ete ca paṭivijjhi yo gahaṭṭho,SC 8SC Verse 90PTS Verse 90Ms 18Sn_104Ete ca paṭivijjhi yo gahaṭṭho,

Suddhaṃ asuddhena samaṃ kareyyā”ti.

Cundasuttaṃ pañcamaṃ.
