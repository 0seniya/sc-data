• Sutta Nipāta 1.2

Dhaniyasutta

SC 1SC Verse 18PTS Verse 18Ms 18Sn_20“Pakkodano duddhakhīrohamasmi,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Anutīre mahiyā samānavāso;
Channā kuṭi āhito gini,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 2SC Verse 19PTS Verse 19Ms 18Sn_21“Akkodhano vigatakhilohamasmi,
(iti bhagavā):
Anutīre mahiyekarattivāso;
Vivaṭā kuṭi nibbuto gini,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 3SC Verse 20PTS Verse 20Ms 18Sn_22“Andhakamakasā na vijjare,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Kacche rūḷhatiṇe caranti gāvo;
Vuṭṭhimpi saheyyumāgataṃ,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 4SC Verse 21PTS Verse 21Ms 18Sn_23“Baddhāsi bhisī susaṅkhatā,
(iti bhagavā):
Tiṇṇo pāragato vineyya oghaṃ;
Attho bhisiyā na vijjati,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 5SC Verse 22PTS Verse 22Ms 18Sn_24“Gopī mama assavā alolā,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Dīgharattaṃ saṃvāsiyā manāpā;
Tassā na suṇāmi kiñci pāpaṃ,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 6SC Verse 23PTS Verse 23Ms 18Sn_25“Cittaṃ mama assavaṃ vimuttaṃ,
(iti bhagavā):
Dīgharattaṃ paribhāvitaṃ sudantaṃ;
Pāpaṃ pana me na vijjati,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 7SC Verse 24PTS Verse 24Ms 18Sn_26“Attavetanabhatohamasmi,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Puttā ca me samāniyā arogā;
Tesaṃ na suṇāmi kiñci pāpaṃ,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 8SC Verse 25PTS Verse 25Ms 18Sn_27“Nāhaṃ bhatakosmi kassaci,
(iti bhagavā):
Nibbiṭṭhena carāmi sabbaloke;
Attho bhatiyā na vijjati,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 9SC Verse 26PTS Verse 26Ms 18Sn_28“Atthi vasā atthi dhenupā,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Godharaṇiyo paveṇiyopi atthi;
Usabhopi gavampatīdha atthi,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 10SC Verse 27PTS Verse 27Ms 18Sn_29“Natthi vasā natthi dhenupā,
(iti bhagavā):
Godharaṇiyo paveṇiyopi natthi;
Usabhopi gavampatīdha natthi,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 11SC Verse 28PTS Verse 28Ms 18Sn_30“Khilā nikhātā asampavedhī,
(iti dhaniyo gopo):
Dāmā muñjamayā navā susaṇṭhānā;
Na hi sakkhinti dhenupāpi chettuṃ,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 12SC Verse 29PTS Verse 29Ms 18Sn_31“Usabhoriva chetva bandhanāni,
(iti bhagavā):
Nāgo pūtilataṃva dālayitvā;
Nāhaṃ punupessaṃ gabbhaseyyaṃ,
Atha ce patthayasī pavassa deva”.

SC 13SC Verse 30PTS Verse 30Ms 18Sn_32Ninnañca thalañca pūrayanto,
Mahāmegho pavassi tāvadeva;
Sutvā devassa vassato,
Imamatthaṃ dhaniyo abhāsatha.

SC 14SC Verse 31PTS Verse 31Ms 18Sn_33“Lābhā vata no anappakā,
Ye mayaṃ bhagavantaṃ addasāma;
Saraṇaṃ taṃ upema cakkhuma,
Satthā no hohi tuvaṃ mahāmuni.

SC 15SC Verse 32PTS Verse 32Ms 18Sn_34Gopī ca ahañca assavā,
Brahmacariyaṃ sugate carāmase;
Jātimaraṇassa pāragū,
Dukkhassantakarā bhavāmase”.

SC 16SC Verse 33PTS Verse 33Ms 18Sn_35“Nandati puttehi puttimā,
(iti māro pāpimā):
Gomā gohi tatheva nandati;
Upadhī hi narassa nandanā,
Na hi so nandati yo nirūpadhi”.

SC 17SC Verse 34PTS Verse 34Ms 18Sn_36“Socati puttehi puttimā,
(iti bhagavā):
Gomā gohi tatheva socati;
Upadhī hi narassa socanā,
Na hi so socati yo nirūpadhī”ti.

Dhaniyasuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.
