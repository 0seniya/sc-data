• Udāna 1.9

Jaṭilasutta

SC 1Ms 18Ud_46Msdiv 9Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​ ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā gayāyaṃ viharati gayāsīse. Tena kho pana samayena sambahulā jaṭilā sītāsu hemantikāsu rattīsu antaraṭṭhake himapātasamaye gayāyaṃ ummujjantipi nimujjantipi, ummujjanimujjampi karonti osiñcantipi, aggimpi juhanti: “iminā suddhī”ti.

SC 2Ms 18Ud_47Addasā kho bhagavā te sambahule jaṭile sītāsu hemantikāsu rattīsu antaraṭṭhake himapātasamaye gayāyaṃ ummujjantepi nimujjantepi ummujjanimujjampi karonte osiñcantepi aggimpi juhante: “iminā suddhī”ti.

SC 3Ms 18Ud_48Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi:

SC 4SC Verse 9Ms 18Ud_49“Na udakena sucī hoti,
bahvettha nhāyatī jano;
Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,
so sucī so ca brāhmaṇo”ti.

Navamaṃ.
