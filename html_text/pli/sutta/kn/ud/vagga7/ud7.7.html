• Udāna 7.7

Papañcakhayasutta

SC 1Ms 18Ud_635Msdiv 67Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​ ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā attano papañcasaññāsaṅkhāpahānaṃ paccavekkhamāno nisinno hoti.

SC 2Ms 18Ud_636Atha kho bhagavā attano papañcasaññāsaṅkhāpahānaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi:

SC 3SC Verse 82Ms 18Ud_637“Yassa papañcā ṭhiti ca natthi,
Sandānaṃ palighañca vītivatto;
Taṃ nittaṇhaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ,
Nāvajānāti sadevakopi loko”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
