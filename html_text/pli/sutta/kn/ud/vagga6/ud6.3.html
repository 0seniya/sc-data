• Udāna 6.3

Paccavekkhaṇasutta

SC 1Ms 18Ud_486Msdiv 53Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​ ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā attano aneke pāpake akusale dhamme pahīne paccavekkhamāno nisinno hoti, aneke ca kusale dhamme bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gate.

SC 2Ms 18Ud_487Atha kho bhagavā attano aneke pāpake akusale dhamme pahīne viditvā aneke ca kusale dhamme bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gate tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi:

SC 3SC Verse 66Ms 18Ud_488“Ahu pubbe tadā nāhu,
nāhu pubbe tadā ahu;
Na cāhu na ca bhavissati,
na cetarahi vijjatī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
