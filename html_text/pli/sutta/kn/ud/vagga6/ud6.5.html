• Udāna 6.5

Dutiyanānātitthiyasutta

SC 1Ms 18Ud_524Msdiv 55Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​ ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena sambahulā nānātitthiyasamaṇabrāhmaṇaparibbājakā sāvatthiyaṃ paṭivasanti nānādiṭṭhikā nānākhantikā nānārucikā nānādiṭṭhinissayanissitā.

SC 2Ms 18Ud_525Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sassato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 3Ms 18Ud_526Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “asassato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 4Ms 18Ud_527Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sassato ca asassato ca attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.sassato ca asassato ca attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 5Ms 18Ud_528Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “neva sassato nāsassato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 6Ms 18Ud_529Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sayaṅkato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 7Ms 18Ud_530Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “paraṅkato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 8Ms 18Ud_531Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sayaṃkato ca paraṃkato ca attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.sayaṃkato ca paraṃkato ca attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 9Ms 18Ud_532Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “asayaṅkāro aparaṅkāro adhiccasamuppanno attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.asayaṅkāro aparaṅkāro adhiccasamuppanno attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 10Ms 18Ud_533Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sassataṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 11Ms 18Ud_534Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “asassataṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 12PTS vp Pali 70Ms 18Ud_535Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sassatañca asassatañca sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.sassatañca asassatañca sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 13Ms 18Ud_536Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “neva sassataṃ nāsassataṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 14Ms 18Ud_537Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sayaṅkataṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 15Ms 18Ud_538Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “paraṅkataṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 16Ms 18Ud_539Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “sayaṃkatañca paraṃkatañca sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.sayaṃkatañca paraṃkatañca sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 17Ms 18Ud_540Santi paneke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: “asayaṅkāraṃ aparaṅkāraṃ adhiccasamuppannaṃ sukhadukkhaṃ attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan”ti.

SC 18Ms 18Ud_541Te bhaṇḍanajātā kalahajātā vivādāpannā aññamaññaṃ mukhasattīhi vitudantā viharanti: “ediso dhammo, nediso dhammo; nediso dhammo, ediso dhammo”ti.

SC 19Ms 18Ud_542Atha kho sambahulā bhikkhū pubbaṇhasamayaṃ nivāsetvā pattacīvaramādāya sāvatthiṃ piṇḍāya pāvisiṃsu. Sāvatthiyaṃ piṇḍāya caritvā pacchābhattaṃ piṇḍapātapaṭikkantā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdiṃsu. Ekamantaṃ nisinnā kho te bhikkhū bhagavantaṃ etadavocuṃ:

SC 20Ms 18Ud_543“Idha, bhante, sambahulā nānātitthiyasamaṇabrāhmaṇaparibbājakā sāvatthiyaṃ paṭivasanti nānādiṭṭhikā nānākhantikā nānārucikā nānādiṭṭhinissayanissitā.

SC 21Ms 18Ud_544Santeke samaṇabrāhmaṇā evaṃvādino evaṃdiṭṭhino: ‘sassato attā ca loko ca, idameva saccaṃ moghamaññan’ti … pe … te bhaṇḍanajātā kalahajātā vivādāpannā aññamaññaṃ mukhasattīhi vitudantā viharanti: ‘ediso dhammo, nediso dhammo; nediso dhammo, ediso dhammo’”ti.

SC 22Ms 18Ud_545“Aññatitthiyā, bhikkhave, paribbājakā andhā acakkhukā; atthaṃ na jānanti anatthaṃ na jānanti, dhammaṃ na jānanti adhammaṃ na jānanti. Te atthaṃ ajānantā anatthaṃ ajānantā, dhammaṃ ajānantā adhammaṃ ajānantā bhaṇḍanajātā kalahajātā vivādāpannā aññamaññaṃ mukhasattīhi vitudantā viharanti: ‘ediso dhammo, nediso dhammo; nediso dhammo, ediso dhammo’”ti.

SC 23Ms 18Ud_546Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi:

SC 24SC Verse 68Ms 18Ud_547“Imesu kira sajjanti,
eke samaṇabrāhmaṇā;
Antarāva visīdanti,
appatvāva tamogadhan”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
