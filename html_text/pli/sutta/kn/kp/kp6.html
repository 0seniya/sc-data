6. Ratanasutta

Msdiv 1Ms 18Kh_45Msdiv 1Ms 18Kh_45SC 1Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Sabbeva bhūtā sumanā bhavantu,
Athopi sakkacca suṇantu bhāsitaṃ.

Msdiv 2Ms 18Kh_46Msdiv 2Ms 18Kh_46SC 2Tasmā hi bhūtā nisāmetha sabbe,
Mettaṃ karotha mānusiyā pajāya;
Divā ca ratto ca haranti ye baliṃ,
Tasmā hi ne rakkhatha appamattā.

Msdiv 3Ms 18Kh_47Msdiv 3Ms 18Kh_47SC 3Yaṃ kiñci vittaṃ idha vā huraṃ vā,
Saggesu vā yaṃ ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Na no samaṃ atthi tathāgatena,
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 4Ms 18Kh_48Msdiv 4Ms 18Kh_48SC 4Khayaṃ virāgaṃ amataṃ paṇītaṃ,
Yadajjhagā sakyamunī samāhito;
Na tena dhammena samatthi kiñci,
Idampi dhamme ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 5Ms 18Kh_49Msdiv 5Ms 18Kh_49SC 5Yaṃ buddhaseṭṭho parivaṇṇayī suciṃ,
Samādhimānantarikaññamāhu;
Samādhinā tena samo na vijjati,
Idampi dhamme ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ;
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 6Ms 18Kh_50Msdiv 6Ms 18Kh_50SC 6Ye puggalā aṭṭha sataṃ pasatthā,
Cattāri etāni yugāni honti;
Te dakkhiṇeyyā sugatassa sāvakā,
Etesu dinnāni mahapphalāni;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 7Ms 18Kh_51Msdiv 7Ms 18Kh_51SC 7Ye suppayuttā manasā daḷhena,
Nikkāmino gotamasāsanamhi;
Te pattipattā amataṃ vigayha,
Laddhā mudhā nibbutiṃ bhuñjamānā;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 8Ms 18Kh_52Msdiv 8Ms 18Kh_52SC 8Yathindakhīlo pathavissito siyā,
Catubbhi vātehi asampakampiyo;
Tathūpamaṃ sappurisaṃ vadāmi,
Yo ariyasaccāni avecca passati;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 9Ms 18Kh_53Msdiv 9Ms 18Kh_53SC 9Ye ariyasaccāni vibhāvayanti,
Gambhīrapaññena sudesitāni;
Kiñcāpi te honti bhusaṃ pamattā,
Na te bhavaṃ aṭṭhamamādiyanti;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 10Ms 18Kh_54Msdiv 10Ms 18Kh_54SC 10Sahāvassa dassanasampadāya,
Tayassu dhammā jahitā bhavanti;
Sakkāyadiṭṭhī vicikicchitañca,
Sīlabbataṃ vāpi yadatthi kiñci.

Msdiv 11Ms 18Kh_55Msdiv 11Ms 18Kh_55SC 11Catūhapāyehi ca vippamutto,
Chaccābhiṭhānāni abhabba kātuṃ;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 12Ms 18Kh_56Msdiv 12Ms 18Kh_56SC 12Kiñcāpi so kamma karoti pāpakaṃ,
Kāyena vācā uda cetasā vā;
Abhabba so tassa paṭicchadāya,
Abhabbatā diṭṭhapadassa vuttā;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 13Ms 18Kh_57Msdiv 13Ms 18Kh_57SC 13Vanappagumbe yatha phussitagge,
Gimhānamāse paṭhamasmiṃ gimhe;
Tathūpamaṃ dhammavaraṃ adesayi,
Nibbānagāmiṃ paramaṃ hitāya;
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 14Ms 18Kh_58Msdiv 14Ms 18Kh_58SC 14Varo varaññū varado varāharo,
Anuttaro dhammavaraṃ adesayi;
Idampi buddhe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 15Ms 18Kh_59Msdiv 15Ms 18Kh_59SC 15Khīṇaṃ purāṇaṃ nava natthi sambhavaṃ,
Virattacittāyatike bhavasmiṃ;
Te khīṇabījā avirūḷhichandā,
Nibbanti dhīrā yathāyaṃ padīpo;
Idampi saṃghe ratanaṃ paṇītaṃ,
Etena saccena suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 16Ms 18Kh_60Msdiv 16Ms 18Kh_60SC 16Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Buddhaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 17Ms 18Kh_61Msdiv 17Ms 18Kh_61SC 17Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Dhammaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotu.

Msdiv 18Ms 18Kh_62Msdiv 18Ms 18Kh_62SC 18Yānīdha bhūtāni samāgatāni,
Bhummāni vā yāni va antalikkhe;
Tathāgataṃ devamanussapūjitaṃ,
Saṃghaṃ namassāma suvatthi hotūti.
