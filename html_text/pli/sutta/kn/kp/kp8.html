8. Nidhikaṇḍasutta

Msdiv 1Ms 18Kh_78SC 1Nidhiṃ nidheti puriso,
gambhīre odakantike;
Atthe kicce samuppanne,
atthāya me bhavissati.

Msdiv 2Ms 18Kh_79SC 2Rājato vā duruttassa,
corato pīḷitassa vā;
Iṇassa vā pamokkhāya,
dubbhikkhe āpadāsu vā;
Etadatthāya lokasmiṃ,
nidhi nāma nidhīyati.

Msdiv 3Ms 18Kh_80SC 3Tāvassunihito santo,
gambhīre odakantike;
Na sabbo sabbadā eva,
tassa taṃ upakappati.

Msdiv 4Ms 18Kh_81SC 4Nidhi vā ṭhānā cavati,
saññā vāssa vimuyhati;
Nāgā vā apanāmenti,
yakkhā vāpi haranti naṃ.

Msdiv 5Ms 18Kh_82SC 5Appiyā vāpi dāyādā,
uddharanti apassato;
Yadā puññakkhayo hoti,
sabbametaṃ vinassati.

Msdiv 6Ms 18Kh_83SC 6Yassa dānena sīlena,
saṃyamena damena ca;
Nidhī sunihito hoti,
itthiyā purisassa vā.

Msdiv 7Ms 18Kh_84SC 7Cetiyamhi ca saṃghe vā,
puggale atithīsu vā;
Mātari pitari cāpi,
atho jeṭṭhamhi bhātari.

Msdiv 8Ms 18Kh_85SC 8Eso nidhi sunihito,
ajeyyo anugāmiko;
Pahāya gamanīyesu,
etaṃ ādāya gacchati.

Msdiv 9Ms 18Kh_86SC 9Asādhāraṇamaññesaṃ,
acorāharaṇo nidhi;
Kayirātha dhīro puññāni,
yo nidhi anugāmiko.

Msdiv 10Ms 18Kh_87SC 10Esa devamanussānaṃ,
sabbakāmadado nidhi;
Yaṃ yadevābhipatthenti,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 11Ms 18Kh_88SC 11Suvaṇṇatā susaratā,
susaṇṭhānā surūpatā;
Ādhipaccaparivāro,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 12Ms 18Kh_89SC 12Padesarajjaṃ issariyaṃ,
cakkavattisukhaṃ piyaṃ;
Devarajjampi dibbesu,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 13Ms 18Kh_90SC 13Mānussikā ca sampatti,
devaloke ca yā rati;
Yā ca nibbānasampatti,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 14Ms 18Kh_91SC 14Mittasampadamāgamma,
yonisova payuñjato;
Vijjāvimuttivasībhāvo,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 15Ms 18Kh_92SC 15Paṭisambhidā vimokkhā ca,
yā ca sāvakapāramī;
Paccekabodhi buddhabhūmi,
sabbametena labbhati.

Msdiv 16Ms 18Kh_93SC 16Evaṃ mahatthikā esā,
yadidaṃ puññasampadā;
Tasmā dhīrā pasaṃsanti,
paṇḍitā katapuññatanti.

SC 17Ms 18Kh_94Nidhikaṇḍasuttaṃ.
