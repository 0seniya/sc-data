• Khuddakapāṭha

1. Saraṇattaya

SC 1Ms 18Kh_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

Msdiv 1Ms 18Kh_2SC 2Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.

Ms 18Kh_3SC 3Dutiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Dutiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Dutiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.

Ms 18Kh_4SC 4Tatiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Tatiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,
Tatiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi.

SC 5Ms 18Kh_5Saraṇattayaṃ.
