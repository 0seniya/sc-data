1. Vatthugāthā

Ms 25Cn_1Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

SC 1 Ms 25Cn_2Msdiv 1Kosalānaṃ purā rammā,
agamā dakkhiṇāpathaṃ;
Ākiñcaññaṃ patthayāno,
brāhmaṇo mantapāragū.

SC 2Ms 25Cn_3Msdiv 2So assakassa visaye,
maḷakassa maḷakassa samāsane;
Vasi godhāvarīkūle,
uñchena ca phalena ca.

SC 3Ms 25Cn_4Msdiv 3Tasseva upanissāya,
gāmo ca vipulo ahu;
Tato jātena āyena,
mahāyaññamakappayi.

SC 4Ms 25Cn_5Msdiv 4Mahāyaññaṃ yajitvāna,
puna pāvisi assamaṃ;
Tasmiṃ paṭipaviṭṭhamhi,
añño āgañchi brāhmaṇo.

SC 5Ms 25Cn_6Msdiv 5Ugghaṭṭapādo tasito,
paṅkadanto rajassiro;
So ca naṃ upasaṅkamma,
satāni pañca yācati.

SC 6Ms 25Cn_7Msdiv 6Tamenaṃ bāvarī disvā,
āsanena nimantayi;
Sukhañca kusalaṃ pucchi,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 7Ms 25Cn_8Msdiv 7“Yaṃ kho mama deyyadhammaṃ,
Sabbaṃ visajjitaṃ mayā;
Anujānāhi me brahme,
Natthi pañcasatāni me”.

SC 8Ms 25Cn_9Msdiv 8“Sace me yācamānassa,
bhavaṃ nānupadassati;
Sattame divase tuyhaṃ,
muddhā phalatu sattadhā”.

SC 9Ms 25Cn_10Msdiv 9Abhisaṅkharitvā kuhako,
bheravaṃ so akittayi;
Tassa taṃ vacanaṃ sutvā,
bāvarī dukkhito ahu.

SC 10Ms 25Cn_11Msdiv 10Ussussati anāhāro,
sokasallasamappito;
Athopi evaṃ cittassa,
jhāne na ramatī mano.

SC 11Ms 25Cn_12Msdiv 11Utrastaṃ dukkhitaṃ disvā,
devatā atthakāminī;
Bāvariṃ upasaṅkamma,
idaṃ vacanamabravi.

SC 12Ms 25Cn_13Msdiv 12“Na so muddhaṃ pajānāti,
kuhako so dhanatthiko;
Muddhani muddhapāte vā,
ñāṇaṃ tassa na vijjati”.

SC 13Ms 25Cn_14Msdiv 13“Bhotī carahi jānāti,
Taṃ me akkhāhi pucchitā;
Muddhaṃ muddhādhipātañca,
Taṃ suṇoma vaco tava”.

SC 14Ms 25Cn_15Msdiv 14“Ahampetaṃ na jānāmi,
ñāṇaṃ mettha na vijjati;
Muddhani muddhādhipāte ca,
jinānaṃ hettha dassanaṃ”.

SC 15Ms 25Cn_16Msdiv 15“Atha ko carahi jānāti,
asmiṃ pathavimaṇḍale;
Muddhaṃ muddhādhipātañca,
taṃ me akkhāhi devate”.

SC 16Ms 25Cn_17Msdiv 16“Purā kapilavatthumhā,
nikkhanto lokanāyako;
Apacco okkākarājassa,
sakyaputto pabhaṅkaro.

SC 17Ms 25Cn_18Msdiv 17So hi brāhmaṇa sambuddho,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Sabbābhiññābalappatto,
sabbadhammesu cakkhumā;
Sabbakammakkhayaṃ patto,
vimutto upadhikkhaye.

SC 18Ms 25Cn_19Msdiv 18Buddho so bhagavā loke,
dhammaṃ deseti cakkhumā;
Taṃ tvaṃ gantvāna pucchassu,
so te taṃ byākarissati”.

SC 19Ms 25Cn_20Msdiv 19Sambuddhoti vaco sutvā,
udaggo bāvarī ahu;
Sokassa tanuko āsi,
pītiñca vipulaṃ labhi.

SC 20Ms 25Cn_21Msdiv 20So bāvarī attamano udaggo,
Taṃ devataṃ pucchati vedajāto;
“Katamamhi gāme nigamamhi vā pana,
Katamamhi vā janapade lokanātho;
Yattha gantvāna passemu,
Sambuddhaṃ dvipaduttamaṃ”.

SC 21Ms 25Cn_22Msdiv 21“Sāvatthiyaṃ kosalamandire jino,
Pahūtapañño varabhūrimedhaso;
So sakyaputto vidhuro anāsavo,
Muddhādhipātassa vidū narāsabho”.

SC 22Ms 25Cn_23Msdiv 22Tato āmantayī sisse,
brāhmaṇe mantapāragū;
“Etha māṇavā akkhissaṃ,
suṇātha vacanaṃ mama.

SC 23Ms 25Cn_24Msdiv 23Yasseso dullabho loke,
pātubhāvo abhiṇhaso;
Svājja lokamhi uppanno,
sambuddho iti vissuto;
Khippaṃ gantvāna sāvatthiṃ,
passavho dvipaduttamaṃ”.

SC 24Ms 25Cn_25Msdiv 24“Kathaṃ carahi jānemu,
disvā buddhoti brāhmaṇa;
Ajānataṃ no pabrūhi,
yathā jānemu taṃ mayaṃ”.

SC 25Ms 25Cn_26Msdiv 25“Āgatāni hi mantesu,
mahāpurisalakkhaṇā;
Dvattiṃsāni ca byākkhātā,
samattā anupubbaso.

SC 26Ms 25Cn_27Msdiv 26Yassete honti gattesu,
mahāpurisalakkhaṇā;
Dveyeva tassa gatiyo,
tatiyā hi na vijjati.

SC 27Ms 25Cn_28Msdiv 27Sace agāraṃ āvasati,
vijeyya pathaviṃ imaṃ;
Adaṇḍena asatthena,
dhammena anusāsati.

SC 28Ms 25Cn_29Msdiv 28Sace ca so pabbajati,
Agārā anagāriyaṃ;
Vivaṭṭacchado sambuddho,
Arahā bhavati anuttaro.

SC 29Ms 25Cn_30Msdiv 29Jātiṃ gottañca lakkhaṇaṃ,
mante sisse punāpare;
Muddhaṃ muddhādhipātañca,
manasāyeva pucchatha.

SC 30Ms 25Cn_31Msdiv 30Anāvaraṇadassāvī,
yadi buddho bhavissati;
Manasā pucchite pañhe,
vācāya visajjessati”.

SC 31Ms 25Cn_32Msdiv 31Bāvarissa vaco sutvā,
sissā soḷasa brāhmaṇā;
Ajito tissametteyyo,
puṇṇako atha mettagū.

SC 32Ms 25Cn_33Msdiv 32Dhotako upasīvo ca,
nando ca atha hemako;
Todeyyakappā dubhayo,
jatukaṇṇī ca paṇḍito.

SC 33Ms 25Cn_34Msdiv 33Bhadrāvudho udayo ca,
posālo cāpi brāhmaṇo;
Mogharājā ca medhāvī,
piṅgiyo ca mahāisi.

SC 34Ms 25Cn_35Msdiv 34Paccekagaṇino sabbe,
sabbalokassa vissutā;
Jhāyī jhānaratā dhīrā,
pubbavāsanavāsitā.

SC 35Ms 25Cn_36Msdiv 35Bāvariṃ abhivādetvā,
katvā ca naṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Jaṭājinadharā sabbe,
pakkāmuṃ uttarāmukhā.

SC 36Ms 25Cn_37Msdiv 36Maḷakassa patiṭṭhānaṃ,
puramāhissatiṃ puramāhissatiṃ tadā;
Ujjeniñcāpi gonaddhaṃ,
vedisaṃ vanasavhayaṃ.

SC 37Ms 25Cn_38Msdiv 37Kosambiñcāpi sāketaṃ,
sāvatthiñca puruttamaṃ;
Setabyaṃ kapilavatthuṃ,
kusinārañca mandiraṃ.

SC 38Ms 25Cn_39Msdiv 38Pāvañca bhoganagaraṃ,
vesāliṃ māgadhaṃ puraṃ;
Pāsāṇakaṃ cetiyañca,
ramaṇīyaṃ manoramaṃ.

SC 39Ms 25Cn_40Msdiv 39Tasitovudakaṃ sītaṃ,
mahālābhaṃva vāṇijo;
Chāyaṃ ghammābhitattova,
turitā pabbatamāruhuṃ.

SC 40Ms 25Cn_41Msdiv 40Bhagavā tamhi samaye,
bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato;
Bhikkhūnaṃ dhammaṃ deseti,
sīhova nadatī vane.

SC 41Ms 25Cn_42Msdiv 41Ajito addasa buddhaṃ,
Pītaraṃsiṃva Pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Candaṃ yathā pannarase,
Paripūraṃ upāgataṃ.

SC 42Ms 25Cn_43Msdiv 42Athassa gatte disvāna,
paripūrañca byañjanaṃ;
Ekamantaṃ ṭhito haṭṭho,
manopañhe apucchatha.

SC 43Ms 25Cn_44Msdiv 43“Ādissa jammanaṃ brūhi,
gottaṃ brūhi salakkhaṇaṃ;
Mantesu pāramiṃ brūhi,
kati vāceti brāhmaṇo”.

SC 44Ms 25Cn_45Msdiv 44“Vīsaṃ vassasataṃ āyu,
so ca gottena bāvarī;
Tīṇissa lakkhaṇā gatte,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū.

SC 45Ms 25Cn_46Msdiv 45Lakkhaṇe itihāse ca,
sanighaṇḍusakeṭubhe;
Pañcasatāni vāceti,
sadhamme pāramiṃ gato”.

SC 46Ms 25Cn_47Msdiv 46“Lakkhaṇānaṃ pavicayaṃ,
bāvarissa naruttama;
Taṇhacchida pakāsehi,
mā no kaṅkhāyitaṃ ahu”.

SC 47Ms 25Cn_48Msdiv 47“Mukhaṃ jivhāya chādeti,
uṇṇassa bhamukantare;
Kosohitaṃ vatthaguyhaṃ,
evaṃ jānāhi māṇava”.

SC 48Ms 25Cn_49Msdiv 48Pucchañhi kiñci asuṇanto,
sutvā pañhe viyākate;
Vicinteti jano sabbo,
vedajāto katañjalī.

SC 49Ms 25Cn_50Msdiv 49“Ko nu devo vā brahmā vā,
indo vāpi sujampati;
Manasā pucchite pañhe,
kametaṃ paṭibhāsati”.

SC 50Ms 25Cn_51Msdiv 50“Muddhaṃ muddhādhipātañca,
bāvarī paripucchati;
Taṃ byākarohi bhagavā,
kaṅkhaṃ vinaya no ise”.

SC 51Ms 25Cn_52Msdiv 51“Avijjā muddhāti jānāhi,
vijjā muddhādhipātinī;
Saddhāsatisamādhīhi,
chandavīriyena saṃyutā”.

SC 52Ms 25Cn_53Msdiv 52Tato vedena mahatā,
santhambhetvāna māṇavo;
Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,
pādesu sirasā pati.

SC 53Ms 25Cn_54Msdiv 53“Bāvarī brāhmaṇo bhoto,
saha sissehi mārisa;
Udaggacitto sumano,
pāde vandati cakkhuma”.

SC 54Ms 25Cn_55Msdiv 54“Sukhito bāvarī hotu,
saha sissehi brāhmaṇo;
Tvañcāpi sukhito hohi,
ciraṃ jīvāhi māṇava.

SC 55Ms 25Cn_56Msdiv 55Bāvarissa ca tuyhaṃ vā,
sabbesaṃ sabbasaṃsayaṃ;
Katāvakāsā pucchavho,
yaṃ kiñci manasicchatha”.

SC 56Ms 25Cn_57Msdiv 56Sambuddhena katokāso,
nisīditvāna pañjalī;
Ajito paṭhamaṃ pañhaṃ,
tattha pucchi tathāgataṃ.

Ms 25Cn_58Vatthugāthā niṭṭhitā.
