5. Sukkātherīapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap2_1323Msdiv 111“Ekanavutito kappe,
vipassī nāma nāyako;
Uppajji cārudassano,
sabbadhammavipassako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_1324Msdiv 112Tadāhaṃ bandhumatiyaṃ,
jātā aññatare kule;
Dhammaṃ sutvāna munino,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_1325Msdiv 113Bahussutā dhammadharā,
paṭibhānavatī tathā;
Vicittakathikā cāpi,
jinasāsanakārikā.

SC 4PTS vp Pali 2.606Ms 20Ap2_1326Msdiv 114Tadā dhammakathaṃ katvā,
hitāya janataṃ bahuṃ;
Tato cutāhaṃ tusitaṃ,
upapannā yasassinī.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_1327Msdiv 115Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
sikhī viya sikhī jino;
Tapanto yasasā loke,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_1328Msdiv 116Tadāpi pabbajitvāna,
buddhasāsanakovidā;
Jotetvā jinavākyāni,
tatopi tidivaṃ gatā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_1329Msdiv 117Ekattiṃseva kappamhi,
vessabhū nāma nāyako;
Uppajjittha mahāñāṇī,
tadāpi ca tathevahaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_1330Msdiv 118Pabbajitvā dhammadharā,
jotayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ;
Gantvā marupuraṃ rammaṃ,
anubhosiṃ mahāsukhaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_1331Msdiv 119Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
kakusandho jinuttamo;
Uppajji narasaraṇo,
tadāpi ca tathevahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_1332Msdiv 120Pabbajitvā munimataṃ,
jotayitvā yathāyukaṃ;
Tato cutāhaṃ tidivaṃ,
agaṃ sabhavanaṃ yathā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_1333Msdiv 121Imasmiṃyeva kappamhi,
koṇāgamananāyako;
Uppajji lokasaraṇo,
araṇo amataṅgato.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_1334Msdiv 122Tadāpi pabbajitvāna,
sāsane tassa tādino;
Bahussutā dhammadharā,
jotayiṃ jinasāsanaṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_1335Msdiv 123Imasmiṃyeva kappamhi,
kassapo munimuttamo;
Uppajji lokasaraṇo,
araṇo maraṇantagū.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_1336Msdiv 124Tassāpi naravīrassa,
pabbajitvāna sāsane;
Pariyāpuṭasaddhammā,
paripucchā visāradā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap2_1337Msdiv 125Susīlā lajjinī ceva,
tīsu sikkhāsu kovidā;
Bahuṃ dhammakathaṃ katvā,
yāvajīvaṃ mahāmune.

SC 16PTS vp Pali 2.607Ms 20Ap2_1338Msdiv 126Tena kammavipākena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap2_1339Msdiv 127Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
giribbajapuruttame;
Jātā seṭṭhikule phīte,
mahāratanasañcaye.

SC 18Ms 20Ap2_1340Msdiv 128Yadā bhikkhusahassena,
parivuto lokanāyako;
Upāgami rājagahaṃ,
sahassakkhena vaṇṇito.

SC 19Ms 20Ap2_1341Msdiv 129Danto dantehi saha purāṇajaṭilehi,
Vippamutto vippamuttehi;
Siṅgīnikkhasavaṇṇo,
Rājagahaṃ pāvisi bhagavā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap2_1342Msdiv 130Disvā buddhānubhāvaṃ taṃ,
sutvāva guṇasañcayaṃ;
Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,
pūjayiṃ taṃ yathābalaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap2_1343Msdiv 131Aparena ca kālena,
dhammadinnāya santike;
Agārā nikkhamitvāna,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap2_1344Msdiv 132Kesesu chijjamānesu,
kilese jhāpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Uggahiṃ sāsanaṃ sabbaṃ,
pabbajitvā cirenahaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap2_1345Msdiv 133Tato dhammamadesesiṃ,
mahājanasamāgame;
Dhamme desiyamānamhi,
dhammābhisamayo ahu.

SC 24Ms 20Ap2_1346Msdiv 134Nekapāṇasahassānaṃ,
taṃ viditvātivimhito;
Abhippasanno me yakkho,
bhamitvāna giribbajaṃ.

SC 25Ms 20Ap2_1347Msdiv 135Kiṃ me katā rājagahe manussā,
Madhuṃ pītāva acchare;
Ye sukkaṃ na upāsanti,
Desentiṃ amataṃ padaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap2_1348Msdiv 136Tañca appaṭivānīyaṃ,
asecanakamojavaṃ;
Pivanti maññe sappaññā,
valāhakamivaddhagū.

SC 27Ms 20Ap2_1349Msdiv 137Iddhīsu ca vasī homi,
dibbāya sotadhātuyā;
Cetopariyañāṇassa,
vasī homi mahāmune.

SC 28Ms 20Ap2_1350Msdiv 138Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 29PTS vp Pali 2.608Ms 20Ap2_1351Msdiv 139Atthadhammaniruttīsu,
paṭibhāne tatheva ca;
Ñāṇaṃ mama mahāvīra,
uppannaṃ tava santike.

SC 30Ms 20Ap2_1352Msdiv 140Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavā.

SC 31Ms 20Ap2_1353Msdiv 141Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap2_1354Msdiv 142Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 33Ms 20Ap2_1355Itthaṃ sudaṃ sukkā bhikkhunī imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 34Ms 20Ap2_1356Sukkātheriyāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
Pañcamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
