1 Kuṇḍalakesātherīapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap2_694Msdiv 1 “Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_695Msdiv 2Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
jātā seṭṭhikule ahuṃ;
Nānāratanapajjote,
mahāsukhasamappitā.

SC 3PTS vp Pali 2.561Ms 20Ap2_696Msdiv 3Upetvā taṃ mahāvīraṃ,
assosiṃ dhammadesanaṃ;
Tato jātappasādāhaṃ,
upesiṃ saraṇaṃ jinaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_697Msdiv 4Tadā mahākāruṇiko,
padumuttaranāmako;
Khippābhiññānamagganti,
ṭhapesi bhikkhuniṃ subhaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_698Msdiv 5Taṃ sutvā muditā hutvā,
dānaṃ datvā mahesino;
Nipacca sirasā pāde,
taṃ ṭhānamabhipatthayiṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_699Msdiv 6Anumodi mahāvīro,
‘bhadde yaṃ tebhipatthitaṃ;
Samijjhissati taṃ sabbaṃ,
sukhinī hohi nibbutā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_700Msdiv 7Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_701Msdiv 8Tassa dhammesu dāyādā,
orasā dhammanimmitā;
Bhaddākuṇḍalakesāti,
hessati satthu sāvikā’.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_702Msdiv 9Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_703Msdiv 10Tato cutā yāmamagaṃ,
tatohaṃ tusitaṃ gatā;
Tato ca nimmānaratiṃ,
vasavattipuraṃ tato.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_704Msdiv 11Yattha yatthūpapajjāmi,
tassa kammassa vāhasā;
Tattha tattheva rājūnaṃ,
mahesittamakārayiṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_705Msdiv 12Tato cutā manussesu,
rājūnaṃ cakkavattinaṃ;
Maṇḍalīnañca rājūnaṃ,
mahesittamakārayiṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_706Msdiv 13Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
devesu mānusesu ca;
Sabbattha sukhitā hutvā,
nekakappesu saṃsariṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_707Msdiv 14Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 15Ms 20Ap2_708Msdiv 15Upaṭṭhāko mahesissa,
tadā āsi narissaro;
Kāsirājā kikī nāma,
bārāṇasipuruttame.

SC 16Ms 20Ap2_709Msdiv 16Tassa dhītā catutthāsiṃ,
bhikkhudāyīti vissutā;
Dhammaṃ sutvā jinaggassa,
pabbajjaṃ samarocayiṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap2_710Msdiv 17Anujāni na no tāto,
agāreva tadā mayaṃ;
Vīsavassasahassāni,
vicarimha atanditā.

SC 18Ms 20Ap2_711Msdiv 18Komāribrahmacariyaṃ,
rājakaññā sukhedhitā;
Buddhopaṭṭhānaniratā,
muditā satta dhītaro.

SC 19Ms 20Ap2_712Msdiv 19Samaṇī samaṇaguttā ca,
bhikkhunī bhikkhudāyikā;
Dhammā ceva sudhammā ca,
sattamī saṃghadāyikā.

SC 20PTS vp Pali 2.562Ms 20Ap2_713Msdiv 20Khemā uppalavaṇṇā ca,
paṭācārā ahaṃ tadā;
Kisāgotamī dhammadinnā,
visākhā hoti sattamī.

SC 21Ms 20Ap2_714Msdiv 21Tehi kammehi sukatehi,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 22Ms 20Ap2_715Msdiv 22Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
giribbajapuruttame;
Jātā seṭṭhikule phīte,
yadāhaṃ yobbane ṭhitā.

SC 23Ms 20Ap2_716Msdiv 23Coraṃ vadhatthaṃ nīyantaṃ,
disvā rattā tahiṃ ahaṃ;
Pitā me taṃ sahassena,
mocayitvā vadhā tato.

SC 24Ms 20Ap2_717Msdiv 24Adāsi tassa maṃ tāto,
viditvāna manaṃ mama;
Tassāhamāsiṃ visaṭṭhā,
atīva dayitā hitā.

SC 25Ms 20Ap2_718Msdiv 25So me bhūsanalobhena,
balimajjhāsayo diso;
Corappapātaṃ netvāna,
pabbataṃ cetayī vadhaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap2_719Msdiv 26Tadāhaṃ paṇamitvāna,
sattukaṃ sukatañjalī;
Rakkhantī attano pāṇaṃ,
idaṃ vacanamabraviṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap2_720Msdiv 27‘Idaṃ suvaṇṇakeyūraṃ,
muttā veḷuriyā bahū;
Sabbaṃ harassu bhaddante,
mañca dāsīti sāvaya’.

SC 28Ms 20Ap2_721Msdiv 28‘Oropayassu kalyāṇī,
mā bāḷhaṃ paridevasi;
Na cāhaṃ abhijānāmi,
ahantvā dhanamābhataṃ’.

SC 29Ms 20Ap2_722Msdiv 29‘Yato sarāmi attānaṃ,
yato pattosmi viññutaṃ;
Na cāhaṃ abhijānāmi,
aññaṃ piyataraṃ tayā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap2_723Msdiv 30Ehi taṃ upagūhissaṃ,
katvāna taṃ padakkhiṇaṃ;
Na ca dāni puno atthi,
mama tuyhañca saṅgamo’.

SC 31Ms 20Ap2_724Msdiv 31‘Na hi sabbesu ṭhānesu,
puriso hoti paṇḍito;
Itthīpi paṇḍitā hoti,
tattha tattha vicakkhaṇā.

SC 32Ms 20Ap2_725Msdiv 32Na hi sabbesu ṭhānesu,
puriso hoti paṇḍito;
Itthīpi paṇḍitā hoti,
lahuṃ atthavicintikā’.

SC 33PTS vp Pali 2.563Ms 20Ap2_726Msdiv 33Lahuñca vata khippañca,
nikaṭṭhe samacetayiṃ;
Migaṃ uṇṇā yathā evaṃ,
tadāhaṃ sattukaṃ vadhiṃ.

SC 34Ms 20Ap2_727Msdiv 34Yo ca uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
na khippamanubujjhati;
So haññate mandamati,
corova girigabbhare.

SC 35Ms 20Ap2_728Msdiv 35Yo ca uppatitaṃ atthaṃ,
khippameva nibodhati;
Muccate sattusambādhā,
tadāhaṃ sattukā yathā.

SC 36Ms 20Ap2_729Msdiv 36Tadāhaṃ pātayitvāna,
giriduggamhi sattukaṃ;
Santikaṃ setavatthānaṃ,
upetvā pabbajiṃ ahaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap2_730Msdiv 37Saṇḍāsena ca kese me,
luñcitvā sabbaso tadā;
Pabbajitvāna samayaṃ,
ācikkhiṃsu nirantaraṃ.

SC 38Ms 20Ap2_731Msdiv 38Tato taṃ uggahetvāhaṃ,
nisīditvāna ekikā;
Samayaṃ taṃ vicintesiṃ,
suvāno mānusaṃ karaṃ.

SC 39Ms 20Ap2_732Msdiv 39Chinnaṃ gayha samīpe me,
pātayitvā apakkami;
Disvā nimittamalabhiṃ,
hatthaṃ taṃ puḷavākulaṃ.

SC 40Ms 20Ap2_733Msdiv 40Tato uṭṭhāya saṃviggā,
apucchiṃ sahadhammike;
Te avocuṃ ‘vijānanti,
taṃ atthaṃ sakyabhikkhavo’.

SC 41Ms 20Ap2_734Msdiv 41Sāhaṃ tamatthaṃ pucchissaṃ,
upetvā buddhasāvake;
Te mamādāya gacchiṃsu,
buddhaseṭṭhassa santikaṃ.

SC 42Ms 20Ap2_735Msdiv 42So me dhammamadesesi,
khandhāyatanadhātuyo;
Asubhāniccadukkhāti,
anattāti ca nāyako.

SC 43Ms 20Ap2_736Msdiv 43Tassa dhammaṃ suṇitvāhaṃ,
dhammacakkhuṃ visodhayiṃ;
Tato viññātasaddhammā,
pabbajjaṃ upasampadaṃ.

SC 44Ms 20Ap2_737Msdiv 44Āyācito tadā āha,
‘ehi bhadde’ti nāyako;
Tadāhaṃ upasampannā,
parittaṃ toyamaddasaṃ.

SC 45PTS vp Pali 2.564Ms 20Ap2_738Msdiv 45Pādapakkhālanenāhaṃ,
ñatvā saudayabbayaṃ;
Tathā sabbepi saṅkhāre,
īdisaṃ cintayiṃ tadā.

SC 46Ms 20Ap2_739Msdiv 46Tato cittaṃ vimucci me,
anupādāya sabbaso;
Khippābhiññānamaggaṃ me,
tadā paññāpayī jino.

SC 47Ms 20Ap2_740Msdiv 47Iddhīsu ca vasī homi,
dibbāya sotadhātuyā;
Paracittāni jānāmi,
satthusāsanakārikā.

SC 48Ms 20Ap2_741Msdiv 48Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ;
Khepetvā āsave sabbe,
visuddhāsiṃ sunimmalā.

SC 49Ms 20Ap2_742Msdiv 49Pariciṇṇo mayā satthā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ;
Ohito garuko bhāro,
bhavanetti samūhatā.

SC 50Ms 20Ap2_743Msdiv 50Yassatthāya pabbajitā,
agārasmānagāriyaṃ;
So me attho anuppatto,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayo.

SC 51Ms 20Ap2_744Msdiv 51Atthadhammaniruttīsu,
paṭibhāne tatheva ca;
Ñāṇaṃ me vimalaṃ suddhaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhassa sāsane.

SC 52Ms 20Ap2_745Msdiv 52Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgīva bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāmi anāsavā.

SC 53Ms 20Ap2_746Msdiv 53Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 54Ms 20Ap2_747Msdiv 54Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 55Ms 20Ap2_748Itthaṃ sudaṃ bhaddākuṇḍalakesā bhikkhunī imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 56Ms 20Ap2_749Kuṇḍalakesātheriyāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
