7 Aḍḍhakāsitherīapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap2_1384Msdiv 168 “Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_1385Msdiv 169Tadāhaṃ pabbajitvāna,
tassa buddhassa sāsane;
Saṃvutā pātimokkhamhi,
indriyesu ca pañcasu.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_1386Msdiv 170Mattaññunī ca asane,
yuttā jāgariyepi ca;
Vasantī yuttayogāhaṃ,
bhikkhuniṃ vigatāsavaṃ.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_1387Msdiv 171Akkosiṃ duṭṭhacittāhaṃ,
gaṇiketi bhaṇiṃ tadā;
Tena pāpena kammena,
nirayamhi apaccisaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_1388Msdiv 172Tena kammāvasesena,
ajāyiṃ gaṇikākule;
Bahusova parādhīnā,
pacchimāya ca jātiyaṃ.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_1389Msdiv 173Kāsīsu seṭṭhikulajā,
brahmacārībalenahaṃ;
Accharā viya devesu,
ahosiṃ rūpasampadā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_1390Msdiv 174Disvāna dassanīyaṃ maṃ,
giribbajapuruttame;
Gaṇikatte nivesesuṃ,
akkosanabalena me.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_1391Msdiv 175Sāhaṃ sutvāna saddhammaṃ,
buddhaseṭṭhena desitaṃ;
Pubbavāsanasampannā,
pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_1392Msdiv 176Tadūpasampadatthāya,
gacchantī jinasantikaṃ;
Magge dhutte ṭhite sutvā,
labhiṃ dūtopasampadaṃ.

SC 10PTS vp Pali 2.611Ms 20Ap2_1393Msdiv 177Sabbakammaṃ parikkhīṇaṃ,
puññaṃ pāpaṃ tatheva ca;
Sabbasaṃsāramuttiṇṇā,
gaṇikattañca khepitaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_1394Msdiv 178Iddhīsu ca vasī homi,
dibbāya sotadhātuyā;
Cetopariyañāṇassa,
vasī homi mahāmune.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_1395Msdiv 179Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_1396Msdiv 180Atthadhammaniruttīsu,
paṭibhāne tatheva ca;
Ñāṇaṃ mama mahāvīra,
uppannaṃ tava santike.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_1397Msdiv 181Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavā.

SC 15Ms 20Ap2_1398Msdiv 182Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 16Ms 20Ap2_1399Msdiv 183Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 17Ms 20Ap2_1400Itthaṃ sudaṃ aḍḍhakāsi bhikkhunī imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 18Ms 20Ap2_1401Aḍḍhakāsitheriyāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
