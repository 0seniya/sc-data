4. Sakulātherīapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap2_831SC 1Ms 20Ap2_831Msdiv 131“Padumuttaro nāma jino,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Ito satasahassamhi,
kappe uppajji nāyako.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_832SC 2Ms 20Ap2_832Msdiv 132Hitāya sabbasattānaṃ,
sukhāya vadataṃ varo;
Atthāya purisājañño,
paṭipanno sadevake.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_833SC 3Ms 20Ap2_833Msdiv 133Yasaggapatto sirimā,
kittivaṇṇagato jino;
Pūjito sabbalokassa,
disāsabbāsu vissuto.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_834SC 4Ms 20Ap2_834Msdiv 134Uttiṇṇavicikiccho so,
vītivattakathaṃkatho;
Sampuṇṇamanasaṅkappo,
patto sambodhimuttamaṃ.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_835SC 5Ms 20Ap2_835Msdiv 135Anuppannassa maggassa,
uppādetā naruttamo;
Anakkhātañca akkhāsi,
asañjātañca sañjanī.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_836SC 6Ms 20Ap2_836Msdiv 136Maggaññū ca maggavidū,
maggakkhāyī narāsabho;
Maggassa kusalo satthā,
sārathīnaṃ varuttamo.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_837SC 7Ms 20Ap2_837Msdiv 137Mahākāruṇiko satthā,
dhammaṃ desesi nāyako;
Nimugge kāmapaṅkamhi,
samuddharati pāṇine.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_838SC 8Ms 20Ap2_838Msdiv 138Tadāhaṃ haṃsavatiyaṃ,
jātā khattiyanandanā;
Surūpā sadhanā cāpi,
dayitā ca sirīmatī.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_839SC 9Ms 20Ap2_839Msdiv 139Ānandassa mahārañño,
dhītā paramasobhaṇā;
Vemātā bhaginī cāpi,
padumuttaranāmino.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_840SC 10Ms 20Ap2_840Msdiv 140Rājakaññāhi sahitā,
sabbābharaṇabhūsitā;
Upāgamma mahāvīraṃ,
assosiṃ dhammadesanaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_841SC 11Ms 20Ap2_841Msdiv 141Tadā hi so lokagaru,
bhikkhuniṃ dibbacakkhukaṃ;
Kittayaṃ parisāmajjhe,
aggaṭṭhāne ṭhapesi taṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_842SC 12Ms 20Ap2_842Msdiv 142Suṇitvā tamahaṃ haṭṭhā,
dānaṃ datvāna satthuno;
Pūjitvāna ca sambuddhaṃ,
dibbacakkhuṃ apatthayiṃ.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_843SC 13Ms 20Ap2_843Msdiv 143Tato avoca maṃ satthā,
‘nande lacchasi patthitaṃ;
Padīpadhammadānānaṃ,
phalametaṃ sunicchitaṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_844SC 14Ms 20Ap2_844Msdiv 144Satasahassito kappe,
okkākakulasambhavo;
Gotamo nāma gottena,
satthā loke bhavissati.

SC 15Ms 20Ap2_845SC 15Ms 20Ap2_845Msdiv 145Tassa dhammesu dāyādā,
orasā dhammanimmitā;
Sakulā nāma nāmena,
hessati satthu sāvikā’.

SC 16Ms 20Ap2_846SC 16Ms 20Ap2_846Msdiv 146Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 17Ms 20Ap2_847SC 17Ms 20Ap2_847Msdiv 147Imamhi bhaddake kappe,
brahmabandhu mahāyaso;
Kassapo nāma gottena,
uppajji vadataṃ varo.

SC 18Ms 20Ap2_848SC 18Ms 20Ap2_848Msdiv 148Paribbājakinī āsiṃ,
tadāhaṃ ekacārinī;
Bhikkhāya vicaritvāna,
alabhiṃ telamattakaṃ.

SC 19Ms 20Ap2_849SC 19Ms 20Ap2_849Msdiv 149Tena dīpaṃ padīpetvā,
upaṭṭhiṃ sabbasaṃvariṃ;
Cetiyaṃ dvipadaggassa,
vippasannena cetasā.

SC 20Ms 20Ap2_850SC 20Ms 20Ap2_850Msdiv 150Tena kammena sukatena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,
tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap2_851SC 21Ms 20Ap2_851Msdiv 151Yattha yatthūpapajjāmi,
tassa kammassa vāhasā;
Pajjalanti mahādīpā,
tattha tattha gatāya me.

SC 22Ms 20Ap2_852SC 22Ms 20Ap2_852Msdiv 152Tirokuṭṭaṃ tiroselaṃ,
samatiggayha pabbataṃ;
Passāmahaṃ yadicchāmi,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 23Ms 20Ap2_853SC 23Ms 20Ap2_853Msdiv 153Visuddhanayanā homi,
yasasā ca jalāmahaṃ;
Saddhāpaññāvatī ceva,
dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 24Ms 20Ap2_854SC 24Ms 20Ap2_854Msdiv 154Pacchime ca bhave dāni,
jātā vippakule ahaṃ;
Pahūtadhanadhaññamhi,
mudite rājapūjite.

SC 25Ms 20Ap2_855SC 25Ms 20Ap2_855Msdiv 155Ahaṃ sabbaṅgasampannā,
sabbābharaṇabhūsitā;
Purappavese sugataṃ,
vātapāne ṭhitā ahaṃ.

SC 26Ms 20Ap2_856SC 26Ms 20Ap2_856Msdiv 156Disvā jalantaṃ yasasā,
devamanussasakkataṃ;
Anubyañjanasampannaṃ,
lakkhaṇehi vibhūsitaṃ.

SC 27Ms 20Ap2_857SC 27Ms 20Ap2_857Msdiv 157Udaggacittā sumanā,
pabbajjaṃ samarocayiṃ;
Na cireneva kālena,
arahattamapāpuṇiṃ.

SC 28Ms 20Ap2_858SC 28Ms 20Ap2_858Msdiv 158Iddhīsu ca vasī homi,
dibbāya sotadhātuyā;
Paracittāni jānāmi,
satthusāsanakārikā.

SC 29Ms 20Ap2_859SC 29Ms 20Ap2_859Msdiv 159Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāmi,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ;
Khepetvā āsave sabbe,
visuddhāsiṃ sunimmalā.

SC 30Ms 20Ap2_860SC 30Ms 20Ap2_860Msdiv 160Pariciṇṇo mayā satthā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ;
Ohito garuko bhāro,
bhavanetti samūhatā.

agārasmānagāriyaṃ;
So me attho anuppatto,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayo.

etadagge ṭhapesi maṃ;
Dibbacakkhukānaṃ aggā,
sakulāti naruttamo.
