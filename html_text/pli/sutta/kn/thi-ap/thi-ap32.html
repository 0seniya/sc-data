2 Caturāsītibhikkhunīsahassaapadāna

Sukhumālahatthapādā,
pure tuyhaṃ mahāmune.

devā nāgā ca kinnarā;

pure tuyhaṃ mahāmune.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_1221SC 3Ms 20Ap2_1221Msdiv 15Kāci pabbajitā atthi,
sabbadassāvino bahū;
Devā ca kinnarā nāgā,
phusissanti anāgate.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_1222SC 4Ms 20Ap2_1222Msdiv 16Anubhotvā yasaṃ sabbaṃ,
Patvāna sabbasampadā;
Tumhaṃ pasādaṃ paṭiladdhā,
Bujjhissanti anāgate.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_1223SC 5Ms 20Ap2_1223Msdiv 17Amhe brāhmaṇadhītā tu,
brāhmaññakulasambhavā;
Pekkhato no mahāvīra,
pāde vandāma cakkhuma.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_1224SC 6Ms 20Ap2_1224Msdiv 18Upāhatā bhavā sabbe,
mūlataṇhā samūhatā;
Samucchinnā anusayā,
puññasaṅkhāradālitā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_1225SC 7Ms 20Ap2_1225Msdiv 19Samādhigocarā sabbā,
samāpattivasī katā;
Jhānena dhammaratiyā,
viharissāma no sadā.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_1226SC 8Ms 20Ap2_1226Msdiv 20Bhavanetti avijjā ca,
saṅkhārāpi ca khepitā;
Sududdasaṃ padaṃ gantvā,
anujānātha nāyaka”.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_1227SC 9Ms 20Ap2_1227Msdiv 21“Upakārā mamaṃ tumhe,
dīgharattaṃ katāvino;
Catunnaṃ saṃsayaṃ chetvā,
sabbā gacchantu nibbutiṃ”.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_1228SC 10Ms 20Ap2_1228Msdiv 22Vanditvā munino pāde,
katvā iddhivikubbanaṃ;
Kāci dassenti ālokaṃ,
andhakāramathāparā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_1229SC 11Ms 20Ap2_1229Msdiv 23Dassenti candasūriye,
sāgarañca samacchakaṃ;
Sineruṃ paribhaṇḍañca,
dassenti pārichattakaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_1230SC 12Ms 20Ap2_1230Msdiv 24Tāvatiṃsañca bhavanaṃ,
yāmaṃ dassenti iddhiyā;
Tusitaṃ nimmite deve,
vasavattī mahissare.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_1231SC 13Ms 20Ap2_1231Msdiv 25Brahmāno kāci dassenti,
caṅkamañca mahārahaṃ;
Brahmavaṇṇañca māpetvā,
dhammaṃ desenti suññataṃ.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_1232SC 14Ms 20Ap2_1232Msdiv 26Nānāvikubbanaṃ katvā,
iddhiṃ dassiya satthuno;
Dassayiṃsu balaṃ sabbā,
pāde vandiṃsu satthuno.

SC 15Ms 20Ap2_1233SC 15Ms 20Ap2_1233Msdiv 27“Iddhīsu ca vasī homa,
dibbāya sotadhātuyā;
Cetopariyañāṇassa,
vasī homa mahāmune.

SC 16Ms 20Ap2_1234SC 16Ms 20Ap2_1234Msdiv 28Pubbenivāsaṃ jānāma,
dibbacakkhu visodhitaṃ;
Sabbāsavā parikkhīṇā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo.

SC 17Ms 20Ap2_1235SC 17Ms 20Ap2_1235Msdiv 29Atthadhammaniruttīsu,
paṭibhāne tatheva ca;
Ñāṇaṃ amhaṃ mahāvīra,
uppannaṃ tava santike.

SC 18Ms 20Ap2_1236SC 18Ms 20Ap2_1236Msdiv 30Pubbānaṃ lokanāthānaṃ,
saṅgamaṃ no nidassitaṃ;
Adhikāraṃ bahuṃ amhaṃ,
tuyhatthāya mahāmune.

SC 19Ms 20Ap2_1237SC 19Ms 20Ap2_1237Msdiv 31Yaṃ amhehi kataṃ kammaṃ,
kusalaṃ sara taṃ mune;
Tuyhatthāya mahāvīra,
puññānupacitāni no.

SC 20Ms 20Ap2_1238SC 20Ms 20Ap2_1238Msdiv 32Satasahassito kappe,
padumuttaro mahāmuni;
Puraṃ haṃsavatī nāma,
sambuddhassa kulāsayaṃ.

SC 21Ms 20Ap2_1239SC 21Ms 20Ap2_1239Msdiv 33Dvārena haṃsavatiyā,
gaṅgā sandati sabbadā;
Ubbāḷhā nadiyā bhikkhū,
gamanaṃ na labhanti te.

SC 22Ms 20Ap2_1240SC 22Ms 20Ap2_1240Msdiv 34Divasaṃ dve tayo ceva,
sattāhaṃ māsikaṃ tato;
Catumāsampi sampuṇṇaṃ,
gamanaṃ na labhanti te.

SC 23Ms 20Ap2_1241SC 23Ms 20Ap2_1241Msdiv 35Tadā ahu sattasāro,
jaṭilo nāma raṭṭhiko;
Oruddhe bhikkhavo disvā,
setuṃ gaṅgāya kārayi.

SC 24Ms 20Ap2_1242SC 24Ms 20Ap2_1242Msdiv 36Tadā satasahassehi,
setuṃ gaṅgāya kārayi;
Saṃghassa orime tīre,
vihārañca akārayi.

SC 25Ms 20Ap2_1243SC 25Ms 20Ap2_1243Msdiv 37Itthiyo purisā ceva,
uccanīcakulāni ca;
Tassa setuṃ vihārañca,
samabhāgaṃ akaṃsu te.

SC 26Ms 20Ap2_1244SC 26Ms 20Ap2_1244Msdiv 38Amhe aññe ca mānujā,
vippasannena cetasā;
Tassa dhammesu dāyādā,
nagare janapadesu ca.

SC 27Ms 20Ap2_1245SC 27Ms 20Ap2_1245Msdiv 39Itthī pumā kumārā ca,
bahū ceva kumārikā;
Setuno ca vihārassa,
vālukā ākiriṃsu te.

SC 28Ms 20Ap2_1246SC 28Ms 20Ap2_1246Msdiv 40Vīthiṃ sammajjanaṃ katvā,
kadalīpuṇṇaghaṭe dhaje;
Dhūpaṃ cuṇṇañca mālañca,
kāraṃ katvāna satthuno.

SC 29Ms 20Ap2_1247SC 29Ms 20Ap2_1247Msdiv 41Setuvihāre kāretvā,
nimantetvā vināyakaṃ;
Mahādānaṃ daditvāna,
sambodhiṃ abhipatthayiṃ.

SC 30Ms 20Ap2_1248SC 30Ms 20Ap2_1248Msdiv 42Padumuttaro mahāvīro,
tārako sabbapāṇinaṃ;
Anumodanīyaṃkāsi,
jaṭilassa mahāmuni.

SC 31Ms 20Ap2_1249SC 31Ms 20Ap2_1249Msdiv 43‘Satasahassātikkante,
kappo hessati bhaddako;
Bhavābhavenubhotvāna,
pāpuṇissati bodhiyaṃ.

SC 32Ms 20Ap2_1250SC 32Ms 20Ap2_1250Msdiv 44Kāci hatthaparikammaṃ,
katāvī naranāriyo;
Anāgatamhi addhāne,
sabbā hessanti sammukhā’.

SC 33Ms 20Ap2_1251SC 33Ms 20Ap2_1251Msdiv 45Tena kammavipākena,
cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;
Uppannā devabhavanaṃ,
tuyhaṃ tā paricārikā.

SC 34Ms 20Ap2_1252SC 34Ms 20Ap2_1252Msdiv 46Dibbasukhaṃ asaṅkhiyaṃ,
mānusañca asaṅkhiyaṃ;
Tuyhaṃ te paricārema,
saṃsarimha bhavābhave.

SC 35Ms 20Ap2_1253SC 35Ms 20Ap2_1253Msdiv 47Satasahassito kappe,
sukataṃ kammasampadaṃ;
Sukhumālī manussānaṃ,
atho devapure vare.

SC 36Ms 20Ap2_1254SC 36Ms 20Ap2_1254Msdiv 48Rūpabhogayase ceva,
atho kittiñca sakkataṃ;
Labhāma satataṃ sabbaṃ,
sukataṃ kammasampadaṃ.

SC 37Ms 20Ap2_1255SC 37Ms 20Ap2_1255Msdiv 49Pacchime bhave sampatte,
jātāmha brāhmaṇe kule;
Sukhumālahatthapādā,
sakyaputtanivesane.

SC 38Ms 20Ap2_1256SC 38Ms 20Ap2_1256Msdiv 50Sabbakālampi pathaviṃ,
na passāma na laṅkataṃ;
