1 Yasavatīpamukhaaṭṭhārasabhikkhunīsahassaapadāna

SC 1PTS vp Pali 2.597Ms 20Ap2_1205Msdiv 1“Bhavā sabbe parikkhīṇā,
bhavā santi vimocitā;
Sabbāsavā ca no natthi,
ārocema mahāmune.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_1206Msdiv 2Purimaṃ kusalaṃ kammaṃ,
yaṃ kiñci sādhupatthitaṃ;
Paribhogamayaṃ dinnaṃ,
tuyhatthāya mahāmune.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_1207Msdiv 3Buddhapaccekabuddhānaṃ,
sāvakānañca patthitaṃ;
Paribhogamayaṃ dinnaṃ,
tuyhatthāya mahāmune.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_1208Msdiv 4Uccanīcamayaṃ kammaṃ,
bhikkhūnaṃ sādhupatthitaṃ;
Uccākulaparikammaṃ,
katametaṃ mahāmune.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_1209Msdiv 5Teneva sukkamūlena,
coditā kammasampadā;
Mānusikamatikkantā,
jāyiṃsu khattiye kule.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_1210Msdiv 6Uppatte ca kate kamme,
jātiyā vāpi ekato;
Pacchime ekato jātā,
khattiyā kulasambhavā.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_1211Msdiv 7Rūpavatī bhogavatī,
lābhasakkārapūjitā;
Antepure mahāvīra,
devānaṃ viya nandane.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_1212Msdiv 8Nibbinditvā agāramhā,
pabbajimhanagāriyaṃ;
Katipāhaṃ upādāya,
sabbā pattāmha nibbutiṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_1213Msdiv 9Cīvaraṃ piṇḍapātañca,
paccayaṃ sayanāsanaṃ;
Upanenti bahū amhe,
sadā sakkatapūjitā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_1214Msdiv 10Kilesā jhāpitā amhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Nāgīva bandhanaṃ chetvā,
viharāma anāsavā.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_1215Msdiv 11Svāgataṃ vata no āsi,
mama buddhassa santike;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_1216Msdiv 12Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_1217Itthaṃ sudaṃ yasavatīpamukhāni khattiyakaññābhikkhuniyo aṭṭhārasasahassāni bhagavato sammukhā imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 14Ms 20Ap2_1218Yasavatīpamukhaaṭṭhārasabhikkhunīsahassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
