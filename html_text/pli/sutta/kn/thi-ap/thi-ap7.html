7 Kaṭacchubhikkhādāyikātherīapadāna

SC 1Ms 20Ap2_74Msdiv 60 “Piṇḍacāraṃ carantassa,
tissanāmassa satthuno;
Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ paggayha,
buddhaseṭṭhassa dāsahaṃ.

SC 2Ms 20Ap2_75Msdiv 61Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Vīthiyā saṇṭhito satthā,
akā me anumodanaṃ.

SC 3Ms 20Ap2_76Msdiv 62‘Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ datvāna,
tāvatiṃsaṃ gamissasi;
Chattiṃsadevarājūnaṃ,
mahesittaṃ karissasi.

SC 4Ms 20Ap2_77Msdiv 63Paññāsaṃ cakkavattīnaṃ,
mahesittaṃ karissasi;
Manasā patthitaṃ sabbaṃ,
paṭilacchasi sabbadā.

SC 5Ms 20Ap2_78Msdiv 64Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,
pabbajissasikiñcanā;
Sabbāsave pariññāya,
nibbāyissasināsavā’.

SC 6Ms 20Ap2_79Msdiv 65Idaṃ vatvāna sambuddho,
tisso lokagganāyako;
Nabhaṃ abbhuggamī vīro,
haṃsarājāva ambare.

SC 7Ms 20Ap2_80Msdiv 66Sudinnaṃ me dānavaraṃ,
suyiṭṭhā yāgasampadā;
Kaṭacchubhikkhaṃ datvāna,
pattāhaṃ acalaṃ padaṃ.

SC 8Ms 20Ap2_81Msdiv 67Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bhikkhādānassidaṃ phalaṃ.

SC 9Ms 20Ap2_82Msdiv 68Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavā.

SC 10Ms 20Ap2_83Msdiv 69Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

SC 11Ms 20Ap2_84Msdiv 70Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

SC 12Ms 20Ap2_85Itthaṃ sudaṃ kaṭacchubhikkhādāyikā bhikkhunī imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

SC 13Ms 20Ap2_86Kaṭacchubhikkhādāyikātheriyāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
