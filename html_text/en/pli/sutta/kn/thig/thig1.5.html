Therīgāthā

Canto 1

5. Another Sister Tissā

“Tissā! lay well upon thy heart the yoke
Of noblest culture. See the moment come!
Let it not pass thee by! for many they
Who mourn in misery that moment past.”

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
