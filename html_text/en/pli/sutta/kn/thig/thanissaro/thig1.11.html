Therīgāthā

Chapter 1

11. Mutta

So freed! So thoroughly freed am I! —
from three crooked things set free:
from mortar, pestle,
& crooked old husband.
Having uprooted the craving
that leads to becoming,
I'm set free from aging & death.

Translated from the Pali by: Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
