Therīgāthā

Canto 2

2. Jentī or Jentā

“The Seven Factors of the awakened mind—
Seven ways whereby we may Nibbana win—
All, all have I developed and made ripe,
Even according to the Buddha’s word.

For I therein have seen as with mine eyes
The Bless’d, the Exalted One. Last of all lives
Is this that makes up Me. The round of births
Is vanquishèd—Ne’er shall I be again!”

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
