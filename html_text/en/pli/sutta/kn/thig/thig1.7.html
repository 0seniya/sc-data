Therīgāthā

Canto 1

7. Another Sister Dhīrā

“Dhīrā, brave Sister! who hath valiantly
Thy faculties in noblest culture trained,
Bear to this end thy last incarnate frame,
For thou hast conquered Māra and his host.”

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
