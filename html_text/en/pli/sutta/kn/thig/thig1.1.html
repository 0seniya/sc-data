Therīgāthā

Canto 1

1. A Bhikkhunī of Name Unknown

Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

“Sleep softly, little Sturdy, take thy rest
At ease, wrapt in the robe thyself hast made.
Stilled are the passions that would rage within,
Withered as potherbs in the oven dried.

Verse uttered by a certain Sister, a Bhikkhunī of Name Unknown

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
