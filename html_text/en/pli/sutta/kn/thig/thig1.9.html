Therīgāthā

Canto 1

9. Bhadrā

“Bhadrā, who camest forth convinced in heart,
To sure felicity, O fortunate!
That heart devote. Develop all that’s good,
Faring to uttermost Security.”

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
