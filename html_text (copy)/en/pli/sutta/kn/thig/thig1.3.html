Therīgāthā

Canto 1

3. Puṇṇā

“Fill up, Puṇṇā, the orb of holy life,
E’en as on fifteenth day the full-orb’d moon.
Fill full the perfect knowledge of the Path,
And scatter all the gloom of ignorance.”

This translation of the Therīgāthā is by Caroline A.F. Rhys Davids and was published under the title Psalms of the Sisters, 1909.

The text is in the public domain.
